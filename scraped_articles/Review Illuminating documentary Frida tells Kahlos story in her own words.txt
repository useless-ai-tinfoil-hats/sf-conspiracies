Review: Illuminating documentary ‘Frida’ tells Kahlo’s story in her own words
-----
Among the many things we learn about Mexican artist Frida Kahlo in the elegant, intimate documentary "Frida" is that she was not particularly fond of the United States. Although her artist husband, Diego Rivera, preferred the U.S. to Mexico, Kahlo found Rivera's rich American patrons vulgar and easily impressed.This makes you wonder what Kahlo, who died in 1954, would think of her unibrow's punctuation of American pop culture over the past several decades.
