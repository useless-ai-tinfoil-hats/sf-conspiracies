Oakland police investigate shooting that injured one
-----
One person was injured early Saturday in an Oakland shooting, police said.Officers first learned of the shooting through a ShotSpotter activation just after 3:30 a.m. on the 900 block of Stanford Avenue, police said. After the ShotSpotter alert, the Police Department received a call about a shooting in the same location, a spokesperson said.
Officers arrived at the scene and found one victim who had been shot at least once, police said. The victim was treated at the scene,
