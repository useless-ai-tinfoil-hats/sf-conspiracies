United Airlines Boeing 737 Max returns to San Francisco after suffering bird strike
-----
A United Airlines flight bound for Denver had to return to the gate at San Francisco International Airport over the weekend due to a bird strike that damaged the pilot's side window shortly after takeoff, the Federal Aviation Administration said.The aircraft involved in the incident, a Boeing 737-9 MAX, had ascended to approximately 5,000 feet after departing at 5:04 a.m. on Saturday. It safely landed about 25 minutes post-departure.
