Body of teenager who went missing in Russian River found
-----
LATEST April 13, 10 a.m. A teenager who was reported missing in the waters of the Russian River Steelhead Beach Regional Park in Forestville was found dead on Friday, the Sonoma County Sheriff's Office said.The Sheriff's Office initially responded to a report of two teens who went missing in the water at 4:40 p.m. on April 11, according to a news release Friday.
