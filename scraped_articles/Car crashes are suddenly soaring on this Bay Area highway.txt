Car crashes are suddenly soaring on this Bay Area highway
-----
With its winding curves, steep grades and heavy traffic, Highway 17 across the Santa Cruz Mountains is no stranger to traffic collisions. But when it rains, as it has Sunday and Monday, the number of crashes climbs.By early afternoon Saturday, traffic reports indicated 10 collisions had taken place in various locations of Highway 17, which connects the San Jose area and Santa Cruz and is heavily traveled by beachgoers and commuters alike.
