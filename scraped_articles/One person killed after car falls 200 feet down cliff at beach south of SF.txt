One person killed after car falls 200 feet down cliff at beach south of SF
-----
One person died Thursday afternoon after their vehicle fell 200 feet down an embankment at a beach in San Mateo County, Cecile Juliette, a spokesperson for Cal Fire, told SFGATE.The car fell over the cliff at Gray Whale Cove State Beach in Montara, officials said. Emergency crews were dispatched to the scene at approximately 1 p.m. and closed all except one lane on Highway 1, Juliette said.
