S.F. election: Prop. C office-conversion measure too close to call
-----
Mayor London Breed's ballot proposal to make it more profitable for developers to turn empty downtown San Francisco offices into housing was too close to call Thursday afternoon.With 37,000 ballots left to count, Proposition C led with 52.79% of the vote as of Friday afternoon.
The measure would exempt office-to-housing conversion projects from the city's real estate transfer tax the first time they're sold to new owners. The transfer tax varies from 0.5% to 6%.
