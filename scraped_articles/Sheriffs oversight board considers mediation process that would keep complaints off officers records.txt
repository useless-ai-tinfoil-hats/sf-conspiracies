Sheriff’s oversight board considers mediation process that would keep complaints off officers’ records
-----
The San Francisco Sheriff's Department Oversight Board leaned toward recommending a complaint mediation process that would prevent low-level misconduct claims from appearing on officers' records on Friday.The board met for the first time since appointing Inspector General Terry Wiley, who would need to implement such a process and who will oversee the sheriff's department, which operates two county jails.
