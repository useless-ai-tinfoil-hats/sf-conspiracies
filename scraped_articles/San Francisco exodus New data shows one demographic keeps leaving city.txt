San Francisco exodus: New data shows one demographic keeps leaving city
-----
San Francisco continues to lose residents who haven't attended college, according to newly released census data.Between 2021 and 2022, the number of San Franciscans ages 25 or older with a high school degree or less fell from 144,000 to 142,000, contributing to a 14% decline during the past three years. That's far higher than the city's estimated 8% overall loss of 25 and older adults during that period.
