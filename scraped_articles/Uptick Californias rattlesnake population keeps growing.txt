'Uptick': California's rattlesnake population keeps growing
-----
For anyone who suffers from ophidiophobia, or the fear of snakes, it's probably unsettling to learn that it's rattlesnake mating season in California with the warmer weather.Moreover, experts say we will likely see more of our slithering neighbors every year.
In response to the growing population, biology professor Emily Taylor at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo started the Central Coast Snake Services for Santa Barbara and San Luis Obispo counties.
