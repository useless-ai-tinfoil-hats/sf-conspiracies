San Francisco's How Weird Street Faire to go on after last-minute cancellation
-----
After a last-minute cancellation earlier this month, San Francisco's How Weird Street Faire is set to happen after all, this time with a new date and theme.The event, an annual outdoor party in which people dressed in wild costumes congregate in the South of Market neighborhood to "celebrate peace, creativity, and weirdness," was initially planned for May 4 as part of the theme "Star Weird Day: May the 4th Be With You."
