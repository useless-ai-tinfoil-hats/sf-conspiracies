Group cited for illegally driving over protected species' habitat in Point Reyes National Seashore
-----
Four people were cited for illegally driving a truck off-road at North Beach in Point Reyes National Seashore, posing a risk of crushing the fragile habitat where flocks of threatened western snowy plovers seek refuge in the winter, park officials said in a Friday statement.The group was also cited for additional violations related to alcohol and drug possession, as well as camping and building a fire in the area without a permit, U.S. park ranger Bonnie Phillips told SFGATE in an email
