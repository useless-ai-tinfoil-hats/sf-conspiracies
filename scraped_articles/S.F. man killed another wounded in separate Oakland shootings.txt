S.F. man killed, another wounded in separate Oakland shootings
-----
A San Francisco man was killed and another was wounded in separate weekend Oakland shootings.Just before 10:30 p.m. Friday, officers responded to reports of gunfire in a home on the 1300 block of 102nd Avenue in East Oakland. Paramedics pronounced the victim, a San Francisco man, dead at the scene, and an Oakland resident was taken into custody, the Police Department said.
The identity of the victim is being withheld pending notification of next of kin.
