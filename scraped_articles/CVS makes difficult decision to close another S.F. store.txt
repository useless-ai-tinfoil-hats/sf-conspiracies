CVS makes ‘difficult decision’ to close another S.F. store
-----
CVS Health is set to close another San Francisco store early next year, a company spokesperson said Wednesday.Amy Thibault, a representative for the chain, stated that CVS made the "difficult decision" to shutter the 499 Haight St. location on Jan. 11. This move follows the closure of CVS pharmacies at 601 Mission St. and 1690 Folsom St. earlier this year.
