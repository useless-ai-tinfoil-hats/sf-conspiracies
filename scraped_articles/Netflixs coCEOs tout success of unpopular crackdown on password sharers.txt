Netflix's co-CEOs tout success of unpopular crackdown on password sharers
-----
When Netflix cracked down on subscribers sharing their passwords in February, viewers were furious. Speculation abounded that the Bay Area streaming giant was overestimating its appeal, handing its rivals an easy win and blowing up hard-won goodwill.But the outrage didn't change Netflix executives' minds. Now they're singing the praises of the very unpopular crackdown, and plotting new ways to get the viewing public to send more cash to Los Gatos' most prominent company.
