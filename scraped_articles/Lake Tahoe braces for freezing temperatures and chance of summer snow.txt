Lake Tahoe braces for freezing temperatures and chance of summer snow
-----
An upcoming cold front is expected to bring unseasonably chilly temperatures to the Lake Tahoe area this week. The forecast even has a chance of snow, a rather rare occurrence for August in the Sierra Nevada.Tahoe's highest elevations may see trace amounts of snow over the weekend. The National Weather Service's forecast for Lake Tahoe's west shore shows a 30% chance of precipitation starting Friday, with snow possible at elevations above 8,200 feet.
