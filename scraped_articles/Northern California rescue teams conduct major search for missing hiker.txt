Northern California rescue teams conduct major search for missing hiker
-----
A major search is underway for a woman who went missing on a hiking trail in Northern California last week.Rescue teams began searching for Ann Herford on Wednesday when she was reported missing on the Arnold Rim Trail in Calaveras County, according to the Calaveras County Sheriff's Office.
