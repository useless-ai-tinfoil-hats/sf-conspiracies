Review: ‘Fallout’ series is a worthy video game adaptation
-----
Sure, they may be irradiated and uninhabitable, but postapocalyptic wastelands tend to make fertile terrain for video games. There's something about the first-person perspective of a role-playing game that satisfies the curiosity of fleet-fingered gamers wondering how they'd navigate around mutated monsters and amoral scavengers for the survival of mankind.At least, that's partly the reason for the long-lived appeal of the "Fallout" franchise.
