House of Prime Rib, more Bay Area restaurants make OpenTable's top US list
-----
Four Bay Area restaurants have major bragging rights after OpenTable named them among the top 100 restaurants in the U.S.OpenTable's annual list, released earlier this month, featured 14 California businesses, including San Francisco institution House of Prime Rib, Kokkari Estiatorio in San Francisco, the Restaurant at Auberge du Soleil in Rutherford, and Animo in Sonoma. Animo is set to close Dec. 17 but plans to relocate and reopen sometime in 2024.
