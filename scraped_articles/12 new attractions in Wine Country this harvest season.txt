12 new attractions in Wine Country this harvest season
-----
Harvest season is known as the best time to visit Wine Country. This year, there are more than a dozen additional reasons to seek out what winemakers lovingly refer to as "crush."Downtown Napa's renaissance showed no signs of slowing down this summer with tasting room openings, like Mondavi Winery's flashy riverfront home; three new cocktail bars, including tequila-focused Chispa and the Haven from the Culinary Institute of America; and a record store with a killer wine list.
