Man fatally shot in North Bay shopping center parking lot, police say
-----
A man was shot and killed Thursday evening in the parking lot of a Rohnert Park shopping center, according to police.The slaying occurred at about 6:30 p.m. on the 900 block of Golf Course Drive, a busy roadway with trees packed against bungalows and apartment buildings. Officers received a 911 call from a bystander who had heard gunfire in the vicinity of a Walmart, Detective Fermin Ramirez said. The caller then glimpsed a person lying on the asphalt.
