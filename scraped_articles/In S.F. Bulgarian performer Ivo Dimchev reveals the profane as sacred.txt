In S.F., Bulgarian performer Ivo Dimchev reveals the profane as sacred
-----
When Ivo Dimchev launched into his "Overrated," a ballad with a bop, he sang with the ghoulish quality of a musical saw. His pitch soared so high it didn't seem like it could come from the strapping figure on Oasis' stage, tattoos tracing down his body like footprints.
