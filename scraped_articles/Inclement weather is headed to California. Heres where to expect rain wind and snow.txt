Inclement weather is headed to California. Here's where to expect rain, wind and snow
-----
A burst of inclement weather is set to arrive in California over the next several days. Low-pressure systems are expected to bring a trifecta of winds, rain and snow that could affect travel and outdoor plans for residents across the Golden State.The first system will usher in rounds of cool breezes and light rain this weekend.
Bay Area residents along the bay shore and delta can look for breezy conditions and daytime temperatures in the upper 60s to lower 70s Saturday afternoon.
