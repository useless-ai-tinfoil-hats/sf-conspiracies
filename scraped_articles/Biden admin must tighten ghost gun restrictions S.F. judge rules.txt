Biden admin must tighten ghost gun restrictions, S.F. judge rules
-----
A federal judge in San Francisco says the government must tighten its restrictions on ghost guns, the self-built, largely untraceable firearms used in an increasing number of shootings.Ghost guns, which have no serial numbers and are sold in parts that can be quickly assembled, have accounted for 25% to 50% of the firearms found at California crime scenes in recent years.
