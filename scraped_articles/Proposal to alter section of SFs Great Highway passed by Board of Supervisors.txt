Proposal to alter section of SF's Great Highway passed by Board of Supervisors
-----
LATEST May 15, 10:45 a.m. The Board of Supervisors unanimously approved the closure of a section of the Great Highway in San Francisco on Tuesday.The ordinance permanently closes two-thirds of a mile of the road between Sloat Boulevard and Skyline Boulevard. It was passed on a first reading last week and added to Tuesday's meeting agenda.
