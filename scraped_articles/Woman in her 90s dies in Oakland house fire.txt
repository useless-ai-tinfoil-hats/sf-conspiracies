Woman in her 90s dies in Oakland house fire
-----
An Oakland woman in her 90s was killed in a house fire early Wednesday morning, officials said.The Oakland Fire Department fire responded at 2:32 a.m. to a fire at a house located in the 2900 block of Partridge Avenue near Eastmont Hills, Michael Hunt, a spokesperson for the Oakland Fire Department, told SFGATE.
Around 25 firefighters responded to the blaze at the single-family home. Upon arrival, they found the woman, who lived alone, dead with burns and signs of smoke inhalation,
