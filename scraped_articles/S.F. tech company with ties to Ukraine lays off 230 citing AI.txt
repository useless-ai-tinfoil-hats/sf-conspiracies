S.F. tech company with ties to Ukraine lays off 230, citing AI
-----
Grammarly is laying off 230 employees, including 82 in San Francisco where it is headquartered.The writing-assistance and learning company said the cuts were necessary to prepare for an artificial intelligence-focused future.
Affected workers in San Francisco at 475 Sansome St. will be laid off on April 8. Cuts include the head of brand design, head of human insights and 17 software engineers, according to a state filing.
