Longtime Downtown Disney restaurant Tortilla Jo's closing after 20 years
-----
A mainstay of Downtown Disney for 20 years is closing as the shopping area outside Disneyland continues its transformation.Tortilla Jo's, which opened in 2004, announced it will serve its last margaritas and chips on March 31. Its adjacent taqueria, which has a separate entrance in the same building, closes April 13.
