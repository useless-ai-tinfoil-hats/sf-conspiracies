Man injured in S.F. Tenderloin shooting
-----
Gunfire injured one person in San Francisco's Tenderloin neighborhood Wednesday morning, police said.Police arrested a suspect in the 9:29 a.m. shooting at the intersection of O'Farrell and Hyde streets, said San Francisco Police Department spokesperson Allison Maxie.
Officers arriving at the scene found a victim with gunshot wounds. Paramedics administered aid but Maxie said it was not immediately clear whether the man survived.
