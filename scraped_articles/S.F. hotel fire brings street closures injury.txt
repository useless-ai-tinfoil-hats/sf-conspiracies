S.F. hotel fire brings street closures, injury
-----
A fire at the Broadway Hotel in San Francisco's Russian Hill neighborhood injured one person and prompted street closures nearby Sunday morning, authorities said.Firefighters were working just before 7:45 a.m. to ensure the blaze at 2048 Polk St. was completely extinguished, said San Francisco Fire Department spokesperson Justin Schorr. Broadway and Polk streets were closed, with no estimated time of reopening, he said.
