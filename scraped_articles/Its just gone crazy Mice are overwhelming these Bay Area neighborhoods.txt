‘It’s just gone crazy’: Mice are overwhelming these Bay Area neighborhoods
-----
Mice are not uncommon denizens of the hilly, waterside neighborhoods of Benicia and Vallejo in Solano County. But this year, they seem to be taking over — moving into garages, homes and businesses, chewing through drywall, nesting in ovens and under the hoods of cars."It's just gone crazy," said Maria Talacona, an owner of Benicia's Mighty Men Pest Control, which provides service across the Bay Area.
