Feds tell motorists to pay attention after video shows Tesla driver wearing computer goggles
-----
U.S. Transportation Secretary Pete Buttigieg told motorists to pay attention to the road even when using "advanced driver assist systems" after a viral video showed a driver in a Tesla Cybertruck wearing computer goggles.The 12-second video reposted by Buttigieg on social media showed a driver wearing the distinctive Apple Vision Pro spatial computer goggles while behind the wheel of a Tesla Cybertruck.
