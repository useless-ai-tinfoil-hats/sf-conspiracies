Good Samaritan drowns while trying to rescue woman, child in San Joaquin River
-----
A man drowned while trying to save a mother and daughter whose SUV plummeted into the San Joaquin River in Tracy on Tuesday afternoon, according to the San Joaquin County Sheriff's Office.Eddie Gerardo Cervantes Lora, 41, of Manteca was among six bystanders who rushed to help after witnessing the car veer off the road and slide down an embankment at the Airport Way overcrossing.
