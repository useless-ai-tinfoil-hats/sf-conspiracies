BMW strikes pedestrian in Santa Rosa intersection, causing critical injuries
-----
A pedestrian walking in Santa Rosa on Tuesday evening was hospitalized with critical injuries after being struck by a motorist at the intersection of Sebastopol Road and Dutton Avenue.Police were dispatched shortly after 11 p.m. following a report of a "vehicle versus pedestrian collision," Santa Rosa police said in a statement. Initial reports suggested the pedestrian, who was not identified, was unconscious in the roadway.
