1 person dies when SUV lands in water at California state park beach
-----
One person died when a driver went off the edge of a parking lot at Pescadero State Beach, about 35 miles south of San Francisco, and landed in the water Thursday night, officials said.CHP Officer Sophie Lu said the fatal crash occurred at about 10 p.m. A driver and two passengers were in an SUV, Lu said. She could not provide details on the deceased and said the other two individuals had moderate injuries. No other vehicles were involved.
