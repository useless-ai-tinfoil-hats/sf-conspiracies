Person missing in Bay Area after possible shark attack
-----
LATEST Oct. 2, 10:35 a.m. The U.S. Coast Guard called off the search for a missing person after authorities could not find them following a possible shark attack off Wildcat Beach in Point Reyes National Seashore Sunday, said Grace Patton, a spokesperson for the San Francisco sector. The Coast Guard said the person's family had been notified of their plans. A fatality has not been confirmed.Oct. 2, 10:10 a.m. Bay Area authorities are still searching for a missing person Monday morning
