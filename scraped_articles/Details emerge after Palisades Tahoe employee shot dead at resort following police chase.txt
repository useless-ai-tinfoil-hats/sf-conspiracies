Details emerge after Palisades Tahoe employee shot dead at resort following police chase
-----
Details are emerging surrounding a wild car chase that ended in the police-shooting death of a Palisades employee on Friday in North Lake Tahoe.The deceased man has been identified as 29-year-old Vinton Miller, who was staying in the Tahoe area on a work visa from Jamaica, the Placer County Sheriff Department has confirmed.
The incident began at around 9.30 a.m. when Miller was involved in a carjacking in Tahoe City, California State Parks told SFGATE Friday.
