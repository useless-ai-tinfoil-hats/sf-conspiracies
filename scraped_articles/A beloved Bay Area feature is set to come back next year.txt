A beloved Bay Area feature is set to come back next year
-----
Tens of thousands of lights are slated to once again make the Bay Bridge twinkle, ending a roughly two-year hiatus since it went dark in 2023. This "portal of public art" is officially set to come back next spring thanks to new funding to Illuminate SF, the nonprofit behind the bridge's sparkle, its founder Ben David told SFGATE."I feel like I'm in a helium balloon today," Davis said.
