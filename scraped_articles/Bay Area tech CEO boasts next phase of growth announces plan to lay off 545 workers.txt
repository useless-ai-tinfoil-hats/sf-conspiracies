Bay Area tech CEO boasts 'next phase of growth,' announces plan to lay off 545 workers
-----
Informatica had a good third quarter, according to CEO Amit Walia's comments in a press release last week. It was a "strong step forward," he wrote, with the period "exceeding guidance for the top and bottom line."Also, he said, the Bay Area software company will lay off 545 people.
The layoffs include 90 workers from the company's headquarters in Redwood City, according to a notice filed with the city government under California's Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act
