Burning Man exodus traffic jam hits '7 hours and climbing'
-----
As a convoy of cars, trucks and RVs attempt to leave Burning Man after a long, muddy delay, the wait just to get out of Black Rock City is "7 hours and climbing," festival organizers said. About 64,000 people were at Black Rock City as of midday Monday.The "exodus" from Burning Man normally starts on Sundays, but this year's departure was delayed for over a day by rainy weather that turned the playa into a sloppy mess.
