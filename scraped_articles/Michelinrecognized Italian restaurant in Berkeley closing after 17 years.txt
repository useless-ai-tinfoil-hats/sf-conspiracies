Michelin-recognized Italian restaurant in Berkeley closing after 17 years
-----
Northern Italian restaurant Riva Cucina is saying farewell to West Berkeley after 17 years in the quiet, industrial neighborhood, as first reported by East Bay Nosh.Owners Jen and Massi Boldrini decided to close the red brick restaurant to spend more time with their family, after spending nearly two decades working long hours in Berkeley and commuting back and forth from their home in Napa Valley.
