Triple threat of international superstars announce concert in San Francisco
-----
Not one, but three, global superstars will share the stage at a newly announced San Francisco arena show.On Tuesday, Pitbull, Enrique Iglesias and Ricky Martin announced a joint Jan. 31, 2024 show at the Chase Center. The concert is one of a new set of dates added to the artists' Trilogy Tour, which is also slated to pass through San Jose in early December.
