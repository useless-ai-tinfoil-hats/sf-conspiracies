Grant McCray a bright spot but Giants fall to A’s in opener of final Bay Bridge Series
-----
OAKLAND — Grant McCray earned his big-league promotion because of his athleticism, something San Francisco Giants brass long promised to infuse into this roster in order to compete with the best of them. Inspired by quick-footed Tyler Fitzgerald's production of late, the team's thought is that doubling down on speed with McCray can jolt an otherwise woefully tepid offense.The Giants needed every step of McCray's speed to avoid potential disaster against the last-place Oakland Athletics
