Retail exodus hitting S.F.’s biggest mall isn’t happening elsewhere in the state. What’s going on?
-----
In its heyday, the mall once known as Westfield San Francisco Centre featured the biggest Nordstrom in the world and was the winner of multiple design awards. It was a symbol of downtown's economic vitality, thronged with locals and tourists a few blocks from the city's prestigious Union Square.These days, the hushed retail center has few shoppers and over half of the cavernous 1.5 million-square-foot complex is empty.
