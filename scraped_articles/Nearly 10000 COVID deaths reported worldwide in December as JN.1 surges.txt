Nearly 10,000 COVID deaths reported worldwide in December as JN.1 surges
-----
Four years after the world's first confirmed COVID-19 death, the coronavirus continues to claim lives at an alarming rate.In December, almost 10,000 people succumbed to the virus globally, the World Health Organization reported on Wednesday. The surge in fatalities was attributed to holiday gatherings and the rapid transmission of the latest coronavirus variant, the highly immune-evasive omicron offshoot, JN.1.
