Earthquake gives San Francisco a shake on Friday afternoon
-----
An earthquake with a preliminary magnitude of 3.4 struck just offshore of San Francisco at 1:32 p.m. on Friday, according to the U.S. Geological Survey.The epicenter was 3 miles west of the San Francisco Zoo. It hit at a depth of 6.2 miles. "It's fairly shallow and that's why you felt it," said Stephen DeLong, a supervisory research geologist with the USGS.
"This could be a precursor to a larger earthquake, but the odds of that are small, but not zero," DeLong said.
