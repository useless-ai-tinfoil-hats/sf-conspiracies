Will new leadership improve police oversight in Oakland?
-----
The Oakland Police Commission met Thursday for the first time since the influential but stymied oversight body elected a new chair and vice chair six days earlier.The meeting served as the first indication as to whether a commission racked by infighting could fulfill its role overseeing the Oakland Police Department.
