Chris Hardman, founder of Antenna Theater and pioneer in experimental works, dies at 73
-----
"High School," the first site-specific production by Sausalito-based Antenna Theater, was a sign of what was to come in Chris Hardman's 40-year run of presenting experimental and immersive theater. There was no stage, no seats, no lines for actors to speak, no theater.
