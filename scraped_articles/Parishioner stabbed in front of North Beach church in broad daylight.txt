Parishioner stabbed in front of North Beach church in broad daylight
-----
A man attending mass was stabbed in broad daylight outside Saints Peter and Paul Church in North Beach on Sunday, San Francisco Board of Supervisors President Aaron Peskin confirmed to SFGATE.Before the stabbing, the suspect walked into the church, disrupting mass, and was removed by church members, according to KRON-TV. The parishioner who was stabbed asked the individual, who was "yelling and being rude," to leave, said Peskin, whose district includes North Beach.
