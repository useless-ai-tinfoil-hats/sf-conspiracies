Popular S.F. Mission taqueria damaged by hit-and-run driver
-----
A popular Mission District taqueria was damaged when a motorist crashed into the restaurant Wednesday before fleeing, San Francisco police said.San Francisco police said they responded to the scene of 18th and Valencia streets regarding a potential hit-and-run incident where a vehicle drove into a business, causing property damage, police said.
The business, Taqueria El Buen Sabor, is at 699 Valencia St.
