Strange de Jim, San Francisco quipster for Herb Caen and Leah Garchik, dies at 81
-----
Strange de Jim was a full-time spinner of quips, a career that did not pay beyond the reward of seeing his pseudonym in Herb Caen's column. This was also the reward for Chronicle readers, who associated the name Strange de Jim with a clever take on the human comedy.Strange, as he was known even to family members, did it better than anybody over the course of 25 years.
