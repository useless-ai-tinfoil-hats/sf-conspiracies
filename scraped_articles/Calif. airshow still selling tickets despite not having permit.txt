Calif. airshow still selling tickets despite not having permit
-----
The future of a popular California airshow is at stake after coastal officials said operators failed to provide proper permits for hosting on an Orange County beach.The Pacific Airshow is an annual event that typically draws over 700,000 spectators to Huntington Beach. Though this year's show is set to take place from October 4 to 6, organizers failed for months to provide documentation or permit applications, according to letters from the California Coastal Commission.
