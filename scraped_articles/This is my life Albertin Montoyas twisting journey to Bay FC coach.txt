‘This is my life’: Albertin Montoya’s twisting journey to Bay FC coach
-----
Albertin Montoya doesn't believe in using a physical whistle. Instead, he whistles using his mouth to get the attention of the 30-some players in Bay FC practice shirts in Santa Barbara, a blur of motion between drills.It's hard to take your eyes off Montoya's high-energy sifting through Bay FC's first-ever training-camp segments. Players toss the ball to each other and learn each other's names, goalies practice footwork, and other field players dribble through cones.
