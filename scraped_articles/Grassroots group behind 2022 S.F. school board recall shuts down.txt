Grassroots group behind 2022 S.F. school board recall shuts down
-----
SF Guardians, the grassroots political organization that led the recall of three San Francisco school board members, is no longer able to cover costs and will cease operating, its co-founder announced Monday."Not in a million years did I expect to get involved in politics, let alone recall a school board," said Siva Raj in a social media post announcing the dissolution of the political action committee and tax-exempt organization. "This has been the journey of a lifetime."
