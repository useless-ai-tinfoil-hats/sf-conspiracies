Thieves steal safes from Vaulin’s Taproom in S.F.’s South of Market neighborhood
-----
Vaulin's Taproom in San Francisco closed Friday after thieves broke in and stole two safes, according to the restaurant and San Francisco police.The Brazilian restaurant, located on Bryant Street in San Francisco's South of Market neighborhood, was broken into just before 5 a.m. Friday, according to San Francisco police. Officials said the restaurant's front door had been damaged and that two safes had been stolen.
