125,000 gallons of raw sewage spill in front of popular Lake Tahoe restaurant
-----
Just after 8 p.m. on Thursday, July 18, a contractor drilled through the sewer main in Carnelian Bay, a neighborhood on Tahoe's north shore, spilling an estimated 125,000 gallons of sewage onto streets and beaches and into Lake Tahoe.Over the next 45 minutes, untreated sewage pooled in a parking lot and then overflowed into the water, just as people were finishing dinner at a popular lakefront restaurant nearby.
