Two youths injured in shooting on East Bay interstate
-----
Two juveniles were injured in a shooting Saturday night on Interstate 880 in San Lorenzo, the California Highway Patrol said.The two were taken to a hospital with non-life-threatening injuries. No arrests had been announced as of Sunday morning.
