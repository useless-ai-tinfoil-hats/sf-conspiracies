Napa Valley's most beloved dive bar has new owners. Pancha's, the weathered Yountville watering hole that's best known for permitting patrons to smoke indoors, has sold to the founders of a prominent Napa winery.Locals have anxiously awaited the bar's fate. Pancha's closed during the pandemic, and while the owners expressed their intention to reopen, it has remained shuttered. The bar's regulars feared the worst: that it would be sold, torn down and replaced by another fancy tasting room
