53-year-old New Yorker with royal ties is still missing in Malibu
-----
Attilio Brillembourg, a 53-year-old New Yorker with ties to European royalty, disappeared in the coastal enclave of Malibu last month. Officials said Saturday that the search for Brillembourg continues and his safety is a concern.Brillembourg, the stepbrother of Princess Tatiana of Greece and Denmark, was last seen at 1:10 a.m. on May 18 in a rural neighborhood in the hills of Malibu near Escondido Canyon Park and Escondido Falls, the Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department said.
