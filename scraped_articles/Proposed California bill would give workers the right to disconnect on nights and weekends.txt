Proposed California bill would give workers the ‘right to disconnect’ on nights and weekends
-----
A new bill from San Francisco Assembly Member Matt Haney would clearly outline when companies can communicate with their employees during off hours, following other countries that have passed so-called "right to disconnect" laws.The bill would require companies to lay out the expected working hours in employment contracts ahead of time.
