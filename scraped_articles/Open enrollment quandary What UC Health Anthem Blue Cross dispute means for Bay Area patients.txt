Open enrollment quandary: What UC Health, Anthem Blue Cross dispute means for Bay Area patients
-----
More than 600,000 Californians who get medical care at UC Health hospitals through Anthem Blue Cross — including 110,000 who go to UCSF and UCSF Benioff Children's Hospitals — may need to find a different health insurer or pay out-of-network rates for services at UC Health next year if the two parties cannot reach a new contract by February.UC Health and Anthem, two of California's largest health industry players, are at odds over the terms of their future relationship.
