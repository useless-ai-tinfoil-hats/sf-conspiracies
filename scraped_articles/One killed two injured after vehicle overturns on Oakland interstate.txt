One killed, two injured after vehicle overturns on Oakland interstate
-----
A woman died and two people were injured early Sunday after a solo crash in which a vehicle overturned on Interstate 580 in Oakland, authorities said.Just after 12:30 a.m., California Highway Patrol officers responded to a report of a car crash on the westbound lanes of the highway, just east of Lakeshore Avenue, and found an overturned vehicle in the No. 3 and 4 lanes, said CHP Officer Adib Zeid.
