What was the unusual contrail in the Bay Area sky on Monday?
-----
Many in the San Francisco Bay Area noticed a mysterious contrail streaking across the sky early Monday morning. The trail of smoke was especially noticeable on a day without any clouds in the sky.No, it wasn't a UFO, or another Elon Musk venture. What people saw was a U.S. Missile Defense Agency test flight of a missile on its way to take out another missile.
