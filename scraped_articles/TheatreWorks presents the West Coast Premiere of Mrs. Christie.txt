TheatreWorks presents the West Coast Premiere of “Mrs. Christie”
-----
Put on your detective cap and investigate Agatha Christie's greatest mystery: her unsolved 1926 disappearance. Almost a century later, Christie superfan Lucy unveils a clue about the author's highly publicized 11-day vanishing and resolves to crack the case. In parallel storylines that blend past, present, and Poirot, Lucy discovers the power of finding yourself by disappearing.
