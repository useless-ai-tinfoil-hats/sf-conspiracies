Thieves drove stolen Porsches through Bay Area dealership windows
-----
A pair of thieves stole two sports cars by driving them off a showroom floor and through the windows of a dealership in Fremont last month. Now, one of the two suspects is in custody.The Fremont Police Department said Wednesday that they have arrested a 22-year-old Hayward man who is accused of stealing a Porsche GT3 from the dealership on April 4. The other car, a Porsche GT3 RS, was found in Milpitas the same day as the burglary and was recovered.
