Boy, 10, arrested in fatal shooting of another 10-year-old in Sacramento
-----
A 10-year-old boy has been arrested on suspicion of using his father's gun to fatally shoot another 10-year-old boy in Sacramento, authorities said.The boy found the gun in his father's parked car while getting cigarettes for him just after 4:30 p.m. Saturday on the 4700 block of Greenholme Drive, according to the Sacramento County Sheriff's Office. The gun was reported stolen in 2017, authorities said.
He flaunted the gun and shot another boy before tossing the firearm in a trash
