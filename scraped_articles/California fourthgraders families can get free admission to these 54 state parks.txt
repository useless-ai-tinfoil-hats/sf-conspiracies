California fourth-graders, families can get free admission to these 54 state parks
-----
California fourth graders and their families can now get free admission to more than 50 state parks through the expansion of a state program that aims to get more families outdoors.Begun in 2021, the California State Park Adventure Pass pilot program provides free one-year passes to fourth-grade students to visit 19 of the state's parks. This year, according to a recent announcement by state officials, the program is expanding to include 54 parks across California.
