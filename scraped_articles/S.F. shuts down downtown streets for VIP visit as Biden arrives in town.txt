S.F. shuts down downtown streets for ‘VIP visit’ as Biden arrives in town
-----
Several streets near Chinatown and Nob Hill are being closed Tuesday and Wednesday for a VIP visit that lines up with President Biden's visit to San Francisco.*  Related: In S.F., Biden announces executive action on AI coming this fall
