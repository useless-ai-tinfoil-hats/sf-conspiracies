S.F. Ballet receives $60 million donation, the largest in its history
-----
The San Francisco Ballet has received a donation of $60 million, the largest in the company's history and believed to be among the largest ever given to an American ballet company.The gift, from an anonymous donor described as a longtime backer, is intended to help support the creation of new work and to strengthen the Ballet's financial security, the company announced Thursday.
