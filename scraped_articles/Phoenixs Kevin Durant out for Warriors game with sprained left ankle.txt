Phoenix’s Kevin Durant out for Warriors game with sprained left ankle
-----
PHOENIX — Suns forward Kevin Durant will not face his former team Tuesday night when the Golden State Warriors try to climb out of their early-season rut against Phoenix. Durant was ruled out of the game with a sprained left ankle.That obviously changes the defensive dynamic for the Warriors (10-12), who have lost 10 of their past 14 games. Durant ranks third in the NBA in scoring, averaging 31 points per game.
