The king of LA food trucks has returned with a backyard taco stand
-----
Although he certainly wasn't the first to serve food out of a truck — taco trucks, stands and other street vendors have been feeding people in Los Angeles for many decades — celebrity chef Roy Choi has been credited with igniting the more upscale food truck craze in America in the late aughts. His big street hit was Kogi, a pull-up-anywhere operation that masterfully combined Korean and Mexican flavors.Now Choi, one of LA's most recognizable culinary faces, is heading back to the street.
