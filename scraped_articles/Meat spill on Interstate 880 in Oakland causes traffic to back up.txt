Meat spill on Interstate 880 in Oakland causes traffic to back up
-----
A truck carrying chicken and beef parts spilled its content onto northbound Interstate 880 in Oakland during the Friday evening commute, forcing Caltrans to temporarily close all lanes as crews work to clean up the mess, according to NBC Bay Area and the California Highway Patrol.The CHP closed three lanes of northbound I-880 near 29th Avenue after the incident, causing traffic to back up. All four lanes were closed when Caltrans came to clean up the spill, the CHP said.
