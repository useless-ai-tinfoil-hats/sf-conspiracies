Supermarket buys San Francisco shopping center in $37 million deal
-----
Korean grocery chain H Mart has acquired Oceanview Village Shopping Center in San Francisco for more than $37 million, as first reported by The Real Deal.The purchase was made on May 1, according to the outlet, and comes three years after the major grocery chain opened its first San Francisco storefront at the same shopping center in 2021. SFGATE reached out to H Mart for comment about the purchase but did not hear back by publication.
