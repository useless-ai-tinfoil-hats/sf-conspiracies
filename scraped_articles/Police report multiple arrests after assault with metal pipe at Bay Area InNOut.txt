Police report multiple arrests after assault with metal pipe at Bay Area In-N-Out
-----
Four Redwood City residents were arrested on suspicion of taking part in an assault at an In-N-Out restaurant, police said Wednesday.Marco Ambriz Rangel, 18, Jesus Bustos, 47, Juan Carlos Guerrero, 47, and an unnamed 17-year-old minor were arrested Friday.
The incident occurred at the In-N-Out restaurant at 949 Veterans Blvd.
Around 9 p.m., a group of people were approached by two men armed with a metal pipe. The men thought the minors had been involved in a robbery earlier
