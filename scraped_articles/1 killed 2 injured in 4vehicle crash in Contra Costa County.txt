1 killed, 2 injured in 4-vehicle crash in Contra Costa County
-----
One person was killed and two were injured in a four-vehicle crash in Contra Costa County, according to the California Highway Patrol.A white Chevrolet Silverado traveling northbound on Byron Highway south of Clifton Court Road veered into oncoming traffic and struck a gray Kia sedan traveling southbound at about 6:25 p.m. Saturday, the CHP said in a statement.
