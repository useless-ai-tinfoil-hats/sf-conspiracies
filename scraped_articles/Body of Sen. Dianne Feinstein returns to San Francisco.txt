Body of Sen. Dianne Feinstein returns to San Francisco
-----
UPDATE: Body of Sen. Dianne Feinstein to arrive at S.F. City HallThe San Francisco fog held back for blue skies to welcome the body of late Sen. Dianne Feinstein back to her home city Saturday evening.
Feinstein died of natural causes at age 90 in her Washington, D.C., home in the small hours of Friday morning Eastern Daylight Time. A military flight transporting her body back to San Francisco landed at San Francisco International Airport at 5:58 p.m. Saturday.
