Crisis of deadly domestic violence in San Mateo County spawns new task force
-----
Five women have died in domestic violence incidents in San Mateo County so far this year, and officials want to know what's behind the troubling trend.Responding to a crisis in intimate partner violence, the county's Domestic Violence Council has formed a task force to investigate possible reasons for the increase in domestic violence deaths and recommend solutions, San Mateo County Supervisor Noelia Corzo announced in a statement Thursday.
