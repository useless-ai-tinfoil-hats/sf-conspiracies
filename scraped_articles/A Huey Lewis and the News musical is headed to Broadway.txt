A Huey Lewis and the News musical is headed to Broadway
-----
Huey Lewis and the News are set to hit Broadway with the Bay Area band's classic songs driving the jukebox musical "The Heart of Rock and Roll," making its New York debut at the James Earl Jones Theatre this spring.This rom-com, featuring a storyline centered on the pop-rock group's 1980s hits like "The Power of Love" and "I Want a New Drug," has been in production for nearly a decade.
