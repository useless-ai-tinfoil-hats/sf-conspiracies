Warriors to reportedly host 2025 NBA All-Star Game at Chase Center
-----
The NBA All-Star Game is reportedly coming to San Francisco for the first time.The Warriors and the NBA are "nearing plans" to bring the 2025 NBA All-Star Game and surrounding weekend festivities to Chase Center in San Francisco, according to The Athletic's Shams Charania.
If those plans are finalized, it would be the third time that the NBA brings its annual all-star contest to the Bay Area but technically the first time the game would be held within the San Francisco city limits.
