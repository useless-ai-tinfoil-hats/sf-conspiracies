Hawaii’s humpback whales have rebounded but not yet recovered
-----
When a call comes in about an entangled whale in Hawaii's waters, Edward Lyman sends out a "code yellow" alert to his team.On the phone, he asks the caller questions and determines if the whale is seriously threatened. If it is, then he green-lights a rescue mission — and his team assembles.
Lyman is the natural resource specialist for the Hawaiian Islands Humpback Whale National Marine Sanctuary. Based on Maui, he monitors and protects humpback whales and is the lead coordinator
