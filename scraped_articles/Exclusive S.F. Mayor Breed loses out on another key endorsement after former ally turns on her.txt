Exclusive: S.F. Mayor Breed loses out on another key endorsement after former ally turns on her
-----
A powerful San Francisco labor group that once supported Mayor London Breed has turned against her and come out in favor of two of her reelection opponents, Mark Farrell and Ahsha Safaí.The San Francisco Building and Construction Trades Council, a politically active coalition of 27 trade unions, revealed Friday that it has granted a dual endorsement to Farrell, a former city supervisor and interim mayor, and Safaí, a current supervisor, for the November mayor's race.
