S.F. driver charged with manslaughter in death of 4-year-old near Oracle Park
-----
The driver who authorities say struck and killed a 4-year-old girl in a San Francisco intersection in August has been charged with vehicular manslaughter, the district attorney's office said Thursday.Karen Cartagena, 71, is accused of striking the girl, who was being pushed in a stroller, and her father near Fourth and King streets in the South of Market neighborhood about 5:15 p.m. on Aug. 15.
