Map: See where Caltrans will close southbound I-680 in East Bay this weekend
-----
Drivers on southbound Interstate 680 will face slow-going this weekend, as three of the five lanes will be shut down to make way for paving work between San Ramon and Dublin.The lanes will be closed from 9 p.m. Friday until 4 a.m. Monday over the 6-mile stretch between Alcosta Boulevard in San Ramon and the junction of I-680 and Interstate 580 in Dublin and Pleasanton.
