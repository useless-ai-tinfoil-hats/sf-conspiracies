Google's founders dipped out for Burning Man at the most epic moment
-----
Long before Google Doodles became known for their playful celebrations of birthdays, holidays and cultural events — before Google was even an incorporated company itself! — the first Google Doodle was launched on Aug. 30, 1998, for a very simple reason: to tell users that its co-founders Larry Page and Sergey Brin were out of office.Where could they be less than a week before their company officially became incorporated? Well, in true Bay Area tech bro style, they were at Burning Man.
