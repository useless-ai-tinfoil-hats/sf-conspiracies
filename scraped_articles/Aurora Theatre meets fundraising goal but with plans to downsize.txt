Aurora Theatre meets fundraising goal, but with plans to downsize
-----
In April, it wasn't clear if Berkeley's Aurora Theatre Company would have another season of plays. On the line were 32 years of tackling big intellectual ideas with first-class actors in an intimate venue. To keep going, the company needed emergency funds — first a total of $200,000, which soon ballooned to $500,000.On Wednesday, Aug. 14, Artistic Director Josh Costello confirmed that the company met its goal.
