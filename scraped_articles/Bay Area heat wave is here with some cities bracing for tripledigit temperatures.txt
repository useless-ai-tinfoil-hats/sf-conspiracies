Bay Area heat wave is here, with some cities bracing for triple-digit temperatures
-----
After a partly to mostly cloudy Monday, the Bay Area will rapidly warm up Tuesday as a high-pressure system moves into Central California. Tuesday will be the hottest day since summer in interior portions of the Bay Area, with highs in the 90s to near 100 degrees under sunny skies.
