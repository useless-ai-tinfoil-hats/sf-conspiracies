Gunfire killed a man in San Francisco's Mission District early Saturday, police said.Officers responding to reports of a shooting found the man with gunshot wounds around 1:58 a.m. near Mission and 18th streets, the San Francisco Police Department said.
Paramedics transported the man to the hospital, where he later died from his injuries, police said Sunday in a statement.
Police have not identified suspects or made any arrests.
