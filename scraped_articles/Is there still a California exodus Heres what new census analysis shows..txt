Is there still a 'California exodus'? Here's what new census analysis shows.
-----
Newly analyzed data from the U.S. Census Bureau shows that California had the country's lowest inmigration rate — defined by the bureau as the "number of people moving into a state as a share of that state's total number of movers" — in 2022.California's inmigration rate was 11.1% last year. In other words, just 11.1% of California's movers were new to the state, arriving from a different state. For comparison, nearby Oregon had a inmigration rate of 21%.
