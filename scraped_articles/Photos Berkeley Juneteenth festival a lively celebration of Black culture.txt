Photos: Berkeley Juneteenth festival a lively celebration of Black culture
-----
Berkeley celebrated Juneteenth on Sunday with a festival featuring music, dance, art, food, a vintage car show and children's activities.Northern California's longest consistently running Juneteenth festival, held on Father's Day each year, attracts up to 3,000 people annually, organizers said.
