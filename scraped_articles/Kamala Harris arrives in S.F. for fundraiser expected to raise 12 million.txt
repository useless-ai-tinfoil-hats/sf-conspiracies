Kamala Harris arrives in S.F. for fundraiser expected to raise $12 million
-----
Vice President Kamala Harris arrived in San Francisco late Saturday for a fundraiser expected to raise $12 million and attract 700 attendees.Speaker Emerita Nancy Pelosi will be the featured speaker Sunday, other than Harris, a campaign spokesperson told the Chronicle. Minnesota Gov. Tim Walz, Harris' vice presidential pick, will not be there.
