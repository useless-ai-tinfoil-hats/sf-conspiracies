Man indicted in Yosemite high-speed chase that ended with 200-foot fall down canyon
-----
On Thursday, a California man was indicted on three counts for a 2022 episode in Yosemite National Park, in which he allegedly stole a park employee vehicle and led authorities on a lengthy car chase. The chase ended when he drove off a cliff and fell 200 feet, officials said.
