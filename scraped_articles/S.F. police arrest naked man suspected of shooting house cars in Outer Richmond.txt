S.F. police arrest naked man suspected of shooting house, cars in Outer Richmond
-----
A man was arrested after shooting at a house and several parked cars during an apparent mental health crisis that played out in San Francisco's Outer Richmond neighborhood on New Year's Eve.Residents of the 700 block of 41st Avenue called police shortly before noon Sunday to report that a man "suffering from apparent mental distress was holding a gun and fired several rounds," San Francisco Police Department spokesperson Paulina Henderson said.
