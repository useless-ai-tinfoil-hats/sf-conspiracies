G-Eazy to open ‘Freak Show’ tour in the Bay Area
-----
G-Eazy plans to return to the East Bay to open his "Freak Show" tour, in support of his upcoming album of the same name, this fall.Slated to kick off at the Greek Theatre at UC Berkeley on Oct. 24, the Oakland-bred rapper is expected to stop in cities across North America before crossing the ocean and continuing in Europe in the new year.
Ticket presales begin at 10 a.m. Wednesday, June 12, with general sale set for 10 a.m. June 14.
