Man shot at Union City movie theater, suspect at large
-----
A man was shot at a Union City movie theater early Wednesday and the suspect remains at large, police said.At about 12:50 a.m., Union City police officers responded to Cinemark Century theater at 32100 Union Landing Blvd. for a shooting that occurred in one of the theaters, police said. Upon arrival, officers evacuated the building and ensured that no threat remained.
