Cute sheep are everywhere in Wine Country. Some say the trend has gone too far
-----
Driving around Napa and Sonoma this time of year, you're bound to see some adorable visitors roaming among the grapevines.Sheep have become an inescapable fixture of Wine Country in the winter, prized for their natural mowing and fertilizing abilities. Instead of running a diesel-fueled tractor through the vineyard or spraying herbicide on sprouting weeds, a vintner can simply let loose a hungry herd.
