John Waters dispels casting rumors about 'Liarmouth'
-----
Editor's note: This story was updated at 3:30 p.m., Feb. 28, after John Waters released a statement to the Baltimore Sun refuting reports that Aubrey Plaza had been cast in his latest film. Waters said that while "Liarmouth" has been optioned by Village Roadshow Pictures, filming has not been greenlit and casting has not been finalized.
