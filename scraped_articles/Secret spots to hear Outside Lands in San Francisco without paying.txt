Secret spots to hear Outside Lands in San Francisco without paying
-----
Three-day general admission passes to Outside Lands cost an eye-popping $528.15, but there are free ways to both watch and eavesdrop on the festival, which runs Friday through Sunday in Golden Gate Park.On paper, Fiorella Sunset might seem like a pretty decent place to hear some buzz from multiple stages. The pizza-centric restaurant on Ninth Avenue has the only rooftop dining area in the Inner Sunset, plus a brunch menu.
