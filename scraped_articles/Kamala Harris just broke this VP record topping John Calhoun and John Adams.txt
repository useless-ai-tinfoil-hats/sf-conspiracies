Kamala Harris just broke this VP record, topping John Calhoun and John Adams
-----
WASHINGTON — Kamala Harris just broke through another barrier: She has now cast the most tie-breaking votes of any vice president in history.In confirming Loren Alikhan as a judge for the U.S. District Court for the District of Columbia Tuesday, Harris has cast 32 such votes in just two and a half years. The previous record-holders — John Calhoun and John Adams — each served about eight years.
