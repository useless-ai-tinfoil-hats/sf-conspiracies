New seafood destination opens on the S.F. Bay (giant jellyfish tank included)
-----
Restaurants often boast of "immersive" experiences inside their dining rooms. But a new seafood destination at Redwood City's Westpoint Harbor Marina will really offer dining with a backdrop typically seen only beneath the tides.Hurrica opens Friday featuring a massive jellyfish aquarium at the heart of the dining room. The floor-to-ceiling tank provides an ethereal underwater scene as customers bite into 1.5-pound lobster wrapped in kombu or pan-seared black cod.
