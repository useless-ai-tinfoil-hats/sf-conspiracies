The hardest worker in Bay Area college basketball never even touches the ball
-----
It's late in the second half of one of the Cal men's basketball team's biggest wins of the season against rival Stanford.And all Fox Sports commentator Casey Jacobsen can talk about on the broadcast is the only guy on the court who never actually touches the ball.
"Keep an eye on the bottom of your screen, watch this guy slide right in," he says, as a hoodie-wearing college kid with a rag in hand glides across the floor with an effortless baseball-style slide over to a wet spot.
