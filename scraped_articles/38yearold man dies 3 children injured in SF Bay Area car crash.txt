38-year-old man dies, 3 children injured in SF Bay Area car crash
-----
A 38-year-old male driver from East Palo Alto died after exceeding the speed limit on a San Francisco Bay Area highway and crashing into a metal guardrail Wednesday, official said. Three children — ages 2, 4 and 8 — from the same city were injured, the California Highway Patrol said.The driver was traveling over 65 mph in a 2002 Toyota Sequoia in lane No. 1 on northbound State Route 85 south of Cottle Road in San Jose when he hit the guardrail, with the car pushing north before halting,
