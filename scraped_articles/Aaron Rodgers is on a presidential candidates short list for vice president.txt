Aaron Rodgers is on a presidential candidate's short list for vice president
-----
New York Jets quarterback Aaron Rodgers could appear on your election ballot in November.That's because Rodgers is under consideration by Robert F. Kennedy Jr. as the candidate's running mate on a joint independent ticket in this year's presidential election. The New York Times, which first reported the development, wrote that Kennedy and Rodgers have been talking "pretty continuously" during the last month.
