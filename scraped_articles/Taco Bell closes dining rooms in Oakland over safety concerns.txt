Taco Bell closes dining rooms in Oakland over safety concerns
-----
Several Taco Bell locations in Oakland have closed their dining rooms due to rising safety concerns.Four out of the five fast-food chain outlets in the beleaguered East Bay city — specifically at 3535 35th Ave., 630 Hegenberger Road, 6900 Bancroft Ave. and 2255 Telegraph Ave. — have limited their services to drive-thru only, no longer accommodating walk-in customers.
