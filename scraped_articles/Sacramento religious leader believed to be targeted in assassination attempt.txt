Sacramento religious leader believed to be targeted in assassination attempt
-----
Shots were fired at a Sikh leader on a freeway near Sacramento in what the Sikh community believes to be an assassination attempt.The shooting happened Sunday at approximately 11:37 p.m. on Interstate 505, near County Road 27 in Yolo County, Rodney Fitzhugh, a spokesperson for the California Highway Patrol, told SFGATE. Fitzhugh did not identify individuals and said the agency's Valley Division Investigative Services Unit is investigating the case.
