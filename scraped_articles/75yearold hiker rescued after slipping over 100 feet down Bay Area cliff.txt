75-year-old hiker rescued after slipping over 100 feet down Bay Area cliff
-----
A 75-year-old hiker was rescued and airlifted to a hospital on Sunday morning after he fell over. 100 feet down a rocky cliff and into a dry creek bed near Mount Hamilton in unincorporated Santa Clara County, authorities said.The unidentified man was "very familiar with the area" and "accidentally slipped" sometime after 8:30 a.m., a public information officer for the San Jose Police Department told SFGATE.
