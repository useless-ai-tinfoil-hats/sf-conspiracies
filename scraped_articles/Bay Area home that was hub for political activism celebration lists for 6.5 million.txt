Bay Area home that was hub for political activism, celebration lists for $6.5 million
-----
The Menlo Park home of a former California politician just hit the market for nearly $6.5 million.With large outdoor spaces and open living areas, the property at 570 Menlo Oaks Drive became a hub for political organizing, activism and celebration for its owners, former California State Assemblyman Rich Gordon and his husband, now-retired physician Dr. Denny McShane.
