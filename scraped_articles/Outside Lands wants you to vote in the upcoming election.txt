Outside Lands wants you to vote in the upcoming election
-----
This election year, Outside Lands is using its platform to get out the vote.A cluster of booths at Golden Gate Park's Hellman Hollow dubbed the Mission — adjacent to the music festival's Panhandle Stage — is serving as the hub for 12 nonprofit organizations aim to encourage the festival's more tens of thousands of attendees to register to vote and get civically engaged.
"Some people don't have time to do it or forget about it," said Damian Zamora, the Southwest regional coordinator
