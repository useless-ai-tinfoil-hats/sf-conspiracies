Longtime San Francisco surfer, father of 2 dies after surf accident
-----
Kirby Lee, a longtime San Francisco surfer and father of two, died Nov. 6 from injuries sustained during a surfing accident four days before, according to his family. Lee was 54.What, exactly, happened in the water on Nov. 2 remains unknown. It was a relatively calm day for Ocean Beach; while waves there can easily reach 20 feet on the biggest days, Jose Lopez, another surfer who was present at the scene, described the waves that day as "tiny."
