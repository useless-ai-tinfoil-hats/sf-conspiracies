Rain set to return to the Bay Area, ending S.F.'s longest stretch of dry weather this year
-----
San Francisco's longest stretch of dry weather so far this year is likely to extend to nine days on Thursday, but the streak will come to an end Friday. A wet weather system is predicted to swing across Northern California, bringing rain to the coast and snow to the Sierra Nevada.Showers are forecast to begin Friday morning spanning Santa Rosa to Eureka, with widespread rain expected to move toward San Francisco during the afternoon and reach San Jose by the evening.
