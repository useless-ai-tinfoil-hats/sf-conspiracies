The reason Trader Joe's employees wear aloha shirts
-----
When you walk into any Trader Joe's, there's an immediate tropical vibe.The walls are painted bright colors, like sky blue, and often adorned with sea-themed murals. Fish netting and plastic lobsters abound. And there is an unmistakable nautical motif among staff: Employees are called crew members, managers are "captains" and communication is done through the clanging of ship bells.
