Vallejo police search for student who brought gun to high school
-----
Vallejo police were searching for a high school student Monday who brought a firearm to school, spokespersons for the Vallejo Unified School District said.Staff members at Jesse Bethel High School found the student in possession of a firearm on Monday, district officials said. Staff members retrieved the firearm, and the student fled the scene before police arrived, they added.
No injuries were reported, district officials said.
