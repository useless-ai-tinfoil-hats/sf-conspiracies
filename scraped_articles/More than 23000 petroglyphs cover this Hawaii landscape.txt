More than 23,000 petroglyphs cover this Hawaii landscape
-----
On the island of Hawaii is the largest petroglyph field in the state. Called Puuloa, it contains more than 23,000 petroglyphs and is accessible by a trail inside Hawaii Volcanoes National Park.Driving down the south side of the Kilauea volcano, I find it near the end of Chain of Craters Road. The trail is short, about 0.7 mile one way, and the National Park Service says it takes an hour and a half to complete.
