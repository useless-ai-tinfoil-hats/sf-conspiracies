Surfing Santa Cruz sea otter now has a pup
-----
The Santa Cruz sea otter that has amazed and flabbergasted the public for commandeering surfboards and then catching waves on them is now a mother.Called 841 for the number given to her by the Monterey Bay Aquarium, the 5-year-old sea otter was observed swimming with a tiny, furry pup on her belly Tuesday afternoon by Mark Woodward, who has followed the otter's movements closely since the summer, with frequent posts on X from the handle Native Santa Cruz.
