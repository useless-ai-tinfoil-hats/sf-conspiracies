‘Sick and tired’: Alcatraz ferry workers strike amid tough labor talks
-----
Workers who operate the ferry to San Francisco tourist destination Alcatraz Island went on strike Saturday amid contentious labor negotiations.Their union, Alcatraz Workers United, voted Tuesday to authorize a strike, according to a social media post. They were on strike for four hours, from 8:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m., said San Francisco regional director Marina V.
