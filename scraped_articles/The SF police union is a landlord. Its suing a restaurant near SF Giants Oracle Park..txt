The SF police union is a landlord. It's suing a restaurant near SF Giants' Oracle Park.

The Brixton, a gastropub across the street from Oracle Park, is being sued by its landlord over allegations of unpaid rent, a lawsuit filed in San Francisco Superior Court on Thursday alleges. Its landlord? The San Francisco Police Officers Association.The Brixton at 701 2nd St. first opened in the spring of 2019, the second location of the popular Marina District restaurant.
