Fudgy brownies get a festive Indian twist for Diwali
-----
In India, Diwali is an emotion, a feeling, a celebration. There is love, laughter, lights, color everywhere, and folks from all backgrounds come together to celebrate. For the South Asian community, the major Hindu festival also known as the Festival of Lights is as important as Christmas is in the United States.A few days before the onset of Diwali, which takes place Nov. 12 this year, mithaiwaalas (dessert vendors) start making kilos and kilos of mithai (sweets) for customers.
