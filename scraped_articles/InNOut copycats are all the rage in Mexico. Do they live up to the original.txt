In-N-Out copycats are all the rage in Mexico. Do they live up to the original?
-----
Customers line up for "double-double" burgers on red trays. Fries topped with cheese, grilled onions and special sauce, "animal style," are popular. But this restaurant, despite all the trappings, is not an In-N-Out.Owner Ricardo Gonzalez has modeled his Tijuana restaurant, Ench-In-Ga Burger, on the California fast-food chain, and he's not the only one.
