First Republic Bank will cease to exist in signage and style on May 24 when the remaining 62 branches nationwide close to complete the San Francisco bank's takeover by JP Morgan Chase, the New York corporation has confirmed.The closure includes the 25 remaining branches in the Bay Area, bank officials said.
Customers will be alerted by mail that their First Republic accounts will convert to JP Morgan Chase over Memorial Day weekend, with immediate access to bank branches and ATMs.
