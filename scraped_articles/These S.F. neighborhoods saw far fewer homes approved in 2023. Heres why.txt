These S.F. neighborhoods saw far fewer homes approved in 2023. Here's why
-----
San Francisco authorized about 1,700 new homes in 2023, the lowest number in more than a decade and a substantial decline from recent years' totals.The count of newly approved housing units last year was more than 1,100 fewer than the average of 2,810 homes the city authorized each year between 2020 and 2022, according to Planning Department data. Those totals reflect the difference between the number of housing units approved for construction and the number set for demolition.
