Kronos Quartet turns 50 with festival marking end of an era
-----
In the universe of Kronos Quartet, every destination reached presents a plethora of new paths.But San Francisco's storied new music ensemble has never encountered a crossroads as starkly delineated as the one that brings the group back to the SFJazz Center on June 20-23 for the ninth annual edition of Kronos Festival, the concluding hometown celebration of the quartet's 50th anniversary.
