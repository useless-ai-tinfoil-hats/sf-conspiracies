Oakland passes resolution calling for immediate cease-fire in Gaza, but some are dismayed
-----
Oakland has become the latest Bay Area city to pass legislation demanding an immediate cease-fire in Gaza.The City Council voted unanimously Monday on a resolution that also calls for the release of all hostages, unrestricted entry of humanitarian assistance to Gaza, and the protection of innocent civilians.
