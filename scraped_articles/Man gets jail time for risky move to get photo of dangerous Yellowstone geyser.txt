Man gets jail time for risky move to get photo of dangerous Yellowstone geyser
-----
A man was sentenced to seven days in jail, banned from Yellowstone National Park for two years and ordered to pay a $1,500 fine after he trespassed into the Norris Geyser Basin and came within 15 to 20 feet of Steamboat Geyser, the tallest and "most dangerous" active geyser in the world, park officials said Thursday.Authorities were dispatched to the basin after a park employee reported seeing a visitor wandering off the boardwalk.
