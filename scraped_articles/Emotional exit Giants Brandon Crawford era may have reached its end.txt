Emotional exit? Giants' Brandon Crawford era may have reached its end
-----
Before Sunday's game even started, Brandon Crawford had some beautiful moments, enveloping his four children in a huge hug after they delivered four first pitches.Then, Crawford took the field alone, to a standing ovation, before his teammates joined him. In what, perhaps, was his final game with the San Francisco Giants, Crawford went 0-for-4 with two strikeouts in San Francisco's 5-2 loss to the Dodgers, the season finale.
