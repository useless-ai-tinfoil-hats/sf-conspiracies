Man strangled girlfriend to death on Santa Cruz beach, police say
-----
Santa Cruz police said they arrested a man after he allegedly killed his girlfriend at Seabright Beach Friday morning.KSBW previously reported that the woman was on life support, but as of Saturday morning, Santa Cruz police informed KSBW she had died. Samuel Stone, 20, was arrested for homicide police said, the arrest was made at around 1:16 a.m. Friday morning near the waterline. Police also said they spotted the unconscious body of the victim next to the suspect when he was arrested.
