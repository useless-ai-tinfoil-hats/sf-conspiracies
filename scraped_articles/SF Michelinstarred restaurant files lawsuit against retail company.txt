SF Michelin-starred restaurant files lawsuit against retail company
-----
San Francisco restaurant Quince filed a lawsuit against retail brand Quince.com, alleging years of confusion between the two companies that's led customers to call in complaints, write poor Yelp reviews and return merchandise to the Michelin-starred restaurant by mistake.Quince, a three Michelin-starred restaurant, filed a trademark infringement lawsuit last November against Last Brand Inc., which is based in San Francisco and operates a lifestyle company also named Quince.
