Death Valley National Park scheduled to reopen next month
-----
Death Valley National Park is tentatively scheduled to reopen Oct. 15, nearly three months after it closed due to the impact of Hurricane Hilary, the National Park Service announced Wednesday.Death Valley and parts of State Route 190 have been closed since Aug. 20, when the area received a year's worth of rain in a single day due to the remnants of Hurricane Hilary, according to the NPS. The rain caused flash floods that damaged pavement and roads throughout the park.
