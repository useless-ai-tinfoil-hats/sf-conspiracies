St. Mary's routs Pacific in fourth consecutive wire-to-wire victory
-----
Seven, 19 and 161 were the most significant numbers for St. Mary's as it took on Pacific in Moraga on Thursday night.The Gaels extended their winning streak to seven with a 76-28 waltz. They used a 19-0 run in the first half to take control of the proceedings. And the 161? That's the consecutive minutes in which St. Mary's has not trailed over its past four-plus games.
"Wow. That's a great stat," St. Mary's swingman Alex Ducas said.
