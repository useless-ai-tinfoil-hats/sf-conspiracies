Gaudy, gigantic California castle goes up for sale for $1.2 million
-----
If you've ever wanted to travel back in time to medieval Germany without ever stepping foot outside the United States, now's your chance.A new real estate listing out of rural California offers the chance to live this past-meets-present fantasy in real life, stone parapets and all. Built in 1995, a 3,600-square-foot home called Laminack Castle in Jamul is up for sale for the modest price of $1.2 million, Zillow shows.
