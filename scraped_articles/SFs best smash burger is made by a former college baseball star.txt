SF's best smash burger is made by a former college baseball star

It seems like every restaurant that has a burger nowadays is calling it a smash burger. It's all the rage; it's the hot "new" kind of burger. But not many of the Bay Area burgers that are called smash burgers are truly smash burgers. Many are just finger-thick patties, rather than the napkin-thin versions that were probably invented in Kentucky some 60 years ago.In an era where fast-casual dining is king and prices are on the rise, the thick bistro burger is a mere memory.
