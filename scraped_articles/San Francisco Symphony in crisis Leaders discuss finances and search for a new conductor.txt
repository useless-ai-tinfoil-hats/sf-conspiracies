San Francisco Symphony in crisis: Leaders discuss finances and search for a new conductor
-----
The San Francisco Symphony has experienced a tumultuous period since Music Director Esa-Pekka Salonen announced his decision not to renew his five-year contract."I do not share the same goals for the future of the institution as the Board of Governors does," Salonen offered as the impetus for stepping down from the role after the 2024-25 season in a statement released on March 14.
Since then, it has come to light that the 113-year-old orchestra has struggled with budget deficits
