Police investigate fatal shooting in East Oakland
-----
A person was shot and killed Wednesday night in Oakland, according to police.Just before 7 p.m., officers responded to the 1000 block of 77th Avenue for a report of a shooting. Officers arrived at the scene and found evidence that a shooting occurred, but there were no victims. Police were later told the shooting victim was taken to a hospital via private transportation and was pronounced dead.
The identity of the victim is being withheld pending notification of family, police
