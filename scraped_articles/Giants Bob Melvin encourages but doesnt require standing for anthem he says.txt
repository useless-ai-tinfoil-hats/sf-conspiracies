Giants’ Bob Melvin encourages but doesn’t require standing for anthem, he says
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — Headlines across the country and reports on national and local networks have suggested that San Francisco Giants manager Bob Melvin is requiring his players to stand for the national anthem before games.That's not the case, according to Melvin, who said he simply has encouraged his players to be on the field standing when the anthem starts in a unifying effort to show his team is ready to play ball.
