Iconic Bay Area landmark to begin work to allow visitors for first time in 20 years
-----
The 151-year-old Pigeon Point lighthouse is the "grand dame" of the West Coast and it's kicking off a reconstruction project that will allow visitors to enter it for the first time in 20 years.Situated just 50 miles south of San Francisco along Highway 1, the 115-foot-tall historic landmark started getting prepped on Friday for repairs, which will begin early next year.
