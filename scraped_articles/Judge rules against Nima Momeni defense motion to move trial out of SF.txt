Judge rules against Nima Momeni defense motion to move trial out of SF
-----
UPDATE March 15, 5:28 p.m. The judge in the murder trial against Nima Momeni ruled Friday afternoon against a motion to move the proceedings out of San Francisco, dealing a blow to the defense team, according to Mission Local.-----
Nima Momeni, the IT consultant accused of killing Cash App founder Bob Lee, is appearing in San Francisco court Friday afternoon. His attorneys are expected to ask the judge to move the trial out of San Francisco.
