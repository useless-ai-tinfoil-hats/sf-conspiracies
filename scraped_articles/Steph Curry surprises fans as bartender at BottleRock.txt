Steph Curry surprises fans as bartender at BottleRock
-----
Stephen Curry revealed his latest side hustle at BottleRock Napa Valley over the weekend.The Golden State Warriors star made a surprise appearance as a bartender in one of the luxe Wine Country music festival's exclusive VIP lounges on Saturday, May 25. At his station within the opulent Salesforce Platinum Lounge, there was only one libation on offer: his signature Gentleman's Cut Kentucky straight bourbon.
