Hikers rescued after getting lost in Sierra snowstorm
-----
Three hikers were found a day after getting lost in the snowy terrain of the Tahoe National Forest, Nevada County sheriff's officials said Wednesday.Officials from the county's search and rescue team responded to a 911 call early Wednesday morning reporting three lost hikers near Basin Peak, a mountain in the northern portion of the Sierra Nevada.
