S.F. police arrest 23 people in two nights for suspected drug dealing near U.N. Plaza
-----
San Francisco police officers arrested 23 people last week for suspected drug-related activities while doing nighttime surveillance around the city's U.N. Plaza, spokespersons said Monday.The most recent arrests were made on Feb. 7 and 9 and are part of consistent nighttime operations the department has rolled out to intensify drug enforcement in the Mid-Market area.
