Gary Hobish, recording engineer and sound guy for S.F. Silent Film Festival, dies at 70
-----
The job of recording engineer is difficult to describe, but Gary Hobish always took delight when people asked what he did. He'd say, "I'm the sound guy at the silent film festival."The suggestion was that Hobish had a job that shouldn't exist. What it actually entailed was designing audio for the San Francisco Silent Film Festival, an annual event at the Castro Theatre.
