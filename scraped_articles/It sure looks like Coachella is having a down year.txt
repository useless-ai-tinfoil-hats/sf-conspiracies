It sure looks like Coachella is having a down year
-----
It looks like 2024 might be a slightly slower year for the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival.New data from AirDNA, which tracks short-term rental data from Vrbo and Airbnb, shows that bookings for the two-weekend festival in Indio are down from last year. For months, AirDNA said, fewer people have been booking nights of stays for the massive festival than during the same stretch last year.
