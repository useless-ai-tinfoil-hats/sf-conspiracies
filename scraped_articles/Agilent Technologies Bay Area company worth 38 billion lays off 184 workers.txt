Agilent Technologies, Bay Area company worth $38 billion, lays off 184 workers
-----
Agilent Technologies, a Santa Clara-based company known best for its laboratory equipment, is laying off 184 workers in California.The biotech giant announced the layoffs in a WARN filing with California on Friday, as required by the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act in the event of mass layoffs. Per the WARN, Agilent is cutting 156 workers from its Santa Clara headquarters, 11 in La Jolla, seven in Carpinteria, six in San Diego and four in Folsom.
