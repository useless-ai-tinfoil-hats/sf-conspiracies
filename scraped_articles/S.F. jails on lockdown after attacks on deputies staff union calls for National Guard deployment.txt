S.F. jails on lockdown after attacks on deputies, staff; union calls for National Guard deployment
-----
The San Francisco Sheriff's Office has locked down both of its jails for the weekend after a series of incidents over the past two weeks in which seven deputies and other jail workers were injured, officials said.
