Stanford women jump to No. 6 ranking after Indiana blowout
-----
The Stanford women's basketball team jumped nine spots to No. 6 in the Associated Press media poll Monday morning.The Cardinal (2-0) started the season ranked 15th, their lowest ranking in eight seasons. Blowout victories over Hawaii and then-No. 9 Indiana propelled Stanford back into the top 10, where it was cemented the past three seasons.
