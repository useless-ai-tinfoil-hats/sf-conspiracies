Cal falls to Texas Tech in Independence Bowl, giving up 27 straight points
-----
Cal won its final three games of the regular season to become bowl-eligible, but the Bears have finished 2023 with a losing record for the third straight full season.In the Independence Bowl in Shreveport, La., on Saturday night, the Bears started well but Texas Tech owned the final three quarters. The Red Raiders prevailed 34-14, dropping Cal to 6-7. Texas Tech (7-6) scored the game's final 27 points.
