Some of the season’s highest ‘king tides’ are coming to the Bay Area
-----
Tides of more than 6 feet came to the Bay Area starting Sunday, ushering in a "king tide" season that's expected to include Christmas Day.King tides don't have a formal definition. The term is rather a nonscientific moniker used to refer to some of the year's highest tides — sometimes 1 to 2 feet higher than the average high tide — that usually occur around the summer and winter solstices.
