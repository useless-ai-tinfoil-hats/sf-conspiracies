Antioch’s police oversight commission desperately needs more power
-----
The seven pioneering members of Antioch's police oversight commission grilled law enforcement about use-of-force policies and civilian complaints for about four hours on Monday.As the meeting dragged, it was obvious the commission has a monumental challenge in transforming a deeply dysfunctional police department. It was equally obvious the commission doesn't have the autonomy it needs — and deserves — to fire problematic officers, investigate misconduct and change department policies.
