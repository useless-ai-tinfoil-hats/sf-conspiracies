Brava Theater needs to raise $50,000 to meet basic expenses
-----
When Anastacia Powers Cuellar opened Brava Theater Center's January utility bill, she said fell "on the floor."Confronting a Pacific Gas and Electic Co. bill that was more than 2½ times as expensive as the same month's four years ago, Cuellar, the theater's executive director, recalled thinking, "This will be impossible" to pay off.
