Toro y Moi to tease new album at San Francisco beach clean-up
-----
Toro y Moi is paying tribute to the Bay Area's land and ocean ahead of the release of his new album "Hole Erth."The lo-fi artist and longtime East Bay resident is scheduled to host a beach clean-up at San Francisco's Ocean Beach on Sunday, Sept. 1, followed by a listening party at Tunnel Records where he plans to spin songs from his eighth full-length studio album, set for release next week.
