California politicians launch last-ditch effort to keep restaurant surcharges legal
-----
In late April, California restaurant owners were outraged to hear that a new California law banning "junk fees" would apply to restaurant surcharges. But now, just weeks before the junk fees law, Senate Bill 478, is set to go into effect on July 1, California legislators have introduced a new bill that would exempt restaurants from the surcharge ban.Sen.
