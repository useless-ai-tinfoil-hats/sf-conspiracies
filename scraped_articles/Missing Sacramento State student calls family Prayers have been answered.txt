Missing Sacramento State student calls family: 'Prayers have been answered'
-----
Noelle Lynch, the 23-year-old Northern California woman reported missing earlier this month, made contact with her parents, according to her mother."We would like to extend our gratitude for all of your prayers for locating our daughter Noelle," Kellie Lynch wrote in a Facebook post on April 13. "Those prayers have been answered and Noelle has reached out to us."
