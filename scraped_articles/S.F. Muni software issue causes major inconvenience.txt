S.F. Muni software issue causes ‘major inconvenience’
-----
Muni service on Market Street was delayed Tuesday morning in both directions due to a software "system issue," transit officials said.The San Francisco Municipal Transit Agency said in a tweet just before 8:30 a.m. that it was "working to resolve" the issue. The transit agency said shortly after 9 a.m. that the issue was resolved, and that service would gradually return to normal.
