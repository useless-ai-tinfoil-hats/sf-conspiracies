Sheriff: Arrests made after 6 killed in Calif. desert during 'marijuana transaction'
-----
Five individuals have been arrested after six men were shot dead during a "marijuana transaction" last week in a remote area of California's Mojave Desert, the San Bernardino County Sheriff's Department said Monday.The five individuals arrested are all male, ranging in age from 26 to 34, and could face homicide charges, according to a news release.
