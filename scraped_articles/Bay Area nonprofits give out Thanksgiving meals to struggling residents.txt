Bay Area nonprofits give out Thanksgiving meals to struggling residents
-----
Volunteers woke up early Thursday to prepare and distribute warm Thanksgiving meals to seniors and homeless people throughout the Bay Area.In Oakland, a group of volunteers from Lake Merritt United Methodist Church walked around the lake Thursday afternoon with wagons filled with lunches and hygiene kits for the homeless community living near the lake.
