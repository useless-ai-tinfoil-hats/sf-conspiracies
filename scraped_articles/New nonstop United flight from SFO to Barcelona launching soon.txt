New nonstop United flight from SFO to Barcelona launching soon
-----
United Airlines will begin offering nonstop flights from San Francisco to Barcelona in May, becoming the only American carrier to offer direct service to the Spanish city from San Francisco International Airport.Tickets are on sale now for the 11-hour daily flight, a summer seasonal offering aboard a Boeing 777 that commences May 24 to serve Primavera Sound Barcelona, a pop music festival that runs May 29-June 2.
