Palisades Tahoe employee dies in snowmobile crash during snowmaking operations
-----
A man who worked for Palisades Tahoe was killed in a snowmobile crash during overnight snowmaking operations at the resort on Thursday. A spokesperson for Palisades Tahoe confirmed the death with SFGATE on Thursday morning.According to multiple reports, CHP received a call at about 7:30 a.m. that a man had been found at Alpine Meadows, south of the main resort. The man was rushed to Tahoe Forest Hospital in nearby Truckee, where he was pronounced dead.
