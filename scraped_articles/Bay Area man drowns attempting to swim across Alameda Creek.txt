Bay Area man drowns attempting to swim across Alameda Creek
-----
A man seen attempting to swim across an East Bay creek has drowned, authorities say.At around 5 p.m. Tuesday, a 911 caller reported that an adult male was seen swimming across Alameda Creek in the Niles District of Fremont, near Third and I streets. The caller said the man struggled before going under the water and never resurfaced.
