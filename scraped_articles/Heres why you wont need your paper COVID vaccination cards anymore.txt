Here’s why you won’t need your paper COVID vaccination cards anymore
-----
Do you still have a dog-eared coronavirus vaccination card tucked away in your wallet? It may someday be a collector's item.The U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention announced on Tuesday that it no longer distributes COVID-19 vaccination cards, which had become ubiquitous during the pandemic.
