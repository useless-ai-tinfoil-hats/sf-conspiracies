Patti LuPone says music ‘is our human right’ ahead of her S.F. concert

For many fans, Patti LuPone is the epitome of a Broadway diva. But the star of musical hits including "Evita," "Anything Goes" and the 2020 Broadway revival of Stephen Sondheim's "Company" said that growing up in Long Island in the 1950s and '60s, rock music was a formative influence."I am a product of rock 'n' roll," said the 74-year-old triple Tony Award winner. "We grew up together."
