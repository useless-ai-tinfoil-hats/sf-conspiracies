Woman shot dead during violent confrontation in East Oakland
-----
Oakland police say they arrested three people in connection with a shooting that left a woman dead Wednesday afternoon, following a tense altercation on the 5600 block of Hilton Street.The woman, whose name was not immediately released, drove with her mother to confront the three suspects in a vehicle on the narrow residential street shortly after 2 p.m.
During that exchange, a passenger emerged from the suspect vehicle and shot the victim, police said.
