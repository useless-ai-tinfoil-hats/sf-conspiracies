Burned remains of slain victim found in San Jose
-----
San Jose police discovered the burned human remains of a slain victim this week at a homeless encampment, the department said Wednesday.About 4:33 p.m. on Monday, officers responded to a suspicious circumstance near Roberts Avenue and Vintage Way, police said. Authorities said a person reported finding the burned remains of a missing family member at a homeless encampment in the area.
