Giants add Beck, Sabol to roster; Luciano, Matos remain in Triple-A
-----
With rosters expanding from 26 to 28 players on Sunday, the San Francisco Giants added right-hander Tristan Beck and catcher Blake Sabol to the active roster.Beck, 28, underwent vascular surgery for an aneurysm in his upper right arm in early March and has been working his way back since. Beck and the team decided to take extra precautions in his recovery and didn't start his rehab assignment until Aug. 8 because the injury isn't common for baseball players.
