Bad combination of weather and construction force ground stop at SFO
-----
The Federal Aviation Administration issued a ground stop at San Francisco International Airport Wednesday morning due to the airport's ongoing runway construction.According to FlightAware, there have been 92 delays at SFO so far today — largely impacting flights on United Airlines and SkyWest Airlines —while 46 flights have been cancelled, mostly from United and Alaska Airlines flights.
