Flag or tackle? Girls now have an option in football with new CIF sport
-----
The first time Madison Perea threw a ball in flag football practice, she knew this was going to be the most fun fall of her life.It was more enjoyable than blocking while the ball sailed above her or she created a hole for someone else to run for a touchdown. In her first season of flag football, Perea has gotten to be the star for Liberty-Brentwood, after three years as an offensive lineman on the tackle team.
The senior is one of a few dozen girls facing a choice across the state
