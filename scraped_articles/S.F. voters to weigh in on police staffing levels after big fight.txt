S.F. voters to weigh in on police staffing levels after big fight
-----
San Francisco voters will weigh in on police staffing in March after the San Francisco Board of Supervisors decided in a close vote Tuesday to place a controversial measure on the March ballot.The measure would set a minimum police staffing level and potentially raise money to pay for recruitment and retention through new taxes or existing, modified taxes.
