S.F. jury could hear closing arguments in Pelosi attack trial next week
-----
Jurors are expected to hear closing arguments early next week in the state case against David DePape, the man who attacked House Speaker Emerita Nancy Pelosi's husband, Paul Pelosi, in the head with a hammer in 2022.Defense attorneys for DePape rested their case Friday morning, noting that they would not bring any witnesses — including DePape — before the jury.
