Two tech giants lay off more workers as cuts continue
-----
Tech giant Meta and the Twitch division of Amazon laid off additional workers in the past week, underscoring that job cuts continue in the hard-hit industry nearly a year after huge reductions began.Menlo Park-based Meta, which owns Facebook, Instagram and WhatsApp, laid off employees in its Reality Labs division, Reuters first reported. An unspecified number of workers creating custom silicon for metaverse products were cut.
