Reports: Cal adds North Texas QB, loses Sam Jackson V to transfer portal
-----
Cal began this season with Sam Jackson V as its starting quarterback in a 58-21 win at North Texas. It appears the Bears will begin next season without Jackson but with a quarterback who had been with the Mean Green.On3sports.com reported Monday that North Texas QB Chandler Rogers, who entered the transfer portal last week, has committed to Cal. Rogers came off the bench against the Bears and completed 3 of 7 passes for 10 yards.
