The life expectancy gap between men and women is getting worse. Here’s why
-----
The gap in life expectancy between U.S. men and women grew to its largest in 25 years in 2021, largely driven by men being more likely to die from COVID-19 and drug overdoses, according to a new study by UCSF and Harvard researchers.The research, published Wednesday in JAMA Internal Medicine, analyzed life expectancy data from the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention and sought to explain why the gap is widening between men and women.
