SF neighborhood sushi restaurant closes original location after 36 years
-----
After 36 years on Valencia Street, neighborhood sushi spot We Be Sushi has closed its original location. Eater was the first to report on the closure earlier this month, when the owners reportedly posted a note on the door announcing the closure as well as a "restaurant for sale" sign.On Tuesday, chef Andy Tonozuka, who runs We Be Sushi with his wife Eiko Tonozuka, confirmed to Mission Local that the restaurant's last day was Feb. 29. He explained that they closed the 1071 Valencia St.
