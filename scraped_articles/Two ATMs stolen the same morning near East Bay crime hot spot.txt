Two ATMs stolen the same morning near East Bay crime hot spot
-----
Two ATMs were stolen on Sunday within little more than an hour of each other near an East Bay street now making headlines for rampant auto burglaries.The Oakland Police Department told SFGATE it believes multiple people arriving in several cars stole the first machine at approximately 6:15 a.m. on Jan. 21. When officers arrived on the scene in the 8300 block of Edgewater Drive, they found damage at a business and discovered the ATM was gone.
