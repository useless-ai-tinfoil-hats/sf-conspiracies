Suspect in SF car bomb case has disturbing history of allegations
-----
The Bay Area man who is being investigated for allegedly throwing bombs out of his car window and attacking a church parishioner had a long history of violence, officials said.The suspect, 42-year-old David Garcia, was arrested on Sunday around 6 p.m. for allegedly punching a parishioner and subsequently leading police on a pursuit that started in San Francisco and ended in Martinez, SFGATE previously reported.
