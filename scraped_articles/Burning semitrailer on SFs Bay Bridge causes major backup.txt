Burning semitrailer on SF's Bay Bridge causes major backup
-----
LATEST May 24, 12:45 p.m. Fire crews have extinguished a semitractor-trailer fire on the Bay Bridge, San Francisco Fire Department Capt. Jonathan Baxter told SFGATE. The Oakland Fire Department also assisted on the scene, the SFFD said in a social media post.Other officials are still on the scene, including the California Highway Patrol.
