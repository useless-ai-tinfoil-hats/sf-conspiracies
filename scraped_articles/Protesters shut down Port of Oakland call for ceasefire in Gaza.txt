Protesters shut down Port of Oakland, call for cease-fire in Gaza
-----
Protesters calling for a cease-fire in Gaza again descended on the Port of Oakland and shut down operations Saturday, organizers said.More than 1,000 protesters gathered early Saturday and marched at the port, waving Palestinian flags and beating drums while chanting for a free Palestine and calling for the United States to stop military aid to Israel.
After disrupting the port's morning operations, protesters left around 8 a.m. and returned around 2 p.m. for the port's afternoon
