Lawsuit claims San Mateo County’s unusual public defender program is ‘defective and unlawful’
-----
San Mateo is the only California county with a population over 300,000 where low-income criminal defendants are represented by a Private Defender Program, an arm of the county Bar Association, rather than a county-employed public defender.
