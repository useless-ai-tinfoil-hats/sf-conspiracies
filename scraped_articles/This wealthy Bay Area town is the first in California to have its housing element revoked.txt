This wealthy Bay Area town is the first in California to have its housing element revoked
-----
The Peninsula town of Portola Valley has become the first jurisdiction in California to have its housing plan revoked by state officials after the wealthy enclave failed to approve changes to allow for denser housing and more multifamily homes.As long as the town doesn't have a compliant blueprint for development, called a housing element, it will not be eligible for state grants for housing and transportation.
