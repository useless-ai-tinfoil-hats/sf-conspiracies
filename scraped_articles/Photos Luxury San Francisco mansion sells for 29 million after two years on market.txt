Photos: Luxury San Francisco mansion sells for $29 million after two years on market
-----
A mansion in one of San Francisco's most exclusive neighborhoods has sold after nearly two years on the market, closing at approximately 10% below its asking price.The property at 2799 Broadway, owned by tech entrepreneurs Michael and Xochi Birch, was originally listed for $32 million in September 2022. It sold for $29,150,000 on Friday, said listing agent Deborah Svoboda of Sotheby's International Realty.
