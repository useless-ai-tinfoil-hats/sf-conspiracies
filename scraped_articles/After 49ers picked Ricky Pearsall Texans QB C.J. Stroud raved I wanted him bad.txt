After 49ers picked Ricky Pearsall, Texans QB C.J. Stroud raved: ‘I wanted him bad’

C.J. Stroud responded as if he had just thrown a game-sealing pick-six after he saw that the San Francisco 49ers had picked Florida wide receiver Ricky Pearsall in the first round of the NFL draft on Thursday night.The Houston Texans quarterback, appearing on Bleacher Report's live draft show, shot back in his chair, put his hands over his head, slumped over the armrest and groaned.
