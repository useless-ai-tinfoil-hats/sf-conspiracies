Man charged in killing of Kevin Nishita arrested near Kansas City
-----
A man suspected in the fatal shooting of security guard Kevin Nishita in Oakland in 2021 was arrested Wednesday near Kansas City, authorities said Thursday.Laron Gilbert is one of three men charged in Alameda County Superior Court with murder and other alleged offenses in the shooting of Nishita. The two other defendants, Hershel Hale and Shadihia Mitchell, were arrested in 2021.
Nishita was a retired police officer who provided security services for several companies in the Bay
