For the first time ever, these Outside Lands leftovers are getting a new life
-----
Festivals are notorious for generating large amounts of trash and not always dealing with it in the greenest of ways. But this year, Outside Lands chose to send its leftover vendor signs to beloved local nonprofit SCRAP, diverting 700 pounds of material from the landfill.For a festival known to annoy locals with its traffic disruptions and noise, this might be a redeeming quality.
