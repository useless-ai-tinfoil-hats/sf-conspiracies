Summer of Music free concert series returns to San Francisco
-----
Summer of Music, a free concert series in San Francisco, is scheduled to return, bringing live music throughout summer with the aim of breaking the doom loop narrative plaguing the city.A joint effort by San Francisco nonprofit Civic Joy Fund and Bay Area music promoter Noise Pop Industries, the live music series will kick off on June 15 and run every weekend through Sept. 1.
