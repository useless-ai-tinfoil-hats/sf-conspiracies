Tesla lays off more than 3,300 California workers, mostly in Bay Area
-----
Tesla's massive, companywide layoff round is set to slam California, with 3,332 workers across four cities losing their jobs in June.The Elon Musk-led automaker revealed the timing and scope of the layoffs in WARN filings with California on Monday; such documentation is required by the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act in the event of mass layoffs.
