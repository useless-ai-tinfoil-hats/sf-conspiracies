Schools chief Tony Thurmond announces 2026 bid for California governor
-----
California schools chief Tony Thurmond announced Tuesday that he's running for governor in 2026.Thurmond joins Lt. Gov. Eleni Kounalakis and former state Controller Betty Yee in the race to succeed Gov. Gavin Newsom.
Thurmond, a Democrat, previously represented Richmond in the state Assembly.
He announced his run with a video posted to social media highlighting a childhood characterized by poverty and the death of his mother when he was 6 years old.
