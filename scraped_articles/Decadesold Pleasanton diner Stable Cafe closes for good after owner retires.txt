Decades-old Pleasanton diner Stable Cafe closes for good after owner retires
-----
Stable Cafe, a 32-year-old Pleasanton diner operating at the horse racetrack of the Alameda County Fairgrounds, served its final meal on Sept. 8.Esther Hall — cook, waitress, cashier and owner — ran the operation for 32 years. She has decided to retire and move closer to her family, according to Pleasanton Weekly. News of the closure was originally announced in a Facebook post on Aug. 6.
