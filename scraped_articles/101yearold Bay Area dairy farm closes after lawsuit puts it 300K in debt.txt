101-year-old Bay Area dairy farm closes after lawsuit puts it $300K in debt
-----
In October, Sonoma County family farm Mulas Dairy closed after 101 years. But the farm's president, Mike Mulas, kept the news quiet until recently, when he spoke with the Press Democrat.The Schellville dairy farm closed after racking up nearly $300,000 in debt from an environmental lawsuit, Mulas said.
