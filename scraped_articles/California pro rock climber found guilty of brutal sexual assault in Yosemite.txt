California pro rock climber found guilty of brutal sexual assault in Yosemite
-----
A California rock climber was found guilty by a federal jury Tuesday of sexually abusing a woman in Yosemite National Park, prosecutors said.Charles Barrett, 39, was convicted on two counts of aggravated sexual abuse and one count of abusive sexual contact, according to a Feb. 13 news release from the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Eastern District of California. The crimes were prosecuted on the federal level because Yosemite National Park is federal land.
