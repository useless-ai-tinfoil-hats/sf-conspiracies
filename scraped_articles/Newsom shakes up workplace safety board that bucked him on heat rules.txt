Newsom shakes up workplace safety board that bucked him on heat rules
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom has removed one member and demoted the chairperson of a state workplace safety board who criticized his administration's handling of a proposed heat protection rule this year.The shake-up comes less than two weeks before the Occupational Safety and Health Standards Board is expected to approve the rule, requiring businesses to shield their indoor workers from the risks of extreme heat.
