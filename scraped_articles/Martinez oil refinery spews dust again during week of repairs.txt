Martinez oil refinery spews dust again during week of repairs
-----
The troubled Martinez oil refinery accidentally released petroleum coke dust into the air Saturday afternoon, the latest in a long string of emissions issues related to the facility. The Contra Costa County health department said there was no impact to the surrounding community.The Martinez Refining Co. launched the dust at about 1:20 p.m., the department said in a social media post.
