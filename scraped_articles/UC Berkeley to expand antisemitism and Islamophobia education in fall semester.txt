UC Berkeley to expand antisemitism and Islamophobia education in fall semester
-----
UC Berkeley will expand its antisemitism education this fall to all new students, leaders of student groups and residential assistants, outgoing Chancellor Carol Christ told an advisory committee this week.The new requirement will run parallel to expanded education against Islamophobia that Christ agreed to last month in exchange for pro-Palestinian protesters dismantling their encampment on Sproul Plaza, said Dan Mogulof, a campus spokesperson.
