Nearly five-year saga of SF Hayes Valley Trader Joe's to end this summer
-----
After nearly five years, San Franciscans will finally see their promised Trader Joe's store in Hayes Valley.On Wednesday night, Melanie Chesley, a regional manager for Trader Joe's, told community members who attended a Hayes Valley Neighborhood Association meeting that the forthcoming Trader Joe's storefront at 555 Fulton St. is expected to open sometime in June and that it was currently hiring staff, according to the San Francisco Standard.
