Kehlani to hold free meet-and-greet in Oakland this week
-----
Oakland R&B singer Kehlani is returning home to celebrate her fourth studio album, "Crash," with a free signing and a record release party.The Grammy-nominated artist plans to stop by Lakeshore Avenue in Oakland on Friday, June 21, for the first in a series of Crash Records pop-up shop events. The activations will offer fans the opportunity to meet Kehlani the day of her new record's release and shop a selection of official merchandise, including vinyl records and CDs.
