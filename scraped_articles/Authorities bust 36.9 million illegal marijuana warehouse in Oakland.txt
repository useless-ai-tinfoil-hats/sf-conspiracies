Authorities bust $36.9 million illegal marijuana warehouse in Oakland
-----
Law enforcement busted a massive illegal cannabis grow in Oakland last week, finding an estimated $36.9 million worth of cannabis in an unlicensed facility.In total, an operation led by the California Department of Fish and Wildlife found 41,082 cannabis plants and 1,841 pounds of processed cannabis, according to Janice Mackey, a spokesperson for the agency.
"This is one of the largest cannabis enforcement actions (in terms of retail value) in the Bay Area this year," Mackey wrote
