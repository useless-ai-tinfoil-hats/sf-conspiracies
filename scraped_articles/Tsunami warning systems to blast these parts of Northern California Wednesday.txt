Tsunami warning systems to blast these parts of Northern California Wednesday
-----
Tsunami sirens will blare along the Northern California coast Wednesday to test emergency alert systems.Mendocino, Humboldt and Del Norte counties along with parts of Sonoma County will have their annual tsunami warning systems test Wednesday from 11 a.m. to noon.
