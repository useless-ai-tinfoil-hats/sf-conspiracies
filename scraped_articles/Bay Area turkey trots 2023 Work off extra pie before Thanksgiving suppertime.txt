Bay Area turkey trots 2023: Work off extra pie before Thanksgiving suppertime
-----
It's Thanksgiving time, when themes of health, gratitude and the need for peaceful coexistence with our neighbors prompt us to get together to celebrate. Turkey trots are a healthy and fun way to kick off the feast, while also providing a much needed boost for local food banks and charities as they collect items vital to those in need at many of these events.Check out the Chronicle's guide to active ways to work off all the turkey and mashed potatoes you can eat.
