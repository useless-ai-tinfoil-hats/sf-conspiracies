Vigil and memorial to honor fallen San Jose police community service officer
-----
A vigil this week and a memorial service next week will honor a San Jose police community service officer who was fatally struck by a car in the line of duty.Long Pham, 34, and a second community service officer were directing traffic when they were struck at the intersection of Monterey Road and Palm Avenue in the southern part of the city on Aug. 3. The second community resource officer was treated at a hospital and released four days later, police said.
The driver was arrested
