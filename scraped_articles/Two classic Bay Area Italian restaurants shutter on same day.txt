Two classic Bay Area Italian restaurants shutter on same day
-----
On June 22, two cherished Bay Area Italian restaurants with nearly 120 years of combined experience closed for good.Pezzella's Villa Napoli, the 67-year-old restaurant at 1025 W. Camino Real in Sunnyvale, was a family business known for classic Italian American dishes. Owner Vince Pezzella, who called the restaurant a "second home" since he was a teenager, felt that it was just the right time to close, according to NBC Bay Area (KNTV-TV).
