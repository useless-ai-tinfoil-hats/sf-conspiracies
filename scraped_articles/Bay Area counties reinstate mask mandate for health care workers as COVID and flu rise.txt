Bay Area counties reinstate mask mandate for health care workers as COVID and flu rise
-----
Officials in Bay Area counties have issued health orders requiring health care workers in patient care settings to wear masks in response to the increasing risk of respiratory illnesses, including COVID-19 and influenza.The orders issued on Tuesday in Sonoma and Contra Costa counties will remain in effect from Nov. 1 through April 30, covering the respiratory virus season.
