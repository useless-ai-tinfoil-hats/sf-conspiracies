With an eye on defense, Giants call up Grant McCray and demote Marco Luciano
-----
Grant McCray thought he was still dreaming when he awoke on Wednesday morning to the phone call he always had wanted. Sacramento River Cats manager Dave Brundage was on the line with the news: The 23-year-old was getting called up to the San Francisco Giants."I cried a little bit," McCray said. "Then I called my mom and my dad. It's a dream come true."
