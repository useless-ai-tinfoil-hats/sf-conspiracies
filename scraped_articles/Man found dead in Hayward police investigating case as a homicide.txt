Man found dead in Hayward, police investigating case as a homicide
-----
Police are investigating the death of a 60-year-old man in Hayward early Wednesday as a homicide, according to a statement from the Hayward Police Department.At about 6:44 a.m., Hayward fire and medical personnel responded to reports of an unconscious man near the 800 block of C Street. The man appeared to have been deceased for several hours, police said.
