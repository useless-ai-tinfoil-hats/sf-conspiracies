Damian Lillard trade makes Warriors’ Steph Curry, Klay Thompson, Draymond Green last of dying NBA breed
-----
The Wednesday trade that sent Damian Lillard from the Portland Trail Blazers to the Milwaukee Bucks sent shockwaves throughout the basketball world — and further showed Golden State Warriors fans that their team's championship core is, truly, the last of a dying breed in the increasingly mercenary NBA.With Lillard moving east, Steph Curry, Klay Thompson and Draymond Green stand alone as the three longest-tenured players to have spent their entire careers with one franchise.
