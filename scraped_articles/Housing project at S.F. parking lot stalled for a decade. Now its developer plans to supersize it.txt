Housing project at S.F. parking lot stalled for a decade. Now its developer plans to super-size it
-----
It took nearly five years for developer Manouch Mosheydi to win approvals to transform a Mission District parking lot across the street from the historic Armory building into 60 apartments. That was in 2019.Nearly five years later, the site remains a surface parking lot, likely a victim to pandemic fallout in the real estate market. Now, almost a decade into plans to develop the lot at 344 14th St., Mosheydi is, essentially, starting over.
