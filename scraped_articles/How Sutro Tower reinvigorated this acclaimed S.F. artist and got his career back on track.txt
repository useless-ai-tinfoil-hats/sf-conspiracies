How Sutro Tower reinvigorated this acclaimed S.F. artist, and got his career back on track
-----
Sutro Tower has been many things in its 51-year life: an object of scorn, a structure likened to alien creatures and, more recently, a cult icon with devotees who tattoo it into their flesh.Now, add another attribute to the list: The skeletal apparition has relit the creative spark of one of San Francisco's most acclaimed artists, Paul Madonna, 18 months after he nearly died in a hit-and-run accident.
