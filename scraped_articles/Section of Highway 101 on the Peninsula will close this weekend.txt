Section of Highway 101 on the Peninsula will close this weekend
-----
Northbound Highway 101 will be closed in part of the South Bay this weekend, forcing drivers either west to Interstate 280 or east to Interstate 880.From 10 p.m. Friday through 5 a.m. Monday, the northbound lanes of 101 between Brittan and Whipple avenues in Redwood City will close, according to Caltrans.
Instead, drivers headed north can either take I-280 or go up I-880 and cross over on the San Mateo-Hayward Bridge.
