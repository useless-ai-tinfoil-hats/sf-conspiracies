Gold Ranch Fire near Interstate 80 at Calif.Nev. border has 8% containment
-----
LATEST Aug. 13, 1 p.m. Fire officials reported 8% containment on the Gold Ranch Fire, near the California-Nevada state line, on Tuesday morning. Red flag conditions have been challenging, the Reno Fire Department said on X, but aerial retardant, dozer lines and 3,500 feet of hose all helped crews build containment despite the winds. Evacuation orders were also downgraded Tuesday morning.Aug. 12, 10 a.m. A 650-acre fire next to eastbound Interstate 80 near the California-Nevada state line
