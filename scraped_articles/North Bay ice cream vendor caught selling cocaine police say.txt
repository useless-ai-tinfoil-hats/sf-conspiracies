North Bay ice cream vendor caught selling cocaine, police say
-----
Three North Bay street vendors were arrested in June for allegedly selling cocaine near an elementary school, officials announced in a Monday press release.In a Monday announcement, the Santa Rosa Police Department explained that detectives from its narcotics unit had launched an investigation in April into Victor Martinez-Diaz, 49, whom they suspected of dealing cocaine on West 9th Street near Abraham Lincoln Elementary School. Martinez-Diaz also had a legal business selling ice cream
