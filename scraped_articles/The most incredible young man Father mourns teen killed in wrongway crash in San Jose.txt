‘The most incredible young man’: Father mourns teen killed in wrong-way crash in San Jose
-----
The father of a teenager killed by a wrong-way driver in San Jose on Monday mourned his son in a statement posted to Reddit on Thursday."He was my world," Cameron Olsen wrote of his 14-year-old son, Charlie Olsen. "He was my best friend, my travel buddy and the only person I ever truly felt at home with."
