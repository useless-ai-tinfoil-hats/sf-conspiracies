Alameda officials urge S.F. to halt Portola Music Festival over noise complaints
-----
The city of Alameda has formally petitioned San Francisco to pull the plug on the annual Portola Music Festival after a barrage of noise complaints that echoed across the bay during last weekend's electronic music event at Pier 80.In a statement posted to Alameda's official social media channels, authorities decried the "significant noise impacts" caused by the two-day festival on Sept. 30 and Oct. 1, despite attempts by the organizers to mitigate the issue.
