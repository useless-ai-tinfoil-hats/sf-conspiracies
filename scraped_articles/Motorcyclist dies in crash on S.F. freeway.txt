Motorcyclist dies in crash on S.F. freeway
-----
A motorcyclist crashed and died Thursday afternoon near the connector of westbound Interstate 80 and southbound Highway 101 in San Francisco, according to firefighters.San Francisco firefighters responded to the scene just before 2:20 p.m., according to Justin Schorr, a spokesperson for the department. Paramedics at the scene found the motorcyclist suffering from "severe injuries," Schorr said.
The medical examiner was requested to respond to the scene.
