‘A spare room and a big heart’: Reentry program pairs formerly incarcerated people with willing homeowners
-----
Philippe Kelly knew finding a place to live after prison would make or break his return to society."To become free, you have to have somewhere to go," said Kelly, 37, who entered prison in 2001 for a fatal Los Angeles shooting he committed when he was 15. "Homelessness is definitely one of the reasons why recidivism with certain types of incarcerated folks is high."
