S.F. is struggling to hire first responders. This new plan would pay student debt to boost staffing
-----
As San Francisco struggles to hire enough police officers, nurses, 911 dispatchers and other first responders, a San Francisco supervisor is proposing a student loan forgiveness fund to help boost staffing.Supervisor Ahsha Safaí, who is running for mayor, wants to start with a $1 million fund to forgive up to $25,000 in student loan debt for newly hired first responders after three years of service.
