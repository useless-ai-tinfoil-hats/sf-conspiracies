Tahoe traffic halted after truck jackknifes in front of highway camera
-----
As the Bay Area shivers from an usually cold spring storm, Tahoe contends with snow showers and a total traffic stop due to a crash involving at least three big rigs.Caltrans said it halted westbound traffic on Interstate 80 at both the state line and Truckee to allow crews space to address a jackknifed truck that's blocking both lanes.
The agency said there is no estimated time for reopening.
Eastbound I-80 remains open, but with active chain controls.
