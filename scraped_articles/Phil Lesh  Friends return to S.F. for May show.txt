Phil Lesh & Friends return to S.F. for May show
-----
Phil Lesh is returning to the Warfield next month with a stacked lineup of his musical friends.The upcoming Phil Lesh & Friends show at the historic San Francisco venue is scheduled for May 9 and marks the former Grateful Dead bassist's only scheduled show for the rest of the year. His first performance of 2024 was at the Market Street venue in January.
