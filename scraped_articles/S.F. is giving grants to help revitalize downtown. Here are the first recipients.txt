S.F. is giving grants to help revitalize downtown. Here are the first recipients
-----
Queer nightclubs, jazz bars and more are among the recipients of San Francisco's new Entertainment & Nightlife Revitalization Grant program.First announced in May, the ENRG program provides local businesses and neighborhood organizations grants of up to $50,000 each, from a pot of $500,000, to produce unique weekend and evening programming downtown.
