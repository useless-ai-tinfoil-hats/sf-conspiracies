California storm live updates: Nearly 400,000 without power in Bay Area
-----
Nearly 400,000 PG&E customers without power in Bay AreaFeb. 4, 7:25 p.m.
Power outages to PG&E customers in the Bay Area continued to rise exponentially Sunday night as reports of fallen trees and power lines proliferated. As of 7 p.m., PG&E said 389,546 Bay Area customers were without power.
That number was at 231,232 customers at 4 p.m., and 70,000 as of 1 p.m.
