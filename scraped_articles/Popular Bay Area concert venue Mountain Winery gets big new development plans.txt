Popular Bay Area concert venue Mountain Winery gets big new development plans
-----
The owners of Mountain Winery, a popular concert venue in the South Bay, have big development plans in the works.Bill Hirschman, who bought the property along with several others about 25 years ago, filed preliminary plans with Santa Clara County in March for a major project that would add an 81-room hotel and 225 units of housing.
