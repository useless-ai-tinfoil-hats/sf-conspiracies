'The ground just gave away': Horse stuck in Point Reyes ravine requires 7-hour rescue
-----
A horse that was trapped in a steep ravine prompted an hourslong rescue saga on Tuesday in Point Reyes National Seashore. The Arabian gelding is expected to make a full recovery.According to a Thursday press release from the National Park Service, the 20-year-old horse fell in a "densely vegetated ravine" near the National Seashore's Coast Trail.
