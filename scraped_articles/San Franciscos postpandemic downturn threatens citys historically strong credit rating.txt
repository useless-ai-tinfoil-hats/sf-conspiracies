San Francisco’s post-pandemic downturn threatens city’s historically strong credit rating
-----
Weakness in San Francisco's commercial real estate market and the slow-moving recovery of economic drivers such as tourism could impact the city's ability to repay its long term debt, according to S&P Global Ratings.The financial services firm this week downgraded its outlook on the city's outstanding general obligation and appropriation debt from "stable" to "negative."
