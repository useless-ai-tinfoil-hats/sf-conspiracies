Map: Closure planned on major East Bay freeway this weekend
-----
A portion of southbound Interstate 680 in the Pleasanton area will be closed for the upcoming weekend, and Caltrans is warning drivers to avoid the area and take transit or alternate routes.Beginning at 9 p.m. Friday, all southbound lanes of I-680 from the Interstate 580 interchange in Dublin to Highway 84 in Sunol will close to make way for crews repaving the freeway, Caltrans said. All lanes are scheduled to reopen by 4 a.m. Monday.
