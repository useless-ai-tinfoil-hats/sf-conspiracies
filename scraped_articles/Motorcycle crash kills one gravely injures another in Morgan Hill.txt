Motorcycle crash kills one, gravely injures another in Morgan Hill
-----
A motorcycle crash in Morgan Hill killed one person and gravely injured a second Wednesday, the California Highway Patrol-Hollister Gilroy told KNTV.The 68-year-old male driving the motorcycle and his 60-year-old female passenger, both from Los Gatos, were struck by a car on Watsonville Road near Morgan Hill and thrown from the bike. A 41-year-old Prunedale man was driving the car that struck them and was uninjured.
