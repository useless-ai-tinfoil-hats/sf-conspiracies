Judges unwind at winery known for culinary experiences
-----
After tasting hundreds of wines over four days, the more than 50 judges at the 2024 San Francisco Chronicle Wine Competition really did not need to tip another glass. Yet the annual celebratory dinner is a highlight of their week, always held at a notable Sonoma or Napa winery, and resplendent with a wine reception, followed by a multi-course feast paired with fine wine and more fine wine.This evening isn't work, however, but an opportunity to savor.
