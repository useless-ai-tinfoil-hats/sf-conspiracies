Eight injured after car strikes bus in East Bay
-----
Eight people were injured when a car struck a bus Thursday afternoon in Antioch, according to officials.Around 12:30 p.m. the Contra Costa County Fire Protection District responded to the crash at Highway 4 East and Laurel Road, according to a spokesperson for the district.
Of those injured, two were taken to a local hospital with moderate injuries and six suffered minor injuries that did not require hospital care, according to the spokesperson.
