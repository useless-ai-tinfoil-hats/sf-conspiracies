Pair with ghost gun accused of shoplifting at Marin County mall, police say
-----
Police arrested two people Saturday after the pair were suspected of shoplifting with a child at Northgate Mall in San Rafael while carrying at least one illegal gun.Officers responded to the mall at 7:45 p.m. after receiving reports that a man, a woman and a child were seen "concealing merchandise for the purpose of shoplifting at a department store," according to the San Rafael Police Department.
