Giants’ Kyle Harrison impresses against Dodgers before rain intervenes
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — Thousands of fans at Scottsdale Stadium lifted umbrellas and burrowed into the hoods of their coats to watch, between raindrops, the San Francisco Giants take on the Dodgers. Even in a spring exhibition, rivalry games captivate.But the steady downpour that began around first pitch eventually became too much for those on the field.
