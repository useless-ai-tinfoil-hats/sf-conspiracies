Newsom vetoes bill to pay low-income jurors $100 a day
-----
A bill to boost the pay for low-income jurors in criminal cases in San Francisco and four other California counties, aimed at increasing jury diversity, has been vetoed by Gov. Gavin Newsom, who said it would cost too much.The bill, AB881 by Assembly Member Phil Ting, D-San Francisco, would have extended a pilot program operating in San Francisco since March 2022 that pays low- and moderate-income jurors $100 a day instead of the usual $15 a day.
