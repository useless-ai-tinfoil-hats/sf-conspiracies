California's Refugio State Beach and Gaviota State Park remain closed after storm damage
-----
Crews are working to remove more than a dozen iconic trees and clean up other damage in two state parks along California's Central Coast, which was battered by storms in early February.Gaviota State Park has "no anticipated date of reopening" according to the park website, while Refugio State Beach is slated to reopen after debris removal and repairs are complete.
