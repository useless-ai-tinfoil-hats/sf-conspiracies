Apple says AllTrails is the best iPhone app of 2023
-----
AllTrails is one of the most popular outdoors apps on the market and a regular mention in roundups of top health and fitness apps. Last week, Apple made official what hikers the world over already knew, naming AllTrails its 2023 iPhone App of the Year.The San Francisco-based program edged out language-learning app Duolingo and real-time flight tracker Flighty for the top spot in the most prestigious category of Apple's annual App Store Awards.
