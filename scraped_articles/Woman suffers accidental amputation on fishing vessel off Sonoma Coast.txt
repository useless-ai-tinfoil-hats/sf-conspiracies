Woman suffers accidental amputation on fishing vessel off Sonoma Coast
-----
A woman lost a limb in an apparent accidental amputation Sunday morning on a recreational fishing vessel off the Sonoma Coast, authorities said.Emergency responders with Sonoma County Fire treated the woman after meeting the vessel at the Bodega Bay boat ramp, California Highway Patrol's Golden Gate Air Operations division said in a statement. The woman was transported by helicopter to Kaiser Vacaville, where she was being treated by the trauma team, officials said.
