Man charged in 2022 Sacramento mass shooting found dead in jail cell
-----
A man charged with murder in connection with a 2022 mass shooting in downtown Sacramento was found dead in his jail cell Saturday, according to his attorney.Officials at the Sacramento County Sheriff's Office said they encountered the unresponsive 29-year-old at 2:15 a.m. as deputies conducted a check of the 5-East 300 Pod in Sacramento County Main Jail.
