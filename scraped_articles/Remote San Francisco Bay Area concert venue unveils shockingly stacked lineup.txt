Remote San Francisco Bay Area concert venue unveils shockingly stacked lineup
-----
The Mountain Winery, a popular but remote concert venue in Saratoga that is also an active winery, has managed to book an all-star lineup for its 2024 concert series. The amphitheater only seats 2,500 and is over an hour's drive from downtown San Francisco. But that doesn't appear to be a hindrance to drawing Rock and Roll Hall of Famers and comedy megastars alike.The concert series kicks off on May 5 with a performance by four-time Grammy winner Seal.
