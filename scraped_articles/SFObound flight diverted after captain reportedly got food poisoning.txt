SFO-bound flight diverted after captain reportedly got food poisoning
-----
A San Francisco-bound flight carrying 184 passengers had to divert to the Incheon International Airport in South Korea last weekend, and it was not due to another mechanical issue or one of the plane's tires falling off and landing in the employee parking lot.United flight 806 was bound for San Francisco International Airport on May 19 but returned safely to Incheon to "address a medical issue," a spokesperson for the airline wrote in a statement to SFGATE.
