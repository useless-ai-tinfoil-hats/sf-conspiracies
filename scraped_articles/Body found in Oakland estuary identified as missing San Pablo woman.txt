Body found in Oakland estuary identified as missing San Pablo woman
-----
A body found in the Oakland Estuary has been positively identified as Que Tran, San Pablo police said Tuesday.Tran and daughter Tho Ly were reported missing by Ly's husband, Phuc Vo, on Sept. 23. Police arrested Vo on Feb. 29 and murder charges were filed against him in Contra Costa County days later.
