Cult favorite UK and NYC sandwich chain opens its first Calif. location
-----
International chain Pret a Manger, known for its grab-and-go sandwiches, organic coffee and fresh baked goods, has quietly opened its first standalone California location in the Westwood neighborhood of Los Angeles. Translated as "ready-to-eat" and commonly referred to as Pret, the popular British company has more than 650 outposts worldwide, but has never come to California with a standalone cafe before.According to the Daily Bruin, LA's first Pret debuted on Saturday, May 11.
