Two San Francisco bars named to top 50 in North America list
-----
The awards for North America's 50 Best Bars were just announced, and two San Francisco hot spots made the list. The list is voted on by 270 anonymous experts ranging from bartenders and bar owners to media members and the ambiguous "well-travelled cocktail connoisseurs."Coming in at No. 30 was True Laurel in San Francisco's Mission District.
