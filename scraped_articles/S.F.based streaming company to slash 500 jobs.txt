S.F.-based streaming company to slash 500 jobs
-----
San Francisco-based live streaming site Twitch is expected to slash about 500 jobs as soon as Wednesday, according to Bloomberg.Bloomberg reported that the cuts come amidst concerns regarding losses at the company and after several top executives left the company in the span of a few months. Twitch did not respond to the Chronicle's request for comment.
