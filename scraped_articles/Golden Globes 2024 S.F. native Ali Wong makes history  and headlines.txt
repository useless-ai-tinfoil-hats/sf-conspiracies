Golden Globes 2024: S.F. native Ali Wong makes history — and headlines
-----
"Oppenheimer" may have been the big winner at the 81st Golden Globe Awards, but Ali Wong was the person everyone was talking about after the show.The San Francisco native, of Vietnamese and Chinese decent, made history Sunday, Jan. 7, as the first Asian American woman to win a Golden Globe for best actress in the limited series or TV movie category for her role in "Beef" — and cameras caught her kissing boyfriend and fellow comedian/actor Bill Hader ("Barry") in celebration.
