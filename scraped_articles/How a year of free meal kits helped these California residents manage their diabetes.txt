How a year of free meal kits helped these California residents manage their diabetes
-----
People with diabetes who received biweekly healthy meal kits for a year showed improvements in managing the disease — including a key measure of blood sugar levels — according to a study released Wednesday by the Oakland research and advocacy group Public Health Institute.The study found that participants saw decreases in their A1c level, which measures average blood sugar levels, over a three-month period.
