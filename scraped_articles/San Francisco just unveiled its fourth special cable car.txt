San Francisco just unveiled its fourth special cable car
-----
San Francisco just unveiled a new cable car dedicated to someone known for singing the city's most famous love letter. As of Wednesday morning, "I Left My Heart in San Francisco" singer Tony Bennett will now have a commemorative cable car that makes its way up and down California Street.Car 53, originally built in 1907, will now sport a plaque as well as other decorative markings, like a heart on its window that says "halfway to the stars," in a nod to the lyrics of Bennett's signature
