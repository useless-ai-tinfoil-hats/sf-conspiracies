College players Bay FC could select with No. 2 pick in the NWSL draft
-----
Bay FC will have a sense of what type of team it wants to be by the time it is selecting players out of college. Though a date is not set, the expansion draft is expected to be in December before the NWSL holds its collegiate draft, which traditionally is in January.The new San Jose-based club will have the second overall selection in the collegiate draft, after the fellow expansion Utah Royals.
