Family of four killed in single-vehicle car crash in Pleasanton
-----
A family of four — two parents and two children — was killed in a single-vehicle car crash late Wednesday, Pleasanton police said Thursday.The accident occurred around 9 p.m. on Foothill Road near Stoneridge Drive, police said. The vehicle appeared to lose control, hitting a curb and crashing into a large oak tree, police added.
