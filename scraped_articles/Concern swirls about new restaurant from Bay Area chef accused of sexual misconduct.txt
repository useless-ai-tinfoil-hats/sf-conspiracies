Concern swirls about new restaurant from Bay Area chef accused of sexual misconduct
-----
Plans from a Bay Area chef accused of sexual misconduct to open a new restaurant in Oakland at the site of a beloved longtime business are being met with concern from former employees and community members.Charlie Hallowell, who was accused by more than 30 former workers of sexual harassment and verbal abuse in 2017 and 2018, has purchased 3724-3726 MacArthur Blvd., currently the site of the longtime World Ground Cafe in the Laurel District.
