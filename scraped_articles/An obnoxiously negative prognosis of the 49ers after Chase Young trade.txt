An obnoxiously negative prognosis of the 49ers after Chase Young trade
-----
I bet you Niners fans are feeling quite chesty right now, aren't you? Yesterday, your team acquired Commanders edge rusher and former No. 2 overall pick Chase Young for essentially nothing. All you had to give up for the man was a compensatory third round pick, one that could replenish itself if Young leaves town when his contract expires at the end of this season.
