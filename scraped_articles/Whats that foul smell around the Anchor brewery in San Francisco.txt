What’s that foul smell around the Anchor brewery in San Francisco?
-----
Neighbors of the closed — but soon to reopen — Anchor Brewing Co. on San Francisco's Potrero Hill were accustomed to the earthy, bready aroma of brewing beer before the Mariposa Street brewery shut down last year.But the pungent stench emanating from the brewery this week is something entirely and disgustingly different. It smells like poop, to be blunt, or manure to be more polite. But there's nothing pleasant about the smell.
