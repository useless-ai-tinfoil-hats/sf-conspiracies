Voting in San Francisco? Here’s a full list of the Chronicle’s local endorsements
-----
The San Francisco Chronicle editorial board has completed its local San Francisco endorsements for the March 5 primary election.Endorsements are made by writers and editors from the Opinion section after researching the issues and interviewing candidates and ballot measure proponents and opponents. Writers and editors involved in the news coverage of the campaigns do not participate in endorsement decisions.
