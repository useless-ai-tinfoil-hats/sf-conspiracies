Trio of Northern California earthquakes rattle area near Eureka
-----
A series of small earthquakes struck near Eureka in Northern California on Monday morning, according to the Chronicle's quake tracker.The first two quakes, with magnitudes of 4.8 and 4.1, struck just before 3:30 a.m. just 34 miles and 18 miles southeast of Eureka, respectively.
They were followed by two 3.1-magnitude tremors in the same area, the first at 6:04 a.m. and the second at 7:15 a.m.
No injuries or damage were immediately reported.
