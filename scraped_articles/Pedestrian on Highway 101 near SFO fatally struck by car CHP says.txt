Pedestrian on Highway 101 near SFO fatally struck by car, CHP says
-----
A person crossing Highway 101 on foot near San Francisco International Airport was fatally struck by a car Monday night, authorities said.California Highway Patrol officers responded to a report of a crash involving a vehicle and a pedestrian just south of the San Bruno Avenue ramp about 10:12 p.m., the CHP said Tuesday in a Facebook post.
