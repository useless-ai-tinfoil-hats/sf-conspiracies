Review: A lack of ‘Wish’ fulfillment in latest Disney animated film
-----
I could listen to Ariana DeBose sing all day long — but I couldn't wait for "Wish" to end.The latest animated Disney feature is a bland and curiously dark-looking film (the visual design was partly inspired by the watercolor animation of some of Disney's classic film, but still) and the amount of silly mental gymnastics needed to buy the plot is exhausting.
