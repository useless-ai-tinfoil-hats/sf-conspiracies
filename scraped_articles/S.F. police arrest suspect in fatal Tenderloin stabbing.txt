S.F. police arrest suspect in fatal Tenderloin stabbing
-----
San Francisco police have arrested a person they said fatally stabbed a man in the Tenderloin in October.Police allege Ronnie Randle, 38, stabbed a man near the intersection of Eddy and Taylor streets on the morning of Oct. 24. The man was taken to a local hospital and died four days later, according to the Police Department.
