Retail giant eBay lays off 9% of full-time staff, 281 Bay Area workers
-----
EBay is laying off 9% of its full-time workforce, or about 1,000 people, the online retail giant announced Tuesday.Included in that huge job cut are 261 workers from the company's San Jose headquarters and 20 workers from an office in San Francisco's SoMa neighborhood, according to a WARN notice eBay filed with the state on Tuesday that was obtained by SFGATE.
