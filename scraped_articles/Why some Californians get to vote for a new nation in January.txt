Why some Californians get to vote for a new nation in January
-----
On Jan. 28, Californians will cast ballots in a historic vote on creating a new independent country.Why is this the first you're hearing of it? Because the only Californians who can vote in the election are Sikhs. The proposed independent country would be in northern India.
But that's no reason to overlook the most important election in the Golden State next year.
