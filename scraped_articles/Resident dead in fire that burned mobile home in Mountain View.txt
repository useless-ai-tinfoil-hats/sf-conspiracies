Resident dead in fire that burned mobile home in Mountain View
-----
One person is dead following a mobile home fire on Saturday morning, the city of Mountain View announced.Mountain View Fire Department units extinguished a fire on the 400 block of Moffett Boulevard shortly after 5:45 a.m. During a search for occupants firefighters found a resident and declared the person dead.
