East Bay killing preceded by domestic violence incident, officials say
-----
The Alameda County Sheriff's Office said it was investigating a homicide that occurred Wednesday morning in San Leandro.The homicide was first reported as a domestic violence incident in the 16000 block of Foothill Boulevard at about 9:30 a.m., the sheriff's office said in a statement posted to Facebook.
A suspect, whose identity was not immediately released, was arrested, officials said.
