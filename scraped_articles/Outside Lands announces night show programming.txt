Outside Lands announces night show programming
-----
Outside Lands has revealed its night show lineup, providing local music lovers the chance to catch some of the festival's performers in more intimate settings.This year's night show lineup features 16 performances from artists such as country musician Sturgill Simpson, pop artist Fletcher and "Put Your Records On" singer Corinne Bailey Rae across a total of nine Bay Area venues.
