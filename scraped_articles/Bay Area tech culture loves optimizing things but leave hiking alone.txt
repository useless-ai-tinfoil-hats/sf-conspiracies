Bay Area tech culture loves optimizing things, but leave hiking alone
-----
"Optimization" is sweeping the Western world, and the Bay Area may be its epicenter. In a tech-dominated culture, the frantic and relentless search for extreme efficiency, productivity and performance has become a sort of dogma that permeates not just work but personal life as well.Even the most basic movement of human beings, walking, is not immune. Perhaps it should be.
