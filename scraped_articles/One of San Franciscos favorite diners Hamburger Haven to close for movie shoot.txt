One of San Francisco's favorite diners Hamburger Haven to close for movie shoot
-----
The foggy streets of the Inner Richmond are about to set the scene for the latest movie shot in San Francisco, and one of the oldest diners in the neighborhood is getting its close-up.A film with the working title "Project Brick Breaker" is slated for production at the 1960s-era Hamburger Haven on the corner of 9th Avenue and Clement Street, with a lengthy shoot lasting from 11:30 a.m. Wednesday to 1:30 a.m. Thursday, the San Francisco Film Commission confirmed with SFGATE.
