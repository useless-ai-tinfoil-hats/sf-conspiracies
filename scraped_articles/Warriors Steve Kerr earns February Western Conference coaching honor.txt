Warriors’ Steve Kerr earns February Western Conference coaching honor
-----
A contract extension isn't the only thing Golden State Warriors coach Steve Kerr earned in February.On Monday, Kerr was named the NBA's Western Conference Coach of the Month after guiding the Warriors to an 11-3 record and back into the postseason picture amid up and down stretches the previous three months.
They were 20-24 when January concluded — and 32-28 amid the NBA's announcement, good for the No. 9 spot in the conference's standings and 2.5 games back of the No. 6 Kings.
