Suspect arrested in early morning South Bay shooting, police say
-----
A suspect was arrested early Tuesday in a shooting that left a person dead in Santa Clara, police said.Officers responded to a shooting at a home in the 3700 block of Poinciana Drive just before 2:30 a.m., the Santa Clara Police Department said on X, formerly Twitter.
A victim was found dead inside the home, and a male suspect was arrested nearby, officials said. Their identities were not immediately released.
