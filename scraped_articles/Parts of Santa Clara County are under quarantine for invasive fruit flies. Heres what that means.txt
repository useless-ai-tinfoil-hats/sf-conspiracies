Portions of Santa Clara County have been placed under quarantine to prevent the spread of the oriental fruit fly, an invasive species.The 112-square-mile area includes the city of Santa Clara and portions of Cupertino, Milpitas, San Jose and Sunnyvale.
Under the quarantine, which is expected to last until June, residents are not supposed to move homegrown produce off of their property, although the produce is safe to eat where it was grown. It can be disposed of in the garbage
