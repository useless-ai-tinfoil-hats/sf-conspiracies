16-year-old fatally shot and 3 injured at East Bay house party
-----
A 16-year-old was killed and three other individuals were injured in a shooting at an East Bay house party Saturday night, officials said.The party occurred at a residence in Oakley, a small town in far east Contra Costa County. Oakley Police Department Chief Paul Beard said in a Facebook post Sunday night that officers were called to the party on the 400 block of Shannon Way for a report of a shooting just after 11 p.m.
