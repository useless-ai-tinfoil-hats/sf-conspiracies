Outside Lands' most troubled stage looks very different this year
-----
After a short-lived attempt to operate a fully enclosed nightclub, complete with long lines, sweaty dancers and superstar DJs at Outside Lands, the scene at Golden Gate Park's Marx Meadow looks much different than at last year's festival.The SOMA stage has now broken free of its box, as the new open-air concept makes its 2024 debut. Organizers say the new space will allow for more dancers and bigger acts.
