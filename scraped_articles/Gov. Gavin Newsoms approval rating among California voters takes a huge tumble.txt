Gov. Gavin Newsom's approval rating among California voters takes a huge tumble
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom's approval rating among California voters is at an all-time low, according to the latest Berkeley IGS poll.The new poll among statewide registered voters, released Tuesday, shows that Newsom's approval rating late last month was 44%, an 11-point drop from his 55% approval rating in February.
