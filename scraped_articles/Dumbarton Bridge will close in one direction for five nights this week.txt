Dumbarton Bridge will close in one direction for five nights this week
-----
The eastbound lanes of Highway 84 on the Dumbarton Bridge are scheduled to close during five nights this week for repaving work, Caltrans said.The bridge will be closed Monday through Friday, Oct. 30-Nov. 3, between 7 p.m. and 5 a.m. while crews repave the roadway from the toll plaza to the end of the bridge, aiming to "create a smoother ride for motorists."
