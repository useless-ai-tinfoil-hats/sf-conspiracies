The chaotic final days of San Francisco's shuttering Target
-----
Naked mannequins, strewn clothing and nearly empty shelves mark the final days of San Francisco's Target store at 1690 Folsom St., which is slated to shut its doors for good on Oct. 21.The chaos belies the rush of shoppers descending on the store as the retailer has rolled out major discounts on all its remaining stock, at the Folsom location as well as the two other stores in Oakland and Pittsburg that Target plans to permanently close this month.
