Thousands of college students trashed a California lake last weekend
-----
A group of roughly 3,000 college students from UC Davis and the University of Oregon trashed Shasta Lake over Memorial Day weekend, according to the U.S. Forest Service.Debbie Carlisi, a recreation officer with the agency's Shasta-Trinity National Forest division, told SFGATE in an interview that the students left bottles, cups, wrappers and cans behind when they left for home, so much so that crews spent six hours this week picking up trash. She said some students even threw their trash
