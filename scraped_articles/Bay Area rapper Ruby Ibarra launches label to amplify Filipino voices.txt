Bay Area rapper Ruby Ibarra launches label to amplify Filipino voices
-----
Filipino American rapper Ruby Ibarra, renowned for her lyrical prowess in English and Tagalog, has embarked on a mission to uplift fellow Bay Area artists.During the last Undiscovered Block Party of the season in San Francisco, the 32-year-old East Bay resident announced the launch of Bolo Music Group, a label dedicated to amplifying the voices and creations of Filipino American artists.
