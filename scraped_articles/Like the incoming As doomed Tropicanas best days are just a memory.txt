Like the incoming A’s, doomed Tropicana’s best days are just a memory
-----
LAS VEGAS — The Tropicana was once the grandest, hottest spot in town.Superstars performed here. Sammy Davis Jr., Louis Armstrong, daredevil Robbie Knievel. This was the home of winners, entertainers who took big swings. When it opened in 1957 and for decades hence, the Tropicana was the very essence of excitement and cutting-edge cool, the pride of the Strip.
