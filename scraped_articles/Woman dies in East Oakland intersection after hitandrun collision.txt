Woman dies in East Oakland intersection after hit-and-run collision
-----
A 66-year-old woman was killed in a hit-and-run collision in East Oakland on Sunday morning, authorities said.A driver in a white Cadillac DeVille was traveling westbound on the 9000 block of MacArthur Boulevard when it struck the woman in a crosswalk just before 9 a.m., Oakland police officials said Tuesday. The driver fled before authorities arrived.
Medics provided aid to the victim and transported her to a hospital, where she died from her injuries.
