Deadly salmonella outbreak tied to cantaloupe reaches California
-----
Two brands are now recalling cantaloupes sold in California due to potential salmonella contamination as an outbreak spreads across the U.S.Salmonella cases potentially caused by cantaloupes have been reported across 32 states and Canada, including one case in California, according to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. Of the 99 cases reported, 45 people have been hospitalized, and two have died.
