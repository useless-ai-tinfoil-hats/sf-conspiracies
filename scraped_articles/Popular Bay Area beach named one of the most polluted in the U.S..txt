Popular Bay Area beach named one of the most polluted in the U.S.
-----
A California beach was named the most polluted beach in the United States in a recent study.High bacteria levels were found in every sample taken during 2023 at Imperial Beach, in San Diego, according to the Surfrider Foundation's annual Clean Water report. Stormwater runoff near beaches is usually to blame for the polluted areas, with the Tijuana River Watershed dumping contaminated water into the ocean every day at this location.
