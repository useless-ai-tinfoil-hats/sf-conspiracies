Windy Bay Area weather causing delays at SFO
-----
UPDATED FORECAST: More thunderstorms, snow expected from unusually cold April storm in CaliforniaA storm raking across the Bay Area Thursday morning caused substantial delays at San Francisco International Airport, according to flight-tracking websites.
Flights coming in to SFO were delayed by an average of two hours and 15 minutes at their origins, according to data from FlightAware.
