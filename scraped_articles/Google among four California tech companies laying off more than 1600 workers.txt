Google among four California tech companies laying off more than 1,600 workers
-----
Four more California tech companies are cutting more than 1,500 combined jobs, the latest in a relentless wave of downsizings. All four companies had previously laid off employees in the past 13 months, underscoring the extent of the industry's pain.Google laid off up to 20 data scientists in its voice assistant division, Insider reported.
