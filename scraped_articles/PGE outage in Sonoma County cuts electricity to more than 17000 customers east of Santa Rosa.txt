PG&E outage in Sonoma County cuts electricity to more than 17,000 customers east of Santa Rosa
-----
A power outage in Sonoma County cut electricity service Wednesday morning to more than 17,000 Pacific Gas & Electric Co. customers, according to data from PowerOutage.us, which tracks disruptions in electricity service.A map of the outages showed that most of the affected customers were east of Santa Rosa, and included Sebastopol and Graton.
It was not immediately clear what caused the outage, or how quickly PG&E expected power to be restored.
