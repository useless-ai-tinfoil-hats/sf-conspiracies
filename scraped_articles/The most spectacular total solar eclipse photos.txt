The most spectacular total solar eclipse photos
-----
Millions of people enjoyed a rare moment of celestial magic during a total solar eclipse that crossed the heartland of the United States on Monday.A total solar eclipse occurs when the moon passes between the sun and the Earth, completely blocking the face of the sun.
During Monday's eclipse, when the moon blocked the sun for up to four minutes, the sky darkened almost to dusk, and stars and planets were visible.
