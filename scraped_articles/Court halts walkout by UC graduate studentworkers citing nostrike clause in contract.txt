Court halts walkout by UC graduate student-workers, citing no-strike clause in contract
-----
Thousands of student workers who walked off the job at six University of California campuses must halt their strike, a judge ruled Friday.The workers — teaching assistants, researchers, graders and other essential campus employees — have been striking at increasing numbers of UC campuses since May 20: in Santa Cruz, Davis, Los Angeles, Santa Barbara, San Diego and Irvine, ahead of finals next week.
