PG&E power outage suspends BART trains between Rockridge and Orinda
-----
A Pacific Gas and Electric Co. outage temporarily suspended BART service between Rockridge and Orinda, spokespersons for the transit agency said Thursday.The "technical problem" was reported Thursday before 11 a.m., BART spokespersons said.
Officials said they requested a bus bridge as they worked to get systems back online from the power outage.
PG&E's outage map showed a large swath of Orinda was experiencing an outage, with up to 5,000 customers affected.
