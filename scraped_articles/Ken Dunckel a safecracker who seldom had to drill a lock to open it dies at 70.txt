Ken Dunckel, a safecracker who seldom had to drill a lock to open it, dies at 70
-----
When Ken "Safecracker" Dunckel arrived at a job he always left his power drill in the van. In his mind, drilling would be "cheating" as a means of gaining entry to a locked box in a home or business. Dunckel prided himself on leaving a safe exactly as he had found it, though the door would now be opened.
