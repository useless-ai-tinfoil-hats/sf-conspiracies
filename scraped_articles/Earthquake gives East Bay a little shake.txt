Earthquake gives East Bay a little shake
-----
An earthquake with a preliminary reading of 3.2 gave the East Bay a little shake on Thursday morning, according to the U.S. Geological Survey.The quake struck with a depth of 5.7 miles at 9:35 a.m., the USGS said. The epicenter was in the hills east of Fremont, just on the edge of Mission Peak Regional Preserve.
More than 700 people reported feeling shaking from the quake with the USGS' Felt Report.
