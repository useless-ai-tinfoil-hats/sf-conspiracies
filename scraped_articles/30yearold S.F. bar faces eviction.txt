30-year-old S.F. bar faces eviction
-----
After spending a purported six figures in repairs on its longtime home, a bar in the Sunset District is in danger of being evicted.Blackthorn, a 30-year fixture at 834 Irving St., is in the middle of a legal dispute over its lease with landlord company McGrath Enterprises. McGrath filed an unlawful detainer — the first step in an eviction case — against Blackthorn in October.
