'You guys are causing slum-ification': Heated Bay Area city council meeting lasts 5 hours
-----
Eighty-three people made impassioned speeches during a city meeting late one January evening as the battle between landlords and renters continues to move from Bay Area metropolises out into their suburbs.The heated city council meeting in Concord on Jan. 30 lasted about five hours and included testimonies from dozens of landlords fighting against the city's proposed rent control ordinance. There were even several bouts of booing aimed at some of the relatively smaller number of people
