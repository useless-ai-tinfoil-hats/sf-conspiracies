Man found dead in Golden Gate Park
-----
A man was found dead in Golden Gate Park on Thursday morning, San Francisco police said.Police said they responded to the area of John F Kennedy Drive and Stow Lake Drive for a report of a deceased person at approximately 9:46 a.m. and found the body.
Officers did not find evidence of foul play. Anyone with information is asked to contact the police department at 415-575-4444.
