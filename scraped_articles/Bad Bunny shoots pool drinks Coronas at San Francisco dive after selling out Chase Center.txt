Bad Bunny shoots pool, drinks Coronas at San Francisco dive after selling out Chase Center
-----
Maybe we should ask Bad Bunny to promote SFGATE's Best of Bay Area. After two sellout shows at the Chase Center, the reggaeton megastar needed somewhere to cool down, so he did some research to find the best bar in the city to shoot pool. The Puerto Rican rapper ultimately headed to Bender's Bar and Grill on S. Van Ness Avenue in the Mission to kick back and play billiards.
