Did your superstitions influence the 49ers’ big win? Tell us the story
-----
When San Francisco quarterback Brock Purdy chucked that deep pass late Sunday afternoon, which pinballed improbably off the facemask of a Lions defender and into the hands of 49ers receiver Brandon Aiyuk, my departed grandparents were watching from above.That's not a figurative claim. I pulled a photo of Raymond and Louise Leal from a scrapbook, brought it to Levi's Stadium and was holding it at chest level for most of the second half — like an airline passenger showing his ID to a TSA
