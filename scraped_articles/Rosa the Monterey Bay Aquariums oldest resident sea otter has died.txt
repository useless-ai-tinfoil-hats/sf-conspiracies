Rosa, the Monterey Bay Aquarium's oldest resident sea otter, has died
-----
Rosa, the Monterey Bay Aquarium's oldest resident sea otter who helped raise 15 stranded pups during her time at the aquarium, died Wednesday, it was announced on social media."It's with great sadness that we share that our beloved sea otter Rosa passed away today," the aquarium said. "At 24 years old, Rosa was the oldest resident otter at the Aquarium and one of our most experienced surrogate moms, having raised 15 stranded sea otter pups in her time with us."
