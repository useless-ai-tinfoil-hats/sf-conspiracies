'Dramatic' sun storm could produce northern lights over California
-----
Auroras could be visible over Northern California on Friday and Saturday night — a dazzling byproduct of the first severe geomagnetic storm the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration's Space Weather Prediction Center has monitored in nearly two decades. The storm is ranked as a Level 4 (out of five) on NOAA's scale."This is an unusual event," the agency said.
