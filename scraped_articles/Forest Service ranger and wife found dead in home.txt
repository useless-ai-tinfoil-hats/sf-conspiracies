Forest Service ranger and wife found dead in home
-----
Two people found dead in Ely, Nev., are a U.S. Forest Service ranger and his wife, Humboldt Toiyabe National Forest officials announced on social media Friday.Ely District ranger Cody Nicholes, 38, and Chelsea Nicholes, a human resources manager for a mining company, died on Oct. 31. According to the Ely Times, the couple was found deceased in their home after Chelsea Nicholes' employer called for a welfare check when she failed to arrive at work.
