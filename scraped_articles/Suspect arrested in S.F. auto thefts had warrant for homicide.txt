Suspect arrested in S.F. auto thefts had warrant for homicide
-----
San Francisco police said Thursday they arrested an Alameda County man wanted for homicide and multiple Bay Area auto thefts.Joseph McBee, 28, was booked in jail Tuesday for warrants on vehicle theft, possession of a stolen vehicle and a homicide that occurred outside of San Francisco, police said. The agency said it is working with the U.S. Postal Inspection Service to investigate an Oct. 10 robbery of a postal worker in San Francisco for which McBee is a suspect.
