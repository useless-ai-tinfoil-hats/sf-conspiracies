Odor subsides in Bay Area city after refinery flaring
-----
A mechanical problem at Benicia's Valero Refinery caused flaring and emitted an odor in the area Saturday morning that had significantly dissipated by the afternoon, the city announced.The source of the odor was a refined hydrocarbon that was being moved between two tanks at the refinery, the city's Fire Department said.
