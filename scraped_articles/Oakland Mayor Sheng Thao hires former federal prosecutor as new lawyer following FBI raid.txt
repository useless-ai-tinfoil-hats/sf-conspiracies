Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao hires former federal prosecutor as new lawyer following FBI raid
-----
Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao has hired a new lawyer, veteran federal prosecutor turned white collar defense attorney Jeffrey Tsai, who prosecuted former presidential candidate John Edwards at the U.S. Department of Justice.Tsai also worked as an assistant U.S. attorney in Miami, prosecuting white collar crimes and narco trafficking crimes, and as an assistant attorney general between 2013 and 2015 to Kamala Harris, when she was California's attorney general.
