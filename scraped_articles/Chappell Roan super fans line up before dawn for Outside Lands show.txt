Chappell Roan super fans line up before dawn for Outside Lands show
-----
Superfans of Chappell Roan lined up early Sunday — before the sun even rose — to secure a primo spot at the main Lands End stage during San Francisco's Outside Lands Music Festival.Before the festival gates opened at 11 a.m., there was already a crowd at the south gate. Outside the festival, Paul Cieri of San Francisco said he arrived at 5 a.m., with a blanket in hand. "I just want to be as close as possible to the stage, have a good spot," Cieri said.
