Napa police arrest 3 people suspected in weekend slayings that left teenagers dead
-----
Three people were arrested on suspicion of fatally shooting two teenagers in Napa over the weekend, authorities said Tuesday.John Nicholson, a 22-year-old resident of Vallejo, was accused of killing a 17-year-old and a 19-year-old on Saturday, according to the Napa Police Department.
He was arrested on Monday and booked into the Napa County Department of Corrections on two murder charges, police said.
