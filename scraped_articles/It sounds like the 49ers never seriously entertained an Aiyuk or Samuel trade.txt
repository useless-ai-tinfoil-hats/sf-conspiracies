It sounds like the 49ers never seriously entertained an Aiyuk or Samuel trade
-----
Any possible interest that the 49ers had in trading wide receivers Brandon Aiyuk and Deebo Samuel, even after the team's controversial first-round pick, appears to have made its way out of San Francisco's front office entirely.As last Thursday's NFL Draft approached, rumors of the Niners moving on from their breakout receiver began to emerge a lot more frequently.
