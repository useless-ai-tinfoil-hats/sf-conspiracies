S.F. rally calls for hostages to be returned to Israel immediately
-----
Roughly 200 people gathered Friday in front of San Francisco City Hall, most holding large blue and white flags of Israel, to call for an immediate return of the hostages taken by Hamas gunmen in the surprise attack on Israel a week ago.The crowd was quiet as speakers, standing on a stage behind posters with photos of some hostages, shared the stories of families whose loved ones had been taken and voiced support for the Israeli people.
