Peninsula police search for missing at-risk woman
-----
Redwood City police are searching for a missing woman they say is particularly at risk.Megan Quiroz, 34, was last heard from on Thursday, according to police. Quiroz is 5 feet, 9 inches tall, weighs about 165 pounds, and has hazel eyes and brown hair, police said.
Authorities said they did not know what Quiroz was wearing and did not release information about her last known whereabouts.
