Grateful Dead lyricist Robert Hunter's lost manuscript recounting the band's origin is headed for publication."The Silver Snarling Trumpet: The Birth of the Grateful Dead," which tells the San Francisco jam band's origin story, is scheduled for release by Hachette Books on Oct. 8. It features a foreword by musician and Dead & Company frontman John Mayer, an introduction by the band's historian Dennis McNally and an afterword by Brigid Meier, a close friend of the band and Jerry Garcia.
