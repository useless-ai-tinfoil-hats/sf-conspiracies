Redwood City-based Electronic Arts is laying off hundreds of workers
-----
Electronic Arts, the Redwood City-based company behind popular video games like Apex Legends and The Sims, is laying off approximately 5% of its staff.CEO Andrew Wilson announced the layoff round in a message to employees on Wednesday, telling them that the company had "deeply considered every option to try and limit impacts to our teams."
