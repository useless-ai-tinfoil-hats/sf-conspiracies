'Blood on your hands': Senators excoriate Mark Zuckerberg over child safety at D.C. hearing
-----
Mark Zuckerberg, at a Wednesday hearing for tech executives to answer questions about children's safety on social media, caught the brunt of senators' ire, indignation and scolding.It started off rough for the Meta CEO. As he entered the chamber, parents who blame social media companies for their children's deaths held up photos of their kids and "audibly hissed," NBC News reported.
