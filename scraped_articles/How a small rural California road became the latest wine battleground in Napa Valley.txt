How a small rural California road became the latest wine battleground in Napa Valley
-----
A few miles north of downtown St. Helena, the narrow Lodi Lane connects Napa Valley's main thoroughfares, Highway 29 and Silverado Trail. Just three-quarters of a mile long, the oak-and-vineyard-dotted road features a rusting, one-lane truss bridge over the Napa River that's prone to flooding.
