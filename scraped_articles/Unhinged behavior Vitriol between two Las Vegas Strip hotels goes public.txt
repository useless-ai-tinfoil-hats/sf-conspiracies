'Unhinged behavior': Vitriol between two Las Vegas Strip hotels goes public
-----
Vulgar text messages and Eagles lyrics have entered the battle between two Las Vegas Strip hotels, with each one suing the other in Clark County court.The fight between Wynn Las Vegas and its new neighbor, the Fontainebleau, dates back several years. It took nearly two torturous decades to finish the Fontainebleau, which opened in December, and while the finishing touches were being added, Wynn claimed Fontainebleau began poaching workers who were bound by non-compete clauses.
