Despite Michelin star cred, SF chef Dominique Crenn's first Paris restaurant has already closed
-----
PARIS — A year after its highly anticipated opening, Golden Poppy — the first restaurant in Paris from San Francisco's lauded French-born chef Dominique Crenn — has closed permanently.The restaurant confirmed the closure to SFGATE on Thursday, saying Crenn — the first (and still only) female chef in America to earn three Michelin stars for her flagship SF restaurant, Atelier Crenn — shuttered the concept at the end of June.
