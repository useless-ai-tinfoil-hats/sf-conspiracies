The little California town with two faces
-----
Murphys is a peculiar town. Celebrated as one of the quaintest travel destinations in the U.S. by Forbes, in the shadow of the majestic giant sequoias in the Sierra foothills, it's also earned a reputation for sloppy bachelorette parties and a rowdy pub scene.I drove into town after wandering Calaveras Big Trees State Park a few miles up the hill in Calaveras County and was soon met by these apparent contradictions.
