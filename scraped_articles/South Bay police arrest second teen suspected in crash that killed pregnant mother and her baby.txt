South Bay police arrest second teen suspected in crash that killed pregnant mother and her baby
-----
San Jose law enforcement officials said they arrested a second 15-year-old boy in connection to a major car crash that killed a pregnant woman and her baby last month.Last week, police officials said they had arrested a 15-year-old boy who they suspected of crashing a stolen Hyundai into a Nissan driven by a pregnant woman, who later died of her injuries in the hospital.
