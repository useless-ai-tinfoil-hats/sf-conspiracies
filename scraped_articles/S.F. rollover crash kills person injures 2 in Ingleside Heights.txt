S.F. rollover crash kills person, injures 2 in Ingleside Heights
-----
One person died and two others were injured in a rollover crash in San Francisco's Ingleside Heights neighborhood on New Year's Day, police said.San Francisco police said they responded to the area of Junipero Serra Boulevard and Palmetto Avenue just before 8:30 p.m. after receiving a report of a single-vehicle accident involved in a rollover crash.
One victim lay on the ground and was declared deceased at the scene, police said.
