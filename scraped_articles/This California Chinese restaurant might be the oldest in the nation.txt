This California Chinese restaurant might be the oldest in the nation
-----
Stepping inside the unassuming Chicago Cafe in Woodland, near Sacramento in Yolo County, is like a trip through time. Owners Paul and Nancy Fong still cook each order for their regulars who sit on stools at the diner counter, waiting on plates of fried prawns and pork fried rice. Old pictures and newspaper clippings hang on the wall, covered in the unmistakable patina of an old family restaurant.But Chicago Cafe isn't just old.
