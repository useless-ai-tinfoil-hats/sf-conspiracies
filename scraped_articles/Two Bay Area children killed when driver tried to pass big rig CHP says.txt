Two Bay Area children killed when driver tried to pass big rig, CHP says
-----
Two children were killed when a driver attempted to pass a big rig on Highway 152 on Friday, causing a chain-reaction crash involving five vehicles.CHP investigators said in a news release that the crash was caused by a 25-year-old man from Los Banos in a 2021 Tesla. Around 7 a.m. Friday, CHP said the man attempted to pass a truck on westbound Highway 152 near Lake Road in Gilroy.
