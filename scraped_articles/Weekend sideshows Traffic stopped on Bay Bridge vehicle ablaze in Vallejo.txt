Weekend sideshows: Traffic stopped on Bay Bridge, vehicle ablaze in Vallejo
-----
A sideshow blocked the eastbound side of the Bay Bridge for about a half-hour early Sunday, the California Highway Patrol said, while a vehicle was reportedly set on fire at another sideshow in Vallejo.Several 911 callers reported the bridge sideshow just before 2 a.m. east of Yerba Buena Island, said CHP officer Efrain Jauregui. Few other details were immediately available.
