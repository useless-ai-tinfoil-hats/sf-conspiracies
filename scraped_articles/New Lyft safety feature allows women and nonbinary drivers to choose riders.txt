New Lyft safety feature allows women and nonbinary drivers to choose riders
-----
Lyft announced a new feature on Tuesday intended to improve safety for women and nonbinary drivers, by allowing them to choose riders who are also women and nonbinary.The feature was available immediately to drivers in five cities including San Francisco and San Jose. Other cities will be added.
The ride-matching program, named "Women + Connect," allows women and nonbinary drivers to switch on an option to choose nearby women and nonbinary riders.
