Pelosi bets a helping of iconic Bay Area candy on the 49ers winning the Super Bowl
-----
California Rep. Nancy Pelosi is betting candy from a Bay Area institution that the San Francisco 49ers will win the Super Bowl.The former House speaker, who represents San Francisco, announced Friday that she has made a friendly wager with fellow Democratic Rep. Emanuel Cleaver, whose district includes Kansas City, Missouri, and GEHA Field at Arrowhead Stadium where the Chiefs play.
