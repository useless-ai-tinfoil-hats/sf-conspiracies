Report: 5 injured in crash, CHP officers attacked after East Bay sideshow
-----
Five people were injured in a crash following an apparent police chase after a sideshow in Antioch on Saturday night, according to a media report.The California Highway Patrol said a vehicle with five occupants fled a sideshow "at a high speed" before crashing into a concrete wall on Bailey Road near the Pittsburg and Bay Point border, the agency told KTVU. All five occupants were taken to a hospital. The condition of the victims Sunday morning was unknown.
