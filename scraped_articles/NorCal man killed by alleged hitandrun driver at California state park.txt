NorCal man killed by alleged hit-and-run driver at California state park
-----
A man walking on a trail near the entrance to Richardson Grove State Park in Humboldt County was killed by a driver who fled the scene, California Highway Patrol officials said in a press release.A 67-year-old man from Carmichael, California, was "walking from a trail on the west roadway edge of US-101, south of the Richardson Grove State Park entrance" on Wednesday afternoon, CHP said.
