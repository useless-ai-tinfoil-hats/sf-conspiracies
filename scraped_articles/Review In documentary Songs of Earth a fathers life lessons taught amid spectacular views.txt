Review: In documentary ‘Songs of Earth,’ a father’s life lessons taught amid spectacular views
-----
Jørgen Mykløen is certainly a man who gets his steps in. He is 84, and for years he has taken long walks through western Norway's Oldedalen valley, up and down the mountainous terrain that frames the valley, occasionally stopping for a cool drink from Oldevatnet Lake.He grew up near there, in a small farming community where his family established roots generations ago; a towering spruce in the Oldedalen was planted by his grandfather in the 1890s.
