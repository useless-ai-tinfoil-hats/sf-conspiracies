Teen rescued at Bay Area beach after getting stuck in mud amid rising tide
-----
A teenager who got "significantly stuck in the mud" amid a rising tide at a beach in Alameda was rescued on Wednesday, the Alameda Fire Department said.The department said in a news released that it got a report of the trapped teen at noon and both firefighters and officers with the Alameda Police Department responded to the scene.
