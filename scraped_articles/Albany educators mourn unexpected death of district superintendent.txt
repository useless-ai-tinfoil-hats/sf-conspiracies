Albany educators mourn unexpected death of district superintendent
-----
Educators are mourning the loss of Albany Unified School District Superintendent Frank Wells, who died unexpectedly Monday."Dr. Wells brought wit, humor, and a willingness to roll up his sleeves to help wherever he was needed. He will be sorely missed, but his passion serves as an inspiration for us all," district officials said in a letter to parents Tuesday informing them of his death.
