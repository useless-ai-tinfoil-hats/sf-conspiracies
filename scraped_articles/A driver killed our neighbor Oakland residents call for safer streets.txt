‘A driver killed our neighbor’: Oakland residents call for safer streets
-----
A group of Oakland residents rallied Thursday at a busy intersection where a bicyclist was killed in a hit-and-run crash, demanding that the city accelerate efforts to make streets safer for pedestrians and cyclists.The demonstration was held in honor of 68-year-old Oakland resident Steve Bloom, who died a week after a vehicle struck him as he was riding a bike at the intersection of Broadway and 27th Street around 5:15 p.m. July 21.
