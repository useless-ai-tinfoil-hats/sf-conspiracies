The oldest, weirdest corner of Hollywood is now a strip mall
-----
Constellations of strip malls dot the Los Angeles landscape, each bearing familiar hallmarks of grayscale adobe walls and diminutive parking lots. Some are more creative — or more famous — than others, but taken all together, there is a kind of sameness to most strip malls around Southern California.And then there is Gower Gulch, the single-story Hollywood mini-mall that resembles an old Western film set. Why here, and why just this one unique design? Probably because this town isn't big
