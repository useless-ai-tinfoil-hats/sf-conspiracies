East Bay Halloween night shooting leaves 1 dead
-----
One person was injured and another killed Tuesday night in a shooting in Bay Point.Officials with the Contra Costa Fire Protection District first learned of the shooting around 9:40 p.m. when a victim showed up at a Bay Point fire station with a gunshot wound to the arm, a spokesperson for the district said.
