Bay Area tech founders' swanky club may not open despite big plans
-----
In 2021, tech entrepreneurs Xochi and Michael Birch announced they would expand their exclusive social club and luxury boutique hotel, the Battery, to Oakland. But three years later, there's still no set opening date, and the site of the new location, the former Bellevue Club, may not open at all.Despite initial ambitious plans, Michael Birch said they're still studying whether the Oakland operation makes economic sense, and to date, no construction has begun.
