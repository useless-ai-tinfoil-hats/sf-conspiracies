Review: Camila Mendes and a strong cast of veterans enlivens ‘Upgraded’
-----
"Upgraded" isn't good enough for us not to notice that it's formula movie. Its rags-to-riches story follows the pattern of "Cinderella," while many elements call to mind other movies, especially "The Devil Wears Prada."Yet "Upgraded" isn't bad at all and makes us enjoy the formula one more time.
