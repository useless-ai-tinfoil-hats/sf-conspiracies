Park Fire crews bracing for erratic fire behavior ahead of thunderstorm
-----
Crews fighting the Park Fire are preparing for potentially erratic and unpredictable fire behavior through the weekend as a thunderstorm makes its way into the area.Higher temperatures and low humidity in the region could amplify the blaze, which has been burning since July 24, Cal Fire officials said on social media.
