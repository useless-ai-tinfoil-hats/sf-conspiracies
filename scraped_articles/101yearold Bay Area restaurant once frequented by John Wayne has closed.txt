101-year-old Bay Area restaurant once frequented by John Wayne has closed
-----
The Milias, a historic restaurant in Gilroy that dates back to 1922, has closed its doors. Owners Adam Sanchez and Ann Zyburra announced the closure on September 1 on Facebook, as reported by the Gilroy Dispatch (the Facebook page has since been deactivated)."We hope that you have some great memories of the times that we shared at the Milias," the post read. "We made some wonderful friends and worked with some amazing people. We will miss each and every one of you."
