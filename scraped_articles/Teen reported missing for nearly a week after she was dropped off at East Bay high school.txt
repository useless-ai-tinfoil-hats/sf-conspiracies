Teen reported missing for nearly a week after she was dropped off at East Bay high school
-----
The Alameda County Sheriff's Office issued an alert Thursday morning asking for the public's help in finding a teenager missing for nearly a week.Allison Bettencourt, 14, was last seen after she was dropped off at Hayward High School at 8 a.m. Oct. 6, sheriff's officials said.
Bettencourt is described as 5 feet, 3 inches tall, 130 pounds with dark brown hair and brown eyes. Officials said they did not have information about what Bettencourt was wearing when she was last seen.
