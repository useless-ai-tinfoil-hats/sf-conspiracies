D.L. Bliss State Park remains closed for the second summer in a row for maintenance
-----
With its lakefront campsites, access to one of Tahoe's most pristine beaches and boulders peppered among woods, D.L. Bliss State Park has long been one of the most sought-after places to visit in Lake Tahoe.But a botched maintenance project means that D.L. Bliss State Park will be mostly closed to visitors for the second summer in a row.
