Sound Summit announces 2024 lineup
-----
Sound Summit, Mill Valley's daylong music festival, has revealed the lineup for this year's Mount Tamalpais event.Set to take place on Sept. 7 at Mountain Theater, the annual celebration is scheduled to feature indie-rock guitarist and lyricist Courtney Barnett and multi-instrumentalist and singer Kurt Vile as co-headliners.
