A dire wolf skull, a celebrity grizzly and a $9 million book: How we invited readers inside the Cal Academy collections

Expectations were high earlier this year as I walked through the labyrinth-like California Academy of Sciences basement with colleagues Guy Wathen and Sarah Feldberg on a quest to see the bear on the California flag.But our minds were still blown into the stratosphere when the thick freezer-style door opened. We were face-to-face with the startlingly well-preserved bear — hello Monarch! — surrounded by an arkful of taxidermied bears, leopards, condors and sea birds.
