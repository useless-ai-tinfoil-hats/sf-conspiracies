$9.2M SF home with connections to punk rock royalty for sale for the first time in 16 years
-----
Built by architectural royalty and likely embellished by punk rock royalty, 1601 Monterey Blvd. in San Francisco's St. Francis Wood rests proudly on the neighborhood's third-largest lot. The home is for sale now for the first time in 16 years, asking $9.2 million.The St. Francis Wood area officially opened in 1912, and today, it "is justifiably lauded as one of the nation's finest examples of a 'residence park,'" according to the Western Neighborhoods Project.
