Famed SF Vietnamese restaurant Slanted Door opening new location in Napa next month
-----
Since the beginning of the pandemic, the doors have been shut at nationally acclaimed San Francisco Vietnamese restaurant Slanted Door. It has yet to reopen, but there is some good news for Charles Phan enthusiasts elsewhere in the Bay Area: The Napa location of Slanted Door is slated to open in October, according to Eater.This will be Slanted Door's third location, following the opening of a second location in San Ramon in 2019.
