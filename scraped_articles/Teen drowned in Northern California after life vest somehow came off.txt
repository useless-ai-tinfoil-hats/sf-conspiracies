Teen drowned in Northern California after life vest ‘somehow came off’
-----
A 16-year-old boy drowned Sunday after jumping off a pontoon boat into Shaver Lake in Fresno County, authorities said.The teenager was wearing a life jacket that "somehow came off" when he jumped into the water near the island at Camp Chawanakee just after 12:30 p.m., according to the Fresno County Sheriff's Office.
A person in the boy's group jumped into the water to rescue him but could not find him, officials said.
