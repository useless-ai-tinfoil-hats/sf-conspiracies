Disneyland is getting six new rides and attractions
-----
"Recuerdame," Ernesto de la Cruz sings in "Coco," the Disney Pixar animated film. But soon, Disney fans won't have to remember the singing skeleton: He'll be a permanent fixture at Disneyland. The movie is getting its first-ever theme park attraction, which will be at Disney California Adventure.It's one of six new rides and attractions currently in progress for the Disneyland Resort that Disney Experiences Chairman Josh D'Amaro announced Saturday at D23, the Disney fan convention.
