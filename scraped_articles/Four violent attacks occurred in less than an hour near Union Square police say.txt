Four violent attacks occurred in less than an hour near Union Square, police say
-----
Four people were violently attacked near Union Square in San Francisco on Sunday morning, all in less than an hour, the San Francisco Police Department confirmed.The alleged attacks occurred on Market Street and included one assault and three stabbings, all allegedly carried out by the same suspect, the police said in a Wednesday news release.
