Dollar Tree purchases dozens of 99 Cents Only locations in California
-----
Dollar Tree Inc. announced Wednesday that it will be taking over 170 locations previously occupied by 99 Cents Only stores across four states, including California.Bill Read, an executive at Retail Specialists, a commercial real estate firm that specializes in retail, posted on LinkedIn a list of 112 locations that Dollar Tree acquired. While some are in the Bay Area, such as Hayward, Antioch and Richmond, most of the 93 California stores on the list are in Southern California.
