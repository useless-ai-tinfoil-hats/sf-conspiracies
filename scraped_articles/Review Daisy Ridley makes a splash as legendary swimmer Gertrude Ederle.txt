Review: Daisy Ridley makes a splash as legendary swimmer Gertrude Ederle
-----
With "Young Woman and the Sea," Gertrude "Trudy" Ederle finally gets the movie she deserves.In 1926, the American swimmer who won a gold and two bronze medals at the 1924 Paris Olympics, became the first woman to swim the English Channel. In the process, this daughter of a Brooklyn butcher became internationally famous and a pioneering role model for women in sports.
