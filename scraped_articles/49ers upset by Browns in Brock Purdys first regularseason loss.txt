49ers upset by Browns in Brock Purdy’s first regular-season loss
-----
CLEVELAND — The San Francisco 49ers lost a game, their aura of invincibility and some key players Sunday.In a three-hour rock fight with the Browns, 9.5-point underdogs who were starting a backup quarterback but were powered by the NFL's top-ranked defense, the 49ers fell 19-17, a defeat that dropped them to 5-1 this season.
