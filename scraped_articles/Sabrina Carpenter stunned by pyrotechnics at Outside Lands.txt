Sabrina Carpenter stunned by pyrotechnics at Outside Lands
-----
Sabrina Carpenter was caught off guard during her headlining set at the Outside Lands festival in San Francisco over the weekend.In a video that quickly spread online, the "Espresso" singer is seen reacting with surprise as pyrotechnics ignited close behind her. Carpenter can be seen leaving the stage momentarily after the unexpected flare-up near the end of her performance on the second day of the three-day festival in Golden Gate Park, which ran Friday-Sunday, Aug. 9-11.
