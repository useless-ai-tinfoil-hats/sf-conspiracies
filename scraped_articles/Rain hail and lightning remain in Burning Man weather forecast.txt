Rain, hail and lightning remain in Burning Man weather forecast
-----
Rain over Nevada's Black Rock Desert turned the annual Burning Man festival into a muddy mess this weekend, trapping up to 70,000 people. The National Weather Service said a chance for rain and thunderstorms remains in the forecast through Sunday night, with conditions expected to clear on Monday.The burning of the man and all other burns have been canceled, and vehicle traffic on the Playa has come to a stop.
