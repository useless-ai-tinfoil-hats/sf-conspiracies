Better Mortgage review 2023
-----
Better Mortgage is an online mortgage lender, offering conventional loans, FHA loans, and home equity lines of credit (HELOCs). The lender originated nearly 45,000 loans in 2022 alone.Better is known for its technology-driven approach to mortgages, one that offers nearly instant approvals, an easy online process, and fast closings. The company also helps pair consumers with real estate agents, insurance services, attorneys, and other providers needed throughout the homebuying process.
