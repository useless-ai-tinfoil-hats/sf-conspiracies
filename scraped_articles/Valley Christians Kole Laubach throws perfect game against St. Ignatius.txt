Valley Christian’s Kole Laubach throws perfect game against St. Ignatius
-----
It wasn't the perfect West Catholic Athletic League baseball season Valley Christian experienced last season, but Friday's 3-0 win at St. Ignatius was perfect indeed.Kole Laubach, a 6-foot-2, 180-pound junior right-hander, set down all 21 batters he faced to record the first perfect game in the 35-year Valley Christian career of coach John Diatte.
