Austin producing 10 times as much housing as San Francisco, analysis shows
-----
It's no secret that the Bay Area is short on housing. But simply stating that the region suffers from a "housing crisis" tends to obscure the exact degree of the problem.The Bay Area Council Economic Institute, a local nonprofit think tank, used census data to rank major U.S. cities by the per-capita number of housing permits issued from January 2022 through August 2023. The organization released its analysis to SFGATE on Monday; it will be released publicly Thursday.
