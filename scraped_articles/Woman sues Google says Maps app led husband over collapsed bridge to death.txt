Woman sues Google, says Maps app led husband over collapsed bridge to death
-----
A North Carolina woman is suing Google for allegedly directing her husband across a collapsed bridge in September 2022. He followed Google Maps' instructions, the lawsuit claims, and died after crashing.Google Maps continued to direct traffic over the bridge and suggest it was a usable roadway until Thursday.
