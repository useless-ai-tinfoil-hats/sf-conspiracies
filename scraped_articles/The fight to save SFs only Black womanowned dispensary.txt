The fight to save SF's only Black woman-owned dispensary
-----
Reese Benton has less than three weeks to raise $200,000, or, as she puts it, she'll lose everything.She's invested almost a decade and a million dollars in Posh Green Cannabis Boutique, all while weathering a pandemic, multiple robberies and California's troubled pot market. But Benton's business was recently dealt another blow. Her landlord wants to sell the space when Benton's lease is up May 31.
