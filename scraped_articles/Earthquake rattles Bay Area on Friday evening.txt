Earthquake rattles Bay Area on Friday evening
-----
A quick but firm rattle was felt throughout the Bay Area on Friday evening as an earthquake with a preliminary magnitude of 3.9 shook the region.According to the US Geological Survey, the earthquake hit at 6:38 p.m. The quake was centered near Millbrae at a depth initially listed as about 9 kilometers, then revised to 13.1 km. No damage was immediately reported, although residents throughout the Bay Area reported feeling the shaking.
