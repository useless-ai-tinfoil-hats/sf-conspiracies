Giants acquire utilityman Otto Lopez from Toronto
-----
As part of some pre-spring-training roster juggling, the San Francisco Giants acquired infielder-outfielder Otto Lopez from the Toronto Blue Jays for cash considerations Tuesday.Lopez, 25, a native of the Dominican, appeared in nine big-league games in 2021 and 2022 — collecting six hits in 10 at-bats — and spent last season at Triple-A, hitting .258 with a .656 OPS in 318 at-bats.
