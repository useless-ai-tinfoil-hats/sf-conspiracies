Brock Purdy, George Kittle connect for 3 TDs in 49ers' historic beatdown of Cowboys
-----
The showdown was a beatdown.The San Francisco 49ers mauled the Cowboys 42-10 on Sunday night at Levi's Stadium as quarterback Brock Purdy had a career-high four passing touchdowns, tight end George Kittle had a career-best three receiving scores and the 49ers matched the longest winning streak in franchise history with their 15th straight regular-season victory.
