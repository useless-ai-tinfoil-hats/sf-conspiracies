Category 5 hurricane that suddenly appeared in Pacific Ocean could send big waves to California
-----
A storm that suddenly strengthened into a Category 5 hurricane in the eastern Pacific Ocean could send strong waves into parts of California this weekend.Hurricane Jova, which is more than 500 miles south-southwest of the southern tip of Baja California, has sustained winds of close to 160 miles per hour, with gusts that are even more powerful, according to the National Weather Service, which noted that it could strengthen further Wednesday night.
