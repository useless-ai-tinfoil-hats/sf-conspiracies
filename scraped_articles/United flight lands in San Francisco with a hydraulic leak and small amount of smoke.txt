United flight lands in San Francisco with a hydraulic leak and ‘small amount of smoke’
-----
A United Airlines flight landed in San Francisco on Thursday with a hydraulic leak and other issues, the latest in a string of alarming malfunctions for the airline.Flight 1816, originating from Dallas-Fort Worth and bound for San Francisco International Airport, landed safely after four hours of travel with 150 passengers and five crew members on board. Emergency vehicles stood by as a precaution as the passengers deplaned at the gate, according to a spokesperson for United.
