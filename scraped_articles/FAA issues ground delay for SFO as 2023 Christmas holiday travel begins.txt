FAA issues ground delay for SFO as 2023 Christmas holiday travel begins
-----
Holiday travel is ramping up at SFO, where the TSA plans to process half a million travelers this week, and just as people arrived at the airport, so did a lingering winter storm.The weather service issued flood advisories across the Bay Area as the Federal Aviation Administration issued a ground delay for flights bound for SFO that started Monday and is persisting into the week.
The FAA also issued a departure delay at 9:14 a.m. Tuesday for 15 minutes and increasing due to wind.
