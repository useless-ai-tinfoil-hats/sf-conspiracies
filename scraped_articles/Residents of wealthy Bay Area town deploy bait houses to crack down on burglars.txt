Residents of wealthy Bay Area town deploy 'bait houses' to crack down on burglars
-----
Some residents in a wealthy Bay Area town have volunteered their homes as "bait houses" for a burglary prevention program.Atherton, an incorporated town in San Mateo County, is facing an uptick in burglaries this year, Daniel Larsen, commander for the Atherton Police Department, told SFGATE.
More than 50 Atherton residents offered their homes to the program after police asked for volunteers.
Residents in the program work with police to identify valuable items in their homes
