I-80 west in Fairfield reopens, 3 eastbound lanes still closed after police standoff
-----
Both directions of Interstate 80 were closed in Fairfield on Friday during the afternoon commute as authorities engaged in a standoff with a "potentially armed suspect," the California Highway Patrol said.Westbound lanes reopened after about 3 hours and two of five eastbound lanes reopened after about 4 hours as police continued to work the scene.
