What was learned about Warriors during their four-game East Coast trip?
-----
The beatdown in Boston for the Golden State Warriors didn't much mar their four-game trip. They were over it before they left TD Garden, loosening their locker room with postgame banter about college course loads instead of basketball.Draymond Green studied communications at Michigan State. Chris Paul had smaller classes — 20 or so students — while attending Wake Forest. Klay Thompson is two semesters away from graduation at Washington State.
