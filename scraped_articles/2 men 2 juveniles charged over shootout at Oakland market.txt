2 men, 2 juveniles charged over shootout at Oakland market
-----
Alameda County District Attorney Pamela Price has filed felony charges against two men and two juveniles in connection with a February shootout at an Oakland convenience store.Keeric Lamont Johnson, 23, and Joshua Smiley, 19, face charges including attempted murder, assault with a semiautomatic firearm, and shooting into an inhabited dwelling in the Feb. 19 attack at the Al-Awdi's Mini Market, the district attorney's office said in a statement.
Price did not detail the charges
