16-year-old US Olympian says funniest possible thing after winning gold medal
-----
Team USA's youngest gold medalist is experiencing quite the crash after a euphoric summer spent at the Paris Olympics. Quincy Wilson, who is just 16 years old, won a gold medal as part of the US men's 4x400-meter relay win on Saturday night. But, alas, real life awaits the teenager back home."Dang, I really got school in 2 and a half weeks," he posted on X, accompanied by a broken heart emoji.
