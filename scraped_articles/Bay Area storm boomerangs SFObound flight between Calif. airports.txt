Bay Area storm boomerangs SFO-bound flight between Calif. airports
-----
Passengers on an international flight to San Francisco International Airport on Sunday boomeranged between airports for over 18 hours as the worst storm so far this season held the Bay Area in a standstill.Cathay Pacific 870 underwent multiple diversions before ultimately arriving at another California airport over 300 miles from its intended destination.
