Wind shift in the Bay Area to impact fires, air quality
-----
The vast majority of red flag warnings issued around the Bay Area and Central Valley expire on Tuesday morning, as winds start to move onshore and relative humidity levels rise. The transition to a lower fire risk environment will be a gradual process though, which will leave some lingering air quality issues for parts of the North Bay as existing incidents like the Point Fire continue to burn.The pattern shift will be marked by the return of low clouds.
