75-year-old hiker dies after 'extremely steep' fall on Calif.'s perilous Mount Baldy
-----
Yet another hiker has been found dead on the dangerous slopes of Southern California's Mount Baldy.Raymond Kopecky, 75, headed out to hike the Sugarloaf Peak trail on Saturday afternoon, and last communicated with friends at around 1:30 p.m., the San Bernardino County Sheriff's Office told SFGATE over email. After receiving reports of the missing hiker, search-and-rescue crews headed out Sunday evening and located a body at the bottom of a cliff.
