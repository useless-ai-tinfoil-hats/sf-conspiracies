South Bay tech company to cut 10% of workforce
-----
San Jose's streaming video company Roku said in a filing that it plans to cut 10% of its workforce, about 300 people, in a broader effort to bring down costs.Tech industry job cuts, which have claimed tens of thousands of jobs across the Bay Area and the country, have slowed but not stopped in recent months. Google, Meta, Twitter and many other companies have slashed their workforces amid soaring inflation and recession concerns that have created pressure to squeeze out profits.
