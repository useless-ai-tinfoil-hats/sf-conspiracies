Berkeley's People's Park is already almost unrecognizable
-----
The decadeslong battle over the future of People's Park reached a new flashpoint early Thursday when UC Berkeley seized control of the site, walling it off with shipping containers.The operation, which began in the early hours, marks the university's most brazen attempt to move ahead with its plans to build housing for students, unhoused people and low-income people on the 2.8-acre park.
