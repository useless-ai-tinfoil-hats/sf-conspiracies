San Francisco dedicates street to Bruce Springsteen and E Street Band
-----
San Francisco is welcoming Bruce Springsteen and the E Street Band by dedicating a street to them.For the first time, Chase Center, where the band is scheduled to perform two sold-out shows on Thursday and Sunday, March 28 and 31, has collaborated with the San Francisco Municipal Transit Authority to create unique street signs in honor of the Boss.
