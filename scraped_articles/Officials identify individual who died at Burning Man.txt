Officials identify individual who died at Burning Man
-----
A man attending this year's annual Burning Man festival died Friday, as a cold and wet weather system bore down on Nevada's Black Rock Desert.The Pershing County Sheriff's Office named the man as 32-year-old Leon Reece in a press release on Monday. The sheriff's office did not share Reece's place of residence. The cause of death remains under investigation.
