Letters: Divisive discourse over Israeli-Palestinian conflict incites hate. Why it’s time to tone down
-----
Regarding "Breed is right to oppose S.F. supervisors' Gaza cease-fire resolution. Here's why" (Letters to the Editor, SFChronicle.com, Jan. 18): Although I agree with San Francisco Mayor London Breed's condemnation of the Board of Supervisors' Gaza cease-fire resolution, the letter writer who commends her action lays out the reasons why Israel should continue its war; the final paragraph bothered me: " … a call for a cease-fire is either willful ignorance or out-and-out antisemitism."
