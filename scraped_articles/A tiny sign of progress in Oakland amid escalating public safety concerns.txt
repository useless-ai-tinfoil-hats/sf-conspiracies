A tiny sign of progress in Oakland amid escalating public safety concerns
-----
As Oakland struggles to reduce crime, with many residents seeing their streets as incubators for violence and disorder, city leaders have been unable to hold many civil conversations about how to solve the crisis because of all the poisonous hostility among people supposedly of the same political party.But last weekend offered something different: a small reprieve from this chaos, and an unexpected glimpse at what is possible when moderates and progressives share space and contrary views
