Entire Disney World hotel rooms, with furniture, for sale for $49,900 each
-----
If you never want your Disney World vacation to end — and you have $50,000 lying around — you can be the proud new owner of an entire hotel room, complete with furnishings.Disney World is in the process of replacing its cabins at Fort Wilderness, a campsite in the Magic Kingdom area. The old cabins, built between 1998 and 2001, don't have a home on Disney property any longer. Disney contracted with third-party company J&J Venture Group, which is selling the old cabins for $49,900 each.
