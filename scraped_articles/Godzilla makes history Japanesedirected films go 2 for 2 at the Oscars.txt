Godzilla makes history: Japanese-directed films go 2 for 2 at the Oscars
-----
Two Japanese films made box office history in North America in December, and now both have won Oscars.Master animator Hayao Miyazaki, the 83-year-old Studio Ghibli director often called "the Walt Disney of Japan," won best animated feature on Sunday, March 10, for "The Boy and the Heron," a unique fantasy about an 11-year-old boy trying to make sense of post-World War II Japan who encounters a mysterious figure in a bizarre dreamworld.
