Why J.D. Davis thinks Giants’ job at third is his, won’t worry about Matt Chapman
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — J.D. Davis is well aware the San Francisco Giants have pursued another third baseman this winter. He knows that even with spring camp well under way, the team still might add Matt Chapman, one of the "Boras Four" free agents who remains unsigned. This does not faze Davis. He was traded from the Astros to Mets and then from New York to San Francisco, so he knows trades can be beneficial.Not that he wants to go anywhere.
