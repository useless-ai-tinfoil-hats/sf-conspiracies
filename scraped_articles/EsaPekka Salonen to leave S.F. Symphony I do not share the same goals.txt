Esa-Pekka Salonen to leave S.F. Symphony: ‘I do not share the same goals’
-----
Esa-Pekka Salonen, the acclaimed Finnish conductor and composer who in 2020 succeeded Michael Tilson Thomas as music director of the San Francisco Symphony, will end his tenure with the orchestra when his contract expires at the conclusion of the 2024-25 season.The news, made public on Thursday, March 14, coincided with the release of the schedule for Salonen's final season.
