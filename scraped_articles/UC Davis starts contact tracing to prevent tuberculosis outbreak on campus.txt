UC Davis starts contact tracing to prevent tuberculosis outbreak on campus
-----
UC Davis officials are working to prevent an outbreak of contagious tuberculosis after someone on campus turned up positive for the potentially lethal disease.The university and the Yolo County health department announced Wednesday that they are notifying everyone who has been in close contact with the infected person that they should be tested for tuberculosis, known as TB.
