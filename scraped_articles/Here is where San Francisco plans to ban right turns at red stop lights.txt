Here is where San Francisco plans to ban right turns at red stop lights
-----
San Francisco is banning right turns at red lights at approximately 200 intersections in the downtown area as part of its ongoing efforts to protect pedestrians and curb the number of traffic deaths.The city's traffic engineer, Ricardo Olea, approved the work on Friday. Installation of the signs stating the new restrictions is scheduled to begin this month and is expected to run through August of next year.
Once the work is complete, motorists will be prohibited from turning right
