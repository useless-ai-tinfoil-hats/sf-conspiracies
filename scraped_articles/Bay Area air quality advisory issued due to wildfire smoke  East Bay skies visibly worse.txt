Bay Area air quality advisory issued due to wildfire smoke — East Bay skies ‘visibly worse’
-----
An air quality advisory was issued Thursday afternoon for the Bay Area due to wildfire smoke, with poor conditions expected to last through Friday.The advisory by the Bay Area Air Quality Management District said "smoke from wildfires in the western United States and Canada is expected to impact air quality in the Bay Area on Thursday and Friday," but did not specify which wildfires were sending the smoke into the region.
