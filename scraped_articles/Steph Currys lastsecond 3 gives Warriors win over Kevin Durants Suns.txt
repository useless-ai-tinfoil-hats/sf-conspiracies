Steph Curry’s last-second 3 gives Warriors win over Kevin Durant’s Suns
-----
Kevin Durant was visiting the Bay Area on Saturday night.Stephen Curry reminded him he still lives here.
The Golden State Warriors superstar guard sank the go-ahead 3-pointer against the Phoenix Suns with 0.7 seconds left in regulation, giving the Warriors a 113-112 victory and sending another sellout crowd into a tizzy for the team's sixth win in seven games.
This one against his former superstar teammate, who supplied 24 points, 10 rebounds and seven assists for the Suns.
