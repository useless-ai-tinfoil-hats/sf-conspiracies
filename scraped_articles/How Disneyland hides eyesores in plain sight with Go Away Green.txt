How Disneyland hides eyesores in plain sight with 'Go Away Green'
-----
Disneyland is all about immersion: stepping into a jungle adventure, getting lost in the alleyways of old New Orleans, escaping to a far-flung planet in the "Star Wars" universe. But as beautifully detailed as these lands are, some things just can't be avoided. Like hand rails and power outlets.For these legal and logistical necessities, Disneyland came up with its own paint colors, the most famous of which is "Go Away Green."
