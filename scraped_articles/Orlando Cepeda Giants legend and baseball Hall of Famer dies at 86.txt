Orlando Cepeda, Giants legend and baseball Hall of Famer, dies at 86
-----
Orlando Cepeda, one of the San Francisco Giants' most cherished and lovable players whose statue has greeted fans outside Oracle Park since 2008, died Friday after a long battle with a series of ailments. He was 86.The Giants made the announcement of Cepeda's death just before the sixth inning of Friday night's Giants-Dodgers game, the public address announcer alerting the crowd of 40,052 about the man known as "Cha Cha" and "the Bull."
