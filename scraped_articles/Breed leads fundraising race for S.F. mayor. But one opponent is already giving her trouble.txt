Breed leads fundraising race for S.F. mayor. But one opponent is already giving her trouble
-----
Mayor London Breed has a fundraising edge over her two most prominent reelection challengers, but one of them is already giving her a run for her money.Breed's campaign reported Wednesday that she raised about $406,800 in 2023 to support her bid for another term this year. Including money Breed raised in previous years, she had pulled in about $657,000 toward her reelection through Dec. 31.
