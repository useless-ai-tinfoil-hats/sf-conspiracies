Presidio Heights killing: Suspect arrested in death of 27-year-old S.F. tech worker
-----
San Francisco police said they arrested a man on suspicion of murdering a 27-year-old tech worker who was found dead in their Presidio Heights home last week.Scott Fisher, 29, was arrested in Concord Thursday morning and booked into San Francisco County Jail at 12:51 p.m., according to police and jail records.
