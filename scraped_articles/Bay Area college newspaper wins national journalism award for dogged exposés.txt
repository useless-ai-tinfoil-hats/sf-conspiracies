Bay Area college newspaper wins national journalism award for dogged exposés
-----
A team of tenacious student journalists in the East Bay — who revealed in 2020 that the Peralta Community College District was paying millions in taxpayer dollars to security companies riddled with problems — has won a national press freedom award, the Student Press Law Center has announced.The students were forced to sue the college district to obtain many of the public records needed to do their reporting.
