Bay Area officers: Road rage driver arrested after harassing victim online
-----
A Santa Rosa man has been arrested in relation to a road range incident and subsequent online harassment, according to the California Highway Patrol.CHP officers responded to a road rage incident on northbound Highway 101 at around 9:45 a.m. Aug. 7. The victim alleged that, after a lane change, the man followed him onto the Steele Lane exit. The man then used a window punch and folding knife to assault the victim and vandalize his vehicle while at a traffic light, the CHP said.
