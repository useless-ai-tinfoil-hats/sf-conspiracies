Oakland liquor store hit again by burglars ramming car into building
-----
Burglars early Tuesday morning rammed a car into an Oakland liquor store, at least the third time recent months the tactic has been used to rob the store, according to its co-owner.The most recent burglary happened just before 4 a.m. at Eddie's Drive In Liquors at 5491 College Ave., the Oakland Police Department said in a statement to SFGATE. When officers arrived on the scene, they discovered that the front door was forced off the hinges, OPD said.
