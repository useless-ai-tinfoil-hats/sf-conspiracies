Ralph Thomas, last living member of history-making USF football team, dies at 94
-----
Ralph Thomas was the last living player of the last University of San Francisco football team. The Division I program peaked with an undefeated season and top-20 ranking in 1951 and then was discontinued for lack of funding.But USF's football team did not go quietly: They made history by turning down an invitation to play in the Orange Bowl in Miami on the condition that they leave behind two Black players, Burl Toler and Ollie Matson.
