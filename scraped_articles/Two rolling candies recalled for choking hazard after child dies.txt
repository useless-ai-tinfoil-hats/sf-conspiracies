Two ‘rolling’ candies recalled for choking hazard after child dies
-----
Two types of "rolling" candies were recalled this week due to a choking hazard that has killed one child.Both the Cocco Candy Rolling Candy and the Slime Licker Sour Rolling Liquid Candy contain balls that roll around to dispense flavored liquid candy. The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission has announced the balls in both candies can dislodge, causing children to choke.
In April, a 7-year-old girl in New York choked to death after the Cocco Candy ball dislodged and got stuck
