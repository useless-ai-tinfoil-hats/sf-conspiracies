Bay Area judge removed from bench for harassing women can resume legal practice
-----
A former Contra Costa County judge who was removed from the bench in 2019 for sexual harassment and mistreatment of attorneys and court staff has won approval from the state Supreme Court to resume work as a lawyer.John Laettner's reinstatement was based on the State Bar Court's findings that Laettner had undergone therapy, understood his past misconduct and was unlikely to repeat it.
