The director who vanished from the 13th tee of a California golf course
-----
On a summer evening in 1997, a theater director headed out to hit a twilight round of golf in Carmel Valley, the wealthy oceanside town on California's Central Coast. There was a gentle breeze rustling the oak trees and tall grass that evening, the sea air a mild 60 degrees.Greg Howells played 12 holes and was never seen again.
