Vallejo police arrest person suspected of torching Target store, causing $1 million in damage
-----
Vallejo police have arrested a person suspected of setting a fire that caused an estimated $1 million in damage at a Target store, officials said.Police on Friday afternoon said they could not immediately identify the person, since they were still being booked.
The fire erupted on the afternoon of Feb. 24 in Target's paper towel aisle at 904 Admiral Callaghan Lane. A store employee was treated for minor smoke inhalation that day, but no other injuries were reported.
