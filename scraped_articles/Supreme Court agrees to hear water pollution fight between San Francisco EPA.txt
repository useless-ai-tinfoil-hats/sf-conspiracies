Supreme Court agrees to hear water pollution fight between San Francisco, EPA
-----
The Supreme Court agreed Tuesday to hear San Francisco's appeal of a ruling that tightened offshore water pollution standards and said the city was failing to adequately protect swimmers and bathers from discharges of sewage into the Pacific.
