Historic S.F. bar with famous views reborn with new drinks
-----
San Franciscans will soon once again sip cocktails and take in sweeping views from the historic rooftop cocktail bar on the 21st floor of the Beacon Grand, formerly known as the Sir Francis Drake Hotel, in Union Square.The bar at 450 Powell St., now renamed Starlite and opening on Feb. 2, 2024, was most famously known as Harry Denton's Starlight Room, named after the legendary barman and nightclub owner who died in 2021.
