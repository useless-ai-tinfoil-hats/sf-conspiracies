How Jung Hoo Lee developed elite contact skills that sold Giants on him
-----
Jung Hoo Lee sets himself in the batter's box with an open stance, his front foot farther from the plate than his back foot. As the pitcher is about to release the ball, Lee swivels his front foot closer to the plate and does a quick toe tap. As the ball approaches, his hips rapidly rotate to begin a swing.
