SF Giants are bringing back Dodgers-supporting PA announcer for Opening Day
-----
The San Francisco Giants are slated to use a die-hard Dodgers fan as their public address announcer for Opening Day on Friday, as well as the whole first regular-season weekend.C.J. Silas is scheduled to be on the mic for the entire three-game series against the Padres, according to the San Luis Obispo Tribune. She was the PA announcer for the Giants' exhibition game against the A's last week. The Giants did not respond to an SFGATE request for confirmation by the time of publication.
