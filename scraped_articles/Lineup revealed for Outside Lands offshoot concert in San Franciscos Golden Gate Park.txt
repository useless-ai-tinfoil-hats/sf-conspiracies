Lineup revealed for Outside Lands offshoot concert in San Francisco's Golden Gate Park

Back in 1967, Golden Gate Park's Polo Fields were the landing pad for the Human Be-In, the massive hippie celebration that paved the way for the Summer of Love. This August, the fields are slated to be the site of another counter-culture gathering, but the style and sound couldn't be more different.On Tuesday, Another Planet Entertainment, the independent Bay Area concert promoter behind Outside Lands, announced the first installation of an annual Golden Gate Park Concerts series.
