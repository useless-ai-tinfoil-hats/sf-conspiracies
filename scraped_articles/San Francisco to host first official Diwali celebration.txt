San Francisco to host first official Diwali celebration
-----
The city of San Francisco is gearing up for its first official Diwali celebration this fall.The festivities will be part of the highly anticipated return of Bhangra and Beats, a free night market series that kicked off last spring, infusing the Financial District with South Asian culture by celebrating bhangra music and a range of local eateries.
