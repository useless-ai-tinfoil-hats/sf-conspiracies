Woman arrested in Wine Country suspected of throwing full wine bottles at cars, wounding a child
-----
Sonoma County sheriff's deputies on Saturday arrested a woman they believe threw full wine bottles at passing cars on Old Redwood Highway, wounding a child.Maria Carmona, 43, of Susanville (Lassen County) was in jail as of Tuesday morning, booked on charges of felony assault with a deadly weapon and felony throwing an object at a vehicle.
Court records show that on Tuesday prosecutors reduced the charges to two misdemeanors for petty theft and throwing a substance at a vehicle.
