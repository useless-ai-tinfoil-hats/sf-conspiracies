The Rolling Stones reveal first new album in 18 years, ‘Hackney Diamonds’
-----
The Rolling Stones have unveiled details of their first studio album in nearly two decades.The surviving members of the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame band — Mick Jagger, Keith Richards and Ronnie Wood — introduced "Hackney Diamonds," including its album cover, track list and lead single, "Angry," during a global livestream hosted by "The Tonight Show" host Jimmy Fallon on Wednesday, Sept. 6.
