Warriors won't get Paul George after NBA superstar declines option
-----
The Golden State Warriors' pursuit of Paul George appears to be dead.George is reportedly declining his $48.8 million player option, sending the superstar into free agency and effectively cutting off any pathway for the Warriors to acquire him. ESPN's Adrian Wojnarowski was first to report on George's decision.
