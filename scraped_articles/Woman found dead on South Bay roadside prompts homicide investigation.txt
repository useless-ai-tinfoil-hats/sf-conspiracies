Woman found dead on South Bay roadside prompts homicide investigation
-----
Santa Clara County sheriff's officials said Monday they were investigating a woman's Oct. 9 death as a homicide.Deputies were dispatched last week to the 2200 block of Mount Pleasant Road in an unincorporated part of San Jose following a report that a woman was found unresponsive near a roadway.
Fire department personnel and medics arrived at the scene and declared the woman dead.
Citing unspecified "evidence located" at the scene, officials began a homicide investigation,
