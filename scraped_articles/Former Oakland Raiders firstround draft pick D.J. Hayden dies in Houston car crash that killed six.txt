Former Oakland Raiders first-round draft pick D.J. Hayden dies in Houston car crash that killed six
-----
Former Raiders cornerback D.J. Hayden died Saturday morning in a car crash in downtown Houston.The crash occurred around 2 a.m. Central Standard Time, Houston Police Assistant Chief Megan Howard said in a media briefing. Police said a car sped through a red light near downtown Houston and struck the vehicle carrying the players.
Four people died at the scene and four more were taken to a local hospital, where two later died, Howard said.
