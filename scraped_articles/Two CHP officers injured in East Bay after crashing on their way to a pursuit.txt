Two CHP officers injured in East Bay after crashing on their way to a pursuit
-----
Two California Highway Patrol officers headed to where other officers were chasing a vehicle suffered moderate injuries when they crashed their patrol vehicle on Highway 24 in the East Bay early Sunday, a CHP official said.The officers lost control of their vehicle on the eastbound side of the highway, causing it to overturn, about 2 a.m. near Wilder Road. The officers got out of the vehicle before it caught fire, CHP officer Adib Zeid said.
Both officers were taken to a hospital
