East Bay favorite for smoky Tijuana-style tacos is coming to S.F.
-----
One of the East Bay's best taquerias will open a new location in San Francisco.Richmond's Tacos El Tucan will open in the Castro District at 3600 16th St., inside the space formerly home to Bonita Taqueria y Rotisserie. Bonita closed on Sunday, and workers were seen putting up signs for the incoming restaurant on Monday morning.
