Missing California mother found dead near hiking trail, police say
-----
The missing California mother of a 3-year-old girl was found dead near a hiking trail in the Santa Cruz Mountains on Sunday, officials said.The body of Lizbeth Arceo Sedano, 25, was spotted by a hiker near Eureka Canyon Road and Grizzly Flat Road, in an area known as Rattlesnake Gulch near the community of Corralitos. Those two roads intersect just outside the Forest of Nisene Marks State Park.
