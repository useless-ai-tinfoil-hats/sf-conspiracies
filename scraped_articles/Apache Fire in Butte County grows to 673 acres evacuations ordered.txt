Apache Fire in Butte County grows to 673 acres, evacuations ordered
-----
Butte County residents in a small town were forced to evacuate Monday evening when a fast-moving wildfire broke out in Oroville, Cal Fire said.The Apache Fire sparked near Apache Hill Road in Oroville around 7:21 p.m., firefighters said.
By Tuesday morning, the fire was 15% contained after growing just slightly overnight to 673 acres.
