Third Eye Blind to return to the Bay Area this summer
-----
San Francisco '90s rock band Third Eye Blind is returning to the Bay Area for its Summer Gods Tour in June.The fourth edition of the tour, announced Monday, Jan. 8, is scheduled to kick off in Airway Heights, Wash., on June 8, making 37 stops across the United States before wrapping up in Houston on Aug. 3. The local band's one stop in Northern California is set for June 15 at Mountain View's Shoreline Amphitheatre.
