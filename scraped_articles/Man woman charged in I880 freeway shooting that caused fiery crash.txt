Man, woman charged in I-880 freeway shooting that caused fiery crash
-----
A man and a woman were charged this week with attempted murder in a road rage-fueled shooting that caused the victim to crash on Interstate 880 in San Leandro.Elias Jimenez, 22, and Maria Martinez, 43, were arrested last week and remained in jail as of Wednesday afternoon. Martinez pleaded not guilty Tuesday to the charges. It was not immediately clear if Jimenez entered a plea.
