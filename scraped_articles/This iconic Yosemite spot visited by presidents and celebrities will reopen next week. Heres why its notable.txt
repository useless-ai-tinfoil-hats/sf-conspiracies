This iconic Yosemite spot visited by presidents and celebrities will reopen next week. Here's why it's notable
-----
The dining room of the Ahwahnee hotel in Yosemite will reopen next week after nearly a year of being shut down, the hotel announced Thursday.Yosemite visitors can now make reservations for a buffet-style dinner in the Ahwahnee Dining Room starting Dec. 14. The space shut down in January for a seismic renovation project taking place at the hotel, according to Yosemite Hospitality.
