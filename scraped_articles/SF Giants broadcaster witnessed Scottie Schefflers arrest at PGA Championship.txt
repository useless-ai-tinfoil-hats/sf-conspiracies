SF Giants broadcaster witnessed Scottie Scheffler's arrest at PGA Championship
-----
One of the San Francisco Giants broadcasters was on the scene for one of the most chaotic scenes in professional golf history.Dave Flemming is in Louisville, Kentucky, to broadcast the PGA Championship for ESPN and was on the scene when Scottie Scheffler was arrested by Louisville police.
Scheffler, who won the Masters in April and is ranked as the No. 1 golfer in the world, was put in handcuffs and taken to jail for not following police orders during a pedestrian fatality
