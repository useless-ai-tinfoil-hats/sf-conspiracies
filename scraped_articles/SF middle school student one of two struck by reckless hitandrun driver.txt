SF middle school student one of two struck by reckless hit-and-run driver
-----
On Tuesday, two pedestrians were hit by the same driver. The first was injured in a hit-and-run in the Tenderloin, and the second, a 14-year-old, was injured during the subsequent police pursuit near a San Francisco middle school. Both were transported to local hospitals with non-life-threatening injuries.The crashes have renewed debate about police policy around high-speed chases, raising concerns about the public safety risks of pursuits on crowded city streets.
