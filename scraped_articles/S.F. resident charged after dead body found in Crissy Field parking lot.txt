S.F. resident charged after dead body found in Crissy Field parking lot
-----
Federal prosecutors have charged a San Francisco resident with aiding and abetting the murder of a man found shot in the head in the Crissy Field parking lot.The U.S. Attorney's Office of the Northern District of California alleges that cellphone location data and surveillance camera footage implicated Leion Butler, 20, in the murder of a man whose body was found in the early morning of Nov. 12. The San Francisco Medical Examiner identified the victim as Hamza Walupupu, 32, of Alameda
