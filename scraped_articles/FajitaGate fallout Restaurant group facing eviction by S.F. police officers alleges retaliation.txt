‘FajitaGate’ fallout? Restaurant group facing eviction by S.F. police officers alleges retaliation
-----
As if the story about off-duty police officers demanding another person's fajitas wasn't bizarre enough, the fallout of that 2002 incident could, some 20 years later, be the reason for a San Francisco eviction lawsuit.That's according to documents filed in San Francisco Superior Court on Tuesday by a local restaurant group that is accusing its South Beach landlord — the San Francisco Police Officers Association — of retaliation.
