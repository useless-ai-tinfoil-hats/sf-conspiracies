‘Misery loves company’: Both S.F. and Oakland more likely to close schools as budget outlook worsens
-----
School closures in San Francisco and Oakland look even more likely after district officials on both sides of the bay concluded this past week that their dire fiscal outlook is now worse.Both the San Francisco Unified School District and Oakland Unified School District have been overspending for years. Both face ongoing declining enrollment and too many empty seats. Both are scrambling to make budget cuts to overcome massive deficits.
