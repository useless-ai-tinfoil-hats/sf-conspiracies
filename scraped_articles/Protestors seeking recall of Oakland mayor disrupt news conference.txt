Protestors seeking recall of Oakland mayor disrupt news conference
-----
A group of demonstrators supporting a campaign to recall Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao disrupted a news conference Wednesday where Thao announced that the city was awarded a $3.5 million state grant to put more "safety ambassadors" on the streets.Oakland police officers arrived just after 10 a.m. at Latham Square after demonstrators got into heated arguments with Thao's supporters.
