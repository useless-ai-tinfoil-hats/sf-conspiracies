This meat mecca smokes 50,000 pounds of pastrami a week for California's top delis
-----
The #19 sandwich at Langer's Deli in Los Angeles is quite possibly as famous as a sandwich can get. Late Los Angeles Times restaurant critic Jonathan Gold called the combination of hot pastrami stacked with coleslaw, Swiss cheese and Russian dressing on double-baked rye bread "the best pastrami sandwich in America."
