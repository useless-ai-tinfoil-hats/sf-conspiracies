San Francisco proposes strongest-in-the-nation ban on ‘forever chemicals’ in firefighter clothing
-----
Firefighters are more likely to develop cancer and die from it than the general population, and it's not just smoke from burning buildings that's suspected of making them sick.Firefighting clothing is made with synthetic "forever chemicals" linked to cancers, organ damage and other illnesses, and though manufacturers have reduced the amount of these chemicals in the materials, they still remain in an inner layer close to the skin.
