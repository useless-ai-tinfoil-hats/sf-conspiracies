Undercover bust of fencing operation on Mission Street recovers $18K in stolen goods, police say
-----
Three people suspected of selling stolen goods worth nearly $18,000 at illegal street vending booths were arrested in San Francisco after undercover police officers discovered the fencing scheme, police said Wednesday.Deysi Ramirez, 59, of San Francisco, and Veronica Villanueva, 40, of Daly City, were charged with possession of stolen property and conspiracy, police said. Marvin Dick Godoy Calderon, 43, of San Francisco, was cited and released.
