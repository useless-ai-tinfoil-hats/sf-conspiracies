There's something fishy about ESPN's deep dive on the Warriors' dynasty
-----
With the Warriors set to kick off the post-All-Star break stretch Thursday night, a big ESPN feature dropped 14 hours before tip-off with a rather punching headline: "Stephen Curry, Steve Kerr and a Golden State Warriors dynasty at a crossroads."The deep dive from ESPN reporter Baxter Holmes looks back at the last five seasons of Dubs basketball, starting from the moment the Warriors lost the 2019 NBA Finals and saw both Kevin Durant and Klay Thompson suffer major injuries.
