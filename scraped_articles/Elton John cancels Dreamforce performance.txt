Elton John cancels Dreamforce performance
-----
Elton John will no longer headline next month's Dreamfest benefit concert in San Francisco.The 77-year-old Rock & Roll Hall of Fame musician scuttled plans to return to the Bay Area at Salesforce's annual Dreamforce conference for the first time since concluding his expansive Farewell Yellow Brick Road tour last year, citing "unforeseen circumstances," Dreamforce organizers announced.
