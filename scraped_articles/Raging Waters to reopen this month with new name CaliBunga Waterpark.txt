Raging Waters to reopen this month with new name: CaliBunga Waterpark
-----
The shuttered South Bay water park long known as Raging Waters will return to San Jose this summer under new management and with a new name: CaliBunga Waterpark.The date for its grand reopening has been set to June 29, according to its newly launched website, where tickets are available for purchase.
