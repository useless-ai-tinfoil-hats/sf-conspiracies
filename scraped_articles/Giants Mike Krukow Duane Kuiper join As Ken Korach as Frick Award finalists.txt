Giants’ Mike Krukow, Duane Kuiper join A’s Ken Korach as Frick Award finalists
-----
Giants TV color man Mike Krukow was still buzzing so much about the San Francisco Giants hiring his friend and former teammate Bob Melvin as manager, he didn't even realize he was named a finalist for the Baseball Hall of Fame's Frick Award, the highest honor for baseball broadcaster."Oh my gosh, really?" Krukow said when a reporter checked with him Wednesday. "That's exciting! What a day."
