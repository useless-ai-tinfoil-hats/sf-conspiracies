At least 4 tunnels once led to Ocean Beach in San Francisco
-----
When San Francisco's Great Highway, which runs down its western shore, closed to cars during the pandemic, it was a novel way to give pedestrians safe and easy access to Ocean Beach. But more than 100 years earlier, San Francisco leaders had the same idea and built permanent infrastructure that ran underground.There were once at least four pedestrian tunnels that led under the street directly to the beach. Today, those tunnels are buried or destroyed.
