West Portal street safety plan is 'over,' but the status quo can't be allowed to prevail
-----
When the driver of an SUV hurtled at high speed into a bus shelter near the busy intersection of West Portal Avenue and Ulloa Street in March, hitting and killing a family of four, it shook San Francisco. Cries for street safety reforms came from every direction, including the Chronicle editorial board.Investigations into the cause of the horrific incident are ongoing. So are calls for action to redesign the crash area to ensure that similar incidents of traffic violence can never happen
