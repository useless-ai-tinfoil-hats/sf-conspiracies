W. Kamau Bell to host Documentary Emmy Awards
-----
W. Kamau Bell has been tapped by the National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences to host the 45th annual Documentary Emmy Awards next month.The Oakland comedian, filmmaker and author has worked within the art form as host and executive producer of CNN's "United Shades of America" and with his own documentary, "1000% Me: Growing Up Mixed," which earned him a Children's and Family Emmy Award last year.
