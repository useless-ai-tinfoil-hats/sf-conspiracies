JP Sears’ stellar stretch ends as Brewers tag him for 7 runs to beat A’s 11-3
-----
Lefty JP Sears has been Mr. Reliable in the Oakland Athletics' rotation for the past two seasons. In his four previous starts before Friday night, Mr. Reliable had been darn close to remarkable.But against the Brewers at the Coliseum on Friday evening, Mr. Reliable proved very hittable. Milwaukee tagged Sears for seven runs (five earned) and nine hits in 3⅔ innings in an 11-3 romp over the A's.
