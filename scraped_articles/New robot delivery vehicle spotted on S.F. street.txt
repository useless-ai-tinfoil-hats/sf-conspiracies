New robot delivery vehicle spotted on S.F. street
-----
An Estonian company is hoping to become the next firm to deploy a fleet of autonomous delivery vehicles onto San Francisco streets.A Clevon robot was spotted Wednesday near the Embarcadero. The company said it was visiting the city to "explore partnerships and engage with investors" in San Francisco. Emblazoned with purple, blue and magenta stripes, the robot carrier delivers "orders to your doorstep."
