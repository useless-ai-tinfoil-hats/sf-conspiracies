California wildfires: Sites Fire in Colusa County now largest in state
-----
The Sites Fire in Colusa County had charred 19,195 acres by Thursday morning, becoming the largest wildfire in California as crews continued battling other blazes in Wine Country and Southern California.Ripping across tawny hills of dry brush and grass, the Sites Fire engulfed an area roughly stretching from East Park Reservoir campground in Lodoga to the unincorporated town of Leesville.
