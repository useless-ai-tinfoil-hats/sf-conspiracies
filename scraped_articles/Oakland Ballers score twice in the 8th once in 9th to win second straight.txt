Oakland Ballers score twice in the 8th, once in 9th to win second straight
-----
The Oakland Ballers, who scored in the first inning in franchise history on Tuesday night, had to wait until the eighth to do so Wednesday but still came away with a 3-2 victory over the host Glacier Range Riders in Kalispell, Mont.The Ballers (2-0), playing their inaugural season in the Pioneer League, took a 2-1 lead in the eighth on Dondrei Hubbard's bases-loaded walk and a RBI sacrifice fly from Jaylen Smith.
