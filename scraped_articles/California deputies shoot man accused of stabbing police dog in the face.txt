California deputies shoot man accused of stabbing police dog in the face
-----
San Joaquin County sheriff's deputies shot a man after he allegedly stabbed a police dog in the face Thursday morning.The man, whom sheriff's officials did not immediately name, was approached in his vehicle by deputies around 5 a.m. Thursday. The deputies deemed the vehicle, parked on the side of the road near Highway 99 and East Frontage Road in Manteca, to be suspicious, officials said in a statement.
The man in the vehicle had a warrant out for his arrest, sheriff's officials
