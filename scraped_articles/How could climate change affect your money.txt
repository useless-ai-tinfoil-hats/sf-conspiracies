How could climate change affect your money?
-----
A new report from the U.S. Department of the Treasury found that alongside risks to health and the physical environment, climate change imposes "substantial financial costs" on communities across the U.S.The total cost of weather and climate disasters from 2018 to 2022 exceeded $617 billion, with a price tag of more than $176 billion in 2022 alone.
