The Oakland legend behind six of Beyonce's 'Cowboy Carter' songs
-----
At Raphael Saadiq's homecoming show in Oakland last year alongside his Tony! Toni! Toné! bandmates, he ran through a medley of familiar tracks that he produced quietly behind the scenes, ranging from D'Angelo's "Untitled (How Does It Feel)" to Beyonce's "Cuff It." He pounded his chest, proudly saying "That's my s—t."It turns out the Grammy-winning Bay Area legend was foreshadowing his work on one of the biggest albums of 2024.
