Monterey Bay Aquarium to offer free admission to low-income families
-----
The Monterey Bay Aquarium has become part of a national initiative offering free admission to low-income families.Starting May 27, the world-renowned aquarium will provide free entry to anyone enrolled in the federal food assistance program, SNAP (known as CalFresh in California), for up to four individuals.
