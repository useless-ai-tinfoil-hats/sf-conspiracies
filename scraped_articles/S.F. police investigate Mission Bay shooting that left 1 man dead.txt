S.F. police investigate Mission Bay shooting that left 1 man dead
-----
San Francisco police said they were investigating a shooting that left one man dead and another injured on the 100 block of Berry Street in Mission Bay on Sunday.Police officials said Tuesday that officers responded to a reported shooting shortly after 12 a.m. Sunday where they found a man suffering from a gunshot wound.
