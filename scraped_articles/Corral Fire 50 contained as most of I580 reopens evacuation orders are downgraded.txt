Corral Fire 50% contained as most of I-580 reopens, evacuation orders are downgraded
-----
The wind-fueled Corral Fire near Tracy was 50% contained Sunday, another sign of progress as evacuation orders were downgraded and most lanes of Interstate 580 reopened.Updates: California Fire Map & Tracker
The fire, which grew to more than 14,000 acres, destroyed a home near the Tracy Golf and Country Club, Cal Fire Battalion Chief Josh Silveira said Sunday as crews continued to assess the extent of the wildfire's damage.
