Bay Area tech company worth $2.5B lays off about 185 workers to boost 'shareholder value'
-----
Five9, a Bay Area company that makes cloud software for call centers, is laying off 7% of its workforce.The tech firm announced the layoffs in a Tuesday filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission. The cut is likely to hit at least 185 workers given that the company had reported a head count of 2,684 at the end of 2023.
