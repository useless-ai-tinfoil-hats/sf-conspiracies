I-680 at San Ramon reopens after police detain armed man
-----
Police activity partially shut down Interstate 680 for less than an hour in San Ramon Saturday evening as authorities confronted and arrested an armed suspect, officials said.The highway was reopened by 7 p.m., the California Highway Patrol said.
