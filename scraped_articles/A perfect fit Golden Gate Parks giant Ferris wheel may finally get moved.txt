‘A perfect fit’: Golden Gate Park’s giant Ferris wheel may finally get moved
-----
The giant SkyStar Observation Wheel may be moving from Golden Gate Park to Fisherman's Wharf, where it would become a waterfront attraction, according to San Francisco officials.The plan is for the Ferris wheel to arrive in time for the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit in November, said Aaron Peskin, president of the San Francisco Board of Supervisors, who is pushing for the deal.
