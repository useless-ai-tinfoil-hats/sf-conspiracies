Review: 'The Watcher' blends folklore with horror movie tropes for a pedestrian attempt at scares
-----
Want to feel like an Irish folk monster? Try watching "The Watchers."The film's title creatures spend their nights observing four captive humans through the two-way mirror wall of a bunker in an enchanted Hibernian forest.
