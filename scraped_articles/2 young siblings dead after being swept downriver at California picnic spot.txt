2 young siblings dead after being swept downriver at California picnic spot
-----
Two young siblings died after being swept downstream in a river near the Thurman Flats Picnic Area in the San Bernardino National Forest just outside of Yucaipa, authorities said Tuesday.A mother took her 4-year-old daughter and 2-year-old son to the picnic area on Tuesday afternoon, according to a news release from the San Bernardino County Sheriff's Office. While there, the two children played near a "river with rapid water moving downstream," the release said.
