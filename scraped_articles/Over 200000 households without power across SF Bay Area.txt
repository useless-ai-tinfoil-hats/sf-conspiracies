Over 200,000 households without power across SF Bay Area
-----
A total of 241,267 people were without power in the San Francisco Bay Area as of 6:30 a.m. Monday after a powerful storm swept the region, according to PowerOutage.us, a website tracking outages. Across California, 533,752 customers were in the dark.The majority of the outages are PG&E customers. You can see the locations of the utility company's outages on its website.
