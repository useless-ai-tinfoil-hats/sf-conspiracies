Fremont police officer shoots, injures carjacking suspect
-----
A Fremont officer shot and wounded a carjacking suspect Sunday morning, police said.The carjacking was reported around 9 a.m. in the parking lot of a store on Osgood Road near Auto Mall Parkway, just west of Interstate 680. The thief was reportedly armed with a knife.
Officers encountered the carjacking suspect armed with a knife, walking on Auto Mall Parkway, police said.
