Metallica fans can now buy the ‘haunted’ home where ‘Kill ‘Em All’ was born
-----
A house standing on the site where Metallica recorded its debut album is now on the market for $3.4 million.Located in downtown Rochester, N.Y.'s East End Cultural District, the modern single-family home occupies the same space where the Bay Area hard rock band created "Kill 'Em All" in 1983 at Music America Studios.
