Bay Area warmup could bring first 80-degree day of the year to these cities
-----
The Northern California warming trend will continue Wednesday, as many inland cities approach or exceed 80 degrees.A high-pressure system will reach its peak strength over the California coast, meaning Wednesday will be the warmest day of the week in San Francisco. Highs in downtown San Francisco should reach the lower 70s, while the coast will be in the mid- to upper 60s.
