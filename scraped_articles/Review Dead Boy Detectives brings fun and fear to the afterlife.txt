Review: ‘Dead Boy Detectives’ brings fun and fear to the afterlife
-----
The protagonists of Netflix's "Dead Boy Detectives" are often disembodied. But unlike them, their show is one solidly put together bingeable series.A fine addition to the Buffy-Sabrina-Wednesday axis of teen supernatural dramedy, "Dead Boy" has the extra benefit of coming from Neil Gaiman, one of the world's most thoughtful and distinctive fantasy writers.
