Tech layoffs: Spotify to cut nearly one-fifth of global workforce
-----
Spotify is slashing its global workforce by nearly one-fifth in its latest cost-cutting measure.In a memo to staff on Monday, CEO Daniel Ek stated that the cuts would impact 17% of the company's more than 9,000 employees, translating to approximately 1,500 people.
