Warriors and Virginia alum Reece Beekman agree to terms on two-way deal
-----
The Golden State Warriors and Virginia guard turned undrafted free agent Reece Beekman have agreed on terms for a two-way contract, a league source confirmed Thursday to the Chronicle.Listed at 6-foot-3, Beekman, 22, averaged 14.3 points, 3.6 rebounds and 6.2 assists during his senior season of 2023-24. Additionally, Beekman was thrice an ACC All-Defensive honoree and the conference's Defensive Player of the Year as a junior and senior.
