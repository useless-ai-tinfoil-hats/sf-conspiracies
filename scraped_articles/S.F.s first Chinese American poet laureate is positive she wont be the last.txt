S.F.’s first Chinese American poet laureate is ‘positive’ she won’t be the last
-----
Following an acclaimed career, Genny Lim, 77, will become San Francisco's first Chinese American poet laureate during a Sept. 6 ceremony at the Asian Art Museum.Lim is the author of five poetry collections and co-editor of the American Book Award-winning "Island: Poetry and History of Chinese Immigrants on Angel Island, 1910-1940," a translated collection of poems that were found written on the immigration port's barrack walls by Chinese detainees.
