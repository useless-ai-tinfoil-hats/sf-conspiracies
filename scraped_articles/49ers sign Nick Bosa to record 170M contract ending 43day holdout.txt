49ers sign Nick Bosa to record $170M contract, ending 43-day holdout
-----
The sack master is signed. Finally.The longer-than-expected negotiations between Nick Bosa and the San Francisco 49ers ended Wednesday when the All-Pro pass rusher signed a contract extension that made him the NFL's highest-paid defensive player.
Bosa's 43-day holdout evidently achieved the desired result: The league's reigning defensive Player of the Year signed a five-year, $170 million deal that included $122.5 million guaranteed and a $50 million signing bonus, the Chronicle
