Rapidly growing boutique fitness chain to debut in two S.F. neighborhoods
-----
Solidcore, a fitness chain that was founded on the East Coast a decade ago, is making its first foray into the Bay Area, with two locations planned in San Francisco.According to records filed with the San Francisco Planning Department, Solidcore is seeking conditional use permits that would allow the company to open in the Castro-Upper Market and Marina neighborhoods, where formula retail — or chain stores — are permitted only with special authorization from the city.
