California lawmakers pass ‘YIGBY’ bill to let churches build affordable housing
-----
A state bill that could allow churches to build affordable housing on their parking lots and excess lands passed the California Legislature on Monday and heads to the governor's desk.If Gov. Gavin Newsom signs SB4 — introduced by State Sen. Scott Wiener, D-San Francisco — into law, it would allow for thousands of urban lots to potentially be used for housing construction.
