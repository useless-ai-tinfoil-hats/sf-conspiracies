He was a small-town newspaper editor who took on Goliath and won
-----
This is a requiem for Dave Mitchell, a small-town newspaper editor who won the biggest prize in journalism. Mitchell died in his sleep in the early hours of Oct. 26 in the cabin overlooking the West Marin countryside he shared with his wife, Lynn.He was 79 and had been fighting a losing battle with illness for some time.
