Man killed, woman arrested in East Bay hit-and-run crash
-----
A 36-year-old man died and a 42-year-old woman was arrested in a hit-and-run crash in Pittsburg early Saturday, police said.Officers responded to a report of an injured pedestrian on the 1200 block of Railroad Avenue and found the man suffering from major injuries, police said.
Despite first aid, the man died en route to a hospital, police said. His name was not released.
