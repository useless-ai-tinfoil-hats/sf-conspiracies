Food bank workers are unionizing in the Bay Area: ‘Some staff access our own pantry’
-----
Staff at the Alameda County Community Food Bank are unionizing their nonprofit workplace.Alameda County Community Food Bank United, which represents around 90 eligible full-time and part-time employees, announced it has organized under the Office and Professional Employees International Union (OPEIU) Local 29.
