California begins demolition of 173-foot dam as part of nation's largest removal project
-----
The historic dam-removal project on the Klamath River, along the remote California-Oregon border, is hitting another milestone this week as demolition of the largest of four targeted hydroelectric dams gets underway.Iron Gate Dam, a 173-foot dam in Siskiyou County, is scheduled to start being disassembled by work crews Wednesday, an endeavor that is expected to continue until September or October.
