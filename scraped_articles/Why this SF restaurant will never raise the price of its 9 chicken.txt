Why this SF restaurant will never raise the price of its $9 chicken
-----
For San Francisco restaurateur Cristina Wu Feng, growing up in Venezuela as the child of Chinese immigrants was not easy. She avoided bringing traditional Chinese foods like duck and pork to school and made sure to only speak Cantonese at home."For people like me, sharing those two cultures, carrying those two cultures on our shoulders, it's a lot," Wu Feng, 25, told SFGATE.
