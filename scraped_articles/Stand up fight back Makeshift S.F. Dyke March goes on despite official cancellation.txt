‘Stand up, fight back’: Makeshift S.F. Dyke March goes on despite official cancellation
-----
If you are a lesbian looking for love in the Bay Area, Dolores Park on the Saturday of San Francisco Pride Weekend is the place to be, with lesbians aplenty in sections of the park marked as Dykes Only for the day.Thousands of people sprawled over every inch of grass in the park Saturday, with Pride flags waving amid wafts of street meat and beer cans "sweating" under the early evening sun.
