Decades-old Bay Area Chinese restaurant remains open despite closure rumors
-----
Ly Luck, a popular 40-year-old restaurant in Oakland, remains open despite rumors that it permanently closed.Earlier this month, reports and rumors on Yelp swirled that the decades-old restaurant, located at 3537 Fruitvale Ave., was closed for good. But Ly Luck isn't going anywhere: The business is now under new ownership, but remains open daily, an employee confirmed to SFGATE on Thursday. It had been temporarily closed earlier this month while staff helped set up new tables and chairs
