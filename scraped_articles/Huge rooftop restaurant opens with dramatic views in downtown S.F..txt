Huge rooftop restaurant opens with dramatic views in downtown S.F.
-----
A new 400-seat rooftop restaurant, decorated with 20 palm trees, a sushi bar, DJ lounge and spectacular views of the city skyline, opens Saturday, Oct. 21, in San Francisco.It's the first West Coast restaurant from Chotto Matte, a global chain with locations in the United Kingdom, Miami and the Middle East.
