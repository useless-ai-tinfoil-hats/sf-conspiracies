CSU students across California locked out of classes as faculty go on strike
-----
Thousands of California State University students found themselves locked out of classes Monday as instructors, counselors, librarians and coaches walked off the job at campuses up and down the state on the first day of a planned weeklong strike.Dozens of striking faculty banged drums under a pounding rain Monday morning at Cal State East Bay in Hayward, chanting, "Whose university? Our university!"
