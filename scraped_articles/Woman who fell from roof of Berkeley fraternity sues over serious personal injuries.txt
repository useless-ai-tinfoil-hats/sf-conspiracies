Woman who fell from roof of Berkeley fraternity sues over ‘serious personal injuries’
-----
A young woman who survived a fall from a fraternity house roof near UC Berkeley last year is suing the Greek organization for being "negligent, careless and reckless."Jasmine Wang sustained "serious personal injuries" from the three-story fall, according to a lawsuit she filed against Delta Kappa Epsilon on Aug. 16 in Alameda County Superior Court.
