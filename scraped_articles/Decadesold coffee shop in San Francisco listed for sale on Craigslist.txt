Decades-old coffee shop in San Francisco listed for sale on Craigslist
-----
Muddy Waters, a 34-year-old fixture in San Francisco, is up for sale, as first reported by Mission Local.The decades-old coffee shop was listed earlier this month at $75,000 on a real estate website as well as on Craigslist by the same realtor. Hisham Massarweh, the cafe's co-owner, told SFGATE that he hopes an interested buyer continues to run his business as is, though he could not guarantee it.
