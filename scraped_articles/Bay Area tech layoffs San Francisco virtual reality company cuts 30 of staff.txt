Bay Area tech layoffs: San Francisco virtual reality company cuts 30% of staff
-----
VRChat, a San Francisco virtual reality gaming company, has announced plans to reduce its workforce by approximately 30%."This is the hardest change we've had to make at VRChat," Graham Gaylor, the company's CEO, said in a staff email on Wednesday. Gaylor added that he and co-founder Jesse Joudrey "take full responsibility for the decisions that brought us here."
