SF woman files lawsuit against Red Lobster alleging harassment, discrimination
-----
A former Red Lobster employee has filed a lawsuit against the national seafood restaurant chain for harassment, discrimination and retaliatory termination, according to a recent filing.An attorney for Nora Nunez filed a lawsuit against Red Lobster Hospitality, LLC, and 50 persons, which included her former general manager and director of operations, on Feb. 22 at the Superior Court of California in San Francisco County following Nunez's firing in July 2022.
