Photos: Historic Bay Area nudist resort listed for $32.8 million
-----
A historic nudist resort in the South Bay, one of California's oldest, is on the market for $32.8 million.The Lupin Lodge Naturist Club property, situated at 20600 Aldercroft Heights Road in the Santa Cruz Mountains foothills, covers 112 acres of redwood and oak forest just five miles from Los Gatos and 13 miles from San Jose.
