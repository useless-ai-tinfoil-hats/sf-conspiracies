Tech layoffs: S.F. video game developer cuts 265 jobs
-----
Unity, a San Francisco-based platform for creating video game software, plans to trim its workforce by 3.8%, equating to 265 jobs globally.The move is part of a comprehensive reset strategy, with the company also terminating its partnership with a digital video effects company founded by the director of "Lord of the Rings," according to a report from Reuters on Tuesday.
