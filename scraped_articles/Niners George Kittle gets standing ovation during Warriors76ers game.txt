Niners' George Kittle gets standing ovation during Warriors-76ers game
-----
Two days after helping the San Francisco 49ers complete their comeback victory over the Detroit Lions, Pro Bowl tight end George Kittle showed up at the Chase Center to watch the Golden State Warriors take on the Philadelphia 76ers.Kittle, wearing a Kevon Looney jersey, received a standing ovation from the crowd as he walked courtside, stopping to shake hands with Warriors owner Joe Lacob.
