Walgreens to close another store in downtown San Francisco
-----
Walgreens is closing another downtown San Francisco store next month, the company confirmed Wednesday.The Walgreens store at 275 Sacramento St. at the corner of Sacramento and Front Streets will close on Feb. 27, a company spokesperson told SFGATE via email.
