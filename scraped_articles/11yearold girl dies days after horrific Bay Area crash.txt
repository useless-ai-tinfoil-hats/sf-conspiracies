11-year-old girl dies days after 'horrific' Bay Area crash
-----
An 11-year-old girl died from her injuries following a major crash in Cupertino last week, officials confirmed to SFGATE.The crash happened on April 27 at approximately 6:14 p.m. at the intersection of Foothill and Stevens Creek boulevards, when a Mazda pickup truck collided with another car and hit a power pole, the Santa Clara County Sheriff's Office said in a news release. The collision also hit four children, ages 11, 8, 7 and 4, who were on the sidewalk.
