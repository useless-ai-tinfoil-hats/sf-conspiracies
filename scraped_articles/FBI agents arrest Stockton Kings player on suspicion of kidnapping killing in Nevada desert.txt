FBI agents arrest Stockton Kings player on suspicion of kidnapping, killing in Nevada desert
-----
FBI agents arrested a Stockton Kings player this week in connection with the kidnapping and killing of a woman whose remains were found in the Nevada desert, police announced Sunday.Chance Comanche, 27, was arrested Friday in Sacramento and is awaiting extradition to Nevada to face murder charges in the disappearance of 23-year-old Marayna Rodgers, the Las Vegas Metropolitan Police Department said in a statement.
