Two drivers arrested on suspicion of DUI in Bay Bridge crash that killed woman
-----
One person died and two people were arrested Sunday after an early morning crash on the San Francisco-Oakland Bay Bridge, according to California Highway Patrol.The pileup began after a Mercedes SUV came to a halt "for unknown reasons" while driving east near Treasure Island around 3:16 a.m., CHP spokesperson Mark Andrews said. The Mercedes was struck by a Honda sedan whose driver exited their car and was "standing in the lane beside the vehicles," Andrews said.
