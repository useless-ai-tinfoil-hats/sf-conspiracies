Cause of huge S.F. fire that destroyed 14 Hayes Valley condos could remain a mystery forever
-----
Nine months after flames consumed a building under construction in San Francisco's Hayes Valley, investigators have concluded they cannot determine whether the fast-moving fire was an accident or arson, according to an incident report reviewed by the Chronicle.The report was produced following a public records request.
