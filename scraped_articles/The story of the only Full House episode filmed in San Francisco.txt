The story of the only 'Full House' episode filmed in San Francisco
-----
Some 30 years ago, a golden retriever named Comet fell in love with a collie in San Francisco. They cuddled on a cable car and trotted up Coit Tower for some alone time. This doggie date drove the plot of the opener of the eighth and final season of "Full House," called "Comet's Excellent Adventure," which aired in September 1994.Up until then, the crew had filmed main title footage — but never an actual episode — in the show's San Francisco setting.
