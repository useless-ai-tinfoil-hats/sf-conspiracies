Richmond drive-by shooting kills 2, injures 1
-----
Two people were killed and another was badly wounded during an apparent drive-by shooting in Richmond, according to the city's Police Department.Officers responded to the 900 block of 23rd Street after a report of a shooting at 7:46 p.m. Friday. One of the victims died at the scene, according to Lt. Matt Stonebraker, the department's public information officer. A second victim died in a local hospital, Richmond police said Saturday.
