Man rescued from S.F. cliff by helicopter after calling for help with cell phone
-----
A man was rescued from a cliff by helicopter at Lands End Sunday evening after using a cell phone to call for help, according to a spokesperson for the San Francisco Fire Department.The adult male was rescued in a joint operation with a helicopter from the California Highway Patrol. The CHP helicopter was able to get a paramedic close enough to rescue the adult male, who was 40-50 feet below the cliff side.
