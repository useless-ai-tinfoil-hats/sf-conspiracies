Part of major East Bay highway to close over upcoming 3-day holiday weekend
-----
A section of Interstate 680 in the East Bay is set to close for more than three days during the Martin Luther King Jr. Day holiday weekend, sending southbound drivers to Interstate 880 instead.From 9 p.m. Jan. 12 to 4 a.m. Jan. 16, Caltrans will close the southbound lanes of I-680 between the Highway 580 interchange and Highway 84 for repaving.
