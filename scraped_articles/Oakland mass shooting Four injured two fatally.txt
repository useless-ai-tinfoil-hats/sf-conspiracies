Oakland mass shooting: Four injured, two fatally
-----
Four people were shot, two fatally, in East Oakland on Saturday morning, according to police.Police and firefighters responded to the shooting on the 1600 block of 83rd Avenue shortly after 9 a.m., Oakland police confirmed Saturday afternoon. The shooting killed two at the scene and wounded two others, who were transported to a hospital in critical condition.
The shooting arose after several people were in a verbal altercation that escalated when one person brought out a firearm
