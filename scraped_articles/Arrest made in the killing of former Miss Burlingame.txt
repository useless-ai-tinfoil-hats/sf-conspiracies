Arrest made in the killing of former 'Miss Burlingame'
-----
An arrest has been made in connection with the brutal bludgeoning death of a "one-time teen-age beauty princess," the Sonoma County Sheriff's Office announced this week.Noelle Russo, 37, was last seen on Friday, June 24, 1983. According to friends, she'd been fighting with a boyfriend and went to stay with a friend in Santa Rosa.
