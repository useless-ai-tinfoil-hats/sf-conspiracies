Salesforce CEO Benioff boosts Hawaii affordable housing with major land donation
-----
Salesforce CEO Marc Benioff and his wife, Lynne, have donated another 158 acres to the Hawaii Island Community Development Corp. to build affordable housing in Ouli in Waimea on the island of Hawaii.This adds to the 282 acres they donated in December, bringing their total contribution to 440 acres.
