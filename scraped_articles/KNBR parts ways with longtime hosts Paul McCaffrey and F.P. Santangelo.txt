KNBR parts ways with longtime hosts Paul McCaffrey and F.P. Santangelo
-----
UPDATE: KNBR unveils new Warriors announcer as part of sweeping overhaulTwo longtime hosts have been let go by KNBR Radio (104.5 FM and 680 AM). Morning-show co-host Paul McCaffrey and nighttime host F.P. Santangelo no longer have those jobs.
Santangelo tweeted about his ouster Wednesday afternoon: "I was informed today that due to budget cuts there will no longer be a 6-10pm show." Longtime executive Lee Hammer and a morning-show producer, Erik Engle, were let go as well.
