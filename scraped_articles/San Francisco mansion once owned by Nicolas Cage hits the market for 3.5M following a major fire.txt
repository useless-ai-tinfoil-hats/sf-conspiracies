San Francisco mansion once owned by Nicolas Cage hits the market for $3.5M following a major fire
-----
A Pacific Heights mansion previously owned by actor Nicolas Cage is back on the market with a major price cut that reflects damage from a 2020 fire.The five-bedroom, six-and-a-half-bathroom home at 1945 Franklin St. is now listed at $3.5 million, after selling for $5.5 million in 2019, according to Compass listing agent Helena Zaludova.
