Oakland just got 120 CHP officers to help tackle crime. Here's what we know.

California Gov. Gavin Newsom deployed 120 California Highway Patrol officers Tuesday to Oakland and the greater East Bay amid soaring crime in the area.CHP officers will work with local law enforcement to target instances of vehicle theft, retail theft and violent crimes, the Governor's Office announced Tuesday.
