This winemaking power couple just bought a remarkable California vineyard
-----
One of the marquee vineyards of California's Central Coast is changing hands. This time, the buyer isn't a corporation, as is typically the case with wine industry deals, but a young, independent winemaking couple.The Bassi Vineyard, located near the ocean in San Luis Obispo County, has been acquired by Mikey and Gina Giugni for an undisclosed price.
