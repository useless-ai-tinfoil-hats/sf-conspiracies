Costco semitruck overturned on I-580 in East Bay, four-vehicle crash
-----
LATEST June 14: 2 p.m. After a Costco truck overturned in a four-vehicle crash on Friday morning, the westbound lanes of Interstate 580 in Dublin won't fully open until at least 5 p.m., the California Highway Patrol said.
