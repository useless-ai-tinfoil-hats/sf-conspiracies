‘Simpsons’ episode skewers Elizabeth Holmes, Silicon Valley startup culture
-----
The Elizabeth Holmes drama has already spawned a best-selling book, an Emmy-winning miniseries and a documentary. It was time then, for the sitcom treatment.In an episode that aired Sunday night, "The Simpsons" took a star-studded romp through Silicon Valley tropes. The central character of the episode was a startup CEO who mimics the trajectory of the Theranos founder Holmes, wooing billionaire investors before ultimately getting outed as a fraud.
