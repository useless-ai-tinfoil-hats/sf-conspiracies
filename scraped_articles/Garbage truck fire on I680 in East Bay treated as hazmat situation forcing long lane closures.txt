Garbage truck fire on I-680 in East Bay treated as hazmat situation, forcing long lane closures
-----
Traffic came to a standstill on Interstate 680 in Contra Costa County on Tuesday morning after a garbage truck caught on fire, forcing ongoing lane closures, the California Highway Patrol said.Black smoke was visible after the garbage truck caught fire while on northbound Interstate 680 near the northbound Highway 242 split about 9:15 a.m. The truck was carrying a full load of trash, according to the CHP.
