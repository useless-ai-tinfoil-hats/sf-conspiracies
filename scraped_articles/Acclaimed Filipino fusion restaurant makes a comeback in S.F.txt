Acclaimed Filipino fusion restaurant makes a comeback in S.F
-----
A Filipino-inspired restaurant whose last iteration was featured on the Chronicle's top 100 restaurants list will return at a new location on Wednesday, April 17, opening at 214 Townsend St., right in the heart of SoMa's Filipino Cultural Heritage District.From 2017 until closing in 2020, Mestiza operated at 595 Bryant St., serving taqueria fare with Filipino flavors like pork adobo and green papaya achara with ginger. Now it's back with a new look and menu, leaning into staple dishes
