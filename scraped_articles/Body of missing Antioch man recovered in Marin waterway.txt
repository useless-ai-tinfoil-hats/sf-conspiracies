Body of missing Antioch man recovered in Marin waterway
-----
A body found in the Petaluma River on Saturday has been identified as Jeremy James Wyatt, 50, of Antioch, the Marin County Sheriff's Office announced Wednesday.Officials found the body floating near the border of Sonoma and Marin counties Saturday afternoon.
The identification concludes a multiday search for Wyatt, who was first reported missing by a manager at his workplace on Aug. 6.
