Teen killed at BART Powell Station identified
-----
The San Francisco medical examiner's office has identified the teen who was fatally shot at the BART Powell Street Station last week.The victim was identified as 17-year-old Jordan Malik Brady, an Alameda County resident. Brady was shot Thursday night shortly before 9:30 p.m., according to police. Officers were dispatched to Hallidie Plaza and found Brady, who was suffering from apparent gunshot wounds, police said.
Despite lifesaving efforts, he died at the scene.
