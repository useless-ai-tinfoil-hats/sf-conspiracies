49ers’ Christian McCaffrey brings family legacy of elite speed to Super Bowl
-----
LAS VEGAS — Peel away the layers of Christian McCaffrey's speed, one of the superpowers fueling his transcendent season for the San Francisco 49ers, and a fascinating picture emerges.His maternal grandfather, Dave Sime, won the silver medal in the 100-meter dash at the 1960 Olympics. His dad, Ed, was a sneaky-fast NFL wide receiver, and his mom, Lisa, was a swift soccer standout at Stanford.
