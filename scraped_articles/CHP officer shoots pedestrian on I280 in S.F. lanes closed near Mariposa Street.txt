CHP officer shoots pedestrian on I-280 in S.F., lanes closed near Mariposa Street
-----
A California Highway Patrol officer shot a pedestrian on southbound Interstate 280, snarling traffic during the Wednesday afternoon commute, according to CHP officials.San Francisco firefighters were dispatched to the freeway near Mariposa Street at 4:42 p.m. after a "shooting call," according to a spokesperson with the department. SFFD said one person was transported to San Francisco General Hospital.
The CHP did not immediately disclose details about what led up to the shooting.
