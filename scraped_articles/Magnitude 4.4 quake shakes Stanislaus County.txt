Magnitude 4.4 quake shakes Stanislaus County
-----
Two earthquakes with preliminary magnitudes of at least 4.0 occurred Monday night near Patterson, California, the United States Geological Survey reported.Tremors in the central California county began around 5:15 p.m., when the first quake struck with a preliminary magnitude of 4.0 centered near Diablo Grande, a small community near Interstate 5 and about 20 miles west of Modesto. The second occurred just before 9:15 p.m. near the same place and had a preliminary magnitude of 4.4.
