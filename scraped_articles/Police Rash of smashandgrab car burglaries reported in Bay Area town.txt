Police: Rash of smash-and-grab car burglaries reported in Bay Area town
-----
On Saturday night, a wave of smash-and-grab auto burglaries was reported in Petaluma, a town 40 miles to the north of San Francisco, officials said.Thieves targeted over 30 vehicles, parked across the town, including at the Outlet Mall, St. Vincent's High School, Capri Creek Apartments and the Sheraton Hotel, the Petaluma Police Department said in a statement posted in social media.
