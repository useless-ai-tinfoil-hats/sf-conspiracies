Giants’ Keaton Winn returns, remains on track to be No. 4 starter
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — Even before Keaton Winn got on the mound this spring, San Francisco Giants brass and coaches have made clear that the right-hander is expected to fill the fourth rotation spot come the first week of the regular season.The only snafu is that elbow soreness postponed Winn's Cactus League debut until Sunday, a week before the team leaves Arizona and less than two weeks before Opening Day on March 28.
