A’s blown out 10-0 by Dodgers in Jack Flaherty’s L.A. debut
-----
The Oakland Athletics were within striking distance of the Dodgers through seven innings Saturday, trailing by two for almost half of the game.Shaky fielding, an error and a wild pitch in the final two innings put that to rest as L.A. scored eight runs — including six in the ninth — to knock off the A's 10-0 at the Coliseum.
"We just didn't play great defense tonight," manager Mark Kotsay said.
The game was a tale of two defenses.
