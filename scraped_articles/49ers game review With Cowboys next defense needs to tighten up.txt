49ers game review: With Cowboys next, defense needs to tighten up
-----
The San Francisco 49ers had no takeaways and one sack Sunday, the first time they had failed to force a turnover and had fewer than two sacks since Sept. 26, 2021.The 49ers also allowed their first 99-yard touchdown drive since 2002. And they surrendered two TD drives of 85-plus yards after allowing two such possessions in their previous 12 regular-season games.
