Giants have had discussions with free agent slugger Jorge Soler
-----
Next up on the San Francisco Giants' list of potential free-agent signings: Jorge Soler.The Chronicle has learned that the team has had talks with the slugger, who hit 36 homers last year and who led the AL in homers with 48 in 2019.
