JD Vance to attend Bay Area fundraiser hosted by crypto bigwig on Monday
-----
Ohio Sen. J.D. Vance, former President Donald Trump's running mate in the 2024 presidential election, is scheduled to be in the Bay Area on Monday to attend a fundraiser hosted by Mike Belshe, the co-founder and CEO of Bitgo.Tickets to the Palo Alto event range from $3,300 to $50,000, according to a copy of an invitation posted on X. Along with dinner, those who pay top dollar will get to attend a roundtable discussion and have their photo taken with Vance.
