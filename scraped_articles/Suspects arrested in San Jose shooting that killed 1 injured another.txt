Suspects arrested in San Jose shooting that killed 1, injured another
-----
San Jose police said Thursday they arrested two men accused of shooting two other men, one fatally, in December.Joshua Sodapop Bocanegra, 20, of San Jose was arrested in San Francisco on Feb. 15. That same day, police also arrested Michael Carabajal, 20, of Gilroy, in Gilroy, police said. Both suspects were booked into Santa Clara County main jail on suspicion of homicide.
Authorities are still investigating the motives and circumstances of the shooting, which occurred on Dec. 17.
