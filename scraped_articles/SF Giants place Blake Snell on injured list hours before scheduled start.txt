SF Giants place Blake Snell on injured list hours before scheduled start
-----
The San Francisco Giants' top offseason signing Blake Snell is headed to the injured list just hours before his next scheduled start.The Giants announced that Snell was placed on the 15-day injured list with a left adductor strain, retroactive to April 23, at 9:45 a.m. on Wednesday, three hours before Snell was set to start against the New York Mets.
