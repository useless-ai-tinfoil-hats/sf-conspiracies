Five puppies rescued from recovered stolen car near Oakland Coliseum
-----
The pursuit of a stolen vehicle led BART police to a surprising recovery: five puppies.On Tuesday afternoon, BART police monitoring the Oakland Coliseum parking lot tried to stop a car that had been reported stolen in Fremont, the agency said in a statement. Instead, the driver — who officials identified as Courtney Taylor — drove off, according to BART police.
As the officers were pursuing Taylor, a dog jumped through an open car window, officials said.
