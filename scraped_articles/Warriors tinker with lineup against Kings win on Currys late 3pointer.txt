Warriors tinker with lineup against Kings, win on Curry’s late 3-pointer
-----
With starting center Kevon Looney falling ill after Wednesday's shootaround at Chase Center, Golden State Warriors head coach Steve Kerr was given an opportunity to tinker with different lineup combinations in the team's 116-115 defeat of the Sacramento Kings.Jonathan Kuminga impressed again with 18 points and six rebounds in his second start of the preseason.
