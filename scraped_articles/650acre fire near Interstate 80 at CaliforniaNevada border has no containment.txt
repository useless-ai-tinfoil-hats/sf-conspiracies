650-acre fire near Interstate 80 at California-Nevada border has no containment
-----
A 650-acre fire next to eastbound Interstate 80 near the California-Nevada state line had zero containment as of Monday evening, fire officials said. A red flag warning is going into place Monday afternoon in response to increasing winds.The Quilici Fire in Verdi, Nevada, has now been renamed the Gold Ranch Fire.
