BART service across much of San Francisco will be suspended this weekend
-----
BART service through San Francisco and direct access to San Francisco International Airport is expected to be curtailed Saturday and Sunday when the agency closes a busy section of rail tracks between its 24th Street/Mission and Daly City stations for maintenance.An alert from the transit agency said riders can expect delays of up to 30 minutes throughout the weekend. Due to the track closure, all BART trains from the East Bay on yellow, blue and red lines will turn back at the 24th
