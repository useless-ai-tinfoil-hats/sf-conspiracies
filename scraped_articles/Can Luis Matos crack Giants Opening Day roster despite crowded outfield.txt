Can Luis Matos crack Giants’ Opening Day roster despite crowded outfield?
-----
MESA, Ariz. — There is a world in which Luis Matos cracks the San Francisco Giants' Opening Day roster as a fifth outfielder, manager Bob Melvin acknowledged. But the chances of that are unlikely.Slim odds have little to do with Matos' performance this spring. The 22-year-old arrived in Scottsdale a few pounds heavier with muscle, carrying a heavier bat — an ounce more than the one he used last season — with a tweaked approach at the plate.
