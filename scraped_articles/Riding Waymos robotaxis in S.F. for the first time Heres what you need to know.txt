Riding Waymo’s robotaxis in S.F. for the first time? Here’s what you need to know
-----
Waymo launched the next phase of its robotaxi expansion in San Francisco on Tuesday, opening its previously restricted driverless ride-hailing service to anyone in the city.The development means the Alphabet-owned company's robotaxis are now just as accessible to the public as traditional ride-hailing companies Uber and Lyft, although with far fewer vehicles on the road.
