Four suspects arrested in $10,000 retail theft from Lululemon store at Bay Area mall
-----
Four suspects accused of stealing more than $10,000 of merchandise from a San Mateo Lululemon store have been arrested, San Bruno police said Friday.Just before 2 p.m. Thursday, a retail theft at the Lululemon store at San Mateo's Hillsdale Shopping Center was reported to officers from the San Bruno, Daly City and San Mateo police departments who are part of an organized retail theft task force. The four suspects fled the scene in a white Subaru Outback, police said.
