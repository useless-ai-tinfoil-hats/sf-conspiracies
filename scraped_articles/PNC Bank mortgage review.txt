PNC Bank mortgage review
-----
PNC Bank was the ninth-largest mortgage originator in 2022, according to federal data. It offers numerous types of mortgage loans, including conventional, government-insured (FHA, VA, and USDA), jumbo, and home equity lines of credit (HELOCs).
