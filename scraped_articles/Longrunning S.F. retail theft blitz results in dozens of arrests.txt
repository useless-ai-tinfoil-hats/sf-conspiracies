Long-running S.F. retail theft ‘blitz’ results in dozens of arrests
-----
In what law enforcement officials described as a "blitz" attack on retail theft, San Francisco police recently arrested 61 suspects as part of a targeted operation in some of the city's stores hardest hit by shoplifting, officials said Monday.The arrests, which occurred between May 13 and July 29, stemmed from alleged crimes at four businesses throughout the city. The locations are on the 3300 block of Mission Street, the 800 block of Market Street, the 5200 block of Diamond Heights
