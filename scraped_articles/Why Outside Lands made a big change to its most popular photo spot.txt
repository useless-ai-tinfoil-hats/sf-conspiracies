Why Outside Lands made a big change to its most popular photo spot
-----
Festivalgoers walking into Outside Lands at Golden Gate Park the past couple of years might have noticed a change: That iconic double-windmill wooden gateway near the entrance has been mysteriously absent.The original design at the festival paid homage to the two functioning Dutch windmills on the western corners of Golden Gate Park, a fitting tribute given the fact that the windmills helped create the park where Outside Lands is held.
