This S.F. burger has a devoted fan base. Now, it has its own restaurant
-----
One of San Francisco's most popular burgers is getting its own spinoff restaurant next month.Louie's Original opens Oct. 18 at 1685 Mariposa St. in San Francisco's Potrero Hill neighborhood. It's one of three restaurants from well-known operator Bacchus Management Group bound for a new luxury apartment complex.
