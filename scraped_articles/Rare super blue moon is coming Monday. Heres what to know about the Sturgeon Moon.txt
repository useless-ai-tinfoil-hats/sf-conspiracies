Rare super blue moon is coming Monday. Here’s what to know about the ‘Sturgeon Moon’
-----
Bay Area residents will get to see a rare super blue moon this week.While the super blue moon reached its peak late Monday morning at a time when it was not visible, Bay Area residents will have more opportunities to see it. The super blue moon will last until early Wednesday morning.
The event kicks off the first of four supermoons for the year. The term supermoon typically refers to a full moon that appears closer than usual to Earth's orbit, resulting in a moon that looks bigger
