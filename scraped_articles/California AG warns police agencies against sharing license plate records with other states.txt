California AG warns police agencies against sharing license plate records with other states
-----
Attorney General Rob Bonta is telling police agencies in California that state law prohibits them from sharing automated license plate records with officers in other states who want to use the information against abortion seekers, immigrants or anyone else.A law in effect since 2016 bars government agencies that record and store license plate information from forwarding it to "private entities or out-of-state or federal agencies, including out-of-state and federal law enforcement
