SF’s Hardly Strictly Bluegrass makes first lineup announcement with supergroup tribute
-----
Hardly Strictly Bluegrass, the quintessential San Francisco music festival, is fast approaching, and the event just released the first round of artists slated to perform from Oct. 4 to 6. The free festival takes place every fall in Golden Gate Park.Hardly Strictly was founded in 2001 by venture capitalist and banjo player Warren Hellman as a one-day local attraction.
