Review: S.F. Symphony welcomes a high-voltage conductor back after decades away
-----
The Finnish conductor Jukka-Pekka Saraste made his debut with the San Francisco Symphony in a 1997 guest appearance. Then he vanished from our lives for 27 years.That's an unusually long hiatus between visits, and I'm at a loss to explain it. Maybe someone said or did something to irritate him the last time he was in town. Maybe it was the other way round.
