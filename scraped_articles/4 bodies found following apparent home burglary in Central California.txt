4 bodies found following apparent home burglary in Central California
-----
Four people were pronounced dead in Fresno County following an apparent residential burglary, officials said.In a social media post, the Reedley Police Department said it found three people dead when responding to a report of a burglary Saturday at the 1100 block of South Church Avenue at about 9 a.m. Two of the people, who were residents of the house, were identified on Monday as 81-year-old Billy Bonds and 41-year-old Guadalupe Bonds.
