Multiple cars reported in flames in SF's Tenderloin on Sunday morning
-----
Multiple cars were engulfed in flames in at least three separate fires early Sunday morning in San Francisco, police said.The first fire occurred at approximately 5:13 a.m. in the Tenderloin, Allison Maxie, a spokesperson for the San Francisco Police Department, told SFGATE. When police arrived on the 400 block of Ellis Street, officers found an unoccupied car in flames. The San Francisco Fire Department subsequently extinguished the blaze, Maxie said.
