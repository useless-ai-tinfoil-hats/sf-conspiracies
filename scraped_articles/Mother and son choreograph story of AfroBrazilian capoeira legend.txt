Mother and son choreograph story of Afro-Brazilian capoeira legend
-----
SeQuoiia lives firmly within multiple sets of eight counts.As he slides across the faded wood floor of MoonSchool studio in San Francisco, counting beats to himself, every movement he makes is calculated. His influence is gentle as he shares details with the cast members he choreographs, but his guidance is authoritative.
Never far away stands his mother, Tania Santiago, ready to guide her son as his mentor and co-collaborator. Often, she provides barely audible instruction to him
