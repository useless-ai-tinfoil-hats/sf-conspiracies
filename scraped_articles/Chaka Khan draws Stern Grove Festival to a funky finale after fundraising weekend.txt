Chaka Khan draws Stern Grove Festival to a funky finale after fundraising weekend
-----
The 87th season of Stern Grove Festival closed out a summer of good vibes and great music with a weekend fundraiser culminating with a performance by the Grammy Award-winning "Queen of Funk," Chaka Khan.The Big Picnic Weekend, which for the first time was celebrated over two days, started with electronic duo Sylvan Esso and nu-disco band Poolside on Saturday, Aug. 24. The next afternoon, fans returned for Khan and her opening act, roots reggae ensemble Steel Pulse.
