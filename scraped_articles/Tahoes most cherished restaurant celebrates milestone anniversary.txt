Tahoe's most cherished restaurant celebrates milestone anniversary
-----
My first drink at a bar was at Lanza's in Kings Beach. I was 4 years old. In the summer months, when my family would visit Lake Tahoe, my grandpa would belly up and get whatever he was having and order "the same" for me. The same, as it turns out, was a Shirley Temple, but I wasn't in on the joke.
