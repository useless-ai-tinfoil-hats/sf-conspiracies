SF restaurant visited by Robin Williams has closed after 41 years
-----
After 41 years in Nob Hill, cozy French restaurant Rue Lepic quietly closed at the end of October, as first reported by Tablehopper.Rue Lepic is listed as permanently closed on both Google and Yelp, and a new alcoholic beverage license for its 900 Pine Street address spotted by the San Francisco Chronicle is currently pending approval (the Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms).
