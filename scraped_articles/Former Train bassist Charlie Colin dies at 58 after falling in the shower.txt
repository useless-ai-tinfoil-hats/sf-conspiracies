Former Train bassist Charlie Colin dies at 58 after falling in the shower
-----
Charlie Colin, the former bassist for the San Francisco rock band Train who contributed to some of the band's biggest hits, has died at the age of 58 after a fall in a shower.The incident occurred while Colin was house-sitting for friends in Brussels, Belgium, his mother told TMZ in an interview published on Wednesday, May 22. She said that authorities had yet to establish the exact date of his death as his friends found Colin upon returning from a trip.
