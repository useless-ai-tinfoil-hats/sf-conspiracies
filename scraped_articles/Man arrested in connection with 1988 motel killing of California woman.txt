Man arrested in connection with 1988 motel killing of California woman
-----
A man has been arrested in Georgia in connection with the 1988 killing of a 30-year-old woman in a motel room in Santa Maria on California's Central Coast.Former California resident Aloysius Winthrop James, 57, was brought into custody last Tuesday by an FBI task force and other agencies and charged with the murder of Ofelia Sandoval, authorities said. Sandoval's body was found on Sept. 18, 1988, in a room in the Town Center Motel on the 200 block of North Broadway in Santa Maria, police
