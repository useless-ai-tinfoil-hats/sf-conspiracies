Here's everything 49ers coach Kyle Shanahan said about Deebo Samuel's injury after the game

The 49ers escaped a stressful playoff opener against the Green Bay Packers Saturday, 24-21, without the services of star wideout Deebo Samuel for much of the game. Now the Niners advance to the NFC title game and all eyes turn to the status of Samuel, who remains a big question mark.Samuel picked up a shoulder injury in the first quarter and, after testing it out on the sidelines and even returning to the locker room before halftime, was ruled out just after halftime.
