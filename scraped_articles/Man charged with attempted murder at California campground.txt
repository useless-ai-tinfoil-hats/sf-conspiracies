Man charged with attempted murder at California campground
-----
A man has been arrested and charged with attempted murder after an argument over a "minor vehicle incident" resulted in gunfire at a California campground.On June 8, a sheriff's deputy heard a gunshot while patrolling Moccasin Campground, on the south shore of Lake Camanche in Calaveras County. As he searched the area, multiple 911 calls were made to county dispatch reporting a shooting at campsite 147, the Calaveras County Sheriff's Office said in a statement.
