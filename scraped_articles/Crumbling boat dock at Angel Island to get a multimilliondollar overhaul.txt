Crumbling boat dock at Angel Island to get a multimillion-dollar overhaul
-----
The crumbling boat dock at Angel Island State Park in San Francisco Bay will soon be replaced, thanks in part to a $1.5 million federal grant.The dock, in a cove that serves as the main entrance for visitors to the popular island, will be torn down and replaced by an expanded facility that is ADA-compliant and adds lighting and potable fresh water, officials said.
