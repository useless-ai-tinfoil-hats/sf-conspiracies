Former A’s manager Bob Melvin finds nostalgic break from Padres’ woes
-----
Bob Melvin found a momentary escape from the San Diego Padres' maddening season Friday: He got lost in East Bay sports nostalgia.Melvin returned to the Coliseum for the first time since he left the Oakland Athletics in 2021, after 10-plus years and 1,617 regular-season games as their manager. He had dinner with A's traveling secretary Mickey Morabito on Thursday night and breakfast with general manager David Forst on Friday morning.
