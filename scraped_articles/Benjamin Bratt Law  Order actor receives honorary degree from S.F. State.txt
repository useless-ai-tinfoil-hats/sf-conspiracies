Benjamin Bratt, ‘Law & Order’ actor, receives honorary degree from S.F. State
-----
San Francisco State University awarded an honorary degree to "Law & Order" actor Benjamin Bratt during its commencement ceremony this past week.Bratt, a San Francisco native who starred as detective Rey Curtis in the NBC crime procedural series, received an honorary doctorate in fine arts from the university at its 2024 commencement Friday. More than 4,000 students attended the ceremony, according to San Francisco State.
