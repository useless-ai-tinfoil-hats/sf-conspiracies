'It's concerning': Hundreds of dead sea lions wash ashore on California coast
-----
Hundreds of dead sea lion pups have washed ashore along the California coast, and researchers are scrambling to understand why.The phenomenon began about two weeks ago, Daniel Costa, a professor at UC Santa Cruz, told SFGATE over the phone.
"It's concerning," he said. "We've never seen anything like this."
