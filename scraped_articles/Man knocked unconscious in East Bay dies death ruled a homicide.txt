Man knocked unconscious in East Bay dies, death ruled a homicide
-----
The death of a 39-year-old man who died after being knocked unconscious in Antioch in January was ruled a homicide, police said Monday, citing the findings of the coroner's investigation.The man was assaulted around 9:45 p.m. on Jan. 7, on D Street just south of Antioch Middle School, police said, citing statements from witnesses and surveillance video.
