Tech billionaire's former Bay Area estate hits the market for $35M
-----
Just 27 miles east of San Francisco, the town of Alamo is home to many grand estates set upon rolling hills overlooking views of Diablo Valley. It is in this bucolic, wealthy enclave that tech billionaire David Duffield established Fieldhaven, a 21-acre estate that took years and over $135 million to build.
