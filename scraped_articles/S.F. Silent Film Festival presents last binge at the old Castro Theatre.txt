S.F. Silent Film Festival presents last binge at the old Castro Theatre
-----
Longtime Artistic Director Anita Monga doesn't know when her San Francisco Silent Film Festival will be back at the Castro Theatre, so she's treating the organization's traditional winter event, this weekend's "A Day of Silents," and a special presentation of Carl Theodor Dreyer's "Vampyr" in January, as something of a last binge."This is the last hurrah," Monga said of the Saturday, Dec. 2, event. "We pulled out all the stops. Every film could have been a centerpiece."
