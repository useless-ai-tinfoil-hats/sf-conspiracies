F1 star Lando Norris makes surprise California appearance
-----
Formula 1 star Lando Norris made a surprise stop in the San Francisco Bay Area Monday, delighting fans by signing autographs at the Google campus.Norris, a 23-year-old driver for McLaren, stopped by Google headquarters in Mountain View for a quick chat and meet-and-greet session. McLaren Racing CEO Zak Brown accompanied him, and their appearance was something of a business synergy move: Google is one of McLaren's main sponsors.
