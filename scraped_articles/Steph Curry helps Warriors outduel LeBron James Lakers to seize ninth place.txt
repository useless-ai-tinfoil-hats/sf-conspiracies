Steph Curry helps Warriors outduel LeBron James’ Lakers to seize ninth place
-----
LOS ANGELES – About that late-season surge that head coach Steve Kerr confidently hoped the Golden State Warriors could spark?It might've begun Saturday night – with the end of Stephen Curry's three-game absence.
Curry returned from a sprained right ankle to key the Warriors to a 128-121 victory over rival LeBron James and the Los Angeles Lakers at Crypto.com Arena. Curry provided 31 points while teammate Klay Thompson added 26 points, scoring 21 points in the first half.
