For someone averse to picking favorites, this one is surprisingly easy: I firmly choose Jimmy Nardello peppers as my favorite sweet pepper.They're an obvious pick. Unlike other sweet peppers, Nardellos, or "nardz" as I like to call them, have got flair. Despite being a pepper grown throughout the United States, you won't find them at Trader Joe's in a neatly packaged cling-wrapped container.
