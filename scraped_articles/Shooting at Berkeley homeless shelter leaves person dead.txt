Shooting at Berkeley homeless shelter leaves person dead
-----
One person died after a shooting at a Berkeley homeless shelter Sunday evening, according to the Berkeley Police Department.Just before 5:30 p.m., police responded to reports of a shooting inside Ursula Sherman Village, a shelter for homeless disabled adults on the 700 block of Harrison Street.
Officers found a shooting victim at the site and performed lifesaving efforts, but the victim succumbed to their injuries and was pronounced dead on the scene, police said.
