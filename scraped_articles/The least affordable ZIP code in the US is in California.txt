The least affordable ZIP code in the US is in California
-----
The small town of Ridgecrest, nestled between the southern tip of Sequoia National Forest and Death Valley, is the most affordable place to buy property in California, according to a new analysis.The city had a relatively small home price to household income ratio of 3.61, which home warranty company American Home Shield calculated using Zillow home prices and data from the U.S. Census Bureau.
