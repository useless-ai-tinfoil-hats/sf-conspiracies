A historic attraction will be torn down for Disney's biggest expansion
-----
Tom Sawyer Island, a classic attraction at Walt Disney World's Magic Kingdom with roots back to Walt Disney and Disneyland, is set to close. It'll be replaced with a new "Cars"-themed land, as part of the biggest expansion in the park's history.Last weekend at fan convention D23, Disney announced the rides and attractions expected to debut in parks across the globe in the coming years. Of note, this included new attractions coming to Avengers Campus at Disney California Adventure Park
