City Hall corruption: Former top Recology executive faces prison time for bribes to Nuru
-----
A former top Recology executive faces prison time of up to two years as he appears for sentencing in federal court Thursday, following his guilty plea to paying bribes to disgraced former San Francisco Public Works Director Mohammed Nuru.John Francis Porter, 40, the former vice president and general manager of SF Recology Group, which has contracted with the city to provide trash services for decades, pleaded guilty in May to paying more than $55,000 in bribes to Nuru.
