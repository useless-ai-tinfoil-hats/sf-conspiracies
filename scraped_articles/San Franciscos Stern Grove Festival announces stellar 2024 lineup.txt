San Francisco's Stern Grove Festival announces stellar 2024 lineup
-----
It wouldn't be summer in San Francisco without Stern Grove Festival, and this year's lineup for the beloved free concert series is one of the best in recent memory.Founded in 1938, the festival takes place amid the eucalyptus trees of the Sigmund Stern Recreation Grove, a 33-acre natural space in the Parkside neighborhood that's home to one of the city's three natural lakes.
