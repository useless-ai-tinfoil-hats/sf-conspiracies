Sprouts Farmers Market to pay $265K to settle federal sexual harassment charge in LA
-----
National grocery chain Sprouts Farmers Market has settled a federal sexual harassment and retaliation charge filed with the U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission. The company will provide $265,000 to a group of Sprouts employees in the San Fernando Valley area of Los Angeles, the EEOC announced Friday.After the group of employees complained about sexual harassment at Sprouts, the company retaliated against them, according to the EEOC.
