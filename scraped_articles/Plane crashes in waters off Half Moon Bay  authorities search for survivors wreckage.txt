San Mateo County Sheriff's Office and several other agencies were searching the waters near Half Moon Bay Monday morning for survivors or signs of a small plane that crashed Sunday evening, officials said.Witnesses observed a plane "flying erratically" around 7:13 p.m. but shortly afterwards, could no longer locate the plane. The Sheriff's Office was dispatched to search the area shortly after, officials said.
The plane had taken off from the East Bay, authorities told KTVU.
