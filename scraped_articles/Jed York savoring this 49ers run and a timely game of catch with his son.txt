Jed York savoring this 49ers run, and a timely game of catch with his son
-----
Jed York has never been known for handling defeats gracefully. In the decade-and-a-half that he has been the San Francisco 49ers' operating owner, York has displayed his frustration with losing in conspicuous ways, from venting on social media to getting rid of three coaches in a two-year period.On Sunday, however, with the top-seeded 49ers on the wrong end of a 24-7 halftime score in the NFC Championship Game, York defied his stereotype in a glaring and heartwarming manner.
