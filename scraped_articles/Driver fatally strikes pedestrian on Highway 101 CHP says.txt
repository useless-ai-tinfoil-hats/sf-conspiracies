Driver fatally strikes pedestrian on Highway 101, CHP says
-----
A driver fatally struck a pedestrian on northbound Highway 101 in San Jose early Thursday morning, the California Highway Patrol told SFGATE.CHP received a call about the crash at 3:40 a.m., rushed to the scene on the highway near Oakland Avenue and found an unresponsive man who appeared to have been hit by a car, CHP spokesperson Ross Lee told SFGATE. The man was declared deceased at the scene, Lee said.
