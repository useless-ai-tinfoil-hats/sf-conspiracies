Fernando Mendoza roughed up as Cal tops Stanford in last Pac-12 Big Game
-----
Much of the lure of the Big Game rests on its history. Cal made history at Stanford Stadium on Saturday.For a third straight year, Cal fans stormed the field after the Bears had prevailed. The Bears' 27-15 victory in the last Pac-12 Big Game before the schools move to the ACC next year gave them three in a row over Stanford for the first time since a five-year run (2002-06).
