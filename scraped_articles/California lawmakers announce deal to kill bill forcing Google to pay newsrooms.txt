California lawmakers announce deal to kill bill forcing Google to pay newsrooms
-----
Google has succeeded in killing a bill that would have forced it and other tech companies to pay news companies for their content.Lawmakers announced Wednesday that they had reached a deal that will mean shelving the bill in favor of creating a new partnership to support California newsrooms.
