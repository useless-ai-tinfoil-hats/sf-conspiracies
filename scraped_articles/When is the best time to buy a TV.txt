When is the best time to buy a TV?
-----
As someone who spends a bit too much time watching TV, movies and sports, I understand the want — the need — for a nice TV. Even though TV prices have reportedly fallen in recent years, the biggest and best models will still set you back a pretty penny. But, there are certain times of the year when you can score great deals on top brands.Black Friday and Cyber Monday are widely known as the best days to find a bargain on hot-ticket TVs.
