Feds delay FAFSA application for a second year, throwing college financial aid timing into doubt
-----
In a new blow to college applicants seeking federal grant money to offset tuition, the U.S. Department of Education will delay its release of the FAFSA — the Free Application for Federal Student Aid — by two months, until Dec. 1, for the second year.A delay creates a domino effect that can cause students to receive a financial award letter after the deadline for accepting a college's offer of admission — if they have been able to submit a FAFSA application at all.
