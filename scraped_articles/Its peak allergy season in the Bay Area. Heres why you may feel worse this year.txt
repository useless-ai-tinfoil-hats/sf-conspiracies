It’s peak allergy season in the Bay Area. Here’s why you may feel worse this year
-----
If you've been sneezing, sniffling, coughing or feeling fatigued from allergies lately, you're not alone.The Bay Area is in the midst of what has traditionally been considered peak allergy season, March and April. But climate change is leading to higher pollen counts earlier in the year, and prolonging those periods of high pollen, according to allergists and recent research — offering one potential explanation for why seasonal allergy sufferers may feel crummier than usual.
