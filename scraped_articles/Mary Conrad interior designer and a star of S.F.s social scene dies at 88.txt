Mary Conrad, interior designer and a ‘star’ of S.F.’s social scene, dies at 88
-----
The doorbell would ring at the big Victorian in Pacific Heights, and Mary Conrad would find Lucille Ball or Bing Crosby or Charlton Heston or Barbra Streisand waiting to come in.Chronicle columnist Herb Caen had a standing invitation at Conrad's home, having introduced her to her husband, Barnaby Conrad Jr., bullfighter, bestselling author and North Beach nightclub owner.
