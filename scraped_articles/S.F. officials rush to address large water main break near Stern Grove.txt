S.F. officials rush to address large water main break near Stern Grove
-----
Crews from the San Francisco Fire Department and the San Francisco Public Utilities Commission were on the scene of a large water main break on Wawona Street near Stern Grove Friday morning, officials said.The cause of the apparent break was not immediately clear. Fire crews were assessing risks to structures and residents and encouraged people to stay away from the area. PUC crews were working to repair the main break.
