Teen arrested on suspicion of South Bay armed carjacking
-----
San Jose police arrested a 16-year-old on suspicion of armed carjacking early Wednesday morning.Around 1:30 a.m., police responded to a report of armed carjacking in south San Jose, they said through social media.
During their investigation, officers located the teen, who had a "loaded stolen Glock pistol with extended magazine, concealed on his person," police said.
