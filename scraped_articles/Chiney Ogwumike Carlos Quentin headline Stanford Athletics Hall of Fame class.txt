Chiney Ogwumike, Carlos Quentin headline Stanford Athletics Hall of Fame class
-----
Basketball player Chiney Ogwumike and baseball player Carlos Quentin headline the 10-person Stanford Athletics Hall of Fame Class of 2024, as announced by the school on Tuesday.Ogwumike, a forward/center who played from 2010 through '14, ranks first in women's program history in career points (2,737), field-goal percentage (58.9) and rebounds (1,567).
