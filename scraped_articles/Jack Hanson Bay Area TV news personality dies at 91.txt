Jack Hanson, Bay Area TV news personality, dies at 91
-----
Jack Hanson, a longtime Bay Area reporter and TV personality, died at 91 after a long illness, ABC7 reported on Monday.Over the course of Hanson's lengthy career in local media, his warm curiosity, dry wit and affable charm found him work as a weatherman, talk show host, interviewer and reporter.
