New California water rule allows sewage to be recycled into tap water
-----
California water regulators on Tuesday approved rules, long in the making, that will allow local water agencies to recycle wastewater directly into tap water after extra cleaning.The unanimous decision by the State Water Resources Control Board will open a new option for water supplies across the drought-prone state.
"This is so critical. It's long overdue," said Nichole Morgan, a board member.
