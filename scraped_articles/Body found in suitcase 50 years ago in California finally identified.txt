Body found in suitcase 50 years ago in California finally identified
-----
A mystery surrounding a gruesome discovery made by two fishermen 50 years ago in Southern California has been partially solved.On June 13, 1973, a woman's dismembered body was found in an orange suitcase and several plastic bags in San Diego Bay. The fishermen initially saw a leg floating in the water before finding the case and green garbage bags washed up on the shore between Laurel Street and the US Coast Guard Station.
