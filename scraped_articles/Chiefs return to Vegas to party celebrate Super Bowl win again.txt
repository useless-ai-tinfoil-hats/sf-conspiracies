Chiefs return to Vegas to party, celebrate Super Bowl win again
-----
The party still hasn't ended for the Kansas City Chiefs.The Super Bowl champs are back in Las Vegas this weekend to celebrate — again — their victory over the San Francisco 49ers on Feb. 11. Immediately after their Super Bowl win, the Chiefs partied at Zouk nightclub in Resorts World. Afterward, quarterback Patrick Mahomes headlined a parade at Disneyland, and the entire team reunited in Kansas City for a parade.
