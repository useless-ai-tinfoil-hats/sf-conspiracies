Mike Fanelli, Bay Area distance runner, coach and storyteller of track, dies at 67
-----
The morning after Mike Fanelli was diagnosed with a glioblastoma, a deadly form of brain tumor, he went out at sunrise, as always, and did his daily run of 4 miles on the country roads of Sonoma County.He had a goal to meet, and on that day, Oct. 2, 2023, one year into a brutal treatment that often left him nauseous and weak from chemo, Fanelli met it.
