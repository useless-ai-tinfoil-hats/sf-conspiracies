Bay Area home built by student of famed SF architect on sale for $1.5M
-----
In 1962, Reece Clark, a student of legendary San Francisco architect Bernard Maybeck, designed 148 Stonewall Road in Berkeley for himself. Decades later, updated but still true to its original midcentury design, the property is on the market, sporting a unique staging strategy and a $1.45 million price tag.In creating 148 Stonewall Road, Clark was "drawing inspiration from the rich heritage of early Bay Area architecture," according to the official listing.
