Bay Area’s smallest airport adding new direct flights and destinations
-----
The Bay Area's smallest commercial airport, Charles M. Schulz-Sonoma County Airport in the North Bay, is taking off — adding new destinations and direct flights and becoming a base for upstart Avelo Airlines.The airport, located in northern Santa Rosa, is on a growth spurt. This week, Avelo, a low-cost carrier headquartered in Houston, added four flights, doubling the number of departures from Sonoma County.
