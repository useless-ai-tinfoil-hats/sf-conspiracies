Human skeletal remains discovered by dog walker in California
-----
A man walking his dog on a dirt road surrounded by open desert in Twentynine Palms, Calif., discovered human skeletal remains, authorities said.The Morongo Basin Sheriff's Station was dispatched to the area of Canyon Road and Two Mile Road after the man reported the sighting at approximately 9 a.m. on Nov. 4. They searched the area and said no additional remains were found; the coroner's office also responded and confirmed the presence of human bones that "appeared to be historical."
