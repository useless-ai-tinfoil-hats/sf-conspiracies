Huge ranch near Tahoe owned by late casino mogul hits market for $15M
-----
A sprawling Tahoe-area cattle ranch known for its rich Western history and illustrious former owners has hit the market for $15 million.Jacks Valley Ranch, a 1,230-acre property nestled against the Sierra Nevada 40 miles south of Reno, dates back to 1860 and was purchased by casino tycoon John Ascuaga in 1969. Ascuaga is known for turning the Nugget Casino in Sparks from a small coffee shop into the giant resort it is today.
