British fashion retailer to close store at San Francisco mall following bankruptcy
-----
Ted Baker, the British high-street clothing retailer whose executives raised concerns about safety at the troubled San Francisco Centre, is the latest casualty at the city's largest mall.The company declared bankruptcy last month, signaling the closure of all its stores across North America.
