Berkeley rally protests wall of shipping containers closing People's Park
-----
Protesters gathered on Telegraph Avenue in Berkeley on Wednesday morning rallying against the closure of People's Park just hours before.Starting around 11 a.m., SFGATE saw about 50 people face off with a line of barricades, and a group of police officers blocking access to Haste Street and the park just beyond. Led by speakers with bullhorns, the protesters chanted slogans like, "Whose park? People's Park!" and, "Hey, hey, ho, ho! These racist cops have got to go!"
