A beloved 67-year-old Calif. general store might close permanently
-----
A NorCal market, known for pulled pork sandwiches with a homemade barbecue sauce, bowls of rich, steamy ramen, pizza puff pastries and more, is potentially closing for good. The owners announced the "very difficult decision" on Facebook."We have come to the very difficult decision to close the Market after this Sunday 12/10 for an extended period of time while we reset and possibly restructure," read the Facebook post.
