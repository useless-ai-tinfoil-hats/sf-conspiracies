2 rounds of rain in Bay Area forecast, one more significant than the other
-----
The southern edge of a low-pressure system focused over British Columbia and the Pacific Northwest is expected to bring a chance of light rain to the San Francisco Bay Area from Friday night into Saturday. Another chance of rain, which is likely to be even lighter, arrives Monday, according to the National Weather Service.Clouds were pushing into the Bay Area on Friday, with a weak cold front approaching the region.
