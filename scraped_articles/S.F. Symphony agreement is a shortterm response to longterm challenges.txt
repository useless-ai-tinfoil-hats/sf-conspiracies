S.F. Symphony agreement is a short-term response to long-term challenges
-----
The musicians, board and management of the San Francisco Symphony have bought themselves a little time with their recent agreement on a short-term labor contract that was ratified Sunday, Oct. 1.But like the 45-day budget bill passed by the U.S. Congress the day before, this new contract seems likely to serve as little more than a stopgap designed to tide both parties over toward a more decisive reckoning.
