California lawmakers lower deficit by $17B through cuts and ‘mother of all gimmicks’
-----
California lawmakers on Thursday approved a series of spending cuts and internal borrowing tricks designed to reduce the state budget deficit by about $17 billion. And it won't be enough.Lawmakers know they must carve deeper before the June deadline to balance the 2024-25 budget. In January, Gov. Gavin Newsom estimated the shortfall would be about $38 billion.
