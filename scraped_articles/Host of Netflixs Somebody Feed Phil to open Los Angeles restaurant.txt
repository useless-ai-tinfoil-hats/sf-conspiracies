Host of Netflix's 'Somebody Feed Phil' to open Los Angeles restaurant
-----
Television star Phil Rosenthal is taking his love of restaurants off the screen and into reality. The Los Angeles-based host of the ongoing food and travel series "Somebody Feed Phil" on Netflix is planning to open his first restaurant this summer in the Larchmont neighborhood, an upscale, walkable area right in the heart of the city.According to Eater LA, Rosenthal is partnering with a big-name California chef to open a diner.
