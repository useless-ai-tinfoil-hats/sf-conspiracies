Review: Mark Morris’ powerful world premiere captures Jesus’ final hours in dance
-----
In an early segment of choreographer Mark Morris' serenely shattering new stage work "Via Dolorosa," a dancer stands stiffly erect, extending his arms straight out to become a human crucifix. He topples back, still fully rigid, onto the shoulders of the two dancers who are there to support him.Then, with a transformative rush, his arms soften and his hands enfold the heads of both his partners in a gesture of immense solace. It's a heartbreaking moment, at once bleak and tender.
