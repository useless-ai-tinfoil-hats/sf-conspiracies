In 2023 Bay Area holiday theater, buy local
-----
2023 is the year to go indie with your holiday theatergoing.One reason is the sheer number of venue closures, emergency fundraising campaigns and even a company "hibernation" of late. I love big-budget musicals as much as the next fangirl, but plug-and-play out-of-town imports aren't what make Bay Area theater special.
