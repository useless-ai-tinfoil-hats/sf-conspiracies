Oakland police release photos of person of interest in 2023 slaying
-----
The Oakland Police Department on Monday released photos of a person of interest in a fatal shooting and asked the public to help identify the person.The shooting occurred in the area of Seventh Avenue and International Boulevard about 5:15 a.m. on July 23, 2023.
Officers found the victim suffering from at least one gunshot wound. He died at the scene, police said in a statement.
