Man arrested on suspicion of shooting Half Moon Bay farmworker
-----
Police on Sunday arrested an 18-year-old suspected of shooting and injuring a man at a farmworker housing development in Half Moon Bay, according to the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office.Officers responded just after 7 p.m. Saturday to a report of a shooting around Tea Road and Pear Orchard Way, according to Heather Enders, a spokesperson for the sheriff's office. Detectives said the shooting appears to have been an isolated incident, Enders said.
