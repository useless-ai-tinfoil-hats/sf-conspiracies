Lila Downs: Día de los Muertos
-----
With "a stunning voice, a confident multicultural vision grounded in her Mixtec Indian roots" (LA Times), GRAMMY and Latin GRAMMY-winning vocalist Lila Downs is one of the most celebrated singers of her generation. She returns Sunday, October 15th, for a special night of music at the historic Paramount Theater in Oakland, celebrating Día de los Muertos, Mexico's Day of the Dead, featuring Folklorico dancing, a mariachi band, stunning thematically-inspired visual projections, and more.
