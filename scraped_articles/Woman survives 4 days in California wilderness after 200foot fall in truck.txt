Woman survives 4 days in California wilderness after 200-foot fall in truck
-----
A woman survived four days trapped inside her pickup truck in the California wilderness after driving off a road to avoid hitting a deer, officials said.The woman was driving on Mount Baldy Road, a two-lane highway that winds up the mountain towering over Los Angeles, in a Ford Ranger on Jan. 3 when she swerved and fell some 200 feet down the mountainside, Matt Brossard, engine captain with the Angeles National Forest, told SFGATE.
