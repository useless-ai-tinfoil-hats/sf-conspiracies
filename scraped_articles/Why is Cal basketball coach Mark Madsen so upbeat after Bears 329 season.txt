Why is Cal basketball coach Mark Madsen so upbeat after Bears’ 3-29 season?
-----
LAS VEGAS — Mark Madsen believes in his group at Cal. And he really means it.Beyond his optimism, the Bears' head basketball coach sees several factors as leading to a big change in Berkeley, among them: the pace at which the Bears are going to play, new personnel on campus via the transfer portal, and his desire to coach Cal out of irrelevance following the worst season in program history.
