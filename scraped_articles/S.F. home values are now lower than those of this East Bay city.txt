S.F. home values are now lower than those of this East Bay city
-----
After several months of decline, home values in much of the Bay Area are on the rise again. But the picture is different in San Francisco.San Francisco home values have remained mostly flat in recent months, with the typical property valued at roughly $1.26 million as of September, according to data from real estate listing site Zillow. That's the same as the city's typical home value in March, six months earlier.
