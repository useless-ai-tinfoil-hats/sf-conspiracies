Historic Bay Area dive bar closing after lawsuit with owner of SF bar Kozy Kar
-----
Historic Santa Rosa dive bar the Dirty is closing following litigation with its landlord, who also owns raunchy 1970s-themed San Francisco bar Kozy Kar."The Dirty is DEAD," read a post on the bar's Facebook page on Feb. 6. Following a liquidation sale this weekend, the Dirty plans to close on March 1.
