Pete Kozachik, legendary Bay Area cinematographer behind ‘The Nightmare Before Christmas,’ dies at 72
-----
Pete Kozachik, the pioneering visual effects artist and cinematographer who brought his unique style to stop-motion animation classics including "The Nightmare Before Christmas," "Corpse Bride," "James and the Giant Peach," "Coraline" and more, died Tuesday, Sept. 12, at his Bay Area home due to complications related to primary progressive aphasia.
