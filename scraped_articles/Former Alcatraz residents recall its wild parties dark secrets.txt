Former Alcatraz residents recall its wild parties, dark secrets
-----
Jolene Babyak remembers her time living on Alcatraz in the mid-1900s as "really fun." That may sound discordant to anyone familiar with the island's history as a holding cell for notorious criminals like Al Capone and Alvin "Creepy" Karpis or with the prison's bloody confrontations and against-all-odds escapes, but for her, it was home."It was a really wonderful place to grow up and to live," Babyak wistfully recalled to SFGATE during a tour of the island this fall.
