Resort-Inspired Spanish Revival in Palo Alto
-----
(ad) When building 6 Tevis Pl. in Palo Alto, the owners turned to the casual elegance of Santa Barbara's Biltmore Hotel for inspiration. They hired architect John Northway, resulting in a five-bedroom Spanish Revival estate defined by its blend of formal and relaxed spaces. Both the home and the Four Seasons resort boast grand arches, private balconies and lush landscaping. An abundance of French doors, retractable glass walls and clerestory windows welcome sunlight into the home and connect it
