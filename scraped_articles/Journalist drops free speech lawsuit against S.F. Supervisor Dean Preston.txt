Journalist drops free speech lawsuit against S.F. Supervisor Dean Preston
-----
A four-year legal battle between San Francisco Supervisor Dean Preston and conservative journalist Susan Dyer Reynolds ended Friday when Reynolds dropped a lawsuit that accused Preston of violating her free-speech rights by blocking her on Twitter, now known as X.In return, the city agreed not to seek attorneys' fees from Reynolds.
