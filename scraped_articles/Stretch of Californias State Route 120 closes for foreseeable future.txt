Stretch of California's State Route 120 closes for foreseeable future
-----
A stretch of California's State Route 120 near Yosemite National Park has officially closed for the winter season.On Tuesday, Caltrans and Yosemite National Park announced the seasonal closures have arrived; each year, due to snowy conditions that are too dangerous for motorists, parts of Yosemite close for months. Tioga Pass Road is now closed from Tuolumne Grove, east of Crane Flat, to the Tioga Pass Entrance Station. The winter closures usually last until late May or early June.
