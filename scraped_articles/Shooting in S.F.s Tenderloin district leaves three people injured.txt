Shooting in S.F.'s Tenderloin district leaves three people injured
-----
Three people were shot in San Francisco's Tenderloin district on Thursday afternoon, according to the San Francisco Police Department.Gunshots rang out after one vehicle pulled up next to another parked vehicle on Leavenworth Street around 12:40 p.m., officer Gonee Sepulveda shared in an email.
Police arrived at the intersection of Golden Gate Avenue and Leavenworth Street after the gunshots were heard, but both of the involved vehicles had fled.
