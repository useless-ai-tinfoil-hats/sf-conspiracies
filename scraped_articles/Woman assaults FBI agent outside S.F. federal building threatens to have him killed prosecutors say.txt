Woman assaults FBI agent outside S.F. federal building, threatens to have him killed, prosecutors say
-----
Prosecutors say a woman assaulted an FBI agent outside the federal courthouse in San Francisco and threatened to have him killed by drug dealers, 10 weeks after she was convicted of assaulting another government worker and was ordered by a judge to stay away from the court building.Erika Melendez was indicted by a federal grand jury Tuesday on a felony charge of assaulting the unidentified agent on March 1. She has been held in custody since then, and prosecutors want her kept in jail
