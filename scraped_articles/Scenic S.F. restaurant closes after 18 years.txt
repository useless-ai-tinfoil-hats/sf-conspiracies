Scenic S.F. restaurant closes after 18 years
-----
One of San Francisco's most scenic restaurants has closed for good.Presidio Social Club owner Ray Tang told the Chronicle the restaurant served its last diners on May 12, Mother's Day.
The restaurant opened in 2006, built inside a structure that was originally military barracks in the Presidio. Since opening, Tang estimates it has served 2 million customers.
