Woman fatally struck by driver of suspected stolen vehicle, Oakland police say
-----
A car crash killed a 23-year-old woman and severely injured a man who appeared to be fleeing in a stolen vehicle from a separate collision in West Oakland, police said.The collision happened Wednesday about 10:45 a.m. at 16th Street and Mandela Parkway, the Oakland Police Department said in a statement. A man in a Ford Fusion was driving at a high speed when he failed to stop at two stop signs and collided with a Honda Accord, witnesses told investigators. The two vehicles then crashed
