Giants bring back Curt Casali to shore up injury-thinned catching corps
-----
The San Francisco Giants are bringing back old friend Curt Casali to supplement a depleted catching corps.Casali, part of the 2021 Giants team that won a franchise-best 107 games, signed a one-year contract on Wednesday prior to the Giants' series finale against the Los Angeles Dodgers.
