UC Berkeley student found dead at Clark Kerr dorm
-----
A UC Berkeley student died at Clark Kerr student housing Tuesday afternoon, the university said.The Berkeley Fire Department was dispatched to the residential hall complex located six blocks from the main campus at 4:23 p.m. on Feb. 13, according to campus spokesperson Janet Gilmore. At the scene, firefighters "administered life-saving measures," but these failed and the individual was declared dead, Gilmore wrote in an email. UC Berkeley police officers also rushed to the scene.
