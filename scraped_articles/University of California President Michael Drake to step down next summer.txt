University of California President Michael Drake to step down next summer
-----
University of California President Michael Drake announced Wednesday that he will step down next summer after five years at UC's helm.Drake, UC's first Black president and the first eye doctor to hold the top job, has led the university system — with 10 campuses, 280,000 students, six medical centers and three national laboratories — since July 2020.
