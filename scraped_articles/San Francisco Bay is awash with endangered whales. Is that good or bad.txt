San Francisco Bay is awash with endangered whales. Is that good or bad?
-----
This summer, large numbers of humpback whales have been diving and lunge-feeding near shore as they follow massive schools of anchovies that have been swarming the bay and coastal waters.The problem is, they particularly like to feed right under the Golden Gate Bridge, where there is a lot of fish activity and also lots of shipping traffic, said Michael Pierson, a naturalist who leads whale watching tours most days of the week.
