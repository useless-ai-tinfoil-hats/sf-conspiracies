Father, son stop pair from stealing $18,000 in merchandise in North Bay, police say
-----
Petaluma police have arrested two women they say tried to steal more than $18,000 of merchandise before they were stopped by fellow shoppers.Police were called to the Petaluma Outlets around 12:30 p.m. Thursday. Authorities say Casey Canion and Keiarra Spencer, both 20-year-old Antioch residents, gathered more than $18,000 of merchandise from a Sunglass Hut and ran away without paying.
