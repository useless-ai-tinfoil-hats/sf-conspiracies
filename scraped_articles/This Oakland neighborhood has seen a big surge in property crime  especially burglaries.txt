Marisa Haskell never imagined moving her jewelry store from Oakland's Temescal district. But after a year of numerous break-ins — including one in which thieves stole two safes that contained hundreds of thousands of dollars of cash — Haskell made the decision to shutter her 12-year-old store, Marisa Mason, and reopen in Berkeley."We were sad to leave Oakland," Haskell said.
