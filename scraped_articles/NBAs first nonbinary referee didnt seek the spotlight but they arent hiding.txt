NBA’s first non-binary referee didn’t seek the spotlight, but they aren’t hiding
-----
Che Flores' heart pounded as they walked onto the WNBA court in Minnesota. It was Flores' officiating debut at the professional level, and it was one of the first times they could remember feeling true nerves about working a game.By that time in 2011, Flores knew they were nonbinary, even if the arena of people around them didn't. Nor would they, for the next decade-plus.
Referees learn early on how to cope with an onslaught of emotion, from themselves and people around them.
