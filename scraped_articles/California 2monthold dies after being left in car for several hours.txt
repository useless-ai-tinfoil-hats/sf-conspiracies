California 2-month-old dies after being left in car for several hours
-----
The death of a 2-month-old girl left in a car for several hours in California on June 13 is under investigation, officials said.The baby was in an SUV parked outside a home in Santee, a suburb of San Diego, for "several hours," the San Diego County Sheriff's Department said in a news release. A family member called 911 after finding the child "unresponsive," and deputies and paramedics rushed to the 10000 block of Settle Road to provide medical aid, the department said.
