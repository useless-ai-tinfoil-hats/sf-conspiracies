‘Show & Tell’ artists turn the Chronicle into couture
-----
Print isn't dead, it's a look.That was the premise of my fourth "Show & Tell" event at Four One Nine, "The Fashion Forward Edition," on Tuesday, April 9, where three San Francisco artists transformed copies of the Chronicle into "clothing."
Armed with tape, scissors, glue and 23 newspapers each, it was amazing what Quan Nguyen, Ann Duskus and Tyson Lee were able to accomplish in just an hour.
