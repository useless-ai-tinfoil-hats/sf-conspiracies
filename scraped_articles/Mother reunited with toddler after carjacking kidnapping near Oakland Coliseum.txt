Mother reunited with toddler after carjacking, kidnapping near Oakland Coliseum
-----
A toddler was reunited with their mother after a carjacking and kidnapping near an Oakland crime hotspot on Monday afternoon.The mother's vehicle was stolen with her 2-year-old child inside around 3 p.m. in a neighborhood near the Oakland Coliseum, a spokesperson for the Oakland Police Department told SFGATE. When officers arrived on the scene, the woman, whose name has not been released, told OPD that her car was taken by an unknown male.
