Verbal clashes mark ‘interfaith’ march to encampment at Stanford
-----
Demonstrators at Stanford University verbally clashed over the war in Gaza on Sunday during a march that organizers billed as an "interfaith rally against terror."Hundreds of students, some carrying Israeli flags, marched a half-mile to White Memorial Plaza on the Palo Alto campus, where about 40 pro-Palestinian campers have been sleeping outside in tents.
