Northern California police rescue 8 children abducted from Arkansas
-----
Northern California police officers recovered eight children nearly 2,000 miles away from where they were originally reported missing in Arkansas, officials said.Police officers in Anderson (Shasta County) located six children and the woman they were with in a parking lot after receiving a welfare check request due to her "bizarre behavior," police said in a statement.
Trista Fullerton, 36, of Rogers, Ark., was the children's mother but previously lost her custodial rights, police
