Retirement has a nice ring to UC Berkeley’s Campanile carillonist
-----
Jeff Davis arrived for his last day of work at UC Berkeley only to find that someone was in his campus parking slot. After calling the tow truck, he went to his campus building to find the elevator malfunctioning.This was problematic because Davis' workstation is 200 feet up in the sky.
