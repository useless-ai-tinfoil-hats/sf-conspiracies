Several massive and ‘severe’ solar storms will hit the Earth on Friday. Could California see auroras?
-----
An uncommonly powerful solar storm is poised to cause potentially severe geomagnetic disturbances in the Earth's atmosphere on Friday, according to officials at the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration. It's one of three such storms set to arrive through the weekend.Officials at NOAA's Space Weather Prediction Center said Thursday that eruptions of solar materials known as coronal mass ejections were "quite likely" to reach Earth beginning late Friday and early Saturday.
