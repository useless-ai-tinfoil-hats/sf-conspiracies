Warriors’ Podziemski collects rebounds like a big man, even if he’s not
-----
By now, the Golden State Warriors almost expect Brandin Podziemski to gather rebounds as if they were his birthright. That's what he does. That's who he is.This helps explain Stephen Curry's strikingly nonchalant reaction in the second quarter of Tuesday night's victory over Dallas. Curry missed a mid-range jump shot, only to see Podziemski sprint from the 3-point line into the lane and snatch the ball from Mavericks guard Luka Dončić.
