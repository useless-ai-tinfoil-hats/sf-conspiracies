More back-to-back retail closures imminent at beleaguered San Francisco Centre mall
-----
J. Crew is the next domino to topple in a series of major retailers exiting the struggling San Francisco Centre mall.The clothing brand is slated to close on Jan. 22, and its inventory will be shipped out to nearby Bay Area locations by Jan. 26, an employee told SFGATE over the phone on Tuesday afternoon. The employee requested anonymity for fear of retaliation from the company and was granted it in accordance with Hearst's ethics policy.
