Inactive military bomb washed up on beach near Santa Cruz on New Year's Eve
-----
An inactive nonfunctional military bomb washed up on a Monterey Bay beach New Year's Eve, after a period of dangerously high surf conditions.A Santa Cruz County bomb team was called to Pajaro Dunes, about 20 miles south of Santa Cruz, around 2:20 p.m. on Sunday, the Santa Cruz County Sheriff's Office said. Officials determined the bomb was a "safe" ordnance from the 1960s used for practice, and removed it.
