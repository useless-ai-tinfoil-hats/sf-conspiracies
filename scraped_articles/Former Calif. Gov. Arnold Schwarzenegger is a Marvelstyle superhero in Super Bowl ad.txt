Former Calif. Gov. Arnold Schwarzenegger is a Marvel-style superhero in Super Bowl ad
-----
Arnold Schwarzenegger has been Mr. Universe, the Terminator and the governor of California. And as of last week, he has a new, albeit minor role: Agent State Farm.In a Super Bowl commercial teaser released last week, California's 38th governor appears to play a heroic State Farm insurance agent who, in a good-neighborly manner, shows up in a moment of crisis to save the day.
