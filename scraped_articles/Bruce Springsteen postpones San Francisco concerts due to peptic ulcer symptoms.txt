Bruce Springsteen postpones San Francisco concerts due to peptic ulcer symptoms
-----
Bruce Springsteen and the E Street Band have postponed the remainder of their scheduled concerts for the year due to the 74-year-old frontman's recovery from peptic ulcer disease.That includes the two San Francisco dates set for Dec. 10 and 12 (previously postponed from Dec. 8) at Chase Center, the venue announced Wednesday, Sept. 27. These shows will be rescheduled for 2024, with all performances taking place at their original venues.
