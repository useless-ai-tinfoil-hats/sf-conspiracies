Bloody clashes erupt between pro-, anti-China demonstrators at APEC
-----
Several people suffered bloody injuries Thursday after fighting broke out between supporters of China President Xi Jinping and anti-Chinese Communist Party protesters in front of the Moscone Center in San Francisco, where Xi was attending the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation.The protest was one of many that have occurred in the Bay Area during APEC, with some of the more heated demonstrations taking place Wednesday and Thursday morning.
