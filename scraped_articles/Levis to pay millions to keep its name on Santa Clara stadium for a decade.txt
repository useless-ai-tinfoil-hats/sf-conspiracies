Levi’s to pay millions to keep its name on Santa Clara stadium for a decade
-----
Levi Strauss & Co. said Thursday that it intends to spend $170 million to keep its name on the stadium where the San Francisco 49ers play and to continue sponsoring the team.Levi Strauss & Co. has had naming rights since the stadium opened in 2014. The new proposal is a 10-year extension of the original 20-year deal that would extend the partnership through the 2043 NFL season.
