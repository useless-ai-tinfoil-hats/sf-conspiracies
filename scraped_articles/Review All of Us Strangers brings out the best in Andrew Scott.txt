Review: ‘All of Us Strangers’ brings out the best in Andrew Scott
-----
One of the most amazing things I've ever seen in a theater was Andrew Scott's 2018 performance as Hamlet in London's West End. There was breathless silence in the auditorium during the familiar speeches, which he made to seem like they were being made up on the spot.
