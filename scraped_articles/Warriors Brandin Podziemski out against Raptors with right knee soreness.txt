Warriors’ Brandin Podziemski out against Raptors with right knee soreness
-----

Warriors starting guard Brandin Podziemski missed Friday's game against the Toronto Raptors with right knee soreness, clearing a spot in the first unit for longtime starting stalwart Klay Thompson.
