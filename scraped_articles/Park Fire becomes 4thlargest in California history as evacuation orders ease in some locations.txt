Park Fire becomes 4th-largest in California history as evacuation orders ease in some locations
-----
UPDATE: Park Fire near Chico surpasses 400,000 acres as thunderstorms add to dangerThe Park Fire grew to nearly 400,000 acres by Friday, according to Cal Fire, enough to make it the fourth-largest wildfire in California history.
The blaze eclipsed the 2020 SCU Lightning Complex, which torched nearly 397,000 acres of Stanislaus, Santa Clara, Alameda, Contra Costa and San Joaquin counties.
