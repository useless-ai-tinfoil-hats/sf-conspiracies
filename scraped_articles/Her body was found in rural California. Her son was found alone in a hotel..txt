The identification of Patricia Ann Rose's body has only deepened the mystery surrounding the fate of a woman who vanished from a Sacramento hotel around 1980.No one is exactly sure when Rose went missing. The only witness, as far as anyone knows, was her son, who told detectives he was found alone in a hotel room when he was 18 months old. Little is known about Rose's life, but SFGATE pieced together some of her story through public records.
