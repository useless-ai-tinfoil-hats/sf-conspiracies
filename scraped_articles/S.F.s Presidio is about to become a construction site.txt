S.F.’s Presidio is about to become a construction site
-----
San Francisco's Presidio soon will have something new to look at: detour signs.Roads will be torn up so water and sewage pipes can be replaced. Overhead wires will go underground. The three last vacant buildings in the Main Post will be brought back to life.
