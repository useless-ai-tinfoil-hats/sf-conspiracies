Court overturns part of 2021 California law modifying three strikes
-----
Part of a California law allowing three-strikes inmates to seek reductions in their life sentence if their third felony conviction was neither serious nor violent was ruled invalid Friday by a state appeals court, which said it conflicted with the voters' intent to protect the public.In a separate case Friday, another state appeals court relied on another section of the same law to order resentencing, with a possible release from prison, for a man serving 11 years for a 2016 assault
