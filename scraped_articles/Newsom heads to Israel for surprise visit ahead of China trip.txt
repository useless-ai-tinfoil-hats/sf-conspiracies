Newsom heads to Israel for surprise visit ahead of China trip
-----
California Gov. Gavin Newsom announced a surprise trip to Israel on Thursday, saying he wanted to offer his state's support to the victims of this month's attack by Hamas that prompted major Israeli airstrikes on Gaza and again pitched the Middle East into conflict.Newsom will visit ahead of a previously announced trip the Democratic governor is taking to China.
