Top S.F. Italian spot opening pizza spinoff at Ferry Building
-----
A top San Francisco Italian restaurant is settling in at the San Francisco Ferry Building for good.Popular pizza and pasta restaurant A16 announced its long-running pop-up at the Ferry Building is transforming into a permanent location. Dubbed A16 La Pala, it will offer Roman-style pizza on an airy, focaccia-like crust, served in whole pies and slices. Toppings will include tomato crudo, mushroom mix and sausage. Up to eight topping options will be available.
