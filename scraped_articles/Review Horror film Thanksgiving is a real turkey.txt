Review: Horror film ‘Thanksgiving’ is a real turkey
-----
Not even the Sexiest Man Alive can save Eli Roth's "Thanksgiving," a turkey of a horror film that should have been much better given its 16-year gestation period.Patrick Dempsey, anointed this year's hunk by People magazine earlier this month, may look like he's having fun as a small-town sheriff — complete with Boston accent — but Roth can't carve out an interesting story to match his meaty concept. It's all stuffing.
