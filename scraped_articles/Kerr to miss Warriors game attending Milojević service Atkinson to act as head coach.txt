Kerr to miss Warriors game attending Milojević service; Atkinson to act as head coach
-----
SALT LAKE CITY — Golden State Warriors coach Steve Kerr will not coach the team Monday against the Utah Jazz, instead attending late assistant coach Dejan Milojević's memorial services in Belgrade, Serbia.Warriors assistant Kenny Atkinson will act as the head coach.
Kerr is joined in Serbia by Warriors general manager Mike Dunleavy, assistant coaches Chris DeMarco and Ron Adams, director of sports medicine and performance Rick Celebrini and basketball operations consultant Zaza
