‘Ring of fire’ eclipse arrives in Bay Area, blocking out portion of sun
-----
Bay Area residents turned their eyes skyward Saturday morning as a "ring of fire" solar eclipse obscured a portion of the sun.The partial eclipse started shortly after 8 a.m., with maximum coverage of the sun between 9:15 and 9:25 a.m. and the sun emerging from the eclipse around 10:45 a.m.
