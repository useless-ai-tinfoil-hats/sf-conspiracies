Can an ultrarunner conquer California’s grueling John Muir Trail in under 3 days?
-----
Joe McConaughy trekked the John Muir Trail two years ago in record time, a feat of near-nonstop forward motion in which he covered 223 miles of High Sierra terrain in 3 days, 1 hour and 34 seconds — a pace of about 3 mph.It's a wild achievement. The JMT exists at or above 8,000 feet in elevation and traces the granite peaks between Yosemite National Park and Mount Whitney.
