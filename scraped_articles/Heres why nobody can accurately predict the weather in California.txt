Here’s why nobody can accurately predict the weather in California
-----
Harris K. Telemacher was a Los Angeles TV weathercaster with an ocean of knowledge — he had a doctorate in humanities and quoted Shakespeare — but no real meteorological training. So, assuming California weather was predictable, he taped his televised forecasts weeks in advance.
