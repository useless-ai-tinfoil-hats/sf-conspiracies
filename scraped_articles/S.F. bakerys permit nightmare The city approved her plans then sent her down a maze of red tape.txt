Sophie Smith was two weeks from opening her San Francisco bakery Butter and Crumble when she got an email from the health department.A requirement to separate a prep table and customer walkway by one foot, which she said had been confirmed by three different city employees, seemed to have suddenly changed. It expanded to three feet — and she would also need to build a new wall and install a sneeze guard.
