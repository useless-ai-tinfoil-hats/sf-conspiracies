Festival La Onda announces all-star lineup for Latin music-themed celebration
-----
Producers of the annual BottleRock Napa Valley festival have unveiled the lineup for the first Festival La Onda, a Latin music-themed outdoor concert in Napa set to take place on the weekend following their flagship Memorial Day concert.The two-day event, set for June 1-2, aims to showcase dozens of acts whose styles span a range of musical genres, encompassing regional Mexican, Latin pop, Spanish rock, reggaetón, banda, mariachi, rap, norteño, cumbia, bolero and more.
