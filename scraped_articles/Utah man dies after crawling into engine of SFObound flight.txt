Utah man dies after crawling into engine of SFO-bound flight
-----
A flight to San Francisco International Airport from Salt Lake City International Airport was canceled Monday night after a man was discovered inside a wing-mounted engine on a commercial aircraft and died at the scene. The Salt Lake City Police Department launched an investigation to determine how Kyler Efinger, 30, breached an airport security door and accessed the airfield.
