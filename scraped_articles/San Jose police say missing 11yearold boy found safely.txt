San Jose police say missing 11-year-old boy found safely
-----
San Jose police said Wednesday that an 11-year-old boy missing since Tuesday afternoon had been found safely.The child was last seen around 4 p.m. Tuesday in the area of East Santa Clara and First streets in San Jose, exiting a VTA bus.
"A good Samaritan located him and called us immediately," San Jose police said on social media.
The child was reportedly unharmed and was reunited with his family.
