A's paint over Coliseum’s large ‘Rooted in Oakland since ’68’ sign
-----
The Oakland A's roots are shriveling.The team's large "Rooted in Oakland since '68" slogan, painted on the west wall of the Coliseum in 2017 and highly visible from nearby Interstate 880, was painted over Monday.
