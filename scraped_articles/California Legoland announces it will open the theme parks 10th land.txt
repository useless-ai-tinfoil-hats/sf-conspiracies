California Legoland announces it will open the theme park's 10th land
-----
In honor of its 25th anniversary, Legoland in Carlsbad, Calif., announced it's adding a brand new land to the theme park.Dino Valley, which will open sometime in the spring, features interactive areas, meet-and-greets and two new rides. The two new rides are called Explorer River Quest and Duplo Little Dino Trail; details are scant on the Legoland announcement, but concept art of the river and Jeep-style rides look like more bucolic versions of Universal Studios' "Jurassic Park" thrill
