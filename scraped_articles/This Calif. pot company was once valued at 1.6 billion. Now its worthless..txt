This Calif. pot company was once valued at $1.6 billion. Now it's worthless.
-----
California's first cannabis unicorn — the pot retailer MedMen, which was once valued at $1.6 billion—is now essentially worthless.Shares of MedMen's stock, which is listed on the Canadian Securities Exchange, finished last week at exactly zero dollars in value, ending a precipitous fall for one of the most famous pot brands in California history.
