Learn how to watch a movie like a film critic
-----
People ask me how to watch movies like a movie critic all the time, and here is my Zen-like answer: If you want to watch movies like a movie critic, the first thing you must do is not try to watch movies like a movie critic.Instead, watch them like a normal human being. Watch them just as you've been watching them your whole life.
