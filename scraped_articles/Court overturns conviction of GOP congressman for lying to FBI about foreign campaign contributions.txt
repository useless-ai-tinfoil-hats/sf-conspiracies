Court overturns conviction of GOP congressman for lying to FBI about foreign campaign contributions
-----
A federal appeals court overturned a former congressman's felony convictions Tuesday for lying to the FBI about alleged foreign contributions to his campaign, saying he should have been tried in Nebraska, where the statements were made, and not in California where the FBI agents were based.Jeff Fortenberry, who will be 63 on Wednesday, was first elected to the House as a Republican from Lincoln, Neb., in 2004, and was re-elected eight times.
