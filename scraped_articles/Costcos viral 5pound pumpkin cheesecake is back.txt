Costco's viral 5-pound pumpkin cheesecake is back
-----
Costco's viral pumpkin cheesecake has made its grand return to the bakery section. The nearly 5-pound dessert, which only appears in the weeks leading up to Thanksgiving, was featured in a video with over 675,000 views by popular TikTok account CostcoHotFinds.The enormous seasonal dessert yields about 16 slices, according to the video.
