Ex-criminal justice lecturer sentenced to five years for setting blazes during Dixie Fire
-----
A former college teacher who admitted setting fires behind crews that were battling the huge Dixie Fire in 2021 was sentenced to five years and three months in federal prison Thursday.Gary Maynard, 49, of San Jose pleaded guilty in February to three charges of arson on federal property. In addition to the prison term imposed by U.S. District Judge Daniel Calabretta of Sacramento, he was ordered to pay $13,081 in restitution to the government under a plea agreement.
