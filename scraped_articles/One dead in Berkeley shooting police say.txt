One dead in Berkeley shooting, police say
-----
One person was killed in a shooting in Berkeley on Friday afternoon, the city's police department said.Police received reports of gunshots on the 3200 block of Adeline Street at around 12:44 p.m., police said. Officers found a shooting victim on the scene and performed life-saving measures, but the person succumbed to their injuries there, authorities said.
Officers were investigating the homicide, Berkeley's third this year.
