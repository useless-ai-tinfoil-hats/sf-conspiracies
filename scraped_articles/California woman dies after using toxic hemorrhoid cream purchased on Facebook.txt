California woman dies after using toxic hemorrhoid cream purchased on Facebook
-----
A woman in Northern California died due to severe lead poisoning after using a hemorrhoid cream she purchased on Facebook, prompting a health alert from state officials.The California Department of Public Health issued an advisory about the Vietnamese ointment, Cao Bôi Trĩ Cây Thầu Dầu (Castor Oil Hemorrhoid Extract), noting tests revealed that the product contained a "highly dangerous amount" of lead, at 4% (39,000 parts per million).
