Northern California set for a weather changeup. Here's what storm forecast looks like now
-----
The recent stretch of quiet weather across the Bay Area is expected to continue for two more days, but a big changeup is coming Saturday, in the form of cooler and wetter conditions.Confidence is increasing that Saturday will be quite wet across Northern California and the Bay Area, at least for May standards. A cold front is likely to reach the North Coast and northern Sacramento Valley on Friday night, arriving in the Bay Area during the early to mid-morning Saturday.
