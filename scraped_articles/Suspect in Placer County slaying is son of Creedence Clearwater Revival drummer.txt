Suspect in Placer County slaying is son of Creedence Clearwater Revival drummer
-----
A man charged with murdering his girlfriend in Placer County is the son of Creedence Clearwater Revival drummer Doug Clifford.Brent Chandler Clifford, 55, is the rock musician's son, ABC 10 News reported Thursday based on social media posts and public records.
Clifford is in custody in the Placer County jail on a murder charge after officials found the body of his girlfriend, 48-year-old Patricia Portella-Wright, off a Nevada highway Wednesday.
