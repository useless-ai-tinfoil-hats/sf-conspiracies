SF bakery owner makes big real estate purchase in the Tenderloin
-----
An internationally known and lauded bread baker from France recently purchased a building in San Francisco's Tenderloin district that currently houses the city's "rock n' roll hotel." It was bought at a total bargain.Michel Suas, co-owner of b. Patisserie, bread mentor to Chad Robertson of Tartine and founder of the San Francisco Baking Institute, is now the owner of the 38,000-square-foot lot at 601 Eddy St. after ponying up $9.1 million.
