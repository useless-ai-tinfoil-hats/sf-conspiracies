Adidas announces closure of its only San Francisco store
-----
Adidas confirmed it will close its only San Francisco store later this month, marking another loss for downtown's San Francisco Centre mall.The store's last day at 865 Market Street will be Saturday, Jan. 13, a company spokesperson told SFGATE. Until then, its merchandise will be sold at half-price, excluding select products.
