Missing Katy Perry songwriter Camela Leierth-Segura found after 3 months
-----
A songwriter, model and actress known for co-writing a Katy Perry hit has been found after going missing from her Beverly Hills home for about three months, officials confirmed.Camela Leierth-Segura, 48, was located by police on Oct. 5 through an anonymous source who reported seeing Leierth-Segura in the city of Santa Monica, Lt. Reginald Evans, a spokesperson for the Beverly Hills Police Department, wrote in an email to SFGATE on Tuesday.
