Malibu's plan to fix PCH, Calif.'s deadly, beautiful highway
-----
At the intersection of Stuart Ranch Road and Pacific Coast Highway in Malibu, 61 tires line the road, all painted white and marked with dates and names.The "ghost tires" represent the 61 traffic fatalities on Pacific Coast Highway in Malibu since 2010. As the coastal community prepares for its annual influx of tourists and beachgoers, safety advocates hope the tires will remind people zooming down the highway to be more cautious.
