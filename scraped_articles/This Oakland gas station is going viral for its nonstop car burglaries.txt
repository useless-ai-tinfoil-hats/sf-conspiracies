This Oakland gas station is going viral for its nonstop car burglaries
-----
Rental car companies at Oakland International Airport have an important message for anyone returning a car: If you're going to gas up before dropping your car, watch your belongings when doing so. And, better yet, don't stop at one of the stations near the airport's entrance.The warning comes during a steady uptick over the last two years at gas stations along Hegenberger Road and 98th Avenue, just east of the 880 freeway.
