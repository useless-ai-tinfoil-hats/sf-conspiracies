Giants will host Orlando Cepeda celebration of life Sept. 15 at Oracle Park
-----
Hall of Famer and San Francisco Giants legend Orlando Cepeda will be honored when the team hosts a celebration of life at Oracle Park on Sept. 15.The celebration will begin at 12:30 p.m. before the Giants-Padres 1:05 p.m. series finale. Gates will open at 11:05 a.m. Fans will need tickets to attend.
