All-WCC selection Adama-Alpha Bal will return to Santa Clara for senior season
-----
Santa Clara won't have another guard taken in the first round of next month's NBA draft, but Herb Sendek's Broncos will have Adama-Alpha Bal for another season.On Wednesday, Bal withdrew his name from consideration for the draft, meaning he'll return to Santa Clara for his senior season. The 6-foot-7 Frenchman averaged 14.4 points and 3.1 assists per game as a junior and was a first-team All-WCC selection.
