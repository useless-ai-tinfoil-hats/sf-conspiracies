Woman dies in Highway 1 crash near Half Moon Bay
-----
A woman was killed when her car collided with a big rig on Highway 1 north of Half Moon Bay on Thursday afternoon, temporarily closing the highway and causing oil to leak onto the roadway.The crash occurred on Highway 1 at Coral Reef Avenue in El Granada around 1:45 p.m., according to the California Highway Patrol. The victim's identity has not been released.
