Bicyclists killed by falling lumber in Napa were a married couple from Portland, authorities say
-----
The two bicyclists who died Tuesday after being hit by lumber falling off a truck were a married couple visiting from Portland, Ore., authorities say.Christian Deaton, 52, and Michelle Deaton, 48, were biking along the Silverado Trail in Napa when a load of lumber fell off a truck in front of them, hitting both, according to Officer Vincent Pompliano, a spokesperson for the California Highway Patrol's Napa office.
