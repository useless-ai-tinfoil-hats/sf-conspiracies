Officials warn influencers from getting too close to Bay Area's famed pink pond
-----
The pink salt ponds at Alviso Marina County Park in the South Bay create a picturesque landscape. While it might look like the perfect backdrop for your Instagram reel or TikTok clip, U.S. Fish and Wildlife is asking visitors to avoid getting too close.On Oct. 13, the agency posted on Facebook to ask visitors to take their photos of pond A12 from a distance.
