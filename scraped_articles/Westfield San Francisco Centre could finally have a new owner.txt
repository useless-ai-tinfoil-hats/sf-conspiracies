Westfield San Francisco Centre could finally have a new owner
-----
A lawsuit filed with the Superior Court of San Francisco shows that the beleaguered Westfield San Francisco Centre could finally have a new owner.Wilmington Trust and Wells Fargo have appointed Gregg Williams of Trident Pacific as the receiver of the mall in a legal action filed Monday, giving him full control of the property with compensation of $30,000 each month. This will take effect after Williams takes the receiver's oath and posts a $10,000 bond.
