‘No one’s here’: S.F. restaurants are suffering inside APEC security zone
-----
Super Duper Burgers near the Metreon shopping center in downtown San Francisco was quiet during the typically busy lunch hour on Wednesday, save for a few police officers and attendees of the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation conference eating burgers.DoorDash orders piled up in the kitchen at 783 Mission St., waiting to be picked up by drivers who would never arrive. They couldn't get into the heavily restricted security zone.
