One of Calif.'s only Native American chefs is making hundreds of breakfast burritos on Thanksgiving
-----
As a little girl growing up in the Bay Area, Crystal Wahpepah always wondered why there were no Native American restaurants around to enjoy her favorite corn and turkey soup, or other dishes common to her Kickapoo heritage."I thought, 'How come we don't see our food?' Everybody has a purpose and I believe that was my spark at a very young age," Wahpepah, now chef-owner of Oakland's popular Wahpepah's Kitchen, told SFGATE.
