Figo Pet Insurance review 2024
-----
Figo Pet Insurance at a glanceFigo is a particularly good option for older pets, given it has no upper age limits for its accident-and-illness policies, and its rates for senior dogs are among the lowest on the market. Plus, its well-rated mobile app lets you submit claims, message vets, and even find playmates or activities for your pet. Still, there are no accident-only policies, and you'll have to pay extra for exam fee coverage.
