Caesars, MGM targeted by Las Vegas casino cyberattacks
-----
In the wake of another cyberattack, malfunctioning slot machines are the least of Vegas guests' concerns.Caesars Entertainment, Inc. reported in a Thursday SEC filing that it was the second casino and hotel giant to fall prey to a cyberattack this week after MGM Resorts shut down its computer systems on Sunday following the discovery of a "cybersecurity issue."
According to Caesars' report, the company determined on September 7 that hackers breached its "loyalty program database"
