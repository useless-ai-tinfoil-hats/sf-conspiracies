Santa Barbara’s wine industry declared war on weed. Pot is winning.
-----
When pot farms showed up in Santa Barbara County in 2017, the wine industry went on the attack, vowing to launch a "giant legal battle" against the "horrifying" pot farms that they said would "be the end" of the wine industry in the region.Six years later, despite such apocalyptic warnings — along with lawsuits, zoning challenges and political pressure — pot farming is booming in Santa Barbara.
