One injured in Bay Bridge shooting, CHP says
-----
A shooting on the Bay Bridge injured one person Saturday night, the California Highway Patrol said.The shooting happened at about 8:20 p.m. near the Yerba Buena Island exit, the agency said. The victim was driving eastbound when a person in another vehicle fired multiple rounds at the car.
The victim pulled over to the right lane and called 911. Medics transported the victim, whose identity was not released, to a hospital with non-life-threatening injuries.
