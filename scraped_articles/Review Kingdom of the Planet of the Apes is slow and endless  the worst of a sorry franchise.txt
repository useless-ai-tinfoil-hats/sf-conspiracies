Review: ‘Kingdom of the Planet of the Apes’ is slow and endless — the worst of a sorry franchise
-----
Someone had an idea about how to make "Kingdom of the Planet of the Apes," and it's a bad one. How about a movie that's set centuries in the future, where the only characters you see for a full hour are apes — as in apes cavorting, apes swinging from trees, apes fighting each other? Wouldn't that be interesting?The answer is no. Not interesting at all. "Kingdom of the Planet of the Apes" runs for two hours and 25 minutes and doesn't have a single moment of cinematic charm.
