Dead Kennedys’ punk classic ‘Fresh Fruit’ achieves gold status after 43 years
-----
The Dead Kennedys have reached a once unfathomable milestone for punk rock in the music industry.More than four decades after its release, the San Francisco group's classic 1980 debut, "Fresh Fruit for Rotting Vegetables," earned the Recording Industry Association of America's gold sales award on Friday, Dec. 15, signifying that the album has sold 500,000 copies in the U.S.
