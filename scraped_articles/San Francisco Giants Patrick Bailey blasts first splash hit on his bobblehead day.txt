San Francisco Giants' Patrick Bailey blasts first splash hit on his bobblehead day
-----
How do you top blasting your first splash hit as a San Francisco Giant? You do it on your bobblehead day.Giants catcher Patrick Bailey did just that on Saturday afternoon, hitting a two-run homer into McCovey Cove that landed in a kayak on a day when the first 15,000 fans at Oracle Park received a Bailey bobblehead.
