BART police arrest man they say had an assault-style rifle hidden in his pants
-----
A 36-year-old man was arrested after officers found an assault-style rifle hidden in his pants at the BART Fruitvale Station on Christmas Eve, police said.The operator on a Richmond-bound train noticed the man bothering other riders and alerted police around 11:20 p.m., the BART Police Department said in a statement.
Two officers intercepted the train at the Fruitvale Station, where they found the gun on the man, police said. Officers described the weapon as an assault-style rifle
