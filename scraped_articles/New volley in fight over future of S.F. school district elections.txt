New volley in fight over future of S.F. school district elections
-----
A Bay Area attorney challenging the way San Francisco elects its school board members has filed the first legal fight in court, asking a judge to authorize a lawsuit that could compel a shift to district elections.The attorney, Scott Rafferty, is also asking the judge to force the San Francisco Unified School District board to follow public meeting and records laws.
