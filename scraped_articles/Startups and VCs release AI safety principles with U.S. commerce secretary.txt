Startups and VCs release AI safety principles with U.S. commerce secretary
-----
Trying to get their hands around the risks and challenges posed by artificial intelligence, technology leaders have proposed yet another responsible AI framework, this time aimed at startups and investors.The new guidelines, announced in San Francisco on Tuesday by leading venture capitalists with the backing of U.S. Secretary of Commerce Gina Raimondo, seek to lay out how software developers should use the technology responsibly, in concert with their moneyed backers.
