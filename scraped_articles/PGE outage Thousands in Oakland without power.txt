PG&E outage: Thousands in Oakland without power
-----
Thousands of Pacific Gas and Electric Co. customers were without power in Oakland on Monday, the utility company said.The outage, whose cause was unknown, affected more than 5,000 households as of Monday morning, after PG&E restored power to some areas, spokesperson Tamar Sarkissian said. The outage initially affected 8,731 customers.
