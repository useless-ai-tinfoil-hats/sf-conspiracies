Hit-and-run car fire closes eastbound bore of Caldecott Tunnel
-----
A car fire in the Caldecott Tunnel on Tuesday evening caused the closure of the second bore resulting in heavy delays for Bay Area motorists, Caltrans said.Authorities responded to the scene of a hit-and-run that occurred around 5:10 p.m. on eastbound Highway 24 near the Fish Ranch Road off-ramp, according to the California Highway Patrol incident page. There were no reported injuries, CHP said.
