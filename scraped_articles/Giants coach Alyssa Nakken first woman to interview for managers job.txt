Giants' coach Alyssa Nakken first woman to interview for manager's job
-----
The San Francisco Giants' managerial search now includes, for the first time in major-league history, a woman candidate.The Chronicle has confirmed that the team interviewed coach Alyssa Nakken - one of several internal options - along with third-base coach Mark Hallberg, who went through the interview process last week.
