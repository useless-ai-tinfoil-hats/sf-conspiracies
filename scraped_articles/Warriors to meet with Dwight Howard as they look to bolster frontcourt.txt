Warriors to meet with Dwight Howard as they look to bolster frontcourt
-----
UPDATE: After consideration, Warriors won't bring in Dwight Howard after allThe Golden State Warriors are expected to meet with eight-time All-Star center Dwight Howard next week, league sources confirmed to the Chronicle on Friday.
Howard, who was drafted No. 1 overall by the Orlando Magic in 2004, last played in the NBA for the Los Angeles Lakers in 2021-22, when he averaged 6.2 points and 5.9 rebounds across 60 games with 27 starts.
