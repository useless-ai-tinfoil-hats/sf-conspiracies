San Francisco is getting 33 speed cameras. Here's where they could go
-----
San Francisco will study where to place cameras that automatically catch and ticket speeding drivers next year, city officials announced Monday.At least 33 cameras will be placed in school zones and other high-risk corridors around the city to catch speeding drivers, officials said. Gov. Gavin Newsom legalized their use Friday in a traffic safety bill.
