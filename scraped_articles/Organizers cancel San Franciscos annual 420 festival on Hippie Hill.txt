Organizers cancel San Francisco's annual 420 festival on 'Hippie Hill'
-----
The organizers of San Francisco's annual "Hippie Hill" cannabis festival on April 20 announced Monday that they will not be hosting the event this year, according to an announcement on the group's website.The organizers said citywide budget cuts and a lack of sponsorships meant they did not have enough resources to host this year's event. The San Francisco Office of Cannabis and the SF Recreation and Park Department did not return a Monday request for comment.
