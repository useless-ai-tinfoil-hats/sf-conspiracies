Cavaliers select Cal's Jaylon Tyson with 20th pick in NBA draft
-----
Jaylon Tyson, a first-team All-Pac-12 selection in his lone season at Cal, was selected by the Cleveland Cavaliers in the first round of the NBA draft Wednesday night.The No. 20 overall pick, Tyson will play for former Golden State Warriors assistant coach Kenny Atkinson, who recently became the Cavs' head coach.
The swingman began his college career at Texas in 2021-22, transferred to Texas Tech for the 2022-23 season and then transferred to Cal before last season.
