Bay Area hike to Picchetti Ranch Preserve has a surprising Hollywood ending
-----
Much like a Hollywood movie with a memorable ending, the Picchetti Ranch Preserve in Santa Clara County is a hike that's got a noteworthy (and delicious) finish.You'll find Picchetti Ranch Preserve off Stevens Canyon Road, nestled in rolling hills just beyond the suburban neighborhoods of Cupertino and the Stevens Creek Quarry. The 308-acre preserve with 3.7 miles of trails and tons of history is one of several managed by the Midpeninsula Regional Open Space District.
