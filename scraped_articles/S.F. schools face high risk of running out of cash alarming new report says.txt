S.F. schools face ‘high’ risk of running out of cash, alarming new report says
-----
San Francisco no longer has full control over its schools, with state officials giving fiscal appointees veto power over spending last week after new deficit projections showed the district could run out of cash to pay the bills in just over a year.State education leaders have been keeping tabs on San Francisco Unified School District for a few years, hoping district officials would pull out of the fiscal tailspin, but their watchful concern has turned to active angst.
