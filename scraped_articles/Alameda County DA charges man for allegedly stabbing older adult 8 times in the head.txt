Alameda County DA charges man for allegedly stabbing older adult 8 times in the head
-----
The Alameda County District Attorney's Office filed charges against a 23-year-old man suspected in the stabbing of an elderly man last month.The district attorney's office said in a statement that James Lambert launched an "unprovoked attack" on an 81-year-old man on Aug. 17, as the victim walked along Whitman Street in Hayward.
Lambert allegedly stabbed the man in the head eight times before striking him with a metal pipe as he lay on the ground. The assault was reportedly stopped
