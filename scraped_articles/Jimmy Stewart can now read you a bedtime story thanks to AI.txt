Jimmy Stewart can now read you a bedtime story, thanks to AI
-----
Jimmy Stewart is now available to narrate your bedtime story.Calm, the sleep and meditation app company headquartered in San Francisco, unveiled "It's a Wonderful Sleep Story" for its premium subscribers on Tuesday, Dec. 5. The first-of-its-kind feature introduces a holiday tale in the style of Stewart's 1947 Christmas classic, "It's a Wonderful Life," using an artificial intelligence-generated version of the late actor's voice.
