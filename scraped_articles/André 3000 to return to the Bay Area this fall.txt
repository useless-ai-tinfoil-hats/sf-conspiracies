André 3000 to return to the Bay Area this fall
-----
Months after his three-day San Francisco residency, André 3000 is ready to head back to the Bay Area.The musician, also known as André Lauren Benjamin, is extending his New Blue Sun Live In Concert Tour, with a stop in Oakland scheduled for Oct. 5. His new run of shows is expected to begin in New Orleans on Sept. 19 and wrap up in Atlanta on Nov. 14.
