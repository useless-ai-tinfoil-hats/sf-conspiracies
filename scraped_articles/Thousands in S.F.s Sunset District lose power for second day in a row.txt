Thousands in S.F.'s Sunset District lose power for second day in a row
-----
Thousands of Pacific Gas & Electric Co. customers in San Francisco's Sunset District again lost power on Friday, just one day after an outage left 14,000 customers without power in the area.* PG&E outage map: Bay Area shut-off details by address
The Chronicle map that tracks PG&E power outages showed that around 3,500 customers were without power just before noon. Around 1 p.m., PG&E reported that there were just under 1,500 customers without power.
