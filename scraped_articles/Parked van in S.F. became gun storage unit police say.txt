Parked van in S.F. became gun storage unit, police say
-----
San Francisco police seized an arsenal of guns from a van parked in the Bayview, which bore the name and logo of a nonprofit on its side door, according to a video released by the Police Officers Association.Patrol officers said they spotted the vehicle at 7:49 p.m. Monday on the 1700 block of Oakdale Avenue. Peering in, they saw a handgun "in plain sight" on the front seat, representatives of the association wrote in a social media post.
