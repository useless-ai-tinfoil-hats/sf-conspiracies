Man allegedly fleeing Vallejo police impales leg on security spike
-----
A man running from police officers in Vallejo suffered severe injuries after trying to jump a fence, where a security spike lanced through his leg, police said Friday.The incident occurred about 4 a.m. on Oct. 16, when police spotted the man driving a stolen 2018 brown Infiniti Q50 west on Tennessee Street near Vervais Avenue, according to a statement from the Police Department.
