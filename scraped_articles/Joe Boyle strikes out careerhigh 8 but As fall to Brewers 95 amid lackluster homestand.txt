Joe Boyle strikes out career-high 8 but A’s fall to Brewers 9-5 amid lackluster homestand
-----
Oakland Athletics pitcher Joe Boyle hoped he was out of the woods after a three-run second inning.That wish dissipated when Jake Bauers took a 98.4 mph fastball near the center of the plate for a two-run homer in the fifth, allowing the Brewers to extend their lead to 5-1. It came on Boyle's 100th pitch, nearing his career high of 104 — which he ultimately tied on Saturday.
