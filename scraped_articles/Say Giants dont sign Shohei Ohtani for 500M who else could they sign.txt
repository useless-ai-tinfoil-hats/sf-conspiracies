Say Giants don't sign Shohei Ohtani for $500M, who else could they sign?
-----
By what they're expressing on social media, San Francisco Giants fans already are bracing for a major letdown in the Shohei Ohtani sweepstakes. That's how they roll, knowing their team has wooed many other elite free agents who ultimately passed on San Francisco.This is different because it isn't expected to be a two-team competition as was the case last winter with the Giants and Yankees bidding for Aaron Judge, who wisely played his hometown team against the team he was returning to
