Jhené Aiko to perform at Chase Center this summer
-----
After a four-year delay, Jhené Aiko is finally setting out on her long-awaited Magic Hour Tour, with plans to stop in Northern California for two nights.The R&B singer is scheduled to bring her first headlining tour, originally announced in 2020, to San Francisco's Chase Center on Aug. 5, and Sacramento's Golden 1 Center the following night.
General ticket sales begin at 10 a.m. Friday, March 29.
Aiko's summer tour, named after the track "Magic Hour" off her third solo album
