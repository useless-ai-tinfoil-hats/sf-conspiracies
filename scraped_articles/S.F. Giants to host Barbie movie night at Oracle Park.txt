S.F. Giants to host ‘Barbie’ movie night at Oracle Park
-----
Oracle Park, home to the San Francisco Giants, is set to transform into Barbieland.Following the success of Greta Gerwig's summer blockbuster, the ballpark plans to host a special screening of "Barbie" on its colossal, state-of-the-art 4K scoreboard on Friday, Oct. 27 — a screen size nearly double that of the IMAX screen at the Metreon, the city's largest movie theater.
