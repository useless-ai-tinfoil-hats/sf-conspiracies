Father dies while kayaking with son off Northern California coast
-----
A 70-year-old Fort Bragg man died while kayaking with his son off the coast of Northern California on Sept. 24, officials said.Gordon Bruce Spence was in a two-person kayak with his son when the boat capsized at about 1 p.m. near a cove tucked between the town of Mendocino and Russian Gulch State Park, Capt. Gregory Van Patten, a spokesperson for the Mendocino County Sheriff's Office, told SFGATE on Friday.
