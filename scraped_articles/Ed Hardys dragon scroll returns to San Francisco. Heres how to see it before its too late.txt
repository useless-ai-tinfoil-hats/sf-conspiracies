Ed Hardy’s dragon scroll returns to San Francisco. Here’s how to see it before it’s too late
-----
Silence filled Grace Cathedral on a recent spring morning as if to pay reverence to its latest addition: a 500-foot-long hand-painted scroll suspended 14 feet in the air.The monumental piece by famed San Francisco Art Institute alum Don Ed Hardy features 2,000 painted dragons of various sizes and has been on view at the Nob Hill church and arts space for nearly a month, marking only its third time in San Francisco.
