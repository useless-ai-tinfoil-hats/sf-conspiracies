Berkeley's Greek Theatre renovations solve an age-old problem
-----
The longest-running outdoor theater in the country got a recent makeover just in time for a sold-out show.The William Randolph Hearst Greek Theatre at UC Berkeley has been a staple in the Bay Area theater and concert scene since its opening in 1903. However, some of its features have come to feel as old as the facility itself.
