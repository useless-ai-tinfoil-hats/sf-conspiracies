Dozens of TGI Fridays restaurants permanently close across the US
-----
TGI Fridays plans to close 36 restaurants across 12 states, including one in California.The series of planned closures was announced Wednesday in a press release that highlighted the chain's growth strategy, which included closing "underperforming" restaurants across the nation. Among them was a Fresno TGI Fridays at 1077 E Herndon Ave.
