Celebrated Bay Area bagel shop opens first S.F. location, and more restaurant openings
-----
Some of the most anticipated restaurants coming to San Francisco finally opened in March.A restaurant from a team of Mister Jiu's chefs is now serving bold Cantonese fare, while Berkeley's Boichik Bagels launched its first location on the opposite side of the Bay Bridge. March was also carb intensive, with bakery Bernal Basket launching a brick-and-mortar shop and a mochi-pizza mashup reaching downtown.
