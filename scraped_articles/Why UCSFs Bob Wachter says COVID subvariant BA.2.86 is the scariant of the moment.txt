Why UCSF’s Bob Wachter says COVID subvariant BA.2.86 is the ‘scariant of the moment’
-----
Dr. Bob Wachter, UCSF's chief of medicine, hosted his first COVID roundtable discussion since June late last week, aiming to assess the current pandemic trajectory amid a summer wave of infections and hospitalizations in the Bay Area.For the introductory Grand Rounds of the new academic year, he brought together three well-known UCSF infectious disease experts with "different but mutually respectful perspectives" — Dr. Monica Gandhi, Dr. George Rutherford and Dr. Peter Chin-Hong.
