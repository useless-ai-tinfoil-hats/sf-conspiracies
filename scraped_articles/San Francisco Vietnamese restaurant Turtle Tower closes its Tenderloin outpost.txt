San Francisco Vietnamese restaurant Turtle Tower closes its Tenderloin outpost
-----
Yet another longtime Bay Area Vietnamese restaurant has closed. After 23 years in the Tenderloin, Turtle Tower served its last bowl of pho ga on Sunday, Sept. 17.Owner Steven Nghia Pham confirmed the news to the San Francisco Chronicle. (The Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms.) Reports that the building at 645 Larkin St. was for sale surfaced back in June, although no sale has been announced as of yet. SFGATE reached out to Turtle Tower for comment
