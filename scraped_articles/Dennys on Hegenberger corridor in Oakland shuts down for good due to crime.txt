Denny's on Hegenberger corridor in Oakland shuts down for good due to crime
-----
Another Oakland business near the Hegenberger Road corridor has permanently closed over concerns regarding rampant crime in the troubled area.The Denny's restaurant, located at 601 Hegenberger Road, shut down for good on Wednesday, KTVU's Henry Lee first reported.
The restaurant cited the "safety and well-being" of Denny's customers and employees as its reason for closing after being in business in the area for 54 years, according to a statement from Denny's provided to SFGATE.
