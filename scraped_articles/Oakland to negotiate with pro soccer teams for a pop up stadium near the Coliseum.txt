Oakland to negotiate with pro soccer teams for a pop up stadium near the Coliseum
-----
Oakland's two professional soccer teams are one step closer to building a temporary stadium near the Coliseum.The City Council voted unanimously Tuesday to enter into an exclusive negotiating agreement with the Roots and Soul minor-league soccer teams and Alameda County for a 10-year lease to host up to 10,000 fans on 8.8 acres of a triangular shaped property at 8000 South Coliseum Way, known as the Malibu Property. The property is currently a vacant asphalt lot used for overflow parking
