Condos on this S.F. island are as high as $4.4 million. What life is like for the first residents
-----
From his sixth-floor condo on Yerba Buena Island, Michael Lee looks out at the idling freighters and crisscrossing ferries, the Port of Oakland's white cranes, and Berkeley's green hills.Occasionally, San Francisco-bound friends will hit him up from the Bay Bridge as they are about to motor into the tunnel far below. "My friends will call and say, 'Hey, I'm about to pass your place,' " said Lee, who works as a senior director at Electronic Arts. "I'll look out the window and wave."
