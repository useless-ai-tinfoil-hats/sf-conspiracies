Man accused in Berkeley homeless shelter shooting charged with murder
-----
Berkeley police arrested a man suspected of shooting and killing a man at a West Berkeley homeless shelter on Sunday, according to a statement.Police arrested Mark Christopher Dowling, 62, on Monday morning after locating him parked in his vehicle near Fourth Street and Camelia Street. Officials searched his vehicle and allegedly found several guns and ammunition.
The Alameda County District Attorney's Office charged Dowling with murder on Tuesday.
