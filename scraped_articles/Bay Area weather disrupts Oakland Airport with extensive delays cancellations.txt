Bay Area weather disrupts Oakland Airport with extensive delays, cancellations
-----
Thick fog created extensive delays at Oakland International Airport on Monday, rocketing the airport to the top of the Misery Map on FlightAware as inbound and outbound flights were held to avoid the hazardous conditions at the Bay Area airport.A spokesperson for OAK confirmed to SFGATE in an email that there have been 22 delays and one cancellation for departing flights and 24 delays and two cancellations for arriving flights.
