State Bar of California nearing deal to sell its 13-story downtown S.F. headquarters
-----
Following reports this summer that San Francisco's Ellis Partners and Boston hedge fund Baupost Group were under contract to purchase the State Bar of California's downtown headquarters, the deal went quiet.That's because the partners ultimately did not transact on the 211,000-square-foot office tower at 180 Howard St., multiple sources with knowledge of the deal confirmed this week.
