Disneyland announces more closures, construction chaos this summer
-----
Disneyland's never-ending construction will be prolonged even further as more closures are set to hit the Critter Country part of the park this summer.On May 1, almost all of Critter Country's attractions closed to make way for refurbishments. On July 8, the Hungry Bear restaurant and Davy Crockett's Explorer Canoes are also slated to close.
