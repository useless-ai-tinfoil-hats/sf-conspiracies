Lawsuit accuses Alameda County sheriff’s deputies of assaulting woman in hospital
-----
Alameda County sheriff's deputies were accused in a lawsuit Thursday of repeated acts of brutality against an Oakland woman: dragging her out of a courtroom and beating her unconscious, then taking her out of a hospital and choking and punching her before hauling her to jail on charges that were later dismissed."These men were trained on how to use force. Instead they resorted to brutish violence on an unarmed woman … beat her up as if she were a runaway slave," attorney Adanté Pointer
