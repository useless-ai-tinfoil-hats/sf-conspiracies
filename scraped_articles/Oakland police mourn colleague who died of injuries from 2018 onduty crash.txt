Oakland police mourn colleague who died of injuries from 2018 on-duty crash
-----
Oakland police are mourning a colleague who died Saturday of injuries sustained in a 2018 crash while responding to a call.Jordan Wingate, 28, died surrounded by family and friends, including his father, retired Oakland police Capt. Randell Wingate, the police department said in a news release Saturday. Jordan Wingate was the 55th Oakland officer killed in the line of duty.
