Suspect held in fatal East Bay shooting
-----
Antioch police say they have arrested a suspect in the fatal shooting of a 26-year-old man.Officers responded to reports of a shooting on the 800 block of Sunset Drive around 6:30 p.m. Friday and found the victim. Paramedics rendered first aid but were unable to revive him, the Antioch Police Department said in a Facebook post.
Patrol officers found a suspect nearby and arrested him without incident, the agency said.
