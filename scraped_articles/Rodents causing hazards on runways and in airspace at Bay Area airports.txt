Rodents causing hazards on runways and in airspace at Bay Area airports
-----
The Federal Aviation Administration sent a notice to a Bay Area county last month indicating that it's opening an investigation to determine whether the county is responsible for "unsafe airfield conditions" at two small airports.Among the safety concerns at the two general aviation airports, Reid-Hillview and San Martin, are wildlife, specifically ground squirrels, "potentially impacting the airfield pavement, signage and lighting," according to the notice.
