Oakland police arrest 10 people from well-known gang for weapons possession
-----
Ten members of a West Oakland gang were arrested in the city after a nearly two-week sting operation, police said Thursday.Nine adults were arrested Tuesday and charged with weapons possession after the Oakland Police Department's Ceasefire Unit, the city's premier anti-violence program, followed them for about two weeks, said Capt.
