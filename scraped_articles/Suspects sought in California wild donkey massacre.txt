Suspects sought in California wild donkey massacre
-----
A massacre on the northern edge of California's Mojave Desert in 2019 left scores of wild donkeys dead by the side of Interstate 15.Federal agents are seeking those responsible and have shared new details on the vehicles and weapon they believed were used in 19 of the 42 shootings that summer. The Bureau of Land Management has also upped a reward to help find the donkey killers.
