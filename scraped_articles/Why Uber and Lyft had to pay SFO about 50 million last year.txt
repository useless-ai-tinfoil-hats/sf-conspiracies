Why Uber and Lyft had to pay SFO about $50 million last year
-----
Uber and Lyft are paying San Francisco International Airport millions to take you to and from the airport. Most of that cash is coming from your pocket.From January to November 2023, SFO pulled $45.1 million in fees from Uber and Lyft, according to airport data obtained by SFGATE. The San Francisco-based ride-hailing companies must pay SFO $5.50 for every dropoff and pickup at the airport (taxis also pay a $5.50 fee, but only for airport pickups).
