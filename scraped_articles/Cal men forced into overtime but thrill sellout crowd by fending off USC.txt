Cal men forced into overtime, but thrill sellout crowd by fending off USC
-----
When Mark Madsen took the job as Cal's head coach in late March, he surely must have envisioned the scene at Haas Pavilion on Wednesday night.There was a sellout, raucous crowd announced at 11,801. The Bears led USC for 37:49 and though Cal squandered a 16-point, second-half lead and had to go overtime, the Bears still pulled out an 83-77 victory.
