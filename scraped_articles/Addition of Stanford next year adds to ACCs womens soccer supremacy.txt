Addition of Stanford next year adds to ACC's women's soccer supremacy
-----
By the time the ACC member schools learned Stanford, Cal, and SMU were joining their conference next season, one team was already on a plane headed west.The Duke women's soccer team was headed to Palo Alto for a game against third-ranked Stanford. The Cardinal were in Durham, N.C., in 2021, and when Duke head coach Robbie Church made the home-and-home agreement with Stanford's Paul Ratcliffe, neither could have anticipated a world in which they would become conference opponents.
