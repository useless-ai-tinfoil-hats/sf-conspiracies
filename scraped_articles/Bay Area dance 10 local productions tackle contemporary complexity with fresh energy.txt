Bay Area dance: 10 local productions tackle contemporary complexity with fresh energy
-----
This spring the Bay Area dance world is full of renewed energy to shine light on our world's complexities. From a Robert Moses' Kin world premiere grappling with book banning to a preview of the Oakland Ballet's "Angel Island Project" about the lives of detained immigrants, dance companies are facing the challenges of our moment with resilience and curiosity.Here are several dance offerings that promise to enliven and enlighten as the Northern Hemisphere finds much-needed revival.
