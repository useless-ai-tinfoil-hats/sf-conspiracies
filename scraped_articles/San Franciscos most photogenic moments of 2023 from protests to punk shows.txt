San Francisco's most photogenic moments of 2023, from protests to punk shows
-----
Looking back on our photos from these past 12 months, we can't tell whether 2023 went quickly or January was three lifetimes ago. That said, the year certainly seemed like a very visual lap around the sun, particularly here in the Bay Area, from our turbulent winter to our fall festival season.This year, we had the pleasure of photographing some seldom-visited locations, such as Red Rock Island, the Chapel of the Chimes and the BART maintenance yard.
