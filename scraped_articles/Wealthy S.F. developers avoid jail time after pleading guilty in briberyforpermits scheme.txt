Wealthy S.F. developers avoid jail time after pleading guilty in bribery-for-permits scheme
-----
Two wealthy San Francisco developers who spent years bribing local building officials in exchange for permit approvals and inspections will avoid jail time, a federal judge ruled Friday.Siavash Tahbazof, 73, and his 39-year-old nephew, Bahman Ghassemzadeh, each pleaded guilty earlier this year to one count of honest services wire fraud. Both men received three years probation and monetary fines, without home confinement as prosecutors had sought.
