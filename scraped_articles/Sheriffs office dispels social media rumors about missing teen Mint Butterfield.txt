Sheriff's office dispels social media rumors about missing teen Mint Butterfield
-----
The Marin County Sheriff's Office issued a news release Thursday to dispel misinformation that's spreading about Mint Butterfield, a 16-year-old who left a family home in Bolinas on April 21 and was reported missing about 9 a.m. April 22."Unfortunately, there has been misinformation disseminated through social media and other outlets regarding potential associates or persons who may know of Mint's whereabouts," the sheriff's office said.
