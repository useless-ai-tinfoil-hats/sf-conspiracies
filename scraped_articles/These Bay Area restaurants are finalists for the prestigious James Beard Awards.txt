These Bay Area restaurants are finalists for the prestigious James Beard Awards
-----
Bay Area restaurants and chefs were nominated Wednesday for James Beard Awards, inching closer to winning what's considered the equivalent of the Oscars for the food world.Geoff Davis, chef and owner of acclaimed 7-month-old Burdell in Oakland, is up for best new chef in California, as are Rogelio Garcia of Auro in Calistoga and Buu "Billy" Ngo of Sacramento Japanese restaurant Kru. Six San Francisco chefs who were named as semifinalists for this award in January did not move forward.
