Bay FC hires Albertin Montoya, former Santa Clara player, as head coach
-----
Bay FC does not yet have a single player, but the organization has begun working to establish an on-field identity. Fast, exciting, possession-oriented and forward-thinking.That's what made Albertin Montoya a perfect fit as the franchise's first head coach in general manager Lucy Rushton's view. A big name in Bay Area women's soccer circles, Montoya wants to lead a team that plays exactly that style.
