Do Californians' efforts to protect themselves from wildfire make a difference to insurers?
-----
Spend any time thinking or talking about insurance in California these days and you're bound to hear the word "mitigation."Fire officials, lawmakers, insurance agents and others are asking homeowners to help lower the risk of devastating wildfires by making improvements to their properties — in some cases at great expense — and often in the context of trying to hang on to their insurance policies. The state has spent about $3.7 billion on forest management in the past seven years.
