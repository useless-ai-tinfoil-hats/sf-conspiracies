Prelude to Pride: Thousands celebrate what makes S.F. fabulous at Civic Center party
-----
In anticipation of Sunday's San Francisco Pride Parade, thousands of people started the party a day early at Civic Center for a celebration and rally.Hallmarks of the Bay Area LGBTQ community — from Castro nudists to tote-bag-sporting Gen Z bisexuals — enjoyed music, drag performances and food trucks at Saturday's event.
The heat was interrupted by occasional but forceful gusts of wind, causing Pride flags worn as capes to swell.
