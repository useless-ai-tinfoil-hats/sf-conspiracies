Oakland police search for hit-run driver in death of pedestrian
-----
Oakland police are asking for the public's help identifying a car involved in a hit-and-run that killed a pedestrian.Police said Kim Barranco, 59, of Oakland was hit by two cars while crossing the street on the 2400 block of San Pablo Avenue on the afternoon of Jan. 9. Officials are enlisting the public's help to search for the driver of the second car, a white Toyota Rav4. Police said the driver may not be aware that they hit Barranco.
