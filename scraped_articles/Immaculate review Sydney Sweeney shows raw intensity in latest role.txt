‘Immaculate’ review: Sydney Sweeney shows raw intensity in latest role
-----
"Immaculate" is a creepy, engrossing thriller and a weirdly successful showcase for newly minted star Sydney Sweeney.The "Euphoria" and "Anyone But You" star plays a young American woman who travels to Italy, where she becomes a nun in a cloistered Roman convent. For both Sweeney and Cecilia, the woman she plays, this is a bizarre career move that works in interesting ways.
