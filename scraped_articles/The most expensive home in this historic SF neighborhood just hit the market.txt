The most expensive home in this historic SF neighborhood just hit the market
-----
The historic, posh San Francisco neighborhood of St. Francis Wood has a home listed for sale at the highest price the historic neighborhood has ever seen.A home in San Francisco's historic St. Francis Wood at 55 Santa Paula Ave. just listed for $5.5 million — a standout price for the neighborhood. At $2,011 per square foot, it sets a new record for the highest price per square foot — and price in general — in St. Francis Wood since at least the year 2000, according to real estate agents.
