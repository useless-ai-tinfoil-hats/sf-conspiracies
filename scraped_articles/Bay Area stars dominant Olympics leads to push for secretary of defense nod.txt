Bay Area star's dominant Olympics leads to push for secretary of defense nod
-----
The United States women's national soccer team is on to the gold medal match after beating Germany 1-0 in extra time Tuesday, and it's a Bay Area athlete who has been the clear superstar of the tournament.San Jose native and former Stanford star Naomi Girma had perhaps her best match yet Tuesday amid a run of stellar play in Paris. She completely stonewalled several promising German opportunities and even sent the first build-up pass that led to the lone U.S. goal.
