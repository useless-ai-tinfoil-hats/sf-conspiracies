Silicon Valley tech titan and former Google CEO Eric Schmidt selling Atherton estate for $25M
-----
One of Silicon Valley's most famous tech execs is selling his Atherton estate.A more than 3-acre property in Atherton owned by former Google CEO Eric Schmidt and his wife, investor and philanthropist Wendy Schmidt, has hit the market for $24.5 million. While Compass listing agent Katherine Carroll declined to confirm or deny the Schmidts' ownership of the Walsh Estate, the property's address and geography match prior reporting on the Schmidts' home.
