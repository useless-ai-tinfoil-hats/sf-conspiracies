Pro rock climber who sexually assaulted woman in Yosemite sentenced to life in prison
-----
A renowned professional rock climber, convicted of sexually abusing a woman during a hike at Yosemite National Park, was sentenced Tuesday to life in federal prison.Charles Barrett, 40, was convicted by a jury in February of two charges of aggravated sexual abuse and one count of abusive sexual contact against the woman, who was 19 when the attacks occurred in 2016. He was sentenced by U.S. District Judge John Mendez in Sacramento.
