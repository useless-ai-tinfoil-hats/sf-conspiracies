Woman kidnapped during carjacking in South Bay
-----
San Jose police are seeking the public's help in locating a woman who was kidnapped early Saturday.Acela Pineda Silva was a passenger in a vehicle that was stolen by several assailants around North Fifth and St. John streets around 2 a.m., the Police Department said. The vehicle has been found, but Silva and the kidnappers are still unaccounted for, police said.
Police ask anyone who sees the victim, who is at risk, to contact 911.
