Police find ghost gun on man accused of shooting woman on Stanford campus
-----
Police say a man shot a woman on Stanford University's campus early Tuesday morning and was later arrested carrying a "ghost gun."Around 2 a.m. Tuesday, the Santa Clara County Sheriff's Office responded to reports of a shooting at the north side of Stanford's campus, near the arboretum, according to the sheriff's office. Once there, they found a 47-year-old woman who had been shot in the leg, but weren't able to find the suspect.
