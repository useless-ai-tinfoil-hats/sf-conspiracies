Police shoot and wound man suspected in burglary at Best Buy warehouse in Tracy
-----
Police shot and wounded a person suspected of breaking into trailers parked at a Best Buy warehouse in Tracy on Sunday morning, authorities said.Officers were called to a burglary in progress at the electronics retailer's warehouse on the 6000 block of Hopkins Road around 3:30 a.m. and found multiple suspects breaking into trailers in the warehouse parking lot, the Tracy Police Department said in a statement Sunday.
