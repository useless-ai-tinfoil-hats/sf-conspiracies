Baby delivered via emergency surgery dies after pregnant mother killed in South Bay car crash
-----
The child of a pregnant woman who died following a hit-and-run car crash in San Jose on Tuesday night has also died, authorities said.Doctors were initially able to save the child, who was listed in critical condition after being delivered via an emergency cesarean operation at a San Jose hospital. The child died Wednesday from injuries suffered during the crash, according to Stacie Shih, a spokesperson for the San Jose Police Department.
