Giant Ferris wheel closes at Golden Gate Park. It will return for APEC in new location
-----
The SkyStar Observation Wheel in Golden Gate Park closed Sunday evening in anticipation of a move to Fisherman's Wharf, where it is expected to open Nov. 10 as an attraction for the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit hosted by San Francisco.The relocation of the 150-foot-tall Ferris wheel was announced on the SkyStar website, and dismantling has begun.
