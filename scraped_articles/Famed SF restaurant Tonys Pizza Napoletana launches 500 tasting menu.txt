Famed SF restaurant Tony's Pizza Napoletana launches $500 tasting menu
-----
San Francisco's world-famous Tony's Pizza Napoletana is getting into the fine dining game. On Feb. 15, award-winning chef Tony Gemignani debuted "Pizza Around the World," his restaurant's new $500 tasting menu.The $500 price tag is a lot, yes, but it includes quite a bit of food.
