One of the most terrifying rescues Half Moon Bay has ever seen
-----
On a January evening in 2023, the sun had already set behind Half Moon Bay when lifeguards Maxwell Hunter and Riley Rhodes got the call. The current had sucked a man 300 yards off Poplar Beach. He was fighting for his life.At first, both questioned whether the call was real. Conditions were treacherous, with 8-foot swells thundering in at low tide and under fading light.
