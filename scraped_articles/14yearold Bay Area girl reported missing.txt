14-year-old Bay Area girl reported missing
-----
The Oakland Police Department is calling on Bay Area residents to help find a 14-year-old girl.Roxana Ramirez Jimenez was last seen just after 8:30 a.m. on Sept. 20 on the 4300 block of Broadway, near Oakland Technical High School, the police said in a news release issued Monday. Ramirez Jimenez has black hair and brown eyes, weighs about 100 pounds, and stands 5 feet tall.
