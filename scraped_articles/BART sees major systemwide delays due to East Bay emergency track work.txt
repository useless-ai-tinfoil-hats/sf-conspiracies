BART sees major systemwide delays due to East Bay emergency track work
-----
BART reported major systemwide delays Sunday evening due to emergency track work at Oakland stations.Green Line service, from Warm Springs/South Fremont to Daly City, was completely unavailable, while Blue Line service, from Dublin/Pleasanton to Daly City, was limited due to emergency track work between West Oakland, 12th Street and Lake Merritt stations, the agency said.
