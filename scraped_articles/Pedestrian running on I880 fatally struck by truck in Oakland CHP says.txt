Pedestrian running on I-880 fatally struck by truck in Oakland, CHP says
-----
A woman running on Interstate 880 in Oakland was fatally struck by a vehicle Tuesday night, the California Highway Patrol said.The CHP responded to a report of a pedestrian running in the northbound lanes of I-880, south of 66th Avenue, and a secondary report of a pedestrian colliding with a vehicle around 9:30 p.m.
When officers arrived at the scene, they found the pedestrian, a woman between 30 and 40 years old, with fatal injuries, according to CHP spokesperson Adib Zeid.
