S.F. police arrest person suspected of setting seven cars on fire
-----
San Francisco police arrested a suspect in connection to a string of seven recent vehicle fires over a single week in San Francisco, police said Monday.The man was arrested on Feb. 27 near Union Square after he was reported to have matched the description of the suspect who lit several vehicles on fire within the last month in the Union Square and South of Market neighborhoods, police said.
