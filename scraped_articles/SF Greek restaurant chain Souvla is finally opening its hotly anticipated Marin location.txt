SF Greek restaurant chain Souvla is finally opening its 'hotly anticipated' Marin location
-----
For Marin fans of Souvla, the wait is finally over: the popular Greek fast-casual chain plans to open its first location outside of San Francisco on Nov. 6.In early 2022, Souvla announced it would be opening at Marin Country Mart in Larkspur that summer. Things didn't quite pan out that way, but at last, the restaurant is in its home stretch.
