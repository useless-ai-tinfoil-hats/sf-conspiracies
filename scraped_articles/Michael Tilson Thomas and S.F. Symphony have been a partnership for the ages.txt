Michael Tilson Thomas and S.F. Symphony have been a partnership for the ages
-----
The San Francisco Symphony and Michael Tilson Thomas spent 50 years making music together, 25 of them with him as the orchestra's music director. For those of us who lived through this era, it can sometimes feel too easy to take this fact for granted, as if it were simply the natural course of events.But it wasn't. It was a gift, and an extraordinarily rare one.
