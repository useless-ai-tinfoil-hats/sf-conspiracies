Stanford’s Tara VanDerveer speaks out on NCAA three-point line failure: ‘Inexcusable’
-----
Days after Stanford lost to N.C. State in the Sweet 16, head coach Tara VanDerveer is speaking out about the disparate 3-point lines on the Moda Center floor in Portland, Ore., that were not corrected before the Cardinal played.The NCAA apologized on Monday for the error, and confirmed the 3-point line on one side of the court was short by 9 inches.
"What happened with the court in Portland is inexcusable and unfair to every team that played on it," VanDerveer said in a statement
