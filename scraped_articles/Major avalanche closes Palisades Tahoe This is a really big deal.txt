Major avalanche closes Palisades Tahoe: ‘This is a really big deal’
-----
Officials were searching for people injured or trapped by an avalanche at Palisades Tahoe Wednesday morning that prompted the closure of the popular ski resort.The avalanche occurred about 9:30 a.m. on the Palisades side of the Lake Tahoe resort, the ski resort said on X, formerly called Twitter. "Our patrol and mountain operations teams are performing a search at this time. Both sides of our mountain will be closed for the day," they said.
