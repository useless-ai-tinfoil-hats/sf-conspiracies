Person critically wounded after being set on fire in car in Oakland
-----
Oakland police found a person inside a car "fully engulfed" in flames early Thursday morning, and are investigating the incident as an attempted homicide, officials said Friday.Police were sent to the 1400 block of 35th Avenue after receiving reports of a person on fire. They found the person inside a vehicle that was also fully engulfed in flames and removed them.
Medical personnel took the person to a local hospital, where they were listed in critical condition, police said.
