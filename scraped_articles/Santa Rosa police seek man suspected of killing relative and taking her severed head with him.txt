Santa Rosa police seek man suspected of killing relative and taking her severed head with him
-----
Santa Rosa police said Friday that they were seeking a man suspected of killing a woman, decapitating her and fleeing with the severed head.Police said Luis Gustavo Aroyo-Lopez, 24, was suspected of killing the woman, whom he was related to. Police did not elaborate on the nature of their familial relationship.
