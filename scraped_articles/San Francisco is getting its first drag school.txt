San Francisco is getting its first drag school
-----
At a time when drag is under attack, San Francisco is getting its first school dedicated to the art form.The Stud Collective, the group behind the soon-to-reopen LGBTQ bar the Stud, is partnering with performing arts organization CounterPulse to create a school that will teach the fundamentals of drag performance and the history of San Francisco's unique, gender-punk drag legacy.
