Police: Driver fatally struck pedestrian in SF grocery store parking lot
-----
A driver fatally struck a pedestrian in the main parking lot at the entrance of Rainbow Grocery in San Francisco on Monday, officials said.The San Francisco Police Department said it responded to a report of a collision on the 1700 block of Folsom Street at 2:50 p.m.
