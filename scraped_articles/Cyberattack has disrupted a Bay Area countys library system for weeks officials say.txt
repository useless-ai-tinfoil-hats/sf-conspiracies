Cyberattack has disrupted a Bay Area county’s library system for weeks, officials say
-----
Solano County's library network systems have been down for more than two weeks due to a cyberattack that has disrupted phone lines, computer services and Wi-Fi at its branches — which remain open, officials said.The April 5 attack targeted the Solano Partner Libraries and St. Helena network, known as SPLASH, according to a news release by officials in St. Helena, in neighboring Napa County.
