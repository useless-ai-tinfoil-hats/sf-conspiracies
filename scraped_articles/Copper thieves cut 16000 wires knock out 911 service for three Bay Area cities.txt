Copper thieves cut 16,000 wires, knock out 911 service for three Bay Area cities
-----
Three Bay Area cities saw their 911 service knocked out for a week in early June when thieves cut 16,000 wires along an AT&T communication cable to steal 400 feet of copper wire, officials said.The 911 lines in Pinole, Hercules and San Pablo went down again on June 22 during an attempt to steal more copper.
