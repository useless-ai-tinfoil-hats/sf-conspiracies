Short of Giants signing Shohei Ohtani, what would take heat off Farhan Zaidi?
-----
The ghosts of Judges and Harpers past hang over this Winter Meetings for the San Francisco Giants, putting all the more pressure on top baseball executive Farhan Zaidi to get something major done in Nashville next week.There is increasing speculation that the Giants are among the frontrunners for Shohei Ohtani, a generational talent who could command $500 million or more, and signing him might improve any team's shot at adding another Japanese star, right-hander Yoshinobu Yamamoto.
