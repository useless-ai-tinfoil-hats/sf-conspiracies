Former Bay Area restaurant owner pleads guilty to wage theft
-----
A former Bay Area restaurant owner pleaded guilty to felony charges for wage theft and tax evasion, California Attorney General Rob Bonta said Wednesday.A state task force in 2017 began investigating David Leung, the onetime owner of Kome Japanese Seafood Buffet in Daly City, Tomi Japanese Seafood and Grill in San Jose and Tomi Japanese Seafood Buffet in Concord, officials said.
