Mark Farrell already leading in San Francisco mayor race, new poll shows
-----
Former San Francisco Interim Mayor Mark Farrell is polling ahead of each of his opponents, including incumbent Mayor London Breed, in the race to become the city's next top executive, according to a new poll from the San Francisco Chronicle.Though Farrell officially announced his mayoral run a little more than a week ago, the poll — released Thursday — shows that 20% of likely San Francisco voters would or probably would rank him first on their ranked-choice voting ballot.
