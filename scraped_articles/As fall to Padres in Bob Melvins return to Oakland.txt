A’s fall to Padres in Bob Melvin’s return to Oakland
-----
For one night, at least, Bob Melvin's San Diego Padres did not disappoint.The Padres jumped on the Oakland Athletics early and coasted to an 8-3 victory before 17,828 spectators Friday night. The game marked Melvin's first appearance at the Coliseum since he left the A's after the 2021 season.
Melvin guided the A's to six playoff appearances and an Oakland-record 853 wins in 10-plus seasons as manager.
San Diego scored two runs in the first inning and two more in the second
