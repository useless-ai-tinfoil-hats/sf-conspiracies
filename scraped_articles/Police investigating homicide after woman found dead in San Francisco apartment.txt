Police investigating homicide after woman found dead in San Francisco apartment
-----
An active homicide investigation is underway after a young woman was found dead inside her San Francisco apartment on Thursday night, authorities said.At approximately 7:12 p.m., police were dispatched to a residential building on the 3200 block of Clay Street in Presidio Heights for a wellness check. Police said that when they arrived, they discovered the woman, who was unresponsive. They summoned paramedics, but despite life-saving efforts, she was pronounced deceased at the scene.
