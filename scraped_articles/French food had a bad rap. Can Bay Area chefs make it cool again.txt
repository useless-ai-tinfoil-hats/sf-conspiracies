French food had a ‘bad rap.’ Can Bay Area chefs make it cool again?
-----
When tapped to relaunch former Michelin-starred restaurant Lord Stanley, chef Nathan Matkowsky thought long and hard about what to cook. He had just spent two years running a rotating, pandemic-era pop-up series, with chefs preparing cuisines from Hungarian to Chinese.But, ultimately — like many others in the Bay Area — Matkowsky elected to return to the indulgent, yet comforting, flavors of French cooking.
