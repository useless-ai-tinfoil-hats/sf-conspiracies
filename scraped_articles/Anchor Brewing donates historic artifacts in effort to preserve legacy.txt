Anchor Brewing donates historic artifacts in effort to preserve legacy
-----
Anchor Brewing Co. said Thursday that it donated several artifacts to the San Francisco Historical Society and the Smithsonian Institution in Washington in an effort to preserve the legacy of the nation's first craft brewery.According to a statement, Anchor — which closed in July after 127 years in operation — donated vintage signs, brewing equipment, artwork, pint glasses and other artifacts to the San Francisco Historical Society, which is planning an exhibit of Anchor items.
