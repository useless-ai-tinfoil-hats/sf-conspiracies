Expert judges select the best wines in North America
-----
The San Francisco Chronicle Wine Competition takes the evaluation of all wines entered into its competition very seriously, trusting only the best palates to taste and judge. During the third week of January, 52 judges from all aspects of the wine industry, including media, culinary and hospitality, education, winemaking and retail, gathered together to take part in a blind judging of more than 5,700 wine entries.
