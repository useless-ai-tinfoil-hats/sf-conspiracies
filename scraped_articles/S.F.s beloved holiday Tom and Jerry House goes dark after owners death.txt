S.F.’s beloved holiday ‘Tom and Jerry House’ goes dark after owner’s death
-----
In the wake of the recent death of its owner earlier this month, a beloved holiday landmark in San Francisco, known as the "Tom and Jerry House," is shrouded in darkness this year.Dr. Jerome "Jerry" Goldstein, an 82-year-old neurologist and gay rights advocate, who, alongside his late husband, Tom Taylor, had been creating vibrant Christmas displays for over three decades at their Noe Valley home, died on Nov. 15.
