Breed hopes to keep San Francisco's Ferris wheel at Fisherman's Wharf until 2025
-----
The enormous Ferris wheel on Fisherman's Wharf is nearing the end of its originally allotted time at its present home near Pier 43. Now, San Francisco Mayor London Breed is asking the Board of Supervisors to allow the wheel, known as the SkyStar Observation Wheel, to stay for another 18 months.The SkyStar was brought to the Music Concourse of San Francisco's Golden Gate Park in 2020 to celebrate the park's 150th anniversary.
