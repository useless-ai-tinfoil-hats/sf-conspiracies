A walk-off win for Willie: Giants score three in the ninth to beat Cubs
-----
The San Francisco Giants spent the last week paying homage to Willie Mays.Before Monday's first pitch, broadcasters Dave Flemming, Mike Krukow and Duane Kuiper were brought to tears telling their favorite stories about the Bay Area's own legend in an on-field ceremony. The organization brought the No. 24 out of retirement — every player, coach and staffer suited up and wore Mays' number on their backs — and projections of Mays' highlights blasted on the clock tower outside the gates.
