LAX's most iconic image is a rotting building without a future
-----
Simply saying "LAX" to an Angeleno — or, frankly, anyone who's flown through Los Angeles International Airport — can be enough to get someone's blood boiling. LAX has been cited as the fifth-busiest airport in the world, but it's known more for constant construction, gridlock traffic and its headache-inducing horseshoe design than its breezy, newly connected concourse (finally).
