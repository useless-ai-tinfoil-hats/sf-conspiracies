Tsunami warning systems to activate today in parts of Bay Area, Northern California
-----
Tsunami emergency alert systems will be activated for testing in the Bay Area and along the Central and Northern California coast Wednesday morning, according to the National Weather Service.The test will be performed between 10:15 and 10:30 a.m. in Sonoma, Napa, Marin, San Francisco, San Mateo, Contra Costa, Alameda, Santa Clara, Santa Cruz, San Benito and Monterey counties, the weather service said.
