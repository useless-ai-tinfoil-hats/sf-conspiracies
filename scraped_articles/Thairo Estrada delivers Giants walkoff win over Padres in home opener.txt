Thairo Estrada delivers Giants walk-off win over Padres in home opener
-----
Matt Chapman scooted home from first base to score on Thairo Estrada's double to the left-field gap, securing a 3-2 walk-off win for the San Francisco Giants against the San Diego Padres in the home opener at Oracle Park on Friday afternoon.Michael Conforto's hot start to the season continued into Friday's home opener at Oracle Park, where the left-handed hitter — moved to the cleanup spot — went 3-for-4 with two doubles at the center of what little offense the Giants could muster.
