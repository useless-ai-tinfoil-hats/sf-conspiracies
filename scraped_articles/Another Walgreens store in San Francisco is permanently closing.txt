Another Walgreens store in San Francisco is permanently closing
-----
Another Walgreens store in San Francisco — this one not far from Union Square — is closing for good next month.The store at 500 Geary St., at Taylor Street near the Curran Theater, will close on Nov. 15. When reached by phone Friday, an associate at the store said he thought shoplifting and lack of profit were the reasons for the impending closure. A store manager told the Chronicle that she was not authorized to comment.
