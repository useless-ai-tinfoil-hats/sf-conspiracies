Shutdown canceled for southbound I-680 over 3-day MLK holiday weekend
-----
The closure of a section of southbound Interstate 680 in the East Bay over the Martin Luther King Jr. Day holiday weekend has been postponed due to weather, officials said Wednesday.Caltrans had planned close all southbound lanes of I-680 between the Highway 580 interchange and Highway 84 for repaving from 9 p.m. Friday, Jan. 12, to 4 a.m. Tuesday, Jan. 16.
However, southbound I-680 will now remain open over the holiday weekend, the agency said in a news release.
