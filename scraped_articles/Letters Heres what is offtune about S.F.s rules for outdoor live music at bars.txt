Letters: Here’s what is off-tune about S.F.’s rules for outdoor live music at bars
-----
Regarding "Keeping San Francisco 'fun': City to cut red tape and fees for live music" (San Francisco, SFChronicle.com, Dec. 11): While it is great news that San Francisco is removing application fees so bars can more easily provide musical performances on their patios, this means that more performers will now be exposed to harmful secondhand smoke.Over 50 Bay Area cities protect workers, performers and patrons by requiring bar patios to be smoke-free, but San Francisco does not.
