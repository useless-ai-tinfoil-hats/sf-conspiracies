Review: S.F. Playhouse cracks ‘The Glass Menagerie’ open
-----
Crack open "The Glass Menagerie," and you might find a dreamscape inside. At San Francisco Playhouse, Tennessee Williams' 1944 masterpiece is like a series of nested worlds with the tops broken off.A cramped, bare-bones apartment rises like a tiny island center stage. When our narrator Tom Wingfield (Jomar Tagatac) jumps off it onto the stage floor, it's like he's plunging into the cold of the subconscious' ocean.
