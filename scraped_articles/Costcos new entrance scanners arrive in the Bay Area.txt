Costco's new entrance scanners arrive in the Bay Area
-----
The days of borrowing your mom's Costco membership card to shop are numbered.Costco has officially rolled out membership card scanners at the main entrances of some Bay Area stores in an effort to help deter non-members from shopping at its warehouses. The new scanners, which have already popped up throughout California, will require customers to scan their physical or digital membership cards at the entrance to confirm they are current members.
