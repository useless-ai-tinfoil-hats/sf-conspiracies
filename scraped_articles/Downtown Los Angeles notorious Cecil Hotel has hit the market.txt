Downtown Los Angeles' notorious Cecil Hotel has hit the market
-----
A notorious and historic downtown Los Angeles hotel, beset by murder, suicide and mystery over its 97 years, is for sale.Cecil Hotel has been listed by developer Simon Baron for an undisclosed price, reports The Real Deal. After being converted from a hotel to homeless housing in 2019, the building now functions as a low-income apartment building, according to the listing on LoopNet.
