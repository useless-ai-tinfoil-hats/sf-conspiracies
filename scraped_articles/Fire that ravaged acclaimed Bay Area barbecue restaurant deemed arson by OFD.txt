Fire that ravaged acclaimed Bay Area barbecue restaurant deemed arson by OFD
-----
Oakland Fire Department investigators have found evidence that the November 2023 fire that destroyed Michelin-recognized Bay Area restaurant Horn Barbecue was the result of arson, the department announced yesterday. The news was first confirmed by Eater SF."Evidence found at the scene indicates that the fire was intentionally set," said Michael Hunt, chief of staff of the Oakland Fire Department, in a statement to SFGATE. "The fire is being investigated as an arson fire."
