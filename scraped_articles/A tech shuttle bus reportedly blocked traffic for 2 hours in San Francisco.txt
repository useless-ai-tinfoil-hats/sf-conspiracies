A tech shuttle bus reportedly blocked traffic for 2 hours in San Francisco
-----
On Tuesday evening, a shuttle bus for tech workers blocked a busy street in San Francisco's Noe Valley for a full two hours, SFist reported.The Van Hool-branded bus stalled on its way up 24th Street just before the intersection with Dolores Street, per the report. Though the drivers of smaller cars could skirt around the stopped vehicle, SFist wrote that when another shuttle bus arrived, the two "hulking behemoths" blocked traffic in both directions.
