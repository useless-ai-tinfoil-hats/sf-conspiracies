Giants’ road game Saturday against Phillies delayed by rain
-----
PHILADELPHIA — The San Francisco Giants' game against the Philadelphia Phillies on Saturday is in a rain delay from the scheduled 3:05 p.m. PT start. The Phillies announced a start time of 4:15 p.m. PT (7:15 p.m. ET).The two teams are set to play Game 2 of this four game series at Citizens Bank Park in Philadelphia, where it's been chilly and wet most of the day.
The Giants lost Game 1, 4-3, on Friday night.
