Former 49ers star Terrell Owens' son to sign with San Francisco as an undrafted free agent
-----
Terique Owens, the son of former 49ers star wide receiver Terrell Owens, is signing with San Francisco as an undrafted free agent, according to multiple reports.Boston Herald reporter Andrew Callahan was the first to report that the younger Owens will sign with the 49ers.
Terique Owens was one of four sons of famous 49ers who could have ended up having their names called during the NFL Draft.
Terique Owens was born in Fremont while his dad Terrell played in San Francisco.
