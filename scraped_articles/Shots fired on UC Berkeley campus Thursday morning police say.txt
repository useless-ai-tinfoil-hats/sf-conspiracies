Shots fired on UC Berkeley campus Thursday morning, police say
-----
The University of California Police Department said multiple local law enforcement agencies are searching for a man suspected of firing shots at the Clark Kerr residence hall track on the UC Berkeley campus Thursday morning.The suspect is described as a man with "bleached" hair and possibly red or orange highlights in a message issued through the university's WarnMe emergency alert service. Witnesses to the shooting also told police that the suspect was wearing a black shirt, black pants
