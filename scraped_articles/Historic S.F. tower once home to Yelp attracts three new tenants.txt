Historic S.F. tower once home to Yelp attracts three new tenants
-----
The nearly century-old Art Deco tower that once housed Yelp's headquarters has attracted three new tenants, showing that there's still demand for high-end downtown San Francisco offices in the age of remote work.A total of 55,913 square feet in new leases have been signed at 140 New Montgomery St. by artificial intelligence data firm Super{set}, scheduling software company Calendly and executive hiring firm Heidrick & Struggles.
