United flight from Boston to San Francisco makes an unscheduled stop in Denver
-----
A United Airlines flight to San Francisco from Boston was diverted to Denver International Airport on Tuesday due to a suspected mechanical issue.United Flight 625, operated with a Boeing 737 Max 9, changed course five hours after its 6:34 a.m. departure from Boston Logan International Airport, according to aviation records.
