Luxury California pet kennel, Wag Hotels, accused of neglect and abuse
-----
After numerous allegations of neglect and cruelty toward animals in its care, luxury California pet-boarding facility Wag Hotels is facing a class-action lawsuit alleging negligence and false advertising. The case was brought by three former Wag customers, Michelle Shelton and Alison and Jeffrey Wisdom."Wag's negligence and misconduct caused at least dozens of pets to contract life-threatening infections and other serious health conditions," the complaint, reviewed by SFGATE, alleges.
