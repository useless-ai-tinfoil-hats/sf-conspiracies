Nine injured, one killed in fiery 12-vehicle crash in Santa Rosa
-----
One person died after a dump truck crashed into a line of 11 vehicles on Highway 12 in Santa Rosa on Thursday, according to the California Highway Patrol.Just before 12 p.m., a dump truck traveling westbound on Highway 12 hit a Toyota Tundra pickup truck that was stopped at a red light near Fulton Road, according to CHP public information officer David deRutte.
