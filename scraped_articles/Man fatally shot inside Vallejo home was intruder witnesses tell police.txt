Man fatally shot inside Vallejo home was intruder, witnesses tell police
-----
A man who broke into a Vallejo home was fatally shot Monday morning, police said.Around 9:43 a.m., officers responded to a call for service on the 1000 block of Georgia Street. Officers arrived to find a man suffering from at least one gunshot wound. He was pronounced dead at the scene by the Vallejo Fire Department. His identity is still being withheld, pending notification of his family, police said.
