Cisco to lay off 350 South Bay employees in October
-----
Cisco Systems will permanently lay off hundreds of workers in the South Bay next month, according to state filings.The San Jose-based tech company will be laying off 227 workers in its San Jose office and another 123 workers in its Milpitas office, according to a WARN notice filed last week. The layoffs will take effect Oct. 16, based on the company's filing.
