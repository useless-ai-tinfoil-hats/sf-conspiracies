Season of Sharing helps former execs stunned to find themselves in a food line
-----
Friends Debra and Teresa never expected they'd be in line at a food bank. But on a recent Friday morning in Petaluma, that's exactly where they were."Both of us used to be executives, and now we're standing in a food line," Debra said.
"I was always a volunteer when I lived in San Francisco," Teresa said. "It's very unusual being on the other side."
