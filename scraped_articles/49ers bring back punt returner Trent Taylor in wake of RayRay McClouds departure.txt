The San Francisco 49ers might not have found their punt returner. But they at least added a legitimate candidate for the job Tuesday.The 49ers signed a familiar face, wide receiver Trent Taylor, who spent his first four seasons in Santa Clara after he was a fifth-round pick in 2017. Taylor, 29, has since made stops with the Bengals (2021-2022) and Bears (2023), becoming best known for his punt-returning skills. He has eight receptions and 63 punt returns the past three seasons.
