California company Riot Games lays off 530 workers with apologetic email
-----
Riot Games, the West Los Angeles company behind hits like "League of Legends" and "Valorant," is laying off 530 workers.CEO Dylan Jadeja told employees about the 11% layoff round in a Monday afternoon email now published to Riot's website. The executive, who has led the company for less than a year, took an apologetic tone but defended the decision as necessary.
