California weather: More thunderstorms, snow expected from unusually cold April storm
-----
An unusually cold April low-pressure system moved across the Bay Area on Thursday, dropping snow down to 1,500 feet and hail to the coast and valleys. On Friday, the storm will continue to move south, popping thunderstorms up between San Jose and San Diego, with accumulating snow as low as 3,000 feet.High temperatures will be as much as 20 degrees below normal in Southern California, in the 50s at the coast and 30s in the mountains.
