The party’s over: 49ers’ Kyle Shanahan turns his attention to Chiefs
-----
On Sunday night, in the afterglow of his team's emotional season-saving win, San Francisco 49ers head coach Kyle Shanahan was asked to look ahead.His thoughts on a Super Bowl rematch against the Chiefs?
"None," Shanahan said. "Going to have a lot of fun tonight probably with my family and everything. Come in a little bit slow motion tomorrow."
