Two hikers found dead on Mount Whitney after 11-hour search
-----
The bodies of two hikers were found on Mount Whitney after an 11-hour search, officials said Thursday.The two were reported missing at approximately 8:30 p.m. Tuesday after they failed to return to their campsite, the Inyo County Sheriff's Office said on social media.
