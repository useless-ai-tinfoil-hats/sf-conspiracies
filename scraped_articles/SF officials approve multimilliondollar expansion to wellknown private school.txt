SF officials approve multimillion-dollar expansion to well-known private school
-----
The San Francisco Planning Commission unanimously approved plans on Thursday for a multimillion-dollar expansion at a well-known college prep school.St. Ignatius College Preparatory School, located in San Francisco's Outer Sunset neighborhood, initially filed plans for the expansion, which it dubbed the "New Learning Commons" and describes as "the most ambitious project" in its history, in late 2022.
