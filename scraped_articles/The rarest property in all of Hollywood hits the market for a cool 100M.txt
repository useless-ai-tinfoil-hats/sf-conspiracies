The rarest property in all of Hollywood hits the market for a cool $100M
-----
According to Variety, owner Elie Samaha has put Yamashiro, Los Angeles' historic, 110-year-old Hollywood estate, up for sale after years of ownership — and it's not just the restaurant itself.For the sum of $100 million, a buyer would receive more than7 acres of land situated between the Hollywood Bowl, the Magic Castle, touristy Hollywood & Highland and the wealthy Hollywood Hills — home to movie stars and business titans.
