200 kids, dogs and Mickeys a day: Inside Disneyland's Silhouette Studio
-----
If you're not looking for it as you stroll down Main Street U.S.A. (or, say, run for a first pickle award), you might miss it altogether. Only one little window reveals what's inside the tiny shop, which is hardly even a shop at all. There's no Disneyland merch, no ears, no spirit jerseys. The only things you can buy in the Silhouette Studio are memories.People line up to get them, maybe for an hour or more.
