Popular Napa County vacation spot could get a ‘Camp Margaritaville’ resort
-----
The late singer Jimmy Buffett's brand of easygoing escapism could one day lure vacationers to Lake Berryessa, a Napa County reservoir just two hours north of San Francisco.County supervisors granted developers working with Buffett's hospitality firm Margaritaville (named after the singer's most famous song) exclusive rights to explore building a resort with cabins and RVs dubbed — what else? — Camp Margaritaville.
