Napa County police officer shot fleeing suspect in the back, surveillance video shows
-----
An American Canyon police officer shot a fleeing armed suspect in the back seconds after he appeared to disarm himself of two guns, surveillance video released Wednesday by the Napa County District Attorney's Office to a local news agency shows.The 12-second video, obtained by the Vallejo Sun under a public records request, shows 18-year-old Demarea Vaughn Rogers III running away from American Canyon police officer Josh Coleman on Jan. 11.
