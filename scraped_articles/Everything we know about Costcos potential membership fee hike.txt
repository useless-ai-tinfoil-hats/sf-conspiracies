Everything we know about Costco's potential membership fee hike
-----
Every five to six years, Costco raises its membership fees, which means that a new fee hike may be on its way soon.In a Q1 earnings call, chief financial officer Richard Galanti confirmed that a new fee hike is coming, though he stopped short of providing a date.
"I'll use my standby answer, my pat answer: It's a question of when, not if," Galanti said.
