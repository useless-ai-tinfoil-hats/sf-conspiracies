Calif. woman returns 2-year-old couch to Costco
-----
In a viral TikTok video that has garnered 2.9 million views, a California woman returned a couch to Costco two-and-a-half years after she bought it, causing a storm of Internet chatter over what might be the most lenient return policy in all of retail.Jackie Nguyen, who made the video in Seattle but whose profile says she is based in California, posted a video on Jan. 23 that shows her process when returning the dark gray sectional, which viewers see strapped to the back of a truck.
