Review: Jessica Chastain and Peter Sarsgaard connect in quiet, powerful ‘Memory’
-----
"Memory" brings together Jessica Chastain and Peter Sarsgaard, two actors who are very different in obvious ways — Chastain an irresistible force and Sarsgaard an immovable object. She may seem completely knowable on a first encounter, while he's someone who always makes you wonder what he's thinking.
