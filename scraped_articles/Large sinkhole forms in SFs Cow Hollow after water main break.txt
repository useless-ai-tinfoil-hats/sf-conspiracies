Large sinkhole forms in SF's Cow Hollow after water main break
-----
LATEST Sept. 11, 2:15 p.m. A large sinkhole consumed a section of Fillmore Street in San Francisco's Cow Hollow neighborhood on Monday, close to an area where a water main broke late Sunday night, the SF Public Utilities Commission said.The break occurred at Fillmore and Green streets, flooding the area with water and debris. Some street-level businesses were inundated by water.
