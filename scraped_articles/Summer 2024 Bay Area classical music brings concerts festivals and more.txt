Summer 2024: Bay Area classical music brings concerts, festivals and more
-----
Summer is nearly here, and whether it's foggy or sunny, it's a season in which the Bay Area's musical organizations offer a wealth of classical music, new, old, and in between. From Rohnert Park to Santa Cruz, here's a taste of what you can hear from June to August.San Francisco Opera
San Francisco Opera presents the U.S. premiere of the late Finnish composer Kaija Saariaho's last opera, "Innocence," a complex, multilingual recounting of a school shooting and its aftermath.
