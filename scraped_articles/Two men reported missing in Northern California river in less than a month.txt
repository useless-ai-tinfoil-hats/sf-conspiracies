Two men reported missing in Northern California river in less than a month
-----
The whereabouts of a 23-year-old Northern California man remain unknown after he was last seen swimming in the Trinity River on Wednesday evening, the second person to vanish in the 165-mile tributary in less than a month.Authorities were called to Kimtu Beach, just a few miles east of Willow Creek, at around 6 p.m. after receiving reports of a possible drowning in the area. Witnesses said the man, identified as Jeshua Wilkinson of Arcata, "showed signs of distress before disappearing
