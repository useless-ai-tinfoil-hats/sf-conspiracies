Outside Lands has announced the lineup for Dolores', the festival's self-proclaimed "DIY queer dance club."The open-air venue on Polo Field debuted at last year's festival and this year's lineup of artists, DJs and Drag performers has been curated once again by Fake and Gay, Oasis nightclub and daytime dance party Hard French. The bill includes Honduran pop singer Isabella Lovestory, winner of Season 14 of RuPaul's Drag Race Willow Pill and electronic dance music DJ Carlos Souffront.
