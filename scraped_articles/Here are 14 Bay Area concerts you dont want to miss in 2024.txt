Here are 14 Bay Area concerts you don’t want to miss in 2024
-----
A variety of talent spanning genres and decades is scheduled to hit the Bay Area in 2024 for an exciting year of live music.Whether it's artists touring in support of new material, like Olivia Rodrigo and Green Day, or those circling back to highly anticipated rescheduled shows, such as Madonna and Bruce Springsteen, there's something for everyone's concert calendar next year — and that's not including the annual string of local music festivals, from Outside Lands to BottleRock Napa
