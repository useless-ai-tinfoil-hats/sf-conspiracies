COVID outbreak at S.F. jails sickens dozens
-----
San Francisco sheriff's officials said Wednesday that more than three dozen people incarcerated in city jails were being isolated due to a "COVID outbreak."Forty-two incarcerated people tested positive for the coronavirus, sheriff's officials said in a statement. The highest concentration of positive cases were recorded in County Jail 3, which is located in San Bruno and is the city jail system's largest facility.
