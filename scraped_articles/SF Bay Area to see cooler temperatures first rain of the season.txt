SF Bay Area to see cooler temperatures, first rain of the season
-----
It's beginning to feel like autumn around the San Francisco Bay Area.Temperatures are running a tad below normal, and the first rainstorm of the season, an atmospheric river, is expected to bring rain to the region Sunday night into Monday.
"We're getting chillier nights," said Dalton Behringer, a forecaster with the National Weather Service's Bay Area office in Monterey. "The nights are longer. There's not as much solar energy to heat things up."
