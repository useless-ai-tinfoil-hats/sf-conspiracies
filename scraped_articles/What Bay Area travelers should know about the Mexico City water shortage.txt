What Bay Area travelers should know about the Mexico City water shortage
-----
One of the world's biggest cities — and a popular destination for Bay Area travelers — may be on the verge of running out of water.Mexico City is in the grips of a severe drought, exacerbated by climate change, causing an unprecedented strain on the city's water supply, according to a CNN report. Residents are already feeling effects of the shortage, with lower-income neighborhoods hit the hardest. In some neighborhoods, residents can go days without any water coming out of the taps, CNN
