Entire town of Paradise under evacuation warning as California's Park Fire rages
-----
As California's Park Fire continues to rage, the entire town of Paradise has been given an evacuation warning, according to a town news release issued on Friday night.An evacuation warning is different from an evacuation order. A warning means that residents should prepare to leave and that residents who think they'll need more time should begin evacuating. An evacuation order means that all residents should leave immediately.
