California radio station 96.1 KKXS drops SF Giants, 49ers for Elvis, oldies on 96 KTO
-----
A Northern California radio station suddenly switched its format from sports to oldies this week.For 14 years, 96.1 KKXS was one of Redding's sports FM stations, broadcasting 49ers and Giants games as an affiliate of San Francisco's KNBR. However, the same week the 49ers lost the Super Bowl, the station stopped broadcasting sports entirely.
