Kevin McCarthy backs Trump, insults DeSantis after months of waffling
-----
House Speaker Kevin McCarthy said Sunday that he believes Donald Trump will be the Republican nominee for president, winding down a monthslong saga in which the California lawmaker repeatedly refused to endorse the former president.During an interview with Fox News over the weekend, McCarthy was asked about Trump's massive lead in early primary polls, and for his thoughts on whether the former president would lock up the party's nomination. He replied, "I think he will be the nominee.
