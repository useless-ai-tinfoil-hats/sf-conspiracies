World-famous ramen chain Kajiken set to open on the way to Tahoe
-----
A world-renowned Japanese specialty ramen restaurant, which opened its first Bay Area location in February 2023, is slated to open a new outpost in Northern California later this year.Kajiken, a noodle house specializing in abura soba, is coming to a Sacramento-area shopping center in "three to four months," according to the Sacramento Bee. The Japanese franchise, which has over 100 outlets worldwide, will be located in the Ridge shopping complex in Elk Grove.
