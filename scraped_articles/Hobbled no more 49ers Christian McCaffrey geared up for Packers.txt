Hobbled no more, 49ers’ Christian McCaffrey ‘geared up’ for Packers
-----
After two weeks off, Christian McCaffrey is a full go.The San Francisco 49ers running back returned to practice Tuesday for the first time since he suffered a strained calf on Dec. 31 in a win against the Commanders. McCaffrey practiced without limitations and will play against the Packers on Saturday night in the divisional playoffs.
