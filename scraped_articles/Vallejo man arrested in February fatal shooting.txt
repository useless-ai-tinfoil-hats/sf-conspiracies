Vallejo man arrested in February fatal shooting
-----
A Vallejo man has been arrested on suspicion of shooting and killing another man in February, police said Thursday.Ethan Menefee, 26, voluntarily surrendered to police on Wednesday and was booked into the Solano County Jail, according to the Vallejo Police Department. The shooting occurred on Feb. 4.
