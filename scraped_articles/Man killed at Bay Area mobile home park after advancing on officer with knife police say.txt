Man killed at Bay Area mobile home park after advancing on officer with knife, police say
-----
A police officer shot and killed a man at a mobile home park in Sunnyvale on Saturday, authorities said.Officers were called to a mobile park home at 1225 Vienna Drive at about 5:30 p.m. for a report of a man walking around with a knife in his hand, the Sunnyvale Department of Public Safety said in a news release.
