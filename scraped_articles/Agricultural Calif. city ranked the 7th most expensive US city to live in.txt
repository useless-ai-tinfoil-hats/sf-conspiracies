Agricultural Calif. city ranked the 7th most expensive US city to live in
-----
The largely working class city of Salinas, California, is the seventh most expensive U.S. city to live in, according to a new U.S. News & World Report list.The list places Salinas just behind San Francisco, which sits in sixth place, and ahead of New York City, ranked 11th.
