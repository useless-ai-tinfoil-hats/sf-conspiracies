Another OpenAI employee resigns, third departure since last week
-----
Another OpenAI employee has resigned from the San Francisco-based tech company over safety concerns.Gretchen Krueger, a policy research employee at the company, announced Wednesday on social media that she is leaving the artificial intelligence company. Krueger said she notified the company of her impending departure on May 14, just hours before former OpenAI chief scientist Ilya Sutskever announced he was leaving the company to pursue an unnamed project.
