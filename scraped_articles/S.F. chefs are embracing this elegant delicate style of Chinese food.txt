S.F. chefs are embracing this elegant, delicate style of Chinese food
-----
Despite having one of the largest and oldest Chinatowns in North America, San Francisco's Cantonese food scene hasn't evolved much over the past 150 years.Now, that's starting to change with new pop-ups and revamped menus at existing spots. Together, these chefs are gently bridging Bay Area diners to a new normal for Cantonese cuisine.
