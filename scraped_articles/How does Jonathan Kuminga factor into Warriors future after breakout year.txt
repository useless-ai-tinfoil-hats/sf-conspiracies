How does Jonathan Kuminga factor into Warriors’ future after breakout year?
-----
Pinned on Dec. 6 to the Golden State Warriors' bench was Jonathan Kuminga, wondering whether he was destined for his first DNP of 2023-24.The Warriors were losing to the Portland Trail Blazers by 11 points through the first 31 minutes of their Friday night matchup — until head coach Steve Kerr summoned Kuminga to the scorer's table, unknowingly manifesting the third-year forward's favorite segment of the season.
Dunk. Layup. Layup. Dunk. Layup. Dunk.
Comeback complete.
