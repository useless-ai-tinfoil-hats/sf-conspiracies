Giants past, present pay tribute to franchise icon Brandon Crawford
-----
Brandon Crawford is winding down his 13th and possibly last season for the San Francisco Giants. The most decorated shortstop in franchise history will come off the injured list Sunday and plans to play the season finale.It'll be an emotional day for Crawford, his family, his teammates and his fans.
