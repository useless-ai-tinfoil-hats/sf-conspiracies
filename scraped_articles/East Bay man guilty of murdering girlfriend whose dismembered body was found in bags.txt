East Bay man guilty of murdering girlfriend, whose dismembered body was found in bags
-----
An Alameda County jury on Monday found a man guilty of second-degree murder in the death of his girlfriend, whose body was dismembered.Joseph Roberts, 43, faces 15 years in state prison after the jury convicted him in the death of Rachel Elizabeth Imani Buckner. He is scheduled to be sentenced on June 14.
The gruesome nature of Buckner's death and Roberts' controversial entanglements in politics and the #MeToo movement drew widespread publicity.
