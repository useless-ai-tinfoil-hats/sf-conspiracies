'We were bullied': Popular downtown Oakland queer bar, Port Bar, facing eviction
-----
Port Bar, one of downtown Oakland's most frequented queer nightlife haunts, is facing eviction due to a conflict between its owners and the landlord of the building it occupies.Lawyers representing Phil Leong, Port Bar's landlord, put in writing that the bar must cease its weekly programming — drag shows, karaoke and trivia nights and DJ performances — or face eviction by 2024, Port Bar's co-owner Sean Sullivan told SFGATE.
