South S.F. police locate missing 15-year-old girl
-----
Police have located a missing 15-year-old girl who was last seen in San Francisco.South San Francisco Police first reached out to the public on Wednesday afternoon seeking help finding the girl, whose last known location was around McLaren Park. Officials described her as an "at-risk runaway."
Police announced Wednesday night that she was located, and thanked the public for their assistance. No information was provided about the girl's whereabouts or condition.
