Steph Curry and Draymond Green sit as Warriors prep for play-in tourney
-----
Stephen Curry and Draymond Green are inactive Sunday for the Golden State Warriors' finale against the Utah Jazz, thereby ending their regular season ahead of the four-team play-in tournament.Speaking pregame, head coach Steve Kerr announced Curry (right ankle sprain) and Green (right knee contusion) would not play against the Jazz. Joining them on the sideline was reserve guard Gary Payton II, who missed the final three games with a left calf strain.
