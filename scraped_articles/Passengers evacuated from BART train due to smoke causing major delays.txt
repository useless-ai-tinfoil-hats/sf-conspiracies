Passengers evacuated from BART train due to smoke, causing major delays
-----
BART service was experiencing "major delays" Monday afternoon after smoke was reported on a train that stopped near Oakland's MacArthur Station, agency officials said.BART officials confirmed the delay just after 2:30 p.m. on social media, saying service was impacted in the Antioch, Berryessa, Richmond, SFO and Millbrae directions.
