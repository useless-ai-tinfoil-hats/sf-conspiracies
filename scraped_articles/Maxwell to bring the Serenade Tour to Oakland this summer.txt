Maxwell to bring the Serenade Tour to Oakland this summer
-----
After 15 years, Maxwell and Jazmine Sullivan plan to reunite on tour.Sullivan, a two-time Grammy-winning R&B singer known for her 2008 hit "Bust Your Windows," is slated as a special guest on Maxwell's 26-stop North American tour, which is scheduled to arrive at Oakland Arena on Oct. 25. The vocal powerhouse, whose most recent album was 2021's "Heaux Tales," previously toured with Maxwell during his 2008 tour.
