Man arrested after bicyclist hit pedestrian in SF's North Beach, causing severe injuries
-----
A man was arrested after a bicyclist hit a pedestrian Wednesday afternoon in San Francisco's North Beach neighborhood and fled the scene.The collision happened at the intersection of Broadway and Columbus Avenue, Paulina Henderson, a spokesperson for the San Francisco Police Department, told SFGATE. After the crash, she said, police responded at about 3:15 p.m. and the pedestrian was taken to the hospital with "life threatening injuries."
