49ers sign WR Terique Owens, son of Terrell Owens, as undrafted free agent
-----
San Francisco 49ers general manager John Lynch recently had flashbacks when he saw a wide receiver running in his direction at the team's local pro day.And there was a reason for that: The wideout, Central Missouri's Terique Owens, is the son of Terrell Owens, the Pro Football Hall of Famer who played his first eight seasons with the 49ers. Lynch, a Hall of Fame safety, had seen the elder Owens running in his direction throughout his career.
