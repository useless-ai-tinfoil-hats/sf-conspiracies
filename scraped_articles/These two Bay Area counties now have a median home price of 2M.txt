These two Bay Area counties now have a median home price of $2M
-----
As housing affordability makes headlines in California and its wealthiest cities continue to fight building more housing, two Bay Area counties have hit a predictable milestone: The median price for sold homes there has climbed to more than $2 million.Median home prices have edged up this spring in both San Mateo County and Santa Clara County, according to the California Association of Realtors report for April.
