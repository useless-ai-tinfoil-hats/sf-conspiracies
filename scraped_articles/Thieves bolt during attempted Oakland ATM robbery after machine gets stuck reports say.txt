Thieves bolt during attempted Oakland ATM robbery after machine gets stuck, reports say
-----
Would-be burglars drove a car through the entrance of a grocery store in East Oakland on Wednesday morning in a failed attempt to steal an ATM, according to police officials and online reports.Officers responded to a report of a burglary just after 5 a.m. at a business in the 3000 block of East Ninth Avenue, the Oakland Police Department said in a statement. Individuals drove a car through the entrance to try to take the ATM before fleeing, police said.
