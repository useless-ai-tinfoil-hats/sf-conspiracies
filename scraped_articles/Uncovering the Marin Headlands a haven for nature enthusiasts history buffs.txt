Uncovering the Marin Headlands, a haven for nature enthusiasts, history buffs
-----
For many people who live in Marin, the Headlands (as the Marin Headlands are commonly called) are both their backyard playground and a heavily-trafficked tourist destination. While some use the term "the Marin Headlands" simply to refer to the hills right across from the Golden Gate Bridge on the Marin County side, the Headlands actually cover the entire hilly section of the Golden Gate National Recreation Area.
