Coastal Calif. sheriffs seek stolen pew from Sea Ranch Chapel
-----
Authorities in a Bay Area county are actively searching for a redwood pew that was stolen from a cherished chapel along the California coast.The Sea Ranch Chapel Foundation, the sanctuary's caretaker, reported the missing three-seated bench to the Sonoma County Sheriff's Office sometime after Nov. 28, when it was last seen in the Sea Ranch Chapel.
