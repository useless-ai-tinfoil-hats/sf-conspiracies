This Joan Baez song influenced star Rachel Zegler's performance in the ‘Hunger Games’
-----
One of the most out-of-left-field influences on the latest "Hunger Games" movie turns out to be none other than Bay Area folk singing legend Joan Baez.Rachel Zegler, the star of "The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes" that opens Friday, Nov. 17, revealed at a virtual press conference from Los Angeles on Sunday, Nov. 12, that she pumped Baez's music through her headphones daily before she was called to set.
