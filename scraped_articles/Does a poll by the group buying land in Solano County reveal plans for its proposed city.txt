Does a poll by the group buying land in Solano County reveal plans for its proposed city?
-----
Millions of olive trees and a "new oak forest"? Sheep that graze underneath solar panels? Stadiums for minor league soccer and baseball teams? Major employers that would be within walking distance of housing? A performing arts center attracting nationally known acts?These are some of the tantalizing possibilities teased in a poll released by the tech moguls behind California Forever, the initiative to build a new city on 50,000 acres of agricultural land between Fairfield and Rio Vista
