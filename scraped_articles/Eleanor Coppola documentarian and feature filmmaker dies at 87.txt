Eleanor Coppola, documentarian and feature filmmaker, dies at 87
-----
Eleanor Coppola — wife of Francis Ford Coppola, mother of Sofia Coppola and an estimable filmmaker in her own right — died at her home in the Napa County town of Rutherford on Friday, April 12. She was 87 years old. No cause of death was given.Eleanor was already involved in filmmaking when she met her husband, serving as assistant art director on the set of "Dementia 13" (1963), Francis' first film.
