Oakland's First Fridays announces temporary closure due to financial constraints
-----
The organization that runs First Fridays, Oakland's most popular monthly street fair, has announced that the event will not operate between January and March 2024 due to financial constraints.Rising event costs and the lingering effects of the pandemic, among other factors, have created a cost-to-profit ratio that just isn't sustainable, First Fridays' accounts manager Venessa McGhee told SFGATE.
