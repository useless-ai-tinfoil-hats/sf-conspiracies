Extremely rare 'firenado' spotted over California's Park Fire
-----
A rare and astonishing form of extreme fire behavior was spotted at the Park Fire on Thursday evening as the enormous blaze rips through forestland north of Chico. Looming hundreds of feet above the ground, a whirling pyrotornado was caught by a wildfire camera on Platte Mountain in Butte County.As UCLA climate scientist Daniel Swain explained to SFGATE, "fire whirls" aren't uncommon.
