Don Horsley, former San Mateo County sheriff and supervisor, dies at 80
-----
Former San Mateo County Sheriff and Supervisor Don Horsley died Friday morning at age 80, according to the county sheriff's office.Horsley was a San Francisco native who graduated from San Francisco State University in 1969, according to the biography he submitted to voters when he first ran for the Board of Supervisors.
