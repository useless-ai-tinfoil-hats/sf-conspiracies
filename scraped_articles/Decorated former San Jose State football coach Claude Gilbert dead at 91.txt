Decorated former San Jose State football coach Claude Gilbert dead at 91
-----
Claude Gilbert, a running back at San Jose State in the late 1950s who became an assistant coach and head coach for the Spartans in the 1980s, died Jan. 6 from lung cancer. He was 91.Gilbert is the only football player to earn a letter with the Spartans, be a team captain and then serve as the team's assistant coach and head coach.
