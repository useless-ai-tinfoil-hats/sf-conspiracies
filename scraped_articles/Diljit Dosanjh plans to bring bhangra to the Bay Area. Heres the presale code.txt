Diljit Dosanjh plans to bring bhangra to the Bay Area. Here's the presale code
-----
Bay Area bhangra lovers are in for a treat in the spring.Punjabi trailblazer Diljit Dosanjh is scheduled to perform at the Oakland Arena on May 11 as part of his Dil-Luminati Tour in support of his 14th studio album "Ghost," which was released in September. The local date is one of only two California stops on the 13-city run; the other is Crypto Arena in Los Angeles on July 10.
Ticket presales start at 10 a.m. Thursday, Feb. 29, with the password DILJIT. General sales begin at 10
