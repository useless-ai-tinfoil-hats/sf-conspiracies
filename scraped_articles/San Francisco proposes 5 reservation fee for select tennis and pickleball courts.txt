San Francisco proposes $5 reservation fee for select tennis and pickleball courts
-----
San Francisco's Recreation and Park Commission has unanimously approved a resolution that could soon require residents to pay a $5 reservation fee at certain tennis and pickleball locations.The agency said the initiative aims to streamline court management and ensure fair access to facilities citywide. It is now awaiting approval from the San Francisco Board of Supervisors.
