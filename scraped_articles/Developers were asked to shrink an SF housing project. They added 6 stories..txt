Developers were asked to shrink an SF housing project. They added 6 stories.
-----
The developers in charge of a residential development in San Francisco's Telegraph Hill neighborhood have submitted new plans for the project, changing its proposed height from 10 stories to 16.San Francisco-based Aralon Properties submitted revised plans for the project on Tuesday, the San Francisco Business Times reported. The development would replace an existing two-story parking garage at 955 Sansome St. with a 16-story, 102-unit residential building.
