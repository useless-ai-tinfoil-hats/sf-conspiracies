East Bay man accused of killing his girlfriend while out on bail in attempted murder case
-----
Locked out of the Fremont liquor store, the gunman rammed a car into the storefront. Then he walked in and shot his girlfriend, police said.Krishan Sharma, 38, was charged with murder in Alameda County Superior Court in the July shooting of 35-year-old Pooja Naidu, of Fremont. On Monday, a plea hearing was scheduled for Sept. 12.
The Alameda County Public Defender's Office, which represents Sharma, declined to comment.
