Bay Area police: Man in custody after firing shot toward officers
-----
A man was taken into custody Sunday after a standoff with San Jose police that unfolded after he threw items out of a window and fired at least one round "in the direction of officers," police said.Officers took the man into custody after he jumped out of a bedroom window around 9:45 a.m., about five hours after the ordeal started, police said.
The standoff unfolded after police were sent to investigate reports of a man breaking glass and throwing items out of an apartment window
