Gavin Newsom increases state police in Oakland to address rising crime
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom is sending 120 additional CHP officers to Oakland and the East Bay to crack down on crime, including carjacking, retail theft and violence.The added officers will represent a ninefold increase in the number of state police patrolling the region, including those already assigned before the surge in crime, according to Newsom's office. The officers will be concentrated in Oakland, where violent crime has surged.
