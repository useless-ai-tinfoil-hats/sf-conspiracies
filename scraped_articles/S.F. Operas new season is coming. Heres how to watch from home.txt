S.F. Opera’s new season is coming. Here’s how to watch from home
-----
Opera fans can once again enjoy San Francisco Opera's main-stage performances from the comfort of their couch, and at a fraction of the price.The Opera's livestream schedule of its 2024-25 season includes the season's opener, Verdi's "Un Ballo in Maschera," the West Coast premiere of "The Handmaid's Tale" and the Bizet classic "Carmen." The three productions are among six performances to be made available online starting Sept. 15.
