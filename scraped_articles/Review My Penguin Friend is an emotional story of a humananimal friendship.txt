Review: ‘My Penguin Friend’ is an emotional story of a human-animal friendship
-----
"My Penguin Friend" is what you'd expect from an animal picture, except that it's better — lifted by a smart script, sensitive direction and a truly beautiful performance by Jean Reno.It's based on real-life events that happened about 15 years in ago in a small village in Brazil. A man rescued a Magellanic penguin who'd been covered in oil following a tanker spill.
