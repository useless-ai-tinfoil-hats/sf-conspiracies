S.F.’s biggest mall loses another store — its fifth closure this month
-----
Madewell will be the latest retailer to close in San Francisco Centre, marking an unprecedented fifth store closure in the mall in January.The clothing and accessories store said on its website that the 845 Market St. location will close on Jan. 22. This month's exodus also includes Adidas, J. Crew, Lucky Brand and Aldo, who have closed or plan to in the next week.
