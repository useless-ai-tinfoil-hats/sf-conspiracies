Pedestrian struck and killed by motorist while walking in San Jose
-----
A woman was struck and killed by a motorist late Sunday in San Jose, police officials said.A black 2003 Mercedes sedan driving north on Snell Avenue at Capital Highway hit a woman crossing east on Snell Avenue, San Jose police said.
Police said the driver, a man who police did not identify, had a green light.
Officers were in the area of Snell Avenue and Capitol Expressway responding to the vehicular collision around 10:30 p.m., officials said in a statement.
