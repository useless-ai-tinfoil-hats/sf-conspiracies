This surprise guest introduced Nicolas Cage at SFFilm Awards Night
-----
SFFilm took a page from the old game show "What's My Line?" in kicking off this year's annual Awards Night with a mystery guest.After Executive Director Anne Lai welcomed the well-heeled crowd seated for dinner at Yerba Buena Center for the Arts on Monday, Dec. 4, and reiterated the mission of the nonprofit organization, it was time for the first of four awards: the Maria Manetti Shrem Lifetime Achievement Award for Acting, to be presented to Nicolas Cage.
