California woman, 22, goes missing while hiking during historic storm
-----
A 22-year-old California woman is missing after attempting to hike the state's most treacherous mountain in the middle of a historic storm. The deluge that hit Southern California on Sunday through Tuesday saw Los Angeles County record more than half of its annual rainfall in just three days. Into this storm, Lifei Huang of El Monte, Calif., set out on a hike on the San Gabriel Mountains' deadliest peak: Mount Baldy.Huang embarked on the hike at 2 p.m. Sunday, and was last heard from
