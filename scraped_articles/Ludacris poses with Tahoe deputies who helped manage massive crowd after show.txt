Ludacris poses with Tahoe deputies who helped manage ‘massive crowd’ after show
-----
Rapper and actor Ludacris posed for a photo with Tahoe deputies as a gesture of appreciation for their help managing a crowd after he performed at the Palisades Tahoe resort, according to the Placer County Sheriff's Department.The show on Saturday, Feb. 24, followed the men's giant slalom and U.S. Ski Team autograph-signing events at the two-day Stifel Palisades Tahoe Cup competition. The Grammy-winning, multiplatinum-selling rapper drew "a massive crowd" at a free concert that night
