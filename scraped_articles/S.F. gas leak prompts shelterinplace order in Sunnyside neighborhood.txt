S.F. gas leak prompts shelter-in-place order in Sunnyside neighborhood
-----
A gas leak on the 600 block of Joost Avenue prompted a shelter-in-place order Tuesday morning in San Francisco's Sunnyside neighborhood.The cause of the leak was traced to a construction crew that struck a gas line, the San Francisco Fire Department said.
Pacific Gas & Electric Co. was at the scene and was working to remedy the leak, Fire Department officials said.
Anyone on the 600 block of Joost was asked to remain inside and to shut windows and doors.
