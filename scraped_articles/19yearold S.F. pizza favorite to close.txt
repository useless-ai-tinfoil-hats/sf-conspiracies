19-year-old S.F. pizza favorite to close
-----
Little Star Pizza, a beloved deep-dish pizzeria, is closing after nearly two decades in San Francisco.The restaurant's original location at 846 Divisadero St. will shutter at the end of the year, according to an announcement posted on Instagram Sunday.
"It has been a marvelous run, and an honor to have been a part of the Western Addition/Nopa/Alamo Square neighborhood," the post reads.
