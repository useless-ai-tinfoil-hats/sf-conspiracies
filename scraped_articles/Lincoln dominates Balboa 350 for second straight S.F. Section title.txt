Lincoln dominates Balboa 35-0 for second straight S.F. Section title
-----
Jamelle Newman saw just one man in front of him."If I get taken down one-on-one I don't want to face my coaches," Newman said with a laugh.
He juked the defensive back, sprinted 76 yards and Lincoln High School, on just the second play of the game against Balboa, was off and running. Literally. It was all the offense the Mustangs needed in a 35-0 San Francisco Section championship game Thursday at Kezar Stadium.
