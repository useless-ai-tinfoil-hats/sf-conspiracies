Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao blasts police commission leader over search for new chief
-----
Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao vowed Wednesday to declare a state of emergency by the end of the year if the city's police commission fails to send her a list of finalists for police chief, escalating the battle between her and the group's leadership.Thao has previously criticized the commission, saying that the body is taking too long in its process to select a new police chief as the city confronts a wave of violent crime.
