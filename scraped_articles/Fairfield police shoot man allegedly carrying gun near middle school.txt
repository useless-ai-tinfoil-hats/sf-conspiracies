Fairfield police shoot man allegedly carrying gun near middle school
-----
Fairfield police shot and wounded a man who was seen possibly carrying a gun near a middle school, according to the Police Department.Around 1:16 p.m. Wednesday, Fairfield police received a report that a man was walking down East Tabor Avenue near Grange Middle School carrying "what appeared to be a firearm" in his hand, according to officials. Officers arrived and confronted the man.
