TravelSkills 05-15-24 Horrendous Bay Area intersection gets a major fix
-----
This Bay Area intersection might be the number one spot on the power rankings for hot conversation topics in the East Bay.The Gilman Street interchange has made drivers fear for their lives for decades due to its outrageous planning design that defines pretzel logic. Eleven lanes interject into one crossroad to create a haphazard area under the highway.
Following nearly 20 years of construction, Caltrans opened two sections of its infrastructure project to the public last weekend.
