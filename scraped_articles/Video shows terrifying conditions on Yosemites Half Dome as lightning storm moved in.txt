Video shows terrifying conditions on Yosemite’s Half Dome as lightning storm moved in
-----
A lightning storm at the top of Half Dome on Sept. 21 turned the popular Yosemite hiking destination into a dangerous, slippery slope, as hikers previously told the Chronicle. One hiker — Ulises Longoria — captured the terrifying experience on video.Longoria, who lives in Texas, began hiking around 7:20 a.m. with a group of pastors who had attended a conference in California. The weather was perfect when the day began but took a turn for the worse as he got higher.
