Why UC Berkeley students spent nearly 6 hours straight on BART
-----
A group of transit-loving UC Berkeley students recently embarked on an ambitious, if unusual, mission: They spent a Saturday trying to travel to all 50 BART stations faster than anyone had before. Not only did the students want to achieve the fastest time ever for a so-called "speedrun," but they also wanted to get the feat officially recognized by the Guinness World Records.
