California insurance crisis: Newsom wants regulators to approve rate increases faster
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom is getting more directly involved in the insurance crisis by proposing legislation to speed up the time it takes for insurance companies to raise their rates.At a Friday news conference discussing his revised budget proposal, Newsom said he is crafting a bill with state lawmakers to expedite the insurance rate approval process. Newsom wants to pass the bill this summer as part of the 2024-25 state budget, which would allow it to take effect as soon as July 1.
