Oakland to pay $450,000 to widow of man allegedly killed by neighbor
-----
Oakland has agreed to pay $450,000 to the family of a man who was shot and killed by his neighbor in 2020, according to the city.Miles Armstead, 44, was doing yardwork at his home on the 7500 block of Ney Avenue in East Oakland when his former neighbor fatally shot him around 2 p.m. on May 1, 2020, according to Oakland police.
The shooting occurred weeks after the suspect, Jamal Thomas, then 43, had been jailed and released after threatening Armstead.
