David Lee Roth agrees to tour with longtime rival Sammy Hagar: ‘Let’s do this’
-----
David Lee Roth has taken up Sammy Hagar's offer to join him on a tour celebrating the music of Van Halen next year.Last week, Hagar announced the dates for the "Best of All Worlds Summer 2024" tour, featuring former Chickenfoot bandmates Michael Anthony and Joe Satriani, along with drummer Jason Bonham. The self-styled Red Rocker has also extended an invitation to former Van Halen singer and longtime rival Roth to join the festivities during an appearance on "The Howard Stern Show."
