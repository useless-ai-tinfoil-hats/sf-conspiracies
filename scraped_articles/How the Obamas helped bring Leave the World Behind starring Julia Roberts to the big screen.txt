How the Obamas helped bring ‘Leave the World Behind,’ starring Julia Roberts, to the big screen
-----
When Rumaan Alam's novel "Leave the World Behind" was published in the fall of 2020, critics praised the author's prescience in writing a story resonant with the tense early months of the COVID-19 pandemic.In the unsettling thriller, a mysterious calamity has hit the East Coast, forcing people to shelter in place. Their electricity, Wi-Fi, GPS and cell service have been knocked out. Planes are falling from the sky, and there are intermittent sonic booms loud enough to loosen your teeth.
