Activists take over building near Berkeley’s People’s Park, police responding
-----
Police responded to an address near People's Park Wednesday afternoon after activists took over a building, a UC Berkeley spokesperson told the Chronicle."This is an active crime scene, it is not nonviolent civil disobedience," said Dan Mogulof, a spokesperson for the university. "Police are responding."
He said that today is the 55th anniversary of the founding ofPeople's Park and that the takeover was coordinated by People's Park activists.
