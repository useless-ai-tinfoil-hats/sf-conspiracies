California’s giant sequoias face a new threat — and the world’s largest tree may be at risk
-----
California wildfires aren't the only thing killing the state's majestic giant sequoia trees. So is a little-known bark beetle.Researchers in the Sierra Nevada, the only place where the giant sequoia naturally grows, have found several of the world's largest trees unexpectedly infested with beetles, some dying from the attacks.
