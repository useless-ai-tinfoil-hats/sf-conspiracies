Two dead following boating accident in San Joaquin County
-----
Two people died in a boating accident Saturday night in San Joaquin County.Around 5 p.m. Saturday the San Joaquin County Sheriff's Office was called to the scene north of Tower Park Marina in Lodi, officials said. The Boating Safety Unit rescued a juvenile from the wetland and pulled an adult from the water who was pronounced dead at the scene, according to the sheriff's office. The adult had suffered severe injuries, officials reported.
