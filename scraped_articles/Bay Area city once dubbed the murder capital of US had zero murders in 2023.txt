Bay Area city once dubbed the 'murder capital' of US had zero murders in 2023
-----
The city of East Palo Alto saw zero homicides last year, according to the East Palo Alto Police Department.It's a stunning turn for a place once known as the nation's murder capital. In 1992, the year it earned its macabre moniker, 42 people were killed in the city of 24,000, giving it the highest murder-per-capita rate in the U.S.
