Disneyland has a holiday food festival and this year everything is somehow delicious
-----
One of the things that makes the holiday season at Disneyland so exciting — and so busy — is that so many things are different in the parks this time of year. Rides like Haunted Mansion and It's a Small World get holiday overlays that make them totally unique experiences. Every surface is covered in festive decorations and nearly a million lights, including the castle.
