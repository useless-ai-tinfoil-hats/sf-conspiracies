From just a lowly can, you can make one of the most convenient and, I would argue, seasonal meals.Preserving — whether it's in a can, a jar or even a freezer bag — is a way of extending the life and season of your favorite produce. For example, a few weeks ago I jarred a batch of apple butter with Meyer lemon that I gave away for the holidays.
