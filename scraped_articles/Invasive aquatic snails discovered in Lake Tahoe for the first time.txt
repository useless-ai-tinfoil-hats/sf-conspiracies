Invasive aquatic snails discovered in Lake Tahoe for the first time
-----
Officials are asking for the public's help after finding invasive New Zealand mudsnails in Lake Tahoe.This is the first time the species has been seen in Lake Tahoe, but they've been found throughout the state, including in the Stanislaus and Russian rivers, according to the California Department of Fish and Wildlife.
