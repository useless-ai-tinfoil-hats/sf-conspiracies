Oakland firefighters scramble to extinguish ‘fully involved’ blaze on 8th Street
-----
Firefighters in Oakland were battling a "fully involved structure fire" Tuesday at what appeared to be a residential building at 600 East Eighth St.Oakland Fire Department officials posted to social media Tuesday afternoon that around 30 firefighters were being summoned to confront the blaze.
Flames were shooting through the building's roof, fire officials said.
There were no immediate reports about anyone injured as a result of the fire, fire official said.
