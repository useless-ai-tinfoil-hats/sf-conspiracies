Andrew Luck throws his weight — and cash — behind reviving Palo Alto sports bar
-----
After the announcement of its revitalization last year, one of Palo Alto's most beloved sports bars scored a touchdown by landing a big-time investor.Andrew Luck, the former Stanford Cardinal quarterback who starred in the NFL for the Indianapolis Colts, recently became a financial partner in the revamp of The Old Pro, according to Palo Alto Online; the space will also now be called The Pro.
