Big Sur group says area is ‘overrun,’ calls for halt to new tourist lodging
-----
A citizens group in Big Sur concerned about the impacts of rampant tourism across the rugged coastal region is calling for an immediate halt on new lodging developments in the area, which receives more annual visitors than Yosemite National Park.The 70-mile area in Monterey County is currently inaccessible by car due to slide damage on Highway 1 at its northern edge as well as ongoing road repairs on Big Sur's south side.
