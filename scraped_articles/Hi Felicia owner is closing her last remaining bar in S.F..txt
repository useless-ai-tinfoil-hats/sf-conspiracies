Hi Felicia owner is closing her last remaining bar in S.F.
-----
The owner of once-buzzy Oakland restaurant Hi Felicia has announced she is closing Sluts, her San Francisco wine bar and dance venue, after one year in business.Owner Imana, who goes by her first name only, posted on Sluts' Instagram page and her personal account Wednesday that the business at 1116 Folsom St. will close Dec. 31.
