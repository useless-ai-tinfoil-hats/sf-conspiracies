Teen snowboarder hospitalized after Mammoth Mountain ski-lift fall that went viral
-----
A 16-year-old snowboarder was hospitalized this week after a fall from a ski lift at Mammoth Mountain resort in the Eastern Sierra. The incident gained widespread attention when several videos surfaced that revealed employees and onlookers struggling to prevent her descent.The footage, shared on social media platforms like Instagram and X, depicts the teen hanging from the chairlift last Saturday morning.
