Stanford softball stifled by Texas again, season ends with 1-0 loss in WCWS semis
-----
Stanford's NiJaree Canady gave her team every chance to beat the No. 1 team in the country Monday, but ultimately the Cardinal bats fell flat at the wrong time to end what had been a memorable postseason run.Canady, pitching her fourth game in five days, allowed only five hits but her teammates had only one and fell 1-0 to Texas in a semifinal game at the Women's College World Series in Oklahoma City.
The Cardinal played the Longhorns twice in the WCWS and did not score. Texas
