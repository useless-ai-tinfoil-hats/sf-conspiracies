Bill Simmons says 49ers play in a dome, their stadium doesn't have 'intimidating atmosphere'
-----
The atmosphere at the 49ers' home, Levi's Stadium, does not pass the smell test for podcaster Bill Simmons.As he previewed the upcoming divisional round games on his eponymous podcast published Friday, Simmons continued to hint at ways the Packers could get one over on the 49ers this Saturday. While he ultimately concluded San Francisco would come out victorious, he cited a toothless Niners home stadium as something going in Green Bay's favor.
