Half Moon Bay farms must pay $450K in workplace violations, back pay
-----
The owners of two mushroom farms in Half Moon Bay where a farmworker shot eight co-workers, seven fatally, must pay more than $450,000 in back wages and damages to employees after a U.S. Department of Labor investigation uncovered workplace violations that included unpaid wages and unsafe housing conditions.Labor department officials said the payments are part of an administrative settlement with Concord Farms Inc. and California Terra Gardens and will go to 62 workers employed by them.
