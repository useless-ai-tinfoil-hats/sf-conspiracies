99 Cents Only Stores to close all Bay Area locations
-----
The discount retail chain 99 Cents Only Stores announced the closure of all its 371 locations, including nearly two dozen stores in the Bay Area."This was an extremely difficult decision and is not the outcome we expected or hoped to achieve," interim CEO Mike Simoncic said in a statement. "Unfortunately, the last several years have presented significant and lasting challenges in the retail environment."
