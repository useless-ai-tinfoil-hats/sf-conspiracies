S.F. high school student shot near Ghirardelli Square, prompting campus lockdown, officials say
-----
A student at San Francisco's Galileo Academy of Science and Technology was shot in the middle of a school day near Ghirardelli Square on Wednesday afternoon, police said.The shooting occurred at North Point and Polk streets during lunchtime at the public high school just a few blocks away, school employees said when reached by phone. Galileo has an open campus policy and it is common for students to spend time off campus during lunch.
The incident prompted a lockdown at the school
