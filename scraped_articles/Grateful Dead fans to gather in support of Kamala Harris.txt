Grateful Dead fans to gather in support of Kamala Harris
-----
A group of Deadheads plans to commemorate Grateful Dead guitarist Jerry Garcia's birthday with a virtual fundraiser for Vice President Kamala Harris' presidential campaign.Spearheaded by David Gans, host of the national radio show "The Grateful Dead Hour," and Steve Leventhal, owner of Acid Flashback Radio, the event dubbed Deadheads for Kamala aims to mobilize voters to support the Oakland native at the polls.
