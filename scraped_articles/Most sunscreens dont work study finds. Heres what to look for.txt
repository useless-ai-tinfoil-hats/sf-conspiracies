Most sunscreens don’t work, study finds. Here’s what to look for
-----
As summer approaches, sunscreen inevitably comes to mind, yet a recent study reveals that only a quarter of sunscreen products on the U.S. market offer truly safe and effective protection.The nonprofit consumer advocacy organization Environmental Working Group conducted a review of over 1,700 sunscreens for its annual report and found that roughly 25% meet contemporary standards for sustained protection, while the rest fall short. Many of these products also contain toxic ingredients
