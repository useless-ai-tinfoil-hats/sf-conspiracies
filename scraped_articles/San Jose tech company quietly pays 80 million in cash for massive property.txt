San Jose tech company quietly pays $80 million in cash for massive property
-----
The fate of Fry's Electronics' old headquarters appears to finally be sealed.Though the defunct, Mayan-themed location in San Jose was previously slated to become a massive housing development, it's likely to turn into a sprawling tech campus instead, Bay Area News Group reported. Super Micro Computer, a multibillion-dollar IT equipment manufacturer based in Silicon Valley, recently purchased the property at 550 East Brokaw Road, per the outlet.
