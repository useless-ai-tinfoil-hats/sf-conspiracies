Review: Buddy comedy ‘Babes’ may make you squirm more than laugh
-----
The buddy comedy "Babes" offers keen insights into pregnancy, parenting and longtime friendships, although many get lost in the movie's bodily function-joke jamboree."Gross" is such a subjective term. Millions of people watch online videos of dermatological procedures, after all. But "Babes" might make those people squeamish.
