Why is 49ers’ Brandon Aiyuk still waiting as NFL’s best wideouts keep getting paid?
-----
NFL wide receivers keep receiving windfalls. And San Francisco 49ers wideout Brandon Aiyuk keeps waiting.The Vikings' Justin Jefferson became the latest pass catcher to get paid Monday when he signed a four-year, $140 million contract extension that made him the league's highest paid non-quarterback, surpassing the $34 million average annual salary of 49ers pass rusher Nick Bosa.
As for Aiyuk, Jefferson's record deal probably won't have a significant impact on his ongoing extension
