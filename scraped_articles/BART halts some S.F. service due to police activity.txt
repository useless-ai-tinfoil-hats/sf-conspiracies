BART halts some S.F. service due to police activity
-----
BART halted service on the San Francisco line between Balboa Park and Daly City on Sunday morning due to police activity "between the trackways," spokesperson Cheryl Stalter said in a 10:04 a.m. voicemail statement.The transit agency encouraged riders to take Muni's 14-Mission lines during the service interruption.
Stalter said she had little information about the police activity.
