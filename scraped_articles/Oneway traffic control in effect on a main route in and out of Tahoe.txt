One-way traffic control in effect on a main route in and out of Tahoe
-----
One-way traffic control was in place on Highway 50 just east of Echo Summit on Friday morning while work crews continued to remove loose boulders to prevent future rock slides, officials said. The partial closure of a main route in and out of the Tahoe Basin comes during the busy holiday travel season.Caltrans advised motorists traveling to and from South Lake Tahoe to prepare for delays Friday and Saturday.
