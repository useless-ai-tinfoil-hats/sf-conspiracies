Olivia Rodrigo’s opener PinkPantheress cancels S.F. performances: ‘I am struggling’
-----
Rising British musician PinkPantheress has canceled the rest of her live appearances for the year, including her scheduled dates on Olivia Rodrigo's "Guts" world tour.The artist, best known for her TikTok hit "Just for Me" and her latest Selena-sampling single "Turn It Up," was slated to open for Rodrigo at San Francisco's Chase Center on Friday and Saturday, Aug. 2-3.
