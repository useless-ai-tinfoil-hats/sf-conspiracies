Review: ‘Alien: Romulus’ is boring sci-fi tied to a dead concept
-----
Almost five decades ago, screenwriters Dan O'Bannon and Ronald Shusett came up with a wild idea: Wouldn't it be cool — and amazingly disgusting — if a monster were to come bursting out of someone's body? You know, as if it had been gestating! Wouldn't that make everybody in the audience scream?They were right. Audiences screamed. It was a very good idea. It's just too bad that, in all the years since the first "Alien" (1979), no one has come up with a second good idea.
