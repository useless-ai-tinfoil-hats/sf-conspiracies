Cyclist struck and killed by driver in San Jose
-----
A man died in San Jose after a car struck him Sunday morning while he was riding a bike, police officials said.Around 9:15 a.m., San Jose Police Department officers responded to a traffic collision in the area of Jackson Avenue and Bambi Lane, police said. Officers found that a 2019 Chevy Silverado pickup truck, with one male occupant, hit the cyclist who was heading northbound on Jackson Avenue and veered into the southbound lanes, police said.
