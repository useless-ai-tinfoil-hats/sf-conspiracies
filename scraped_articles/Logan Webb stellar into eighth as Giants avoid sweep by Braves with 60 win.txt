Logan Webb stellar into eighth as Giants avoid sweep by Braves with 6-0 win
-----
His team desperate for a win and the bullpen thinned by the previous night's loss, Logan Webb lit up the radar gun (by his standards) and took it upon himself to hoist the San Francisco Giants out of a losing streak that had done plenty of damage to their postseason hopes.Reaching 95 mph on his sinker, a determined Webb came one out shy of eight shutout innings to deliver a 6-0 win against the Atlanta Braves on Thursday afternoon, preventing the four-game sweep.
