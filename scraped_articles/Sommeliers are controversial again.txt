Sommeliers are controversial again
-----
The sommelier is in peril once again.Since the pandemic, the dedicated wine professional has become a rarer sight in dining rooms around the country, the New York Times' Eric Asimov wrote recently. Asimov's observation that employing sommeliers has become "a luxury, nice to have but expendable in the blunt calculations of the post-pandemic restaurant model," has fired wine people up.
