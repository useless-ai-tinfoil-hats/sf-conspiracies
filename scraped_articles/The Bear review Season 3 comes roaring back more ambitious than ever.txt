‘The Bear’ review: Season 3 comes roaring back more ambitious than ever
-----
One of TV's most heralded series is back with its own problem of the Man or "The Bear."The show, which won 10 Emmys for its first season, including for comedy series, writing, directing and three for acting, is poised to run the table for its even-more ballyhooed Season 2 when the 2024 nominations are announced in a few weeks. Meanwhile, fans can binge Season 3, which drops in its entirety on Hulu on Wednesday, June 26 — a day earlier than initially planned.
