Bay Area mother disappears after missing flight to SFO for Thanksgiving
-----
A Bay Area mother was reported missing after not boarding her flight to San Francisco from Houston shortly before Thanksgiving, according to multiple outlets.Danielle Becker Friedland, 36, was receiving mental health treatment at a clinic in Houston for six weeks, several outlets, including the Houston Chronicle, reported. She was set to board a flight that would arrive at San Francisco International Airport on Wednesday night.
