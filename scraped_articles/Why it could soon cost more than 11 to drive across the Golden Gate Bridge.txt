Why it could soon cost more than $11 to drive across the Golden Gate Bridge
-----
North Bay motorists who use FasTrak transponders could soon spend up to $11.25 per vehicle to cross the Golden Gate Bridge, under new proposals to raise toll fees.The Golden Gate Bridge, Highway & Transportation District, which oversees the namesake bridge and bus and ferry transit from the North Bay to San Francisco, is considering four increases to narrow a steep budget gap.
