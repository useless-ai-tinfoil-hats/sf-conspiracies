Richmond man accused of stockpiling weapons pleads not guilty
-----
A Richmond man has pleaded not guilty to multiple felony charges related to the discovery of a stockpile of military grade weapons found by authorities during a January search of his home, the Contra Costa County District Attorney's Office said Tuesday.Lawrence Robert Hansen, 68, pleaded not guilty on Monday to 21 felony counts in connection with illegal weapon possession, according to the district attorney's office. He is being held at the Martinez Detention Facility without bail.
