CamelBak closing Bay Area headquarters, relocating to Southern California
-----
A Bay Area outdoors company will soon shutter its headquarters and decamp to Southern California amid a company restructuring.CamelBak, best known for its hydration backpacks, plans to close its Petaluma offices at 2000 S. McDowell Blvd. and lay off 43 employees by Aug. 1, with the remaining workers transferring to an office in Irvine. The job cuts will eliminate duplicative roles, the company told SFGATE.
