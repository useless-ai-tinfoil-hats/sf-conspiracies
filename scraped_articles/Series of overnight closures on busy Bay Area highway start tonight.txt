Series of overnight closures on busy Bay Area highway start tonight
-----
A series of overnight closures on busy Highway 37 between Vallejo and Sears Point will take place this week — starting Monday — to stripe the recently repaved roadway.The four consecutive nightly shutdowns will occur in one direction at a time between the Highway 29 interchange in Vallejo and the Highway 121 interchange in Sonoma County, according to Caltrans.
Westbound closures are planned Monday and Tuesday, and eastbound closures will take place overnight Wednesday and Thursday.
