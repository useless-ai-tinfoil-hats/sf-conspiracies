Exclusive: S.F.’s Stonestown mall owner closes on refinancing $180M mortgage
-----
Stonestown Galleria owner Brookfield Properties has successfully closed on its bid to refinance a $180 million mortgage for the San Francisco mall on Friday, the real estate giant confirmed.The terms of the new loan for the property are not yet publicly available — and while Brookfield confirmed to the Chronicle that it has refinanced the loan, it declined to provide comment.
