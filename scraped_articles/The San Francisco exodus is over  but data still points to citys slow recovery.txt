The San Francisco exodus is over — but data still points to city’s slow recovery
-----
San Francisco's pandemic exodus is over, and its population grew slightly last year, new census data shows.The city's population increased to an estimated 808,988 residents as of July 2023, up 0.15% from the prior year's revised 807,774, according to census data released late Wednesday. It remains far below its pre-pandemic level, down nearly 65,000 people or an estimated 7.4% compared to April 2020, after seeing some of the heaviest losses in the country.
