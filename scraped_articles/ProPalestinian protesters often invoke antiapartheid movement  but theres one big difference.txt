Pro-Palestinian protesters often invoke anti-apartheid movement — but there’s one big difference
-----
When he addressed 60,000 people at the Oakland Coliseum in 1990 after his release from prison, Nelson Mandela credited California activists for pushing the UC system to divest from companies with ties to South Africa — a pivotal moment in the fight against the country's racist apartheid system.Today, pro-Palestinian student activists are drawing inspiration from that same movement, which successfully pressured the UC to divest $3.1 billion from companies doing business in South Africa.
