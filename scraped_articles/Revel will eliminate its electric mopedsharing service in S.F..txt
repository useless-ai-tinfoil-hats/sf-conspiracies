Revel will eliminate its electric moped-sharing service in S.F.
-----
Revel announced on Friday that it is discontinuing its electric moped-sharing service in San Francisco and New York.Frank Reig, the CEO of the Brooklyn-based transportation company, informed employees via email about the decision, revealing the company's strategic shift toward focusing exclusively on its electric ride-hailing and EV charging ventures.
