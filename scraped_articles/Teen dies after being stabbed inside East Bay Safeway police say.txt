Teen dies after being stabbed inside East Bay Safeway, police say
-----
An 18-year-old died after being stabbed in a San Leandro Safeway store on Tuesday evening, officials said. The teen's homicide is the second in two days in the East Bay suburb, the San Leandro Police Department said.Lt. Abe Teng, a spokesperson for the department, said it's unusual to have back-to-back homicides in the city that sees four to six homicides in a typical year.
