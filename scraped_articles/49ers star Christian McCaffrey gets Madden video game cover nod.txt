49ers star Christian McCaffrey gets Madden video game cover nod
-----
Star running back Christian McCaffrey of the San Francisco 49ers scored a new contract extension last week, and he's set to marry model and influencer Olivia Culpo later this month. Now, CMC can add a new achievement to his busy June: Madden cover athlete.The lone NFL-sponsored video game unveiled McCaffrey as its choice for the cover athlete for Madden NFL 25 on Tuesday, with McCaffrey calling the decision a "lifelong dream come true" on social media.
