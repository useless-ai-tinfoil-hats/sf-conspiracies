Heiress from wealthy Bay Area town paid preteen kids $10M in wrongful death suit
-----
Wealthy Bay Area heiress Tiffany Li paid her two young daughters $10 million in the settlement of a wrongful death lawsuit that arose from the 2016 killing of Keith Green, the father of the girls and Li's former boyfriend.Each girl was given $5 million (minus attorney and litigation fees) and will have access to the money when they turn 18, Duffy Magilligan, a lawyer for Green's family, told SFGATE on Friday.
