Review: S.F. Symphony celebrates forests with a triumphant new orchestral masterpiece

It's daunting, it's weird, it doesn't have any memorable tunes.Then they hear a creation as magnificent and mind-blowingly original as "Breathing Forests," the recent organ concerto by the Berkeley-born composer Gabriella Smith, and they reconsider.
