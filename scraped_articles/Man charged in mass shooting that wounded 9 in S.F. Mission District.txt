Man charged in mass shooting that wounded 9 in S.F. Mission District
-----
A 23-year-old Richmond man will face a raft of felony charges in connection with a June mass shooting that left nine people wounded in San Francisco's Mission District.Prosecutors have charged Javier Campos with one count of shooting from a vehicle and causing great bodily injury, eight counts of assault with a semiautomatic firearm causing great bodily injury and three counts of unlawful gun possession.
