Photos: A jazz icon’s stunning midcentury Oakland ‘tree house’ hits the market for $3 million

A one-of-a-kind East Bay tree house, where jazz musician Dave Brubeck lived and composed some of his most beloved songs, has hit the market.The creative haven at 6630 Heartwood Drive in Oakland's hilly Montclair neighborhood is considered a beacon of midcentury design.
