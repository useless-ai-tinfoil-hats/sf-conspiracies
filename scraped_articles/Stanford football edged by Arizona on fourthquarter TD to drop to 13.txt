Stanford football edged by Arizona on fourth-quarter TD to drop to 1-3
-----
One week after Stanford lost to visiting Sacramento State when the Hornets scored the go-ahead touchdown with a minute and a half to go, the Cardinal lost 21-20 to visiting Arizona on Saturday as the Wildcats ran out the final 3:05 on the clock.And things figure to get a lot tougher for the Cardinal next Saturday. No. 10 Oregon (4-0) comes to Stanford Stadium after a 42-6 thumping of No. 19 Colorado.
