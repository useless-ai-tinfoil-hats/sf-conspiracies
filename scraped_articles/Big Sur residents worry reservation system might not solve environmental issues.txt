Big Sur residents worry reservation system might not solve environmental issues
-----
A sweeping new proposal for how the U.S. Forest Service envisions managing the millions who visit Big Sur each year has both garnered support and raised concern among those who call the densely forested yet sparsely populated region home.The suggestions within the report include some radical departures from the area's current policies. Most notably, there could be a reservation system for popular spots like the Pine Ridge Trail and Pfeiffer Beach.
