Zendaya named co-chair of 2024 Met Gala
-----
It looks like Zendaya will have yet another opportunity to turn heads with her iconic fashion statements this spring.The Oakland native was tapped as one of the 2024 Met Gala's co-chairs on Thursday, Feb. 15. She joins co-chairs singer/actress Jennifer Lopez, Puerto Rican rapper/singer Bad Bunny and actor Chris Hemsworth for this year's event, along with Vogue editor-in-chief Anna Wintour, who has chaired or co-chaired since the mid-'90s.
