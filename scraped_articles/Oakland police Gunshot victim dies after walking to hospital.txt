Oakland police: Gunshot victim dies after walking to hospital
-----
A gunshot victim died after walking to a nearby hospital for treatment Saturday afternoon, according to the Oakland Police Department.Officers responded to a ShotSpotter activation just before 1:30 p.m. on the 6900 block of Hamilton Street, near the Oakland Coliseum, police said. They found a vehicle that had been struck by gunfire but no victim.
