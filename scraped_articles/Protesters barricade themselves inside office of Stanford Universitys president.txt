Protesters barricade themselves inside office of Stanford University's president
-----
Protesters took over the office of Stanford University's president on Wednesday morning, barricading themselves in the room in an outcry against the school's response to the group's demands over Israel's war on Gaza.KCBS Radio reported after 7 a.m. that sheriff's deputies had forced themselves inside Stanford President Richard Saller's office. The radio station said about 100 demonstrators had gathered outside the president's office.
