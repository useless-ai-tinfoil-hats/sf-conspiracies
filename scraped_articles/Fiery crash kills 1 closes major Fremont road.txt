Fiery crash kills 1, closes major Fremont road
-----
A brutal car crash and fire early Tuesday morning in Fremont left one person dead and another injured, local outlets reported from the scene.Footage from NBC Bay Area (KNTV-TV) shows that the car hit a tree on the side of Fremont Boulevard and caught fire, with a maw of flame engulfing the vehicle and burning the tree trunk. Onlookers told KRON-TV and KTVU-TV that they'd come to the scene because they'd heard a family member was in the crash, and that the person died at the site. Fremont
