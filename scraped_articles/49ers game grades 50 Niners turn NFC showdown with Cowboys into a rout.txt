49ers game grades: 5-0 Niners turn NFC showdown with Cowboys into a rout
-----
The San Francisco 49ers clobbered the Dallas Cowboys 42-10, winning by the largest margin of victory in the two teams' 40-year rivalry. How did the individual units of the team contribute to a historic night for the 5-0 Niners?OFFENSE: A
Brock Purdy entered the game as the NFL's highest-rated passer (115.1) and proceeded to drop a 144.4 rating on the Cowboys. He was 17-of-24 for 252 yards, four touchdowns and — again — no interceptions as his pick-free streak reached 172 passes.
