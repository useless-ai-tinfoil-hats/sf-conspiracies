Bay Area mom identified as woman killed at East Bay car dealership
-----
A 24-year-old Bay Area mother was identified by a GoFundMe page as the Toyota employee who was killed on Sept. 21 in a shooting that police say was an act of domestic violence.Alonna Gallon was a mother of a 7-year-old and was allegedly killed by her child's father at a car dealership in Albany, according to Alonna's mother, Yuvonda Gallon, who created the GoFundMe page. (The Alameda County Coroner's Bureau has not confirmed the identity of the woman killed in the shooting.)
