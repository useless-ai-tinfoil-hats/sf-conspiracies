Six injured in three-car crash in North Bay
-----
Six people were injured in a three-car crash on Highway 29 in Yountville, Cal Fire reported Saturday.While the three vehicles received significant damage Friday evening in the crash, the six victims sustained only minor injuries, Cal Fire said in a statement.
Crews with Cal Fire, the California Highway Patrol, AMR Napa County and the Napa Sheriff's Office responded to the crash. The northbound lanes of Highway 29 were reopened shortly before 7:30 p.m. Friday.
