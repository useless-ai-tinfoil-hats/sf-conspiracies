Last person to see missing Calif. woman Nancy Ng on yoga retreat speaks out
-----
The last person to see missing California woman Nancy Ng on a lake in Guatemala has finally spoken publicly, after repeated pleas from Ng's family.Ng was last seen kayaking at a yoga retreat on Lake Atitlan on Oct 19. Footage released last week shows Nancy waving at the camera that day, as other kayakers paddle out nearby. After nearly four weeks, one of those kayakers, Christina Blazek, has spoken out through her attorney.
