Motorcyclist killed in crash at Golden Gate Park
-----
A motorcyclist was killed Friday in a crash at San Francisco's Golden Gate Park.Around 3:20 p.m., police officers responded to a report of a vehicle collision at the intersection of Martin Luther King Jr. Drive and Music Concourse Drive, police said. Upon arrival, officers found a motorcycle and motorcyclist, but no other vehicles, according to police. Despite lifesaving efforts, the motorcyclist was pronounced dead at the scene, police said.
