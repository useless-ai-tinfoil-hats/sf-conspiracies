Evacuations ordered in Marin County as dangerous surf surges on coast
-----
Marin County residents have been issued an evacuation order amid high surf and a storm surge on Thursday morning.The evacuation order was issued to people in the area of Stinson Beach, specifically in "Calles Pinos, Pradero, Sierra, Onda Resaca, Ribera, and Embarcadero" and "sections of Calle Del Arroyo," the Marin County Fire Department said on social media. People in the area are being urged to leave immediately.
As of Thursday morning, people can evacuate to the Stinson Beach
