Person shot on East Bay BART platform, station closed
-----
A person was shot on the platform of BART's Pittsburg station Friday during the afternoon commute, shutting down the station as police launched an investigation.The transit agency said the shooting occurred around 3:50 p.m. and that the victim was taken to the hospital in critical condition.
"We don't know yet whether any of the involved parties had been riding a train before the incident," a BART spokesperson said. "There has not yet been an arrest."
