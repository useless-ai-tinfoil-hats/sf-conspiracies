Metallica invades ‘Fortnite’ with new concert and gameplay modes
-----
Metallica is venturing into new territories by participating in numerous "Fortnite" events this month.The Bay Area rock band will be the first to feature across all of Epic Games' international megahit experiences, including "Fortnite Festival," "Battle Royale," "Lego Fortnite" and "Rocket Racing," starting Thursday, June 13.
A string of exclusive in-game concert performances titled "Metallica: Fuel. Fire. Fury." will begin on June 22, with six showtimes over two consecutive days.
