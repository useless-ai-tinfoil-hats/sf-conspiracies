Travel experts reveal the best day to drive to Tahoe for a winter weekend
-----
If you frequently drive to Tahoe from the Bay Area, you have a system — or, better yet, a rule — for when to beat the traffic."Our rule was to never drive up on Friday and never leave on Sunday," Steve Nelson, a spokesperson for Caltrans, told SFGATE about his family's system for visiting Tahoe City. "Some weekends it's fine, others it's 10 to 12 hours from the bay and back."
