Ricky Pearsall shooting offers chilling reminder, ‘It really can happen to anyone’
-----
Rob McDonald shares simple, straightforward guidance to San Francisco Giants players: Be aware of your surroundings.That's the message McDonald, the Giants' director of team security, repeated in a Chronicle interview Sunday, one day after San Francisco 49ers wide receiver Ricky Pearsall was wounded in an attempted robbery in Union Square.
As McDonald noted, what happened to Pearsall on Saturday afternoon "can happen in any major city."
