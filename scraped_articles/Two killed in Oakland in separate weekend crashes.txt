Two killed in Oakland in separate weekend crashes
-----
Two people were killed in Oakland over the weekend in a pair of unrelated vehicle crashes.In the first incident, officers were dispatched to a single-vehicle crash on the 3500 block of West Street in West Oakland just before 10:30 a.m. Saturday. Police said they found a truck driver who suffered major injuries after his three-axle Volvo D16 with an elevated hydraulic belt struck the bottom of the freeway overpass at 35th and West streets.
