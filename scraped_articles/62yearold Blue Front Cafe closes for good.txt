62-year-old Blue Front Cafe closes for good
-----
Blue Front Cafe, a 62-year-old restaurant in San Francisco's Haight-Ashbury neighborhood, is closing its doors for good. The closure was confirmed in an email sent to SFGATE by Hani Khoury, who co-owns the restaurant with his brother.The cafe, located on Haight and Masonic, is among the oldest restaurants in the neighborhood.
