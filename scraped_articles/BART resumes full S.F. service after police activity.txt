BART resumes full S.F. service after police activity
-----
BART resumed service on the San Francisco line between Balboa Park and Daly City on Sunday afternoon, four hours after it was halted due to police activity.Service had been stopped on the line in the morning because of activity "between the trackways," spokesperson Cheryl Stalter said in a 10:04 a.m. voicemail statement. Stalter said she had little information about the police activity.
The transit agency had encouraged riders to take Muni's 14-Mission lines during the service
