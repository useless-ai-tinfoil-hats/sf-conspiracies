Crews halt progress of 44-acre grass fire near Livermore
-----
Crews stopped the forward progress of a grass fire near Livermore that burned about 44 acres Saturday, authorities said.The Greenville Fire at Altamont Pass Road and North Greenville Road in Altamont, a community about 10 miles east of Livermore, started at about 11 a.m. Saturday, growing to 15 acres about an hour later.
The fire was 65% contained as of 3 p.m., according to Cal Fire.
The cause of the fire remained under investigation.
