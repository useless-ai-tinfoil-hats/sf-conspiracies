DA Pamela Price charges three Alameda police officers in controversial death of Mario Gonzalez
-----
Alameda County District Attorney Pamela Price is filing felony charges against three officers in the death of Mario Gonzalez, a man they arrested in Alameda in April 2021 and pinned to the ground until he went limp, according to court records.Price scheduled a 6 p.m. news conference Thursday on the case. It's not clear what charges she filed. Her office did not immediately respond to a request for comment.
