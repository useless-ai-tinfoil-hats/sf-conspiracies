Officer shoots pedestrian on Interstate 280 in San Francisco, CHP says
-----
LATEST Jan. 31, 9:17 p.m. An officer with the California Highway Patrol shot a pedestrian on Interstate 280 on Wednesday, CHP said.The pedestrian was transported to a local hospital for treatment, Officer Andrew Barclay, a CHP spokesperson, told SFGATE.
Southbound Interstate 280 near the Mariposa Street exit was closed to traffic for several hours after the shooting, which occurred about 4:30 p.m., Barclay said. All lanes reopened just after 9 p.m., he said.
