Thieves may be using 'distraction method' to get into Bay Area homes
-----
A group of suspects may be using a "distraction method" to steal family valuables, distraught Bay Area residents said.The reported robberies occurred in San Jose. Residents Raj Pokalori and Renuka Rajagopal told SFGATE that in September, thieves left their house with $100,000 in jewelry after posing as customers trying to buy a dress from Rajagopal, who is a fashion designer.
