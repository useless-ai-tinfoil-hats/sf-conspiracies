Oakland police investigating reported shooting near Skyline High School
-----
Oakland police were investigating a reported shooting near Skyline High School on Tuesday, officials said.Officers were dispatched to an area near the high school just after 11:30 a.m. following a report of a shooting and did not find any victims, police said.
The school was placed on lockdown, they added.
Officers also detained individuals believed to be suspects and recovered a firearm, police said, without specifying who had been detained.
