Heads up, S.F. Muni riders: You need to download new app to buy tickets
-----
Starting this week, Muni riders must download the newly released version of the Muni app on their smartphones to buy fare tickets, according to San Francisco transit officials.The San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency announced Monday that riders can no longer buy tickets on the previous MuniMobile app because an updated model of the app was released that day. The older version of the app will be discontinued on Jan. 15.
