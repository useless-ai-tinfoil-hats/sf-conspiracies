Napa power player buying one of California’s biggest Chardonnay wineries for $400 million
-----
One of the country's biggest names in Chardonnay is changing hands.The Duckhorn Portfolio announced it will buy Windsor winery Sonoma-Cutrer Vineyards from Brown-Forman Corp. for $400 million — $350 million in stock and $50 million in cash. The sale is expected to be completed next year.
