Is this S.F. policy a NIMBY weapon or way to block ‘monster homes’? One neighborhood is at the center of the debate
-----
The proposed building would be too tall, a neighbor said in one case, drowning an outdoor deck in shadow. In another case, a couple living next door to a duplex protested that a plan to remodel it would push it too close to their own home. A third building was scrutinized over the plans for windows, which according to a neighbor would let its inhabitants look directly into the adjacent living room.These were three of the reasons cited by residents of San Francisco's Noe Valley last year
