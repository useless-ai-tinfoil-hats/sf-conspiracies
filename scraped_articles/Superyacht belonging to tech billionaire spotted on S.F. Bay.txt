Superyacht belonging to tech billionaire spotted on S.F. Bay
-----
A superyacht owned by one of California's billionaires has been spotted on the San Francisco Bay this week.Mogambo, one of four yachts owned by Whatsapp founder and former CEO Jan Koum, could be seen in the middle of the bay on Wednesday. Koum, 48, an Atherton resident, was ranked this year by Forbes as the ninth wealthiest Californian and the 117th wealthiest person in the world. His net worth is $16.1 billion, according to Forbes.
