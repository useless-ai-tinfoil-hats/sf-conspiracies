S.F. Mayor Breed loses latest housing fight as supervisors override her veto of controversial legislation
-----
The battle over San Francisco housing policy took center stage at City Hall on Tuesday when the Board of Supervisors overturned Mayor London Breed's veto of legislation that will impose housing development restrictions on 15-20 blocks in historic areas of San Francisco.The legislation was sponsored by Board of Supervisors President Aaron Peskin, who is widely expected to run against Breed for mayor in November.
