Video shows woman being robbed and dragged along sidewalk in Oakland
-----
Surveillance video shared by KTVU crime reporter Henry Lee shows a woman being robbed and dragged along a sidewalk near the Rockridge BART station on Sept. 13.The Oakland Police Department is investigating the robbery that was reported just before 2 p.m. on Sept. 13 on the 6000 block of Hillegass Avenue, near Claremont Avenue, according to Rosalia Lopez, a spokesperson for department.
