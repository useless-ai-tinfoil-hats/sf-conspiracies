Injured tiger cub rescued by Oakland Zoo is ready for move to sanctuary, officials say
-----
A tiger cub who was rescued from an illegal private owner and brought to the Oakland Zoo suffering from multiple fractures has recuperated to the point of being cleared for release to a big-cat sanctuary, officials said."She is mobile, she's strong, she's playful, and we're all optimistic for her recovery," said Dr. Alex Herman, vice president of veterinary services at the Oakland Zoo.
