Ayelet Waldman, famed Berkeley author, released from Israel-Gaza border after arrest
-----
Ayelet Waldman, a famed Berkeley author, was arrested and released Friday after protesting the war with a group of American rabbis at the Erez Crossing, the border between Israel and Gaza.Waldman's husband, novelist Michael Chabon, initially announced her arrest on social media.
Chabon, an award-winning novelist and author, posted a video of Waldman being detained by Israeli police officers as the protesting group, Rabbis for Ceasefire, approached the border.
