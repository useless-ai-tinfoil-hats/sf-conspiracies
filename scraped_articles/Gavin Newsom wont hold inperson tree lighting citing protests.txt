Gavin Newsom won't hold in-person tree lighting, citing protests
-----
SACRAMENTO — California Gov. Gavin Newsom opted to make the Capitol's annual tree-lighting ceremony virtual amid safety concerns over protests, his office said Tuesday, prompting harsh criticism from the organizers of a pro-Palestinian rally."As we continue to see protests across the country impacting the safety of events of all scales — and for the safety and security of all participating members and guests including children and families — the ceremony this year will be virtual,"
