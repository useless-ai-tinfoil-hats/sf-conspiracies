The Calif. observatory that changed how we think about the universe
-----
On Jan. 1, 1925, astronomer Edwin Hubble completely changed the way we view the universe and our place in it. The discovery came from peering through what was then the world's largest telescope, located on a remote mountaintop in Angeles National Forest. Not only was the view world-altering, but it was also life-changing for Hubble, who became one of the most well-known names in astronomy.
