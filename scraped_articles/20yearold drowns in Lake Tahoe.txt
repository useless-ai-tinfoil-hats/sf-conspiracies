20-year-old drowns in Lake Tahoe
-----
A 20-year-old man drowned in Lake Tahoe on Sunday afternoon. The victim was identified as Owen McDonough of Michigan, said Paul Robbins, a detective with the Carson City Sheriff's Office. McDonough's family lives in Kentucky.McDonough was swimming at Whale Beach, on Tahoe's East Shore, with a group of friends.
