Cal alum traded ecology career for Olympic breakdancing dream as ‘B-Girl La Vix’
-----
Coyote mint has a bit of an ugly phase as it grows. It doesn't do so well in the full sun, instead thriving in foggy environments, rebounding often in coastal landscapes as weather changes. It never gets too large.It is Vicki Chang's favorite plant. The former restoration ecologist — better known as "B-Girl La Vix" in the breakdancing circuit — brightens up when talking about plants. Her dog, an 11-year-old Formosan Mountain/German shepherd mix, is named Oaky after valley oak trees.
