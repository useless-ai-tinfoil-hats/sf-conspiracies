Kitka postpones ‘Wintersongs’ concert program after a COVID outbreak
-----
Kitka, the Bay Area women's chorus specializing in choral music from the Balkans, has postponed the final three concerts of its annual "Wintersongs" program because of an outbreak of COVID-19 among members of the group.The concerts, scheduled for Friday-Sunday, Dec. 15-17, at venues in Oakland and San Francisco, have been rescheduled to Feb. 9-11 at the same locations.
