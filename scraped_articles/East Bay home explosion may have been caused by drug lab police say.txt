East Bay home explosion may have been caused by drug lab, police say
-----
A person was hospitalized in critical condition after an explosion Tuesday at a Fremont home that police suspect was used for a drug lab.Authorities were dispatched to the residence on the 37000 block of Waverly Terrace around 9:30 a.m. following the explosion. Police said the residents of the home had left, and one person checked into an area hospital after the incident and was listed in critical condition.
