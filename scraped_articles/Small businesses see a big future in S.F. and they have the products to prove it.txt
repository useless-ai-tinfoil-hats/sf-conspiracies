Small businesses see a big future in S.F., and they have the products to prove it
-----
For Herbert Gracia, the co-founder of clothing brand State of Flux, the decision to keep his business operations entirely in San Francisco, from designing to manufacturing to production, was easy."We live here. We want to invest back into the community," said the San Francisco resident of more than two decades. "And if more people that look like us have businesses here, more kids like us can have the chance to do a little more, too."
