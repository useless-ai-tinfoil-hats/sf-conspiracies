Warriors’ 2024-25 schedule release: When they’ll face Klay Thompson, and other key games
-----
The Golden State Warriors are a staple this season on national television.Yet again.
The Warriors are booked to play 24 nationally televised games — on ESPN, TNT or ABC — in 2024-25, they announced Thursday in conjunction with the NBA's schedule release. Their season begins Oct. 23 on the road against the Portland Trail Blazers. Their home opener is Oct. 27 against the Los Angeles Clippers.
