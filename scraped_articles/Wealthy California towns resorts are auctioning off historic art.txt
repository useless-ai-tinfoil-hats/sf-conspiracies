Wealthy California town's resorts are auctioning off historic art
-----
Historic artwork that once lined the walls of some of California's priciest resorts is up for auction.Water colors, posters and portraits, among other art, will go to the highest bidder starting May 9 at 10 a.m. PT, as part of a sale by Abell Auction Co. The works were once featured in Pebble Beach resorts and date back to the early 20th century.
