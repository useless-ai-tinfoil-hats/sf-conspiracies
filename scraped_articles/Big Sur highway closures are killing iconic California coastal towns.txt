Big Sur highway closures are killing iconic California coastal towns
-----
Big Sur's past two years of storm-related road closures and the efforts of the community to survive in that time have been well-publicized. But Big Sur's closure has also had a significant impact on the surrounding areas of the Central Coast, namely the three towns just south of there: San Simeon, Cambria and Harmony.Even as one section of Highway 1 reopens, many travelers, like Max Bostma and Larissa Hodnik from the Netherlands, are only able to go so far.
