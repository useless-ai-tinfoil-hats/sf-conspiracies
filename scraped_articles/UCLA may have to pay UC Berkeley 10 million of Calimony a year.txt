UCLA may have to pay UC Berkeley $10 million of ‘Calimony’ a year
-----
University of California President Michael Drake is recommending that UCLA pay Cal $10 million a year through the 2029-30 school year to help the school shore up its athletic department in the wake of the collapse of the Pac-12.The "Calimony" payments — which will be considered by the UC Board of Regents at its meeting next week at UC Merced — follow UCLA's divorce from the Pac-12 to join the Big Ten.
