NFL Germany 2023: How to watch the games this November
-----

Continuing the league's international series, two games are slated for November at Frankfurt Stadium: Miami Dolphins vs. Kansas City Chiefs (Nov. 5) and Indianapolis Colts vs. New England Patriots (Nov. 12).
