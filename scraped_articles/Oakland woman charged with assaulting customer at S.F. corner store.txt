Oakland woman charged with assaulting customer at S.F. corner store
-----
San Francisco prosecutors have charged an Oakland woman suspected of attacking a person without provocation in a corner store on Potrero Hill, spokespersons said.Jessica Blazee, 26, was charged with assault with a deadly weapon and assault with force likely to cause great bodily injury for an incident Tuesday in which she is suspected of attacking an unsuspecting victim at a corner store on the 2200 block of 23rd Street, spokespersons said Thursday.
