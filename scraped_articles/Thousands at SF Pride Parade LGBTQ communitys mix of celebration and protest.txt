Thousands of people came out Sunday for San Francisco's Pride Parade, a rainbow-colored love letter to the LGBTQ community that attracts celebrants from all over the world.Spectators lined up along Market Street hours in advance for SF Pride, one of the largest LGBTQ celebrations in the world and the culmination of weeks of events. This year's parade theme, "Beacon of Love," resonated with many in the crowd because of political divisions among people domestically and globally.
