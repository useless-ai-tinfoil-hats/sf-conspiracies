Bay Area man found guilty of beheading mom of two
-----
A man was found guilty Monday of beheading a Bay Area mother of two in a domestic violence murder that shocked the region.According to her father, Karina Castro, 27, was confronted outside her San Carlos residence by her ex-partner Jose Rafael Solano Landaeta on Sept. 8, 2022. Prosecutors say Landaeta, who lived in Hayward and drove to Castro's home with a sword and the intention to murder her, then took the weapon to her neck repeatedly and killed her in the front yard.
