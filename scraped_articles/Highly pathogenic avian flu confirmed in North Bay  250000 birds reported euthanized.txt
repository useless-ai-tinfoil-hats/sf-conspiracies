‘Highly pathogenic’ avian flu confirmed in North Bay — 250,000 birds reported euthanized
-----
California agriculture authorities have confirmed cases of avian flu in commercial bird flocks in Sonoma and San Benito counties, marking the spread of a nationwide outbreak of the deadly and highly infectious virus that began in 2022, according to the state Department of Food and Agriculture.In Sonoma County, the Santa Rosa Press Democrat reported that the two affected poultry farms near Petaluma, Sunrise Farms and Reichardt Duck Farm, were euthanizing about 250,000 birds in response
