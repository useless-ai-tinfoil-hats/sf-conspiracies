APEC in San Francisco: Filoli estate closed to public amid reports of Biden, Xi meeting
-----
Today marks the beginning of the busiest period of the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit in San Francisco as the international conference kicks into high gear. Tens of thousands of visitors, including corporate executives, foreign dignitaries and protestors, are descending on San Francisco. President Biden and Vice-President Harris landed Tuesday, as did Chinese President Xi Jinping; Biden is expected to meet with Xi on Wednesday, reportedly at the beautiful Filoli estate in San Mateo
