Love dorado-style burritos? Try crisping up tamales

There's something about "tamales" that rolls off the tongue for me. It's kind of fitting because tamales — essentially big dumplings of savory masa — are easy to eat and easy to enjoy. With fillings that range from cheese and sauteed peppers to braised pork or even sweet options, there's a tamale for everyone.Typically steamed and enjoyed as-is, tamales can also be treated like other dumplings.
