Stormy Bay Area weather leads to SFO delays
-----
Tuesday's stormy conditions and low-hanging clouds have prompted a ground delay at San Francisco International Airport, causing delays for in-bound flights, federal officials said.The ground delay will last from 9 a.m. to 10 a.m., according to the Federal Aviation Administration.

As of 8:30 a.m. there were 115 flight delays and 3 cancellations at SFO, according to the flight tracking website FlightAware.
