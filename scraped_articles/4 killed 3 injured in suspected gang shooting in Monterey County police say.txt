4 killed, 3 injured in suspected gang shooting in Monterey County, police say
-----
Four people were killed and three others injured Sunday in a suspected gang shooting in Monterey County, police said.A woman and three men were killed in the shooting, the King City Police Department said in a news release late Sunday.
Three other victims, all men, were taken to Natividad hospital in Salinas for treatment, police said.
