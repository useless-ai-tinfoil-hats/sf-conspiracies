Warriors' Steve Kerr in a foul mood after Christmas Day loss in Denver
-----
The NBA's schedule-makers slotted Golden State Warriors-Denver Nuggets as its prime Christmas Day showdown for a simple reason: Stephen Curry against Nikola Jokic and the defending champions figured to be must-watch TV.But after witnessing his team surrender 14 second-half free throws to Jokic on Monday, Warriors head coach Steve Kerr admitted that he wouldn't have wanted to watch the final two quarters of Golden State's 120-114 loss to the Nuggets at Ball Arena if he didn't have to.
