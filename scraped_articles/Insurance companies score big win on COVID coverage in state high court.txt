Insurance companies score big win on COVID coverage in state high court
-----
In a major victory for insurance companies, the California Supreme Court ruled unanimously Thursday that businesses that were forced to shut down because of COVID-19, or had to cancel concerts or sporting events during the pandemic, did not suffer property damage that was covered by insurance.Standard insurance policies cover only financial losses caused by physical damage to the policyholder's property.
