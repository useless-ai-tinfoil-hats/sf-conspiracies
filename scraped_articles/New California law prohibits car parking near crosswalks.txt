New California law prohibits car parking near crosswalks
-----
California Gov. Gavin Newsom signed a bill Tuesday that will ban vehicles from parking near crosswalks, a measure that aims to make roadways safer for pedestrians, cyclists and other drivers.Assembly Bill 413 is designed to prohibit drivers from parking "within 20 feet of the vehicle approach side of any unmarked or marked crosswalk."
