San Jose State University to convert large downtown hotel tower into student housing
-----
In downtown San Jose, a large hotel building will soon be converted into undergraduate housing for San Jose State University students.SJSU is partnering with investment firm Throckmorton Partners to convert the south tower of the Signia by Hilton San Jose hotel complex at 170 S. Market St. into housing for over 700 students, the university announced in a news release. The hotel sits four blocks from the school's campus in the center of downtown San Jose.
