Outside Lands standout Grace Jones stuns from 60 feet in the air
-----
Grace Jones knows how to make an entrance. As the sun held onto its last light on Saturday at San Francisco's Outside Lands, the curtains at the Lands End stage unfurled to reveal Jones towering over her band, standing on a riser several stories high.
