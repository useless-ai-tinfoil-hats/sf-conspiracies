A’s fans plan Opening Day boycott after MLB owners approve Las Vegas move
-----
Perhaps it's not any shock, but outrage consumed Oakland Athletics fans on Thursday following the Major League Baseball owners' unanimous vote to relocate the franchise to Las Vegas.Owners voted in Arlington, Texas, to give permission for John Fisher to move his club — which has been in Oakland since 1968 — to Vegas. Some fans had flown to the meetings to protest, even as various efforts to convince Fisher to sell the team and stop the move have had little effect.
