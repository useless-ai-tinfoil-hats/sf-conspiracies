Pizza Hut franchises in California lay off all delivery drivers ahead of minimum wage increase
-----
Pizza Hut franchises in California are laying off their staff delivery drivers in anticipation of a new state law raising the minimum wage to $20 per hour in 2024. The move will result in over 1,200 job cuts.In regulatory filings, PacPizza in San Ramon, which operates Pizza Hut establishments throughout California, said it plans to "eliminate first-party delivery services" starting in February.
