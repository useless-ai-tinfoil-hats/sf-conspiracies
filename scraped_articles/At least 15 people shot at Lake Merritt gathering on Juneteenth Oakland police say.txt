At least 15 people shot at Lake Merritt gathering on Juneteenth, Oakland police say
-----
At least 15 people were shot Wednesday night when gunfire erupted near Oakland's Lake Merritt during Juneteenth gatherings that devolved into a chaotic sideshow, police said.The mass shooting involved multiple shooters, and police recovered at least 50 shell casings, Oakland Police Chief Floyd Mitchell said during a news conference Thursday. Police had not yet determined how many shooters were involved, and no arrests had been made in the shootings as of Thursday afternoon, Mitchell
