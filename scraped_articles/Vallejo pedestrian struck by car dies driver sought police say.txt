Vallejo pedestrian struck by car dies; driver sought, police say
-----
A Vallejo resident who died Friday night on a city street was struck by a car that fled the scene, police said.Responding to a call shortly before 9 p.m., patrol officers found the pedestrian, described by police as a 37-year-old man, lying unresponsive in the street on the 3200 block of Sonoma Boulevard, at the intersection of Valle Vista Avenue, the Vallejo Police Department said in a news release.
