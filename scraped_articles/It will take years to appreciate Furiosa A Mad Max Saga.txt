It will take years to appreciate 'Furiosa: A Mad Max Saga'
-----
"Mad Max: Fury Road" is the greatest action movie I've ever seen. I've tried to make a case to myself for "Die Hard," but "Die Hard" doesn't have double guitars, which leaves it hopelessly behind. The first time I saw "Fury Road" in the theater, I jumped up in my seat and made all kinds of spontaneous dad sounds, much to the chagrin of my friend sitting next to me.
