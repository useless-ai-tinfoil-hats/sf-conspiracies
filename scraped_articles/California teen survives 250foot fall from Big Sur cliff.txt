California teen survives 250-foot fall from Big Sur cliff
-----
A hiker was rescued on Jan. 4 after a 250-foot fall from a trail in California's Ventana Wilderness near Big Sur, officials said.The 19-year-old from Atascadero was on the Pine Ridge Trail near the Sykes Campground when he fell off a cliff, according to the California Highway Patrol's Coastal Division Air Operations. He fell at about 4:30 p.m.
