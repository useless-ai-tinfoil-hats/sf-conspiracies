Bay Area inflation jumps amid soaring electricity rates
-----
Fast-rising electricity prices partly fueled a stubborn rise in the San Francisco Bay Area's rate of inflation, which was 3.8% higher in April compared to this time last year, according to a recent Consumer Price Index report.The inflation rate in the Bay Area was the highest it's been since April 2023, when it was 4.2% year over year.
