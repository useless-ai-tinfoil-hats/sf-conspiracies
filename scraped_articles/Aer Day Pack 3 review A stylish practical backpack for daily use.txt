Aer Day Pack 3 review: A stylish, practical backpack for daily use
-----
The Aer Day Pack 3 backpack is a serious upgrade from what I've been sporting to and from the office the last few weeks. My previous work backpack was bulky, had very little organization, and looked like a super-sized version of something I would have brought to summer camp when I was eight years old (I like this bag, but it's not age-appropriate and didn't suit my needs). I needed something sleeker and designed to make my life feel more put together, with pockets, pouches, and all that good
