Girl, 13, still missing in Berkeley
-----
Police are looking for a Berkeley girl who went missing in the early hours of Sunday morning. Amayah Brumfield, 13, is considered "at risk," Berkeley police said in a missing person bulletin.Amayah was last known to be on the 700 block of Harrison Street in Berkeley, which is a few blocks from Interstate 580. An initial report from the Berkeley Police Department said she was last seen at 12:30 a.m. Sunday. On Monday, a new bulletin said she was last seen at 4 p.m. Sunday on the 700 block
