A Chez Panisse alum just opened this pizza and pasta restaurant in S.F.
-----
A casual California Italian restaurant from a Chez Panisse alum is now serving wood-fired pizza and handmade pastas in San Francisco.The Rustic is now open at 215 Church St., near the border of the Mission and the Castro. Zoti Ali Turap, a former Chez Panisse dining room manager, is the main partner and wine director behind the restaurant.
