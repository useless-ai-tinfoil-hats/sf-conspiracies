Sacramento man sentenced after admitting to stealing 700 pieces of mail in San Leandro
-----
A Sacramento man was sentenced to two years of prison after he was convicted of breaking into a mail truck and stealing more than 700 pieces of mail in San Leandro, federal prosecutors said Wednesday.Craig Curtis Freeman pleaded guilty to the Dec. 30, 2022, robbery. Freeman said in a plea agreement that when driving a stolen car he broke the lock of a U.S. Postal Service mail truck, removed more than 700 pieces of mail, stashed them in the car and drove away.
