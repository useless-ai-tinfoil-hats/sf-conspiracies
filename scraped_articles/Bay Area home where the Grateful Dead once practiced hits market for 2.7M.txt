Bay Area home where the Grateful Dead once practiced hits market for $2.7M
-----
The Grateful Dead gathered in their early days to practice in the garage of this Palo Alto home, and it is here that its members officially named the band that would become one of the most iconic in the world. Now, the "Dead House" has come on the market, for an asking price of $2.695 million.This home at 1012 High St. is a 1918 Craftsman, featuring two bedrooms and one bathroom in 1,008 square feet of living space.
