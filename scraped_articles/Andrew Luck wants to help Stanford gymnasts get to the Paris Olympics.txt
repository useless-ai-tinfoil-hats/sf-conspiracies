Andrew Luck wants to help Stanford gymnasts get to the Paris Olympics
-----
Andrew Luck's commitment to Stanford athletics is reaching for the international stage, as he and his wife, Nicole Pechanec, plan to provide housing for five gymnasts trying to make it to Paris this summer.NBC Sports' Zora Stephenson made note of this decision during the U.S. Gymnastics Championships this past weekend in Fort Worth, Texas.
