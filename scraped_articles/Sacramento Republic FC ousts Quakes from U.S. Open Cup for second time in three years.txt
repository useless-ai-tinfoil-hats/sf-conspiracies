Sacramento Republic FC ousts Quakes from U.S. Open Cup for second time in three years
-----
Sacramento Republic FC scored two goals a minute apart in extra time Tuesday night and beat the San Jose Earthquakes 4-3 in a U.S. Open Cup round-of-16 game at Heart Health Park.It marked the second time in three years Sacramento — a USL Championship team — eliminated the MLS-entry Quakes from the U.S. Open Cup. In 2022, Sacramento won 2-0 on its way to a surprising run to the tournament's championship game, which it lost to Orlando City.
