Group including Laurene Powell Jobs interested in buying S.F. Art Institute
-----
A group of nine arts and business leaders, including philanthropist Laurene Powell Jobs, is pursuing the purchase of the bankrupt San Francisco Art Institute's vacant Russian Hill campus.David Stull, president and CEO of the San Francisco Conservatory of Music, confirmed that he is part of a group that has been working on a plan for the past four months, with members including Powell and Brenda Way, founder of the ODC/Dance Company.
