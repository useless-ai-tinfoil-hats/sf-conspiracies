Suspect, 4 bystanders injured during S.F. police chase
-----
Five people, including a pedestrian and a suspect wanted by police, sustained injuries after police officers chased the suspect in his car through San Francisco this week.The chase began around 6 p.m. Tuesday when officers spotted a vehicle involved in a previous hit-and-run collision and tried to pull over the driver, who fled, San Francisco Police Department spokesperson Robert Rueca said Thursday. The chase began near Civic Center Plaza at at Larkin and Hyde streets, he said.
