Mysterious illness sickens 25 students at a North Bay high school
-----
A mysterious illness sickened 25 students at a Napa high school on Thursday and five were taken to the hospital, officials said.The incident at Vintage High School was reported to dispatchers at 9:10 a.m. after students reported feeling ill in their choir class, said Jaina French, a spokesperson for the city of Napa. French said Napa firefighters and police arrived at the school and the students were removed from the choir room to get examined by medical personnel.
