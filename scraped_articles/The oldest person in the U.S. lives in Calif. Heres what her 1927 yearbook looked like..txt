The oldest person in the U.S. lives in Calif. Here's what her 1927 yearbook looked like.
-----
Edith Ceccarelli, the oldest known person in the United States at 116 years old, hails from the NorCal town of Willits – and the Historical Society of Mendocino County still has a copy of her senior high school yearbook.In celebration of Ceccarelli's birthday on Feb. 5, the historical society created a video showcasing stills from the 1927 "Mistletoe" yearbook for Willits High School, many of which feature Ceccarelli née Edie Recagno.
