‘Internal matter’ sidelines Bay FC captain Alex Loera in tough loss to Spirit
-----
The first player to join the Bay FC roster, Alex Loera, was out for Saturday's 2-1 loss against the Washington Spirit due to what head coach Albertin Montoya said was an "internal matter." She was spotted in Washington, D.C., after the game in street clothes by reporters on site.Montoya said after the match that the club would not be commenting further.
