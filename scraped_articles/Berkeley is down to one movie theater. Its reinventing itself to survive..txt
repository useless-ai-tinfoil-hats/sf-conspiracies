Berkeley is down to one movie theater. It's reinventing itself to survive.
-----
Rialto Cinemas in Elmwood is Berkeley's last remaining commercial movie theater. But even with no competition left in the city, it's still having to find new ways to survive. Enter: beer, wine and charcuterie.Earlier this month, the Elmwood theater introduced a new hot food and drink menu to entice audiences to the silver screen, as first reported by East Bay Nosh.
