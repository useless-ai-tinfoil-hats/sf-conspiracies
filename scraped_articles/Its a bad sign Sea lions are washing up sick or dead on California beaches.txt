'It's a bad sign': Sea lions are washing up sick or dead on California beaches
-----
A couple of weeks ago, the Marine Mammal Center started receiving dozens of calls reporting sick or dead sea lions on Bay Area beaches and along the California coastline, and a recurring disease in the pinnipeds is to blame.From Doran and Salmon Creek beaches in Bodega Bay to San Luis Obispo, Monterey and Fort Bragg in Mendocino County, the sea lions appeared lethargic and woozy; some were underweight and were later found to have stomach ulcers.
