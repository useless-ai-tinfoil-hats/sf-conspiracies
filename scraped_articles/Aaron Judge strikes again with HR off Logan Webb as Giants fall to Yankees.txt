Aaron Judge strikes again with HR off Logan Webb as Giants fall to Yankees
-----
The New York Yankees needed two swings to bury the San Francisco Giants. That's just the type of team it is.Giancarlo Stanton's two-run blast in the eighth inning off one of the more consistent relievers in Ryan Walker dashed any dreams of a late comeback, helping turn the Yankees' slim lead into a comfortable one. It also bookended the scoring after Aaron Judge's two-run homer nearly cleared the left-field bleachers at 464 feet in the first inning off Logan Webb, his third of the series
