The story behind Calif.'s 'Hate Vegans' billboard campaign
-----
An eye-catching new billboard is turning heads near one of the busiest intersections in Los Angeles. The maroon and yellow ad, complete with a large red siren light and a lawyerly type in a suit, asks a question that seems to demand attention: "Injured by a vegan?"What exactly does it mean to be "injured by a vegan"? And who is the guy in the suit with the smug face? Odd California billboards that ask as many questions as they answer are nothing new, but this one turns up the heat a bit
