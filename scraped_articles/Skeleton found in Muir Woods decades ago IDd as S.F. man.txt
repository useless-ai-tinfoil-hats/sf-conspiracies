Skeleton found in Muir Woods decades ago ID’d as S.F. man
-----
The skeletal remains of a man found in Muir Woods National Monument more than 40 years ago have been identified, officials said.The Marin County Sheriff's Office identified the man — found in November 1982 near the Deer Park Fire Trail — as Sigvard Svensson of San Francisco. Svensson was born in 1908 and immigrated to the United States from Sweden in 1926, spokespersons said.
