When it comes to public safety, rolling back police oversight is never the answer
-----
When San Francisco Mayor London Breed earlier this month condemned the city's Police Commission for "prioritizing ideology before community safety," she was essentially arguing that police should not be bothered by tedious, but vital accountability measures.
