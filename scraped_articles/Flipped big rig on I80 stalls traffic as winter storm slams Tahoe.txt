Flipped big rig on I-80 stalls traffic as winter storm slams Tahoe
-----
A big rig flipped on eastbound Interstate 80 near Truckee, according to California Highway Patrol, just as a strong storm started plowing through Tahoe and the Sierra.At 7:37 a.m., a semitruck flipped on its side, blocking all lanes near the Donner Lake Interchange. The driver walked away with only minor injuries, CHP Truckee said on social media. Cal Fire and a towing company were called to assist.
