Man alleges wife was killed by food at a Disney World restaurant
-----
A New York doctor died from an allergic reaction after eating at a Disney Springs restaurant, her husband alleges in a lawsuit filed last week in Florida.According to the suit, Kanokporn Tangsuan, her husband Jeffrey Piccolo and Piccolo's mother visited Disney Springs, the outdoor shopping and dining complex in the Disney World Resort, on Oct. 5, 2023. They decided to eat at Raglan Road, an Irish pub, in part "because they believed Disney would have proper safeguards to protect patrons"
