Roger Federer’s impression of S.F. on first visit? ‘Wonderful’ city for tennis
-----
Roger Federer's first visit to San Francisco came on a glorious sunny day. Beyond the windows on the top floor of Chase Center, the bay sparkled, the Oakland hills popped and the selection of the locale as the site for the 2025 Laver Cup, Federer's brainchild event, seemed perfect.The only gloomy cloud was the tennis icon's realization that Chase's residing superstar, Stephen Curry, would likely not be playing in Saturday's game, which Federer planned to attend.
