Review: Opera San José offers a nimble array of delights with ‘Barber’
-----
In "The Barber of Seville," Rossini offered a swift compendium of everything comic opera could be in the early decades of the 19th century. The piece includes romance, lyricism, vocal fireworks, and humor that's both broad and subtle, all in the space of a fast-paced three hours.There's a reason it never fails to charm and delight its audiences, and "Barber" worked its magic again Sunday, Nov. 12, during the second performance of a new production at Opera San José.
