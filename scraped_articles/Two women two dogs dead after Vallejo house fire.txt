Two women, two dogs dead after Vallejo house fire
-----
One woman and two dogs were found dead after a Vallejo house caught fire early Monday morning, and a second woman died at the hospital after she was rescued from the burning home, officials said.Around 2:45 a.m. firefighters responded to multiple reports of a fire coming from the front of a single-family home at 107 De Paul Drive in North Vallejo.
