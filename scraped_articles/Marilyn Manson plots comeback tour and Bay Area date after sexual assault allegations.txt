Marilyn Manson plots comeback tour and Bay Area date after sexual assault allegations
-----
Marilyn Manson is set to embark on his first series of tour dates since facing accusations of sexual abuse by multiple women in recent years.The 55-year-old musician, whose legal name is Brian Warner, is slated to join the metal band Five Finger Death Punch for performances in amphitheaters and arenas across North America later this year, including Shoreline Amphitheatre in Mountain View on Sept. 3.
