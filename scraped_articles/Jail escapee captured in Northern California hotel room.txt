Jail escapee captured in Northern California hotel room
-----
A man who escaped San Benito County Jail in Hollister has been captured in a San Jose hotel room, authorities said.Daniel Lopez Zavala, 38, had hopped a barbed wire fence at the jail early Tuesday after being arraigned in early May on misdemeanor charges of assault and battery, the San Benito County Sheriff's Office reported.
