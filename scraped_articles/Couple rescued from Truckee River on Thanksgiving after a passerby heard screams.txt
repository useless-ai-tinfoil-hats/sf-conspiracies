Couple rescued from Truckee River on Thanksgiving after a passerby heard screams
-----
A couple was rescued from the Truckee River on Thanksgiving Day after being stranded in the water, officials said.Fire crews arrived at the river in east Truckee Canyon, Nevada, just before 5 p.m. on Thursday after receiving a report of people screaming, Adam Mayberry, a spokesperson for the Truckee Meadows Fire and Rescue Department, told SFGATE.
