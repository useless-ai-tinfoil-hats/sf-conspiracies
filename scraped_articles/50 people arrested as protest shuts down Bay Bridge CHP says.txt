50 people arrested as protest shuts down Bay Bridge, CHP says
-----
All westbound lanes opened on the San Francisco-Oakland Bay Bridge just before noon Thursday, California Highway Patrol said. Throughout the morning commute, protesters fully shut down westbound traffic as world leaders gathered in San Francisco for the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit.Approximately 50 protesters were arrested and 15 cars were towed, CHP said.
