UC Davis looking for missing student last seen in San Francisco
-----
The UC Davis Police Department is asking the public for help locating a missing student last seen in San Francisco. Diane Shoyeon Min, 18, was reported missing on Monday and is thought to be "in distress," police said in a social media post.Min, a first-year engineering student, left campus Friday night and took Amtrak to San Francisco, police said. She was last seen near Mission Street wearing a dark top and pants and black shoes, police said.
