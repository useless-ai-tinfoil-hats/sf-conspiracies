Anne Hathaway creates femme fatale persona in 1964-set ‘Eileen’
-----
Anne Hathaway has a routine that keeps her grounded while she's making movies: At the end of every day, the actor goes home to her husband, actor/producer Adam Shulman, and two young sons, and shakes off her character to resume domestic life.But that changed when it came to her latest role as Rebecca, the glamorous psychologist at a New England juvenile facility in William Oldroyd's "Eileen." A woman with a mysterious past, Rebecca quickly becomes enmeshed with the eponymous jail clerk
