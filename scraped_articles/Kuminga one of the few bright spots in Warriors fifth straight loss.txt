Kuminga one of the few bright spots in Warriors' fifth straight loss
-----
With Draymond Green unavailable for four more games, the Golden State Warriors have an opportunity to jump-start Jonathan Kuminga's season. Shorthanded and losers of five straight games, at least the Warriors have that going for them."He's had some good games and some bad games," head coach Steve Kerr said. "He'll get Draymond's spot in the lineup and we'll give him every opportunity to succeed."
