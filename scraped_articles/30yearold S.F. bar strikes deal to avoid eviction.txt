30-year-old S.F. bar strikes deal to avoid eviction
-----
After being threatened with eviction, a popular spot for cocktails and pool in San Francisco's Sunset District will remain open.Blackthorn co-owner David Zimmerman told the Chronicle the bar's ownership and landlord company McGrath Enterprises came to an agreement to let the bar operate at its longtime space at 834 Irving St. for the next 18 months. Zimmerman says he and business partner Christopher Lamotte will look to remain in the vicinity after that period is up.
