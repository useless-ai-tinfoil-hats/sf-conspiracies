McDonald's drops Shamrock Shake 2 weeks earlier than expected
-----
In an earlier-than-expected luck of the Irish moment, McDonald's added its famous green shake back to menus two weeks earlier than last year.On Feb. 5, the famous Shamrock Shake returned to menus nationwide, according to Food & Wine. The neon green, mint-flavored soft serve ice cream shake didn't hit menus last year until Feb. 20, about a month before St. Patrick's Day.
