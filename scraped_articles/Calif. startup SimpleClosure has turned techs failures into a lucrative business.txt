Calif. startup SimpleClosure has turned tech's failures into a lucrative business
-----
If you catch your boss browsing SimpleClosure's website, it's likely that bad news looms for you and your colleagues.The Los Angeles-based company doesn't come visiting in the best of times; it helps other companies die less painful deaths. When customers-to-be arrive with languishing businesses, SimpleClosure advises them on everything from filing regulatory paperwork to sending final paychecks.
