No one has guarded Sabonis more than Warriors' Looney. Could Kerr still sit him?
-----
When the Golden State Warriors took down the Sacramento Kings in the playoffs last season, the unsung hero of the series was Kevon Looney. The big man completely shut down Kings All-Star center Domantas Sabonis, reaffirming that he has a sturdy place in Dubs lore forever.But it's a year later now, and with a single game against the Kings looming Tuesday in a win-or-go-home setup, the Warriors may not play Looney at all.
