San Francisco men charged with selling drugs out of RV in the Mission
-----
The San Francisco District Attorney's Office charged two men in connection with narcotics trafficking out of an RV in the Mission.San Francisco residents Ronald Butera and Robert Pritchard, both 46 years old, were charged after being arrested at the end of May. San Francisco police seized 9 pounds of drugs and over $630,000 from the two as part of an investigation, prosecutors said.
