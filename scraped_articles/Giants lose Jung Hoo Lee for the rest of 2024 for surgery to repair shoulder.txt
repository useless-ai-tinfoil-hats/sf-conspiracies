Giants lose Jung Hoo Lee for the rest of 2024 for surgery to repair shoulder
-----
The San Francisco Giants announced Friday that Jung Hoo Lee will have season-ending surgery on his left shoulder.The rookie center fielder will undergo the surgery in the next two weeks with Dr. Neal ElAttrache in Los Angeles with a six-month timeline for return, President of Baseball Operations Farhan Zaidi said.
