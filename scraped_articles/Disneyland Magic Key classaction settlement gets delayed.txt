Disneyland Magic Key class-action settlement gets delayed
-----
Disneyland Magic Key holders will have to wait a bit longer before the details of a class-action settlement are finalized in court.On Thursday, a district court judge in California's Central District agreed to extend the deadline for Disney Parks and plaintiff Jenale Nielsen to file a motion for a class-action settlement. They now have until Sept. 7 to file the preliminary motion for the court's approval.
