Fleet Week: Jet team to honor late Sen. Feinstein with ‘missing woman formation’
-----
This year, San Francisco Fleet Week will feature a special tribute to the late Sen. Dianne Feinstein, who founded the annual air show in 1981 as mayor of San Francisco.While the entire air show is dedicated to the senator, on Saturday and Sunday at 12:57 p.m., the Patriots Jet Team, a civilian aerobatic formation team, will fly over the city to commemorate Feinstein's contributions to San Francisco and the nation. The team will fly in a "missing woman formation," confirmed the Air Show
