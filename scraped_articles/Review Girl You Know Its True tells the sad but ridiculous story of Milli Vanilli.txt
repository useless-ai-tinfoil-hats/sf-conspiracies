Review: ‘Girl You Know It’s True’ tells the sad but ridiculous story of Milli Vanilli
-----
In the 1980s, responding to the influence of MTV, people used to fret that future pop stars would rise to prominence on the basis of how they looked, not how they sounded. As if on cue, Milli Vanilli emerged, a pop duo from Germany that became the focus of one of the biggest scandals in music history.They looked great and their synchronized dance moves were kinetic — but they couldn't sing. They wanted to sing, but their German producers used them instead as frontmen, to appear on album
