Despite protest, S.F. Gashouse Cove renovation plan gets first approval
-----
In the face of overwhelming public dissent, the San Francisco Recreation and Park Commission voted Thursday to advance a controversial plan to renovate Gashouse Cove to the Planning Department for environmental review.The unanimous vote came after an amendment to the motion that will direct staff to do a financial feasibility study to minimize boat slips that will obstruct the view in front of the Marina Green.
