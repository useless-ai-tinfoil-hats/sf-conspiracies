S.F. Symphony musicians sign new contract after nearly a year-long battle
-----
The musicians and management of the San Francisco Symphony ratified a two-year contract on Sunday, Oct. 1, bringing a halt — at least for the time being — to the labor struggle that has occasionally bubbled into view for the orchestra's patrons.The new contract extends through November 2024, according to violist David Gaudry, the longtime head of the players' committee.
