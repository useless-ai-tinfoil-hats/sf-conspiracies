‘The Cher Show’ coming to San Francisco
-----
Attention all gypsies, tramps and thieves: "The Cher Show" is slated for a San Francisco visit in June.The Tony Award-winning jukebox musical tracing the life and career of the timeless pop and film diva is scheduled for a limited run at the Curran on June 18-23, courtesy of BroadwaySF.
Tickets, which are $44.50-$124.50, go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday, March 15, via www.broadwaysf.com. Group orders of 10 or more may be placed by calling 888-746-1799 or emailing
