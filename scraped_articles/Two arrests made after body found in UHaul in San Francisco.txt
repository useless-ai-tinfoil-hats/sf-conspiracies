Two arrests made after body found in U-Haul in San Francisco
-----
Northern California authorities arrested two individuals in connection with a body that was brought to a San Francisco Police Station in a U-Haul on Aug. 4, according to a Tuesday press release from the Butte County Sheriff's Department.Harold Rodriguez-Blanco, 24, and Graciela Buenrostromendoza, 31, were arrested last month and both people were charged with felony counts of attempted robbery, kidnapping and false imprisonment, according to the department.
