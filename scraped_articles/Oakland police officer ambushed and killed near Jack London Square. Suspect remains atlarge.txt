Oakland police officer ambushed and killed near Jack London Square. Suspect remains at-large
-----
An Oakland police officer who responded to a report of a burglary was fatally shot near Jack London Square on Friday, police officials said.Few details were immediately available about the shooting on Embarcadero near Fifth Avenue. Officials said officers had responded to the reported burglary about 4:40 a.m. Barry Donelan, president of the police union, said an officer was ambushed and shot.
