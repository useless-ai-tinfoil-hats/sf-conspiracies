Santa Barbara Airport closes indefinitely as runways flood
-----
Santa Barbara Airport is closed with no reopening time due to flooding on the runways. The closure began shortly before 5 a.m. Monday as more rain fell on the already soaked soil of the Central Coast."Commercial flights have been canceled, general aviation operations are paused, and the Terminal is closed," airport officials said in a statement. "For info about specific flights, please contact your airline directly."
