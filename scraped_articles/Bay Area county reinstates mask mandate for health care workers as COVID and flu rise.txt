Bay Area county reinstates mask mandate for health care workers as COVID and flu rise
-----
Officials in Sonoma County have issued a health order requiring health care workers in patient care settings to wear masks in response to the increasing risk of respiratory illnesses, including COVID-19 and influenza.Dr. Karen Smith, the county's interim health officer, stated that the order issued on Tuesday will remain in effect from Nov. 1 through April 30, covering the respiratory virus season.
