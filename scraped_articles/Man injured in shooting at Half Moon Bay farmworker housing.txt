Man injured in shooting at Half Moon Bay farmworker housing
-----
A man was injured in a shooting at a farmworker housing development in Half Moon Bay, according to the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office and media reports.The shooting occurred at about 7:30 p.m. Saturday at the Moon Ridge apartments, a housing development for farmworkers at 2001 Miramontes Point Road, according to Heather Enders, a spokesperson for the sheriff's office. The details were first reported by ABC7.
