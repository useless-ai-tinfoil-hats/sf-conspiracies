A’s Joey Estes takes perfect game into 7th; Oakland holds on to beat Seattle
-----
Strikeout, flyball, strikeout, flyball, flyball, flyball, nothing wildly overwhelming, and yet Joey Estes retired the first 18 Mariners he faced Wednesday night at the Coliseum, two full turns through the batting order.Estes, part of the Matt Olson trade with Atlanta in 2022, didn't allow a hit until J.P. Crawford's leadoff double in the seventh and wound up working 7⅓ shutout innings in the Oakland A's 2-1 victory over Seattle and earned his second career win.
