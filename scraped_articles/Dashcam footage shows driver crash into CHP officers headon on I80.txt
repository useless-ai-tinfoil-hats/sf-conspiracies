Dashcam footage shows driver crash into CHP officers head-on on I-80
-----
Two California Highway Patrol officers were rushed to the hospital after a driver "intentionally" crashed into their vehicle on Wednesday, the department wrote in a March 14 Facebook post.The two officers had just completed an enforcement stop in Davis and were on the right shoulder of Interstate 80 when a driver "targeted" them and plowed into them head-on, Assistant Commissioner Ezery Beauchamp said in a recorded announcement.
