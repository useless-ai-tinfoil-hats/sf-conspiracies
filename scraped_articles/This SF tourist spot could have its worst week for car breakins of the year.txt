This SF tourist spot could have its worst week for car break-ins of the year
-----
If you do any sight-seeing around San Francisco this week, be extra-wary of leaving anything in the car, especially at popular tourist spots.This is historically the worst week of the year for smash-and-grabs by Alamo Square in particular, according to data crunching from the San Francisco Chronicle, which analyzed police department records from 2021 and 2022. (The Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms.)
