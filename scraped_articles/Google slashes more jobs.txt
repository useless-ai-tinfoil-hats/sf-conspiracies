Google slashes more jobs
-----
Google employees were hit by another round of layoffs this week, this time in its recruiting department, a result the company said was due to a drop in hiring needs."As we've said, we continue to invest in top engineering and technical talent while also meaningfully slowing the pace of our overall hiring. In line with this, the volume of requests for our recruiters has gone down," Bailey Tomson, a Google spokesperson, said.
