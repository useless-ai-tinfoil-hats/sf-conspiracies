Marisa Ingemi wins Excellence in Sports Writing award from NLGJA
-----
Chronicle staff writer Marisa Ingemi was awarded the Excellence in Sports Writing award by NLGJA, the Association of LGBTQ+ Journalists, the organization announced in July.Established in 1993, NLGJA's annual awards aim to recognize and encourage journalism on issues related to the LGBTQ+ community.
