MESA, Ariz. — One of the best stories at the Oakland Athletics' spring training complex has nothing to do with barreling up a fastball or throwing a breaking pitch on the black. It's more about history, inspiration and breaking down barriers.It's the story of Jenny Cavnar, who was hired last week to broadcast A's games this season, the first woman to handle primary play-by-play duties in Major League Baseball history.
