Two suspects arrested in quadruple shooting in Oakland
-----
Two suspects were arrested in a quadruple shooting that killed a man and a woman in East Oakland on Saturday morning, police said Tuesday.Police declined to release the names of the suspects or provide further information, except to say the case will be forwarded to the Alameda County District Attorney's Office.
