This S.F. high school football field has world-class views. Many don’t know it exists
-----
Donnell Scobie teaches physical education at George Washington High School, but when he speaks about the views from the school's football field, he sounds like a poetry professor.Scobie recalls taking the school's sweeping views for granted when he was a teen attending Washington High in the 1990s — then returning as a teacher and coach and cherishing it every single day: the Golden Gate Bridge, one of the world's most recognizable landmarks, so close it feels like you could touch it.
