Down 12, Santa Clara turns the tables on Stanford and wins by 12
-----
Santa Clara trailed Stanford by a dozen in the first half at Maples Pavilion on Tuesday night, but Herb Sendek's team responded impressively.The Broncos used balanced scoring to improve to 3-0 as they wound up winning by 12, 89-77.
Guard Adama-Alpha Bal, a transfer from Arizona, led the Broncos with 23 points. Carlos Marshall Jr. added 20 and Johnny O'Neil had 17.
