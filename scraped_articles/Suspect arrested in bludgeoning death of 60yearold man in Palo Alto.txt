Suspect arrested in bludgeoning death of 60-year-old man in Palo Alto
-----
A Palo Alto man was arrested Thursday on suspicion of killing a 60-year-old unhoused person who was found bludgeoned to death in a downtown parking lot, police officials said Friday.Alvaro Javier Lopez, 34, was arrested Thursday morning in a parking garage near the spot where the unhoused person's body was found, police said. Lopez is also unhoused, they added.
Lopez was booked into Santa Clara County Main Jail.
