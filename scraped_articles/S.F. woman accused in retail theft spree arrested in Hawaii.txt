S.F. woman accused in retail theft spree arrested in Hawaii
-----
A San Francisco woman accused of retail theft was arrested by U.S. Marshals after she boarded a flight to Honolulu this month, San Francisco police said Wednesday.Denayaha Duree was allegedly one of two women who entered a store in Stonestown Galleria mall and stole a number of items from store display cases just after 12:30 p.m. on Jan. 29.
Police did not specify the store she allegedly stole from.
