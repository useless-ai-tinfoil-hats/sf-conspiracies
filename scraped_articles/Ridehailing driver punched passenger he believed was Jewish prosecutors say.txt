Ride-hailing driver punched passenger he believed was Jewish, prosecutors say
-----
Law enforcement officers arrested a former ride-hailing driver Tuesday on federal hate crime charges for assaulting a passenger he believed to be Jewish or Israeli, according to U.S. prosecutors in San Francisco.The driver, identified by authorities as 39-year-old Daly City resident Csaba John Csukás, is accused of punching the passenger in the face in October, prosecutors said.
