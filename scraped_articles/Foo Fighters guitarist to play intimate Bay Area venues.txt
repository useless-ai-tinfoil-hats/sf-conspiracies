Foo Fighters guitarist to play intimate Bay Area venues
-----
Foo Fighters' Chris Shiflett is heading to the Bay Area for a limited run of intimate solo shows.The lead guitarist of the Dave Grohl-fronted band plans to stop at Half Moon Bay's Old Princeton Landing on Sept. 19, and Novato's HopMonk Tavern on Sept. 20, in support of his third solo album, "Lost at Sea," which was released last October.
