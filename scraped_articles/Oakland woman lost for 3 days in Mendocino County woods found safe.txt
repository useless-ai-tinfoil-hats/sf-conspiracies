Oakland woman lost for 3 days in Mendocino County woods found safe
-----
An Oakland woman who went missing in a wooded area north of Fort Bragg (Mendocino County) earlier this week was found alive Thursday and reunited with her family, authorities said.Elizabeth Schenk, 70, had been missing since around 9 a.m. Tuesday, according to the Mendocino County Sheriff's Office.
