Girl, 13, reported missing in Berkeley
-----
Police are looking for a Berkeley girl who went missing in the early hours of Sunday morning. Amayah Brumfield, 13, was last seen at 12:30 a.m., Berkeley police said in a missing person bulletin.Amayah was last known to be on the 700 block of Harrison Street in Berkeley, which is a few blocks from Interstate 580.
