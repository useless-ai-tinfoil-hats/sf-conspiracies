Why S.F.'s 400 license plate readers could be held up for months
-----
San Francisco officials, long beset by relentless car break-ins and theft at downtown department stores, are seeking to install 400 license plate readers on streets to catch perpetrators.But the plan — funded by a state grant to combat organized retail theft — could get tied up for months, grinding through an approval process.
