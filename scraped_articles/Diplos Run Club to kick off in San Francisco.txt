Diplo’s Run Club to kick off in San Francisco
-----
Diplo plans to help San Francisco runners ride that post-workout high with a new club — and no, it's not the after-hours kind.Diplo's Run Club, set to launch in San Francisco on Sept. 8, pairs a 5k run with an afterparty DJed by Diplo and his friends. The new venture melds the Grammy-winning DJ and producer's personal passion for fitness with his love of music.
Tickets are on sale now, including VIP and virtual ticket options.
