San Francisco author and Salon founder suffers near-fatal stroke
-----
San Francisco writer David Talbot, who co-founded the left-leaning web magazine Salon and wrote "Season of the Witch," suffered a near-fatal stroke on Sunday, June 2, his son confirmed.Talbot, who is 72 years old, has been deeply entrenched in San Francisco's political and journalistic landscape for decades.
