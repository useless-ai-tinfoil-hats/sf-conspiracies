Mark Zuckerberg is building a sprawling compound on the Lake Tahoe shore, blueprints show
-----
In December 2018 and January 2019, Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg quietly bought two neighboring estates on the west shore of Lake Tahoe for a total of $59 million.
