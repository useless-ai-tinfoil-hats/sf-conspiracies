LA Innocence Project to take up Scott Peterson case
-----
This article originally appeared on KCRA.com.The LA Innocence Project will be taking up the case of Scott Peterson, a man convicted of killing his pregnant wife, Laci, and their unborn baby on Christmas Eve in 2002.
Peterson's attorney confirmed to KCRA 3 Thursday the Innocence Project's involvement in the case.
New motions have been filed in the case for a post-conviction discovery, sealed court records, DNA testing and exhibits in support of the DNA testing
