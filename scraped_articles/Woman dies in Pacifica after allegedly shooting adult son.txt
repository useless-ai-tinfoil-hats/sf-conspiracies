Woman dies in Pacifica after allegedly shooting adult son
-----
A woman was fatally shot Wednesday morning after allegedly shooting her adult son at an apartment complex in Pacifica, officials said.The shooting happened on the 2500 block of Francisco Boulevard, the Pacifica Police Department said in a social media statement. Police responded to the scene around 8 a.m. after a man called 911 and said his mother had shot him inside their apartment.
