Man walking on Bay Area highway dies after being struck by 3 cars
-----
A man who was apparently involved in a car crash on Interstate 580 in Richmond Wednesday night was fatally struck by three other vehicles while fleeing by foot, highway officials said.California Highway Patrol officers responded to a report of a multi-vehicle crash near Central Avenue about 10:25 p.m., said officer Adib Zeid, a CHP spokesperson.
