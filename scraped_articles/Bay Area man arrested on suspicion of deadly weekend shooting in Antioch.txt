Bay Area man arrested on suspicion of deadly weekend shooting in Antioch
-----
A 40-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of fatally shooting another man in Antioch on Saturday afternoon, police said.The shooting was reported about 4:30 p.m. near the railroad tracks in the 700 block of West First Street.
When officers arrived, they found a 29-year-old man suffering from a gunshot wound, Antioch police said in a statement. Officers rendered aid, but the victim died at the scene.
