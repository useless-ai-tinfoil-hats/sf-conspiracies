49ers traffic to bring closures to these freeway off-ramps Sunday
-----
Several off-ramps along U.S. Highway 101 and Interstate 280 in San Francisco are set to close Sunday as authorities prepare to grapple with traffic during the NFC Championship game in Santa Clara, Caltrans said.The closures will be in effect from 7 p.m. until midnight Sunday.
Caltrans said the off-ramps that will be closed are:

Cesar Chavez Street on southbound U.S. Highway 101
