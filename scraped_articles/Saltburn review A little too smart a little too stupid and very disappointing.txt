'Saltburn' review: A little too smart, a little too stupid and very disappointing
-----
"Saltburn" is a remarkable combination of smart and stupid. Its problem is that it's superficially smart and deeply stupid. It's clever and amusing in 20 different ways, but when it really matters, it descends into ridiculousness.In fact, the film is worse than merely ridiculous. What's on display here might better be described as a preening form of emptiness.
