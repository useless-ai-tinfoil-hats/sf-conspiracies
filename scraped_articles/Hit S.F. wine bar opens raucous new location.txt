Hit S.F. wine bar opens raucous new location
-----
Two partners behind a pair of bustling San Francisco wine bars are about to launch their latest venue in the Marina district.Celeste opens Saturday, Oct. 14, at 2165 Union St., formerly the site of West Coast Wine & Cheese, with a lineup of natural wines along with more classic California and European vintages. It comes from Lalo Luevano and Paria Sedigh, two of the partners behind the popular Bodega in North Beach and Nob Hill's Key Klub, which draw crowds with an approachable attitude
