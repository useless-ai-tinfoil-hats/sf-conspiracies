S.F. man convicted of sexually assaulting relative while she slept
-----
A jury convicted a San Francisco man of sexually assaulting a 70-year-old woman while she was sleeping, the district attorney's office announced Thursday.Mohamad Alajtal, 40, was convicted of assault with intent to commit sexual penetration of an unconscious person and two counts of sexual penetration of an unconscious person, prosecutors said. He faces up to 10 years in state prison and must register as a sex offender for life. His sentencing was scheduled for May 21.
