After a perfect Sunday, 49ers' Brock Purdy's MVP campaign is so back
-----
By at least one measure, 49ers quarterback Brock Purdy had a perfect Sunday — and is led many to say that his MVP campaign is so, so back.Purdy finished Sunday's 27-14 win over the Buccaneers with a perfect passer rating of 158.3, the first 49er quarterback to ever do so on more than 20 passing attempts (21-for-25, 333 yards, three touchdowns).
