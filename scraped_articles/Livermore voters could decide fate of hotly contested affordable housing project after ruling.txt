Livermore voters could decide fate of hotly contested affordable housing project after ruling
-----
A state appeals court says voters in Livermore can decide the fate of a hotly contested and long-delayed plan to build downtown housing and a public park.The city designated the land for affordable housing after acquiring it in 2008 and chose Eden Housing as the developer a decade later, with $14.4 million in bond funding from Alameda County and a loan from the state.
