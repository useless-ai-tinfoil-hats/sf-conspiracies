Southwest finally kills ‘number one reason’ travelers ditched airline
-----
In an attempt to attract more customers, Southwest Airlines announced Thursday that it's ending the airline's unassigned seating system.The new policy kills off the airline's polarizing group boarding process, sometimes called the Southwest cattle call, which assigns flyers to a boarding group after checking in. The revamp brings the airline more in line with major carriers like American, Delta and United.
