Review: A 7-year-old Mexican girl confronts mortality, learns about life in the wonderful ‘Tótem’
-----
Fleshed-out characters played in totally persuasive ways are rare enough anywhere in world cinema. Put dozens of them together for one day in a single location and you've got something miraculous. That is "Tótem."The second feature written and directed by Mexico's Lila Avilés ("The Chambermaid") ups the ante even more by, at its open-hearted core, being all about a 7-year-old girl.
