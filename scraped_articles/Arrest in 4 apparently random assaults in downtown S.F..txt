Arrest in 4 apparently random assaults in downtown S.F.
-----
A man was arrested on suspicion of assaulting one person and stabbing three others Sunday in downtown San Francisco, authorities said.All four victims were taken to a hospital for their injuries, and their conditions were not immediately released, said San Francisco police spokesperson Robert Rueca.
