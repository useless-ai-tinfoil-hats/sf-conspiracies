Person dies after homebuilt plane crashes onto Bay Area street
-----
One person was reported dead after a small, home-built plane crashed in the East Bay on Tuesday morning, officials said.The plane crashed at approximately 10:30 a.m. near the intersection of Concord Avenue and Diamond Boulevard in Concord, Lauren Ono, a spokesperson for the Contra Costa Fire Protection District, told SFGATE. The person who died was the only occupant of the plane, Ono said.
One vehicle was also involved in the crash and the driver was transported to a local hospital
