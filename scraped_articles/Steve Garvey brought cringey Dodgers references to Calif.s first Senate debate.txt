Steve Garvey brought cringey Dodgers references to Calif.'s first Senate debate
-----
Steve Garvey isn't shy about highlighting his MVP-winning baseball career in his Republican bid to be California's next United States Senator. And Monday night, voters saw just how goofy politicians get when there's a sports pun at arms' reach.Democratic Reps. Barbara Lee, Adam Schiff and Katie Porter subjected the former Los Angeles Dodger and San Diego Padre to various baseball-themed quips in Los Angeles at the first debate of the 2024 Senate race.
