Traffic jams and off-trail selfies plague Calif. wildflower superbloom
-----
January and February showers have brought March flowers to the Southern California desert.A big wildflower bloom is underway at Anza-Borrego Desert State Park, prompting the spring rush of visitors trekking out from near and far to see the bright purple and yellow blooms.
Most of the blooms are concentrated along the northeast side of Henderson Canyon Road, according to Anza-Borrego's wildflower hotline, last updated March 17.
