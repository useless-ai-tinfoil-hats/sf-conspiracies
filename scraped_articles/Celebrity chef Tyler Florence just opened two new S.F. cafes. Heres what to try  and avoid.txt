Celebrity chef Tyler Florence just opened two new S.F. cafes. Here’s what to try — and avoid
-----
Two previously vacant buildings in Union Square Plaza opened Monday, hoping to attract life to downtown San Francisco with pastries and rotisserie chicken.Earlier this year, Mayor London Breed announced that celebrity chef Tyler Florence would open extensions of his steak house Miller & Lux, known as Miller & Lux Provisions. On one end of the plaza is the "patisserie" and on the opposite end is the "rotisserie" for spit-roasted chicken and sides.
