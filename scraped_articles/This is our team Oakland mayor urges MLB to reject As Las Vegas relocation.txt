‘This is our team’: Oakland mayor urges MLB to reject A’s Las Vegas relocation
-----
Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao on Tuesday urged Major League Baseball to reject the A's bid to relocate to Las Vegas — her latest effort to keep the team in the city.Dozens of Oakland A's fans filed into City Hall chanting "Let's go Oakland" and "Stay in Oakland" as Thao outlined the city's commitment to making a deal happen with the team. MLB owners plan to vote on the relocation plan at next week's owners meeting in Arlington, Texas.
