Cinco de Mayo 2024: The best celebrations around the Bay Area
-----
Observed annually on May 5, Cinco de Mayo honors an 1862 military victory in which a vastly outnumbered Mexican army defeated invading French troops in the Battle of Puebla. The holiday was first celebrated in California during the Gold Rush in the town of Columbia by Mexican miners living there who honored the victory with a fiesta.Join in the celebration with events featuring cultural activities, food trucks, folkloric dance and more.
