Car crash on Richmond-San Rafael Bridge leaves 1 dead, 1 injured
-----
A car crash on the Richmond-San Rafael Bridge killed one person and injured another on Sunday night, the California Highway Patrol said.The crash happened just before 8 p.m. on the westbound lanes of the bridge. A driver in a silver Honda sedan collided with the rear of a Mazda sport utility vehicle, causing the Honda to overturn on its roof, the agency said on Facebook.
