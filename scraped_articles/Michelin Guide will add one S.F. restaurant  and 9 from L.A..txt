At least one new Bay Area restaurant will be added to the 2024 Michelin Guide for California.In a preview Tuesday morning of its final selections, to be released in a later month, the famed guidebook revealed that 7 Adams in San Francisco would be included in its next edition.
It was one of 10 California restaurants mentioned in the preview, and the only one from the Bay Area; the rest were in Los Angeles, including pasta hot spot Funke and pizza parlor Quarter Sheets.
