2 injured, 2 detained in shooting after Oakland high school graduation
-----
A shooting after a graduation ceremony at Skyline High School in Oakland injured two people and sent graduates and their families running, police and witnesses said.Two people were detained after Thursday night's attack, and police sealed off the area around the high school for hours before they deemed the scene safe.
