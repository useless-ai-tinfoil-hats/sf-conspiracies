Man charged in slashing of tires on cars in S.F. Richmond District
-----
A San Francisco man has been charged in connection with the slashing of tires on multiple cars in the Richmond District last week, according to the district attorney's office.Ossiel Torres, 22, pleaded not guilty to charges for 22 counts of vandalism last Friday, the DA's office said. About 8:58 p.m. on March 19, officers responded to the area of 12th Avenue and Anza Street for a report of a vandalism suspect who had slashed tires, police said.
