Antioch police detail events leading up to officers shooting a man
-----
Antioch police say a man who pointed a gun at police officers, leading officers to shoot him multiple times earlier this month, was wanted on suspicion of murder.Antioch police officers came across Daniel Mackin as he slept in a van on Oct. 1, officials said in a report released Thursday.
