A 'big psychological impact': Isolated and alone, how Big Sur is functioning in a time of crisis
-----
Right now, Big Sur is an island.It started with a blast of consecutive atmospheric rivers slamming into the community at the edge of the continent in January 2023. The violent storms resulted in a landslide where some 500,000 cubic yards of debris were deposited on a stretch of Highway 1 called Paul's Slide near the southern end of the 71-mile swath of coastline that defines the region.
