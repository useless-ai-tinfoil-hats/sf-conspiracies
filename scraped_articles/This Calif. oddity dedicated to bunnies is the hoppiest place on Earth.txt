This Calif. oddity dedicated to bunnies is the hoppiest place on Earth
-----
Dust bunnies. Rabbit foot keychains. The Energizer Bunny. Peter Rabbit.When you hop right to it, there are an awful lot of references to bunnies in our vernacular, literature and pop culture. And nowhere is that more apparent than at The Bunny Museum, one of the weirdest, wildest places you can visit in Los Angeles.
