SF Planters parody site promises to displace homeless in San Francisco
-----
SF Planters, a local company that installs planter boxes on the sidewalks outside of Bay Area businesses, appears to have some new, fake competition: a near-identical parody website that promises to use plants to displace homeless people in San Francisco.The real company's website, sfplanters.com, says that its products will result in "clean and beautiful streets." Advocates have countered that the planter boxes — which weigh between 1,000 and 1,800 pounds when filled — are more so meant
