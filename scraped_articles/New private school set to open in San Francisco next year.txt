New private school set to open in San Francisco next year
-----
An international private school group is set to open its first U.S. campus in San Francisco next year.The new school, Hiba Academy, will teach Bay Area students, kindergarten through eighth grade, in both English and Chinese, a March 13 news release said. School group officials selected San Francisco for the new campus because the city is "an epicenter of forward-thinking ideas."
