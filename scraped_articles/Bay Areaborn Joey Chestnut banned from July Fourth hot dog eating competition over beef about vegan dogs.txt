Bay Area-born Joey Chestnut banned from July Fourth hot dog eating competition over beef about vegan dogs
-----
Vallejo-born Joey Chestnut, arguably the most prolific competitive eating champion, won't be allowed to compete in the Nathan's Famous Hot Dog Eating Contest because of a brand deal with a vegan meat company.Major League Eating, the governing body in charge of the annual July Fourth competition, put out a statement on Tuesday lambasting Chestnut's decision to prioritize "a new partnership with a different hot dog brand over our long-time relationship."
