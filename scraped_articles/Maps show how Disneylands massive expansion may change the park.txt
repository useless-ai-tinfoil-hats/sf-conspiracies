Maps show how Disneyland's massive expansion may change the park
-----
With the news that Disneyland's decade of expansion is officially approved, the rampant speculation begins.Disney officials have been deliberately quiet about what the $1.9 billion Disneyland Forward project will entail. They've teased new lands, hotels, shopping and dining, but, with the exception of one movie franchise, are coy about what's actually becoming part of the Disneyland Resort. A number of artists' renderings and maps do give a sense of what may be on the horizon, however.
