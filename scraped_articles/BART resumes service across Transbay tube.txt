BART resumes service across Transbay tube
-----
UPDATE Wednesday 6:20 p.m. BART has restored service across the Transbay tube, the transit agency announced on X.-----
BART has halted all service between San Francisco and Oakland after a person was discovered on the tracks inside the Transbay tube during the busy Wednesday evening commute.
The agency posted on X, formerly Twitter, that as of 5:15 p.m., the person had still not been removed from the tunnel and that the police were "responding to the situation."
