Oakland superintendent to have record longevity if board approves contract
-----
The Oakland Unified School District board will vote Wednesday on a new contract for Kyla Johnson-Trammell to continue serving as superintendent for three additional years.If approved, Johnson-Trammell would be the longest serving superintendent in the district's history, according to school district officials.
