Powerhouse Bay Area food photographer dies at 42
-----
Aubrie Pick, an acclaimed Bay Area food photographer whose images brought to life many of the country's best-known chefs and restaurants, died Nov. 2 in San Francisco after an eight-month battle with an aggressive form of cancer. She was 42.Her death was confirmed by her husband, Erik Newton.
