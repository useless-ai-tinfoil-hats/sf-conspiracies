Review: Hulu’s ‘Supremes at Earl’s All-You-Can-Eat’ thrives when it goes for less
-----
The new Hulu movie "The Supremes at Earl's All-You-Can-Eat" offers a smorgasbord of potential high-drama elements, including illness, affairs, alcoholism and 1960s interracial romance in the South.Yet most every time this movie about three longtime friends (Aunjanue Ellis-Taylor, Uzo Aduba and Sanaa Lathan) threatens to veer into melodrama, the filmmakers yank it back to authenticity.
