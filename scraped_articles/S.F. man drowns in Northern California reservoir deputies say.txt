S.F. man drowns in Northern California reservoir, deputies say
-----
A San Francisco man drowned in a Stanislaus County reservoir over the weekend, deputies said.Deputies and several agencies responded just before 2 p.m. Sunday to a report of a distressed swimmer at Woodward Reservoir, about 28 miles east of Stockton, the county's sheriff's office said in a statement.
The swimmer, identified as 24-year-old Oscar Rafael Coello-Flores, apparently became fatigued while trying to swim from the Mountain View Point shore to "Area-A," deputies said.
