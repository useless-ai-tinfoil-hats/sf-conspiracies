The exodus continues at San Francisco Centre, the city's biggest mall.Lucky Brand, a jeans and apparel store, is shuttering on Jan. 29, according to red signs posted in the mall viewed by the Chronicle. Discounts up to 70% are advertised, with the company directing shoppers to Hillsdale Shopping Center in San Mateo in the future.
J. Crew is also closing on Jan. 22, a store associate told SFGATE. (Hearst owns the Chronicle and SFGATE, but the two newsrooms operate separately.)
