A plum-pistachio dessert to celebrate the last days of summer
-----
I can see we're at the tail end of summer when I step outside for my evening walks: the evening light changing, the earlier sunsets with cotton candy skies, the cooler air and birds migrating south.Plums are a metaphorical transition fruit for me, going from summer to fall. Colors range from a beautiful shade of light green (greengage plums) to brighter red varieties to a darker purple.
