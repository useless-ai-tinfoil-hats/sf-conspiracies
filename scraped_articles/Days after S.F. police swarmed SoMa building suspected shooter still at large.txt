Days after S.F. police swarmed SoMa building, suspected shooter still at large
-----
San Francisco police officials said Wednesday that the suspect who fled a South of Market building after shooting and injuring someone Monday remained at large.Police officials swarmed a residential building in the area of Sixth and Mission streets shortly before noon Monday and found a person suffering from gunshot wounds.
Investigating officers tried to negotiate with the suspect, who they believed was still in the building, but were unable to make contact.
