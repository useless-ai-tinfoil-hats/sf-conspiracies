Hardly Strictly Bluegrass Festival officials mourn death of producer
-----
Greg Wynn, a producer for San Francisco's iconic Hardly Strictly Bluegrass Festival, died Thursday after a battle with cancer, a publicist for the festival told the Chronicle.Wynn, 54, joined HSB in 2008, and handled offstage operations for the festival, which included parking, signage, decor and security. According to Wynn's Linkedin, he graduated from San Francisco State University in 1993 with a bachelor's degree in broadcast communications.
