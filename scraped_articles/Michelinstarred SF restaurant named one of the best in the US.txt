Michelin-starred SF restaurant named one of the best in the US
-----
Food & Wine magazine announced the winners of its Global Tastemakers awards, and only one San Francisco restaurant made the cut. However, the city was lauded for its bakeries.Mister Jiu's, a one-Michelin-star restaurant in San Francisco's Chinatown, ranked 18th among the 20 best restaurants in America, according to a news release.
