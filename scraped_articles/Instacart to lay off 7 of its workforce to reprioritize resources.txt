Instacart to lay off 7% of its workforce to ‘re-prioritize resources’
-----
Instacart and Mozilla have joined the ranks of Bay Area tech firms announcing workforce reductions.On Tuesday, Instacart revealed a restructuring plan that would fire 250 employees, approximately 7% of its staff.
