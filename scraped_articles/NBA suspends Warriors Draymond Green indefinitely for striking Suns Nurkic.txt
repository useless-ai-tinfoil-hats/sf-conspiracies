NBA suspends Warriors’ Draymond Green indefinitely for striking Suns’ Nurkic
-----
Golden State Warriors forward Draymond Green has been suspended indefinitely for hitting Phoenix center Jusuf Nurkic in the face with a wild swing of his arm, the NBA announced Wednesday evening.According to a league-issued news release, Green must meet certain criteria before returning. The Athletic reported that he is expected to receive counseling during his suspension, though it remains unclear just how long Green could be missing from the Warriors' lineup.
