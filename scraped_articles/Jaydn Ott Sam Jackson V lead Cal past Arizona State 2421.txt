Jaydn Ott, Sam Jackson V lead Cal past Arizona State 24-21
-----
Arizona State outgained Cal by more than 100 yards at Memorial Stadium on Saturday afternoon, but the Bears did just enough to prevail.Cal handed the Sun Devils their fourth straight loss in a 24-21 decision.
After Sam Jackson V and Ben Finley each started two of the first four games for the Bears (3-2, 1-1 Pac-12), Jackson went the distance Saturday. He completed only 12 of 29 passes for 130 yards, but he did get a victory despite ASU outgaining Cal 430 yards to 326.
