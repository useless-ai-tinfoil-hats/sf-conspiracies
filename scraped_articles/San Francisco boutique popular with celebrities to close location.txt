San Francisco boutique popular with celebrities to close location
-----
A 23-year-old shopping mainstay in San Francisco's Marina District is set to permanently close its doors next month.Women's boutique clothing store Ambiance announced in an Instagram post in August it would shutter its Marina location following the owners' retirements. The store's Inner Sunset location will temporarily close for a few months while the legacy business's new owners work with their landlord and prepare to reopen.
