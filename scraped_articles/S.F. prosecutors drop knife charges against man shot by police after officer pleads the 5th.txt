S.F. prosecutors drop knife charges against man shot by police after officer pleads the 5th
-----
A San Francisco police officer who shot a homeless man who was armed with a knife in the Tenderloin in August refused to testify in the man's trial last week, forcing prosecutors to drop all charges against the man, according to the prosecutors and defense attorneys.Officer Russell Lucia was one of two officers who shot Richard Everett. Two other officers fired beanbag shotguns at him.
