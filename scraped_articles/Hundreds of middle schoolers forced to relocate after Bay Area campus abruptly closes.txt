Hundreds of middle schoolers forced to relocate after Bay Area campus abruptly closes
-----
Hundreds of Berkeley middle-school students will start school Wednesday at a temporary campus after dry rot was discovered at Longfellow Middle School. Crews had found the wood-destroying fungus earlier this summer as they were replacing a window on the south side of the main academic building.The building's structural beams are crumbling, making it dangerous for occupants, the district said in a news release.
