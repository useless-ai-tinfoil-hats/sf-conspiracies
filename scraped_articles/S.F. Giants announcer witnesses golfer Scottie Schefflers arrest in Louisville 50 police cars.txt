S.F. Giants announcer witnesses golfer Scottie Scheffler’s arrest in Louisville: ‘50 police cars’
-----
San Francisco Giants announcer Dave Flemming likely didn't expect to find himself delivering the play-by-play of the dramatic arrest of a two-time Masters champion when he traveled to Louisville to cover the PGA Championship for ESPN.But Flemming and ESPN reporter Jeff Darlington had front-row seats when Scottie Scheffler was arrested on Friday after police say he dragged an officer while attempting to navigate around the scene of a fatal accident ahead of the tournament's second round.
