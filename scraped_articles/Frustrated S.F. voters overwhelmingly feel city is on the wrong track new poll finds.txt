‘Frustrated’: S.F. voters overwhelmingly feel city is on the wrong track, new poll finds
-----
San Francisco voters continue to fret about the state of the city, with just over half arguing that crime, drugs and homelessness are the biggest issues, according to a new poll. Nearly three quarters feel the city is on the wrong track, a gloomy sentiment that could endanger Mayor London Breed's reelection bid.And downtown is at the heart of those worries.
