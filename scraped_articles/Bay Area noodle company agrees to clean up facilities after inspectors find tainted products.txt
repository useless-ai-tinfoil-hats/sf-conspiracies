Bay Area noodle company agrees to clean up facilities after inspectors find tainted products
-----
A federal judge approved a settlement Wednesday requiring a Bay Area rice noodle company to clean up its operations after government inspectors reported contamination from insects and bacteria.The Justice Department said Cali Rice Valley, formerly based in San Francisco and now in Antioch, had agreed to be bound by an injunction issued by U.S. District Judge James Donato. Among other things, it requires the company to hire experts in food safety and labeling, clean up its facilities
