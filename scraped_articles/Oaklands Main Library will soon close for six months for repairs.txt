Oakland’s Main Library will soon close for six months for repairs
-----
Oakland's Main Library will soon close its doors for six months to complete much-needed repairs to the downtown building, library officials announced Wednesday.The branch, at 125 14th St. near Lake Merritt — which officials say is one of the largest public libraries in the Bay Area — will close May 27 and stay shut through early November. An exact reopening date has not been determined, library officials said.
