S.F. souffle pancake sensation is closing
-----
A popular cafe that sells fluffy Japanese souffle pancakes will close its San Francisco location at the end of the month.Gram Cafe & Pancakes opened its first U.S. location at the Stonestown Galleria in 2019, bringing wobbly stacks of tall, perfectly round pancakes to the city. It expanded to Los Angeles last year.
