'We tried everything': Another long-running Calif. music festival is canceled
-----
The psych-rock music festival Desert Daze announced its cancellation on Friday, just a month after releasing the lineup, due to high production costs, low ticket sales and stiff competition.This would have been the 11th instance of the annual festival, which takes place in October at Lake Perris, near Riverside. It has been running since 2012 (when it was free and 11 days long), though it skipped 2020 and 2023.
