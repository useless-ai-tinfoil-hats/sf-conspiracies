San Jose police seek suspect in fatal shooting near freeway
-----
San Jose police are seeking the public's help in locating a suspect accused of fatally shooting a man near a freeway off-ramp last month.The suspect is identified as Joseph "Jo Jo" Martinez and a warrant has been obtained for his arrest, police said. Martinez is described as 5 feet, 3 inches tall, and around 115 pounds, with a teardrop tattoo on his face, according to police. He is suspected in the shooting that occurred May 9 near an Interstate 280 off-ramp in San Jose.
