Woman rescued after near-drowning in S.F. near Pier 14
-----
A woman was rescued from the waters off Pier 14 in San Francisco, Fire Department officials said.Bystanders observed a woman struggling in the water just before 1:25 p.m. Tuesday, said San Francisco fire spokespersons.
Emergency personnel were dispatched and transported the victim to a local hospital with non-life-threatening injuries, spokespersons said.
The victim was not immediately identified.
