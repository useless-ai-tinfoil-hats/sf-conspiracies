Guy Fieri to host free tailgate party in Las Vegas at Super Bowl LVIII
-----
Guy Fieri is definitely not a part of the 49ers faithful, but the noted Raiders fan is bound to see plenty of them on Super Bowl Sunday when he hosts a massive tailgate party in Las Vegas.Ahead of the 3:30 p.m. kickoff between the Niners and the Kansas City Chiefs, Fieri is scheduled to welcome all to an outdoor venue behind the High Roller Ferris wheel for a good time, starting at 10:30 a.m and lasting until kickoff.
