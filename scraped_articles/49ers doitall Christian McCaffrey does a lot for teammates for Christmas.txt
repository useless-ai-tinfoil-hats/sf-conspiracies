49ers’ do-it-all Christian McCaffrey does a lot for teammates for Christmas
-----
Christian McCaffrey really does do it all: He runs, catches and gives — generously.The San Francisco 49ers' versatile All-Pro running back gave every offensive teammate a PXG golf bag, adorned with their last name and jersey number, a coupon for a custom fitting for a set of PXG golf clubs and a bottle of Celaya Tequila for Christmas.
The gifts, which were at his teammates' lockers Friday, created a particularly lively post-practice locker room.
