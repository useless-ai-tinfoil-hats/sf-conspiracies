Pedestrian fatally struck by Caltrain on the Peninsula, officials say
-----
A pedestrian was hit and killed Friday morning by a Caltrain near the California Avenue station in Palo Alto, a spokesperson said.Trains were stopped in the area while medical personnel responded to the collision, which involved northbound train No. 107, according to Caltrain spokesperson Randol White.
The fatality was confirmed shortly before 8 a.m.
