Bay Area house where meth, bombs were allegedly made is listed for $1.5 million
-----
A San Jose home that was most recently the site of a meth lab and bomb-making operation is now on the market for $1.5 million, according to the real estate website Zillow.San Jose Police and the FBI descended on 668 Potomac Court in March after they claimed that Peter Karasev was stockpiling bomb-making chemicals, methamphetamine and firearms. Investigators accused Karasev of planting multiple bombs around San Jose that targeted infrastructure, including Pacific Gas & Electric Co.
