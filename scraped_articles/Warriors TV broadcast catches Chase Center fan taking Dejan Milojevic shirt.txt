Warriors TV broadcast catches Chase Center fan taking Dejan Milojevic shirt
-----
TV cameras caught a fan at Chase Center surreptitiously taking a memorial T-shirt off the Warriors' bench Wednesday, drawing outrage and calls for a lifetime ban in San Francisco.The moment was captured by NBC Sports Bay Area during the postgame show after the Warriors' 134-112 win over the Atlanta Hawks.
