Review: Angus Cloud in ‘Your Lucky Day’ is one of last chances to see late Oakland native onscreen
-----
An unforgiving little thriller with a conscience and irony to burn (and boy, do they burn), "Your Lucky Day" is one of the last chances to see beloved Oakland native Angus Cloud onscreen.His "Lucky" performance as a guy with reservoirs of intelligence and heart behind a spaced-out, threatening demeanor is a no-sweat testament to everything Cloud, who died in July of an accidental overdose at age 25, did so well.
