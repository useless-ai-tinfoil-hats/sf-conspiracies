Bay Area tech layoffs: Apple announces rare wave of job cuts
-----
Apple has implemented a rare wave of layoffs, affecting about 100 positions within its services group.The impacted employees at the Cupertino-based tech giant were informed of the cuts on Tuesday, Bloomberg reported. The layoffs spanned various teams under Senior Vice President Eddy Cue.
