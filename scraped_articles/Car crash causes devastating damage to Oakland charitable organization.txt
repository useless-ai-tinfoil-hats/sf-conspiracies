Car crash causes ‘devastating’ damage to Oakland charitable organization
-----
A car crashed into the building of an Oakland charitable organization early Thursday, causing thousands of dollars' worth of damages for a group that provides free food and other services to East Oakland residents.At about 3 a.m., two cars collided near 77th Avenue and MacArthur Boulevard, and one of the vehicles smashed into the corner of the nonprofit Homies Empowerment's care center, said Viviana Montano, the center's coordinator.
