George Michael stage musical coming to S.F. for one night only
-----
George Michael's musical legacy will be celebrated in a new jukebox production at San Francisco's Curran Theater next year."The Life and Music of George Michael" is scheduled for a single performance on Feb. 3, offering an account of the late British pop superstar who reached early fame with Wham and became a platinum-selling solo artist before he died on Christmas 2016 at the age of 53.
