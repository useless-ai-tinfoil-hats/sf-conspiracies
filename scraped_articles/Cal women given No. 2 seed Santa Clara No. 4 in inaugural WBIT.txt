Cal women given No. 2 seed, Santa Clara No. 4 in inaugural WBIT
-----
The Cal women's basketball team will make its first postseason appearance under head coach Charmin Smith after receiving a No. 2 seed in the inaugural Women's Basketball Invitational Tournament on Sunday night. Santa Clara received a No. 4 seed.The NCAA is sponsoring this tournament, an equivalent to the men's NIT.
