Why A’s Billy Beane loves Giants’ Zaidi-Melvin reunion across the bay
-----
Oakland Athletics executive Billy Beane hired Farhan Zaidi and Bob Melvin, and developed deep friendships with both when all three worked for the A's.Now, with Zaidi and Melvin the top baseball bosses for the San Francisco Giants, it's "getting tougher and tougher to root against the Giants," Beane said in a Chronicle interview. Zaidi "went over there, and now Bob. Before you know it, I'll be wearing black and orange and grinding over every game."
