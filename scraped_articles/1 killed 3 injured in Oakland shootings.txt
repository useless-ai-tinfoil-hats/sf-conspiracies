1 killed, 3 injured in Oakland shootings
-----
One person was killed and three others were injured in Oakland shootings this weekend, according to the city's Police Department.Officers responded to the first shooting just after 8:30 p.m. Saturday on the 500 block of Merritt Avenue. Paramedics treated a victim, who was hospitalized and listed in stable condition Sunday.
