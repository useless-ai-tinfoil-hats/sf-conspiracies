5 shot at Oakland sideshow involving hundreds of participants
-----
Three men and two women were shot when gunfire erupted during a large sideshow in Oakland early Sunday in what marked the city's second mass shooting in the past two months, police said.The sideshow drew about 100 vehicles and 400 participants to MacArthur Boulevard and Fruitvale Avenue — a busy intersection surrounded by businesses — around 3:30 a.m. The shooting occurred about 15 minutes later, said Oakland Police Department Sgt. Huy Nguyen.
