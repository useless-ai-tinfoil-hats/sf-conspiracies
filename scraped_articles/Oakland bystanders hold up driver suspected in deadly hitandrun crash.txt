Oakland bystanders hold up driver suspected in deadly hit-and-run crash
-----
A person accused of fatally striking a pedestrian with a vehicle in Oakland on Monday was surrounded by bystanders after allegedly attempting to flee the scene, according to authorities.The suspect, whose name has not yet been released, was allegedly involved in multiple crashes in the area before being stopped by community members. SFGATE talked to one bystander, who said he saw the second crash.
