Celebrated S.F. Russian restaurant will close
-----
One of the Bay Area's few Russian restaurants will close this month after two years in business.Birch & Rye in San Francisco will shutter on March 30, the restaurant announced Wednesday.
Owner Anya El-Wattar said it became "unsustainable" to run a restaurant that made so much of its food from scratch, from cultured butter to vodka infusions.
