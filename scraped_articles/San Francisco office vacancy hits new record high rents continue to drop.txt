San Francisco office vacancy hits new record high, rents continue to drop
-----
San Francisco's office vacancy rate climbed even higher in the third quarter of 2023, according to new preliminary data recently released by commercial real estate firm CBRE.Office vacancy rose from 31.6% to 33.9%, a new record high for the city, which has hit record highs each quarter for nearly two years. This still likely isn't the peak, though, as the city's availability rate, which includes both vacant space and leases ending soon, has already hit 37.5%.
