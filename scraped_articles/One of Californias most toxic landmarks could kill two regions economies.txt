One of California's most toxic landmarks could kill two regions' economies
-----
For decades, the Salton Sea has mystified public imagination.Positioned in a rural desert region just miles from popular travel destinations like Palm Springs, it's California's largest inland lake — and one of its biggest ecological disasters.
Millions of dollars of federal government funding, however, may help prevent the disaster from getting even worse.
