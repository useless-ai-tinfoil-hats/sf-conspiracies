Here’s when S.F.’s Twin Peaks Tunnel will be closed for repairs
-----
San Francisco's Twin Peaks Tunnel will close two weekends in June and July, ahead of an eight-day closure in August to repair the deteriorated 106-year-old tunnel.Muni Metro riders commuting to the Forest Hill and West Portal stations will experience service disruptions during the tunnel closures, which are scheduled to happen June 22-23, July 20-21 and Aug. 22-29.
