Celebrity chef Dominique Crenn is closing one of her S.F. restaurants
-----
Famed chef Dominique Crenn is closing one of her acclaimed French restaurants: the more casual Petit Crenn in San Francisco.Petit Crenn will close at 609 Hayes St. in September, when its lease ends, according to an announcement.
"It has been an incredible 9-year journey," Crenn said in the announcement. "This place is so close to my heart and a true extension of my life."
