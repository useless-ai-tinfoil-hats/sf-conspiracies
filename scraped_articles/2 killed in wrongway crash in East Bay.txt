2 killed in wrong-way crash in East Bay
-----
A wrong-way crash killed two drivers Saturday morning in the East Bay, according to the California Highway Patrol.A Scion traveling westbound in the eastbound lanes of Highway 4 near Antioch, west of Lone Tree Way, collided head-on with an eastbound Mazda around 4:30 a.m., CHP Contra Costa reported in its preliminary investigation. Both drivers, who had no passengers, were pronounced dead at the scene.
