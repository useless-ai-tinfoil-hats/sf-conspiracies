California bills to reform prisons have a mixed record of success this session
-----
California lawmakers' attempts to reform prisons and other detention facilities met a mixed fate in the legislative session that ended last month, a sign that the blue state is not as progressive in its criminal justice reform agenda as many have hoped it would be.Gov. Gavin Newsom has spent the past few weeks reviewing, signing and issuing vetoes for hundreds of bills passed by the Legislature.
