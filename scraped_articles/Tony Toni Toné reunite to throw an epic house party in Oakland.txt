Tony! Toni! Toné! reunite to throw an epic 'house party' in Oakland
-----
At Oakland's Paramount Theatre, a rapturous crowd stood at attention as former chair of the Black Panther Party Elaine Brown warmed up the room with a history lesson on the rise, fall and rebirth of Oakland's 7th Street District, putting into context one of The Town's greatest musical success stories."Tonight we came to celebrate the resurrection of the great sons of soul — Tony! Toni! Toné!" she declared.
