S.F. Korean mid-autumn festival draws thousands to Presidio
-----
San Francisco's fifth annual Korean Chuseok Festival, a celebration of the mid-autumn harvest, drew thousands of people to the Presidio on Saturday.The festivities, which included dance and choir performances, chopstick crafting and dozens of food and merchandise vendors, were another example of the city's live gatherings roaring back from the pandemic.
