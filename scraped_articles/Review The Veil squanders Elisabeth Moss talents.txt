Review: ‘The Veil’ squanders Elisabeth Moss' talents
-----
"The Veil" is a six-part miniseries starring Elisabeth Moss, and the first thing you need to know is that it has nothing to do with "taking the veil," as in becoming a nun. Moss has it in her to be the most terrifying nun ever committed to film — right up there with Melissa Leo in "Novitiate" — and let's hope that sooner or later a filmmaker realizes this.But "The Veil" is something else, a deadly serious and truly awful spy series.
