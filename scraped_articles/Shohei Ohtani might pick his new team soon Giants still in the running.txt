Shohei Ohtani might pick his new team soon, Giants still in the running
-----
Internet sleuths were hard at work Friday tracking private-plane flights from Southern California in an effort to determine Shohei Ohtani's whereabouts.Ohtani is poised to sign the biggest player deal in the history of professional sports in North America, and reportedly was going to make his decision later in the day, with the Toronto Blue Jays and Los Angeles Dodgers considered the front-runners, but the San Francisco Giants, Chicago Cubs and Seattle Mariners still among the hopefuls.
