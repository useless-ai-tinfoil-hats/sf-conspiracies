New report shows dramatic drop in San Francisco traffic enforcement
-----
San Francisco drivers are getting stopped at far lower rates for traffic violations than they were five years ago, according to a report published this week by the New York Times, which showed traffic enforcement significantly dropped in the city between 2018 and 2023.The Times found a decrease in police enforcement since 2018 in a wide range of major U.S. cities. The newspaper reported that the traffic stops in San Francisco decreased by more than 50% since 2018.
