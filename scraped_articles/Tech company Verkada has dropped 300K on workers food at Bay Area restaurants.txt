Tech company Verkada has dropped $300K on workers’ food at Bay Area restaurants
-----
San Mateo tech company Verkada has a dreamy company perk: If three or more workers go out nearby the office after 3 p.m., the company will fund up to $30 of food and drink each — no questions asked. So far, though, employees aren't exactly partying hard.Workers have spent just $300,000 of the security company's money in San Mateo and $200,000 near other offices around the world since the policy began last April, chief financial officer Kameron Rezai told SFGATE Wednesday.
