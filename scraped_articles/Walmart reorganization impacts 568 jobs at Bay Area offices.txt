Walmart reorganization impacts 568 jobs at Bay Area offices
-----
Walmart is planning a significant reorganization at two Bay Area offices, impacting over 560 jobs.According to regulatory filings, the retail giant plans to permanently lay off 388 employees at its San Bruno e-commerce center and an additional 180 at its Sunnyvale location, beginning Aug. 9. However, many of those employees will be able to reapply for new positions within the company.
