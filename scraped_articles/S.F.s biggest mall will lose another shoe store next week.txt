S.F.’s biggest mall will lose another shoe store next week
-----
Shoe store Aldo will close in San Francisco's biggest mall next week — and it's directing shoppers to its other location in Stonestown Galleria, a shopping center that is thriving in comparison.The third-floor San Francisco Centre store will close on Jan. 21, according to a posted sign seen on Tuesday by the Chronicle.
