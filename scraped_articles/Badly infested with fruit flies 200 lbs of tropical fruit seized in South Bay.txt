‘Badly infested with fruit flies’: 200 lbs of tropical fruit seized in South Bay
-----
Two San Jose women who allegedly imported 200 pounds of fly-infested tropical fruit from Vietnam are facing felony charges for violating federal and state agricultural laws, authorities said.Hanh Hong Huynh, 42, and Thanh Tuyen Huynh, 36, were charged with conspiracy to import and sell langsat fruit from Vietnam that was "badly infested with fruit flies," according to the Santa Clara District Attorney's Office.
