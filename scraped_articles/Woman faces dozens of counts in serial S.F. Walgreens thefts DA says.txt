Woman faces dozens of counts in serial S.F. Walgreens thefts, DA says
-----
A woman who allegedly stole $34,000 worth of items from three San Francisco Walgreens stores is facing a raft of charges for organized retail theft, District Attorney Brooke Jenkins announced Friday.Prosecutors say that Savannah Church, 24, committed eight burglaries between June 10 and Sept. 4, targeting stores at 456 Mission St., 1189 Potrero Ave. and 2494 Potrero Ave. Police arrested her on Wednesday.
During a court arraignment Thursday, Church pleaded not guilty to eight counts
