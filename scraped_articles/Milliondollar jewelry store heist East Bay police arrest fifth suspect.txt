Million-dollar jewelry store heist: East Bay police arrest fifth suspect
-----
San Ramon police have arrested a fifth suspect in a million-dollar jewelry store heist, the Police Department announced Wednesday.On March 17, at least 11 people stormed into Heller Jewelers, a family-owned jewelry store in San Ramon, and stole $1.1 million worth of watches and jewelry, the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Northern District of California alleges. Five of those individuals have been charged with planning and executing the armed robbery.
