Photographers capture spectacular streaking lights of Southern California
-----
A streak of light appeared in the sky over Southern California on Monday night, and many captured photographs of the spectacle and posted the images on social media."An absolute stunner tonight," wrote one photographer who posted images on X (formerly Twitter).
People questioned whether the lights were space debris, an asteroid, or even a UFO.
It quickly became clear that Elon Musk's SpaceX was responsible for the show.
