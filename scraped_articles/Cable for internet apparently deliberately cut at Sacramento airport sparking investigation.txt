Cable for internet apparently ‘deliberately cut’ at Sacramento airport, sparking investigation
-----
Authorities in Sacramento are investigating the cutting of a cable that interrupted internet access Thursday and caused delays at Sacramento International Airport. They believe the cutting was intentional.According to the Sacramento County Sheriff's Office, an AT&T aerial internet and phone line was cut, affecting internet communications to at least one airline — Southwest — at the airport. The airport alerted travelers to the outage at 5:07 a.m. and said it was causing delays.
