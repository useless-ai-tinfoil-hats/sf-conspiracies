Armed robbery suspects fled East Bay officers with child in the car, police say
-----
Two suspects in the armed robbery of a fast-food restaurant in Lafayette led police on a chase with a 7-year-old child in their car before colliding with an oncoming vehicle, Lafayette police said Thursday.Jajuan Ojeda, 24, of Oakland and Aevra Traylor, 39, of Sacramento were arrested Wednesday and booked into the Martinez Detention Facility on suspicion of robbery, felony evasion and child endangerment.
