Cal, Mark Madsen agree to two-year contract extension through 2029-30 season
-----
Cal men's basketball coach Mark Madsen reaped a tangible reward for quickly guiding the Bears back to respectability.Madsen agreed to a two-year contract extension through the 2029-30 season, the school announced Tuesday. He originally signed a five-year deal when Cal hired him last March.
