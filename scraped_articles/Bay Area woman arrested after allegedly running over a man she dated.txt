Bay Area woman arrested after allegedly running over a man she dated
-----
A Bay Area woman was arrested after she allegedly ran over a man she was previously dating, officials said Thursday.The woman, a 24-year-old from Dublin, was arrested Wednesday on suspicion of attempted murder and booked into the Elmwood Complex Women's Facility in Santa Clara County, according to jail records. She was not allowed bail, records show.
