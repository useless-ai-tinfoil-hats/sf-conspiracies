Former S.F. HQ of Charles Schwab is latest downtown building to extend mortgage deadline
-----
A $195 million mortgage for Charles Schwab's former downtown San Francisco headquarters building was the latest office loan to be sent into special servicing after its due date passed this week — but its landlord said it has negotiated terms to extend the deadline tied to the 17-story office tower.The involvement of a special servicer — an entity that acts on behalf of a building's lender — can spell trouble for real estate owners who fall behind on payments or other obligations dictated
