4-year-old found under tree in Sierra forest after rescuers searched through the night
-----
A 4-year-old who was missing for nearly a day was found sitting under a tree Friday in the Sierra National Forest northeast of Fresno, authorities said.Christian Ramirez, 4, was found around 8 a.m. after more than 50 deputies and volunteers searched through the night to find him. He was near Huntington Lake, about a quarter-mile from where he reportedly disappeared 22 hours earlier, according to the Fresno County Sheriff's Office.
It is unclear what led to the boy's disappearance.
