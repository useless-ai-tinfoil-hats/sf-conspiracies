49ers wife Kristin Juszczyk's viral jackets are already getting the cheap knockoff treatment
-----
Viral fame, in the fashion world, is a double-edged sword.Kristin Juszczyk, the wife of San Francisco 49ers fullback Kyle Juszczyk, is raking in attention this postseason for her NFL-themed jacket designs. During wild-card weekend, Taylor Swift rocked her one-of-one Travis Kelce jacket, then Taylor Lautner donned a Lions version in Detroit. But without the legal apparatus of an established company, Juszczyk has to contend with fast fashion's voracious ripoff artists.
