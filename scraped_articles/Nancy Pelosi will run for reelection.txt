Nancy Pelosi will run for reelection
-----
Former Speaker Nancy Pelosi will seek reelection to the House in 2024, bucking expectations that she would retire at the end of her current term.The speaker emerita — who took on the honorific after stepping down from leadership in November 2022 — announced the decision to supporters at a labor event in San Francisco on Friday, and her spokesperson Aaron Bennett confirmed the decision to the Chronicle.
