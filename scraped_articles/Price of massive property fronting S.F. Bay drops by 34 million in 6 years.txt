Price of massive property fronting S.F. Bay drops by $34 million in 6 years
-----
Still awaiting a buyer after years on the market, an expansive piece of undeveloped bayfront land primed to become a lavish estate in the Marin County enclave of Tiburon recently had its price — once $47 million — knocked down again, to just $12.9 million.The 14.5-acre plot sports more than 2,100 feet of private shoreline, and is the largest undeveloped residential property on the San Francisco Bay waterfront in Marin County.
