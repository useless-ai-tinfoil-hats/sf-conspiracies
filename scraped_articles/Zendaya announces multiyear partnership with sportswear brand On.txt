Zendaya announces multiyear partnership with sportswear brand On
-----
On the heels of her tennis-centric film "Challengers," Zendaya is sticking close to the world of sports with her first-ever fashion collaboration with On sportswear.The multiyear partnership, announced on Thursday, June 6, will see the Oakland actress contributing to product design, creative campaigns and more, according to On, a company founded in Switzerland that specializes in athletic clothing and running shoes.
