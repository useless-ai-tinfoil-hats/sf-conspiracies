Newsom PAC runs ad in Alabama depicting abortion seeker as ‘fugitive’ in red-state blitz
-----
California Gov. Gavin Newsom's political action committee is launching a new advertisement in Alabama this week, targeting a proposed bill that would make it illegal there to help a minor seek an out-of-state abortion.In the ad, titled "Fugitive," two young women are speeding toward the Alabama border when a police cruiser appears in the car's rear-view mirror, siren blaring. A police officer approaches the car and holds up a pregnancy test.
