S.F. teachers authorize strike, move one step closer to walkout
-----
San Francisco teachers overwhelmingly voted this week to authorize a strike, the first step in what would be a long process before an actual walkout, but a key indicator that a large majority of the union's 6,000 members are ready to do so if talks continue to disappoint.More than 3,000 teachers cast ballots, with 98% voting Wednesday evening to support a strike, union officials said at a news conference Thursday after counting the ballots.
