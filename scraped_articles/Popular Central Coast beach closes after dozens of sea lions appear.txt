Popular Central Coast beach closes after dozens of sea lions appear
-----
A popular Central Coast beach for divers and tourists closed Monday after a large crowd of sea lions appeared over the weekend.Monterey city officials temporarily closed San Carlos Beach, the city announced on Facebook. A photo on the post shows dozens of sea lions lying together on the sand.
Anyone visiting the coastline is advised to stay at least 50 yards from the sea lions, the city said, as the animals are one of several marine mammal species protected by the Marine Mammal
