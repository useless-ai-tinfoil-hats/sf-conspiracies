100 people rescued in one day at Waikiki-area beaches amid big surf in Hawaii
-----
Lifeguards at beaches in Oahu's Waikiki area on Monday pulled 100 people from big waves and rough currents, officials said. The rescues came as a big swell churned up in the waters off New Zealand hit Hawaii and created dangerous conditions in the waters off south-facing beaches.People were getting stuck out at sea in strong currents, said Kurt Lager, acting chief for the Honolulu Emergency Services Department's Ocean Safety and Lifeguard Services Division.
