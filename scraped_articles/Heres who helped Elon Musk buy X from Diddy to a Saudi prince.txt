Here’s who helped Elon Musk buy X, from ‘Diddy’ to a Saudi prince
-----
Elon Musk is the majority owner and public face of X, formerly Twitter, which he bought for $44 billion in 2022. Almost 100 investors helped finance the bid, and thanks to a newly unsealed court filing, a complete list has been revealed.The list includes numerous entities with overlapping names, with mutual fund giant Fidelity affiliates accounting for more than two dozen businesses.
Disgraced rapper Sean "Diddy" Combs is one of the most prominent non-tech investors on the list.
