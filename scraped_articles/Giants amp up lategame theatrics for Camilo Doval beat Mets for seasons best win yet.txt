Giants amp up late-game theatrics for Camilo Doval, beat Mets for season’s best win yet

Monday's San Francisco Giants game ended with drama.Oracle Park's new bulbs went out, turning the ballpark pitch black — shocking the crowd into a scream — then faded into a dark red as if it were a rotisserie chicken under a giant heating lamp. A swarm of spotlights centered on the home bullpen's door as closer Camilo Doval stepped onto the grass with "Bandoleros" by Don Omar blaring through the speakers.
