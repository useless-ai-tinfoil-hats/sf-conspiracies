Where to get Jeremy Allen White's plaid farmer's market tote bag (and lookalikes)
-----
Over the past few weeks, a particular photo of Jeremy Allen White on one of his now-famous farmer's market runs made the rounds, I've been fixated on adding a plaid tote of this persuasion to my collection.
