Stanford loses fourth top administrator in less than a year
-----
Stanford's vice provost for student affairs will step down at the end of the fall quarter, university officials announced Thursday, marking the school's fourth high profile resignation in less than a year.Susie Brubaker-Cole, who has been in her role for six years and at Stanford for a total of 14 years, plans to return to her home state of Oregon, according to the school.
