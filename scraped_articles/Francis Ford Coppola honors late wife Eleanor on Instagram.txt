Francis Ford Coppola honors late wife Eleanor on Instagram
-----
Francis Ford Coppola paid tribute to his late wife on social media with the first Instagram post from the family since her death.The 85-year-old director of "The Godfather" shared a photo of Eleanor, a respected filmmaker in her own right, standing behind a bouquet. The post, shared on Tuesday, April 16, did not include a caption.
