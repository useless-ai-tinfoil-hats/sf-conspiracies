Biden’s FCC restores internet ‘neutrality’ to ensure level playing field for content providers
-----
The Federal Communications Commission has once again made net neutrality the rule, if not the law, of the land, more than six years after the agency during the Trump administration allowed internet providers to speed up or slow down their networks as they saw fit to boost their profits.The 3-2 party line vote by the five member commission Thursday comes as little surprise after a publicity blitz from FCC Commissioner Jessica Rosenworcel telegraphed the impending move.
