U.S. Coast Guard suspends search for survivors of downed aircraft near Half Moon Bay
-----
The U.S. Coast Guard announced Monday morning it had suspended the search for two potential survivors of a downed aircraft that crashed in the waters off Half Moon Bay on Sunday evening.The agency and the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office had been actively searching the waters off the coast of Moss Beach on Monday for any signs of a small plane that crashed Sunday evening, officials said.
