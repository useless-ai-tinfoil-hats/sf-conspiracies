S.F. schools expect to cut more than 500 positions in future budget
-----
San Francisco school officials are poised to pass another budget that allows the district to spend significantly more than it brings in. It's the last time it can afford to do so.The board is expected to approve the 2024-25 school year budget on Tuesday and finalize it by Sunday, but the San Francisco Unified School District is already looking beyond it and anticipating steep cuts for the 2025-26 school year.
