Deadly avalanche closes Palisades Tahoe: ‘This is a really big deal’
-----
A major avalanche at the Palisades Tahoe ski resort Wednesday morning left one person dead and three others with non life-threatening injuries.The avalanche occurred about 9:30 a.m. on the Palisades side of the resort. All lifts closed for the day, and first responders from around the region immediately joined the search and rescue effort.
