Police book Bay Area mom on murder charge after baby dies with fentanyl in his system
-----
Fremont police arrested a woman on suspicion of murder after her child allegedly died with a large amount of fentanyl in his system.On the morning of Oct. 18, Sophia Gastelum-Vera, 26, brought her son to an emergency room because he was unresponsive and not breathing, according to Fremont police. The child, who was just under 2 years old, later died in the hospital.
