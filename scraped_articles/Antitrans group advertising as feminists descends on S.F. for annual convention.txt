Anti-trans group, advertising as feminists, descends on S.F. for annual convention
-----
San Francisco is hosting a "radical feminist" group aligned with anti-transgender efforts in the U.S. and abroad for a convention this weekend that is drawing fury from local trans and other LGBTQ individuals and allies.Women's Declaration International USA — the U.S. chapter of a group that began in the United Kingdom — is expecting about 100 people at the three-day conference, to be followed by a protest in front of City Hall on Monday.
