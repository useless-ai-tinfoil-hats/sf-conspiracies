Oakland woman killed in house fire that started in wall furnace
-----
A woman was killed in a house fire early Wednesday morning in Oakland on the 2900 block of Partridge Avenue, Oakland fire officials said.Firefighters arrived at the home shortly after 2:30 a.m. and found the woman, whom Fire Department officials said was in her 90s.
The woman, who was not immediately identified, had apparently died as a result of smoke inhalation and burns she suffered. She was apparently the sole occupant of the home, officials said.
