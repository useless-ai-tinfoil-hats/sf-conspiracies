Pro-Palestine protesters block entrance to ship docked in San Francisco
-----
A group of pro-Palestine protesters gathered where the USNS Harvey Milk was docked in San Francisco on Friday morning, with some reportedly chaining themselves to the ship's gangway in an attempt to disrupt a media event held by local and national politicians, and to block the vessel from reportedly transporting supplies to the Middle East.Roughly 800 people attended the action, according to Wassim Hage, media coordinator for the Arab Resource & Organizing Center.
