'Horrendous': Wealthy Bay Area enclave worried about sprawling new development
-----
Marin County residents are expressing concern over a new, sprawling development that they fear will bring grating noise and noxious air pollution to the small, affluent town of San Rafael, documents show.The Northgate Mall Redevelopment project is marketed as an "open-air main street experience" that could include nearly 1,500 new residential units, according to the city.
