San Francisco Opera Presents 'The Magic Flute'
-----
Journey into Love. Adventure into Fantasy.Infiltrate the castle. Rescue the princess. Bring her home. That's the mission that unites two unlikely allies: a dashing young prince and a bumbling bird-catcher, armed only with a set of magical musical instruments. Their journey into a world of sorcerers and serpents, spirits and secrets, takes an unexpected turn in The Magic Flute, a fairy-tale quest for love and wisdom.
