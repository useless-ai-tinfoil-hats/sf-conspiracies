Two 16-year-olds arrested in deadly SF shooting outside Powell Street BART Station
-----
Two teenagers were arrested in connection with a deadly shooting Thursday night outside the Powell Street BART Station in San Francisco.Officers from the San Francisco Police Department responded to the Hallidie Plaza area of the station around 9:30 p.m., where they found a 17-year-old male with gunshot wounds, the department said in a Monday news release. The teen died at the scene after receiving medical assistance from law enforcement and emergency responders.
