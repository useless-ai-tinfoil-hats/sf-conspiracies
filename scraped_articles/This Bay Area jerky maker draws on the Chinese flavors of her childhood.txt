This Bay Area jerky maker draws on the Chinese flavors of her childhood
-----
Anica Wu couldn't stop talking about meat.She chatted on and on — about using Chinese flavors to make jerky for her company, Bonjerk; about moving away from factory farms — as she poured small spoonfuls of chile sauce into china bowls. Then, standing in her San Leandro kitchen, she dribbled in other flavorings: 1 gram fish sauce. Half a gram ginger juice. Some of the bowls got pineapple juice; others, shrimp paste. Wu dropped a slice of raw pork into each bowl, mashing the ingredients
