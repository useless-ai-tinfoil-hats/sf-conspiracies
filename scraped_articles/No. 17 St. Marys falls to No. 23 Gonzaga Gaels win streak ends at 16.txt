No. 17 St. Mary’s falls to No. 23 Gonzaga; Gaels’ win streak ends at 16
-----
Gone for the St. Mary's Gaels is what had been the nation's longest active winning streak, the second longest in program history. Gone for the Gaels is the opportunity for the first unbeaten conference season in program history.Randy Bennett's team still owns the outright WCC regular-season title but it was clearly the second-best team on the floor in Moraga on Saturday night.
No. 23 Gonzaga overpowered No. 17 St. Mary's 70-57, ending the Gaels' winning streak at 16 games.
