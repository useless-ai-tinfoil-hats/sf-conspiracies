Man trapped in Northern California drainage ditch for 2 days treated for hypothermia
-----
A man who fell into a drainage ditch in Colfax remained stranded for at least two days before search and rescue teams swooped in to extricate him, according to the Placer County Sheriff's Office.The 55-year-old victim, who has not been identified, apparently slipped down a steep hillside from the Canyon Creek trail into a canal. He may have tried to climb out before a passerby heard cries for help on Dec. 30 and notified the sheriff.
