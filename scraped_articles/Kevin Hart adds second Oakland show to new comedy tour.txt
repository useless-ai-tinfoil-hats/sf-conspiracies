Kevin Hart adds second Oakland show to new comedy tour
-----
Award-winning actor and comedian Kevin Hart announced he's adding a second Oakland performance to his forthcoming "Acting My Age" comedy tour "due to fan demand."The new date at Paramount Theatre is scheduled for Oct. 26, following his previously announced Bay Area show on Oct. 25, at the Uptown venue.
Hart also added eight other dates to the tour, extending his run in cities such as Seattle, Denver and Phoenix, with an additional stop in Kansas City, Mo.
