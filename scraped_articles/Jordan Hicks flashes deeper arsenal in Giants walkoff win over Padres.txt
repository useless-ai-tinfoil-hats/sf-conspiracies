Jordan Hicks flashes deeper arsenal in Giants’ walk-off win over Padres
-----
Jordan Hicks could have called it a day after six innings.Michael Conforto's RBI double tied the game and, having thrown 80 pitches, Hicks neutralized the San Diego Padres' lineup, holding it to two runs, just one earned. Between innings, manager Bob Melvin extended his hand and told Hicks he was ready to shake it — take the starter out — if he was ready.
