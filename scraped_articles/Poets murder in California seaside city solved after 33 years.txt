Poet's murder in California seaside city solved after 33 years
-----
The brutal murder of a 42-year-old woman in a California seaside city 33 years ago has been solved.On the evening of July 15, 1991, Danielle Clause was seen talking to a man driving a small white pickup on a quiet street corner two blocks from the ocean in Ventura. The following morning, her partially clothed, beaten body was found on a hillside a mile away.
