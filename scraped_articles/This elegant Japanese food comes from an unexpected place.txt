This elegant Japanese food comes from an unexpected place
-----
Here's senior editor Janelle Bitker:Believe it or not, I think some of the Bay Area's most delicious Japanese food is coming out of a tent.
That tent sets up on Saturday afternoons in the back patio at Berkeley winery Broc Cellars. Called Daruma Kiosk, it's a two-year-old pop-up that critic Cesar Hernandez put on his Top Berkeley Restaurants list.
