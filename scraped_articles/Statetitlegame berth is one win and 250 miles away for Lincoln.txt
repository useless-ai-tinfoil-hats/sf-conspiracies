State-title-game berth is one win and 250 miles away for Lincoln
-----
It's 250 miles from San Francisco to Strathmore, and culturally, the distance is probably larger than that.But Lincoln football coach Phil Ferrigno is all-in on his team's long journey to play Saturday in a Northern California title game. His Mustangs, a three-time state and 14-time San Francisco Section champion, travel to Tulare County for the 7-A title matchup.
