Plan to turn an S.F. auto repair into a new city park wins key vote
-----
A property that holds an auto repair shop on a ragged block on Sixth Street in San Francisco's South of Market neighborhood could be bought by the city and turned into a park under a plan advanced with a key vote Thursday.The shop at 240 Sixth St. abuts the former site of the Gene Friend Recreation Center, which has been demolished and is now being rebuilt.
