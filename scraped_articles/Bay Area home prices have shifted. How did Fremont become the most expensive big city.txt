Bay Area home prices have shifted. How did Fremont become the most expensive big city?
-----
For decades, San Francisco homes boasted the highest values among the Bay Area's five biggest cities. That's no longer the case.The top spot is now held by Fremont, according to data from real estate listing site Zillow, with a typical home value of nearly $1.4 million in August. San Jose had the second-highest typical home value of $1.3 million, with San Francisco just behind at $1.27 million.
