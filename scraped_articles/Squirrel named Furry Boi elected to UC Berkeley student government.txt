Squirrel named Furry Boi elected to UC Berkeley student government
-----
A lack of affordable housing is such a critical issue at UC Berkeley that even the squirrels roaming the wooded campus are worried about it. Or at least that's what a satirical — and successful — candidate in the university's recent student government elections is saying.Last week, Furry Boi, a squirrel from Berkeley's eucalyptus grove, won a student senate seat for the next academic year alongside 19 human candidates.
