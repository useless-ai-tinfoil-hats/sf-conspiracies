Chronicle Live at Manny's: Stories of San Francisco’s Dead
-----
There are bodies beneath San Francisco.While the city famously banned burials in 1901 and banished its dead to Colma, human remains remain in San Francisco — including underneath famous landmarks like the Legion of Honor and United Nations Plaza.
Join the San Francisco Chronicle live at Manny's on October 18 at 6 p.m. for a night of hauntings and strange history about the second lives of San Francisco's dead. (Is the Chronicle archive haunted? You'll find out!)
