Pfizer to charge $1,390 for its lifesaving Paxlovid COVID treatment on commercial market
-----
The price of Paxlovid will more than double when the lifesaving COVID-19 treatment enters to the commercial market next month.Pfizer, which manufactures the drug, announced Thursday a price tag of $1,390 for a five-day course of Paxlovid, which has been sold to the federal government for $529 since it received emergency use authorization in December 2021.
