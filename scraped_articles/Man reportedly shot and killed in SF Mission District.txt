Man reportedly shot and killed in SF Mission District
-----
A man was shot and killed in San Francisco's Mission District on Friday afternoon, local media outlets reported.The San Francisco Police Department said the shooting happened around 1:40 p.m. on the 2300 block of Mission Street, near the El Capitan Hotel, Mission Local reported.
The man was pronounced dead at the scene after suffering gunshot wounds, the outlet said.
