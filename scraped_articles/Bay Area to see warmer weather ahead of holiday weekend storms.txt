Bay Area to see warmer weather ahead of holiday weekend storms
-----
After Wednesday's widespread rain, a two-day stretch of dry weather is expected for the Bay Area before a stormy holiday weekend.Lingering drizzle is possible over the North Bay on Thursday morning, but by afternoon, the entire Bay Area should be dry under partly cloudy skies. Highs will be slightly below normal, in the upper 50s to lower 60s. Lows will drop to the mid- to upper 40s.
