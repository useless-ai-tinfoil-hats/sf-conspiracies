Crime in Oakland has been rising. These charts show how trends vary by neighborhood
-----
Crime in Oakland was up for the third year in a row in 2023, as the city struggles to get a handle on public safety post-pandemic. But trends at the city level don't tell the whole story. A Chronicle analysis of crime data shows that certain crimes are rising in some neighborhoods far more than others.
