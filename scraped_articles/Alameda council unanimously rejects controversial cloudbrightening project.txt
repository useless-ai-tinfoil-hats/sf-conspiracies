Alameda council unanimously rejects controversial cloud-brightening project
-----
After a passionate and emotional debate, the Alameda City Council early Wednesday voted unanimously to reject a controversial climate geoengineering project aboard the USS Hornet, a decommissioned aircraft carrier docked in the city's southwest corner.The first-of-its-kind effort, aimed at slowing global warming by reflecting sunlight back to space, began in early April. But soon afterward, city officials ordered a halt to review potential risks.
