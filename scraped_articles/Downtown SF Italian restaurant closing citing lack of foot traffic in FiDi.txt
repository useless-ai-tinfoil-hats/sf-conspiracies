Downtown SF Italian restaurant closing, citing lack of foot traffic in FiDi
-----
Italian restaurant Barbacco is closing at the end of the month after 14 years, citing a lack of foot traffic in downtown San Francisco, according to the San Francisco Chronicle (the Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms)."We hoped 2023 would be the year the neighborhood returned to the way it was pre-pandemic, however that hasn't been the case," owner Umberto Gibin shared in an Instagram post.
