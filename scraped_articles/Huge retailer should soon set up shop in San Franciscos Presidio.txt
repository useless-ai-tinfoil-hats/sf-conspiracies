Huge retailer should soon set up shop in San Francisco's Presidio
-----
Bay Area-based retailer Pet Food Express signed a lease to occupy renovated warehouse space in San Francisco's Presidio, the Presidio Trust announced Monday. It joins longtime retail tenant Sports Basement, which has been in the park since 2003.The mixed-use area on former military property has had only one other traditional retailer, specialized bike shop Roaring Mouse Cycles, since Sports Basement opened.
