El Cerrito del Norte station reopens after ‘major medical emergency’
-----
BART's El Cerrito del Norte station was shuttered Wednesday evening due to a "major medical emergency," officials said.The emergency began at about 6 p.m., said BART spokesperson Anna Duckworth via the agency's media voicemail. "We do have emergency personnel on scene," she said.
