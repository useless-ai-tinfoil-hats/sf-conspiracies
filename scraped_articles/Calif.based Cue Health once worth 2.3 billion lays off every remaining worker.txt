Calif.-based Cue Health, once worth $2.3 billion, lays off every remaining worker
-----
Cue Health has reached the end of the road. The San Diego-based medical tech company announced Monday that it's laying off its entire staff.The company once had 1,500 workers, thriving during the COVID-19 pandemic with sales of its test devices to Google, the NBA and the Department of Defense.
