S.F. police make arrest in fatal Bayview shooting
-----
San Francisco police arrested a man they allege fatally shot another man near the former San Francisco Naval Shipyard in August, police announced Thursday.Officials allege Shilo Mitchell, a 35-year-old San Francisco resident, shot and killed another man on the night of Aug. 4 on the 1000 block of Quesada Avenue, just outside the former shipyard.
Paramedics treated the victim at the scene, but he died at a hospital, according to police.
