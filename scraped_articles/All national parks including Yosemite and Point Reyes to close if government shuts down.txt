All national parks, including Yosemite and Point Reyes, to close if government shuts down
-----
Yosemite, Alcatraz and Point Reyes National Seashore are among the popular national parks that will begin closing down this weekend in the case of a government shutdown.The National Park Service will close all of its parks if lawmakers are unable to come to an agreement on how to keep the government funded by the deadline of midnight Saturday, the Department of the Interior announced Thursday.
