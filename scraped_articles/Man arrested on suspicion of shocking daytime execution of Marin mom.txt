Man arrested on suspicion of shocking daytime execution of Marin mom
-----
An Idaho man has been arrested in connection with the killing of a Marin County mother that shocked the quiet community in the 1970s. Michael Eugene Mullen, 75, is in custody and awaiting extradition to California.On the morning of Nov. 15, 1973, Swedish business executive Gunnar Fischer left for work in San Francisco.
