SF seafood restaurant Woodhouse Fish Co. closes original location after 18 years
-----
After 18 years of buttery Dungeness crab rolls and fresh oysters, Woodhouse Fish Co. is closing its flagship location this week, as first reported by Eater SF.The seafood restaurant at 2073 Market St. is expected to close on Jan. 23, after a long run near the Castro and Duboce Triangle.
