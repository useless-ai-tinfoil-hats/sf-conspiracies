Drug overdose killed UC Berkeley student, son of former YouTube CEO, officials say
-----
The Alameda County Sheriff's Office said Tuesday that the death of a 19-year-old UC Berkeley freshman found in his dorm in February was caused by acute combined drug toxicity.Marco Troper, who was in his second semester at the university, was found unresponsive in a college dorm on Feb. 13. His grandmother, Esther Wojcicki announced his death on social media the next day. Troper, a math major, was the son of former YouTube CEO Susan Wojcicki, who stepped down from her role last year
