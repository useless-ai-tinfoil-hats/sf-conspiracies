East Bay hikers, bicyclists are about to gain new access to 18 miles of scenic trails
-----
Starting this weekend, it'll become easier for walkers, bicyclists and equestrians to access more than 18 miles of scenic trails in the East Bay.The Tyler Ranch Staging Area in Sunol is set to open Sunday after more than two years of construction, according to the East Bay Regional Park District. It will make it easier to access the Sunol Ridge Trail and Tyler Ranch Trail, which offer views of the bay, Mount Diablo, Brushy Peak, Mission Peak, Mount Umunhum and Mount Tamalpais.
