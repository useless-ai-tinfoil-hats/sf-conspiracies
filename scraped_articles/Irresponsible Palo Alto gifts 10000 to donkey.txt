'Irresponsible': Palo Alto gifts $10,000 to donkey
-----
Palo Alto Mayor Greer Stone's plan to give $10,000 to a local donkey was met with some pushback during the most recent Palo Alto City Council meeting on May 20.The donkey, a Jerusalem burro named Perry, lives in a patch of grass in Palo Alto's Barron Park neighborhood called Bol Park. The animal famously posed for photos, video and sketches used to model Donkey from DreamWorks' Academy Award-winning 2001 film "Shrek."
