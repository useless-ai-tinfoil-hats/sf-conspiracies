Controversial development at abandoned Bay Area mall finally moves forward
-----
After years of delays and controversy, the development of a new neighborhood at the site of an abandoned shopping center in Cupertino is set to kick off later this year.Dubbed The Rise, the 50-acre project includes apartments, restaurants, retail and office space that will transform the former site of the Vallco shopping center, according to a press release. Cupertino city officials approved the plans on Friday.
