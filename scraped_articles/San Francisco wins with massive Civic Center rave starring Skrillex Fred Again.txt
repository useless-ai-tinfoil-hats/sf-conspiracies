San Francisco wins with massive Civic Center rave starring Skrillex, Fred Again
-----
It probably wasn't on anyone's rave bingo card that the biggest shout-out of Saturday night, at the biggest party of the year so far, would go to West Portal Elementary School.The surprise Skrillex and Fred Again.. concert in the heart of downtown San Francisco was announced only a week ago, and the pair of EDM superstars managed to sell out 25,000 tickets in an hour.
