Calistoga pedestrian struck by police vehicle, suffers major head injuries
-----
The California Highway Patrol is conducting an investigation after a Calistoga police sergeant ran into a woman in a crosswalk on July 29, inflicting major head injuries, according to the police agency.Brenda Sarabio, 22, was struck by the officer's car while crossing Berry Street at Washington Street at 7 p.m., according to a highway patrol officer. Sarabio was then airlifted to Providence Santa Rosa Memorial Hospital, the officer said.
