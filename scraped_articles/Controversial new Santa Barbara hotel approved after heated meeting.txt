Controversial new Santa Barbara hotel approved after heated meeting
-----
A controversial hotel proposed for Santa Barbara's popular Funk Zone received narrow approval from the city's planning commission this week, a step toward actually building something from a proposal that's been decades in the making.The 250-room hotel at 101 Garden Street received a 4-2 vote last week after a heated four-hour meeting, the Santa Barbara Independent reported.
