Bay FC announces practice home at SJSU for inaugural season in 2024
-----
Bay FC will have its practice facilities on the campus of San Jose State while it continues to seek a location for permanent facilities, the expansion NWSL club announced Tuesday.The 14th NWSL team will play home games at PayPal Park for at least the next five seasons, and it is investing more than $2 million to develop its interim facility. The locker rooms will be built out of the former SJSU football visiting-team locker rooms.
