S.F. Macy’s closure could hurt Union Square’s shops, restaurants
-----
The impending closure of Macy's huge flagship store on Union Square will be one of the largest blows to downtown San Francisco as it struggles to recover from the pandemic. But it's far from the only recent setback for the area, piling on top of an exodus that included a 35-year-old Nordstrom shuttering, along with long-standing challenges over public safety and homelessness.Macy's departure will cut another major anchor from the Union Square and Powell shopping district, nearby
