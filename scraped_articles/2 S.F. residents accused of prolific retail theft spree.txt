2 S.F. residents accused of prolific retail theft spree
-----
Two San Francisco residents were charged with organized retail theft after being arrested several times for allegedly stealing from businesses across the city, officials said Wednesday.The San Francisco District Attorney's Office charged Emoni Thomas and Erykah Thomas, both 21, with two counts of felony organized retail theft, two counts of second-degree commercial burglary and two counts of grand theft. It wasn't immediately clear whether the two are related.
