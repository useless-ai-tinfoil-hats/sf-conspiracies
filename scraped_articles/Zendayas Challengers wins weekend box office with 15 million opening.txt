Zendaya’s ‘Challengers’ wins weekend box office with $15 million opening
-----
The tennis drama "Challengers" starring Oakland native Zendaya scored the top spot at the box office this weekend with $15 million in ticket sales.The film, directed by Luca Guadagnino ("Call Me by Your Name"), opened strong in previews on Thursday and continued its dominance throughout the weekend, pushing A24's "Civil War" from the No. 1 spot. The Amazon MGM film, which also stars Josh O'Connor and Mike Faist, opened in 3,477 locations in the U.S. and Canada, the Associated Press
