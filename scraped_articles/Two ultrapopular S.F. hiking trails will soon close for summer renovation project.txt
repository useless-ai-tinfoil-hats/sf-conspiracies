Two ultrapopular S.F. hiking trails will soon close for summer renovation project
-----
Two hiking trails to the Twin Peaks lookout over San Francisco — one of the city's top outdoor attractions — will close for renovation in mid-June, just as the summer tourism season gets under way in earnest, officials announced Wednesday.Both the trail at Noe Peak and the switchback trail just south of it will be closed for several months, according to the city Recreation and Park Department.
