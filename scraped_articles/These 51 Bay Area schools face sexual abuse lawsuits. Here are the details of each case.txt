These 51 Bay Area schools face sexual abuse lawsuits. Here are the details of each case
-----
The Chronicle identified more than 50 child sexual abuse lawsuits filed against Bay Area school districts during the last six months of 2022, a key deadline under a state law that provided a window for people to file claims that would otherwise be too old.The lawsuits include instances of alleged abuse as early as 1962 and as recent as 2017. Of the 52 lawsuits the Chronicle identified, just eight have been previously reported.
