Person injured in downtown Oakland shooting
-----
A person was injured in a shooting in downtown Oakland early Saturday, according to police.Just before 2:15 a.m., Oakland police responded to reports of a shooting on the 1100 block of Franklin Street, outside the University of California president's office. There, officers found a victim who had been shot at least once, according to officials.
The victim was taken to a hospital and was in stable condition Saturday afternoon.
