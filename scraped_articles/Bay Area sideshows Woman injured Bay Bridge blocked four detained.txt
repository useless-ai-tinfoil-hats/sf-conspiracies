Bay Area sideshows: Woman injured, Bay Bridge blocked, four detained
-----
Large back-to-back sideshows swept the Bay Area late Saturday into Sunday, injuring a 20-year-old woman in Mountain View and later blocking the Bay Bridge for about 20 minutes.In Menlo Park around 11 p.m. Saturday, a sideshow shut down the intersection of Willow Road and Newbridge Street, with about 100 vehicles and 200 spectators in the street. Some in the crowd kicked a Menlo Park patrol officer's vehicle and jumped on the hood, police said.
