Letters: If there are going to be reparations, this group is the most deserving
-----
I've heard arguments for and against reparations of some sort for African Americans who suffered 400 hundred years of slavery and discrimination. Treatment of African Americans has been abominable in our country and that must change.However, there is a group of people who have had everything taken from them from the day Europeans arrived on our shores. The Native American tribes have suffered the loss of their land, lives, language and culture.
