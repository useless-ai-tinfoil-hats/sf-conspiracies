Dianne Feinstein: Photos of her life and career
-----
Sen. Dianne Feinstein has died at age 90 after a political career that took her from City Hall in San Francisco to Washington, D.C. Her career took off after one of the city's bloodiest and most devastating tragedies.From the first time she dipped her toe into politics as an appointee to the state Women's Board of Terms and Parole in 1961 to her longtime tenure as the state's senior U.S. senator, Feinstein broke barriers and left an indelible imprint on San Francisco and California.
