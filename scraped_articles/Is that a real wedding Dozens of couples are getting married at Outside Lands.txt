'Is that a real wedding?': Dozens of couples are getting married at Outside Lands
-----
On Friday morning, John and Sharon Van Akkeren slipped on their Outside Lands wristbands. Then, just a few hours later, they slid on their wedding rings.The Van Akkerens were the first of several couples who tied the knot Friday at the music festival. Standing under a flower-lined wooden arch, they faced each other as the festival's wedding officiant, Brian Fiore-Silfvast, gave a short speech.
