The private passion of Grateful Dead genius Jerry Garcia
-----
"I hope that nobody takes them too seriously." That was how Jerry Garcia described his paintings, even as dealers and galleries were mounting high-profile exhibitions and marketing prints. It was a revealing comment, typical of his modesty, and tellingly, it concluded the back cover blurb of his first book of art, "J. Garcia: Paintings, Drawings, and Sketches."
