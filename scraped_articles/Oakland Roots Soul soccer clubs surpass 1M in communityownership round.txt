Oakland Roots, Soul soccer clubs surpass $1M in community-ownership round
-----
The Oakland Roots and Soul soccer clubs surpassed $1 million on the first day of a community-investment campaign that allows fans to purchase ownership stakes.The organization reached the halfway mark of its $2 million goal after just six hours Wednesday, and it stood at $1.64 million Friday afternoon. Investors will receive an official stake in ownership in the club, regardless of amount. Fans can invest anywhere from $100 to $25,000 or more as options on the "Wefunder" crowd-funding
