Giants thrilled about Blake Snell’s upcoming return to rotation
-----
The San Francisco Giants anticipate a far more productive Blake Snell when he rejoins their rotation. Manager Bob Melvin said there's a "good chance" the two-time Cy Young Award winner will return to the mound Wednesday in Pittsburgh.It would be Snell's first Giants appearance since April 19, when the lefthander struggled for the third consecutive start, overall producing an 11.57 ERA while yielding 18 hits and issuing five walks in 11⅔ innings.
