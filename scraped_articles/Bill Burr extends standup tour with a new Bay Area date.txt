Bill Burr extends stand-up tour with a new Bay Area date
-----
Comedian Bill Burr has expanded his 2024 North American tour with an additional Bay Area date.The Grammy- and Emmy-nominated actor is set to bring his show "Bill Burr Live" to the Greek Theatre in Berkeley on June 8. His previously announced performances are set for June 19-22 at the San Jose Civic.
General ticket sales for the new show begin at 10 a.m. Friday, March 1, via Ticketmaster.
