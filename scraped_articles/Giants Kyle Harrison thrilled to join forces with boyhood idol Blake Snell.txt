Giants’ Kyle Harrison thrilled to join forces with boyhood idol Blake Snell
-----
GOODYEAR, Ariz. — Kyle Harrison couldn't hide his smile.His dad had texted him the news. Blake Snell signed a two-year, $62 million deal to join Harrison in the San Francisco Giants' rotation. The first thing Harrison, 22, thought about was his own full-circle moment.
In 2018 while a student at De La Salle-Concord, Harrison met Snell, then with the Tampa Bay Rays, after a Giants game Harrison was attending as a fan. He remembers Snell making a point of coming out to shake his hand
