Billionaire MacKenzie Scott donates $20M to S.F. affordable housing trust
-----
Philanthropist MacKenzie Scott has donated $20 million to the San Francisco Community Land Trust, a local affordable housing nonprofit, according to a Wednesday news release. The organization will use the gift to accelerate its efforts to acquire property and turn buildings into permanently affordable homes by placing them under community ownership."It's absolutely game-changing for our organization," nonprofit executive director Saki Bailey told the Chronicle.
