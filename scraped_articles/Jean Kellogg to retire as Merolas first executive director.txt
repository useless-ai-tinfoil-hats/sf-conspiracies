Jean Kellogg, the first executive director of San Francisco's acclaimed Merola Opera Program, plans to retire next year.In 2011, Kellogg joined the world-renowned opera organization, affiliated with the San Francisco Opera Center, and saw it through the COVID-19 outbreak and its fallout. She swiftly transitioned the training model to digital programming in 2020, and helped launch the Merola Artists Emergency Fund to support alumni financially impacted by pandemic shutdowns.
