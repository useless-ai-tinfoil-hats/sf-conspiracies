Violation notices issued to Chevron over Richmond refinery flaring
-----
Following flaring at Chevron's refinery in Richmond on Monday afternoon that sent smoke across the bay, the Bay Area Air Quality Management District said Wednesday that it has issued four notices of violation to the company.Three of the violations are for visible emissions, and one is for creating a public nuisance.
"The investigation is still ongoing and more violations may be issued," the air quality district wrote on X, formerly known as Twitter.
