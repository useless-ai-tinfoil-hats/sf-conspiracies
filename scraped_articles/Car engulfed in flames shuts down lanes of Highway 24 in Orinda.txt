Car engulfed in flames shuts down lanes of Highway 24 in Orinda
-----
Two lanes of Highway 24 in Orinda were shut down Saturday night as firefighters attempted to extinguish flames that had engulfed a car, sending plumes of smoke into the air.Preliminary information from the California Highway Patrol said the rear of the car caught on fire as it was driving east down Highway 24. The car pulled over to the shoulder just before 7 p.m., and all occupants were able to get out, according to CHP.
