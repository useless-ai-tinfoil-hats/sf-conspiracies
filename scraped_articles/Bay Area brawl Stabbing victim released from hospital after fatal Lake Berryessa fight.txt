Bay Area brawl: Stabbing victim released from hospital after fatal Lake Berryessa fight
-----
An investigation into a large fight at a Lake Berryessa picnic ground in which one man died and several others were injured continued Sunday, with no arrests made, according to the Napa County Sheriff's Office.Deputies responded to the 5500 block of Knoxville Road at Oak Shores at 5:30 p.m. Saturday, where paramedics transported two stabbing victims to hospitals by helicopter. Family and friends drove other injured people away, authorities said.
