This classic San Francisco restaurant is making a comeback
-----
Park Tavern, a popular San Francisco restaurant that closed last year amid an eviction battle, is reopening under new ownership.James Nicholas, owner of Marlowe and the Cavalier in San Francisco, is resurrecting the 1652 Stockton St. business as Park Tavern on Washington Square. He said the previous owner, his ex-wife and business partner Anna Weinberg, is no longer involved in the restaurant.
