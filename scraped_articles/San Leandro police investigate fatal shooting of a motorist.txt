San Leandro police investigate fatal shooting of a motorist
-----
A homicide investigation is underway in San Leandro after a person was shot, crashed their car and died in the East Bay city last weekend.At 6:24 p.m. Sunday, officers responded to a report of a solo car collision on the 1800 block of Benedict Drive to find the driver of the car suffering from a gunshot wound, police said. The victim was pronounced dead by paramedics, according to police.
