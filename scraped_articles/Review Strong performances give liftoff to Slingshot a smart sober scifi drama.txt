Review: Strong performances give liftoff to ‘Slingshot,’ a smart, sober sci-fi drama
-----
"Slingshot" presents Casey Affleck with the best acting opportunity since winning the Oscar for "Manchester by the Sea," and he makes the most of it. Though on the surface something of a forlorn sci-fi thriller, it's really a tense psychological study, mostly taking place in a single location: a spaceship heading for Saturn's moon Titan.The mission is to get into the gravitational orbit of Jupiter, then slingshot around that and go to Saturn.
