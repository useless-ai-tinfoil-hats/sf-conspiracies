Kuminga’s 29 points, Podziemski’s 14 assists lead Warriors past Grizzlies
-----
MEMPHIS — That he had 14 assists without a turnover Friday wasn't the most gratifying thing about the Golden State Warriors' 121-101 victory over the Memphis Grizzlies for Brandin Podziemski.The rookie guard was more satisfied with the way the Warriors defended.
"I don't think we've held anybody to the 100-mark recently," he said. "Just proud of how everybody that was able to get on the floor just kind of impacted winning."
