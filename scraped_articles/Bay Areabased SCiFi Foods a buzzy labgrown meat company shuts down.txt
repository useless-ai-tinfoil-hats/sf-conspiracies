Bay Area-based SCiFi Foods, a buzzy lab-grown meat company, shuts down
-----
SCiFi Foods, a buzzy Bay Area startup that hoped to grow burgers in a lab from cow cells, is shutting down. The company announced the closure in a LinkedIn post on Tuesday, signed by SCiFi's two co-founders.
