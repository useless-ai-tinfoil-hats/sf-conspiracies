A ‘derelict’ winery and abandoned tasting rooms: How a Sonoma wine empire crumbled
-----
The site of Sonoma's Ravenswood Winery, once considered the premier producer of California Zinfandel, has sat abandoned for five years. The doors and windows of the historic stone building are boarded up. A massive pile of debris occupies the parking lot.
