First ‘Megalopolis’ trailer drops: Francis Ford Coppola calls it his ‘best work’
-----
The classic American Zoetrope logo appears, invoking a San Francisco-born maverick spirit. Sirens blare over an image of the Statue of Liberty. A voiceover ominously asks, "When does an empire die? Does it collapse in one terrible moment? No, no. But there comes a time when people no longer believe in it."So begins the first trailer for Francis Ford Coppola's long awaited "Megalopolis," which is set to make its world premiere at the Cannes Film Festival this week.
