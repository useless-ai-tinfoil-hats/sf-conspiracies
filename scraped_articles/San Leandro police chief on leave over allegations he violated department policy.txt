San Leandro police chief on leave over allegations he violated department policy
-----
San Leandro has put its police chief on leave amid an investigation into whether he may have violated department policy, city officials announced Friday.Police Chief Abdul Pridgen was placed on paid leave starting last Monday while the Danville-based firm Kramer Workplace Investigations conducts interviews and reviews documentation related to the allegations. The city did not release details into the specific policies Pridgen is alleged to have violated.
