Warriors exercise fourth-year options for Jonathan Kuminga, Moses Moody
-----
As expected, the Golden State Warriors have exercised the fourth-year contract options for Jonathan Kuminga and Moses Moody.Kuminga will earn $7.6 million and Moody will earn $5.8 million next season. The lottery picks, selected by the Warriors seventh and 14th in 2021, will be eligible for restricted free agency in summer 2025.
