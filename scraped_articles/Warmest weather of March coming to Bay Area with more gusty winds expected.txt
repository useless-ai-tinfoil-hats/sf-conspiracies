Warmest weather of March coming to Bay Area with more gusty winds expected
-----
Thursday is shaping up to be the warmest day so far this month in the Bay Area, with temperatures in the upper 60s to lower 70s. Highs will be about 5 degrees above normal in San Francisco, Oakland and San Jose and nearly 10 degrees above normal in Half Moon Bay.In addition to the warmth, strong winds are in the forecast for the higher elevations of the Bay Area.
