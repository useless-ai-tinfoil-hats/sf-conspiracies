Evacuation order lifted in Lake County Scotts Fire
-----
UPDATE June 19 8:28 p.m. The evacuation order for the Scotts Fire has been reduced to a warning, the Lake County Sheriff's Office posted on Facebook on Wednesday evening.The Sheriff's Office also lifted the evacuation warning for zones NIC-E042 and NIC-E047B.
Cal Fire posted on X that air and ground crews have halted the fire's progress at 38 acres and that containment was at 15%.
