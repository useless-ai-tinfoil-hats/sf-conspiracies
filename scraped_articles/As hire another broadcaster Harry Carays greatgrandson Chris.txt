A’s hire another broadcaster, Harry Caray’s great-grandson Chris
-----
MESA, Ariz. — A week after broadcaster Jenny Cavnar was hired to call Oakland Athletics games on television, another announcer was added to the A's booth: Chris Caray, great-grandson of the legendary Harry Caray.The tentative plan is for Cavnar to call approximately 95 games and Caray, the fourth-generation announcer in his family to broadcast Major League Baseball, about 65 games. Dallas Braden will continue to provide color.
