49ers-Giants game not likely to be affected by wildfire smoke
-----
The NFL will monitor the air quality in the South Bay in case worsening conditions from Northern California and Oregon wildfires prompt the league to consider postponing the San Francisco 49ers' Thursday night game against the Giants at Levi's Stadium.At midday Wednesday, the game didn't appear to be in danger.
