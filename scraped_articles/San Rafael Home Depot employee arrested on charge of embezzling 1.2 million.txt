San Rafael Home Depot employee arrested on charge of embezzling $1.2 million
-----
An employee at a Home Depot in San Rafael was arrested on a charge of embezzling $1.2 million in cash from the store, officials confirmed.The San Rafael Police Department received a call from the store on Tuesday alleging that the employee had embezzled approximately $1.2 million from the store since August 2022, the department said in a Facebook post. Home Depot had come to this conclusion via an internal investigation.
