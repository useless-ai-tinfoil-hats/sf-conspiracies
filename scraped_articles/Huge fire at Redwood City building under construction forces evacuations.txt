Huge fire at Redwood City building under construction forces evacuations
-----
A huge blaze at a structure near Redwood City on Monday morning has prompted an air quality advisory and forced the evacuation of residents up to several blocks away, according to authorities.Redwood City fire crews were battling the eight-alarm fire at the 2700 block of Middlefield Road, which began around 10 a.m., on the fifth floor of the building, according to officials with the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office.
