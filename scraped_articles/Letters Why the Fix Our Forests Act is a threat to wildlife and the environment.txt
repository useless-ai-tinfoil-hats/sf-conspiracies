Letters: Why the Fix Our Forests Act is a threat to wildlife and the environment
-----
Regarding "With Park Fire raging, here's what Congress can do to prevent wildfires" (Letters to the Editor, SFChronicle.com July 30): The letter fails to mention the more dangerous implications of HR8790, the Fix Our Forests Act.The author doesn't mention that the legislation removes Endangered Species Act protections from many wildlife habitats and that the proposed logging to do all the "fixing" would further imperil many species.
