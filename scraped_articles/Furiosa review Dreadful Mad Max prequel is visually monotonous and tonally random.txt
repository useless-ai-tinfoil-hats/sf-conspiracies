‘Furiosa’ review: Dreadful ‘Mad Max’ prequel is visually monotonous and tonally random
-----
"Furiosa: A Mad Max Saga" takes place in a postapocalyptic future where everybody lives in a desert, rides motorcycles and fights over limited natural resources. It would be hell to live in such a world. It's purgatory just watching it for 2½ hours.Directed by George Miller, who has directed all the previous "Mad Max" films going back to 1979, this prequel distills the big artistic misconceptions of the current era into one film.
