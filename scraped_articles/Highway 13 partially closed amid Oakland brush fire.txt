Highway 13 partially closed amid Oakland brush fire
-----
Some of Highway 13 in Oakland is closed as four vegetation fires burn near the Holy Names University campus above the highway, officials said.The fires are located between Redwood Road and Joaquin Miller Road, the Oakland Fire Department said in a social media update around 3:15 p.m.
All lanes of northbound Highway 13 are closed from the westbound Interstate 580 to the Joaquin Miller Road exit, Adib Zeid, a spokesperson for California Highway Patrol's Oakland office, told SFGATE.
