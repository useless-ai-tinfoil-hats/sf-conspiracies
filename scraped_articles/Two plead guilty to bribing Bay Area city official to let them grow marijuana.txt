Two plead guilty to bribing Bay Area city official to let them grow marijuana
-----
Two Bay Area men pleaded guilty Thursday to conspiring to bribe a city building inspector in Vallejo to let them grow marijuana illegally.Steven Chu of San Bruno and Ben Guan of San Francisco were notified in July 2020 that their marijuana operation in a downtown building was illegal because they lacked a permit, which the city had denied to them.
Between August 2020 and May 2021, federal prosecutors said, Chu and Guan made six payments totaling $27,000 to a city building
