Photos: Billionaire Gordon Getty’s Greco-Roman villa for sale in Berkeley Hills
-----
The Temple of Wings, a Greco-Roman villa nestled in the Berkeley Hills, is coming to the market for $5 million.Owned for decades by billionaire Gordon Getty and his late wife, Ann, the historic home was built in the early 1910s and showcases classical Greek and Roman architectural elements. The estate's striking façade features 16 Corinthian columns, bridging the pavilion and gardens.
