Police seek help in mysterious 1996 disappearance of California man
-----
In 1996, a 19-year-old California man headed down Highway 101 from Humboldt County to Southern California, where he said he planned to visit his girlfriend. After a mysterious string of events in and around Redcrest — a small town near the Avenue of the Giants scenic highway — he was never seen again.California authorities are now seeking the public's help to solve the 27-year-old mystery of missing person Scott Paul Hayes.
