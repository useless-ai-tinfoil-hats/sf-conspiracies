A day at the Arizona swap meet 49ers QB Brock Purdy made famous
-----
For even the most diehard San Francisco 49ers fans, Brock Purdy was just a mostly unknown backup rookie quarterback up until Dec. 4, 2022.But at the Mesa Market Place Swap Meet in Mesa, Arizona, where Shawn and Carrie Purdy's Fantastic Spa Outlet company has had a booth for several decades, Purdy has always been a celebrity.
