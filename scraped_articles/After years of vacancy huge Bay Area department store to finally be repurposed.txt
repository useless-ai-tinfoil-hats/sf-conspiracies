After years of vacancy, huge Bay Area department store to finally be repurposed
-----
A Korean grocery store featuring a bakery, a seafood department and a restaurant from a fine dining chef is headed to Daly City.Korean market Jagalchi is expected to open a 75,000-square-foot store at Serramonte Center in the former JCPenney space later this year, as reported by CoStar. JCPenney shuttered its outpost at the center in 2021 as part of a restructuring after it filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in May 2020.
