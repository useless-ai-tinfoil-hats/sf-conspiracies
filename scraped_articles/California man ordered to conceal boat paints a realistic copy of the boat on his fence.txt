California man ordered to conceal boat paints a realistic copy of the boat on his fence
-----
A Monterey County resident ordered by local authorities to conceal a boat parked in his driveway behind a fence has drawn international attention for his innovative solution to the problem.Upon receiving a legal notice from the city, Seaside resident Etienne Constable took an unconventional route: After building the fence, he commissioned his neighbor to paint a lifelike image of the boat on the fence, effectively sidestepping a potential $100 fine.
