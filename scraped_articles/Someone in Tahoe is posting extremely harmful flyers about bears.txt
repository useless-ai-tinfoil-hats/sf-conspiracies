Someone in Tahoe is posting ‘extremely harmful’ flyers about bears
-----
The California Department of Fish and Wildlife warned Tahoe visitors and residents about flyers posted in the Lake Tahoe basin with "extremely harmful misinformation" about bears.The flyers encouraged people to feed bears and allow them to access garbage to help them survive, according to the department. But doing so, it said, is actually detrimental to the animals and is prohibited by law in California.
