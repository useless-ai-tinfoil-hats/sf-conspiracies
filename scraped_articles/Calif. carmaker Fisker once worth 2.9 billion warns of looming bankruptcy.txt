Calif. carmaker Fisker, once worth $2.9 billion, warns of looming bankruptcy
-----
Fisker, a California-based electric vehicle designer that hoped to compete with Tesla, is teetering on the edge of bankruptcy.The company revealed its dire situation in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission on Tuesday. Once valued at $2.9 billion, Fisker's coffers have dwindled to the point where the company failed to make an $8.4 million interest payment in March. Without fresh capital or relief from debt holders, the carmaker expects it will need to file for bankruptcy
