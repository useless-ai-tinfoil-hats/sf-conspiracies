S.F. cult favorite grocery store faces wage theft lawsuit
-----
A popular San Francisco grocery store is facing a proposed class action lawsuit filed by a former employee alleging an array of wage and labor violations.The lawsuit, which Connor Brown filed in San Francisco County Superior Court on Jan. 3, alleges Bi-Rite engaged in a "pattern and practice of wage abuse" by not paying workers overtime and regular wages, requiring them to work off the clock without pay and not providing rest and meal breaks.
