I spent a week in a Japanese ‘America Town.’ Here’s what it taught me about myself
-----
On a recent trip to Japan, I repeatedly found myself face-to-face with Michael Jordan. That's to say, a facsimile of him.Cartoon depictions of Jordan, in full 1990s Chicago Bulls regalia, were pasted onto several buildings in Amerikamura — "America village," the trendy neighborhood in central Osaka where I stayed for a week.
