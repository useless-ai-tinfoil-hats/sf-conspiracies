Bay Area health startup conducts fourth layoff round as COVID needs recede
-----
Carbon Health's business boomed during the pandemic as patients flocked to clinics for coronavirus tests and vaccines. Revenue quintupled and the San Francisco startup was valued at over $3 billion in 2021.But, with COVID receding, the company is conducting its fourth layoff round since 2022, cutting 56 medical jobs across California, according to a state filing. Carbon Health's general counsel cited an "economic restructuring that is necessary for the long-term health of the company."
