The secrets of the San Jose airport, from Bill Murray film shoots to an underground river
-----
From the beginning, San Jose Mineta International Airport embraced quirkiness.The first commercial airline flight to land at the airport was in 1949 on a precursor to Delta Air Lines. Unlike a typical inaugural journey, the flight's passenger list included two pilots, seven passengers and 2,550 baby chickens, which deplaned at San Jose.
