Review: Vince Vaughn embodies Carl Hiaasen’s comic crime novel ‘Bad Monkey’
-----
Carl Hiaasen's Florida crime novels are inventive, complex, observant and hilarious — all qualities of great reads that can be difficult to adapt to other media.A veteran Miami Herald journalist, Hiaasen packs his books with thousands of kooky but convincing details. Because of that, it's hard to choose which will work best onscreen, and even harder to capture the sardonic, yet deep-down righteous tone that gives the outlandish greed and grotesquerie meaning.
