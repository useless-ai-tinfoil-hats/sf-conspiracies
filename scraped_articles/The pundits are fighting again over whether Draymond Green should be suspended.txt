The pundits are fighting again over whether Draymond Green should be suspended
-----
When Draymond Green was suspended during last season's playoffs, the NBA made it clear that him being a "repeat offender" was a factor in the decision to force Green to sit.Given that, it's easy to expect another suspension is coming after Tuesday night's brouhaha with the Minnesota Timberwolves, where Green held Rudy Gobert in a chokehold for about 10 seconds. But while the NBA reportedly reviews the fracas, the pundits have already started the mad-dash to make their opinions known.
