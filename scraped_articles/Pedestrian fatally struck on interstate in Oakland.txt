Pedestrian fatally struck on interstate in Oakland
-----
A pedestrian was struck and killed on Oakland's Interstate 980 on Saturday morning, the California Highway Patrol said.Officers responded to a report of a pedestrian who had been struck near the 12th Street exit around 5:38 a.m., said CHP Officer Adib Zeid.
The victim's body was found on the No. 2 lane of the exit, Zeid said. The pedestrian's identity was not immediately released.
