Fairfield man was holding a BB gun when police shot him near local middle school
-----
Police say a Fairfield man was carrying a BB gun that looked like a semi-automatic weapon when police shot him near a middle school Wednesday afternoon.At 1:16 p.m. Wednesday, Fairfield police said they got reports that a man now identified as Osiris Santos was seen carrying "what appeared to be a firearm" in his hand near Grange Middle School. Fairfield police responded to the scene and Grange Middle School was temporarily put on lock down.
