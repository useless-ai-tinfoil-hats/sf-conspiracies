‘I only think about this tragedy’: Families shattered by Half Moon Bay mass shooting search for help
-----
HALF MOON BAY — One year after a gunman opened fire in Half Moon Bay, killing seven farmworkers, Pedro Romero told an assembly of local and state lawmakers on Tuesday about his brother, who was among the dead, and their shattered family."I came to his country to help my family have a better life," Romero said through a Spanish translator. "My father, my brothers, we didn't have anything."
