Thousands were without power in San Francisco
-----
LATEST Dec. 27, 10:51 a.m. Power has been restored for many San Francisco residents who were affected by a major outage Wednesday morning. As of 11:09 a.m., 565 customers are without power. The outage started at 8:15 a.m., affecting nearly 7,050 customers, Tamar Sarkissian, a spokesperson for the utility company, told SFGATE.PG&E is investigating the cause of the outage and doesn't have an estimate for when all the power will be restored, Sarkissian said.
