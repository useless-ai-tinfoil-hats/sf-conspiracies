Highway 13 temporarily closed by Oakland brush fire
-----
LATEST Nov. 13, 5:25 p.m. The brush fire along Highway 13 is contained and under control as of 5 p.m., the Oakland Fire Department said in a social media update. Around 50 firefighters from OFD helped control the fire, which didn't reach any neighborhoods or cause any injuries, the department said. The cause of the fire is currently under investigation.Around 5:30 p.m., all lanes of the highway reopened.
