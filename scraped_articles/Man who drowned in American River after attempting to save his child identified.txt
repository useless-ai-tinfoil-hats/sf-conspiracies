Man who drowned in American River after attempting to save his child identified
-----
Placer County Sheriff's Office officials identified the man who drowned Sunday morning in the American River near the historic "No Hands Bridge" after trying to save his child.After seeing his child struggle in the water, the father — identified as Zabiullah Haliqyar — went after his son, sheriff's officials said in a post on X Monday afternoon.
