17 Bay Area restaurants, chefs make James Beard 2024 semifinalist list
-----
Top Bay Area restaurants, bars and chefs made the cut in the James Beard 2024 semifinalist list that was released Wednesday.The list, which was broken down into 22 categories, highlighted Outstanding Restaurant, Best New Restaurant and Outstanding Hospitality, among other distinctions. In total, 17 Bay Area restaurants and chefs were called out for excellence, with a total of 39 semifinalists in California.
