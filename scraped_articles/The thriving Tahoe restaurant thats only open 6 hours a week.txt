The thriving Tahoe restaurant that's only open 6 hours a week
-----
Lines in restaurants at towns in north Lake Tahoe are nothing new, but a business that has a constant line from opening to closing — and sells out every day it's open — is still something unusual. But that's de rigueur for Sierra Bakehouse, a small bakery in Truckee that opened in 2018.The secret to its success isn't much of a secret: It's high demand meets scarcity. Put in other words: It's extremely popular, and it's only open six hours a week.
