Salesforce to cut 700 tech jobs in latest round of layoffs
-----
Salesforce is the latest prominent tech company set to implement layoffs this year.According to credible reports from various news outlets, the cloud software management giant aims to trim 700 positions, equivalent to approximately 1% of its global workforce of 70,000.
* Tech layoffs: Here are the job cuts impacting Bay Area workers
This move is the latest in Salesforce's ongoing efforts, as San Francisco's largest private employer, to streamline costs amid investor pressure.
