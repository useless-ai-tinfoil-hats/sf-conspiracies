126th Big Game set for 3:30 p.m. kickoff Nov. 18 with Cal visiting Stanford
-----
Kickoff for the 126th Big Game between Cal and Stanford will take place a little after 3:30 p.m. Nov. 18 at Stanford Stadium, the Pac-12 announced Monday. The Pac-12 Network will carry the telecast of the Bay Area's storied college football matchup.This will be the final Big Game with the two schools as members of the Pac-12. With the imminent demise of the conference, Cal and Stanford will join the ACC next season.
