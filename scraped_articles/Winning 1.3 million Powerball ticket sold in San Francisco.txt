Winning $1.3 million Powerball ticket sold in San Francisco
-----
A Powerball ticket worth more than $1.3 million was sold in San Francisco, according to the California Lottery.The ticket, which was sold at Town & Country Market at 600 Post St., matched the first five numbers in Saturday's Powerball drawing: 47, 54, 57, 60 and 65.
It is worth $1,311,695.
