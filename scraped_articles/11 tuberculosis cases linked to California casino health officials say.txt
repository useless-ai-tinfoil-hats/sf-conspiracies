11 tuberculosis cases linked to California casino, health officials say
-----
Public health officials have confirmed that 11 tuberculosis cases are tied to a casino in California.The cases were reported in individuals who spent time at the California Grand Casino in Pacheco, 22 miles northeast of Oakland, between 2018 and 2023, the Contra Costa Health wrote in an emailed statement to SFGATE on Friday.
