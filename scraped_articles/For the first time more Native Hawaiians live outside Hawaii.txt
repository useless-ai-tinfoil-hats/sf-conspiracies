For the first time, more Native Hawaiians live outside Hawaii
-----
A newly released analysis of the 2020 U.S. census marks a major milestone for Native Hawaiians. For the first time, Native Hawaiians living in the continental U.S. outnumber those living in Hawaii.The total population of Native Hawaiians increased, too. The last U.S. Census, in 2010, counted 527,077. The 2020 census counted 680,442.
"Less than half of the Native Hawaiian alone or in any combination population lived in Hawaii (46.7%) in 2020, down from 55.0% in 2010," the report
