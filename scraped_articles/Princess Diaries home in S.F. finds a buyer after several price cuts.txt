‘Princess Diaries’ home in S.F. finds a buyer after several price cuts
-----
The San Francisco home featured in "The Princess Diaries" has found a buyer after months on the market and several price cuts, its listing agent confirmed Monday.The property, at 2601 Lyon St. on the outskirts of Pacific Heights, was marked as a "pending sale" on listings websites Dec. 29 with an asking price of $6.5 million.
"I am happy to report that we received multiple offers on the property, and that it is now in contract," listing agent Steve Mavromihalis told the Chronicle.
