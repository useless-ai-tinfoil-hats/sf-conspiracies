Alt-rock icon cancels U.S. book tour due to ‘deblitating’ health issue
-----
Thurston Moore has scrapped a planned U.S. tour to promote his memoir, "Sonic Life," due to a "debilitating" health issue.The former frontman for the seminal alternative rock group Sonic Youth was scheduled to appear at City Arts & Lectures in San Francisco on Nov. 4. He did not disclose his ailment but said it was "utterly distressing" to cancel the tour after working on the book for so long.
