Alamo Drafthouse to add two more Bay Area cinemas
-----
The Alamo Drafthouse cinema chain is quickly expanding its presence in the Bay Area just two months after it was acquired by Sony Pictures.The Texas-based chain is poised to take over the former ShowPlace Icon theater spaces in Santa Clara's Westfield Valley Fair mall and in Mountain View's Village at San Antonio Center, the San Francisco Business Times reported Aug. 14.
