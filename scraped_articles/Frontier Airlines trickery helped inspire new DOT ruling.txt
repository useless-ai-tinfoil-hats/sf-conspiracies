Frontier Airlines trickery helped inspire new DOT ruling
-----
The Biden administration and the Department of Transportation announced Wednesday a new policy to help travelers receive a reimbursement for flight cancellations, significant delays and other travel inconveniences caused by an airline.The ruling caps a multi-year effort from the DOT that began investigating airlines after consumers reported they were getting stiffed on reimbursements.
