Portion of major California highway to close for 2 weeks
-----
Tracing beneath the jagged ridges of the eastern Sierra Nevada, a portion of one of California's best highways is fully closing for more than two weeks as Caltrans continues an ongoing effort to install a wildlife crossing under the roadway.Caltrans announced this week that U.S. 395 is closing between Bridgeport and state Route 108, starting Sept. 4. The closure will last 15 days. The impacted stretch is nearly 17 miles between the eastern Sierra hidden gem Bridgeport and Sonora
