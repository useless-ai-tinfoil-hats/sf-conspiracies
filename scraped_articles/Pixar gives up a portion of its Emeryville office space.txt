Pixar gives up a portion of its Emeryville office space
-----
Pixar is looking for a tenant for its 16,800-square-foot office space in Emeryville, which hit the sublease market earlier this year.The Disney animation company entered a lease for 1201 Park Ave., across the street from its 20-acre headquarters, in May. The 12,800-square-foot building underwent a $3.6 million renovation that included wall demolition as well as power, data and lighting improvements. Those upgrades expanded the space by 4,000 square feet.
