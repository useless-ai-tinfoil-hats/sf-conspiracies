Review: ‘American Fiction’ matches drama with biting satire
-----
The hilarious trailer for "American Fiction" might leave you unprepared for the actual movie, which is funny at times but also sober in tone and measured in pace.It seems writer-director Cord Jefferson, in his feature debut, has the confidence to slow down and let his movie breathe, knowing that his audience is with him.
