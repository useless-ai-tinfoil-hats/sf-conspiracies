Costco rolls out membership card scanners to curb unauthorized entry
-----
To curb unauthorized access to its warehouse stores, Costco has introduced membership scanners at various locations in California.Moving forward, customers must scan their membership cards or QR codes to enter the store, rather than showing them to an employee. Non-members must be accompanied by a member, Costco said in a policy update.
The change comes after an increase in non-members using other people's cards following the expansion of Costco's self-checkout options, the company
