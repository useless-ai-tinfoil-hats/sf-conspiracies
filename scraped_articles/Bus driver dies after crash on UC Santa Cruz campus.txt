Bus driver dies after crash on UC Santa Cruz campus
-----
A bus driver has died two weeks after being injured in a single-vehicle crash on the UC Santa Cruz campus, according to announcements by the school and his union.Dan Stevenson, a longtime driver for both the city of Santa Cruz and the city's UC campus, had been hospitalized after the crash of the bus he was driving into a historic campus building on December 12. A UC Santa Cruz spokesman says that Stevenson died due to the injuries from the crash.
