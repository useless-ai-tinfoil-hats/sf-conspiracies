Hoping to curb vaping, California school district uses Juul settlement for sensors
-----
In 2023, nearly 8% (2.1 million) of students reported using e-cigarettes, including 550,000 middle schoolers and 1.56 million high schoolers. In efforts to curb the use of e-cigarettes and vaping devices among students, two high schools in Stockton's Lincoln Unified School District installed vape-detecting sensors earlier this year.In the two weeks since this academic school year started, multiple students have already been caught using e-cigarettes due to the sensors, according to LUSD
