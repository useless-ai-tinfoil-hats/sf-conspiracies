Bay Area man one of four people who died in Arizona hot air balloon crash
-----
A Bay Area man was among the people who died in a hot air balloon crash in Arizona on Sunday.Atahan Kiliccote, 24, of Cupertino is one of four people who died in the crash in the city of Eloy, about 60 miles south of Phoenix, the Eloy Police Department said.
