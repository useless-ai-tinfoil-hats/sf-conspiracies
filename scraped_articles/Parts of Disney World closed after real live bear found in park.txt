Parts of Disney World closed after real live bear found in park
-----
On Monday morning in Disney World's Magic Kingdom, guests were greeted by Pooh Bear, the Country Bears and one real, living and breathing, bear.It's not clear how the bear got in or where it is currently holed up, but Frontierland, Adventureland and Liberty Square were all temporarily closed Monday morning as a precautionary measure to keep crowds away from the wayward critter.
