5 S.F. Symphony members retire as Esa-Pekka Salonen wraps up penultimate season
-----
As its 2023-24 season draws to a close this weekend, the San Francisco Symphony will be saying farewell to five veteran players whose years of service total more than 180.The retirements of the five longtime orchestra members — Nadya Tichman, Steven Dibner, Amy Hiraga, Jill Rachuy Brindel and Peter Wyrick — come as Music Director Esa-Pekka Salonen heads into his final year with the Symphony, having chosen not to renew his contract when it expires at the end of the 2024-25 season.
