Sushi chef known for rare Japanese fish opening Bay Area restaurant
-----
It took two years for Joji Nonaka and Anna Osawa to find the ideal, permanent home for their popular omakase pop-up, Sushi Salon.The space at 4008 Martin Luther King Jr. Way in Oakland is tiny. At 500 square feet, there's barely enough room for an eight-seat sushi bar.
In other words: "It's perfect," Osawa said.
