S.F. Mayor Breed could face another opponent in 2024: her predecessor
-----
A handful of San Francisco residents who are dissatisfied with Mayor London Breed are trying to persuade her predecessor, fellow moderate Mark Farrell, to run against her in the November 2024 election.Farrell, who served a brief stint as mayor five years ago, indicated in a Tuesday statement to the Chronicle that he's considering a mayoral bid next year, a possibility he said he had not contemplated until others encouraged him to do so.
