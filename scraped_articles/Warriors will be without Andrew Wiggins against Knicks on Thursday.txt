Warriors will be without Andrew Wiggins against Knicks on Thursday
-----
NEW YORK — Golden State Warriors wing Andrew Wiggins will miss his second straight game — Thursday against the New York Knicks — for personal reasons.Wiggins was scratched Tuesday from a 123-112 win over the Washington Wizards.
Moses Moody replaced him in the starting lineup.
Speaking pregame Tuesday about Wiggins, coach Steve Kerr didn't identify a timetable for return: "We'll just respect Andrew's wishes to remain private and personal. … And we'll just go from there."
