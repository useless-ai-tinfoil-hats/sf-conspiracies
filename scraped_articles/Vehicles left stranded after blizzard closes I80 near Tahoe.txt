Vehicles left stranded after blizzard closes I-80 near Tahoe
-----
Interstate 80 is closed in both directions from Colfax to the Nevada state line due to blizzard conditions and multiple spinouts, according to Caltrans.Videos posted on social media show a chaotic scene at Donner Summit on Friday night after a brutal winter storm caused multiple car crashes. Many travelers were stranded on the highway with their cars snowed in.
