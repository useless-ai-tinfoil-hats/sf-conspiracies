Tickets for Kamala Harris fundraiser in S.F. this weekend cost as much as $500,000
-----
Kamala Harris will attend a San Francisco fundraiser Sunday where attendance will cost as much as $500,000, according to an invitation obtained by the Chronicle.The trip will be her first to San Francisco since launching her presidential campaign and her first fundraiser since naming Minnesota Gov. Tim Walz as her running mate.
Donors will need to spend at least $3,300 to enter the fundraiser; tickets costing less than $250,000 were sold out as of Thursday morning.
