Why San Francisco's emergency sirens still won't turn back on
-----
San Francisco Board of Supervisors President Aaron Peskin says the board has seen no movement on a long-overdue plan to upgrade the city's emergency sirens, despite Mayor London Breed's pledge six months ago to begin overhauling and implementing a new system.The city's 100 or so sirens have been offline since 2019 due to concerns about their vulnerability to cyberattacks.
