Mayoral candidate Daniel Lurie pledges to bring more tech companies to San Francisco
-----
Daniel Lurie, a candidate in November's San Francisco mayoral election and heir to the Levi Strauss & Co. fortune, is betting that attracting more tech companies is an answer to the city's problems.On Friday during a brief interview on the television show Bloomberg Technology, the moderate Democrat proposed the creation of a "climate innovation hub" in San Francisco. He pledged to make getting permits easier in downtown and "win business back."
