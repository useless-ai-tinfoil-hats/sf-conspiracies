ESPN, the Athletic barely care about Warriors guard Klay Thompson's free agency
-----
Golden State Warriors guard Klay Thompson has been noticeably absent from some mainstream free agency coverage this offseason, as showcased by the fact that he's not listed as one of the league's top free agents in the Athletic or ESPN.For the first time in his career, Thompson is set to test the NBA's free market for his services after he and the Warriors have been unable to agree to a contract extension over a year of negotiations. Yet his availability isn't exactly moving the needle
