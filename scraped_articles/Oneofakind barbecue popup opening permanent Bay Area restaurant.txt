One-of-a-kind barbecue pop-up opening permanent Bay Area restaurant
-----
One of the Bay Area's top barbecue pop-ups is preparing to open its first brick-and-mortar location.Texas-meets-Indonesia grill Fikscue is moving to Alameda, where it will offer trays of ribs, links and juicy grilled chicken along with rice bowls topped with almond wood-smoked brisket. Owners Fik and Reka Saleh aim to open by the end of October at 1708 Park St., the former home of the short-lived but popular Mama Judy's Singapore Hawker restaurant.
