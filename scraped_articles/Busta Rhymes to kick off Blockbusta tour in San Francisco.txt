Busta Rhymes to kick off ‘Blockbusta’ tour in San Francisco
-----
Busta Rhymes plans to embark on a solo headlining tour in celebration of "Blockbusta," his first album since 2020, with his first stop in the Bay Area.The 12-time Grammy-nominated rapper and 2023 BET Icon/Lifetime Achievement Award recipient is scheduled to begin his "Blockbusta" North American tour at the Masonic in San Francisco on March 13, with concert dates in nearly two dozen cities across the U.S.
