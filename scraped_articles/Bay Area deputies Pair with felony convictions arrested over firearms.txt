Bay Area deputies: Pair with felony convictions arrested over firearms
-----
Two men with felony convictions were arrested Sunday on suspicion of possessing firearms, according to the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office.Deputies responded just after midnight to a report of a party at a home, where they said dozens of people were reportedly disturbing neighbors. The sheriff's office did not provide a location or immediately respond to a request for more information.
