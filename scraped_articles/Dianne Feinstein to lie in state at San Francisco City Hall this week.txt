Dianne Feinstein to lie in state at San Francisco City Hall this week
-----
Late California Sen. Dianne Feinstein will lie in state at City Hall in San Francisco on Wednesday. The public is invited to sign a condolence book and pay their respects to the trailblazing lawmaker between 9 a.m. and 7 p.m.A private funeral service for Feinstein, who was the mayor of San Francisco before ascending to the Senate, is scheduled for Thursday.
