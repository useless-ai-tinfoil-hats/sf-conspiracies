Trader Joe’s new S.F. location in Hayes Valley to open this week
-----
After most recently having its launch delayed by a year, the Trader Joe's that's long been planned to activate a new Hayes Valley condo building will now open ahead of schedule.According to a new timeline given last year for the store's public debut inside of roughly 13,000-square-foot commercial space at the 555 Fulton St. condo project — which was anticipated to be in late June — Trader Joe's confirmed Tuesday that it will open its doors at 9 a.m. Friday.
