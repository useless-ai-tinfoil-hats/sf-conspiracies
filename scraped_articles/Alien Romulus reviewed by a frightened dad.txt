'Alien: Romulus,' reviewed by a frightened dad
-----
The last time I went to an "Alien" movie in a theater was for James Cameron's "Aliens" back in 1986. I was 9 years old. My mom brought me because that's what parents did in the '80s. I was wary of going, because I was small and because, not surprisingly, R-rated horror movies scared me to death.
