Fifty-year-old Tahoe City grocery store to close permanently
-----
One of Lake Tahoe's longest-serving grocery stores is set to close permanently in October.The Save Mart at 100 River Road in Tahoe City has been forced to close after the landlord chose not to renew the lease, the company said in a statement.
