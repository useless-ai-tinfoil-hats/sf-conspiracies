Linda Ronstadt helped this Bay Area musician pen his first book. Here’s how he’s celebrating
-----
A Bay Area musician who got help from Linda Ronstadt while writing his first book is celebrating becoming an author by throwing a party in collaboration with San Francisco's storied City Lights bookstore.Eugene Rodriguez, Ronstadt's longtime friend and collaborator, plans to host a free book launch party for "Bird of Four Hundred Voices: A Mexican American Memoir of Music and Belonging," on Friday, Aug. 23, in Jack Kerouac Alley off Columbus Avenue in North Beach.
