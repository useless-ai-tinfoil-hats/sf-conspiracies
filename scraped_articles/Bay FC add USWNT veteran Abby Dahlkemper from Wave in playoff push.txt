Bay FC add USWNT veteran Abby Dahlkemper from Wave in playoff push
-----
Bay FC made their first move of the NWSL transfer and trade period and got their first United States women's national team veteran. On Monday, the expansion club announced it had acquired USWNT veteran defender Abby Dahlkemper in a trade with San Diego, sending $50,000 in allocation money to the Wave.Dahlkemper, who grew up in Menlo Park, has 155 games of NWSL experience under her belt between the San Diego, Houston, and North Carolina organizations.
