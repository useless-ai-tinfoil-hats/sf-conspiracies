Pack of coyotes surround, kill dog on popular San Francisco beach
-----
New signs have popped up at the entrance of one of San Francisco's most popular beaches warning visitors to be aware of coastline-dwelling coyotes after one reportedly killed a dog there last week.Justin Rivard told NBC Bay Area (KNTV-TV) that he and his partner were walking three of their dogs along Baker Beach early Thursday morning when five coyotes suddenly surrounded them and snatched up their 3.5-year-old Pomeranian, Peter.
