Daulton Jefferies’ Giants debut goes awry in 13-4 loss to Padres
-----
SAN DIEGO — For a brief moment Sunday, Daulton Jefferies savored his comeback with the San Francisco Giants.The 28-year-old right-hander stood atop a big-league mound for the first time since May 2022 — when he was with the Oakland A's. Between now and then, Jefferies endured a dark period spent rehabbing from his second Tommy John operation on top of thoracic-outlet surgery.
Those prolonged rehab stints can get lonely, leading a player to question whether he'll ever make it back
