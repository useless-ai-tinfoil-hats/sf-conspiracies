United Airlines flight diverted to San Francisco after mechanical issues detected
-----
A United Airlines flight from Sacramento to Denver was diverted to San Francisco International Airport on Sunday after mechanical issues were suspected on the aircraft.United Flight 2285 was forced to land at SFO at 9:55 a.m., about an hour after takeoff, due to what United representatives said was a "possible mechanical issue." It was unclear what caused the problem or which parts of the aircraft were affected, but a spokesperson said that no emergency was declared.
