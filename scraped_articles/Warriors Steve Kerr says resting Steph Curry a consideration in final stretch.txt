Warriors’ Steve Kerr says resting Steph Curry a consideration in final stretch
-----
DALLAS — The Golden State Warriors — with six straight wins entering Friday — are nearing surefire play-in position, meaning rest might be available for their veteran players.Including guard Stephen Curry.
Speaking pregame Friday before the Warriors played the Dallas Mavericks, head coach Steve Kerr indicated that he's managing his rotation "day by day." That might mean giving Curry a night off should Golden State's playoff positioning crystallize to Kerr's liking.
