Highly unusual feature appears at California ski resort: 30-foot deep crevasse
-----
Strong winds from a recent storm have led to a never-before-seen phenomenon at Sierra-at-Tahoe ski resort: a 30-foot-deep crack in the snow, according to resort officials.In a video posted to the resort's social media, Ski Patrol Director Shannon Maguire said a wind-driven storm earlier in March led to a 30- to 40-foot crack running parallel to the full length of the Middle Castle run.
