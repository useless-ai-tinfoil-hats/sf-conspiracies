Bay Area's iconic 'Flintstone House' almost had a little sister
-----
Growing up in the 1980s and early '90s near the Hillsborough property now known as the Flintstone House, family and friends called the then all-white structure the Dome House or the Barbapapa House. The latter nickname was taken from a French cartoon series from the '70s featuring blobby creatures who lived in similarly curvy homes.
