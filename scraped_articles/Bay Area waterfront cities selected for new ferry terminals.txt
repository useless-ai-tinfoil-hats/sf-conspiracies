Bay Area waterfront cities selected for new ferry terminals
-----
Several new ferry terminals are underway for three Bay Area cities, with hopes to ease commuters off the road and onto the water. The expansion is part of the San Francisco Bay Ferry's 2050 Service Vision, adopted by its Board of Directors on Thursday.The policy outlines how the Water Emergency Transportation Authority expects to expand across the bay for the next 25 years.
