San Francisco Art Week becomes official with branding, online schedule
-----
San Francisco Art Week is official.The third week of January is when the Fog Design + Art fair, hosted at Fort Mason Center for Arts & Culture, has come to colloquially be known as San Francisco Art Week since the fair's founding in 2014. In recent years as Fog has continued to attract international visitors to the city, local arts organizations have taken advantage of the crowds by opening new shows and creating special programming during the week.
