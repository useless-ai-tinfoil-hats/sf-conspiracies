Grateful Dead ties Elvis Presley and Frank Sinatra for most Top 40 albums
-----
Nearly three decades after breaking up, the Grateful Dead has achieved another significant milestone, tying the record for the most Billboard Top 40 albums by any act in musical history.The San Francisco band clinched its 58th entry on the Billboard 200 chart, debuting at No. 33 with "Dave's Picks Vol. 48" on Tuesday, Nov. 7.
