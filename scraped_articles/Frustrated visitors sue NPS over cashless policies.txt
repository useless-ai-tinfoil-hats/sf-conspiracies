Frustrated visitors sue NPS over cashless policies
-----
A couple of East Coasters and a person from Ojai, California sued the National Park Service this month for refusing cash payments.NPS instituted cashless policies at several locations in 2023. The Park Service claimed it was losing money due to costs related to an armored car contract for transporting the legal tender and rangers' time counting money and processing paperwork.
