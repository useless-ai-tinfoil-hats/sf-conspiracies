‘It’s kind of a slap in the face’: Hiero Day 2024 canceled due to $36,000 debt
-----
Hiero Day, the East Bay's annual hip-hop festival, has been canceled this year as organizers continue to struggle to pay bills from last year's free event.Tajai Massey and Khari Bailey, co-organizers of the one-day concert founded by the Oakland hip-hop collective Hieroglyphics in 2012, owe $36,130, plus late fees, to Ungaffable Productions, owner Tony Garcia told the Chronicle.
