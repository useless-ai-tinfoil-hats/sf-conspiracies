Alameda County man killed in weekend shooting in the Tenderloin
-----
An East Bay man was fatally shot in the Tenderloin early Saturday morning, San Francisco police said.Around 4:20 a.m. Saturday, San Francisco police officers responded to a reported shooting on the 200 block of Golden Gate Avenue. They found two male gunshot victims, both of whom were taken to a hospital, police said.
One of the men died at the hospital, officials said. The medical examiner identified him as Jose Valle Rodriguez, a 40-year-old Alameda County resident.
