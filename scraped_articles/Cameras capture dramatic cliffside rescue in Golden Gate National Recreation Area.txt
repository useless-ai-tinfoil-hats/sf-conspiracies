Cameras capture dramatic cliffside rescue in Golden Gate National Recreation Area
-----
Sonoma County Sheriff's officials rescued a person clinging to the face of a cliff Sunday night in the Golden Gate National Recreation Area.The person, who was not identified, had fallen 50-60 feet from a cliff near the Battery Alexander Trailhead.
Rescuers in a helicopter found the person with a thermal imaging camera and placed the person in a rescue device before flying to the top of the cliff, sheriff's officials said.
The person was placed in the care of fire personnel
