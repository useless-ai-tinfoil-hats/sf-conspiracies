San Francisco Silent Film Festival announces move to the Palace of Fine Arts
-----
The San Francisco Silent Film Festival, one of the world's preeminent events dedicated to showcasing and preserving pre-sound era movies, announced it will be moving its 2024 festival to the Palace of Fine Arts after calling the Castro Theatre home since 1996.The move, according to Artistic Director Anita Monga, is hoped to be temporary.
