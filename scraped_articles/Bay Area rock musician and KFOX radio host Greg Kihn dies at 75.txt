Bay Area rock musician and KFOX radio host Greg Kihn dies at 75
-----
Greg Kihn, the Bay Area rock musician behind the 1980s hits "Jeopardy" and "The Breakup Song," died on Tuesday of Alzheimer's, according to a statement shared on his official website. He was 75.Kihn grew up in Baltimore, Maryland, but his career took off when he moved to the Bay Area in the '70s and signed to Berkeley's Beserkley Records.
