Driver fatally strikes pedestrian in parking lot near San Francisco store
-----
A pedestrian died Monday afternoon after being hit by a vehicle in San Francisco's Mission District, police said.Officers responded to a private parking lot on the 1700 block of Folsom Street, near the location of Rainbow Grocery Cooperative, around 2:50 p.m. after the crash was reported. Upon arrival, officers located the pedestrian and provided aid. Despite life saving efforts from medics, the pedestrian was declared dead at the scene, police said.
