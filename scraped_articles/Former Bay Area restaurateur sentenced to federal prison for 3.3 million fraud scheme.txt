Former Bay Area restaurateur sentenced to federal prison for $3.3 million fraud scheme
-----
A former Bay Area restaurateur has been sentenced to 30 months in federal prison after he fraudulently used more than $3 million worth of COVID-19 relief funds at a casino, for mortgage fees and for luxury car payments.David Tai Leung, of Sacramento, was sentenced to 30 months in prison Thursday after pleading guilty in February to three counts of wire fraud.
