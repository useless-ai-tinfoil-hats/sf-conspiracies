Should memorials at S.F.'s Portsmouth Square be removed, replaced?

Portsmouth Square, a single small block in the heart of San Francisco's Chinatown, was once the center of the great city that grew up around it. Now it's the center of a controversy that pits the past against the present.The square is one of the smallest parks in the city — only 1.37 acres — and the oldest.
