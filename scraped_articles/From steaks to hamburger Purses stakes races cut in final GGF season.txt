‘From steaks to hamburger’: Purses, stakes races cut in final GGF season
-----
The final season of thoroughbred racing at Golden Gate Fields begins Tuesday. The East Bay track that opened briefly in 1941 and has operated continuously since 1947 isn't exactly going out with a bang.Purses have been cut 25% from recent seasons and number of stakes races has been slashed from 15 to just two: the $100,000 El Camino Real Derby (Feb. 10) and the Grade 3, $175,000 San Francisco Mile (April 27).
