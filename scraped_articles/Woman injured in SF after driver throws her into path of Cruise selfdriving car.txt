Woman injured in SF after driver throws her into path of Cruise self-driving car
-----
A female pedestrian was severely injured after being struck by an alleged hit-and-run driver and then thrown into the path of a Cruise driverless vehicle that ran over her in downtown San Francisco on Monday night, officials and the autonomous car company said.
