Noise Pop announces SF Live, Summer of Music lineups
-----
Noise Pop is tapping the rock band Eagles of Death Metal, former Talking Heads members and more than 175 local musicians to perform in San Francisco as part of two free concert series aiming to stimulate the city's arts scene and economy this summer.In collaboration with San Francisco Parks Alliance, the Bay Area music and live events promoter has scheduled a handful of free outdoor concerts for the SF Live concert series.
