Warriors' Draymond Green remains sidelined, won't play against Kings
-----
Golden State Warriors forward Draymond Green will not play in Friday's game at the Sacramento Kings.Green missed the entire preseason and Tuesday's season opener against the Phoenix Suns at Chase Center because of a left lateral ankle sprain suffered in an offseason workout. He was cleared to participate in 5-on-5 drills Sunday.
