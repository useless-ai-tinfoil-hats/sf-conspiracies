Bay Lights installation set to return in 2025 with twice as many LEDs
-----
The Bay Bridge is set to sparkle once more.The team responsible for the beloved Bay Lights art installation, which dimmed 14 months ago, announced on Thursday that it secured over $10 million in funding to illuminate a 1.8-mile section of the bridge.
Illuminate, the San Francisco public arts nonprofit behind Bay Lights, has again tapped artist Leo Villareal for a new design and anticipates the installation's return early next year.
