Encampment fire under S.F. Central Freeway prompts safety inspection
-----
A fire erupted at an encampment under San Francisco's Central Freeway on Tuesday afternoon, officials said, prompting an inspection of freeway infrastructure to ensure nothing was substantially damaged.San Francisco police officers detained a man near Vermont and Division streets suspected of starting the fire, but it was not immediately clear if the man was arrested.
A 911 caller reported someone setting fire to tarps at the scene, said Fire Department Capt. Justin Schorr. Fire
