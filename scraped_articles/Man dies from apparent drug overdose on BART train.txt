Man dies from apparent drug overdose on BART train
-----
A man died from an apparent overdose on a train at the Walnut Creek BART Station on Friday, the transit agency said.Officers found an unresponsive man on a train while performing a train patrol check just after 8 p.m., BART spokesperson Alicia Trost said.
The man appeared to be suffering from a drug overdose, so authorities began performing life-saving measures and administered one dose of Narcan, the powerful opioid-reversal medication.
