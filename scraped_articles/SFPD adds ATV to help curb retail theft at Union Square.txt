SFPD adds ATV to help curb retail theft at Union Square
-----
San Francisco police are rolling out a new way to patrol Union Square during the busy holiday shopping season.The department was gifted an all-terrain vehicle, dubbed Polaris, by the Union Square Alliance, a local business marketing group that advocates for economic development at the square. Neither the department nor the alliance disclosed the cost of the vehicle.
