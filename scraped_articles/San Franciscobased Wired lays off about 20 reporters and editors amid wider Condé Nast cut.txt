San Francisco-based Wired lays off about 20 reporters and editors amid wider Condé Nast cut
-----
Wired, the San Francisco-based science and technology magazine, has laid off around 20 employees, according to company staff.Condé Nast, Wired's corporate owner, told employees in early November the media giant would be laying off about 5% of its wider workforce. But last Thursday Wired's reporters, editors and other staffers learned the cuts would be more sweeping, with at least 15% of staff receiving layoff notices that day, per reports from workers.
