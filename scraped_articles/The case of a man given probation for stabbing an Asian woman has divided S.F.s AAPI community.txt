The case of a man given probation for stabbing an Asian woman has divided S.F.’s AAPI community
-----
Both Hudson Liao and Jeremy Lee walked in the 2020 Black Lives Matter protests calling for criminal justice reform and racial justice.But today, the two Asian American activists in San Francisco are often far apart in how they think those convicted of attacking Asian Americans should be dealt with, including the recent high-profile case of a mentally ill man who was given probation after stabbing a 94-year-old woman — a case that has become a flashpoint in the AAPI community.
