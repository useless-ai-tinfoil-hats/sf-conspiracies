San Francisco Ballet fans say goodbye to Yuan Yuan Tan
-----
San Francisco was not ready to say goodbye to Yuan Yuan Tan.In the basement of the War Memorial Opera House, as Tan prepared to take the stage a final time after 29 years as San Francisco Ballet's star dancer, Lydia Reviglio of Walnut Creek trawled among bar patrons in an old fur coat, seeking signatures for her handwritten petition.
