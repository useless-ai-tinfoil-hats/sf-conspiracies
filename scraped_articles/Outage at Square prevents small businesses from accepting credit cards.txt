Outage at Square prevents small businesses from accepting credit cards
-----
A service outage at Square left Bay Area businesses struggling to make sales without access to their payment platform from Thursday afternoon through Friday morning.In a statement posted to its website, Square said it began investigating a service disruption linked to its data center at 11:47 a.m. on Thursday. Just before 7 a.m. Friday, the company said the service disruption had been resolved, though some sellers could still experience delays processing offline payments or updating
