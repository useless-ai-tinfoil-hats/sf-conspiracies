Warriors’ new City Edition uniform pays tribute to S.F.’s cable cars
-----
The Golden State Warriors revealed their City Edition uniform for the 2023-24 season on Thursday. The wavy, rising typeface across the chest is meant to represent the steep hills of San Francisco.The black and gold uniform was also inspired by San Francisco's famous cable cars. According to a news release, the thick streaks on the shorts are parallels for the city's cable car tracks.
