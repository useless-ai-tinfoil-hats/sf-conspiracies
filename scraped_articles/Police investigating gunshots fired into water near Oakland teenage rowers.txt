Police investigating gunshots fired into water near Oakland teenage rowers
-----
West Sacramento police are investigating an incident on April 20, in which gunshots hit the water near teenage rowers from the Oakland Strokes Rowing Club as they raced in a regatta hosted by the River City Rowing Club along the Sacramento River Deep Water Ship Channel.Video footage of the incident, originally obtained by Fox40, shows the rowers passing an embankment when several shots are fired. At least two bullets can be seen striking the water near the boat.
