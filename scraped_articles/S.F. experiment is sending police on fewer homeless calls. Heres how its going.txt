S.F. experiment is sending police on fewer homeless calls. Here’s how it's going
-----
Rachel Felix walked up to a couple lying under an umbrella on Eddy Street in the Tenderloin and crouched down to see if they had accidentally overdosed."Is she breathing?" she asked.
A year ago, a cop would have probably responded to this kind of call. But on Wednesday, it was Felix, a member of the city's newest street outreach team: the Homeless Engagement Assistance Response Team, or HEART.
