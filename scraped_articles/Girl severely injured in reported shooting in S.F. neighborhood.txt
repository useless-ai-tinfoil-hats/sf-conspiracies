Girl severely injured in reported shooting in S.F. neighborhood
-----
A girl was severely injured in a possible shooting in San Francisco's Crocker-Amazon neighborhood Friday evening, according to police.San Francisco police were called to a home on Chicago Way at approximately 5:24 p.m. to investigate a report of a shooting. Upon arrival, police said they "located a juvenile female suffering from injuries." Officers rendered aid and the victim was transported to a local hospital with life-threatening injuries. The police did not confirm that she was shot.
