A new North Beach bar is serving $8 glasses of wine and poetry
-----
North Beach is getting a new Riesling-focused wine bar, bookstore and poetry venue this week, with $8 glasses of organic wine, tinned fish and regular poetry readings.Golden Sardine will open Friday, Jan. 26, at 362 Columbus Ave. in the space formerly occupied by Family Cafe. At its grand opening party on Sunday, poets including the actress Amber Tamblyn will read their work, musicians will play improvisational piano and winemakers will make paella.
