5-year-old dies after being stabbed by twin in Central Coast
-----
A 5-year-old boy died after being stabbed by his twin brother during a fight Wednesday near Santa Cruz, officials said.Deputies from the Santa Cruz County Sheriff's Office arrived on a report of a stabbing at the 200 block of Tucker Road, in an unincorporated area in Scotts Valley near Highway 17. Officers on the scene discovered that a 5-year-old had stabbed his brother with a "small kitchen knife," the sheriff's office said in a Thursday statement.
