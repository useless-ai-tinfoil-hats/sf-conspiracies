Photos: Coyote found lounging on outdoor couch in S.F.
-----
A San Francisco resident woke up to an unusual sight last week: A coyote was sleeping, curled up and basking in the sun, on the resident's outdoor couch.Concerned that the animal might be sick or injured, the person called San Francisco Animal Care & Control, which sent an officer to the scene.
The resident took the officer to the backyard, where the coyote was "comfortably resting," the officer wrote in a social media post.
