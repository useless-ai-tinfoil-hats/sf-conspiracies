Independent S.F. bookstore is closing after a decade in business
-----
Folio Books, an independent bookstore that has operated in San Francisco's Noe Valley for a decade, plans to close at the end of the month.The owners of the bookstore confirmed the closure to the Chronicle on Monday in a statement. They declined to publicly discuss the reasons, calling the decision to close the store "an intensely personal one for all five co-owners."
