S.F. police searching for missing 14-year-old girl last seen in Tenderloin
-----
Police and worried family members are searching for a 14-year-old girl who disappeared from her San Francisco home on Friday.Genesis "Raquel" Barrera-Hernandez was last seen near Turk and Hyde streets in the Tenderloin, according to a San Francisco Police bulletin. She is described as 5-foot-4, 130 pounds with brown eyes and hair.
