State Supreme Court leaves employers on the hook for work-from-home expenses
-----
The state Supreme Court refused Wednesday to shield employers from financial penalties for refusing to compensate employees for expenses when they had to work from home because of COVID-19.The court denied review of an appeal by IBM in a case involving several thousand workers in San Francisco who were sent home in March 2020 after Gov. Gavin Newsom issued his stay-at-home order at the outset of the pandemic.
