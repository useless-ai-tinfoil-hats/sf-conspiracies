Stanford students defy orders to clear pro-Palestinian encampment, decry ‘fear-mongering tactics’
-----
A tent encampment of pro-Palestinian demonstrators remained on the Stanford University campus over the weekend despite orders to leave the night before.Administrators had warned students to clear out of White Plaza, a grassy area outside the student union, by 8 p.m. Friday or face discipline, including arrest.
Deputies with the Santa Clara County Sheriff's Office made their way through the encampment of about 20 tents around 7:30 a.m. Saturday, trying to identify those involved.
