How Stephen Curry learned to play Stephen Curry in new Peacock series
-----
As if the world can't get enough of Stephen Curry, this week the Golden State Warriors' great is not only going for the gold medal with the United States men's national basketball team at the Paris Olympics, he is also making his debut as the star of a scripted series in Peacock's "Mr. Throwback."The six-episode mockumentary begins streaming Thursday, Aug. 8, the same day the U.S. plays Serbia in the Olympic semifinals in Paris.
