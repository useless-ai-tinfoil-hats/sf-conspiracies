Rare bear sighting baffles coastal California community
-----
A bear reportedly caused a surreal scene in the seaside town of Monterey early Sunday morning, leading some community members to feel "on edge," KSBW-TV reported.The bear — seen skulking through the La Mesa neighborhood near Jacks Peak Park — was apparently trailing behind a coyote that kept "howling" and "looking back" at it, according to residents who spoke to the station.
