Warm, flaky egg tarts keep selling out at this new S.F. bakery
-----
Down a narrow downtown San Francisco side street, customers crowd into a tiny, charming space. They're waiting for pasteis de nata at the Bay Area's only bakery dedicated to the iconic Portuguese treat.Holy Nata opened in August at 8 Trinity Place, just off Montgomery Street, and has been selling out nearly every day since.
