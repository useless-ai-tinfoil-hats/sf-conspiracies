Berkeley police arrest suspect in July 3 fatal shooting
-----
Berkeley police arrested a man in connection with a fatal shooting near the Ashby BART Station last month.The shooting occurred on July 3 on the 2900 block of Martin Luther King Jr. Way, where police found a bloody trail that led to a man with gunshot wounds. The man — later identified at Matthew Gullette, 55, of Alameda — was pronounced dead at the scene, according to police.
