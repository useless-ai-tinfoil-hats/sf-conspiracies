Iconic Hawaii bakery to close after more than 60 years
-----
A long-standing Hawaii family business in Wailuku on the island of Maui has made the difficult decision to shut its doors at the end of March. Home Maid Bakery, known for its crispy manju, Chantilly cake and malasadas, is closing due to financial hardships and the death of its patriarch."It is with deep sadness, that after 60 years in business, we have to announce that Home Maid Bakery will permanently close the door at the end of the month," the business announced on its Facebook page.
