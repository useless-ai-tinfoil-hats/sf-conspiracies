Michael Tilson Thomas withdraws from some S.F. Symphony concerts
-----
Michael Tilson Thomas has withdrawn from two of his three scheduled weeks with the San Francisco Symphony in early 2024 to focus on his health.The Symphony's music director laureate will not conduct the concerts for the weeks of Jan. 18-20 or Feb. 23-25. He does, however, plan to conduct the orchestra in Mahler's Fifth Symphony on Jan. 25-27. Those will be his final appearances on the Symphony's subscription series, the orchestra announced Wednesday, Dec. 20.
