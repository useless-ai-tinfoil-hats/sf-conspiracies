California beach closes as 94,000 gallons of sewage spill into water
-----
An estimated 94,000 gallons of sewage spilled into the waters near Laguna Beach on Wednesday, leading city officials to close a stretch of public beach in the community about an hour's drive southeast of Los Angeles.The spill began after a sewer line broke, according to a news release from the Orange County Health Care Agency.
The closure impacts the stretch of beach between Laguna Avenue and Blue Lagoon.
