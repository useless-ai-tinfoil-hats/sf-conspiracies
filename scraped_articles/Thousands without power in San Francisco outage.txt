Thousands without power in San Francisco outage
-----
Thousands of customers are without power in San Francisco's Sunset District on Thursday morning, according to an outage map from PG&E.The outage generally falls in an area stretching between the Great Highway and Stanyan, and Fulton and Santiago. Parts of Golden Gate Heights and Forest Hill are also in the dark, the map shows.
KCBS Radio reports that 9,200 customers are without power in SF. PowerOutage, a website that tracks outages across the country, has shown the outage
