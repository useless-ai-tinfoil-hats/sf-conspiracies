ODC to honor Eleanor Coppola at summer event
-----
ODC plans to honor the memory of documentarian, filmmaker and longtime supporter Eleanor Coppola at its Summer Sampler next month.The four-day collection of performances set for July 18-21 aims to provide audiences the chance to see the San Francisco dance company perform in the intimate setting of its less than 200-seat theater.
A formal dedication to Coppola is scheduled during the program, including a nightly speech in her honor at the beginning of each show.
