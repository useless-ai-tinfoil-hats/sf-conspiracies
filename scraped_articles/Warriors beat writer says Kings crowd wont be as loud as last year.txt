Warriors beat writer says Kings crowd won't be as loud as last year
-----
A Warriors beat reporter believes the Sacramento Kings' home crowd won't match the energy levels it reached last year for this season's postseason matchup against Golden State.This bold prediction came from Monte Poole from NBC Sports Bay Area on the podcast "Dubs Talk" during a preview of Tuesday's play-in tournament game between the podcast's namesake and their Northern California neighbors.
