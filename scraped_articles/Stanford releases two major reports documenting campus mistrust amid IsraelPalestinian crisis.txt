Stanford releases two major reports documenting campus mistrust amid Israel-Palestinian crisis
-----
Two long-awaited reports from Stanford University published this past week attempt, in 150 pages each, to convey the escalating mistrust and emotional anguish felt by two campus groups this year: Jews, and those who identify as Muslim, Arab or Palestinian.Stanford on Thursday released the extraordinary documents, which contain hundreds of hours of testimony from students, employees and alumni about their experiences at the university since last fall.
