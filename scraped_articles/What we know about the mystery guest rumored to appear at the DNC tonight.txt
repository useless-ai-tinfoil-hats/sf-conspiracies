What we know about the 'mystery' guest rumored to appear at the DNC tonight
-----
Social media is abuzz with a rumor about a surprise "mystery" guest on the final night of the Democratic National Convention, which began at 6:30 p.m. Eastern time.The speculation is the result of a rumored block of unspecified time in the Thursday evening schedule. Exactly when that block of time might be, though, is difficult to verify, as the official schedule doesn't include a minute-by-minute breakdown of the evening, nor does it mention an unnamed celebrity appearance.
