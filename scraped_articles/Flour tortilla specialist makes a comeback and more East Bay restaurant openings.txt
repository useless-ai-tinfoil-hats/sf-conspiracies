Flour tortilla specialist makes a comeback, and more East Bay restaurant openings
-----
The East Bay restaurant world in February saw some exciting comebacks and long-awaited spots that finally opened.Northern Mexican taqueria Mama Cuca returned to Oakland, while Fowl + Fare found a new location to make its popular fried chicken sandwiches. A brewery from longtime industry vets and a bookstore and bar both opened after nearly two-year waits. Meanwhile Fremont, the epicenter of a halal restaurant boom, got a new halal burger spot and Walnut Creek got a new pizzeria.
