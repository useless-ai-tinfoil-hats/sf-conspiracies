Review: More style than substance in Tilman Singer’s bizarre horror film ‘Cuckoo’
-----
"Cuckoo" will leave no one in doubt that German writer-director Tilman Singer can make movies. His images create mood and tension. He has wildness and a love of the absurd, and all this is appealing. He is someone who definitely should have a hand in writing his own films.
