Aaron Peskin blasts London Breed as he enters S.F. mayor’s race: ‘No consistency’
-----
Aaron Peskin, the progressive president of the San Francisco Board of Supervisors, says he is entering the city's already-crowded mayor's race partly because of what he sees as a chaotic governing strategy from incumbent London Breed.Peskin, who plans to formally declare his candidacy with the city's elections department on Friday, told the Chronicle in a Thursday morning interview that he would be a mayor who "loves the city and doesn't beat up on it for cynical political gain."
