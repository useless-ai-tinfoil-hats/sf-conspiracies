BART paper tickets are officially no longer usable on Nov. 30
-----
It's the end of an era: BART will officially stop accepting paper tickets on Thursday, Nov. 30.BART, which announced the transition earlier this year, will soon be installing new "state of the art" fare gates that won't be able to accept the old tickets.
