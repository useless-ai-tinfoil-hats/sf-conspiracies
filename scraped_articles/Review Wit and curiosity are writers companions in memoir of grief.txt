Review: Wit and curiosity are writer's companions in memoir of grief
-----
At 5:15 p.m. on June 27, 2019, Sloane Crosley steps out of her New York City apartment for one hour. She returns home to find all of her jewelry stolen, including her grandmother's heirlooms. She sees her first clues: dark boot prints across her white comforter that lead to the window over the bed, the fire escape outside the window, the alley below.
