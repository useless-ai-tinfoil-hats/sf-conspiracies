Katie Ledecky’s historic gold medal in 800 is ‘the one that means the most’
-----
PARIS — Katie Ledecky likes symmetry. She likes numbers. Ever since she was a little girl, she has written them down, memorized them, aimed toward them. She has been fascinated by split times, stroke counts, by the math behind swimming.So the fact that she won her fourth consecutive gold medal in the 800-meter freestyle on Aug. 3 — 12 years to the day from when she won her first one in London — resonated with her deep sense of symmetry and order.
