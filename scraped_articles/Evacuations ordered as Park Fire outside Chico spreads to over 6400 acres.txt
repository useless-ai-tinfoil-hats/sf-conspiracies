Evacuations ordered as Park Fire outside Chico spreads to over 6,400 acres
-----
This article originally appeared on KCRA.com.UPDATE July 24,10:38 p.m. The Park Fire burning near the city of Chico has consumed 6,465 acres, according to the latest update from Cal Fire.
-----
Residents near a fire just outside of Chico are being evacuated, according to the Butte County Sheriff's Office.
The Chico Fire Department said the fire is burning in Upper Bidwell Park. Every fire engine for the city of Chico is being used to help.
