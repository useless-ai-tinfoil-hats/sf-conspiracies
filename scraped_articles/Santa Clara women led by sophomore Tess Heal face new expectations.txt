Santa Clara women, led by sophomore Tess Heal, face new expectations
-----
Tess Heal thinks she needs to be better. A year after winning 12 WCC Freshman of the Week awards — a conference record — she knows there is a target on her.The Santa Clara point guard burst onto the scene last season as one of the top mid-major freshmen guards in the country. The Broncos are expected to improve upon their 15-17 record from last season despite roster losses.
Heal is going to be looked at as a leader for the Broncos, who are ranked third in the WCC preseason poll.
