Ex-KTVU anchor Frank Somerville sentenced to jail time
-----
Ex-news anchor Frank Somerville has had all charges dismissed related to an alleged altercation with family members, but could serve up to 30 days in jail for other violations.Somerville was given a 30-day jail sentence Monday after pleading no contest to reckless driving under the influence and admitting to a probation violation, his attorney Shannan Dugan told SFGATE. But it's not certain that Somerville will actually end up behind bars; Dugan said she has applied for an alternative
