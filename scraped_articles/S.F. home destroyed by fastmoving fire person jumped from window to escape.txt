S.F. home destroyed by fast-moving fire; person jumped from window to escape
-----
A fire early Monday morning in San Francisco's Panhandle neighborhood charred three duplexes and left two people injured, one who leaped from a window, spokespeople for the Fire Department said.At least 100 firefighters battled the flames that ignited at 3:45 a.m., devouring a three-story Edwardian building in the 700 block of Masonic Avenue, then spreading to homes on either side of it.
