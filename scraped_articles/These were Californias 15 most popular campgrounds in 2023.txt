These were California’s 15 most popular campgrounds in 2023
-----
California campers love the coast.That's the clear takeaway from a Chronicle review of the 1.4 million campsite bookings placed at state parks and beaches in 2023.
Of last year's 15 most popular state park campgrounds, 13 are situated at the edge of the continent, most with beachfront views. Their locations cover the full range of settings along California's 840-mile coastline — from the state's dark redwood forests in the north, down to sunny blufftop perches in San Diego.
