Inside former San Francisco Mayor Willie Brown's swanky 90th birthday party at Harris' Restaurant
-----
Former San Francisco Mayor Willie Brown turned 90 on Wednesday, and was celebrated both at City Hall and at Van Ness Avenue steakhouse Harris' Restaurant. Several high-profile movers and shakers in California politics turned up for the events.They included Mayor London Breed, one of Brown's proteges, as well as the highest-ranking state officials in California.
