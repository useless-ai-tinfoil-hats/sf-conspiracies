Review: This ‘Comedy of Errors’ is undermined by actual errors
-----
Harping on line memorization in a review can risk appearing to miss the point. No one goes to the theater to test, schoolmarm-style, how word-perfect a recitation is.But mastery of the text is the foundation on which everything else rests, as Marin Shakespeare Company's "The Comedy of Errors" demonstrates. Fail to make the script your native language, your toy, your weapon, and all those rich, world-building choices by actors, director, playwright and designers start to crumble.
