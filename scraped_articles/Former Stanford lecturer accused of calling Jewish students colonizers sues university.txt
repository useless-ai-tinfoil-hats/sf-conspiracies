Former Stanford lecturer accused of calling Jewish students ‘colonizers’ sues university
-----
A Stanford lecturer who was suspended after delivering a controversial lesson on colonization three days after Hamas invaded Israel in October has sued the university in federal court, alleging wrongful termination. His contract expired in March and was not renewed.Varying accounts of the lessons Ameer Hasan Loggins taught on Oct. 10 — which some Jewish students called deeply offensive and other students did not object to — were picked up around the world, including in the Chronicle.
