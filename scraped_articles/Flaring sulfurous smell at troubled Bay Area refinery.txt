Flaring, sulfurous smell at troubled Bay Area refinery
-----
Contra Costa health officials Friday issued public health alerts related to strong chemical odors and flaring taking place at an oil refinery in Martinez run by PBF Energy, a facility under investigation by federal and state agencies for coating the surrounding community with industrial dust last year.The Martinez Refining Company reported the flaring on its Facebook page at 10:11 a.m. Friday and cautioned that flaring "may be visible and audible offsite due to an operations incident."
