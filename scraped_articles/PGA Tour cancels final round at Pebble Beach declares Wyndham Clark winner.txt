PGA Tour cancels final round at Pebble Beach, declares Wyndham Clark winner
-----
And then the rain came, the wind howled and the final round of the AT&T Pro-Am simply disappeared.PGA Tour officials first postponed the last 18 holes of their annual tournament at Pebble Beach on Sunday, with designs on trying again Monday. Later on Sunday they canceled the final round entirely, shortened the event to 54 holes and declared Wyndham Clark the winner.
