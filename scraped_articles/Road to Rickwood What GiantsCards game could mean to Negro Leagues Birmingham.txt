Road to Rickwood: What Giants-Cards game could mean to Negro Leagues, Birmingham
-----
Bob Kendrick, the president of the Negro Leagues Baseball Museum, never could have imagined uttering the following five words:"Negro Leagues baseball is trending."
Yet, he did so in a Chronicle interview when asked about the anticipated impact of the June 20 Rickwood Field game in Birmingham, Ala., that Major League Baseball is promoting as a tribute to the Negro Leagues.
