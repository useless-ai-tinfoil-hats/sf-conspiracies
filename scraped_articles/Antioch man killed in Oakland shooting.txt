Antioch man killed in Oakland shooting
-----
An Antioch man was shot and killed in Oakland's Eastlake neighborhood Monday afternoon, authorities said.The man was found suffering from gunshot wounds at about 3 p.m. in the 500 block of International Boulevard, according to Oakland police spokesperson Kim Armstead.
Medics pronounced the man dead at the scene. His identity was not immediately released.
No arrests have been announced and no further details were available.
