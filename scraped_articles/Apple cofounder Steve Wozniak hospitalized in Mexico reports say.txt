Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak hospitalized in Mexico, reports say
-----
Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak was reportedly hospitalized abroad while attending the World Business Forum, sources first told TMZ.Wozniak, 73, was attending the conference in Mexico City, where he was scheduled to give a speech, according to the outlet. The conference was held in the Santa Fe neighborhood of the city Nov. 7 and 8. A nearby hospital in the neighborhood was not able to confirm with SFGATE whether Wozniak was there.
