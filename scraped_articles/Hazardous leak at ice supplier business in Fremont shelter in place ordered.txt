Hazardous leak at ice supplier business in Fremont, shelter in place ordered
-----
A hazmat event at a Fremont business has caused a shelter-in-place order Friday.Fremont officials are advising the public to shelter in place until 1 p.m. in the area north of South Grimmer Boulevard, east of Grimmer Boulevard, south of Auto Mall Parkway and west of Fremont Boulevard, the Fremont Police Department said on social media.
