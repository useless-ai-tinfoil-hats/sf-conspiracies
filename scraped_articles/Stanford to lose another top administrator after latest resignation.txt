Stanford to lose another top administrator after latest resignation
-----
Stanford's vice provost for undergraduate education will step down from the role at the end of the fall quarter, according to a university announcement Wednesday.Sarah Church cited a health issue as the reason for her departure. She will become the third top official at Stanford to leave the university since May.
