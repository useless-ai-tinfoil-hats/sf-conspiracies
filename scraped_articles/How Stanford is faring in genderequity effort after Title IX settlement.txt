How Stanford is faring in gender-equity effort after Title IX settlement
-----
Fans packing into Maples Pavilion during the first weekend of the NCAA Tournament might have been redirected around a giant pit in front of the basketball facility and avoiding land taken up by bulldozers. Perhaps they saw the turf of the nearby field-hockey stadium ripped up.The construction is one result of Stanford's gender-equity report, born from a Title IX lawsuit two years ago, that has forced the school to reinvest in its facilities, media and more among its women's sports
