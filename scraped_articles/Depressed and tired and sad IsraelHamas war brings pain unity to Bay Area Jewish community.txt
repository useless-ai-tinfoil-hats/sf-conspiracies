‘Depressed and tired and sad’: Israel-Hamas war brings pain, unity to Bay Area Jewish community
-----
Friday night, Alon Sachar planned a Shabbat dinner at the San Francisco home he shares with his husband and daughter for the first time in a long while. He and their friends have been texting in the wake of the war in Israel and wanted to connect."There's just obviously a deluge of text messages between folks, between friends trying to comfort one another," he said.
