Luxe winery designed like a Moorish castle just sold for nearly $1 billion
-----
The most luxurious winery in Paso Robles is being sold to an Australian wine corporation for almost $1 billion.Treasury Wine Estates has announced it will buy Daou Vineyards for $900 million, describing Daou in a press release as the fastest-growing luxury wine brand in the U.S. The acquisition, which is expected to be completed later this year, includes the Daou brand, Daou's 600-acre mountain estate, four wineries and 400 acres of vineyards in Paso Robles (San Luis Obispo County).
