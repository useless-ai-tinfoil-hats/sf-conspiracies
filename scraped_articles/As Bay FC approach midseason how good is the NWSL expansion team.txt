As Bay FC approach midseason, how good is the NWSL expansion team?
-----
Bay FC's game against Utah on Sunday will be their 13th of the season. At the halfway point of the NWSL's 26-game balanced schedule, they find themselves a surprising three points out of a playoff spot.The first season for the expansion club has had its ups and downs, recovering from a five-game slump to win two of three matches and bring itself back into the postseason race. Not shabby for a new team that initially struggled to find its footing defensively.
