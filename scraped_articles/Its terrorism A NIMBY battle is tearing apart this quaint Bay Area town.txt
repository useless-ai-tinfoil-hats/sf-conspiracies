‘It’s terrorism’: A NIMBY battle is tearing apart this quaint Bay Area town
-----
It takes about two minutes to drive from one end of Port Costa to the other. During that time, you'll likely be impressed with how much concentrated quirkiness this sleepy 251-person town can pack into its small footprint. About 40 minutes from Oakland, this formerly bustling port town on the Carquinez Strait has transformed into a hub for artists, weirdos, queer San Francisco expats and the occasional swarm of leather-clad bikers.
