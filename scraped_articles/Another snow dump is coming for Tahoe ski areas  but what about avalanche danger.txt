Another snow dump is coming for Tahoe ski areas — but what about avalanche danger?
-----
A series of storms is lining up to hammer Tahoe ski areas this weekend — news that is likely to delight skiers but that could also trigger concern about avalanche danger after a deadly inbounds slide last week.These storms, however, will be warmer than the system that dropped several feet of snow last week and elevated avalanche risk across the Sierra Nevada. The avalanche danger has dropped to moderate, but experts still warn skiers to be cautious.
