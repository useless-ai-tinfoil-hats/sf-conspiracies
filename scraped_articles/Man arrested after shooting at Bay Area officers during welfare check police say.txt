Man arrested after shooting at Bay Area officers during welfare check, police say
-----
San Jose police arrested a man after they say he shot at them while they were conducting a welfare check on him, though no one was injured.Officers approached the man near Ross and Leigh avenues after receiving a request for a welfare check at about 5 a.m. Friday, according to the San Jose Police Department. Police said the man was standing outside a home with a firearm when officers arrived and that he shot "at least one round" at them when they tried to negotiate with him.
