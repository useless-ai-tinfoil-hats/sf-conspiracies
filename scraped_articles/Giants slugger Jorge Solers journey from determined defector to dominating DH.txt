Giants slugger Jorge Soler’s journey from determined defector to dominating DH
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — Jorge Soler didn't just grow up outside Havana. The San Francisco Giants' new DH shot way up, and quickly. His early growth spurt is why he's now making millions playing in the major leagues rather than working with horses, which is what he wanted to do.A scout and former Cuban player named Luis Ignacio González spotted a 7-year-old Soler among a group of schoolkids one day, putting his future in motion.
