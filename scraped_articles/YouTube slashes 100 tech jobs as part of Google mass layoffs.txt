YouTube slashes 100 tech jobs as part of Google mass layoffs
-----
UPDATE: Google CEO tells employees to expect more job cuts in 2024On Wednesday afternoon, YouTube cut 100 positions as part of the ongoing layoffs at its parent company, Google.
The impacted employees belonged to YouTube's operations and creator management teams, responsible for supporting content producers on the platform.
