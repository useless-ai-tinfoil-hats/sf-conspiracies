Arizona officials seek Bay Area family of man found in shallow grave nearly 50 years ago
-----
Authorities in Arizona are searching for Bay Area relatives of a man found dead in a shallow grave about 50 years ago in the Mojave Desert.Detectives believe the man, Luis Alonso Paredes, may have family in San Francisco and in Alameda County, according to a Facebook post from the Mohave County Sheriff's Office.
