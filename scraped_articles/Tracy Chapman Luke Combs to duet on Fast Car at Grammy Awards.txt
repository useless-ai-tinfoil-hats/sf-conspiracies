Tracy Chapman, Luke Combs to duet on ‘Fast Car’ at Grammy Awards
-----
Tracy Chapman is set to take the stage alongside country sensation Luke Combs to duet on her classic hit "Fast Car" at this year's Grammy Awards on Sunday, Feb. 4.The performance, confirmed by Rolling Stone, comes after the remarkable success of his rendition of Chapman's 1988 hit, which has dominated the charts over the past year.
