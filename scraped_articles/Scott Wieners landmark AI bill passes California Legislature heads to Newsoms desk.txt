Scott Wiener’s landmark AI bill passes California Legislature, heads to Newsom’s desk
-----
Lawmakers passed state Sen. Scott Wiener's landmark artificial intelligence regulation bill Thursday over fierce opposition from both the tech industry and members of his own party.The bill passed 41-9 in the Assembly on Wednesday, with all votes against from Republicans except for one Democrat, giving the measure the exact number of votes it needed to pass. It then passed 29-9 in the Senate on a concurrence vote Thursday morning.
