Stanford volleyball enters NCAA tourney looking to go ‘full circle’ with title
-----
It has been championship or bust for Stanford volleyball all along, and entering the postseason, that philosophy has been empowered.Stanford (26-3) is the No. 2 overall seed in the NCAA tournament, which begins Friday night. Just Nebraska — one of the three teams to defeat Stanford this season — is seeded higher.
With an experienced starting lineup, and a group still pained by an Elite Eight upset a year ago, this season seemed prime for a title run. Pac-12 Player of the Year
