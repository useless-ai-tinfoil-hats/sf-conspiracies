After fire, top Oakland barbecue restaurant abandons original location
-----
One of the Bay Area's top barbecue restaurants will not reopen at its West Oakland location.Last year, a fire caused extensive damage at Horn Barbecue, forcing the restaurant to shut down temporarily. Owner Matt Horn announced on Instagram Thursday morning that he is leaving his Mandela Parkway location, citing fire damage along with other stressors like vandalism and crime in the area.
