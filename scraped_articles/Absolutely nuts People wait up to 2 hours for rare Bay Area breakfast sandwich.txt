'Absolutely nuts': People wait up to 2 hours for rare Bay Area breakfast sandwich
-----
Hash N Dash, the popular Bay Area pop-up known for its elevated take on a classic McDonald's Egg McMuffin, has found a permanent home.Last Friday, a crowd of breakfast sandwich devotees queued up in a line that stretched down the block to check out Hash N Dash's new digs inside downtown San Jose nightclub Rec Room. Owner Brandon Salmon was behind the grill, whipping up his famous smashburger-style breakfast sandwich, the 408 Smash, for the hundreds of customers that showed up.
