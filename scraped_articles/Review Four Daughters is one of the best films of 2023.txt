Review: ‘Four Daughters’ is one of the best films of 2023
-----
That two of Olfa Hamrouni's four children in the documentary "Four Daughters" are played by actresses is a big tip-off that something has gone very wrong within this Tunisian family. The heartbreaking story behind that rift and the unique way Oscar-nominated filmmaker Kaouther Ben Hania tells it is remarkable and devastating.Interestingly, the film opens Friday, Nov. 10, at the Roxie Theater in San Francisco.
