Stanford’s NCAA tourney drought and another losing season sealed Haase’s fate
-----
Stanford on Thursday night made official what has seemed inevitable for at least the past few weeks: Jerod Haase is out after eight seasons as the head coach of the men's basketball team.After the Cardinal's 79-62 loss to Washington State in the Pac-12 tournament, Stanford issued a news release announcing Haase's departure.
