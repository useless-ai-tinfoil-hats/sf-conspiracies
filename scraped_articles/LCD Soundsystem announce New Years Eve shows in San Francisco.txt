LCD Soundsystem announce New Year's Eve shows in San Francisco
-----
"Where are your friends tonight?"Back in 2007, LCD Soundsystem asked that proverbial question on their breakout album "Sound of Silver." On New Year's Eve 2023, there's a good chance your friends will be at Bill Graham Civic Auditorium, as the band just announced two San Francisco shows, on Dec. 30 and 31. Opening acts include electroclash provocateur Peaches, and 2manydjs, whom frontman James Murphy collaborates with on the Despacio DJ tent at Coachella.
