Restaurant inside historic lighthouse reopens, and more new East Bay restaurant openings
-----
Some exciting restaurant openings and reopenings made for a rewarding March for East Bay diners.Hella Bagels opened its permanent location in Albany, while Brix Factory brewing also welcomed in customers after a nearly two-year wait. Two local haunts, Ruby Room and Quinn's Lighthouse, reopened after months of inactivity under new owners.
