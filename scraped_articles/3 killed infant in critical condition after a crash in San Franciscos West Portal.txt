3 killed, infant in critical condition after a crash in San Francisco's West Portal
-----
LATEST Mar. 17, 2:54 p.m. A third person has died and an infant remains in critical condition after a car crashed into a bus shelter in the West Portal neighborhood of San Francisco on Saturday afternoon, officials said.Two people were killed on the scene, including a child, and one of the three people taken to the hospital "succumbed to their injuries" on Saturday night, according to a statement on Sunday from Supervisor Myrna Melgar, whose district includes West Portal.
