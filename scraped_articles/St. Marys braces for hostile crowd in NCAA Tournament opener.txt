St. Mary’s braces for hostile crowd in NCAA Tournament opener
-----
SPOKANE, Wash. – St. Mary's will not be the people's choice Friday night.The Gaels find themselves in the heart of Gonzaga country for their NCAA Tournament first-round game against Grand Canyon. Some Bulldogs players even suggested their fans come to Spokane Arena wearing purple, to show their support for Grand Canyon.
Or more accurately: To root against St. Mary's.
Gonzaga and St. Mary's have a long and fierce rivalry, fueled by their near-annual meetings in the West Coast
