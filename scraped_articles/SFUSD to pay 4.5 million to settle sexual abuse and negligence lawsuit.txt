SFUSD to pay $4.5 million to settle sexual abuse and negligence lawsuit
-----
The San Francisco Unified School District reached a $4.5 million settlement last month with two former student-athletes who said they were sexually abused by an ex-school employee for several years.The agreement was reached Feb. 1, according to the settlement document obtained by SFGATE. Both SFUSD and Lawrence Young-Yet Chan, a former athletic director of George Washington High School in San Francisco's Richmond District, are named in the complaints filed by the two former students.
