Vallie Brown to leave Grants for the Arts after three years as director
-----
Vallie Brown, whose tenure as director of Grants for the Arts saw a large and controversial shift in the way San Francisco apportioned public money for the arts, will step away from the agency in February to join the San Francisco Human Rights Commission.In a phone interview with the Chronicle, Brown said her decision to move on after three years in the post was prompted by her sense that she had accomplished what she'd set out to do.
