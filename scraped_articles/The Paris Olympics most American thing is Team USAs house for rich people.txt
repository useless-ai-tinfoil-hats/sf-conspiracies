The Paris Olympics' most American thing is Team USA's house for rich people

PARIS — It's an odd sight to see at the Paris Olympics. In the middle of France's capital city sits the Palais Brongniart — a beautiful, 19th-century palace commissioned by Napoleon himself in 1808 to house the Paris stock exchange.
