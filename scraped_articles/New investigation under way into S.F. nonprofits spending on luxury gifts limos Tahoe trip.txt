New investigation under way into S.F. nonprofit’s spending on ‘luxury’ gifts, limos, Tahoe trip
-----
The nonprofit San Francisco Safe is already under scrutiny after a city audit found it improperly spent grant money on a lavish trip to Lake Tahoe, limo services and other "excessive" expenses.Now the organization, which provides crime prevention services to the police department, is facing calls for a hearing at City Hall on the expense scandal and a separate, independent investigation launched by a billionaire tech investor who donated $1.8 million to the group.
