San Francisco PG&E outage knocks out power to thousands and all of Golden Gate Park
-----
UPDATE: Thousands in S.F.'s Sunset District lose power for second day in a rowA Pacific Gas & Electric Co. power outage temporarily left more than 14,000 customers in parts of San Francisco's Sunset District without electricity Thursday morning.
The outage was centered at Golden Gate Park and areas south of it, according to the Chronicle map that tracks Pacific Gas & Electric Co. outages. About 14,226 customers were affected by the outage just before 8:30 a.m., according to PG&E.
