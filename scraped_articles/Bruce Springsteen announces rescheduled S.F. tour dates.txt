Bruce Springsteen announces rescheduled S.F. tour dates
-----
Bruce Springsteen and the E Street Band have announced new dates for a pair of San Francisco concerts postponed last week due to the 74-year-old frontman's recovery from peptic ulcer disease.The band now plans to perform at San Francisco's Chase Center on March 28 and 31, making up shows originally scheduled for Dec. 10 and 12 (previously postponed from Dec. 8), respectively.
