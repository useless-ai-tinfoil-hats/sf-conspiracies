Boichik Bagels, a hit in Berkeley, making its first foray into Marin County
-----
Boichik Bagels, Berkeley-based purveyor of a traditional malty sweet New York-style staple, will open its first Marin County store next month in Larkspur.Owner Emily Winston told the Chronicle on Sunday that the outpost will open in early November on Magnolia Avenue, Larkspur's main strip, in a space formerly occupied by Urban Remedy that is next to Equator Coffees.
