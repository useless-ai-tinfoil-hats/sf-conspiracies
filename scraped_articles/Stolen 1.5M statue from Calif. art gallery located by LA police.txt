Stolen $1.5M statue from Calif. art gallery located by LA police
-----
Los Angeles police found a $1.5 million statue over the weekend that was stolen from a California art gallery, authorities said.The 250-pound bronze Buddha statue was stolen from the Barakat Gallery near West Hollywood around 3 a.m. on Sept. 18, Drake Madison, a spokesman for the Los Angeles Police Department, told SFGATE.
A man forced his way through the driveway gate of the gallery and entered the space through the backyard, Madison said. He then used a dolly to wheel the statue
