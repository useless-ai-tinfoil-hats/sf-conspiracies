Even the gardening world is now ensnared in San Francisco’s culture war over homelessness
-----
During the past few months, San Francisco's sidewalks have become noticeably fertile ground for no small number of upstart gardeners — who have improvised new plots out of halved wine barrels and livestock feeding troughs. Many are helping to turn our concrete jungle into the regular kind of jungle.
