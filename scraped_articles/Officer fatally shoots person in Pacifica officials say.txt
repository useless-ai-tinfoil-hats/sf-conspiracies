Officer fatally shoots person in Pacifica, officials say
-----
An officer fatally shot a person Wednesday in Pacifica, according to the San Mateo County District Attorney's Office.District Attorney Stephen Wagstaffe said his office was investigating the shooting and did not provide more information about the incident.
A spokesperson for the Pacifica Police Department did not immediately respond to a request for information.
