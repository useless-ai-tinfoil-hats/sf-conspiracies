Disney's first adult-geared holiday event shows promise, despite its bumps
-----
On the evening of Nov. 11, thousands upon thousands of people poured into Disney's Hollywood Studios. That alone isn't unusual for a Saturday night at a Walt Disney World park, especially during the holiday season. But this crowd was different. These guests were there for Disney Jollywood Nights, and they glowed. Most of them were wearing formalwear: suits and fedoras, gowns with sequins and fringe, dress shoes, even high heels. Some were in black tie. All of them were buzzing with excitement
