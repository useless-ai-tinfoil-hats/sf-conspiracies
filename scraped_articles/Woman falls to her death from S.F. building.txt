Woman falls to her death from S.F. building
-----
A woman fell to her death from a building on Van Ness Avenue near Russian Hill on Saturday afternoon, according to the San Francisco Police Department.Police said the incident happened at about 4 p.m. at the 2900 block of Van Ness Avenue between Francisco and Chestnut streets. Officers tried to administer CPR to the woman, who died at the scene.
