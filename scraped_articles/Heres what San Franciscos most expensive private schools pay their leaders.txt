Here’s what San Francisco’s most expensive private schools pay their leaders
-----
Many of San Francisco's dozens of private schools pay their heads of schools hefty sums — well into six figures — typically to oversee one school.San Francisco University High School's head of school made $750,000 in total compensation in 2022, for example, to oversee about 450 students. By comparison, the city's public school superintendent Matt Wayne, who oversees more than 100 schools and nearly 50,000 students, has a base salary of $345,000.
