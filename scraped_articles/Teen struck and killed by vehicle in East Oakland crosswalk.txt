Teen struck and killed by vehicle in East Oakland crosswalk
-----
A 17-year-old girl was fatally struck by a vehicle while walking in East Oakland on Monday evening, police said.An adult was also struck and taken to a hospital for treatment, police said.
The driver was headed east on International Boulevard near 72nd Avenue at a high speed when the vehicle struck the teen and adult as they were walking in a marked crosswalk, police said.
