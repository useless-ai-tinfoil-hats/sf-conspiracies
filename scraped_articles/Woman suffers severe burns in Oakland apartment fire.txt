Woman suffers severe burns in Oakland apartment fire
-----
A woman was hospitalized with severe burns in a fire early Saturday at her Oakland apartment.The fire occurred around 4 a.m. in a unit on the second floor of the California Hotel apartments, an affordable housing complex near the border with Emeryville. Staff found the woman in a hallway with her clothes still ablaze, the Oakland Fire Department said. She was taken to a local hospital with severe burns on about 65% of her body.
