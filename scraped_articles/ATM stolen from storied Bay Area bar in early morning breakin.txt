ATM stolen from storied Bay Area bar in early morning break-in
-----
A string of early-morning robberies caused a trail of damages to multiple Oakland restaurants after burglars smashed entrances and took off with various items, including a whole ATM.Storied piano bar The Alley was among the businesses broken into Wednesday. Manager Adele Lee told SFGATE that she received a call from owner Jacqualine Simpkins at around 5 a.m. letting her know of the break-in and asking if she could speak with Oakland police officers already on-site.
