Walgreens drops prices on 1,300 products: ‘Customers are under financial strain’
-----
Walgreens is the latest retail giant to announce price cuts on hundreds of items in response to consumers pushing back against the rising costs of everyday products.The pharmacy chain began its "summer of savings" campaign on Wednesday, declaring reductions on approximately 1,300 items across its stores, ranging from pretzels and gummy vitamins to swim goggles and Squishmallow plush toys.
