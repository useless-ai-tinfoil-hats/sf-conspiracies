California weather: More rain, snow expected before potential fierce weekend storm
-----
An arctic blast is expected to hit the Pacific Northwest this week, with some of the chilly air spilling toward California. With more storm systems on their way to the Golden State, the Sierra Nevada is in line for several feet of snow in the next seven days.Closer to the coast, parts of the Bay Area will face freezing temperatures Monday morning before two weather systems hit Tuesday and Wednesday.
