San Francisco Ballet’s ‘Mere Mortals’ to return this spring
-----
Bay Area ballet buffs haven't seen the last of "Mere Mortals."The production, which had its world premiere at the beginning of San Francisco Ballet's 2024 season, has been renewed for a slew of encore performances.
The Ballet announced Thursday, Feb. 1, that following its initial success, "Mere Mortals" is slated to return with seven encore shows this spring at the War Memorial Opera House. It initially ran from Friday, Jan. 26, through Thursday, Feb. 1.
