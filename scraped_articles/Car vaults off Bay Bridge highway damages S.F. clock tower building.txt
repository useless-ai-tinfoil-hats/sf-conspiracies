Car vaults off Bay Bridge highway, damages S.F. clock tower building
-----
A driver traveling at unsafe speeds vaulted off a Bay Bridge exit Sunday evening and smashed into the clock tower building in San Francisco, damaging the car and structure, the California Highway Patrol said in a news release.The driver was wearing a seatbelt and was taken to the hospital with unknown injuries.
