Stinson Beach home at center of Dianne Feinstein legal battle sells for $9.1 million
-----
The daughter of the late Sen. Dianne Feinstein requested to dismiss a lawsuit over her mother's Stinson Beach home earlier this week after the house sold for more than $9 million.The 3,565-square-foot home in the gated Seadrift community was sold for $9.1 million on Nov. 21, according to Zillow and Compass, which represented the buyer. The home was initially listed in October for just under $8.5 million.
