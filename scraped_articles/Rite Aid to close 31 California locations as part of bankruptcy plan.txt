Rite Aid to close 31 California locations as part of bankruptcy plan
-----
National pharmacy chain Rite Aid revealed this week that it will close 31 stores in California — two of them in the South Bay — as part of a financial restructuring plan prompted by slumping sales and opioid-related lawsuits.In court filings Tuesday, the company said it plans to shut 154 of its roughly 2,000 stores nationwide, with most of the closures coming in California, Pennsylvania and New York.
