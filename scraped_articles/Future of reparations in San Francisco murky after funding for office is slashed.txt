Future of reparations in San Francisco murky after funding for office is slashed
-----
San Francisco Mayor London Breed's midyear budget cuts will slash funding for the city's proposed Office of Reparations, the first of its kind in the United States.In a letter to city department heads last week, Breed said that she'd approved $75 million in cuts, a step she says must be taken to avoid a city budget deficit that could grow to $1 billion in the coming years.
