California's historic '49er Saloon has survived shootouts, heart attacks
-----
Larry Krask was pulling a double when he started feeling funny.The co-owner of the '49er Saloon in Acton was working in the kitchen on a Sunday night in June 2022 when some repetitive twinges started back up again in his chest. A few months prior, he had visited a local walk-in clinic where they told him it was acid reflux, so the chef was back in the restaurant filling orders.
