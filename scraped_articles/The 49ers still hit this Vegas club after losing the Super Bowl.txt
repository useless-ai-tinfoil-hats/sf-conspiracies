The 49ers still hit this Vegas club after losing the Super Bowl
-----
After losing the Super Bowl in overtime, the San Francisco 49ers had a choice: They could cry in their hotel rooms, or they could cry in the club.Both teams plan their Super Bowl parties well in advance because an impromptu postgame bash is not something you want to leave to chance. But inevitably, only one team has something to celebrate, while the other has something to forget. Hours after their heartbreaking loss to the Chiefs, the 49ers decided to go on with their party in Las Vegas.
