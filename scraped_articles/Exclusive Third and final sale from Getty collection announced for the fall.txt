Exclusive: Third and final sale from Getty collection announced for the fall
-----
After two blockbuster sales benefiting San Francisco arts and science organizations, Christie's has announced the final auction from the collection of Ann and Gordon Getty.The live auction, to be held in New York on Oct. 18 and 19 with a concurrent online sale that will end Oct. 20, will focus on lots from the late Ann Getty's childhood home, once a peach and walnut farm known as Wheatland named for the unincorporated Yuba County community where it is located.
