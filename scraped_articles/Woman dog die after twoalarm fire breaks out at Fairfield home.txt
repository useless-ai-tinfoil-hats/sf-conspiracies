Woman, dog die after two-alarm fire breaks out at Fairfield home
-----
A woman and a dog died in an early morning fire at a Fairfield home, the city's fire department said.Around 1:14 a.m. Thursday, Fairfield firefighters received reports that a blaze had broken out at a home in the 200 block of Stephen Street, the fire department said. Residents reported that a woman was trapped in a bedroom located at the back of the house.
