Injuries forcing Warriors to turn to youth corps with greater frequency
-----
NEW YORK – Nearly half of the 265 available minutes Saturday for the Golden State Warriors during their 141-134 overtime loss to the Atlanta Hawks at State Farm Arena were given to players age 23 or younger – reflecting both reliance upon the youthful fleet and the trust coach Steve Kerr is developing in it."They're all playing well this year," Kerr said Saturday pregame. "They're developing well with a lot of hard work, and it's exciting to see them play well."
