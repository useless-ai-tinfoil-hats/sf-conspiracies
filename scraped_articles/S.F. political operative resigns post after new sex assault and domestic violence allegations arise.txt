S.F. political operative resigns post after new sex assault and domestic violence allegations arise
-----
Three years after a tenants rights activist alleged that city commissioner and affordable housing official Jon Jacobo had raped her, three additional women have come forward to accuse the once-rising San Francisco political star of sexual assault or domestic violence, according to a report published Tuesday in the San Francisco Standard.Hours after the report was published, Jacobo resigned from his leadership position at TODCO Group, a San Francisco-based affordable housing nonprofit.
