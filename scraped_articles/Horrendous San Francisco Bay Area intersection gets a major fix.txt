Horrendous San Francisco Bay Area intersection gets a major fix
-----
The collective blood pressure in West Berkeley may have lowered significantly in recent days as a new roundabout just opened at one of the most dreaded intersections in the Bay Area.Caltrans announced last week that the Gilman Street interchange, which has made drivers fear for their lives for decades, opened two sections of its multi-year infrastructure project to the public on May 11.
These include a bike and pedestrian overpass that travels above Interstate 80 near Gilman Street
