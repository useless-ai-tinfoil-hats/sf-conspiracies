Giants’ Kyle Harrison rocked by Padres, surrenders 4 homers in loss

SAN DIEGO — Kyle Harrison doesn't scare, apparently. In his third major-league start, the San Francisco Giants' top prospect engaged in an eventful duel with Juan Soto and didn't back down no matter how much the three-time All-Star tried to intimidate him.Overall, Harrison had a rough start Saturday night at Petco Park and got hammered by the San Diego Padres, coughing up four home runs in a 6-1 loss.
