The North Face in San Francisco's Union Square will close at the end of the month
-----
The outpost of outdoor apparel store The North Face in San Francisco's Union Square will close on March 31, a spokesperson confirmed to SFGATE.The news follows a slew of retail closure announcements in downtown SF, including Macy's and multiple stores at the nearby mall. The North Face has no other stores in San Francisco, and the company declined to provide a rationale for the closure for the 180 Post St. location.
