Big-name cosmetics company to shut down Calif. headquarters, relocate
-----
A well-known California cosmetics company is ditching the West Coast this year. Neutrogena, the popular skin care brand with its ever-present ads featuring celebrities like Jennifer Garner and Kristen Bell, is packing up and moving to New Jersey.Currently based in the Westchester neighborhood near Los Angeles International Airport, Neutrogena has been a big-name player in the home cosmetics game for three quarters of a century.
