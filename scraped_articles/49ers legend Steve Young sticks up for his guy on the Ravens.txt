49ers legend Steve Young sticks up for his guy on the Ravens
-----
Ahead of the Christmas clash between the 49ers and the Ravens, Niner legend Steve Young is taking every opportunity he can to talk about one of "my guys" in the NFL: Baltimore quarterback Lamar Jackson.Young joined a Ravens-focused podcast called "The Vault" to discuss Monday night's matchup and his long-held affinity for Jackson, who is one of the top MVP candidates this season.
Young said he feels a "tremendous kinship" with players who use their mobility to be dual-threat
