Strong California storm system brings significant rain, gusty winds over holiday weekend
-----
April may quickly be approaching, but March is going out like a lion, not a lamb.A strong low-pressure system is anticipated to nudge toward the California coast and stall about 100 miles west of San Francisco on Friday. The proximity of the system will result in a period of gusty wind, heavy rain and possible thunderstorms in the Bay Area during the afternoon and evening.
