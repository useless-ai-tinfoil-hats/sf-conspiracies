Bank of America shuttering nearly two dozen Bay Area branches
-----
Bank of America has shuttered or plans to shutter nearly two dozen Bay Area branches, according to recent filings.Banks are legally required to report branch closures to the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency at least 90 days before their scheduled shuttering, so customers will know if they'll be impacted. (The San Francisco Business Times first reported on the filings).
