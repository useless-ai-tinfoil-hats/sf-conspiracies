UC spent $341K on a security fence for its president’s Berkeley home. It didn’t work
-----
The University of California spent months installing a security fence at the Berkeley home of President Michael Drake earlier this year. But as protection against vandalism and racist graffiti it didn't work very well — belying the idea that you get what you pay for.UC shelled out $341,477 for the fence, the Chronicle has learned.
