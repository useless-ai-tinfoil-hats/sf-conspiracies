Fire at historic San Francisco building prompts major emergency response
-----
LATEST May 2, 1:30 p.m. A fire at 1275 Jackson St., San Francisco, has been completely extinguished, according to Justin Schorr, a rescue captain with the San Francisco Fire Department. While no cause can be declared definitively at this time, Schorr said the fire appears to have been an accident.The San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency announced on social media that the Powell/Hyde Cable Car line had resumed normal service.
