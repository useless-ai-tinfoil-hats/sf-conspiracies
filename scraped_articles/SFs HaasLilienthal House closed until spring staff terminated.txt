SF's Haas-Lilienthal House closed until spring, staff terminated
-----
The historic Haas-Lilienthal House is temporarily closed after nearly the entire staff of SF Heritage, which managed the museum, was laid off this week.Tours are suspended through the end of the year, president and CEO Woody LaBounty confirmed to SFGATE, as the museum "takes a pause" to examine its tough financial situation. Planned private events will still occur.
