Bay Area police officer shown punching a woman during an arrest in viral TikTok video
-----
A Bay Area police officer was filmed punching a woman in a video that has gone viral on TikTok.The video begins by showing a blue sedan in front of a concrete truck stopped in the middle of an intersection. A police officer can be seen pulling a woman out of the sedan and slamming her into the nearby truck before pulling her to the ground.
Rashad Hollis, a spokesperson for the Vallejo Police Department, confirmed to SFGATE that the viral clip shows a VPD officer making an arrest
