Bay FC add international signing Deyna Castellanos to boost offense
-----
Bay FC signed their first player via international slot, and their newest addition might be their highest-profile signing yet.The team announced Friday it would add versatile Manchester City midfielder Deyna Castellanos in exchange for an undisclosed transfer fee. She will join the team in training camp in Santa Barbara pending the receipt of her P-1 visa and International Transfer Certificate, and will be under contract through 2026 with an option for 2027.
