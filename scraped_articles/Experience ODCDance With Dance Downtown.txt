Experience ODC/Dance With 'Dance Downtown'
-----
ODC/Dance returns to the YBCA Blue Shield of California Theater for Dance Downtown, March 27 - 31, 2024. Two bracing programs feature world premieres and major reprises by ODC's Founding Artistic Director Brenda Way, Associate Choreographer Kimi Okada, ODC Fellow KT Nelson and guest choreographer Sonya Delwaide.
