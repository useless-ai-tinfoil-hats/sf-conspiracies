‘Freakish, bizarre accident’: Actor killed in S.F. on way to rehearsal for his stage debut
-----
A pedestrian killed Saturday morning after being hit by a vehicle in San Francisco's Lower Nob Hill neighborhood was a formerly homeless actor set to make his debut with the African-American Shakespeare Company, the theater company said Tuesday.Richard May was on his way to a rehearsal for the company's production of "Death of a Salesman" when he was struck and killed in a "freakish, bizarre accident," Sherri Young, the company's founder and executive director, said in an interview.
