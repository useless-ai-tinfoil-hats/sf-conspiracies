Yosemite tells visitors to leave, will close as extreme weather approaches
-----
Yosemite National Park closed as a major winter storm approached the area, with officials expecting several feet of snow and high winds.The closure began at 12:01 a.m. Friday and will last until at least noon Sunday, according to the National Park Service.
Visitors in the park were advised Thursday afternoon to leave as soon as possible and no later than noon Friday.
