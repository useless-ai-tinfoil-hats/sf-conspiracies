Oakland police investigate shooting that killed man, injured youth
-----
Oakland police are investigating a shooting that killed a man and injured a juvenile.Officers were called to the 900 block of 105th Avenue in East Oakland just after 5 p.m. Friday and found a man suffering from gunshot wounds, the Oakland Police Department said Sunday in a statement.
The victim was pronounced dead at the scene, police said. His identity was withheld until his next of kin could be notified.
