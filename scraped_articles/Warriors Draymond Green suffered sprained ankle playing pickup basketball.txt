Warriors' Draymond Green suffered sprained ankle playing pickup basketball
-----
Draymond Green suffered a left lateral ankle sprain during a recent pickup game at Chase Center, the Golden State Warriors confirmed Monday. He will be reevaluated in two weeks.Green, who is expected to miss four to six weeks, said he went for a layup and accidently landed on Jonathan Kuminga's foot. During Monday's Media Day at Chase Center, Green did not approach the podium with crutches or a walking boot. He did not walk with a noticeable limp, either
