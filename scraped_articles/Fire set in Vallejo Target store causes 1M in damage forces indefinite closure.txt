Fire set in Vallejo Target store causes $1M in damage, forces indefinite closure
-----
A Target in Vallejo closed indefinitely this weekend after someone started a fire in the store's paper towel aisle that caused an estimated $1 million in damage, authorities said.Multiple people called 911 around 3 p.m. Saturday to report the fire in the paper towel aisle in the Target store at 904 Admiral Callaghan Lane, said Kevin Brown, a captain with the Vallejo Fire Department.
