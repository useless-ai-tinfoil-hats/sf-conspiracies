Justin Timberlake announces three California shows and debuts new single
-----
After debuting his first new single in six years, Justin Timberlake announced a national tour on Friday with a show in the San Francisco Bay Area and two in Southern California.Timberlake is scheduled to perform on Monday, May 6, at SAP Center in San Jose.
The "SexyBack" singer's other California dates are May 14 at Pechanga Arena in San Diego and May 17 at the Kia Forum in Inglewood, near Los Angeles.
