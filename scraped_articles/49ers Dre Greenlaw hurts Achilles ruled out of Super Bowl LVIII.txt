49ers’ Dre Greenlaw hurts Achilles, ruled out of Super Bowl LVIII
-----
LAS VEGAS — San Francisco 49ers linebacker Dre Greenlaw was carted to the locker room in the second quarter of Sunday's Super Bowl after injuring his Achilles while running onto the field.One CBS camera caught Greenlaw hopping on the sideline, next to teammate Fred Warner. Greenlaw took one step onto the field, lost his balance and fell awkwardly.
The 49ers announced Greenlaw had an Achilles injury and will not return to the game.
