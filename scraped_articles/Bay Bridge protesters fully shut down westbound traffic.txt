Bay Bridge protesters fully shut down westbound traffic
-----
As world leaders gather in San Francisco for the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit, protesters have blocked all westbound lanes east of Treasure Island on the San Francisco-Oakland Bay Bridge on Thursday morning, the California Highway Patrol said. The protesters are holding banners calling for an Israel-Hamas ceasefire.Traffic is at a standstill, according to KCBS, and Caltrans and CHP are on scene with tow trucks.
