Napa confirms 2 killed in weekend shooting were teenagers
-----
The two people killed in a shooting in Napa on Saturday evening were a 17-year-old and a 19-year-old, police said Monday.Their identities will be released once medical examiners complete autopsies, scheduled for Thursday, Dustin Dodd, a detective with the Napa Police Department, said in a statement.
