2 women found slain in San Lorenzo home, person of interest in custody
-----
A person of interest has been taken into custody in connection with a fatal double shooting at a San Lorenzo home last week, authorities said Friday.The Alameda County Sheriff's Office did not release the person of interest's identity. The shooting occurred on or around May 1 at a home on the 15000 block of Dermody Avenue, according to the sheriff's office, which added that the bodies of the two female victims, both in their 30's, were not discovered until May 4.
