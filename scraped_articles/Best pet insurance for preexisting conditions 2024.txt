Best pet insurance for pre-existing conditions 2024
-----
As a rule, pet insurance excludes pre-existing conditions. If your pet had a health issue before you bought insurance (or developed one during the policy's waiting period), you'll have to pay 100% of the vet bills related to that condition.Some pet insurers stand out in the broader market by covering what they consider "curable" pre-existing conditions.
