The home of S.F.’s iconic Anchor Brewing Co. is on the market. Here’s the price
-----
The property that for 44 years has housed San Francisco's historic Anchor Brewing Co.'s "steam beer" plant in Potrero Hill officially hit the market on Thursday.Japanese brewing giant Sapporo Holdings Ltd., Anchor's current owner, is seeking $40 million for the 2.17-acre property, which includes two buildings at 495 and 501 De Haro streets, totaling 108,728 square feet, sources familiar with the listing have confirmed.
