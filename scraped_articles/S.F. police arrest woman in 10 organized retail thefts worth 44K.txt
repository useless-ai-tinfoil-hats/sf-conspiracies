S.F. police arrest woman in 10 organized retail thefts worth $44K
-----
San Francisco police have arrested a woman they said was involved in 10 organized retail thefts in which groups of people entered businesses and fled with stolen merchandise worth a total of $44,000.Savannah Church, 24, is accused of 10 separate incidents of organized retail theft stretching from April to September in which she and as many as eight other suspects allegedly entered stores, took merchandise and then fled without paying.
