S.F. police investigate youth brawl, reportedly at former Westfield mall
-----
Police responded to a call about a youth brawl Saturday evening in downtown San Francisco, reportedly at the former Westfield mall.The call, at about 5:45 p.m., said one of the juveniles may have had a knife, according to officer Sgt. Kathryn Winters, a police spokesperson.
Police met with security officers and a juvenile who had been involved in the incident. The other youths fled before their arrival.
