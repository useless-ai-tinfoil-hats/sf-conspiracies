S.F. police arrest Mission District homicide suspect
-----
A Richmond man was arrested on suspicion of fatally shooting another man Friday afternoon on a busy commercial strip in San Francisco's Mission District, police said.Alexander Martinez, 30, of Richmond was charged with homicide and booked into San Francisco County Jail. Police have not disclosed whether Martinez knew the victim or what his motive may have been.
