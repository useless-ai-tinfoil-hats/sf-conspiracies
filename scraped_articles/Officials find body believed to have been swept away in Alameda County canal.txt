Officials find body believed to have been swept away in Alameda County canal
-----
LATEST June 10, noon Officials found a body believed to be a missing man who was swept away in an Alameda County canal last week.In a news release, the Alameda County Sheriff's Office said the body matched the description and clothing of Lester Ashcraft, 56, who fell into the Delta-Mendota Canal near Grant Line Road on May 31 while fishing with family and friends.
