East Bay prison guard sentenced to 6 years behind bars in sexual abuse of inmates
-----
A former correctional officer at a women's federal prison in Dublin was sentenced Wednesday to six years in prison for sexually abusing inmates, according to the Northern District of California's U.S. Attorney's Office.Multiple employees at the low security East Bay prison — the Federal Correctional Institute Dublin — have also been charged with sexual abuse and the prison's leadership has been investigated by the FBI.
