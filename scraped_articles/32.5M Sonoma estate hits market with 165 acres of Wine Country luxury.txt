$32.5M Sonoma estate hits market with 165 acres of Wine Country luxury
-----
Commanding 156 acres of Wine Country luxury, 7596 Sonoma Mountain Road has come to market with an equally commanding price tag of $32.5 million. The property offers a plethora of impressive amenities, including a custom-made mansion, a 42-acre vineyard and a state park for a neighbor.The estate rests behind a private gate and fence that encompass the entire property. The main home itself is a modern mansion of 14,033 square feet, with four en-suite bedrooms plus two half-bathrooms.
