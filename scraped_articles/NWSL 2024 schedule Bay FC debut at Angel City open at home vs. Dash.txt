NWSL 2024 schedule: Bay FC debut at Angel City, open at home vs. Dash
-----
Bay FC will open their inaugural NWSL season on March 16 on the road against one of the league's most recent expansion clubs, Angel City.Just under two months before the start of the NWSL season, the league announced its schedule on Thursday. Bay FC are set to begin their schedule with a 7 p.m. kick time at BMO Stadium in Los Angeles.
Bay FC then head to Washington D.C. to face the Spirit on March 23 before their home opener against the Houston Dash at 7 p.m. March 30.
