Car slams into home in S.F.'s Sunset District
-----
A car crashed into a home in the Sunset District of San Francisco on Sunday, police said.Officers responded to the 1300 block of 17th Avenue on a report of a collision involving two vehicles — one of which struck a building — just after 4 p.m.
A photo from Citizen, a neighborhood crime app, shows the car struck a home at the corner of Judah and 17th Avenue.
No injuries were reported during the crash.
Officers are still investigating the incident.
