Bay FC acquire ‘instant impact’ forward Penelope Hocking from Chicago
-----
A day after a win over the Portland Thorns pushed Bay FC back into NWSL playoff position, the team delivered some more encouraging news on Saturday, announcing the acquisition of forward Penelope Hocking from the Chicago Red Stars.In a transaction made prior to the transfer window closing on Friday, according to the news release, Bay FC agreed to send $350,000 in intraleague transfer funds to Chicago — $250,000 this year and $100,000 next year — for the 24-year-old.
