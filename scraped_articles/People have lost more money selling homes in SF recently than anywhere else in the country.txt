People have lost more money selling homes in SF recently than anywhere else in the country
-----
A higher percentage of people in San Francisco lost money selling their homes than in anywhere else in the country over the past few months, according to an analysis published Wednesday by real estate company Redfin.Between December 2023 and February 2024, about 17.8% of homes that changed ownership in SF sold at a loss, which is more than four times the 4.3% of homes that sold for less than their purchase prices nationally.
