49ers like their front five, but could draft competition for Colton McKivitz
-----
After quarterback Brock Purdy absorbed 11 hits in Super Bowl XLVIII, many from an unblocked defender on crucial snaps, San Francisco 49ers head coach Kyle Shanahan defended his much-criticized offensive line.Shanahan noted the 49ers' front five wasn't physically overwhelmed, said the Chiefs created confusion with blitzes and suggested Purdy could have done a better job of getting the ball out quicker in the 25-22 overtime loss to Kansas City.
