Lodi Offers Wine Tasting Like it Used to Be
-----
Have you ever wondered what happened to all of the grapes during Prohibition? Did their removal by government agents lead to the end of Old World varietals? Not in Lodi, California. Farmers here maintained hidden vineyards or produced wine for local churches. Importantly, these operations sustained heritage varieties like Zinfandel, for which the region became famous.
