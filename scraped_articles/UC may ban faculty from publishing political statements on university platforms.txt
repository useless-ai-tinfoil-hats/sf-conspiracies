UC may ban faculty from publishing political statements on university platforms
-----
University of California officials are proposing a policy that could ban faculty members from posting "personal viewpoints" on university platforms. And though the text of the policy is not yet official, it's already generating controversy across the system."Under the First Amendment and principles of academic freedom, faculty members, individually and collectively, have the right to express their views," says a draft of the policy obtained by SFGATE.
