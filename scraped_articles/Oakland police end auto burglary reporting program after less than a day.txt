Oakland police end auto burglary reporting program after less than a day
-----
An Oakland police pilot program meant to make it easier for people to report auto burglaries in progress ended abruptly after less than a day.The Police Department created social media pages on Instagram and X, formerly Twitter, that would allow people to directly message the agency with details and photos or video of auto burglaries in progress as an alternative method to report the crimes.
