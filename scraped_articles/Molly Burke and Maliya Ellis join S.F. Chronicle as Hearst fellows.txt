Molly Burke and Maliya Ellis join S.F. Chronicle as Hearst fellows
-----
This week, the San Francisco Chronicle welcomed reporters Molly Burke and Maliya Ellis to the newsroom as part of the Hearst Journalism Fellowship.The 25-year-old multimedia training program places early-career journalists in top Hearst newspaper newsrooms for two yearlong rotations. During the program, fellows report a variety of stories, participate in projects and receive mentorship from newsroom colleagues.
