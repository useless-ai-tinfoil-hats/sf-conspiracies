Bridges, parrots and a giant eyeball: Vote for S.F.’s next ‘I Voted’ sticker
-----
It remains to be seen how much voter participation we'll see in San Francisco for the next presidential and mayoral elections in November 2024.But turnout for the election of the city's next "I Voted" sticker is already trending through the roof.
The San Francisco Department of Elections decided to let citizens design a new sticker, borrowing an idea that has become popular across the nation. (Lake County in Illinois and the New Hampshire are both hosting contests.)
