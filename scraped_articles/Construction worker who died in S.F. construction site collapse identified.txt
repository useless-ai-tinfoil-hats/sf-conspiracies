Construction worker who died in S.F. construction site collapse identified
-----
The construction worker who died after a construction site collapse last week in San Francisco has been identified as 25-year-old Javier Romero of Alameda County.The man was employed by D'Arcy and Harty Construction, which did not respond to multiple requests for comment.
Romero became trapped under approximately 8 feet of dirt, sand and concrete after a trench collapsed at a construction site in the Lower Haight, at Oak and Divisadero streets.
