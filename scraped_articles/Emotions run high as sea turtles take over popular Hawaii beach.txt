Emotions run high as sea turtles take over popular Hawaii beach
-----
Hawaiian green sea turtles are coming ashore on a popular tourist spot on the island of Kaua, triggering an effort to protect them from intruders.A team of volunteers set up safety cones and signage to keep back the semi-circle of onlookers sipping cocktails and snapping photos from 10 feet away.
