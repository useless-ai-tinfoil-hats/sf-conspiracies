All lodging on Grand Canyon's South Rim closes ahead of busy holiday weekend
-----
Multiple breaks in a water line prompted officials to temporarily close lodging on the Grand Canyon National Park's South Rim, beginning Thursday, according to a news release.The temporary shutdown of five hotels — El Tovar, Bright Angel Lodge, Maswik Lodge, Phantom Ranch and Yavapai Lodge — comes just days before the busy three-day Labor Day weekend.
