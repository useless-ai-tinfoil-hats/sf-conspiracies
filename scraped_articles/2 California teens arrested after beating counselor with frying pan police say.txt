2 California teens arrested after beating counselor with frying pan, police say
-----
Two teenage girls were arrested after beating their counselor at a group home in Salinas with a frying pan and fire extinguisher on Sunday night, the police said.The teenage girls — ages 14 and 17 — fled the home before police arrived and were later located and taken into custody, the Salinas Police Department said in a news release posted on social media Monday. The girls were booked into a juvenile facility and could face attempted homicide charges, the department said.
