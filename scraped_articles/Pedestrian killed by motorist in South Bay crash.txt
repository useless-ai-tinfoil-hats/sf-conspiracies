Pedestrian killed by motorist in South Bay crash
-----
A pedestrian walking on the 2000 block of Fruitdale Avenue in San Jose was struck and killed by a motorist Friday morning, San Jose police said.Police indicated traffic would be impacted "for several hours" on Fruitdale as officials conducted an investigation.
Police offered no other immediate details about the deadly collision, other than the fact that the pedestrian was a man.
