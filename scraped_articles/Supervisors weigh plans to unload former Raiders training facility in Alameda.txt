Supervisors weigh plans to unload former Raiders training facility in Alameda
-----
The Alameda County Board of Supervisors is negotiating with two groups, including the owner of the Oakland Roots and Soul soccer teams, to sell its share of the former Raiders training facility in Alameda.The 17-acre site served as the headquarters and practice facility for the former Oakland football team and includes two buildings with offices, conference and media rooms, and fitness spaces.
