Tahoe blizzard: Wind gusts up to 190 mph on peaks, I-80 closed indefinitely
-----
UPDATE: Tahoe blizzard: I-80 remains closed with 'treacherous' conditionsA blizzard blasting the Tahoe region brought wind gusts of up to an astounding 190 miles per hour on ski resort peaks.
Interstate 80 closed through the mountains late Friday afternoon.
By 5 p.m. Friday, I-80 westbound traffic was being held indefinitely at the Nevada state line, and eastbound at Drum Forebay, with Caltrans citing spinouts, high winds, and poor visibility. Further south, I-395 was closed
