San Francisco Chinese New Year Parade draws thousands to welcome the Year of the Dragon
-----
Thousands flocked to San Francisco's Chinatown on a balmy February afternoon to celebrate Chinese New Year and welcome the Year of the Dragon.Saturday's festivities began with a festival of live music, crafts, food vendors and merchants that stretched down a closed Grant Avenue. The event ended with a parade around Chinatown and a fireworks display, as one of the largest celebrations of Asian culture outside of Asia has for many years.
