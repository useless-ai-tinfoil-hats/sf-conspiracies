2 teens in 2 weeks reportedly die from riding on top of BART trains
-----
Two males were found dead near BART train tracks in the last two weeks, according to police logs, and the San Francisco Standard reported on Tuesday that the deceased are both teenagers who had been riding on the tops of moving trains before falling to their deaths.One male was found dead near the Balboa Park BART station on Jan. 29, and the other near the Daly City BART station on Feb. 11, BART Police records showed. The agency wrote in an email that the deaths are under investigation
