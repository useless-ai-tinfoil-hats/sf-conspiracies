Bay Area tech layoffs: Intel slashes 235 jobs in fifth round of cuts
-----
Intel has initiated its fifth round of job cuts this year, marking the latest wave of layoffs in the Bay Area tech industry.The semiconductor giant, headquartered in Santa Clara, said it plans to lay off 235 employees at its research and development facility in Folsom (Sacramento County), as indicated by regulatory filings to the state. The layoffs are scheduled to begin on Dec. 31 and take place over two weeks.
