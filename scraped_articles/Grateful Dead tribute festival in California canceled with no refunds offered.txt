Grateful Dead tribute festival in California canceled with no refunds offered
-----
Organizers of an annual Grateful Dead tribute festival in California have canceled this year's event just days before it was set to begin and told ticket holders they should not expect a refund.The sixth annual Skull & Roses concert was set for April 19-21 in Ventura, with headliners Dark Star Orchestra, former Jerry Garcia collaborator Melvin Seals, Golden Gate Wingmen and dozens of other bands associated with the Dead.
