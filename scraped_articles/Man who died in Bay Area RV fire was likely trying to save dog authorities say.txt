Man who died in Bay Area RV fire was likely trying to save dog, authorities say
-----
A man died while apparently trying to save his dog from a fire that engulfed a recreational vehicle in Vallejo over the Memorial Day weekend, authorities said.Firefighters responded about 1:14 p.m. Sunday to reports of smoke and flames coming from an RV parked near the Solano County Children's Services building at Carolina and Tuolumne streets, the Vallejo Fire Department said. The blaze posed "an imminent threat to adjacent structures," officials said.
