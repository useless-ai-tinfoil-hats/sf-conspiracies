Suspected Oakland sideshow participant hospitalized after crash
-----
An alleged sideshow participant was hospitalized Sunday after their motorcycle collided with a vehicle near Oakland's Adams Point neighborhood, police officials said.The crash happened just before 6:15 p.m. at Grand Avenue and Perkins Street, according to the Oakland Police Department.
Emergency responders took the motorcyclist to a hospital, where the victim was listed in stable condition. The driver of the vehicle stayed at the scene of the crash, officials said.
