LA sushi hero Sugarfish makes its long-awaited Orange County debut this week
-----
California is known for great sushi, thanks to its proximity to the Pacific and easy access to direct purveyors in Japan. Outstanding sushi restaurants of all sizes can be found up and down the coast, but perhaps no brand is better known than Los Angeles-based Sugarfish, which has amassed a cult following over the past 15 years. The austere, upscale-casual restaurants are known for their simple preparations, super-fresh fish and no-fuss style of ordering. Each location is consistently packed
