Review: ‘Ultraman: Rising’ is a rousing reinvention of a classic character
-----
Since his first appearance in 1966, Ultraman has been one of Japan's most enduring pop culture fixtures. In live action and animation, on TV shows and feature films, the supersize red-and-silver alien superhero has defended the world from skyscraping space monsters and other colossal creatures.Now creators are hoping to expand the franchise's reach stateside with "Ultraman: Rising," an ambitious animated feature that reinvents the character for a new generation.
