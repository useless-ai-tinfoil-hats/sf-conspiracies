Tech pioneer opens new downtown S.F. office once occupied by Macy’s: ‘Always long on the Bay’
-----
Yahoo, the once-dominant internet search company that has shrunk after a series of different owners, is opening a new, smaller downtown San Francisco office after its longtime lease expired last year.The company signed a sublease for the 14th floor of 680 Folsom St. from work management tech firm Asana, which itself subleased the space from Macy's, which once had its tech division in the South of Market building.
