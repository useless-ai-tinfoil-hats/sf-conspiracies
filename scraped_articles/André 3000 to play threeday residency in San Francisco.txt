André 3000 to play three-day residency in San Francisco
-----
Flute and hip-hop fans alike can bask in the music of André 3000 next month as the artist gears up for several shows in San Francisco.Part of his "New Blue Sun Live" tour, announced Tuesday, Jan. 23, André 3000 is set to perform two shows at Bimbo's 365 Club on Feb. 20 and Feb. 22, and two at the Independent on Feb. 24.
Ticket sales begin at 10 a.m. on Wednesday, Jan. 24.
The 29-show tour kicks off Monday, Jan. 29, in Brooklyn and wraps up on March 9 in Los Angeles. André
