Scammers again targeting owners of lost pets in S.F., city shelter says
-----
Scammers are once again targeting people in San Francisco who have lost their pets, city animal shelter officials said this week.In a ruse similar to one last year, scammers are pretending to be shelter staff and calling owners of lost pets to ask for money — usually through an app — in exchange for the animal's return, the San Francisco Animal Care and Control agency said Tuesday. The agency is the city's only open-door shelter accepting all animals.
