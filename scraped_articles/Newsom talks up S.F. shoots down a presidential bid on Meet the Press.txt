Newsom talks up S.F., shoots down a presidential bid on ‘Meet the Press’
-----
California Gov. Gavin Newsom, in an interview Sunday on NBC's "Meet the Press," called San Francisco "a remarkably resilient city" and emphasized the work he's doing to reform two of the state's most contentious issues: homelessness and insurance.It was Chuck Todd's final interview as the program's host.
