Is Gen Z less supportive of Israel than older generations? We don’t know. Recent polls can’t be trusted
-----
Protests on college campuses against American ties to and support for Israel may have died down, but given the rising power of 18-to-24-year-old voters, who in 2020 accounted for nearly 1 in 10 ballots, accurately understanding the beliefs of young voters is vital. With the deluge of sensational headlines about the protests, it's easy to assume that the protesters' views are reflective of the majority of young adults, and at least one poll has fueled that belief.
