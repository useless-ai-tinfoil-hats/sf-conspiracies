Burning Man removes From the River to the Sea sculpture from website amid controversy
-----
In most years, the announcement of the Burning Man art installations isn't particularly controversial. But this year, the inclusion of one work caused a stir: an 8-by-14-foot sculpture of a watermelon slice, titled From the River to the Sea.Watermelons, which share the colors of the Palestinian flag, have become a symbol for the Palestinian protest movement, with protesters using watermelon emojis to game social media algorithms.
