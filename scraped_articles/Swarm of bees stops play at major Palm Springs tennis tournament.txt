Swarm of bees stops play at major Palm Springs tennis tournament
-----
On a picture-perfect afternoon at Palm Springs' premier sporting event, the BNP Paribas Open at Indian Wells, play was suspended because of a bee invasion.A swarm of bees descended on Stadium 1 just minutes after the quarterfinal match between Carlos Alcaraz and Alexander Zverev began. The match was tied at one game apiece, with Alcaraz leading 15-0 while on serve in the third game of the first set, when the bees halted play.
