Hundreds of Bay Area PG&E customers still without power amid high winds
-----
LATEST Oct. 29, 4:16 p.m. PG&E crews were still working to restore power to hundreds of customers without power on Sunday afternoon.J.D. Guidi, a spokesperson for the utility company, said outages in the East Bay were down to 930 impacted customers, most of whom were in Oakland, and that power is expected to be restored by 6 p.m. In the North Bay, 420 customers were still without power, with no time estimate for restoration.
