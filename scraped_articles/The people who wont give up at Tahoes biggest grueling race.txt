The people who won't give up at Tahoe's biggest, grueling race
-----
There is no warmup at the Broken Arrow Skyrace, a grueling trail and ultramarathon race at Lake Tahoe this weekend. The starting line is at the base area of Palisades Tahoe ski resort, and from there, the route goes up, an immediate vertical spike gaining thousands of feet in elevation.
