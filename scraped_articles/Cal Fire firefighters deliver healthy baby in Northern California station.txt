Cal Fire firefighters deliver healthy baby in Northern California station
-----
A Cal Fire station in Hollister (San Benito County) turned into a delivery room Thursday morning when a couple racing to the hospital realized they wouldn't make it time for the birth of their child.The couple, from Los Banos (Merced County), whose names have not been released, pulled into the Cal Fire Pacheco Station at about 6:30 a.m. when the mother was experiencing contractions.
Firefighters rushed into action, and delivered the baby at 7:04 a.m. without complications,
