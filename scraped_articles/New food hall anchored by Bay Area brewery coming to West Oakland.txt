New food hall anchored by Bay Area brewery coming to West Oakland
-----
A new food hall called the Prescott Market is slated to open in West Oakland later this year. Right next door to Raimondi Park, the home of new independent baseball team the Oakland Ballers, it plans to feature eight food and drink options, including a taproom from Alameda's Almanac Beer Co.Prescott Market is to be housed in a 1923 building at 1620-1640 18th St. that was used for manufacturing in the mid-20th century.
