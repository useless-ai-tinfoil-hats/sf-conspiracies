Disney Imagineer's custom-built Ojai estate hits the market for the first time
-----
A unique compound on a stunning 10-acre parcel in Ojai, California, has hit the market for the very first time. Built for Disney artist Frank Armitage and his then-partner Jill Penkhus in the early 1970s, 1807 Ladera Road comes with a $2.995 million price tag.Armitage and Penkhus had great influence on the home's design, according to co-listing agent Adam McKaig of Douglas Elliman Real Estate.
