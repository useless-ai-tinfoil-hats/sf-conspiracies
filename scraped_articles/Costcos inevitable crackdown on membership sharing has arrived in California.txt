Costco's inevitable crackdown on membership sharing has arrived in California
-----
The day, it seems, has finally come. For months now, Costco, America's beloved big-box retailer, has been quietly promising big shakeups in how it handles its customers at individual store locations. Now, California is starting to get a real sense of what that looks like.Over the past two weeks, multiple Costco locations across Southern California have implemented new membership scanners at their entrances.
