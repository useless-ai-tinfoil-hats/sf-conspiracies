Missing 13-year-old girl found safe, Berkeley police say
-----
A 13-year-old girl who went missing in Berkeley last weekend has been safely located, authorities said.The Berkeley Police Department said Monday that Amayah Brumfield was found after she was last seen around 12:30 a.m. the day before on the 700 block of Harrison Street in West Berkeley.
