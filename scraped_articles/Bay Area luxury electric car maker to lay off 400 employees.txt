Bay Area luxury electric car maker to lay off 400 employees
-----
Lucid Motors, a Menlo Park startup specializing in luxury electric cars, has unveiled a restructuring plan that involves slashing 400 positions.The company disclosed in regulatory filings that the layoffs will affect about 6% of its workforce by the end of the third quarter.
