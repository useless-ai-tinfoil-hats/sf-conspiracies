Review: Comedy-thriller ‘Blink Twice’ is a sterling directing debut for Zoe Kravitz
-----
For some movies, it's possible for directors to lean on the talent of their colleagues — the cinematographer, editors, designers, the composer — to create a plausible product, something that people can look at and think, "OK, that's decent enough." But with a film like "Blink Twice," there could be no such safety net.The directing debut of Zoe Kravitz, the film walks a line between genres. It's a suspense movie, a comedy, a thriller and a satire, with some of the flavor of a horror
