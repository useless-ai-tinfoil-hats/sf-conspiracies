Northern California wildfires: Scorching heat around Sites, Point blazes
-----
Firefighters battling the Sites and Point fires in Northern California prepared for high temperatures Saturday as they worked to control the spread, according to Cal Fire.The Sites Fire in Colusa County had burned more than 19,200 acres and was 38% contained Saturday night, the agency said, up from 25% Friday. Temperatures surpassed 100 degrees in the area Saturday for the second straight day.
"Firefighters are aggressively mopping up the area and continue to improve control lines.
