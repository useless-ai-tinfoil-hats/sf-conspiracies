Pittsburg BART station closes due to shooting, 1 injured
-----
One person was injured in a shooting at BART's Pittsburg Center Station on Friday afternoon, the public transportation agency said.The shooting was reported at 3:50 p.m., BART spokesperson Chris Filippi said. The condition of the individual who was shot is unknown at this time, he said.
