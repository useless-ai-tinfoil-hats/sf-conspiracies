Police activity blocks traffic on southbound 280 in San Francisco
-----
Traffic came to a standstill on southbound 280 near Mariposa Street in San Francisco on Wednesday night due to police activity, KCBS Radio reported.Interstate 280 southbound between Brannan Street/6th Street, King Street/5th Street and Cesar Chavez Street were all being impacted, the San Francisco Department of Emergency Management said at 5:10 p.m. on social media.
It's unclear how many lanes are being impacted and it's unknown when the situation will be resolved.
