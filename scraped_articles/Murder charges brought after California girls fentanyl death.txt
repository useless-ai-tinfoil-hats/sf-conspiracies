Murder charges brought after California girl's fentanyl death
-----
In a first for Lake County, the overdose death of a teenage girl in November has resulted in a murder charge against the man who allegedly provided the drugs, the county sheriff's office announced.On Nov. 10, 2023, sheriff's deputies were contacted by the coroner's office in Lakeport regarding the death of 17-year-old Native American teen Illeanna Frease. Frease had died of an overdose; toxicology reports would later conclude a combination of alcohol and fentanyl led to her death.
