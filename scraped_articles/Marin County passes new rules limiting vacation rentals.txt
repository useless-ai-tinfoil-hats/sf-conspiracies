Marin County passes new rules limiting vacation rentals
-----
Marin County supervisors Thursday voted to cap the number of short-term rentals in some of the North Coast's prime vacation areas from Stinson Beach to Tomales Bay, a long-debated move aimed at revitalizing local communities amid a housing crunch.The unanimous decision limits the number of short-term rental licenses to 1,200 in the unincorporated parts of the county, including specific caps for communities in coastal West Marin where vacation rentals have proliferated.
