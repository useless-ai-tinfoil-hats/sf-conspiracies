Shooting at East Bay car service center leaves 2 dead
-----
Two people involved in a shooting on Thursday morning at the Toyota Service Center in Albany have died, according to law enforcement.The incident occurred shortly before noon, when a man allegedly shot a woman inside the business on the Eastshore Highway. Berkeley police cleared everyone else from the building but were met with gunfire as they entered, an official said during a news briefing on Thursday.
