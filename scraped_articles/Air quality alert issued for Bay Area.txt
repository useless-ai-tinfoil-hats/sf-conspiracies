Air quality alert issued for Bay Area
-----
Warmer weather is coming to the Bay Area this week, prompting air district officials to issue a Spare The Air alert for Tuesday and Wednesday.Rising temperatures and light winds, combined with vehicle exhaust, will result in a layer of smog settling over the region, the Bay Area Air Quality Management District said in an alert sent Monday.
