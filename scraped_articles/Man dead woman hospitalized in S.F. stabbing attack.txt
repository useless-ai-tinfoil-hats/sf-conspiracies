Man dead, woman hospitalized in S.F. stabbing attack
-----
A stabbing attack in San Francisco's South of Market neighborhood left one man dead and a woman hospitalized early Thursday morning, according to police.San Francisco police said the attack happened around 5:40 a.m. near the intersection of Mission and Washburn streets, between Ninth and 10th streets. Two victims — a 45-year-old man and a 38-year-old woman — were stabbed, according to police. The weapon was not identified.
