Air quality hits ‘unhealthy’ red zone across S.F. Bay Area
-----
Many Bay Area residents woke up to smoky skies and poor air quality levels Wednesday morning due to smoke from wildfires in Northern California and Oregon.The unhealthy air quality prompted the Bay Area Air Quality Management District to issue a Spare the Air alert for Wednesday and Thursday. District officials urged people to avoid exposure to the bad air by staying indoors.
