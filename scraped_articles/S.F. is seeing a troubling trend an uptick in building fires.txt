S.F. is seeing a troubling trend: an uptick in building fires
-----
San Francisco officials are sounding the alarm about troubling fire safety trends, as residential and commercial building blazes reached a 10-year high last year and outside conflagrations have risen over the past five years.San Francisco Fire Marshal Ken Cofflin told city supervisors Thursday that firefighters in 2022 responded to more fires in high-rise and low-rise buildings than any time since 2013.
