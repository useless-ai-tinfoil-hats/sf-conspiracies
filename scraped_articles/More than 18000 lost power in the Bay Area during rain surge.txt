More than 18,000 lost power in the Bay Area during rain surge
-----
The outage began in San Jose starting around 1:00 p.m. in an area ranging from the San Jose State University campus downtown to the south San Jose neighborhood of Willow Glen, the Mercury News reported. San Jose Clean Energy, a city government office, confirmed the outages in a social media update.PG&E's map shows the outage was resolved as of 5:30 p.m. Currently, only 24 customers are without power, according to the map.
The heaviest downpours surged through the Bay Area Monday
