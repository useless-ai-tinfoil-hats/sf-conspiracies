California congressman says his office was vandalized with anti-Israel messages
-----
Republican Rep. David Valadao said his Hanford (Kings County) district office was vandalized by "anti-Israel protesters," he posted Monday.Valadao shared a photo of the outside of his office in a post on X, formerly known as Twitter. The photo showed a red liquid splattered across the front doors and facade of the building and flyers that read "MURDERED BY ISRAEL" with photos of children and what appears to be information about each child.
