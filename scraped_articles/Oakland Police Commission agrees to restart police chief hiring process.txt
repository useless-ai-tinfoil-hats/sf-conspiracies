Oakland Police Commission agrees to restart police chief hiring process
-----
The Oakland Police Commission announced Thursday it will restart the application process for the position of the city's police chief and send another list of finalists to the mayor in March.Chair Marsha Peterson made the announcement after the commission met behind closed doors and hours after Mayor Sheng Thao's office said she had asked commissioners to reopen the application process.
