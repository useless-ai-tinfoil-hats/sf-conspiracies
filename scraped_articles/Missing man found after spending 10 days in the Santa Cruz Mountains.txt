Missing man found after spending 10 days in the Santa Cruz Mountains
-----
Lukas McClish was declared missing on Father's Day.By that time, he had already spent five days in the Santa Cruz Mountains. On June 11, McClish had decided to go on a three-hour hike in his hometown of Boulder Creek. He was last seen that morning near Big Basin Redwoods State Park.
On Friday, McClish was found after witnesses reported hearing cries for help near Foreman Creek, off Big Basin Highway, with the help of drones from the Santa Cruz County Sheriff's Office.
