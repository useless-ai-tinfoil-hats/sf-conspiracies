New COVID boosters work against highly mutated ‘Pirola’ variant, vaccine makers say
-----
Moderna and Pfizer have reported positive findings regarding the efficacy of their updated COVID-19 vaccines against the highly mutated omicron BA.2.86 coronavirus subvariant, which has raised concerns about a potential resurgence of infections.Moderna stated on Wednesday that its vaccine generated an 8.7-fold increase in neutralizing antibodies in humans against BA.2.86 in clinical trials.
