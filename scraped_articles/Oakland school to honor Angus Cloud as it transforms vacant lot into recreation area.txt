Oakland school to honor Angus Cloud as it transforms vacant lot into recreation area
-----
A plot of land that has sat empty for 18 years in Oakland's Uptown neighborhood could be transformed — at least temporarily — into a school recreation area including a skate plaza dedicated to the late actor Angus Cloud, a beloved Oakland native, officials say.Cloud, the "Euphoria" star who died at 25 of an accidental overdose in July, was an alumnus of the Oakland School for the Arts, which is leasing the city-owned property at 1911 Telegraph Ave. and plans to build OSA Uptown Art Park
