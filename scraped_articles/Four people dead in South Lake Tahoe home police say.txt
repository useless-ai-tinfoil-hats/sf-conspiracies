Four people dead in South Lake Tahoe home, police say
-----
Four people died Monday at a home in the California resort town of South Lake Tahoe, according to police.The names of the three men and one woman — all between the ages of 30 and 40 — who died have not been released, the South Lake Tahoe Police Department said in a news release. "At this point, substance abuse is suspected but not confirmed," police said.
Police and first responders rushed to the home home on the 500 block of Roger Avenue after receiving a 911 call at 9:11 a.m.
