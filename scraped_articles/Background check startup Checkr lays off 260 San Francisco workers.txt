Background check startup Checkr lays off 260 San Francisco workers
-----
Checkr, a San Francisco-based tech company that runs background checks for other companies, is laying off 382 workers.The startup filed a WARN notice with California on Tuesday, as is required under the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act in the event of mass layoffs. Checkr is letting go 260 workers from its San Francisco office, the notice said, including dozens of engineers and eight director-level employees. The workers' jobs will officially end in June, per the WARN
