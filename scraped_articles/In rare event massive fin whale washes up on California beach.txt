In rare event, massive fin whale washes up on California beach
-----
A massive 52-foot-long fin whale, an endangered species, was found dead Sunday at Pacific Beach on the north end of San Diego.While it's not unusual for whale carcasses to wash on California's beaches, it's atypical with this species.
