Police in riot gear stand off with UC Santa Cruz protesters
-----
Police in riot gear surrounded pro-Palestinian protesters at UC Santa Cruz early Friday morning, according to video footage and news reports.Officers on megaphones told the protesters — who this week blocked the main entrances to the campus, forcing the university to move classes online — to "disperse immediately" while the protesters linked arms and continued chanting in support of Palestine.
