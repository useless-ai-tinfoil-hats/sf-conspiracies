Multiple attendees of California music festival hospitalized with valley fever
-----
The California Department of Public Health is warning those who attended Lightning in a Bottle, an outdoor music festival held in Kern County May 22-27, that five attendees developed valley fever. Three of them were hospitalized with the fungal infection, officials said.The festival's attendees are advised to contact a health care provider if they are experiencing respiratory symptoms like cough, fever, fatigue, difficulty breathing and chest pain, CDPH said in a news release Monday.
