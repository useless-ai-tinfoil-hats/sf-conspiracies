Burning Man's bathrooms have a secret
-----
In 2016, I came out of a portable toilet at Burning Man and stepped not onto dust, but a red carpet. The group of well-dressed individuals who had quietly placed it there seconds before were standing in two neat rows on either side, giving me a standing ovation.It was one of the funniest things I've ever experienced on the playa. It was also my first indication that at Burning Man, even portable toilets — and what happens in and around them — can become art.
