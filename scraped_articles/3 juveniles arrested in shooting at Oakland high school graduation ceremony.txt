3 juveniles arrested in shooting at Oakland high school graduation ceremony
-----
An additional three suspects were arrested in connection with a shooting at an Oakland high school graduation ceremony last week, the East Bay Regional Park District Police Department said Tuesday.Officers from the department listening to Oakland police scanners at the time of the graduation were among the first on the scene when gunfire broke out in a parking lot at Skyline High School on the evening of May 23, the agency said in a Facebook post.
