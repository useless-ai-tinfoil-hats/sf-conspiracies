San Francisco Pride organizers ramp up security following warning of ‘terrorist threats’
-----
San Francisco Pride officials are stepping up security measures ahead of next month's celebrations, prompted by an advisory from federal authorities suggesting that terrorist groups could target LGBTQ-related events and venues."Security is of utmost importance to San Francisco Pride," Pride spokesperson Carole Barrow said in a statement to the Chronicle on Tuesday, May 14.
