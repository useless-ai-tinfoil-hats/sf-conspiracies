Missing girl, sex trafficking victim rescued from trunk of car in San Jose
-----
A 16-year-old girl who was reported missing was found in the trunk of a car and later told investigators she was the victim of sex trafficking, San Jose police announced on Monday.Detectives and patrol officers discovered the girl, an East Palo Alto resident, in the trunk of a car parked outside a San Jose home in late March, San Jose Police Department spokesperson Stacie Shih said in a statement. The girl had been reported missing in late February.
