Jonathan Mogbo's 30-18 double-double lifts USF to OT win in WCC opener
-----
Jonathan Mogbo, the only player in the West Coast Conference averaging a double-double, scored a season-high 30 points and collected a season-best 18 rebounds as USF withstood a stern test from Pacific to win its conference opener 92-88 in overtime in Stockton on Thursday night.The 6-foot-8 forward entered the game with the nation's sixth-best field goal percentage (66.7) and improved on it by making 10 of 13 shots. He also made all 10 of his free-throw attempts.
