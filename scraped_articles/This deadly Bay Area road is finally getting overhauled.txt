This deadly Bay Area road is finally getting overhauled
-----
A multi-year project to revamp a problematic Bay Area road was underway when tragedy struck a young family crossing an intersection last summer.King Road stretches 6 miles through eastern San Jose and nearly runs parallel with Highway 101. The busy thoroughfare, connecting neighborhoods with commercial districts, is concentrated with "low-income families and a lot who do not speak English," Wilson Tam, the city's transportation planning manager, told SFGATE.
