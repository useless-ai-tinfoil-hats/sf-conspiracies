Abducted 4-month-old baby found in Sacramento
-----
A 4-month-old baby who was abducted from his home in San Joaquin County was found later the same day in Sacramento, according to officials and news reports.Officials said the child was taken from his home in Thornton around 4 p.m. Saturday.
Authorities initially suspected the ex-boyfriend of the child's mother, who is not the baby's father, may have taken him, KXTV reported, but shortly after the baby was reported missing, officials found him in Sacramento without the child.
