Starbucks closes another downtown S.F. store, company says
-----
Starbucks is closing another of its downtown San Francisco locations after shutting seven a month ago, the company confirmed Wednesday.The Starbucks at 74 New Montgomery St. has closed permanently, a company spokesperson said, and all employees were offered jobs at other locations. The decision was part of the company's "standard course of business" to "ensure a healthy store portfolio," a spokesperson said.
