This is the worst week of year for car break-ins in one of S.F.’s famous tourist spots
-----
The scene unfolded in seconds, as Keith Paulson watched — anger rising — from a car parked near San Francisco's Alamo Square Park.He saw a dark BMW roll up Steiner Street, a block north of the famed Painted Lady Victorians. A passenger jumped out and began casing the sidewalk, cupping his gloved hands to peer into car windows. After glancing into three cars the thief found his target: a backpack tossed enticingly on a seat. In one motion, he broke a window and grabbed it.
