California skydiving instructor and student die after hitting 'dust devils'
-----
A skydiving instructor and her student have died after "dust devils" impeded a tandem dive in Southern California. Devrey LaRiccia Chase, 28, and an unnamed student, who was harnessed to her during the tandem jump, deployed the parachute during their dive at Skydive Perris in Riverside County on Aug. 2 when a pair of small whirlwinds appeared."While preparing for landing, the tandem pair hit disturbed air known as a dust devil.
