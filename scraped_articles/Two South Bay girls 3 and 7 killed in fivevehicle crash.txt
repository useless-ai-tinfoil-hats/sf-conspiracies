Two South Bay girls, 3 and 7, killed in five-vehicle crash
-----
Two South Bay children died Friday morning after the car they were riding in was hit during a five-vehicle collision, according to California Highway Patrol.The crash happened on Highway 152 in Gilroy, CHP Officer Alfredo Uribe said in a statement. Just after 7 a.m., a 25-year-old man driving west in a Tesla crossed into the eastbound lane to try to overtake the Peterbilt truck driving in front of him, according to Uribe. The Tesla driver then hit a Honda driving east with three people
