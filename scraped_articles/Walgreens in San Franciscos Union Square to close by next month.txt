Walgreens in San Francisco's Union Square to close by next month
-----
The Walgreens at the corner of Geary and Taylor streets in San Francisco's Union Square is closing next month, according to a sign posted near the store's entrance.The pharmacy within the store will close by 3 p.m. on Nov. 15, the sign reads.
The closure comes amid a slew of other pharmacy closures across the state: Rite Aid announced earlier this month that it is closing 154 stores, including 31 in California, following the company's recent Chapter 11 bankruptcy filing.
