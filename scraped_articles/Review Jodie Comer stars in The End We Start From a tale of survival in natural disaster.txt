Review: Jodie Comer stars in 'The End We Start From,' a tale of survival in natural disaster
-----
"The End We Start From" is a different kind of apocalypse movie. It's apocalypse light, and it feels truer than the grand-scale Hollywood disaster movies in which everything is destroyed and civilization is back at square one. This movie instead envisions a climate change catastrophe that, with a little luck, could be survivable.It begins in London, with Jodie Comer as a married woman who is extremely pregnant with her first child.
