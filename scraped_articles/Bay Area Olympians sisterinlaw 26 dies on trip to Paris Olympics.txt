Bay Area Olympian's sister-in-law, 26, dies on trip to Paris Olympics
-----
Bay Area water polo star Maggie Steffens and her family are grieving in Paris after the sudden death of her sister-in-law. Lulu Conner, 26, died Tuesday after traveling to Paris to cheer on Steffens at the Olympics. The news was first reported by the San Francisco Chronicle."She was so excited for the Olympic Games," Steffens told The Associated Press. "We're really close. She's the light of the world. She just brings so much joy to everyone. She always brings people together."
