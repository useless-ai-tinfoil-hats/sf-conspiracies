‘Very unpredictable’: Bay Area housing market still facing uncertainty
-----
San Francisco median home sale prices fell again in September, though the city was buoyed by more new listings coming to market than in any of the previous 12 months, according to a recent report from real estate firm Compass.The total number of new listings still remains extremely low by long-term historical standards, according to Compass analyst Patrick Carlisle, and when coupled with rising interest rates, that has continued to instill uncertainty in the market.
