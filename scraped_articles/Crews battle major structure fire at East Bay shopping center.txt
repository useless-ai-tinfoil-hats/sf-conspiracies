Crews battle major structure fire at East Bay shopping center
-----
Firefighters battled and quelled a major structure fire at a Livermore shopping center Sunday evening.The Livermore-Pleasanton Fire Department was "actively working" the blaze, at the intersection of Holmes Street and Concannon Boulevard, as of 6 p.m, the Livermore Police Department said on Facebook.
Traffic was rerouted for several blocks as firefighters worked to control the flames, the department said. Streets surrounding the area were reopened around 9:30 p.m.
