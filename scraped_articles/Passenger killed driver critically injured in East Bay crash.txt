Passenger killed, driver critically injured in East Bay crash
-----
A passenger was killed and a driver critically injured early Sunday after a vehicle struck a tree in Hayward's 10th fatal crash this year, police said.Officers responded to a report of a single-vehicle crash shortly after 2 a.m. near the intersection of Winton and Magna avenues, the Hayward Police Department said in a statement. They found a vehicle that had struck a tree after failing to make a turn from southbound D Street to westbound Winton Avenue.
