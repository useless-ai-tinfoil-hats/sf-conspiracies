Viral Bay Area Zillow listing of sad office-to-housing conversion sold
-----
The Bay Area real estate listing that went viral on TikTok for what appeared to be a very sad office-to-housing conversion last summer has sold.The "live/work" opportunity in San Rafael was listed for $520,000 in May 2023, reduced in price in late July to $480,000 and ultimately sold for $440,000 in November. At just over 1,000 square feet, the unit has a washer and dryer, a bathroom converted to include a shower, and a new kitchen.
