Burning Man: Festival's fiery main event delayed for third time due to rain and mud
-----
Burning Man organizers have rescheduled the main event of the festival — the burning of a large effigy known as "the Man" — for a third time as rain and deep mud kept the entire event and its 73,000 attendees stuck until at least Monday and authorities continued to investigate one death.The burning ceremony, which initially was planned for Saturday then was delayed to Sunday, is now scheduled for Monday night, organizers posted on X, formerly Twitter, on Sunday evening.
