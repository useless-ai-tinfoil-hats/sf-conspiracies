E-40 to perform at Alameda County Fair
-----
E-40 is returning to the Alameda County Fair this summer.The Vallejo rapper is scheduled to perform on June 20, hittings the fair's stage again after a hyphy performance last year. A local icon, E-40 received the key to the city of Vallejo from officials in October, had a stretch of Magazine Street where he grew up named "E-40 Way" in his honor and is a staple at Bay Area sporting events.
