Sleeping pods in S.F. building spark city probe into potential code violations
-----
San Francisco officials are opening an investigation into a pod housing complex at Mint Plaza that recently went viral on social media for its unusual living conditions.The sleeping pods — which San Francisco techies say they are renting for $700 a month — appear to have been installed without permits, according to a Department of City Planning complaint filed Tuesday.
