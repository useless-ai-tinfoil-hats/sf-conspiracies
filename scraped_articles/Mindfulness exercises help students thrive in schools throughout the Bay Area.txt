Mindfulness exercises help students thrive in schools throughout the Bay Area
-----
Credit: Mindful Life ProjectStudents at Marsh Elementary School in Antioch participate in a mindfulness exercise. The Mindful Life Project sends coaches to classrooms throughout the Bay Area to lead movement and mindful exercises for children.
Today's classrooms don't look much like the days of yore. White boards have replaced blackboards, sleek computer screens have replaced the chunky film projectors and many students are doing their work on laptops or iPads rather than with No.
