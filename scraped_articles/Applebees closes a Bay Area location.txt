Applebee's closes a Bay Area location
-----
The residents of Antioch are no longer "eatin' good in the neighborhood."On Sunday, Feb. 25, the Antioch Applebee's, at 2737 Hillcrest Ave., served up its last plate of riblets, the company's signature dish. Customers on Monday showed up to a sign on the door directing them to the Pinole outpost, at 1369 Fitzgerald Dr., according to KRON-TV.
