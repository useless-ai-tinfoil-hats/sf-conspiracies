Ube Yule log? Christmas classic gets modern spins in the Bay Area
-----
Here's senior editor Janelle Bitker:Bay Area chefs are busy right now making all kinds of Christmas treats: panettone, stollen and, my favorite, bûche de Noël.
Also known as a Yule log, this rolled cake is typically a chocolate lover's dream with beautiful details like chocolate ganache bark and meringue mushrooms. Perhaps you've seen it on "The Great British Baking Show" or tried making one for French club in high school. (It's the taste that counts, right?)
