San Jose officer quits after ‘disgusting’ text messages: ‘I hate black people’
-----
A San Jose officer, who had shot a man later hailed as a hero last year, has left the force after the police chief said he sent "disgusting text messages," including one saying, "I hate black people."Officer Mark McNamara, who had been with the San Jose Police Department for six years, resigned after learning of an internal affairs investigation into his text messages, an agency spokesperson confirmed to the Chronicle.
