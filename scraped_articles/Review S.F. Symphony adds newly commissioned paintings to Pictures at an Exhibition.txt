Review: S.F. Symphony adds newly commissioned paintings to ‘Pictures at an Exhibition’
-----
Mussorgsky's "Pictures at an Exhibition" is notable not only for its beauty and inventiveness, but for the number of metamorphoses it has gone through.The San Francisco Symphony's concert under guest conductor Ludovic Morlot on Thursday, Nov. 2, added another to the list by fitting the suite out with some new and wonderfully evocative artwork to match the music's pictorial content.
