Man rescued from water near S.F. ferry docks after guards hear cries for help
-----
Firefighters pulled a man from the water Sunday morning near the San Francisco Bay ferry docks near Pier 1, officials said.A boat crew rescued the unidentified man around 7:30 a.m. after security guards heard him yelling for help and called 911, San Francisco Fire Department spokesperson Lt. Mariano Elias said. The man told rescuers that he jumped into the water because someone was chasing him, Elias said.
