BottleRock producers announce La Onda, a new Latin music festival in Napa
-----
Downtown Napa is set to host another large outdoor music extravaganza next year.Producers of the annual BottleRock Napa Valley festival revealed plans to introduce La Onda, a new multi-day outdoor concert focused on Latin music, at the Napa Valley Expo.
The two-day concert, set for June 1-2, aims to showcase a range of musical genres, encompassing regional Mexican, Latin pop, Spanish rock, reggaetón, banda, mariachi, rap, norteño, cumbia, bolero and more.
