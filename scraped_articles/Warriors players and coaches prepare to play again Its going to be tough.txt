Warriors players and coaches prepare to play again: ‘It’s going to be tough’
-----
After five days of heartbreak and sorrow, mourning a friend and trusted colleague, Golden State Warriors head coach Steve Kerr returned to work Monday to face a daunting challenge.Less than a week removed from the death of assistant coach Dejan Milojevic, the Warriors now begin to turn their attention back to basketball. They resumed practice Monday at Chase Center, will practice again Tuesday and then will host Atlanta on Wednesday night.
