How to save Burning Man from itself
-----
Burning Man started in 1986 as a small gathering on Baker Beach, where Larry Harvey and Jerry James built effigies of a man and dog out of scrap wood to burn down — an act of what they called "radical self-expression."Nearly 40 years later, that San Francisco beach party has turned into an unrecognizably huge gathering in the Nevada desert — one that's in desperate need of reinvention to reclaim the spirit of Harvey and James' original vision and purpose.
