California wildfires: More than a dozen new fires sparked by lightning
-----
More than a dozen small fires started in the Sierra Nevada foothills on Monday after thunderstorms and lightning swept through Central California.Cal Fire incident maps logged at least 12 new wildfires in the foothills of the Sierra on Monday afternoon. The fires appeared soon after the area was struck by lightning storms that dropped little to no rain, indicating that they were likely sparked by lightning, Chronicle newsroom meteorologist Anthony Edwards said.
