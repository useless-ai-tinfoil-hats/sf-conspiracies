Overturned big-rig spilling fuel blocks creates Bay Area traffic nightmare
-----
An overturned semi-truck blocked a major connection point on a San Francisco Bay Area Highway on Thursday morning, officials said.The truck was on its side near the ramp connecting northbound Interstate 238 to southbound Interstate 880 in San Lorenzo with all lanes blocked, the California Highway Patrol said.
Just before 7 a.m., KCBS Radio reported that traffic was a mess and backed up into Castro Valley.
