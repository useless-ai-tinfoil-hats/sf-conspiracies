Three family members killed by West Portal driver identified
-----
The three people killed Saturday after an SUV crashed into a West Portal bus stop were a Brazilian-Portuguese family, the San Francisco Medical Examiner's office confirmed Tuesday.The victims were Diego Cardoso de Oliveira, 40, Matilde Ramos Pinto, 38, and 1-year old Joaquin Ramos Pinto de Oliveira, medical examiners said.
The family's gravely injured 3-month-old infant remained hospitalized with life-threatening injuries on Monday, the San Francisco Police Department said.
