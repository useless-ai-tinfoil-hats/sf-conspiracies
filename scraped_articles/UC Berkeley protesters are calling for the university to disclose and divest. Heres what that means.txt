UC Berkeley protesters are calling for the university to ‘disclose and divest.’ Here’s what that means
-----
Students across at least eight University of California campuses have protested in support of Palestinians over the last several months, including a weeks-long encampment at UC Berkeley that was vacated May 14.At Berkeley, as at college protests nationwide, students have not only called for a cease-fire in Gaza but have also urged administrators to "disclose and divest" the institution's investments in companies that profit from Israel's occupation of the Palestinian territories.
