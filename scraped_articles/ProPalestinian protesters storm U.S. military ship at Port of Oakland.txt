Pro-Palestinian protesters storm U.S. military ship at Port of Oakland
-----
In a dramatic demonstration against Israel's military actions in Gaza, protesters stormed a large U.S. military ship at the Port of Oakland on Friday.At least three people scaled the Cape Orlando and held on to the ladder to prevent the ship from leaving the dock.
