Man charged with murder of two San Lorenzo women he lived with
-----
A 31-year-old man was charged with two counts of murder in the fatal shootings of two women he lived with in San Lorenzo, according to the Alameda County Sheriff's Office and court records.Howard Reed was taken into custody as a "person of interest" and later arrested in Kansas. He will be extradited to Alameda County to face criminal charges, the sheriff's office said.
