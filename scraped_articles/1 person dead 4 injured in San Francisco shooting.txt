1 person dead, 4 injured in San Francisco shooting
-----
One person was killed and four people were injured when gunfire broke out in San Francisco's Tenderloin neighborhood early Thursday morning, San Francisco police said.The San Francisco Police Department said in a news released issued Thursday afternoon that it first received a report of the shooting near Hyde and Turk streets at 2:34 a.m.
Officers on the scene found four men and one woman with gunshot wounds, police said. Paramedics rushed to the scene and provided medical aid. All
