Tony Bennett’s heart finds a permanent home inside S.F.’s Fairmont hotel
-----
Tony Bennett's heart has found a permanent home in the hotel where he introduced the world to his signature song, "I Left My Heart in San Francisco."The heart sculpture — hand-painted by the Grammy-winning singer, artist, humanitarian and philanthropist — had been displayed in Union Square for nearly 20 years. It will now take pride of place in the lobby of the Fairmont hotel atop Nob Hill, where Bennett premiered the tune in the Venetian Room in 1961.
