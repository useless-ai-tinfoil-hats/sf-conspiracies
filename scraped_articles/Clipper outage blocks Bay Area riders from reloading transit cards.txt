Clipper outage blocks Bay Area riders from reloading transit cards
-----
Bay Area Clipper experienced an outage Monday that blocked some public transportation riders from reloading their cards.Clipper warned that a technical issue was preventing mobile card users from reloading their Clipper cards through its app and via digital wallets, including those offered by Apple and Android, according to an 8:30 a.m. social media post.
The problem, which was caused by a malfunctioning computer server, was resolved around noon, said Metropolitan Transportation
