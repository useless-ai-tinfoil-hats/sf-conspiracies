Warriors' Steph Curry intends to play for Team USA at 2024 Olympics
-----
Of all the things Steph Curry has accomplished in his career, there is one thing he's never done: bring home Olympic gold.But when speaking at the Golden State Warriors' media day Monday, Curry made it clear that he intends to suit up in Paris for the 2024 Summer Olympics.
"If all things stay the same, I want to be playing," Curry said.
