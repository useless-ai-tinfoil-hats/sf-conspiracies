Parachute saves plane occupants in Northern California crash
-----
Three people, including a 2-year-old, survived a crash in a small plane flying over Northern California on Friday when the aircraft's built-in parachute was deployed, officials said. There were no other occupants on the plane, according to a news release from the Mendocino County Sheriff's Office.An image shared on Instagram by the Shelter Cove Fire Department showed the plane floating from a red-and-white parachute over a thick canopy of evergreens in Mendocino County.
