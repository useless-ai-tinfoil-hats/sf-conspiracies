Cal alum’s first 24 hours as gold medalist: Celebrate past 3 a.m., awaken a star in Paris
-----
PARIS — Camryn Rogers punctuated her gold medal with an impromptu celebration at the gates of the Olympic village in the wee hours of Wednesday morning.Rogers, a Cal alum and the reigning world champion in the women's hammer throw, added another coveted title Tuesday night at Stade de France: Olympic champion. She earned the gold medal with a clutch throw of 76.97 meters on her next-to-last attempt.
