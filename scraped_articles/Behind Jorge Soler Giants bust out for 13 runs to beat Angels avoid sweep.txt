Behind Jorge Soler, Giants bust out for 13 runs to beat Angels, avoid sweep
-----
Designated hitter Jorge Soler admired seeing Hunter Strickland's 96 mph offering soar into the left-field bleachers at Oracle Park. Sunday's sellout crowd of 41,008 admired – and cheered – one of the San Francisco Giants' best offensive showings this season.Soler's towering three-run home run – his eighth of the season – punctuated a nine-run fourth inning for the Giants, who avoided a sweep against the Los Angeles Angels with a 13-6 victory in the series finale.
