California had the worst job growth in the country in 2023
-----
California had the lowest job growth in the country in 2023 at a rate of just 0.87%, a disappointing figure considering an overall strong U.S. labor market, dipping far below 2022's increase of 5.5%, according to an analysis of Bureau of Labor Statistics data by the Los Angeles Daily News.
