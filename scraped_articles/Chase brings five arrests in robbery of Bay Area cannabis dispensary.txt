Chase brings five arrests in robbery of Bay Area cannabis dispensary
-----
After a chase involving several law enforcement agencies, Santa Rosa police have arrested five people on suspicion of robbing a cannabis dispensary.Officers responded to reports of a robbery on the 1800 block of Empire Industrial Court around 8 p.m. Thursday, the Santa Rosa Police Department said in a news release. Police said an employee told them that four vehicles arrived and several people assaulted him and stole marijuana products. The employee provided descriptions of the vehicles
