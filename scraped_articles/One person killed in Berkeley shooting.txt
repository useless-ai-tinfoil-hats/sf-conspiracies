One person killed in Berkeley shooting
-----
A person was fatally shot in south Berkeley Friday afternoon, police said.At about 12:44 p.m., Berkeley police received reports of gunshots on the 3200 block of Adeline Street, police said. Officers rushed to the scene to find the shooting victim and began administering life-saving measures.
The person died at the scene as a result of their injuries, according to police. The department did not provide information about possible suspects.
