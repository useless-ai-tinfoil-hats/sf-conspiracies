A government shutdown could close San Francisco attractions
-----
San Francisco tourists may want to check their itineraries.As Congress remains mired in a budgetary deadlock, a partial government shutdown appears to be only days away. While some essential services – Social Security payments, for example – will remain, funding for others could be curtailed or put on ice. This means that certain federally-run facilities may temporarily close as employees are furloughed.
