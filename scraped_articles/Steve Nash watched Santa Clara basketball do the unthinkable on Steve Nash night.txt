Steve Nash watched Santa Clara basketball do the unthinkable on Steve Nash night
-----
A nearly 13-year losing streak came to an end in Santa Clara on Thursday with two-time NBA MVP Steve Nash in attendance.The Santa Clara men's basketball team upset No. 23 Gonzaga, 77-76, ending a 26-game streak of losses against the Zags that went back to 2011. The victory happened on Steve Nash jersey night, which was dedicated to the basketball Hall of Famer who played there from 1992 to 1996; the school handed out replica Nash Santa Clara jerseys before the game.
