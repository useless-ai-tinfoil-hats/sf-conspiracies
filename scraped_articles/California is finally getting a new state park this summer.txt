California is finally getting a new state park this summer
-----
After a decade in the works, California is getting a new state park this summer.Dos Rios Ranch, a 1,600-acre plot west of Modesto where the San Joaquin and Tuolumne rivers converge, has long been slated to become the next state park. On Monday, the Department of Parks and Recreation announced it would open June 12.
