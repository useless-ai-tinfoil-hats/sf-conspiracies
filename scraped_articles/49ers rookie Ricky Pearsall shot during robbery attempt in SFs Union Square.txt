49ers rookie Ricky Pearsall shot during robbery attempt in SF's Union Square
-----
Niners rookie receiver Ricky Pearsall was shot during a robbery attempt in San Francisco's Union Square on Saturday, according to NBC Bay Area.The San Francisco Police Department released a statement that said officers responded to the area of Geary Street and Grant Avenue, where two people were found with gunshot wounds, Pearsall and another man, who police said tried to rob Pearsall.
