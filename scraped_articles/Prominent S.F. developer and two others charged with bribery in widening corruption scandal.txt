Prominent S.F. developer and two others charged with bribery in widening corruption scandal
-----
Federal authorities have charged three current and former executives from a San Francisco design and engineering firm, accusing them of bribing employees from the city's Department of Building Inspection.Siavash "Sia" Tahbazof, 72, was charged Thursday with one count of conspiracy to commit honest services wire fraud for allegedly bribing former senior building inspector Bernie Curran and former plan checkers Rudy Pada and Cyril Yu. None of the workers is still a city employee.
