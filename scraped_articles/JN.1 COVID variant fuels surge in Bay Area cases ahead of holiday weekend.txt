JN.1 COVID variant fuels surge in Bay Area cases ahead of holiday weekend
-----
The Bay Area is experiencing a rapid increase in COVID-19 cases and hospitalizations ahead of the holiday weekend, driven by the nationwide surge of the newly identified JN.1 coronavirus variant.
