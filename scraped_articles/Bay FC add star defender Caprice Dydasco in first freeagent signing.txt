Bay FC add star defender Caprice Dydasco in first free-agent signing
-----
Bay FC's first free-agent signing — and second-ever player — is defender Caprice Dydasco, the team announced Tuesday morning.Dydasco, who played for the Houston Dash, was the NWSL's Defender of the Year in 2021 while with Gotham FC and is one of 32 players in league history to eclipse 10,000 minutes played. Her deal is through the 2026 season.
