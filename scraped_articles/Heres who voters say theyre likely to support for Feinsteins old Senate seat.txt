Here’s who voters say they’re likely to support for Feinstein's old Senate seat
-----
Reps. Katie Porter and Adam Schiff are near-tied to lead the 2024 U.S. Senate race to represent California, according to a new UC Berkeley poll.About 17% of likely voters told the UC Berkeley Institute of Governmental Studies they would back Porter, D-Irvine, if they had to vote now. Another 16% said they would vote for Schiff, D-Burbank.
