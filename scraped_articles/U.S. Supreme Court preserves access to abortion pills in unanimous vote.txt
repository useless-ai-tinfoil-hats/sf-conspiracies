U.S. Supreme Court preserves access to abortion pills in unanimous vote
-----
Two years after repealing the constitutional right to abortion that it had declared in 1973, the Supreme Court voted unanimously Thursday to preserve women's access to mifepristone, the pill used in nearly two-thirds of all U.S. abortions.A group of antiabortion doctors said that mifepristone, found to be safe and effective by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration in 2000, was actually dangerous and had been wrongly approved.
