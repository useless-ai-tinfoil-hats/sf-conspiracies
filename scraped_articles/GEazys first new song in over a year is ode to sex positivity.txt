G-Eazy’s first new song in over a year is ode to ‘sex positivity’
-----
G-Eazy, the multi-platinum-selling East Bay rapper, has released his first new song in over a year.Titled "Femme Fatale," the high-energy single features female rap sensations Coi Leray and Kaliii. G-Eazy debuted the track on "The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon" with his collaborators on Thursday, April 4.
