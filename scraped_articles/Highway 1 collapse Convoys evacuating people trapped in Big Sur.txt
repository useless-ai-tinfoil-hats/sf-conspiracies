Highway 1 collapse: Convoys evacuating people trapped in Big Sur
-----
UPDATE: Highway 1 convoys to halt in Big Sur as more rain moves inResidents and essential workers will have two daily opportunities to join convoys crossing the portion of Highway 1 in Monterey County that collapsed during weekend storms, California transit officials said.
