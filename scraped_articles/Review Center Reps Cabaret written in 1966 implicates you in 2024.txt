Review: Center Rep’s ‘Cabaret,” written in 1966, implicates you in 2024
-----
If you want to understand how a republic becomes a fascist state, behold Jessica Chen's choreography in "Cabaret."Her kick lines are so fun — till they start to look a little relentless, then militaristic. Before long, everyone in the Weimar-era Kit Kat Klub is marching offstage with a Sieg Heil salute. Drum rolls go on so long they're no longer introducing an act, it seems, but revving up a firing squad.
