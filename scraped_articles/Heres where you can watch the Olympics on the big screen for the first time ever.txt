Here's where you can watch the Olympics on the big screen for the first time ever
-----
The Olympic Games are heading to the big screen this summer for the first time ever.AMC Theatres has partnered with NBCUniversal to bring the 2024 Paris Olympics to approximately 160 theaters across the United States from July 27 to Aug. 11. Select daytime coverage of the 329 medal events, from swimming to gymnastics, will be broadcast live at participating venues. Events playing in theaters can be found here.
