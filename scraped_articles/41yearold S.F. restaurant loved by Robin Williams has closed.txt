41-year-old S.F. restaurant loved by Robin Williams has closed
-----
A French bistro known as a perennial Nob Hill darling has quietly closed after more than four decades in business.The cozy, intimate Rue Lepic, at 900 Pine St., closed at the end of October. The owner of a neighboring hair salon confirmed that longtime chef-owner Michiko Boccara sold the restaurant, which is slated to reopen next year under new ownership. A new beer and wine license for the property is pending approval.
