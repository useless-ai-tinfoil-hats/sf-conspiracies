California could eliminate deadlines for filing child abuse lawsuits under bill sent to Newsom
-----
California lawmakers on Tuesday passed a bill to eliminate the statute of limitations for people to file lawsuits over child sexual assault starting in 2024. The bill wouldn't apply to any past incidents.The bill now heads to Gov. Gavin Newsom, who will have until mid-October to sign or veto it.
