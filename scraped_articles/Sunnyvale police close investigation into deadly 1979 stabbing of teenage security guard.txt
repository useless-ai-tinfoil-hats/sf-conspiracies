Sunnyvale police close investigation into deadly 1979 stabbing of teenage security guard
-----
Sunnyvale police said Thursday that they were closing a 44-year investigation into the killing of Estella Elena Mena, who was stabbed to death while working as a security guard in 1979.Mena, 18, was suffering from stab wounds when she was found in the lobby of a business at 444 De Guigne Drive on Oct. 16, 1979. She later died from her injuries.
Police went for years without a suspect or a viable lead into who might have committed the crime, officials said in a statement.
