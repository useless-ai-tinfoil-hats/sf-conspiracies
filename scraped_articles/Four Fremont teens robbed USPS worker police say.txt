Four Fremont teens robbed USPS worker, police say
-----
Four Bay Area teenagers robbed a postal worker last week, officials said.Police responded to a robbery report in Fremont at 11:30 a.m. Wednesday in the area of 3rd Street, the Fremont Police Department said in a news release. The suspects, a 13-year-old, a 14-year-old and two 15-year-olds, who are all from Fremont, stole the keys to a mail truck from the employee and fled the scene in a stolen Jeep Grand Cherokee, the police wrote.
