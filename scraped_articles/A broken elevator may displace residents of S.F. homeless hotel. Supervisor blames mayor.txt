A broken elevator may displace residents of S.F. homeless hotel. Supervisor blames mayor
-----
More than two dozen HIV-positive tenants living in a single-room-occupancy hotel in the Tenderloin are at risk of being displaced unless an elevator that has been broken for nearly two years is fixed.The owner of the Kinney Hotel on Eddy Street has failed to maintain the elevator, making living conditions there "untenable," according to Emily Cohen, spokesperson for the San Francisco Department of Homelessness and Supportive Housing.
