This cheesy 1970s trend is making its Bay Area comeback
-----
Fondue restaurants have vanished from the Bay Area in recent years, from 61-year-old Berkeley institution Fondue Fred to national chain the Melting Pot's last-remaining local outpost. There are only three dedicated fondue restaurants left.Yet despite the lack of local opportunities to dip bread into a steamy pot of melted cheese — or to swoosh strawberries through silky dark chocolate — the 1970s favorite appears to be poised for a comeback.
