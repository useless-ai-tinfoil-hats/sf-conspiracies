Longtime Bay Area hot dog chain shutting down another location
-----
Bay Area hot dog favorite Caspers is closing another restaurant, this time in Hayward.One of two Hayward locations, which has sold snappy hot dogs at 951 C St. since the 1940s, will shut down July 13, the restaurant announced Tuesday. It follows the closure of another longtime Caspers in Walnut Creek last year.
"We are deeply grateful to our loyal, multi-generational customers who have supported us over the decades," co-owner and general manager Ron Dorian said in the announcement.
