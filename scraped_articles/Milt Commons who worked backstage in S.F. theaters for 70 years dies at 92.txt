Milt Commons, who worked backstage in S.F. theaters for 70 years, dies at 92
-----
In the last performances of "A Christmas Carol" at San Francisco's Geary Theater before COVID-19 hit in 2020, Milt Commons was the dresser who ensured that costuming for the more than half-dozen boys in the cast went without a hitch.It was eight shows a week and the nights were long, but Commons kept up with the kids, seeing to it that they all had their mittens and hats before taking the stage. He had something to prove. He was 92.
