Doctor burnout is rampant. Online patient messaging portals aren’t helping
-----
A news headline reads: "Popular heartburn medicine increases dementia risk by 33%." Your heart sinks. Should you message your doctor and ask whether you should stop your heartburn medication?The answer may seem like an obvious yes. But what if I told you that, patient messages are a leading driver of doctor burnout?
Since the COVID-19 pandemic, more people have turned to patient portals — a secure online health platform — to communicate with their doctors via online messaging.
