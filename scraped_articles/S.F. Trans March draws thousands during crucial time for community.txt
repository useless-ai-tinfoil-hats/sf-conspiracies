S.F. Trans March draws thousands during crucial time for community
-----
Thousands of transgender people and their allies marched proudly and defiantly through San Francisco's streets Friday evening to the traditional epicenter of the community's activism.The Trans March, marking its 20th anniversary this year, drew a multigenerational, multiracial crowd, including many trans parents and their children.
