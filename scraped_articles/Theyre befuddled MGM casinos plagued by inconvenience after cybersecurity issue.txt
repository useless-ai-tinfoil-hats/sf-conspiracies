'They're befuddled': MGM casinos plagued by inconvenience after cybersecurity issue
-----
On Monday morning, MGM Resorts announced in a social media post that it had discovered a "cybersecurity issue" in its computer systems.The company responded by shutting down its computer systems in order to protect data.
The shutdowns disrupted the day-to-day operations of MGM's casinos and resorts in Las Vegas, as well as across the country in states such as Massachusetts, New Jersey and New York, a MGM spokesperson told the Associated Press.
