One killed, 3 seriously injured in wrong-way collision near Bay Bridge
-----
One person was killed and three people were seriously injured Tuesday in a three-vehicle collision involving an individual driving a pickup truck the wrong way on the westbound Interstate 580 connector that leads to the San Francisco-Oakland Bay Bridge, officials said.Lanes 1 and 2 of westbound 580 at the westbound I-80 transition in the MacArthur Maze remained shut down to traffic as of 7:30 a.m., CHP spokesperson Adib Zeid told SFGATE.
