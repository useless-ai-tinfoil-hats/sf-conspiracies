California man goes missing while hunting in Sequoia National Forest
-----
Authorities are searching for a man who went hunting in Tulare County on Sunday morning and hasn't been seen since.Juan Duron Velasquez, 75, was last seen at around 7 a.m. that day, when he became separated from his hunting group in Parker Meadow, a hiking and hunting area between Johnsondale and Pine Flat on the western edge of the Sequoia National Forest.
