Chappell Roan doubles down on message to invasive fans: ‘Please stop touching me’
-----
Chappell Roan has doubled down on her message to "predatory" fans, urging them to respect her boundaries.After posting several TikTok videos to air her grievances with sudden fame earlier this week, the 26-year-old artist posted a candid message on Instagram, lamenting her experience with intrusive interactions following a string of viral performances.
Roan wrote, "I feel the most unsafe I have ever felt in my life."
