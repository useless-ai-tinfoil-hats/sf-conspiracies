Cleanup operations paused at Bay Area refinery after flaring, odor
-----
Cleanup operations paused Saturday night after a work on a mechanical problem at Benicia's Valero Refinery that caused flaring and emitted an odor in the area, the city announced.The source of the odor was a refined hydrocarbon that was being moved between two tanks at the refinery, the city's Fire Department said.
