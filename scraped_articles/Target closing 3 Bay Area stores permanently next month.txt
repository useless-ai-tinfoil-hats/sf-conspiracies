Target closing 3 Bay Area stores permanently next month
-----
Target announced Tuesday it will permanently close a San Francisco store, blaming organized retail crime and unsuccessful anti-theft measures.The store at 1690 Folsom St. is slated to close Oct. 21. The Folsom location opened in 2019.
The retail giant also plans to close stores in Oakland at 2650 Broadway and in Pittsburg at 4301 Century Boulevard.
