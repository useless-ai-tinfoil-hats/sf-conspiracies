These Bay Area dumpling restaurants are breaking all the rules
-----
In the Bay Area, soup dumplings aren't bound by tradition. You might find red dumplings bursting with Thai tom yum soup, tingly-spicy versions or ones filled with kimchi butter.Over the past several years, chefs have been turning out increasingly creative renditions of these Chinese soup-filled dumplings known as xiao long bao. The phenomenon is particularly ripe in the Bay Area, thanks to being home to a large Asian American population as well as diverse customers who are adventurous
