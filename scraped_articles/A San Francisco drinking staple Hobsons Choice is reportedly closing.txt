A San Francisco drinking staple Hobson's Choice is reportedly closing
-----
A San Francisco drinking staple in the middle of Haight Street will reportedly soon be closing its doors for good.Popular Victorian-looking rum pub Hobson's Choice, on the corner of Clayton and Haight, will shutter after 26 years in business, owner Chris Dickerson told SFGATE columnist Stuart Schuffman this weekend. Dickerson cited the lack of pedestrian traffic on the famous street for the decision, and said the neighborhood hasn't yet bounced back from the pandemic.
