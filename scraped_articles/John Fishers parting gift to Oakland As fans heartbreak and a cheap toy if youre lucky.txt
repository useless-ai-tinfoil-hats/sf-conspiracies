John Fisher’s parting gift to Oakland A’s fans: heartbreak and a cheap toy (if you’re lucky)
-----
If festive yet poorly planned funerals are your thing, you won't want to miss the Oakland Athletics' final home game on Sept. 26.You'll laugh, you'll cry, maybe you'll be trampled in the rush to get the game's giveaway item, a replica of the Coliseum.
That final game will be a microcosm of the A's last quarter-century in Oakland under team owner John Fisher: costly and messy, ending in broken hearts, with a touch of toy rage.
