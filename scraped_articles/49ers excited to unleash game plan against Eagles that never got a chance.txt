49ers excited to unleash game plan against Eagles that never got a chance
-----
Last season, the Eagles had the NFL's second-ranked defense, allowed the fewest passing yards in the league and had 70 sacks, the most since the 1987 Bears.And the San Francisco 49ers couldn't wait to attack them.
Of course, they didn't get the chance to reveal their offensive game plan in last season's 31-7 loss in the NFC Championship Game. Quarterback Brock Purdy suffered a torn elbow ligament on their sixth offensive snap, they ran out of healthy QBs early in the third quarter
