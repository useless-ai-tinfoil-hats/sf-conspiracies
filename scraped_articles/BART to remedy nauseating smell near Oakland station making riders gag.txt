BART to remedy nauseating smell near Oakland station making riders ‘gag’
-----
For months, some Bay Area Rapid Transit riders have noticed a pungent stench filling trains heading south toward San Francisco from the 12th Street Station in Oakland."It smells like a mix of burning plastic, burning hair and burnt toast," said commuter Rowan Bariteau, one of several people who unsuccessfully sought answers on a Reddit forum.
Bariteau wondered why she frequently detected the scent traveling to San Francisco, but never on her return to Oakland.
