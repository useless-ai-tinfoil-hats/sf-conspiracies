S.F. Airbnb host wages viral campaign to get company to pay, saying guests caused thousands in damage
-----
A San Francisco woman has launched a viral public relations offensive against Airbnb, blaming the tech company for a plumbing leak that forced her to move out of her Mission District duplex.Erika Gemzer said she's lost upward of $300,000 from paying the mortgage and property taxes for a home she cannot use, and from potential income she might have earned from renting it out as an Airbnb. Gemzer said the leak occurred in April after her short-term rental guests clogged the upstairs toilet
