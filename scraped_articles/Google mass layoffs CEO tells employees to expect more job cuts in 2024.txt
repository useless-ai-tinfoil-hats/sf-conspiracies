Google mass layoffs: CEO tells employees to expect more job cuts in 2024
-----
Despite having already cut hundreds of jobs since the beginning of the year, Google CEO Sundar Pichai has informed employees to brace themselves for further cuts throughout 2024.In an internal memo shared with multiple media outlets on Wednesday, Pichai stated, "We have ambitious goals and will be investing in our big priorities this year. The reality is that to create the capacity for this investment, we have to make tough choices."
