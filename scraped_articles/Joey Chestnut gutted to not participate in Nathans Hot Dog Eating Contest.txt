Joey Chestnut ‘gutted’ to not participate in Nathan’s Hot Dog Eating Contest
-----
The world of competitive eating faced a shock on Tuesday when news leaked that Joey Chestnut, a 16-time winner of the Nathan's Hot Dog Eating Contest, was banned from this year's event due to a recent partnership with Impossible Foods, a plant-based food company.However, Major League Eating said Chestnut was not banned but asked not to participate given the deal with a rival brand.
