8 rain gauges on Kauai record over 10 inches of rain in 12 hours
-----
A band of moisture associated with a storm several hundred miles off the coast of Kauai dropped remarkable amounts of rain over the Hawaiian island Thursday night into Friday morning, causing flooding and school and road closures. The system also brings a chance of snow on the Island of Hawaii's tallest peaks Friday.Eight rain gauges, mostly on the southeast side of the island of Kauai, recorded over 10 inches of rain across 12 hours.
National Weather Service forecaster Derek Wroe
