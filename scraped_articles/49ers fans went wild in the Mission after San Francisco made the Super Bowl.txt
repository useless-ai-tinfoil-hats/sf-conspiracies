49ers fans went wild in the Mission after San Francisco made the Super Bowl
-----
A sea of red and gold flooded the streets of the Mission District late Sunday night following an improbable 49ers comeback to punch a ticket to Super Bowl LVIII.The Niners overcame a 21-7 halftime deficit to defeat the Detroit Lions, 34-31, marking the first time the Niners won after going down 14 points in the Brock Purdy era.
