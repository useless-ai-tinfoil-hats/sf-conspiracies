Evacuation order lifted in Marin County amid dangerous surf surges
-----
LATEST Dec. 28, 3:10 p.m. An evacuation order in Marin County was lifted for the areas around Stinson Beach, the Marin County Fire Department announced on social media. Officials said it's safe for residents to return to their homes.The evacuations come as a high surf and an intense storm made for dangerous conditions along Bay Area coasts. Some social media videos show the storm surge washing into neighborhoods and community members placing sandbags in the flooded areas.
