SF deli chain closing final downtown locations after 40 years in business
-----
Lee's Deli, a local San Francisco chain that once had a dozen locations around the city, is closing its final two locations. The downtown stores, located at 303 2nd St. and 280 Battery St., are listed as "temporary closed until further notice" on Lee's Deli's website, but owner and co-founder Lee Quan told the San Francisco Business Times those closures are permanent.Quan opened the original Lee's Deli in 1983 on Kearny Street, and expanded the business over the past 40 years.
