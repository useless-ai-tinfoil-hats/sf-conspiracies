Eight dead in Central Valley crash, including farmworkers on their way to work
-----
Eight adults died in a head-on crash in the Central Valley on Friday morning, officials said.The deadly crash happened at approximately 6:17 a.m. on Avenue 7, a rural road in Madera County, and west of Road 22, Javier Ruvalcaba, a spokesperson for California Highway Patrol, told SFGATE.
