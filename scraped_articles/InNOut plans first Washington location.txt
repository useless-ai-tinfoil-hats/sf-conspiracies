In-N-Out plans first Washington location
-----
California burger chain In-N-Out has submitted an application to open its first restaurant in Washington, reported the Columbian. The outlet spotted an application submitted by In-N-Out on Feb. 16 for a location in Ridgefield, a small town in southwest Washington close to the Oregon border.Mike Abbate, vice president of store development at In-N-Out, told the Columbian that the Ridgefield location is too early in the development process to comment on a timeline for an opening date.
