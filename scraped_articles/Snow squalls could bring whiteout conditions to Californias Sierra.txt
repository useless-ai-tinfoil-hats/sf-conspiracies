Snow squalls could bring whiteout conditions to California's Sierra
-----
Light snow was falling across California's northern Sierra Nevada Range early Wednesday as a storm system that has been focused over the Pacific Northwest approached the region. Snowfall rates are expected to pick up through the day, bringing dangerous driving conditions to mountain roads.The National Weather Service is expecting heavy snow in the northern Sierra, including the Tahoe Basin, between 10 a.m. and 5 p.m. Wednesday, with snowfall rates as high as 1 to 3 inches per hour
