Iconic hip-hop group cancels US tour 3 days before opening night
-----
Lauryn Hill and the Fugees just canceled their 2024 U.S. tour three days before it was scheduled to begin with a show in Florida.The artists provided no explanation for the cancellation. Instead, ticket holders received a Live Nation message that said, "Your event has been canceled. A refund will be on its way to your account soon," according to Variety.
