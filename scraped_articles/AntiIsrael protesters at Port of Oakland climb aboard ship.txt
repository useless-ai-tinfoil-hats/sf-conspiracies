Anti-Israel protesters at Port of Oakland climb aboard ship
-----
Protesters objecting to Israel's war with Hamas in Gaza attempted to block the departure of a large ship from the Port of Oakland on Friday morning, with at least three of them climbing aboard the hulking vessel.Three people were holding onto the ladder leading to the entrance or the Cape Orlando ship at the port Friday morning, and may have chained themselves to it. There were police officers at the entrance of the boat blocking other protesters and officers at the foot of the boat.
