Antioch residents arrested for alleged California bank robbery spree
-----
Police arrested two people accused of a string of bank robberies across Northern California, most recently in San Jose.The two suspects, Antioch residents Brandon Lopez, 37, and Tamara Bush, 43, were accused of robbing six banks, including one in San Jose on Feb. 12, San Jose police said. There, police said a male suspect entered a bank, passed a note over to the teller demanding money and walked out with $1,000 in cash, police said.
