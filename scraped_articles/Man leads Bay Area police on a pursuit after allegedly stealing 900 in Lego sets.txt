Man leads Bay Area police on a pursuit after allegedly stealing $900 in Lego sets
-----
A man led police on an 11-mile pursuit in Solano County on Friday after allegedly stealing Lego sets worth $900, officials said.Police responded to a burglary at a business in Vallejo located on the 700 block of Admiral Callaghan Lane, the Vallejo Police Department said in a news release. Employees told police that the suspect was driving a Hyundai Sonata. Police said they then spotted the car, which had stolen license plates, traveling west on Redwood Street near Fairgrounds Drive.
