San Francisco Caltrain station reopens after man climbs power pole
-----
The San Francisco Caltrain station reopened just after 9 a.m. on Thursday morning after a temporary three-hour closure due to a man climbing overhead wires, officials said.KCBS reported at 9:05 a.m. that the man climbed down a power pole, returning to the ground. He was taken into police custody
Trains were delayed systemwide this morning, Caltrain said.
San Francisco police and firefighters were on scene to assist the man, the agency said.
