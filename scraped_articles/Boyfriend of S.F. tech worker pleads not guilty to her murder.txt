Boyfriend of S.F. tech worker pleads not guilty to her murder
-----
A 29-year-old man accused of stabbing his girlfriend to death in their Presidio Heights apartment pleaded not guilty to murder charges Friday.Scott Fisher was arrested last week in connection with the death of Kimberly Wong, a 27-year-old tech worker who was found mortally wounded late last month. The alleged act of domestic violence stunned residents of the quiet, affluent neighborhood.
In addition to murder, Fisher faces an allegation that he used a knife in the attack.
