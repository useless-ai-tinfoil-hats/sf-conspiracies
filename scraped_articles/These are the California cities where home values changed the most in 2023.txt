These are the California cities where home values changed the most in 2023
-----
Home values in most of California's large cities started growing again in 2023 after mortgage rate hikes had pushed them down. But in San Francisco and Oakland, values continued to tumble.The typical home value in San Francisco fell from $1.29 million in December 2022 to $1.22 million in December 2023, a 5.6% decline. That was the biggest drop among any of California's 100 most-populous cities, according to data from real estate listings site Zillow, and was San Francisco's lowest
