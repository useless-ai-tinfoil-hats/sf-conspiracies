Police arrest man in two fatal shootings in San Leandro fitness center parking lot
-----
San Leandro police have arrested a man they allege was responsible for two separate fatal shootings in a San Leandro fitness center parking lot.On two mornings in September, 10 days apart, eyewitnesses discovered two people shot dead inside of their cars outside the 24 Hour Fitness at the Bayfair Center shopping mall, according to San Leandro police. Detectives also located 9mm shell casings at both scenes, authorities said.
