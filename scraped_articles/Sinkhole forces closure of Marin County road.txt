Sinkhole forces closure of Marin County road
-----
A portion of Highway 131 in Marin County will remain closed indefinitely after a large sinkhole formed near Tiburon Boulevard and Stewart Drive in Tiburon, Caltrans said Thursday.Caltrans said engineers reviewed the damage and that contractors would complete repairs "as soon as possible." The Stewart Drive crosswalk will remain closed due to storm damage, Caltrains said.
The sinkhole formed just days after heavy rainfall swept through the Bay Area.
