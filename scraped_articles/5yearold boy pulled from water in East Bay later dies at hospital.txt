5-year-old boy, pulled from water in East Bay, later dies at hospital
-----
A 5-year-old boy who was pulled from the water off Bethel Island died Saturday at a hospital, according to the Contra Costa Sheriff's Office.The boy was reported missing around 4:30 p.m. Saturday on Halcyon Place, a street in the Delta Coves housing development on the island in the Sacramento-San Joaquin River Delta.
Deputies and firefighters searched for the boy.
About 30 minutes later, a Contra Costa County firefighter with water rescue gear pulled the boy from the water.
