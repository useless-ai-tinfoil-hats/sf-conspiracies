‘Occupation no more’: Hundreds rally in S.F. to support Palestinian resistance to Israeli control
-----
Hundreds of Bay Area residents rallied in support of Palestinians in downtown San Francisco on Sunday afternoon, voicing impassioned support for Palestinian resistance to Israeli military control and demanding the United States end financial and political support to Israel.The event was organized by several groups including Palestinian Youth Movement and Arab Resources and Outreach Center after this weekend's attacks by the militant group Hamas on Israeli civilians.
