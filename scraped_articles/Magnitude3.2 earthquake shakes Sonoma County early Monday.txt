Magnitude-3.2 earthquake shakes Sonoma County early Monday
-----
A magnitude 3.2 earthquake struck in Sonoma County on Monday morning, giving a largely rural part of the North Bay a shake, the U.S. Geological Survey said.The quake hit at 5:22 a.m. with a depth of 1.1 miles (1.8 km) miles nearly 4.1 miles west of Cobb, a community just south of Clear Lake and 4.1 miles southwest of the Geysers geothermal field, which includes several geothermal power plants.
