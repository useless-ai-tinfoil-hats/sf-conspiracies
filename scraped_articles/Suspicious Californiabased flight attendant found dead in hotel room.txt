'Suspicious': California-based flight attendant found dead in hotel room
-----
Police are investigating the death of an American Airlines flight attendant who was found in a hotel room on Monday.According to Philadelphia police, Diana Ramos, 66, was discovered by housekeeping staff at the Philadelphia Airport Marriott adjacent to the airport's Terminal B. She was already deceased by the time first responders got to the scene.
