Bay Area woman suspected of stabbing mother to death on Facebook Live arrested
-----
A woman was arrested in San Rafael on suspicion of stabbing her mother to death in an apartment complex while broadcasting the killing on Facebook Live.Tonantzyn Oris Beltran, 28, was taken into custody Monday after officers found her in an apartment at the Terra Linda complex along with a 55-year-old woman identified as Beltran's mother, police said. Beltran was allegedly on a balcony holding a knife when police arrived, standing next to her mother, who had been stabbed multiple times.
