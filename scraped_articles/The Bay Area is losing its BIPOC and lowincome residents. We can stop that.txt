The Bay Area is losing its BIPOC and low-income residents. We can stop that
-----
The Bay Area is facing a real estate apocalypse.Of course, housing prices in the region have been and continue to be unaffordable for so many. Sky-high rents are leading to widespread displacement, particularly of communities of color and working-class people. While large-scale affordable housing developments are the primary focus of most housing advocates, we need to think creatively about what additional solutions there might be to keep residents housed.
