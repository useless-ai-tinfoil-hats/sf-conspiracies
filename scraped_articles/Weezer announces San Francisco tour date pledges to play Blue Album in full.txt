Weezer announces San Francisco tour date, pledges to play 'Blue Album' in full
-----
Once upon a time, the suggestion that Weezer would spin a whole tour out of their acclaimed 1994 debut album — colloquially known as the "Blue Album" — would elicit a disbelieving "Say It Ain't So" from most fans.But 30 years after the release of the recording, an anniversary "Blue Album" tour is actually underway. On Monday, the Los Angeles band announced on social media its Voyage to the Blue Planet Tour, which includes a stop at San Francisco's Chase Center on Oct. 9.
