California regulators propose $45 million fine for PG&E over Dixie Fire
-----
California regulators Monday announced Pacific Gas and Electric Co. could face $45 million in fines for violating state laws leading up to the 2021 Dixie Fire that burned nearly 1 million acres in the Sierra Nevada.Cal Fire investigators determined PG&E equipment ignited the Dixie Fire on July 13, 2021, when a tree fell on energized power lines in the Feather River Canyon in remote Butte County.
