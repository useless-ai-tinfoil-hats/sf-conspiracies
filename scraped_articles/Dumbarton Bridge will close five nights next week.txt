Dumbarton Bridge will close five nights next week
-----
The eastbound lanes of Highway 84 on the Dumbarton Bridge are scheduled to close during five nights next week for repaving work, Caltrans said.The bridge will be closed Monday through Friday between 7 p.m. and 5 a.m. while crews repave the roadway from the toll plaza to the end of the bridge, aiming to "create a smoother ride for motorists."
Caltrans said it expects the closures will affect motorists traveling from San Francisco and San Mateo, and those traveling from the South Bay
