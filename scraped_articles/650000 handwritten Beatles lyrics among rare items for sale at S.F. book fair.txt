$650,000 handwritten Beatles lyrics among rare items for sale at S.F. book fair
-----
Beatles fans will have a unique opportunity to acquire a rare piece of the band's history next month in San Francisco. But it might require a substantial investment, possibly even a second mortgage.The 56th California International Antiquarian Book Fair, scheduled for Feb. 9-11 at San Francisco's Pier 27, will feature a page from Paul McCartney's notebook, showcasing handwritten lyrics for the song "Lovely Rita."
