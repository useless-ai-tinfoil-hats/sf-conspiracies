Review: Meg Ryan’s ‘What Happens Later’ is a fun rom-com — and a serious movie — about mature love
-----
People tend not to take romantic comedies seriously, and so it may be a while before "What Happens Later" gets recognized for the artful and lovely breakthrough that it is. It's a movie about love in late middle age, and thankfully it has nothing to do with death or somebody getting sick, but rather about passion, regret and staying awake to the promise of life.The film stars Meg Ryan, the exact person you'd want in a movie like this.
