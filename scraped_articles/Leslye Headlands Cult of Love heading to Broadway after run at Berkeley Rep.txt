Leslye Headland’s ‘Cult of Love’ heading to Broadway after run at Berkeley Rep
-----
Leslye Headland's "Cult of Love," which ran at Berkeley Repertory Theatre earlier this year, is heading to Broadway."Cult of Love" marks the Broadway debut for Headland, best known as co-creator of the Netflix series "Russian Doll," and the last in her series of "Seven Deadly Plays," based on the seven deadly sins. "Cult of Love" specifically addresses pride.
