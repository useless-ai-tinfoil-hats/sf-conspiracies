Palestinian film ‘The Teacher’ captures SFFilm’s top audience award
-----
With the Israel-Hamas war dividing the political spectrum and sparking protests in cities and on college campuses across the nation, San Francisco International Film Festival audiences responded to "The Teacher," a movie about Palestinian life in the West Bank under Israeli occupation.Farah Nabulsi's feature debut won the Audience Award for narrative feature, organizers of the 67th edition of the oldest film festival in the Americas announced Tuesday, April 30.
