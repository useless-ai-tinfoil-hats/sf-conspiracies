Oakland residents urge reinstatement of fired police chief as commission churns
-----
Nearly a dozen Oakland residents called for reinstatement of the city's former police chief, LeRonne Armstrong, at a raucous town hall Thursday night organized by some members of the police commission.Commissioners held a scheduled meeting at City Hall where they heard from citizens about the former police chief. Many people were frustrated and emotional, saying Armstrong was what Oakland needed amid an uptick in crime and increased concerns about public safety.
