Has S.F. turned the corner on retail theft? New reports suggest it has
-----
While videos of brazen lootings of San Francisco chain stores continue to rack up views on social media, several new reports — as well as fresh statistics from over the Thanksgiving weekend — suggest that the city's crackdown on organized shoplifting rings may be paying off.Larceny cases over Thanksgiving weekend were down about 75% from last year, with 100 reported thefts compared with 401 in 2022, according to the San Francisco Police Department.
