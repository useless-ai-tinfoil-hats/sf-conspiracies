Pleasant Hill standoff: Ex-officer arrested after shooting at vehicles, police say
-----
A former police detective was taken into custody after he shot at armored vehicles from inside his Pleasant Hill home early Saturday on the third day of a standoff between him, a SWAT team and crisis negotiators, police said.Chunliam Saechao barricaded himself in his home Thursday after shooting his wife when she tried to get inside through a barricaded garage door, according to police. She was taken to a hospital with non-life-threatening injuries and later released.
