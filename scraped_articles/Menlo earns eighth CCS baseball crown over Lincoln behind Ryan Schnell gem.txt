Menlo earns eighth CCS baseball crown over Lincoln behind Ryan Schnell gem
-----
In this age of big arms and lots of strikeouts, Menlo School senior Ryan Schnell is refreshingly crafty. And effective.The right-hander threw a four-hitter with four strikeouts and one walk Thursday, leading the Knights to their eighth Central Coast Section crown and second straight with a 3-1 Division 6 title win over Lincoln-San Jose at Excite Ballpark in San Jose.
