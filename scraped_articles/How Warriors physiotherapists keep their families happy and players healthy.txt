How Warriors' physiotherapists keep their families happy and players healthy
-----
Rick Celebrini didn't think Danielle Langford — or Nadine Terlicher — would consider his offer.Working for the Golden State Warriors as physiotherapists meant leaving Vancouver — and their families.
Rather abruptly.
Said the Warriors' vice president of player health and performance, he, too, hailing from British Columbia: "I took a swing for it and tried to make it as manageable as possible. Both of them have so far made it work and we're just so lucky to have them."
