This luxury Hawaii resort is likely the island's most expensive
-----
Hawaii's mission to attract higher-spending tourists may be playing out at the rebranded and reopened Kona Village on the Big Island. At the very least, guests seem undeterred when it comes to knocking down money on a stay at what is likely now the island of Hawaii's most expensive resort.Designed to resemble the traditional homes of the long-gone fishing village of Kaupulehu, Kona Village's new thatched-roof, wood-paneled bungalows exude eco-friendly luxury, with a price to match.
