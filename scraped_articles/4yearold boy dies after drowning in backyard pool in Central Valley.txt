4-year-old boy dies after drowning in backyard pool in Central Valley
-----
A 4-year-old boy died after accidentally drowning in a backyard swimming pool in the Central Valley, officials said. The drowning happened just before 11:30 a.m. on Monday at a home at 542 Cistus Court in Manteca, about 80 miles east of San Francisco, the Manteca Police Department said in a news release.The child's mother was home and found him unresponsive. His brother was also home, but neither of the family members knew the child had gone into the backyard pool, police said.
