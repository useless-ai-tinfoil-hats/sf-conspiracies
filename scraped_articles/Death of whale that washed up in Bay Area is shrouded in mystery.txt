Death of whale that washed up in Bay Area is shrouded in mystery
-----
A humpback whale washed ashore about 2 miles south of the Ritz-Carlton hotel in Half Moon Bay over the weekend, and experts said its cause of death remains a mystery.The carcass has been monitored by both the California Academy of Sciences and the Marine Mammal Center since it was first spotted about three weeks ago, floating in the surf near Eel Rock, a small island off the coast of San Mateo County.
