Earthquake swarm hits just south of Bay Area near San Andreas Fault
-----
An earthquake swarm in Monterey County rattled California's Central Coast on Thursday, according to the U.S. Geological Survey.Five separate quakes, ranging in magnitude from 2.6 to 3.3, all hit within about a two-hour time span. The first in the series, a magnitude 3.3 quake, hit at 9:32 a.m.; the last, a magnitude 2.8 shaker, struck at 12:17 p.m.
