Review: In ‘Larry the Musical,’ Filipinos fight to make labor leader a household name
-----
In "Larry the Musical: An American Journey," fighting is a critical theme. Filipinos who came to the United States for the same reason all immigrants come to the country had to fight to be heard, seen and respected.To hear Larry Itliong say it, the mission is clear: "Let's fight for the voiceless."
The Filipino American civil rights activist, who died in 1977, has been ignored by mainstream historians for decades when he should be more widely known as one of the fathers of the West
