Outside Lands 2024 presale tickets sell out ‘almost instantly’
-----
Early bird tickets for this year's Outside Lands vanished within an hour, with VIP passes disappearing "almost instantly," according Another Planet Entertainment, the festival's promoter.While the lineup for the 16th annual festival, scheduled for Aug. 9-11 in San Francisco's Golden Gate Park, will not be revealed until April 23, fans wasted no time securing discounted three-day passes during the presale event on Wednesday, April 3.
