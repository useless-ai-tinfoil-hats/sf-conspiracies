Bay Area rap legend E-40 to join post-debate Biden rally
-----
E-40 has shared the stage with plenty of big names (Snoop Dogg, Big Sean) during his career, but none quite like the person he'll share it with Friday: Joe Biden. The Vallejo rapper will join the president, First Lady Jill Biden and rapper Fat Joe at a rally in Raleigh, North Carolina, Axios reported.
