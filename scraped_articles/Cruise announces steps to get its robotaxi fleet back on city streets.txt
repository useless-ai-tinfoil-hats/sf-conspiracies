Cruise announces steps to get its robotaxi fleet back on city streets
-----
Six months after a Cruise robotaxi dragged a San Francisco pedestrian and pinned her under the vehicle, officials for the autonomous vehicle company on Tuesday announced the beginning of what they hope is their return to driverless operations.The California Department of Motor Vehicles suspended Cruise from operating its driverless taxis in San Francisco about three weeks after the Oct. 3 crash, alleging that the company withheld footage of the incident.
