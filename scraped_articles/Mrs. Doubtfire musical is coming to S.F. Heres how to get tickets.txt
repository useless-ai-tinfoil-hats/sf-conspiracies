‘Mrs. Doubtfire’ musical is coming to S.F. Here’s how to get tickets
-----
Beloved nanny Mrs. Doubtfire is set to return to San Francisco for a summer of musical laughs.The stage adaptation of the classic film, which was shot in the Bay Area and starred Robin Williams and Sally Field, opened in Seattle in 2019 and expects to close out its U.S. tour at the Orpheum Theatre with a limited run, July 2-28.
Tony Award-nominated performer Rob McClure will reprise his role as Daniel Hillard, a recent divorcee who pretends to be a female nanny named Euphegenia
