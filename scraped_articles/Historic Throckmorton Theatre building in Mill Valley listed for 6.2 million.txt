Historic Throckmorton Theatre building in Mill Valley listed for $6.2 million
-----
The century-old Throckmorton Theatre, a cultural cornerstone in Mill Valley, is up for sale for $6.2 million. This does not spell the end for the beloved venue, which has hosted events ranging from concerts and Broadway musicals to youth productions and comedy shows featuring luminaries such as Robin Williams and Mort Sahl.Located near Lytton Square, the building at 138-144 Throckmorton Ave. dates to 1912 and once hosted silent-film icon Charlie Chaplin.
