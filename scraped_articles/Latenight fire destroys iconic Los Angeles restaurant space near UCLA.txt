Late-night fire destroys iconic Los Angeles restaurant space near UCLA
-----
A fire at an iconic Los Angeles restaurant space brought streets to a standstill late Wednesday night.The beloved former Junior's Deli, an East Coast-style Jewish delicatessen near UCLA that first opened in 1959, was deemed a knockdown after a heavily destructive fire ripped through a line of commercial buildings on Westwood Boulevard, per the Los Angeles Fire Department.
No injuries were reported in the late-night blaze, though it did take nearly two hours to extinguish the flames
