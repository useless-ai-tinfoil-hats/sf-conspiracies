Former Contra Costa County deputy pleads no contest to possessing illegal gun, filing false police report
-----
A former Contra Costa County sheriff's deputy accused of stealing gun parts he seized during an investigation pleaded no contest Wednesday to three felonies.Matthew Buckley, 42, was sentenced to 3 years, 8 months in prison. As part of a plea deal, he will be allowed to serve the time on supervised release so long as he completes a six-month drug rehab program, according to the Contra Costa County District Attorney's Office.
