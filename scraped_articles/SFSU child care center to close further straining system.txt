SFSU child care center to close, further straining system
-----
A child care center at San Francisco State University that largely serves faculty and staff while providing training opportunities for students is slated to shut down next year, further constraining an overwhelmed day care system.Children's Campus, a day care and preschool located on campus near Lake Merced, will shutter by Aug. 1, 2025, as the university faces an enrollment and budget downturn.
