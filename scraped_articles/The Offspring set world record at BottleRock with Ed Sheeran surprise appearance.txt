The Offspring set ‘world record’ at BottleRock with Ed Sheeran surprise appearance
-----
The Offspring drew one of the most impressive crowds during the last day of BottleRock Napa Valley 2024 — in part thanks to a surprise appearance by Ed Sheeran.On Sunday, May 26, as the Orange County punk band played in the sweltering Napa heat on the Wine Country festival's Verizon Stage — after frontman Dexter Holland and guitarist Kevin "Noodles" Wasserman cooked up hot wings with Food Network star Alton Brown on the Williams Sonoma Culinary Stage — the rockers shocked festivalgoers
