Inside the SF restaurant that hosted Dodgers' Shohei Ohtani during free agency
-----
In the world of Bay Area food media "best of" listicles and viral influencers making food content, Akari Japanese Bistro is almost never mentioned. However, around Major League Baseball, it's no secret that the San Francisco kaiseki restaurant that's barely 2 years old is one of the finest Japanese dining destinations the city has to offer.And while the Bay Area may not know it yet, the Giants clearly do. This is the restaurant they chose as a stop during important free agency meetings
