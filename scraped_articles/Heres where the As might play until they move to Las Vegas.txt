Here's where the A's might play until they move to Las Vegas
-----
The path for the A's to move to Las Vegas has been cleared by Major League Baseball.MLB's 30 owners did as expected and unanimously gave the A's and owner John Fisher approval to relocate to Las Vegas at the league's owners meeting Thursday morning in Arlington, Texas (The Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms). The approval comes five months after the Nevada legislature passed Senate Bill 1, making $380 million of taxpayer dollars available to the A's
