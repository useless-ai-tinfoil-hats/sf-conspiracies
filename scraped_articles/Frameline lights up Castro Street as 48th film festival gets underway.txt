Frameline lights up Castro Street as 48th film festival gets underway
-----
It was almost beside the point that there was a movie screened at the opening night of the 48th Frameline film festival.By the time the documentary "Lil Nas X: Long Live Montero" began showing on the digital screen on Castro Street between 17th and 18th streets on Wednesday, June 19, the Juneteenth-themed block party that kick-started the San Francisco International LGBTQ+ Film Festival was hours underway.
