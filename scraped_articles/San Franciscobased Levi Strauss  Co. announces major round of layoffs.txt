San Francisco-based Levi Strauss & Co. announces major round of layoffs
-----
San Francisco-based Levi Strauss & Co. is the latest Bay Area company to announce a major round of layoffs. In a quarterly report released Thursday, the denim company said it would be reducing its global corporate workforce by 10% to 15% over the coming months.The cuts are part of an initiative that Levi Strauss hopes will save the company $100 million, and are expected to come within the first half of the year. The quarterly report did not indicate which offices the layoffs will affect.
