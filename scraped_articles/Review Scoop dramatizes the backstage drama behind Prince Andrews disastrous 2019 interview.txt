Review: ‘Scoop’ dramatizes the backstage drama behind Prince Andrew’s disastrous 2019 interview
-----
Until recently, it was possible to make a complete fool of yourself on television and not have somebody make a movie about it. For example, though Dan Quayle will be remembered eternally for being told by Lloyd Bentsen that he was "no Jack Kennedy," no one has chosen to dramatize the 1988 vice presidential debate.No such luck for Britain's Prince Andrew, whose embarrassing and disastrous 2019 BBC interview has been made into the solid Netflix movie "Scoop."
