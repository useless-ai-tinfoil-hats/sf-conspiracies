Red Hot Chili Peppers to do casino tour of California
-----
Californians may scratch their heads at some of the Red Hot Chili Peppers' newly announced tour dates.On Monday, the band announced twenty new dates for its Unlimited Love Tour, set to run from December 9 to July 30. Starting in the Chili Peppers' home city of Los Angeles and winding through the Pacific Northwest and Midwest, the new stretch features a revolving cast of support acts, among them Kid Cudi, Otoboke Beaver and, oddly enough, Ice Cube.
