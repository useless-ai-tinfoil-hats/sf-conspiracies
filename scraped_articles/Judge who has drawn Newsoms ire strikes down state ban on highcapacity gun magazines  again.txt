Judge who has drawn Newsom’s ire strikes down state ban on high-capacity gun magazines – again
-----
A federal judge who has repeatedly overturned California's firearms laws struck down the state's voter-approved ban on large gun magazines Friday, saying it has no historical basis, violates individuals' rights and threatens their safety."There is no American history or tradition of regulating firearms based on the number of rounds they can shoot, or of regulating the amount of ammunition that can be kept and carried," said U.S. District Judge Roger Benitez of San Diego in a ruling
