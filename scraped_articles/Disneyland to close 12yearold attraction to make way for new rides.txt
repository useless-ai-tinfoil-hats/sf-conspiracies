Disneyland to close 12-year-old attraction to make way for new rides
-----
A 12-year-old attraction introduced in the much-needed renovation of Disney California Adventure is closing to make way for Disneyland's next expansion. The Red Car Trolley will close next year, theme park journalist Scott Gustin first reported Monday.The trolleys were added in 2012 as part of California Adventure's first major revamp.
