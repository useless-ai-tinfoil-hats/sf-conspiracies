Oakland's Horn Barbecue red-tagged after fire rips through building
-----
Two days after Horn Barbecue in West Oakland was defaced with graffiti, the restaurant's building was red-tagged after a fire caused significant damage Tuesday morning, officials said.The Oakland Fire Department received a call at 4:19 a.m. of a fire at the rear of the restaurant at 2534 Mandela Pkwy., Oakland Fire Department spokesperson Michael Hunt said.
