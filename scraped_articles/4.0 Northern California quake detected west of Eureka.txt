4.0 Northern California quake detected west of Eureka
-----
A 4.0-magnitude earthquake struck in the Pacific Ocean about 41 miles west of Eureka in Humboldt County on Friday morning, according to preliminary data from the U.S. Geological Survey.* Earthquake tracker: Mapping the latest quakes in California and across the world
The quake was detected at 9:26 a.m. at a depth of just under a mile beneath the earth's surface, USGS data showed.
There were no immediate reports of damage as a result of the quake.
