Kanye West drops price of Malibu estate from $53 million to $39 million
-----
Kanye West has slashed the listing price of a mansion on the California coast that he acquired for $57.25 million just three years ago, after months of struggling to find a buyer.The platinum-selling rapper, now known as Ye, is offering the property at 24844 Malibu Road, which he purchased in 2021, for $39 million after gutting and abandoning it.
