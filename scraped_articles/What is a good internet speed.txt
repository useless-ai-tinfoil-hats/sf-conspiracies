What is a good internet speed?
-----
A good internet speed depends on what you use the internet for and how many people will be connected at any given time.For someone who mainly sends emails and downloads a few photos here and there, a slower speed may be good enough. But for households with avid gamers or several people working remotely, faster speeds will be necessary.
