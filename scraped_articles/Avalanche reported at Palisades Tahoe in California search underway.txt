Avalanche reported at Palisades Tahoe in California, search underway
-----
An avalanche sent an explosion of snow down a mountainside at Palisades Tahoe in California's Olympic Valley around 9:30 a.m. Wednesday, according to a statement from the ski resort on social media. The avalanche occurred "on the Palisades side, specifically above the GS gully area of KT-22," the resort said.The Placer County Sheriff's Office said it was responding to the scene of the avalanche and would provide updates as they become available. A patrol team was on the mountain
