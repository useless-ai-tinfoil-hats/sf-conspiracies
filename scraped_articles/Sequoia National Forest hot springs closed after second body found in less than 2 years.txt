Sequoia National Forest hot springs closed after second body found in less than 2 years
-----
Miracle Hot Springs along California's Kern River will close indefinitely after the death of a second person there in less than two years.The U.S. Forest Service said in a news release this week that a body was found in one of the hot springs' manufactured stone-and-mortar tubs on Feb. 17. Another death was reported in the same location in October 2022.
Specific details about the victims and what caused their deaths were not provided by the agency.
