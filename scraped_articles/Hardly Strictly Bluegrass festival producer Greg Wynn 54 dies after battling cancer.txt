Hardly Strictly Bluegrass festival producer Greg Wynn, 54, dies after battling cancer
-----
Greg Wynn, a producer for San Francisco's Hardly Strictly Bluegrass festival, died Thursday after a two-year battle with cancer, a publicist for the festival confirmed. He was 54 years old.For nearly two decades, Wynn served as a cornerstone of the free annual festival in Golden Gate Park's operational team, overseeing critical offstage functions including parking logistics, decor arrangements and security measures.
In a 2018 interview with the Chronicle, Wynn described his role.
