Record collectors of all ages flock to S.F. to find solace in vinyl
-----
One of San Francisco's most storied record shops is Amoeba Music, and it is always bustling.Rain or shine, music lovers shuffle in and out of the store, some staying for hours to browse the shelves. On a recent visit just before Record Store Day, which lands on Saturday, April 20, this year, dozens paced through the aisles, searching for the perfect album to add to their collections.
