NorCal D-1 championships for Amador Valley softball, Granada baseball
-----
Mountain View to Fresno to El Dorado Hills. The Amador Valley-Pleasanton softball team ventured to some true hot spots of Northern California last week, an apparent Bermuda Triangle of a trip for the No. 8 seed of the Northern California Division 1 tournament.But instead of quickly vanishing, the Dons turned the trek into a dream vacation, finishing off Saturday with a wild three-hour, nine-inning 13-11 championship game victory at Oak Ridge-El Dorado Hills.
