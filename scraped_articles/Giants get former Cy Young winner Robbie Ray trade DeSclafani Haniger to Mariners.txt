Giants get former Cy Young winner Robbie Ray, trade DeSclafani, Haniger to Mariners
-----
Weeks after missing out on MLB's top two free agents, Shohei Ohtani and Yoshinobu Yamamoto, the San Francisco Giants took an alternate route to landing talent by trading for a former Cy Young Award winner.Swinging a deal with a familiar trading partner, the Seattle Mariners, the Giants acquired left-hander Robbie Ray in exchange for outfielder Mitch Haniger, pitcher Anthony DeSclafani and cash considerations.
