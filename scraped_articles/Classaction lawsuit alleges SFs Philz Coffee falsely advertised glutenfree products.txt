Class-action lawsuit alleges SF's Philz Coffee falsely advertised gluten-free products
-----
Popular Bay Area coffee chain Philz Coffee has been hit with a class-action lawsuit alleging the company "misbrands and falsely advertises" certain products as gluten-free even though they contain gluten.Plaintiff Ayaunna Martinez, a San Francisco resident, filed the lawsuit to the San Francisco Superior Court on Jan. 23 on behalf of herself and other class members. The lawsuit alleges that in August 2023, Martinez suffered a severe allergy attack after visiting a Philz Coffee location
