Flight attendants for LA Dodgers sue United Airlines over alleged discrimination
-----
A pair of flight attendants working charter flights for the Los Angeles Dodgers filed a lawsuit against United Airlines alleging racial and age discrimination, among other claims.The lawsuit, filed Wednesday in Los Angeles County Superior Court, accuses members of United's management and the traveling secretary of the Dodgers of selecting flight attendants based on race and appearance to work on charter flights for the team.
