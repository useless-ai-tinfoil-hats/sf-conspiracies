Amber Alert in Vallejo canceled after infant is found safe
-----
An Amber Alert issued Saturday morning after an alleged parental abduction in Vallejo was canceled after authorities said the boy was found safe.The California Highway Patrol reported the incident at 111 Amador St. shortly after 3 a.m.
Officials said the 2-week-old boy was taken by his 28-year-old mother.
Vallejo police were assisting the Solano County Child Protective Services in locating the boy.
