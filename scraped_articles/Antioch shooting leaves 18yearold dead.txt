Antioch shooting leaves 18-year-old dead
-----
An 18-year-old man was fatally shot in a residential neighborhood near Antioch High School on Monday evening, police said.The shooting was reported around 6 p.m. on Macaulay Street, just north of the school.
Officers found the man near the driveway, and paramedics took him to a hospital, where he died, police said.
Investigators identified him as Henry Granado II of Pittsburg.
Police did not say what led up to the shooting.
