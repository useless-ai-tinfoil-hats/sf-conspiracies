Cruise’s robotaxis are returning to paid driverless ride-hailing — with the help of a competitor
-----
General Motors' Cruise hasn't given up on its effort to deploy autonomous vehicles for paid ride-hailing and is now teaming up with a former competitor to reach that goal.Cruise announced on Thursday that it's partnering with Uber to bring the company's robotaxis to the ride-hailing giant's platform. The companies' announcement characterized the alliance as "a multiyear strategic partnership," though it included few details about where and at what scale Cruise's robotaxis would be used
