Woman dies, over 100 people trapped after flash flood in Grand Canyon National Park
-----
LATEST Aug. 25, 7:31 p.m. The body of Chenoa Nickerson, 33, was recovered by Grand Canyon National Park rangers, the park announced Sunday evening. A group rafting in the Colorado River found Nickerson's body around River Mile 176 at around 11:30 a.m., the park's statement said.Nickerson's body was transported to the rim by helicopter and transferred to the Coconino County Medical Examiner's office.
Aug. 25, 3:38 p.m. Over 100 people were stranded and one woman was reported missing
