277,000 gallons of sewage spills into Russian River in Sonoma County, officials say
-----
Last weekend's heavy storms caused a sewage spill into the Russian River in Sonoma County, water district officials reported Thursday.The spill, which happened overnight Friday into Saturday at the Russian River County Sanitation District plant in Guerneville, caused 277,000 gallons of partially treated wastewater to overflow onto the treatment plant property, Sonoma Water said in a news release.
