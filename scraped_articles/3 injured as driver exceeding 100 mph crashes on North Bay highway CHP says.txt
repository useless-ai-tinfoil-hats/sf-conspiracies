3 injured as driver exceeding 100 mph crashes on North Bay highway, CHP says
-----
Two adults and a child were injured in a car crash involving a driver who was fleeing from authorities on Highway 101, the California Highway Patrol said.CHP authorities tried to stop a driver in a Ford Mustang for speeding in Rohnert Park about 1:30 p.m., but he fled onto northbound Highway 101 "at speeds exceeding 100 mph," the agency said in a statement.
The driver of the Ford Mustang collided with a sports utility vehicle, causing major damage to both cars, officials said.
