Ed Sheeran smashes Levi’s Stadium attendance record, beating Taylor Swift, Beyoncé
-----
Ed Sheeran has smashed the attendance record at Levi's Stadium.The British singer-songwriter's performance at the venue on Saturday, Sept. 16, drew nearly 80,000 people to the home of the San Francisco 49ers, eclipsing the audiences drawn by Taylor Swift and Beyoncé earlier this summer.
