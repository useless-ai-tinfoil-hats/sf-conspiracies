Hawaii home mistakenly built on Bay Area woman's land to be torn down
-----
A bizarre nightmare that started last summer when a Bay Area woman discovered that a house had been mysteriously built on her acre of pristine Hawaii land may be nearing an end.A Hawaii judge has ordered that the home mistakenly built on Anne Reynold's land on Hawaii's Big Island be torn down, a court ruling seen by SFGATE shows.
"This was not an instance of minor encroachment," the court order read, "but an entire house was built on Lot 114 instead of Lot 115."
