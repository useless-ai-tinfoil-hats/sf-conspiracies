Giants pound Dodgers as Spencer Bevins surprises with five strong innings
-----
Spencer Bivens was expected to be the opener Sunday when the San Francisco Giants concluded their three-game series against the Dodgers. Maybe two innings of work, three at a real push.There he was in the fifth, though, striking out Shohei Ohtani for the second time to end the inning, whirling, pumping his fists and yelling after the longest outing of his career. His excellent work helped the Giants to a 10-4 victory and a series win over first-place Los Angeles.
