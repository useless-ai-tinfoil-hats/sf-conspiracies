Boy, 12, struck, killed by car in San Jose
-----
A boy was struck and killed by a car Tuesday afternoon in San Jose, police said.The collision occurred around 4:21 p.m. in the area of Leigh Avenue and Camden Avenue. The boy was pronounced dead at the scene, police said. ABC 7, a local TV station, reported that the boy was 12 years old.
