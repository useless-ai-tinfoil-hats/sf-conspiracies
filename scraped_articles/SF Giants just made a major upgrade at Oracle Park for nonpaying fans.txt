SF Giants just made a major upgrade at Oracle Park for nonpaying fans
-----
The San Francisco Giants typically worry about things at Oracle Park for their paying customers, but they just made a change that will serve fans who don't even enter the ballpark.The Giants installed a massive television on their stadium's portwalk over the weekend, with the TV facing out to McCovey Cove. It means that kayakers and boaters in the waterway now have a way to watch the game.
