BART police arrested teen suspected in East Bay double shooting
-----
BART police arrested a teenager they allege shot two men at an East Bay BART station last Friday.The 17-year-old boy surrendered to BART police detectives in Walnut Creek on Tuesday morning, four days after he allegedly shot two men inside the Pittsburg Center Station, according to BART police. The teen was not identified publicly.
