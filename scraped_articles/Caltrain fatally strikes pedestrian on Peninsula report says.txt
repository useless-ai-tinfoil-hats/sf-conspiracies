Caltrain fatally strikes pedestrian on Peninsula, report says
-----
A Caltrain struck and killed a pedestrian north of Redwood City Station on Friday afternoon, a local news station reported.The commuter train hit the person just before 3 p.m., KTVU reported. It was carrying 149 passengers at the time.
Transit police are investigating the death, and information about the victim was not immediately available Saturday.
Nearly an hour after the accident, another train arrived to carry the remaining passengers on the train.
