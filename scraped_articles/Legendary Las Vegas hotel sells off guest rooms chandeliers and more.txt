Legendary Las Vegas hotel sells off guest rooms, chandeliers and more
-----
Las Vegas history buffs and business owners alike have been perusing the Tropicana one last time as the hotel sells everything that's not bolted down.The Tropicana, one of the oldest hotels on the Strip, closed permanently in April to make way for the new A's ballpark. The hotel was in a sad state long before the official closure; demolition of smaller buildings is already underway, with implosion of the main structure scheduled to take place later this year.
