Civil rights leader apologizes to S.F. rapper over conduct in Breed ‘diss track’ saga
-----
A prominent local civil rights leader has apologized to a San Francisco rapper and restaurant owner over how he reacted to a video the rapper made slamming Mayor London Breed over crime in the city.The apology from Rev. Amos Brown comes just over a week after Chino Yang said he had been threatened by Brown, a close ally of Breed, and pressured into apologizing for the video.
