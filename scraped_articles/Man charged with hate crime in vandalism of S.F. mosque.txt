Man charged with hate crime in vandalism of S.F. mosque
-----
A San Francisco man accused of using his skateboard to smash several windows at a mosque in the Lower Nob Hill neighborhood last week has been charged with a hate crime, prosecutors said Thursday.Robert Gray, 35, was charged with felony vandalism with the special allegation of a hate crime, according to the San Francisco District Attorney's Office.
