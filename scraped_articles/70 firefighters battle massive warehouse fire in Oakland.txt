70 firefighters battle massive warehouse fire in Oakland
-----
A fire that erupted in West Oakland Wednesday night left a warehouse gutted, officials said.The Oakland Fire Department said on social media that it was called to the fire on the 1700 block of 24th Street at 10:15 p.m. The building housed a roofing company, the department said. Some 70 firefighters battled flames at the site. "Area residents should keep windows closed," the fire department added in the post.
Just after midnight, the fire department reported the blaze was contained
