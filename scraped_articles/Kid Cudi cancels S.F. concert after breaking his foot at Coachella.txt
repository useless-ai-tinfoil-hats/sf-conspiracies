Kid Cudi cancels S.F. concert after breaking his foot at Coachella
-----
Kid Cudi has been forced to postpone his forthcoming Insano: Engage the Rage Tour, which included a stop in San Francisco, after breaking his foot at Coachella over the weekend.The North American leg of the tour was to kick off June 28 in Austin and make its way to the Bay Area for a concert at Chase Center on Aug. 20, before wrapping up in Los Angeles on Aug. 30. The tour was in support of his ninth studio album, "Insano," which was released in January.
