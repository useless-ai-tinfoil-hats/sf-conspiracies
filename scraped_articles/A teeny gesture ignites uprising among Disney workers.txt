A teeny gesture ignites uprising among Disney workers
-----
Even the smallest of buttons can shoulder the weight of a workers' rights dispute.Four unions representing thousands of Disneyland employees filed an unfair labor practice charge this week claiming that its members were "intimidated, surveilled and disciplined" for pinning union buttons on their park costumes in reference to an ongoing contract campaign.
