SF Giants part ways with beloved PA announcer Renel Brooks-Moon after 24 seasons
-----
The San Francisco Giants announced Monday that beloved public address announcer Renel Brooks-Moon would not return to her role this upcoming season.In a statement, the team said that the two sides were unable to come to an agreement on a contract extension, and agreed to "mutually and amicably" split up.
