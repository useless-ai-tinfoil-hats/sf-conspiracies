Lily Gladstone, ‘Reservation Dogs’ star gets spotlight at 48th American Indian Film Festival
-----
Lily Gladstone's commanding performance in Martin Scorsese's "Killers of the Flower Moon" and the FX series "Reservation Dogs" have stoked interest in Native American stories for screens big and small.This month, both have a presence in the 48th American Indian Film Festival, which runs Nov. 3-11 at several Bay Area locations as well as online.
