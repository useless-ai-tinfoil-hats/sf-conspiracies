‘Definitely still a shortage’: Data shows the S.F. real estate market stuck in the mud
-----
The Bay Area housing market is heating back up — but not by much, with the mortgage rate drops that many home buyers were awaiting showing little sign of materializing anytime soon.More homes in the San Francisco metropolitan area are selling this year compared to early 2023, and properties are entering the market slightly faster. But those numbers are still far lower than they were prior in 2021 and 2022, and behind pre-pandemic levels.
