49ers in need of new QB backup plan as Sam Darnold exits for Vikings
-----
Quarterback Brock Purdy will have a new backup in 2024.That became official Monday night when QB Sam Darnold, the San Francisco 49ers' second-stringer last season, agreed to terms on a one-year, $10 million deal with the Vikings, NFL Network reported. Darnold, 26, will have a chance to compete for a starting spot with Minnesota, which had its longtime starter, Kirk Cousins, agree to a four-year, $180 million deal with the Falcons on Monday.
