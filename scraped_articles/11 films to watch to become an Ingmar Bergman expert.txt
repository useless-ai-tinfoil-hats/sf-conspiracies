11 films to watch to become an Ingmar Bergman expert
-----
In the opinion of many (me included), Ingmar Bergman (1918-2007) is the greatest filmmaker who ever lived. He left behind an enormous body of work of profound emotional depth, and to explore his films is like stepping into an entire universe.Yet that universe is so complete and multifaceted that I imagine it might be intimidating to novices. He made about 60 movies, depending on how you count, so where do you start? And where do you go next?
