GM to ‘substantially’ reduce Cruise investment after S.F. crash, CEO says
-----
General Motors plans to "substantially" scale back investments into the company's Cruise autonomous vehicle division next year, citing the crisis of confidence surrounding the safety of driverless cars.GM CEO Mary Barra said Wednesday in a letter to investors that the company needed to "rebuild trust with regulators at the local, state and federal levels, as well as with the first responders and the communities in which Cruise will operate."
