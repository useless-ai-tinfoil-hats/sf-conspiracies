Why did Stanford’s Tara VanDerveer decide to retire? ‘Nothing to do’ with ACC
-----
Tara VanDerveer is not leaving coaching because Stanford is going to the ACC. She emphasized that early in her retirement news conference on Wednesday.She had plenty of reasons, but said the shifting college sports landscape is not among them.
"I wanted to give myself time when the season was finished, but the time has felt right," VanDerveer said Wednesday. "It has nothing to do with going to the ACC — that was a motivator to want to stay."
