Which NWSL free agents could Bay FC sign to add star power to first roster?
-----
NWSL expansion team Bay FC added its first player in franchise history last week with the addition of midfielder Alex Loera in a trade with Kansas City. It won't fill the bulk of its inaugural roster until the league's expansion draft on Dec. 15, but free agency officially opens for the team — and the rest of the league — on Monday.For the first time, players only need five years of NWSL service to qualify for free agency, according to the new collective bargaining agreement.
