On this Bay Area bridge, a celebrated bike path might revert to being a breakdown lane for cars
-----
Regulators are contemplating dramatic constraints for a 4-mile bike path along the Richmond-San Rafael Bridge, long a source of controversy between cyclists and motorists.Soon, it may be open only Friday through Sunday.
