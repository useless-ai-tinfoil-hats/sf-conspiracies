Hit Netflix show ‘You Are What You Eat’ started with this groundbreaking Stanford study
-----
Stanford University scientists are the driving force behind a hit Netflix documentary "You Are What You Eat: A Twin Experiment" that tries to answer the question of how to eat for better health.But how solid is the science in the show, which features Bay Area residents as test subjects, and what does it tell us about how to nourish ourselves?
At the heart of the Stanford research and the Netflix series is the question of whether a vegan diet is healthier than one that includes meat
