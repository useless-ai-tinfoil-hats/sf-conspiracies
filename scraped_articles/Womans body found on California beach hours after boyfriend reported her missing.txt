Woman's body found on California beach hours after boyfriend reported her missing
-----
A body was found on a Southern California beach hours after a man reported his girlfriend missing, authorities said.Early Friday morning, an unnamed man reported his girlfriend missing, telling authorities he last saw her near the waterline on Manhattan Beach, a few miles south of Los Angeles International Airport. The man told the Manhattan Beach Police Department that he and his friends were searching for the missing woman with no success.
