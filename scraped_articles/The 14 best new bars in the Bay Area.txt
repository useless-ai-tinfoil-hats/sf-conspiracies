The 14 best new bars in the Bay Area
-----
In the Bay Area beverage world, 2023 was undeniably the year of the wine bar. While once stuffy and staid, with bulky glasses and boring cheese plates, a flood of new bars from the Marina to San Jose has remade the genre.
