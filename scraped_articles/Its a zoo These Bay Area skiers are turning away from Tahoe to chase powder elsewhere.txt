‘It’s a zoo’: These Bay Area skiers are turning away from Tahoe to chase powder elsewhere
-----
One Friday morning last winter, San Francisco resident Brian Quinn loaded his skis, poles and boots into his car for a long weekend of skiing.But instead of driving east to Lake Tahoe, as he had done hundreds of times since learning on the Sierra slopes as a child, he headed south to the San Jose airport and hopped an 8 a.m. flight to Salt Lake City. By 11 a.m., he was carving turns at Park City in Utah, taking in the alpine air and loving life.
