Former health care executive arrested on suspicion of brutal Bay Area rape
-----
After 22 years, police in Oakley say they've arrested the man responsible for a brutal kidnapping and rape of a girl walking her dog.The girl was walking near Laurel Road in April 2002 when police say she was approached by a man with a gun. He allegedly ordered her into a car and sexually assaulted her at gunpoint. The girl was eventually able to escape and run to a home in the neighborhood for help. Police at the time took DNA samples, but no match was found to a suspect.
