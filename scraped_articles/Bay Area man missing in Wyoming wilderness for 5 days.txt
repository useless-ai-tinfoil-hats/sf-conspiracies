Bay Area man missing in Wyoming wilderness for 5 days
-----
A Bay Area man has been missing in the remote Wyoming wilderness for more than five days as of Wednesday, according to spokespersons from the Fremont County Sheriff's Office.Mountain View resident Steve Keller, 76, was last seen in the Wind River range near Steamboat Lake on Saturday before he was separated from his hiking group, according to the Sheriff's Office and his niece Betsy Briggs Miller, who posted about her uncle on Facebook.
