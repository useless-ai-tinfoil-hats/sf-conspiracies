California mom arrested on suspicion of killing 9-year-old daughter
-----
A Southern California woman was taken into custody at a shopping mall on suspicion of killing her nine-year-old daughter, police said.Khadiyjah Aliyyah Ali Pendergraph, 32, and her daughter resided on the 14100 block of Goldenwest Street in Westminster, a city in Orange County. Westminster police say they were called to the home on Friday after a worried family member called for a welfare check. They entered the home and found the girl dead from trauma, police said.
