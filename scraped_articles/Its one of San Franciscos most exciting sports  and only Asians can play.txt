It’s one of San Francisco’s most exciting sports — and only Asians can play
-----
On a warm Sunday at Lincoln Square Park in Oakland Chinatown, two nine-man teams lined up on opposite ends of a makeshift volleyball court, along serving lines drawn in white chalk. Each player sank into a deep bow before marching, single-file, to meet opponents at the net and shake hands.The pregame ritual is a nod to their sport's nearly century-old heritage in Chinese American communities.
