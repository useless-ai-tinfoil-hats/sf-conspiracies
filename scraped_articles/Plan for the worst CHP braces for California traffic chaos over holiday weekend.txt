'Plan for the worst': CHP braces for California traffic chaos over holiday weekend
-----
Take note if you're hitting the road during Memorial Day weekend: The California Highway Patrol will deploy "maximum enforcement" throughout the state as it prepares for traffic chaos. All available officers will be on duty specifically looking for impaired drivers, not just from alcohol, but also impairment from other substances, CHP spokesperson Art Montiel told SFGATE.
