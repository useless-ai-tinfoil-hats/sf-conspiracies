Body of mother who dove into California river to save daughter has been recovered
-----
UPDATE May 11, 6:22 p.m. The body of Brenda Duran, who dove into the San Joaquin River to save her 11-year-old daughter, has been recovered, the Stanislaus County Sheriff's Office announced Saturday.A young mother disappeared after jumping into the San Joaquin River to save her child from drowning Thursday evening, the Stanislaus County Sheriff's Office wrote in a May 10 Facebook post.
