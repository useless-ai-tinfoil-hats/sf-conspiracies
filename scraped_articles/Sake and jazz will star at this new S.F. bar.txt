Sake and jazz will star at this new S.F. bar
-----
Here's reporter Elena Kadvany:The owner of a pioneering Bay Area natural wine shop is expanding with a new bar in San Francisco.
Alex Bernardo of Vineyard Gate in Millbrae is opening a sake bar at 1327 Mason St. Vineyard Gate Saké Bar will occupy a tiny space — just 400 square feet — and channel the intimate, serious vibe of bars in Japan.
