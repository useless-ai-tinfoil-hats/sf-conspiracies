3.7 quake with epicenter at SFO rattles Bay Area
-----
A magnitude 3.7 earthquake with an epicenter at the San Francisco International Airport shook the Bay Area on Friday night, according to the U.S. Geological Survey.A spokesperson for San Francisco International Airport said the airport conducted runway inspections after the 6:38 p.m. earthquake and found no damage. Within 20 minutes, the airport was back to normal operations, according to the spokesperson.
