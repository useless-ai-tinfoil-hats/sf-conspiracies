Israeli family relieved at one hostage’s release — but another remains captive
-----
UC Berkeley student Yael Nidam Kirsht felt overwhelming joy when her sister-in-law Rimon Kirsht was among the Israeli hostages released by Hamas in a negotiated swap that began Nov. 24. But that relief has been chilled by the reality that Rimon's husband is still being held captive and that war has irrevocably changed her world."For all of us, the joy of her being released is sort of bitter in a way as well," Nidam Kirsht said.
