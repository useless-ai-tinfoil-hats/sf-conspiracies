Dream Team completes sweep of Bay Raiders for S.F. Pro-Am title
-----
Salesian-Richmond alum Te'Jon Sawyer and Mitty alum Nigel Burris each scored 20 points to lead Dream Team to a 98-92 win over Bay Raiders on Wednesday night at Kezar Pavilion to sweep the best-of-three championship series of the San Francisco Pro-Am Basketball League.Dream Team prevailed 104-100 in Monday's series opener.
