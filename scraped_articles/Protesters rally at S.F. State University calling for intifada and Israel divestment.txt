Protesters rally at S.F. State University, calling for ‘intifada’ and Israel divestment
-----
As hundreds of San Francisco State students shouted "intifada!" and "Free! Free! Palestine, from the river to the sea!" on Monday, demonstrators set up a dozen tents in the middle of campus and joined a burgeoning nationwide student movement against Israel's 6-month old war in Gaza."Five, six, seven, eight, Israel is a terrorist state!" chanted students, many of them wrapped in Palestinian keffiyeh scarves. "Long live the intifada!"
