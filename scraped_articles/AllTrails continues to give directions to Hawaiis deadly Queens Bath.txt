AllTrails continues to give directions to Hawaii’s deadly Queen’s Bath
-----
Queen's Bath, one of the most dangerous spots in Hawaii, has been closed since Oct. 6. And yet San Francisco-based AllTrails, the "world's largest and most trusted outdoor platform," still gives directions to the spot, where there have been many rescues and drownings.
