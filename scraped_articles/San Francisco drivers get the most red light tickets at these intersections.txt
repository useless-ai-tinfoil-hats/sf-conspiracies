San Francisco drivers get the most red light tickets at these intersections
-----
Drivers barreling down Highway 1 out of the Presidio should think twice before speeding through the traffic signal to beat the red light: the red-light camera stationed at Park Presidio and Lake Street has issued almost 2,500 tickets in the past two years. That makes it San Francisco's most prolific red-light camera during that period.The camera is one of 19 at 13 different intersections in the city.
