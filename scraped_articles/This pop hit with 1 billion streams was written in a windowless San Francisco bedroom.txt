This pop hit with 1 billion streams was written in a windowless San Francisco bedroom
-----
Even if the name Linda Perry isn't familiar, you likely know every word of her most famous song. But the chart-topping 4 Non Blondes hit "What's Up?", which is often mistakenly referred to as "What's Going On" due to the chorus, is just one part of Perry's story. She is the definition of a multi-hyphenate — a singer, songwriter, musician, producer, manager and so on — and in juggling those roles, she struggles to know and understand her true self.That identity-grappling takes center
