When will Giants’ Blake Snell, Keaton Winn debut? And who starts Sunday?
-----
SAN DIEGO – Blake Snell and Keaton Winn are progressing toward making their season debuts. Neither, though, will start Sunday's series finale against the San Diego Padres.Who starts Sunday will be determined by how Saturday's game goes, manager Bob Melvin said without revealing the list of options.
