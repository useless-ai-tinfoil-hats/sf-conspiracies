At least 2 more Bay Area grocery stores have removed self-checkout
-----
More Bay Area retail stores have joined the ranks of those shutting down their self-checkout machines. Safeway officials confirmed to KTVU-TV on Monday that the chain is removing the kiosks at several stores due to theft issues, although they wouldn't specify which stores."Operational changes have been made at select stores throughout the Bay Area given the increasing amount of theft," read Safeway's statement provided to SFGATE.
