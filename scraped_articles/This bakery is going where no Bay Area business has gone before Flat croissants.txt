This bakery is going where no Bay Area business has gone before: Flat croissants
-----
The experimental croissant creations that have gone viral in recent years — the spiral, the cube, the croffle — have reached their inevitable, geometric conclusion. They've gone completely flat.Peninsula bakery Alexander's Patisserie announced in January their latest product, a golden, flattened croissant, with an Instagram video of a disembodied hand crushing a regular croissant with a terrifying thud.
