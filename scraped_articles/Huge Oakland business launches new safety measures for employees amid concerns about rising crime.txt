Huge Oakland business launches new safety measures for employees amid concerns about rising crime
-----
Another large Oakland business has launched new safety measures for employees — including escorts to and from BART stations — amid rising fears of crime downtown.Representatives of Clorox, the cleaning product manufacturer that opened its global headquarters in Oakland 110 years ago, said they have hired uniformed security guards to chaperone employees to the office from BART stations, parking garages, restaurants and coffee shops.
