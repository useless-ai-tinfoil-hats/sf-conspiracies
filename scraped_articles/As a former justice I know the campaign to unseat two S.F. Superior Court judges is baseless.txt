As a former justice, I know the campaign to unseat two S.F. Superior Court judges is baseless
-----
The fact that judicial elections look and feel like elections for partisan political offices masks the reasons they should be treated differently. Unlike the legislative and executive branches, trial courts do not make public policy. They apply legal rules and constitutional principles.
