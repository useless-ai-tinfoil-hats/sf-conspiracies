Abode Home Security review: Pros, cons, and pricing
-----
Abode Home Security at a glanceAbode is an affordable and highly customizable home security option. It integrates with speakers and other devices from more smart home brands than any other top home security company we've reviewed. One potential drawback to Abode is inconsistent customer service. It was largely fast and helpful for our team, but some customers have reported the opposite on third-party review platforms.
* Equipment costs: $39.99 to $399.99*
* Monthly service
