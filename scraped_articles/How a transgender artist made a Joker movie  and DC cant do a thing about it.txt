How a transgender artist made a Joker movie — and DC can’t do a thing about it
-----
No way. Someone did not make a low-budget Joker movie using DC comic book characters without getting permission, because … that would be crazy, right?But it's true. "The People's Joker" is the creation of Vera Drew, a transgender comedian, editor and writer who has worked in podcasts and television and was nominated for an Emmy for her work editing Sacha Baron Cohen's Showtime series "Who Is America?"
Drew, who co-wrote the film with comedy writer Bri LeRose, plays herself as well
