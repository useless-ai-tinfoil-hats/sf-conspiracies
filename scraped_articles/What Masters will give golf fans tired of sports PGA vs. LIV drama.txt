What Masters will give golf fans tired of sport’s PGA vs. LIV drama
-----
Augusta National romanticizes golf more than any venue other than Pebble Beach.The grass seems impossibly green, as if covered by a coat of paint. The azaleas offer even more vibrant colors. The elevation changes add a sense of drama — steeply uphill on No. 9, sharply downhill on No. 10, back up on No. 18.
History lurks everywhere, embedded in golf fans' memories: Jack Nicklaus charging, Arnold Palmer styling, Tiger Woods exulting, Phil Mickelson leaping.
