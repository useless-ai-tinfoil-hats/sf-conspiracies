S.F. construction site collapse kills utility worker in Lower Haight
-----
San Francisco Fire Department officials said a construction worker died Thursday after an excavated trench collapsed at a construction project in the Lower Haight, trapping the person beneath 8 feet of dirt.The worker became trapped around 10 a.m. at Divisadero and Oak streets, officials said.
