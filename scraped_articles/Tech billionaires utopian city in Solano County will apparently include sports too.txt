Tech billionaires' utopian city in Solano County will apparently include sports, too
-----
The much-maligned California Forever planned community for east Solano County has unveiled its newest way to try to rally support: a sprawling sports complex.Earlier this week, the group behind the would-be utopia unveiled a rendering for a Solano Sports Complex, a planned development that would include facilities for baseball, basketball, football, gymnastics, pickleball, rugby, soccer, softball, swimming, tennis, volleyball and more.
