1950s Bay Area burger joint to close its final restaurant
-----
The last remaining outpost of a much-loved South Bay burger joint announced it would be closing in the coming months.At 1349 Blossom Hill Road in San Jose, the Burger Pit's lease ends in April 2024 and won't be renewed due to an ongoing dispute over it, according to San Jose Spotlight. Co-owner Paul Berger, who spoke to San Jose Spotlight, said the restaurant building has been "out of compliance" with San Jose's building code for years.
