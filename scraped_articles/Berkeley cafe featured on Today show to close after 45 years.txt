Berkeley cafe featured on 'Today' show to close after 45 years
-----
A Berkeley cafe that became a national news story for its free breakfast tickets is closing after 45 years. Homemade Cafe, which opened in 1979 at the corner of Sacramento St. and Dwight Way, will serve its last breakfast on Jan. 1."It is with great sadness and disappointment that we have to close.
