Oakland author Tommy Orange illustrates the toll of intergenerational trauma in ‘Wandering Stars’
-----
When Tommy Orange first started imagining the characters and storylines for his second novel, "Wandering Stars," he was fairly certain he "didn't want to write anything historical."As a Cheyenne-Arapaho author attuned to the limiting, stereotypical ways Native people have been portrayed in everything from history books to movies and literature, the Oakland native felt that "we're always seen as stuck in history," as the vanquished in what he calls "America's longest war."
