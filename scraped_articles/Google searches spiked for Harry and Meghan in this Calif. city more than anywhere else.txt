Google searches spiked for Harry and Meghan in this Calif. city more than anywhere else
-----
The Central Coast city of Santa Barbara is known for lots of things: Spanish-style architecture, beautiful beaches, delicious food and wine, a world-class party university. But in 2023 it acquired one additional claim to fame. Santa Barbara is where Google searches for "Harry & Meghan" increased the most, according to Google.The Duke and Duchess of Sussex made big news on this side of the pond in 2019, when they ran away to North America on a "break" from their official duties.
