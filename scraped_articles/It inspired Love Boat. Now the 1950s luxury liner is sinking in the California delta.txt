It inspired ‘Love Boat.’ Now the 1950s luxury liner is sinking in the California delta
-----
This past week, as an old, giant cruise ship sank in the California delta, so too did the dreams of Chris Willson.The longtime boatman and his wife spent a decade living aboard the idled luxury liner called the Aurora in a far-flung slough 15 miles from Stockton where they worked to restore the 293-foot-long vessel to its former glory.
The ship, which Willson bought in 2008 after responding to a curious ad on Craigslist, has the distinction of bringing cruising to the masses
