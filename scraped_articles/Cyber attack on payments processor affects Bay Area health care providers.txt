Cyber attack on payments processor affects Bay Area health care providers
-----
Kaiser Permanente and John Muir Health are among the health care providers nationwide affected by a cyber attack on Change Healthcare, a widely used payments processing vendor in the health care industry.A ransomware group calling itself BlackCat hacked into Change Healthcare, a unit of UnitedHealth, in February.
