Police clear pro-Palestinian encampment and roadblock at UC Santa Cruz
-----
Police clad in riot gear broke up a pro-Palestinian encampment at UC Santa Cruz Friday, arresting many protesters in the early hours of the morning, the university said, in the latest spate of arrests connected to the on-campus protest movement.Videos shared on social media show officers on megaphones moving in, telling the protesters to disperse while the protesters linked arms and continued chanting in support of Palestine.
