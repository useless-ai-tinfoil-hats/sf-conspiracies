S.F. police arrest 54 people, seize 1.3 pounds of narcotics in Tenderloin crackdown
-----
Fifty-four people were arrested and 1.3 pounds of narcotics were seized during a drug enforcement operation in the Tenderloin neighborhood this week, San Francisco police said Friday.The operation occurred Wednesday, authorities said, adding that it was the latest large-scale enforcement operation carried out by the city's Drug Market Agency Coordination Center.
