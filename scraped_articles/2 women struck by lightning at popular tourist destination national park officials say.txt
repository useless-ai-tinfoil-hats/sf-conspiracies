2 women struck by lightning at popular tourist destination, national park officials say
-----
Two women visiting a popular national recreation area were struck by lightning just four days after one of its iconic landmarks collapsed, officials said.The unidentified visitors from the Netherlands and Australia, ages 22 and 23, were standing near the rim of Horseshoe Bend in Glen Canyon National Recreation Area just before 4 p.m. on Monday when they were hit by the lightning, a news release from the National Park Service said.
