Benicia police arrest 2 suspected of driving stolen car with handwritten license plate
-----
Two people were arrested early Friday for driving a stolen vehicle outfitted with a handwritten license plate, Benicia police said.According to Benicia Police Department officials, an officer pulled over the driver and passenger of a silver Kia near the 5000 block of East Second Street shortly before 1:30 a.m.
A check of the Kia's vehicle identification number indicated the car had been reported stolen out of Alameda County, according to Benicia Police Lt. Kenneth Hart.
