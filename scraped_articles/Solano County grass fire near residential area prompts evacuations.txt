Solano County grass fire near residential area prompts evacuations
-----
LATEST Aug. 21 11:52 a.m. Evacuation orders in Solano County, prompted by a brush fire, have been downgraded to warnings. The Amaral Fire, which burned through several acres in Green Valley, is 50% contained, Cal Fire's Sonoma-Lake-Napa unit said on social media.Fire crews were able to stop the blaze's forward progress at approximately 12 acres, Cal Fire said. The most updated evacuation information can be found on the Solano County Office of Emergency Services' Facebook page.
