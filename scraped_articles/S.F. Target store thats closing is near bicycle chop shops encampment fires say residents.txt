S.F. Target store that’s closing is near bicycle chop shops, ‘encampment fires,’ say residents
-----
The San Francisco Target store closing next month due to violent crime and theft is next to a homeless encampment that, for years, has raised safety concerns among local residents.The store at 1690 Folsom St. is closing on Oct. 21 after opening in 2019. It's on the same block as the encampment, which is strewn with people's belongings and makeshift housing, a Chronicle reporter confirmed on Tuesday.
