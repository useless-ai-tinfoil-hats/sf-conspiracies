S.F. leaders slam Police Department oversight of scandal-plagued nonprofit
-----
San Francisco officials slammed the Police Department at a heated hearing Thursday over the embattled crime prevention nonprofit SF SAFE that imploded several weeks ago after an audit found improper spending on luxury items and trips, and the nonprofit's board accused its executive director of forging checks and draining its bank accounts.The district attorney's office is investigating the nonprofit, which had close ties to the Police Department.
