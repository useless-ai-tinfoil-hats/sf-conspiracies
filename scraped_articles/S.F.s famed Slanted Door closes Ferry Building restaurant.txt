The Slanted Door, the acclaimed Vietnamese restaurant from chef-owner Charles Phan, is pulling its flagship location from the San Francisco Ferry Building after 15 years.In a statement, the restaurateur said construction costs to remodel the long-dormant space were too high to make an opening feasible.
