'Wrong then and are wrong now': Disneyland ride faces racist reckoning
-----
In Disneyland's latest effort to correct its past cultural transgressions, park officials have confirmed that one of its oldest rides, dating back to the park's opening day in 1955, is receiving an upgrade.Changes are coming to Peter Pan's Flight, a popular Fantasyland ride, according to Disneyland Park officials. The changes update the scene of the Never Land Tribe to remove racial stereotypes and caricatures of Indigenous people.
