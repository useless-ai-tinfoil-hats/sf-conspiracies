Cal Academy of Sciences penguin baby boom: 10 chicks hatched in 14 months
-----
A penguin baby boom has unfolded at the California Academy of Sciences in San Francisco, ending a four-year period without any new penguin chicks at the natural history museum, officials announced Wednesday.Ten African penguin chicks hatched over a 14-month period, from November 2022 through January 2024, as part of an international survival plan aimed at conserving the endangered bird, the museum said in a news release.
