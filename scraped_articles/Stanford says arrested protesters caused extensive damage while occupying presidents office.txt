Stanford says arrested protesters caused ‘extensive damage’ while occupying president’s office
-----
Stanford University police arrested 13 pro-Palestinian protesters Wednesday who entered the president's office early in the morning and, the university said, caused extensive damage in the building and injured one of the officers trying to clear the building.Outside, the stone walls were painted in dozens of places with swear words and death threats against police, America and Israel, though a group claiming to be behind the occupation disavowed responsibility for the graffiti.
