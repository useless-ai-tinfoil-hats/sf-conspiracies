Bay Area solar company to cut 350 jobs as demand plummets
-----
Enphase Energy announced late Monday it intends to implement a restructuring plan early next year, resulting in a 10% reduction of its global workforce.The Fremont-based company, which makes equipment for solar panels to convert direct current from sunlight into power for grids, revealed in regulatory filings that the cuts will impact approximately 350 employees and contractors.
