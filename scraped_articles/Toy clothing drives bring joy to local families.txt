Toy, clothing drives bring joy to local families
-----

It is editorially independent of both the newsroom and any one advertiser.Stuffed animals, dolls, comfy jackets and delicious food for your beloved dog and cat. They seem like minor things but can make all the difference for families in need. For people fighting poverty, illness or other challenges, even minor expenses can be too much to take on. The struggle often becomes even more painful around the holidays.
