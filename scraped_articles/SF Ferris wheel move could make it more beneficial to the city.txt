SF Ferris wheel move could make it more beneficial to the city
-----
The controversial Ferris wheel that's revolved riders in Golden Gate Park since late 2020 could potentially move to Fisherman's Wharf within the next two months.The San Francisco Port has received a short-term special event application to explore the possibility of relocating the ride before the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) summit in mid-November, according to The San Francisco Standard.
