Speed may have played role in deadly Oakland motorcycle crash, police say
-----
A motorcyclist crashed into a light pole and died Monday night in Oakland, police said.The motorcyclist was traveling north on Seminary Avenue when he attempted to change lanes and lost control, crashing into a light pole in the center median near Outlook Avenue, according to the Oakland Police Department.
Speeding appeared to be a factor in the crash but it is unknown whether alcohol or drugs played a role, police said.
