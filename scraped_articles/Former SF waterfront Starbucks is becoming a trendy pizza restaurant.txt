Former SF waterfront Starbucks is becoming a trendy pizza restaurant
-----
What once was an unexpectedly beautiful waterfront Starbucks is slated to become a trendy Detroit-style pizza restaurant. Joyride Pizza, which has several locations across San Francisco, is opening its newest outpost on the Embacardero at Pier 1 this spring, according to Eater.Joyride co-owner and founder Jesse Jacobs told Eater that the location just steps from the Ferry Building was "the Starbucks with the best view in San Francisco."
