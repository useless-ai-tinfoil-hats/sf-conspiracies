Review: ‘Madame Web’ is one half of a really good superhero movie
-----
"Madame Web" has a lot to recommend it, and for a while — a good long while — it seems like it's going to be one of the best Marvel superhero movies of the past few years.It has the superhero thing, but it's more like an action thriller with supernatural elements. And it has a heroine who is interesting in her own right, with a life and a personal history that would make us want to watch her in any kind of movie.
