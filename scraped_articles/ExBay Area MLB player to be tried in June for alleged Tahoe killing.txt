Ex-Bay Area MLB player to be tried in June for alleged Tahoe killing
-----
After a preliminary hearing where new evidence was presented, a Placer County judge decided to move a Lake Tahoe murder case forward to trial. One of the two suspects in the trial, Daniel Serafini, is a former Bay Area resident and MLB player.Six gunshots were fired in the Lake Tahoe home of Robert Gary Spohr and Wendy Wood on Saturday, June 5, 2021.
