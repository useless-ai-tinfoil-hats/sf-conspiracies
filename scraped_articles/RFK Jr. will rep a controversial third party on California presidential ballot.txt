RFK Jr. will rep a controversial third party on California presidential ballot
-----
Robert F. Kennedy Jr. will be on the California ballot in November under the banner of a controversial political party that lawmakers have tried — and failed — to clarify.Kennedy, who lives in Los Angeles, and his running mate, Nicole Shanahan, of Portola Valley, will appear before voters under the American Independent Party of California, whose roots go back to Alabama segregationist presidential candidate George Wallace, who was the party's nominee in 1968.
