Want to see the ‘planetary parade’ from the Bay Area? Lower your expectations
-----
A "parade" of planets will soon unfold over the Bay Area — but astronomers say that anyone hoping for a spectacle like the rare northern lights that recently wowed the region is probably in for a letdown.It sounds exciting, at least in theory: Early Monday morning, about an hour before sunrise, six planets will appear in the sky along the same line of sight as viewed from Earth: Mercury, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.
