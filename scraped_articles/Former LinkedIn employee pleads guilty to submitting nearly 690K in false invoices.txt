Former LinkedIn employee pleads guilty to submitting nearly $690K in false invoices
-----
A former LinkedIn employee pleaded guilty Monday to defrauding his employer of hundreds of thousands of dollars using invoices for fake podcast work, according to federal prosecutors.Kent Laird, 50, of Bothel, Wash., used his position as head of content, video and studios for LinkedIn Media Productions to submit fake invoices for independent contractors, according to the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Northern District of California.
