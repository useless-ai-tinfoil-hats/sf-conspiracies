California's Miracle Hot Springs closes indefinitely after second death
-----
Officials announced Monday that California's Miracle Hot Springs will be closed indefinitely, due to "safety concerns" after a second person died in the area in the last two years.A body was found on February 17 in one of the Miracle Hot Springs tubs. Previously, a body was found in that same area in October 2022, Amy Masi, a spokesperson for the Sequoia National Forest Kern River Ranger District, said in a news release.
