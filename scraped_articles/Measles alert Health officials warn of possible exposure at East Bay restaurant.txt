Measles alert: Health officials warn of possible exposure at East Bay restaurant
-----
Alameda County health officials alerted the public Tuesday about a possible measles exposure at a San Leandro restaurant earlier this month.Health officials said the possible exposure occurred at Sons of Liberty Alehouse in San Leandro between the hours of 4:45 p.m. and 7:30 p.m. on March 9.
