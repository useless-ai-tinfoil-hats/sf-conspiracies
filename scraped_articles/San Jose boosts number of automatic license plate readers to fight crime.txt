San Jose boosts number of automatic license plate readers to fight crime
-----
San Jose expanded the number of automatic license plate readers on its streets Monday with the installation of new devices that the city hopes will deter crime.Mayor Matt Mahan, whose office posted footage to Facebook and TikTok on Monday of Mahan helping workers install the city's 108th automatic license plate reader, said "these cameras have already made our city safer."
