This SF relic hasn't had running water for 15 years, but residents won't leave
-----
At the Hunters Point shipyard, outside former Navy building 116, San Francisco Bay glitters just offshore on a sunny August day. But inside the aging structure, plastic buckets are grouped at its seams, ready to catch the water that regularly seeps through the roof when it rains. The 27 artists who work inside seem to barely notice the crumbling ceiling anymore, adding an extra bucket when water from a new leak trickles in and spending their limited funds on roof patching when they can.
