Passengers fight on flight to San Francisco, forcing crew to intervene
-----
A viral video circulating on social media appears to capture a violent altercation between two passengers aboard a long-haul flight from Taipei to San Francisco.The incident occurred on EVA Air Flight BR 8, departing from Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport and bound for San Francisco International Airport on Tuesday. In the footage, an argument between two men escalates into a physical confrontation, prompting the intervention of three female flight attendants.
