SFMOMA encourages sports fans to ‘Get in the Game’ with open art call
-----
Bay Area sports fans will have the opportunity to have their art featured in the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art.On Sept. 1, SFMOMA will begin accepting 2D, 3D and digital art submissions inspired by the region's sports culture in conjunction with its forthcoming "Get in the Game" exhibition, scheduled to open Oct. 19.
