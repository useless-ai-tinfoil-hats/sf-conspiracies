Bystander tackles ex-teacher who came to Bay Area football game despite warning to stay away
-----
A bystander tackled and helped Brentwood police arrest a former teacher who showed up at a high school football game despite being warned to stay off campus, police said.Brentwood police were alerted by the Heritage High School principal at about 7:25 p.m. that Carlos Hurtado, 27, was at the game and that he had previously received a trespass admonishment to stay away from the school. Officers attempted to talk to Hurtado but he ran away. Video shot at the game shows a man in a suit
