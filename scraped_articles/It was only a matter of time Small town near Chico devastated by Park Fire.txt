'It was only a matter of time': Small town near Chico devastated by Park Fire
-----
The fast-growing Park Fire caused a "great deal of destruction" in a small town near Chico on Thursday night, Butte County Sheriff Kory Honea said in a Friday afternoon press conference.The blaze made its way through Cohasset, a town with 847 people about 18 miles northeast of Chico, as the fire grew by thousands of acres.
