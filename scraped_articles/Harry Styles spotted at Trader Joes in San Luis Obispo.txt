Harry Styles spotted at Trader Joe's in San Luis Obispo
-----
Do we think he was out of watermelon or out of sugar?Either way, pop star Harry Styles had to make a quick stop at a San Luis Obispo Trader Joe's on Monday, where he was apparently swarmed by Cal Poly fans. It was not immediately clear why he was in the Central Coast city.
Dressed in an all-gray sweatsuit, Styles was captured on video while being approached by a fan.
A Trader Joe's employee confirmed to SFGATE that Styles was indeed at the store.
