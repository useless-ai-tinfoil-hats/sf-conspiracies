Man struck by Richmond police officer files assault claim against city
-----
Kwesi Guss, the man repeatedly struck by Richmond police as he filmed an arrest earlier this month, filed a claim against the city of Richmond alleging officers assaulted him and violated his civil rights.Civil rights attorney John Burris filed the claim on behalf of Guss on Wednesday. A claim is a precursor to a lawsuit against a government agency.
