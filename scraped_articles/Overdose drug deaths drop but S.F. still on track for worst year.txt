Overdose drug deaths drop but S.F. still on track for worst year
-----
Overdose deaths in San Francisco dropped to 54 in September from a high of 84 in August, a 36% decrease, according to the public health department.In an update on overdoses Tuesday, Dr. Grant Colfax said 54 accidental overdose deaths were reported in September based on preliminary numbers from the medical examiner, a month-over-month drop in deaths and a decrease from 2022 when officials recorded 61 overdose deaths.
