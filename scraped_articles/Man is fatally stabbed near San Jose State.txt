Man is fatally stabbed near San Jose State
-----
A man was fatally stabbed near the San Jose State University campus early Friday morning, officials said. A "person of interest" was detained, the San Jose Police Department said on social media.The department said at 6:09 a.m. that it received a report of the stabbing at South Fourth and San Carlos streets in downtown San Jose at 4:17 a.m. Police units responded to the scene, started an investigation and closed streets.
