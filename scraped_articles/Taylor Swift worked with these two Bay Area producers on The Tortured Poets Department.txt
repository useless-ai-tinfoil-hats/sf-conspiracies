Taylor Swift worked with these two Bay Area producers on ‘The Tortured Poets Department’
-----
Some of Taylor Swift's most gut-wrenching new songs were developed in the Bay Area.Union City's Maryam Qudus, better known as singer-songwriter Spacemoth, and her husband, producer and engineer Beau Sorenson, worked on a handful of songs on Swift's latest release, "The Tortured Poets Department," and its subsequent "secret" second installment, "The Tortured Poets Department: The Anthology."
Qudus confirmed their involvement with the Chronicle, but said they couldn't share further
