Thousands of stranded Burning Man campers begin Labor Day exodus
-----
The exodus from Burning Man started Labor Day after two days in which thousands of festivalgoers were stranded due to rain and muddy conditions.As some 73,000 storm-stranded "Burners" were finally allowed to begin exiting the giant mud pit that this year's Burning Man had become, concerns arose about the future of the playa and the cleanup work to come.
