The dusty corner of California where airplanes go to die
-----
The boneyard is easy to spot in the arid outskirts of Mojave, a 10-block town in a corner of the southern California desert.Several rows of dormant aircraft bake in the yellow basin, kept behind a heavily monitored section of the Mojave Air & Space Port. When a commercial jet airliner expires, its final destination is an airport like Mojave.
