GAYPEC didn't attract APEC participants but it was a party anyway
-----
From the red-lit stage at the club Beaux, drag queen Khmera Rouge looked out over the packed floor and welcomed them to the Castro District on a Wednesday night.She asked how many in the crowd were from out of town – delegates, perhaps, from nations participating in the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit – and no one responded. That's all right, she told the audience with a chuckle, before launching into her performance. She knew the locals knew how to have a good time.
