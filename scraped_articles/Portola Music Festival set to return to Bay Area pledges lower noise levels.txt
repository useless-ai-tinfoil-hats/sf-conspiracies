Portola Music Festival set to return to Bay Area, pledges lower noise levels
-----
To address noise complaints from the first two years of the Portola Music Festival, organizers are proactively seeking feedback from Bay Area residents on how to lessen the expected disturbance from this year's concert.Goldenvoice, the promoter behind the two-day outdoor event, has distributed a letter to Alameda residents and city officials announcing plans to host the electronic music festival at Pier 80 in San Francisco for the third consecutive year.
