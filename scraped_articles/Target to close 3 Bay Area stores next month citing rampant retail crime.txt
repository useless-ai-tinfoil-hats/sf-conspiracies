Target to close 3 Bay Area stores next month, citing rampant retail crime
-----
UPDATE: The San Francisco Target store that's closing is near bicycle chop shops, 'encampment fires,' say residentsTarget announced Tuesday that it planned to close three of its Bay Area stores next month, citing "theft and organized retail crime" that was "threatening the safety of our team and guests, and contributing to unsustainable business performance," the company said in a statement.
