Tech millionaire buys up Tahoe's oldest waterfront bar
-----
A tech entrepreneur and investor from San Francisco has acquired Tahoe's oldest waterfront bar, Chambers Landing, on the west shore.Chris Fanini, best known as a co-founder of Weebly, was an investor in a group that acquired Chambers Landing in 2023, purchasing the restaurant from its previous owner, Rick Brown.
