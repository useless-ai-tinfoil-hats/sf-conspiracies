Historic California building and land to be returned to Indigenous people
-----
In a historic move, a California state agency is returning 40 acres of storied creekside land to a Native American tribe.Last week, the California Department of Fish and Wildlife announced the Mount Whitney Fish Hatchery on the banks of Oak Creek in Inyo County will be transferred to the Fort Independence Indian Community for no cost in early 2024.
