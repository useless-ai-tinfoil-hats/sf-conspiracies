Bay Area mail carrier beaten, robbed of keys as postal attacks mount nationwide
-----
A Palo Alto mail carrier was robbed and beaten Thursday, yet another attack in an ongoing national trend where thieves target carriers for their postal keys.Palo Alto police said they're investigating the robbery, which took place in the parking lot of the Southwood Apartments in the city's Midtown neighborhood. Two men believed to be in their 20s approached a U.S. Postal Service carrier in his 60s and demanded his postal keys, according to police.
