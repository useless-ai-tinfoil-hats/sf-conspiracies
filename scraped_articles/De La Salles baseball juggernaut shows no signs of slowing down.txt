De La Salle's baseball juggernaut shows no signs of slowing down
-----
For De La Salle-Concord baseball coach David Jeans, the focus is always the game at hand. But even he might be a little distracted from his team's recent onslaught.The Spartans started a six-game winning streak March 28 that featured romps over Foothill-Pleasanton (28-2), Freedom-Oakley (24-1) and Sac-Joaquin Section power Granite Bay (17-1), and finished last week with East Bay Athletic League routs over then-12th-ranked San Ramon Valley (11-0 and 12-2).
