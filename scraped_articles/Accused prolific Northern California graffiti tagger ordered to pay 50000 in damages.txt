Accused ‘prolific’ Northern California graffiti tagger ordered to pay $50,000 in damages
-----
A Sacramento County Superior Court judge ordered a man prosecutors described as a "prolific" tagger to pay just over $50,000 in restitution after he was accused of splattering graffiti on Sacramento buildings.Scott Smith pleaded no contest to two felony vandalism charges, Sacramento County prosecutors said Monday. Damage to one building he was accused of tagging resulted in more than $36,000 in damages, officials said.
