Sonoma County residents convicted in pig-blood vandalism case
-----
Two Sonoma County residents were convicted of vandalism this week after pleading no-contest to accusations that they splattered pig's blood on the former home of a defense witness in the Derek Chauvin murder trial and atop a sculpture outside the Santa Rosa Plaza in 2021.Amber Lucas and Colin Metcalfe were found guilty of felony vandalism after reaching plea agreements with the Sonoma County District Attorney's Office, spokespersons for the agency said Friday.
