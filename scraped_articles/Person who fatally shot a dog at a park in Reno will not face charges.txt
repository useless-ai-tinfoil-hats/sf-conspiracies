Person who fatally shot a dog at a park in Reno will not face charges
-----
A dog owner will not face charges after they shot a Rottweiler during a dog fight at a Reno park on Sunday.The shooting happened at Rancho San Rafael Regional Park, after a Rottweiler bit a smaller dog on its neck and started "thrashing" it, Kristin Vietti, a spokesperson for the Washoe County Sheriff's Office, told SFGATE.
