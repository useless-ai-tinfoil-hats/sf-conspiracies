Neeli Cherkovski, longtime S.F. poet and beatnik, dies at 78
-----
San Francisco poet and memoirist Neeli Cherkovski has died at age 78, according to City Lights Bookstore, which posted a message on social media Tuesday.Cherkovski wrote the biographies of fellow poets Lawrence Ferlinghetti and Charles Bukowski. All three of them co-edited the Los Angeles zine Laugh Literary and Man the Humping Guns, according to Cherkovski's website.
