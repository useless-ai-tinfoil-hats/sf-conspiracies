Harris outshines Biden in their first joint appearance since his exit
-----
LARGO, Md. — In their first official joint appearance since Joe Biden ended his presidential campaign and Kamala Harris began hers, the torch appeared to have already been passed.The speakers praised Biden, but the crowd at Prince George's Community College in Maryland on Thursday was clearly there to see Harris. Dozens of attendees headed for the doors after Harris spoke and Biden began his remarks — something he could probably see in the well-lit room.
