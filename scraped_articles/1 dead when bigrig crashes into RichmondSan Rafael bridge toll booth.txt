1 dead when big-rig crashes into Richmond-San Rafael bridge toll booth
-----
One person died on Monday night when a big-rig collided into a toll both on the Richmond-San Rafael Bridge Monday night and erupted into flames, according to the California Highway Patrol.The collision occurred in the westbound lanes of Interstate 580 and on Tuesday morning the two right lanes remained closed, CHP said.
It's unknown when the lanes will reopen.
