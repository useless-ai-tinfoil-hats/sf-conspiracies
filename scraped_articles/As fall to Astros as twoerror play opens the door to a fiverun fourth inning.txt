A's fall to Astros as two-error play opens the door to a five-run fourth inning
-----
At the outset of this season, the Oakland Athletics looked remarkably inadequate on defense. They soon improved greatly with their arms and gloves, but a defensive miscue — two, actually — cost them dearly in a 5-2 loss to Houston on Sunday at the Coliseum.The A's committed 13 errors in their first five games, but they had made only 25 in the ensuing 49 heading into Sunday. A two-error play turned the series finale in the Astros' favor.
