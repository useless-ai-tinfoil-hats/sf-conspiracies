‘Spectacularly hard’: Why this hit S.F. dumpling shop is closing

Dumpling Club, a popular dumpling pop-up whose first San Francisco store drew huge lines, is closing.Dumpling Club's last day of business will be Oct. 8, owner Cathay Bi announced in an email and on social media.
