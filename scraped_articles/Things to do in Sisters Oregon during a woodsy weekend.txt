The reason behind the quirky name of Sisters, Oregon, becomes evident as soon as you arrive in the Deschutes County town. A trio of volcanic mountains — Faith (North Sister), Hope (Middle Sister) and Charity (South Sister) — provide a knockout backdrop for this little spot in central Oregon.Sometimes referred to as the "Gateway to the Cascades," Sisters was established in 1901 and grew into a prosperous lumber town.
