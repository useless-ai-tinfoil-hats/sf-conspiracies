Tesla recall: Maps show where crashes involving autopilot happened across California
-----
Before Tesla's recall of more than 2 million vehicles for a software update related to their autopilot systems, more than 100 Tesla accidents in the Bay Area had drawn attention from federal investigators looking into whether autopilot played a role.A National Highway Traffic Safety Administration database identifies 113 Tesla accidents in the nine-county Bay Area since July 2021 in which autopilot had been switched on before the incident.
