‘Under the Bridge,’ a well-acted mini-series, based on a real-life 1997 murder
-----
"Under the Bridge" is an eight-part Hulu miniseries based on the book of the same name by Rebecca Godfrey, about the 1997 murder of a teenage girl by fellow teenagers in a small Canadian town. The series casts a wide net, showing the crime, the events leading up to the crime, and the subsequent investigations and trials.It's an entertaining series, well-cast and well-acted, and featuring what is, at its core, a compelling story. However, two flaws keep it from being a complete success.
