Thousands in California to finally be sent cash payments by the end of September
-----
A few thousand California residents will likely be sent at least a few hundred dollars from the state by the end of September.The California Franchise Tax Board began sending out inflation relief payments in October of last year as a way of helping residents deal with the rising costs of goods. Since then, the agency has sent out a total of $9.2 billion that has benefited roughly 32 million people.
