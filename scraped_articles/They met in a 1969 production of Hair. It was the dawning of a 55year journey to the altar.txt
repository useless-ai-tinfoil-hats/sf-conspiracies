Spending time with Ron Shetler and Jenny Lee is like watching a musical come to life.They finish each other's sentences with a cadence that feels as if it's coming from Leonard Bernstein's portable typewriter. Both are likely at any moment to break into song. And their love story — romantic, complicated and at times almost tragic — is hard to match.
