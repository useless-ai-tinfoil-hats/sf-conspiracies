Willie Mays’ death adds searing poignancy to Rickwood Field ceremonies
-----
BIRMINGHAM, Ala. — Willie Mays pulled a fast one on us, and how typical of him.Thursday was to be the day we were to celebrate him, not have a celebration of life for him. The day we were to honor his legacy and career and life, not mourn his passing. The day we were to watch a baseball game while thinking of him back in the Bay Area, not in heaven.
Emotions the past couple of days have run the gamut and beyond.
