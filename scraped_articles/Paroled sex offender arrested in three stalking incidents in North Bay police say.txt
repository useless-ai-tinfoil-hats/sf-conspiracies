Paroled sex offender arrested in three stalking incidents in North Bay, police say
-----
San Rafael police this week arrested a 44-year-old parolee and registered sex offender whom they accuse of attacking and stalking three victims downtown.Jail records show that officers booked Willie Jessie Clinton-Jones into custody late Wednesday night on charges of felony stalking and violating the terms of his parole. Police say they received calls beginning at around 9 p.m. that a man, later identified as Clinton-Jones, had pursued and harassed women in three separate incidents.
