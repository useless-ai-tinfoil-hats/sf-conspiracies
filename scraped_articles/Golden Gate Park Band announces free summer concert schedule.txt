Golden Gate Park Band announces free summer concert schedule
-----
The Golden Gate Park Band is back with 25 free shows lined up for this summer.San Francisco's oldest musical organization, the Golden Gate Park Band has been performing consistently since 1882. Featuring more than 30 professional musicians, the band plans to showcase a variety of wind-band music that highlights the cultures that make up the Bay Area.
The summer season is scheduled to kick off April 28 with "The Pines of Rome," Gustav Holst's "Second Suite in F" and more. Concerts
