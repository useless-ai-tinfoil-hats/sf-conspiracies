Two homicides reported within days at Bay Area 24 Hour Fitness parking lot, police say
-----
San Leandro police are investigating two homicides that occurred within 10 days of each other in the parking lot of a 24 Hour Fitness at Bayfair Center.Officers initially responded to reports of an unconscious man inside a car in the parking lot on Sept. 14 at around 11:50 a.m. When they arrived on the scene, they discovered the man was dead from what appeared to be a single gunshot wound, police said in a statement.
