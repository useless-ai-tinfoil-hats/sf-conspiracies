Huey Lewis to star in mockumentary series about his life and hearing loss
-----
Huey Lewis is set to star in a new mockumentary series that delves into his life and struggles with hearing loss.The half-hour show, tentatively titled "Whatever Happened to Huey Lewis," is being developed by Fox and is described by Deadline as reminiscent of "Curb Your Enthusiasm," with Lewis playing a fictionalized version of himself, coping with hearing loss.
