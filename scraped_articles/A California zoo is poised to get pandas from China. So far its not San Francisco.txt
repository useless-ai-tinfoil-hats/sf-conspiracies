A California zoo is poised to get pandas from China. So far, it’s not San Francisco
-----
Giant pandas are on the cusp of returning to a California zoo — but as of now, it's not San Francisco.The San Diego Zoo, which last hosted pandas in 2019, said Thursday that it is in the final stages of getting approved to bring a pair of the animals back to California. Pandas could arrive at the zoo by the end of the summer, zoo officials told the Associated Press.
