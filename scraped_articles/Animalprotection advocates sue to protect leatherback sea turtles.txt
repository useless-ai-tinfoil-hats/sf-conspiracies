Animal-protection advocates sue to protect leatherback sea turtles
-----
Animal-protection groups sued the Biden administration Thursday in San Francisco for opening nearly 2,000 square miles of West Coast offshore waters to pot fishers, saying their nets will fatally entangle an already endangered turtle species.The leatherback sea turtle was classified as endangered in 1970, and its population has been declining by 6% a year, the Center for Biological Diversity and the Turtle Island Restoration network said in their federal court lawsuit.
