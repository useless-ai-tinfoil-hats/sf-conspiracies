Covered California open enrollment deadline extended: What to know
-----
Californians aiming to sign up for health insurance through the state's Covered California marketplace have a little extra time, with the open enrollment deadline extended until Friday, officials said.Covered California extended the Jan. 31 deadline after seeing "record-breaking enrollment nationally" and high demand in the state for health insurance, officials announced last week. The new deadline to sign up for 2024 coverage is midnight Feb. 9, with coverage effective from Feb. 1.
