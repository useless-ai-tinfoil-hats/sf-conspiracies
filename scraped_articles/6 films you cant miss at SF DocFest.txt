6 films you can’t miss at SF DocFest
-----
The San Francisco Documentary Film Festival, aka SF DocFest, unreels Thursday, May 30 to June 9. With films that focus on a wide range of subjects, from a San Francisco photographer to a personal look at the migrant crisis through the eyes of undocumented women living through it to a profile of artist Thomas Kinkade and more, the festival offers a breadth of works that enlighten and entertain. These six films — all screening at the Roxie Theater and online — are among our favorites.'The
