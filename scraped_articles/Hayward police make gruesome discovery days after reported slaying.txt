Hayward police make gruesome discovery days after reported slaying
-----
Hayward police made a gruesome discovery this week of the body of a man believed to have been slain last week, his remains concealed with dirt and debris in an industrial part of the city.Police say the man was killed on April 26 near San Antonio and Hayman streets during an argument with another man, who pulled a handgun and shot him. Witnesses saw the attack but did not contact police or call 911 until two days later, police said.
