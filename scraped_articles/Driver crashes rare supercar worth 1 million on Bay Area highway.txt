Driver crashes rare supercar worth $1 million on Bay Area highway
-----
A driver crashed an incredibly expensive Porsche Carrera GT on Skyline Boulevard on Sunday morning, according to the California Highway Patrol.The car, one of only 1,270 produced by the luxury automaker, went off-road and completely smashed its front right bumper against a tree, according to photos posted on social media.
