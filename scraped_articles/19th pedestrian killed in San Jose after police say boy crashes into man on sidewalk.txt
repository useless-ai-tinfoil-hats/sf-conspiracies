19th pedestrian killed in San Jose after police say boy crashes into man on sidewalk
-----
San Jose police arrested a boy early Sunday morning after he allegedly drove a stolen SUV onto a sidewalk and hit and killed a man before crashing into a tree, the department said.The death marked the 19th pedestrian killed this year and the 33rd fatal collision in the South Bay city. Neither the adult victim nor the juvenile suspect has been identified by police.
