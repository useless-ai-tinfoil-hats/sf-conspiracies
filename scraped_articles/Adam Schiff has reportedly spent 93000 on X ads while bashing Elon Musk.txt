Adam Schiff has reportedly spent $93,000 on X ads while bashing Elon Musk
-----
Rep. Adam Schiff is an outspoken critic of Elon Musk and his stewardship of X, the social media site formerly known as Twitter. But that hasn't stopped him from paying X thousands of dollars for advertising.Schiff is running to represent California in the Senate and his campaign and its political action group supporters have dropped $93,432 to promote his X account in 2023, according to data provided by X to Business Insider and The Washington Post.
