Man, 47, killed in East Bay shooting, police say
-----
A 47-year-old man was fatally shot in Antioch on Friday morning, police said.At about 7:30 a.m., Antioch police responded to East 8th Street and A Street after a neighbor reported gunshots earlier in the morning and suspected another neighbor was shot. Officers found a 47-year-old man inside a residence on East 8th Street with a fatal gunshot wound, police said Friday afternoon.
No arrests were reported. Police did not immediately respond to requests for further information.
