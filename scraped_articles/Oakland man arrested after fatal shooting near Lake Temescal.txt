Oakland man arrested after fatal shooting near Lake Temescal
-----
Oakland police have arrested an Oakland man they say shot and killed a man near Lake Temescal on Friday.Police say the suspect shot a Berkeley man on the 6500 block of Broadway in Oakland, just off Lake Temescal Beach, around 7:45 p.m. Friday. When officers and paramedics arrived, they tried to treat the victim, but he was pronounced dead at the scene, according to the Oakland Police Department.
