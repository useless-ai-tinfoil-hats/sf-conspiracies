Industrial plant on San Francisco pier to close this month
-----
A construction-related processing plant on one of San Francisco's last industrial piers will close at the end of October due to pollution concerns, the Bay Area Air Quality Management District announced this week.The facility, operated by Martin Marietta Materials, receives rocks on cargo boats.
