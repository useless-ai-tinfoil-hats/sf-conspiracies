Why the U.S. celebrates Labor Day about four months late every year
-----
Why Labor Day?It was supposed to be May Day.
The May Day holiday — International Workers Day — is celebrated by over 100 countries. Only in the United States and Canada is a similar holiday — Labor Day — held in September.
This is ironic because the events inspiring the birth of May Day occurred here.
