Giants told of Willie Mays’ death amid loss to Cubs: ‘We have heavy hearts’

CHICAGO — Logan Webb will always remember where he was when he first heard the news that Willie Mays had died.The San Francisco Giants' right-hander was on the mound Tuesday night at Wrigley Field getting ready to pitch in the bottom of the sixth when the Chicago Cubs' public-address announcer gave the crowd of 36,297 the news. Then, Mays' likeness was put on the left-field scoreboard.
