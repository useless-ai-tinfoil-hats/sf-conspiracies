Ben Folds to perform with S.F. Symphony this summer
-----
Pianist and singer-songwriter Ben Folds is scheduled to join the San Francisco Symphony as part of the orchestra's Summer with the Symphony programming.The concert in Davies Symphony Hall is set for July 27, and will be devoted to music from the artist's career as a soloist and the frontman for the alternative rock trio Ben Folds Five.
