NFL owners complaining 49ers are benefiting too much from hiring people of color
-----
NFL owners are reportedly unhappy with how the San Francisco 49ers have benefited from hiring people of color into coaching and executive positions, according to ESPN's Seth Wickersham.This detail is buried in a profile of Niners head coach Kyle Shanahan that was published Wednesday. While the tidbit is meant to highlight Shanahan's keen eye for talent within the context of the paragraph it's in, it instead showcases a frustration within the league's billionaire class.
