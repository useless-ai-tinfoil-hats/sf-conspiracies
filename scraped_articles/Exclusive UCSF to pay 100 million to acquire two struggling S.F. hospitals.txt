Exclusive: UCSF to pay $100 million to acquire two struggling S.F. hospitals
-----
UCSF Health has confirmed that its $100 million deal to acquire two struggling San Francisco community hospitals is just weeks away from closing.The city's preeminent medical research institution has signed an agreement to purchase St. Mary's Medical Center at 450 Stanyan St. — San Francisco's oldest Catholic hospital — and St. Francis Memorial Hospital at 900 Hyde St. from Dignity Health by the end of March.
