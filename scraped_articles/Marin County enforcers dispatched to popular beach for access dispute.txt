Marin County enforcers dispatched to popular beach for access dispute
-----
Several slapdash fences have appeared in front of multimillion-dollar homes on a popular Bay Area beach that are blocking public sections of the coastline without authorization, according to a formal complaint reviewed by SFGATE.The complaint, filed to Marin County's Community Development Agency on Aug. 13, alleges that at least five houses near Calle del Pradero in Stinson Beach have installed various forms of fencing that use rope, wooden posts or metal stakes.
