Giants shore up bullpen lefty depth, get Brewers’ Ethan Small for cash considerations
-----
Adding some depth to their left-handed relief, the San Francisco Giants acquired Ethan Small from the Milwaukee Brewers for cash considerations on Monday evening.Small, 26, was a first-round pick out of Mississippi State in 2019 who had brief call-ups with the Brewers each of the past two seasons, pitching in four games and allowing 10 earned runs in 10⅓ innings.
