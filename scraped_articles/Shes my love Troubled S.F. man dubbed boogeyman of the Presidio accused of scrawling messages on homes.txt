‘She’s my love’: Troubled S.F. man dubbed ‘boogeyman of the Presidio’ accused of scrawling messages on homes
-----
After a resident of the Presidio of San Francisco recently discovered an unsettling message scrawled on her front door — "She's my love" — it didn't take long for police to pinpoint a suspect.U.S. Park Police investigators who viewed surveillance footage said they immediately recognized James Durgin.
