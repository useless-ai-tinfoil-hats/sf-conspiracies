Man arrested in kidnapping, assault of teen in South San Francisco
-----
A South San Francisco man has been arrested in the kidnapping and assault of a 16-year-old girl, police said Tuesday.Rupinder Singh, 19, was arrested Monday on suspicion of kidnapping, assault to commit lewd acts on a child, false imprisonment and sexual battery. Singh was booked into the San Mateo County Jail, according to police.
