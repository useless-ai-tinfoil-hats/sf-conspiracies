California Gov. Newsom sets out guidelines for state agencies wanting to use AI
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom has encouraged the dozens of departments that make up California's state government to use artificial intelligence. Some have jumped at the chance to use the emerging technology in everything from traffic prediction to tax filing.Now Newsom's administration has released a road map to guide state agencies that want to buy or use the technology.
