American Eagle suing Westfield over 'neglect' of SF mall, requesting damages
-----
American Eagle filed a lawsuit against the former owners of the Westfield San Francisco Centre mall this week, alleging the group mismanaged the building.The clothing retailer is claiming the company "neglected" its obligation to maintain the common areas of the mall, "allowing the mall to become a lightning rod for, in Westfield's words, 'rampant criminal activity.'" The lawsuit was first spotted by the San Francisco Business Times.
