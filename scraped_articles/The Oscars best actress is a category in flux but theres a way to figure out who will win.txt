The Oscars’ best actress is a category in flux, but there’s a way to figure out who will win
-----
Among Oscar categories, best actress is the one most in flux. It used to be that the winners were almost always 35 or younger, but in the past six years, the winners' average age has been 53.When Michelle Yeoh, at 60, won best actress last year for "Everything Everywhere All at Once," it only confirmed a trend that began with Frances McDormand, at 60, winning for "Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri" (2017).
