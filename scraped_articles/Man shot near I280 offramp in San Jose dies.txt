Man shot near I-280 off-ramp in San Jose dies
-----
A man shot last week near a freeway off-ramp in San Jose has died, marking the city's 13th homicide of the year.The shooting occurred on May 9 in the area of the Interstate 280 off-ramp at McLaughlin Avenue, police said. At about 5:46 p.m., officers responded to a report of a shooting at a homeless encampment near the off-ramp to find the man, who was unconscious and not breathing, suffering from at least one gunshot wound.
The assailant fled before officers arrived at the scene
