How new airline refund rules could benefit thousands of SFO flyers
-----
The newly announced federal policy requiring airlines to give automatic refunds for delayed or canceled flights will likely be a major boon for Northern California air travelers, data shows.Nearly a quarter of a million air passengers at San Francisco International and Northern California's three other major airports would have been eligible for automatic reimbursement last year alone, according to a Chronicle analysis of flight data.
