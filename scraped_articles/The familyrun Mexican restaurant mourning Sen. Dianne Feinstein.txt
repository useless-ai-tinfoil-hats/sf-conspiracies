The family-run Mexican restaurant mourning Sen. Dianne Feinstein
-----
It was 8:20 a.m. on Friday when Nati Ramirez heard the news. Ramirez, the marketing director of San Francisco's celebrated Don Ramon's restaurant, was at the gym when she saw headlines flash across the TV screen announcing Sen. Dianne Feinstein's death at age 90.Feinstein, who spent the last three decades in the Senate representing California, died Thursday evening at her home in Washington, D.C. after months of health struggles. The exact cause of death is still unknown.
