It’s already time to table the No Labels fable
-----
House Speaker Emerita Nancy Pelosi has recently had a bit of a tangle with the political group No Labels, which is devoted to … what?Maybe ask West Virginia Sen. Joe Manchin, who dropped out of his 2024 reelection bid to mull retirement or run as its potential presidential nominee.
Calling itself the "commonsense majority," No Labels says it is "an organization of over 1 million Democrats, Republicans and independents working to bring American leaders together to solve problems."
