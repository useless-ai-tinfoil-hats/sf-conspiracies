Northern California music festival featuring SZA canceled 2 weeks before start
-----
Organizers of a major Northern California music festival unexpectedly pulled the plug on this year's event just two weeks before its scheduled start due to safety concerns.The Sol Blume Festival, headlined by SZA, was set to take place from May 3-5 at Sacramento's Discovery Park. But due to the park's condition following heavy rains and flooding earlier this year, festival promoters said, the event has been postponed for the second consecutive year.
