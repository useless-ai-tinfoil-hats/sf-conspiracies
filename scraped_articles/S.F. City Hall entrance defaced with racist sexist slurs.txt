S.F. City Hall entrance defaced with racist, sexist slurs
-----
A main entrance to San Francisco City Hall was defaced with racist and sexist slurs, Mayor London Breed said on Wednesday, denouncing the graffiti as "disgusting."Addressing reporters steps from the vandalism site, Breed pledged a "full, thorough and transparent investigation" and said the city was working to remove the graffiti as soon as possible.
