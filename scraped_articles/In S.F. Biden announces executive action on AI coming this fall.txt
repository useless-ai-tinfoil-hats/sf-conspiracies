In S.F., Biden announces executive action on AI coming this fall
-----
President Joe Biden plans to issue an executive order this fall to help ensure the responsible innovation of the artificial intelligence industry, he said at an appearance in downtown San Francisco Wednesday.AI was a topic of discussion for his meeting with the President's Council of Advisors on Science and Technology at the Fairmont Hotel in San Francisco, which was private. Biden did not share details of what the executive action would entail during his remarks before the event.
