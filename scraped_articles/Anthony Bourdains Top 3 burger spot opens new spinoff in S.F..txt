Anthony Bourdain’s ‘Top 3’ burger spot opens new spinoff in S.F.
-----
The family behind a historic San Francisco burger restaurant is expanding with a brand-new venture right next door.Sam's East is now open at 620 Broadway, beside the 60-year-old Sam's Burgers on the border between Chinatown and North Beach. Owner Emad El Shawa described the new restaurant's menu as "small but mighty," with chicken, rib eye steak and falafel served inside lavash wraps and pita sandwiches or atop salad bowls or fries.
