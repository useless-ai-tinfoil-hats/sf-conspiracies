They became unlikely crusaders for LGBT immigrant rights. And they just overcame one final hurdle
-----
Shirley Tan and Jay Mercado can recall every moment of the January 2009 morning when Tan was taken from their Pacifica home by federal immigration authorities.Mercado was making breakfast. Tan was in the shower. Their twin boys, age 12, were sleeping. It was 6 a.m. when the knock came. The two U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement agents at the door were huge.
