S.F. proposes $2 million for small businesses hurt by APEC, Chinese New Year parade parking
-----
San Francisco lawmakers have proposed a $2 million APEC relief fund, along with $250,000 in parking and transit subsidies for Chinatown during the Lunar New Year parade in a bid to help small businesses.Under a bill introduced this past week, South of Market businesses that saw sales plunge during the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit in November would be eligible for assistance. The Office of Economic and Workforce Development would manage the funding.
