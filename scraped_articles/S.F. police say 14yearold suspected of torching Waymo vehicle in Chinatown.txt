S.F. police say 14-year-old suspected of torching Waymo vehicle in Chinatown
-----
San Francisco police said Wednesday that they identified a 14-year-old boy suspected of setting ablaze a Waymo robotaxi in Chinatown during Lunar New Year celebrations in February.Prosecutors have filed charges against the teen, police said. His name was not released due to his age. The city's Juvenile Probation Department will decide whether the teen will be arrested or appear in court out of custody, officials said.
