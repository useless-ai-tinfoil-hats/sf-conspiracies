Newsom appoints Oscar Grant's uncle to police advisory board
-----
The uncle of Oscar Grant, a 22-year-old Bay Area retail worker and father who was fatally shot by BART police in 2009, was appointed to a state board that reviews police misconduct cases, according to a Thursday announcement by Gov. Gavin Newsom.Cephus "Uncle Bobby" Johnson, a San Jose resident, will serve on the Peace Officer Standards Accountability Advisory Board.
