‘The world is missing out:’ Family remembers Lowell High School grad slain in Dolores Park
-----
Before he was shot to death in Dolores Park, Luis Manuel Arguello-Inglis was a humble, dedicated young man full of life."He was the go-to guy," said Irving Arguello, his father. "He had a sense of what was good and what was bad. As a family, we're devastated."
Just after 10 p.m. on Wednesday, San Francisco police found Arguello-Inglis at Dolores Park with gunshot wounds. He was 19 years old.
