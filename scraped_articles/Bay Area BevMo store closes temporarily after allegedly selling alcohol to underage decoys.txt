Bay Area BevMo store closes temporarily after allegedly selling alcohol to underage decoys
-----
LATEST Sept. 17, 5:16 p.m. A spokesperson for BevMo said the company responded to the California Department of Alcoholic Beverage Control's suspension of the Menlo Park store's liquor license after clerks there recently sold alcohol to underage decoys."We have always taken the responsible sale of alcohol extremely seriously and continually enhance the robust systems we have in place to prevent the sale of alcohol to underage customers," a BevMo spokesperson said in a statement on Sunday
