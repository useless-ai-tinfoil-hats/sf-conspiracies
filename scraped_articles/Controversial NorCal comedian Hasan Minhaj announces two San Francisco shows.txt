Controversial NorCal comedian Hasan Minhaj announces two San Francisco shows
-----
Comedian Hasan Minhaj is going on tour in early 2024 and will make three stops near his Northern California birthplace, including two at San Francisco's Masonic Auditorium on Feb. 9, 2024. But Minhaj's reputation as comedy's darling, and heir apparent to Jon Stewart and Trevor Noah at the Daily Show, has taken a hit in recent months.The tour announcement comes amid ongoing controversy surrounding alleged fabrications and embellishments in Minhaj's standup.
