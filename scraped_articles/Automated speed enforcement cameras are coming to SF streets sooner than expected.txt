Automated speed enforcement cameras are coming to SF streets sooner than expected
-----
Cameras that would automatically cite speeding drivers are coming to San Francisco earlier than expected.The San Francisco Board of Supervisors unanimously approved legislation Tuesday that expedites the process for installing 33 automated speed enforcement cameras, San Francisco Mayor London Breed's office said in a news release. The San Francisco Municipal Transportation Authority will be responsible for finding a contractor to design, operate and maintain the cameras by 2025.
