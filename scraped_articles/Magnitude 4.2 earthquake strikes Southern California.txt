Magnitude 4.2 earthquake strikes Southern California
-----
A magnitude 4.2 earthquake hit Southern California at 7:43p.m. on Wednesday evening, with the epicenter 3 kilometers southwest of San Bernardino, according to the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS).The quake was initially reported as a magnitude 4.5 temblor, and later downgraded.
There were "no immediate reports of damage or injuries," said the San Bernardino County Fire Department on X, formerly known as Twitter.
