California A.G. Rob Bonta joins call for feds to rein in robocalls, AI-generated scams
-----
Robocalls and texts, a common nuisance of modern life, are becoming more advanced and pernicious as AI technology improves. And a coalition of more than two dozen attorneys general is asking the federal government to act."In addition to being a daily annoyance, robocalls are often used by scammers to cause real financial damage," said Attorney General Rob Bonta in a statement Wednesday as he joined 25 other top state attorneys in response to a Federal Communications Commission inquiry
