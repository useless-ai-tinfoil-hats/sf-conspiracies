S.F. students demand district do more to prevent gun violence
-----
Students from San Francisco's public high schools vowed to walkout Friday afternoon to protest what they say are deficient security measures to prevent gun violence on campuses.The action comes after reports of firearms on two San Francisco high school campuses in December renewed calls to beef up security systems, with students and families questioning whether district officials are taking the threats seriously.
