S.F. police searching for missing 16-year-old girl
-----
San Francisco police said Friday that they were searching for a 16-year-old girl who has been missing for two days, according to the Police Department.Police said that Mizan Dadres-Oman was last seen on Wednesday afternoon in the 800 block of Foerster Street. Dadres-Oman frequently visits the area around Sunnyside Playground and Mount Davidson, close to where she went missing, according to officials.
