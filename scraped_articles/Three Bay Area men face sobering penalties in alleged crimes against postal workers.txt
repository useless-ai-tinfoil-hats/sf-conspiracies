Three Bay Area men face 'sobering' penalties in alleged crimes against postal workers
-----
Three Bay Area men are facing severe penalties for alleged crimes against postal workers and federal officials are warning would-be thieves to think twice about interfering with the delivery of U.S. mail.The suspects were identified as Anthony Medina, 42, of American Canyon, Robert Bell, 19, of Antioch and Derek Hopson, 33, of Oakland, in a news statement released Tuesday by the the Northern District of California U.S. Attorney's Office.
