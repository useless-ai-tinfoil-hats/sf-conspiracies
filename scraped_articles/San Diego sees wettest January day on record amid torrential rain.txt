San Diego sees wettest January day on record amid 'torrential rain'
-----
LATEST Jan. 23, 7:15 a.m. The final rainfall totals are in: The gauge at San Diego International Airport measured 2.73 inches of rainfall on Monday when a moisture-rich storm passed over the region, the National Weather Service said. The total marks the wettest January ever measured in the county with records going back to 1850. The previous January record was 2.57 inches measured on Jan. 31, 1979."January 22 was a remarkable rainfall day in San Diego County," the weather service said.
