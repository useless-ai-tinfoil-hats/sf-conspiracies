Woman found dead in South Bay home suffered ‘physical trauma,’ prompting investigation
-----
A woman was found dead in a house on a residential block in San Jose Sunday afternoon, police said, prompting an investigation.Police responded to the 900 block of Thelma Way following a report of a "possible deceased person."
When they arrived, they found the woman, who was pronounced dead at the scene. Her body had suffered "obvious signs of physical trauma," police said Monday.
Her name was not immediately released as officials work to notify the woman's next of kin.
