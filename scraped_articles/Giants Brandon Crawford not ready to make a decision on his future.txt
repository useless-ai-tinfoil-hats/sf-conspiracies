Giants’ Brandon Crawford not ready to make a decision on his future
-----
SAN DIEGO — In Brandon Crawford's mind, this is no time to reflect on his career, his contract or his future. It's more about focusing on contributing daily to the San Francisco Giants' mission to reach the playoffs.At some point, the four-time Gold Glove shortstop will make a decision that goes beyond the task at hand. He's 36. He hasn't had a Crawford-like year. He has been on the injured list three times. His contract expires after this season.
