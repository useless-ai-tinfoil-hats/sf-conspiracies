Man dies in custody at East Bay jail, authorities say
-----
A man in custody at the West County Detention Facility in Richmond died Monday afternoon, authorities said.Deputies responded to a report of an unresponsive prisoner just before 2 p.m. Monday, the Contra Costa County Sheriff's office said in a news release. The deputies alerted medical personnel, who unsuccessfully attempted to perform life-saving measures on the man, the Sheriff's Office said.
