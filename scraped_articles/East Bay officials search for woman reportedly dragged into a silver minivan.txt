East Bay officials search for woman reportedly dragged into a silver minivan
-----
Officials with the Alameda County Sheriff's Office said they were searching for a woman who was reportedly dragged into a silver minivan Tuesday morning in the area of Ranspot Drive and Selby Drive in unincorporated San Leandro.Sheriff's officials said they had arrested a "possible suspect with a similar vehicle" to the van, but deputies were still attempting to locate the woman shortly before 1 p.m.
Officials released an image of the woman in an effort to solicit the public's help
