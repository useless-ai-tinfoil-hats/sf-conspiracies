Berkeley police shoot and kill man suspected of driving stolen car at officer
-----
Berkeley police shot and killed a man suspected of stealing a vehicle and driving it toward an officer on Monday morning, authorities said.Officers were called to Grayson and Seventh streets just before 5:30 a.m. for a report of a car burglary in progress and found several suspects, Berkeley police spokesperson Jessica Perry said in a statement.
Two of the suspects fled in a vehicle, and the other was arrested, police said.
