Conversion of San Francisco industrial building to housing proposed
-----
Developers have filed plans with the San Francisco Planning Department to convert an industrial building in the Potrero Hill neighborhood into mixed-use housing.San Francisco-based Align Real Estate submitted the application for the building at 790 Pennsylvania Ave. on Tuesday. The property sits on the border of the Potrero Hill and Dogpatch neighborhoods, adjacent to Interstate 280 and the 22nd Street Caltrain station.
