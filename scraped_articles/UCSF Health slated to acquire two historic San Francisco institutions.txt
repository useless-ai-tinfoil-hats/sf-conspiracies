UCSF Health slated to acquire two historic San Francisco institutions
-----
UCSF Health struck a deal to acquire Saint Francis Memorial Hospital and St. Mary's Medical Center, two of San Francisco's "longest-serving" hospitals, according to a Feb. 5 news release. The $100 million acquisition, which is expected to be completed in the spring, aims to keep these historic hospitals and their associated outpatient clinics accessible to the community.Located in Nob Hill, Saint Francis has operated for over 100 years, and treats thousands of workers and tourists each
