A's fan protests fading, but common cause found with White Sox in town
-----
"Sell the team" chants have become commonplace at the Coliseum.New jeers joined Oakland A's fans' refrains during Tuesday's White Sox matchup: "Jerry sucks" and "Reinsdorf sucks," referencing White Sox owner Jerry Reinsdorf whose team entered the night 27-88 and riding a 21-game losing streak.
