Andre Iguodala defends Jordan Poole but adds he would ‘fall on purpose’ with Warriors
-----
If you're a Warriors fan who felt like Jordan Poole fell all the time, you're not alone.Andre Iguodala, who has still not retired from the NBA, went on JJ Redick's podcast this week and talked about how Poole's time in the Bay Area crashed on the rocks. Iguodala was sympathetic to how Poole's youth and aggressiveness clashed with the veteran core but also had a unique theory on Poole's infuriating penchant for hitting the deck.
