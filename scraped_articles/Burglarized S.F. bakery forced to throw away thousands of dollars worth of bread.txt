Burglarized S.F. bakery forced to throw away thousands of dollars worth of bread
-----
One of San Francisco's top sourdough bakeries is recovering from a burglary with estimated damages as high as $30,000.On Tuesday morning, staff at Rize Up Bakery, known for its unconventional loaves stuffed with sausage and blended with purple ube, arrived at their SoMa headquarters at 1160 Howard St. to find burglars had broken in overnight, taking the iPad stations used at farmers markets, other computer equipment and cash boxes.
