Fired SF Giants manager Gabe Kapler issues lengthy Instagram statement
-----
Gabe Kapler has written a lengthy Instagram farewell to San Francisco after being fired as the Giants manager on Friday.The dismissal blindsided many: Just two years ago, Kapler was named National League Manager of the Year with a record of 107-55. Since joining the Giants in 2020, Kapler accrued an overall record of 295-248 but only made it to the playoffs once. After a bleak 2023 season, on Friday the organization announced Kapler was out.
