St. Mary’s takes rare continuity into NCAA matchup against Grand Canyon
-----
SPOKANE, Wash. — Grand Canyon's men's basketball team falls perfectly in line with the itinerant world of college sports in 2024: All five Antelopes starters began their career at another school.St. Mary's is the outlier.
Friday night's first-round NCAA Tournament matchup at Spokane Arena offers a sharp contrast, and a relevant reminder of the unique program head coach Randy Bennett has built. Players stay, transfers are rare and somehow the Gaels keep cranking out 25-win seasons.
