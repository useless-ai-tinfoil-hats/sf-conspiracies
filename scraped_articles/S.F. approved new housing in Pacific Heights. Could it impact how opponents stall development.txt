S.F. approved new housing in Pacific Heights. Could it impact how opponents stall development?
-----
A 24-unit condo building proposed for a parking lot next to a historic building in Pacific Heights will go forward after the San Francisco Board of Supervisors rejected a neighbor's efforts to force the city to delay the project for further environmental review.
