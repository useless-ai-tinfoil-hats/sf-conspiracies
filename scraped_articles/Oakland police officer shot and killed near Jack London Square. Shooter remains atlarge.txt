Oakland police officer shot and killed near Jack London Square. Shooter remains at-large
-----
An undercover Oakland police officer was shot and killed while responding to a reported burglary at a cannabis dispensary near Jack London Square early Friday morning, officials said.The killing of the officer, whose name was not released, rippled across the city, which continues to struggle with a rise in violent crime and burglaries. The shooter was not in custody as of Friday afternoon, but officials vowed to bring the killer to justice.
