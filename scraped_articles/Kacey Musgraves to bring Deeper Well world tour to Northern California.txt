Kacey Musgraves to bring ‘Deeper Well’ world tour to Northern California
-----
Grammy-winning singer-songwriter Kacey Musgraves is gearing up for a global tour in support of her upcoming album, "Deeper Well."The "Deeper Well World Tour 2024" is scheduled to kick off in Ireland on April 28, with Musgraves performing across Europe and the U.K. throughout the spring.
The North American leg of the tour will resume in the fall, featuring two Northern California dates: Sept. 23 at the Golden 1 Center in Sacramento and Sept. 24 at the Chase Center in San Francisco.
