Outside Lands announces Dolores’ entertainment lineup
-----
Outside Lands is bringing back Dolores', the San Francisco festival's self-proclaimed "DIY queer dance club," with a new lineup of entertainment this summer.The pop-up venue debuted on Golden Gate Park's Polo Field in 2023, with drag queen Adore Delano; local DJ Sadgayboi; and author, poet and comedian Alok.
