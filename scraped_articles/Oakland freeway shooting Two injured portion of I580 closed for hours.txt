Oakland freeway shooting: Two injured, portion of I-580 closed for hours
-----
Two occupants of a vehicle were injured in a shooting that closed a portion of an Oakland freeway for several hours Saturday, according to the California Highway Patrol.One victim's injuries were life-threatening, and the other suffered major injuries, the CHP told CBS News. No suspect information was available.
All lanes on eastbound Interstate 580 at Edwards Avenue were closed after the shooting, a CHP spokesperson said.
Drivers were encouraged to seek alternative routes
