Tahoe's ski resorts are about to open as snow arrives. Here are the dates
-----
With snow falling in the Sierra this week, Lake Tahoe ski resorts are eagerly gearing up for what many hope is a strong season ahead.Resorts have spent the offseason upgrading lifts, improving terrain and devising new parking reservation systems to help alleviate the maddening bursts of ski traffic gridlock. Now, with 3 inches of snow having accumulated at Donner Pass on Monday and a storm forecast for next week, ski season is on the cusp of returning.
