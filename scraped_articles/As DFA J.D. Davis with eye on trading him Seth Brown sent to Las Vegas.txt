A's DFA J.D. Davis with eye on trading him; Seth Brown sent to Las Vegas
-----
Lengthy losing streaks can prompt any team to make personnel moves, and so it was for the Oakland Athletics on Tuesday. Before snapping a nine-game skid, the A's designated corner infielder J.D. Davis for assignment and outrighted outfielder Seth Brown to Triple-A Las Vegas.The corresponding moves were recalling outfielder Lawrence Butler from Vegas and selecting utilityman Tyler Nevin from the Aviators.
