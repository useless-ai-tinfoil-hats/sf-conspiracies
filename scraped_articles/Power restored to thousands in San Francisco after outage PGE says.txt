Power restored to thousands in San Francisco after outage, PG&E says
-----
Power was restored to thousands of customers in San Francisco after an outage on Thursday morning, PG&E said.Tamar Sarkissian, a spokesperson for the utility, said 8,897 customers were impacted in the outage that started at 7:04 a.m. The lights were back on by 8:44 a.m., Sarkissian said.
A PG&E map showed that the outage mainly occurred in the Sunset District, Golden Gate Heights and Forest Hill.
