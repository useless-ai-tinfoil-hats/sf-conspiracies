Salt built this California ghost town. Now salt is destroying it.
-----
Saltdale is not California's most picturesque ghost town. It's certainly not the largest, nor is it the most historical. But it may be the next to disappear.Saltdale sinks into Koehn Lake, a dry lakebed with salt deposits on its surface in the Mojave Desert, about 20 miles north of California City.
