Walgreens is giving away a free 8" x 10" photo when you checkout online
-----
Wouldn't it be nice if you could look at some of your favorite Instagram photos without having to open the app? Thanks to a nifty little thing called "printing your photos for free," you can. Walgreens, the beloved pharmacy, is offering shoppers a free 8x10 glossy photo print with the promo code DEAL-OF-NOV.
