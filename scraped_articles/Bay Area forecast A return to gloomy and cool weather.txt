Bay Area forecast: A return to gloomy and cool weather
-----
The weather got interesting around the Bay Area over the last few days as a moisture-rich air mass generated lots of cloud cover and some scattered showers and lightning. The increased high cloud cover made for picturesque sunsets but did not provide much precipitation, as most of the rain evaporated before reaching the surface.Former Tropical Storm Alberto has been pumping tropical air into the southwestern part of the country for several days now.
