Florida State blows up Cal, Stanford in fiery ACC lawsuit
-----
If you thought that college sports' realignment merry-go-round ended once Stanford and Cal joined the Atlantic Coast Conference, think again.Florida State University sued the ACC on Friday morning, with the apparent intention of getting a court to give it a cheaper way out of the conference. Right now, to ditch the ACC would cost the school more than $500 million. Florida State's argument is that the ACC made decisions that "appear dedicated to self-preservation and self-perpetuation
