Three people killed, two injured in Richmond shootings, police say
-----
Three people were killed and two others were injured in two separate shootings eight hours apart in Richmond, police said.The first shooting occurred around 4:15 p.m. Tuesday on Pennsylvania Avenue near Seventh Street. One of the victims died at the scene, while three others showed up at a hospital, where one of them died, police said. The two other victims were expected to survive, police said.
