Losing bronze-medal match can’t tarnish a golden era for U.S. fencing
-----
PARIS — When Alexander Massialas took the fencing strip for the final, anchor match, with his foil team trailing France by 10 points, the packed house of 9,000 at the Grand Palais broke out in a spontaneous rendition of "La Marseillaise."Massialas and the Americans couldn't overcome the crowd or France's momentum. They lost the bronze-medal match for the first time in three Olympics.
