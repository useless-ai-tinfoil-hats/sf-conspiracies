Tickets for 90 Bay Area concerts for just $25 each, from Missy Elliott to Peso Pluma
-----
Music and comedy lovers can purchase all-inclusive tickets for just $25 to more than 5,000 shows nationwide, as part of Live Nation's annual Concert Week promotion.The week-long event, running from Wednesday, May 8, through May 14, marks the beginning of the summer concert season and features discounted tickets for more than 900 music and comedy acts, including singer-songwriter Alanis Morissette, rock band Weezer, hitmaker Janet Jackson, comic Chelsea Handler and many more.
