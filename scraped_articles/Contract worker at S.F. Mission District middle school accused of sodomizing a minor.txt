Contract worker at S.F. Mission District middle school accused of sodomizing a minor
-----
A San Francisco school worker accused of sexually assaulting a child made his first court appearance on Friday.Police arrested Jeremy Reyes, 20, on Tuesday afternoon at Everett Middle School in the Mission District.
Officers who initially responded to reports of an "inappropriate relationship between a student and a school staff member" later booked Reyes on a raft of felony charges, including sexual battery and sodomy of a person under 18.
