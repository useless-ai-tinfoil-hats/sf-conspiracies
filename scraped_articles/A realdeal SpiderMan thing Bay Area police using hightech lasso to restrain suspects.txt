‘A real-deal Spiderman thing’: Bay Area police using high-tech lasso to restrain suspects
-----
David Wentworth, an El Cerrito police sergeant, aimed a flashlight-size yellow device, shouted, "Bola, bola, bola," and pressed a button. A loud bang sounded, and in a split second, a slender cord wrapped three times around Veronica Ceja, a police department worker.
