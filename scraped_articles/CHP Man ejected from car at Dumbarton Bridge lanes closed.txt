CHP: Man ejected from car at Dumbarton Bridge, lanes closed
-----
Eastbound lanes of Highway 84 approaching the Dumbarton Bridge were closed on Thursday morning due to a gravel spill and at least two traffic collisions, according to a California Highway Patrol Sig Alert.CHP reported a gravel spill just after 5:30 a.m. Shortly after a vehicle spun out of control and a Tesla overturned onto its roof, CHP said in the alert.
In a separate collision, CHP also reported that a man was ejected from a vehicle.
