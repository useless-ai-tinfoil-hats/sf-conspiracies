S.F. Civic Center concert ends abruptly at curfew during set by Skrillex, Fred Again..
-----
Superstar DJs Fred Again.. and Skrillex's last-minute Civic Center Plaza "rave" came to an abrupt end Saturday as the duo hit the city's 10 p.m. curfew.The two were performing midsong when the audio stopped. The music ended, but the pair remained onstage waving to the crowd and garnering cheers for five minutes afterward, providing the San Francisco audience a proper farewell.
