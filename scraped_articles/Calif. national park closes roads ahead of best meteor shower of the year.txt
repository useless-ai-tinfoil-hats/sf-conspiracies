Calif. national park closes roads ahead of ‘best meteor shower of the year’
-----
The Perseid meteor shower is sending streaks of dazzling light across the California sky and one of the state's national parks is closing roads in anticipation of stargazing crowds this evening.Joshua Tree National Park has closed six roads and one road area through Tuesday. These are all dirt roads with limited parking and were closed to discourage vehicles from illegally parking on vegetation, according to Donovan Smith, a park ranger at Joshua Tree.
