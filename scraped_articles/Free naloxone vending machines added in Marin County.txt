Free naloxone vending machines added in Marin County
-----
Vending machines that dispense free naloxone, a nasal spray that can reverse the effects of opioid overdoses, have been installed throughout Marin County, public health officials announced Tuesday.The installation comes ahead of International Overdose Awareness Day on Saturday, and it's part of the county's effort to reduce opioid overdoses, the county's leading cause of death in residents 55 and under, according to a news release.
