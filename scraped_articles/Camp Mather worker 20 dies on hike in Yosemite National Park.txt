Camp Mather worker, 20, dies on hike in Yosemite National Park
-----
An employee of San Francisco's Camp Mather died in an accident while on a hike in Yosemite National Park on Thursday, officials said. Matt Mullen, 20, was hiking in the park with other camp staff on his day off, San Francisco Recreation and Parks wrote in an emailed statement.
