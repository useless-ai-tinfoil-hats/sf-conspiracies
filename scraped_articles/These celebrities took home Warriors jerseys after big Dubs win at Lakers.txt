These celebrities took home Warriors jerseys after big Dubs win at Lakers
-----
It was a star-studded night on and off the court in Los Angeles for the Golden State Warriors' win on Tuesday night over the Lakers.The final regular season matchup between the two California rivals had all sorts of celebrities making their way to Crypto.com Arena.
Some of the notable celebs in attendance included Kim Kardashian and her daughter, North West, Brazilian music star Ludmilla, DJ and music producer Diplo, actor Will Ferrell and YouTube star IShowSpeed.
