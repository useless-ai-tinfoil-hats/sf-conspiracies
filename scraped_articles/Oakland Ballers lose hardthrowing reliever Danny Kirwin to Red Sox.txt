Oakland Ballers lose hard-throwing reliever Danny Kirwin to Red Sox
-----
The Oakland Ballers won't open their first training camp for another three weeks, but they've already lost a player to a big-league team.The Boston Red Sox swiped hard-throwing reliever Danny Kirwin from the independent team Tuesday after the Ballers posted video of Kirwin throwing 98 mph.
