What’s causing the Warriors’ fourth-quarter flailing? ‘Honestly, everything’
-----
MIAMI — After another tough loss, another blown lead, Stephen Curry identified the issues informing the Golden State Warriors' fourth-quarter struggles."Honestly, everything," the guard said Sunday after a 114-110 loss to the Minnesota Timberwolves.
"Getting outshot from the 3-point line. Giving up offensive rebounds. Disorganized on offense with a couple of tough turnovers," he added. "Any game, a play here, a play there, can define 48 minutes."
