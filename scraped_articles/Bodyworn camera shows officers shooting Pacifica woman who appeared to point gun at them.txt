Body-worn camera shows officers shooting Pacifica woman who appeared to point gun at them
-----
The Pacifica Police Department released body-worn camera footage Thursday showing two officers shooting and killing a 50-year-old woman who appeared to point a gun at them.The department identified the officers as Kevin Contreras and Hayden Fry and the woman as Michelle Arrais.
Police said they were called after Arrais shot her 23-year-old son in their apartment on Francisco Boulevard just before 8 a.m. March 27.
"I was shot twice in my arm," her son told a dispatcher
