Review: ‘War Game’ imagines a Jan. 6 insurrection even worse than 2021
-----
"War Game" is one of the more timely and disturbing movies of recent months. It imagines a Jan. 6-type insurrection, but one on steroids, with an armed attack on the U.S. Capitol by terrorists claiming that the 2024 presidential election has been stolen.It takes place in the near future — Jan. 6, 2025 — and watching it, one can't help but wonder whether this near future is our future. Likewise, it's hard not to wonder how this film will be seen years from now, after present-day anxieties
