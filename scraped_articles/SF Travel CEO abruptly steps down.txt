SF Travel CEO abruptly steps down
-----
An unexpected shakeup rattled the San Francisco tourism industry this week as it continues to recover from the pandemic. The San Francisco Travel Association abruptly announced Wednesday that Scott Beck is stepping down as president and CEO of the marketing organization, citing "personal reasons."Beck, who succeeded Joe D'Alessandro in October 2023, served as San Francisco's lead marketer for just seven months.
