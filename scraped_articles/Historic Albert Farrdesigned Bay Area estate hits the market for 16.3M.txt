Historic Albert Farr-designed Bay Area estate hits the market for $16.3M
-----
Albert Farr was one of the most prestigious residential architects in the San Francisco Bay Area for much of the first half of the 20th century. His statement homes adorn the most expensive enclaves of the most expensive cities and communities, from San Francisco to Belvedere to Piedmont.
