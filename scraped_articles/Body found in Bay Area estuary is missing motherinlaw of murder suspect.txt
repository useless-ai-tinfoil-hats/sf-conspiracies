Body found in Bay Area estuary is missing mother-in-law of murder suspect
-----
DNA tests revealed that a body found in the Oakland Estuary last year is the missing mother-in-law of a murder suspect, Bay Area police announced Tuesday.According to San Pablo police, Phuc Vo, 40, reported his wife and mother-in-law missing on Sept. 13. When he spoke with detectives, he allegedly said they'd left their home on the 1900 block of Sutter Avenue about a week prior because of an argument.
