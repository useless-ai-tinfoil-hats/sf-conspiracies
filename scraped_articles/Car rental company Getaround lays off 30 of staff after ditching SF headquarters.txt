Car rental company Getaround lays off 30% of staff after ditching SF headquarters
-----
Getaround, a company that facilitates car rentals, is laying off 30% of its North American staff, the company announced in a blog post Wednesday. The news comes almost four months after Getaround ditched its San Francisco headquarters.The layoff round, set to impact dozens of workers, is part of Getaround's attempt to "lengthen its cash runway and accelerate its path to profitability," a Thursday press release said. The company has been unprofitable for years.
