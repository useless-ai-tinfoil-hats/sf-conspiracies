Back to school in S.F.: Excited students, anxious adults as consequential year kicks off
-----
Parents outside San Francisco's Marina Middle School snapped pictures, straightened collars and hugged their eye-rolling adolescents, who then headed to the first day of class Monday. Some kids were excited; others wanted a few more days of summer vacation.Across the district, similar scenes played out: happy kids heading back to school with new backpacks, eager to see friends and meet their teachers.
