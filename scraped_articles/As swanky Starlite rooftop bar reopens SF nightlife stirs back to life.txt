As swanky Starlite rooftop bar reopens, SF nightlife stirs back to life
-----
On a rainy winter night, the streets of Union Square were quiet, people huddling beneath umbrellas. But on the 21st floor of the Beacon Grand, a party was roaring.The hotel's historic rooftop bar Starlite — previously known as Harry Denton's Starlight Room — sparkled back to life at a grand opening party on Feb. 1. Revelers showed up in sequins and fur coats, sipping cocktails on green velvet couches overlooking panoramic views of the city.
