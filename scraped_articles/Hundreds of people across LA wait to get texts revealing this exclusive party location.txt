Hundreds of people across LA wait to get texts revealing this exclusive party location
-----
It's late on a chilly, overcast night as partygoers wind through the labyrinthine alleys of an empty office park. Thumps of bass lurch through one building, located in a lonely stretch of downtown Los Angeles, as if calling out to a growing crowd. Most revelers are wearing preppy striped ties and button-downs, while some wear capes to shield themselves against the chill. Everyone is holding their phones to load the text that announced the night's location, sent about an hour before doors
