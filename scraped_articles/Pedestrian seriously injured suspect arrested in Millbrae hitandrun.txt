Pedestrian seriously injured, suspect arrested in Millbrae hit-and-run
-----
A pedestrian suffered life-threatening injuries in a hit-and-run crash in Millbrae on Sunday, and the suspect was tracked down and arrested, investigators said.The crash occurred around 5:40 a.m. near the busy intersection of Millbrae Avenue and El Camino Real, where a witness reported that the driver struck the pedestrian and fled, according to the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office.
