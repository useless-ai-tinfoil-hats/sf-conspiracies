Body of missing California woman found by civilian drone on Mount Baldy
-----
A 22-year old California woman who was reported missing while hiking Mount Baldy during a massive storm has been found dead, authorities say.The body of El Monte resident Lifei Huang was spotted by a civilian drone on Saturday, but high winds prevented a recovery by search and rescue crews that day, the San Bernardino County Sheriff's Office said in a statement. "Search and rescue team members stayed the night near the location to preserve the scene," the department added.
