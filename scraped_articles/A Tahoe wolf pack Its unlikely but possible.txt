A Tahoe wolf pack? It's unlikely, but possible
-----
There was a time in California's history when wolves thrived in the state. Records from the mid-1700s show that gray wolves lived in coastal areas of California, but started migrating toward the central and northern mountain ranges — Lake Tahoe included — when the human populace burgeoned on the coasts in the late 1700s.
