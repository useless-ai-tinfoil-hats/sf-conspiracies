Sitting in the last row of Allegiant Stadium: Four California siblings surprised with $38,000 Super Bowl tickets
-----
LAS VEGAS — Harlan Carlson was playing blackjack at the Green Valley Ranch Resort Spa on Friday night when he got a phone call from his brother. He had an exciting surprise — and needed to meet.Carlson moved his remaining $125 all in, busted and raced to find his brother, Curt, who runs a company in California but lives in Henderson, on the outskirts of Las Vegas. The siblings had met in Las Vegas to watch the game from Curt's living room. Harlan soon forgot about his big loss.
