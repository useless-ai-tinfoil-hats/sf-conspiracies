Old-school San Francisco Italian restaurant to close after 53 years
-----
After 53 years, the aptly named North Beach Restaurant, in San Francisco's North Beach, plans to close at the end of the year, as first reported by the San Francisco Chronicle (the Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms).When the restaurant's co-founders, Bruno Orsi and Lorenzo Petroni died (Orsi in 2013, and Petroni in 2014), it was the beginning of the end, general manager Maureen Donegan told SFGATE.
