Burning Man attendees call their moms from the playa ‘pay phone'
-----
BLACK ROCK CITY, Nev. — Outside of the Temple of the Flying Spaghetti Monster, a long-standing Burning Man camp led by tech professionals, a line formed at what looked like a pay phone.Using the phone — which works perfectly and can call most countries around the world — costs nothing. That's because Burner Aaron Hopkins ripped out its guts over a decade ago and replaced the older, coin-operated technology with a touch-tone phone circuit board plugged into a voice-over-IP adapter.
