First of its kind 'Star Wars' movie slated to film entirely in California
-----
Several upcoming movies are slated to be filmed throughout the Bay Area soon, proving once again that California's diverse landscape feels closer to fantasy than reality.Overall, 15 projects will be filmed in California for about a year and a half, with 41% of those planned filming days taking place outside of Los Angeles, press representatives with the California Film Commission told SFGATE in an emailed announcement. Alameda County, Marin and San Francisco are on the locations list.
