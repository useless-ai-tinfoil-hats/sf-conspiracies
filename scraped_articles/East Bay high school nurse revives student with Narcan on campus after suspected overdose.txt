East Bay high school nurse revives student with Narcan on campus after suspected overdose
-----
A high school nurse in Antioch administered Narcan to a student Tuesday who was apparently overdosing on drugs at lunchtime, a spokesperson for the school district said Wednesday.The Deer Valley High School nurse administered 4 mg of Narcan to a student who was "under the influence" and unconscious just before 12:08 p.m., spokesperson Lindsay Wisely told the Chronicle.
Narcan, also known as naloxone, is a powerful opioid overdose-reversing nasal spray.
