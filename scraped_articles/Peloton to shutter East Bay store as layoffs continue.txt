Peloton to shutter East Bay store as layoffs continue
-----
Peloton is closing its Walnut Creek store as the bike maker continues to cut costs and shrink its workforce.The company said in a California filing that six workers at the store will be laid off by February 2024 and the showroom at 1245 Broadway Plaza will permanently close.
