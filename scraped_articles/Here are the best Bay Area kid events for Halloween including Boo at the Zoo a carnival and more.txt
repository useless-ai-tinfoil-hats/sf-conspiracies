Here are the best Bay Area kid events for Halloween, including Boo at the Zoo, a carnival and more
-----
It's Halloween time and Bay Area kids are getting ready to hit neighborhood streets, suburban downtown areas and shopping malls loaded with spooky style for some serious trick-or-treating.And with the official Oct. 31 holiday falling on a Tuesday, there's plenty of time for showing off creative costumes and collecting candy to fill trick-or-treat buckets, as many kid- and family-focused events and activities are set to take place leading up to the holiday over the preceding weekend.
