70-year-old SF ice cream shop, other restaurants added to legacy business registry
-----
The San Francisco Small Business Commission just added 11 new businesses to its Legacy Business Registry, according to a press release Friday. Five restaurants and bars were recognized: Mitchell's Ice Cream, Mario's Bohemian Cigar Store Cafe, The Irish Bank Bar & Restaurant, The Dubliner and Frascati Restaurant.Mitchell's Ice Cream, a Noe Valley institution that offers 40 different ice cream flavors daily, recently celebrated its 70th anniversary with 70-cent scoops and a new mural.
