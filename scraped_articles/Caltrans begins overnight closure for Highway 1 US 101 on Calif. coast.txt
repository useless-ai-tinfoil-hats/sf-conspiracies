Caltrans begins overnight closure for Highway 1, US 101 on Calif. coast
-----
Road crews begin a multiweek overnight project on Monday that closes lanes and on-ramps while redirecting one-way traffic on two quintessential California highways along the Central Coast.Caltrans is closing sections of both U.S. 101 and state Route 1 in Pismo Beach to repair culverts under the roadways from 10 p.m. to 6 a.m. One lane in each direction will remain open during construction hours.
The northbound lane on U.S. 101 between Bello Street and Mattie Road will close
