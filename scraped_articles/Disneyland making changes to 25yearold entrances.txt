Disneyland making changes to 25-year-old entrances
-----
With Disney drawing increasing crowds year-round, the company is investing $4.8 million in a project that will, hopefully, get you to the magic faster than ever before.Disney will begin construction at Disneyland this year to replace the old entry turnstiles with new automated entry gates, Disneyland officials told the Orange County Register. The company plans to have 38 new gates at Disneyland and 24 at Disney California Adventure Park.
