One dead in three-car crash on Highway 101
-----
One person died early Friday morning after two cars crashed into another car that was disabled on Highway 101 in Palo Alto.A driver called authorities at approximately 2:56 a.m. and said they had hit something and their car was disabled in the fourth lane of the highway, Sophie Lu, a spokesperson for the California Highway Patrol, told SFGATE. The driver was stopped on the northbound shoulder of the freeway near the San Antonio Road onramp.
