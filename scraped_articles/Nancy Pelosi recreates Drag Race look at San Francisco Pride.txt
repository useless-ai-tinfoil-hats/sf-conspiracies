Nancy Pelosi recreates ‘Drag Race’ look at San Francisco Pride
-----
"Oh my God, it's Nancy Pelosi." This could be heard by multiple audience members about halfway through Billy Porter's headlining show at San Francisco's Pride celebration Sunday night as he introduced a special guest onto the stage in between City Hall and the Civic Center Plaza."For over six decades, this person had been an ally to our community long before it was fashionable and before it was politically in vogue," Porter said to the hundreds of people wearing rainbows and sparkles.
