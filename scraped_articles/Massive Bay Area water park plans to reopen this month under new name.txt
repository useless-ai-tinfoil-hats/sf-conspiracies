Massive Bay Area water park plans to reopen this month under new name
-----
One of Northern California's largest water parks is set to open later this month, bringing a 23-acre park filled with waterslides and attractions to San Jose just in time for summer.CaliBunga Water Park announced in an Instagram post yesterday that it will open its gates on June 29, giving guests access to attractions including waterslides, a lazy river and multiple bar areas for adults to enjoy.
