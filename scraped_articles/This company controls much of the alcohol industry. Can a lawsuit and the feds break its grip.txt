This company controls much of the alcohol industry. Can a lawsuit and the feds break its grip?
-----
The average American drinker wouldn't know Southern Glazer's Wine and Spirits by name. But chances are good that they've consumed its beverages, which include top-selling brands like Grey Goose, Smirnoff, Bacardi, Jameson, Patron, Josh Cellars and Meomi.Southern Glazer's doesn't make these products; it distributes them, serving as the middleman between the maker and the seller.
