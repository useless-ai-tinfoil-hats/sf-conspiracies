Billionaire MLB owner accused of stealing sand from wealthy Calif. beach
-----
Billionaire Milwaukee Brewers owner Mark Attanasio has allegedly been stealing sand from an exclusive Southern California beach to use on a personal construction project at his home, according to a lawsuit filed last week in Los Angeles.Attanasio's next-door neighbor along Malibu's Broad Beach filed the lawsuit against the MLB owner after gathering video and photographic evidence of excavators operating in a tidal zone and bringing sand from the public beach to Attanasio's property.
