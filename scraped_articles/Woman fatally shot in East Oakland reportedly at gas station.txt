Woman fatally shot in East Oakland, reportedly at gas station
-----
Police are investigating a fatal shooting that occurred Saturday morning in East Oakland, reportedly at a gas station.Officers responded to the 2200 block of East 12th Street shortly after 5:45 a.m. and found a victim with gunshot wounds, the Oakland Police Department said. Paramedics rendered aid, but the victim was pronounced dead at the scene, police said.
