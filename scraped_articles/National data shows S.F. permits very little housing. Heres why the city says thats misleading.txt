National data shows S.F. permits very little housing. Here’s why the city says that’s misleading
-----
The most widely cited national dataset on home permitting shows San Francisco lagging dramatically behind other major cities. San Francisco, the preliminary data claims, greenlit fewer than 347 privately owned housing units from January to September 2023, or 43 units per 100,000 people.
