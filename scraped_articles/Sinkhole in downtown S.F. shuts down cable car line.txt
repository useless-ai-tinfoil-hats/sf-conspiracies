Sinkhole in downtown S.F. shuts down cable car line
-----
A sinkhole opened up in downtown San Francisco on Tuesday night, sending a motorcyclist to the hospital, forcing traffic detours, snarling Muni routes and shutting down the California line of the cable car.The sinkhole developed near the intersection of California and Montgomery streets, San Francisco firefighters said.
