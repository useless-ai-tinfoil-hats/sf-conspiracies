Yosemite once almost hosted the Winter Olympics
-----
There have been some radical ideas proposed to attract tourists to Yosemite National Park over the years.Some transformational projects never came to fruition, like a gondola running from Yosemite Valley to Glacier Point or a railroad built through the heart of the park. Others, like an airstrip for small planes constructed in a meadow just west of Yosemite Lodge, did happen despite the outrageous logistics of landing an aircraft in the Sierra Nevada.
