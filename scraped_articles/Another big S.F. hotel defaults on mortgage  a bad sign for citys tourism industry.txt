Another big S.F. hotel defaults on mortgage — a bad sign for city’s tourism industry
-----
Another big San Francisco hotel has defaulted on its mortgage, a sign of widening distress in the city's critical hospitality sector.The owner of the Hilton Financial District at 750 Kearny St. said Wednesday that it defaulted on a $97 million loan, according to a securities filing. Portsmouth Square Inc.'s affiliate Justice Operating Company LLC was issued the notice of default for the 544-room hotel.
