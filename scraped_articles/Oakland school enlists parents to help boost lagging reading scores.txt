Oakland school enlists parents to help boost lagging reading scores
-----
In the black-and-white world of standardized tests, Hoover Elementary, located a few blocks from a freeway entrance in West Oakland, is failing its 300 students.Just 15% of them read at grade level, according to the most recent scores, including just 9% of those in the third grade. The principal and teachers are under immense pressure to bring those numbers up as students sit for the annual round of testing this month.
