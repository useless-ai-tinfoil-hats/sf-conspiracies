Warriors trade back in to 2024 NBA Draft after initially trading out
-----
LATEST June 27, 3:30 p.m. The Golden State Warriors have reportedly re-acquired the 2024 NBA Draft pick they traded away.According to multiple reports, Golden State traded with the Portland Trail Blazers to get the 52nd pick in the draft, a pick the Warriors initially traded away about two hours before the draft to the Oklahoma City Thunder. (Oklahoma City had traded the pick to Portland.) With the selection, the Warriors took Quinten Post, a center out of Boston College.
