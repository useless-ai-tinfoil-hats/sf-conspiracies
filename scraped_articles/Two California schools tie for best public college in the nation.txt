Two California schools tie for best public college in the nation
-----
UC Berkeley and UCLA tied for the No. 1 spot on the recently released U.S. News and World Report list of the nation's top public colleges and universities. The two schools also tied in the overall national ranking, which includes both public and private schools, in the No. 15 slot.UCLA has been the No. 1 public university for the last seven years in the U.S. News rankings.
