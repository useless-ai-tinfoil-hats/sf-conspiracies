Southwest Airlines flight came within 400 feet from slamming into ocean off Hawaii: report
-----
A Southwest Airlines flight in April came within 400 feet of plunging into the ocean off the coast of Hawaii after a landing attempt that was aborted due to weather conditions, according to a Bloomberg news report.A company memo sent to Southwest pilots, obtained by Bloomberg, detailed the April 11 incident in which the Boeing 737 Max 8 dropped from almost 1,000 feet to 400 feet in a few seconds.
