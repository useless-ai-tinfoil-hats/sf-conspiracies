49ers will release Arik Armstead after contract-restructuring talks fail
-----
The San Francisco 49ers reportedly will release Arik Armstead after the team and its longtime defensive tackle failed to reach agreement on a restructured contract.Armstead, 30, a four-time team captain who is the 49ers' longest-tenured player, was scheduled to earn a $17.4 million base salary as part of the five-year, $85 million contract extension he signed in 2020. Bleacher Report first reported Armstead's pending release.
