7 Calif. men to serve jail time for animal poaching scheme in Ventura County
-----
Seven men in Ventura County pleaded guilty in an animal poaching scheme in which they illegally hunted wildlife, including bears, state officials said.In a Wednesday news release, the California Department of Fish and Wildlife said the men conspired with a clerk to fraudulently obtain a "highly unusual" amount of replacement hunting licenses and tags from a license vendor.
