East Bay teen missing for nearly a week found safely
-----
The Alameda County Sheriff's Office said Thursday that a teenager missing since Oct. 6 had been found safely.Officials issued an alert Thursday morning asking for the public's help in finding the teenager.
Allison Bettencourt, 14, was last seen after she was dropped off at Hayward High School at 8 a.m. Oct. 6, sheriff's officials said.
The Sheriff's Office did not immediately provide additional information about the circumstances about how she was located.
