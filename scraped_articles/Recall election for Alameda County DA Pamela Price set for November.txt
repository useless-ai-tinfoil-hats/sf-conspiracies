Recall election for Alameda County DA Pamela Price set for November
-----
Alameda County District Attorney Pamela Price is set to face a recall vote in November.The Board of Supervisors on Tuesday agreed to consolidate the recall vote with the general election on Nov. 5, setting the stage for six months of campaigning for and against Price and her progressive policies.
The vote was unanimous, with Supervisors Lena Tam and David Haubert absent.
