Woman fatally shot while driving in East Oakland
-----
A woman was shot and killed while driving in East Oakland on Saturday night, authorities said.The woman was found suffering from gunshot wounds after colliding with a traffic light in the 8200 block of International Boulevard just before 9 p.m., the Oakland Police Department said in a statement.
The woman, whose identity was not immediately released, was pronounced dead at the scene of the crash.
Authorities were investigating the circumstances and motive for the shooting
