Tina Fey and Amy Poehler announce shows in 2 San Francisco Bay Area cities
-----
Good things come in pairs: two comedy legends, two Bay Area shows.Emmy-winning best pals Amy Poehler and Tina Fey are bringing their "Restless Leg Tour" to the Bay Area for back-to-back sets in San Francisco and Oakland early next year.
The San Francisco show is scheduled to take place at the Masonic on Friday, Jan. 26, with the Oakland show coming a day later at the Paramount Theatre on Saturday, Jan. 27.
