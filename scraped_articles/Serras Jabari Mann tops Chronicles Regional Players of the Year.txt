Serra’s Jabari Mann tops Chronicle’s Regional Players of the Year
-----
PENINSULAJabari Mann (Serra) — Separating senior players on the Chronicle's top Metro team was very difficult, but Mann was picked by West Catholic Athletic League coaches as the league's Player of the Year. The 6-foot, 205-pound linebacker, running back and wildcat quarterback did it all for the Padres (12-1), who were selected for the third straight year as Northern California's Open Division representative for the state playoffs.
