Apple store at tech giant's former Bay Area headquarters closes for good
-----
Apple's famed Infinite Loop store at the tech giant's former Silicon Valley headquarters permanently closed Saturday after three decades of launching new products, selling highly sought-after branded merchandise and developing retail strategies there.A spokesperson for Apple confirmed the closure of the flagship store with SFGATE on Sunday.
