Northern California wildfire crews ‘doing a great job’ on Sites, Point blazes under high heat
-----
Firefighters were battled the Sites and Point fires in Northern California through another day of high temperatures Sunday after making progress overnight, according to Cal Fire.Both fires did not grow in acreage overnight, according to a Sunday update.
