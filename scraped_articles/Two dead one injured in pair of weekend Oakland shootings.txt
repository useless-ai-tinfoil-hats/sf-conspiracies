Two dead, one injured in pair of weekend Oakland shootings
-----
Oakland police are investigating a pair of weekend shootings that killed two people and injured another.Officers responded to a report of a double shooting on the 7100 block of International Boulevard, about a mile east of the Oakland Coliseum, just after 4:15 p.m. Saturday, the Oakland Police Department said in a statement. They found a victim outside a business and one inside.
