Yosemite rock climbing: Proposed new rule sparks fear of 'existential crisis'
-----
Could big-wall rock climbing in places like Yosemite Valley soon face an "existential crisis?"That's a question swirling around the climbing community this week in the wake of new proposals by federal land agencies to tighten regulations on outdoor pursuits like rock climbing, ice climbing, mountaineering, canyoneering and caving, all of which typically depend on the use of fixed anchors for protection.
