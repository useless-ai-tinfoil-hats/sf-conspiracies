S.F. Symphony cancels one SoundBox program and plans ongoing reduction of the series
-----
The San Francisco Symphony plans to reduce the schedule for SoundBox, its 10-year-old experimental performance venue and concert series, as a cost-cutting measure.The first on the chopping block is a program planned for March 8-9, which was to be curated by Music Director Esa-Pekka Salonen. There are no plans to reschedule the canceled performance, the orchestra announced Thursday, Jan. 4.
The orchestra added that future plans for SoundBox will include just two programs per season
