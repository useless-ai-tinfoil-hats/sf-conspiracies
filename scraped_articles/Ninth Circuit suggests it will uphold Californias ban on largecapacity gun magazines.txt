Ninth Circuit suggests it will uphold California’s ban on large-capacity gun magazines
-----
A divided federal appeals court says California's ban on large-capacity gun magazines is likely to be upheld — despite the Supreme Court's rulings expanding the right to own and carry firearms — and can remain in effect while the state challenges a judge's ruling that found the law unconstitutional.The state has presented evidence that magazines holding more than 10 cartridges "pose a significant threat to public safety," the Ninth U.S. Circuit Court of Appeals said in a 7-4 decision
