Bay Area marketplace razed in fire, destroying six businesses
-----
A Bay Area market was razed in a fire early Thursday morning, destroying the premises of six food vendors within.According to the Alameda County Fire Department, the blaze at Castro Valley's Lake Chabot Public Market began around 2 a.m. Thursday. The building was engulfed, causing the roof to collapse. Fire officials believe the building is likely a total loss due to the damage.
The market was home to C&H Meat Company, Mama Cho's BBQ, Milk & Cookie Bar, Poke Go, Shumi Ramen and T4.
