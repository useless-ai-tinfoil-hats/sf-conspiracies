Warriors officially land 2025 NBA All-Star Game at Chase Center
-----
Golden State Warriors guard Stephen Curry has made the All-Star team nine times in his transcendent career, but he hasn't played in an All-Star Game in the Bay Area. Now he has a chance.The NBA announced Monday that the Warriors will host the 2025 All-Star Game at Chase Center in San Francisco, the first time since 2000 it will be held in the Bay Area. The game will be played on Feb. 16, 2025, barely more than 15 months from now.
