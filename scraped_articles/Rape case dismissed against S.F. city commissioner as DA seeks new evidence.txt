Rape case dismissed against S.F. city commissioner as DA seeks new evidence
-----
A Superior Court judge on Tuesday granted a San Francisco District Attorney's Office motion to dismiss sexual assault charges against a city commissioner, citing a lack of evidence and unanswered questions surrounding the alleged victim's credibility.The motion was granted by Judge Alexandra Gordon, according to the district attorney's office.
