Hiker missing since Tuesday on California's tallest mountain
-----
Rescue teams are scouring the flanks of California's tallest mountain in an urgent search for a hiker missing since Tuesday.Tom Gerbier, described as 6'2" tall and French-speaking, set out for a day hike on Mount Whitney on Tuesday and did not return, according to a Facebook post from Inyo Search and Rescue.
"If you were on either side of Mt. Whitney and have seen him, please let us know!" the group posted, adding that it is also "looking for photos of the summit register."
