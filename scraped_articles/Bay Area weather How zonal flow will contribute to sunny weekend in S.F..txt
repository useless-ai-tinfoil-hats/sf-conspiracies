Bay Area weather: How ‘zonal flow’ will contribute to sunny weekend in S.F.
-----
Patchy coastal fog, inland sunshine and seasonal temperatures are expected for the final three days of June across the Bay Area.The tranquil weekend forecast is a product of a stable atmospheric pattern known as "zonal flow." Zonal flow prevails when the jet stream, a ribbon of winds high in the atmosphere, blows directly from west to east.
