Cal football opens practice behind healthy and bulked-up QB Fernando Mendoza
-----
Exactly one month before it plays its first football game as an ACC team against UC Davis, Cal held its initial preseason practice at Memorial Stadium on Wednesday morning."Best day of the year right here," head coach Justin Wilcox said.

Wilcox added, "First practice is always a lot of energy, a lot of mistakes, so it gives us plenty to work on."
