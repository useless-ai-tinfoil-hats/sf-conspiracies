Costco has planned a sprawling gas station in Marin. Not everyone is happy
-----
A protest of plans to build one of the Bay Area's largest gas stations at the Novato Costco turned heads on a busy shopping Saturday.The proposed gas station would be about an acre large and able to fuel 28 cars at a time, according to plans filed with the city of Novato. It would also include three 40,000-gallon gasoline storage tanks and one 1,500-gallon tank, all underground.
