Revisiting the Chronicle’s 25 predictions for the 49ers’ 2023 season
-----
In July, before the start of training camp, someone predicted San Francisco 49ers tight end George Kittle would have 65 catches, rookie kicker Jake Moody would miss four field goals, the 49ers would trade quarterback Trey Lance, make a deadline deal for a pass rusher, barely beat the Lions in the playoffs and have their season end again in heartbreaking fashion, with the latest postseason soul-crusher involving a special-teams snafu.Apologies for being so amazingly spot-on about that
