S.F.’s iconic Cliff House finally announces new restaurant reopening
-----
After years of collective anxiety and speculation, the Bay Area now has confirmation that the former Cliff House building's restaurant space is expected to reopen in late 2024 — following a major remodel and change in operator.The National Park Service announced Wednesday that Sutro Lands End Partners, LLC, has signed a 20-year lease to operate the famous property, set on a rugged cliff with sweeping views of the Pacific Ocean.
