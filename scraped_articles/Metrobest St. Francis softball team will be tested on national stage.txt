Metro-best St. Francis softball team will be tested on national stage
-----
The St. Francis softball team erased one major blemish on its 2023 season and this week, it aims to correct another.The Lancers (7-0), the Chronicle's No. 1 team and No. 3 in California according to SBLive Sports, won 7-1 at state No. 2 Hollister on Saturday behind three hits, two RBIs and a five-hitter from Kate Munnerlyn.
