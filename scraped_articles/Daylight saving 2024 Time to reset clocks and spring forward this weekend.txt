Daylight saving 2024: Time to reset clocks and ‘spring forward’ this weekend
-----
Just when you finally got all your clocks changed and your body and attitude adjusted, it's time to take another leap in time — ahead to daylight saving time.Daylight saving begins at 2 a.m. Sunday. Californians and most other Americans will set their clocks ahead an hour, losing an hour of sleep and making the mornings darker and the daylight later — until Nov. 3.
