Now first in the NFC, 49ers dealing only with first-world NFL problems
-----
The San Francisco 49ers gained 527 yards Sunday, their fourth most since 2000, and they averaged 9.94 yards per play, their most since at least 1965.Brock Purdy threw for 368 yards, the most by a 49ers QB since 2019, and Christian McCaffrey (16 carries, 145 yards) became their fourth running back since 1977 to average nine-plus yards a carry on at least 15 attempts.
