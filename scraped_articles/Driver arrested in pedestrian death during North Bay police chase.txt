Driver arrested in pedestrian death during North Bay police chase
-----
A Vallejo driver who fatally struck a pedestrian while fleeing from police last October was arrested this week, police said Thursday.Jovon Woodard, 22, was arrested Tuesday in the 400 block of Tennessee Street and booked into the Solano County Jail on suspicion of homicide, the Vallejo Police Department said.
