Chiefs opt for red, so 49ers will wear white jerseys in Super Bowl LVIII
-----
The San Francisco 49ers will wear their road white jerseys in Super Bowl LVIII on Feb. 11 against the Chiefs, which is the same uniform they wore in the first Super Bowl win 42 years ago.The bad news for the superstitious among the Faithful: They also wore white in their last Super Bowl appearance, a 31-20 loss to the Chiefs in Super Bowl LIV four years ago.
Kansas City is the designated home team for their meeting in Las Vegas and chose to wear red jerseys.
The 49ers are 2-1
