Juvenile shot near SF high school and popular tourist area
-----
A male juvenile was shot near a San Francisco high school on Wednesday afternoon, police said. The shooting happened at approximately 12:38 p.m. at North Point and Polk streets between Galileo Academy of Science and Technology and Ghirardelli Square.When officers arrived at the scene, they found the juvenile with gunshot wounds. He was transported to a local hospital with non-life-threatening injuries, Allison Maxie, a spokesperson for the San Francisco Police Department, told SFGATE.
