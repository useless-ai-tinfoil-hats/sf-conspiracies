A man was killed and two people were wounded in shootings late Monday on and near Market Street, police said Tuesday afternoon.Officers responded to the 1000 block of Market Street around 10:49 p.m. Monday after ShotSpotter sensors indicated a shooting had occurred in that area.
While en route, officers also learned a person was shot at Sixth and Market streets. Medical personnel gave aid and the man was taken to a hospital with non-life-threatening injuries, police said.
