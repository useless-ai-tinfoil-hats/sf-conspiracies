Aerosmith postpones farewell tour as Steven Tyler injures vocal cords
-----
Aerosmith has postponed a series of dates on its farewell tour after frontman Steven Tyler shredded his voice during a performance over the weekend."I'm heartbroken to say I have received strict doctor's orders not to sing for the next thirty days," Tyler, 75, posted on Instagram on Monday, Sept. 11. "I sustained vocal cord damage during Saturday's show that led to subsequent bleeding. We'll need to postpone a few dates so that we can come back and give you the performance you deserve."
