Person driving U-Haul truck in South Bay leads authorities on chase, shuts down 101
-----
The driver of a U-Haul box truck led authorities on a chase, closing both directions of Highway 101 in the area for "several hours" Sunday evening, the Santa Clara County Sheriff's Office said.At approximately 6:23 p.m., the San Jose Police Department asked the sheriff's office for assistance with stopping the truck. SJPD did not respond to SFGATE's request for information about why it made the request.
