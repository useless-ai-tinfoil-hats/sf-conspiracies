East Bay man pleads guilty to selling counterfeit goods to U.S. military
-----
An East Bay businessman has admitted defrauding the government when he sold the Defense Department cooling fans and blowers, for possible installation on missiles and a nuclear submarine, for more than $3.5 million while falsely claiming that the devices were new.Steve H.S. Kim, 63, pleaded guilty Wednesday in federal court in Oakland to felony charges of wire fraud and trafficking in counterfeit goods.
