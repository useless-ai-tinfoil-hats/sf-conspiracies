Giants pull off third straight late comeback on Patrick Bailey’s slam in 8-7 win over Mets
-----
NEW YORK — Falling behind early, giving up homers, those things are the norm on this road trip, apparently. This isn't recommended, but the San Francisco Giants keep responding with big comeback wins.In their previous two games, at Pittsburgh, the Giants gave up early grand slams to fall behind. Friday night at Citi Field, the Giants were the slammers — catcher Patrick Bailey clocked the first of his career in the eighth to give San Francisco the lead after falling behind after trailing
