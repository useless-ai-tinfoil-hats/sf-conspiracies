Pedestrian killed in hit-and-run crash in SF's Tenderloin neighborhood
-----
A driver fatally struck a pedestrian in San Francisco's Tenderloin neighborhood early Wednesday morning, officials said.Officers from the San Francisco Police Department arrived at the area of Polk and Ellis streets around 1:46 a.m., where they found a male pedestrian with injuries, Gonee Sepulveda, a spokesperson for the department, told SFGATE in a statement.
Medics attempted life-saving efforts on the man but he died at the scene, Sepulveda said.
