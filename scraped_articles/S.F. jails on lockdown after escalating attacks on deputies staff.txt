S.F. jails on lockdown after ‘escalating attacks’ on deputies, staff
-----
The San Francisco Sheriff's Office has locked down both of its jails for the weekend after a series of incidents over the past two weeks in which seven deputies and other jail workers were injured, officials said.Some of the injuries were serious, sheriff's office spokeswoman Tara Moriarty said, but she declined Saturday to comment on whether anyone had been hospitalized.
