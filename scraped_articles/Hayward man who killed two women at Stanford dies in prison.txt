Hayward man who killed two women at Stanford dies in prison
-----
John Getreu, a Hayward man serving two life sentences for fatally strangling two young women at Stanford in the 1970s, has died in prison, state officials reported Wednesday. He was 79.Getreu was a former Stanford hospital employee and had previously served a prison term in Germany for raping and fatally strangling a teenage girl in 1963, when he was 18. He was later convicted of the 1975 rape of a Palo Alto woman.
