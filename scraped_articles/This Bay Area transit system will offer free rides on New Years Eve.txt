This Bay Area transit system will offer free rides on New Year’s Eve
-----
Muni buses and trains will be free to ride on New Year's Eve.Caltrain, AC Transit and SamTrans, the Peninsula's bus service, will also offer free transit rides on the last night of the year, starting at 8 p.m. Sunday.
Free Muni service runs from 8 p.m. Sunday until 5 a.m Monday. Muni Metro turnstiles will be open during those hours — simply board buses and trains without tagging your Clipper card.
