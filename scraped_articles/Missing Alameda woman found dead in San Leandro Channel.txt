Missing Alameda woman found dead in San Leandro Channel
-----
Authorities have called off the search for a missing Alameda woman after her body was found in the San Leandro Channel on Tuesday afternoon.Desiree "Lisa" Huey was last heard from around 10 a.m. Saturday, when she told a family member she was going to go on a walk around her neighborhood, according to the Alameda Police Department. A family member told police Huey regularly went on 30-minute walks in the area.
