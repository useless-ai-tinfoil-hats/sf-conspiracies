Steph Curry struggles as Warriors booed, blown out by lottery-likely Raptors
-----
At halftime of Golden State's 133-118 loss to the Raptors at Chase Center on Sunday night, boos emanated from the crowd as Warriors players headed toward the locker room.Few could have blamed those frustrated fans for assuming their favorite team was well on its way to a nadir. Less than 72 hours after they blew a fourth-quarter, 18-point lead to fall to the Nuggets on a 39-foot buzzer-beater from Nikola Jokic, the Warriors trailed Toronto by 27.
