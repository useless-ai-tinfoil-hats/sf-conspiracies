Klay Thompson ready from the start for Warriors’ preseason action
-----
One of the more surprising developments last preseason was Klay Thompson's absence from the Golden State Warriors' first four exhibition games.Thompson was still rebounding from the two devastating leg injuries that cost him 2½ seasons to recover before contributing to the Warriors' title run in 2022.
