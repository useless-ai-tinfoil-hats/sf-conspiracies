Mysterious web-like substance spotted all over California skies
-----
Reports of a white, fluffy, web-like substance floating in skies over Central California and the Bay Area emerged on social media on Wednesday and continued into Thursday. Observations of the mysterious gossamer strands and blobs were reported in dozens of locations, including King City, Salinas, Hollister, Monterey, Gilroy, Morgan Hill, Santa Cruz, Oakland and Berkeley. Photos showed webbing hanging from power poles and trees."Was on my power line this morning," wrote one Twitter user.
