Grammy Awards 2024 adds Joni Mitchell and SZA to performance lineup
-----
Joni Mitchell and SZA have been confirmed as additional performers for this year's Grammy Awards, scheduled at Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles on Sunday, Feb. 4.This marks the first-ever performance at the ceremony for the 80-year-old Mitchell, despite having previously won nine Grammys and receiving a lifetime achievement honor from the Recording Academy in 2002.
