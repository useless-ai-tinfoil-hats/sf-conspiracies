Fatal two-car collision snarls I-880 Memorial Day traffic in South Bay
-----
A portion of both northbound and southbound Interstate 880 in San Jose remained closed Monday evening after a fatal two-car collision earlier in the day, according to authorities.The closure was in effect at the Montague Expressway off-ramps, Caltrans said.
The collision occurred around 1:52 p.m. near Montague Expressway and O'Toole Avenue, according to police. One driver suffered "major injuries" and was pronounced dead at the scene, police said.
