How many homeless people are in San Francisco? It depends on where you look.According to an annual one-night tally, the most frequently cited measure, the city had about 8,300 people experiencing homelessness in January 2024. That's the number that'll be repeated by public officials, compared to counts from previous years and weighed by state agencies to help determine where funding should go. It's also widely cited in the media, by the Chronicle and others.
