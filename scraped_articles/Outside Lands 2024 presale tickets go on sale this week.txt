Outside Lands 2024 presale tickets go on sale this week
-----
Those planning to attend Outside Lands have the opportunity to secure a limited number of presale tickets this week.The music and food festival is set to mark its 16th year in San Francisco's Golden Gate Park from Aug. 9-11, and its Eager Beaver sale offers concertgoers the only chance to purchase discounted tickets before the official lineup announcement later this month, according to promoters Another Planet Entertainment and Superfly.
