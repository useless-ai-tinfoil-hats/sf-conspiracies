Comedian Jo Koy announces S.F. stop on first standup tour since Golden Globes backlash
-----
Comedian Jo Koy is attempting to bounce back from his disastrous performance at the 81st Golden Globe Awards with a new stand-up show.The North American leg of the Just Being Koy Tour is set to kick off in Houston on Sept. 6, with a performance scheduled at San Francisco's Chase Center on Oct. 12. Ticket presales begin at noon Tuesday, May 7, with the general sale to follow at noon Thursday, May 9, on Ticketmaster.
