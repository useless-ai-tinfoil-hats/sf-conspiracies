California trail connecting Morro Bay, Cayucos finally could be built
-----
A beachside trail connecting coastal California communities that was first proposed nearly two decades ago could finally be constructed after one the largest oil companies in the world stepped aside.The Morro Bay to Cayucos Connector Trail will eventually become part of the California Coastal Trail system.
