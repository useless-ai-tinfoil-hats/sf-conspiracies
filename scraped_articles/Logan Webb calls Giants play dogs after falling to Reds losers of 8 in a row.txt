Logan Webb calls Giants’ play ‘dogs—’ after falling to Reds, losers of 8 in a row

The Reds, losers of eight in a row coming into Friday's game at Oracle Park, seemed like ripe pickings, but Cincinnati's crazy-great speed instead made for a trying evening for Logan Webb and the Giants.
