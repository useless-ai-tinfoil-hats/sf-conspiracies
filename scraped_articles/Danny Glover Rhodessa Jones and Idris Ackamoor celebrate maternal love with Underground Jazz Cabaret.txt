Danny Glover, Rhodessa Jones and Idris Ackamoor celebrate maternal love with ‘Underground Jazz Cabaret’
-----
"The old band is getting back together," said Danny Glover as he shot a sly glance at Rhodessa Jones and Idris Ackamoor.The three gathered recently in Ackamoor's Fillmore district apartment to talk to the Chronicle, via Zoom, about their new production, "Underground Jazz Cabaret," a spoken word meets Afrofuturist soundscape that kicks off Black History Month at the Lab on Friday and Saturday, Feb. 2-3.
