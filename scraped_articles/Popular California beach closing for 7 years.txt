Popular California beach closing for 7 years
-----
Viral videos capturing visitors invading the personal space of sea lions at La Jolla Beach helped inspire a policy shift to protect the wildlife.Last year, the San Diego City Council voted to adopt a seasonal 150-yard closure pertaining to both Point La Jolla and Boomer Beach from May through October.
On Sept. 18, the council unanimously voted to close the area to the public — banning people from the popular beach area for the next 7 years. Read more »
