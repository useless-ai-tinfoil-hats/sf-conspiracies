Bay Area driver arrested after running over her ex, police say
-----
A driver has been arrested on suspicion of attempted murder after she purposefully ran over her ex-boyfriend Tuesday night in Mountain View, authorities said.Alexis McBride, a 24-year-old Dublin resident, was booked into the Santa Clara County Jail on attempted murder, hit and run and corporal injury to a spouse or co-inhabitant charges, according to the Mountain View Police Department.
