Jordan Hicks’ solid start, Michael Conforto’s slam lead Giants past Padres
-----
SAN DIEGO — Bases loaded in the fourth inning, Jordan Hicks had to channel his inner closer.As a converted starter, Hicks has to tame his triple-digit fastball down to a milder high-90s offering to keep his stamina. Nursing a two-run lead with the potential go-ahead run on base, though, Hicks dug back and hurled pitches at 99 and 100 mph to get the next San Diego Padres hitters on their toes. Tyler Wade struck out swinging and Jackson Merrill lined out sharply to shortstop Nick Ahmed
