Warriors’ Draymond Green to miss Nuggets game for ‘personal reasons’
-----
DENVER — Golden State Warriors forward Draymond Green will miss Wednesday's game against the Nuggets for personal reasons. He may not be the only veteran player in the rotation that head coach Steve Kerr could be without going up against the reigning NBA champs in Denver, as Gary Payton II is questionable because of illness.Green is averaging 10.3 points, 5.0 rebounds and 6.7 assists per game through his first six appearances this season. He signed a four-year, $100 million extension
