Mother's boyfriend fatally shoots 3-year-old at Bay Area home, police say
-----
A 3-year-old boy was shot and killed by his mother's boyfriend at a Santa Clara residence early Tuesday, officials said.The mother called the San Clara Police Department at 2:25 a.m., reporting the shooting on the 3700 block of Poinciana Drive, police said.
First responders rushed to the scene and found the child with gunshot wounds and immediately started providing medical aid and performed CPR. Their efforts failed, and the child was declared deceased, police said.
