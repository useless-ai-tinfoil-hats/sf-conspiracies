Peso Pluma delays S.F. concert. Here are the details on his rescheduled show
-----
Peso Pluma has pushed back his upcoming Chase Center show by four days.The concert, now scheduled for Aug. 31, is part of the Mexican musician's Éxodo Tour, which supports his third studio album "Génesis." He was initially scheduled to bring his act to San Francisco on Aug. 27.
There has been no official explanation for the postponement, but tickets purchased for the original date will be transferred to the new one, and those unable to attend the new date are eligible for a refund.
