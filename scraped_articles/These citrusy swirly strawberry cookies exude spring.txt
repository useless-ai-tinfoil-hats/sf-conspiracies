These citrusy, swirly strawberry cookies exude spring
-----
Spring is one of my favorite seasons — coming out of the dreary, rainy winter and into the pleasant, crisp mornings. When I go for my brisk morning walks, I now see pops of color everywhere, with the cherry blossoms and spring flowers.During this transition from winter to spring — from oranges and lemons to strawberries — I am sharing a metaphorical treat: Strawberry, Orange and Lemon Sugar Cookies. This is my take on the internet-famous Neapolitan cookie by Sarah Kieffer, a classic
