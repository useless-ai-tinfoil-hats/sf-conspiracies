‘Who Is Michael Jang?’ reveals the life of a prolific San Francisco photographer
-----
Back in the early 1970s, San Francisco photographer Michael Jang had a Chronicle press pass. And one from Rolling Stone. And one from the New York Times. None of them were real. In that analog era, he would snap a picture of a publication's logo, add his headshot and an illegible scrawl to mimic an editor's signature and then laminate it.
