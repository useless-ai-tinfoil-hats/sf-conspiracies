How Giants’ additions of rookies Landen Roupp, Erik Miller impact the pitching staff
-----
SAN DIEGO — Landen Roupp had a sneaking suspicion that he made the San Francisco Giants' Opening Day roster. But having never pitched above Double-A in his professional career before, logic kept his dreams grounded.He first got a hunch when coaches told Roupp he was still in the mix and, therefore, would be scratched from his scheduled outing in Tuesday's exhibition at Oracle Park against the A's. He could barely focus during the game, anticipating an update once it was over.
