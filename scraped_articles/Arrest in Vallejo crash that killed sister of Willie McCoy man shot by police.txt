Arrest in Vallejo crash that killed sister of Willie McCoy, man shot by police
-----
Vallejo police arrested a man on suspicion of murder after an investigation into a July car crash that killed Sharmell Mitchell, the sister of Willie McCoy, whom the city's officers fatally shot in 2019.Police identified Jacoby Etter, 46, of Rancho Cordova (Sacramento County) as the suspect in the three-vehicle collision that killed Mitchell, 48, on July 16 around 12:30 p.m. at the intersection of Tennessee Street and Vervais Avenue in Vallejo. Gunshots were also reported in the area.
