Kelly-Moore Paints abruptly ends operations, closes 61 Bay Area stores
-----
Kelly-Moore Paints, a paint supply giant founded in the Bay Area 78 years ago, said Friday it will cease operations immediately, citing financial constraints stemming from asbestos settlements and other financial hardships.According to its website, 61 out of its 157 stores are in the Bay Area.
