The San Francisco corner store where cops and Hells Angels come together
-----
Like most corner stores in San Francisco, Reno's Liquor is a community hub, a place where you can find a sewing kit and a quart of milk just when you need it, or a friend to bend your ear.Situated on 22nd Street in the Dogpatch, Reno's has been open since 1989, beckoning to passersby with its black awning and gold pinstripe sign that says, "Liquor, Wine, Beer, Lotto, ATM".
