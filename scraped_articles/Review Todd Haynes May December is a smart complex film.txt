Review: Todd Haynes’ ‘May December’ is a smart, complex film
-----
"May December," a skillful and inspired movie, is the product of enormous directorial confidence.Here Todd Haynes ("Carol") juggles a number of styles and points of view — some of them contradictory — yet he harmonizes everything into a single whole. The movie doesn't tell you what to think or announce what the filmmakers think.
