JPMorgan Chase has now closed 12 First Republic Bank branches in Bay Area
-----
JPMorgan Chase, which acquired First Republic Bank in May after the embattled San Francisco lender failed, has now closed 22 First Republic branches, including a dozen in the Bay Area.First Republic's website shows the closures, with some locations now labeled, "This office is permanently closed." The shuttered branches include seven in San Francisco and five elsewhere in the Bay Area.
