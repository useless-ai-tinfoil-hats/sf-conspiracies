Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao's home raided by FBI, reports say
-----
A home owned by Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao was raided by the FBI on Thursday morning, according to multiple news outlets.The FBI and Thao's office did not immediately reply to SFGATE requests for comment, but an FBI spokesperson told the San Francisco Chronicle that "the FBI is conducting court authorized law enforcement activity on Maiden Lane."
