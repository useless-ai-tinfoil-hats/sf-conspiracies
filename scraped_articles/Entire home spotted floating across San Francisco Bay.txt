Entire home spotted floating across San Francisco Bay
-----
If you were near the San Francisco waterfront over the weekend, you may have caught an odd sight floating by.A large, wood-shingled, two-story home was gliding across the water, towed by a much smaller boat at a glacial pace on Sunday as it made its way from the Docktown Marina in Redwood City toward Sausalito. The U.S. Coast Guard confirmed to SFGATE the boat was still in transit on Monday afternoon.
