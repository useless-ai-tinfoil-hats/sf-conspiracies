Educator David Lee enters S.F. race for District 19 Assembly seat
-----
David Lee, a San Francisco native and administrator at a Bay Area community college, has filed paperwork to run for Assembly member of District 19, which covers much of San Francisco and Daly City.Term-limited incumbent Assembly Member Phil Ting will vacate the seat at the end of next year. Lee's announcement comes months after Supervisor Catherine Stefani announced she would be running for the seat.
