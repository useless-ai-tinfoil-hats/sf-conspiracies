Timeless Piedmont estate offers indoor/outdoor living
-----
(Ad) A striking piece of famed Bay Area architect Albert Farr's iconic portfolio is on the market in Piedmont. Located at 395 Hampton Road, the meticulously remodeled home blends timeless details, modern amenities, resplendent grounds and ample opportunities for indoor/outdoor living. The estate, built in 1926 for William Cavalier, features a traditional floor plan, a seven-bedroom main home, a one-bedroom guest house and a pair of two-car garages.Set on more than an acre of level
