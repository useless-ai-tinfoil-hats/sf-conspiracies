Denim is back, and more queer than ever
-----
Levi's 501 jeans are woven into the fabric of the Bay Area.San Francisco is the birthplace of the signature pant and has served as the headquarters of the venerable Levi Strauss & Co. for 171 years. An heir to the company, Daniel Lurie, is running for mayor. The San Francisco 49ers call Levi's Stadium in Santa Clara, which hosted Super Bowl 50 in 2016, their home.
