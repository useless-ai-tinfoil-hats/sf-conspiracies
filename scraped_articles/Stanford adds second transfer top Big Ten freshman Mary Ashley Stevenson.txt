Stanford adds second transfer, top Big Ten freshman Mary Ashley Stevenson
-----
Stanford women's basketball secured its second undergraduate transfer in two days with the addition of 2023-24 Big Ten Freshman of the Year Mary Ashley Stevenson from Purdue.A 6-foot-2 forward, Stevenson started 33 games as a freshman, scoring 9.7 points per game on 41.4% shooting with 5.1 rebounds for the Boilermakers, whose season ended in the WNIT Elite Eight.
