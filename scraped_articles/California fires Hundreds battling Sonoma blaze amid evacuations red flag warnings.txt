California fires: Hundreds battling Sonoma blaze amid evacuations, red flag warnings
-----
About a dozen wildfires burned across California on Monday morning, with one of the largest, the Point Fire in Sonoma County, at 1,100 acres and 20% containment after crews battled the blaze overnight amid mandatory evacuations.The Point Fire roared to life around noon Sunday west of Healdsburg near Lake Sonoma and quickly spread across hundreds of acres. Evacuation orders were issued for several hundred residents north and east of Chemise Road, south of Stewarts Point-Skaggs Springs
