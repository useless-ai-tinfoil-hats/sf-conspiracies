Newsom can improve California’s court system with one signature
-----
Retired Alameda County Judge Brenda Harbin-Forte spent her nearly 30-year career trying to bring more diversity into the courtroom. One place where she didn't see enough of it was in the jury box.She said she was often disappointed in the lack of diversity in juries for civil cases involving workplace discrimination and in some criminal matters in which the main evidence for conviction was from police testimony — a practice the retired judge joked is sometimes called "testilying."
