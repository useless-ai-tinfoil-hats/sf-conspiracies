West Oakland sideshow draws hundreds of onlookers
-----
Oakland police responded Sunday night to an illegal sideshow in West Oakland that blocked traffic and drew hundreds of onlookers.Cars screeched in circles around the intersection of 14th and Peralta streets, surrounded by dozens of parked cars, some waiting their turn to spin. Tire smoke filled the air and fireworks were shot off sporadically.
