‘This does not make sense’: Las Vegas mayor says team should stay in Oakland
-----
Outside of John Fisher, Dave Kaval and MLB commissioner Rob Manfred, no one seems to really want the Oakland A's to make their proposed move to Las Vegas. And one very prominent name who seemed to publicly join that list on Tuesday was Las Vegas Mayor Carolyn Goodman.In two different formats, Goodman made it clear that she prefers for the A's to remain in Oakland, despite expressing excitement at the possibility of an MLB team coming to Las Vegas.
