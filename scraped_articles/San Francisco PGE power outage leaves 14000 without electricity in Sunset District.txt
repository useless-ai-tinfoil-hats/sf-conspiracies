San Francisco PG&E power outage leaves 14,000 without electricity in Sunset District
-----
A Pacific Gas & Electric Co. power outage left more than 14,000 customers in parts of San Francisco's Sunset District without electricity Thursday morning.The outage is centered at Golden Gate Park and areas south of it, according to the Chronicle map that tracks Pacific Gas & Electric Co. outages. About 14,226 customers were affected by the outage, according to PG&E.
