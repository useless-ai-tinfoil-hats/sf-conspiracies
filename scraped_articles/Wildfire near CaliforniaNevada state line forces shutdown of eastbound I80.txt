Wildfire near California-Nevada state line forces shutdown of eastbound I-80
-----
LATEST Aug. 11 9:40 p.m. The Quilici Fire had burned around 400 acres with progress being made against it, the Truckee Meadows Fire Protection District said.Aug. 11 8:53 p.m. Evacuations were ordered and Eastbound Interstate 80 was closed near the California-Nevada border Sunday night after a brush fire was sparked by a vehicle fire, officials said.
The Quilici Fire in the Gold Ranch area near Verdi, Nevada, had burned around 300 acres by 8 p.m., Truckee fire officials said.
