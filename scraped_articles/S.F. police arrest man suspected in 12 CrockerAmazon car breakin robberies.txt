S.F. police arrest man suspected in 12 Crocker-Amazon car break-in robberies
-----
San Francisco police arrested a man they accused of breaking into a dozen cars in the Crocker-Amazon neighborhood, allegedly fleeing each scene on a stolen electric scooter.Police encountered Victor Matute-Casco, 27, early Friday morning around Athens and Naples streets, where he was allegedly casing the blocks and peering into vehicles with a flashlight.
When officers approached Matute-Casco he ran and briefly hid. They apprehended him at Rolph and Cordova streets at about 5:15
