Costco price codes can tell you a lot—if you know how to read them
-----
Pop quiz for all the bargain hunters: How can you tell if an item's on sale at Costco? … Anyone? That's what we thought.Instead of red slashes, plastered-over labels and dedicated clearance sections, Costco runs its deals on a secret code of numbers and symbols printed right on every price tag posted around the store.
