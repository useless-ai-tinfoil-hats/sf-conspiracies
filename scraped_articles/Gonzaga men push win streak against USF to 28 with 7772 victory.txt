Gonzaga men push win streak against USF to 28 with 77-72 victory
-----
Gonzaga's Graham Ike had 22 points as the Bulldogs continued their dominance of USF with a 77-72 victory in Spokane, Wash., on Thursday night.Gonzaga (14-5, 5-1 WCC) extended its win streak against the Dons to 28. The last time USF (15-6, 4-2) beat Gonzaga was Feb. 18, 2012; the Dons' last win in Spokane came in 1989.
Ike also contributed seven rebounds for the Bulldogs. Anton Watson added 15 points, going 6-for-8 from the field with seven rebounds and three steals. Ryan Nembhard
