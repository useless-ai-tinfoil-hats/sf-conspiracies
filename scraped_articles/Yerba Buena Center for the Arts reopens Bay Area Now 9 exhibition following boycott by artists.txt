Yerba Buena Center for the Arts reopens ‘Bay Area Now 9’ exhibition following boycott by artists
-----
Yerba Buena Center for the Arts announced plans to reopen the group exhibition "Bay Area Now 9" on Friday, March 15.The organization's signature triennial survey of Northern California art has been closed to the public since Feb. 15, when a group of protesters, including eight artists, interrupted a public program in the galleries with calls for a cease-fire in the Israel-Hamas war and allegations that YBCA had censored pro-Palestinian artwork. Several artists altered their work on view
