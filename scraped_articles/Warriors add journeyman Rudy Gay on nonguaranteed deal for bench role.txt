Warriors add journeyman Rudy Gay on non-guaranteed deal for bench role
-----
The Golden State Warriors have agreed to a one-year, non-guaranteed deal with forward Rudy Gay, a league source confirmed with the Chronicle on Wednesday morning.Gay, 37, is expected to compete for the team's open roster spot in training camp, which starts Tuesday. Though the Warriors have two vacancies on their 15-man roster, they intend to enter the season with one opening to save money and maintain flexibility should anyone intriguing become available in coming months.
