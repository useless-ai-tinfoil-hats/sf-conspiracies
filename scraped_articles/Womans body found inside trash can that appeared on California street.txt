Woman's body found inside trash can that appeared on California street
-----
A trash can that showed up on a Sunland, Calif., street contained the body of a woman, LA County investigators said. The cause of her death and how the trash can appeared on a normally quiet residential street is under investigation.Police responded to the 8500 block of Wentworth Street on the morning of April 16 for a report of a suspicious trash can.
