Berkeley pastor is first openly gay man elected bishop of Bay Area-based Lutheran synod
-----
Berkeley pastor Jeff R. Johnson has been named bishop of the Northern California congregations of one of the country's largest Christian denominations, becoming the first openly gay man elected to the synod's top role, church officials announced this week.The Rev. Johnson was elected Sunday to serve a six-year term as bishop of the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America's Sierra Pacific Synod, officials announced in a press release.
