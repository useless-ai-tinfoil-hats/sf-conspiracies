How an San Francisco local went from college student to rock star overnight
-----
When she isn't dropping an avalanche of sound on Golden Gate Park, Natalie Lu is surprisingly reserved.The 20-year-old shoegazer, better known to her million monthly listeners as Wisp, paused her Outside Lands set on Sunday to ask the crowd a sincere question: "Who are you guys most excited to see today?" They respond in chorus: "You!"
Lu doesn't directly respond to the adoring cheers, but a smile cracks on her face, mirrored on the jumbotron behind her.
