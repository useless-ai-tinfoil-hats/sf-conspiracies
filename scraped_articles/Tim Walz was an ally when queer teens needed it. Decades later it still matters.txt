Tim Walz was an ally when queer teens needed it. Decades later, it still matters
-----
When I heard that Minnesota Gov. Tim Walz had been the faculty advisor for Mankato West High School's first-ever Gay-Straight Alliance in 1999, I felt my memory jolt back to the turn of the millennium.It was during that time, as a freshman in high school, when I joined the Ruth Asawa School of the Arts' GSA. It also made me think of Caren Streb, our GSA faculty advisor, and how much she would have loved this news.
