Police shoot person in Vallejo after car chase
-----
American Canyon police shot a person in Vallejo after a car chase on Thursday afternoon, the Police Department said.The person's condition was unknown as of Friday morning.
Around 3:15 on Thursday afternoon, police attempted to pull over a car for a "vehicle code violation," near southbound State Route 29 and American Canyon Road. Police said the driver did not stop, prompting the police to chase the vehicle.
