‘Absolutely chaotic’: Elite surfers ride historic waves at Mavericks on California coast
-----
Kai Lenny was nervous as he pulled on his wetsuit as dawn broke along the California coast.Lenny is one of the best big-wave surfers in the world, slipping down monster walls of water on a regular basis, tossing in flips and jumps because he can.
But he was about to face Mavericks, where some of the world's most impressive swells roll through when the conditions are just right, luring the best big-wave professional surfers from far corners of the world.
