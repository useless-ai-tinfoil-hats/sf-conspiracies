S.F. police arrest man suspected in 2 deadly shootings
-----
San Francisco police arrested a Solano County man suspected in two deadly shootings that took place in 2022 and 2023, officials said Friday.Demetri Moore, 43, was arrested Feb. 29, after police officers patrolling in the Tenderloin near O'Farrell and Jones streets recognized him from a crime bulletin. Police said they found a gun on Moore during the arrest.
Moore was booked at San Francisco County jail on several charges, including murder and carrying a loaded gun, police said.
