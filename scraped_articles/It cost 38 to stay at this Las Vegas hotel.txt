It cost $38 to stay at this Las Vegas hotel
-----
The years have not been kind to Circus Circus.Located on the far northern end of the Las Vegas Strip, the hotel and casino's outdated theme, garish clown marquee and shabby rooms have given it a scuzzy reputation. In 2018, the casino turned 50, one of only eight hotels currently on the Strip to reach that milestone. There was no celebration.
