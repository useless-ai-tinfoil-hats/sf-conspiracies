2 dead, 3 injured in S.F. after SUV crashes into bus stop
-----
A vehicle ran into a bus stop near Muni's West Portal Station on Saturday, killing two and injuring three, according to the San Francisco Fire Department.The sport utility vehicle struck four people at 12:13 p.m. at a bus stop next to the transit station on Ulloa Street, a Fire Department spokesperson said. Two people were pronounced dead at the scene and three were taken to a hospital with injuries, said the spokesperson, who did not specify which was the SUV driver or the pedestrians.
