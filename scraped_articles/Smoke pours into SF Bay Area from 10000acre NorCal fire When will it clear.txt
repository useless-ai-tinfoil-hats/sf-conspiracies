Smoke pours into SF Bay Area from 10,000-acre NorCal fire: When will it clear?
-----
LATEST June 18, 3:15 p.m. Air quality levels worsened in the North Bay and East Bay on Tuesday afternoon as smoke from the Sites Fire in Colusa County poured into the Bay Area. The fire is burning about 140 miles north of San Francisco.Matt Mehle, a meteorologist with the National Weather Service, said northerly winds were blowing smoke from the fire south. The winds are expected to shift to westerly at 7 or 8 p.m. and blow the smoke to the east, giving the Bay Area some relief Tuesday
