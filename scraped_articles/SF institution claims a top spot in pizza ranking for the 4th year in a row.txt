SF institution claims a top spot in pizza ranking for the 4th year in a row
-----
For the fourth year in a row, San Francisco institution Tony's Pizza Napoletana has claimed a top spot in an annual pizza guide's ranking of the best pizzerias in the U.S.On Tuesday, Tony Gemignani's restaurant Tony's Pizza Napoletana was ranked No. 2 in the U.S. by 50 Top Pizza, an Italian media company. The SF restaurant was the only Bay Area pizzeria mentioned in the U.S. pizza category. Gemignani's Las Vegas restaurant Pizza Rock also made the U.S. list, at No. 28.
