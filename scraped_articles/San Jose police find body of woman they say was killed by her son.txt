San Jose police find body of woman they say was killed by her son
-----
A woman found dead inside a San Jose home was killed by her son, police said Friday.Police found the body of the woman around 11 a.m. Thursday after responding to a report of suspicious circumstances at the home on the 200 block of Shadow Dance Drive, police said.
The son, Roger Le, was detained inside the home. After a "comprehensive investigation" into the incident, police arrested Le on suspicion of murder and booked him into Santa Clara County jail, police said.
