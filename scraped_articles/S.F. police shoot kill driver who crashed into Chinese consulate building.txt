S.F. police shoot, kill driver who crashed into Chinese consulate building
-----
UPDATE: Person killed by police after S.F. Chinese consulate crash was swinging knifeSan Francisco police shot and killed a suspect at the Chinese consulate building on Monday afternoon after the person drove a car into the visa office.
The driver sped into the consulate visa office on Geary just after 3 p.m. and when police arrived, they shot the driver after they "made contact" with the person, police Sgt. Kathryn Winters said. Police administered first aid and paramedics
