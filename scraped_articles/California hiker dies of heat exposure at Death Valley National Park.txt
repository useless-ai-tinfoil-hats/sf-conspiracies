California hiker dies of heat exposure at Death Valley National Park
-----
A Los Angeles man died of heat exposure at Death Valley National Park earlier this month, marking the second heat-related fatality in the California national park this year, officials said.On the afternoon of Aug. 1, Peter Hayes Robino, 57, of Duarte, was returning to the trailhead of the one-mile Natural Bridge Trail when bystanders saw him stumble, a news release from the National Park Service read. They offered to help Robino, but he declined and kept walking.
