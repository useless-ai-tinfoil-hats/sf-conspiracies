Will affordable housing bond on March ballot pass? Advocates worry about tepid support
-----
San Francisco voters have embraced every affordable housing bond on the ballot for the past 15 years.But with a generally disgruntled electorate and a low turnout expected in next week's election, affordable housing leaders are worried that the $300 million bond on the March 5 ballot, dubbed Proposition A, may not get the two-thirds needed to pass.
