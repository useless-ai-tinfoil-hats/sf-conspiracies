Review: Sofia Coppola’s ‘Priscilla’ is pretty, but too careful
-----
To watch "Priscilla" is to never be in doubt that it's a serious and considered work by a conscientious filmmaker. The movie looks beautiful, and there are touches of wit and drama throughout that, on their own, add up to an almost worthwhile experience.But there's something off about "Priscilla" — or perhaps something missing.
There's style, but not much in terms of a point of view. There's perspective — we know who we're following, but we don't know exactly why we're following
