Christmas morning encampment fire under I-980 sends smoke billowing near downtown Oakland
-----
A Christmas morning homeless encampment fire sent smoke billowing near downtown Oakland.The fire at Northgate Avenue and Sycamore Street under the Interstate 980 highway overpass resulted in no reported injuries, said Michael Hunt, chief of staff at the Oakland Fire Department.
Two fire engines and eight firefighters responded to the blaze, he said.
Encampment fires have been a persistent challenge on both sides of the bay.
