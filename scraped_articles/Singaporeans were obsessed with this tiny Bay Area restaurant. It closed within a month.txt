Singaporeans were obsessed with this tiny Bay Area restaurant. It closed within a month
-----
Mama Judy Singapore Hawker, the tiny Singaporean restaurant that caused a frenzy when it opened in downtown Alameda last month, is closing due to overwhelming demand, the owners announced Saturday."I don't think being a restaurant proprietor and chef is a good fit for me," said chef and owner Judy Wee.
