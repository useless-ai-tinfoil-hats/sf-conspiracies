49ers’ Brock Purdy hits it big in NFL’s performance-based pay program
-----
San Francisco 49ers quarterback Brock Purdy landed a big bonus — without team CEO Jed York opening his own vault.Purdy, whose famously affordable rookie contract has allowed the 49ers to maintain an accomplished roster around him, hit it big Monday. The NFL announced its performance-based pay program, and Purdy pocketed $739,795 atop his annual earnings. His base salary last season was $870,000, according to spotrac.com.
The program compensates players based on their playing time
