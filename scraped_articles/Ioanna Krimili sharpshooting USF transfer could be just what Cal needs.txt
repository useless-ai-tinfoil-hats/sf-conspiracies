Ioanna Krimili, sharp-shooting USF transfer, could be just what Cal needs
-----
Ioanna Krimili didn't take long to get accustomed to Berkeley. Her transfer experience took her just 12 miles away from her first NCAA home at USF, but in some ways, she is in a far different place.Krimili, who joined Cal women's basketball for her senior season, was known as one of the best mid-major shooters in the nation with the Dons. The USF offense relied on her night in and night out, especially last season while it struggled to establish an identity.
