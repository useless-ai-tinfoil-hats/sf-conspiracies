Bay Area solar company SunPower, once worth $10 billion, files for bankruptcy
-----
SunPower Corporation, a once-mighty solar panel company based in Richmond, filed for bankruptcy on Monday in a Delaware court. It marks the end of the road for the 39-year-old business, which will now look to find buyers for its assets, pay back creditors and quell customers' uncertainties.The news was expected — on Thursday, SFGATE reported on SunPower's dwindling fortunes and plans to lay off 290 California workers — but it still represents a brutal fall for the company.
