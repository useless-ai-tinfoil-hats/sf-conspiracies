Review: Awful horror film ‘Abigail’ wastes Angus Cloud’s final film role
-----
Not that far into "Abigail," the Universal Pictures "remake" of its 1936 classic "Dracula's Daughter" (more on that in a minute), a realization dawns: You're rooting for the monster. That's pretty much No. 1 on the list of "Signs You're Watching a Terrible Horror Movie."A half-dozen jerks are trapped in a mansion, terrified by the titular vampire, but they're such disgusting human beings that you can't wait for them to die. Go, Abigail!
