Two children die in river near Shasta Dam, Northern California sheriff says
-----
Two young people died in the Sacramento River just downstream from Shasta Dam in Northern California on Thursday morning, officials said.The juveniles were at a campground in the Chappie-Shasta Off-Highway Vehicle Recreation Area when they fell over a riverbank, Tim Mapes, a spokesperson for the Shasta County Sheriff's Office, told SFGATE.
"Whether it was a slide or they slid, somehow they lost their footing and ended up down near the river," Mapes said.
