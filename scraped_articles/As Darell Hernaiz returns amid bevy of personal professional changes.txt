A's Darell Hernaiz returns amid bevy of personal, professional changes
-----
Much has changed since infielder Darell Hernaiz last saw the Coliseum field on May 7.The Oakland A's, 9-19 in May, turned it around for a 15-9 July. The call-up of another young shortstop, Jacob Wilson, came and went quickly, ending with an injury. Hernaiz now has a son, Beau, born July 16. And Hernaiz rehabbed an ankle injury, earning his callup back to the big leagues on Friday.
