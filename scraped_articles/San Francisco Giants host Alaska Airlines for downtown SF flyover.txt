San Francisco Giants host Alaska Airlines for downtown SF flyover
-----
An Alaska Airlines flight — with logos from the San Francisco Giants adorning the aircraft's hull — was spotted Sept. 10 dipping below 1,000 feet near downtown San Francisco.Although the low-flying aircraft sparked some confusion on social media, the air stunt was predetermined.
The flyover was for Alaska Airlines and the Giants' annual BOGO — "buy one, get one" — airfare promotion at Oracle Park, where the Giants were hosting the Colorado Rockies.
