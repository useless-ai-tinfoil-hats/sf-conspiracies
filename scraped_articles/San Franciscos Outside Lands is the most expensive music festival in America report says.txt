San Francisco's Outside Lands is the most expensive music festival in America, report says
-----
Anyone who has attended a music festival in recent memory knows it isn't cheap, but according to a new study, San Francisco's signature music fest Outside Lands may be the priciest in the nation.The report does not exactly come from the most scientific of sources, but Casino.org crunched some numbers to find that an average day at Outside Lands costs $301.70.
