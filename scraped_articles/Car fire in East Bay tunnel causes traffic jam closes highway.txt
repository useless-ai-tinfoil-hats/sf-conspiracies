Car fire in East Bay tunnel causes traffic jam, closes highway
-----
A car fire inside the Caldecott Tunnel has partially closed Highway 24 eastbound, officials said.The blaze occurred in the tunnel's second bore, Caltrans District 4 said on social media. As of 11 a.m. the second bore, which is the left eastbound tunnel, remains closed, according to a 511 traffic alert. The right tunnel has since reopened.
