SF Giants organist Steve Hogan knows he's a 'dying breed'
-----
Last month, a lifelong musician played for a ravenous 36,798 fans at Oracle Park, all of whom knew the words to his cover-laden set. The selection brought people to their feet and voices to the air. He has performed this gig — sometimes as often as three times a week — for the past 13 years.
