A16 founder’s empire expands to Napa with Italian-inspired wine tasting room
-----
Bay Area pizza maven A16 has expanded to Wine Country — sort of.The Apero Wine & Spritz Bar debuted Thursday in Napa with Italian-inspired wines and snacks from A16 founder Shelley Lindgren, plus refreshing, low-ABV cocktails from the popular aperitivo brand Mommenpop.
The Apero (920 Franklin St.) showcases three women-owned wine brands — Mommenpop, Poe and Tansy — that complement one another in style: They're fresh, light and lower in alcohol than most Napa wines.
