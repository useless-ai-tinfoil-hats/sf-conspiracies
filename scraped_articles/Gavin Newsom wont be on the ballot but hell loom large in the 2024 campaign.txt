Gavin Newsom won’t be on the ballot, but he’ll loom large in the 2024 campaign
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom's name won't appear on ballots next year. But as one of the most influential Democrats in the country, he'll still have a role in several key races.Despite speculation, there's no chance Newsom will challenge Joe Biden for the Democratic presidential nomination. In fact, Newsom has been one of Biden's top campaign surrogates and one of his most vocal boosters.
