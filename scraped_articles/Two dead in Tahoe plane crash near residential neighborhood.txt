Two dead in Tahoe plane crash near residential neighborhood
-----
Two people are confirmed dead after a single-engine plane crashed off a residential neighborhood while trying to land at Truckee Tahoe Airport on Saturday evening.The plane, which took off from Centennial Airport in Colorado earlier in the day, was attempting to land shortly after 6:30 p.m. It went down near Glenshire Drive and Olympic Boulevard, crashing into railroad tracks upon impact.
