Men’s roundup: St. Mary’s earns first outright WCC crown since 2012
-----
Augustas Marciulionis had 17 points and 11 assists, Luke Barrett added 14 points and No. 17 St. Mary's routed Pepperdine 83-57 in Malibu on Thursday night to clinch the WCC regular-season championship.The Gaels (24-6, 15-0) extended the nation's longest active winning streak to 16 games in wrapping up their first outright regular-season championship since 2012. St. Mary's has one game left — at home against No. 23 Gonzaga on Saturday — to try to run the table in the conference.
