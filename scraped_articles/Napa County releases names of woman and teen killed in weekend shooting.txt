Napa County releases names of woman and teen killed in weekend shooting
-----
Authorities in Napa County on Thursday released the names of a woman and girl who were shot to death Saturday.One victim, 19-year-old Aileen Villasenor of Rohnert Park, was the mother of a 9-month-old daughter, according to the GoFundMe page set up to cover her funeral expenses. The other, 17-year-old Sasha Mufich, smiles in family photographs on a separate fundraising page, alongside her parents and younger siblings.
