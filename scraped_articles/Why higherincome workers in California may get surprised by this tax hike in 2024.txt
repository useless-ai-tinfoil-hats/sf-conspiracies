Why higher-income workers in California may get surprised by this tax hike in 2024
-----
A payroll tax increase that has gone largely unnoticed will hit high-earning employees in California starting Jan. 1.The tax hike will pay for an increase in benefits under California's state disability insurance and paid family leave programs starting Jan. 1, 2025. At that point, the wage-replacement rate — how much of their weekly pay participating workers get in benefits — will rise to up to 70% to 90% depending on income. That's up from 60% to 70% today.
