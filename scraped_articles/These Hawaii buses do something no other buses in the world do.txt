These Hawaii buses do something no other buses in the world do
-----
It must be the only bus in the world that makes you smile when it merges in front of you. In Hawaii, when you let the bus in, a rectangular LED screen on the back lights up with a flashing shaka, followed by the Hawaiian word "mahalo" (thank you).The digital shakas appearing on the City and County of Honolulu buses are as cute as they are useful, giving motorists an incentive to let TheBus into their lane.
