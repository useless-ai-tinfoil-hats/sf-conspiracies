S.F. IKEA food hall: Here are the restaurants moving in
-----
Visitors to a forthcoming food hall in the new San Francisco mall anchored by IKEA will be able to bite into some favorite local food vendors with an emphasis on vegetarian and vegan options.Business registrations show five incoming tenants, which include Puerto Rican restaurant Casa Borinquena, Indian chain Curry Up Now, Chinese banmian specialists Momo Noodle, Filipino restaurant the Sarap Shop and Oakland vegan taqueria Tacos La Venganza.
