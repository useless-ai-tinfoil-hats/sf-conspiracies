Group seeking to oust Alameda County DA Pamela Price to begin collecting signatures
-----
Opponents of Alameda County District Attorney Pamela Price have five months to gather more than 73,000 voter signatures — the last step to get their recall measure on the ballot — after the registrar of voters approved their petition on Wednesday.The effort to unseat Price, a progressive former civil rights attorney, has been building since residents and community organizers started a recall committee in July. Called Save Alameda for Everyone, the group rallied support from crime victims
