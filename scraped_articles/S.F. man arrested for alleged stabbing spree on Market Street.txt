S.F. man arrested for alleged stabbing spree on Market Street
-----
San Francisco police arrested a man who allegedly attacked multiple people Sunday morning, stabbing three people and injuring another.From 7:30 a.m. through 8:10 a.m. Sunday, San Francisco police responded to four different reports of attacks on Market Street near Union Square, according to the Police Department. One victim — an older adult male in a wheelchair — was knocked to the ground and bleeding from the face. Three others, all adult men, had been stabbed, officials said.
