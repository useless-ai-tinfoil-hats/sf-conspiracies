San Francisco's office vacancy rate hit yet another record high this month, climbing to 36.6%, as leasing activity remains muted.The vacancy rate was up 1 percentage point from the end of last year and jumped from 29.4% from the first quarter of 2023, according to preliminary data from real estate brokerage CBRE. It's now up ninefold from the 4% low in the first quarter of 2020, at the start of the pandemic.
