Ahead of Jackie Robinson Day, A's praise his courage and impact on the game
-----
Oakland Athletics outfielder Lawrence Butler made his major-league debut Aug. 11, so he'll experience Jackie Robinson Day as a big leaguer for the first time when the A's host St. Louis on Monday night."He just paved the way for me and a lot of other guys in this game, to be able to be big leaguers and play baseball, the game that we love," Butler said in the A's clubhouse Sunday morning.
