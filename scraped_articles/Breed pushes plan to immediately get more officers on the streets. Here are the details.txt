Breed pushes plan to immediately get more officers on the streets. Here are the details
-----
Mayor London Breed said Thursday she would seek to expand the ranks of San Francisco's police reserves to increase the number of officers patrolling the city on foot.Under Breed's proposed plan, the 30 retired police officers and trained civilians who make up the city's Reserve Police Officer Program would be immediately dispatched around the city to conduct foot patrols.
