The Bay Area clothing store that turns 49ers stars into repeat customers
-----
On the fields of 30 NFL stadiums across the country, the sight of an incoming Deebo Samuel evokes a sense of dread. With the ball in his hands, the 49ers' star wideout is just one jump cut or spin move away from forcing a would-be tackler into an embarrassing whiff.For Emile Saadeh, founder of streetwear fashion store Fresh Society, the feeling is quite the opposite. That's because if Saadeh sees Samuel coming, it means that the 49er is approaching Saadeh's storefront in the Westfield
