Tahoe bear shooter may face criminal investigation
-----
The El Dorado County District Attorney's Office is considering a criminal investigation into the shooting of a 16-month-old bear cub on Memorial Day in South Lake Tahoe."We are aware of the case," a spokesperson for the district attorney's office told SFGATE Wednesday afternoon. "We're looking into it."
On the afternoon of Monday, May 27, a bear entered a South Tahoe resident's home and was huffing and advancing, the California Department of Fish and Wildlife told SFGATE last week.
