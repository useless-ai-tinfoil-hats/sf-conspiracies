These San Francisco streets will be closed for the Folsom Street Fair
-----
Several streets in downtown San Francisco will be closed this weekend ahead of the Folsom Street Fair, which attracts thousands of attendees who celebrate by wearing leather, latex harnesses and, sometimes, nothing at all. The annual event in the city's SoMa district is notorious for honoring sexual expression, BDSM and kink, and features DJs, dancers and various booths.The 40th annual event will take place on Sunday, Sept. 24, from 11 a.m. to 6 p.m.
