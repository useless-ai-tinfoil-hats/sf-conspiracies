Ex-Stanford lineman celebrates Big Game win with new friends at Cal
-----
Amid the revelry of Cal fans once again storming the field after a Big Game victory, the Bears' Barrett Miller got to make a few dozen new friends."I think I took maybe 40-50 selfies with people I have no idea (who they are) and (they) somehow know me on a first-name basis," the left tackle said following Cal's 27-15 win at Stanford on Saturday. That gave the Bears a three-game winning streak in the series.
