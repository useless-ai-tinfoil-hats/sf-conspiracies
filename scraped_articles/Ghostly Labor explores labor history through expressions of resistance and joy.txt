‘Ghostly Labor’ explores labor history through expressions of resistance and joy
-----
The rhythms of Mexican folklorico footwork fused with the syncopations of tap and the roiling torsos of Afro-Caribbean styles at the Mission District's Brava Theater for the world premiere of La Mezcla's "Ghostly Labor" on Friday, Dec. 15. By the end of 90 ear-teasing, hip-shaking minutes, two things were clear.
