49ers lose to Ravens 33-19 on Brock Purdy's career-worst 4 interceptions
-----
The "robot" turned into an interception machine.San Francisco 49ers quarterback Brock Purdy, whose season-long efficiency and unflappability had inspired tight end George Kittle to liken him to an automaton, fell apart in a four-interception performance in a 33-19 loss to the Ravens on Monday night at Levi's Stadium.
