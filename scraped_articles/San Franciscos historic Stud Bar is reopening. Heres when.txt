San Francisco’s historic Stud Bar is reopening. Here’s when
-----
After months of anticipation, a famed San Francisco gay bar plans to reopen in the South of Market neighborhood.The 15-person collective that owns and operates the Stud announced last week on Instagram an April 20 opening date at the bar's new home at 1123-1125 Folsom St., promising more details soon. Representatives from the Stud could not be reached Tuesday, March 26, for further comment.
