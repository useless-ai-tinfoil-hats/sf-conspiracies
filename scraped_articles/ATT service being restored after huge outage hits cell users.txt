AT&T service being restored after huge outage hits cell users
-----
LATEST Feb. 22, 8:30 a.m. AT&T said in an emailed statement at 8:20 a.m. that customers' service was being restored. "Some of our customers are experiencing wireless service interruptions this morning," AT&T said. "Our network teams took immediate action and so far three-quarters of our network has been restored.
