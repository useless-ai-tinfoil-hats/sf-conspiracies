What Stanford can take from Nebraska’s example as volleyball’s rock stars
-----
In 2023, women's sports audience records are being shattered. But what Nebraska volleyball — visitors at Stanford this week — accomplished at the end of August stands out.Nebraska's crowd of 92,003 for an August regular-season match against Nebraska-Omaha ended up being the highest attended women's sporting event in recorded history. Women's sports — especially Olympic women's sports — have never before gotten the platform the Cornhuskers have created for themselves.
