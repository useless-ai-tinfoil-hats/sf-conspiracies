Man dies after 80-foot fall into silo at former Calif. sugar plant
-----
A man died Saturday after falling about 80 feet into a silo at the former Holly Sugar plant in Tracy, California, officials said.The deceased was identified Monday by the San Joaquin County Medical Examiner's Office as Dean Melavic, a 53-year-old Elk Grove resident.
