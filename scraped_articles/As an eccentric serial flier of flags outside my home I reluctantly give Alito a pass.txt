Last year my city council member stopped me as he strolled by."Are you Mexican?" he asked.
"Nope, I'm Scots-Irish," I replied. "But Mexico is a great country. I'm doing a lot of work there these days."
The council member's question was not out of the blue. I had been flying the flag of Mexico outside my house for six months. He assumed it was some sort of statement.
