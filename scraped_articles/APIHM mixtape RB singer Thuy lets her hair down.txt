APIHM mixtape: R&B singer Thuy lets her ‘hair down’
-----
Raised in Newark, Vietnamese American R&B singer Thuy Thi Thu Tran — mononymously known as Thuy — is riding a year of highs. Along with touring Europe, opening for Ella Mai, and headlining her own show at the Warfield, she performed at the 2024 Coachella Valley Music & Arts Festival, representing Vietnamese Americans, women, independent artistry and the Bay on music's most influential stage.Ever the perfectionist, Thuy studied her first weekend's performance like a coach studying game
