Two children dead after falling into river near Shasta Dam, officials say
-----
Shasta County Sheriff's Office officials said two children died after falling from a camping area into a river area north of the Shasta Dam on Thursday morning.The children, whose ages were not provided, fell from a camping area into the river and were later located deceased in the water, officials said.
Officials said recovery efforts were ongoing as of Thursday afternoon but did not elaborate.
