Beloved SLO bar closes permanently after 30 years
-----
A legendary San Luis Obispo bar and music venue has closed after a 30-year run. The downtown restaurant quietly shuttered without notice this week, and surprisingly, a new restaurant has already been announced as a replacement.Mother's Tavern, which opened in 1994, was for decades a well-traveled hangout for tourists and locals with a strong contingent of nearby Cal Poly college students descending on the weekends.
