'She was the Queen': Beloved DJ who started career in SF dies
-----
Dusty Street, a trailblazing female radio disc jockey who started her career in San Francisco and became a recognized voice on Los Angeles radio station KROQ-FM, died last week at her home in Oregon. She was 77.Her death was announced on Oct. 21 on social media by Geno Michellini, a former DJ at KLOS-FM in LA. "I have been in Eugene the last two days at Dusty Street's bedside," Michellini wrote.
