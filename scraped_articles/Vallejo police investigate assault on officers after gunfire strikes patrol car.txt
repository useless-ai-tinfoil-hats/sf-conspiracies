Vallejo police investigate assault on officers after gunfire strikes patrol car
-----
Vallejo police said they are investigating an assault on two officers after one of their cars was struck by gunfire early Saturday.The officers were on routine patrol near Harbor Way and Mare Island Way when at least one bullet struck their car around 1 a.m., said Vallejo Police Department spokesperson Sgt. Rashad Hollis.
