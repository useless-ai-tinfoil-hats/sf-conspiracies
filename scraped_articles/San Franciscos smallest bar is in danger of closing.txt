San Francisco's smallest bar is in danger of closing
-----
Black Horse London Pub, the smallest bar in San Francisco, is in danger of closing. Owner James King said he fears it may be the end for the tiny dive on the edge of Cow Hollow and Russian Hill if he doesn't raise $14,000 through a GoFundMe he created earlier this week.King said he will use the funds to clear debt with the California Department of Tax and Fee Administration and to pay for licenses.
