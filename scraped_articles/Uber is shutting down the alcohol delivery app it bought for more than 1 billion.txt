Uber is shutting down the alcohol delivery app it bought for more than $1 billion
-----
Uber will shut down the alcohol delivery service Drizly three years after acquiring it for $1.1 billion.Drizly will continue operating until the end of March. Uber will still offer alcohol delivery through its Uber Eats platform.
An Uber spokesperson, Meghan Casserly, confirmed the closure, first reported by Axios.
