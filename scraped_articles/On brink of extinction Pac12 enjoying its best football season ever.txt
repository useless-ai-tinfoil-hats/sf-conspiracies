On brink of extinction, Pac-12 enjoying its best football season ever
-----
What in the name of Sonny Sixkiller is going on here? The Pac-12 Conference dissolves at the peak of its existence?This is no idle conversation. It's getting very close to an unassailable argument. More than 60 years into its modern history, Pac-12 football is the best it has ever been.
You figured it would take a potent blend of cowardice and greed to turn the conference into a pile of ashes — just two teams remain after the desperate cash grab — but that's where we are.
