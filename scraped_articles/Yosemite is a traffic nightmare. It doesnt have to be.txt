Yosemite is a traffic nightmare. It doesn’t have to be
-----
Last week, the National Park Service released for public comment its draft plan for visitor access management at Yosemite National Park. The plan — which aims to reduce overcrowding and traffic congestion as well as better pace vehicle volume into the park and provide equitable access — would require reservations to enter the park during peak hours from April to October.While the plan is an important first step toward better managing the park, it doesn't go far enough.
