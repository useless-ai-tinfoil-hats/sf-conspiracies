A’s radio duo Korach & Cotroneo call last Bay Bridge Series ‘a reminder of what it was like’
-----
Vince Cotroneo opened the Oakland Athletics' pregame show Sunday by saying, "The reality is starting to set in."The last game of the Bay Bridge Series did, indeed, seem to bring the A's impending departure into sharp focus. The Coliseum will see no more of these games, and though the A's will continue to play games against the Giants at Oracle Park, they will not do so as near next-door neighbors.
