Plan to turn an S.F. auto garage into a new city park and greenspace wins key vote
-----
An auto repair shop on a ragged block on Sixth Street in San Francisco's South of Market neighborhood could be bought by the city and turned into a park under a plan that advanced with a key vote Thursday.The garage at 240 Sixth St., which is leased to Euro Motorcars, abuts the Gene Friend Recreation Center, which has been demolished, scraped and graded in a separate, major start-over construction project already underway.
