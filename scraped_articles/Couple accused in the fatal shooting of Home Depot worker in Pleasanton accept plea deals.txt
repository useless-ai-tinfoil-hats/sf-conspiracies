Couple accused in the fatal shooting of Home Depot worker in Pleasanton accept plea deals
-----
A woman and a man accused in the fatal shooting of a loss prevention officer at a Home Depot store in Pleasanton pleaded no contest this week to various felony charges as part of a plea deal, according to the Alameda County District Attorney's Office and court records.The woman accused of shooting the worker, Benicia Knapps, pleaded no contest to second-degree murder and a gun sentencing enhancement, and agreed to a term of 19 years to life in state prison.
