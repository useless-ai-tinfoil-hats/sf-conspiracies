SF bar named one of the best new bars of 2024 by Bon Appetit
-----
Just ahead of San Francisco's annual Pride parade during Pride month, a local queer bar was named one of Bon Appetit Magazine's 11 Best New Bars in the US.Mother, a queer women-centric neighborhood bar at 3079 16th St. in the Mission District, was one of 11 bars across the country that made it onto the revered food and drink magazine's annual list. The publication does not rank the bars numerically, instead collectively elevating the bars as some of the best in the entire country.
