After 106 years, this James Beard Award-winning Hawaii restaurant is being sold
-----
Just 12 miles from historic Kailua-Kona town, Hawaii's oldest continually operating restaurant, Manago Hotel, sits on the hills of the Big Island's Mauna Loa volcano overlooking Kealakekua Bay. Founded in 1917, and cared after by four generations of a single family, the Manago Hotel is big news around here: It's expected to be sold to new owners soon.The drive takes me to an elevation of 1,350 feet through Kona coffee country.
