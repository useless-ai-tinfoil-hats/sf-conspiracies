These Bay Area commuter highways are approaching 2019 traffic levels
-----
Traffic on the four major highways used to enter San Francisco and the Bay Area has yet to fully return to pre-pandemic levels, though it might already feel like 2019 for North Bay commuters.Five-year data, provided by Caltrans to the Chronicle, shows ongoing disparities in how traffic has reemerged on the Bay Area highways many drivers use to commute to work, from places such as Sacramento and the Central Valley.
