Will 49ers draft a third high-quality cornerback to team with Ward and Lenoir?
-----
Last season, the San Francisco 49ers had two high-quality cornerbacks, Charvarius Ward and Deommodore Lenoir, and a quandary: They didn't have a reliable No. 3.The 49ers began with Isaiah Oliver, turned to Ambry Thomas and finished with since-retired Logan Ryan, whose final NFL snap highlighted their season-long issues. Ryan was trailing wide receiver Mecole Hardman on his game-winning, 3-yard touchdown catch in the 49ers' 25-22 overtime loss to the Chiefs in Super Bowl LVIII.
