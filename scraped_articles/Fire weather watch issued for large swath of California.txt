Fire weather watch issued for large swath of California
-----
Bone-dry air and powerful wind gusts are expected to bring the perfect recipe for rapid wildfire spread across parts of Northern California this weekend, and the National Weather Service issued a series of fire weather watches on Thursday to start ringing the alarm bells.Portions of Trinity, Lake and Mendocino counties will be under a watch Friday night through Sunday afternoon, the weather service said. The fire risk is expected to be high, with humidity levels dropping into the teens
