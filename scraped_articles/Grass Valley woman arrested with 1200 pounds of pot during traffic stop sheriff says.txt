Grass Valley woman arrested with 1,200 pounds of pot during traffic stop, sheriff says
-----
A Grass Valley woman was arrested Tuesday on suspicion of transporting marijuana for sales after police found 1,200 pounds of pot in her cargo van during a traffic stop, officials said.A deputy pulled the 41-year-old woman over at Interstate 5 and Jayne Avenue near Coalinga and discovered a bounty of marijuana, the Fresno County Sheriff's Office said.
The van was stuffed with 52 large trash bags of cannabis that weighed 1,200 pounds in total, according to a news release issued
