Kamala Harris says she’d appoint a Republican to her Cabinet
-----
WASHINGTON — Democratic presidential nominee Kamala Harris promised to appoint a Republican to her Cabinet in an interview with CNN Thursday.The announcement, immediately touted by her campaign in a news release, follows extensive outreach to Republicans and aligns with a recommendation to govern "from the center" from Speaker Emerita Nancy Pelosi.
