Pro-Palestine demonstrators disrupt Berkeley meeting on allowing taller buildings
-----
The Berkeley City Council delayed its vote Tuesday on a measure to permit taller buildings in a densely populated neighborhood near UC Berkeley after pro-Palestine demonstrators disrupted the meeting.The demonstrators demanded that the city introduce a resolution calling for a cease-fire in Gaza.
"Draft a resolution or expect continued disruptions," one speaker said.
