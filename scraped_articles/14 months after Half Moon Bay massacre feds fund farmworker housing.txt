14 months after Half Moon Bay massacre, feds fund farmworker housing
-----
San Mateo County is set to receive $3 million in federal funding to help build affordable housing for farmworkers, officials said Tuesday.The funding was given to the Half Moon Bay Farmworker Ownership Project at the request of Bay Area Rep. Anna Eshoo, and U.S. Senators Dianne Feinstein, who died last year, and Alex Padilla, county officials said. The money will be used to purchase up to 20 home units for farmworker housing and create a recreation space for residents, county officials
