Man shot and killed in SF Mission District, police say
-----
A man was shot and killed in San Francisco's Mission District on Friday afternoon, the San Francisco Police Department told SFGATE.The shooting happened at 1:37 p.m. on the 2300 block of Mission Street, Allison Maxie, a spokesperson for the department, said.
Officers and paramedics at the scene administered life-saving efforts to the man with gunshot wounds, including CPR, but he was pronounced dead at the scene, police said.
