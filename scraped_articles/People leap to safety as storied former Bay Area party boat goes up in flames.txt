People leap to safety as storied former Bay Area party boat goes up in flames
-----
Thick black smoke cascaded over Vallejo on Saturday afternoon as a three-story 19th-century replica riverboat known as the Grand Romance caught fire, sending people leaping into the water for safety.The Vallejo Fire Department responded to reports of a possible blaze near the corner of Lemon and Derr streets at approximately 4:47 p.m. that afternoon and found "a large column of smoke" emanating through the air, firefighter Aaron Klauber told SFGATE.
