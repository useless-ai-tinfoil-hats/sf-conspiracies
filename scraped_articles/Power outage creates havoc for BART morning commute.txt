Power outage creates havoc for BART morning commute
-----
A PG&E power outage shut down two BART service lines and caused major delays throughout the system on Thursday morning.The outage near Castro Valley prompted BART to stop train service between the Castro Valley and West Dublin/Pleasanton stations in both directions around 9:30 a.m., according to a social media post from BART Alert. The agency provided bus services between the two stations during the outage.
Service has since resumed, Jim Allison, a spokesperson for BART, confirmed
