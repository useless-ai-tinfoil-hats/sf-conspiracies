Teen arrested on suspicion of knife threat, hate crime, San Bruno police say
-----
San Bruno officers this weekend arrested a 17-year-old on suspicion of brandishing a knife while making a death threat and committing a hate crime, police officials said.About 2:40 p.m. Saturday, police received a report that a man was threatened with a knife at a shopping center parking lot while he was praying outside his vehicle, according to a news release from the San Bruno Police Department.
