Swimmer ‘badly beaten,’ carjacked while parking near S.F. Maritime Museum
-----
A San Francisco swimmer was beaten and carjacked while on the way to his swimming club early Friday, according to the club.On Friday, the South End Rowing Club sent an email to members announcing a fellow member had been "assaulted and badly beaten" while on his way to the club, located close to Hyde Street Pier. The man was parking near the intersection of Hyde and Beach streets when he was attacked and his car was stolen, according to the letter.
