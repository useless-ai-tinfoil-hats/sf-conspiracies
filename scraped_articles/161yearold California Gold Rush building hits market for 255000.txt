161-year-old California Gold Rush building hits market for $255,000
-----
A 161-year-old building, still standing strong in the Sierra foothills, has hit the market.Built in 1863 after a fire ravaged the mining camp of French Corral in Nevada County, the two-story brick structure was originally used as a hardware store.
