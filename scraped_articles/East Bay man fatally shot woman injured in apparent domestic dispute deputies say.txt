East Bay man fatally shot, woman injured in apparent domestic dispute, deputies say
-----
A man was fatally shot and a woman was injured this week in what appeared to be a domestic dispute in San Leandro, the Alameda County Sheriff's Office said.Sheriff's deputies were called at about 10:17 p.m. Tuesday to a home in the 16000 block of Carolyn Street in the Fairmont Terrace neighborhood for a report of a shooting involving two people, the Sheriff's Office said. Deputies there found a woman, who reported the shooting, with a gunshot wound in her leg.
