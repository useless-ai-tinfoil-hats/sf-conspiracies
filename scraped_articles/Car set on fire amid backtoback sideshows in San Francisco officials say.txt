Car set on fire amid back-to-back sideshows in San Francisco, officials say
-----
Plumes of smoke drifted into the dark sky after a vehicle went up in flames during the first of two active sideshows that occurred in San Francisco early Sunday morning.Authorities were called to the intersection of the Embarcadero and Washington Street near the Ferry Building just after 2 a.m., where they found "multiple vehicles engaged in stunt driving," the San Francisco Police Department said in a statement.
