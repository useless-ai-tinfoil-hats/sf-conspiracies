Three Bay Area Lucky supermarkets just closed in one day
-----
Following the news that Lucky supermarkets would close two Bay Area stores on Nov. 3, the grocery chain has now announced a third.The Lucky store at 40055 Mission Blvd. in Fremont closed on Friday, along with the Dublin store at the Fallon Gateway shopping center and the Millbrae store at 45 Murchison Drive. SFGATE reported the impending Dublin and Millbrae store closures last month.
