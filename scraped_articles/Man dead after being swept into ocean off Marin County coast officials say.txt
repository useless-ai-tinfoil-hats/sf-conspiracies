Man dead after being swept into ocean off Marin County coast, officials say
-----
A man has died after being swept into the water at Point Reyes, Marin County Fire Department officials said Friday.Units responded to Point Reyes South Beach on Friday morning around 9 a.m. after receiving reports that a victim needed rescue, fire officials said.
The man was airlifted to South Beach just after 10 a.m, where he was pronounced dead, officials said.
