This SF business owner is battling graffiti — with rubber ducks
-----
San Francisco business owner Shorty Maniace has embraced the fact that he's recently become known around the neighborhood as "the duck guy." After all, in the past three months he's affixed 700 or so small rubber duckies to the boarded-up business next to his Lower Haight barbershop, J.P. Kempt.The colorful flock started as a whim — he had some ducks left over from a Halloween giveaway and loves quirky street art — but quickly morphed into a deliberate strategy to spare one slice of wall
