Bay Area burger chain opening restaurant in quiet SF neighborhood
-----
In San Francisco's Inner Sunset District, mom-and-pop restaurants reign, and large chain restaurants are few and far between. But now, a burger chain is planning to open in the quiet SF neighborhood.San Francisco chain Super Duper Burgers has its sights set on opening its next restaurant at 737 Irving St., according to public planning documents.
