Shutdown on major Bay Area freeway set for this weekend
-----
A full closure of southbound Interstate 680 in the Pleasanton area for repaving is scheduled to take place this weekend, after it was postponed twice due to weather, officials said.The shutdown is set to start at 9 p.m. Friday and will last through 4 a.m. Monday, Caltrans said in a news release.
