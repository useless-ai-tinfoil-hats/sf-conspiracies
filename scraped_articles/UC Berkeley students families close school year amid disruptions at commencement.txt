UC Berkeley students, families close school year amid disruptions at commencement
-----
UC Berkeley's campus-wide commencement began peacefully Saturday morning but soon was disrupted by pro-Palestinian protesters who gathered in one area of Memorial Stadium to chant.While many universities nationwide altered or canceled commencement ceremonies in the wake of campus encampments protesting Israel's war with Hamas, UC Berkeley's graduation went on as planned. Nearly 7,000 graduates were expected along with another 23,000 family and friends, for a total of about 30,000 people
