'I feel like it's going to change my life': New SF Trader Joe's opens to cheers
-----
"Let's f—king go, let's go!" "I love Trader Joe's!" "Oh my, they opened the Trader Joe's!"These were just some of the sentiments that passersby shouted with glee in front of San Francisco's newest Trader Joe's on Friday, opening day.
The Hayes Valley Trader Joe's at 555 Fulton St., on the corner of Laguna Street, has been a long time coming — five years to be exact. People in the neighborhood could not be more excited.
