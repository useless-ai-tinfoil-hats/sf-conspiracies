Salesforce Park's much-hyped, NFT-backed Sho Restaurant is dead
-----
Sho Restaurant, the extravagant, NFT-driven fine-dining pipe dream atop San Francisco's Salesforce Park, is dead, both the Transbay Joint Powers Authority and Sho Group CEO Joshua Sigel have confirmed to SFGATE."We have reached the difficult conclusion that bringing SHO to life atop Salesforce Park is not possible at this time," Sigel wrote in an emailed statement.
