Warriors’ Steph Curry, Liberty’s Sabrina Ionescu to face off in 3-point challenge
-----
Golden State Warriors guard Stephen Curry and Bay Area native Sabrina Ionescu of the WNBA's New York Liberty will compete during the NBA's All-Star Weekend in "Stephen vs. Sabrina," a head-to-head 3-point shootout between the two deadeyes, the NBA announced Tuesday morning.Curry, the NBA's all-time leading 3-point shooter and a former two-time NBA 3-point contest champion, teased the challenge with Ionescu on Thursday while conversing with rookie guard Brandin Podziemski before a game
