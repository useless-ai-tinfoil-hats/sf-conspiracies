California’s high-speed rail needs $100 billion to finish — scrapping the project is a possibility
-----
California's high-speed rail project has teased residents with recent renderings of how its futuristic trains and massive stations would look. But, despite recent progress and the excitement those renderings have produced, the project remains about $7 billion short of the cost to complete the initial segment from Merced to Bakersfield.The rail project also needs about $100 billion to make the original vision of linking San Francisco and Los Angeles via bullet trains a reality.
