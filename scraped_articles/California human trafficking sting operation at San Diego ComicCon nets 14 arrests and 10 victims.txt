California human trafficking sting operation at San Diego Comic-Con nets 14 arrests and 10 victims
-----
A covert sting operation at the San Diego Comic-Con Convention resulted in 14 arrests and the rescue of 10 suspected human trafficking victims, the California Attorney General's Office said this week.The sting focused on people suspected of soliciting sex for money and potential victims of sex trafficking at the convention, which draws more than 100,000 comic-book fans to San Diego each year, officials said.
