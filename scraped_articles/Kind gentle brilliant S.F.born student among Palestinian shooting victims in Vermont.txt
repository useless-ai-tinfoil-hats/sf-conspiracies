‘Kind, gentle, brilliant’: S.F.-born student among Palestinian shooting victims in Vermont
-----
One of three men shot in a possible anti-Palestinian hate crime in Vermont, who was left paralyzed from the chest down after one of the bullets struck his spine, was born in San Francisco.Hisham Awartani was visiting family Nov. 25 in Burlington, Vt., when he and two friends, Kinnan Abdalhamid and Tahseen Ali Ahmad, were shot. Abdalhamid and Ali Ahmad were expected to make a full recovery, according to friends and family who created a GoFundMe to aid medical expenses.
