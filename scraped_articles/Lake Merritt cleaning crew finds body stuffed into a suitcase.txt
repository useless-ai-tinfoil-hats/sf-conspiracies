Lake Merritt cleaning crew finds body stuffed into a suitcase
-----
A crew cleaning Lake Merritt on Tuesday morning removed a suitcase from the water and opened it to find a man's body stuffed inside it, according to two law enforcement sources who spoke with the Chronicle.Oakland police responded to the disturbing discovery at the 1900 block of Lakeshore Avenue shortly before 11 a.m. Homicide detectives were investigating the scene.
