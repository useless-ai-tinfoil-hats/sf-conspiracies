The Chronicle’s girls basketball regional players of the year
-----
San FranciscoKelis Powell (Lincoln) — The Academic Athletic Association Player of the Year led the Mustangs to a 24-5 season, a San Francisco Section championship and 18 straight wins before losing in the NorCal Division 4 quarterfinals at eventual state champion St. Bernard's-Eureka. Powell averaged more than 12 points on per game this season, but the 5-foot-8 senior upped those numbers to more than 18 ppg in the postseason.
