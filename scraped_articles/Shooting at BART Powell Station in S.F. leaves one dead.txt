Shooting at BART Powell Station in S.F. leaves one dead
-----
A person was fatally shot at BART Powell Street Station Thursday night, according to the San Francisco Fire Department.The shooting occurred at 9:30 p.m., S.F. fire Lt. Mariano Elias told the Chronicle. The victim was declared dead at the scene, according to Elias. San Francisco police did not immediately respond to a request for comment.
