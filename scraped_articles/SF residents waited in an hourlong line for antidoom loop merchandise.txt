SF residents waited in an hourlong line for anti-doom loop merchandise
-----
If you live in San Francisco, you may soon start noticing a strange message emblazoned across T-shirts, jackets and bags around town: "SF is DEAD."The graphic is meant to be celebratory, or at least ironically negative. The new "anti-doom loop" merchandise was created at a screenprinting event last week at Madrone Art Bar.
