Why Southwest, JetBlue decided to stop 'prioritizing' passengers
-----
Low-cost carriers — airlines like Southwest and JetBlue — are experiencing an identity crisis.Southwest just announced that it was ending one of its signature practices for boarding, while JetBlue recently upended part of its customer-friendly business model by adopting dynamic pricing for checked bags. Both airlines are increasing fees and slashing routes as their profits fluctuate between decline and stability.
