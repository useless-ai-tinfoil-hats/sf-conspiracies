Three injured in shooting in parking lot of Napa County Safeway
-----
Three men were shot in the parking lot of a Safeway grocery store in American Canyon on Monday afternoon, police said.At about 4:30 p.m., officers were dispatched to the Safeway at 103 W. American Canyon Road after a report of a shooting, police said. Upon arrival, authorities located three men with gunshot wounds. Officers provided aid and the victims were transported to a hospital, according to police. Their condition is unknown.
Police said they detained two men for questioning
