Santa Rosa police: 2 arrested after drug bust nets 5 pounds of suspected meth
-----
Santa Rosa police arrested two people on suspicion of drug possession and felony child endangerment Thursday.In June, the Santa Rosa Police Department investigated a suspected drug trafficking organization believed to be distributing methamphetamine and cocaine in and around Santa Rosa, police wrote in a news release. Detectives identified two Santa Rosa residents, Juan Carlos Beltran-Granados, 31, and Belen Santos-Hernandez, 43, for alleged involvement.
