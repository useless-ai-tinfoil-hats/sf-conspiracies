Crews recover body of man who drowned in Northern California river
-----
Rescuers on Sunday recovered the body of a man who drowned while rafting on the American River in the Sacramento area, authorities said.The man was not wearing a life jacket during the rafting trip Saturday with a large party of people, according to the Sacramento Metropolitan Fire District. He was swept into the water and was last seen at Clay Banks in Rancho Cordova.
Rescue crews patrolled the water for hours but could not find him, officials said.
