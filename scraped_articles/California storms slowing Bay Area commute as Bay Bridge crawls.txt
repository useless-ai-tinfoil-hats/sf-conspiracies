California storms slowing Bay Area commute as Bay Bridge crawls
-----
Rain showers and strong winds swirling across the Bay Area Wednesday were slowing morning traffic and transit.The San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency replaced F-Market streetcars with buses due to the flooding along the Embarcadero, the transit agency said.
