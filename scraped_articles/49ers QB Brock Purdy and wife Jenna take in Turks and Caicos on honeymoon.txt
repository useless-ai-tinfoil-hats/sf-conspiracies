49ers QB Brock Purdy and wife Jenna take in Turks and Caicos on honeymoon
-----
After getting hitched in the Hawkeye State, 49ers quarterback Brock Purdy and his wife Jenna Purdy have escaped to the sunshine for their honeymoon.The college sweethearts married in Des Moines, Iowa, on March 9, just a short drive from Iowa State where they met. Thanks to an Instagram post from Jenna, we now know the Purdys went to Turks and Caicos for their honeymoon.
Jenna posted a five-photo collage Monday, showing the couple enjoying their time on the beach and in the water.
