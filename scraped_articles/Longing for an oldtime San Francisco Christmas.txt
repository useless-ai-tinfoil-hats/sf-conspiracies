Longing for an old-time San Francisco Christmas
-----
Sunday is Christmas Eve, one of the most festive and complex days of the year. It is both happy and sad, a day of anticipation: Santa Claus, presents, good company and mulled wine. Also a sense that the season is coming to an end."A Christmas hush fell over the city,'' a Chronicle article began one Christmas Eve, describing the last-minute rush to buy gifts. When the day was over, "weary shop girls kicked off their shoes,'' the story said.
