East Bay man accused of fake real estate scam that bilked dozens of people out of a small fortune
-----
A Nigerian man who allegedly posed as a Bay Area real estate agent to scam people into paying him what they thought were rental deposits has been charged with defrauding people out of more than half a million dollars in a yearslong scheme, authorities said.Barnabas Jime, 29, is facing one charge of conspiracy to commit wire fraud after he allegedly posted false real estate listings online and illegally took money from people looking to lease the properties, according to the U.S.
