California’s summer COVID swell may have peaked, but ‘tripledemic’ threat looms
-----
After weeks of steadily rising COVID-19 metrics in California, there are now signs that the late summer swell may have peaked.Hospital admissions for COVID-19 patients in the state fell by around 8% over the past week, dropping from an average of 395 to 366 per day, as reported by state health department data published on Friday.
