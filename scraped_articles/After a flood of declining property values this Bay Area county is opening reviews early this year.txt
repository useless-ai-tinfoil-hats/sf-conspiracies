After a flood of declining property values, this Bay Area county is opening reviews early this year
-----
Santa Clara County will allow commercial property owners to request reviews for potentially lower tax bills four months earlier this year after a flood of value declines in 2023.The county assessor said Tuesday that the review process has begun ahead of the end of fiscal year in June due to ongoing turmoil across the real estate industry. Vacant space, remote work, and higher interest and mortgage rates have all added uncertainty.
