Bay Area grocery store set to permanently close after 50 years
-----
After five decades of offering fresh organic produce and niche goods, Country Sun Natural Foods in Palo Alto will close its doors by the end of the year. The permanent closure, which was first reported by Palo Alto Online, is expected to occur around Christmas once store shelves and inventory are cleared out, CEO and general manager Scott Otte told SFGATE.
