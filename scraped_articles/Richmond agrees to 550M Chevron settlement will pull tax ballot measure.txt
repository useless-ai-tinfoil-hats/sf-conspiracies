Richmond agrees to $550M Chevron settlement, will pull tax ballot measure
-----
The Richmond City Council voted unanimously Wednesday to approve a $550 million settlement with Chevron in exchange for pulling a November ballot measure that could have imposed an oil-refining tax on the company.Per the agreement, Chevron will pay $50 million a year for the next five years, then $60 million a year for the following five. The company initially offered $300 million, but raised the sum after a week of negotiations.
