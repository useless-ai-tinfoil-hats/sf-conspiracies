This is the ‘weirdest stuff’ Yosemite hikers have dropped into Half Dome
-----
Once a year, as summertime winds down in Yosemite National Park, a cohort of rangers and volunteers head to special spots around Half Dome to see what junk and litter has fallen from the pockets and packs of hikers who ascend the crag's cables during the park's peak season.Tens of thousands of people summit the famous 8,842-foot granite cliff each year via a steep route up its eastern shoulder bolted with steel guide cables.
