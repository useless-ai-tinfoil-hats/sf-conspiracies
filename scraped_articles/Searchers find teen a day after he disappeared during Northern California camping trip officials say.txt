Searchers find teen a day after he disappeared during Northern California camping trip, officials say
-----
Search teams on Wednesday found a 16-year-old boy a day after he disappeared while on a family camping trip at Camp Far West Lake in Yuba County, authorities said.The boy, Mikhael Nazorov, was reported missing by his mother at around 7:25 p.m. on Tuesday after he was separated from his older sister as the two walked around Far West Reservoir near Wheatland, according to the Yuba County Sheriff's Office. His sister and family were unable to find him, officials said.
