Coachella rumors swirl with Lana Del Rey and Tyler, the Creator reportedly tapped to headline
-----
Rumors are flooding the internet that Lana Del Rey and Tyler, the Creator have been tapped to headline Coachella 2024. Doja Cat is believed to be a likely third headliner.The rumors began thanks to a report from industry publication Hits Daily Double. The outlet reported that Del Rey and Tyler, the Creator are rumored to be confirmed for the festival while discussions concerning Doja Cat are ongoing. No official word has come from the festival or artists.
