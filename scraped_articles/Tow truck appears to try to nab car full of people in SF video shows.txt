Tow truck appears to try to nab car full of people in SF, video shows
-----
When San Francisco software engineer Jeff Ferland was walking back to his office from Escape From New York Pizza in the Financial District on Tuesday, the last thing he expected to do was witness every local car owner's worst nightmare. But he got that and so much more.That morning, he saw a tow truck with flashing lights suddenly pull in front of an occupied car next to a Waymo vehicle at the corner of Bush and Montgomery. At a glance, it didn't seem terribly out of the ordinary.
