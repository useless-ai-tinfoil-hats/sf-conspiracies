Tech billionaire's Bay Area mansion finally sells after $62 million price cut
-----
After years on the market, a luxury Bay Area mansion finally sold — $61.8 million below its original asking price.Scott McNealy, co-founder of Sun Microsystems, listed his Portola Valley estate more than six years ago with an asking price of nearly $100 million. On July 18, the property sold for $35 million, the Real Deal first reported.
