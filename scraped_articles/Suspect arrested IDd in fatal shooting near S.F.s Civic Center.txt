Suspect arrested, ID’d in fatal shooting near S.F.’s Civic Center
-----
A 35-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of chasing and fatally shooting a victim in the South of Market neighborhood Friday night, police said.The victim was shot around 7:15 p.m. on Mission Street near Eighth Street. Police said they believe the shooter chased the victim while firing multiple rounds at him.
Officers started CPR before paramedics took the victim to a hospital, where he died, police said. His age and name were not released.
