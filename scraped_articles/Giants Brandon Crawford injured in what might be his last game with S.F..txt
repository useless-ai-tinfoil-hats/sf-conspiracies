Giants’ Brandon Crawford injured in what might be his last game with S.F.
-----
UPDATE: The Giants on Thursday put Brandon Crawford on the injured list and recalled shortstop Marco Luciano.PHOENIX — Their playoff hopes growing dimmer, the San Francisco Giants piled up yet another loss on the road and lost another key veteran player midgame.
One day after starter Alex Cobb exited with a hip injury in the third inning, longtime Giants shortstop Brandon Crawford, 36, came out of Wednesday's game because of right hamstring tightness in a 7-1 loss at Arizona.
