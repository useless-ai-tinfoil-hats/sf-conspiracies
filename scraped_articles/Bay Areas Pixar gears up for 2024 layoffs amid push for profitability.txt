Bay Area's Pixar gears up for 2024 layoffs amid push for profitability
-----
Pixar is gearing up for a round of 2024 layoffs, TechCrunch reported on Thursday.The company, which is headquartered in Emeryville, confirmed the layoff plans to TechCrunch, telling the outlet that it was still determining the number of positions to be eliminated, pending production schedules and staffing needs.
