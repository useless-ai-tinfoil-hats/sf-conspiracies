Two killed, three injured in wrong-way crash near Tracy
-----
A wrong-way driver died in a crash near Tracy that also killed another motorist and injured three passengers, according to the California Highway Patrol.A Honda Accord driver who was traveling eastbound in the westbound lane of Interstate 580 west of Corral Hollow Road was ejected after crashing into a Kia Telluride shortly after 2 a.m. Sunday, the CHP reported. The impact caused both vehicles to overturn.
