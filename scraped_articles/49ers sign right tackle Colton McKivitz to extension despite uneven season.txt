49ers sign right tackle Colton McKivitz to extension despite uneven season
-----
Colton McKivitz was rewarded with some security Friday after his first full season as a 49ers starter.The 49ers signed their right tackle to a one-year, $7 million extension, with 65% of his money fully guaranteed, according to his agency, AMDG Sports. McKivitz, who is scheduled to count $3.64 million against the salary cap in 2024, is under contract through 2025.
