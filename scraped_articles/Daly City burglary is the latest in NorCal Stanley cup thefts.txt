Daly City burglary is the latest in NorCal Stanley cup thefts
-----
The Stanley Quencher craze isn't just a viral sensation on social media. The sturdy, insulated cups also have become the target of multiple thefts in Northern California this year.Most recently, police say an unidentified person broke into a Target at 100 Westlake Center in Daly City early on March 26 and stole almost $500 worth of Stanley products (the Quenchers sell at Target for $45 each).
