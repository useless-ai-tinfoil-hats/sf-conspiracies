Blood reportedly found in hallway in California missing woman case
-----
Details are emerging in the case of a missing Bakersfield woman, as authorities are asking for the public's help in identifying a vehicle.Ana Barbosa, 53, was last seen by family members on May 6. The following day, a late '90s white GMC pickup truck pulling a rusted trailer was captured on surveillance footage in the Lamont and Arvin areas of Kern County. The county sheriff's office is describing the truck as a "vehicle of interest."
