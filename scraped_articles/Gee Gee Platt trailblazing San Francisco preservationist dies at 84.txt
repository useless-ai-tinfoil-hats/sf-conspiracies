‘Gee Gee’ Platt, trailblazing San Francisco preservationist, dies at 84
-----
With her genteel roots and refined personal style, G. Bland Platt wasn't an obvious force to be reckoned with in San Francisco's development battles of decades past.But there she was again and again, filing lawsuits one moment and conducting historical research the next. Exerting what influence she could have as leader of a city board with limited powers and, more than once, showing up to protest the demolition of an aged building.
