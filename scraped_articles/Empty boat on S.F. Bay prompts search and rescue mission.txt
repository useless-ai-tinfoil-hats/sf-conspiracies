Empty boat on S.F. Bay prompts search and rescue mission
-----
No victims were located during a search and rescue in the San Francisco Bay after authorities found an unoccupied small boat off Embarcadero North with two fishing poles deployed Sunday morning, fire officials said.The San Francisco Fire Department and U.S. Coast Guard concluded their search of the area around 1:30 p.m. after no victims were located. It was unclear whether someone had abandoned the boat.
