‘No questions asked’: Here’s how many guns were collected during S.F. buyback
-----
A San Francisco violence prevention organization collected almost 200 firearms Saturday during a buyback intended to make the streets safer.United Playaz, seeking to "help end the pollution and be the solution," collected firearms at its headquarters on Howard Street in the South of Market neighborhood.
