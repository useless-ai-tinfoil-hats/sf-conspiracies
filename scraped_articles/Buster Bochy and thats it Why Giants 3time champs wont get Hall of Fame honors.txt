Buster, Bochy and that’s it? Why Giants’ 3-time champs won’t get Hall of Fame honors
-----
Sunday is the deadline for eligible baseball writers to turn in their Hall of Fame ballots, and while there are many compelling choices, there are no members of the San Francisco Giants' championship years to vote for.Tim Lincecum was a one-and-done two years ago, failing to get to the threshold of 5% to survive on the ballot despite two Cy Young awards, four All-Star appearances and three rings. Bruce Bochy was already a shoo-in sometime down the line even before winning a fourth ring
