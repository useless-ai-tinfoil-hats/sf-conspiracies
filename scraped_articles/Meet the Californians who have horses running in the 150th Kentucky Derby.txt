Meet the Californians who have horses running in the 150th Kentucky Derby
-----
When the 150th Kentucky Derby at Churchill Downs takes place Saturday, several horses in the running will have some distinct California roots.Three of the 20 horses slated to run in the world's most popular horse race are at least partially owned by folks from the Golden State: Just Steel, Endlessly and Stronghold. For the three horses' owners, this is the first time a horse they own is racing in the Kentucky Derby.
