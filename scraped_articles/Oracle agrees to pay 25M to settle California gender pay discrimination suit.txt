Oracle agrees to pay $25M to settle California gender pay discrimination suit
-----
Oracle Corp. has agreed to pay $25 million to settle a suit by 4,000 California women who held technology jobs and said the software giant paid them less than men who did the same work.The settlement was announced Tuesday after being negotiated with a mediator in December. One-third of the money would be paid to attorneys and the rest to women who worked in Oracle's product development, support or information technology departments in California since June 2013.
