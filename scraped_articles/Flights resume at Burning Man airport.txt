Flights resume at Burning Man airport
-----
UPDATE Aug. 24, 5:07 p.m. Flights have resumed at Black Rock City Municipal Airport, according to event organizers."The BRC Airport is open, and Burner Express Air has resumed normal operations," organizers said on X.
-----
The same storm that caused Burning Man organizers to close the gate to the playa has grounded all activity at the Black Rock City Municipal Airport (88NV).
