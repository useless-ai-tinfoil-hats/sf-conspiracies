Burning Man unveils 2024's 'Temple of Together'
-----
Burning Man has unveiled the design for its 2024 temple, the second-most important structure at the weeklong desert festival.A pair of enormous hands frame the main entrance to Caroline Ghosn's "Temple of Together," meeting at the fingertips. Neo-Gothic arches echo throughout the temple's design, flaring at the tops of the structure's concentric circular walls.
