How to grocery-shop in ultraexpensive Bay Area? Chefs and food pros agree on one thing
-----
Paying for food in the Bay Area — whether it's dining out, ordering takeout or grabbing a daily to-go coffee — can be a constant reminder of how expensive it is to live here. That may be especially true given that one of the most common ways to save money on food (cook at home and dine out less) is more difficult to pull off as the cost of grocery shopping has shot up.In 2022, grocery prices surged as inflation soared.
