What the long history of the Irish in San Francisco and the U.S. portends for the future
-----
There is an old Irish saying, "Ar scáth a chéile a mhaireannn na Daoine," which translates as "people live in each other's shadows." It speaks to the interdependence of our world — it means we rely on each other for shelter, for support, for love.There are not many places in the world where we see this in its full multicultural color.
