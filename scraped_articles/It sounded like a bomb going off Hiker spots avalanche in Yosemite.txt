'It sounded like a bomb going off': Hiker spots avalanche in Yosemite
-----
California resident Mikey Wright was hiking in Yosemite National Park on Thursday afternoon when he saw ice and snow tumbling down a mountainside in the distance.Wright, who hikes in the park about 50 days a year, said he was on a ridge that sits opposite Half Dome when he observed the avalanche.
"It sounded like a bomb going off," he said. "The avalanche was coming off that subdome off Half Dome ... it came off that subdome right where the cables start."
