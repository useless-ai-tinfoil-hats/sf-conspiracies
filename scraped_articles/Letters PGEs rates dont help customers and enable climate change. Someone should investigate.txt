Letters: PG&E’s rates don’t help customers and enable climate change. Someone should investigate
-----
Regarding "Here's the real reason PG&E rates are skyrocketing in California" (Open Forum, SFChronicle.com, April 20: I commend Loretta Lynch's commentary on the California Public Utilities Commission and PG&E's electric rates. If rates keep rising, the benefits of driving an electric vehicle or installing a home heat pump will disappear. The off-peak rate for charging my EV has doubled in five years.The proposed flat fee of $24 for PG&E customers will not encourage electrification.
