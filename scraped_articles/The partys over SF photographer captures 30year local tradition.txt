'The party's over': SF photographer captures 30-year local tradition
-----
When photographer Erik Burg takes photos of the Christmas trees on the curbs in San Francisco, his wife often walks away."It got a little depressing for her at times," he explained to SFGATE via email. "These trees spent a month or so in someone's home during the holidays, witnessing everything that goes along with that, and then — boom — they're on the streets. Sometimes, they even have ornaments and tinsel still attached to them!"
