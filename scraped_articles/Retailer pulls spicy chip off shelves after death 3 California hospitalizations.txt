Retailer pulls spicy chip off shelves after death, 3 California hospitalizations
-----
The manufacturer of a spicy chip that led to the hospitalization of three Northern California high school students is pulling the chip off the shelves after a teen in Massachusetts died.Austin, Texas, maker Paqui's One Chip Challenge is a blue corn chip flavored with sea salt, Carolina Reaper peppers and Naga Viper peppers, according to the company's website. The Carolina Reaper pepper holds the Guinness World Record for being the hottest chili pepper.
