Mary Hile-Nepfel will give and receive at eighth annual S.F. high school banquet
-----
Mary Hile-Nepfel, a Hall of Famer as a player and a coach at USF, is set to receive two more honors May 13 at the San Francisco Italian Athletic Club.Hile-Nepfel, 64, is scheduled to be given a lifetime achievement high school coaching award and have her name permanently inscribed on another trophy, highlighting the eighth annual San Francisco High School Awards Banquet.
