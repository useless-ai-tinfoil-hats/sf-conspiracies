YIMBYs and preservationists actually agree on a California bill to create more housing
-----
It is the political equivalent of cats and dogs playing nice together. And something rarely seen in California.Two groups often at odds — YIMBYs and historic preservationists — are uniting behind a measure by San Francisco Assembly Member Matt Haney that is designed to make it easier to convert office buildings to residential housing, something that could help to revive San Francisco's fading downtown and others around California.
