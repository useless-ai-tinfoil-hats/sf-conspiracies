S.F. police investigate deadly crash near Central Freeway
-----
San Francisco police are investigating a fatal traffic incident in the South of Market near the Central Freeway following a Thursday morning collision that spanned multiple scenes.Officers responded to Harrison and 13th streets just after 7:30 a.m. and were still actively investigating several related locations by early Thursday afternoon.
