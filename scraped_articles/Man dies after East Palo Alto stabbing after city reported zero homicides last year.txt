Man dies after East Palo Alto stabbing after city reported zero homicides last year
-----
A man who was stabbed last month in East Palo Alto died from injuries he sustained in the attack, police said Monday.Police spokespersons said Frank Finney, who was severely injured in a Feb. 8 stabbing on a major thoroughfare in East Palo Alto, died in the hospital on Saturday.
Police spokespersons said they have not identified a suspect in the stabbing.
