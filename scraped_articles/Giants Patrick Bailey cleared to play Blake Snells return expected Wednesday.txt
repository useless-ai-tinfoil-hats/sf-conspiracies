Giants’ Patrick Bailey cleared to play; Blake Snell’s return expected Wednesday
-----
PITTSBURGH — Patrick Bailey saw the top concussion expert in the country Tuesday morning, and Dr. Mickey Collins of the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center cleared the San Francisco Giants' catcher to play."He's as good as it gets," Bailey said of Collins. "He said, 'Just go out there and play and think about it as little as you can.' "
The Giants still had Bailey go through normal pregame activity before adding him to the roster. He was not in the starting lineup for the game
