New SFO airline now offering ‘ultra-affordable’ flights to Canada
-----
An "ultra-affordable" airline offering round-trip flights from Toronto is launching at San Francisco International Airport next year, the Canadian airline announced Wednesday.Lynx Airline said it is aiming to offer low-cost flights from Toronto Pearson International Airport to cities, such as San Francisco, where airfares have historically been high. The airline plans to start operating at SFO on May 3, 2024.
