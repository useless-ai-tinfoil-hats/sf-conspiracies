From NWSL title to expansion experience, Katelyn Rowland ready for Bay FC
-----
The Rowland residence became the home of a "screaming session" the moment CBS Sports Network announced Katelyn Rowland's name last month during the NWSL expansion draft. The folks there suspected it was coming, but seeing it become real for the goalkeeper of the NC Courage was a relief.After eight years in the NWSL, the Walnut Creek native was headed home to represent the Bay Area's new professional women's soccer team.
