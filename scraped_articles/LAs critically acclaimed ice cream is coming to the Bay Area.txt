LA's critically acclaimed ice cream is coming to the Bay Area
-----
San Francisco has a lot of great ice cream — Swensen's, Mitchell's, Humphry Slocombe, Bi-Rite. But thanks to a Los Angeles-based soft serve company, San Franciscans will be able to get their hands on what Eater LA recently called "LA's food item of the year."But get this — it's served in a pouch.
