Arnold Schwarzenegger detained over luxury watch at German airport
-----
Former California Gov. Arnold Schwarzenegger was detained at a German airport for several hours on Wednesday and will face criminal proceedings for not declaring a luxury watch at customs.The "Terminator" star turned politician was held by authorities at Munich International Airport after they discovered a custom-made Swiss brand Audemars Piguet wristwatch worth approximately $21,000 in his suitcase, according to a report from the German news outlet Bild.
