Two tourists visiting Bay Area killed by lumber sliding off truck
-----
Two tourists biking in the Bay Area died Tuesday when they were struck by a load of lumber on a passing truck, California Highway Patrol officials said.The bikers were on Silverado Trail near Oak Knoll Avenue, a stretch of Napa County that is lined with wineries. Around 11 a.m., they came alongside a truck hauling lumber; the wood was leaning off the truck and it struck both bikers, CHP said.
