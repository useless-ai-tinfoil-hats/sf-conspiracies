The list of grievances the 49ers can cite for their Super Bowl week in Las Vegas just added another item: an early-morning fire alarm going off at their hotel.A guest services representative for the Hilton Lake Las Vegas told SFGATE that an alarm went off at the hotel early Thursday morning. According to the hotel representative, it went off around 6 a.m. and rang for "no more than 10 minutes" in a portion of the hotel.
