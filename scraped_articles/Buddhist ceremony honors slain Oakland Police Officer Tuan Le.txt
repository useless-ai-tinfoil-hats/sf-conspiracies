Buddhist ceremony honors slain Oakland Police Officer Tuan Le
-----
Incense smoke drifted into the air at Oakland's Pacific Renaissance Plaza on Friday as three Buddhist nuns chanted prayers during a traditional Buddhist ceremony Friday honoring Oakland police officer Tuan Le, who was shot and killed in the line of duty a week ago.Several community leaders remembered Le for his smile and ability to build bridges between the Oakland Police Department and residents and business owners of Asian descent.
