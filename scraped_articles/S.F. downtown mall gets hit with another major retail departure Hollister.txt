S.F. downtown mall gets hit with another major retail departure: Hollister
-----
Another national retailer quietly departed from the San Francisco Centre last month.Hollister, a subsidiary of the clothing retail giant Abercrombie & Fitch, exited from the city's largest mall in December, joining the departure of shoe store Adidas in the same month.
The San Francisco store is notably absent from the company's website.
