Mill Valley teacher suspected of child sexual abuse
-----
A Mill Valley teacher was arrested Tuesday on suspicion of sexually abusing a child, the Marin County Sheriff's Office said.Darren Smith of Fairfax, who works for Mill Valley School District, was booked on suspicion of lewd acts with a child and continuous sexual abuse of a child, officials said.
School district officials said in a statement that they informed the sheriff's office about the alleged crimes when they became aware of them, as required by law.
