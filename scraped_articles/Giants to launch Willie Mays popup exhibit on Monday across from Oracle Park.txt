Giants to launch Willie Mays popup exhibit on Monday across from Oracle Park
-----
Talk of opening a Willie Mays museum in San Francisco goes back decades, including one proposed at Pier 48 back in 2003 that involved negotiations with the Board of Supervisors, Port Commission and developers.That didn't reach fruition, but the San Francisco Giants have come up with another Mays-centric idea to honor the Hall of Fame center fielder, a pop-up exhibit across the street from Oracle Park that will open to the public on Monday.
