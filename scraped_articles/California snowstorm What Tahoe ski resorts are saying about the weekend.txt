California snowstorm: What Tahoe ski resorts are saying about the weekend
-----
UPDATE: Avalanche watch issued for Tahoe area, some ski resorts announce closuresSkiers and snowboarders hoping to cash in on the monster blizzard creeping towards Lake Tahoe should probably already be packing to hit the road or driving into the mountains.
Weekend hopefuls should prepare to hole up in their ski leases or hotel rooms for a couple of days, with the expectation they won't get in their first turns at ski areas until potentially Sunday when conditions improve.
