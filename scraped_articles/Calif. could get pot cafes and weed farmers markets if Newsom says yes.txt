Calif. could get pot cafes and weed farmers markets, if Newsom says yes
-----
Californians could soon win the right to smoke weed at some restaurants and buy their pot at cannabis farmers markets. But only if Gov. Gavin Newsom agrees to it.The California Legislature passed two consequential cannabis bills this week. One legalizes cannabis farmers markets, giving pot farmers the right to legally sell pot directly to consumers for the first time ever.
