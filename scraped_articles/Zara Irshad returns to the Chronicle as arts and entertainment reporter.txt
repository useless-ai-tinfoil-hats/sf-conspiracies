Zara Irshad returns to the Chronicle as arts and entertainment reporter
-----
From Senior Editor of Arts and Entertainment Mariecar Mendoza:The Chronicle is excited to announce that Zara Irshad has joined the Datebook team as an arts and entertainment engagement reporter.
Irshad previously served as a summer intern at the Chronicle, covering pop culture and music for Datebook, including reporting on the Outside Lands festival, the record-breaking Taylor Swift Eras Tour stop in Santa Clara and the "Barbenheimer" phenomenon.
