Man electrocuted, dies after climbing into utility vault in Richmond
-----
A man died in Richmond after he climbed into a utility vault Tuesday, according to fire officials.The Contra Costa County Coroner's Office identified Richmond resident Salvador Cachu, 28, as the deceased on Wednesday.
Around 3 a.m., the man climbed into an underground Pacific Gas and Electric Co. vault at South Second Street and Cutting Boulevard, where he was electrocuted by high-voltage power lines, said Michael Smith, interim chief of Richmond Fire Department.
