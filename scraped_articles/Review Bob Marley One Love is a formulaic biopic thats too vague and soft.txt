Review: ‘Bob Marley: One Love’ is a formulaic biopic that’s too vague and soft
-----
Audiences will come out of "Bob Marley: One Love" wanting to know more about the reggae artist, which is a good thing. What's not good is that a lot of what they'll want to know should have been contained within the movie.What was he like? What drove him? Those answers about Marley's personality can be better gleaned from a six-minute interview on YouTube than from all 104 minutes of this biopic.
