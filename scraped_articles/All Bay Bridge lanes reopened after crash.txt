All Bay Bridge lanes reopened after crash
-----
A crash between an SUV and a pickup truck on the lower deck of the Bay Bridge caused the closure of four of the five lanes for more than an hour on Thursday night, creating a traffic backup across the span and into San Francisco.The collision occurred around 9:20 p.m. just west of the Treasure Island off ramp.
