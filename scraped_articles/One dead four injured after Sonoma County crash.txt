One dead, four injured after Sonoma County crash
-----
A multicar crash on Lakeville Highway in Sonoma County killed one person and injured four others Tuesday afternoon, authorities said.The crash happened at 2:20 p.m. on the northbound and southbound lanes of Lakeville Highway, south of Old Lakeville Road No. 3, according to the California Highway Patrol.
A driver in a Toyota Camry was traveling north when he failed to safely decelerate before coming to a stop. It was not immediately known why he failed to stop.
