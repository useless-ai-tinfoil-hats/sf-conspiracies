Bimbo Bakeries fined $162,000 for air quality violations at South San Francisco facility
-----
Bimbo Bakeries USA will pay $162,000 in fines to settle violations of air quality regulations at its South San Francisco facility, the Bay Area Air Quality Management District announced Wednesday.The baked goods manufacturer failed to conduct required annual emissions testing on its facility's boilers for five years, between 2016 and 2019 as well as in 2021 the Air District said.
