Exclusive: Aaron Peskin announces financing plan to create more ‘missing middle’ housing in S.F.
-----
Over the past year, San Francisco has slashed its building fees and cut its affordable housing requirements almost in half. The city has also come up with a rezoning plan that promises to produce thousands of new homes in the coming years.Now, a local legislator is working to create a permanent funding source for a section of the housing market that's long been viewed as extremely challenging to finance: the so-called "missing middle."
