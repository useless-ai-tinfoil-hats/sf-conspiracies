The 4 best burgers at Outside Lands 2024
-----
There's a bit of a burger craze happening at Outside Lands 2024. Walk around the grounds at this year's festival and you'll see attendee after attendee with burger in hand.You'll find everything from thin and lacy new-age smashburgers to classic patties of the thick and juicy persuasion. But that's just the start of it. At the festival, the genre extends far beyond Americana with burgers with Greek feta cheese, Thai fried pork meatball burgers, pita burgers and even burger tacos.
