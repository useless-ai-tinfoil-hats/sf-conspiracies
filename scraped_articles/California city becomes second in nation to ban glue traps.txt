California city becomes second in nation to ban glue traps
-----
Last week, Ojai became the second city in the U.S. to ban the sale and use of glue traps. Intended to capture rodents, the devices consist of "trays coated with a sticky adhesive that ensnare small animals, who can suffer for days before dying, their faces and limbs mired in the glue," according to a recent PETA press release about the ban.Now, animal welfare officials in San Francisco are pushing for a similar ban in the city, along with a nationwide ban on the traps.
