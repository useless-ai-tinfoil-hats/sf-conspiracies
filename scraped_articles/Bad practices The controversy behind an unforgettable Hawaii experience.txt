'Bad practices': The controversy behind an unforgettable Hawaii experience
-----
It's one of Hawaii's unforgettable experiences. In the evening hours off the Kailua-Kona Coast of Hawaii Island, snorkelers by the dozens jump into the dark ocean. They swim toward bright lights illuminating the water to see reef manta rays with wingspans of about 12 feet wide.The lights attract swarms of zooplankton, the manta rays' preferred food.
