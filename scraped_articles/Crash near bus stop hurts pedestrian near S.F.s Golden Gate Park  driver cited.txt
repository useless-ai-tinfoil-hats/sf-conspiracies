Crash near bus stop hurts pedestrian near S.F.’s Golden Gate Park — driver cited
-----
A pedestrian was injured by a vehicle that crashed near a bus stop near San Francisco's Golden Gate Park on Saturday, authorities said.The victim was struck just before 4:50 p.m. near a bus stop at Fulton Street and Park Presidio Boulevard, the San Francisco Police Department said. He was taken to a hospital with non-life-threatening injuries.
The man driving was cited for "multiple moving vehicle violations," police said.
No further details about the crash were released,
