49ers aren’t willing to meet Brandon Aiyuk’s asking price and are poised to pay the cost
-----
The offseason-long contract impasse between the San Francisco 49ers and Brandon Aiyuk has reached this point: The 49ers are poised to pay the on-field price for their unwillingness to meet their All-Pro wide receiver's asking price.On Tuesday, a day after it was reported the 49ers had agreed to the framework of trades involving Aiyuk with the Browns and Patriots, head coach Kyle Shanahan confirmed that Aiyuk has been given permission to negotiate contracts with other teams.
