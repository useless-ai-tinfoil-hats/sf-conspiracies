What you need to know about the Highway 101 lane closures this week
-----
Portions of Highway 101 in a large swath of the Peninsula will be closed this week to allow for maintenance, Caltrans said.Caltrans crews will perform lane reductions on both southbound and northbound directions of Highway 101 from 13th street in San Jose to Shoreline Boulevard in Mountain View from Tuesday to Friday this week.
The closures will take place from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. and will affect the center divide and right-hand shoulder lanes, Caltrans said.
