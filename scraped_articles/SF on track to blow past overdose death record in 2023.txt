SF on track to blow past overdose death record in 2023
-----
San Francisco is on track to record more than 800 accidental drug overdoses in 2023, according to preliminary city data published Wednesday, a staggering number indicating that the drug epidemic is not slowing down.The preliminary count of 752 overdoses for Jan. 1 to Nov. 30 surpasses the past total-year record of 726 in 2020. That year, overdoses surged during the pandemic marked by a stay-at-home order.
