Bay Area Trader Joe's stores place one per customer limit on popular new frozen item
-----
Bay Area Trader Joe's stores are putting a limit on a new frozen item after social media posts made it go viral.The Dutch Griddle Cakes have been hard to come by since they were released last fall after viral videos suggested that the griddle cakes had a strong resemblance to McDonald's McGriddle breakfast sandwiches. Since then, folks have taken to social media to post videos of themselves recreating the breakfast sandwich at home with the coveted Trader Joe's griddle cake.
