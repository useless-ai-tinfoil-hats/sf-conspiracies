Warriors contain Embiid, beat 76ers as defense makes a rare appearance
-----
Draymond Green was effusive Tuesday in his praise of Philadelphia 76ers center Joel Embiid, the supremely skilled 7-footer doubling for the third consecutive season as the NBA's leading scorer."You just try to make it tough on him. You want to try to make the shots that he's getting tough," said Green after guarding him for the better part of his 29 minutes.
"You don't lock a player like Joel Embiid down."
His Golden State Warriors teammates might beg to differ.
