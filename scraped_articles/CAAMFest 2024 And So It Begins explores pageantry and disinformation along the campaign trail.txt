CAAMFest 2024: ‘And So It Begins’ explores pageantry and disinformation along the campaign trail
-----
Donald Trump may have cornered the market on red hats, but when it comes to the phrase "Make America Great Again," the former U.S. president is even less original.The motto was first uttered nearly 60 years ago and an ocean away as "Make the Philippines Great Again," and the man who coined it on his own campaign trail, Ferdinand Marcos, would serve two terms as that country's duly elected president (1965-72). He went on to be known as a tyrant, declaring martial law to extend his rule
