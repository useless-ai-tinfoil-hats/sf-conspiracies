Here’s your last chance to ride BART’s historic ‘legacy’ trains
-----
BART debuted its regional rail service almost 52 years ago with eight futuristic-looking, three-car trains that carried passengers from Oakland to Fremont. On April 20, a Saturday, the rail agency's historic legacy trains will make one final run before retiring from BART's active fleet for good.The legacy trains have been a sight synonymous with the Bay Area for more than five decades.
