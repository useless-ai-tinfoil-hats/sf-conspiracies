Highway 580 reopens as Corral Fire burns near Calif.'s Lawrence Livermore Lab
-----
LATEST June 3, 7:30 a.m. Highway 580 between Highway 5 and Highway 205 was reopened in both directions by Sunday evening, according to California Highway Patrol. The closure was a result of the Corral Fire in San Joaquin County, which began Saturday and was 50% contained Sunday night, according to CalFire.While the highway's westbound direction was opened at 11:30 a.m. Sunday, its eastbound direction had remained closed.
