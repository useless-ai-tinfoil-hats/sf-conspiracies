Defendants in killing of Oakland police officer ordered held without bail
-----
Two men charged with murder in the fatal shooting of an undercover Oakland police officer were ordered Thursday to remain held in custody without bail.The alleged shooter, Mark Sanders, 27, and co-defendant Allen Brown, 28, did not enter pleas during a brief arraignment at Wiley Manuel Courthouse in Oakland. A hearing for them to enter pleas was set for Jan. 18.
Sanders also faces a special allegation that he knew Le was a police officer, among other allegations and charges.
