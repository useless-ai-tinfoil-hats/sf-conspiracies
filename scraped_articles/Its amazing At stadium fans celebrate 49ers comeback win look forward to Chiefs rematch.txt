‘It’s amazing’: At stadium, fans celebrate 49ers’ comeback win, look forward to Chiefs rematch
-----
Fans at Levi's Stadium on Sunday night celebrated the San Francisco 49ers' comeback win over the Detroit Lions and immediately began looking forward to a rematch against the Kansas City Chiefs in the Super Bowl."It's like the old days," said Jeff Athens, 59. "It's always been that way. We've always come back."
Though this win pits the Niners against the Chiefs, who beat the Niners in the 2020 Super Bowl, Athens said he was confident the team could pull off a win this time.
