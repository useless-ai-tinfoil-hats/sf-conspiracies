The couple that lived full-time at Disneyland for nearly 20 years
-----
The housing market in Los Angeles is competitive but nothing compares to where one couple lived full-time from 1955-1971.Animal handlers Owen and Dolly Pope were hired personally by Walt Disney to tend to horses and advise his Imagineers on the use of animals. Disney was thinking about starting an amusement park and the animals had to be just right.
