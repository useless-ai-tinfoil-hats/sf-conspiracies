Hundreds of thousands of peculiar sea creatures wash up on California beaches
-----
Marine biologist Carolyn Belak has a thing for small and unusual sea creatures.So when hundreds of thousands of Velella velella — gelatinous creatures better known as by-the-wind sailors — drifted ashore on Trinidad State Beach over the weekend, Belak decided to play a game. She called it: "Can I find the smallest velella?"
