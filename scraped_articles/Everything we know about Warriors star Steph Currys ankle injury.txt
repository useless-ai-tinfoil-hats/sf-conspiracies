Everything we know about Warriors star Steph Curry's ankle injury
-----
LATEST March 8, 12:40 p.m. Multiple reporters who cover the Golden State Warriors are saying the team won't announce an update on Steph Curry's right ankle injury until Saturday.Both NBC Sports Bay Area's Monte Poole and the San Francisco Chronicle's Sam Gordon posted messages on X, formerly Twitter, about the Saturday update. (The Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms.) Curry was expected to get an MRI on Friday.
