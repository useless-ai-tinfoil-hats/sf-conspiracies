Sheriff: Shooting at California's Palisades Tahoe ski resort, situation now 'stable'
-----
A shooting occurred near Palisades Tahoe ski resort in Olympic Valley, California, on Friday morning, the Placer County Sheriff's Office said.California State Parks responded to the shooting on the resort grounds, said Sgt. Dave Smith, a spokesperson with the sheriff's office.
"The situation is stable now," Smith told SFGATE at 11 a.m. "Just an officer and suspect involved. All officers involved are OK. No information currently on the suspect," he added.
