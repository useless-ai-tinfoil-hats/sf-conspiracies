Mickey Hart announces immersive art exhibition as part of ‘Dead Forever Experience’
-----
Before beginning a 24-show residency with Dead & Company at the Sphere in Las Vegas this summer, longtime Grateful Dead drummer Mickey Hart will roll out a different kind of show: an immersive art exhibition featuring "vibrational expressionism."Hart's "Art at the Edge of Magic" show at the Venetian Hotel will include sensory-enhancing pieces that span a variety of media, such as black light immersion, exclusive videos of him in the studio, and "psychopomps" that will guide guests
