Body found in Muir Woods in 1982 identified as San Francisco man
-----
A body found in Muir Woods in 1982 has been identified over four decades later as a San Francisco man.The skeletal remains of the individual were found in November 1982 near the Deer Park Fire Trail, the Marin County Sheriff's Office said this week in statement.
