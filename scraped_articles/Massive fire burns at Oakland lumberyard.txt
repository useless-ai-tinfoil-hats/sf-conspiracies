Massive fire burns at Oakland lumberyard
-----
More than 80 firefighters battled a massive fire at a lumber warehouse near Interstate 880 in Oakland on Sunday, fire officials said.The four-alarm fire broke out around 8 p.m. at Economy Lumber Company on the 700 block of High Street, said Oakland Fire Department spokesperson Michael Hunt. The warehouse is located a few blocks from the High Street homeless encampment, but it appeared that no one was living in the area of the fire, Hunt said.
