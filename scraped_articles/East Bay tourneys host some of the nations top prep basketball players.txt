East Bay tourneys host some of the nation’s top prep basketball players
-----
Two of Northern California's top basketball showcases — one boys, one girls — take place Saturday at East Bay venues.The ninth annual Crush in the Valley Basketball Showcase, featuring eight boys games including state top 25 teams Salesian-Richmond, Riordan, Monterey Trail-Elk Grove and Centennial-Corona, starts at 10 a.m. at Contra Costa College in San Pablo.
