Bay Area man who reported family missing likely killed them, police say
-----
A Bay Area man who reported his family missing in September is likely responsible for their deaths, San Pablo police said Monday.According to police, Phuc Vo, 40, reported his wife and mother-in-law missing on Sept. 13. When he spoke with detectives, he allegedly said they'd left their home on the 1900 block of Sutter Avenue about a week prior because of an argument.
