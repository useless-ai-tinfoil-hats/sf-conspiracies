First meteor shower of 2024 peaks this week. Will it be visible in the Bay Area?
-----
It's a new year, and the first meteor shower of 2024 is predicted to peak this week.The Quadrantid meteor shower began Dec. 26 and lasts through Jan. 16, with its peak happening late Wednesday night and into the early hours of Thursday morning.
Like the annual Geminids, which graced the night sky last month, the Quadrantids stand out among meteor showers because they are composed of dust and debris from an asteroid rather than ice and dirty debris from a comet.
