Bay Area ‘Red Bull thief’ arrested for allegedly stealing $14,600 in energy drinks, vodka
-----
A man accused of stealing $14,600 worth of Red Bull energy drinks and vodka from a business in Vallejo has been arrested, authorities said.The Vallejo Police Department said Sunday it arrested a man who targeted a store on the 900 block of Admiral Callaghan Lane more than 20 times to steal cases of Red Bull and bottles of New Amsterdam vodka. He had warrants out for his arrest in Solano County for possessing a stolen vehicle and in Napa County for theft.
