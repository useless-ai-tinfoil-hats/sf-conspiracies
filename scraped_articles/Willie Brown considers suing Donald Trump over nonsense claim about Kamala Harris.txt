Willie Brown considers suing Donald Trump over ‘nonsense’ claim about Kamala Harris
-----
Willie Brown is considering legal action against Donald Trump if the former president continues repeating a "nonsense" claim that the ex-San Francisco mayor bad-mouthed Kamala Harris, Brown and his longtime attorney, Joe Cotchett, said Saturday.Despite Brown's denial of the story, Trump maintains that the two were involved in an emergency helicopter landing and that Brown spoke poorly of the vice president, who is competing with Trump for the presidency.
