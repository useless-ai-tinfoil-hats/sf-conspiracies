Review: Del Seymour's miraculous journey from addiction to mayor of the Tenderloin
-----
Del Seymour is one of the hardest-working advocates for homeless people in San Francisco, beloved for leading walking tours of the economically battered Tenderloin and starting a wildly successful jobs program there for the downtrodden. At 77, he has been invited to the White House, rubs shoulders with San Francisco's mayors and tech titans, is nicknamed "Mayor of the Tenderloin" and is regularly consulted for his street wisdom.That wisdom came the hard way.
