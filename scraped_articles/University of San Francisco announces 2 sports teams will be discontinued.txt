University of San Francisco announces 2 sports teams will 'be discontinued'
-----
With less than a month left in their seasons, the University of San Francisco told its men's and women's tennis programs that this season will be the last for each program.USF's athletic director Larry Williams and vice president of business and finance Charlie Cross sent an emailed statement to the school's faculty, staff and students Friday morning, announcing that both Dons tennis programs will "be discontinued" at the end of the 2023-24 season.
