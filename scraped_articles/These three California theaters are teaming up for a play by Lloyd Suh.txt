These three California theaters are teaming up for a play by Lloyd Suh
-----
Normally, when multiple theaters in a region eye the same new script, the largest one gets the production rights, and the conversation is over.Accordingly, next year, TheatreWorks Silicon Valley could have been the sole Northern California company to produce "The Heart Sellers," Lloyd Suh's 1973-set play about two young Asian women who have just immigrated to America.
