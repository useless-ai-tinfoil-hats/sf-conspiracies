David DePape, man accused of attacking Paul Pelosi, found guilty
-----
David DePape, the man accused of breaking into former House Speaker Nancy Pelosi's home and attacking her husband with a hammer, was found guilty on federal charges by a San Francisco jury on Thursday.DePape was found guilty of "attempted kidnapping of a federal official and assault on the immediate family member of a federal official," the United States Attorney's Office for the Northern District of California said. DePape now faces a maximum sentence of life in prison.
