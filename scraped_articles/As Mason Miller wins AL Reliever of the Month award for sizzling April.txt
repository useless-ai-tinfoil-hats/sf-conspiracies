A’s Mason Miller wins AL Reliever of the Month award for sizzling April
-----
The fastball of Oakland Athletics closer Mason Miller routinely reaches 100 mph, and he has converted every one of his eight save opportunities this season. On Friday, he became a clearly deserving winner of the AL Reliever of the Month award for April.The 6-foot-5, 200-pound right-hander ended the month having allowed two earned runs in 13⅓ innings, good for a 1.35 ERA.
