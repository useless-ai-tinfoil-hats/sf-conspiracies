S.F. warns of ‘heavy rain,’ offers free sandbags
-----
UPDATED FORECAST: Bay Area is about to see heaviest rain of week. Here's a forecast timelineAs they brace for downpours in the week prior to Christmas and perhaps beyond, San Francisco officials are encouraging people to get sandbags to protect their homes and businesses.
The mayor and other city officials issued a reminder about the potential for winter storms to cause flooding. The region expects "heavy rain beginning this weekend and next week," they warned on Friday.
