Robin Williams’ daughter takes director’s chair in ‘Lisa Frankenstein’

Growing up the daughter of Robin Williams in San Francisco, Zelda Williams remembers visiting film sets — especially while shooting "Mrs. Doubtfire." She recalls a few celebrities who would come through, such as her Sea Cliff neighbor Sharon Stone and family friend Dr. Oliver Sacks, whom Robin essentially portrayed in the Oscar-nominated film "Awakenings."But outside of her family experiences, what she remembers mostly is the food.
