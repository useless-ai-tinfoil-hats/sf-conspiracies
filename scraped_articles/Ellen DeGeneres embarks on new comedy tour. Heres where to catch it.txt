Ellen DeGeneres embarks on new comedy tour. Here's where to catch it
-----
Ellen DeGeneres is returning to her stand-up roots, and she's letting Bay Area audiences in on the jokes.The former talk show host is scheduled to bring her new comedy show, "Ellen's Last Stand ... Up" to Santa Rosa's Luther Burbank Center for the Arts on June 30, where she previously opened her stand-up tour in 2003.
