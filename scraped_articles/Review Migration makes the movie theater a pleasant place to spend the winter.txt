Review: ‘Migration’ makes the movie theater a pleasant place to spend the winter
-----
"Migration" is a welcome escape for the family.With a vibrant color palette and appealing characters, Illumination's latest animated adventure-comedy has a story that packs plenty of laughs that'll easily win over the young kiddos and keep teens and adults engaged throughout its 91-minute run time.
