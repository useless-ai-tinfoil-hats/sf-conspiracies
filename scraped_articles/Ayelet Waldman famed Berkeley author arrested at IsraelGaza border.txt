Ayelet Waldman, famed Berkeley author, arrested at Israel-Gaza border
-----
Ayelet Waldman, a famed Berkeley author, was arrested Friday at the Erez Crossing — the border between Israel and Gaza — while protesting the war with a group of American rabbis.Waldman's husband, novelist Michael Chabon, announced her arrest on social media.
Chabon, an award-winning novelist and author, posted a video of Waldman being detained by Israeli police officers as the protesting group, Rabbis for Cease Fire, approached the border.
