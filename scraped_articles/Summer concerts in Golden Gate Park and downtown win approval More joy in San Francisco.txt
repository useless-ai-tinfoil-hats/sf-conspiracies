Summer concerts in Golden Gate Park and downtown win approval: ‘More joy in San Francisco’
-----
A new annual concert series proposed by the producer of Outside Lands that would bring live entertainment to Golden Gate Park and downtown San Francisco got the green light despite pushback from residents in nearby neighborhoods who worried about traffic, congestion and park closures.In a 10-1 vote Tuesday, the Board of Supervisors approved an agreement between the city and Another Planet Entertainment for three years of concerts beginning in 2024.
