SF's Union Square Macy's store is among those slated to close
-----
Macy's Inc. confirmed late Tuesday morning that its flagship store fronting San Francisco's Union Square is among dozens of locations it's considering closing. A closure would mark yet another retail loss in the struggling shopping district, which has seen a steady stream of stores call it quits ever since the COVID-19 pandemic.
