‘Tragic loss’: Sonoma County firefighter dies after suffering medical emergency
-----
A Sonoma County firefighter suffered a medical emergency and died unexpectedly Friday.Rancho Adobe Fire District Capt. Kyle Heard, 43, was about to head to work from his home in Santa Rosa when he suffered the medical emergency, Battalion Chief Herb Wandel said Sunday. Few other details about the captain's death were immediately available.
