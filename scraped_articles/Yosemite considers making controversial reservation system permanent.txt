Yosemite considers making controversial reservation system permanent
-----
Officials at Yosemite National Park said Wednesday that they were seeking input from the public as they consider changes to better regulate access to the park, including making its controversial reservation system permanent.Park officials said in a statement that they would prefer to "implement a parkwide daily reservation system during peak hours," but invited members of the public to weigh in on other potential alternatives.
