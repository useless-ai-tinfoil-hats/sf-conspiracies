22-mile ‘walking prayer’ in East Bay draws hundreds calling for Gaza cease-fire
-----
Hundreds of people, including members of dozens of faith communities, walked 22 miles from Berkeley to Alameda on Saturday to advocate for a cease-fire in Gaza.The 22-mile journey was intended to mirror the distance that those fleeing Gaza must travel to reach the Rafah crossing, where more than half of Gaza's population has taken refuge, according to the Associated Press.
