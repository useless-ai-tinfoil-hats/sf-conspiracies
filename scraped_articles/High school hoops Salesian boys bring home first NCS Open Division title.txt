High school hoops: Salesian boys bring home first NCS Open Division title
-----
After 26 seasons, more than 600 wins and two state championships, Salesian-Richmond boys basketball coach Bill Mellis rarely is seen celebrating overtly.But before the final buzzer had sounded Friday night, the 54-year-old, with arms raised, made a beeline on the Contra Costa College hardwood to embrace his Pride after a 52-43 victory over De La Salle-Concord for the North Coast Section Open Division championship. It was Salesian's 11th NCS crown and first at the Open level.
