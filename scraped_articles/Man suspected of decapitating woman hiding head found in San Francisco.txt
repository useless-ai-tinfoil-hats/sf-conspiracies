Man suspected of decapitating woman, hiding head found in San Francisco
-----
A Bay Area man suspected of killing a woman and hiding her head has been found in San Francisco, officials said. On Thursday afternoon, police in Santa Rosa responded to the 2500 block of Pomo Trail for a report of a possible homicide. Inside a home, police discovered the decapitated body of a woman; it appeared she had been stabbed to death, and her head was missing.A search was announced for Luis Gustavo Aroyo-Lopez, 24, who police believe killed the woman.
