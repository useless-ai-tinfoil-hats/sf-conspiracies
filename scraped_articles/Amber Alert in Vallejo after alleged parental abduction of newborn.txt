Amber Alert in Vallejo after alleged parental abduction of newborn
-----
An Amber Alert was issued Saturday morning for a newborn baby in Vallejo after what police believe was a parental abduction by his mother.The California Highway Patrol reported the incident at 111 Amador St. shortly after 3 a.m.
The 2-week-old boy, Braxton Manning, is described as 22 inches long, 7 pounds, with brown hair and brown eyes. Braxton's 28-year-old mother, Valerie Manning, is white, 5 feet 7 inches, 175 pounds, with brown hair and eyes.
