Child saved after pregnant mother dies in South Bay car crash
-----
Doctors were able to save the child of a pregnant woman who died during a car crash Tuesday night, according to San Jose police.The woman was driving outside the Westfield Oakridge shopping mall around 8:40 p.m. when she got into a collision in the intersection of Blossom Hill Road and Winfield Boulevard, according to the Police Department.
She suffered life-threatening injuries and was taken to a local hospital, where she died, officials said. Doctors performed emergency surgery
