Bay Area law student found dismembered on shoreline
-----
A young Bay Area law student was found dismembered on the Alameda shoreline, and police say the man she lived with is responsible for her death.The woman's remains were found on July 20 inside a bag left near Bay Farm Island Bridge, Alameda police said in a statement Thursday. Whoever put her there had removed her head, feet and hands, making identification difficult, according to charging documents reviewed by KTVU.
