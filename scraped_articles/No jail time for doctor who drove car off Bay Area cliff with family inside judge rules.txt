No jail time for doctor who drove car off Bay Area cliff with family inside, judge rules
-----
A Southern California doctor who was charged with attempted murder for driving off a Bay Area cliff with his wife and children in the car is eligible for a mental health diversion program rather than jail time, a San Mateo Superior Court judge ruled Thursday.If he completes the program, criminal charges against him will be dropped.
