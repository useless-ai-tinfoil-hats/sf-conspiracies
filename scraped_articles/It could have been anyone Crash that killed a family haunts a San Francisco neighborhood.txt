‘It could have been anyone’: Crash that killed a family haunts a San Francisco neighborhood
-----
Photographs of the San Francisco family killed in the West Portal crash fluttered in the wind outside West Portal Branch Library on Sunday, where an impromptu memorial overtook a patch of grass beside the Muni stop where the young family was struck as they waited for a bus to the zoo.A steady stream of mourners stopped to observe the memorial Sunday afternoon, taking in the array of stuffed animals, grief-stricken notes and flower bouquets.
