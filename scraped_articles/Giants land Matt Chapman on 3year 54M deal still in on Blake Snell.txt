Giants land Matt Chapman on 3-year, $54M deal, still in on Blake Snell
-----
The San Francisco Giants' pursuit of Matt Chapman ended midway through spring training with a deal to sign the third baseman to a three-year, $54 million contract, sources confirmed to the Chronicle.The contract includes opt outs after the 2024 and 2025 seasons, with Chapman set to earn $20 million this season, $18 million in 2025 and $16 million in 2026.
