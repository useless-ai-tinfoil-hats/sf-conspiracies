Burning Man expects to begin Exodus Monday morning, organizers say
-----
LATEST Sept. 3, 9:50 p.m. Burning Man organizers announced that people attending will be able to start leaving Black Rock City on Monday morning, and that they would inform festival-goers by 9 a.m. of their decision.Organizers asked that as Exodus begins, people still avoid Gate Road, which remained unusable due to the mud.
"We have created alternative routes that if used on Monday you will be flagged to the best area to drive across," the statement said.
