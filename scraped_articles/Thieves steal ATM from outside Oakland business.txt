Thieves steal ATM from outside Oakland business
-----
Thieves stole an ATM from outside a business in Oakland on Sunday morning, police said.A 911 caller told police that a group was trying to take the ATM on Edgewater Drive near Hegenberger Road about 6:15 a.m. When officers arrived, they found damage at the business, police said.
The investigation revealed that the thieves had arrived in several vehicles and used tools to remove the ATM, police said. Police did not provide further details.
