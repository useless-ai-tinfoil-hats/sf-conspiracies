S.F. Vietnamese food institution closes
-----
One of San Francisco's most beloved and longtime Vietnamese restaurants has closed.Turtle Tower's last day at 645 Larkin St. was Sunday, Sept. 17, owner Steven Nghia Pham confirmed. Since opening in the Little Saigon strip of the Tenderloin in 2000, Turtle Tower has become a Bay Area destination for Northern-style pho, particularly the fragrant pho ga, or chicken noodle soup.
