Stanford’s Tara VanDerveer speaks out on NCAA 3-point-arc failure: ‘Inexcusable’
-----
Days after Stanford lost to N.C. State in the Sweet 16, head coach Tara VanDerveer criticized the disparate 3-point arcs on the Moda Center floor in Portland, Ore., that were not corrected before the Cardinal played.On Monday, the NCAA apologized for the error, and confirmed the 3-point arc on one side of the court was short by 9 inches.
