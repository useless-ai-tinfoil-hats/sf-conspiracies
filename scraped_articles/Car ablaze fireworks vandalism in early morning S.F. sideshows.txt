Car ablaze, fireworks, vandalism in early morning S.F. sideshows
-----
Back-to-back sideshows took over two intersections in San Francisco early Sunday, police said.A fire engulfed a car at the first sideshow, which was reported around 2:10 a.m. in the area of the Embarcadero and Washington Street near the Ferry Building, according to the San Francisco police and fire departments and video posted to social media.
Multiple vehicles were "engaged in stunt driving," with spectators near the intersection, police said. Videos show more than 100 spectators
