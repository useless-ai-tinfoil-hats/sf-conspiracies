Review: In Alonzo King’s best works, Lines Ballet offers a moving prayer for peace
-----
If you seek space for contemplation in these times of painful world conflict, go to Alonzo King Lines Ballet's fall home season at the Yerba Buena Center for the Arts this weekend.Not a single line in the program or sentence spoken from the stage at the Thursday, Oct. 12, opening addressed the horrifying escalation of conflict in the Holy Land, yet the performance was relevant.
