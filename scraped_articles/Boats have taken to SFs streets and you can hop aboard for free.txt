Boats have taken to SF's streets, and you can hop aboard for free
-----
Move over, cable cars. There's a hot new streetcar in town. But it's actually pretty old. And it's not exactly a streetcar, either. A fan favorite, the boat tram hit the streets of San Francisco mid-July as a part of Muni's Summer Heritage Service, which features a range of classic streetcars operating along the Embarcadero.Boat 233, one of three "boats" in Muni's roster, is free to ride, offering passengers a unique view of the city from its open-air, green-and-cream design.
