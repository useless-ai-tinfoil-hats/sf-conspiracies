Couple killed in Half Moon Bay plane crash were two weeks from wedding, family said
-----
The family of an Oakland resident identified their daughter and her fiance as two of the four victims who died in a plane crash off Half Moon Bay last week.In an obituary published online, family members said engaged couple Cassidy Rae Petit, 26, and Lochie Ferrier died on Jan. 14 when their plane crashed into the waters off Half Moon Bay.
