Giants’ Logan Webb goes the distance, shuts out A’s on five singles
-----
Any question that Logan Webb would be back out for the ninth inning Wednesday night was answered before he ascended the home dugout steps.Closer Camilo Doval wasn't warming, and neither were any of his bullpen mates despite a precarious one-run lead. The base sounds of "Killa Whale" by Andre Nickatina, Webb's warm-up song, blared from the Oracle Park speakers as a prelude.
