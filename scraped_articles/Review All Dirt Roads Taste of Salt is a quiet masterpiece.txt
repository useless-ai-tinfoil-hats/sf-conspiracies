Review: ‘All Dirt Roads Taste of Salt’ is a quiet masterpiece
-----
"All Dirt Roads Taste of Salt" is a cinematic miracle, a spare, meditative film about the decades-long life of a Black woman and her family in rural Mississippi.But to say it is "about" just that is misleading. These are impressions of a life, conveyed by visual poetry, made with confidence by a first time filmmaker intent on expanding the possibilities of narrative filmmaking.
