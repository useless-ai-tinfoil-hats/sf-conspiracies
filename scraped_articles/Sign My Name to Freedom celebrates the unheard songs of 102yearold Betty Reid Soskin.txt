‘Sign My Name to Freedom’ celebrates the unheard songs of 102-year-old Betty Reid Soskin
-----
Betty Reid Soskin's life has had so many distinct chapters — flood survivor, political activist, mother, folk singer, racial integrator, entrepreneur and, eventually, park ranger — it's hard to fathom that at the age of 102 she's embracing one more endeavor.And yet the longtime Richmond resident, who became an unlikely celebrity in the early 2000s as the oldest ranger in the National Park Service, is now the subject of a new musical, "Sign My Name to Freedom."
