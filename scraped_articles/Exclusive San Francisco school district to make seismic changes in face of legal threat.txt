Exclusive: San Francisco school district to make seismic changes in face of legal threat
-----
A Bay Area attorney has tossed a legal grenade at San Francisco school officials, demanding they make the seismic shift from citywide board elections to smaller district races by November or face a massive and expensive legal battle that could result in paying millions of dollars in damages.Based on legal advice and the likelihood of losing, the seven-member school board is expected to approve the change to district elections, the Chronicle learned Tuesday.
