The feds want to slaughter 470,000 barred owls on the West Coast. Is the plan ethical?
-----
In the dead of night, hunters armed with 12-gauge shotguns hit the woods of Northern California in search of barred owls. Pre-recorded bird calls lure the large predators from the treetops. As shots ring out, owls plummet from the sky.From 2013 to 2019, 2,485 barred owls were killed like this on the West Coast. This wasn't poaching. The hunters had the blessing of the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, which greenlit efforts to eradicate the invasive owls.
