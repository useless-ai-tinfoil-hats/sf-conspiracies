More than a half-dozen small businesses go from ‘pop-up’ to permanent in downtown S.F.
-----
In the years leading up to the pandemic, many small businesses felt the squeeze in downtown San Francisco — partly due to high rents and because they often weren't the first choice for some of the area's most coveted spaces.Today, with nearly one-third of downtown's offices vacant and an exodus of big-name retailers from the area in recent years, the situation is drastically different.
