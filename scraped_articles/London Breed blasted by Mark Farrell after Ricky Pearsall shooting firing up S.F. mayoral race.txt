London Breed blasted by Mark Farrell after Ricky Pearsall shooting, firing up S.F. mayoral race
-----
Mayoral candidate Mark Farrell blasted Mayor London Breed in the aftermath of the shooting of San Francisco 49ers rookie wide receiver Ricky Pearsall in Union Square on Saturday, thrusting the incident to the forefront of an already heated mayoral race in which public safety is a major concern for voters."Enough is enough," Farrell tweeted. "If we want public safety in San Francisco, then we need change in City Hall."
