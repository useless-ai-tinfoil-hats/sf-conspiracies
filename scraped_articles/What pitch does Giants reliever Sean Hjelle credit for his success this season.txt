What pitch does Giants reliever Sean Hjelle credit for his success this season?
-----
Sean Hjelle is carrying himself with a different kind of confidence this season.With long levers and impressive extension, the San Francisco Giants' 6-foot-11 right-handed reliever has a naturally imposing presence on the mound. But a new pitch has made Hjelle into a trusted arm in high-leverage situations who can eat multiple innings, too.
That pitch: A cutter that Hjelle throws to left-handed hitters with plenty of success.
