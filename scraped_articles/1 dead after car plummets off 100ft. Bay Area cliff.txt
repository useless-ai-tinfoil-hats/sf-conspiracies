1 dead after car plummets off 100-ft. Bay Area cliff
-----
One person was killed and two others injured when a vehicle they were riding in plummeted off a 100-foot cliff at Pescadero State Beach Thursday evening, according to officials with the California Highway Patrol.The driver, identified as 22-year-old Madeline St. Pierre, was arrested on suspicion of driving under the influence and will be detained following medical clearance, California Highway Patrol Officer Sophie Lu said.
