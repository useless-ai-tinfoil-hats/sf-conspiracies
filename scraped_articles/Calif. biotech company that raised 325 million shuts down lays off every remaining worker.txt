Calif. biotech company that raised $325 million shuts down, lays off every remaining worker
-----
EFFECTOR Therapeutics, a biotech company based in California that raised hundreds of millions of dollars for cancer research, is shutting down.The 12-year-old company laid off its remaining employees and is asking to be delisted from the NASDAQ stock exchange as it wraps up operations, according to a Monday press release and filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission. On Friday, eFFECTOR's CEO, CFO and chief medical officer each left the company, as did a slew of board members.
