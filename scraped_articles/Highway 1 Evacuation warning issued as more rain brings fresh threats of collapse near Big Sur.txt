Highway 1: Evacuation warning issued as more rain brings fresh threats of collapse near Big Sur
-----
Caltrans-led convoys across a cratered portion of Highway 1 near Big Sur in Monterey County will temporarily halt Thursday and Friday as the region prepares for another round of rainfall that authorities fear could cause more slip-outs on the iconic coastal road.More thunderstorms were expected to batter the coast beginning Thursday afternoon, with heavy rain predicted on Friday from Santa Cruz to Big Sur, according to the National Weather Service.
