Calif. pot recalls surge following SFGATE investigation
-----
California state regulators are warning the public that over 20 cannabis products are potentially unsafe for human consumption. The surge in recalls by the Department of Cannabis Control, which has issued warnings regarding 24 different pot products in the past six months, is a marked change for an agency that had issued only four recalls in the entirety of 2023.The products have been flagged for a variety of issues, including mold, pesticides and inaccurate potency labels.
