ASPCA Pet Health Insurance review 2024
-----
ASPCA Pet Insurance at a glanceASPCA Pet Health Insurance offers an impressive range of coverage limits to fit your pet and budget. It's also one of the only pet insurers that'll cover horses. However, the waiting period for accidents is longer, and you have to call the company directly to access the full menu of coverage options.
* Standard waiting period: 14 days for accidents and illnesses
* Deductible range: $100. $250, or $500
