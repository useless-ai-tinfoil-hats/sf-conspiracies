Pavement’s ‘Harness Your Hopes,’ saved from the dumpster, goes gold
-----
Pavement has earned its first-ever gold certification from the music industry for a once obscure track released a quarter-century ago."Harness Your Hopes," a B-side from a 1999 EP, became the only song by the influential indie-rock band from Stockton to sell more than 500,000 copies, according to the Recording Industry Association of America.
