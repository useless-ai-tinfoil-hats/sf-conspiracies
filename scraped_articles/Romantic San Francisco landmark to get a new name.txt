Romantic San Francisco landmark to get a new name
-----
One of the most picturesque, romantic and possibly haunted landmarks in San Francisco's Golden Gate Park is close to adopting a new name after 130 years. Stow Lake's boathouse, blue herons and island hike have long been a bucolic getaway for San Franciscans, but the lake's name has come under scrutiny in recent years due to the antisemitic beliefs of its namesake, William Stow.The 19th century California Assemblymember was outspokenly antisemitic throughout his political career.
