Tyla plays brief Outside Lands set after starting half hour late
-----
Highly anticipated South African artist Tyla was more than 30 minutes late to her scheduled Outside Lands performance as fans gathered for her 6:05 p.m set at the Sutro Stage.The Grammy-winning artist, who broke into the Billboard Hot 100 last year with the Afrobeats-infused single "Water," performed for less than 20 minutes after starting at 6:37 p.m. on Friday, Aug. 9.
