‘It’s a sleeper’: This East Bay downtown is poised for a comeback with new housing, restaurants
-----
Downtown Hayward, long a scruffy step-sibling to its more affluent and bustling East Bay neighbors, is starting to stir.Already, more than 500 residents have moved in to Lincoln Landing, a 474-unit apartment complex that opened late last year on the site of the old Mervyn's headquarters, which finally closed in 2008 after years of downsizing.
