14 spots along Bay Area freeways did not pass fire inspections
-----
Fourteen state-leased storage spaces near freeways in the San Francisco Bay Area failed safety inspections by California state fire marshals, according to records from Caltrans.The inspections came after an alleged arson fire was started in November under Los Angeles' Interstate 10 freeway in a storage area leased to a private company. A milelong segment of the freeway in downtown LA, one of the state's busiest corridors, was closed for more than a week.
