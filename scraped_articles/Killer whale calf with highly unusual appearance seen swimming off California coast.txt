Killer whale calf with highly unusual appearance seen swimming off California coast
-----
An unusual white killer whale calf was seen Sunday in Monterey Bay swimming with its mother and another pod of orcas, one of just a handful of appearances since it was first sighted there as a baby in 2019.A videographer took overhead footage of the white 4-year-old, nicknamed Frosty, swimming parallel to five other killer whales, which were later seen hunting sea lions.
