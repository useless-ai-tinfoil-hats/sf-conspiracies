Clint Eastwood's former Carmel mansion hits the market for $21 million
-----
A century-old estate in Carmel-by-the-Sea once owned by legendary movie star and former town mayor Clint Eastwood has hit the market for the first time in 28 years.The Spanish revival mansion, tucked away on San Antonio and 9th avenues, a block from the beach, has been listed for $21 million.
