Oil spills into California Delta as decades-old cruise ship springs leak
-----
Officials have contained leaking fuel from a ship in the Sacramento-San Joaquin River Delta that began sinking late Wednesday morning.The ship, a nearly 70-year-old cruise vessel named the Aurora, "suffered a hole" and began taking on water, the San Joaquin County Sheriff's Office said on Facebook. It sank 13 feet into the water and spilled an unspecified amount of pollution into the Little Potato Slough area north of Stockton.
