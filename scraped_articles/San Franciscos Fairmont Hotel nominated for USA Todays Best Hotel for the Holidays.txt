San Francisco’s Fairmont Hotel nominated for USA Today's ‘Best Hotel for the Holidays’
-----
A San Francisco legend is in the middle of a competition to secure bragging rights as the best hotel for the holidays.The Fairmont San Francisco on Nob Hill, where President Biden is staying during the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation conference this week, is currently No. 3 on the leaderboard in a competition organized by USA Today. The only other California hotel nominated is the Tenaya Lodge in Yosemite National Park. The Roosevelt New Orleans in Louisiana is at the top of the list
