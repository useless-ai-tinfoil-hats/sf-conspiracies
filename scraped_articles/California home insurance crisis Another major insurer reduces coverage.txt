California home insurance crisis: Another major insurer reduces coverage
-----
Another home insurance provider will reduce future coverage in California, as other companies have done in previous months in response to costly wildfires.United Services Automobile Association Group (USAA) outlined restrictions for new California property insurance policies that will take effect next March in a filing to the California Department of Insurance.
