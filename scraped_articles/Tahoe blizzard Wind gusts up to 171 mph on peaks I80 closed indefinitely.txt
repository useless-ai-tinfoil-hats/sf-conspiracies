Tahoe blizzard: Wind gusts up to 171 mph on peaks, I-80 closed indefinitely
-----
A blizzard blasting the Tahoe region brought wind gusts of up to an astounding 171 miles per hour on ski resort peaks.Interstate 80 closed through the mountains late Friday afternoon.
By 5 p.m. Friday, I-80 westbound traffic was being held indefinitely at the Nevada state line, and eastbound at Drum Forebay, with Caltrans citing spinouts, high winds, and poor visibility. Further south, I-395 was closed from approximately Mammoth Lakes to Lee Vining in Mono County.
