Bay Area tech layoffs: GoPro to slash 15% of workforce in cost-cutting move
-----
GoPro, the popular camera maker, announced plans to cut 15% of its workforce by the end of the year.The San Mateo company said Monday that the reductions, beginning in the third quarter, are part of an ongoing strategy to reduce operating expenses. Approximately 139 positions will be affected.
