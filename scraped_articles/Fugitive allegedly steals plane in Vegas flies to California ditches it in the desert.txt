Fugitive allegedly steals plane in Vegas, flies to California, ditches it in the desert
-----
A 40-year-old-man has been arrested after allegedly stealing a plane from an airport in Las Vegas, flying it to California, ditching it near Barstow and fleeing through the desert on foot. While authorities have not yet revealed the suspect's motive, SFGATE learned the man has a history of criminal charges, including felony stalking, and is a wanted fugitive in Ada County, Idaho.
