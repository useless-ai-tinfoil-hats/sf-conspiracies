Disneyland employee taken to hospital after chemical accident backstage
-----
An accidental chemical spill led to two Disneyland employees being treated for injuries.On Dec. 28, shortly after 2 a.m., Anaheim Fire and Rescue responded to a call about a possible chemical spill, a spokesperson for Anaheim Fire and Rescue told SFGATE in a statement.
Two Disneyland employees had trouble breathing after the spill caused a "vapor cloud of chlorine and another cleaning agent," according to the Orange County Register.
