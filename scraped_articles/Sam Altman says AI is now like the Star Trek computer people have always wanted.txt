Sam Altman says AI is now like the ‘Star Trek’ computer people have always wanted
-----
Sam Altman, CEO of OpenAI, said the past year has been a turning point for artificial intelligence, in part because of his San Francisco company's ChatGPT chatbot."Something has qualitatively changed," Altman said at the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation CEO Summit on Thursday. "Now I can talk to this thing. It's like the 'Star Trek' computer I was always promised. … So I think a lot of the world has collectively gone through a lurch this year to catch up."
