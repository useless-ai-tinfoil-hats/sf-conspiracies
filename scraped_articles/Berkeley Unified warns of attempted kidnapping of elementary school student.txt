Berkeley Unified warns of attempted kidnapping of elementary school student
-----
Berkeley police are investigating an attempted kidnapping, according to a safety alert released by the Berkeley Unified School District on Monday.According to the alert, a suspect in a white van or large SUV approached an elementary school student from Ruth Acty Elementary School as the student walked home on Rose Street after school. The suspect asked the student if they wanted to get in the vehicle. The student refused and ran home unharmed.
