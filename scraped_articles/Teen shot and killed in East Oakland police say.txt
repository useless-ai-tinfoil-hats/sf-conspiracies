Teen shot and killed in East Oakland, police say
-----
A teenager was fatally shot in East Oakland on Sunday night, authorities said.The boy was shot just after 9 p.m. on the 9600 block of Edes Avenue, according to the Oakland Police Department. He was taken to a hospital, where he died from his injuries, police officials said.
The boy's identity was withheld pending notification to next of kin, police officials said. No further details were released.
