Man who punched protester in S.F. during APEC charged with assault
-----
A man who punched a woman protesting outside the APEC security zone has been charged with assault, according to the San Francisco District Attorney's Office.The district attorney's office charged Michael Davies, 40, with assault with force likely to cause bodily injury, alleging that Davies did inflict great bodily injury on another person. Fellow activists identified the woman Davies punched as Sarah White and said she suffered a broken jaw and bleeding near the brain.
