Burning Man releases ‘Wet Playa Survival Guide’ amid disastrous conditions at Black Rock City
-----
Burning Man officials released a "2023 Wet Playa Survival Guide" Saturday night for as many as 70,000 festivalgoers who remain trapped in the sopping wet Black Rock Desert with no way out until further notice.The resource will be updated regularly and aims to inform people on how to stay safe and prepared as uncertain weather conditions loom, officials said. They are already planting mobile cell towers and booting up WiFi for Black Rock City's 3 o'clock and 9 o'clock plazas and center
