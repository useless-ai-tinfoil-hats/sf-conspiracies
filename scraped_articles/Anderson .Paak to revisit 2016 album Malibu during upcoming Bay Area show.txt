Anderson .Paak to revisit 2016 album ‘Malibu’ during upcoming Bay Area show
-----
After joining Skrillex and Fred.. Again onstage at their Civic Center Plaza concert earlier this month, Anderson .Paak is circling back to the Bay Area for a solo show.The Grammy-winning artist is scheduled to perform at Mountain View's Shoreline Amphitheatre on Sept. 21 as part of his recently announced "Malibu" tour, which celebrates his sophomore album of the same name. He is slated to be joined by his band, the Free Nationals, jazz trumpeter Maurice Brown and R&B duo GAWD.
