Former Bay Area prep star Drew Gordon, brother of Aaron, dies at 33
-----
Former NBA forward Drew Gordon, a Mitty-San Jose High School alum who played at UCLA and New Mexico, died Thursday in a car accident in Portland, Ore. He was 33.Gordon was the older brother of Denver Nuggets forward Aaron Gordon. Elise Gordon, sister of Drew and Aaron, also played at Mitty and later played at Harvard.
The Nuggets confirmed Drew Gordon's death in a post on X on Thursday night.
