Fluffy Korean egg sandwiches are luring crowds to this new Bay Area restaurant
-----
A new East Bay restaurant is specializing in one of South Korea's most popular street eats.Tucked into the corner of an outdoor mall in Dublin, Egg Happiness is now serving Korean egg toast, a thick piece of puffy bread that's gently toasted and stuffed with mayo-topped scrambled eggs.
The restaurant, which opened at the end of August, has been unexpectedly busy, according to owner Sonia Yim.
