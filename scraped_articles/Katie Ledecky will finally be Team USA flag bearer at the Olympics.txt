Katie Ledecky will finally be Team USA flag bearer at the Olympics
-----
For the first time in her illustrious Olympics career, Katie Ledecky is slated to help Team USA as a flag bearer at the closing ceremony for the 2024 Games.The record-breaking swimmer was in tears when Bobby Finke — her swimming teammate at both the Olympics and the University of Florida, her current training home — delivered the news over FaceTime on Thursday morning.
