Meet the ultra-wealthy pouring cash into San Francisco's elections
-----
In San Francisco politics, perhaps more than anywhere else in the nation, money talks.The city, a global hub for technology and finance, is the de facto capital of the Bay Area, a region that boasts several of the wealthiest ZIP codes in the U.S. Some of the world's richest people live here, and those with an interest in politics often use their fortunes to wield outsized influence in the elections and policies that shape the city they call home.
