Review: Kaveh Akbar’s darkly comic ‘Martyr!’ is an electrifying portrait of a young man’s search for meaning
-----
In Kaveh Akbar's deep-feeling, beautifully bruised debut novel, progatonist Cyrus Shams recounts late in the book an old Muslim fable about Satan encountering Adam for the first time.
