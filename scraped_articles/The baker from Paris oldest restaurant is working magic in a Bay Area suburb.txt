The baker from Paris' oldest restaurant is working magic in a Bay Area suburb
-----
Before the spiral, cube and unfortunate flat croissant, there was the cronut and the cruffin. Now, a new hybrid croissant craze has hit the Bay Area — and this one is full of possibilities.The crookie — a cookie-stuffed croissant — has been popping up at local French bakeries since March, including Le Marais in San Francisco and Mill Valley. The cross-bred viennoiserie was invented in Paris in 2022 by Stephane Louvard, now of Boulangerie Louvard, and has been going viral on social media
