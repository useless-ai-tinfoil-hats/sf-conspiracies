1,200 people evacuated from park as largest wildfire in California explodes
-----
A major wildfire burning in the hills along Interstate 5, north of Los Angeles, is the largest blaze in California and is expected to continue to spread Monday with gusty, hot weather in the forecast.The Post Fire that's spread across Ventura and Los Angeles counties ignited Saturday in Gorman near Gorman Road; it spread wildly on Sunday, growing to over 10,000 acres and forcing the evacuation of 1,200 campers, off-roaders and hikers from areas around Hungry Valley park and Pyramid Lake,
