A new Latin music festival is heading to the Bay Area
-----
A month after Napa Valley's inaugural Festival La Onda, another Latin music festival is carving out space in Northern California.Latin Rock on the Dock music festival is scheduled to land on Mare Island on July 27. Produced by Bernardo Gonzalez III and Annie Rodriguez of Latin Rock Inc. and Frazier Trager Presents' co-founders Kevin Frazier and Jeff Trager, the festival aims to showcase a range of established and budding talent within the Latin rock music world.
