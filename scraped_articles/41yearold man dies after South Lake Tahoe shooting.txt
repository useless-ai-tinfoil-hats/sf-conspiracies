41-year-old man dies after South Lake Tahoe shooting
-----
The man who was hit by gunfire on July 16 in a South Lake Tahoe shooting died from his injuries, officials said. Dennis Crowe, 41, was a former resident of South Lake Tahoe and had "ties to the area," police said in a Tuesday news release.Crowe was shot on the 2000 block of Eloise Avenue on the morning of July 16, according to the South Lake Tahoe Police Department.
