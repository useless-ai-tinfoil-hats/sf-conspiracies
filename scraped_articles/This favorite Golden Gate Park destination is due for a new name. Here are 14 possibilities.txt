This favorite Golden Gate Park destination is due for a new name. Here are 14 possibilities
-----
Stow Lake, an idyllic spot in the middle of Golden Gate Park, is a popular spot for human-powered boating, picnicking and walking. But it's named for a 19th century politician known for his antisemitic statements and is due to be renamed.After a series of public hearings conducted by Supervisor Myrna Melgar, whose district includes the lake, 14 potential new names have been suggested.
