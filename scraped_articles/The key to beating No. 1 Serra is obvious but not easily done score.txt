The key to beating No. 1 Serra is obvious but not easily done: score
-----
In the simplest terms, the only way to knock Serra off its West Catholic Athletic League football perch is to score touchdowns.So say WCAL coaches heading into the opening week of Northern California's most storied and perhaps strongest league.
The Padres dominated the league like never had been done before in 2022, going 7-0 while outscoring foes 237-42. Every WCAL opponent except Valley Christian — which lost 36-0 — scored one touchdown against them.
