Oakland's only In-N-Out to permanently close
-----
Oakland's only In-N-Out will serve its last double-double on March 24 after more than 18 years in operation at 8300 Oakport Street, the burger restaurant's chief operating officer Denny Warnick confirmed with SFGATE in an emailed statement Sunday.Warnick described the store as "busy and profitable" for the company, and drive-thru lines during the dinner rush often stretched all the way out into neighboring parking lots.
