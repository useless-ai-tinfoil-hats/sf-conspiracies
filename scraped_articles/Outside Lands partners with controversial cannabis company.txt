Outside Lands partners with controversial cannabis company
-----
Outside Lands is taking its love of cannabis to a new high this year, releasing a limited edition line of cannabis edibles, vape cartridges and pre-rolled joints that are decorated with the festival's logo. Outside Lands partnered with the pot brand Stiiizy on the products, which are slated to be sold at the festival's cannabis marketplace inside Golden Gate Park.Stiiizy is one of the largest pot brands in California.
