SF mayoral candidate admits hitting a man over the head with a chair in documentary
-----
A candidate who's running to become the next mayor of San Francisco admits to hitting a local bartender over the head with a chair in a new documentary by Bay Area blogger Vincent Woo.The 15-minute video, "Honest to a Fault: The Menace Who Would be Mayor," focuses on little-known mayoral candidate Honest Charley Bodkin, a young software engineer who once worked at the Los Angeles Times. The documentary was released Tuesday online.
