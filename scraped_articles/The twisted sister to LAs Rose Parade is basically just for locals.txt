The ‘twisted sister’ to LA’s Rose Parade is basically just for locals
-----
A tan-colored '90s limousine holds three dancers on its roof as it rolls slowly down a sunny street. The view is blocked, on and off, by the following scenes: a man on a unicycle juggling pinballs; a red-haired pirate swallowing swords; a person in a KISS-themed rhinestone cowboy outfit singing out-of-tune rock ballads, backed by a live band being pulled on a flatbed truck.Welcome to the Doo Dah Parade, a quirky California tradition that has no equal.
