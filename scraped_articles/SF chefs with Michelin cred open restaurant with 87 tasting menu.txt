SF chefs with Michelin cred open restaurant with $87 tasting menu
-----
Months after a rocky departure from Marlena restaurant, chefs David Fisher and Serena Chow Fisher are ready for new beginnings at 7 Adams.The chefs debuted their new restaurant on Wednesday at 1963 Sutter St. in a newly remodeled space that formerly housed Gardenias restaurant. The couple is excited to unveil their new 52-seat restaurant near Japantown with an $87 prix fixe menu featuring fresh handmade pasta and Serena's signature milk bread.
