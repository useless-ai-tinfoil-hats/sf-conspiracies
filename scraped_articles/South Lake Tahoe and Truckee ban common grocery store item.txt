South Lake Tahoe and Truckee ban common grocery store item
-----
Two Tahoe towns are saying no to plastic water bottles.South Lake Tahoe's ban on single-use plastic water bottles is slated to go into full effect next month, soon after neighboring Truckee passed an ordinance to implement a similar ban.
