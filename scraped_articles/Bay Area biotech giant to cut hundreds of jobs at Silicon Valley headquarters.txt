Bay Area biotech giant to cut hundreds of jobs at Silicon Valley headquarters
-----
Bay Area molecular diagnostics giant Cepheid has announced plans to eliminate 626 positions at its Sunnyvale headquarters by Sept. 27, according to regulatory filings.The cuts will affect approximately 13% of the company's 4,800-strong workforce.
