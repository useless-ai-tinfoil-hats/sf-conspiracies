5-year-old dies, 11-year-old injured in Bay Area DUI crash, CHP says
-----
A driver was arrested on suspicion of manslaughter and driving under the influence in Martinez after a solo-vehicle crash that left a 5-year-old dead and an 11-year-old with life-threatening injuries, the California Highway Patrol said.The adult driver was in a grey Honda CR-V on westbound State Route 4 near Morello Avenue in the East Bay city just after midnight Monday. The driver lost control of the vehicle, veering off the roadway and hitting a tree, CHP said in a statement.
