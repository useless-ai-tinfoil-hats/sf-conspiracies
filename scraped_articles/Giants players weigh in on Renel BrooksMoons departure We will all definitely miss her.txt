Giants’ players weigh in on Renel Brooks-Moon’s departure: ‘We will all definitely miss her’
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — Renel Brooks-Moon announced Mike Yastrzemski's name when he stepped to the plate in the big leagues for the first time as an outfielder for the San Francisco Giants in 2019. She befriended LaMonte Wade Jr. and his parents when he joined the team in 2021.Players, like Giants fans, felt as if they had a strong personal connection to Brooks-Moon, and the players were just as sad as the fans to learn Monday that Brooks-Moon will not be behind the public-address microphone
