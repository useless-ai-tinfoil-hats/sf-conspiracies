CVS reverses course on its plan to close an SF store
-----
LATEST Dec. 12, 3:30 p.m. CVS Health has reversed course on its decision to close one of its stores in San Francisco."We've re-evaluated our upcoming store closure plans and have decided NOT to close the CVS Pharmacy at 499 Haight Street in San Francisco in January as previously disclosed," Carla Saporta Cheng, CVS senior director of state government affairs, wrote in an email to San Francisco Supervisor Dean Preston, according to a press release.
