K-pop superstar IU to stop at Oakland for ‘H.E.R.’ world tour
-----
K-pop superstar IU's first U.S. tour will include a stop in Oakland.The singer-songwriter and actress, whose hit songs have hundreds of millions of listens, will perform at Oakland Arena on July 30, according to a tour announcement Tuesday. The tour is called "H.E.R."
IU, whose real name is Lee Ji-eun, debuted in 2008 and has amassed a string of hits including 31 No. 1 singles in South Korea, the most of any artist, including "Eight," "Blueming" and "Good Day."
