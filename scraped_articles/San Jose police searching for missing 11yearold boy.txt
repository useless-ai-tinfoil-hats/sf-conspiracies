San Jose police searching for missing 11-year-old boy
-----
San Jose police said Wednesday morning that they were seeking the public's help in locating an 11-year-old boy missing since Tuesday afternoon.Manuel "Angel" Gonzalez was last seen around 4 p.m. Tuesday in the area of San Clara and First streets in San Jose, exiting a VTA bus.
Manuel is 4 feet 11 inches tall and weights 105 lbs, police said. He has brown eyes and short, buzzed black hair that he wore in a ponytail.
