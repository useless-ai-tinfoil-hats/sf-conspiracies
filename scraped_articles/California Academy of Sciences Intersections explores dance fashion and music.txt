California Academy of Science’s ‘Intersections’ explores dance, fashion and music
-----
A newly announced series at the California Academy of Sciences aims to explore cross-cultural connections and celebrate a diverse range of identities through dance, fashion and music."NightLife Intersections represents a big step toward the goal of inclusive programming," said Lin Kung, who began as NightLife's program manager in 2020. "As a woman of color, I don't see as many spaces where underrepresented communities intersect and have a space to reflect on their shared experiences
