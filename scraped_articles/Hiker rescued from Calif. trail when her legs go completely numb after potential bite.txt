Hiker rescued from Calif. trail when her legs go completely numb after potential bite
-----
A hiker was rescued last week from a rugged and dangerous trail in California's Sierra Nevada after claiming she was bitten and her legs became numb to a point where she could no longer feel them, officials said.The rescue happened on Wednesday night, after the hiker was unable to walk down Taboose Pass in the central Sierra Nevada near Big Pine, the Inyo County Search and Rescue team said in a Sunday social media post.
