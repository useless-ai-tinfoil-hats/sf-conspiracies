Esquire names 3 San Francisco martinis among the best in America
-----
In Esquire's recent list of best martinis in America, three distinct versions of the savory cocktail from San Francisco made the cut.In December 2023, the men's magazine sent four beverage writers out to taste test the best of the best of this venerable cocktail. From San Francisco, the writers chose the martinis at Aphotic, The Progress and Martuni's.
