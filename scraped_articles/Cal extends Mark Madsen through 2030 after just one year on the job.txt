Cal extends Mark Madsen through 2030 after just one year on the job
-----
Mark Madsen hasn't even finished his first season as Cal's men's basketball head coach, but that's apparently enough for the Golden Bears.The Bears announced a two-year extension for Madsen on Tuesday, even though he is only in the first year of his initial five-year deal. It means the East Bay native is now under contract through the 2029-30 season. Cal did not immediately release any other contractual details.
