Man, 45, and six minors accused of using stolen Kias in Bay Area robberies
-----
A 45-year-old Bay Area man and six minors were arrested last week on suspicion of burglarizing 26 businesses, police said.The burglaries, which occurred from March to August of this year, were all investigated by the San Jose Police Department. Many of the targeted businesses were smoke shops, but other businesses were also broken into, including some outside of San Jose, SJPD said in a Tuesday news release.
