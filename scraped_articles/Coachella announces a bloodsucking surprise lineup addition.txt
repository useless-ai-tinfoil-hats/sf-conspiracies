Coachella announces a blood-sucking surprise lineup addition
-----
Another year, another mid-aughts favorite announced as a surprise Coachella addition.Vampire Weekend is headed to Indio, California, where the band is slated to play the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival's first weekend with a performance at the Outdoor Theatre at 5 p.m.
The news broke Tuesday afternoon via simultaneous social media posts from the festival and the band.
In 2023, Coachella organizers announced Blink-182's surprise set, the band's first live performance
