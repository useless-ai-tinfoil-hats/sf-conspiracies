Among a cluster of state parks and open space preserves in the south Bay Area, Castle Rock truly rises above them all.The California state park was established in 1968 and added another 200 acres to its footprint last year. Located outside Los Gatos near a number of other gorgeous sites dotted along the Santa Cruz Mountains — Big Basin Redwoods, Portola Redwoods, Año Nuevo and Butano state parks — Castle Rock manages to stand out from the pack.
