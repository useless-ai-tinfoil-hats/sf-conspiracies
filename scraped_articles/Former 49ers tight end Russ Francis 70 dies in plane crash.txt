Former 49ers tight end Russ Francis, 70, dies in plane crash
-----
Russ Francis, a colorful Pro Bowl tight end who spent six seasons with the San Francisco 49ers and was part of their 1984 Super Bowl team, died at age 70 on Sunday in a plane crash in Lake Placid, N.Y.The 49ers confirmed Francis' death, which was first reported by the Lake Placid News. Francis and Richard McSpadden, a former commander and flight leader of the U.S. Air Force Thunderbirds demonstration team, reportedly died when their plane crashed at the end of a runway at the Lake Placid
