‘Do we have to close?’ Square outage devastates Bay Area restaurants
-----
On Thursday afternoon, in the middle of busy lunch service at Gumbo Social in San Francisco, the restaurant's payment system suddenly went dark.During what would become an hours-long, nationwide outage of payment system Square, Gumbo Social had to turn away about 50 customers. The potential lost revenue, owner Dontaye Ball estimated, was about $1,000 — a major hit on what's usually the second-busiest day of the week for the Bayview restaurant.
