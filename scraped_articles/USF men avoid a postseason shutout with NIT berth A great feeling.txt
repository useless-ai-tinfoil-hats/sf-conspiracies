USF men avoid a postseason shutout with NIT berth: 'A great feeling’
-----
Tension on Selection Sunday isn't limited to the bubble teams that do or don't get into the NCAA Tournament. There's also tension for teams that expect — but aren't assured — to receive a bid to the NIT.Count USF head coach Chris Gerlufsen and the Dons (23-10) among the folks who experienced some NIT anxiety Sunday. Early in the evening, the Dons got the welcome news: They're in the field of 32, and will play at Cincinnati (20-14), a No. 2 seed, on Wednesday night.
