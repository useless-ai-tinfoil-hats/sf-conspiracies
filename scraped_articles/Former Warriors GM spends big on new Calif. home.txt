Former Warriors GM spends big on new Calif. home
-----
Former Warriors general manager Bob Myers made a big California property purchase last month, though it's far from his longtime Bay Area home.Myers and his wife Kristen purchased a seven-bedroom, 11-bathroom mansion in Santa Monica, as first reported by the Real Deal. The home spans more than 10,000 square feet and includes an additional 5,000-square-foot "entertainment pavilion," which features a professional theater, game room, office and gym.
