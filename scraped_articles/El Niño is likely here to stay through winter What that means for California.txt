El Niño is likely here to stay through winter: What that means for California
-----
El Niño — a weather pattern that can cause impacts around the world — developed in summer and is expected to persist through winter, long-term forecasters said Thursday.In its latest monthly forecast, the federal Climate Prediction Center said there's a 95% chance El Niño will continue through winter, January to March, and it will most likely be strong, as opposed to weak or moderate.
