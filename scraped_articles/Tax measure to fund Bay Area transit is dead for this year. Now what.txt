Tax measure to fund Bay Area transit is dead for this year. Now what?
-----
Bay Area lawmakers abandoned a plan to place a tax measure to fund BART, Muni and other Bay Area transit agencies on the 2026 ballot, vowing to try again next year.State Sen. Scott Wiener, D-San Francisco, and state Sen. Aisha Wahab, D-Fremont, said in a statement Friday that state officials need more time to work out differences in Senate Bill 1031 and plan to reintroduce similar legislation in 2025.
