One person injured in downtown Oakland shooting
-----
One person was injured in a shooting in downtown Oakland early Saturday, according to police.Just before 2:15 a.m., Oakland police responded to reports of a shooting in the 1100 block of Franklin Street, outside the University of California Office of the President. There, officers located a victim who had been shot at least once, according to officials.
The victim was taken to a local hospital and was in stable condition Saturday afternoon.
