S.F. marchers join global rally to commemorate woman who sparked a revolution in Iran
-----
Thousands of people marched in San Francisco streets on Saturday, chanting "say her name," to commemorate an Iranian woman whose death last year ignited uprisings in Iran and an international protest movement.Similar rallies took place across the globe one year after Mahsa Amini, 22, was arrested for allegedly violating Iran's hijab law and died in police custody in Tehran.
