Police arrest man accused of stealing motorhome in Palo Alto while owner slept inside
-----
East Palo Alto police arrested a man suspected of carjacking a motorhome last week.Police received a call from a man on Oct. 20 who said he was asleep in the motorhome while it was parked on the 400 block of Matadero Avenue in Palo Alto when the sound of the engine starting awakened him.
He found a man he did not recognize in the driver's seat, police said. After a brief struggle, the motorhome owner fell out of the vehicle as the alleged thief drove away.
