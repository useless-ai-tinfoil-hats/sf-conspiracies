‘It’s really painful’: Maggie Steffens copes with sister-in-law’s death with team’s support
-----
PARIS — Maggie Steffens' team won a water-polo match over Italy on Wednesday afternoon, and afterward, she got a little time with her husband and family members. She was able to connect and hug and cry, and try to keep working through the shock of the death of her 26-year-old sister-in-law, Lulu Conner.And then Steffens went back to the Olympic Village, and into the embrace of her other family: her teammates, who are there to support her.
