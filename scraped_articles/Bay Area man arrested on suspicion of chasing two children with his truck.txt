Bay Area man arrested on suspicion of chasing two children with his truck
-----
A Windsor man has been arrested after allegedly chasing two juveniles in his truck and stealing their scooter.Windsor police received a report of two children fishing at a private community pond in Lakewood Hills, a gated community in Windsor, at about 1:15 p.m. Friday, according to the Sonoma County Sheriff's Office, which staffs the police department. Before police arrived, Windsor resident Manfred Ortmann confronted the two children from his vehicle, saying that they were trespassing
