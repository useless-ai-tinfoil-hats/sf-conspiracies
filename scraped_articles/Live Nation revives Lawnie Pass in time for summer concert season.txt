Live Nation revives Lawnie Pass in time for summer concert season
-----
Summer concert season just got a lot cheaper.Live Nation announced the return of its Lawnie Pass, which allows for lawn access to concerts at participating venues.
A presale for previous pass holders is on now, with general pass sales available at 10 a.m. Wednesday, Feb. 7. Each pass costs $239 and will grant access to unlimited Live Nation shows through Dec. 31.
