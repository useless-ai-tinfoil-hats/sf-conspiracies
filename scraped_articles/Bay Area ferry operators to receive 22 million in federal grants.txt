Bay Area ferry operators to receive $22 million in federal grants
-----
The Bay Area's ferry transit operators are receiving a combined $22 million in federal grants, the Federal Transit Administration announced Thursday in San Francisco.The federal agency, which distributes competitive capital funds to transit agencies across the country, awarded a $15.9 million grant to the San Francisco Bay Ferry that will aid its transition to an all-electric fleet.
