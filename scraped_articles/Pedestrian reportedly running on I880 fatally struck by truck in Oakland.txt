Pedestrian reportedly running on I-880 fatally struck by truck in Oakland
-----
A woman running "erratically" on Interstate 880 in Oakland was fatally struck by a vehicle Tuesday night, according to media reports.The California Highway Patrol responded to the collision on northbound lanes near the Oakland Coliseum around 9:30 p.m., KRON4 reported. The woman was hit by a pickup truck, and the driver remained at the scene and cooperated with officers, NBC reported.
Three lanes of traffic were reportedly shut down while officers investigated the collision.
