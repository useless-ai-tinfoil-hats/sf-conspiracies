BART and Muni are crowded again. Is Bay Area transit ridership finally back?
-----
You can see it and feel it while riding Muni's 49 buses or during weekday morning trips on BART's Blue Line trains to San Francisco.Lots of people seem to be taking transit again.
The standing-room-only, shoulder-to-shoulder crowds seen riding the region's two largest transit operators in recent months contrast with the ghostly sights of near-empty train cars and buses during the height of the pandemic.
