Goregous video shows Yosemite's Half Dome as snowstorm clears
-----
A breathtakingly beautiful timelapse video from the nonprofit Yosemite Conservancy shows clouds rushing past snow-capped Half Dome as this week's snowstorm cleared.The video, which condenses two hours of footage into one minute, appears to come from the conservancy's High Sierra webcam on Sentinel Dome.
