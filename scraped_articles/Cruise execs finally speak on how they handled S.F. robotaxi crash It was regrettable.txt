Cruise execs finally speak on how they handled S.F. robotaxi crash: ‘It was regrettable’
-----
Cruise executives spoke publicly for the first time about the Oct. 2 crash that sidelined its robotaxis in San Francisco and said they regret how they responded to state regulators, who alleged a cover-up attempt.The remarks came during a Tuesday afternoon hearing at the Van Ness Avenue headquarters of the California Public Utilities Commission, a state regulator of driverless ride-hailing services that allowed for Cruise's unlimited expansion in the city just six months prior.
