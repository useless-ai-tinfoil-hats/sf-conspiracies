Man acquitted of assaulting ex-S.F. commissioner accused of attacking person with a guitar
-----
A homeless man who was found not guilty last month of assaulting an ex-S.F. fire commissioner was arrested on suspicion of assault with a deadly weapon Monday, jail records show.Garret Doty, 25, was booked into San Francisco County Jail at 2:22 a.m.
San Francisco police said Tuesday that they arrested Doty after another person reported they were assaulted by a man wielding a guitar. The victim sustained non-life-threatening injuries and declined treatment, police said.
