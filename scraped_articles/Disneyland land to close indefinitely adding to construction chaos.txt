Disneyland land to close indefinitely, adding to construction chaos
-----
Another major closure is hitting a part of Disneyland that's been overtaken by construction this year.On Monday, Disney announced that Critter Country will temporarily shutter on May 1. The closure makes way for a complete redo of the Briar Patch, a Splash Mountain-themed gift shop that needs a renovation due to the ride's closure.
