Sideshow stops Bay Bridge traffic: 'It's improper and illegal'
-----
A sideshow involving more than 100 vehicles brought westbound Interstate 80 traffic on the San Francisco-Oakland Bay Bridge to a full stop for more than 15 minutes early Sunday morning, officials said.The California Highway Patrol said it received a report of the sideshow on I-80 westbound, just east of Treasure Island, at 3:17 a.m.
A video posted on X (formerly Twitter) by KTVU-TV crime reporter Henry Lee showed cars doing donuts with people hanging their bodies out of the car
