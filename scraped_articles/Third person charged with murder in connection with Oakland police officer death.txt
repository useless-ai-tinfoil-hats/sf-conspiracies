Third person charged with murder in connection with Oakland police officer death
-----
A third person was charged in connection with the killing of Tuan Le, an Oakland Police officer who was shot on Dec. 29.Marquise Cooper, 34, faces one murder charge and two charges of commercial burglary, according to Alameda County jail records. Cooper was arrested on Friday and booked into Santa Rita Jail without bail. He is scheduled to be arraigned on Tuesday at 9 a.m.
