Two men fatally shot in East Oakland
-----
Two men were fatally shot Friday afternoon in East Oakland, according to the Oakland Police Department.Officers responded to a shooting on the 1300 block of 99th Avenue just before 1 p.m. Friday and found one of the victims, a San Francisco man, police said. Paramedics pronounced him dead at the scene.
Officers later found a San Leandro man with apparent gunshot wounds at hospital, where he was pronounced dead.
Both victims' identities are being withheld pending notification
