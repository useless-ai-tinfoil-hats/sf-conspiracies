Pedestrian struck near San Jose shopping center dies from injuries
-----
A woman hit by a car last week outside a San Jose shopping center has died from her injuries, police said Wednesday.The incident occurred around 6 p.m. on Dec. 18, near a shopping area on the 6200 block of Santa Teresa Boulevard. San Jose police said the driver of a 2013 black Honda sedan was heading north out of a parking lot when he struck the woman, who was walking east on a sidewalk.
