Oakland man charged with murder in fatal San Leandro barbershop shooting
-----
An Oakland man has been arrested and charged with murder in the fatal shooting of a San Leandro barber in October, according to law enforcement and news reports.On Oct. 25, longtime barber Jonathan Clay, 47, died after being shot in Harrison Brothers barbershop in San Leandro, according to San Leandro police. Alexis Garcia, 26, was arrested by California Highway Patrol two days after the shooting after being spotted sleeping in a Maserati stopped in a traffic lane, according to CHP.
