The 10 best tacos in Los Angeles
-----
Personally, I don't subscribe to the longstanding adage: you eat tacos in Los Angeles and burritos in the Bay Area. It speaks to an ongoing feud between the two regions that I think is outdated. Plus, a recent influx of regional taquerias and new-school taqueros have made the Bay Area a special destination for tacos.The main difference between the regions' taco game is age. It might require some searching here for a prime version of a specific style, whereas L.A.'s taco culture is mature
