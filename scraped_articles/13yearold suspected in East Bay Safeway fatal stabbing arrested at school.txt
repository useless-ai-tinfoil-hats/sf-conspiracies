13-year-old suspected in East Bay Safeway fatal stabbing arrested at school
-----
A 13-year-old was arrested last week after police said he was suspected in a fatal stabbing that occurred during a fight in a Safeway in San Leandro last month.Police said Monday that surveillance footage helped them identify the teen as the suspect. The teen, whose name was not released, was taken into custody at his school Thursday, police said.
When police served a search warrant at his home, they found evidence linking the teen to the homicide, police said.
