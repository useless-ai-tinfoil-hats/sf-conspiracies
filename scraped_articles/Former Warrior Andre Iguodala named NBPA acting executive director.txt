Former Warrior Andre Iguodala named NBPA acting executive director
-----
Former Golden State Warriors forward Andre Iguodala has been named the acting executive director of the National Basketball Players Association.The four-time NBA champion will replace Tamika Tremaglio, who is stepping down to pursue other opportunities, on a temporary basis. He will be a candidate for the permanent position once the search begins.
