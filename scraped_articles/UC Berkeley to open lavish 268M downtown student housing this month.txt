UC Berkeley to open lavish $268M downtown student housing this month
-----
A lavish $268 million UC Berkeley residence hall is set to open this month, making it the first university-owned housing complex in Downtown Berkeley.Anchor House, located across from the university at 1950 Oxford Street, will open on August 21 and is specifically designed for transfer students. The 14-story, 360,000 square-foot building can accommodate 772 residents. The units include studios and shared apartments two to four single bedrooms, all with kitchens and washers and dryers.
