'There is no excuse': Hawaii bill wants visitors requiring rescue to pay up
-----
Kauai draws visitors to its shores for its beautiful and rugged nature, but it's also a Hawaiian island where many people are rescued on land and in the ocean. Some of these rescues are caused unintentionally. Others, though, are due to a blatant disregard of warning and "no trespassing" signs.The Kauai County Council hopes that the new Bill 2910 will further deter people from acting irresponsibly.
