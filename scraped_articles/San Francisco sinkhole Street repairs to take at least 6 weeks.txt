San Francisco sinkhole: Street repairs to take at least 6 weeks
-----
San Francisco officials on Wednesday said repairs to the large sinkhole that opened at Green and Fillmore streets could take about six weeks."The construction team is working on a schedule for repairs," officials with the San Francisco Water Power Sewer said in an update on its website. "We will provide a more specific timeline when one is available."
A 74-year-old water main burst on Monday morning, opening a sinkhole and sending water onto the street and inundating nearby
