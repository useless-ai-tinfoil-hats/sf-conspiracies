Apple used billions of dollars and thousands of engineers on a ‘spectacular failure,’ WSJ reports
-----
For years, Apple has built its iPhone technology to be lighter, faster and — importantly to the nation's most valuable company — designed in-house. But according to a Wednesday article in the Wall Street Journal, the firm just saw a massive engineering project come up utterly short.Cupertino-based Apple has spent billions of dollars and hired thousands of engineers to build its own wireless carrier connection chip, or modem chip, for its flagship iPhone products, the Journal reported.
