SJSU football close to naming Ken Niumatalolo as head coach
-----
San Jose State is close to naming former Navy head coach Ken Niumatalolo as its next head football coach, the Chronicle confirmed.An announcement of Niumatalolo's hiring was expected to happen this weekend.
He would replace Brent Brennan, who spent the past seven seasons leading the SJSU program before taking the head-coaching job at Arizona on Tuesday.
Niumatalolo spent 15-plus seasons (2007-22) with the Midshipmen, compiling a 109-83 record, including 6-5 in bowl games.
