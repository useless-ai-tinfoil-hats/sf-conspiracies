49ers add punch to pass rush by agreeing to terms with Leonard Floyd
-----
The San Francisco 49ers hope they finally found an elite partner to pair with All-Pro edge rusher Nick Bosa.On Monday, the 49ers agreed to terms with pass rusher Leonard Floyd, 31, who had 10.5 sacks last season with the Bills and has a strong connection with newly hired assistant head coach Brandon Staley. Floyd signed a two-year, $20 million deal that includes $12 million guaranteed in 2024, a league source said.
