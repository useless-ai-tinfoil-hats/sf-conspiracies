California man swept out to sea after jumping from rocks on Maui
-----
A California man went missing on Monday off Kapalua on Maui, Hawaii, after jumping from rocks into the ocean at a popular diving spot known as "Cliffhouse." The 42-year-old man was reported to have been washed out to sea in rough ocean conditions.The man went missing amid a cold front that moved across the Islands on Monday, bringing rain and dangerous surf, with some shores expecting waves as tall as 25 feet.
