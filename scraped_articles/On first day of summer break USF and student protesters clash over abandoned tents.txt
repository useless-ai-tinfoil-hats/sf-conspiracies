On first day of summer break, USF and student protesters clash over ‘abandoned’ tents
-----
University of San Francisco officials began dismantling a student encampment to the surprise of student organizers Monday morning, who told the Chronicle they had planned to pack up their belongings themselves.USF staff members began packing up tents and items scattered across Welch Field, where a student encampment protesting the war in Gaza had been stationed over the past month, at around 10 a.m., students said.
