San Francisco dive bar known for kitsch closes after 25 years
-----
Beauty Bar, a kitschy Mission District destination where customers could enjoy martinis while getting manicures, has closed its doors after a 25-year run.First opened in 1998 on a rough stretch of Mission Street at the intersection of 19th, the San Francisco Beauty Bar was part of a national chain with outposts in cities like New York and Los Angeles.
