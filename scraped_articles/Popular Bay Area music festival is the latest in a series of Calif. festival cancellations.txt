Popular Bay Area music festival is the latest in a series of Calif. festival cancellations
-----
An annual hip-hop festival and celebration of Oakland slated to take place the first weekend of September has been postponed, the festival announced via Instagram.Oakland-based hip-hop collective Hieroglyphics has put on the free, one-day concert called Hiero Day annually since 2012. This year's event, which was supposed to take place at Frank Ogawa Plaza, will be postponed until 2025, according to the July 23 post.
