A’s Chris Caray adds fourth generation to family’s broadcasting legacy
-----
Between his lengthy broadcasting careers in St. Louis and Chicago, Hall of Famer Harry Caray sneaked in a year with the Oakland Athletics in 1970. More than a half-century later, Caray's grandson and great grandson were together at the Coliseum calling Monday night's Cardinals-A's game."Harry would be astounded," said Cardinals broadcaster Chip Caray, 59, who called the game two booths from his son, new A's announcer Chris Caray, 24.
