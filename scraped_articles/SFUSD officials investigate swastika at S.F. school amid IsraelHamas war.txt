SFUSD officials investigate swastika at S.F. school amid Israel-Hamas war
-----
San Francisco school officials were investigating what appeared to be a swastika on the wall of a stairwell at George Washington High School on Friday, an antisemitic image that heightened ongoing fear and division across the Bay Area over the war between Israel and Hamas."SFUSD takes this matter very seriously," officials said in a statement. "The school immediately removed the graffiti, and is investigating to determine who is responsible."
