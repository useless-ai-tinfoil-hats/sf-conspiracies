A’s add another former Giants pitcher, lefty reliever Scott Alexander
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — The Oakland Athletics are loading up on former San Francisco Giants pitchers, adding left-handed reliever Scott Alexander on a big-league contract Wednesday.Alexander, who is from Santa Rosa, joins ex-Giants starters Alex Wood, who signed with the A's earlier this month, and Ross Stripling, acquired in a trade last week.
