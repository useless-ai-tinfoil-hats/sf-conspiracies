Latest map of California's Park Fire shows a monstrous wildfire
-----
At over 350,000 acres, the Park Fire is the largest wildfire in California so far this year. The blaze has been exploding in size since it ignited Wednesday in Chico's Bidwell Park and grew an astonishing 70,000 acres Friday night into Saturday morning.
