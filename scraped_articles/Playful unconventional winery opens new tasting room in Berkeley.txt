Playful, unconventional winery opens new tasting room in Berkeley
-----
A new tasting room is opening in west Berkeley, as one independent winemaker takes over the space formerly occupied by another.Tessier, a 15-year-old natural wine label that's always operated out of other people's facilities, is the new occupant of 1335 Fourth St. It will open to the public for tastings on April 5, in time for the monthly first Friday event held by a group of neighboring wineries.
