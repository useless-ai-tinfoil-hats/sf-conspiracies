Genentech layoffs continue with 93 Bay Area job cuts
-----
Genentech is set to lay off 93 employees at one of its South San Francisco production facilities this fall, according to a state filing.The layoffs will begin in the second week of October and continue through December. The job cuts will span various roles, including senior scientists, software engineers, project managers and data analysts.
