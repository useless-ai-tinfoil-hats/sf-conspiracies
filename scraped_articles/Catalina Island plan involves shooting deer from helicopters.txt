Catalina Island plan involves shooting deer from helicopters
-----
Santa Catalina Island officials have devised a unique plan to manage an invasive species.The Catalina Island Conservancy, the nonprofit in charge of protecting the island, plans to kill the nearly 2,000 mule deer using helicopter sharpshooters, according to the Los Angeles Times. The mule deer will be shot with leadless bullets so as not to poison any scavengers who find the carcasses.
