South Bay pedestrian killed in hit-and-run after being struck by 2 drivers
-----
A South Bay pedestrian was killed early Sunday after being struck by two separate drivers, authorities said.Officers arrested Hok Gwan Jong, 66, on suspicion of felony hit-and-run when the San Mateo resident allegedly fled after driving over the pedestrian, who had already been struck by a car, Sgt. Javier Acosta of the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office said Sunday afternoon.
