SNL takes aim at Warriors' Draymond Green over choking suspension
-----
"Saturday Night Live" took aim at Warriors star Draymond Green this week for choking Rudy Gobert during a game.On Tuesday, Green grabbed the Timberwolves center and put him in a chokehold, resulting in a five-game suspension and condemnation around the league, even from his own coach. "Draymond has to find a way to not cross the line — I'm not talking about an ejection or a technical — I'm talking about a physical act of violence," Steve Kerr said. "That's inexcusable."
