Pitching gem takes Granada past De La Salle again to advance to NorCal D1 title game
-----
Engulfed by neighborhood kids and teammates around the infield grass on Thursday, Stanford-bound Parker Warner reflected on his youth in Livermore and wanting to be a Granada High School baseball player."Big dreams," he said, "but never quite like this."
Better still was imagining beating De La Salle-Concord, which he did for the second time in less than a week, a 5-0 gem that vaulted the Matadors (31-1) into the CIF Northern California Division 1 title game Saturday at home
