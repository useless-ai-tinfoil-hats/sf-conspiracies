Corral Fire near California's Lawrence Livermore Lab burns 12,500 acres
-----
Northern California's first major wildfire of the season has burned 12,500 acres in San Joaquin County. The Corral Fire, which began Saturday afternoon along Corral Hollow Road near the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory Site 300, is 15% contained as of Sunday morning.
