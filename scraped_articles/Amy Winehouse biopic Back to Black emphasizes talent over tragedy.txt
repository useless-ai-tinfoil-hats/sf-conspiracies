Amy Winehouse biopic ‘Back to Black’ emphasizes talent over tragedy
-----
Amid our recent collective regret over the callous media coverage of Britney Spears, Lindsay Lohan and Paris Hilton comes "Back to Black," a new biopic about Amy Winehouse, the young star subjected to perhaps the cruelest tabloid treatment of the 2000s.The powerhouse singer was found dead at her home in London from alcohol poisoning at age 27 in 2011 after a yearslong battle with substance abuse that had been breathlessly, relentlessly recorded by the British press.
