S.F. corruption scandal: Former building inspections worker pleads guilty to taking bribes
-----
A former plan checker from the Department of Building Inspection pleaded guilty Friday to accepting bribes from a local developer to rubber-stamp building designs.Cyril Yu, 41, faced one count of conspiracy to commit honest services wire fraud, with penalties of up to 20 years in prison and a fine of as much as $250,000.
