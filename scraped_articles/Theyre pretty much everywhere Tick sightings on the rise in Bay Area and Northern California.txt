'They're pretty much everywhere': Tick sightings on the rise in Bay Area and Northern California
-----
Tick season has arrived, and California agencies are warning that sightings could be more rampant than usual in the Bay Area and across the state thanks to the deluge of late spring rains."Ticks love the wet weather," Linda Giampa, executive director of the Bay Area Lyme Foundation, told SFGATE Tuesday. "And that's why this year is going to be a big year."
