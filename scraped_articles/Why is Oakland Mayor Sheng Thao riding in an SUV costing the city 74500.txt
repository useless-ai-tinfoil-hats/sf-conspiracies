Why is Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao riding in an SUV costing the city $74,500?
-----
Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao has come under scrutiny for having the city buy a high-end Ford Expedition SUV for her official use until the end of her tenure, at a cost of nearly $75,000. The mayor also receives a $650 monthly allowance for a personal car.The Oaklandside reported on the cost of the Expedition this past week in a story highlighting how Oakland has failed to pay a local dealership for the large sport utility vehicle even though Thao has been using it for more than a year.
