Why San Francisco's Market Street is torn up
-----
If you've been to downtown San Francisco in the past week, you may have noticed several sections of Market Street are torn up between Fifth and Eighth streets.What's going on? In a nutshell, the city is making some infrastructure updates that are tied to the larger Better Market Street plan designed to improve mobility on one of San Francisco's main thoroughfares.
