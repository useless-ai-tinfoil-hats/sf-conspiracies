South San Francisco police search for man who shot at church
-----
South San Francisco police are searching for a man who fired a gun at a church Tuesday, the department said.Around 1:25 p.m., officers responded to St. Augustine's Catholic Church after the shooting was reported. No one was injured by the gunfire and the man fled the area, police said.
Officials associated with the church and the San Francisco Archdiocese did not respond to a request for comment.
