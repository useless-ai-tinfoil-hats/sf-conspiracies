California skydiving student who died in 'dust devil' fall identified
-----
A California skydiving student who died in an unusual accident while harnessed to an instructor has been identified.On Aug. 2 at around 2:30 p.m., a tandem jump at Skydive Perris in Riverside County took a tragic turn when two "dust devils," or small whirlwinds, impeded the dive after the parachute was deployed.
"While preparing for landing, the tandem pair hit disturbed air known as a dust devil. The interaction with this sudden disturbed air caused turbulence and a hard landing
