What makes Berkeley's Tilden Park a Bay Area wonderland
-----
From rolling hills and panoramic vistas to hidden valleys and dense forests, the East Bay's Tilden Regional Park is a wild expanse full of charming secrets that attracts avid hikers, cyclists, equestrians and families year-round.Though it's just a few minutes from downtown Berkeley, this is no average city park.
