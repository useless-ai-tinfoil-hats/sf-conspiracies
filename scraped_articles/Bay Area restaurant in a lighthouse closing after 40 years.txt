Bay Area restaurant in a lighthouse closing after 40 years
-----
The lights are going off inside a restaurant and bar located inside a historic landmark on the Oakland waterfront.Quinn's Lighthouse at 1951 Embarcadero will serve its final fish and chips plates and pints on Wednesday, Jan. 31. A manager who identified himself only as Grady confirmed the closure. The restaurant, he said, has been slammed by common problems such as increasing costs.
