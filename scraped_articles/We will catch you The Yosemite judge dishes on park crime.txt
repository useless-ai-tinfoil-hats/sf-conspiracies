‘We will catch you’: The Yosemite judge dishes on park crime
-----
Most visitors to Yosemite wouldn't know it existed but if you're arrested in the park, you earn a date with a nondescript courthouse located in the shadow of Yosemite Falls.The courthouse is presided over by Helena Barch-Kuchta, U.S. magistrate judge who was assigned to the Yosemite courthouse three years ago. The court bailiff Grady Bryant has worked in the park for decades.
