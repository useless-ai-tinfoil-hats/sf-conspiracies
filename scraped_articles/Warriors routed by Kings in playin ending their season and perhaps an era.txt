Warriors routed by Kings in play-in, ending their season and perhaps an era
-----
SACRAMENTO – The Golden State Warriors' season is over, ending brutally Tuesday night at Golden 1 Center.Ending with it might be the 12-year tenure of Stephen Curry, Draymond Green and Klay Thompson playing together in their blue and yellow jerseys.
The Warriors bowed out of the play-in tournament, falling 118-94 to the No. 9 seed Sacramento Kings as the No. 10 seed to end 2023-24 without a berth in the NBA playoffs despite boasting the highest payroll – $208 million – in league
