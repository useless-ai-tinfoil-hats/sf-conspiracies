Wine Country’s popular ‘gateway’ winery has sold
-----
Sonoma's Ram's Gate Winery, which produces single-vineyard Chardonnay and Pinot Noir from top California sites, has sold to beverage conglomerate O'Neill Vintners & Distillers.At first glance, O'Neill's acquisition of the Sonoma winery is unexpected, since the company has historically focused on less expensive and trend-driven wines, like low-sugar brand FitVine and Intercept from former Oakland Raiders defensive back Charles Woodson.
