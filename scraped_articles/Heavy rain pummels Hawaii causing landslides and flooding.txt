Heavy rain pummels Hawaii, causing landslides and flooding
-----
The entire state of Hawaii is now under a flood watch through Friday evening, with more heavy rainfall and flooding possible across the Islands.Severe weather, including a torrential downpour, caused flooding and landslides on the island of Oahu this week.
"Constant rain since 11 o' clock this morning going steady, and a few hours later we had this raging rapid river behind our house," Oahu resident Irwin Santos told Hawaii News Now on Monday.
