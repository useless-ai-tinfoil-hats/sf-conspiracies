Review: ‘Silent Night’ makes wordless mayhem a Christmas tradition
-----
"Silent Night" is being called Hong Kong action genius John Woo's return to American theatrical features after 20 years. This holiday-themed bloodbath was shot in Mexico with a mute lead character and a supporting cast that maybe utters 10 lines total. What isn't communicated through anguished faces and physical expression is said with phone texts. But the movie's real languages are battering violence and sentimental manipulation.It's all done in masterful style, with flourishes Woo
