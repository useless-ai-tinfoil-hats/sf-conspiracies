Controversial statue removed in Bay Area town after years of delay
-----
After years of community effort and activism, officials removed a controversial statue in Pacifica on Thursday morning.The statue of Spanish military officer Gaspar de Portola, which stood near the Pacifica Community Center for more than 30 years, started drawing attention in the summer of 2020, as discussions about racial justice and the legacies of colonization reached a boiling point across the nation.
