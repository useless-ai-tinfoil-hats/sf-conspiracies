Body found on BART tracks in San Francisco, officials say
-----
A body was found early Monday on the BART tracks near the Balboa Park Station in San Francisco, officials said.Police responded to "an unattended death on the tracks" that was discovered between the Balboa Park and Daly City stations shortly after 1 a.m., according to BART spokesperson Jim Allison.
BART police and the San Francisco coroner's office recovered the body, and no foul play was suspected, Allison said.
