‘Please expect delays’: East Bay traffic snarled after truck hits highway sign
-----
All northbound lanes of Interstate 680 at Stoneridge Drive in Pleasanton were closed Wednesday morning after a truck struck an overhead sign, highway officials said.Caltrans was inspecting an overhead sign that was struck by a big-rig truck, the California Highway Patrol said at 7:10 a.m. on X, formerly Twitter.
"Please expect delays," Caltrans wrote on X.
An estimated time for reopening all lanes was not provided.
