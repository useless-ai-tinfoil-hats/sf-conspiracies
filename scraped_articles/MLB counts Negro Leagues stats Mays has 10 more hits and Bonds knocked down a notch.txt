MLB counts Negro Leagues stats; Mays has 10 more hits and Bonds knocked down a notch
-----
Negro Leagues statistics finally became official Wednesday morning when Major League Baseball announced it had incorporated the numbers of seven Negro Leagues into its statistical database.The Chronicle reported this month that MLB would add the Negro Leagues stats before its June 20 tribute to the Negro Leagues, a game between the San Francisco Giants and St. Louis Cardinals at historic Rickwood Field in Birmingham, Ala.
