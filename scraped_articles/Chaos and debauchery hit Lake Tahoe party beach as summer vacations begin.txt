Chaos and debauchery hit Lake Tahoe party beach as summer vacations begin
-----
What is the sign that school's almost out for summer in Lake Tahoe? Teenagers throwing drunken parties on the beaches until the cops shut it all down.In May, Douglas County sheriff's officers were dispatched to break up teenage fights and parties at Zephyr Cove beach, on Lake Tahoe's east shore, 19 times, Sheriff Dan Coverley told SFGATE.
Half of those reports involved people under the age of 18, and 10 juveniles were arrested on suspicion of drinking, obstructing law enforcement
