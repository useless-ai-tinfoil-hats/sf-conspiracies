S.F. pasta hot spot is already expanding. Here’s where it’s opening next
-----
Just a year after opening in San Francisco, a popular pasta shop and restaurant run by a noted chef is opening a second location.Pasta Supply Co., which debuted on Clement Street last March, is set to open in April at 3233 22nd St., a large space in the Mission District last occupied by Dumpling Club. The new location will serve the same fresh pasta and sauces made in-house and a small, walk-in only menu for people who want to dine in.
