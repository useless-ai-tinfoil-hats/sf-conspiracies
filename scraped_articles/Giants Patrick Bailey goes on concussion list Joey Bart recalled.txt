Giants’ Patrick Bailey goes on concussion list, Joey Bart recalled
-----
CHICAGO — For at least a week, the San Francisco Giants will be without catcher Patrick Bailey, their top rookie and a Gold Glove candidate, who was placed on the seven-day concussion injured list Wednesday.The Giants promoted catcher Joey Bart from Triple-A Sacramento and inserted him into Wednesday's lineup along with outfielder Luis Matos, who also was promoted. Outfielder Wade Meckler was optioned to Sacramento.
