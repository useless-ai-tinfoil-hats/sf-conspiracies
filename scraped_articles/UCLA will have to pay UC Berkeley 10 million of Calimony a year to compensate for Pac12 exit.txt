UCLA will have to pay UC Berkeley $10 million of ‘Calimony’ a year to compensate for Pac-12 exit
-----
The University of California regents voted Tuesday to require UCLA to pay UC Berkeley $10 million a year for three academic years to prop up its athletic department after the collapse of the Pac-12.The three-year duration is less than the six years recommended by UC President Michael Drake, but the regents agreed to check back in in 2027.
