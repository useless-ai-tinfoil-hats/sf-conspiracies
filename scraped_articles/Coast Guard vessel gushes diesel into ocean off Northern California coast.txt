Coast Guard vessel gushes diesel into ocean off Northern California coast
-----
A U.S. Coast Guard vessel spilled approximately 500 gallons of diesel fuel off the Mendocino County coast last week, the agency said.The spill, the result of an operator error after a pump was not turned off, gushed diesel into the ocean about 30 miles west of Fort Bragg from midnight to 1:30 a.m. Friday, according to a hazardous material report from the Governor's Office of Emergency Services.
