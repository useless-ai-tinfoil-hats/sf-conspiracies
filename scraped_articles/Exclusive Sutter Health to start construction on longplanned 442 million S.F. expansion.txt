Exclusive: Sutter Health to start construction on long-planned $442 million S.F. expansion
-----
Sutter Health is nearing the last phase of its long-planned build-out of its California Pacific Medical Center Mission Bernal hospital campus, with plans to break ground on a $442 million care center next summer.The five-story, 129,000-square-foot medical office building will sit adjacent to Sutter's existing 120-bed CPMC hospital at the corner of Cesar Chavez and Valencia streets, which was built in the place of the former St. Luke's Hospital and officially opened in 2018.
