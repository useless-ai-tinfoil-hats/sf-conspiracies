Storm-damaged stretch of Hwy. 1 to reopen this weekend after a year and a half
-----
A 4.3-mile section of Highway 1 at Paul's Slide in Monterey County will reopen on Sunday, a year and a half after the stretch was damaged in severe winter storms, Caltrans officials said.The area, about 27 miles south of Big Sur, has been closed since Jan. 14, 2023, when storms caused a landslide that displaced half a million cubic yards of material across the coastal highway.
