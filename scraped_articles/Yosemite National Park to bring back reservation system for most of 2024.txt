Yosemite National Park to bring back reservation system for most of 2024
-----
After large crowds caused grueling, hourslong wait times to enter Yosemite National Park last summer, the park is reinstating a reservation system for much of 2024, the National Park Service announced Wednesday.Reservations will be required to enter the park on weekends from April 13 to June 30 and Aug. 17 to Oct. 27. Between July 1 and Aug. 16, reservations will be required every day of the week.
