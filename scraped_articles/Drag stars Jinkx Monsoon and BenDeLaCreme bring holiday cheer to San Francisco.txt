Drag stars Jinkx Monsoon and BenDeLaCreme bring holiday cheer to San Francisco
-----
With due respect to Brenda Lee and Mariah Carey, drag stars Jinkx Monsoon and BenDeLaCreme are the rising new queens of Christmas.After six tours and a film version, their annual "Jinkx and DeLa Holiday Show" returns to San Francisco's Warfield on Dec. 18 and 19, and for many audiences, it's become as much a part of the holiday season as hearing "Rockin' Around the Christmas Tree" or "All I Want for Christmas."
