Why Warriors’ effort to keep Klay Thompson is more complex than it seems
-----
The clock is ticking on a Klay Thompson decision. And the hard-to-digest possibility that Thompson could be the first of the Big Three to depart the Warriors.General manager Mike Dunleavy has a myriad of options, restrictions and decisions to face while assembling next season's roster. A cool, calm operator, Dunleavy is unlikely to be moved by his heart or brought to tears, like his predecessor Bob Myers was at times.
But…
Within days Thompson's future will be determined.
