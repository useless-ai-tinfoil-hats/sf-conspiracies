Man falls 30 feet down popular Central Coast hiking trail to waterfall
-----
Montecito officials are warning people of the hazards at a popular Santa Barbara County hiking spot after a 28-year old man fell 30 feet from a trail over the weekend.Rescue crews from the Montecito Fire Protection District and Santa Barbara County Fire Department rescued the man after he fell at Tangerine Falls on Saturday around 3 p.m., Montecito fire officials said in a social media post.
