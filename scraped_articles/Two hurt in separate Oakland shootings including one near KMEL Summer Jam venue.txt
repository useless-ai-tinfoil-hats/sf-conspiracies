Two hurt in separate Oakland shootings, including one near KMEL Summer Jam venue
-----
Two people were wounded in separate shootings 15 minutes apart early Sunday in Oakland, police said.The first victim was shot shortly after midnight in the area of Coliseum Way, where the Oakland Arena hosted the 106.1 KMEL Summer Jam concert. The lineup included rappers E-40 and Jeezy.
The victim was taken to a hospital and listed in stable condition, police said.
Officers took the suspected shooter into custody and recovered a gun, police said.
