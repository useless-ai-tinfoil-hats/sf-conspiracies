Former San Francisco Giants reliever Trevor Gott is back in the Bay Area, this time with a Coliseum address for the coming season.The Oakland A's signed Gott, 31, to a one-year, $1.5 million deal Tuesday. The terms include potential bonuses of $50,000 at the 45- and 50-appearance marks, and $100,000 if traded, according to Ken Rosenthal of the Athletic.
Gott was 0-5 with a 4.19 ERA and one save with the Mariners and Mets last year. The right-hander struck out 62 in 58 innings
