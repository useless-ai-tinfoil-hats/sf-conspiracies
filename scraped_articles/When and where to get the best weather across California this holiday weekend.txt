When and where to get the best weather across California this holiday weekend
-----
As Californians head into the three-day Memorial Day weekend, weather conditions across the state are set to be quite pleasant. A pair of relatively weak storm systems will move through the Pacific Northwest on Friday and Saturday, leading to an upper-level pattern that promotes onshore flow and cooler temperatures initially, with Saturday being the coolest day of the holiday weekend.
