‘Retribution’: S.F. supervisor running for mayor asks whether Breed is blocking a project to punish him
-----
San Francisco Supervisor Ahsha Safaí is accusing Mayor London Breed of putting the brakes on plans to build a new library in his district because he is challenging her for mayor next year.Safaí on Tuesday publicly asked Breed about the city's intention for the Ocean View branch of the San Francisco Public Library system, which is currently located on Randolph Street. Officials for the past several years had been looking to build a newer and larger library branch nearby on city-owned land
