Aerosmith has announced it will retire from touring due to frontman Steven Tyler's ongoing vocal injury.The "Walk This Way" rockers were meant to perform in San Francisco at Chase Center on Nov. 30, a concert originally scheduled for last December, as part of their Peace Out farewell tour.
"As you know, Steven's voice is an instrument like no other. He has spent months tirelessly working on getting his voice to where it was before his injury," the band tweeted on Friday, Aug. 2.
