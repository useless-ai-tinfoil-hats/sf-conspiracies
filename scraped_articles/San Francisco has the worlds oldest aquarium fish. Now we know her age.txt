San Francisco has the world’s oldest aquarium fish. Now we know her age
-----
With a 1938 arrival date at the Steinhart Aquarium — four years before President Joe Biden was born — Methuselah the Australian lungfish was already established as the oldest living fish in a zoological setting.But now scientists can get much more specific. A recent DNA sample, sent to Methuselah's birthplace in Australia, estimates an actual age: 92 years old.
