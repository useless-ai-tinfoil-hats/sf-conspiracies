California has the worst owner in baseball. But is it Anaheim’s Moreno or Oakland’s Fisher?
-----
In California, a land blessed with more than its fair share of winners, we learn our most important lessons by dwelling among the losers.So, as the baseball season ends, your columnist visited the bottom of the standings in American League West to ask: Which pro sports owner is the more instructive California failure — the failed heir fleeing Oakland or the billboard billionaire sticking around in Anaheim?
Bay Area fans have their answer: John Fisher of the Oakland A's.
