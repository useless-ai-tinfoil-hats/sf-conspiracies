Truck driver shot after entering Vallejo intersection during sideshow
-----
A sideshow turned violent Sunday in Vallejo when a truck driver entered an intersection during the sideshow, collided with a car performing doughnuts, and was mobbed by a crowd, chased into a nearby store, beaten and shot as his truck was set on fire, according to police and videos posted to social media.The horrific incident occurred at Springs Road and Rollingwood Drive near a quiet residential area and a retail district.
Police received reports of the sideshow around 5:30 p.m.
