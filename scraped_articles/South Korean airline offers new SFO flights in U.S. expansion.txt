South Korean airline offers new SFO flights in U.S. expansion
-----
Air Premia, a South Korean airline, is now flying between San Francisco International Airport and Incheon International Airport in Seoul four days a week.Service started on Friday, according to the airline and SFO. Flights on the San Francisco route will take place on Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays and Sundays, Air Premia said, with departures from SFO at 3 p.m. and arriving in Seoul at 7:50 p.m. the following day. Flights from Incheon will leave at 5:30 p.m. local time and arrive at SFO
