Burbank Airport breaks ground on a long-awaited new terminal
-----
With its small size and close proximity to both Hollywood and downtown Los Angeles, Hollywood Burbank Airport has a devoted following among Southern California flyers. Even the aging terminal, lacking in the sleek amenities business travelers love, is far more user-friendly than the sprawling mass of Los Angeles International.On Thursday, though, the airport finally broke ground on giving the cramped facility a sparkling new replacement after decades of false starts.
