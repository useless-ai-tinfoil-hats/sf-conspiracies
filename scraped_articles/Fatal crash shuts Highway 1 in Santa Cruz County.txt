Fatal crash shuts Highway 1 in Santa Cruz County
-----
A person was killed Sunday in a single-vehicle crash on Highway 1 just north of Wilder Ranch State Park in Santa Cruz County, the California Highway Patrol said.The crash shut down both sides of the highway, with the closure expected to last several hours, the CHP said on X an hour after the crash.
The crash occurred about 12:40 p.m. on the northbound side of the highway. The victim was reportedly thrown from the vehicle and died at the scene, according to a CHP traffic log.
