115-year-old Union Square restaurant John's Grill throwing free lunch block party Wednesday
-----
Widely known for its bipartisan Election Day luncheons studded with local and state political luminaries, historic John's Grill is throwing a similar block party Wednesday to celebrate its 115 years in business.On Oct. 4 from 11:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m., the downtown SF restaurant invites community members to share a meal at no charge in the closed street in front of its 63 Ellis St. location. That's right, John's Grill in Union Square is serving free lunch (and wine) Wednesday, no RSVP
