GM Ohemaa Nyanin sees Warriors-WNBA link: ‘Winning is the foundation’
-----
The Bill King Interview Room inside Chase Center was as filled with media members as Ohemaa Nyanin was with gratitude and joy.For the New York Liberty.
Her family and friends.
The Golden State Warriors — and the franchise's WNBA team.
"I know that this area has such a rich women's basketball history," she said Monday morning with owner Joe Lacob perched proudly beside her.
"This area is ready for a WNBA team."
And she, like Lacob, is ready to build it.
