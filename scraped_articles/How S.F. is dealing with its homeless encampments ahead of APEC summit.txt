How S.F. is dealing with its homeless encampments ahead of APEC summit
-----
San Francisco is walking a fine line over homeless encampments as the biggest international event it's hosted since 1945 is set to unfold this month.As the city rolls out the welcome mat to foreign leaders and dignitaries, CEOs, thousands of attendees and a crush of journalists, it's attempting to be as aggressive as possible about clearing homeless encampments while trying to connect more unhoused people to shelter and other services ahead of the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit
