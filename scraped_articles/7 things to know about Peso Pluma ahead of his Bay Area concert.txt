7 things to know about Peso Pluma ahead of his Bay Area concert
-----
Peso Pluma, the 24-year-old whose mixture of corridos, reggaeton and hip-hop, signature mullet hairstyle and dance moves propelled him above Bad Bunny on Spotify's charts, is set to perform Friday, Oct. 13, at SAP Center at San Jose.Here's what you need to know about the chart-topping Mexican singer ahead of his must-see show.
Where is Peso Pluma from?
Peso Pluma, whose real name is Hassan Emilio Kabande Laija, was born in Guadalajara in 1999 (as his neck tattoo confirms).
