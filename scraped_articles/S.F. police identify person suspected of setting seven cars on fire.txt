S.F. police identify person suspected of setting seven cars on fire
-----
San Francisco police identified the person they suspected of setting seven vehicles on fire over a single week in San Francisco, police said Tuesday.Armando Salvador, 61, was arrested on Feb. 27 near Union Square after he was reported to have matched the description of the suspect who lit several vehicles on fire within the last month in the Union Square and South of Market neighborhoods, police said.
