'Foot of mud in the street': Debris floods Cow Hollow in water main break
-----
A section of streets was closed in San Francisco's Cow Hollow on Monday morning after a water main break flooded the area with water and debris, impacting local businesses and disrupting traffic and public transportation, officials said.Laurie Thomas, owner of Rose's Cafe on the corner of Union and Steiner streets, called it a "huge mess in the streets."
"There's got to be a foot of mud in the street," Thomas said. "There was a semi stuck in the intersection this morning."
