San Francisco Ballet School appoints new director amid period of profound leadership change
-----
The San Francisco Ballet School, one of the world's top training grounds for young dancers, has named its new director as the company moves deeper into its next era under Artistic Director Tamara Rojo.Grace Maduell Holmes, a former San Francisco Ballet student and company soloist who went on to dance at the Birmingham Royal Ballet in England, officially steps into the role in January.
