College basketball: San Jose State, St. Mary's women roll to victories
-----
Freshman Jyah Lovett scored 17 points and sophomore Sabrina Ma added 15 as the San Jose State women used a big second half to roll past host Cal State Northridge 70-53 on Monday afternoon.Lovett, in reaching double figures in scoring for the seventh time this season, went 6-of-14 from the field (including 3-of-6 from distance) and added three steals and three assists. Ma collected game-high totals in rebounds (nine) and assists (six).
