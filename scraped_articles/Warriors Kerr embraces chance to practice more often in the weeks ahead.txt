Warriors’ Kerr embraces chance to practice more often in the weeks ahead
-----
Golden State Warriors head coach Steve Kerr was talking about practice Monday – as in, his team needs more of it.That's often a challenge during the grind of a long NBA season. Kerr also is mindful of giving his players enough rest: They had Sunday off, for example, ahead of Monday night's home game against Houston at Chase Center.
But the upcoming schedule helps. In a span of 21 days – starting Tuesday and running through Dec. 11 – the Warriors probably will play only seven games.
