Kamala Harris, Tim Walz rally draws 14,000 in Philadelphia
-----
PHILADELPHIA — Two months ago, a Joe Biden-Kamala Harris rally filled one-third of a Philadelphia school gym. Tuesday, a deafening cacophony of 10,000 attended the first joint Harris-Tim Walz rally — with an additional 4,000 listening from a nearby building.The crowd included a Kennedy (Jack Schlossberg, son of Caroline Kennedy), numerous members of Congress from Delaware and Pennsylvania and comedian Ziwe, best known for her biting satirical interviews on politics and race.
