S.F. Chronicle honored by Excellence-in-Features journalism contest
-----
The San Francisco Chronicle won four awards in the 2024 Excellence-in-Features journalism contest, including honors for food coverage and storytelling centered on historically marginalized or undercovered communities.The Society for Features Journalism on Tuesday announced the winners in its annual competition, which recognizes features storytelling from narratives to videos to podcasts.
