Shake Shack closes ‘underperforming’ Oakland restaurant in another loss for downtown
-----
Burger chain Shake Shack is closing its "underperforming" Oakland restaurant, becoming the latest business to shutter in the beleaguered city, along with five locations in Southern California.The closure follows In-N-Out's departure from Oakland in the spring, though Shake Shack is downsizing across the country. Two Shake Shack locations in Houston and one in Columbus, Ohio, are also closing by Sept. 25 due to underperformance.
