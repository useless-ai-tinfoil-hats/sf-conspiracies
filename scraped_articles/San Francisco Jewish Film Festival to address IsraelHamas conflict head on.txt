San Francisco Jewish Film Festival to address Israel-Hamas conflict head on
-----
As the Israel-Hamas war rages on, coupled with a rise in antisemitic hate crimes around the globe, this summer's San Francisco Jewish Film Festival will have a feeling of urgency.The 44th edition of the festival, which runs July 18-Aug. 4, plans to feature several films that "offer unique perspectives that help to contextualize the current crisis in Israel and Gaza," including a program of short films made by students at Sapir College, the only academic institution in the Gaza envelope.
