Man dies after apparent fight on Bay Area hiking trail, police say
-----
A man was found dead on a Bay Area hiking trail late last week following what appeared to be a fight, according to law enforcement officials.Members of the Antioch Police Department and the Contra Costa County Fire Protection District were called to a section of the Delta de Anza Regional Trail near the 4100 block of Null Drive in Antioch around 9:30 a.m. on Friday following reports of a physical fight.
