Oakland Ballers release renderings of Raimondi Park ahead of inaugural season
-----
Barely one month from their inaugural home game, the Oakland Ballers released renderings Wednesday of their planned refurbishment of Raimondi Park in West Oakland.The Ballers are a new, independent minor-league team hoping to fill the impending baseball gap in the East Bay, given the Oakland Athletics' departure to Sacramento next year ahead of their planned move to Las Vegas.
