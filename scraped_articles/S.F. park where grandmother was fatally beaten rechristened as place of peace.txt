S.F. park where grandmother was fatally beaten rechristened as place of peace
-----
Family and friends of a grandmother who was fatally attacked at a San Francisco playground gathered Saturday to mark a new era in which the park will bear her name.Local officials unveiled a green sign at the entrance to the Yik Oi Huang Peace and Friendship Park, formerly known as Visitacion Valley Playground.
"When tragedy struck in 2019, the family of grandma Huang came together to ensure that this community was not divided," Mayor London Breed told about 50 people who gathered.
