At least one child eats cannabis-infused candy from Bay Area school's Halloween event
-----
At least one child ingested cannabis-infused fruit chews that were found among candy from an Alameda elementary school event over the weekend, district officials said in an emergency alert to families Monday afternoon.Three other families reported finding the fruit chews among the chocolates and other items from the PTA-sponsored event on Sunday at Amelia Earhart Elementary, located on Bay Farm Island.
The items were wrapped in blue paper and identified as Kiva Lost Farm
