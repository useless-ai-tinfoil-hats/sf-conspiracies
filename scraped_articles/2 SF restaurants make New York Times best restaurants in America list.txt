2 SF restaurants make New York Times' best restaurants in America list
-----
Two San Francisco restaurants made the New York Times' annual America's Best Restaurants list this year.The list, which showcases 50 eateries across the country, featured five California restaurants. The two San Francisco spots recognized this year were Noodle in a Haystack and Prik Hom.
