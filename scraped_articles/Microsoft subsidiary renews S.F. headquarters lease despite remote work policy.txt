Microsoft subsidiary renews S.F. headquarters lease despite remote work policy
-----
GitHub has renewed its San Francisco headquarters lease, despite embracing remote work.The popular software development manager, which is owned by Microsoft, signed a 57,000-square-foot lease for five more years at 275 Brannan St. in South of Market. It has been headquartered there since 2013 and the company maintained the same square footage.
The company and landlord Hudson Pacific Properties confirmed the lease.
