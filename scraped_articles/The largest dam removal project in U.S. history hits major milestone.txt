The largest dam removal project in U.S. history hits major milestone
-----
One dam down, three to more go.Work crews completed the deconstruction of Copco No. 2 — a hydroelectric dam on the Klamath River near the Oregon-California border — in early November, according to a press release from Klamath River Renewal Corp., the nonprofit organization removing the dams. It's a major milestone in the largest dam removal project in U.S. history, and the three remaining dams are slated to come down next year.
