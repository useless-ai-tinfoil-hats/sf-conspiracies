Pizza delivery driver held in assault on grandmother in North Bay, police say
-----
A pizza delivery driver was arrested on suspicion of felony assault on an elder after pushing a grandmother to the sidewalk in Petaluma, police say.Carlos Barreda, 53, of Santa Rosa was booked into the Sonoma County Jail on Friday for the assault, which occurred shortly before 6 p.m. Monday as he was delivering food, according to the Petaluma Police Department.
