San Francisco's Portola Music Festival announces stacked lineup for 2024
-----
Electronic music fans in San Francisco are in for a treat this fall. Portola Music Festival just announced its 2024 lineup, which is basically a bucket list of the best DJs and producers working today (with a few throwbacks for good measure).Now in its third year, the festival is scheduled to take place at Pier 80 on Sept. 28 and 29.
