Former Silicon Valley ‘party animal’ convicted of defrauding investors of millions
-----
Federal jurors convicted former tech venture capitalist Michael Brent Rothenberg, whose lavish lifestyle once earned him a reputation as a Silicon Valley "party animal," of defrauding banks and investors of $18.8 million, the U.S. Attorney's Office announced this week.Rothenberg, 39, of San Francisco could face decades in prison after jurors on Thursday found him guilty of 21 federal crimes, including wire fraud, money laundering and bank fraud.
