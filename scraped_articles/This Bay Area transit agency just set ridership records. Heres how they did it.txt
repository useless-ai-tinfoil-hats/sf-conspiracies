This Bay Area transit agency just set ridership records. Here’s how they did it
-----
Fleet Week festivities helped the San Francisco Bay Ferry soar to records for weekend ridership as vessels carried standing-room only crowds across the bay last weekend, with Saturday the ferry's busiest day ever.The Water Emergency Transportation Authority, the agency that operates the region's ferry system, said ferries carried 29,552 passengers during the two-day weekend — the ferry's busiest weekend since it was established in 2007.
