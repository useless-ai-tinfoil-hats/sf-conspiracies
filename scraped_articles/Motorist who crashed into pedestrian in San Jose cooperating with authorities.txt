Motorist who crashed into pedestrian in San Jose cooperating with authorities
-----
A pedestrian was gravely injured Saturday morning when he was struck by a motorist in San Jose, police said.The man was rushed to a hospital with life-threatening injuries after the 6 a.m. crash near Phelan and South Seventh avenues, authorities said.
"The driver did remain on scene and is cooperating with the investigation," a San Jose Police spokesperson said in an email.
