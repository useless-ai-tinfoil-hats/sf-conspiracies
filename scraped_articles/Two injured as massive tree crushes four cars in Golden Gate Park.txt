Two injured as massive tree crushes four cars in Golden Gate Park
-----
A massive eucalyptus tree crushed four cars in Golden Gate Park on Monday afternoon, injuring two occupants, San Francisco firefighters said.The tree fell onto northbound lanes of 19th Avenue, which traverses the park in the city's Richmond District, around 4 p.m. Eleven occupants were in the vehicles when the large tree smashed the cars, and seven patients were initially evaluated.
The road was temporarily closed in both directions, with only one southbound lane allowing traffic,
