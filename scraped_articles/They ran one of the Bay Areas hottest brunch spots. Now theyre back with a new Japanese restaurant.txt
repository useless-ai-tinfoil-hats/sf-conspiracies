They ran one of the Bay Area’s hottest brunch spots. Now they’re back with a new Japanese restaurant
-----
Two months after closing a hit Bay Area brunch spot, this acclaimed chef couple is back with a new, different restaurant.The owners of Morning Wood, which closed in San Mateo in September, are opening a cozy Japanese restaurant in a Burlingame strip mall at 1861 El Camino Real, on Wednesday, Dec. 6.
