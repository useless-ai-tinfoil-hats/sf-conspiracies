Review: ‘Origin,’ though original in concept, comes up short in both drama and ideas
-----
"Origin" is a movie unlike any other.It's not a documentary, and yet it contains historical reenactments. It's a dramatic feature, but it's not really about telling a story. It's about putting forward the ideas expressed by Isabel Wilkerson in her 2020 book, "Caste: The Origins of Our Discontents."
