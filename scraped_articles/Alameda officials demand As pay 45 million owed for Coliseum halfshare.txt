Alameda officials demand A's pay $45 million owed for Coliseum half-share
-----
Alameda County sent notice on Thursday to John Fisher and the Oakland Athletics: Pay up.The county sent a registered letter to Coliseum Way Partners LLC demanding that the A's, owned by Fisher, pay the county $45 million by May 14. The A's purchased the county's 50% interest in the Coliseum site for $85 million in 2019, and have paid $40 million. Per terms of the contract, the remainder was due in three equal payments of $15 million, in January 2024, February 2025 and January 2026.
