Person struck and killed by Caltrain in South Bay
-----
A person was struck and killed by a Caltrain in San Jose on Tuesday morning, the transit agency said.A northbound train struck the person near Skyway Drive just before 6:30 a.m., according to a statement from Caltrain. Identifying information for the victim was not immediately available.
The incident brings to 12 the number of people killed by a Caltrain this year.
There were 97 people aboard the train during the incident, none of whom were injured.
