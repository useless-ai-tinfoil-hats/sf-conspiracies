49ers-Browns could see rain, up to 30 mph gusts of wind
-----
The undefeated San Francisco 49ers could face their biggest test this Sunday. Not the host Cleveland Browns, who will be without starting quarterback Deshaun Watson, but rather north Ohio weather, which is predicted to be chilly, wet and windy.Around game time, AccuWeather is predicting a 60% chance of rain with 30 mph gusts. The temperature is expected to stay in the low 50s throughout the day.
