At Bay Area show, Foo Fighters frontman describes seeing his late bandmate in animal form
-----
The Foo Fighters' Dave Grohl has lost not one, but two, of his closest bandmates during his legendary nearly 40-year career. So it's not surprising that aging and the passage of time is on his mind.But that focus only enhanced the Foo Fighters' rollicking three-hour show at the Toyota Pavilion in Concord on Tuesday, which Grohl said was preceded by a visit from one of those fallen bandmates.
