Festival La Onda to return next year after successful first run. Here's how to get tickets
-----
Festival La Onda is set to return to Wine Country.Minutes after the first-ever Latin-centric concert ended at 10 p.m. Sunday, June 2, tickets for the 2025 Festival La Onda went on sale at www.laondafest.com. The two-day festival is scheduled for May 31 and June 1, and will once again take over Napa Valley Expo the weekend after BottleRock Napa Valley, the luxury festival produced by the same local company that paved the way for such events in the area more than a decade ago.
