CHP releases video of horrific head-on crash in which wrong-way driver struck their cruiser
-----
Video released by the California Highway Patrol on Friday shows a horrific crash in which a wrong-way driver crashed head-on into a CHP cruiser, injuring the two officers inside.CHP officials said they believe the motorist intentionally crashed into the CHP vehicle. The video of Wednesday's crash, taken along eastbound Interstate 80 just west of Mace Boulevard in Davis, shows a white car moving against the flow of traffic along the shoulder of the road then slamming into a stopped CHP
