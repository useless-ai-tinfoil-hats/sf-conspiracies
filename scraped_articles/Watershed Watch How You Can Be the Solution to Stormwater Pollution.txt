Watershed Watch: How You Can Be the “Solution to Stormwater Pollution”
-----
With our changing climate and periods of extreme drought, many of us might feel lots of rain is a timely and much-needed solution. But rain also washes pollution into our storm drains, which lead to our creeks and the San Francisco Bay. How can we prevent this type of pollution and protect our waterways?In natural landscapes, most of the rainwater soaks into the soil.
