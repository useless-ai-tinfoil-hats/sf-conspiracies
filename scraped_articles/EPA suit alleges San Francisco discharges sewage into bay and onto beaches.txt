EPA suit alleges San Francisco discharges sewage into bay and onto beaches
-----
The federal government and the state of California sued the city of San Francisco on Wednesday over the city's aging combined stormwater-sewer systems, which they allege can release raw sewage into the bay and onto beaches when overwhelmed during heavy rain, as first reported by the San Francisco Chronicle.
