S.F. Symphony brings in new talent for fall concert as one performer drops out
-----
An opera singer scheduled to perform one of the San Francisco Symphony's first concerts this season has withdrawn from the program, just three weeks before opening night.Bass singer Peixin Chen is expected to replace Eric Owens in the Symphony's performance of Giuseppe Verdi's "Requiem," scheduled for three nights, Sept. 19-21. Owens, a bass-baritone who performed frequently with the San Francisco Opera, withdrew from the program for "personal reasons," the orchestra announced Thursday,
