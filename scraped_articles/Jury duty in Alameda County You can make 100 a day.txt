Jury duty in Alameda County? You can make $100 a day
-----
Jurors in Alameda County will begin receiving $100 a day — and increased transportation benefits — starting next week thanks to a state-funded pilot program intended to increase and diversify juror participation.Alameda County Superior Court is the only Bay Area court participating in the pilot program. Created by state lawmakers in 2022, the Jury Pilot Program is an experiment to determine if financial incentives might enable a broader swath of people to serve on juries.
