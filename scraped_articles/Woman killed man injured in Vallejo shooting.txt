Woman killed, man injured in Vallejo shooting
-----
A woman died and a man was injured in gunfire Saturday night in Vallejo, police said.The shooting was reported around 11 p.m. on Redwood Parkway near Hanns Park, east of Interstate 80.
Officers started medical aid, but the woman died at the scene, police said. The man was taken to a hospital for treatment and was expected to survive.
The woman's name was withheld, pending family notifications.
