California storm live updates: Evacuations ordered, thousands without power
-----
Power outages climbing throughout CaliforniaFeb. 4, 10:45 a.m.
Power outages are rapidly climbing in California as a storm takes aim at central and northern California. Shortly before 9 a.m. Sunday, PG&E reported about 56,000 customers were in the dark. At 10:30 a.m., that number ballooned to 142,580 customers. Southern California Edison is reporting 3,803 customers currently impacted by power outages. The National Weather Service is forecasting wind gusts of 45 to 60 mph
