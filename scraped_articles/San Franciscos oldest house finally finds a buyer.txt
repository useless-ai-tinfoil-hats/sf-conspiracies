The oldest home in San Francisco has finally found a buyer.The Abner Phelps House, located at 1111 Oak Street near the Panhandle of Golden Gate Park, sold for $2 million this summer to a firm tied to an artist management company, according to city property records and seller Pamela Munn.
Munn said Thursday that she listed the property for $3 million earlier this year after giving up trying to find a rental tenant to lease it. The property is zoned for commercial use.
