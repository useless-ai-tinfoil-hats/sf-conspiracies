Bay Area diner known for 'world-famous' coffeecake to close original store
-----
Hobee's Restaurants, known for its self-proclaimed "world-famous coffeecake," will permanently close its original Mountain View store after 49 years.The Mountain View restaurant, located at 2312 Central Expressway, is expected to shutter at the end of Thanksgiving weekend, according to a Facebook post shared by the business on Tuesday. The owners cited "infrastructure" issues as a reason behind the closure.
