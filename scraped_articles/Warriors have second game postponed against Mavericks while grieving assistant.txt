Warriors have second game postponed, against Mavericks, while grieving assistant
-----
The NBA has postponed Friday night's game at Chase Center between the Golden State Warriors and Dallas Mavericks as the Warriors continue to grieve the death of assistant coach Dejan Milojevic, the league announced Thursday morning.The new date for the game will be determined later; tickets for Friday will be honored on the rescheduled day. Thursday's news comes a day after the league postponed Wednesday night's Warriors-Jazz game at Delta Center for the same reason.
