How Pixar’s ‘Inside Out’ sequel uses a wrecking ball to build a new coming-of-age story
-----
When a random notification appeared on "Inside Out 2" director Kelsey Mann's computer several years ago, he had no idea he was about to be tapped to direct a sequel to one of Pixar's most successful films."A meeting just popped up on my calendar with Pete Docter and Jim Morris," said Mann, referring to the Oscar-winning director and the Disney-owned studio's president, respectively. "I thought, 'What is this?' It's either going to be the worst meeting in the world or the best."
