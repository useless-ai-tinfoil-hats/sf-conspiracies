After 47 years, Bay Area tortilla factory to move to Kansas
-----
After nearly five decades of producing fresh tortillas in Sonoma County, La Tortilla Factory will close its plant and move out of state.Flagship Food Group, which owns a large stake in La Tortilla Factory, shared a statement with SFGATE indicating that the plant will relocate to Moundridge, Kansas. The Santa Rosa factory is expected to close on March 31. The group not owning the Santa Rosa site and "prohibitive land acquisition costs in the area" were deciding factors in the move,
