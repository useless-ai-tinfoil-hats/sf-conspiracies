Florida man steals plane in Palo Alto, flies it to Half Moon Bay beach, police say
-----
A Miami man was arrested Thursday after allegedly stealing a plane in Palo Alto and flying it to a sandy beach in Half Moon Bay.The San Mateo County Sheriff's Office said Luiz Gustavo Aires, 50, stole a plane from a Palo Alto airport and then landed it on the sand just south of Poplar Beach in Half Moon Bay around 5 p.m.
A witness reported seeing the plane land and then watching as the pilot walked away, according to the sheriff's office.
