Bay Area mom suspected of torturing child arrested after 22 years on the run
-----
A Bay Area mother who allegedly tortured at least one of her children has been taken into police custody after more than two decades, jail records show. The news was first reported by KTVU-TV's Henry K. Lee.In 2002, an 11-year-old girl was found alone on a street in Manteca. When police arrived to help her, they discovered she had burns, scars and bruises all over her body.
