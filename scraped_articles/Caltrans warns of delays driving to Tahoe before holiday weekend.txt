Caltrans warns of delays driving to Tahoe before holiday weekend
-----
As Memorial Day approaches, many Bay Area travelers will undoubtedly be making their way to Tahoe to enjoy the holiday weekend.However, this year, travelers will need to be mindful of road conditions that could complicate and slow down the journey depending on when they leave for the Sierra.
Travelers can expect ongoing delays on Interstate 80 due to emergency construction starting in Colfax and extending to the Nevada state line.
