The NBA’s first wine partner makes America’s favorite Chardonnay
-----
In recent years, the wine world has gained an unlikely group of advocates: professional basketball players. Now the National Basketball Association (NBA) — which has earned the nickname "the Wine League" — has partnered with two of California's most recognizable wineries.Santa Rosa's Kendall-Jackson, producer of the best-selling Chardonnay in America, is the first official wine partner of the NBA.
