In-N-Out exec blames California for menu price hikes
-----
The rumors are true: In-N-Out has raised its prices in California. The question is: Why?Like other California fast food restaurants, the beloved fast food burger chain decided to raise its famously low prices as a result of the state's new $20 minimum wage law for fast food workers, which went into effect on April 1.
