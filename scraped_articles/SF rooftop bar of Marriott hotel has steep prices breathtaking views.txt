SF rooftop bar of Marriott hotel has steep prices, breathtaking views
-----
Nobody comes to the View Lounge for the food. Or even the drinks.Located on the 39th floor of the jukebox-shaped San Francisco Marriott Marquis, the real reason anyone comes to the View Lounge is for, well, the view. Tourists love the panoramic views of the city, but even locals will brave the hotel crowd for special date nights or watching the Blue Angels during Fleet Week, which is in full swing this week.
