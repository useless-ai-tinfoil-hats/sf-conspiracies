Gilroy juvenile suspected of stabbing high school student during locker room fight
-----
Gilroy police said Wednesday that they arrested a student at Christopher High School who allegedly stabbed another student during a fight in the school's locker room.Police were dispatched to the school around noon Wednesday following a report of a stabbing. School staff, including a security officer, "were on campus at the time and quickly intervened," police said. The juvenile suspected of committing the stabbing had been arrested and held until police arrived.
