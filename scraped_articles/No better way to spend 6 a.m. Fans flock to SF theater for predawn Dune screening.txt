'No better way to spend 6 a.m.': Fans flock to SF theater for pre-dawn 'Dune' screening
-----
The desert landscapes and innovative action scenes in "Dune: Part Two" make for some of the most epic visual moments in recent cinematic history. For the rare privilege of seeing the blockbuster spectacle in 70mm IMAX, fans endured bleary eyes at a pre-dawn screening at a San Francisco theater.Tickets for the sci-fi epic's opening weekend were so highly coveted that fans piled into the AMC Metreon in SoMa on Sunday for a 6 a.m. screening of the film.
