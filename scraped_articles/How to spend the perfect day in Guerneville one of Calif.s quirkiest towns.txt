How to spend the perfect day in Guerneville, one of Calif.'s quirkiest towns
-----
For the unobservant, it might be easy to overlook the Sonoma County town of Guerneville, CA. With a downtown that takes five minutes tops to drive through, it's a blink-and-you'll-miss-it kind of place.But if you take the time to stop and walk along main street, you'll see evidence of Guerneville's unique, artistic charm: koi painted on the outside of businesses, intentionally placed glitter that fills cracks in the sidewalks, a stenciled painting of a pink and purple pug.
