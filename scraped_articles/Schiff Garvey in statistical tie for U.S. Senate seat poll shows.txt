Schiff, Garvey in ‘statistical tie’ for U.S. Senate seat, poll shows
-----
Rep. Adam Schiff and former Los Angeles Dodger Steve Garvey are poised for a November face-off to represent California in the U.S. Senate, according to a new poll.The poll shows about 27% of likely voters said they'd back Garvey, the race's leading Republican, a "statistical tie" to Schiff's 25%, according to the UC Berkeley Institute of Governmental Studies, which conducted the poll in late February.
