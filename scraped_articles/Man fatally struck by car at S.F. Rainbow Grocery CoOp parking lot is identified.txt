Man fatally struck by car at S.F. Rainbow Grocery Co-Op parking lot is identified
-----
The San Francisco medical examiner's office has identified the man who died this week after being hit by a vehicle in the parking lot of a Mission District grocery store.San Francisco police were dispatched to Rainbow Grocery Cooperative on Monday afternoon, where they found a pedestrian who had been struck by a car. The driver was cooperative, but the victim was declared deceased at the scene, police said.
