Suspect in Chinese Consulate building crash identified as SF man
-----
LATEST Oct. 12, 5 p.m. The driver who died after crashing into the Chinese Consulate building in San Francisco was identified as Zhanyuan Yang, age 31, of San Francisco, the city's Office of the Chief Medical Examiner told SFGATE in an email.Yang was shot by police after the crash and died in the hospital, the Associated Press reported.
