California Skechers store draws crowds with knockoff Costco food court
-----
In May, the Gardena, California, Skechers outlet hatched a plan to draw in customers that was as bizarre as it was brilliant: attach a Costco-style food court to the store.Yes, Skechers. The shoe company.
Skechers CEO Michael Greenberg told Eater that the concession area, called Food Spot, has boosted the store's sales, describing it as "a beautiful concept." Eater reported that on a weekday visit, lines were long and the outdoor seating area was full.
