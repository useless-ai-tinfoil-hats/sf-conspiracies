Suspected DUI driver fatally hits man on Highway 101 in San Jose
-----
A man was fatally struck Tuesday night on Highway 101 by a woman driving under the influence, according to the California Highway Patrol.Justine Rivas, 25, was driving with a 3-year-old passenger north on Highway 101 near McKee Road in San Jose in the high-occupancy vehicle lane when she struck the pedestrian, the CHP said. The front right portion of her Toyota Camry hit the man, who the CHP described as elderly.
