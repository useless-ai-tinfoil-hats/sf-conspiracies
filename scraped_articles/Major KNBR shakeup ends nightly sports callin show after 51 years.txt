Major KNBR shakeup ends nightly sports call-in show after 51 years
-----
LATEST Nov. 29, 6:50 p.m.: KNBR program director Adam Copeland confirmed that the station would no longer have a nightly call-in show after a 51-year run as part of some major shakeups at the sports talk station.Speaking about the "dark day" at the end of the "Tolbert and Copes" afternoon show, Copeland confirmed that F.P. Santangelo had been let go by the station, as was morning show host Paul McCaffrey.
