Bay Area sourdough star opens big new bakery
-----
One of the Bay Area's best bakeries is about to open a much-anticipated new location.San Mateo's Backhaus, known for its sourdough bread and pastries with German influences, will open a second bakery at 261 California Drive in Burlingame on Nov. 7. Fans can expect the same fresh loaves and sweets like pistachio-raspberry croissants, plus an array of new items.
