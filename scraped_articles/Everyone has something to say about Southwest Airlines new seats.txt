Everyone has something to say about Southwest Airlines' new seats
-----
Soon after Southwest Airlines debuted its new interior cabin design last week, controversy swirled online. The first sticking point? Thinner seats.On Feb. 2, the airline unveiled designs for its new cabin by dropping a video on its TikTok page showing before and after interior images of its planes.
The animated video has been viewed 8.5 million times in the last four days, and the responses from viewers are mixed.
