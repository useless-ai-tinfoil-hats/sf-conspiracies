Bay Area nonprofit Signal shows how bloated tech companies have become
-----
Signal, the messaging app run by a Bay Area nonprofit, counts Apple's iMessage and Meta's Messenger and WhatsApp as its main rivals. But a new report from Signal shows how it's playing a different game entirely.Signal serves tens of millions of users who are drawn to its end-to-end encrypted messaging platform.
