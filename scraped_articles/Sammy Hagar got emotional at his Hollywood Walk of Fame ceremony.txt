Sammy Hagar got emotional at his Hollywood Walk of Fame ceremony
-----
Some celebrities view receiving a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame as a promotional obligation. For Sammy Hagar, it marked the realization of a lifelong fantasy.The Bay Area rocker shared with his fans that he and his mother had been fascinated with the Walk of Fame in the 1960s, as he accepted the honor during a ceremony on Tuesday, April 30, becoming the 2,779th star recipient.
