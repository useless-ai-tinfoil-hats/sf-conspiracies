New data shows grim tally for S.F.'s worst year for overdose deaths. These groups were hit hardest
-----
San Francisco recorded 806 accidental drug overdose deaths last year, the highest level the city has ever seen and a sign that officials are still struggling to confront the fentanyl crisis despite huge public pressure to rein in the epidemic.The proliferation of fentanyl, a synthetic opioid up to 50 times stronger than heroin, remains at the center of the crisis. The powerful opioid was found in 4 out of 5 overdose victims last year, most cases in combination with other dangerous drugs.
