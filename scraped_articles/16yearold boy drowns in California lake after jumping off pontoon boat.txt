16-year-old boy drowns in California lake after jumping off pontoon boat
-----
A 16-year-old California boy drowned Sunday afternoon after jumping off a pontoon boat into Shaver Lake, an artificial lake an hour northeast of Fresno in the central Sierra Nevada, officials said.Alejandro Araujo was from a small town outside Fresno; he was on the boat with family and friends, Tony Botti, a spokesperson for the Fresno County Sheriff's Office, told SFGATE.
