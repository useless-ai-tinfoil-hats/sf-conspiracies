Shutdowns set for key stretch of major Bay Area highway this weekend
-----
Drivers headed south through the Peninsula this weekend may want to steer clear of Highway 101 through Redwood City, as Caltrans plans to close a nearly one-mile stretch of all southbound lanes from Friday night through Monday morning for construction work.A similar closure of the northbound lanes will take place two weekends later, Oct. 13-16, as workers replace the Corralitos Creek Bridge.
