These Bay Area food influencers are not it for the money or fame
-----
A well-timed social media video from an influencer has the potential to blow up a restaurant and boost business.But it's not all rosy. These posts are often the result of a business transaction, exchanging exposure for a fee or free food.
In recent years, public perception about influencers has soured as stories about the inner mechanics of the industry have come to light. A Los Angeles Times report in 2022 revealed that several popular food influencers work much like marketers.
