Golden Gate Bridge briefly blocked by pro-Palestinian protesters
-----
Pro-Palestinian protesters briefly blocked several lanes of the Golden Gate Bridge Wednesday morning.The protest began around 7:45 a.m. and all northbound and southbound lanes were stopped at mid-span on the bridge, according to the California Highway Patrol. Both lanes reopened by 8:30 a.m.
