Californians now have less than a year to get a Real ID. Here’s what to know
-----
The deadline for Californians to get a Real ID is now less than a year away — and state officials are reminding residents to apply sooner rather than later.Starting May 7, 2025, travelers in the U.S. will need to present a Real ID — or a passport — to board a domestic flight. Real IDs comply with federal requirements, including verified proof of identification and residency, mandated by Congress in 2005 in the wake of the Sept. 11, 2001, terrorist attacks.
