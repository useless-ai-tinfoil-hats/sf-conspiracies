With roster all but finalized, can Bay FC succeed in inaugural NWSL season?
-----
Finally, the picture of who will play for Bay FC is becoming clear.With the additions of Scottish defender Jen Beattie, Nigerian forward Asisat Oshoala and Ghanan attacker Princess Marfo, Bay FC has 23 players. Last week, the team added Venezuelan star midfielder Deyna Castellanos.
Some players, like Castellanos, have yet to arrive in Santa Barbara to begin training but will as soon as their visas clear.
