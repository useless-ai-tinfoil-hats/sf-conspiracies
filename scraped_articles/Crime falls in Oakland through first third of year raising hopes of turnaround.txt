Crime falls in Oakland through first third of year, raising hopes of turnaround
-----
Oakland officials are hailing data showing that crime has fallen significantly, after a spike in 2023 drew national attention and raised concerns among residents about the direction of the city.Crime is down 33% overall in Oakland so far this year compared with the same period in 2023, according to the latest police statistics through April 28, with significant drops in categories including homicides, assaults, rapes and burglaries.
