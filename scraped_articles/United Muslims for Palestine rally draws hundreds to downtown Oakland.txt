‘United Muslims for Palestine’ rally draws hundreds to downtown Oakland
-----
Hundreds of Bay Area residents gathered in Oakland on Sunday in support of Palestinians.To the tune of chants including "Free, free, Palestine," attendees at the "United Muslims for Palestine" rally at downtown's Oscar Grant Plaza waved flags and carried signs calling for an end to the occupation of Palestinian territories held by Israel. The rally happened as Israel intensified strikes on Gaza and the West Bank in its conflict against Hamas.
