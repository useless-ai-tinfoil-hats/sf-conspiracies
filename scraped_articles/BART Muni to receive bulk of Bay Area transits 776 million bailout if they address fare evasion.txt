BART, Muni to receive bulk of Bay Area transit’s $776 million bailout, if they address fare evasion
-----
BART and the San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency will receive the overwhelming majority of $776 million in state and regional subsidies for Bay Area transit — if they take certain steps to address ongoing fare evasion on their trains and buses.The funds, which will be distributed by the Metropolitan Transportation Commission through fiscal year 2026, partially come from the Bay Area's share of the $1.1 billion transit bailout included in the California budget.
