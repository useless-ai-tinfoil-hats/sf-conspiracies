‘Like no nightmare I can ever imagine’: Sacramento man’s Israeli family held hostage by Hamas
-----
Two days after the Oct. 7 Hamas terrorist attack on Israel, Ryan Pessah's mother texted him an eerie video.Through a cloud of smoke and ash, a frantic-looking woman on the ground appears to be pleading as various indistinct figures grab at her, shouting and dragging her out of a door. Then the video pans to a slim bald man seated on the floor, his face anguished, his clothing askew.
