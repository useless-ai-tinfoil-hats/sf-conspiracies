Record-setting night: San Jose State women score 122 in 94-point win
-----
Two school records fell and career highs were in abundance Monday night as the San Jose State women's basketball team obliterated winless Bethesda University 122-28 at the Event Center.The Spartans set school records for margin of victory (94) and field goals (49), eclipsing the previous marks of 66 and 44, respectively, set Dec. 14, 2019, against La Verne. In its previous two games combined, San Jose State had scored 100 points.
