An Oakland man opened fire on two home invaders, killing one. The other is facing the murder charge
-----
Two men were toting guns when they broke into the back door of an East Oakland home last month, looking for valuables, according to court documents.It's unclear if they knew the residence on 102nd Street near International Boulevard was occupied, but the homeowner was there, also armed, according to police.
After an exchange of gunfire, one of the men, Charles Calloway, was dead. His alleged accomplice, David Washington, took off, only to be arrested 10 days later.
