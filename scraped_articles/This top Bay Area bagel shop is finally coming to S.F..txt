This top Bay Area bagel shop is finally coming to S.F.
-----
One of the Bay Area's most popular — and rapidly growing — bagel shops is coming to San Francisco for the first time.Berkeley's Boichik Bagels is opening three new locations in San Francisco this spring, as first reported by the San Francisco Business Times. They will be at 1946 Fillmore St. in Lower Pacific Heights, 22 Battery St. in the Financial District and 3665 Sacramento St. in Presidio Heights, owner Emily Winston confirmed to the Chronicle.
