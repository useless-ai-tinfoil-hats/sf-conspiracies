'Significant traffic congestion' expected during SF's Dreamforce
-----
Dreamforce kicks off Tuesday, welcoming up to 35,000 visitors to San Francisco and its Moscone Center. Salesforce's annual conference is bringing business to downtown restaurants and hotels, but also a few road closures that can create headaches for local drivers."Significant traffic congestion" is expected "in and around Moscone Center" between 7 a.m. and 7 p.m., through Thursday, Sept. 14, the San Francisco Municipal Transit Agency warned in a Sept. 7 news release.
