Kite surfer rescued from Santa Cruz County beach after writing 'help' with rocks
-----
A kite surfer was rescued from a Santa Cruz County beach after using rocks on the beach to spell out "HELP."In a news release posted Sunday, Cal Fire's San Mateo-Santa Cruz Unit said the surfer was stranded south of Davenport Landing and spotted and reported by a private helicopter. The surfer did not require any medical attention, just assistance getting off the beach, Cal Fire said.
