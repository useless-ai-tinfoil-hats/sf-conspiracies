Heavy winds trigger long delays at SFO as APEC begins
-----
The Federal Aviation Administration implemented an extensive ground delay for San Francisco International Airport on Tuesday morning just as world leaders began arriving for the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) conference.Some SFO arrivals are delayed an average of 98 minutes due to heavy winds generated by an inbound storm that's expected to drop 1 to 1.5 inches of rain on San Francisco this week.
