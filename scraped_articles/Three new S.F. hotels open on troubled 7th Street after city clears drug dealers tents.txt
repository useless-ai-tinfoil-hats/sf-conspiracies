Three new S.F. hotels open on troubled 7th Street after city clears drug dealers, tents
-----
Two months ago, nine tents and open drug use marked the blocks next to three shuttered hotels on South of Market's Seventh Street. Safety concerns prompted the federal government to advise workers to stay home and avoid coming into the nearby Federal Building.On Thursday, the tents and drug-dealing were gone, at least temporarily, as three newly renovated hotels opened for guests. Mayor London Breed arrived to tout the area's progress a year before she's up for reelection.
