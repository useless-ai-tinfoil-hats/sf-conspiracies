USWNT's match against Canada played on a ridiculous, dangerous field
-----
The U.S. women's national team's semifinal match of the CONCACAF W Gold Cup against Canada was ostensibly played on a field in San Diego. But after a night of torrential rain, it looked more like a lake, putting all 22 players' health on the line.The pitch at Snapdragon Stadium was so thoroughly waterlogged that large standing puddles of water were clearly visible on the Paramount+ broadcast, and even had the official contemplating whether to stop the game.
