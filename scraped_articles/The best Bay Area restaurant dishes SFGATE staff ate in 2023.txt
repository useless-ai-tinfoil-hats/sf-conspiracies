The best Bay Area restaurant dishes SFGATE staff ate in 2023
-----
Food is the glue that binds us here at SFGATE.We inhale authentic shrimp po'boys in Bayview and find the most New York-style pizza slices in the Tenderloin. We head north in search of the slurpiest oysters Marin has to offer, and wait in long night market lines for skewers of Chinese barbecue bliss. Our writers and editors find food nirvana in unexpected places, too, like a Michelin-cred gastropub in Lodi and a legit Hawaiian shave ice shop 60 miles east of San Francisco.
