Another S.F. theater is ditching the hierarchical leadership model
-----
Being good at sharing isn't always glamorous enough to make headlines, but for years Crowded Fire Theater has low-key excelled at it. The 26-year-old San Francisco theater company co-employs resident playwright Star Finch with Campo Santo, and shares administrative space and an office worker with Golden Thread Productions.
