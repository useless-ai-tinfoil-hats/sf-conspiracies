Shayna Rubin joins the Chronicle as Giants beat reporter
-----
From Sarah Feldberg, Editor of Emerging Product:The San Francisco Chronicle is thrilled to welcome Shayna Rubin as its new San Francisco Giants beat reporter.
Rubin joins the Sporting Green staff from the Bay Area News Group, where she reported on the Oakland A's stadium saga and the Golden State Warriors' 2022 championship run, among other things.
