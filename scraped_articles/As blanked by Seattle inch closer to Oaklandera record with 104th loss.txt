A's blanked by Seattle, inch closer to Oakland-era record with 104th loss
-----
The Oakland Athletics' final homestand of the season has dampened momentum for an over-.500 month to end the year.With 12 games to play, the A's loss for the 104th time Monday, a 5-0 defeat at the Coliseum to the Mariners to open a three-game set in front of a crowd of 4,972. The A's, losers of five in a row, are four defeats away from tying their Oakland-era record 108 losses in 1979.
