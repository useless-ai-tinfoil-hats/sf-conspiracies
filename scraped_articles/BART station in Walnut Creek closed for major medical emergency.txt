BART station in Walnut Creek closed for ‘major medical emergency’
-----
The Walnut Creek BART station was closed late Tuesday morning due to a "major medical emergency" after a person entered the trackway and "may" have collided with a train, officials for the transit agency said.Transit officials said that service had stopped between Pleasant Hill and Lafayette just after 11 a.m, and asked people to seek alternate means of transportation between Walnut Creek, Pleasant Hill and Lafayette stations.
BART officials said police found no signs of foul play
