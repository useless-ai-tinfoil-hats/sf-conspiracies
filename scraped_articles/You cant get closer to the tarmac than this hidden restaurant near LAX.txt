You can't get closer to the tarmac than this hidden restaurant near LAX
-----
Fly in and out of Los Angeles' LAX airport often enough, and you'll eventually hear someone proclaim that the In-N-Out Burger next to the airport is the destination for pre- or post-flight eats. Sure, Kevin Hart's vegan fast food joint Hart House now sells crispy chick'n sandwiches just a block away, and there are some real gems in the airport's surrounding areas (like the outstanding Ayara Thai), but for many, the promise of a classic burger with a clear view of planes taking off is reason
