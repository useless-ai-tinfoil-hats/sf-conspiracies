S.F. police arrest men suspected in string of frightening armed robberies in Mission District
-----
San Francisco police said they arrested two men suspected in a series of armed robberies in the Mission District, separate incidents in which the men allegedly held merchants at gunpoint while stealing money, merchandise and ATMs.The three robberies occurred between Sept. 26 and Oct. 4 "in and around the Mission District," all in the wee hours of the morning, according to a statement from the Police Department.
