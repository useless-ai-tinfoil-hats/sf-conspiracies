Pedestrian in East Oakland killed by hit-and-run driver, police say
-----
A hit-and-run driver struck and killed a pedestrian early Monday in East Oakland, police officials said.The crash happened just after 12:45 a.m. at the intersection of 75th Avenue and San Leandro Street, according to the Oakland Police Department. Authorities said they found an unresponsive, injured person and no driver at the scene.
Medical personnel pronounced the victim dead at the scene. The victim's identity has not yet been released by the Alameda County Coroner's Office,
