Oakland Ballers tout overwhelming interest in shareholder proposal
-----
Would-be baseball-team owners rejoice, at least on a small scale: The Oakland Ballers are moving ahead with their plan to offer fans the ability to buy a tiny slice of the operations.Better still, according to the Ballers and their legal filings: There will be a fan representative on the independent league team's five-member board of directors. Fan shareholders will elect their representative via proxy vote; all shareholders also can vote on issues such as the team moving and decisions
