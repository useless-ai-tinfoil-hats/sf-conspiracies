Google to limit access to California news sites amid fight with state lawmakers
-----
Google announced Friday that it will temporarily remove links to news websites in California for a small number of users, representing an escalation in the Bay Area tech giant's fight with state lawmakers over a bill authored by Assemblymember Buffy Wicks, a Democrat from Oakland.At issue is Assembly Bill 886, officially called the "California Journalism Preservation Act."
