Alameda agrees to $11M settlement in case of man who died after police restraint
-----
The city of Alameda will pay over $11 million to the family of Mario Gonzalez, who died in 2021 after being pinned to the ground by police.Gonzalez's death drew comparisons to the death of George Floyd in Minneapolis and became a touch point in a national debate over police response. It also sparked several investigations and legal battles between the city and Gonzalez's family.
