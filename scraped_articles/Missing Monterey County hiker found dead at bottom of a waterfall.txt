Missing Monterey County hiker found dead at bottom of a waterfall
-----
The body of a Monterey County woman, who never returned from a hike Monday, was found at the bottom of a waterfall on Friday, bearing injuries consistent with falling down a cliff, the Monterey County sheriff said.Caroline Meister, 30, was reported missing Monday night from the Tassajara Zen Mountain Center, a remote Zen training center, according to Monterey County Sheriff Tina Nieto. Authorities believed Meister had gone for a hike that morning in the Carmel Valley on the Horse Pasture
