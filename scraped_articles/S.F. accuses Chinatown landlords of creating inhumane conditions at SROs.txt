S.F. accuses Chinatown landlords of creating ‘inhumane’ conditions at SROs
-----
San Francisco officials are suing a group of Chinatown property owners they say ignored years of tenant complaints and warnings from officials alleging their living conditions have been "inhumane."City Attorney David Chiu announced Tuesday that the city is suing Jeff Appendrodt, Shailendra Devdhara and Kamlesh Patel, all of whom own single room occupancy hotels and are accused of ignoring years of tenant complaints and notices of code violations from the Department of Building
