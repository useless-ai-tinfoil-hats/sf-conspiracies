Oakland violence: 5 injured in 4 shootings within hours of each other
-----
Oakland police are investigating four shootings that occurred within hours of each other Saturday night and injured five people, all of whom survived. One victim was reportedly a 16-year-old girl.Officers responded to a ShotSpotter activation just after 7:30 p.m. Saturday on the 1300 block of Mandela Parkway, the Oakland Police Department said in a statement. A victim was treated for gunshot wounds and transported to a hospital, where he was listed in stable condition Sunday.
