Dozens evacuated from Bay Area senior center after suspected sewage leak
-----
More than 50 people living at a senior residential center in the East Bay were temporarily relocated to a hotel Sunday after a sewer leak in their building forced Pacific Gas and Electric Co. to cut the power until repairs are made, officials said Monday.Firefighters were called Sunday to Oak Grove Senior Terrace Apartments on Carol Lane in Oakley after the building's elevator stopped working, according to Steve Aubert, a spokesperson for the Contra Costa County Fire Protection District.
