How offstage tragedies made ‘Rent’ seem even more real
-----
Some 19 years before the phenomenon of "Hamilton," there was "Rent."Back in 1996, the baby-faced cast of the future Tony- and Pulitzer Prize-winning Broadway musical found themselves in the middle of a cultural explosion. Fans known as Rent-heads camped out for hours to score a $20 rush ticket, a younger, hipper demographic that planted its flag on Broadway.
