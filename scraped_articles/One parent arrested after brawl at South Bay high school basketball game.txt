One parent arrested after brawl at South Bay high school basketball game
-----
A brawl broke out just after a boys high school basketball game in the South Bay this week, reportedly leading to one parent's arrest and multiple ongoing investigations.A heated contest Tuesday night between the host Gunn High School of Palo Alto and Santa Clara's Wilcox High School ended with a one-point overtime win for Gunn. The outcome led to several minutes of contentious blustering immediately after the game, according to videos reviewed by SFGATE, and then pushing and shoving
