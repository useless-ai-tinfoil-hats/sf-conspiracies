South Bay man pleads guilty to setting additional fires during Dixie Fire, trapping firefighters
-----
A San Jose man pleaded guilty to going on an "arson-setting spree" during the destructive Dixie Fire in 2021, sometimes trapping firefighters battling the blazes, according to federal prosecutors.While the Dixie Fire was burning nearly 1 million acres of land across Butte, Plumas, Shasta, Lassen and Tehama counties, Gary Stephen Maynard, 49, was lighting several fires of his own, according to the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Eastern District of California.
