'This could have happened to any of us': San Francisco grieves loss of family in crash
-----
Mountains of flower bouquets and stuffed animals adorned a sidewalk in San Francisco's West Portal neighborhood Monday night, marking where a driver plowed into a bus shelter and killed three members of a local family.The community is mourning the tragic loss of the family, whom the SF Chief Medical Examiner's Office identified to SFGATE on Tuesday as Matilde Moncada Ramos Pinto, 38; Diego Cardoso de Oliveira, 40; and their 1-year-old son Joaquin Ramos Pinto de Oliveira.
