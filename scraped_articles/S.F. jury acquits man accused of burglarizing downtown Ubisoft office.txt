S.F. jury acquits man accused of burglarizing downtown Ubisoft office
-----
A San Francisco jury acquitted a man of felony burglary charges related to a July break-in at the downtown headquarters of a gaming company, the city's Public Defender's Office announced Tuesday.Public defenders claimed the San Francisco Police Department "carried out an incomplete investigation that was riddled with confirmation bias and ignored the fact that the evidence did not support the allegations" against defendant David Wesser, 37.
Wesser has been booked at least 17 times
