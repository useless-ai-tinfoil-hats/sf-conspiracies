Suspects allegedly steal $23K worth of Lululemon, leading police on a multicity North Bay pursuit
-----
Three minors and one adult led police on a pursuit through four Bay Area counties on Monday after allegedly stealing more than $23,000 worth of Lululemon products.The pursuit started around 4 p.m. after Napa police were alerted about a theft at the Lululemon store on First Street in Napa, the department said in a social media post. The suspects allegedly took more than 200 pairs of leggings from the store.
