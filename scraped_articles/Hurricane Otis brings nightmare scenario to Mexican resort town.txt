Hurricane Otis brings nightmare scenario to Mexican resort town
-----
Hurricane Otis ripped through the resort town of Acapulco on Mexico's southern Pacific coast early Wednesday morning as a Category 5 storm with winds of 165 mph, the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration said.The fierce storm that many forecasters said intensified unexpectedly triggered landslides, flooded roadways, knocked out power and all communications, and smashed buildings and hotels.
