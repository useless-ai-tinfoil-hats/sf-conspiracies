These are the next BART stations to get new entry gates designed to reduce fare evasion
-----
BART's Civic Center station, which has one of the highest rates of fare evasion in the rail system, will be among the first batch of stations to get the transit agency's new, hardened entry gates.The downtown San Francisco station is among the first batch of eight stations that will have their waist-level, fin-like gates replaced with the taller, sturdier entries meant to prevent fare evasion.
