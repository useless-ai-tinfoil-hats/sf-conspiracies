Infant dies after Saturday's deadly crash in San Francisco's West Portal
-----
LATEST March 20, 6:03 p.m. An infant whose parents and brother were killed in a crash in San Francisco's West Portal neighborhood on Saturday has died, according to NBC Bay Area and The San Francisco Chronicle. (The Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms.)Attempts by SFGATE to contact San Francisco Supervisor Myrna Melgar, who represents the West Portal area, were unsuccessful on Wednesday evening.
