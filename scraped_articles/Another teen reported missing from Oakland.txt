Another teen reported missing from Oakland
-----
On Monday, Oakland police sought the public's help in locating a teenager who has been missing for several days.Joseph Eisner-Gordon, 17, was last seen on the evening of Sept. 22, heading west on the 700 block of E. 11th Street, police said.
He was wearing a black sweatshirt, a black T-shirt and black sweatpants, according to a description provided by police. He is 5-feet, 5-inches tall and weighs 140 pounds, with brown hair and brown eyes.
