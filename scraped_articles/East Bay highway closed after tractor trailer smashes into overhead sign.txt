East Bay highway closed after tractor trailer smashes into overhead sign
-----
Northbound Interstate 680 at Stoneridge Drive in the East Bay was closed Wednesday morning and remained shut down to traffic as of 10:30 a.m. after a tractor trailer smashed into an overhead highway sign, CHP said.The closure occurred around 7 a.m.
CHP asked drivers in this area to use exits south of Stoneridge Drive or state Route 84 east into Livermore as a detour.
