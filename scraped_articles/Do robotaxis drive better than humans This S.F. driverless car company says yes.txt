Do robotaxis drive better than humans? This S.F. driverless car company says yes
-----
A new study by Waymo says the company's fully driverless taxis have a crash rate in San Francisco that is 90% lower than that of human drivers in the city.The peer-reviewed study, published Wednesday, is the latest in a series of reports by the Alphabet-owned company examining its robotaxis' performance, and is the first to attempt to compare how fully driverless Waymo taxis compare to human driving.
