Chance of rain possible for these Bay Area cities. Here's what to expect this weekend
-----
UPDATE: Heavy rain, thunderstorms and mountain snow are in California forecastThe stage is set for unsettled weather across the Bay Area this weekend. Over the next few days, a low-pressure system will usher in showers and breezy conditions, starting with a batch of rain in the North Bay on Saturday morning. More widespread showers are expected by afternoon and into Sunday as the system's humid winds extend into San Francisco Bay.
