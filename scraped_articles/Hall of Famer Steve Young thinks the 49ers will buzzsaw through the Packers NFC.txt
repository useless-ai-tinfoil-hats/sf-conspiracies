Hall of Famer Steve Young thinks the 49ers will 'buzzsaw' through the Packers, NFC
-----
Hall of Fame quarterback Steve Young believes the San Francisco 49ers should already act as though the NFC is theirs.The former Niners quarterback previewed Saturday's playoff game against the Packers — along with ostensibly the rest of the postseason — on his weekly appearance on KNBR's "Tolbert and Copes" show on Wednesday.
