Bay Area artists join open letter to Biden calling for cease-fire in Israel-Hamas war
-----
Thousands of artists and writers, including prominent figures from the Bay Area, have penned open letters urging President Joe Biden to de-escalate and secure a cease-fire in the ongoing Israel-Hamas war.Hollywood heavyweights such as Bradley Cooper, Cate Blanchett and Alan Cumming, alongside musicians Michael Stipe, Dua Lipa and Ani DiFranco, are among the signatories.
