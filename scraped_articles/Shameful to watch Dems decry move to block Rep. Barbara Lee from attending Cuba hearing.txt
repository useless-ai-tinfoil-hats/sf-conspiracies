‘Shameful to watch’: Dems decry move to block Rep. Barbara Lee from attending Cuba hearing
-----
WASHINGTON — Rep. Barbara Lee was removed from a hearing on Cuba policy Thursday, a move she says amounted to censorship by the Republican chairwoman.The House Foreign Affairs Western Hemisphere Subcommittee hearing started with a request by Rep. Joaquin Castro, D-Texas, that two lawmakers who were not members of the committee be allowed to attend the hearing on the Biden administration's Cuba policy.
