S.F. police arrest 9 in ‘blitz’ operation to curb retail theft at former Westfield mall
-----
San Francisco police staked out a popular downtown mall and arrested nine people they suspected of shoplifting — the latest in a series of "blitz" operations to curb organized retail theft.Bolstered by a $15.3 million state grant awarded to the Police Department in September, police are tackling the problem surgically, dispatching several officers at once to a particular department store, shopping center or pharmacy.
Tuesday night's operation required uniformed and plainclothes
