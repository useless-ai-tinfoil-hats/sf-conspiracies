Pelosi, Jeffries wade into Bay Area House race after flyer includes their images
-----
Top Democratic leaders waded into a Bay Area House race after they said voters received flyers that wrongly imply they endorsed the Democratic incumbent's challenger.Charlene Nijmeh, chair of the Castro Valley-based Muwekma Ohlone tribe, is running against Rep. Zoe Lofgren, D-San Jose, who's represented the district since 1995.
