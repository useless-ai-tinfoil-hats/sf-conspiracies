Shooting in S.F.'s Tenderloin leaves man dead, another injured
-----
One man died and another was injured after a shooting in the Tenderloin on Saturday night, according to San Francisco police.The shooting happened around 9 p.m. on Jones Street near the intersection with McAllister Street, according to the Police Department. When police arrived, they found one man suffering from a life-threatening gunshot wound and another with non-life threatening injuries, officials said.
