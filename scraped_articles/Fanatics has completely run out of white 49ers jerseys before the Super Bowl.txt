Fanatics has completely run out of white 49ers jerseys before the Super Bowl
-----
The frustration that San Francisco 49ers fans have with Fanatics, the oft-maligned sports merchandise company, is growing with each day that the white jerseys the 49ers will wear for the Super Bowl aren't available for purchase.Now, we have an answer as to why the team's standard away jerseys aren't available almost anywhere you can purchase NFL jerseys: The company literally ran out of them.
