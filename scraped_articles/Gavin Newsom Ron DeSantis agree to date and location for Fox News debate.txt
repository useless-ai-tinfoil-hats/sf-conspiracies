Gavin Newsom, Ron DeSantis agree to date and location for Fox News debate
-----
After weeks of negotiations, the debate between California Gov. Gavin Newsom and Florida Gov. Ron DeSantis is set for Nov. 30 in Georgia.The 90-minute debate, which will be moderated by Fox News commentator Sean Hannity, will take place at a site to be determined.
