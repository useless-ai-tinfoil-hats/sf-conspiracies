Body recovered from Folsom Lake identified as missing S.F. woman
-----
A body found on Folsom Lake last month has been identified as a 27-year-old woman from San Francisco.On Aug. 19, a person visiting Folsom Lake discovered a body near Rattlesnake Bar. On Wednesday, officials with the El Dorado County Sheriff's Office identified the body as Natalee Heilaman.
Heilaman had been reported missing out of Nevada County — located northeast of Folsom Lake — in January.
