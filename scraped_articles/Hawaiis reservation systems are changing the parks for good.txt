Hawaii's reservation systems are changing the parks for good
-----
A few years since Hawaii enacted reservations and fees at some of its most popular spots, the parks are seeing improvements by balancing their visitor capacity and conservation needs. They're also seeing an infusion of funds like never before."It's the largest budget we've ever had. We're approaching like $40 million a year.
