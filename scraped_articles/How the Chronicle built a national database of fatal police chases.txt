How the Chronicle built a national database of fatal police chases
-----
To build the Chronicle's national dataset of at least 3,336 people killed in police vehicle pursuits from 2017 through 2022, we used information from three primary sources: the federal government, private research organizations and our reporting.While no government agency counts every police pursuit death, the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration comes closest.
