Bay Area radio legend Joe McConnell shares secrets from the road
-----
Let's check in with Joe McConnell, who for decades was a treasured voice of traffic for the Bay Area.The longtime radio personality could be heard on several stations in the region, but he's perhaps best recognized for his nearly four decades at KQED, which finished up last fall. Months before filing his final report, McConnell suffered a freak accident that bewildered his doctor. One morning, the broadcaster woke up and couldn't deliver his words. He had been rendered speechless.
