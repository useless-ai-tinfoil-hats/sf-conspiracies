SF tech exec buys one of the country's most well-known, loved media companies
-----
When the news emerged Thursday that someone had bought the Onion, the beloved satirical news outlet, details were sparse and strange.A message from G/O Media CEO Jim Spanfeller to staff, shared by Axios media reporter Sara Fischer, simply said that G/O had sold the Onion to a Chicago-based firm made up of "four digital media veterans" called Global Tetrahedron. If the name feels gag-worthy, it is: The satirical paper has used the name for years in parodies of ridiculous business
