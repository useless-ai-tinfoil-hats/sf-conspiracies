Three who died after vehicle fell down cliff at Devil's Slide are identified
-----
LATEST July 28, 3:30 p.m. Three people who died in the vehicle that went off a cliff at Devil's Slide have been identified.The San Mateo County Coroner's office identified the crash victims as Brylyn Aroma, 36, a woman from Fort Riley, Kansas; Mohammad Noory, 29, of South San Francisco; and Angelica Gacho, 28, of South San Francisco.
July 27, 2:00 p.m. A third person has been pronounced dead after a vehicle plunged over a cliff at Devil's Slide on Friday morning.
