Mahaney, Saxen propel St. Mary’s past Gonzaga in WCC tourney title game
-----
LAS VEGAS — On a familiar and boisterous stage, against a familiar and imposing rival, St. Mary's unleashed its old-school style of basketball Tuesday night — and scaled the mountain.The Gaels played methodically on offense and tenaciously on defense, slowing Gonzaga's high-powered offense in the WCC tournament championship game. St. Mary's seized an early lead, withstood several Bulldogs charges and emerged with a 69-60 victory at Orleans Arena.
