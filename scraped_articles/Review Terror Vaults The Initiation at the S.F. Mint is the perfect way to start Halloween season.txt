Review: Terror Vault’s ‘The Initiation’ at the S.F. Mint is the perfect way to start Halloween season
-----
If you haven't yet joined the cult of Peaches Christ, "The Initiation" she has masterminded will convert you.Got a favorite kind of scare? The walk-through show, the sixth Terror Vault haunted attraction at the San Francisco Mint by Into the Dark, the production company composed of Peaches Christ, David Flower Productions and Non Plus Ultra, has just the effect to make your stomach lurch, the nerves in the back of your neck pinch and your breath choke off.
