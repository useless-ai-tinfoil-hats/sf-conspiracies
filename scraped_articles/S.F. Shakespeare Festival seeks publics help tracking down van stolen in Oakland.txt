S.F. Shakespeare Festival seeks public’s help tracking down van stolen in Oakland
-----
San Francisco Shakespeare Festival representatives are asking the public to keep an eye out for their 12-passenger white van, which they said was stolen last month in Oakland.The van, which actors used to take plays on the road as part of Shakespeare on Tour, was stolen Feb. 20 while it was parked in the area of 62nd and Fremont streets, said Executive Director Toby Leavitt. Cast members of "As You Like It" met there — near the home of one of the actors — to take the play to a school
