Arnold Schwarzenegger ‘more of a machine’ following recent surgery
-----
Arnold Schwarzenegger recently underwent a surgical procedure that he said made him "more of a machine," like his iconic character in "The Terminator."The 76-year-old action star and former California governor shared the revelation on his podcast, Arnold's Pump Club, on Monday, March 25, revealing that he had a pacemaker implanted.
"I have some personal news to share. Last Monday, I had surgery for a pacemaker and became a little bit more of a machine," Schwarzenegger said.
