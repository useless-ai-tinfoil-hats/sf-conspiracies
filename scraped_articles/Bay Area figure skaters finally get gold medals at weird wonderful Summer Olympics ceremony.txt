Bay Area figure skaters finally get gold medals at weird, wonderful Summer Olympics ceremony
-----
PARIS — The fans thronged into Trocadéro, drawn by a free Olympic event and a super cool venue next to the Eiffel Tower. The August sun beat down and temperatures soared to about 80 degrees.And the U.S. figure-skating team was awarded its gold medals from the Beijing 2022 Winter Olympics.
That's not a misprint. That was what happened in one of the oddest, and ultimately moving, ceremonies in Olympic history.
