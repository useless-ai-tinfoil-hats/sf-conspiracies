Letters: Why fare evasion on Muni is not as common as people think it is
-----
Regarding "BART, Muni to receive bulk of Bay Area transit's $776 million bailout, if they address fare evasion" (Bay Area, SFChronicle.com, Nov. 26): The article unfairly impugns the high rates of fare compliance on Muni.Data unearthed by transit advocate Chris Arvin and posted on Twitter/X shows that when fare inspections are conducted, the compliance rate was at about 90%, at least on the 38 Geary in 2022.
