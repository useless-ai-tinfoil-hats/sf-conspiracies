Are the Mission District’s streets cleaner after S.F.’s illegal vending ban?
-----
Street cleaning requests in the Mission District dropped 21% in the three months after a ban on illegal sidewalk vending versus 6% citywide, prompting city officials to argue the ban led to cleaner streets — as well as less chaos and safer neighborhood conditions.Street cleaning requests through the city's 311 system for just the area where the ban was in place were down 23% during the three months after it compared with the three months prior, Department of Public Works spokesperson
