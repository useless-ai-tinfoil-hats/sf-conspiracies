Oakland Police Commission agrees to reopen hiring process for chief
-----
The Oakland Police Commission announced Thursday that it will reopen its application process for the position of the city's police chief and send another list of finalists to the mayor in March.Chair Marsha Peterson made the announcement after the commission met behind closed doors and hours after Mayor Sheng Thao's office said she had asked the commissioners to take another look at the current pool of applicants, as well as consider new ones.
