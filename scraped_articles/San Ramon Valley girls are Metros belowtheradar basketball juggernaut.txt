San Ramon Valley girls are Metro’s below-the-radar basketball juggernaut
-----
It seems nearly impossible to go 25-1 and go unnoticed.To average 67 points per game and allow just 34.4 against many of the best Metro teams would normally grab headlines.
But San Ramon Valley-Danville's girls basketball team — in the midst of another sparkling season under eighth-year coach John Cristiano — is sitting in the dark, under the long and deep shadow cast by Mitty, the nation's No. 1 team.
