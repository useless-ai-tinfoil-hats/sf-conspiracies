Antioch police shoot man they say tried to flee during investigation
-----
Antioch police said they shot and wounded a man Sunday morning after he tried to flee during an investigation.The incident began around 7:40 a.m. as officers investigated a suspicious vehicle on West Third Street near the city's waterfront and found a man inside the car with a firearm, the Antioch Police Department said in a news release.
