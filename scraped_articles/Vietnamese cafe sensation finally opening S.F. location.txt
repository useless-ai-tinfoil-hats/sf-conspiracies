Vietnamese cafe sensation finally opening S.F. location
-----
A popular cafe the Los Angeles Times once called "the Vietnamese American Starbucks" is ready to launch its long-awaited and first San Francisco location.Southern Californian chain 7 Leaves is set to open at 3931 Alemany Blvd., a few steps from San Francisco's Korean grocer H Mart in Ingleside Heights. The shop's first day in business is slated for Thursday, May 9, according to franchise operator Alex Anderman.
