Tasting menus shouldn’t be longer than ‘Oppenheimer’
-----
Here's associate restaurant critic Cesar Hernandez.Tasting menus are too long.
When a dinner makes me look forward to going home, there's a problem. I think menus with double-digit courses could use condensing. Of course there are some amazing restaurants where time melts away, but usually there's little reason for a dinner to run longer than the latest Christopher Nolan flick.
