A's late rally lifts them past Rockies and out of last place in the AL West
-----
Last-place Western Division teams met at the Coliseum on Tuesday night. The Colorado Rockies, in the NL West cellar, and the Oakland Athletics, at the bottom of the AL West, each hit three home runs and one team ended the evening out of last place.That was the A's, who halted their losing streak at eight games with a 5-4 decision. With the Angels falling 6-5 in 10 innings at Texas, Oakland moved into fourth place, a half-game ahead of the Angels.
