Cautious optimism as new cases of HIV among San Francisco Latinos decrease
-----
For years, Latinos represented the biggest share of new HIV cases in San Francisco, but testing data suggests the tide may be turning.The number of Latinos newly testing positive for HIV dropped 46% from 2022 to 2023, according to a preliminary report released in July by the San Francisco Department of Public Health.
