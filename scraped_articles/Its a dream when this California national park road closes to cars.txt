It's a dream when this California national park road closes to cars
-----
On a moody afternoon in Redwood National and State Parks, some 300 miles north of San Francisco, I was pedaling up Newton B. Drury Scenic Parkway. Towering redwoods flank the old two-lane road, a main artery and park attraction that was paved in the 1920s as part of the historic Redwood Highway.
