49ers legend Jerry Rice leaves signed drinks in Bay Area Costco
-----
If you're looking for an unusual piece of 49ers memorabilia, a Bay Area Costco may have you covered.On Thursday, Niners legend Jerry Rice made a special appearance at the Hayward Costco on Hesperian Boulevard to promote his sports energy drink G.O.A.T. Fuel. Cases of the product were left behind, and Rice wrote on Instagram that he had signed them.
