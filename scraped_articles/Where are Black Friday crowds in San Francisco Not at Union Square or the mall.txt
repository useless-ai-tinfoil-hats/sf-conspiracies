Where are Black Friday crowds in San Francisco? Not at Union Square or the mall
-----
About 10 people had lined up outside the San Francisco Centre on Market Street to be among the first shoppers walking in when mall workers threw open the doors for Black Friday.In years past, John and Michelle McGuire would leave their San Francisco home to head downtown, arriving at the mall before dawn and joining throngs of eager shoppers looking for deals only getable on this day.
But this year, the kids were grown and the best deals were online. So the McGuires waited in line
