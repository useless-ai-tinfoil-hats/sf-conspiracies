S.F. police arrest man after hours-long Park Merced standoff
-----
San Francisco police said they arrested a man potentially suffering from a mental health emergency with whom they were locked in a tense standoff since early Monday morning.Police officers responded to the 400 block of Gonzalez Drive in the Park Merced neighborhood after 6 a.m., after receiving a call of a person breaking into a home and making threats, spokespersons said.
