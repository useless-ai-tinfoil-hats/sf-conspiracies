‘Everything is about Oakland’ as fans fill Raimondi Park for Ballers’ home opener
-----
Tuesday night's inaugural Oakland Ballers home game felt like a big deal, a real happening.Raimondi Park hadn't hosted a pro game in more than 100 years, and the combination of the fledgling independent-league team and the revitalization of a historic field in West Oakland was irresistible. Even a former president of the Baseball Hall of Fame, Jeff Idelson, was in the crowd.
