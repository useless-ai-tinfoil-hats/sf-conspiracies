Kamala Harris called for a cease-fire in Gaza. Some progressives are unsatisfied
-----
WASHINGTON — Vice President Kamala Harris' call Sunday for an immediate, temporary cease-fire in Gaza — which marked a forceful shift for the administration — has received a mixed response from Democrats, and some progressives have characterized it as too little, too late.The Israel-Hamas war has become a key flash point in the Democratic Party this election season, with liberal voters reconsidering which candidates they will support based on their position on the war.
