Inside the Salesforce Tower's 62nd floor overlooking San Francisco
-----
There are 61 floors on the Salesforce Tower. Unless you're Jim Campbell or Emma Strebel, in which case there are 62.Right above the penthouse, beyond stacks of corporate offices and three floors of WeWorks, sits the employee-only top floor. It's a mechanical area with no walls, save for glass windows and the top of the tower's aluminum shell, with catwalks standing overhead.
