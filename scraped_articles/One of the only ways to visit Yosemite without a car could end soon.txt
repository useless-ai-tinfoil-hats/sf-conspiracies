One of the only ways to visit Yosemite without a car could end soon
-----
A one-of-a-kind option for visiting Yosemite National Park could face the budgetary guillotine if officials decide against funding the only public transportation into the park from Fresno.The bus is the only way travelers flying into the Fresno Yosemite International Airport can access Yosemite without a car. However, if left unfunded, the public transit option could end as soon as the start of summer.
There was a record demand for Yosemite last year, with multihour traffic jams
