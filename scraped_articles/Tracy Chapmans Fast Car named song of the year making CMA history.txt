Tracy Chapman’s ‘Fast Car’ named song of the year, making CMA history
-----
Tracy Chapman has again made history with her song "Fast Car," which was released more than three decades ago.The somber folk ballad was honored as the song of the year at the 57th annual Country Music Awards on Wednesday, Nov. 8, following a cover by singer Luke Combs that topped the country charts earlier this year.
