SF is saving Trocadero Clubhouse, a damaged roadhouse that's a historic 'gem'
-----
In March 2023, a eucalyptus tree toppled onto a San Francisco landmark in Stern Grove, damaging it so badly that officials at the time described the event as "soul crushing." The historic Trocadero Clubhouse suffered a crushed roof, interior flooding from its busted sprinkler system and an uncertain future as the city assessed the feasibility of repairs.Now, almost a year later, reconstruction has officially begun to save the building, the San Francisco Recreation and Park Department
