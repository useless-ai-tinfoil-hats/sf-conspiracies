Pregnant woman found slain in San Mateo home, boyfriend arrested
-----
A man was arrested on suspicion of killing his pregnant girlfriend in San Mateo on Sunday, police said in a statement.Officers responded to a call Sunday at 6:01 p.m. that a nine-month pregnant woman was in "full arrest," police wrote. Paramedics declared the 37-year-old woman and her "unborn child" dead at the home in the 1300 block of South B Street.
After gathering witness statements, detectives believed the woman's boyfriend, Andrew Coleman, was the likely suspect, police
