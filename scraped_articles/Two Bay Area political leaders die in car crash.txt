Two Bay Area political leaders die in car crash
-----
Two longtime East Bay political leaders — married couple Peggy Moore and Hope Wood — died Friday night in a car crash in San Diego County, according to friends.The two met while working on President Barack Obama's campaign in 2008 and went on to found the political consulting firm Hope Action For Change in 2019. Moore also spent three years working as a special projects manager for former Oakland Mayor Libby Schaaf.
