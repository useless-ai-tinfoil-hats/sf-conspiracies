How Giants first-rounders Will Bednar, Reggie Crawford are faring in fall league
-----
Some years, the Arizona Fall League is a showcase for older, more prominent minor-leaguers, but the San Francisco Giants' two biggest names playing for Scottsdale this year are lower-level players who need to get as much game action as possible.That's why there's little concern about Will Bednar and Reggie Crawford, the Giants' 2021 and 2022 first-round picks. Injuries have restricted Bednar, 23, to 20 appearances, including 19 starts, and Crawford, the 22-year-old two-way player, to 24
