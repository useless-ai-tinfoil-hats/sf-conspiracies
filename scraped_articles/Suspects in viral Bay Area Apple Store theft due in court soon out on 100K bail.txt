Suspects in viral Bay Area Apple Store theft due in court soon, out on $100K bail
-----
Two men charged in a burglary spree of two East Bay Apple Stores are set to appear in court on May 20 for their pretrial hearings, according to Alameda County Court records.The 23-year-old man and 28-year-old man are accused of being involved in organized retail thefts of Apple Stores in Berkeley and Emeryville between Nov. 27, 2023, and Feb. 7, 2024, the Alameda County District Attorney's Office said in a Feb. 16 news release.
Emeryville police arrested the 23-year-old on Feb. 7
