Here’s how S.F. tourists can ride in Waymo’s driverless robotaxis
-----
The ability to hail a driverless Waymo robotaxi in San Francisco is still not fully open to the public, but the company says there's a way to experience its autonomous vehicles if you're visiting the city.The Alphabet-owned company began deploying its white-and-black robotaxis for commercial driverless ride-hailing in late August after state regulators approved Waymo's unlimited expansion in San Francisco. The vehicles tend to attract curiosity from tourists visiting San Francisco
