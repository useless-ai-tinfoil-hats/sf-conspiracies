Rare animal discovered dead in 'ghost gear' by San Francisco
-----
An endangered Pacific leatherback sea turtle was found dead in a discarded crab trap off California's Farallon Islands last week, marking yet another grim discovery for marine biologists and U.S. officials."Leatherbacks along the West Coast have declined so far in number that the loss of any leatherback is sad and serious," Michael Milstein, a National Oceanic and Atmospheric Association public affairs officer, told SFGATE.
