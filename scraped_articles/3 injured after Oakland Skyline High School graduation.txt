3 injured after Oakland Skyline High School graduation
-----
A shooting after a graduation ceremony at Skyline High School in Oakland injured three people and sent graduates and their families running, police and witnesses said.Multiple people were detained after Thursday night's attack, and police sealed off the area around the high school for hours before they deemed the scene safe. Police later arrested one person.
