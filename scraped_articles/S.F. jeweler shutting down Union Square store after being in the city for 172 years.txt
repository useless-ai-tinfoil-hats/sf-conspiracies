S.F. jeweler shutting down Union Square store after being in the city for 172 years
-----
Shreve & Co., the historic San Francisco jeweler that has been in the city for 172 years, is shutting down its Union Square store and consolidating operations at its two Palo Alto locations.In a statement released Monday, Shreve managing partner Lane Schiffman thanked customers "for allowing us to be a part of your life's most precious moments."
