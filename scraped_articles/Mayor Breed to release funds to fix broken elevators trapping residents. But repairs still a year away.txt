Mayor Breed to release funds to fix broken elevators trapping residents. But repairs still a year away
-----
For more than a year, San Francisco Mayor London Breed delayed spending $10 million to repair faulty elevators throughout dozens of the city's aging single-room-occupancy hotels for formerly homeless people, effectively trapping residents.Last week, her administration agreed to release the money following inquiries from the Chronicle.
