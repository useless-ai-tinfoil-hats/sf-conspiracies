Ubiquitous Bay Area pizza chain opens more restaurants in Texas
-----
A well-loved pizza chain with roots in the Bay Area is expanding to Texas again.Round Table Pizza, which was acquired by Fat Brands in 2021 through its purchase of Global Franchise Group, recently opened a new parlor near Houston, in Richmond, Texas, according to a press release. It is the sixth outpost in the Lone Star state.
The brand, which was founded in 1959 in Menlo Park, California, now has more than 410 Round Table locations; California is home to 338 of them. Bill Larson
