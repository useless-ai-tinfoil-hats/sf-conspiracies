S.F. doughnut shop worker riding scooter to work critically injured in hit-run, police say
-----
A man remained in critical condition this week after being struck by a hit-and-run driver on his way to work at a San Francisco doughnut shop over the weekend, according to authorities and his employer.Just before 3 a.m. Sunday, Jesus Zamudio was riding an electric scooter to his job at Bob's Donuts when a vehicle struck him at Duboce Avenue and Guerrero Street, according to statements from San Francisco police officials and the business's owner.
