Bay Area's famed seafood shack the Marshall Store opens second location
-----
The Marshall Store, an iconic seafood restaurant once named one of the 50 best restaurants in the country by the New York Times, is expanding with a new Tomales restaurant called Out the Door, which opened June 21.Out the Door is take-out only, serving the same style food as the Marshall Store — tacos, ceviche and campechana — but with Tomales Bay residents and beachgoers in mind.
