San Francisco resident identified as victim in Half Moon Bay plane crash
-----
The San Mateo County Coroner's Office identified the woman whose body was found floating near where an airplane crashed in the waters off Half Moon Bay on Sunday evening.The victim was identified as Emma Pearl Willmer-Shiles, 27, a San Francisco resident.
A commercial fishing boat found her body floating about a mile south of where crews found pieces of the plane that had floated to shore in the wreckage, San Mateo County Sheriff's Office's officials said.
