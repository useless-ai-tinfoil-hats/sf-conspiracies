Robotaxis debuted in two U.S. cities. Only S.F. has a problem with it
-----
Driverless cars roam downtown's compact streets at night, picking up patrons from bars and restaurants. A parade of them calmly arrives at the airport, dropping off hurried travelers. It's not uncommon to catch them on the freeway, even if they're not carrying riders.They do all this with little regulation, attracting little concern from public officials or residents, many of whom eagerly embrace the idea of people ditching their private automobiles to ride in a robotaxi.
