Over protests from students, faculty and some state leaders, California State University trustees on Wednesday voted to raise tuition by 34% over five years beginning next fall, with an annual increase of 6%.The price hikes are the first since 2017 and will infuse CSU with $840 million through 2028-29. A third of the money will be used to buy back tuition entirely for about 60% of undergraduates.
