A gorgeous Yosemite phenomenon that lasts only a few minutes a day
-----
Some people will argue that California has some of the best sunsets in the world. But in Yosemite, gorgeous colors are spotted not only in the sky but also on the mountain peaks.With the right atmospheric conditions and perfect timing, visitors witness one of the park's most gorgeous moments when the hills blush. The phenomenon, which lasts only a few minutes, is called alpenglow.
