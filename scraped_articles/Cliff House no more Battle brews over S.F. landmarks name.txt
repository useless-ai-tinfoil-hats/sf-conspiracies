Cliff House no more? Battle brews over S.F. landmark’s name
-----
When the San Francisco landmark long known as the Cliff House reopens next year, will it be called the Cliff House?Not without a major payout.
The previous operators of the iconic oceanfront property, Dan and Mary Hountalas, have held Cliff House trademarks since the 1980s, which they say gives them "incontestable" rights to the name. This would prevent the new tenant, San Francisco attorney Alexander Leff, from using it — unless he's willing to pay.
