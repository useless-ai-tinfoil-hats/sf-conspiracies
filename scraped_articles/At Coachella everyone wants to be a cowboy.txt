At Coachella, everyone wants to be a cowboy
-----
If you had closed your eyes and thrown a rock at the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival last weekend, you probably would have hit a cowboy. Or an Angeleno dressed like one.That may be an exaggeration but not by much more than a horse hair. It was nearly impossible to look anywhere around the Empire Polo Club grounds without spotting the familiar swoop of a cowboy hat.
