S.F. officials call for sustained Gaza cease-fire after tense meeting
-----
San Francisco supervisors have joined the ranks of local legislative bodies calling for a sustained cease-fire in Gaza.The Board of Supervisors voted on Tuesday in favor of a resolution spearheaded by Supervisor Dean Preston that urges the Biden administration and Congress to support a cease-fire in the Israel-Hamas war, along with the delivery of humanitarian aid and the release of all hostages in the region.
