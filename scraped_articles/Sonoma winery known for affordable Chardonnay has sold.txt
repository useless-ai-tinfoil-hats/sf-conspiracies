Sonoma winery known for affordable Chardonnay has sold
-----
Toad Hollow Vineyards, a longtime Sonoma County producer best known for unoaked Chardonnay, has been sold.The buyer is WarRoom Cellars, a Central Coast company founded in 2018 with the purpose of acquiring legacy wine brands. In 2020, it made a splash for buying Bonny Doon Vineyard, the highly influential winery founded by Randall Grahm.
Rodney Strong Vineyards will continue to make Toad Hollow's wines, and there are no plans to change the winemaking style, according to a press
