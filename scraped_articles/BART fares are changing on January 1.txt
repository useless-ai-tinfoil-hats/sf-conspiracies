BART fares are changing on January 1
-----
The cost of your BART ride will change next year when Bay Area Rapid Transit adjusts prices on Jan. 1, 2024.Regular fares will increase by 5.5% system-wide, which means that the average fare will climb by 23 cents to $4.43. Meanwhile, the means-based Clipper START program will reduce its prices by raising its discount to 50% in the new year, up from 20%.
