San Francisco music festival Noise Pop takes over 25 venues this week
-----
Although Outside Lands has the pop headliners and Hardly Strictly brings the vintage-SF vibes, one could make a good case that Noise Pop is San Francisco's premiere music fest. With 160 bands spread across 25 venues, this weeklong festival is one of San Francisco's best ways to discover new music.Noise Pop has been preaching the gospel of live shows for over 30 years, on through tech bubbles and doom loops.
