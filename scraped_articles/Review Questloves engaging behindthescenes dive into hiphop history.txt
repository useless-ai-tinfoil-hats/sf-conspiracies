Review: Questlove's engaging behind-the-scenes dive into hip-hop history
-----
Questlove opens his book "Hip-Hop Is History" in the most predictable way. He gives us an origin story, skimming through the canonical story of the single event that kickstarted hip-hop (a Bronx party in August 1973 presided over by DJ Kool Herc) in one paragraph, then throws it aside.
