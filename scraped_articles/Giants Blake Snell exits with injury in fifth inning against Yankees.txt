Giants' Blake Snell exits with injury in fifth inning against Yankees
-----
San Francisco Giants pitcher Blake Snell, who returned from the IL just eight days earlier, exited Sunday's game against the Yankees at Oracle Park with what the team said was a tight left groin.Snell looked as if he felt some discomfort after an 0-1, bases-loaded pitch to Alex Verdugo in the fifth inning, and trainer Dave Groeschner went to the mound with manager Bob Melvin and pitching coach Bryan Price.
