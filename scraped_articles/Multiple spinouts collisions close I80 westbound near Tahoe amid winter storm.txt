Multiple spinouts, collisions close I-80 westbound near Tahoe amid winter storm
-----
California Highway Patrol has closed Interstate 80 off to westbound traffic in Truckee after snowy conditions caused multiple collisions and spinouts over Donner Summit on Saturday, the agency announced.Closures are impacting westbound traffic in Truckee and at the Nevada state line, CHP Truckee announced on social media.
