3 takeaways from the California Senate debate
-----
The three California House Democrats running for Senate don't just agree on most of the issues they've faced in Washington, now they agree on something else: Their Republican rival Steve Garvey is an empty suit.Reps. Barbara Lee, Katie Porter and Adam Schiff described the former Los Angeles Dodger first baseman — who has never sought elective office — as "patronizing," "minor league" and devoid of specifics during the first debate between the top four candidates Monday in Los Angeles.
