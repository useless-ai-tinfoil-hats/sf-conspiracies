Find Calif.'s best NY-style pizza once a week in a church parking lot
-----
Good pizza is easy to find in California, though many an East Coast transplant still bemoans the lack of good New York-style pizza in the Golden State. As it turns out, they need look no further than a nondescript church parking lot in sunny Los Angeles.Every Friday (and only on Fridays) boxing gym owner Steve Petramale parks his flatbed truck, outfitted with a double-decker pizza oven, at the Venice Church parking lot in the neighborhood of the same name. He sets up a few picnic tables
