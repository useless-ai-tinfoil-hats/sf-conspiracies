S.F.’s parking crackdown is coming. Here’s which tickets have plummeted in recent years
-----
The San Francisco Municipal Transit Agency has warned residents that, with its staff back to 2019 levels, it is ramping up its parking enforcement this year. But to get back to pre-pandemic ticketing numbers, the agency has a lot of ground to make up.Total parking citations declined from 1.21 million in 2019 to 1.05 million in 2023, a 13% drop, according to an analysis by the Chronicle. And certain types of violations plummeted to less than half the level seen in 2019.
