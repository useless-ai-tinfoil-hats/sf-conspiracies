Police in Berkeley investigate more than 70 car break-ins in 48 hours
-----
Berkeley police said Monday that they were still investigating the break-ins of 53 cars parked in West Berkeley late Thursday night.Footage of the aftermath recorded the following morning has since gone viral, showing a long line of parked cars with smashed windows police say were burglarized.
