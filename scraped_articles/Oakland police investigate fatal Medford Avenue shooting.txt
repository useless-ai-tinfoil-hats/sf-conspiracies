Oakland police investigate fatal Medford Avenue shooting
-----
Oakland police are investigating a fatal shooting that occurred Tuesday on the 9900 block of Medford Avenue.Police were dispatched to the area to investigate a reported shooting shortly after 3:30 a.m. When officers arrived they found a person suffering from gunshot wounds.
Officers provided medical assistance until paramedics arrived, but the person — who was not immediately identified pending notifications to family members — died at the scene.
