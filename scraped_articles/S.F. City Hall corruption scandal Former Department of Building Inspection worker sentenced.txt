S.F. City Hall corruption scandal: Former Department of Building Inspection worker sentenced
-----
Cyril Yu, a former San Francisco Department of Building Inspection plan checker, was sentenced Friday to a year and a day in prison for taking $15,000 worth of bribes from a local developer in return for expediting building plans.He was also sentenced to serve two years of supervised release following prison time and will have to pay a $20,000 fine.
