San Francisco Chronicle Hosts Dining Out For Life 2024
-----
Dining Out For Life San Francisco is a day-long fundraising event when the San Francisco restaurant community invites you to join them in the fight for health justice. On Wednesday, April 24, 2024, participating restaurants will donate 25 percent or more of their proceeds to San Francisco AIDS Foundation.The San Francisco Chronicle is proud to be returning as SFAF's media partner and will be hosting dinner and drinks at Per Diem - Financial District that evening.
