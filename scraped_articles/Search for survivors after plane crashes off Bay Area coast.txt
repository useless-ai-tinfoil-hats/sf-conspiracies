Search for survivors after plane crashes off Bay Area coast
-----
A small plane crashed off the coast near Half Moon Bay on Sunday night where wreckage has been found in the nearby waters, according to the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office.Sergeant Phillip Hallworth of the SMCSO told KRON News that a call regarding the incident first came in at 7:13 pm. People in the area of the Moss Beach Distillery, which is about 2 miles north from the Half Moon Bay airport, had contacted authorities after seeing the aircraft flying erratically.
