Warriors’ Joe Lacob: If A’s were for sale, ‘we might be interested’
-----
In a perfect world for Oakland Athletics fans, John Fisher's relocation plan would collapse and he would sell the A's to a group committed to keeping them in the Bay Area.Perhaps a group involving Joe Lacob.
A couple of years ago, Lacob went on record saying he had a standing offer to buy the A's and would keep the team in Oakland. But that was before Fisher committed to relocating to Las Vegas.
