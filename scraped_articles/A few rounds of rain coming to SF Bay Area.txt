'A few rounds of rain' coming to SF Bay Area
-----
The San Francisco Bay Area is expected to see a "few rounds of rain" in coming days, the National Weather Service said.The first round of rain is forecast to start Saturday night across the North Bay, "spreading south" of the Golden Gate and into the greater Bay Area into Sunday.
A second round of precipitation is expected to sweep the area "late Sunday night into Monday," with scattered showers lingering into Tuesday.
