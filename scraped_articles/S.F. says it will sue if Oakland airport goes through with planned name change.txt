S.F. says it will sue if Oakland airport goes through with planned name change
-----
UPDATE: Oakland airport name change approved, setting up showdown with SFOSan Francisco and Oakland's escalating fight over the potential renaming of the East Bay's international airport could reach the courtroom.
San Francisco on Monday said it will sue the Port of Oakland for alleged trademark infringement if it moves forward with plans to rename Oakland's airport as the San Francisco Bay Oakland International Airport.
