And the winner for best actor in a motion picture is ...
-----
The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences' choices for best actor category have remained consistent for 96 years.The academy tends to give the award to the actor who best meets the following criteria: He should be middle-aged, roughly between 38 and 60, but preferably in his 40s. If he's been previously nominated in this category, it's a help. It's important that he should be nominated for playing someone of high status, not wormy or weird, but noble or good in some way.
