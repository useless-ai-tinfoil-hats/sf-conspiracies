X, formerly Twitter, sues California over content moderation law
-----
X, the site formerly known as Twitter, sued the state of California Friday over a law passed last year requiring social media companies to divulge their terms of service, including how they moderate content on their sites.The lawsuit claims the law, authored by Assembly Member Jesse Gabriel, violates the company's freedom of speech, while Gabriel said the law is designed to increase transparency around an important topic.
