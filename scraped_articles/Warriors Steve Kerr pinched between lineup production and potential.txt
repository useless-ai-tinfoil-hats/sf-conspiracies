Warriors' Steve Kerr pinched between lineup production and potential
-----
Golden State Warriors head coach Steve Kerr typically likes to have a set rotation by the season's 20-game mark. But here he is 17 games in, still changing his lineups night to night."We have three more games," said Kerr, whose team enters Tuesday night's game at Sacramento with an 8-9 record. "We're not there yet."
