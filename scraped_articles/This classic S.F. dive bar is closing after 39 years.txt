This classic S.F. dive bar is closing after 39 years
-----
A storied dive bar on the San Francisco Legacy Business Registry will soon close for good.Uptown, at 200 Capp St. in the Mission, will close in January when its lease ends. "We love you Uptown community," a representative for the bar wrote on Instagram. "Thank you for 39 wonderful years."
Mission Local reported the imminent closing. The Chronicle could not immediately reach a representative for the business.
