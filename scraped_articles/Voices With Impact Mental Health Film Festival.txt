Voices With Impact Mental Health Film Festival
-----
Voices With Impact is a short film festival inspiring transformative mental health conversations.Join us at the SF LGBT Center on June 6 for the opening night of the Voices With Impact Festival and the World Premiere of 10 brand new short films exploring mental health.
The filmmakers, who produced these films through the Voices With Impact grant, will join us for a live interactive panel answering audience questions.
