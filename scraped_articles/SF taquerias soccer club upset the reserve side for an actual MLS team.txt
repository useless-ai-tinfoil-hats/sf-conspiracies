SF taqueria's soccer club upset the reserve side for an actual MLS team
-----
The soccer team owned by beloved San Francisco taqueria El Farolito saw its semi-pro soccer team come from behind to win a match on Tuesday night against a reserve side for an actual MLS team in the U.S. Open Cup, 2-1.El Farolito SC, which typically plays in the National Premier Soccer League, defeated the reserve team for the Portland Timbers in Portland thanks to two second-half goals.
