S.F. police release image of ‘person of interest’ in investigation of racist threats against dog walker
-----
San Francisco police released images Friday of a "person of interest" in their investigation into racist threats sent to a well-known dog walker.The person, who can be seen wearing a long, black coat and a black hat while carrying a brown bag, was not officially identified as a suspect, according to a statement from the San Francisco Police Department.
