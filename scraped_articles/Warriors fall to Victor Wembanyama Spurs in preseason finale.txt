Warriors fall to Victor Wembanyama, Spurs in preseason finale
-----
The Golden State Warriors ended their preseason with a 122-117 loss to the San Antonio Spurs on Friday night at Chase Center. They finish their exhibition schedule with a 4-1 record.After Wednesday's preseason thriller at home that saw all five starters play at least 28 minutes, Stephen Curry, Chris Paul, Klay Thompson, Andrew Wiggins and Kevon Looney played 20 minutes or less against San Antonio. Head coach Steve Kerr used a 14-man rotation in the team's final tune-up.
