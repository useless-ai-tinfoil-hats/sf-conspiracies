Plans for the redevelopment of SF's Stonestown Galleria just got an upgrade
-----
The massive project of converting San Francisco's Stonestown Galleria shopping mall into a bustling new neighborhood just got a few major updates.First introduced by Brookfield Properties in 2021, the plan involves developing almost 30 acres of parking lots adjacent to the mall into a mixed-use community complete with parks, plazas, office space, thousands of residential units and a new retail corridor.
