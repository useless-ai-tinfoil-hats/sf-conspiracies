Horse racing fans relish Golden Gate Fields' past and rail against its pending demise
-----
The sand has all but run out of the hourglass for Golden Gate Fields, which will shut down forever after the racing on June 9. But on a sunny Sunday, the old place is very much alive. The ghosts of the past mingle with the ghosts of the future, and they all seem to be having a good time.There aren't a lot of fans at the old race track that straddles Berkeley and Albany. Maybe a couple thousand, in grandstands that once held 20,000.
