Brock Purdy exits unscathed in 49ers’ 16-10 preseason win over Saints
-----
It was among the dreariest products professional sports has to offer — an NFL preseason game — but the fans who occupied not-filled Levi's Stadium on Sunday spent the early part of their evening on the edge of their seats.The reason for the suspense? Seeing whether San Francisco 49ers franchise quarterback Brock Purdy, the 23-year-old in whom so many Super Bowl hopes and dreams reside, could survive the no-stakes game unscathed.
