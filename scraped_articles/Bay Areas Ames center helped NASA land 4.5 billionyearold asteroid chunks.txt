Bay Area's Ames center helped NASA land 4.5 billion-year-old asteroid chunks
-----
NASA says there is a 1 in 1,750 chance that Earth gets hit next century by the asteroid Bennu, which is wider across than Salesforce Tower is tall. But we already have some of its 4.5 billion-year-old rocks and dust right here in the United States.That's thanks in part to the NASA Ames Research Center in the Bay Area, which helped land NASA's first ever asteroid sample in Utah on Sunday morning.
