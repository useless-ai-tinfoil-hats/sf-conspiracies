Even 49ers reporters are sick of the 'ridiculous' Brandon Aiyuk reporting
-----
It's been one full month since the San Francisco 49ers reported to training camp, and the Brandon Aiyuk contract saga ratcheted up several levels.Clearly, everyone is sick of the exhaustive cycle of reports without action — including one of the people who make some of those reports.
In a radio hit on KNBR's "Tolbert and Copes" show Thursday, San Francisco Chronicle columnist Mike Silver went on a rant about the drawn-out drama and the reporting — or lack thereof — surrounding it.
