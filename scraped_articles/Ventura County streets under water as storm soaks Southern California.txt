Ventura County streets under water as storm soaks Southern California
-----
Ventura County, a swath of coastal California just north of Los Angeles, saw severe, heavy rainfall Thursday morning, leading to flooding and evacuation warnings, officials said. Portions of streets in Oxnard were under water due to the torrential rains spawned by a low-pressure system that soaked the Golden State."Many of the streets and intersections are heavily impacted by flooding.
