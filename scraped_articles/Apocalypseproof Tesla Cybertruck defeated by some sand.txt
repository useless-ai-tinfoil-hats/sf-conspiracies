'Apocalypse-proof' Tesla Cybertruck defeated by some sand
-----
Tesla's Cybertruck can survive machine gun fire, extraterrestial terrain and even the apocalypse, if you believe Elon Musk. A sandy beach, however, proved too much for the $80,000 stainless-steel toy this week.The Cybertruck met its match on California's Marina State Beach, around 10 miles north of Monterey, on Monday afternoon, when it got stuck fast in the sand after illegally driving onto the beach in the state park.
