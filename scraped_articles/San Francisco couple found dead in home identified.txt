San Francisco couple found dead in home identified
-----
The San Francisco Police Department said Wednesday that there were no outstanding suspects in Sunday night's double fatal shooting of a couple in their home. There is no threat to the public associated with the shooting, Evan Sernoffsky, director of communications for SFPD, told SFGATE over the phone.The two individuals found fatally shot at the home in SF's Portola neighborhood were identified Tuesday by the San Francisco medical examiner's office.
