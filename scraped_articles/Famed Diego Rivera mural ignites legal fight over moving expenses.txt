Famed Diego Rivera mural ignites legal fight over moving expenses
-----
A historic mural by renowned Mexican artist Diego Rivera is at the heart of a recent lawsuit filed by the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art against City College of San Francisco.The colossal, 10-panel "Pan American Unity" fresco was painted by Rivera in 1940 and featured in the Golden Gate International Exposition on Treasure Island that year. The mural was then moved to CCSF and eventually housed in the school's Diego Rivera Theatre in 1961.
