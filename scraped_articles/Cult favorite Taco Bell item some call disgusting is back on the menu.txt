Cult favorite Taco Bell item some call 'disgusting' is back on the menu
-----
As of Thursday, Taco Bell's cult favorite nacho fries are officially back on the menu. The seasoned fries served with a side of nacho cheese sauce have come and gone from the menu many times since they first debuted in 2018, but this time, they will be back for their longest run ever, according to a news release.How long that is exactly remains unclear, but Taco Bell has promised "nearly all summer long!"
