S.F. fire stations light up with return of holiday decoration contest
-----
San Francisco firefighters are bringing back holiday lights, block parties and (almost certainly) snow as the city's firehouse decoration contest returns for a fourth year.The new tradition is officially longer than the one that inspired it: San Francisco Fire Department stations from 1948 to 1950 used lights, false facades and, in one case, real farm animals to dress up neighborhood stations.
