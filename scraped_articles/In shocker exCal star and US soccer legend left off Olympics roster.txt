In shocker, ex-Cal star and US soccer legend left off Olympics roster
-----
When the U.S. women's national team heads to Paris for the Olympics next month, Alex Morgan won't be going.The two-time World Cup winner and four-time CONCACAF Women's Player of the Year was left off the 18-player American roster, which was announced Wednesday morning by new coach Emma Hayes. Morgan, who will turn 35 next week, was not named one of the four alternates, either.
