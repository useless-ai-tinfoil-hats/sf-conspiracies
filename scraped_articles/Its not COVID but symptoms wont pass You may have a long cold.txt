It’s not COVID, but symptoms won’t pass? You may have a ‘long cold’
-----
People who test negative for the coronavirus but experience a lingering cough, fatigue or digestive issues may suffer from "long colds," according to a new study.Contrary to the common belief that respiratory infections like colds and flu vanish within a week, the findings published Friday in the Lancet scientific journal suggest that they can lead to health complications persisting for more than four weeks after the initial illness, often escaping detection.
