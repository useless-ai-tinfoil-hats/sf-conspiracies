5.0 quake jolts Northern California
-----
A 5.0 magnitude earthquake jolted a fairly remote region about 7 miles west of Fall River Mills (Shasta County) on Friday morning.*  Earthquake tracker: Mapping the latest quakes in California and across the world
The epicenter of the quake was detected shortly before 10:30 a.m. in an area between the Shasta and Lassen national forests, according to data from the U.S. Geological Survey.
