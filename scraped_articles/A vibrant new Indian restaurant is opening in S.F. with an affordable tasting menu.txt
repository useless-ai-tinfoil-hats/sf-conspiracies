A vibrant new Indian restaurant is opening in S.F. with an affordable tasting menu
-----
Two brothers with a rich resume cooking modern Indian cuisine are opening an ambitious and vibrant San Francisco restaurant together, with a tasting menu that's relatively affordable by the city's standards.Tiya opens Wednesday at 3213 Scott St. in the Marina District, inside the old Maybeck's space, featuring vibrant a la carte dishes and a $90 four-course tasting menu filled with fresh ingredients.
