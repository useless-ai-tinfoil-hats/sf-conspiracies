Tech layoffs: Major online furniture retailer to cut 1,650 jobs
-----
Wayfair, the online furniture retailer, is set to cut approximately 1,650 jobs, which accounts for 13% of its global workforce.The Boston-based company, with a warehouse in San Leandro and corporate offices in Mountain View, confirmed the layoffs on Friday. The move is part of a restructuring plan that includes reducing team sizes company-wide and adjusting seniority in specific roles.
