Has S.F. really turned a corner on car break-ins? Hayes Valley residents aren't so sure
-----
After suffering her third car break-in this year in San Francisco's Hayes Valley neighborhood, Emi Uyehara wrangled a small consolation prize: a loyalty discount at the glass repair shop.It was a dubious win for Uyehara, whose car windows were smashed in May, October and November while she attended an opera performance and two events produced by City Arts and Lectures.
