The words we leave behind can be powerful if not always poetic
-----
Which words are left behind?In the sixth grade, at PS 60 in Queens, I wrote a poem for an English assignment. Mrs. Roth mailed it into a local radio station contest and the next week, they announced I'd won an honorable mention. Nurse Vivian made spaghetti and meatballs to commemorate the occasion.
Somehow, I knew it would be downhill after that. Sic transit gloria mundi: All glory is fleeting.
