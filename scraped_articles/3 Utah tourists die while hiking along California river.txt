3 Utah tourists die while hiking along California river
-----
Three visitors from Utah drowned while canyoneering along the Seven Teacups trail north of Bakersfield.According to family members, friends Jeannine Skinner, David Bell and Peter On were part of a group exploring the Tulare County trail on Friday afternoon. They were near the end of their excursion when Skinner was apparently caught in a whirlpool in the Kern River.
