Eight miles of highway through Kettleman City to close for months
-----
A stretch of Highway 41 that runs through Kettleman City to the Central Coast will be closed for five months for pavement repairs, Caltrans announced. The closure will stretch about eight miles from Quail to Nevada avenues, and impact both north and southbound lanes.The closure begins on Feb. 26 and "will include resurfacing and restoration of the pavement to extend and preserve the life of the roadway," Caltrans officials said in a statement.
