Mount Waterman, the closest ski area to Los Angeles, is for sale
-----
A historic ski resort that offers the closest powder to Los Angeles is looking for a new owner.Mount Waterman ski area, first opened in 1939, is listed for sale for $2,275,000, meaning that for about the price of a single-family home in one of LA's pricier neighborhoods, a 390-acre slice of the San Gabriel Mountains could be yours. Well, assuming you specifically want to operate a ski area according to a special use permit from the U.S. Forest Service.
