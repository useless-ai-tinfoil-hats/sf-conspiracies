2 suspects charged in robbery at SF's Dior store near Union Square, DA says
-----
A 23-year-old woman and 28-year-old man pleaded not guilty on Tuesday to multiple charges associated with a robbery at San Francisco's Dior store near Union Square, officials said.In an arraignment, the suspects were charged with second-degree commercial burglary, grand theft, organized retail theft, conspiracy and resisting arrest, the San Francisco District Attorney's Office said in a news release.
