California brothers in deadly mountain lion attack identified
-----
Two brothers have been identified in an exceptionally rare mountain lion mauling in Northern California.Taylen Robert Claude Brooks, 21, and Wyatt Jay Charles Brooks, 18, of Mount Aukum, California, were the victims in the attack, the El Dorado County Sheriff's Office confirmed Monday.
