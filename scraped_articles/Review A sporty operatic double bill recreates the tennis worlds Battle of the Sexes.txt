Review: A sporty operatic double bill recreates the tennis world’s ‘Battle of the Sexes’
-----
Sports and opera have a lot of overlap, including spectacle, devoted fandom and a system of elaborately arcane rules and rituals. (Happily, we have yet to see any gambling scandals in the world of opera.)Yet operas about athletics are few and far between. So "Birds & Balls," a compact double bill of sports-themed one-act pieces offered over the weekend by Opera Parallèle, comes as a welcome corrective.
