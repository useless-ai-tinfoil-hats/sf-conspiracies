Biotech company Sangamo Therapeutics laying off 40% of workers, closing HQ
-----
Bay Area-based biotechnology company Sangamo Therapeutics is closing its Brisbane headquarters and conducting a round of layoffs as part of a restructuring process, the company announced.Sangamo announced in a news release on Wednesday that it will shutter its Brisbane facility and move its headquarters to the company's office in Richmond in an effort to reduce costs.
