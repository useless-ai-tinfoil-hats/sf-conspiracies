Three hospitalized after getting trapped in 'extremely hot' Hawaii hotel elevator
-----
Two teenagers and one adult were hospitalized Wednesday night after getting trapped in an elevator with seven other people at a Holiday Inn Express in Waikiki, Honolulu Emergency Medical Services confirmed to SFGATE. Hawaii News Now first reported the news.Authorities, including firefighters and an elevator technician, were called to the hotel on Kuhio Avenue just before 7 p.m. for the group of guests, who were stuck for roughly an hour.
