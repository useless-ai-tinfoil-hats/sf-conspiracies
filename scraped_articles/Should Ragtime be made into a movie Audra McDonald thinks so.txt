Should 'Ragtime' be made into a movie? Audra McDonald thinks so
-----
Emmy-nominated actress Audra McDonald is currently gracing the small screen as Dorothy Scott on the MAX drama "The Gilded Age." But for theater fans, her work on the stage has special poignancy.The first and only performer to win six Tony Awards, McDonald has played a range of characters from student Sharon Graham in Terrence McNally's "Master Class" to Sarah in "Ragtime."
