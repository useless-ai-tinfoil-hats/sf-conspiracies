I was captivated and haunted by the visual of glowing, spindly fish skeletons hanging over a hearth flame when I first sat down at San Francisco's Aphotic. Lined in a row, the fish carcasses' mouths were agape, with a hook through the center, and engulfed by smoke as if they were swimming upstream.This ghoulish scene set the tone for the restaurant's dark abyss-like setting, and it wouldn't be the last I saw of the skeletons.
