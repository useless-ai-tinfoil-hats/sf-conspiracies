Google layoffs continue as Bay Area tech giant trims staff from YouTube, ad sales
-----
Google's January layoffs are hitting team after team at the Mountain View-based company.The tech giant is laying off hundreds of workers across YouTube's business arm and Google's advertising sales teams. The layoffs come just days after a swath of job cuts hit a few hundred employees each on several teams: design and services, knowledge and information products, core engineering and Google Assistant.
