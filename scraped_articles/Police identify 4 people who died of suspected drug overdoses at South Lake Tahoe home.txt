Police identify 4 people who died of suspected drug overdoses at South Lake Tahoe home
-----
The names of four people who died of suspected drug overdoses at a South Lake Tahoe home on Monday were released by police Tuesday.South Lake Tahoe residents Abraham Lemus, 32, Adam Joy, 35, Keely Pereira, 33, and Douglas County, Nevada, resident Clifford Joy, 30, died at the residence on the 500 block of Roger Avenue, the South Lake Tahoe Police Department said in a statement Tuesday.
Police said that substances found at the home tested positive for fentanyl, a synthetic opioid
