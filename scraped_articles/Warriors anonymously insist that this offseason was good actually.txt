Warriors anonymously insist that this offseason was good, actually
-----
In an offseason rife with loss, the Warriors are coping as best as they can: anonymously, and through the press.Two reports published Wednesday cited anonymous team sources as feeling fine, really, about how things have gone over the past few months. The first came from ESPN's Kendra Andrews, who cited "sources" that said both Steph Curry and Draymond Green were "excited about the moves Golden State has made so far this summer," she wrote.
