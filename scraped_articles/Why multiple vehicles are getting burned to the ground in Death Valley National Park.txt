Why multiple vehicles are getting 'burned to the ground' in Death Valley National Park
-----
A soft drink truck was engulfed in flames in Death Valley last Thursday, marking the fourth vehicle fire in the California national park in just over a month.The driver was not injured, but the truck was "burned to the ground" on state Route 190 near Mesquite Flat Sand Dunes, according to a news release from the National Park Service. Firefighters speculated that overheated brakes caused the blaze.
