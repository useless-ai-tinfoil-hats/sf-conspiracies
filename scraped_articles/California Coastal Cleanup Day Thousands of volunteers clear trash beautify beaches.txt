California Coastal Cleanup Day: Thousands of volunteers clear trash, beautify beaches
-----
Tens of thousands of volunteers throughout California grabbed gloves, buckets and bags Saturday morning and headed out to hundreds of designated beaches and waterways to clean up trash and other debris.The huge cleanup effort marked the California Coastal Commission's 39th California Coastal Cleanup Day, dubbed the state's largest annual volunteer event.
Dozens of waterways and beaches in all nine Bay Area counties were part of the annual event, which included nearly 700 sites
