Two downtown S.F. businesses closing after three decades
-----
Two downtown San Francisco businesses are shuttering after three decades, signs that the toll from the pandemic and remote work is far from over.A three-story 24 Hour Fitness gym at 100 California St. is closing on Dec. 29, according to the company's website. A block away, a McDonald's at 235 Front St. closed last Friday, its owner confirmed. Both businesses opened in 1994.
