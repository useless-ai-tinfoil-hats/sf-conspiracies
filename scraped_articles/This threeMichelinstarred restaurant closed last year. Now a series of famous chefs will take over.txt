This three-Michelin-starred restaurant closed last year. Now a series of famous chefs will take over

Some of the world's most acclaimed chefs will be coming to the Bay Area as part of a series of pop-ups taking over a famous local kitchen.Ritual at Manresa will occupy the former three-Michelin-starred Los Gatos restaurant Manresa, which closed at the end of 2022, with 10 international chefs bringing their dishes for three-week stints.
