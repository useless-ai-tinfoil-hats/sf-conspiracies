Stanford and Colorado made similar moves with much different results
-----
Stanford and Colorado, which meet Friday night in Boulder, found themselves in similar circumstances in December. Each school was looking for a head coach as the Cardinal had gone a combined 6-18 in the previous two seasons and the Buffaloes 5-19.Each chose a man in his mid-50s who had played in the NFL and had put together a strong three-season record as the head coach of an FCS team.
