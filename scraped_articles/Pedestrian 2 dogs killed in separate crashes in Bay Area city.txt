Pedestrian, 2 dogs killed in separate crashes in Bay Area city
-----
Two separate crashes involving pedestrians and their dogs killed one man and both animals in Rohnert Park on Sunday morning, authorities said.The two incidents happened within an hour of each other and do not appear to be related. The pedestrian and his dog died from the first crash. The second victim survived a hit-and-run crash, but his dog died, according to the Rohnert Park Department of Public Safety.
