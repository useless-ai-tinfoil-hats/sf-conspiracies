49ers retain QB Brandon Allen, potential backup to Brock Purdy in 2024
-----
The San Francisco 49ers retained quarterback Brandon Allen with a one-year contract Monday, a league source said, providing them with insurance behind starter Brock Purdy.Allen, 31, served as the 49ers' third-stringer last season behind Purdy and Sam Darnold. Darnold, an unrestricted free agent, is expected to explore his options on the open market.
