California State Fair concert series features household names for dirt cheap
-----
If you can't bring yourself to pony up for major California festivals like Outside Lands and Coachella, the lineup for the California State Fair, where admission is a fraction of the price, doesn't look too shabby. The fair takes place at the Cal Expo in Sacramento from July 12-28.
