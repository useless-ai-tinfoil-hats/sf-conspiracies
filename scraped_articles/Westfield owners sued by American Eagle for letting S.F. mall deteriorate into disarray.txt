Westfield owners sued by American Eagle for letting S.F. mall ‘deteriorate into disarray’
-----
One of the largest remaining tenants at San Francisco Centre on Market Street has sued the former owners, charging that they let the massive mall "deteriorate into disarray" and allow crime to flourish.The suit is by the company that owns American Eagle, a popular clothing brand that has a large retail space at the mall's main entrance near Fifth Street. It alleges that after the pandemic began in 2020, co-owner and operator Westfield neglected security efforts and basic mall management
