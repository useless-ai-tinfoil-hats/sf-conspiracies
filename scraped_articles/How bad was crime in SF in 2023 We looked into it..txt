How bad was crime in SF in 2023? We looked into it.
-----
Crime, and just how widespread it is, has been a surging topic of conversation in San Francisco for years, particularly since the pandemic emptied downtown of workers. With high-profile homicides and plenty of viral videos documenting retail theft, car break-ins and robberies, it's also a conversation that has gone nationwide. Salesforce has debated moving its giant Dreamforce conference out of San Francisco, and retailers and restaurants have blamed rising crime when closing locations.A
