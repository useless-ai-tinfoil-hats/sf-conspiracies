One dead after incident with BART train at Downtown Berkeley Station
-----
One person was killed after apparently coming into contact with a BART train arriving at the Downtown Berkeley Station Wednesday, according to the transit agency. The station was closed due to the emergency.An individual was apparently struck around 5:46 p.m. as a train approached the station. The individual was on the platform when the incident occurred, the agency said.
BART police confirmed that the person was dead and said no foul play was suspected.
