PG&E service restored in Sonoma County after big morning outage
-----
A power outage in Sonoma County temporarily cut electricity service Wednesday morning to about 17,000 Pacific Gas & Electric Co. customers, officials said.The outage began just before 7:30 a.m. after a device inside a PG&E substation in Sebastopol malfunctioned while crews were carrying out planned safety work, said PGE spokesperson Megan McFarlan.
Roughly 27,000 people west of Santa Rosa to West County were impacted, the Fire Department said on X.
