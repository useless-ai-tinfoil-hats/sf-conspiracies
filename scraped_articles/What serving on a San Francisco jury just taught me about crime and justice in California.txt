What serving on a San Francisco jury just taught me about crime and justice in California
-----
Like many people, I've long lived under the assumption that jury duty is a pox to be avoided. Year after year, I have sat in bleak courthouse basements after a summons — only to be sent home after a wasted day.Earlier this month, however, I was finally called up to the big leagues, and empaneled on my first jury. At a time when issues of crime and justice are at the top of everyone's mind in San Francisco, I found the experience enlightening — far different from the trials I have covered
