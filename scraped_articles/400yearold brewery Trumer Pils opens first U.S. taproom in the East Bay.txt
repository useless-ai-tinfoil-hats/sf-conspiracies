400-year-old brewery Trumer Pils opens first U.S. taproom in the East Bay
-----
Trumer Pils, an Austrian beer company that was founded in 1775, has opened its first U.S. taproom in Berkeley, Calif.Trumer Pils' East Bay brewery, which has been around since 2004, will celebrate the new taproom, Trumer Brauerei, located at 1404 Fourth St. in Berkeley, on Oct. 7 from 12 p.m. to 6 p.m. as part of an Oktoberfest celebration.
