How to watch the ‘ring of fire’ solar eclipse in California
-----
A "ring of fire" solar eclipse will unfold this weekend — though only some parts of the U.S. will see its most dramatic effects.The event on Saturday — called an annular eclipse because the moon blocks all but the sun's fiery outer edge, or annulus — differs from a full solar eclipse because of the relative position of the moon to our planet, astronomers said.
