North Bay Catholic school said it's not closing after filing an EDD notice
-----
LATEST: March 6, 4 p.m. Officials at St. Vincent de Paul High School in Petaluma said the school will not be closing its doors after filing a notice on Friday with the California Employment Development Department saying the school is closing "permanently."The WARN notice was just a "procedural step," Adrian Peterson, the Diocese of Santa Rosa superintendent, said in a statement to SFGATE on Wednesday, despite the notices' language detailing closure.
