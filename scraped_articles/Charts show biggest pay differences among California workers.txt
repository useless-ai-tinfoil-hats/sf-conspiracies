Charts show biggest pay differences among California workers
-----
Recently released data by the California Civil Rights Department reaffirms the well-known pay gap that exists between people of different races and genders in the state: White male workers are dominantly represented in white-collar, high-paying jobs, while women and people of color are overrepresented in lower-paying, blue-collar jobs.California-based private companies employing more than 100 workers are mandated by state law to disclose pay data that includes demographic profiles.
