Chubba Purdy, Brock's younger brother, is headed to San Jose State
-----
The number of quarterback-playing Purdys in the South Bay is about to double.Chubba Purdy, the younger brother of San Francisco 49ers quarterback Brock Purdy, announced on Sunday his intention to transfer to San Jose State.
The Spartans will be Chubba Purdy's third college team. He spent two seasons at Florida State — during which he played in only four games — before transferring to Nebraska, where he played in six games in each of the past two seasons. He will join San Jose State
