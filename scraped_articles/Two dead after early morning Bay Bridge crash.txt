Two dead after early morning Bay Bridge crash
-----
Two people were killed early Thursday morning in a crash on the Bay Bridge, a San Francisco Fire Department official said.San Francisco emergency personnel were dispatched to a reported car crash on the bridge shortly before 4:30 a.m. Thursday, according to San Francisco Fire Lt. Mariano Elias.
The driver of a Toyota Tacoma pickup truck had collided with a Mini Cooper that was stopped in the eastbound slow lane, Elias said, just after the tunnel.
