Why did the 49ers give Jauan Jennings $10.5 million after a 19-catch season?
-----
Given that Jauan Jennings is a wide receiver who has never amassed big numbers — he had 19 catches last year, and he has not had more than 35 in his three-season career — this number could be surprising to some: $10.5 million.That's how much guaranteed money the San Francisco 49ers included in the two-year contract Jennings, 26, signed Wednesday that will keep him with the team through the 2025 season.
