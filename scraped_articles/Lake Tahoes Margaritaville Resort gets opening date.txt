Lake Tahoe's Margaritaville Resort gets opening date
-----
Winter in Lake Tahoe may not bring to mind images of palm trees, fruity cocktails and travelers clad in hibiscus-patterned shirts. But that could change on Dec. 14, the opening day for the new Margaritaville Resort Lake Tahoe."We're brand new and this is a unique design," said resort general manager Bill Cottrill. "It's a lot of fun. It's not the standard 'make it look like a mountain inside our rooms.'"
