Grass fire spreads to buildings at state prison grounds in Vacaville
-----
Solano County crews on Friday battled a grass fire that spread to buildings on the grounds of the California Medical Facility prison.The fire had reached 4 acres by about 4:45 p.m. Friday and was burning off Peabody Road in Vacaville, in the hills and on the sprawling prison property, where several buildings away from the main prison structure had burned.
By 5:37 p.m., the fire was declared under control and mop-up was underway. No injuries were reported.
