49ers adding Chase Young might not fix pass rush if secondary is the problem
-----
The San Francisco 49ers employ a defensive end with a $170 million contract, a defensive tackle with a $85 million contract and another interior lineman with an $84 million deal.Given those massive numbers, one stat is wildly perplexing: 2.3. That's how many sacks the 49ers are averaging through eight games. It ranks 22nd in the NFL, meaning the 49ers are paying top dollar for below-average production.
"We've put a lot of resources there," general manager John Lynch said Wednesday.
