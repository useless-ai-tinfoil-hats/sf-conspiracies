Letters: Why is driverless Cruise car blamed for S.F. accident? Here’s who’s at fault
-----
Regarding "Driver hits woman in S.F., then Cruise driverless car runs her over; photo shows victim trapped" (San Francisco, SFChronicle.com, Oct. 2): How can anyone find fault with the driverless car involved in the accident in San Francisco?The car that hit the woman was piloted by a human (who police said fled on foot); the driverless car stopped with the victim under it, which is exactly what it should have done, according to the San Francisco Police Department.
