Woman caught smuggling 53 pounds of sea cucumbers into California
-----
U.S. Customs and Border Protection announced Monday that officers had found more than 50 pounds of sea cucumbers concealed in a vehicle driven by a woman who was crossing the U.S.-Mexico border into California last week.According to a news release, on Aug. 21, the 55-year-old woman was driving a 2014 sedan and was attempting to enter the U.S. at the border crossing in Calexico, roughly 117 miles east of San Diego.
