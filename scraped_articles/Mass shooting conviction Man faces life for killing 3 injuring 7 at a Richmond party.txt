Mass shooting conviction: Man faces life for killing 3, injuring 7 at a Richmond party
-----
A man was convicted of murder for a mass shooting that killed three people and injured four in 2021 at a Richmond house party, according to the Contra Costa District Attorney's Office.Following a four-month trial, Enrique Ramirez-Calmo was convicted Wednesday on three counts of first-degree murder and four counts of attempted murder, the District Attorney's Office said Friday.
