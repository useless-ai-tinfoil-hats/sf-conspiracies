BART service suspended between Hayward and Berryessa due to computer issue
-----
Train service between BART's Hayward and Berryessa stations was temporarily suspended Thursday morning due to a "computer network issue," the transit agency said.Shortly before noon, BART announced that service had been restored at Warm Springs, Milpitas, and Berryessa, but remained closed between Hayward and Fremont. Just before 6:30 p.m., the agency said full service had been restored between Bayfair and Berryessa, adding that all stations were reopened.
