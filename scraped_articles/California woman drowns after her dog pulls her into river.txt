California woman drowns after her dog pulls her into river
-----
A California woman was rafting with a group of friends on the Kings River in Fresno County on Saturday when she and her German shepherd drowned, officials said.Mary Marshall, 63, of Menifee, was floating in the raft and wearing a harness attached to her dog's leash, the Fresno County Sheriff's Office said in a news release.
