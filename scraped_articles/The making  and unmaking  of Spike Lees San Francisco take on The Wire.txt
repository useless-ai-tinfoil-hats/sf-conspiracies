The making — and unmaking — of Spike Lee's San Francisco take on 'The Wire'
-----
When screenwriter Alex Tse ran into his former colleague Spike Lee recently, Lee greeted him with a burning question — "What year did 'Sucker Free City' come out?" Tse, who collaborated with Lee on the San Francisco-set TV pilot, told SFGATE that he guessed that it must have been 2004. Lee then broke into an emphatic proclamation."We were ahead of our time! They f—ked up man!"
