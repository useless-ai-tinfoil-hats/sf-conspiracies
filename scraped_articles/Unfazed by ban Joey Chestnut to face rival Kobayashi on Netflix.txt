Unfazed by ban, Joey Chestnut to face rival Kobayashi on Netflix
-----
A day after Joey Chestnut learned that he was banned from Nathan's Fourth of July hot dog-eating contest due to a partnership with a rival brand, Netflix revealed he will appear in an upcoming live special.Chestnut, a Vallejo native and 16-time hot dog-eating champion known as "Jaws," is scheduled to face off against his biggest rival, six-time champion Takeru Kobayashi, in "Chestnut vs. Kobayashi: Unfinished Beef" on Sept. 2.
