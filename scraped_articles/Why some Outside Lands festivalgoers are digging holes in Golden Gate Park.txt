Why some Outside Lands festivalgoers are digging holes in Golden Gate Park
-----
A weekend at Outside Lands can cost a pretty penny. After forking over around $530 on a three-day general admission ticket, festivalgoers will end up shedding even more cash on merchandise, food and their dazzling 2024 festival fits.
