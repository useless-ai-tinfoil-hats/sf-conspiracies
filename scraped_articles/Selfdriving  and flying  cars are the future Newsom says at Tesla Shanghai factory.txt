Self-driving — and flying — cars are the future, Newsom says at Tesla Shanghai factory
-----
SHANGHAI — In 30 years, Gov. Gavin Newsom thinks we'll wonder why we ever let human drivers on the road, he told reporters Sunday after touring a Tesla car factory in Shanghai on his final day in China."Autonomy is the future," California's Democratic leader said. "I think we're going to look back in 20, 30 years and go, 'Why were we allowed to drive and allow 30,000-plus Americans to die every single year in accidents? ' "
