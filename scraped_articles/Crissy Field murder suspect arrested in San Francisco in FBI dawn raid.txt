Crissy Field murder suspect arrested in San Francisco in FBI dawn raid
-----
The discovery of a lifeless body in San Francisco's Crissy Field on Nov. 12, and the dawn raid on a home in the Excelsior this week are connected, the FBI confirmed to SFGATE on Wednesday.
