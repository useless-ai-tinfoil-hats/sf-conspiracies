Soon, you won’t need a Clipper card to ride BART or Muni. Here’s how it will work
-----
Starting next year, Bay Area transit riders won't need a Clipper card to ride a train or ferry, and instead will be able to pass BART turnstiles or board Muni buses with the simple tap of a credit card.The new contactless debit or credit card payment option launching in summer 2024 realizes a perk for the region's riders that's already enjoyed by New York and London riders.
