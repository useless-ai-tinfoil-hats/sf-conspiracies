S.F.’s Westfield mall has new management. Here’s who it is
-----
San Francisco's biggest mall is under new management.A San Francisco Superior Court judge appointed Gregg Williams of Trident Pacific to "take possession, custody and control" of the troubled San Francisco Centre as its receiver on Monday.
Williams plans to hire the real estate brokerage JLL as the mall's manager and leasing agent, according to a court filing.
