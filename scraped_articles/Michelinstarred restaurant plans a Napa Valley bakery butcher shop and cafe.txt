Michelin-starred restaurant plans a Napa Valley bakery, butcher shop and cafe
-----
For decades, Dean & DeLuca was the unofficial pit stop of Napa Valley. It was where local winemakers and tourists swung by for their morning coffee, a sandwich at lunch, and wine and provisions to take home for dinner. Now, five years after its closing, a Michelin-starred restaurant hopes to recreate its famous energy, but with something completely different: a tapas-style cafe called Under-study.Press Restaurant, awarded its first Michelin star in 2022, is slated to open Under-study
