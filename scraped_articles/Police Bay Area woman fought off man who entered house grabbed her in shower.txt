Police: Bay Area woman fought off man who entered house, grabbed her in shower
-----
A man was arrested Thursday on suspicion of entering a house in Napa, grabbing a woman who was taking a shower and sexually assaulting her, officials said.Amador Solano Ortiz, a 32-year-old man, was booked at the Napa County Department of Corrections and could face multiple charges, including home invasion robbery, burglary, assault with intent to commit a felony and sexual penetration by a foreign object, the Napa Police Department said in a news release.
