Neither fog nor hills nor cancer’s return will keep us from the dogs’ daily rounds
-----
The dogs, Queenie and Moxie, and their pet humans, my husband Brian and I, have a regular route — and routine — for our afternoon walk.I get out the tam o'shanters for the humans and the pink leather motorcycle vests for the girls. Most often we promenade past Aunt JJ's, say hello to the dogs in the window on Baltimore Way, then let Queenie decide whether to take the uphill or downhill side of Canyon Drive, which is separated by an exotic greenway.
