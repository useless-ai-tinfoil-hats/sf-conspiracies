San Pablo man charged with murdering wife, mother-in-law after reporting them missing
-----
A San Pablo man was charged with murdering his wife and mother-in-law months after he reported them missing.Forty-year-old Phuc Vo reported his wife, Tho Ly, 40, and her mother, Que Tran, 74, missing on Sept. 13, police said.
At the time, Vo told officers the women had been missing for a week and had driven to Southern California in his wife's 2017 Honda Fit after a "family argument," police said.
