‘How did she end up dead on a sidewalk?’ Family of East Bay teen desperately searching for answers
-----
Three days after Jázmin Pellegrini left her East Bay home, telling her mother that she was going for a walk, the 15-year-old was found dead, facedown in a San Francisco driveway.It was a startling end to a troubled life that saw Jázmin in and out of child psychiatric units at area hospitals as she struggled to cope with memories of childhood sexual abuse by her grandfather in her native Hungary, family members said.
