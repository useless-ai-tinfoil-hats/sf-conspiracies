Crews stop progress of South Bay grass fire
-----
Crews stopped the progress of a grass fire in the San Jose foothills Saturday, authorities said.The San Jose Fire Department and Cal Fire responded to the vegetation fire that broke out near Meadowlands Lane and Wild Meadow Way shortly after 8 a.m. The Felipe Fire burned 19 acres and was completely contained Sunday morning, Cal Fire said.
No structures were damaged, no residents or firefighters were injured, and motorists were advised to avoid the area.
