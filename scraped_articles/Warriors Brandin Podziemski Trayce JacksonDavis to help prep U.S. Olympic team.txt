Warriors’ Brandin Podziemski, Trayce Jackson-Davis to help prep U.S. Olympic team
-----
Steve Kerr and Stephen Curry won't be the only members of the Golden State Warriors representing USA Basketball this summer. Brandin Podziemski and Trayce Jackson-Davis were named Friday to USA Basketball's 15-man Select Team, which practices and trains with the senior national team in preparation for Olympic play.Training camp for the American team — coached by Kerr and featuring Curry — begins July 6 in Las Vegas, concluding July 10 with an exhibition versus Canada at T-Mobile Arena.
