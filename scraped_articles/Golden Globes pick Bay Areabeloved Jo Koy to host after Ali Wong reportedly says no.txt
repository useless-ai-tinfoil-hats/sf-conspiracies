Golden Globes pick Bay Area-beloved Jo Koy to host after Ali Wong reportedly says no
-----
Jo Koy is set to host the 2024 Golden Globes, bringing his brand of observational humor to the oft-maligned awards show. However, Koy was only offered the gig after a different San Francisco Bay Area comedian reportedly turned it down.While not from the Bay Area originally, Koy based his 2022 film "Easter Sunday" in Daly City. The South Bay suburb thanked the comedian for the honor by granting him the keys to city.
