Man convicted in hit-and-run that injured woman outside S.F. emergency room
-----
A San Francisco jury convicted a 59-year-old man for a hit-and-run crash that badly injured a woman outside Kaiser Permanente San Francisco Medical Center in Lower Pacific Heights last year, according to the District Attorney's Office.Will Thomas faces up to three years in state prison after the jury found him guilty on one count of felony hit-and-run with injury, the DA's office announced Monday.
