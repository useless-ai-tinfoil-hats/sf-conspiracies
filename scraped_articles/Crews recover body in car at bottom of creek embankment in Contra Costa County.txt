Crews recover body in car at bottom of creek embankment in Contra Costa County
-----
Contra Costa County firefighters pulled a body from a car that had tumbled into a creek embankment in Martinez on Sunday, authorities said.Firefighters dispatched to Franklin Canyon Road around 10 a.m. found the person inside the overturned vehicle about 30 feet down the embankment, said Joe Ottolini, a captain with the Contra Costa County Fire Protection District. The person was dead when the firefighters arrived.
"The vehicle landed in some very shallow water," Ottolini said.
