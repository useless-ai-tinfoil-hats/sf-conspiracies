Ida Mojadad joins the Chronicle as a breaking news reporter
-----
From Metro Editor Greg Griffin:The Chronicle is pleased to announce that San Francisco journalist Ida Mojadad will join the newsroom as a temporary breaking news reporter starting May 21.
Mojadad will report to Night Editor Suzanne Espinosa, who leads a team that provides quick and accurate coverage of Bay Area news and events into the night.
