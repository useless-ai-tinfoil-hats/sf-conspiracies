Pedestrian killed in San Jose — city’s 8th traffic death of 2024
-----
A pedestrian died after being struck by a vehicle Saturday morning in San Jose — the city's eighth fatal collision and eighth traffic death of 2024, according to the Police Department.Police and paramedics responded to the collision at Story Road and Galahad Avenue around 10 a.m. The pedestrian died after being taken to a local hospital with life-threatening injuries.
Eastbound Story Road from Leeward to Capital avenues was closed, and police urged drivers to use alternative
