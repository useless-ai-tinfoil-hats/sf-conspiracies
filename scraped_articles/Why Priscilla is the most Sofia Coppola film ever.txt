Why ‘Priscilla’ is the most Sofia Coppola film ever
-----
"Priscilla" is the most Sofia Coppola movie that Sofia Coppola has ever made.The 52-year-old filmmaker has built a career portraying what it's like to be an outsider confined in an insulated world, so it's fitting that her latest film's subject, Priscilla Presley, is the ultimate celebrity plus-one.
If you're a fan of the writer-director's dreamy, sometimes opaque style of filmmaking and its themes of loneliness and alienation amid privilege, you know what I mean.
