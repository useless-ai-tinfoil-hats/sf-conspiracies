Zendaya makes $100,000 donation to the Bay Area theater where she got her start
-----
Oakland native Zendaya got her start at California Shakespeare Theater, and now she's paying it forward.The East Bay theater company announced Wednesday, Feb. 21, that the Emmy-winning star of "Euphoria," "Malcolm & Marie," "Spider-Man: Homecoming" and both parts of the "Dune" film franchise has given the theater $100,000 via the Women Donors Network.
