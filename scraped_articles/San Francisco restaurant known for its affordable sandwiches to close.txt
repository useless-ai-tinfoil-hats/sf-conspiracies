San Francisco restaurant known for its affordable sandwiches to close
-----
Little Paris restaurant, which has been best known for affordable Vietnamese meals and coveted $6.50 banh mi sandwiches, is expected to close after four decades.Owner Becky Ng confirmed with SFGATE that the Chinatown fixture, located at 1131 Stockton St., will shutter once she finds an interested buyer. The restaurant is currently listed for sale with an asking price of $119,000, but Ng shared that she won't pass on the rights to the Little Paris name.
