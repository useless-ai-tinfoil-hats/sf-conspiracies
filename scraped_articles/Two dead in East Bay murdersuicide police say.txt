Two dead in East Bay murder-suicide, police say
-----
Two people died Thursday in what Pleasanton police are calling a murder-suicide.Pleasanton police said the slaying occurred around 11:10 a.m. at a residence on Saginaw Circle, where they found a woman unresponsive and suffering from multiple gunshot wounds. Police did not say whether she died at the scene or at a hospital.
A suspect vehicle was seen by police leaving the scene and entering westbound Interstate 580, where it was followed by an unmarked police car, authorities said.
