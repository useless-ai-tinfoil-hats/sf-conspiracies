Man arrested in UC Berkeley arsons; his attorney calls it ‘persecution’ for Gaza activism
-----
An Oakland man who published essays on antiracist movements was arrested Monday in connection with three arson incidents at UC Berkeley, as well as the firebombing of a UC Berkeley police vehicle, authorities said.Casey Robert Goonan, 34, was booked into Santa Rita Jail in Dublin on seven felony charges, including multiple counts of arson, carrying an explosive or destructive device on a public street and making a destructive device without a permit, according to a Cal Fire news release.
