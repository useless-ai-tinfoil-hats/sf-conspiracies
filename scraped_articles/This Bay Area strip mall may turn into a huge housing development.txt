This Bay Area strip mall may turn into a huge housing development
-----
Developers have filed plans with the city of San Jose to potentially tear down a large strip mall and turn it into a housing development.Plans filed with the city hope to turn the retail center at 865 Blossom Hill Road, currently home to a Scandinavian Designs furniture store, BevMo! and Party City, into 172 residential units, the San Jose Mercury News reported. A preliminary application shows that the development would include 137 townhomes and 35 accessory dwelling units, or ADUs.
