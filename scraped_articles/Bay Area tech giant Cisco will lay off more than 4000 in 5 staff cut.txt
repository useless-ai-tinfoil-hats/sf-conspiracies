Bay Area tech giant Cisco will lay off more than 4,000 in 5% staff cut
-----
Cisco, the San Jose-based networking and telecommunications giant, is laying off 5% of its workforce.The company announced the cuts in a Wednesday filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, alongside its quarterly earnings report. Based on the company's reported head count, the layoffs will hit at least 4,000 workers. Cisco wrote in the filing that the cuts are aimed to "realign the organization and enable further investment in key priority areas."
