Man caught leaping from house to house in Northern California suburb
-----
A man led police on a wild chase through a Northern California neighborhood earlier this week, and helicopter footage of the pursuit looks like something out of a "Mission: Impossible" movie.On Monday at around 1:30 a.m., California Highway Patrol's Valley Division Air Operations group was called in to help Sacramento police. According to a Facebook post by CHP, Sacramento police were looking for a suspect who allegedly jumped a fence into the yard of a Natomas home.
