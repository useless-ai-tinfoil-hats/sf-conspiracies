Logan Webb indirectly calls out SF Giants teammates for second-half free fall
-----
Sometimes it takes a big win to expose what's been causing all of the losses.After Logan Webb pitched a complete game in the Giants' 2-1 win over the Padres on Monday night, Webb took the time to call for "big changes" from the team this offseason.
"It's definitely something I would like to do, but to be honest with you, winning is more important," Webb told reporters in the locker room when asked about winning the Cy Young. "If we don't do that, then it's kind of a waste to me.
