Embattled City College of San Francisco chancellor takes job in Placer County
-----
City College of San Francisco's embattled chancellor — embraced by the fiscal pragmatists who hired him but opposed by a newer, union-backed majority — will take a job at Sierra College in Placer County, the Chronicle has learned.David Martin's departure this summer ends speculation about whether his supporters could persuade him to stay, and it leaves a leadership vacuum at the financially troubled college that is under scrutiny by accreditors and by the California Community College
