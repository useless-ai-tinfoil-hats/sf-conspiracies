Man accused of Half Moon Bay mass shooting pleads not guilty
-----
A man suspected of killing seven people at two Half Moon Bay mushroom farms last year pleaded not guilty Thursday in San Mateo County Superior Court.Chunli Zhao, 67, is accused of fatally shooting seven people — and severely injuring another — in what prosecutors described as a fit of workplace rage that became the deadliest mass shooting in San Mateo County's history.
Thursday was the second time Zhao had pleaded not guilty to the crimes, but this time on a superseding indictment
