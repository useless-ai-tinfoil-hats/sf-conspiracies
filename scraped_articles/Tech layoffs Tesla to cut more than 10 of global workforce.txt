Tech layoffs: Tesla to cut more than 10% of global workforce
-----
Tesla is preparing to lay off at least 10% of its global workforce in an effort to cut costs, news outlets reported Monday morning.Amid a sagging stock price and intensifying competition in the electric car market, particularly from automakers in China, CEO Elon Musk sent a company-wide memo saying company officials made the "difficult decision" to slash headcount, according to a copy of the memo first obtained by Electrek, which covers electric vehicles.
