Site of beloved San Francisco party Church of 8 Wheels lands landmark status
-----
The site of one of San Francisco's favorite parties just cemented a new designation. The Board of Supervisors voted on Tuesday to give landmark status to the Fillmore District's Sacred Heart Parish.
