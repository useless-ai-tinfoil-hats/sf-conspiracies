Video: Two injured in separate Bay Area paragliding accidents rescued by helicopter
-----
Two people injured in separate paragliding incidents in the same area of the Vallejo hills were rescued by helicopter Sunday, California Highway Patrol officials said.In the first incident, a "paraglider down" was reported Sunday morning in the hills above Columbus Avenue in Vallejo, the CHP said in a Facebook post. A CHP helicopter responded, along with the Vallejo Fire Department and Medic Ambulance, officials said.
