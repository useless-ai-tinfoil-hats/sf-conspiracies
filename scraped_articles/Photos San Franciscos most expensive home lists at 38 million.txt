Photos: San Francisco's most expensive home lists at $38 million
-----
The most expensive home on the market in San Francisco, listed for $38 million, carries an illustrious history.The residence at 2990 Broadway St., nestled against the Presidio wall in the city's exclusive Pacific Heights neighborhood, was the estate of philanthropists George "Fritz" and Lucy Jewett, renowned for hosting politicians, royals and socialites for decades.
