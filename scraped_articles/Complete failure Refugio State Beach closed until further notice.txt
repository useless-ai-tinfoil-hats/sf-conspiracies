'Complete failure': Refugio State Beach closed until further notice
-----
As the summer approaches, Refugio State Beach visitors are usually frolicking in the surf, pitching tents in the campground, casting their rods from the shore and kayaking, hiking and biking the date-palm-dotted coastline. Lifeguards are usually perched in the towers, and lifeguards-in-training are doing safety drills in the sand.Not this year, though.
About 20 miles up the coast from Santa Barbara, this beloved family beach park was slammed by winter storms and inundated with rain
