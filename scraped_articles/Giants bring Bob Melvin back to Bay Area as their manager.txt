Giants bring Bob Melvin back to Bay Area as their manager
-----
Bob Melvin is returning to his roots yet again.Melvin, 61, has agreed to become the 18th manager in San Francisco Giants history, following interim manager Kai Correa, who held the job the final three games of last season after the team let Gabe Kapler go. The Giants plan to formally announce the decision Wednesday.
This is the second time Melvin has been under contract elsewhere and allowed to interview for another position; Oakland let him talk to the Padres after the 2021 season
