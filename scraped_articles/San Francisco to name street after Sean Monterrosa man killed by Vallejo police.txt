San Francisco to name street after Sean Monterrosa, man killed by Vallejo police
-----
San Francisco will name a street after Sean Monterrosa, the 22-year-old city resident who was shot and killed by a Vallejo police officer during a night of protests and riots stemming from the Minneapolis police killing of George Floyd in 2020.San Francisco supervisors approved legislation on Tuesday to rename Park Street at the intersection of Holly Park Circle "Sean Monterrosa Boulevard."
