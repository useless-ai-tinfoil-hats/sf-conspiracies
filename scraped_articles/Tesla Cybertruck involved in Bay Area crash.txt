Tesla Cybertruck involved in Bay Area crash
-----
* UPDATE: A Toyota that collided with a Tesla Cybertruck sustained major damage in the first reported crash involving the electric vehicle.The driver of a Tesla Cybertruck sustained minor injuries Thursday after a collision on Highway 35, California Highway Patrol said.
The crash occurred after the 17-year-old driver of a Toyota Corolla traveling south near Page Mill Road "struck a dirt embankment on the right shoulder," CHP said.
