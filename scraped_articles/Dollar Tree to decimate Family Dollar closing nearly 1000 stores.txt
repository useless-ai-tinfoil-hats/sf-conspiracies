Dollar Tree to decimate Family Dollar, closing nearly 1,000 stores
-----
Discount retailer Dollar Tree plans to close nearly 1,000 Family Dollar stores over the next several years, the company announced in its disappointing fourth quarter earnings report on Wednesday morning.Specifically, Dollar Tree will close about 30 Dollar Tree stores and 970 Family Dollar locations over the next three years. The latter equates to nearly 12% of its current Family Dollar locations, an analyst told the Associated Press.
