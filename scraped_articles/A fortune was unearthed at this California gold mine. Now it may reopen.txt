A fortune was unearthed at this California gold mine. Now it may reopen
-----
In California's long affair with gold, the 160-year-old Idaho-Maryland Mine stands as one of the great mother lodes.From the 1860s to 1956, its 73 miles of tunnels, an hour north of where the discovery of gold kicked off the historic rush of Forty-Niners, bustled on and off with rickety mine cars and workers in hard hats.
