New controversy for S.F. bike lane: Has it led to a drop in cycling on popular street?
-----
A heated dispute between community advocates, businesses and the San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency has deepened after a new report shows Valencia Street saw a decline of up to 53% in cycling activity after the introduction of the highly controversial center-running bike lane last year.The clash revolves around conflicting interpretations of year-over-year data collected by the SFMTA for September, a month after the bikeway opened to traffic.
