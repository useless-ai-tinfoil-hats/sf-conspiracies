49ers optimistic Deebo Samuel will be available Sunday against Lions
-----
Deebo Samuel wasn't ready to practice Wednesday, but the San Francisco 49ers' wide receiver with the banged-up left shoulder was showing signs of progress."He's feeling better," head coach Kyle Shanahan said.
Does that provide optimism about Samuel's ability to play against the Lions on Sunday in the NFC Championship Game?
"It makes me feel better," Shanahan said.
