Oakland police chase after attempted carjack leads to crash
-----
An attempted carjacking in Oakland led to a police car chase that resulted in a crash near Interstate 880 Tuesday morning, authorities said.Officers were investigating an attempted carjacking in the 7700 block of International Boulevard in East Oakland just before 4 a.m. when they saw a car connected to the incident, the Police Department said in a statement.
