This winemaker just bought back his winery from a giant conglomerate
-----
A California winemaker has pulled off the unimaginable: James Hall, the founding winemaker of famed Sonoma producer Patz & Hall, has reclaimed the brand from one of wine's biggest conglomerates, Ste. Michelle Wine Estates.The sale, first reported by Wine Spectator, is a surprising wrinkle in the consolidation trend that's dominating the California wine industry. But it could also be the latest indicator of troubled waters for Ste. Michelle Estates and the industry as a whole.
