Here’s why San Francisco was crowned America’s healthiest city in 2024
-----
San Francisco's invigorating ocean breezes, abundant hiking trails, expansive parks and open spaces, and diverse dining and food options are more than just attractions — they are the foundation of a healthy lifestyle.In a study published by WalletHub, which evaluated more than 180 major U.S. cities using 41 key indicators, San Francisco was crowned as America's healthiest city.
This comes at a time when over 70% of U.S. adults are overweight and health care expenses are escalating.
