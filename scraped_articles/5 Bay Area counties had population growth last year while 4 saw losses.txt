5 Bay Area counties had population growth last year, while 4 saw losses
-----
Napa County saw the highest percentage population gain in the nine-county San Francisco Bay Area last year, according to new data released by the state. Four other Bay Area counties saw population growth in 2023: Contra Costa, San Francisco, Solano and Santa Clara.
