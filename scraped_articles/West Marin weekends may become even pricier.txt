West Marin weekends may become even pricier
-----
Does hiking in Point Reyes National Seashore, shucking oysters at Tomales Bay Oyster Company or snapping photos of a dilapidated shipwreck necessitate an overnight stay?These were some of the questions guiding Marin County's lawmakers as they attempted to address housing issues by culling the number of Airbnbs and VRBOs allowed in popular vacation destinations in unincorporated Marin.
