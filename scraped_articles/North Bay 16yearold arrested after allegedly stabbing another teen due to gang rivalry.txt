North Bay 16-year-old arrested after allegedly stabbing another teen due to gang rivalry
-----
A 16-year-old high school student was arrested Wednesday after allegedly stabbing another student with a kitchen knife, according to Santa Rosa police.Around 8:30 a.m., a 16-year-old male student approached a 14-year-old male student near the baseball field at Santa Rosa High School and the two began arguing, police said. The argument escalated into the 16-year-old knocking the other student to the ground and pulling out a kitchen knife with a 7-inch blade, officials said.
