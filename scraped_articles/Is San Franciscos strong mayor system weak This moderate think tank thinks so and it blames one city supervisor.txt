Is San Francisco’s ‘strong mayor’ system weak? This moderate think tank thinks so, and it blames one city supervisor
-----
A moderate think tank blames San Francisco Supervisor Aaron Peskin for the city's most pressing problems, arguing that he has supported or authored a series of ballot measures over two decades that eroded the power of the mayor's office.The attack on Peskin comes as rumors persist that the Board of Supervisors president will challenge Mayor London Breed in next year's election. But Peskin has said repeatedly he has not decided whether he will run.
