Bruce Springsteen back on the road and headed for S.F. Here’s the setlist

Following a significant delay, Bruce Springsteen and the E Street Band's 2024 North American tour is finally back on track.The group began its trek with an impressive 29-song set in Phoenix on Tuesday, March 19 — marking its first performance since the band postponed tour dates last September, including two shows in San Francisco, due to the 74-year-old frontman's battle with peptic ulcer disease.
