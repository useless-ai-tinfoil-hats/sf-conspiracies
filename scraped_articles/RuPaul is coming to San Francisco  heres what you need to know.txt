RuPaul is coming to San Francisco — here’s what you need to know
-----
Bay Area drag fans can sashay their way to the Curran Theater for an intimate night with drag icon RuPaul. The Emmy winner is heading to San Francisco on March 14 in celebration of his memoir, "The House of Hidden Meanings," scheduled to be released March 5.At "Unscripted: An Evening With RuPaul," fans will get to hear the star share his story, personal philosophy and more.
