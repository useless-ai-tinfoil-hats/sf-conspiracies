Klay Thompson on loss of Pac-12 basketball: ‘Piece of your childhood is gone’
-----
LAS VEGAS — This week's Pac-12 men's basketball tournament carries a heavy dose of nostalgia, strong enough to overshadow the customary story lines playing out ahead of Selection Sunday.The event starting Wednesday at T-Mobile Arena marks the final basketball chapter for the conference, which will shrink from 12 members to two this summer. Washington State and Oregon State strangely and suddenly will find themselves alone.
