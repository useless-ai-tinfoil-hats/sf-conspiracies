Teen shot near S.F.’s Ghirardelli Square, prompting school lockdown, officials say
-----
A teenager was shot in the middle of a school day near Ghirardelli Square on Wednesday afternoon, police said.The shooting at North Point and Polk streets prompted a lockdown — and then a full lockout — at the Galileo Academy of Science and Technology, a high school just a few blocks away from Ghirardelli Square, employees at the high school said when reached by phone Wednesday.
