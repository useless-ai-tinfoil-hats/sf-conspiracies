SF's iconic 'rock 'n' roll motel' Phoenix on the market for $15M
-----
Phoenix Hotel, the Tenderloin's two-story, Hollywood-style hotel known for its party scene and famous clientele, recently hit the market for $15 million and could be slated for major redevelopment.The hotel, which sits on the corner of Larkin and Eddy streets, is lauded as an "oasis" in multiple online reviews.
