Lego Store in downtown San Francisco Centre closed permanently
-----
Another retailer has closed its doors permanently in downtown's beleaguered San Francisco Centre mall.The Lego Store shuttered last week, a spokesperson for the Lego company confirmed to SFGATE, though the company declined to give a reason for the closure. The store had been open since 2016.
