Pedrick Produce is a tasty stop en route to Tahoe, but you might sign a waiver
-----
A fruit stand in Dixon, California has some of the cheapest groceries in the Bay Area, but that's not the only reason drivers are pulled into Pedrick Produce. On an afternoon in early December, I discovered their fabled "Wall of Flame" within the massive quonset hut which can be found just off Interstate 80.The wall is a purveyor of pain.
