Tech layoffs: Snap, owner of Snapchat, to cut 10% of workforce
-----
Snap Inc., the parent company of social media giant Snapchat, is set to reduce its workforce by 10%, affecting around 530 employees globally.The company disclosed this information in a regulatory filing on Monday, saying that the layoffs are a strategic move for long-term growth.
