49ers put pass rusher Drake Jackson on injured reserve with knee injury
-----
Pass rusher Drake Jackson, the San Francisco 49ers' 2022 second-round pick who hasn't registered a sack or quarterback hit since his three-sack performance in their season opener, was placed on injured reserve Saturday with a knee injury.Jackson was expected to be inactive for Sunday's game in Jacksonville due to last week's trade for Washington pass rusher Chase Young.
