Bay Area deputies confiscate more than 80 firearms from home
-----
San Mateo County sheriff's deputies seized more than 80 firearms from a home, including many that the agency said were owned illegally.The weapons were taken Friday from a home in San Mateo's Eichler Highlands neighborhood, according to the sheriff's office. The collection included several "ghost guns," self-built and hard-to-trace firearms whose possession is heavily restricted, as well as assault rifles and suppressed handguns, the agency added. Some were also found to be stolen.
