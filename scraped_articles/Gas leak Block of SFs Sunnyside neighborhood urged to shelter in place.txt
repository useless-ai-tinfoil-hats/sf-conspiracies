Gas leak: Block of SF's Sunnyside neighborhood urged to shelter in place
-----
Residents in part of San Francisco's Sunnyside neighborhood are being asked to shelter in place as emergency crews respond to a gas leak, officials said.The leak happened on the 600 block of Joost Avenue after a construction crew struck a gas line, the San Francisco Fire Department said in a social media post just before 11 a.m.
