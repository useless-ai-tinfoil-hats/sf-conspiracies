Giants’ deadline moves pale in comparison with other NL contenders
-----
National League contenders with the same intentions as the San Francisco Giants — a spot in the playoff bracket — were mostly productive at the trade deadline, not only strengthening themselves but further widening the disparity between the Giants and the pack, on paper at least.The Giants didn't beef up at the deadline, but their fellow contenders did. Not with superstars but with enough solid players and role players to fill roster holes and advance their chances heading to October.
