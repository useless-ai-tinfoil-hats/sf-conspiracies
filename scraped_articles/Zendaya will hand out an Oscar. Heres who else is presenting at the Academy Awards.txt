Zendaya will hand out an Oscar. Here’s who else is presenting at the Academy Awards
-----
Zendaya has booked her next gig: She'll hand out a gold statuette at the 96th Academy Awards. And she won't be the only Bay Area native to utter the famous words, "and the Oscar goes to …"Fellow Oakland actor Mahershala Ali and Daly City native Sam Rockwell, both Oscar winners, are also on the initial list of presenters released by the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences on Monday, Feb. 26.
