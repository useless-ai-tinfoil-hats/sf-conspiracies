S.F. police swarm SoMa building near 6th and Mission streets
-----
San Francisco police officers swarmed what appeared to be a residential building in the area of Sixth and Mission streets in the city's South of Market neighborhood Monday afternoon.Police did not immediately respond to requests for more details about the incident, but officers at the scene were unspooling caution tape and preventing people claiming to be residents of the building from entering.
Officials sent out an alert warning the public to avoid the area of Sixth and Mission
