Weird odor prompts evacuation of Albany Target
-----
An "unusual odor" prompted the evacuation of an Albany Target store Friday evening, city officials said.The odor was reported at the Target store located at 1057 Eastshore Highway around 4:10 p.m., according to Brennen Brown, a spokesperson for the city. Shortly after, Albany firefighters arrived on the scene and evacuated the store.
There were no injuries reported and the chemical causing the odor has not been confirmed, Brown said.
The store has since reopened.
