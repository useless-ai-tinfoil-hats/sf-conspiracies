California's Central Coast rattled by earthquake
-----
Residents up and down California's Central Coast were rattled by an earthquake shortly after noon Wednesday.The U.S. Geological Survey estimated it was a magnitude 3.2 earthquake; initial magnitude measurements can change as the USGS gets more accurate data. It was centered about 8 miles northwest of Avila Beach near San Luis Obispo at a depth of about 6 kilometers.
Although shaking was felt throughout the region, there were no initial reports of damage or injuries.
