Man missing for days is rescued in Santa Cruz County redwood forest, officials say
-----
A missing Santa Cruz County man who was last seen more than a week earlier was rescued in Big Basin Redwoods State Park in Santa Cruz County on Thursday evening.Lukas McClish, 34, was reunited with family after authorities said they found him in the forest near Foreman Creek.
His last known whereabouts had been on St. Francis Drive in Boulder Creek on June 11, the Santa Cruz County Sheriff's Office said.
