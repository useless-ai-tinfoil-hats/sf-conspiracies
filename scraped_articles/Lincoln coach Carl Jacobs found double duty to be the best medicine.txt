Lincoln coach Carl Jacobs found double duty to be the best medicine
-----
The remedy for a heart attack normally isn't more work or, in the case of Lincoln basketball coach Carl Jacobs, adding another team to lead.But the 41-year high school coaching veteran does not consider any realm of basketball to be taxing. Especially adding the Lincoln girls team.
"Basketball is not stressful for me," said Jacobs. "If anything, it's healing. It's actually really helped in this healing process."
