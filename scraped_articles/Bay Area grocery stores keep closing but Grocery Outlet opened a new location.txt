Bay Area grocery stores keep closing but Grocery Outlet opened a new location
-----
Despite several grocery stores closing Bay Area outposts over the last year, such as Whole Foods on SF's Market Street, a Lucky Supermarket in Dublin and a Safeway in Santa Clara, a San Francisco-founded supermarket chain is opening a new store in an under-served part of Marin County.The new outpost of the chain Grocery Outlet opened to a line down the street on Thursday at 150 Donahue St. in Sausalito, according to KRON4.
