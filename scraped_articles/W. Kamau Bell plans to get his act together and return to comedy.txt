W. Kamau Bell plans to ‘get his act together’ and return to comedy
-----
Documentarian, TV host and author W. Kamau Bell is getting back to his roots in comedy after a five-year hiatus.The Oakland comic plans to bring "W. Kamau Bell Gets His Act Together," an eight-show stand-up residency, to Berkeley Repertory Theatre's Bakery Studio. Each hourlong set will showcase new material inspired by current events and his own life.
The limited engagement is set to kick off Jan. 20 and continue every Saturday night through March 16.
