Woman shot by Pacifica police identified, allegedly shot her adult son and dog prior
-----
LATEST April 4, 4:40 p.m. The Pacifica Police Department identified 50-year-old Michelle Arrais as the woman who died after reportedly shooting her 23-year-old son on March 27, according to police. The woman was shot by two Pacifica police officers, Hayden Fry and Kevin Contreras, after she pointed a gun at them, the department said in a Thursday news release.Police said Arrais' son called 911 just before 8 a.m. "to report he had been shot by his mother."
