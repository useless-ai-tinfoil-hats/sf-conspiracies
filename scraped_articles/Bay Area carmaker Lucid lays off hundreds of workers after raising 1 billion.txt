Bay Area carmaker Lucid lays off hundreds of workers after raising $1 billion
-----
Lucid Group, a Bay Area-based luxury electric vehicle company, announced on Friday that it's laying off about 400 employees.The layoff round will cut 6% of Lucid's staff by the end of October, according to the company's filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission on Friday. Most of the layoffs will hit California employees, company spokesperson Jesse Caputo told SFGATE; the company is headquartered in Newark but manufactures its cars in Arizona.
