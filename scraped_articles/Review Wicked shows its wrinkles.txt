Review: ‘Wicked’ shows its wrinkles
-----
"Frozen," "Legally Blonde," "Harry Potter" and "Mean Girls" might not seem like bedfellows. But they might have a polyamorous love child in "Wicked."That might sound backward since "Wicked," at least as a novel, predates the others. But to revisit the show now, as I did at BroadwaySF's Orpheum Theatre on Thursday, Aug. 29, is to feel reverse déjà vu. However unfairly, the first idea now seems like the hurried, cliched pastiche.
