The prettiest coastal road in Northern California you've never heard of
-----
Unless you're from Humboldt County or you've traveled extensively in California, you probably haven't heard of Scenic Drive. A 300-mile journey north of San Francisco, this short but astounding coastal road isn't advertised anywhere, but really, it should be famous.The narrow, crumbling lane snakes along the dramatic California coastline, extending down from wind-whipped Trinidad to boulder-strewn Moonstone Beach.
