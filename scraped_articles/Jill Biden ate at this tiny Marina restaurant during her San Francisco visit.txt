Jill Biden ate at this tiny Marina restaurant during her San Francisco visit
-----
First lady Jill Biden made a pit stop in San Francisco's Marina District for a seafood lunch during her whirlwind trip Wednesday.Biden, who arrived at San Francisco International Airport on Tuesday for a speech in support of President Joe Biden's reelection campaign, visited Popi's Oysterette, a cozy spot at 2095 Chestnut St. co-owned by former "Top Chef" contestant Melissa Perfit, before heading to a fundraising event in Southern California in the evening.
