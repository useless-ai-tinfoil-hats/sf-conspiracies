Rite Aid to close 31 stores in California, 2 in Bay Area following bankruptcy
-----
Rite Aid is closing 154 stores, including 31 across California and two in the Bay Area, following the company's Chapter 11 bankruptcy filing earlier this week.The drugstore chain filed for bankruptcy with the U.S. Bankruptcy Court for the District of New Jersey on Sunday, citing competitive pressures, weakening sales and debt from lawsuits pertaining to the opioid crisis as reasons for the company's need for a massive restructuring plan.
