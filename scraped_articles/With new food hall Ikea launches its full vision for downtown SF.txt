With new food hall, Ikea launches its full vision for downtown SF
-----
A massive new food hall from Ikea's sister company opens Thursday, bringing to fruition the firm's grand vision for downtown San Francisco. The two-floor, 450-seat Saluhall space features five local food vendors, as well as two bars, a bakery, a cooking school and more.
