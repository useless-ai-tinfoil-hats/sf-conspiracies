Weekend-long Union Street Festival boasts bounty of ‘what makes San Francisco fun’
-----
Union Street in San Francisco's Cow Hollow neighborhood was abuzz Saturday as thousands of residents flocked to the stretch for the start of a weekend-long festival.The 46th annual Union Street Festival, which stretches along Union between Fillmore and Gough streets, has a bit of everything — food, drinks, music performances, local artisans and artists, facepainting and balloon art.
