A bullet tore through the home of an Oakland ACLU attorney. She's not going anywhere
-----
Growing up in Oakland, Allyssa Victory said she witnessed multiple shootings. And as an attorney who advocates for criminal justice reform, she's familiar with the effects of violence on communities.But seeing bullets burst through the walls of her home Friday night was the closest she's personally been exposed to it, she said. No one in the house, including her teenage godson, her husband and her father-in-law, was injured.
