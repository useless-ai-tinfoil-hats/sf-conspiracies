Bay Area woman found alive after disappearing in Northern California wilderness
-----
A Bay Area woman visiting Mendocino County who went missing while on a hike near Fort Bragg last week was found alive after spending two nights and nearly three days alone in the remote wilderness, authorities said.Elizabeth Schenk, 70, a psychotherapist living in Oakland, was last seen at 9 a.m. last Tuesday, when she went for a walk on Bruhel Point Road and called an acquaintance nearby to ask for directions.
