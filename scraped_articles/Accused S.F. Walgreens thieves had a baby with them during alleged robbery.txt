Accused S.F. Walgreens thieves had a baby with them during alleged robbery
-----
A group arrested after allegedly stealing from a San Francisco Walgreens last week had a baby with them during the theft, according to San Francisco police.Police Chief Bill Scott said eight individuals allegedly brought a baby with them as they entered the Walgreens while wearing masks and began throwing merchandise into bags and a wagon. Nearby police officers arrested six individuals, five of whom were charged with burglary, attempted grand theft and attempted organized retail theft.
