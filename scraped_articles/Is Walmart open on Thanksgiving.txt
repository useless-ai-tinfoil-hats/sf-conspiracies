Is Walmart open on Thanksgiving?
-----
Walmart is known for being a great place to shop for deals and for having an incredible selection of grocery items. After all, there aren't many places where you can run out on a holiday like Thanksgiving to pick up that cranberry sauce you forgot and shop for a big-screen TV at the same time. But if you find yourself having a grocery emergency on Thanksgiving 2023, there's bad news: Walmart will not be open on Nov. 23.Walmart used to be open on Thanksgiving Day, but announced in 2021
