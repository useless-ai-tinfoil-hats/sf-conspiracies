Snow falls in California's Sierra, first chain control of season in effect
-----
LATEST Oct. 25, 2:45 p.m. Snow fell in California's Sierra Nevada on Wednesday afternoon, marking the first significant snowfall of the season.Chain control was put into effect for the first time this season on Interstate 80 between the Donner Lake interchange and Kingvale, CHP said. It's the first time chain control has been implemented on the roadway since the last snow season.
