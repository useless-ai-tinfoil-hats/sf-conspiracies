‘We were shocked’: How Warriors got Dario Saric on one of NBA’s best deals
-----
During NBA free agency this past July, several Golden State Warriors decision-makers made pitches to journeyman forward Dario Saric that could be distilled to the same four words: "We need you. Badly."After ping-ponging among four teams in seven years, Saric, 29, appreciated being wanted. There was just one problem: The Warriors could only offer him a one-year minimum contract worth $2 million, an 80% pay cut from his most recent salary with the Suns and Thunder.
