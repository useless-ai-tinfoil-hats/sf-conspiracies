Man dies in his cell at Bay Area jail
-----
A 27-year-old man died Saturday in his cell at Santa Clara County's main jail in San Jose, a sheriff's spokesperson said.Jail deputies found the man unresponsive around 4:15 a.m. Deputies and medical staff started CPR on him, but he was pronounced dead at the scene, Deputy Felicia Segura said in a statement.
No foul play was suspected in the man's death, Segura said, adding that he was held alone.
