Driver who sped 120 mph over Bay Bridge arrested after 3-county chase, CHP says
-----
California Highway Patrol officers arrested a man it said drove more than 120 mph across the Bay Bridge while evading law enforcement early Saturday.The man initially caught the attention of CHP officers while driving recklessly in San Francisco close to midnight, according to the agency. Officers called in a helicopter to pursue the vehicle as it headed toward the Bay Bridge, the CHP said.
