Residents warned to shut windows after S.F. fire drapes Presidio Heights in smoke
-----
A block of Presidio Heights in San Francisco was engulfed in a smoky haze Friday morning due to a sizable fire at a four-story residential building.About 60 firefighters were required to keep the blaze contained to the building, which appeared to be under construction. Images the San Francisco Fire Department posted to social media showed scaffolding outside the building.
There were no injuries immediately reported.
