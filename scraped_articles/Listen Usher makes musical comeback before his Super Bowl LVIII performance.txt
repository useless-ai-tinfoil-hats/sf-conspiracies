The Last Dinner Party, 'Prelude To Ecstasy' (Island Records)
Fresh off a tour with "Take Me to Church" singer Hozier, indie rock band the Last Dinner Party has its debut album, "Prelude to Ecstasy," out Friday, Feb. 2. The 12-track record will include a number of singles that the group has already put out, including "Nothing Matters," "Sinner," and "My Lady of Mercy."
