Body reportedly found near Vallejo sideshow
-----
A body was found early Saturday near a Vallejo sideshow that involved more than 20 gunshots and cars racing around a ring of fire, according to reports.Witnesses said the sideshow lasted about an hour before police arrived around 1 a.m., according to KTVU. Video posted by NBC Bay Area showed cars doing doughnuts around participants standing in a ring of fire at Lewis Brown and B.W. Williams drives.
