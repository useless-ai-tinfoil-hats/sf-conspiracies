California traffic fatalities rise 5% while the national rate declines
-----
In the first quarter of 2024, traffic fatalities in California grew by approximately 5%, contrasting with a national decline of 3.2%, according to preliminary data from the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration.The agency reported on Monday that an estimated 8,650 individuals died in traffic crashes nationwide during the year's first three months, down from 8,935 in the same period last year.
