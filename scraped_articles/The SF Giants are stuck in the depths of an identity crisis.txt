The SF Giants are stuck in the depths of an identity crisis
-----
Who are these San Francisco Giants?A month into the 2024 season and it feels impossible to come up with a true answer to this question. At the end of April, the Giants sit at one game under .500, firmly in the mix for one of the National League wild card spots while not losing complete sight of the Dodgers in their own division.
