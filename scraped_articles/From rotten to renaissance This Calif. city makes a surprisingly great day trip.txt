From 'rotten' to 'renaissance': This Calif. city makes a surprisingly great day trip
-----
With a footprint of just 1.2 square miles, Emeryville is one of the Bay Area's smallest cities. But unlike similarly sized towns such as Kensington and Piedmont, it's not a wealthy residential enclave tucked away in the hills.
