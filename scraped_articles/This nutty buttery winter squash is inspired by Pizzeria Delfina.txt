This time of year, simple and easy meals are what I yearn for, and lately roasting has really taken a firm place in my kitchen.For today's recipe — Roasted Delicata Squash with Brown Butter and Calabrian Chile — I was heavily inspired by one of our favorite pizza joints in the Bay Area, Pizzeria Delfina.
