Target is shutting down its East Palo Alto store
-----
Target is shutting down its East Palo Alto store, the company confirmed to SFGATE on Tuesday, citing "prolonged underperformance."In a statement to SFGATE, Joe Unger, a Target spokesperson, said the decision came "after much consideration."
"We're grateful to our team at this store, and we're working with all eligible team members to offer them an opportunity to transfer to other Target locations," Unger said.
