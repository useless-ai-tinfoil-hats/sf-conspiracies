Giants’ Gabe Kapler answers Logan Webb’s call for ‘big changes’
-----
UPDATE: Giants fire Gabe Kapler after team's stretch run stumble, September swoonA day after San Francisco Giants pitcher Logan Webb called for "big changes" in 2024 and for a better "winning culture" and "winning mentality," manager Gabe Kapler expressed support for his ace's message.
"Loved it and completely agree with everything that Logan said," said Kapler, who repeatedly emphasized the need for better preparation in an interview before Tuesday night's game at Oracle Park.
