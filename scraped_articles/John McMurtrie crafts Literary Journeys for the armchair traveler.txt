John McMurtrie crafts ‘Literary Journeys’ for the armchair traveler
-----
John McMurtrie doesn't need to venture far to travel the world.The Albany writer, editor and book curator arrived at Heyma Yemeni Coffee on University Avenue in Berkeley on a bike he bought on Treasure Island. In his bag, he carried a copy of his new book "Literary Journeys: Mapping Fictional Travels Across the World of Literature," a collection of essays, illustrations and archival materials that map fictional travels across literature.
