Much has changed since A’s and Rays filled Coliseum for 2019 wild-card game
-----
Oakland A's fans gazing at Mount Davis now see the team's retired numbers and Athletics logo printed on green tarps.But, on Oct. 2, 2019, the level was filled with fans rocking plenty of green and gold — jerseys, rally towels, flags — as the A's took on the Tampa Bay Rays in the American League wild-card matchup.
