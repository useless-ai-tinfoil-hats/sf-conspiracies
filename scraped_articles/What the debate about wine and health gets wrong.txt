What the debate about wine and health gets wrong
-----

Hungry for any writing assignment I could get, I constantly volunteered for the beat that no other staffer appeared eager to cover: wine and health.It was a strange exercise, because seemingly every week a study was published that contradicted the study I'd covered the week before.
