What should the Bay Area's new WNBA team be called?
-----
With the WNBA officially announcing Thursday that the Bay Area is going to receive an expansion team for the 2025 season, the big question that remains is what it should be called.It's not an easy decision. The ideal name needs to land at some intersection of cultural resonance, local pride and, for better or for worse, marketability that can last for years to come. In other words, the opposite of the Washington Wizards.
