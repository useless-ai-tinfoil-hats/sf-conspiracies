California officials warn drinks sold at Total Wine are 'unsafe'
-----
The state of California warned the public on Thursday, May 30, not to drink multiple batches of Mary Jones drinks made by the Jones Soda Company because they are "unsafe" and posed a "risk to consumers," according to an advisory from the California Department of Public Health.The Mary Jones drinks sold in green apple, root beer, berry lemonade and orange and cream flavors contain an illegal form of intoxicating THC derived from hemp, according to the agency. CDPH Director Tomás J. Aragón
