Super Bowl rematch: 49ers will try to avenge loss to Chiefs 4 years ago
-----
The San Francisco 49ers have a chance to grab their long-awaited sixth Super Bowl win against the franchise that handed them their last Super Bowl loss.The 49ers will meet the defending-champion Chiefs in Super Bowl LVIII on Feb. 11 in Las Vegas, a rematch of their gutting 31-20 title-game loss to Kansas City in Miami Gardens, Fla., four years ago. The defeat dropped the 49ers to 5-2 in Super Bowls and extended their championship drought, which has reached 28 seasons.
