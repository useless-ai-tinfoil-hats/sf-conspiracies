The pride and power of Catch One, LA's trailblazing Black gay disco
-----
In decades past, when filmmaker C. Fitz would travel to Los Angeles, she knew where she'd go out dancing: Jewel's Catch One. The cavernous multi-story club thrummed with music and sweating bodies, with subwoofers scattered throughout the space that revelers sometimes sat on. Any given night brimmed with every kind of person.
