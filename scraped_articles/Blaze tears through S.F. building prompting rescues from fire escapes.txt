Blaze tears through S.F. building, prompting rescues from fire escapes
-----
San Francisco firefighters rescued five people from a burning building on Valencia Street in the Mission District on Tuesday morning.Three people were hospitalized with non-life-threatening injuries, Fire Department officials said.
The fire burned on each of the building's three floors, "with people rescued from the inside as well as off fire escapes," Fire Department officials said in a post on X.
