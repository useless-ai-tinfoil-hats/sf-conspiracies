Port of Oakland shipping container fire that spewed dark smoke is extinguished
-----
A shipping container erupted in flames at the Port of Oakland on Sunday, spewing dark smoke until firefighters extinguished it, officials said.The cause of the fire remained under investigation and it was unclear whether anyone was injured. The blaze was extinguished around 4 p.m., the Oakland Fire Department said on X.
Social media users said the smoke was visible from across San Francisco Bay at Oracle Park, where the Giants scored a 6-5 win over the Cincinnati Reds on Sunday
