Man rescued from S.F. cliff by helicopter after calling for help with cellphone
-----
A man was rescued from a cliff by helicopter at Lands End on Sunday evening after using a cellphone to call for help, according to a spokesperson for the San Francisco Fire Department.The adult male was rescued in a joint operation with a helicopter from the California Highway Patrol. The helicopter was able to get a paramedic close enough to rescue the adult male, who was 40 to 50 feet below the cliff summit. The man was then transferred to the care of ground medics, who transported him
