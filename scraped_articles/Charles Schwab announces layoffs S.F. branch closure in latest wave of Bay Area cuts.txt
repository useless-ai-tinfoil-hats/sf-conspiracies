Charles Schwab announces layoffs, S.F. branch closure in latest wave of Bay Area cuts
-----
The staggering wave of layoffs affecting the Bay Area will persist into the upcoming year.Charles Schwab announced this week plans to close a downtown San Francisco retail branch at 100 Post St. on Dec. 1. The move comes as a spokesperson for the financial services firm confirmed a workforce reduction of approximately 5%-6%, affecting an estimated 2,000 of the 35,900 employees the company reported in its latest quarterly statement.
