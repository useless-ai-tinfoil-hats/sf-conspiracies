Honorary doctorate for Betty Reid Soskin, 102, longtime park ranger and East Bay resident
-----
Longtime Bay Area resident Betty Reid Soskin, the country's oldest national park ranger before retiring two years ago, has received an honorary doctorate from Cal State East Bay for her "exceptional accomplishments and contributions," administrators said.The university in Hayward honored Soskin, 102, with a doctorate in humane letters during Saturday's commencement ceremony for its College of Letters, Arts and Social Sciences, university officials announced.
