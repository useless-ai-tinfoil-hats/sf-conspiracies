49ers interview former Chargers coach Brandon Staley for coordinator opening
-----
The San Francisco 49ers have interviewed former Chargers head coach Brandon Staley for their defensive coordinator opening, a league source said Monday.Staley and in-house candidate Nick Sorensen are the only candidates who have interviewed for the vacancy, which was created when the 49ers fired defensive coordinator Steve Wilks on Feb. 14 after his one season with the team.
