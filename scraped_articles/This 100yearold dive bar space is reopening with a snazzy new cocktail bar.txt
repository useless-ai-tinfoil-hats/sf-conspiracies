This 100-year-old dive bar space is reopening with a snazzy new cocktail bar
-----
A crew of San Francisco bar industry veterans is ready to bring a storied location back to life with a cocktail spot inspired by their mothers.April Jean opens its doors on Friday night in North Beach, at 1371 Grant Ave., the longtime home of dive and live music venue Grant & Green Saloon. The new bar keeps the address' longtime tradition as a bar, which goes back 100 years, with a hip, midcentury look and a concise list of craft cocktails.
