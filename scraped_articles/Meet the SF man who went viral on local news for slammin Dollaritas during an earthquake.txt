Meet the SF man who went viral on local news for 'slammin' Dollaritas' during an earthquake
-----
When you go to Applebee's, you often get more than you expect. Ask Steve Mazzari, better known to some as "Dollarita Steve."The 28-year-old software engineer was spending his Friday evening with friends at the Applebee's on Fisherman's Wharf. They were drinking Dollaritas, the chain's $1 margaritas, when they felt the shake of a 3.7 magnitude earthquake hitting the Bay Area. For a moment, the restaurant went quiet.
