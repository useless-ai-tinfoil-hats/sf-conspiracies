Hours before first pitch, in the corner of Oracle Park's Triples Alley, Joey Bart and Logan Webb got a chance to catch up.It has been several weeks since Bart, the San Francisco Giants' second overall pick in the 2018 draft, was designated for assignment and traded to the Pittsburgh Pirates. The weirdness of seeing longtime teammates now as competitors set in and Bart kept the reunions in his return to Oracle Park to a minimum.
