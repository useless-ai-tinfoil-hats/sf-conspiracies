S.F. bar known for Fernet flights reopens after four-year closure
-----
A San Francisco bar dedicated to a popular genre of herbaceous, bitter liqueurs has finally reopened after a four-year closure.Amaro-focused spot Bar 821 reopened a few weeks ago on Fridays and Saturdays and is now open Thursdays to Saturdays, owner Khaled Dajani told the Chronicle. The space at 821 Divisadero St. is among a handful of bars dedicated to amari in the United States, going beyond well-known options like Fernet and Campari to include over 200 amari selections before it shut
