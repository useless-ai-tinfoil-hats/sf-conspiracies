Only 4 Bay Area restaurants, chefs make James Beard 2024 finalist list
-----
Only four Bay Area restaurants and chefs are finalists in the 2024 James Beard awards, which were announced Wednesday morning. In January, the James Beard Foundation's semifinalists list included 17 Bay Area nominees.
