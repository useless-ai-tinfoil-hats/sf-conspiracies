East Oakland broad-daylight shooting leaves 1 dead
-----
Oakland police are investigating a shooting that left one person dead Tuesday morning.The shooting happened just before 11:15 a.m. on the 10900 block of MacArthur Boulevard, a residential area in East Oakland, according to Oakland police spokesperson Darryl Rodgers.
A person with gunshot wounds was found there and died, despite lifesaving measures performed by officers and firefighters. The victim's identity was not immediately released pending notification of next of kin,
