Glamorous near-century-old SF rooftop bar is finally making its return
-----
Since 1928, the bar on the 21st floor of the Beacon Grand, formerly the Sir Francis Drake Hotel, was home to some of the best views and liveliest parties in San Francisco. Now, the cocktail bar once known as the Starlight Room is being reborn as Starlite.The iconic Union Square destination, which opened during the Prohibition era, was once a hangout for celebrities like Alan Ginsberg and Herb Caen.
