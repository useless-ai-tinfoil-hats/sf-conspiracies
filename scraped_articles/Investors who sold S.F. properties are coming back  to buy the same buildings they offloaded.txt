Investors who sold S.F. properties are coming back — to buy the same buildings they offloaded
-----
At the height of San Francisco's tech boom in 2018, a real estate private equity firm run by one of Hong Kong's richest families sold an office campus located on the city's waterfront to America's largest private landlord. They earned a whopping $245 million from the deal.Now, Gaw Capital Partners — the private equity firm run by Goodwin Gaw — wants the property back, but at about one-third of its pre-pandemic value.
