Oregon State’s thumping of San Jose State caps Pac-12’s perfect weekend
-----
San Jose State has 10 regular-season games left in its season. The Spartans must be thankful to know they don't have any other Pac-12 teams on the schedule.At CEFCU Stadium on Sunday, No. 18 Oregon State dominated SJSU 42-17. In the Spartans' opener at No. 6 USC on Aug. 26, they fell 56-28.
Against USC, the Spartans put up 396 yards, split evenly between running and passing. Against OSU, the Spartans managed only 279, including 108 in the first half.
