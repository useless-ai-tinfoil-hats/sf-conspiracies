Driver killed after hitting concrete divide, 2 other vehicles on East Bay highway
-----
A driver died Friday night in a car crash on Interstate 680 near Concord, the California Highway Patrol said.The crash was reported around 9:52 p.m. Friday, according to the CHP Contra Costa office. A driver in a Honda was traveling south on the highway when the car crashed into the concrete center divide, causing two other vehicles — a Chevrolet and a Tesla — to collide with the Honda, police said.
