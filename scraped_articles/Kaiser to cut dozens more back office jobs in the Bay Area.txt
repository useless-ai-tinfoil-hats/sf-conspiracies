Kaiser to cut dozens more back office jobs in the Bay Area
-----
Kaiser Foundation Hospitals is set to reduce its workforce by more than 70 employees by April, primarily in the East Bay, according to information provided last week to the California Employment Development Department.In regulatory filings, Kaiser's Human Resources Director Christine Neubauer said the layoffs would affect 49 workers at the company's Pleasanton location, one in Oakland, two in Stockton, and 20 from elsewhere around the state, mainly in Pasadena.
