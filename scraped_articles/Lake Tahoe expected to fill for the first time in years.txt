Lake Tahoe expected to fill for the first time in years
-----
Lake Tahoe is expected to fill this spring for the first time in nearly five years, thanks to the lingering effects of two particularly wet winters.The last time the lake filled up was in June 2019, but research published by the U.S. Department of Agriculture in a recent water supply outlook report predicts that this year's snowmelt will be enough to fill the lake once more.
