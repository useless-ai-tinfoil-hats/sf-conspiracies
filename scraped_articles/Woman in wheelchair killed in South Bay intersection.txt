Woman in wheelchair killed in South Bay intersection
-----
A woman died after being struck by an SUV while crossing the street in her wheelchair on Monday night, according to San Jose police.San Jose police said the collision happened just after 9:30 p.m. Monday near the intersection of Chesbro Avenue and Blossom Hill Road.
Officials believe the woman was in the crosswalk when a man driving an SUV, who had the green light, hit her. The driver remained at the scene of the crash, while the woman was taken to a local hospital, police said.
