Pinnacles National Park ends shuttle service after 'unexpectedly losing' drivers
-----
The National Park Service announced on Friday that shuttle service at Pinnacles National Park has been suspended indefinitely due to the park "unexpectedly losing" both of its shuttle drivers.No staff is left to operate the park's shuttles, which took visitors from the park's overflow parking lots to various day use areas, the Park Service announced via social media posts.
