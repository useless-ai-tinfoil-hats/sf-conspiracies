St. Mary’s falls flat in NCAA Tournament opener, loses to Grand Canyon
-----
SPOKANE, Wash. — St. Mary's perpetual, frustrating quest to make a deep run in the NCAA Tournament will extend at least another year.This time, the Gaels didn't even make it to the second round. They brought an experienced team and abundant momentum into Friday night's first-round game at Spokane Arena — 18 wins in their previous 19 games — only to fall 75-66 to Grand Canyon.
