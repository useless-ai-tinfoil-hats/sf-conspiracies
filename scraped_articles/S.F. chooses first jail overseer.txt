S.F. chooses first jail overseer
-----
Terry Wiley, a veteran Alameda County prosecutor who lost that county's district attorney's race to Pamela Price last year, was picked to be San Francisco's first inspector general overseeing the Sheriff's Department and its two jails holding roughly 800 people.Wiley, who spent more than three decades with the Alameda County District Attorney's Office, the last couple as a chief assistant overseeing human resources, will be responsible for spearheading investigations into misconduct
