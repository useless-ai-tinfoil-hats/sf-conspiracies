'Crazy Rich Asians' director plugs his dad's Bay Area restaurant to Stephen Colbert
-----
Last night, a beloved family-owned Bay Area restaurant got a major plug on national television.Director Jon M. Chu, known for "Crazy Rich Asians," "In the Heights" and this fall's "Wicked," shouted out his parents' 54-year-old Los Altos restaurant, Chef Chu's, on "The Late Show with Stephen Colbert."
The subject came up while Chu was talking about his new memoir, "Viewfinder," which explores his childhood growing up as a Chinese American in Los Altos in the 1980s.
