Two arrested in connection to death of woman found in duffel bag near Outside Lands
-----
San Francisco police have arrested two people on suspicion of unlawfully disposing of human remains after a woman's body was found in a duffel bag during Outside Lands.On Aug. 13, the last day of the Outside Lands music festival in Golden Gate Park, a dog walker found the body of a woman stuffed inside a duffel bag just outside the park, according to San Francisco police.
