S.F.’s Noe Valley is finally getting its public toilet. Here’s what it will look like
-----
The infamous expensive toilet in Noe Valley is expected to open to the public in April, according to San Francisco officials.Construction for the restroom is scheduled to start this month with installation at Noe Valley Town Square in March, the San Francisco Recreation and Park Department announced last month.
Plans to build the public toilet prompted backlash when Assembly Member Matt Haney, a former city supervisor, announced in 2022 that he secured $1.7 million in state funding
