Oakland Police Commission to announce police chief candidates this week
-----
The Oakland Police Commission is expected to release the names of candidates for the city's next police chief this week after announcing it will host a public forum Thursday where the candidates will answer questions and lay out their respective visions for leading the Police Department.The names of the candidates were not made public as of Tuesday afternoon.
The forum will be held at 6:30 p.m. Thursday at City Hall, although the candidates will attend virtually, commissioners said
