Richmond council probes foul odor releases by local wastewater plant
-----
Donning a poop emoji surgical mask, Richmond Mayor Eduardo Martinez presided over a meeting where City Council members grilled representatives of a local wastewater facility operator following several days of foul odors coming from the area.On Tuesday night, the council convened at a special session to question employees of Veolia North America, which has run the Richmond Wastewater Treatment Plant since 2002.
