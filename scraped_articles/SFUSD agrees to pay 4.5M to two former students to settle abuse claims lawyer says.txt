SFUSD agrees to pay $4.5M to two former students to settle abuse claims, lawyer says
-----
San Francisco Unified School District has agreed to pay $4.5 million to settle with two former George Washington High School students who alleged they were abused on campus by the school's athletic director, the students' lawyer said Friday.One of the students alleged the athletic director raped her multiple times, according to their lawsuit. The other alleged he fondled her and forced her to perform oral sex.
