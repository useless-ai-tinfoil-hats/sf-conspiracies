Here's where smoke from California's Park Fire is going
-----
Southerly winds over the Park Fire have been pushing wildfire smoke into northeast California and northwest Nevada since the blaze started in Chico's Bidwell Park on Wednesday. As the raging fire pumped out massive plumes of smoke, small communities such as Madeline, Mineral and Susanville in California and Lovelock and Gerlach in Nevada have been blanketed in acrid air, according to the National Weather Service.The National Weather Service's smoke model showed the same pattern
