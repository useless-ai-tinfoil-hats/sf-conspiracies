A’s Brent Rooker says ‘no validity or merit’ to Wristbandgate ‘scandal’
-----
"Wristbandgate" was dismissed out of hand Wednesday morning by one of the men allegedly directly affected by it, Oakland Athletics outfielder Brent Rooker.After the A's sent outfielder Esteury Ruiz to Triple-A Las Vegas on Monday, a type of conspiracy theory arose on social media. The thrust was that Ruiz and Rooker — who had not played in two of the A's four games to that point — were being punished for wearing wristbands from the Last Dive Bar fan group.
