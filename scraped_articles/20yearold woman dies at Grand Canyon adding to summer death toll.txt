20-year-old woman dies at Grand Canyon, adding to summer death toll
-----
The Grand Canyon Search and Rescue team located the body of a missing 20-year-old woman on Thursday, marking the third death in the park in a week.Park officials believe Leticia A. Castillo, 20, of Albuquerque, New Mexico went for a hike on Aug. 3. She was reported missing, and after several days of searching, the SAR team found her body about 150 feet below the rim at Twin Overlooks along Desert View Drive. Castillo's cause of death is pending.
