Yelp's best ramen list ranks two Bay Area restaurants in the top 10
-----
Yelp released a new top 100 list of the best ramen shops in California, and two Bay Area restaurants cracked the top 10.Coming in at No. 5 was Ramen Hajime in Santa Clara. The restaurant, founded by ramen master Hajime Kitayama in 2020, features Tokyo-style ramen, according to the website, which allows you to build your own bowl.
