Will Kamala Harris answer these 3 big questions at the Democratic National Convention?
-----
Vice President Kamala Harris can't win the presidency by continuing to surf the good vibes that swelled when she replaced President Joe Biden at the top of the Democratic ticket.Vibes, like hope, are not a strategy.
Instead, her goal at the Democratic National Convention, which begins Monday in Chicago, is to introduce herself to voters and explain what she would do as president and how she differs from Biden.
