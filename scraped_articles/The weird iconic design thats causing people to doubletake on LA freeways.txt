The weird, iconic design that's causing people to double-take on LA freeways
-----
In 2013, Jennifer Gross and her husband purchased a dark gray Volkswagen GTI for their three children to share. The zippy hatchback was passed down, one by one, to each kid. One warm afternoon in 2020, Gross briefly took back the car from her teenage son, returning it later with a surprising (and divisive) new look.To the surprise of her kids, Gross had the car done up with purple graffiti art showing a series of grinning, warped cartoon creatures, some bearing long eyelashes.
