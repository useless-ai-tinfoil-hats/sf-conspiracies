Lake Chabot Public Market in East Bay probably a total loss after huge fire
-----
Alameda County fire officials said the Lake Chabot Public Market, a popular food hall in Castro Valley, was probably a total loss after a fire ripped through the shared commercial building early Thursday morning.Firefighters with the Alameda County Fire Department received a call about the fire around 2:20 a.m., according to Battalion Chief Justin Hoglund, after someone smelled smoke.
The fire was "through the roof when we showed up. It was well involved." Hoglund said.
