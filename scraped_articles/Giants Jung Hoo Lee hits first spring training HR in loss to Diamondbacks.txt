Giants’ Jung Hoo Lee hits first spring training HR in loss to Diamondbacks
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — The loudest sound of the afternoon at Salt River Fields came off Jung Hoo Lee's bat.Lee met Arizona Diamondbacks starter Ryne Nelson's 94 mph fastball with a popping swing that sent the offering away at 109.7 mph. Lee, thinking it would be a double or triple off the wall, flew around first base and slowed his turbo trot just as the ball escaped over the left-center-field fence.
