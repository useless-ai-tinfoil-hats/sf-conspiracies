Chinese roast duck legend opens new S.F. restaurant
-----
Chinatown staple Hing Lung — beloved for its expertly roasted Cantonese duck and pork — closed its historic space on Sunday, but San Francisco didn't go long without the shop's succulent meats.Hing Lung owners Eric and Simon Cheung opened their long-awaited Bernal Heights restaurant, Go Duck Yourself, on Wednesday.
