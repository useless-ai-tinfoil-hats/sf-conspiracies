Cal Hall of Fame adds eight for 2024 Class, including hoops stars Gutierrez, Gray-Lawson
-----
Basketball guards Jorge Gutierrez and Alexis Gray-Lawson highlight the eight-person 2024 Cal Athletics Hall of Fame class, announced Wednesday.One of the most dynamic backcourt players in Cal history, Gutierrez was part of the 2009-10 team that won the regular-season Pac-10 title, the program's first conference championship since 1960.
