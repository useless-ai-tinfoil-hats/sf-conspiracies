Protesters shut down major Calif. freeway during rush hour
-----
Protesters calling for a cease-fire in the Israel-Hamas war on Wednesday morning stopped southbound traffic on the 110 freeway that runs through downtown Los Angeles, officials said.The protest started just after 9 a.m. and brought traffic to a halt. All lanes were reopened by 11:25 a.m., Sgt. Alejandro Rubio, a CHP spokesperson, told SFGATE.
"It affected all freeways in downtown LA," Rubio said. "It backed up every single freeway and it's going to be backed up for several hours."
