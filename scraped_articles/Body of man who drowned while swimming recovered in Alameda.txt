Body of man who drowned while swimming recovered in Alameda
-----
Alameda Fire Department crews said they found a man's body underwater Friday evening after he started having trouble swimming.The Fire Department received a request for a water rescue near Willie Stargell Field at about 4:30 p.m. Friday, the agency said. Before Fire Department crews arrived, the man went under the water.
