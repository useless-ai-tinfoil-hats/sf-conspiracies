De La Salle football will play European all-stars in London in October
-----
They've played football games in, among other places, Texas, Florida, Ohio and Hawaii. Most famously, they hit Hollywood as the subject of the 2014 movie "When the Game Stands Tall."The De La Salle-Concord Spartans now are headed overseas.
The school, the NFL and Nike announced Wednesday morning that the Spartans will play Oct. 8 at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium in London against an all-star team of European players as part of the NFL's expansion to grow the game.
