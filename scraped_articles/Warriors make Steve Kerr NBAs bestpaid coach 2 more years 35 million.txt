Warriors make Steve Kerr NBA's best-paid coach: 2 more years, $35 million
-----
Golden State Warriors coach Steve Kerr isn't going anywhere, after all.Kerr and the Warriors agreed Friday to a two-year contract extension worth $35 million, a league source told the Chronicle, confirming a report from ESPN. The deal makes the four-time NBA championship coach the highest paid coach in league history.
The contract also coincides with the remainder of star guard Stephen Curry's deal with the franchise, both of which expire after the 2025-26 season.
