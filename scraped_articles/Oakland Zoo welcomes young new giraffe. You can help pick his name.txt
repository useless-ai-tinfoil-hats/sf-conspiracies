Oakland Zoo welcomes young new giraffe. You can help pick his name
-----
The Oakland Zoo this week welcomed a 13-month-old male giraffe from Louisiana to its herd, officials announced Thursday.The 10-foot-tall giraffe, yet to be named, arrived at the zoo Tuesday night from Audubon Nature Institute in New Orleans. The Zoo is allowing the public to vote on three Swahili options for the giraffe's name: Enzi, which means powerful; Nuru, which means light; and Kovu, which means scar.
