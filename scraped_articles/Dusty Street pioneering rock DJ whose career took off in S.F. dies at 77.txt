Dusty Street, pioneering rock DJ whose career took off in S.F., dies at 77
-----
Legendary radio DJ Dusty Street's on-air era in San Francisco was brief, but a name like that — her real name, by the way — doesn't fade.After spinning records at night at KSAN, the renowned freeform station in San Francisco, in the late 1960s and early '70s, she moved to the bigger market of KROQ-FM in Los Angeles. There she built an equal reputation as a member of "KROQ's Greatest Generation."
