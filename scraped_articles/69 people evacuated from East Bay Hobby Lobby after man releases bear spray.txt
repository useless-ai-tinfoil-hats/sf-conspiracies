69 people evacuated from East Bay Hobby Lobby after man releases bear spray
-----
Three people were taken to a hospital on after a man released bear spray at an Antioch Hobby Lobby on Wednesday.Just before 3 p.m., a man deployed bear spray near the entrance of the Hobby Lobby at 5849 Lone Tree Way, according to Antioch police.
It took police and Contra Costa Fire Protection District crews several hours to identify what the substance was. Fire crews evacuated 69 people.
