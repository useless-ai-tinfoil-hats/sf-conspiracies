Four people rescued from water after boat capsizes off S.F. beach
-----
Four people were rescued after their 32-foot vessel capsized off the coast of San Francisco's Ocean Beach on Sunday morning, authorities said.Firefighters responded to a report of a capsized boat with four people on board just after 9:30 a.m., the fire department said on X, formerly called Twitter. The vessel was 3 miles off the coast, fire officials said.
