Major spin campaign from 49ers reporters over Aiyuk deal is underway
-----
At 3:41 p.m. on Thursday, news broke that the 49ers and star wide receiver Brandon Aiyuk had agreed to a four-year contract extension worth $120 million.And at 3:42 p.m., the NFL's access journalism merchants started an all-time spin campaign to make the deal seem like a triumphant moment for the Niners front office. The problem is, it was San Francisco that acquiesced to a player's contract demands for a second straight offseason after they badly screwed up the Nick Bosa negotiations
