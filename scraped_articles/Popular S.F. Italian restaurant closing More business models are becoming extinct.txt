Popular S.F. Italian restaurant closing: ‘More business models are becoming extinct’
-----
Che Fico Alimentari, the more casual downstairs restaurant from the owners of acclaimed San Francisco Italian restaurant Che Fico, is closing.The 834 Divisadero St. restaurant's last day is May 9. But it won't go dark, the owners said in an Instagram announcement. It will become part of Che Fico, "creating a single, cohesive experience."
