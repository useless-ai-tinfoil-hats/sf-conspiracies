Lockdown at UC Berkeley campus after individual shoots gun into air
-----
An individual shot several bullets into the air on UC Berkeley's campus Friday night, sending panicked students into lockdown.Around 8:40 p.m., an individual got into an "altercation" in Lower Sproul Plaza and fired nine shots into the air, Assistant Vice Chancellor Dan Mogulof said. That individual was arrested within minutes of firing the weapon, and no one was injured, according to Mogulof.
