Popular Sriracha hot sauce is halting production for months. Here’s why
-----
The maker of the popular hot sauce Sriracha has halted production until after Labor Day, citing the color of its red jalapeño peppers, according to reports."After reevaluating our supply of chili, we have determined that it is too green to proceed with production as it is affecting the color of the product," Huy Fong Foods, the Southern California maker of the hot sauce bottles with green caps, told wholesale buyers last week in a letter obtained by USA Today.
