Giants remain winless against Dodgers after falling 6-4 in 10 innings
-----
The San Francisco Giants went to extra innings for a second night in a row, but had no walk-off magic in them in Monday night's series opener against their rivals.The Los Angeles Dodgers scored a pair on Will Smith's two-run double off Taylor Rogers in the top of the 10th to secure a 6-4 win. The Giants have lost four in a row to the Dodgers having been swept in L.A. in the first week of the season.
