Joshua Coffy, artist behind San Francisco animal murals, dies at 47
-----
Local artist Joshua Coffy, whose bright, animal-inspired murals adorn homes and buildings around San Francisco, died Dec. 20 from complications related to kidney failure, according to a GoFundMe a colleague posted to support his family. He was 47 years old.Coffy was a prolific artist and a champion of San Francisco's arts scene. He produced hundreds of paintings, prints and drawings, many of which he gave away for free.
