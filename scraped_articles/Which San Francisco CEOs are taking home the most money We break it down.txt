Which San Francisco CEOs are taking home the most money? We break it down
-----
Charles Scharf, the CEO of San Francisco's largest publicly traded company Wells Fargo, made $24.6 million in 2022. That was 324 times more than the median employee at the company, who made $76,000.Those numbers come from the AFL-CIO Executive Paywatch database, which is based on company statements filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.
