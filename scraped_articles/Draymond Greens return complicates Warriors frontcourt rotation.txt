Draymond Green’s return complicates Warriors’ frontcourt rotation
-----
MEMPHIS — Steve Kerr's rotation puzzle became infinitely more complicated Monday, with forward Draymond Green available for the first time in more than a month.Green's return for the Golden State Warriors, against the Memphis Grizzlies, came after he missed 16 consecutive games. His NBA suspension lasted 12 games, for striking Phoenix's Jusuf Nurkic in the face Dec. 12, and then Green missed four more games while working his way back into basketball shape.
