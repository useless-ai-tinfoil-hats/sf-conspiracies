This missionary failed to convert a remote Indigenous group. Two S.F. filmmakers examine his final days
-----
Jesse Moss and Amanda McBaine first heard about John Chau, the subject of their engrossing and troubling new National Geographic documentary, "The Mission," the same way the rest of the world did five years ago. They saw the outlandish newspaper headlines that ran all over the world in November 2018 about the 26-year-old Christian missionary from a suburb of Portland, Ore., who was killed while attempting to visit the Indigenous population of a tiny North Sentinel Island in the Bay of Bengal.
