Weekend sideshows: Fireworks and lasers on Bay Bridge, vehicle ablaze in Vallejo
-----
A sideshow involving fireworks and lasers blocked the eastbound side of the Bay Bridge early Sunday, the California Highway Patrol said, while Vallejo police reported a vehicle set on fire at another illegal gathering.Several 911 callers reported the bridge sideshow just before 2 a.m. east of Yerba Buena Island, said CHP officer Efrain Jauregui.
