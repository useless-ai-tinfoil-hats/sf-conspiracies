Wildfire near California-Nevada border cuts power in Truckee and closes portion of I-80
-----
A roughly 500-acre wildfire near the California-Nevada border forced utility companies to preemptively cut power to tens of thousands of customers in Truckee and North Lake Tahoe, authorities said.The Quilici Fire — which was subsequently renamed the Gold Ranch Fire on Monday — near Verdi in Washoe County, Nev., also forced evacuations and prompted the closure of eastbound Interstate 80 at Gold Ranch, with traffic diverted at Highway 267 in Truckee, authorities said.
