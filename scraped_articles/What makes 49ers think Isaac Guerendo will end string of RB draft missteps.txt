What makes 49ers think Isaac Guerendo will end string of RB draft missteps?
-----
Joe Williams, Trey Sermon and Ty Davis-Price.And Isaac Guerendo?
The San Francisco 49ers, who've had a habit of making mid-round mistakes on running backs, weren't dissuaded by their track record Saturday when they traded two fifth-round selections for a fourth-round pick they used to take Guerendo.
Guerendo made just one college start. And he didn't rush for more than 400 yards until his fifth and final season. Still, it's easy to see the Louisville running back's appeal.
