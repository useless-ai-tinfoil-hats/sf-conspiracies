‘It looks like a rat’: How the Giants’ Lou Seal went from disaster to the Bay Area’s favorite mascot
-----
When Lou Seal took the field at Candlestick Park for his debut on April 4, 1997, he was an insult to mascots and seals.The San Francisco Giants' costumed pinniped stood awkwardly, seemingly burdened by the weight of a too-big headpiece — an oversize pair of mirrored Oakley-style sunglasses completely hiding his eyes. His whiskers were a little too long, his nose a little too pointy. Dark brown fur obscured his features, except for white, fang-like teeth.
