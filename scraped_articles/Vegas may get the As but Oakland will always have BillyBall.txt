Vegas may get the A’s but Oakland will always have ‘BillyBall’
-----
When a baseball team moves to another city, the popular culture around it moves with it. And then almost inevitably fades.In the 1980s, no one was bigger in Oakland's popular culture than Billy Martin. Today, as the A's get into what could be their final season in Oakland before slinking off to Las Vegas, let's stop for a moment and remember 1980 to 1982, the incandescent three years that Billy managed the team.
