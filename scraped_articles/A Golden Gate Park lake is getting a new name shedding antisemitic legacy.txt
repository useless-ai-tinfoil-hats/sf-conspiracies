A Golden Gate Park lake is getting a new name, shedding antisemitic legacy
-----
After months of public input and deliberation, Golden Gate Park's Stow Lake officially has a new name: Blue Heron Lake.San Francisco supervisors first urged the city's Recreation and Park Commission to rename the lake back in October 2022, saying the lake's original namesake — 19th century state Assembly Member William W. Stow — had a history of supporting antisemitic policies and xenophobic views.
