Based in tradition and backed by science, Redtone Records supports ‘the well-being of Black folk’
-----
While record labels were a driving force in American culture for nearly a century, the companies that once nurtured new artists and defined emerging genres have fallen into near irrelevance. But Redtone Records, a community-based studio in East Palo Alto, is proving that a label can still realign the way we understand music.Redtone is best known for catapulting Black country music artist Miko Marks into prominence with a series of releases featuring her band, the Resurrectors.
