UPDATE: Palisades Tahoe ski resort to reopen a day after deadly avalancheA major avalanche at the Palisades Tahoe ski resort Wednesday morning left one Bay Area man dead and three others with non life-threatening injuries.
The avalanche occurred about 9:30 a.m. on the Palisades side of the resort, which closed for the day (it will reopen on a delayed basis Thursday), and first responders from around the region immediately joined the search and rescue effort.
