Multiple people shot during Juneteenth celebration in Oakland
-----
Multiple people were shot during a Juneteenth celebration near Lake Merritt in Oakland on Wednesday night, the Oakland Police Department confirmed.In a statement released on Thursday morning, the department said the shooting began just after 8:45 p.m. Until about 8:15 p.m., the unauthorized event with a crowd of as many 5,000 people had been mostly peaceful.
