GoFundMe campaigns show how transgender people are funding gender-affirming care
-----
At one point, Prestin Bailey felt like he and his Oakland friends were just passing money back and forth. His friend group was "chock-full of trans people," and the funds they raised again and again were for gender-affirming health care. Bailey, a trans man, created his own GoFundMe campaign to support his recovery from top surgery in 2021.His experience isn't unique.
