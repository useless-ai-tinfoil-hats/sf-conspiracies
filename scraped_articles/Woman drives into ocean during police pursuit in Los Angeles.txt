Woman drives into ocean during police pursuit in Los Angeles
-----
A woman drove into the ocean near Venice Beach in Los Angeles over the weekend as she attempted to evade officers during a police pursuit, officials said.The pursuit started on Interstate 105 westbound at approximately 11:49 p.m. Saturday when a California Highway Patrol officer attempted to pull the driver over for speeding, Steven Salas, a CHP spokesperson, told SFGATE. The woman then exited the freeway, he said, and drove toward Venice Beach.
