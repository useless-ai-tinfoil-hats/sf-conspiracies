Google CEO Sundar Pichai explains slew of layoffs and threatens more
-----
CEO Sundar Pichai acknowledged Google's newest spate of layoffs in a memo to staff on Wednesday, and said more are likely on the way.The memo, reported by the Verge and verified by Google spokesperson Courtenay Mencini, said the current "role eliminations" won't reach the scale of last year's 12,000-person layoff round. Pichai explained that the goals of the layoffs are to free up cash, likely for artificial intelligence research, and to speed up the work of teams across the company.
