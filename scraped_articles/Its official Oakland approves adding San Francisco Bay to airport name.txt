It’s official: Oakland approves adding ‘San Francisco Bay’ to airport name
-----
Oakland officials voted formally to change the name of the city's airport to San Francisco Bay Oakland International Airport on Thursday — openly defying San Francisco leaders who are suing the city for control of the moniker.The Port Commission, which runs the Oakland airport, voted 7-0 to finalize the name change, which they announced and initially approved last month.
