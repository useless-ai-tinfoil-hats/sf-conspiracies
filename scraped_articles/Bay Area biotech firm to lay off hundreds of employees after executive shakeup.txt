Bay Area biotech firm to lay off hundreds of employees after executive shakeup
-----
BioMarin Pharmaceutical, a biotech firm based in San Rafael that specializes in treatments for rare diseases, is set to lay off approximately 225 employees. This comes just a week after a significant executive reorganization within the company.According to an SEC filing, the firm announced it will reduce its global workforce by an additional 7%, following a previous announcement in May regarding 170 layoffs.
