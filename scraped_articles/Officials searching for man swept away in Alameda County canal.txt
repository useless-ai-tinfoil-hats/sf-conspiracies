Officials searching for man swept away in Alameda County canal
-----
A 56-year-old man was swept away by a strong current in an Alameda County canal on Friday.Officials are working on recovery efforts for Lester Ashcraft, who slipped from a steep embankment at the Delta-Mendota Canal at Grant Line Road near the San Joaquin County border, the Alameda County Sheriff's Office said in a news release.
