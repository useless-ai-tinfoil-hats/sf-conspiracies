Pedestrian fatally struck by vehicle in East Bay
-----
A pedestrian was fatally struck by a vehicle Friday night in Hayward, authorities said.The crash happened shortly before 10:30 p.m. near the intersection of Tennyson Road and Lustig Court, according to the Hayward Police Department. It was the city's fourth fatal collision this year, police said.
A 56-year-old man was found unresponsive and declared dead at the scene. His identity was not released pending notification of next of kin, police said.
