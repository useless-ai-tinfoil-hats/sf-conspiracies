Bay Area legislator proposes combining all 27 local public transit agencies
-----
A Bay Area legislator proposed legislation Wednesday to combine all of the region's 27 public transit agencies into one in the wake of continuing financial challenges to public transportation.State Sen. Aisha Wahab, D-Fremont, amended an existing bill to ask the California State Transportation Agency to come up with a plan to consolidate the public transit agencies across all nine Bay Area counties.
