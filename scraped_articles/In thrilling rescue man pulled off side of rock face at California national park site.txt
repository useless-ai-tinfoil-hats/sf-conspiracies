In thrilling rescue, man pulled off side of rock face at California national park site
-----
In a thrilling Sunday night rescue, a man clinging to a gravel rock face at a national park site in California was winched to safety by a crew in a helicopter, officials said.The man had fallen 50 to 60 feet from a trail in the Golden Gate National Recreation Area's Battery Alexander in Mill Valley, the Sonoma County Sheriff's Office said on Facebook.
