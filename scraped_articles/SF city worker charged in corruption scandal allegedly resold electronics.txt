SF city worker charged in corruption scandal, allegedly resold electronics
-----
A San Francisco city employee was arrested this week in connection with a public corruption scheme in which people are accused of misusing thousands of dollars in city funds.The San Francisco District Attorney's Office announced Thursday that it filed multiple felony charges against Stanley Ellicott, 38, an Oakland resident who worked as a manager in the city's Department of Human Resources. Ellicott was arrested Thursday and is out on bail, the DA's office told SFGATE.
