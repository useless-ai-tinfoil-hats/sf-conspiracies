2 children drown in pool at Bay Area residential day care, police say
-----
Two children drowned and one child was injured Monday in a swimming pool at a residential day care in South San Jose, officials said.All three children fell into the pool on the 1000 block of Fleetwood Drive near Almaden Hills Estates, the San Jose Police Department said in a statement.
The fire department was initially called to the scene, and the police department said it was called to assist just after 9 a.m. due to a report of several children falling into a pool.
