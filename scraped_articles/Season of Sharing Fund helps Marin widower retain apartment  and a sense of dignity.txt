Season of Sharing Fund helps Marin widower retain apartment — and a sense of dignity
-----
Almost a year after his wife died, Michael Sharpe still keeps her clothes in the drawers and closets of his small Novato apartment."I say to myself — a little internal dialogue, soliloquy — 'How come you don't close the doors, Mike, so you can't see the clothes?' But I know they're there, so I just leave them open, and it'll trigger some emotion," he said. "I cry every day sometimes."
