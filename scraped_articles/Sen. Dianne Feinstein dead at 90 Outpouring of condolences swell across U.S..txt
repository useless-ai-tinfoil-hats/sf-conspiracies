Sen. Dianne Feinstein dead at 90: Outpouring of condolences swell across U.S.
-----
Sen. Dianne Feinstein died in the small hours of Thursday morning at her home in Washington, D.C., according to her office, leaving a storied legacy of public service stretching from San Francisco to the nation's capitol.Feinstein's death prompted an outpouring of condolences and remembrances from officials across the country.
