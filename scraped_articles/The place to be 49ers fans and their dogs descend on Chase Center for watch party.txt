‘The place to be’: 49ers fans and their dogs descend on Chase Center for watch party
-----
A cacophony of cheers ricocheted through the San Francisco 49ers' official watch party at the Chase Center on Sunday, but if the din bothered Monster Man, he didn't let on. The 12-year-old Chihuahua-pit bull mix appraised the capacity crowd through custom red goggles and blinked."He has multiple changes of clothes," owner Ron Divino said.
