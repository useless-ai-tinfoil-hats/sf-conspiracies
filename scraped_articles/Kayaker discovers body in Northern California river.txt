Kayaker discovers body in Northern California river
-----
An investigation is underway after a kayaker discovered a body while paddling along the Russian River in Northern California earlier this week.The Ukiah Police Department received a call from the kayaker on Sunday afternoon at about 4:30 p.m. and notified the Mendocino County Sheriff's Office, which arrived at the scene where the body was found near Morrison Creek and Old River Road, about 2 miles south of Ukiah. They began conducting a search in collaboration with local fire agencies,
