Thousands of Bay Area PG&E customers without power amid high winds
-----
More than 9,000 PG&E customers were without power across the Bay Area Sunday as howling winds pummeled the region amid a red flag warning that's set to expire later this evening.J.D. Guidi, a spokesperson for the utility company, said outages began at 9 a.m. Just before noon, a total of 8,962 customers were still without power in the East Bay all the way from Oakland to Antioch, he said. Smaller outages were also reported throughout the region, with 345 customers affected in the North
