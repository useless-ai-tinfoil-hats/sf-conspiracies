Where S.F. ranks on Zillow’s list of the hottest housing markets in the U.S.
-----
It's a good time to be a home seller — especially if you live in the Bay Area.The San Francisco and San Jose metropolitan areas are both among the top five hottest markets of the country's 100 largest metro areas. That's according to a new "market heat index" rating from real estate brokerage site Zillow, which showed both metros' markets are far more competitive than the nation overall.
