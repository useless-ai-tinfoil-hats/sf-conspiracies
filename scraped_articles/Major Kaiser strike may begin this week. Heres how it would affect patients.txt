Major Kaiser strike may begin this week. Here’s how it would affect patients
-----
UPDATE: California Kaiser workers begin multi-day strike — what it means for youAbout 75,000 Kaiser Permanente health care workers around the country, including in the Bay Area, are poised to begin a three-day strike Wednesday if Kaiser and the group of unions representing the workers cannot reach an agreement on a new contract.
The umbrella organization of unions, the Coalition of Kaiser Permanente Unions, says it would be the largest health care worker strike in U.S. history.
