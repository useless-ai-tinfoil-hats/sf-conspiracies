USGS significantly downgrades quake that struck NorCal on Monday morning
-----
LATEST Oct. 23, 8:48 a.m. The U.S. Geological Survey downgraded the magnitude of an earthquake that struck in Sacramento County on Monday morning from 4.0 to 2.9.The magnitude 2.9 shaker hit at 7:42 a.m. at Twitchell Island in the Delta, 3 miles south of Isleton.
The epicenter was less than a mile south of the 4.2-magnitude quake that hit in the same area on Oct. 18.
