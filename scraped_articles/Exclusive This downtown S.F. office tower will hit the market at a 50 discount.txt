Exclusive: This downtown S.F. office tower will hit the market at a 50% discount
-----
Across the street from San Francisco's luxurious Palace Hotel, a 20-story office tower at the corner of New Montgomery and Market streets is headed for a major sale.Multiple sources have confirmed that, come January, global investment management firm Barings will launch a search for a buyer for 33 New Montgomery St., a 240,000-square-foot building that's still home to tech tenants like Bitly, Shopify and Stackla.
