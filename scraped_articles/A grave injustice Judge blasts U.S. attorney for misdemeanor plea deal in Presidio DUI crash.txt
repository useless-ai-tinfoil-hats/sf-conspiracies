‘A grave injustice’: Judge blasts U.S. attorney for misdemeanor plea deal in Presidio DUI crash
-----
A federal judge approved a plea deal for the San Francisco man who killed champion cyclist Ethan Boyes in the Presidio last year while driving under the influence of alcohol — but not before blasting prosecutors for charging the crimes as misdemeanors.Arnold Kinman Low, 81, pleaded guilty last month to vehicular manslaughter and driving under the influence of alcohol in the fatal crash that killed Boyes, a USA Cycling champion, according to court documents.
