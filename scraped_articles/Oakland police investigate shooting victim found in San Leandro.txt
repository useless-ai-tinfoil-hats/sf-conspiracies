Oakland police investigate shooting victim found in San Leandro
-----
Oakland police are investigating the slaying of a person involved in a vehicle collision Monday night.Oakland officers found evidence of a shooting after 11 p.m. on the 9800 block of MacArthur Boulevard but found no victims struck by gunfire, police said. Minutes later and about a mile south of the shooting scene near the Oakland Zoo, San Leandro police responded to a vehicle collision.
