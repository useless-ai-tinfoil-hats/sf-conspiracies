‘It’s a constant attack’: LGBTQ youth say political attacks are fueling mental health crisis
-----
The current American political landscape, including repeated attacks on LGBTQ rights that lately have focused on young people, is a major cause of stress for almost all young queer people that affects their overall quality of life, according to a national survey released Wednesday by the Trevor Project.About 90% of the 18,000 respondents to the annual survey reported that politics negatively impacted their well-being, including their sense of self and safety.
