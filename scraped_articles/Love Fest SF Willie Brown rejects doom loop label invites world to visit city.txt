Love Fest SF: Willie Brown rejects ‘doom loop’ label, invites world to visit city
-----
Former Mayor Willie Brown encouraged San Franciscans to shed their doom and share their love for the city Saturday by launching a community arts and music festival to support small business recovery.The inaugural Love Fest SF in the Tenderloin featured three stages of DJs and artists devoted to presenting the neighborhood in a positive light often overlooked by news accounts of its struggles.
