Why did two kids spend a month trying to catch bunnies in S.F. park?
-----
A roundup of four wayward bunny rabbits in San Francisco's Golden Gate Heights Park has earned a pair of quick-thinking kids a neighborhood commendation.Young rescuers Sam Gray and Allison Yeun proved essential to the operation, which unfolded over a month last summer in the hilltop park above the Sunset District and involved more than a dozen neighbors and rabbit advocates from the city, the Peninsula and the East Bay.
Gray at one point climbed a tree to help direct the search
