Towering nude woman sculpture, previously displayed at Burning Man, proposed for S.F. Union Square
-----
As early as winter, a 48-foot sculpture could tower over visitors and buses at Union Square Plaza."R-Evolution," a work by Marco Cochrane, portrays a nude woman standing confidently with her stance wide. Her body, made of stainless steel mesh, can be lit up from the interior or exterior. When the wind causes her chest to expand and contract, she almost appears as if she is breathing, according to materials provided by the San Francisco Arts Commission Visual Arts Committee.
