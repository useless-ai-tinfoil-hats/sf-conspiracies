Man charged with rape, sexual assaults at his Concord hair salon
-----
A man has been charged with rape in connection with sexual assaults at his Concord hair salon, police said Thursday.Chi Ngoc Vu, 41, was arrested Monday and charged by the Contra Costa District Attorney's Office with forcible rape, unlawful sexual intercourse, forcible oral copulation, oral copulation of a minor under 16 and two counts of lewd acts on a child, police said. Vu is the owner of Chi Hair Salon on Clayton Road in Concord, according to police.
