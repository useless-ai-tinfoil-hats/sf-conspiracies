Google, Meta withdraw from major tech conference after founder accuses Israel of war crimes
-----
Google and Meta are the latest companies to drop out of a major tech summit over the event founder's remarks on Israel, company representatives told multiple outlets.The tech giants joined a growing number of attendees and sponsors withdrawing from Web Summit's global technology conference in Lisbon, Portugal, next month after Chief Executive Paddy Cosgrave condemned Western support for Israel.
