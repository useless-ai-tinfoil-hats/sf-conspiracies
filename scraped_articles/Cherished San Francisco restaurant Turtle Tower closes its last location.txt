Cherished San Francisco restaurant Turtle Tower closes its last location
-----
Vietnamese restaurant Turtle Tower has closed its remaining San Francisco location after 23 years.The popular pho restaurant quietly closed its doors at 501 6th St. on Nov. 19, according to the San Francisco Chronicle, which first reported the closure. (The Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms.)
