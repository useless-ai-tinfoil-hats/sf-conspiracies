Photos: Dead & Company pay epic tribute to Bill Walton at Las Vegas Sphere
-----
Dead & Company paid tribute to Bill Walton during a performance at the Sphere in Las Vegas this week, just days after the NBA champion and broadcaster's death.During the show Thursday, May 30, while playing Grateful Dead's "Fire on the Mountain," accompanied by an immersive display of images from the San Francisco band's archives, the group flashed a photo of Walton in a tie-dye shirt on the giant digital screen, to which the crowd erupted in cheers.
