From a Zambia mining town to Bay FC star, Racheal Kundananji is living her dream
-----
Racheal Kundananji was barefoot, streaking through clouds of dust as she lined up to strike a wad of plastic bags stuffed into a flour sack. She wore a men's soccer jersey that fit more like a dress as she sailed the makeshift ball across a chalk line on a dirt road in Zambia, marking a goal.Her mother spent her days working in the copper mines of Northwest Zambia and didn't know her daughter was playing soccer at all.
