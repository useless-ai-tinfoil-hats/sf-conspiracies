S.F. surfer in critical condition after ocean rescue
-----
A surfer sustained critical injuries Friday after they were pulled from the water near the Great Highway and Judah Street, San Francisco emergency responders said.The San Francisco Fire Department said shortly after 10 a.m. that paramedics were treating the surfer and that they would be transported to a nearby hospital.
No additional information was immediately available.
