Climbers rescued from California's Mount Shasta after barely surviving avalanche
-----
Two climbers required an 11-hour-long rescue effort Saturday evening after narrowly surviving an avalanche on Mount Shasta, officials announced.The climbers had intended to summit Shasta and snowboard down Avalanche Gulch, a 7,000-foot descent off the south side of the mountain, according to a Facebook post by the Siskiyou County Sheriff's Office.
