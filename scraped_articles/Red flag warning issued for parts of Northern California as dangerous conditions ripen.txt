Red flag warning issued for parts of Northern California as dangerous conditions ripen
-----
National Weather Service officials issued a red flag warning for parts of northeastern California and northwestern Nevada that will last for most of Wednesday afternoon and evening, a signal that a confluence of weather conditions would increase the risk of wildfires starting and spreading.The warning, which will begin at 2 p.m. and last until 11 p.m., includes Carson City, Nev. and Reno, east of Tahoe and Plumas National Forest.
