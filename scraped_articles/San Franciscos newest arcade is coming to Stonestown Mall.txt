San Francisco's newest arcade is coming to Stonestown Mall
-----
The Japanese-based bowling alley and arcade Round1 will soon take Nordstrom's old spot at San Francisco's Stonestown Galleria.This will be Round1's first location in San Francisco. Known for its huge entertainment spaces and arcade games imported from Japan, the company already has locations in Hayward, Concord and San Jose, with nearly 100 more around the country.
