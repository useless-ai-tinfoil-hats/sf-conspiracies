SoCal fire explodes overnight amid 'unfavorable' weather conditions
-----
A wind-driven wildfire that ignited Monday afternoon in Riverside County grew thousands of acres overnight, prompting evacuations.The Nixon Fire, which started about 60 miles southeast of Riverside near Tule Valley Road and Richard Nixon Boulevard in Aguanga, had grown to 4,500 acres as of Tuesday afternoon. The fire has no containment.
Evacuation orders were issued for many parts of southern Riverside County. The Cal Fire/Riverside County Fire Department's Nixon Fire webpage
