Alameda County DA Price charges leader of nonprofit youth swimming group with embezzlement
-----
The president of a nonprofit youth swimming organization in Alameda was charged with embezzlement after she allegedly stole an unspecified amount of donations, Alameda County District Attorney Pamela Price announced Wednesday.Sarah New, 45, was charged with three counts of felony embezzlement for allegedly stealing funding from the Alameda Gators Swim Team while she oversaw the organization's finances, Price said. The district attorney declined to say how much money New allegedly stole.
