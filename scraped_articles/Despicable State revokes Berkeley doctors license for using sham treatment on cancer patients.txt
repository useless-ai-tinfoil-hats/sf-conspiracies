‘Despicable’: State revokes Berkeley doctor’s license for using ‘sham treatment’ on cancer patients
-----
The Medical Board of California has revoked the license of a Berkeley doctor, finding that he harmed numerous terminally ill cancer patients and their families with negligent and incompetent "sham treatment" that included a therapy he called "side-effect free chemotherapy," according to an order issued by the board this month.In a decision released Nov. 8, the board revoked Dr. Kenneth Matsumura's medical license, effective Dec. 8.
