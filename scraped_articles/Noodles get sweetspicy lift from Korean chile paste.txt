As much as I love all winter greens, I am partial to Swiss chard. It is an underrated green that we don't talk about enough.There is a lot of variety at the market these days: dark green chard, ruby red chard, white-stemmed chard and colorful rainbow chard. Swiss chard may look tough with its fibrous stem; you'll want to strip the leaves from both sides. The greens will wilt in a hot pan.
