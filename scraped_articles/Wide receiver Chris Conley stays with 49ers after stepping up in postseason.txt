Wide receiver Chris Conley stays with 49ers after stepping up in postseason
-----
Wide receiver Chris Conley, 31, who last season had a clutch catch in the divisional-playoff win over the Packers and a stellar special-teams performance in the Super Bowl, signed a one-year contract Saturday to return to the San Francisco 49ers.Conley, a nine-year veteran, likely earned his 2024 return in the postseason after he spent time on the practice squad and played just 110 snaps in eight regular-season games, collecting three catches for 69 yards.
