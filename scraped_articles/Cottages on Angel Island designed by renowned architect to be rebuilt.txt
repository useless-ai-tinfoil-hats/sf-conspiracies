Cottages on Angel Island designed by renowned architect to be rebuilt
-----
Cottages on Angel Island that were designed by famous architect Julia Morgan are slated to soon be rebuilt thanks to new state funding.Twelve cottages were designed by Morgan for families and staff who worked at the Angel Island Immigration Station before its closure in 1940. The cottages burned down in 1971 in a fire training exercise, the footage of which appears in the movie "The Candidate" starring Robert Redford, and only their concrete foundations remain today.
