Here’s when to expect the worst Bay Area traffic during Labor Day weekend
-----
The last holiday weekend of the summer tends to bring a crush of traffic in the Bay Area on Friday and Monday, and this Labor Day weekend isn't expected to be different.The Labor Day holiday, historically, has not led to the sorts of intense traffic congestion Bay Area drivers have come to expect during, say, the Fourth of July, Memorial Day and Thanksgiving weekends.
