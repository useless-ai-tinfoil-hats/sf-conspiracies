65-year-old Sacramento burger stand set to close
-----
Sacramento has a bevy of well-loved burger joints. It's almost a rite of passage on your way to Tahoe. But one legendary 65-year-old drive-in is closing its doors for the last time Saturday. The news was announced on the restaurant's Instagram page.Lou's Burgers, at 6229 Watt Ave. in North Highlands, was known for its affordable burgers — $6.35 for a single, $7.50 for a double and $9.10 for a triple — and its old-school decor.
