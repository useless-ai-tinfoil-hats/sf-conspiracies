Review: A look at the spectacular buildings that never got built — in the Bay Area and beyond
-----
"That a building could become anything tangible — be it remarkable or despicable, banal or glorious, revolutionary or preposterous — despite not being built," write the authors of "Atlas of Never Built Architecture," "is a testament to the fact that buildings are much more than simply concrete, stone, timber, and glass. They are foremost a collection of ideas, but also of stories, struggles, cultures, and fateful circumstances — materialized, or not, in built form."Architecture writers
