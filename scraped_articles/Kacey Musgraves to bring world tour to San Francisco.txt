Kacey Musgraves to bring world tour to San Francisco
-----
Grammy-winning singer-songwriter Kacey Musgraves is gearing up for a global tour in support of her upcoming album, "Deeper Well."The "Deeper Well" world tour is scheduled to kick off in Ireland on April 28, with Musgraves performing across Europe and the U.K. throughout the spring.
The North American leg of the tour will resume in the fall, featuring two Northern California dates: Sept. 23 at the Golden 1 Center in Sacramento and Sept. 24 at the Chase Center in San Francisco.
