Exclusive: This troubled S.F. neighborhood could get a monthly block party with outdoor drinking
-----
San Francisco's Mid-Market neighborhood could get a monthly block party with outdoor drinking starting in September as part of an effort to expand "entertainment zones" to more parts of the city.The Mid-Market Business Association hopes to win approval from the Board of Supervisors in the coming months to create a zone on Market Street between Fifth and Sixth streets and the surrounding area.
