Bay Area weather: These places will be windiest today
-----
The Bay Area is no stranger to breezy summer afternoons, but Wednesday's gusts are expected to be unusually strong due to the influence of a shortwave trough.The weak but fast-moving atmospheric disturbance will move over the Bay Area on Wednesday afternoon, and high pressure building in its wake along the coast. In the Central Valley, a hot afternoon will result in rising air and a local region of low pressure at the ground known as a thermal low.
