Bay Area weather: ‘Shortwave ridge’ to bring slightly warmer temperatures
-----
A quiet weather pattern is settling in across the Bay Area, as the hottest temperatures shift to the Plains and Gulf Coast for mid-August. Without a strong weather system nearby, Northern California is reverting to typical summer weather, with highs in the 80s to 90s inland, rather than nonstop triple-digit temperatures.
