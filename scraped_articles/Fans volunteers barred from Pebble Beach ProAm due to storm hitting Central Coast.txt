Fans, volunteers barred from Pebble Beach Pro-Am due to storm hitting Central Coast
-----
Fans and volunteers will not have access to the AT&T Pebble Beach Pro-Am on Sunday due to the incoming storm, officials announced Saturday evening. Officials also said that the Lone Cypress Club will be closed, and the event's final round has been delayed until further notice."Due to the forecast of high winds and heavy rain and out an abundance of caution, gates will be closed to spectators and volunteers on Sunday, February 4th. Lone Cypress Club will also be closed," it was announced
