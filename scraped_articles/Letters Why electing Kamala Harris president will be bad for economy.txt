Letters: Why electing Kamala Harris president will be bad for economy
-----
President Joe Biden inherited a growing economy with little inflation after Donald Trump signed the 2020 COVID relief bill for $2.2 trillion.Biden then increased spending by trillions, which caused the highest inflation in 40 years. This inflation reduced purchasing power by about the amount of the inflation even when adjusted for wage increases. What the government gave with one hand it took back with inflation. That's why inflation is called a "hidden tax."
