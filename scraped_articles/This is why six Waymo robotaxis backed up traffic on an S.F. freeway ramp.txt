This is why six Waymo robotaxis backed up traffic on an S.F. freeway ramp
-----
Six Waymo robotaxis ensnared traffic on a Highway 101 on-ramp in San Francisco on Tuesday night, according to video accounts posted on Reddit.The incident happened at the southbound Highway 101 on-ramp on the southern end of Potrero Avenue, where the corridor splinters into a freeway on-ramp to the right and an on-ramp that transitions to Bayshore Boulevard on the left lane. The Bayshore on-ramp was blocked off by traffic cones.
