Review: ‘How to Have Sex’ a harsh, powerful coming-of-age tale from Britain
-----
The great strength and slight weakness of "How to Have Sex" is that it's just like being there — except you might not want to be there.Three teenage girls arrive in Crete for a blowout holiday and find that every other young British person seems to have had the same idea. It's just like spring break, but with English accents. There's loud music, binge drinking and drug taking, interspersed with interludes of vomiting.
If you have an idealized memory of your teenage years, see this
