Burning Man rain: All about the ancient lake bed where the festival takes place
-----
The rain that has swamped the Burning Man festival this weekend is actually falling on an ancient lake bed, the site of the former Lake Lahontan.The dusty, sandy playa, where the festival takes place, is the remnants of that deep lake, which existed until roughly 15,000 years ago, according to the U.S. Geological Survey.
