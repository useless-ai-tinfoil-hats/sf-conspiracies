Former Giants manager Kapler interviews for Red Sox's top baseball job
-----
Three weeks after Gabe Kapler was fired as the San Francisco Giants' manager, a report surfaced in Boston that he has been interviewed for the Red Sox's top baseball job.The Red Sox, who finished in last place in the American League East the past two years, need someone to replace chief baseball officer Chaim Bloom, who was fired late in the season.
