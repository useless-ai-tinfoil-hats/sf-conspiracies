Tents, cars torched in Mission District encampment fire
-----
An encampment fire burned several tents and vehicles in San Francisco's Mission District on Monday morning, according to Fire Department officials.Crews responded to multiple calls of a fire at an encampment at Julian Avenue and 14th Street at 1:33 a.m., said San Francisco Fire Lt. Mariano Elias. No injuries were reported.
Some nearby vehicles were damaged from the fire, Elias said. The number of cars damaged was not immediately available.
