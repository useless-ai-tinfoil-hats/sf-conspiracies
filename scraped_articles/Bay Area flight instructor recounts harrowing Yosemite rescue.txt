Bay Area flight instructor recounts harrowing Yosemite rescue
-----
A military flight instructor stationed at Travis Air Force Base was in the right place at the right time for one Yosemite hiker in need of an urgent rescue.The ordeal began in overcast, gusty conditions atop Half Dome on Sept. 2. Among the hikers braving the elements was 60th Air Evacuation Squadron flight instructor Capt. Joshua Haveman, who was on the last stretch of his summit attempt.
