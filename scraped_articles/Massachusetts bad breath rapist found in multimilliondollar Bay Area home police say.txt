Massachusetts' 'bad breath rapist' found in multimillion-dollar Bay Area home, police say
-----
A fugitive featured on "America's Most Wanted" and dubbed the "bad breath rapist" by police was found living comfortably in a multimillion-dollar Bay Area home, law enforcement said Tuesday.In Feb. 2005, Tuen Kit Lee broke into a coworker's house in Quincy, Massachusetts and raped her at knifepoint.
