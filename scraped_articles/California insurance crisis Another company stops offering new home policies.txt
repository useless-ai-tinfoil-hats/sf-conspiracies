California insurance crisis: Another company stops offering new home policies
-----
Home insurance options in California will soon shrink further as another company pauses coverage in the state.The Hartford Financial Services Group Inc. will stop offering new California homeowners policies starting in February. Included in the cut is Hartford's joint homeowners insurance program with AARP. Those with existing policies, meeting the company's underwriting guidelines, will continue to be renewed.
