S.F. tech company closing office that once boasted a speakeasy
-----
Glassdoor is set to close its last offices in San Francisco and Chicago, doubling down on its commitment to remote work, CEO Christian Sutherland-Wong revealed Thursday.The company, which offers employees a platform to anonymously review their workplaces, intends to completely give up the lease for its 117,000-square-foot headquarters at 300 Mission St. in San Francisco, a location known for its speakeasy and video game room.
