SF Giants are really sorry they almost wrecked their bullpen catcher
-----
The San Francisco Giants are incredibly sorry they almost put their catcher in life-threatening danger during a Monday light show for their closer, who didn't even get a save.Giants catching coach Alex Burg received a flood of concern and gifts from those inside and outside the organization after he was forced to catch a pitch from Camilo Doval in almost complete darkness, according to stories published Tuesday from the Athletic, MLB.com and the San Francisco Chronicle.
