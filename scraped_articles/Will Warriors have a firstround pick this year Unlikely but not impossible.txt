Will Warriors have a first-round pick this year? Unlikely but not impossible
-----
By missing the NBA playoffs this season, the Golden State Warriors are back in the league's draft lottery. But whether they keep their pick depends on how the pingpong balls bounce Sunday at McCormick Place Convention Center in Chicago.The Warriors are relinquishing their first-round draft pick this year unless it falls within the top four because of a trade they made in 2019 with the Memphis Grizzlies that helped them offload Andre Iguodala's contract.
