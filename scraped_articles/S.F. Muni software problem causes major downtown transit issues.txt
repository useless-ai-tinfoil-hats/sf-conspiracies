S.F. Muni software problem causes major downtown transit issues
-----
A software issue with the Market Street subway train control system caused congestion and slow-moving service in San Francisco's downtown Muni stations, spokespersons for the San Francisco Municipal Transit Agency said Friday.Congestion and sluggish service was reported between the Van Ness and Embarcadero stations, spokespersons said.
