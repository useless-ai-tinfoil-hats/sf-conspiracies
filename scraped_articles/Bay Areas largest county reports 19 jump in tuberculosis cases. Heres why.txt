Bay Area’s largest county reports 19% jump in tuberculosis cases. Here’s why
-----
The largest county in the Bay Area witnessed a 19% jump in new tuberculosis diagnoses in 2023 compared to the previous year.According to data released by the Santa Clara County Public Health Department on Tuesday, the increase follows a 6% rise in cases in 2022, indicating a troubling trend. San Jose now holds the unfortunate position of having the nation's second-highest rate of tuberculosis, or TB, among metropolitan regions in the United States, trailing only Honolulu.
