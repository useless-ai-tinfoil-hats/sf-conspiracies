All-Star analyst Draymond Green eager to trash-talk Warriors’ Steph Curry
-----
INDIANAPOLIS — Golden State Warriors forward Draymond Green is participating in the NBA's All-Star Weekend.Not on the basketball court, but rather in the broadcast booth — alongside one of his most notable critics.
Green on Sunday was set to team with Hall of Famer Barkley to call the All-Star Game on an alternative telecast via truTV, bringing together two of the most prominent voices in basketball for what figures to be a couple of opinionated hours of banter and analysis.
