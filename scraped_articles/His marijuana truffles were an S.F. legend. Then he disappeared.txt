His marijuana truffles were an S.F. legend. Then he disappeared
-----
Almost like an apparition, there he would be — sauntering through Dolores Park on sunny days, long before cannabis was legal. In all-white garb and a crown-like straw hat, he would sell his pot-laced truffles out of handmade copper pots, like some kind of time traveler from a 16th-century bazaar.Most people knew him as the Truffle Man.
