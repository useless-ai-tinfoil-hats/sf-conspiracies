69 people evacuated from East Bay Hobby Lobby after man sprays bear mace
-----
Three people were taken to a local hospital after a man released bear mace into the air at an Antioch Hobby Lobby.Just before 3 p.m., a man deployed bear mace near the entrance of the Hobby Lobby located at 5849 Lone Tree Way, according to Antioch police.
