4.4 magnitude quake rattles homes near the heart of Los Angeles
-----
An earthquake with a preliminary magnitude of 4.4 rattled dishes and raised blood pressure through much of central Los Angeles midday Monday. The quake, with an epicenter near South Pasadena just northeast of Downtown LA, is not known to have caused any immediate damage. The United States Geological Survey reported the earthquake at 12:21 p.m.The shaking was immediate and intense for those closest to the epicenter of hilly Monterey Road near the Highland Park neighborhood.
