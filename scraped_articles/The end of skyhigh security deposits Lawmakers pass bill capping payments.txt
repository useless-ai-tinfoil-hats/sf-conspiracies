The end of sky-high security deposits? Lawmakers pass bill capping payments
-----
California renters may finally get a break on budget-busting security deposits after legislation aimed at limiting deposits to one month's rent passed the California Legislature on Thursday.AB12 is now headed to Gov. Gavin Newsom's desk. Newsom has until Oct. 14 to decide whether to sign or veto all bills.
