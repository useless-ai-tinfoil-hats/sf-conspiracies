At least 10 people displaced in S.F. as fire spreads to vehicles, buildings
-----
A fire in the Mission District early Saturday set multiple vehicles and at least two structures ablaze, according to the San Francisco Fire Department.The fire department was on the scene of the fire at Wiese Street, a small side alley between Mission and Valencia at 15th and 16th streets, around 2 a.m. Video shared by the department shows firefighters hosing down vehicles engulfed in flames as well as blazes in the building above.
