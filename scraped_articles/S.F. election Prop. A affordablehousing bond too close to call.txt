S.F. election: Prop. A affordable-housing bond too close to call
-----
An affordable-housing bond that would pump $300 million into subsidized homes was narrowly winning in early returns, but the margin was narrow enough that the outcome remained too close to call.As of Friday afternoon, Proposition A was leading with 70%. It requires a two-thirds majority to pass.
