Ellen Corbett, longtime East Bay public servant, dies at 69
-----
Ellen Corbett, a director of the East Bay Regional Park District and former San Leandro mayor, died Friday at age 69. The cause of death was not released.Corbett, who was born in Oakland and lived in San Leandro, attended Chabot College and the former Cal State Hayward (now Cal State East Bay), according to her state Senate biography. She earned her bachelor's degree from UC Davis and law degree from the University of the Pacific, later working as an attorney, community college professor
