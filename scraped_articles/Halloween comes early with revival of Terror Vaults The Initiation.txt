Halloween comes early with revival of Terror Vault’s ‘The Initiation’
-----
Ever wonder what it's like to join a cult?Get a peek inside that world, minus the commitment, with a limited revival of Terror Vault's "The Initiation." The popular walk-through horror show is the sixth Terror Vault haunted attraction at the San Francisco Mint produced by Into the Dark, launched by David Flower Productions, Non Plus Ultra and the drag queen of Halloween, Peaches Christ.
The hourlong experience, which debuted on Sept. 29 and ran through Halloween, is set to return
