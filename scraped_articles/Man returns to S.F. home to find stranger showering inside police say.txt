Man returns to S.F. home to find stranger showering inside, police say
-----
San Francisco police were summoned to the 1100 block of Folsom Street on Thursday evening after a man returned to his home to find a stranger showering inside.The man came home around 7:45 p.m. Thursday to hear the unexpected sound of his shower running, according to a police summary.
The man left the residence and returned with San Francisco officers "who confirmed a suspect had broken in and showered inside," the summary stated.
