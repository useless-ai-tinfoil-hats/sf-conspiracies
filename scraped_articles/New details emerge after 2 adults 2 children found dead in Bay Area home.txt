New details emerge after 2 adults, 2 children found dead in Bay Area home
-----
Police discovered a 9 mm pistol and a loaded magazine in the San Mateo home where a man, woman and two children were found dead, officials said Tuesday.Police have said little about the unusual tragedy in this San Francisco suburb since it occurred Monday morning, but on Tuesday they issued a few more details. The two children, whose ages have not been released, were found dead in a bedroom. The man and woman were found in a bathroom; both had gunshot wounds, the police said.
