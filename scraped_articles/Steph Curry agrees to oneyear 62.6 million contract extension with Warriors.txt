Steph Curry agrees to one-year, $62.6 million contract extension with Warriors
-----
The Golden State Warriors and Stephen Curry have agreed to a one-year contract extension that will keep him with the franchise through the 2026-27 season, his agent, Octagon's Jeff Austin, confirmed Thursday to the Chronicle.Worth $62.6 million, the extension ensures Curry approximately $178 million over the next three seasons.
