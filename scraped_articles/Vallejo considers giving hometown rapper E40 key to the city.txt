Vallejo considers giving hometown rapper E-40 key to the city
-----
Rapper E-40 may soon hold the key to his hometown.The Vallejo City Council will consider the motion at its next regular meeting.
In a memo issued by the mayor's office, city officials said the honor would recognize the Vallejo native, born Earl Stevens, for his exceptional contributions to arts, culture, business and philanthropy in Vallejo.
