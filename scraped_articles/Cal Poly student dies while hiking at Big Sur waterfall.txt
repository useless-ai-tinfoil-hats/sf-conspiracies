Cal Poly student dies while hiking at Big Sur waterfall
-----
A Cal Poly student known for his exuberant attitude and love of the outdoors died while hiking at a Big Sur waterfall, school officials confirmed.Kenneth Taylor, 21, departed for a hike to Salmon Creek Falls in Monterey County's Silver Peak Wilderness on April 6. When Taylor failed to return, he was reported missing.
