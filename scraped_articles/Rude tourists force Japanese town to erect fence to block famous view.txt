Rude tourists force Japanese town to erect fence to block famous view
-----
A Japanese town that is a popular jumping-off point for visitors to Mount Fuji is fed up with tourist swarms taking one particular photo. The problem is so bad that city officials are erecting an 8-foot barrier to obscure the view of Mount Fuji and hopefully deter the behavior.Fujikawaguchiko locals are "exasperated by crowds of badly behaved foreign tourists" who ignore traffic rules and leave behind trash, the Guardian reported.
