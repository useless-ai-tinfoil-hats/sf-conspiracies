Quirky California dome home lists for $3 million
-----
A Southern California "dome home" that's spiritually similar to the Bay Area's iconic Flintstone house has listed for nearly $3 million.The 44-acre property at 13600 Diamond Point Drive in Yucaipa in the San Bernardino Mountain foothills features a three-bedroom, four-bathroom monolithic dome, lovingly dubbed the Vista Dhome by its previous owners, the Braswell family.
