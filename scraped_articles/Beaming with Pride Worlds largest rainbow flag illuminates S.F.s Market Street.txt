Beaming with Pride: World’s largest rainbow flag illuminates S.F.’s Market Street
-----
The world's largest Pride flag, a 4.1-mile laser spectacular, returned to San Francisco on Friday night to kick off Pride Weekend.Illuminate, the organization that lit up the Bay Bridge for 10 years with the "Bay Lights" installation, beamed the laser flag up Market Street from the Embarcadero to Twin Peaks during a ceremony featuring music, dancing and dignitaries, including Mayor London Breed, state Sen. Scott Wiener and longtime AIDS activist Cleve Jones.
