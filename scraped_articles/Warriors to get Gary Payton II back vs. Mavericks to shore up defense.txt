Warriors to get Gary Payton II back vs. Mavericks to shore up defense
-----
The Golden State Warriors finally have at least one reinforcement on defense on the way. Guard Gary Payton II will be available for Saturday's game against the Dallas Mavericks at Chase Center after missing the previous 12 games with a strained right calf.Combined with the indefinite suspension of forward Draymond Green, Golden State has been without its two best stoppers for the last two-plus weeks.
