Apple lays off hundreds of Bay Area workers in first mass cuts since the pandemic
-----
Apple is laying off 614 employees in Santa Clara as part of its first mass job cuts in years, according to a state filing.An attorney for the tech giant wrote that the layoffs are effective on May 27, in a letter last week to the California Employment Development Department.
