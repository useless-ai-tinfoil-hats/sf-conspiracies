Rain helps firefighters quell California’s Park Fire
-----
Firefighters working to finish controlling the massive, month-old Park Fire northeast of Chico made steady progress in recent days as rainfall suppressed the flames and allowed crews to finish assessing damage caused by the historic blaze.Authorities said Saturday night — exactly one month after the fire started — that all weather stations in the burn zone recorded more than a half-inch of rain over the previous day and a half.
