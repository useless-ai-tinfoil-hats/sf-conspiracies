Fatal crash shuts stretch of East Bay commuter corridor, officials say
-----
A driver died after crashing into a power pole in Walnut Creek early Thursday, leading to the closure of a major commuter corridor, officials said.Officers responded about 1:07 a.m. to a report of a vehicle that crashed into a power pole on Ygnacio Valley Road between Civic Drive and North Broadway near downtown, the Walnut Creek Police Department said on Facebook.
The driver was ejected from the vehicle and pronounced dead, police said. The victim's identity was not immediately
