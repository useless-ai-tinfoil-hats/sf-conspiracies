Police arrest suspect in killing of woman in Pinole on Friday
-----
Police arrested a man who they said shot and killed a woman in Pinole on Friday morning.Around 8:45 a.m., Jeffrey Kendall, 36, entered a home in the 500 block of Summit Drive and shot a woman inside, killing her, according to Pinole police.
