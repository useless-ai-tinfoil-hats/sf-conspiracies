Major crash on I-580 in Livermore snarls traffic, lanes closed for 5 hours
-----
A collision between two cars and a big rig on eastbound Interstate 580 in Livermore snarled traffic Friday night and closed five of seven lanes, according to the California Highway Patrol.Authorities responded to the crash around 8:08 p.m., according to the CHP incident page. The big rig, which was carrying an estimated 22,000 pounds of wood, was overturned at the scene, according to authorities. Two lanes remained open, CHP said.
