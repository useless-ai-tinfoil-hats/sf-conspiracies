Justin Timberlake announces second Bay Area tour date, with a side of drama
-----
Justin Timberlake has expanded his Forget Tomorrow World Tour with an additional date in the Bay Area.The former 'N Sync singer is now scheduled to perform at the SAP Center in San Jose on May 7, following a previously announced performance on May 6. The tour is in support of his upcoming solo album, "Everything I Thought It Was," marking his return to the stage after five years.
