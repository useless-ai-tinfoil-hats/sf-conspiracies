Bay Area prosecutors get nearly $4 million to fight wage theft
-----
The state awarded the Bay Area nearly $4 million in grants to prosecute wage theft, officials said.The grants will also enable prosecutors to offer resources to address exploitive labor industry trends, barriers that workers confront in reporting violations, and obstacles to holding perpetrators accountable, according to the California Department of Industrial Relations and the Labor Commissioner's Office.
