Why this year has been ‘terrifying’ for Bay Area restaurants
-----
Friday nights at Tacolicious in San Francisco's Mission District are quiet. Very quiet. Sales, once brisk at happy hour, are down a shocking 35% — not from pre-pandemic, but from last year.Business has dropped most precipitously at the Valencia Street Tacolicious. But all of founder Joe Hargrave's restaurants have seen alarming dips this year.
