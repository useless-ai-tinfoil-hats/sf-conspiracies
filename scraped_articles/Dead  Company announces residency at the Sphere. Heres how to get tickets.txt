Dead & Company announces residency at the Sphere. Here’s how to get tickets
-----
Dead & Company is set to make a return with a residency at the Sphere in Las Vegas this spring.The Grateful Dead spinoff band — which features Bob Weir, Mickey Hart and Bill Kreutzmann as well as John Mayer, Oteil Burbridge and Jeff Chimenti — plans to perform 24 shows at the high-tech venue May 16 through July 13, following the success of U2's recent run.
