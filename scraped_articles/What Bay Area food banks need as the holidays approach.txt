What Bay Area food banks need as the holidays approach
-----
A few years after one of the largest food banks in the Bay Area opened 21 pop-up food pantries in response to the coronavirus pandemic, the pantries are shutting down for good.The San Francisco-Marin Food Bank announced the impending closure of all of its farmers market-style pantries by June 30, 2025, as funding from the federal, state and local government for emergency pandemic services ends.
