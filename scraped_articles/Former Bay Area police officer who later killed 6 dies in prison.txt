Former Bay Area police officer who later killed 6 dies in prison
-----
Anthony "Jack" Sully, a former Millbrae police officer sentenced to death for committing six brutal murders related to drugs and prostitution in 1983, has died in prison. He was 79.Prison officials said Sully died of natural causes last Friday at San Quentin, where he had been held since being convicted and sentenced to death in 1986.
