After their sixth child was born, he got fired. Season of Sharing caught them up
-----
When Juan Murillo and Sonia Montes left their hometown of Guadalajara in Mexico, they hoped to improve their lives north of the border. They arrived in Fairfield, encouraged by Murillo's brothers, who had found steady work in the area, and settled in.The couple welcomed their first child in 2005. By 2013, Montes had left her job at a commercial laundry facility, where she washed tablecloths and napkins from nearby hotels and restaurants, to care for what had become a bustling household.
