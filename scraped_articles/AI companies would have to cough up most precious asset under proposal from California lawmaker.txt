AI companies would have to cough up most precious asset under proposal from California lawmaker
-----
The days of artificial intelligence companies sweeping up endless data, copyrighted or not, could be coming to an end.That is if Rep. Adam Schiff, D-Burbank, gets his way after introducing a bill in Congress Tuesday that would force AI companies to say where they got the reams of data needed to make their super smart chatbots and image generators.
