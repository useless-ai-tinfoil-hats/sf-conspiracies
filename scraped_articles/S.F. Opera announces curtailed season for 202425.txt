S.F. Opera announces curtailed season for 2024-25
-----
The San Francisco Opera is planning a sharply reduced season for 2024-25, including just six operatic productions and featuring a greater reliance than before on familiar repertoire items.The company's 102nd season, announced Tuesday, Feb. 20, is scheduled to open Sept. 6 and run through June 27, 2025, when it concludes with a Pride Concert.
