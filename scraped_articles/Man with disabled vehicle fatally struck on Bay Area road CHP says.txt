Man with disabled vehicle fatally struck on Bay Area road, CHP says
-----
A driver whose vision may have been impaired by headlights fatally struck a motorist whose vehicle was disabled Saturday morning on a Napa road, according to the California Highway Patrol.A Toyota Rav 4 struck the victim and his vehicle on the Silverado Trail north of Oak Knoll Avenue shortly after 4 a.m., the CHP said Sunday in a news release.
