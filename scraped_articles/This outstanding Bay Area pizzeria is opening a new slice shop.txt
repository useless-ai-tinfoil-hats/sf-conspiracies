This outstanding Bay Area pizzeria is opening a new slice shop
-----
One of the Bay Area's top pizzerias will open a new San Francisco location.Outta Sight Pizza announced on Instagram it will launch a second restaurant, at 643 Clay St. in Chinatown, which will offer New York-style pies, slices and sandwiches. Chef and co-owner Eric Ehler said the new location will offer a similar experience and menu as the original in the Tenderloin, but that fans and customers can expect pies drawing inspiration from Chinatown staples like dim sum and Peking duck.
