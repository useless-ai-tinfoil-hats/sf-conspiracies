Giants’ game in Denver postponed; split doubleheader Saturday
-----
DENVER — With inclement weather rolling in and expected to stick around most of the evening, the Rockies decided to postpone Thursday's game with the San Francisco Giants nice and early, more than three hours before the scheduled game time.The teams are playing a four-game series, so they'll cram in a split doubleheader Saturday, with the first game starting at 11:10 a.m. PDT and the second at 5:10 p.m., with separate admissions.
