Exclusive: S.F. mega-landlord Veritas is selling off 762 rent-controlled apartments
-----
After its lender sold off troubled loans tied to nearly one-third of Veritas Investment's apartment buildings in recent months, the San Francisco mega-landlord will see its once vast real estate empire shrink even further.A total of 23 buildings that collectively hold 762 rent-controlled apartments are being marketed for sale, according to a sales brochure obtained by the Chronicle.
