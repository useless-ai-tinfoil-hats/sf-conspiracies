Dog gone: Pup exits Muni Metro tunnel after causing huge delays, closure
-----
A dog in the Muni Metro Market Street tunnel caused major delays on the Monday night commute.The trouble began shortly after 6 p.m. when Muni received a report of a lost dog in the tunnel near West Portal. Subway traffic was slowed down as officials began looking for the pooch and Muni warned riders to expect long delays.
Around 7 p.m., Muni said it was holding trains from Embarcadero to Church Street after the dog was spotted on the outbound trackway between Church and Van Ness
