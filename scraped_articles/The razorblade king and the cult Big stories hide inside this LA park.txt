The razor-blade king and the cult: Big stories hide inside this LA park
-----
What do Gillette razors, Bob Hope and the reality show "The Biggest Loser" have in common?They all have ties to King Gillette Ranch, a historic property tucked away in the Santa Monica Mountains with a roller coaster of a backstory. After multiple reinventions over the past century, the Spanish Colonial Revival-style mansion and its sprawling grounds are open to the public as one of the more recent additions to the Santa Monica Mountains National Recreation Area.
