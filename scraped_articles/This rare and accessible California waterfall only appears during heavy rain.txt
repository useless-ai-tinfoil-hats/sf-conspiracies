This rare and accessible California 'waterfall' only appears during heavy rain
-----
This week's historic Southern California rainfall has brought a ton of problems to the region's coastline, from flooding and erosion to sewage spills. But there has been one unique phenomenon to come out of the deluge: the rare return of Dana Point Falls, right in the middle of a wealthy coastal suburb.The falls, located just off Dana Point Harbor Drive in the Orange County community of Dana Point, appear only when significant rainfall occurs.
