VTA light-rail train derails in South Bay
-----
A Valley Transportation Authority light-rail train derailed near Capitol Station in south San Jose on Monday afternoon, agency officials said.There were no injuries following the derailment, VTA officials said. The train "slipped off" a crossover track as it attempted to switch from the southbound to northbound direction at the station, according to VTA officials.
A bus bridge for affected passengers is being provided from Santa Teresa station to Curtner station, according to VTA
