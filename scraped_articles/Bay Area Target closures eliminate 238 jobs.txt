Bay Area Target closures eliminate 238 jobs
-----
The recent closures of three Bay Area Target stores have led to the elimination of more than 200 jobs, according to recent filings.Target's permanent closure of three locations in San Francisco, Oakland and Pittsburg in October will affect 238 positions by Dec. 30, according to recent WARN notices.
