Tahoe estate for sale for $12.8M, includes Tesla Cybertruck
-----
A sprawling, still-under-construction estate has hit the market in Tahoe, listing for $12.8 million.The Clear Creek, Nevada, property is being built by developer Whisper Homes with "sustainable luxury living" in mind. The house is slated to have a Tesla solar roof, Powerwall battery system, generators and a battery backup system, allowing the home to go "off-grid" as needed.
