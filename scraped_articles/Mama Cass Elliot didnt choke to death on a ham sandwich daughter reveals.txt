‘Mama’ Cass Elliot didn’t choke to death on a ham sandwich, daughter reveals
-----
Cass Elliot's daughter is dispelling one of rock music's enduring myths.Owen Elliot-Kugell reveals in her new book that her mother, famously known as "Mama" Cass from the 1960s West Coast folk-rock group the Mamas and the Papas, did not die by choking on a ham sandwich. Instead, the myth was concocted by her manager to protect her legacy.
"There was a ham sandwich, but she didn't eat it and she didn't choke on it. So enough with the jokes," Elliot-Kugell said in a new interview
