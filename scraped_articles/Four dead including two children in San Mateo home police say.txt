Four dead, including two children, in San Mateo home, police say
-----
San Mateo police found two adults and two children dead inside a home on Monday morning, spokespersons said.Officers were dispatched to the 4100 block of Alameda de las Pulgas for a welfare check just after 9 a.m. Upon arrival, they found one adult male, one adult female and two children inside with "obvious signs of death."
