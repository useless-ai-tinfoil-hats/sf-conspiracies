Bay Area county reports 'cluster of fentanyl overdoses'
-----
Marin County reported a recent "cluster of fentanyl overdoses," with five people dying from Feb. 14 to March 1, Dr. Matt Willis, the county's public health office, said on YouTube Friday. Preliminary investigations found all five individuals may have ingested both fentanyl and methamphetamine, the county said.This is more than normal for the San Francisco Bay Area county, even during a period when the entire country is experiencing a fentanyl epidemic with overdose deaths skyrocketing.
