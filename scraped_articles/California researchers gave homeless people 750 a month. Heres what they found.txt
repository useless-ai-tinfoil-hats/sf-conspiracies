A preliminary report found what researchers say is a potentially promising solution in the fight against homelessness: giving people who are unhoused $750 a month with no strings attached.The study found that the funds, which were given to 69 people in San Francisco and Los Angeles for just six months, helped people meet their basic needs and reduced their likelihood of being unsheltered. The final report will look at outcomes for 100 participants over the course of a year.
