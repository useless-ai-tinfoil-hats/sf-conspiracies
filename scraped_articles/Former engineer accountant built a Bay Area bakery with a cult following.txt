Former engineer, accountant built a Bay Area bakery with a cult following

On a recent Saturday, as I waited for my pastries outside of 1 Oz Coffee in Mountain View, I couldn't help but watch a family of four dig into theirs. The mother pulled apart a raspberry rose croissant dusted with crushed rose petals for her daughter. The flaky top crinkled and shattered; the dough tore into thin delicate layers as rich white mascarpone spilled out, which the girl caught in her palms and brought to her face.
