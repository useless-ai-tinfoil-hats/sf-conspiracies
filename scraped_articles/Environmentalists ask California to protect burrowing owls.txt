Environmentalists ask California to protect burrowing owls
-----
The number of burrowing owls — small, cute creatures that live below ground in grasslands and deserts all over the state — is shrinking, wildlife experts say.On Tuesday, a coalition of conservation groups filed a petition with the state seeking to protect the birds by declaring them an endangered or threatened species.
