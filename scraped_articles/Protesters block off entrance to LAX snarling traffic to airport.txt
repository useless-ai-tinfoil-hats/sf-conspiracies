Protesters block off entrance to LAX, snarling traffic to airport
-----
A large group of protesters snarled traffic on Century Boulevard in Los Angeles Wednesday morning, blocking the main entrance to Los Angeles International Airport.Helicopter news footage showed several people standing in front of cars attempting to enter or leave the airport.
