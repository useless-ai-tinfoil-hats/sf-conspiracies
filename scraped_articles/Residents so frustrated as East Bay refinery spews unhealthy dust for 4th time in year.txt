Residents ‘so frustrated’ as East Bay refinery spews unhealthy dust for 4th time in year
-----
An oil refinery in Martinez spewed petroleum coke dust into the air Friday morning, the latest in a series of emissions problems at the facility since last year that have riled community members worried about how much the company pollutes their air.The Martinez Refining Co. said in a Facebook post that the brief emissions burst occurred about 10:30 a.m. and contained petroleum coke dust emitted "when equipment was opened for maintenance."
