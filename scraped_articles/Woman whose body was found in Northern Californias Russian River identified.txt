Woman whose body was found in Northern California's Russian River identified
-----
LATEST April 1, 3:31 p.m. A woman whose body was discovered by a kayaker in the Russian River last week has been identified.After completing a post-mortem examination as part of a coroner's investigation last Wednesday, officials with the Mendocino County Sheriff's Office said they combed recent missing person reports and found an individual who was a possible match based on the physical characteristics of the decedent in the case.
