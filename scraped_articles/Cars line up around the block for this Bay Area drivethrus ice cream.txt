Cars line up around the block for this Bay Area drive-thru's ice cream
-----
When Bay Area temps inch toward the triple digits, there's one small suburb where everyone knows how to cool off in a delicious way.At the corner of Neal Street and Railroad Avenue in Pleasanton is the storied Meadowlark Dairy, a third-generation family-owned business that sells everything from fresh milk to soft service ice cream via drive-thru. Meadowlark Dairy has a deep history in the Bay Area that dates back more than a century. In 1919, it was Alameda County's first certified dairy
