Review: An ambitious new opera by S.F. composer Jake Heggie tackles the legacy of slavery

HOUSTON — "The past is never dead," William Faulkner famously wrote. "It's not even past."The applicability of Faulkner's truism is vast, but never more so than in connection with the particular subjects that were so often on his mind — race, slavery, the U.S. Civil War and the deep, scarcely atonable guilt of the American South.
