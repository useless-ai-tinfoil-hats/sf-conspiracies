This Bay Area state park will reopen after eight-month closure due to road damage
-----
Novato's Olompali State Historic Park, which has been closed since a mudslide in March damaged a nearby road, will reopen this weekend, state park officials said Tuesday.The park will reopen Friday at 3 p.m. after Caltrans completes road repairs on Redwood Boulevard, according to Ryan Forbes, a spokesperson for the California Department of Parks and Recreation. Starting Saturday, the park will resume its normal operating hours between 9 a.m. and 5 p.m.
