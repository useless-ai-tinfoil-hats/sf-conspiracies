Woman, 29, dead after slipping on Hawaii clifftop
-----
A 29-year-old woman has died after slipping from a clifftop on Hawaii Island in the early hours of Sunday morning, police say.At around 2 a.m. on Feb. 18, police and the fire department responded to a report that a woman slipped and fell into the ocean near the intersection of Paradise Drive and Beach Road in Keeau, near the Paradise Cliffs trail.
