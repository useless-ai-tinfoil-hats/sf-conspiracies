For one day only, System of a Down and Deftones fans can buy exclusive S.F. merch online
-----
System of a Down and Deftones fans have one more chance to score their exclusive San Francisco merchandise.Those who were unable to purchase items while the heavy metal bands performed at Golden Gate Park over the weekend will have one last opportunity when the uniquely branded apparel goes on sale Friday, Aug. 23. Deftones' sale begins at 3 a.m., and System of a Down's starts at 6 a.m. through their respective official band sites.
