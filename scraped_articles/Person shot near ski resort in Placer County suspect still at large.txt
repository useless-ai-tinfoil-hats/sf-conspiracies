Person shot near ski resort in Placer County, suspect still at large
-----
A person was shot near Homewood Mountain Resort in Placer County on Monday, authorities said.The Placer County Sheriff's Office said the shooting occurred near the 5000 block of West Lake Boulevard. A picture posted on social media by the sheriff's office appeared to show a crime scene near Swiss Lakewood Restaurant, which is closed on Mondays.
The victim was rushed to the hospital and their condition is unknown, the sheriff's office said. The suspect is still at large.
