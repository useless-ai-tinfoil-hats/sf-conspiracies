Map shows where S.F. drivers and pedestrians will have to submit to security checks during APEC
-----
An oblong section of downtown San Francisco will become a heightened security zone during the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation, or APEC, conference in November, with checkpoints to enter and leave.The zone's perimeter, which stretches from Chinatown to South of Market, is bound by Clay Street to the north and Folsom Street to the south, with freeway off-ramp closures at Fifth Street for motorists heading to or from the Bay Bridge.
