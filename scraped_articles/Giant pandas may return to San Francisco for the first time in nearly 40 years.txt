Giant pandas may return to San Francisco for the first time in nearly 40 years
-----
The San Francisco Zoo reportedly may soon be home to at least one giant panda by 2025.The news follows a much-talked-about comment from Chinese President Xi Jinping while he was in San Francisco for the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation event in November. During a dinner with business leaders at the conference, Xi said he hoped to "deepen the friendly ties" between the U.S. and China by sending giant pandas back to California.
