A construction site in San Francisco just turned into a trash-filled lake
-----
San Francisco's SoMa neighborhood has a new attraction: a turbid, trash-filled lake.The "lake" is actually a vacant lot at the corner of Shipley and Fifth streets that mysteriously flooded. On Tuesday, an SFGATE reporter observed roughly 5 feet of standing water at the site, which has been empty and fenced off for several years. Chunks of debris floated on the water's slimy, opaque surface.
