Two Bay Area boys considered 'endangered missing' are found safe
-----
LATEST April 7, 4:30 p.m. Mountain View police announced that both Daniel Wu and David Wu were found safe.April 7, 6:02 a.m. Mountain View police are asking people to be on the lookout for two missing Bay Area boys who were last seen on Saturday.
Daniel Wu, 9, and David Wu, 4, disappeared from Montecito Avenue in Mountain View around 1:30 a.m. Saturday. They're believed to have been taken by Taylor Wu, 38, whose relationship to the boys was not clarified by Mountain View police.
