Billionaire real estate heir agrees to grant public access to Big Sur coastline
-----
The "no trespassing" signs dotting one of the most iconic views in Central California are about to come down.The California Coastal Commission struck a deal with Monaco-born billionaire Patrice Pastor last month to grant public access to the 2.5-acre parcel he owns along Highway 1 on the Big Sur coastline, the Mercury News first reported.
