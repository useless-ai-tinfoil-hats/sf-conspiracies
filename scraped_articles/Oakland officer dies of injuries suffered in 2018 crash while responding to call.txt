Oakland officer dies of injuries suffered in 2018 crash while responding to call
-----
An Oakland officer died Saturday morning of injuries sustained in a 2018 crash while responding to a call, the police department reported.Officer Jordan Wingate, 29, died surrounded by family and friends, including retired Oakland police Capt. Randell Wingate, police said in a news release Saturday. He was the 55th Oakland officer killed in the line of duty.
