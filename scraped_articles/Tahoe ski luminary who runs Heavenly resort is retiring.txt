Tahoe ski luminary who runs Heavenly resort is retiring
-----
A luminary of California's ski industry is retiring from his post as leader of Heavenly Mountain Resort in South Lake Tahoe.Tom Fortune has helped lead three of Tahoe's premier ski areas in Heavenly, Kirkwood and Northstar — all owned by Vail Resorts — since 2010. After 45 years in the industry, he will retire with the conclusion of Heavenly's ski season in June, according to a Monday news release from Vail.
