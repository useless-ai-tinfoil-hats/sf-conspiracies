Giants’ best no-Ohtani option? Paying Padres’ steep price for Juan Soto
-----
NASHVILLE — While the San Francisco Giants focus on Shohei Ohtani, the trade market for outfielder Juan Soto is developing.The Yankees are believed to be the team most heavily involved in talks with the Padres, though reports Sunday indicated that the price tag was giving New York pause: young right-handers Michael King and Jhony Brito, starter Clarke Schmidt and as many as four prospects for one year of Soto's service.
