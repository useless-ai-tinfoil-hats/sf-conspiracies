SF parking meter that charges more than $10 an hour raked in $17,000 last year
-----
A parking spot near the Palace of Fine Arts in San Francisco has the dubious honor of extracting the most money from drivers this past year.The single parking meter in the Marina District raked in $17,000 between September 2022 and 2023, according to a recent analysis by the San Francisco Standard. That made it the city's highest single-spot revenue generator based on city data, the Standard found.
