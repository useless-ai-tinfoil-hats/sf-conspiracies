Here's how to snag three-day passes to Outside Lands before they sell out
-----
With its lineup no longer secret — or the subject of subreddit sleuthing — tickets to Outside Lands are on sale, and they're moving quickly.Single-day passes are still easy-ish to come by, but only a limited number of three-day passes are still up for grabs. Lucky for you, we've spotted several good options on the secondary market starting at $375. That's less than face value, and cheaper even than the $425 Eager Beaver passes announced in April before the lineup even dropped.
