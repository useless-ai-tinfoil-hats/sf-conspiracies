San Mateo parents, twins who died in suspected murder-suicide ID'd
-----
The San Mateo County coroner's office has released the identities of the victims of a suspected murder-suicide in a quiet San Mateo neighborhood this week.The family members found dead in their home were Anand Henry, 37, his wife Alice Benziger, 38, and their 4-year-old twin sons, Noah and Nathan, spokespersons said. The family moved into the home in 2020, according to public records.
