49ers' Kyle Shanahan decides to fire defensive coordinator Steve Wilks
-----
San Francisco 49ers head coach Kyle Shanahan announced Wednesday on an impromptu conference call that he has fired defensive coordinator Steve Wilks.The 54-year-old coach's tenure with the team has ended after one season. Wilks joined the Niners following the departure of former defensive coordinator DeMeco Ryans, whom the Houston Texans hired in the offseason to be the team's new head coach.
