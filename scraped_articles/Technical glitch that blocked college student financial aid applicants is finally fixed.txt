Technical glitch that blocked college student financial aid applicants is finally fixed
-----
A technical glitch that prevented hundreds of thousands of students — including thousands in the Bay Area — from submitting their federal financial aid applications for college has been resolved, officials said.The issue with the Free Application for Federal Student Aid, or FAFSA, affected students from families with "mixed immigration status," or those who are citizens or legally in the United States but whose parents don't have a Social Security number or numbers.
