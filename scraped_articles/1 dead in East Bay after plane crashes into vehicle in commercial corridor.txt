1 dead in East Bay after plane crashes into vehicle in commercial corridor
-----
A plane crash in a busy Concord intersection left at least one person dead, Contra Costa County fire officials said Monday.Officials said they were on scene shortly after 10:30 a.m. following a crash involving an aircraft that collided into a vehicle, officials said. One person, the pilot, was pronounced dead on the scene, fire spokespersons said.
