Pro-Palestine protesters block SFO security gates as passengers get re-routed
-----
A protest that saw approximately 200 pro-Palestine demonstrators streaming through San Francisco International Airport blocked multiple roadways and security gates for several hours Wednesday morning.Just after 8:30 a.m., protesters barricaded the A and G checkpoints at SFO's International Terminal to demand a permanent ceasefire in the Israel-Hamas war raging in Gaza.
