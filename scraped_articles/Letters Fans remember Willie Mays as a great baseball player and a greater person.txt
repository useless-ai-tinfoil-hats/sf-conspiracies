Letters: Fans remember Willie Mays as a great baseball player and a greater person
-----
What a treatGrowing up in San Francisco, my dad introduced me to baseball at a young age with an outfielders' glove and a trip to Candlestick Park.
Watching Willie Mays play was mesmerizing, and every chance I had, even missing school, to watch him play was worth it. Sometimes I wonder how I finished school and became a lawyer with all those days at Candlestick watching Willie.
