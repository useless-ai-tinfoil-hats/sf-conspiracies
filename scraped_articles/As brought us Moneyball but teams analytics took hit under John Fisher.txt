A’s brought us ‘Moneyball,’ but team’s analytics took hit under John Fisher
-----
When reflecting on the Oakland Athletics' historically awful 2023 season, it's easy to blame owner John Fisher for the stinginess that has turned the organization into a shell of itself, with no hope for on-field success.But a deeper look shows that Fisher was particularly cheap in an area the A's once dominated: analytics.
In the early 2000s, the A's were the envy of huge-revenue clubs, driven by data and strategies that helped them field contending teams despite low-end payrolls
