Mystery problem at S.F.’s Coit Tower has everyone puzzled: ‘We need the elevator whisperer’
-----
There's an added cost for taking in sweeping San Francisco vistas from atop Coit Tower on Telegraph Hill these days: sweat and fatigue from hiking up 13 flights of stairs.After several years of unreliable service and more than a few visitors getting trapped, the city has suspended rides on the tower's vintage 1934 elevator for the foreseeable future.
The elevator began sporadically stalling mid-flight about two years ago, forcing staffers to race to the 12th floor and press a reset
