Man arrested on suspicion of stealing 50 iPhones from East Bay Apple Store
-----
A man was arrested on suspicion of stealing 50 iPhones from an East Bay Apple Store, police said.The viral video apparently showing the theft, which has circulated on multiple social media platforms this week, shows a masked man forcefully taking iPhones off their display chargers. As multiple alarms sound and customers and employees watch in the background, he puts them in his pants and leaves the store.
