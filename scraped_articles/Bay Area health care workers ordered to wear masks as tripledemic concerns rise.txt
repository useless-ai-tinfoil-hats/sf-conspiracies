Bay Area health care workers ordered to wear masks as ‘tripledemic’ concerns rise
-----
In response to the increasing threat of respiratory illnesses, including COVID-19 and influenza, officials in multiple Bay Area counties have issued orders requiring health care workers in patient care settings to wear masks during the upcoming respiratory virus season.The measures announced in Sonoma, Contra Costa, Alameda and San Mateo counties remain in effect from Nov. 1 through April 30.
