An acclaimed coffee shop and another Walgreens are both closing in downtown San Francisco, adding to the retail toll in the area.Mazarine Coffee, at 720 Market St. near the foot of Kearny Street, will close by Jan. 31, according to a sign posted outside the shop. The French-inspired cafe serves espressos alongside waffles, quinoa bowls and hummus and was a Chronicle pick for best downtown coffee.
