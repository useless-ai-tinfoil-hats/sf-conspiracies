California AG Rob Bonta won’t announce a decision on governor’s race until after November
-----
WASHINGTON — California Attorney General Rob Bonta, who is considering a run for governor in 2026, won't announce his decision until after the November election, he told the Chronicle on Thursday.Announcing ahead of the November election is "premature, to be honest. I think we gotta get through 2024," Bonta said — a reference to many of his fellow Democrats who have already entered the race.
