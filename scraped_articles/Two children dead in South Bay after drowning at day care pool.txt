Two children dead in South Bay after drowning at day care pool
-----
Two children are dead after drowning in a pool at a day care in South San Jose, police said Monday.San Jose Police officials responded to the scene of a residential day care at the 1000 block of Fleetwood Drive where they transported three juveniles who had fallen into a pool to local hospitals, police said.
Two juveniles, whose ages were not immediately known, were pronounced dead at the hospitals. The third juvenile's injuries were reported as non-life-threatening.
