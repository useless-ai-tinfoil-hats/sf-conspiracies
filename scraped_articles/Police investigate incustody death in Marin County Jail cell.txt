Police investigate in-custody death in Marin County Jail cell
-----
San Rafael police are investigating an in-custody death of a man at Marin County Jail, authorities said.Deputies conducting a routine check shortly after 2 a.m. Thursday found the man alone in his cell in need of medical attention, the Marin County Sheriff's Office reported. They began lifesaving measures and summoned paramedics from the San Rafael Fire Department, but he was pronounced dead about 30 minutes later.
The man's next of kin has been notified, said the sheriff's office,
