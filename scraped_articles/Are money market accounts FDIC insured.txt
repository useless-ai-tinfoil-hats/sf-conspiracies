Are money market accounts FDIC insured?
-----
If you want to earn extra income from your savings balance, you might consider a money market account. A money market account has many of the features of a checking account, but typically earns interest at a higher rate than a traditional checking or savings account. Like other deposit accounts, money market accounts are federally insured up to FDIC limits, so you won't lose your cash if your bank fails.
