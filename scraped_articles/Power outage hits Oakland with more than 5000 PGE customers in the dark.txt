Power outage hits Oakland, with more than 5,000 PG&E customers in the dark
-----
Residents in a large swath of Oakland lost power Monday morning, according to PG&E's outage map.More than 5,000 households went dark, mainly in the Fruitvale area, starting at 9:42 a.m., according to the map.
Power is expected to be restored by 4:45 p.m.
Get the latest outage information from PG&E's website.
PG&E did not immediately respond to a request for comment.
