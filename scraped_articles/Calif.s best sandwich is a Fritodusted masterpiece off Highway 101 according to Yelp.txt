Calif.'s best sandwich is a Frito-dusted masterpiece off Highway 101, according to Yelp
-----
A Frito-dusted tri-tip sandwich from a tiny deli on the way to Monterey has claimed the title of best sandwich in the state, according to Yelp.In a recent ranking for the best sandwiches in every state, Yelp honored Prunedale Market & Deli in Prunedale for its decadent tri-tip sandwich, generously topped with Flamin' Hot Fritos. Yelp also featured Prunedale Market in its list of the top sandwich spots in every state in 2022 and in its top 100 places to eat in 2017 and 2018.
