Stanford softball ace NiJaree Canady enters transfer portal
-----
Stanford softball ace NiJaree Canady is entering the transfer portal, the Chronicle confirmed on Sunday. The USA Softball Player of the year led the Cardinal to consecutive Women's College World Series appearances, including this past season.Canady led the country this season with a 0.73 ERA and 337 strikeouts to average 10.2 strikeouts per seven innings. She will have two years of eligibility left wherever she goes.
A Stanford spokesperson declined comment to the Chronicle.
