PG&E outage leaves more than 8,000 without power in S.F.
-----
A PG&E outage in San Francisco left more than 8,000 customers without power for several hours Friday night.PG&E reported an outage affecting Golden Gate Park and parts of the Sunset District around 7:17 p.m. Friday. An estimated 8,888 customers have been affected, according to the utility.
Power is expected to be restored by 10:30 p.m. As of 9 p.m., PG&E was still assessing the cause of the outage, according to its website.
