I-780 in Solano County closed due to police investigation
-----
Interstate 780 in Benicia was closed Friday night due to a police investigation.Eastbound traffic was being taken off at Columbus Parkway and westbound traffic was being diverted at Southampton Road, according to KCBS.
The nature of the investigation was not known, but police from neighboring areas joined in a search for suspects near the freeway and a police helicopter was used.
