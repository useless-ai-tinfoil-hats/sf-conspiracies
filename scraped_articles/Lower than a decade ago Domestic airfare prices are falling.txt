‘Lower than a decade ago’: Domestic airfare prices are falling
-----
The post-pandemic travel boom is slowing down, and flight prices are falling along with it."Everyone assumes, because airfare was sky-high last summer, that this is still the case when, in reality, the average price of airfare is as cheap today as it was in 2006. It has fallen 11 out of the past 14 months, so cheap flights are very much available," Katy Nastro, a travel expert at Going, wrote to SFGATE in an email.
