S.F. restaurant Foreign Cinema owed workers more than $200K for health care mandate violations
-----
A 25-year-old Mission District restaurant known for dinner and brunch with films playing in the background has paid out hundreds of thousands of dollars in employee compensation after San Francisco officials determined it hadn't met the local health care mandate.The S.F. Office of Labor Standards Enforcement found Foreign Cinema had broken the city's minimum health care expenditure requirements between April 1, 2020, and March 31, 2013, SFGATE reported.
