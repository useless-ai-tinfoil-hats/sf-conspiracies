One dead after experimental plane crashes near Tahoe
-----
One person died in a plane crash near Lake Tahoe on Tuesday morning, officials said. The crash happened at the Minden-Tahoe Airport in Nevada at approximately 8:19 a.m., the Douglas County Sheriff's Office said in a news release.The plane, which had two people on board, crashed during takeoff, the Federal Aviation Administration said in a statement to SFGATE. The condition of the second person has not been comfirmed.
