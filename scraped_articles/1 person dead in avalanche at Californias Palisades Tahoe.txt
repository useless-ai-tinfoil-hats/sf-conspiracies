1 person dead in avalanche at California's Palisades Tahoe
-----
One person died and another was injured in an avalanche that occurred around 9:30 a.m. Wednesday at the top of the iconic KT-22 chairlift at Palisades Tahoe in California's Olympic Valley, officials said."The search is complete," Sgt. Dave Smith, a spokesperson for the Placer County Sheriff's Office, said over the phone at 1:30 p.m. Dozens of rescue workers had descended on the scene to search in the snow.
