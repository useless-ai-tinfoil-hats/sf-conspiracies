With Hippie Hill canceled, here are the best 420 events in San Francisco
-----
For decades, Hippie Hill in Golden Gate Park has been the gravitational center of celebrating 420, the international cannabis holiday that was invented in the Bay Area. But for the first time in years, Hippie Hill will officially be closed to cannabis enthusiasts April 20.The event's organizers had to cancel the 420 party, and the city is now asking pot fans to not congregate in that area of the park, with an adult kickball game taking over the world-famous smoking destination.
