Review: ‘Mother of the Bride’ is full of cliches, but Brooke Shields shines
-----
Laden with clunky dialogue and romantic comedy cliches, "Mother of the Bride" is the kind of movie where people talk to each other right on the edges of ponds or swimming pools. Conversations are mere preludes to pratfalls.But the film, premiering Thursday, May 9, on Netflix, features an ebullient performance by Brooke Shields as Lana, a Bay Area geneticist whose influencer daughter, Emma (Miranda Cosgrove, "iCarly"), is getting married in Thailand.
