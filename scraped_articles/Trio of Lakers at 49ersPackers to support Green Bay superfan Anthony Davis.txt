Trio of Lakers at 49ers-Packers to support Green Bay superfan Anthony Davis
-----
A divisional playoff game between the 49ers and Packers was bound to draw celebrities to Levi's Stadium.But perhaps the most surprising group was a trio of Los Angeles Lakers, led by apparent Packers superfan Anthony Davis.
Davis, D'Angelo Russell and Jarred Vanderbilt all made the trip up from Los Angeles to Santa Clara for Saturday's rainy game, with Davis rocking a Packers jersey and beanie during the pre-game.
