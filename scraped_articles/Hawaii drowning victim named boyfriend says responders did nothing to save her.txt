Hawaii drowning victim named, boyfriend says responders did 'nothing' to save her
-----
Police have identified the 29-year-old Hawaii woman who slipped from a cliff and drowned in the ocean on Sunday. Her boyfriend, who was with her that night, is now alleging that first responders didn't do enough to save her.Hawaii Island police identified the deceased as Kalaiokealaula Ashley Nicole Reyes Kanekoa of Keaau, in a statement sent to SFGATE.
At around 2 a.m. on Feb. 18, police and the fire department responded to a report that a woman slipped and fell into the ocean
