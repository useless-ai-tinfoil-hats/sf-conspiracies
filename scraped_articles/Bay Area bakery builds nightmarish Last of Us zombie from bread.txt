Bay Area bakery builds nightmarish ‘Last of Us’ zombie from bread
-----
Avoiding bites from zombies was key to survival in the hit TV series "The Last of Us." But this incarnation of the monstrous "clickers" is one you could sink your teeth into.A mother-daughter team of bakers in Benicia has created a life-size bread sculpture of a clicker — nightmarish, hulking humans infected with mind-controlling fungi bursting from their skulls.
