S.F. DA’s office employee fired after email to Brooke Jenkins asking ‘what color panties you have on’
-----
The San Francisco District Attorney's Office on Friday fired an employee who sent an email to his boss, District Attorney Brooke Jenkins, reading, "What color panties you have on."In the email sent Friday morning, Jovan Thomas, a victim advocate in the department's critical incidents team, appeared to be responding to a calendar invite for an anti-discrimination meeting this week to memorialize the killing of gay college student Matthew Shepard in 1998 in Wyoming.
