Steph Curry joins S.F. ownership group in Tiger’s virtual golf league
-----
Stephen Curry found another path into the golf realm.TGL, the team golf league developed by Tiger Woods and Rory McIlroy, announced its ownership group Thursday for the San Francisco entry — with Curry as one of the lead investors. He joins Marc Lasry, a billionaire businessman and former co-owner of the NBA's Milwaukee Bucks.
