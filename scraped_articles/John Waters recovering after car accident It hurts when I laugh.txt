John Waters recovering after car accident: ‘It hurts when I laugh’
-----
Cult filmmaker John Waters is on the mend after a car accident in Maryland led to a brief hospitalization this week."Since it hurts when I laugh I will have no witty answer about being in a car accident that no one has said was my fault," Waters, 78, wrote in a statement on Tuesday. "Hope you understand. I'm released from the hospital and all is OK."
