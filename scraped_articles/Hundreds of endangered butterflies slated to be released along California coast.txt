Hundreds of endangered butterflies slated to be released along California coast
-----
Hundreds of rare, blazing orange butterflies are slated to be released in Northern California over the next two years, marking a victory for conservationists working tirelessly to restore them across the region.In 2023, the Mendocino Land Trust received a $1.5 million grant to restore critical habitat for the Behren's silverspot butterfly, an endangered butterfly endemic to California's lush Mendocino coastline.
