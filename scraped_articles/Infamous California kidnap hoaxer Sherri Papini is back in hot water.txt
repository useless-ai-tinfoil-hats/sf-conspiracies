Infamous California kidnap hoaxer Sherri Papini is back in hot water
-----
Infamous California kidnapping hoaxer Sherri Papini is back in hot water with federal prosecutors, who filed a new motion against her earlier this week.Papini, who became an international pariah after it was revealed that she faked her own kidnapping in 2016, was sentenced to 18 months in prison for lying to investigators and defrauding the California Victim Compensation Board. In addition to prison time, a judge ordered her to pay over $300,000 in restitution.
