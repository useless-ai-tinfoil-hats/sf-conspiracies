Google co-founder Sergey Brin sued over fatal plane crash near Half Moon Bay
-----
A man piloting Google co-founder Sergey Brin's plane crashed and died near Half Moon Bay in May 2023. Now, his widow is taking the billionaire tech executive to court with a wrongful death lawsuit.Maria Magdalena Olarte filed an amended complaint on Feb. 13 in the California Superior Court, Santa Clara County, alleging that Brin is partially to blame for her husband Lance Maclean's death and the ensuing struggle to retrieve his body.
