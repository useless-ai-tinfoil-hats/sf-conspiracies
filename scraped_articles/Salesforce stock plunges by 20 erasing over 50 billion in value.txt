Salesforce stock plunges by 20%, erasing over $50 billion in value
-----
Salesforce, San Francisco's largest private employer, suffered a 20% drop in its stock on Thursday following a disappointing earnings report.The cloud computing company lost over $50 billion in value in a single day after reporting fiscal first quarter revenue of $9.13 billion, missing analyst forecasts of $9.17 billion in the first miss since 2006, according to LSEG data. It's now valued at $211.7 billion.
