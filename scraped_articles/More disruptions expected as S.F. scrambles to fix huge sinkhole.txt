More disruptions expected as S.F. scrambles to fix huge sinkhole
-----
UPDATE: San Francisco sinkhole repairs to take at least 6 weeksRepairs to the massive sinkhole that ruptured layers of pavement and sent rivers of water cascading into San Francisco's Marina district on Monday continued Tuesday, though city officials said they could not estimate when the work would be completed.
The timeframe for repairs and safety assessments were "still undetermined" as of Tuesday morning, said San Francisco Public Utilities Commission spokesperson Joseph Sweiss.
