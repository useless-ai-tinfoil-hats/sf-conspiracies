Review: Finally, a good ‘Hunger Games’ movie — ‘Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes’ is the best of the series
-----
"The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes" has an overwrought title, but it's the best movie of the film franchise.Many of the elements that fans of the series have come to expect are not in this latest installation — no flashy costumes, no campy bits and, of course, no Jennifer Lawrence bringing a bow and arrow to a gunfight. Good riddance.
