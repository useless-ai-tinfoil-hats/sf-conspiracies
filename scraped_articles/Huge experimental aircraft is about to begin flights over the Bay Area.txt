Huge, experimental aircraft is about to begin flights over the Bay Area
-----
A massive white airship almost double the size of the Goodyear blimp could soon take to the skies in the San Francisco Bay Area as test flights begin from Moffett Field off Highway 101 near Mountain View.Called Pathfinder 1, the experimental airship emerged this week from one of the airfield's hulking hangar's visible from the highway.
