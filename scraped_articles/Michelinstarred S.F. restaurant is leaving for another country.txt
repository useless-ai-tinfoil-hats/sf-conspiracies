Michelin-starred S.F. restaurant is leaving for another country
-----
A San Francisco Michelin-star recipient will soon be closing its doors.Avery, at 1552 Fillmore St., announced it is closing in November after seven years of service so chef Rodney Wages can open a location in Edinburgh, Scotland, a message on social media states.
The chef told the Chronicle he is happy with what he accomplished with his restaurant, but is also excited to open a new version of the restaurant in another country.
