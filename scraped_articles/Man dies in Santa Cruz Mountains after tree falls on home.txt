Man dies in Santa Cruz Mountains after tree falls on home
-----
A 45-year-old man died after a tree fell onto a home in Boulder Creek amid high winds brought on by a so-called bomb cyclone spinning off the California coast, officials said.One person escaped from the home but the man was trapped inside, Ashley Keehn, public information officer with the Santa Cruz County Sheriff's Office told SFGATE.
