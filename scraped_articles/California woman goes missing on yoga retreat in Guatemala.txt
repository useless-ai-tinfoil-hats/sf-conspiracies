California woman goes missing on yoga retreat in Guatemala
-----
A California woman is missing after attending a yoga retreat at a volcanic lake in Guatemala, according to multiple news outlets.Nancy Ng, 29, of Monterey Park in LA County, disappeared on Oct. 19 while at Lake Atitlan in the Guatemalan Highlands. Ng had previously attended the same retreat in 2022, and liked it so much she returned for a week-long stay, KTLA reports.
