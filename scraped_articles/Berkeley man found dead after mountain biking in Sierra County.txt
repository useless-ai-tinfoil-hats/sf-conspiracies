Berkeley man found dead after mountain biking in Sierra County
-----
The body of a Berkeley man was recovered Tuesday after he went missing while camping and mountain biking in Sierra County.On Monday, Joe Lusk, 61, was reported missing by his wife after he didn't return home on time from a weekend trip, according to the Sierra County Sheriff's Office. Lusk's wife told officials he had planned to take a shuttle out of Downieville, bike back into town and go camping in the Gold Lake basin area.
