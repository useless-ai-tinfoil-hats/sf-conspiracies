Get a free Amazon Smart Plug and Echo Pop when you buy a Christmas tree
-----
Sure, Christmas tree light-timers have been around for years, but the 2023 version — a Smart Plug and voice assistant device — can do a lot more than an old-fashioned timer. Even better, this combo comes free with the purchase of a Christmas tree when you buy from a select (and generous) assortment on Amazon.If you're thinking about getting an artificial holiday tree this year, all you have to do is go to Amazon, log into your Prime account, pick out one of the qualifying holiday trees
