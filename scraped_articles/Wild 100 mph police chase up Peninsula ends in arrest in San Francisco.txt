Wild 100 mph police chase up Peninsula ends in arrest in San Francisco
-----
Police in Broadmoor said they arrested an adult and a juvenile Wednesday following a dramatic high-speed chase up the Peninsula and into San Francisco.Police arrived on the 800 block of Beechwood Drive early Wednesday morning following a report of a vehicle burglary in progress. When officers tried to stop the vehicle containing the suspected thieves, the driver sped off after attempting to ram a Broadmoor police car, officials said.
