Inside the Calif. test kitchen of a legendary fast food brand

The San Diego office park that fast food behemoth Jack in the Box calls home is pretty similar to office parks everywhere: giant concrete buildings, sprawling parking lots, branded street names. Even inside, save for a statue of the brand's iconic, round-faced mascot Jack and an illuminated vintage menu listing hamburgers for 24 cents in the front lobby, it's pretty much the same as any corporate headquarters.
