Oakland police find third person shot at high school graduation ceremony
-----
Oakland police located a third gunshot victim at a local hospital as they continued to investigate a shooting at a Skyline High School graduation ceremony, the department said in a social media post on Friday.Police said two groups leaving the ceremony got into a fight in a parking lot just before 7:45 p.m. Thursday night. Gunfire broke out as the fight escalated, the post said. When police arrived, they found two people with injuries who were taken to an area hospital.
