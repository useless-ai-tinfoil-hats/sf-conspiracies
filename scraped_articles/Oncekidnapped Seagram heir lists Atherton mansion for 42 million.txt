Once-kidnapped Seagram heir lists Atherton mansion for $42 million
-----
An heir to the Seagram fortune who was kidnapped for ransom as a young adult and whose sisters were heavily involved in the infamous NXIVM scandal recently listed his longtime Atherton mansion for $42 million.Samuel Bronfman II purchased the home at 30 Atherton Ave. in 1986 for $3.3 million, according to Mansion Global. The Compass listing shows that the home has seven bedrooms, eight full bathrooms and sits on more than 3 acres of land. It features a home theater, wine cellar, library
