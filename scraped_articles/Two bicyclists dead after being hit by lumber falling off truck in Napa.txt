Two bicyclists dead after being hit by lumber falling off truck in Napa
-----
Two bicyclists died in Napa on Tuesday morning when a load of lumber fell off a truck, hitting them both, according to the California Highway Patrol.The two — a man around 50 years old and a woman around 45 — were biking on the Silverado Trail in Napa around 11 a.m., according to officer Vincent Pompliano, a spokesperson for the CHP Napa office.
