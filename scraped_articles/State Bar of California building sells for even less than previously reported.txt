State Bar of California building sells for even less than previously reported
-----
A downtown San Francisco building has officially sold for just $54 million, nearly $10 million less than SFGATE previously reported.The State Bar of California announced Tuesday that its headquarters at 180 Howard St. was purchased by Ridge Capital Investors.
