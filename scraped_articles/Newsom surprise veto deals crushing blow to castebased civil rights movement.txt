Newsom surprise veto deals crushing blow to caste-based civil rights movement
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom vetoed legislation making caste a protected characteristic in California akin to race, gender or age — a move that supporters hailed as historic and opponents denounced as an attack on Hinduism.The bill, SB403 from state Sen. Aisha Wahab, D-Hayward, makes caste — the ancient form of determining one's place in society from birth — an extension of ancestry, which is already protected under the state's Unruh Civil Rights Act and its Fair Employment and Housing Opportunity
