The police reports from Burning Man 2023 are out
-----
Police reports for Burning Man are finally out, and according to the Pershing County Sheriff's Office, arrests were down, with just 13 arrested at the 2023 event.That number is actually way down compared with previous years — there were as many as 58 arrests in 2019 — though the dip can very likely be attributed to the rain-caused boggy mess that made navigating Burning Man almost impossible for several days.
