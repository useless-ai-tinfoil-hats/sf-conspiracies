How drones became a ‘game changer’ for finding missing people in California’s wilderness
-----
Days after purchasing their first drone last winter, the Butte County sheriff's search-and-rescue team members flew it over a flooded farm plot east of Chico and located a man stranded in his pickup truck in rising water.Once searchers had driven to the general area of the man's emergency call, they launched the quadcopter, equipped with a 56x zoom lens and thermal imaging, as their eye in the sky, soaring over submerged homes and crop fields.
