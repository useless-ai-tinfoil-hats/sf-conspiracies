Alaska air to blast Bay Area and bring chance of low-elevation snow
-----
Winter is far from over in the San Francisco Bay Area.Two separate cold fronts from the Gulf of Alaska are expected to blast the region with cold air, kick up winds and bring soaking rains and a chance for low-elevation snow to the region Thursday through the weekend.
