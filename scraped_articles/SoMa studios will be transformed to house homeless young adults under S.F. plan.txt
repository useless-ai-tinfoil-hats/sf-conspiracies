SoMa studios will be transformed to house homeless young adults under S.F. plan
-----
San Francisco will turn a building in the South of Market Street neighborhood into supportive housing for young adults, the city said Thursday after receiving a key grant from the state.Under its plan, the city will convert 24 studio units in the building at 42 Otis St. into housing for homeless residents aged 18 to 24. The project is supported by an $8.2 million grant from the state.
