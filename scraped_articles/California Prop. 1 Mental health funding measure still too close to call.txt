California Prop. 1: Mental health funding measure still too close to call
-----
Proposition 1, the ballot measure to enact a key part of Gov. Gavin Newsom's plan to get people off the streets and into treatment, was leading slightly but still too close to call Wednesday morning.Initial results showed the measure's lead had narrowed to 50.2% to 49.8% Wednesday morning, with potentially millions more votes left to tally.
