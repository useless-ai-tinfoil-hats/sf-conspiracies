Fairfax police make arrest after assault, arson at homeless encampment
-----
A homeless encampment at the center of controversy in the Marin County town of Fairfax was the site of an alleged assault and arson attack on Wednesday, police said.Fairfax police officers responding to reports of a disturbance at the encampment, located in Peri Park near a children's playground, discovered around 12:38 p.m. that an unhoused person had allegedly ignited a fire following a fight with another encampment resident, Police Chief Rico Tabaranza said.
