North Coast and Central Coast section football playoff brackets announced
-----
There were no major surprises Sunday when the playoff brackets for the North Coast and Central Coast sections were announced.The games kick off Friday and conclude Nov. 24 with champions in all divisions advancing to the CIF State Northern Regional championships on Dec. 1-2. The winners of those advance to the state championships, which take place Dec. 8-9 at three Southern California venues: Pasadena College, El Camino College-Torrance and Saddleback College-Mission Viejo.
