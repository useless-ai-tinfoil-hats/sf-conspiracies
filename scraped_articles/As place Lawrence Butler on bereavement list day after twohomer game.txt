A's place Lawrence Butler on bereavement list day after two-homer game
-----
Just as things had been looking up for the Oakland Athletics and their young core to start September, the club endured a setback Tuesday.The team placed outfielder Lawrence Butler on the bereavement list prior to game time, recalling infielder Kevin Smith from Triple A-Las Vegas in a corresponding move.
Butler left the A's following a two-homer game in their 6-5, 10-inning loss to the Toronto Blue Jays on Monday. The 23-year-old is joining his family in his native state of Georgia
