Earthquake shakes Bay Area, emergency alert hits phones
-----
An earthquake rattled the Bay Area on Wednesday morning, and many residents were alerted to it by their phones. At approximately 9:30 a.m., a magnitude 4.1 earthquake centered near Isleton in Sacramento County was felt throughout the region.
