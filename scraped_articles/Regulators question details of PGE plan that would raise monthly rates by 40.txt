Regulators question details of PG&E plan that would raise monthly rates by $40
-----
State regulators pushed back Wednesday against Pacific Gas and Electric Co.'s plans to bury thousands of miles of power lines, a top expense in the utility's four-year budget that would add nearly $40 onto typical monthly customer bills each month.Instead, the California Public Utilities Commission said the company could prevent wildfires for billions of dollars less than proposed.
