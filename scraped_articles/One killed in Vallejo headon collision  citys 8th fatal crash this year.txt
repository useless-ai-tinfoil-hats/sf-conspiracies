One killed in Vallejo head-on collision — city’s 8th fatal crash this year
-----
A head-on collision in Vallejo early Sunday killed one person, marking the city's eighth fatal car crash this year, authorities said.The crash happened just after 12:30 a.m. near Broadway Street and Sereno Drive, according to the Vallejo Police Department.
A driver in a Toyota Sequoia was traveling north in the southbound lanes of Broadway Street — the lanes are separated by a raised center median — when the car collided with a Cadillac sedan, police said.
