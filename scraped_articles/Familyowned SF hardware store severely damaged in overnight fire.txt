Family-owned SF hardware store severely damaged in overnight fire
-----
A fire severely damaged a longtime San Francisco hardware store over the weekend.The fire happened at approximately 3:48 a.m. on Sunday at the Great Wall Hardware, at 1821 Taraval St., in San Francisco's Sunset neighborhood, Justin Schorr, a spokesperson for the San Francisco Fire Department, told SFGATE.
