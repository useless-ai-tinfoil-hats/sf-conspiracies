49ers will stay far away from Las Vegas Strip once they go to Super Bowl
-----
As the 49ers get set for Super Bowl LVIII in Las Vegas, Sin City is expecting hundreds of thousands of fans to travel in for the game. But while many of those fans will crowd hotels right on the Strip, the 49ers and their opponents will stay far away from the bright lights and revelry by the Strip.The NFL confirmed last month that both Super Bowl teams would stay about 25 miles east of the Strip at hotels near Lake Las Vegas.
