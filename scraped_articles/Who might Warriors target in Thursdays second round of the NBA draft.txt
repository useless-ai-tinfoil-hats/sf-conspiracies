Who might Warriors target in Thursday’s second round of the NBA draft?
-----
The Golden State Warriors are on the clock in the NBA draft.Eventually, anyway.
The first round of the draft came and went Wednesday in New York without involvement from the Warriors, who pick Thursday during the second round – having traded, in 2019, their first-round pick.
They have the No. 52 pick, unlikely to secure a rotational player. Last year, though, with the 57th pick, the Warriors drafted Trayce Jackson-Davis, their starting center by season's end.
