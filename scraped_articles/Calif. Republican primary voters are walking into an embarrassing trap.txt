Calif. Republican primary voters are walking into an embarrassing trap
-----
California Republican voters absolutely despise Adam Schiff. They may be about to hand him a Senate seat.Late last week, a new poll from the Berkeley Institute of Governmental Studies showed Schiff and former Los Angeles Dodgers first baseman Steve Garvey as the favorites to advance out of Tuesday's primary to November's general election. Garvey, who is running on a platform most charitably described as good vibes without substance, has consolidated the Republican field and led the poll
