Bank of America to close more than two dozen Bay Area branches
-----
Bank of America will shutter more than 100 branches by the end of the year, with more than two dozen closures in the Bay Area.That list includes locations that have already shut down at 3250 Mission St. in the Mission District, 50 Fremont St. in the Financial District, 501 Brannan St. in the South of Market and at San Francisco International Airport. Another, at 1275 Fell St., is expected to close before the year wraps up, and two other San Francisco branches are on the chopping block
