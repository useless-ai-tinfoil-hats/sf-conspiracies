Woman arrested in stabbing at Emeryville Chick-fil-A
-----
A woman stabbed a person during an unprovoked attack at an Emeryville Chick-fil-A restaurant and has been arrested, police said.Anastasia Magganas, 35, of Oakland was arrested Monday on suspicion of assault with a deadly weapon other than a firearm and booked into Santa Rita Jail, according to the Emeryville Police Department.
