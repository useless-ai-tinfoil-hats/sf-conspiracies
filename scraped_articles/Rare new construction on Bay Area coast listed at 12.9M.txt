Rare new construction on Bay Area coast listed at $12.9M
-----
In California, where the California Coastal Commission is fiercely protective and erosion risks are high, new construction near the ocean is hard to come by.But the Gray Whale House in Moss Beach is a rare example of recent coastal construction, which required developers and environmentalists to convene on how to build while protecting the bluff. Perched above the sea, this property is now on the market for $12.9 million.
