San Francisco has a lot of dogs, and a lot of cliffs.Turns out that's a perilous combination.

Like the time a professional dog walker lost control of one distracted pooch, and somehow all five ended up stranded, still leashed together, on a sandy slope overlooking the Pacific Ocean.
