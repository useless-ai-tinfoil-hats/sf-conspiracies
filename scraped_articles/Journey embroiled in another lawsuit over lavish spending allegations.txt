Journey embroiled in another lawsuit over lavish spending allegations
-----
Jonathan Cain, the longtime keyboardist for Journey, has filed a lawsuit against lead guitarist Neal Schon, accusing him of extravagant spending that is impacting the band's financial health.In the court filing unsealed this week, Cain alleges that Schon regularly exceeded the band's hotel fee cap of $1,500 per night, maxed out a band American Express card with a $1 million limit, chartered private jets and hired a friend as a personal security guard, among other expenses.
