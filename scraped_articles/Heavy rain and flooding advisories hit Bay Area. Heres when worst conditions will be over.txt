Heavy rain and flooding advisories hit Bay Area. Here's when worst conditions will be over
-----
The rain showers that poured down on the Bay Area on Tuesday evening continued overnight and into the morning on Wednesday, setting the region up for a messy commute. Already, San Francisco has seen about 3/4 of an inch of rain since Tuesday evening.Shower chances continue through the Bay Area all day, but the heaviest rain should be done by noon in San Francisco. Lighter showers are possible during the afternoon, except for the South and East Bay, where heavy showers may continue
