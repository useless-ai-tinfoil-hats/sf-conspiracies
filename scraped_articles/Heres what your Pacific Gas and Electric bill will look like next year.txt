Here's what your Pacific Gas and Electric bill will look like next year
-----
Be prepared for your PG&E bill to be higher next year.Starting with their bill sent in February, customers will pay on average about $30 to $35 per month more than what they paid in 2023.
The increase, which takes effect Jan. 1, comes as the result of unanimous approval last month by the California Public Utilities Commission of a 12.8% rate hike. And if PG&E gets its way, an additional increase could land later in the year.
