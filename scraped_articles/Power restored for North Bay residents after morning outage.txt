Power restored for North Bay residents after morning outage
-----
LATEST June 26, 11 a.m. Following a morning power outage affecting nearly 30,000 people in Sonoma County, power was restored as of 11 a.m., according to the PG&E outage map.June 26, 9:07 a.m. Nearly 30,000 people were without power in Sonoma County on Wednesday morning, according to PowerOutage.us.
The PG&E outage map indicated the outages were spread across Sebastopol and Santa Rosa and power would be restored by 4 p.m.
