At least one killed in small plane crash near Truckee Tahoe Airport
-----
At least one person died in a plane crash Saturday evening in Truckee, said Truckee Fire Protection District officials.The plane crashed around 7 p.m. near Glenshire Drive and Olympic Boulevard, north of the Truckee Tahoe Airport, according to the Truckee Police Department. The number of people in the plane and the exact type of aircraft were unknown as of Saturday night, though Truckee police spokesperson Deverie Acuff said it appeared to be a smaller plane.
