Shaquille O'Neal — aka DJ Diesel — returns to San Francisco for Breakaway Music Festival
-----
Following a memorable Outside Lands performance, DJ Diesel — the alter ego for basketball great Shaquille O'Neal — is headed back to San Francisco for another music festival. The NBA star-turned-disc jockey is slated to headline the first night of Breakaway Music Festival, a traveling electronic music festival that is scheduled to stop in the Bay Area on Oct. 13 and 14.O'Neal was a last-minute addition to the festival.
