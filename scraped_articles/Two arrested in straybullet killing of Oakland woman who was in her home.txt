Two arrested in stray-bullet killing of Oakland woman who was in her home
-----
Oakland police on Tuesday announced the arrest of two men in connection with the death of a woman who was struck by a stray bullet in her home in September.Tramonn Gray, 32, of Oakley and Alonzo Dyer, 35, of Oakland were taken into custody last week after evidence related to the shooting was recovered at their homes, police said. The Alameda County District Attorney's Office charged Gray with murder, shooting at an inhabited dwelling, attempted murder and shooting at an occupied motor
