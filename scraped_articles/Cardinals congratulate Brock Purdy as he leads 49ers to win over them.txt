Cardinals congratulate Brock Purdy as he leads 49ers to win over them
-----
It's safe to say that Brock Purdy had a perfect homecoming back to his home state of Arizona for the first time as the 49ers' quarterback. So perfect, in fact, that the home team was congratulating him.Purdy had another stellar day in the Niners' 45-29 win over the Cardinals, completing 16-of-25 passes for 242 yards and four touchdowns.
