Former SFMTA director to run for supervisor seat held by Peskin
-----
Former Municipal Transportation Agency board member Sharon Lai is expected to announce her candidacy Friday for the Board of Supervisors' seat held by Supervisor Aaron Peskin, who is termed out. Lai plans to share her plans at a rally in Chinatown, where she's supported by a number of community leaders.
