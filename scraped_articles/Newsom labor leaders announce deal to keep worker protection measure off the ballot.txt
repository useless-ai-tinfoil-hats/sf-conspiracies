Newsom, labor leaders announce deal to keep worker protection measure off the ballot
-----
Business and labor leaders announced an agreement with Gov. Gavin Newsom on Tuesday to rewrite a unique California law — the Private Attorneys General Act, or PAGA — that allows workers to join one another and sue employers for violating state labor laws.
