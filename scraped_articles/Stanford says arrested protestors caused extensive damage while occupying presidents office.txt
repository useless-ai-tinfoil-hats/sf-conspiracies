Stanford says arrested protestors caused ‘extensive damage’ while occupying president’s office
-----
Dozens of protesters occupied the office of Stanford University's president early Wednesday morning to call attention to the ongoing war in Gaza.Stanford said that 13 people were arrested and that there was "extensive damage" to the interior and exterior of the building that was occupied. The Stanford Daily reported that one of its journalists was among those arrested. University officials did not immediately elaborate about the extent of the damage.
