East Bay park worker stabbed to death — discovered by wife when he didn’t return from shift
-----
An employee of a Pleasant Hill park was found stabbed to death Saturday morning, according to the city's Police Department.Officers responded to a report of an unconscious man lying on the ground of the main parking lot of Pleasant Hill Park at about 4:50 a.m. and determined that he had died of stab wounds, police said.
