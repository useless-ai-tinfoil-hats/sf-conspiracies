‘Extremely tragic’: 14-year-old California boy accused of killing parents, injuring sister
-----
Fresno County detectives have arrested a 14-year-old boy who they say killed his parents at their home.The boy's parents were identified as father Lue Yang and mother Se Vang, both 37 years old. His 11-year-old sister was also severely injured but has undergone surgery and is expected to survive, according to a statement from the Fresno County Sheriff's Office.
The sheriff's office said the boy, whose identity is not being released because he is a minor, called police Wednesday
