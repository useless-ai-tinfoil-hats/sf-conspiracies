Are Californians over Black Friday shopping?
-----
Before our lives became so deeply digital, Black Friday deals were primarily found in stores and — for some dedicated shoppers — worth lining up for, hours before dawn. And while Californians will still head into stores to shop today, Black Friday shopping is increasingly done online.
