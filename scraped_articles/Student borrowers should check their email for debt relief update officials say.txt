Student borrowers should check their email for debt relief update, officials say
-----
Folks with at least one outstanding student loan should check their email this week, officials said.The Biden administration announced Wednesday that emails are going out from the U.S. Department of Education to update borrowers on the status of loan forgiveness and advise them that they have until Aug. 30, to notify their loan servicer if they intend to opt out of the program.
