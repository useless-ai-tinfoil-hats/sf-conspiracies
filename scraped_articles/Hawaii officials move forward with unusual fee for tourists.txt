Hawaii officials move forward with unusual fee for tourists
-----
Hawaii visitors are one step closer to paying a tourist fee for traveling to the islands.Over the past two weeks, two Hawaii House committees heard testimonies regarding a bill that would impose a $25 tourism tax on visitors that the state would use to address the effects of climate change on the islands. Both committees overwhelmingly supported the bill.
