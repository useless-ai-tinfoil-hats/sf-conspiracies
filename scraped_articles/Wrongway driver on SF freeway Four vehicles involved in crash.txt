Wrong-way driver on SF freeway: Four vehicles involved in crash
-----
Four cars were involved in a collision on a San Francisco freeway Monday afternoon, officials said.The crash occurred on southbound Interstate 280 at the Geneva Ave. onramp, Lt. Mariano Elias, a spokesperson for the San Francisco Fire Department told SFGATE. According to the California Highway Patrol traffic incident report, the collision, which happened at 2:31 p.m., was a result of a wrong-way driver.
The collision, which Elias said happened at "high speed," involved five people.
