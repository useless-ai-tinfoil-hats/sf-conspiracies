San Francisco Fleet Week faces cancellation as government shutdown looms
-----
With a government shutdown looking more likely by the day, it seems very possible that San Francisco Fleet Week, the city's annual armed forces celebration, will be canceled. There is precedent for such a situation: Fleet Week was canceled almost exactly 10 years ago due to the 2013 government shutdown.While organizers are continuing to work on plans for the 2023 show as normal, they have been honest about the fluidity of the situation.
