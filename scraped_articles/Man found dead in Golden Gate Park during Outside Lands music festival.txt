Man found dead in Golden Gate Park during Outside Lands music festival
-----
A man was found dead in Golden Gate Park during the busy Outside Lands music festival on Sunday, police said. It's the second year in a row that a dead body was discovered in the park during Outside Lands.According to San Francisco police, officers were called to Nancy Pelosi Drive, about 1.5 miles from the festival perimeter, to conduct a death investigation around 1:15 p.m. Sunday.
