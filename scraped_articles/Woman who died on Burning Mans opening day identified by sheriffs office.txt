Woman who died on Burning Man's opening day identified by sheriff's office
-----
The opening day for Burning Man 2024 began in tragedy as authorities investigate the death of a female participant.Burning Man's emergency services personnel responded to "a report of an unresponsive individual" on Sunday at 11:29 a.m., event officials wrote in a statement. They performed life-saving measures but were unsuccessful. The Pershing County Sheriff's Office was notified immediately, and deputies were dispatched to the scene.
