These SF streets are scheduled to be closed this weekend for the Juneteenth Parade
-----
Several San Francisco streets are slated to be closed this weekend for the second annual Juneteenth Parade & Festival.The parade, featuring floats from schools, community organizations and corporate sponsors, is scheduled to begin at Market and Spear streets at 11 a.m. and end at United Nations Plaza between 7th and 8th streets, the San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency said. The subsequent festival is scheduled to be held at Fulton Plaza from noon to 6 p.m.
