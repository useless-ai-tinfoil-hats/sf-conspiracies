California crime ‘ringmaster’ had team of thieves steal makeup from these Bay Area stores, AG says
-----
An alleged San Diego crime ring selling stolen goods online stretched into Northern California, where suspected thieves targeted beauty supply stores in Alameda, Contra Costa, Napa and Marin counties.Michelle Mack, her husband and eight accomplices are accused of organized retail theft for conspiring to steal and resell an estimated $8 million worth of beauty products from Ulta Beauty and Sephora, the California Attorney General's Office said.
