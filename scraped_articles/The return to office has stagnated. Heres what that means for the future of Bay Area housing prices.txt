The return to office has stagnated. Here’s what that means for the future of Bay Area housing prices
-----
Bay Area offices aren't getting any fuller.While record-high vacancies are already causing San Francisco's office prices to drop, they're also reshaping where workers look for homes — and the cost of buying one.
Workers in the San Francisco metropolitan area are going into their offices at less than half the rate they were before the pandemic, according to data the city collects from security company Kastle Systems. That's virtually unchanged from the rate a year ago.
