Two students injured in stabbing at Hayward High School
-----
Two students were taken to a hospital after a stabbing at Hayward High School on Monday, according to the school district.Three students were involved in an altercation on the school's campus around 1 p.m. Monday, when one of the students pulled out a small knife and injured the other two.
