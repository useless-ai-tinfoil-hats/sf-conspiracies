S.F. will see a ‘major increase’ in police patrols at Union Square this holiday season
-----
San Francisco's Union Square will have more police officers than ever this holiday shopping season as part of the city's effort to deter retail crime in the commercial corridor, officials say.The San Francisco Police Department has made a "major increase" in the number of uniformed officers patrolling the area around the clock, said Police Department spokesperson Evan Sernoffsky, as well as a boost in the number of undercover officers, though he declined to give exact numbers for either.
