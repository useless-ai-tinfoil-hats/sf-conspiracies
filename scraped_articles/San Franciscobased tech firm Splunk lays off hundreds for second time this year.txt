San Francisco-based tech firm Splunk lays off hundreds for second time this year
-----
Splunk, a large San Francisco-based tech firm in the midst of a sale to Cisco, is laying off hundreds of workers for the second time this year.The firm announced the layoff in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission dated Tuesday.
