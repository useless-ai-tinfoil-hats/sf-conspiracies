Bay Area brawl: Hospitalized man arrested, victim identified in fatal shooting
-----
A man who was stabbed during a large fight at a Lake Berryessa picnic ground over the weekend was arrested on suspicion of fatally shooting another man during the chaotic brawl, according to the Napa County Sheriff's Office, which identified the shooting victim.The identity of the suspect was not immediately released "for facility security and the security of the detectives detaining the person," the sheriff's office said in a Facebook post Sunday afternoon.
