Review: Marga Gomez’s ‘Swimming With Lesbians’ is still finding its sea legs
-----
It's a little wobbly aboard the Celesbian, the lesbian cruise ship paddling toward the Isle of Lesbos. As the valiant crew and wayward passengers keep colliding on the "lezzo deck" and accidentally brushing each other's boobs in the slow, tight elevator, you might feel some character development leaks, some narrative squalls.But with a few patches and some corrections at the steering wheel, "Swimming With Lesbians" is bound to be a seaworthy vessel.
