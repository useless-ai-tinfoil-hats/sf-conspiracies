SF's beloved church-turned-roller rink could survive new development plans
-----
Developers are moving forward with a new proposal to convert the building that currently hosts San Francisco's iconic Church of 8 Wheels roller disco to housing and an events space.Newly filed plans for the former church at 554 Fillmore St. show how the building could evolve in the coming year. In a welcome twist, the developments may not require the permanent ousting of the beloved skate parties, according to Church of 8 Wheels founder Rev. David Miles Jr.
