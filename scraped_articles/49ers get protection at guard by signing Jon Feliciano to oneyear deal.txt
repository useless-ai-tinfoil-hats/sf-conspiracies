49ers get protection at guard by signing Jon Feliciano to one-year deal
-----
Jon Feliciano, a nine-year veteran who solidified the right-guard position last season after replacing shaky second-year player Spencer Burford, signed a one-year contract Friday to return to the San Francisco 49ers.Feliciano, 32, indicated on social media that he planned to retire after the 2024 season: "Last ride," he wrote, "#Year 10."
