3.8 quake rattles East Bay
-----
An earthquake of magnitude 3.8 has shaken eastern Contra Costa County. It was centered in Discovery Bay, according to preliminary data reported by the U.S. Geological Survey.The quake struck at 6:21 p.m. Wednesday. People reported feeling the quake in areas stretching from Fremont to Fairfield and Stockton to San Leandro, according to the U.S.G.S.
