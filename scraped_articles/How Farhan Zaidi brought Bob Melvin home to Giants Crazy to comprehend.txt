How Farhan Zaidi brought Bob Melvin home to Giants: ‘Crazy to comprehend’
-----
Not long after it became clear Farhan Zaidi would fire Gabe Kapler with three games remaining in the San Francisco Giants' season, the president of baseball operations pivoted to Bob Melvin.Even though Melvin was under contract for one more year with the San Diego Padres.
"We were going to let that play out," Zaidi said Wednesday after a news conference to introduce Melvin as the Giants' manager. "We thought, 'Well, if we had a chance to bring Bob here, he'd be the perfect guy.'
