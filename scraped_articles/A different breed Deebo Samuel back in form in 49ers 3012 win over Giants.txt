'A different breed': Deebo Samuel back in form in 49ers' 30-12 win over Giants
-----
Well, Deebo Samuel was certainly anti-awful Thursday night, wasn't he?The San Francisco 49ers' wide receiver who was disgusted by his 2022 performance — he described it as "awful" in June — provided overwhelming evidence that he's back to his tackle-busting, All-Pro form during a 30-12 win over the Giants at Levi's Stadium.
Samuel had six receptions for 129 yards and provided the nail-in-the-coffin, 27-yard touchdown grab from quarterback Brock Purdy to provide the final points
