Cyclist found fatally injured on Napa road, officials say
-----
A bicyclist was found fatally injured on a Napa road Tuesday night, according to city officials.A driver spotted the 45-year-old man around 8:40 p.m. on the ground with serious injuries, officials said. The man was with his bike on the south side of Wine Country Avenue between Dumas Street and Emperor Way.
Napa Fire Department and American Medical Response personnel arrived at the scene and took the cyclist to Queen of the Valley Medical Center, where he later died, officials said.
