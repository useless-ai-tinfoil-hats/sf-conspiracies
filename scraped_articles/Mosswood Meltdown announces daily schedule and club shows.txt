Mosswood Meltdown announces daily schedule and club shows
-----
Mosswood Meltdown, the annual Oakland punk festival hosted by "Hairspray" filmmaker John Waters, announced its daily schedule and pre-party and after-party club shows.The two-day festival, which began in 2009 under the name Burger Boogaloo, takes place each year on Fourth of July weekend in Oakland's Mosswood Park.
