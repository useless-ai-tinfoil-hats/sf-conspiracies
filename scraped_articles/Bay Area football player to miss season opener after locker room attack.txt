Bay Area football player to miss season opener after locker room attack
-----
A Dublin high schooler is expected to miss his football team's first game of the season after four teenagers attacked the student in the school's locker room.The attack happened last Friday. Dublin police say that the football player was chased into the locker room, and subsequently attacked, after the parent of another Dublin high schooler confronted him.
