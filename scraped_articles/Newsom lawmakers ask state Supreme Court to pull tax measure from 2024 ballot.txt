Newsom, lawmakers ask state Supreme Court to pull tax measure from 2024 ballot
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom and legislative leaders are asking the state Supreme Court to remove a measure from the November 2024 ballot that would require voter approval for any increase in state or local taxes.The initiative, a state constitutional amendment supported by business groups, has collected enough signatures to qualify for the ballot.
