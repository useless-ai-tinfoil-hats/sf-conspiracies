What will replace historic Fisherman’s Wharf restaurants that closed? The search has begun
-----
Alioto's Restaurant was born at Fisherman's Wharf in 1925 as a fish stand run by a family of Italian immigrants. The business later matured in the first building ever to be constructed in the neighborhood that is now recognizable around the world.But Alioto's shut its doors for good in 2020 after nearly a century serving locals and visitors.
