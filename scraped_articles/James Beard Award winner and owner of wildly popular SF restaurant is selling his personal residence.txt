James Beard Award winner and owner of wildly popular SF restaurant is selling his personal residence
-----
In Lower Pacific Heights, an award-winning chef and the creator of one of San Francisco's — and now the world's — most iconic restaurant concepts is selling his personal residence. With an asking price of $5.5 million, it's a restaurant lover's dream house, not only because it's equipped with an actual "chef's kitchen" but because it comes with its own successful restaurant on the bottom floor.Chef Charles Phan, who started the Slanted Door in San Francisco, has lived at 2232 Bush St.
