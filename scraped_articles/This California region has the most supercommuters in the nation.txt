This California region has the most supercommuters in the nation
-----
The share of supercommuters — people who travel more than 90 minutes to get to work — is rising across the nation, but it may be rising fastest in California.The bottom half of the state is spending the longest time on the road. A new study analyzing U.S. Census data, released by Apartment List, found that Southern California had more than 300,000 supercommuters in 2022 — more than any other region in the country.
