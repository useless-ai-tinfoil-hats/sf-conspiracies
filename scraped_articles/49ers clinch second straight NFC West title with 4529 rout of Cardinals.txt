49ers clinch second straight NFC West title with 45-29 rout of Cardinals
-----
GLENDALE, Ariz. — Late in the second quarter Sunday, Brock Purdy was being examined in the medical tent and Christian McCaffrey was about 10 yards away on the sideline, grabbing at his right knee.Uh-oh? Um, no.
The San Francisco 49ers' apparent nightmare — injuries to their two NFL MVP candidates on offense — lasted only a few season-flashing-before-their-eyes minutes in a 45-29 win over the Cardinals at State Farm Stadium that clinched a second straight NFC West title.
