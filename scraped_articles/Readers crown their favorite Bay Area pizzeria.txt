Readers crown their favorite Bay Area pizzeria
-----
The Cheese Board Collective Pizzeria's thin-crust, seasonal vegetarian pizza is the best in the Bay Area — at least, according to a survey of Chronicle readers.Last week, the New York Times published a list of 22 top pizzerias around the country, and one local spot made the list: Berkeley's Rose Pizzeria.
