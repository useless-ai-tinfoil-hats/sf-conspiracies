College stars Bay FC could select with No. 2 overall pick in the NWSL draft
-----
Bay FC will have a sense of what kind of team they want to be by the time they are selecting players out of college in January. Though a date is not set, the expansion draft is expected to be in December before the NWSL holds its collegiate draft, traditionally held in January.The new San Jose-based club will have the second overall selection in the collegiate draft, after the fellow expansion Utah Royals.
