A seafood shack opens in Wine Country from the owners of a top Sonoma restaurant
-----
A new restaurant from the owners of Valley Bar + Bottle, Wine Country's most popular natural wine hub and one of Sonoma County's best restaurants, opens Tuesday, Oct. 3.But wine will not be the focus at Sonoma's Valley Swim Club. Instead, this roadside eatery will specialize in classic seafood staples like New England-style rolls ($16) and fried whole belly clams ($20).
