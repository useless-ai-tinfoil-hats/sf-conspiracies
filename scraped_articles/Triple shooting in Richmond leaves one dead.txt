Triple shooting in Richmond leaves one dead
-----
One person was killed and two others were badly wounded during an apparent driveby shooting in Richmond, according to a spokesperson for the city's Police Department.Officers responded to 921 23rd Street after a report of the shooting which occurred at 7:46 p.m. Friday. One of the victims died at the scene, according to Lt. Matt Stonebraker, the department's public information officer. Stonebraker said a second victim may have died at the hospital, but he could not say with certainty.
