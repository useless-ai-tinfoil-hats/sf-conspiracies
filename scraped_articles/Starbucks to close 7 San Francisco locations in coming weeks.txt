Starbucks to close 7 San Francisco locations in coming weeks
-----
Starbucks is shuttering seven of its downtown San Francisco locations over the next several weeks, the company confirmed to SFGATE."Each year as a standard course of business, we evaluate the store portfolio to determine where we can best meet our community and customers' needs. This includes opening new locations, identifying stores in need of investment or renovation, exploring locations where an alternative format is needed and, in some instances, re-evaluating our footprint,"
