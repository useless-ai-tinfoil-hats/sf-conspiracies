Stanford hit-and-run that injured Arab Muslim student investigated as hate crime
-----
The California Highway Patrol is investigating a hit-and-run that injured an Arab Muslim student at Stanford University as a hate crime, the school's Department of Public Safety said.Just before 2 p.m. Friday, police received reports of a pedestrian being stuck at Campus Drive and Ayrshire Farm Lane, near student residences. The student suffered non-life-threatening injuries and is receiving medical care, officials said.
