Center Kevon Looney’s status with Warriors enveloped in uncertainty
-----
Kevon Looney concluded his ninth NBA season Tuesday in his team's 118-94 play-in loss to the Sacramento Kings, his tenure the fourth longest among the Golden State Warriors.The backup big man hopes it continues, knowing he awaits an uncertain summer.
Of the $8 million Looney is owed next season in the final year of his contract, $3 million is guaranteed — making him a potential trade piece or candidate for release before the guarantee date of June 24.
