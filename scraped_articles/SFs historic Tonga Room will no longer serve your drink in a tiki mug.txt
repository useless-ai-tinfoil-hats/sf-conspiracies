SF's historic Tonga Room will no longer serve your drink in a tiki mug
-----
At an establishment as beloved and historic as San Francisco's 79-year-old Tonga Room, even the smallest of alterations can feel like a stab to the heart for longtime patrons. Recently, the tiki bar in the basement of the Fairmont Hotel made some pretty big changes to its cocktail menu, including this whopper: They're no longer serving drinks in ceramic tiki mugs.The Tonga Room made the switch to a new glassware program several months ago for "uniformity purposes," Fairmont Hotel
