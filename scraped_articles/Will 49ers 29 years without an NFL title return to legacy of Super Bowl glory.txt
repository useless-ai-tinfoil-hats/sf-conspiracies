Will 49ers, 29 years without an NFL title, return to legacy of Super Bowl glory?
-----
An entire generation has never seen the San Francisco 49ers win a Super Bowl. It has been 29 years since any member of the organization has hoisted the Lombardi Trophy above his head.An eternity.
But the weight of the past, the achievements of a long-ago dynasty, still hang heavy over the franchise.
