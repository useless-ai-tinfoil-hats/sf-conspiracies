Ryan Gosling got an Oscar nod but Greta Gerwig and Margot Robbie didn’t. Here's why
-----
Let's be very clear: Ryan Gosling absolutely deserves his Academy Award nomination for best supporting actor.As Ken in "Barbie," he gives every line reading the layers of meaning of Russian nesting dolls. On top of the pituitary-gland consciousness of Ken's basic needs — attention, affection and validation from Barbie (Margot Robbie) — Gosling layers a budding awareness in Ken that maybe those desires aren't really very cool, or enough to make a full person.
