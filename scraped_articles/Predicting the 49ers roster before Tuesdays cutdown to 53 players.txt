Predicting the 49ers’ roster before Tuesday’s cutdown to 53 players
-----
Kyle Shanahan is forthcoming by NFL standards, which doesn't necessarily mean he's particularly forthcoming.Still, the head coach of the San Francisco 49ers lacks the paranoia of so many of his peers (see: Harbaugh, Jim) and doesn't act as if revealing basic injury information or some of his thinking about X's and O's will doom his team to an 0-17 season.
