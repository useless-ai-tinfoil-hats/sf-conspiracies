Three co-workers killed Pleasant Hill park custodian, police say
-----
Pleasant Hill police arrested three people Friday in connection with a fatal February stabbing.Santiago Jacobo, 37, of Pittsburg died of stab wounds in Pleasant Hill Park on Feb. 10, police said. He was working a late custodian shift at the park but hadn't returned home, leading his worried wife to check on him there and find him unresponsive in the parking lot, according to police.
