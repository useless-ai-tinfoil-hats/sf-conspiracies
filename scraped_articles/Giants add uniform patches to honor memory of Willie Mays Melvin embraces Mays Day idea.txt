Giants add uniform patches to honor memory of Willie Mays, Melvin embraces Mays Day idea
-----
CHICAGO — Bob Melvin someday would like to see Major League Baseball celebrate a Willie Mays Day, similar to Jackie Robinson Day, which started in 2004."For sure, for sure — I think so," the San Francisco Giants' manager said Wednesday when asked if there should be a day to honor the Hall of Fame center fielder.
Melvin also was quick to say he would let others figure out how to pay proper tribute to the life and career of Mays, who died Tuesday at the age of 93.
