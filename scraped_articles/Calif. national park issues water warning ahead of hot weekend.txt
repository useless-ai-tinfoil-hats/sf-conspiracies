Calif. national park issues water warning ahead of hot weekend
-----
Pinnacles National Park, a hiking and camping spot within easy reach of the San Francisco Bay Area, said Friday that it will not have potable water on its west side throughout the weekend during an inbound heat wave.In a social media post, the park said water on the west side, which is accessible from the west entrance via Highway 146, failed a recent test.
