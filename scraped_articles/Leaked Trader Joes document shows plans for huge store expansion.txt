Leaked Trader Joe's document shows plans for huge store expansion
-----
After opening a new store in San Francisco's Hayes Valley neighborhood this month, Trader Joe's is showing no signs of slowing down.According to a map in a recent Instagram post, a whopping 25 more stores across the country are on the path to opening. Trader Joe's spokesperson Nakia Rhode confirmed the number of forthcoming stores at 24, telling SFGATE in an email that the map posted on Instagram was leaked from a "confidential internal document."
