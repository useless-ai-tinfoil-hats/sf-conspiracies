Overnight closures set for three major Bay Area highways all week
-----
Starting Monday and continuing through Saturday, several overnight ramp and lane closures will affect three highways in Marin County due to an ongoing bridge preservation project.The California Department of Transportation said Monday that it plans to continue the project by replacing old concrete with polyester concrete on parts of Highway 101, state Route 131 and Interstate 580.
Caltrans began its bridge preservation project in October 2023, replacing old concrete bridge decks
