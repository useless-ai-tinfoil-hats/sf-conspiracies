Gov. Gavin Newsom plans to move family back to Bay Area
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom and first partner Jennifer Siebel Newsom plan to move their family back to the Bay Area on a part-time basis.The couple intends to move to Marin, while keeping their Sacramento-area home, "to ensure continuity in their children's education," during the 2024-2025 academic year, Izzy Gardon, a spokesperson for Newsom, told the Chronicle on Monday.
