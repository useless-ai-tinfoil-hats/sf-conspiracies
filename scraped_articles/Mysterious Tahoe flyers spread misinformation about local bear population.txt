Mysterious Tahoe flyers spread misinformation about local bear population
-----
Experts in South Lake Tahoe are concerned about mysterious flyers spreading misinformation about feeding bears.The flyers, which were posted on street poles and along Highway 50 around Lake Tahoe Basin, encourage people to feed bears, which the California Department of Fish and Wildlife said is "false and extremely harmful misinformation that is detrimental for bears" in a social media post Friday morning.
