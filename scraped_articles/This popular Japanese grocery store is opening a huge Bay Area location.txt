This popular Japanese grocery store is opening a huge Bay Area location
-----
A popular Japanese grocery store will debut a huge new location at an East Bay mall next year.Tokyo Central will open at Bay Street Emeryville, marking the chain's first store in the East Bay and second in the Bay Area, according to a press release. It's taking over a brand-new 40,635-square-foot building as the shopping center's anchor grocery tenant.
