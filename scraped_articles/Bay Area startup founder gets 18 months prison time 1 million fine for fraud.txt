Bay Area startup founder gets 18 months prison time, $1 million fine for fraud
-----
Manish Lachwani, co-founder of HeadSpin, was sentenced on Friday to 18 months in prison and a $1 million fine for defrauding investors in his Palo Alto-based software testing startup.Lachwani pleaded guilty to two counts of wire fraud and a count of securities fraud in April 2023, after federal prosecutors accused him of, for years, lying to investors about HeadSpin's finances to raise more money. HeadSpin, founded in 2015, grew to a $1.1 billion valuation by 2020 with over $115 million
