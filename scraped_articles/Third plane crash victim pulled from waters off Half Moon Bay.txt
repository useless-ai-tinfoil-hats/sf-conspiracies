Third plane crash victim pulled from waters off Half Moon Bay
-----
San Mateo County officials said they have pulled a third body out of the ocean in connection with the crash of a small plane that was carrying four people.Two bodies pulled from the ocean off Half Moon Bay earlier this month were identified as Emma "Pearl" Willmer-Shiles, 27, and Cassidy Petit, 26. The identity of the body recovered from the water this weekend has not yet been released.
