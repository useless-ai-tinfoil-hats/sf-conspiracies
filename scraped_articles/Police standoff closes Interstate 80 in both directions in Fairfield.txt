Police standoff closes Interstate 80 in both directions in Fairfield
-----
Both directions of Interstate 80 were closed in Fairfield on Friday as authorities engaged in a standoff with a "potentially armed suspect," the California Highway Patrol said.The freeway was closed between Green Valley Road and Interstate 680 interchange just after 4 p.m., according to the CHP. Authorities did not immediately provide an estimated time of reopening.
An alert from 511.org advised motorists to expect delays and use alternate routes.
