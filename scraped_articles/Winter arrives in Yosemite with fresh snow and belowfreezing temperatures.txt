Winter arrives in Yosemite with fresh snow and below-freezing temperatures
-----
Yosemite National Park was in the grips of winter on Monday with fresh snow coating the valley and surrounding granite peaks. More snow is expected this week, along with frigid temperatures dropping below freezing.The overnight temperature in the valley got down to 13 degrees Sunday night into Monday, according to the National Weather Service.
