‘We’re in a street crisis’: S.F. records 10th pedestrian death this year
-----
A Muni stop on Mission Street turned into a memorial Thursday for a man killed in a hit-and-run, the 10th pedestrian death in San Francisco this year.Street safety advocates with the nonprofit Walk San Francisco affixed a sign to the utility pole marking the bus stop to call attention to the death of a pedestrian that occurred early Monday morning, and urged action to prevent more deaths.
