S.F. medical examiner IDs victim in Dolores Park shooting
-----
The 19-year-old man fatally shot at Dolores Park this week has been identified as a Lowell High School graduate.The Medical Examiner's Office on Friday identified Luis Manuel Arguello-Inglis as the victim. He was a graduate of Lowell High and freshman at San Jose State University, SF Gate reported.
San Francisco police arrived at Dolores Park in the Mission District shortly after 10 p.m. on Wednesday and found Arguello-Inglis on the ground with gunshot wounds.
