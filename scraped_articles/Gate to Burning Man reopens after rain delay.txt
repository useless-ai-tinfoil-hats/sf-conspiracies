Gate to Burning Man reopens after rain delay
-----
LATEST Aug. 24, 3:20 p.m. The Gate to Burning Man has reopened as of 3:15 p.m. Saturday, according to an update from event organizers.The Burner Express Bus has also "resumed normal operations" and the Black Rock Airport is open again, organizers said.
"All San Francisco buses are en route and will proceed directly to Black Rock City," the update on X said. "Reno buses have begun loading and are on a two-hour delay currently."
