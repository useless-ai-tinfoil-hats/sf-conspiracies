Willie Mays ‘fan for life’: Chronicle readers share stories of how the MLB great changed lives
-----
Willie Mays, the legendary "Say Hey Kid" known for his athleticism and style, passed away Tuesday at 93. The iconic center fielder was synonymous with baseball, the Giants and San Francisco, where his statue stands in front of Oracle Park.Since the news of his passing, San Francisco Chronicle readers have been sharing their stories of seeing Willie Mays play, what his incredible life and career meant to them, and how it connected them to the city.
