Jury convicts man of murder in beheading of his partner in San Carlos
-----
Jose Rafael Solano Landaeta, the man accused of brutally beheading the mother of his child with a samurai sword in San Carlos last year, was found guilty of first-degree murder and an additional allegation of using a sword on Monday.The jury verdict was read after prosecutors for the San Mateo District Attorney's Office and Solano Landaeta's defense attorneys gave their closing statements on Friday.
