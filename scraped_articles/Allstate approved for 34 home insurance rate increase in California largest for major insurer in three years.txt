Allstate approved for 34% home insurance rate increase in California, largest for major insurer in three years
-----
Allstate has been approved for the largest rate increase in California of any major insurer in the past three years.This month, the Department of Insurance approved Allstate's request to hike its home insurance rates by an average of 34.1% for its roughly 350,000 California customers. Within that average, some homeowners could see their rates decrease as much as 57%, while at least one will see rates increase by nearly 650%.
