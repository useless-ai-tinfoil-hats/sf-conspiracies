Here's what's next for People's Park in Berkeley
-----
UC Berkeley took over People's Park early Thursday, with police and work crews clearing all people out the famous and controversial site and stacking shipping containers around the perimeter.Protesters against the university's plans to develop the park with student housing were met by police, and seven people were arrested before being released, the university said. This all mostly happened before sunrise and at a time when UC Berkeley students are on winter break.
