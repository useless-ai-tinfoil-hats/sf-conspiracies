Astonishingly high waves batter the iconic Lone Cypress
-----
One of America's most beautiful trees got a little more photogenic when huge waves crashed into the shoreline and cliffs along the Central Coast.Late last week, the Lone Cypress was doused by the powerful surf kicked up by a winter storm. The National Weather Service warned that waves between 28 and 33 feet were forecast along the coast. The scene at Pebble Beach's 17-Mile Drive was captured by several onlookers, including photographer Colette English.
