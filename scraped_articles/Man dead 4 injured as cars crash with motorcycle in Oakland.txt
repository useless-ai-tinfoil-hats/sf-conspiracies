Man dead, 4 injured as cars crash with motorcycle in Oakland
-----
A motorcyclist died and four others were injured in a crash that occurred Wednesday afternoon in Oakland's Fruitvale district, authorities said.The wreck happened just before 3:15 p.m. on the 3700 block of International Boulevard, according to Oakland police spokesperson Rosalia Lopez. A motorcycle collided with multiple vehicles, sending the motorcyclist and four people to the hospital, Lopez said.
