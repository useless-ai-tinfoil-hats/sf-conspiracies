This Disneyland employee has one of the park's tastiest jobs
-----
"Just overlap your fingers," Disneyland photographer David Nguyen said, "so it already looks like a heart." The person he's directing is holding the carnitas-style pork belly from the LA Style kitchen from the Disney California Adventure Food & Wine Festival."I like having people hold food almost like a heart shape," he explained, "so everyone is already in love with the food."
