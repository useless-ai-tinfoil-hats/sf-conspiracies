Berkeley home best known for hosting decades of hot tub parties is for sale
-----
A notorious Berkeley home best known for hosting decades of hot tub parties just hit the market.Former owner Deward Hastings, who famously welcomed the public to his backyard hot tub, died in September 2022 doing what he loved best, hot tubbing, officials said at the time. Now, the administrator of his estate has put the bungalow at 2133 Essex St. up for sale for $899,000.
