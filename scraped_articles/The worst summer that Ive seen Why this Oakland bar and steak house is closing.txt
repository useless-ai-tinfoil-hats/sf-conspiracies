‘The worst summer that I’ve seen’: Why this Oakland bar and steak house is closing
-----
A popular, tropical-themed restaurant in Uptown Oakland and its adjoining bar will close at the end of the month.Christ Aivaliotis and Matt Reagan, owners of Palmetto and the Kon-Tiki Room, announced that after three years of steak-house inspired food and rum cocktails, their last day of business will be Oct. 29. The business partners told the Chronicle revenue has taken a dive this summer, with an average 30% year-over-year dip, and some days with sales down 50%.
