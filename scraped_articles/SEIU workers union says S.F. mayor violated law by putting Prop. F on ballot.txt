SEIU workers union says S.F. mayor violated law by putting Prop. F on ballot
-----
Attorneys representing a public employees' union in San Francisco say Mayor London Breed violated the law by placing Proposition F on the March ballot because she did not meet with the union first to discuss impacts to workers.In a letter Tuesday to the city attorney's office, attorneys for Service Employees International Union Local 1021 argued that Prop. F would negatively impact employee work conditions and workload.
Prop. F, introduced by Breed, would mandate drug screenings
