Stolen-car driver crashes, dies after fleeing law enforcement in Contra Costa County
-----
A driver was killed Monday in Contra Costa County while fleeing police in what officers said was a stolen vehicle.The Contra Costa County Sheriff's Office stopped chasing the fleeing driver about three minutes before the driver's car crashed on Cummings Skyway near Crockett early Monday, according to California Highway Patrol. Officers arriving on scene determined the car "had left the roadway and overturned," ejecting the driver, CHP said.
