'Going into the city hot': Popular Bay Area bagel shop finally expanding to SF with 3 locations
-----
Boichik Bagels lovers in San Francisco, your time has finally come: The extremely popular, New York Times-hyped Berkeley bagels are coming to the city. Three Boichik Bagels shops are slated to open, at 1946 Fillmore St. in Lower Pacific Heights, 22 Battery St. in the Financial District and 3665 Sacramento St. in Laurel Heights, as first reported by the San Francisco Business Times.Owner Emily Winston has been busy expanding lately, with new shops opening in Santa Clara and Larkspur late
