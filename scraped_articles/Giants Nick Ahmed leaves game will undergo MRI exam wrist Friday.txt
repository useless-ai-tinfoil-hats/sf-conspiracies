Giants’ Nick Ahmed leaves game, will undergo MRI exam wrist Friday
-----
DENVER — San Francisco Giants shortstop Nick Ahmed exited Thursday's loss to the Colorado Rockies in the fourth inning with left wrist discomfort and was scheduled to undergo an MRI exam Friday to determine the extent of the injury, Ahmed said.Ahmed, 34, initially was hurt April 27 when he took a 108-mph line drive off the wrist in a game against the Pittsburgh Pirates. He has been dealing with pain off-and-on since then, though it has felt worse over the past few days, he said.
