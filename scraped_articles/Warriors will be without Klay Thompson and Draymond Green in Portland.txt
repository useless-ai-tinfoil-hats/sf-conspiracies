Warriors will be without Klay Thompson and Draymond Green in Portland
-----
PORTLAND, Ore. — The Golden State Warriors were without forward Draymond Green and guard Klay Thompson in Thursday night's regular-season road finale against the Trail Blazers at Moda Center.Questionable on Wednesday with a right knee contusion, Green was ruled out Thursday afternoon — as was Thompson, who emerged questionable Thursday morning with right knee tendinitis.
Backup guard Gary Payton II also was a late scratch with tightness in his left calf.
