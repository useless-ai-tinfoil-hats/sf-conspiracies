Search ensues for woman swept away by SoCal river on popular hiking trail
-----
Los Angeles County officials are looking for a 59-year-old woman who was swept away by a river in a popular hiking area of the San Gabriel Mountains.The woman was hiking on the Heaton Flats Trail on Saturday at 9:51 a.m., trying to cross a river, the Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department said on Monday in a social media post. She then lost her footing and was swept away by strong currents, the department said.
