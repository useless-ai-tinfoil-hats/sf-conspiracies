Nearly 2,500 Bay Area hotel workers strike at seven hotels for in biggest walkout since 2018
-----
Nearly 2,500 hotel workers at four of downtown San Francisco's largest hotels, two in San Jose and one by San Francisco International Airport went on strike starting Sunday in the city's biggest walkout since 2018.In San Francisco, 2,080 Unite Here Local 2 union members are seeking better wages and benefits through the three-day strike after labor contracts expired in August and the Bay Area's cost of living has continued to soar. But the city's hotel occupancy remains below 2019 levels
