Jill Biden ate at small seafood restaurant while visiting SF
-----
First lady Jill Biden dined at small Marina District restaurant Popi's Oysterette while visiting San Francisco on Wednesday, according to the San Francisco Chronicle (the Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms)."What a wonderful surprise to serve @flotus yesterday," read an Instagram post from the restaurant on Thursday. "Thank you for stopping by Popi's Oysterette and supporting small business."
