As bird flu devastates Bay Area farms, egg prices could start to spike
-----
An outbreak of avian flu is hitting the California poultry industry hard. Since the outbreak first reached California in October, it's killed nearly 5.5 million birds across the state and 1.15 million birds in Sonoma County alone, according to the U.S. Department of Agriculture.
