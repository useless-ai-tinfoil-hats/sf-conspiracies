Couple plucked from California desert in dramatic rescue
-----
A team of first responders plucked two exhausted hikers from the desert floor in California's Painted Canyon southeast of Palm Springs in a dramatic helicopter hoist that was shared on Instagram.The Riverside County Sheriff's Office said it was dispatched to the area just north of the Salton Sea and south of Joshua Tree National Park before 5:43 p.m. after a man called 911.
