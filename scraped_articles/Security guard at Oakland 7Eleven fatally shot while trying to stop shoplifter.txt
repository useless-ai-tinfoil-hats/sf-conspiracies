Security guard at Oakland 7-Eleven fatally shot while trying to stop shoplifter
-----
A security guard at a convenience store near Oakland's Lake Merritt was fatally shot while trying to stop a shoplifter Friday night, police said.Officers responded to a shooting on the 2300 block of Harrison Street shortly before 10:15 p.m. Friday, the Police Department said in a statement Saturday. Media reports and the Citizen app identified the store as a 7-Eleven.
