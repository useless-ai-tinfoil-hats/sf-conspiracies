Hawaii may get interisland ferry service between three islands
-----
It may soon become easier to travel between the Hawaiian islands of Maui, Molokai and Lanai. The County of Maui, which includes all three islands, is exploring the idea of launching its own interisland ferry service.Operating like a bus system, the ferry would offer Lanai and Molokai residents a reliable transportation option to and from Maui.
