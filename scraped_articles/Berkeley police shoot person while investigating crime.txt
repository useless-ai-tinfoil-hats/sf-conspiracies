Berkeley police shoot person while investigating crime
-----
Berkeley police shot and injured a person while investigating a crime on Monday morning, officials said.The Berkeley Police Department said in a statement that it was responding to a "crime in progress" at Seventh and Grayson streets near Aquatic Park around 5:26 a.m. when an officer shot someone. One person was rushed to Highland Hospital in critical condition, police said.
