The only thing for sale at Burning Man
-----
For one week every year, Burning Man turns a stretch of Nevada's Black Rock Desert into a utopian city. Streets are lined with massive, surreal artworks; citizens conduct themselves according to principles like "radical inclusion" and "leaving no trace"; the economy is built entirely around gifting, with Burners giving everything from beers to airplane rides away for free.Crucial to this utopian vision is the festival's principle of decommodification.
