Cal Fire releases image of person suspected of starting blaze at South Bay college campus
-----
Cal Fire and San Jose police officials said Tuesday that they were seeking a person suspected of starting a fire on San Jose State University's campus last month who was captured on security camera footage.The fire was set on the afternoon of March 31, officials said. Cal Fire-Office of the State Fire Marshal's Arson and Bomb Unit asked the public for help in identifying and locating the suspect.
