Chiefs star to appear in Disneyland Super Bowl parade Monday
-----
The Kansas City Chiefs' MVP is once again going to Disneyland.With their overtime win over the San Francisco 49ers in Sunday's Super Bowl, the tradition of visiting Disneyland falls once again to Chiefs quarterback Patrick Mahomes. In a postgame appearance on NFL Network, Mahomes confirmed he'll be in Disneyland on Monday morning. "I got to go to Disneyland at like 8 in the morning," he said. "Dude, there might not be any sleep."
