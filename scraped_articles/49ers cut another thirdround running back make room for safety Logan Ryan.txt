49ers cut another third-round running back, make room for safety Logan Ryan
-----
The San Francisco 49ers signed safety Logan Ryan on Tuesday and released running back Ty Davis-Price, a 2022 third-round pick who appeared in just one game this season.The 49ers added Ryan, 32, a 10-year veteran who won two Super Bowls with the Patriots during his 121-start career, to provide secondary depth after starting safety Talanoa Hufanga and backup safety George Odum suffered season-ending injuries last month.
