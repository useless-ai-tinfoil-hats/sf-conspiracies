Oakland police officer killed while on-duty near Jack London Square
-----
An Oakland police officer was killed in the line of duty near Jack London Square on Friday, the Police Department said.Few details were immediately available about the incident, which occurred on Embarcadero near Fifth Avenue, the department said on X, formerly known as Twitter.
Police officials scheduled a news conference for Friday afternoon to provide further details.
