‘Superhuman feat’: Woman, 55, makes history with swim from Golden Gate Bridge to Farallon Islands
-----
The marathon swim from the Golden Gate Bridge to the Farallon Islands is considered one of the hardest in the world, combining strong currents, fierce wind and frigid water frequented by great white sharks.But UCSF nurse Amy Appelhans Gubser conquered it this weekend — when she got off work, took a quick nap and then swam into history.
