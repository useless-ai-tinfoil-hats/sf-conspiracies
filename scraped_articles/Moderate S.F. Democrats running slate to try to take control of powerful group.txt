Moderate S.F. Democrats running slate to try to take control of powerful group
-----
Moderate San Francisco politicos are throwing their weight behind a new slate of candidates to wrest control of a little known but powerful group that determines who gets coveted party endorsements in future elections.SF Democrats for Change announced Thursday it is running a slate of 24 candidates for the Democratic County Central Committee that includes current Supervisors Matt Dorsey and Catherine Stefani and former Supervisor Michela Alioto-Pier as well as community leaders.
