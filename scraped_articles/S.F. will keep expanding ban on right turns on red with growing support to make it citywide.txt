S.F. will keep expanding ban on right turns on red, with growing support to make it citywide
-----
San Francisco's transportation board held off on deciding this week whether to endorse a citywide "no turn on red" ban that garnered city supervisors' support last year. But several board members said they support such a ban because it would be less confusing and more intuitive for drivers.The San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency is expected to take up the issue again in September.
