How Weird Street Faire postpones 25th anniversary event
-----
San Francisco's annual How Weird Street Faire has been postponed just days before its milestone anniversary celebration due to this weekend's forecasted storm."We are looking at a half inch of rain and 36 mph winds," organizers told the Chronicle via email on Thursday, May 2. "Due to severe weather and unforeseen circumstances, we unfortunately have to postpone the event."
The all-ages event, originally scheduled for Saturday, May 4, would have marked the outdoor festival's 25th
