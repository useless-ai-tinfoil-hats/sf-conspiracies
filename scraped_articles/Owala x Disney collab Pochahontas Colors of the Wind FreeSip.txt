Owala x Disney collab: Pochahontas Colors of the Wind FreeSip
-----
With so many reusable water bottle brands releasing new colorways and collaborations — looking at you Stanley x Calia — it feels like there's an option for every color under the sun. And now, Owala is taking it a step further and bringing us a bottle inspired by all the colors of the wind.The new Pochahontas-themed bottle is a collaboration between Disney and Owala, aptly named Colors of the Wind. It's available now, but act fast if you want one, as these collabs tend to sell out
