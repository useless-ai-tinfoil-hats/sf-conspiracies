San Francisco drivers may soon be banned from turning right on a red light
-----
San Francisco drivers may soon be barred from turning right at red lights.A "no turn on red" resolution was presented at a Board of Supervisors meeting this week by District 5 Supervisor Dean Preston, who represents the Tenderloin, Western Addition, Japantown and Haight-Ashbury neighborhoods.
