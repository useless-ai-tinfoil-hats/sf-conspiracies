Wine enthusiasts eagerly await return of public tasting
-----
Credit: Michael ShortThe San Francisco Chronicle Wine Competition Public Tasting will be from 1:30 to 4:30 p.m. Feb. 17 at Fort Mason Center.
After a three-year hiatus due to COVID-19, the San Francisco Chronicle Wine Competition Public Tasting returns this Presidents Day weekend.
Thousands of wine lovers will gather on Feb. 17 to explore the competition's 2024 award-winning selections at the Fort Mason Center's Festival Pavilion. Many bottles will be poured by the winemakers
