KNBR rehires longtime host for a third stint at the station
-----
For the third time in his career as a local sports radio host, Larry Krueger has been hired by KNBR.Krueger announced that he's rejoining the station in a post on X on Tuesday, saying he'll be helping the station's 49ers coverage as well as being a regular contributor during the week. In a phone call with SFGATE on Tuesday, Krueger said the move was "somewhat surprising" even to him but that he's excited to be back at a station he knows so well.
