Mayor Breed wants a Chinese university to set up in downtown S.F.
-----
Mayor London Breed will embark Saturday on a one-week trip to China, where she will try to secure investments in San Francisco's struggling economy and boost tourism as part of an effort to accelerate the city's slow pandemic recovery.Breed told the Chronicle on Wednesday that she would use the trip to advocate for more direct flights from China, the expansion of a Chinese university to downtown San Francisco and for the country to send pandas to the San Francisco Zoo.
