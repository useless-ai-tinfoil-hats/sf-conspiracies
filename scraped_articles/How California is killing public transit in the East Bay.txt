How California is killing public transit in the East Bay
-----
While the rest of the Bay Area saw its transit ridership increase or remain unchanged during the 2010s, Oakland-based AC Transit stood alone in losing riders. Despite Oakland's population increasing by 10% in the past decade, fewer neighborhoods in Oakland have local bus service than they did 30 years ago.
