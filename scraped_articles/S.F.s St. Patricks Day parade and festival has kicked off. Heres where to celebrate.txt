S.F.’s St. Patrick’s Day parade and festival has kicked off. Here’s where to celebrate
-----
San Francisco's St. Patrick's Day celebrations kicked into full gear Saturday with a parade and festival featuring traditional Irish dancing and music.The parade started at 11:30 a.m. at Second and Market streets and was making its way up Market to Civic Center for the free festival, featuring a mix of entertainers until 6 p.m.
