Eagles’ James Bradberry responds to 49ers’ Deebo Samuel calling him ‘trash’
-----
Philadelphia cornerback James Bradberry took the high road when asked about Deebo Samuel's low blow.On Wednesday, two days after Samuel — a San Francisco 49ers wide receiver — doubled down on his belief that the Eagles cornerback was "trash," Bradberry, 30, didn't fire back when asked about the insult.
"Of course, I don't necessarily like what he said," Bradberry said to Philadelphia-area reporters. "I wish he would have used a better word to describe my play. But it is what it is.
