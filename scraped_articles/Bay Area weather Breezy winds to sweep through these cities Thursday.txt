Bay Area weather: Breezy winds to sweep through these cities Thursday
-----
An area of low pressure will bring breezy winds to the Bay Area on Thursday. This system is expected to brush through high-elevation areas such as Mount Tam, Twin Peaks and the East Bay hills before whisking across the shoreline and downtown San Francisco.Gusts up to 40 mph will be possible in the summits of the mountains of Sonoma and Marin counties as the low pressure hurls northwest breezes toward the coastline Thursday afternoon.
