Man arrested in fatal stabbing of a woman at a San Francisco home
-----
A man was arrested in the fatal stabbing of a woman Wednesday morning in a Holly Park home, according to the San Francisco Police Department.Around 9 a.m. Wednesday, officers arrived at the home on the 100 block of Appleton Avenue in response to a report of a possible burglary, police said. Upon arrival, they found a woman with stab wounds, according to police. Those at the scene rendered aid to the woman as they waited for paramedics, police said.
