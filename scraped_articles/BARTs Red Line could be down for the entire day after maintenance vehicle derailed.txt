BART’s Red Line could be down for the entire day after maintenance vehicle derailed
-----
BART planned to close its Red Line service for a significant portion of Wednesday after a track maintenance vehicle derailed overnight near downtown Oakland's 19th St. Station, officials said.The Red Line service — which runs between Millbrae and Richmond — was expected to be canceled "most of the day and potentially the entire day," BART said on X.
"We will run longer Orange Line trains all day," the agency tweeted.
