Luxury retailer to replace North Face in space at S.F.’s Union Square
-----
The luxury retail brand Bulgari is set to occupy a 9,000-square-foot retail space in San Francisco's Union Square neighborhood previously held by the North Face.The building's landlord, Grosvenor — an international property owner and developer — revealed the new tenant in an announcement Wednesday.
