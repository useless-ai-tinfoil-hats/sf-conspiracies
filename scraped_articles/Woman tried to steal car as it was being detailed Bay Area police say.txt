Woman tried to steal car as it was being detailed, Bay Area police say
-----
A woman was arrested on suspicion of attempting to steal a vehicle as it was being detailed in Vallejo, the Police Department said Sunday.Latrice Billingsley, 40, was arrested Tuesday on suspicion of trying to steal a parked Toyota Sienna minivan that was getting detailed on a driveway of a home on the 100 block of Laurel Street, according to Sgt. Rashad Hollis of the Police Department.
