Sightings of multiple dead whales reported across Bay Area
-----
A dead humpback whale washed up on a San Francisco beach Friday after officials received several reports of other dead whales observed floating in the water last week.Scientists with the California Academy of Sciences performed a necropsy of the 31-foot-long whale, which stranded on Fort Funston and was determined to be a young male that had likely been attacked by an orca based on multiple markings found throughout its body.
