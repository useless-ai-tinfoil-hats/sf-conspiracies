SF Giants star Blake Snell doesn't take well to fan comment on ERA
-----
It hasn't been a great start to Blake Snell's tenure with the San Francisco Giants after the reigning NL Cy Young award winner signed in March — and, turns out, it hasn't been a great start off the field either.On Friday, Snell got into an internet fight with a Yankees fan in — of all things — the replies of a "Sidetalk NYC" video posted after a New York Knicks game. The video shows New Yorkers celebrating the Knicks' win over the Philadelphia 76ers outside of Madison Square Garden.
