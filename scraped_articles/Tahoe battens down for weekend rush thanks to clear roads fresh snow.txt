Tahoe battens down for weekend rush thanks to clear roads, fresh snow
-----
A tumultuous blizzard struck the Sierra last weekend, stranding drivers and blocking the road to Tahoe for days. This week it's a different story and Bay Area travelers can expect an easier effort to drive up to the mountains to enjoy what the blizzard left behind."Even though it's not going to be totally dry, we're not expecting any heavy snowfalls between now and Sunday afternoon," Justin Collins, a meteorologist from the National Weather Service, told SFGATE.
