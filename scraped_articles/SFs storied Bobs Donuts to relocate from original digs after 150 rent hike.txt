SF's storied Bob's Donuts to relocate from original digs after 150% rent hike
-----
San Francisco's beloved 24-hour haunt Bob's Donuts is expected to relocate one block away from its original store at 1621 Polk St. after lease negotiations couldn't be reached between the owner and landlord.After years of "significant rent increases" without "room for negotiation," Bob's Donuts is set to move to the former site of Lotta's Bakery at 1720 Polk St., according to a press release from San Francisco Board of Supervisors President Aaron Peskin's office.
