Not many California downtowns feature an idle F-4 Phantom supersonic jet tilted on a cement perch, but that's exactly how the east end of main street in Lancaster begins.The jet is in Boeing Plaza, at the mouth of a central boulevard in the Southern California city. From there, the road diverts into two slow lanes that straddle a nine-block promenade of shade on the edge of the Mojave Desert.
