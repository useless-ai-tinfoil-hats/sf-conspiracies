Peaches Christ cuts deep at first ‘Show & Tell’ live interview
-----
Peaches Christ handles a knife with the ease of a horror movie villain.Barely batting a spidery eyelash, San Francisco's drag queen of Halloween plunged a serrated blade deep into the flesh of her latest victim. Carving with the ease of Michael Myers, she slashed an ear-to-ear grin on its orange face — all without losing one of her pointed, glittering nails.
The "victim" was, of course, a pumpkin.
