'I have to get off this plane': Flyers confront their fears at SFO
-----
On a recent Saturday morning, San Francisco International Airport seemed busier than ever.Tech workers sprinted toward airport security with their laptops strapped to their carry-ons. Bleary-eyed families trapped in the throes of flight delays sat cross-legged on the carpeted floor, nibbling on bites of stale cereal. Young couples sobbed and kissed like they were never going to see each other again.
They had places to go. Friends to see. An entire globe of possibilities awaited
