Where does Rep. Barbara Lee’s Senate campaign go from here?
-----
Rep. Barbara Lee had the most to gain — and the most to lose — when Gov. Gavin Newsom appointed an interim successor to replace the late Sen. Dianne Feinstein.Lee lost.
And that will make her campaign to win the full six-year Senate term — and become only the third Black woman to ever serve in the Senate — even harder. Her supporters were counting on, and demanding, that Newsom fulfill his pledge to appoint a Black woman to finish the rest of Feinstein's term by tapping Lee.
