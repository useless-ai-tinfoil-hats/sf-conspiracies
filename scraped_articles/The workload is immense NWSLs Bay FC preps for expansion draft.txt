‘The workload is immense’: NWSL’s Bay FC preps for expansion draft
-----
When Bay FC picks first in Friday's NWSL expansion draft, the new team's front office will have about an hour to act on months of planning.The result will be filling the majority of Bay FC's inaugural 23-person roster. The team has already signed two free agents and will have to sign more — and make trades and pick in the collegiate draft in January — to complete the team.
