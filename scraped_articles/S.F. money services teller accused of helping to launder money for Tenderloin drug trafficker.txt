S.F. money services teller accused of helping to launder money for Tenderloin drug trafficker
-----
A former employee of a money services business in San Francisco has been accused of conspiring to launder drug proceeds for a known Tenderloin drug trafficker, federal prosecutors said Wednesday.U.S. attorneys charged Yessenia Margarita Barrientos-Lainez, 42, of Daly City with conspiring to launder drug proceeds for a man known to Drug Enforcement Administration agents as a Bay Area drug trafficker who was arrested in June.
Barrientos-Lainez was arrested on Oct. 26.
