Train derailment spills coal into popular Northern California river
-----
A train carrying coal derailed on Sunday, spilling into a scenic Northern California river outside the town of Blairsden, officials said.The train derailed shortly before 7 a.m. near the Middle Fork of the Feather River in Plumas County because of a "track defect," according to a hazardous materials report from the Governor's Office of Emergency Services.
