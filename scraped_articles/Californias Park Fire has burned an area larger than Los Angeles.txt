California's Park Fire has burned an area larger than Los Angeles
-----
Cooler temperatures and higher humidity levels slowed the growth of California's Park Fire over the weekend, with firefighters gaining 12% containment and some evacuations being lifted, including an evacuation warning for Paradise.But this raging, monstrous inferno, with a 370,237-acre burn area that's larger than Los Angeles, is expected to present new challenges with drier, hotter weather in the forecast.
