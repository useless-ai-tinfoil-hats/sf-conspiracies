S.F. man’s death in suspected shark attack leaves many questions unanswered. Here’s what we know
-----
San Franciscan Felix Louis N'Jai, 52, was with a group who had hiked to Wildcat Beach in Point Reyes National Seashore to celebrate a wedding that friends said had occurred the day before. When he and two friends went into the water, witnesses reported around 10:30 a.m. on Sunday that a shark attacked him. He then disappeared without a trace.Authorities ended the search on Thursday, according to Christine Beekman, a spokesperson for the Point Reyes National Seashore.
