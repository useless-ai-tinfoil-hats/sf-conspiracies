A 1983 ride is closing permanently at Knott's Berry Farm
-----
A Knott's Berry Farm ride dating back to the 1983 opening of Camp Snoopy is shuttering permanently this weekend.The Huff and Puff is a rare theme park ride to enforce a height limit: Riders must be under 52 inches to partake. It's also powered by kid propulsion. Riders hop into a single mine-style cart and push a handle up and down to move the car. The closure makes way for a major renovation to the Camp Snoopy area.
