Free COVID tests by mail are back — here’s how to order them
-----
With COVID-19 hospitalizations and deaths ticking back up over the summer and cases expected to rise further as winter approaches, the federal government has relaunched its program to provide free coronavirus tests to Americans.Starting Sept. 25, each household can order up to four rapid at-home tests through the user-friendly website covidtests.gov. The United States Postal Service will deliver the tests at no cost, with shipments expected to start the week of Oct. 2.
