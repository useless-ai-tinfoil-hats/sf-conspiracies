Uber-popular Santa Cruz brewery to open first SF taproom
-----
One of Santa Cruz's most popular breweries is coming to San Francisco.Humble Sea Brewing Co., based in Santa Cruz, is set to open at Pier 39 sometime in July, according to marketing director Lee DeGraw. There is no exact opening date yet, as construction of the taproom is still underway, but it will be at Pier 39, Space N-111-1A.
