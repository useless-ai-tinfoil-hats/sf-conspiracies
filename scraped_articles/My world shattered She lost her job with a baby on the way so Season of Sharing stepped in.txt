‘My world shattered’: She lost her job with a baby on the way, so Season of Sharing stepped in
-----
When Ina Albert was laid off from her job as a school office manager in 2021, her world fell apart.She lost her work community, income and benefits. The 35-year-old Oakland resident fell into depression, consumed by anxiety and financial stress. She started working as a DoorDash driver to make ends meet. Then, in December 2021, she found out she was pregnant.
