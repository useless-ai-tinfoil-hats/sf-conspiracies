49ers live blog: Christian McCaffrey scores four TDs vs. Cardinals
-----
The undefeated San Francisco 49ers hosted the Arizona Cardinals, who were fresh off an upset of the Dallas Cowboys. Was it a trap game? Not so much, as the 49ers romped to victory behind Christian McCaffrey to achieve a 4-0 start to their season.By game's end, McCaffrey had four touchdowns (three rushing, one receiving), 106 yards gained on the ground (on 20 carries) and 71 yards through the air (on eight targets).
