Body found under 'dirt and debris' by Bay Area BART tracks
-----
A Bay Area man is in custody on suspicion of murder after police found a body hidden in the dirt near the Hayward BART yard.On the evening of April 26, multiple witnesses saw two men fighting near San Antonio and Hayman streets. One of the men allegedly pulled a gun on the other, shooting and killing him. Although the witnesses thought they'd just seen a homicide, they didn't call police for two more days.
