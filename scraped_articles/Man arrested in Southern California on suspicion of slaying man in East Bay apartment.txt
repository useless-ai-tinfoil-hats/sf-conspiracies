Man arrested in Southern California on suspicion of slaying man in East Bay apartment
-----
A man was arrested in Barstow (San Bernardino County) on suspicion of shooting and killing a man in Dublin, police said Monday.Dublin police officers responded to the Emerald Park Apartments at 5050 Hacienda Drive for a reported shooting on Friday at 2:21 p.m. Officers found a man, 37, with a gunshot wound to his head who died at the scene according to police. Police searched the area but did not find the suspect, according to a news release.
