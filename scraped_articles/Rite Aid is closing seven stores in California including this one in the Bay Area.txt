Rite Aid is closing seven stores in California, including this one in the Bay Area
-----
Rite Aid is reportedly closing an additional seven stores in California — including one in the Bay Area — as part of a financial restructuring plan, according to reports.Bankruptcy court filings in November revealed that the national pharmacy company plans to shut 31 stores in a dozen states, including the 1350 North Vasco Road location in Livermore, according to CNN Business and the East Bay Times. Several stores remain in the Bay Area region.
