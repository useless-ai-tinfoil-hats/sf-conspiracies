'Long Sunday on social media': 49ers fans blast NFL Network host
-----
The San Francisco 49ers' shellacking of the Jaguars on Sunday didn't just cause most Jags fans to leave in the third quarter. It had some NFL pundits realizing just how far off they were in their hot takes over the past two weeks.Perhaps none bore the brunt of the social media firestorm harder than Kyle Brandt from NFL Network's "Good Morning Football," forcing the analyst to put his tail between his legs all of three days after he ranted about the Jags and the 49ers.
