Oakland, soccer teams move closer to agreement over stadium site near Coliseum
-----
Oakland could soon enter into talks with two soccer teams to build a stadium near the Coliseum.The City Council will vote Sept. 19 on whether to allow Oakland to negotiate exclusively with the Roots and Soul soccer teams and Alameda County for a 10-year lease to host up to 10,000 fans on about 8.8 acres of a triangular shaped property at 8000 South Coliseum Way, known as the Malibu Property.
