S.F.'s Section 8 housing wait list is open for first time in 10 years. Thousands rush to apply
-----
More than 40,000 people have already thrown their hat in the ring to get a spot on a wait list for federal rental assistance in San Francisco, an indication of how many people are desperate for housing help in one of the nation's most expensive markets.Even if someone snags one of the 6,500 spots that are opening up on the wait list, they could still face a long wait for a rental voucher.
