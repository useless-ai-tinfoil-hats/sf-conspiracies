Group steals ATM from Mission District bar, crashes into patrol car
-----
A group stole an ATM from a bar in the Mission District and then crashed into a patrol vehicle as they were fleeing, according to San Francisco police and news reports.Just after 3:15 a.m. Monday, San Francisco police said they received a report that someone had broken into a business in the 1400 block of Valencia Street. At the scene, the officers saw two people exiting the business and getting into a car where a third person was already waiting, according to authorities.
