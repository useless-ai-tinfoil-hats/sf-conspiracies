Two centuries ago, 3 Californians crossed an ocean and changed Hawaii forever
-----
Long before the cowboy became the popular symbol for America's "Wild West," Hawaiian cowboys were wrangling long-horned cattle in the rolling green pastures of Hawaii Island.In 1793, British Capt. George Vancouver brought the first livestock to Hawaii and delivered them to King Kamehameha I as a gift. Hawaii has no endemic large land animals; even the pig and dog were brought over with the Polynesian voyagers. When the cows arrived, the king put a kapu (prohibition) on killing any cattle
