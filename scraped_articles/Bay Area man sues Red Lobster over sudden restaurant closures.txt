Bay Area man sues Red Lobster over sudden restaurant closures
-----
A former Red Lobster worker in the Bay Area is leading a proposed class-action lawsuit against the bankrupt seafood chain, alleging it violated fair warning laws in the sudden shuttering of several locations.George Parker, who said he previously worked at the Rohnert Park location, is the sole named plaintiff in a complaint against Red Lobster entities on behalf of California employees filed in late May in the U.S. Bankruptcy Court Middle District of Florida.
