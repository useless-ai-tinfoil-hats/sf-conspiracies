Dr. Walter Bortz, Stanford expert on aging who always kept moving, dies at 93
-----
As a professor of clinical medicine and expert on aging at the Stanford University School of Medicine, Dr. Walter Bortz preached a simple mantra and practiced it every day: Keep working, and keep moving.He taught deep into his 80s while writing books and submitting academic papers, after starting each day at 6 a.m. with a run while wearing a T-shirt that read "Dare to be 100."
