Man dies after fight in Pittsburg, suspect arrested
-----
A man died following a fight in Pittsburg early Tuesday morning, according to the Pittsburg Police Department.Officers arrived at the 2200 block of Railroad Avenue just after 6:30 a.m and found an unresponsive 65-year-old man with significant facial trauma. Though life-saving measures were performed, the victim was pronounced dead on the way to the hospital, according to William Hatcher, a Pittsburg lieutenant in charge of the department's investigations bureau.
