Parts of California just weathered biggest storm of the season. More is on the way
-----
The biggest storm of the season swept across the Bay Area on Wednesday and Thursday, pelting much of the region with surges of heavy rain and winds that flooded streets, knocked down trees and caused scores of traffic collisions that snarled the morning commute.And more is on the way.
