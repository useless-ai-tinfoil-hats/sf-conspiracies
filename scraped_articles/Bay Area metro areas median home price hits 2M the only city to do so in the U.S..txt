Bay Area metro area's median home price hits $2M, the only city to do so in the U.S.
-----
The median single-family home price in the San Jose metro area just surpassed $2 million, setting a nationwide record as the first region ever to hit that benchmark.The vast majority of single-family home prices in metros across the country rose last quarter, according to a recent report from the National Association of Realtors, which has been tracking prices since 1979.
