Cal cadre of NFL veterans ‘drop some gems’ for current Bears at spring practice
-----
With Cal football heading into an unfamiliar future when it joins the ACC this season, head coach Justin Wilcox has called on some familiar names from the Bears' past to enlighten and inform his players during the spring-practice schedule that ends Saturday.From Ron Rivera, Kyle Boller and Alex Mack to Stephen Anderson, Elijah Hicks and Jaylinn Hawkins, Cal alums who have had — or still have — an NFL career have spoken about their experiences on campus and what they've done since then.
