A 25-year-old pedestrian was killed in a hit-and-run crash in the Tenderloin early Wednesday, the San Francisco Police Department said.The collision was reported about 1:45 a.m. in the area of Polk and Ellis streets, officer Robert Rueca said in a statement.
Officers and paramedics tried to save the victim, but the man died at the scene, Rueca said.
The driver left and was not found, the officer said. A description of the car involved was not immediately available.
