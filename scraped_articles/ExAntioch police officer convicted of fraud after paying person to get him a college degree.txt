Ex-Antioch police officer convicted of fraud after paying person to get him a college degree
-----
A former Antioch police officer accused of paying another person to take college courses for him in an effort to increase his salary was convicted Thursday of federal charges related to fraud and conspiracy.Morteza Amiri faces up to 40 years in prison after a jury found him guilty of wire fraud and conspiracy to commit wire fraud, the U.S. attorney's office said. The jury deliberated for less than a day following a brief trial.
