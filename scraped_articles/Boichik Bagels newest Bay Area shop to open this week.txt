Boichik Bagels' newest Bay Area shop to open this week
-----
Santa Clara's long-awaited location of Boichik Bagels is finally expected to open on Dec. 7, owner Emily Winston announced on Instagram.Winston first announced her popular Berkeley bagel business would be opening a bagel shop at 2050 Wyatt Drive, Santa Clara, back in January. Since then, Winston opened an enormous new bagel factory on Berkeley's Sixth Street in March as well as her first Marin County bagel shop in Larkspur in November.
