High school in Fremont put on lockdown after student injured
-----
American High School in Fremont went into a brief lockdown Thursday afternoon after a student was injured on campus, according to police.Fremont police placed the school in a lockdown for about 15 minutes starting at 1:30 p.m. due to a "medical emergency" on campus, according to the police department. Officials said a student suffered non-life-threatening injuries, but they did not release information about how the student was injured.
