Triple stabbing in Union City leaves 1 dead
-----
Union City police arrested a Mountain View man suspected of stabbing three people Thursday morning. One of the victims died.Officers responded to 33000 Almaden Boulevard, where they found three victims with injuries, one of whom died at the scene, police said. The other two victims were taken to a hospital for non-life-threatening injuries, police said.
