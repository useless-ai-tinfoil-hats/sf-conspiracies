New wildland fire prompts immediate evacuations in Lake County
-----
The Lake County Sheriff's Office ordered an immediate evacuation for an area near Clear Lake due to a wildland fire.Residents in the following evacuation zone are required to leave: north of Lakeshore Boulevard; south of Highway 20 and Point Lands Farm Road; east of Nice-Lucerne Cutoff Road; and west of Collier Avenue and Lakeshore Boulevard. The evacuation order is considered "extreme," as there is an "immediate threat to life or property," the alert read.
