How police think South Bay day care workers allowed two babies to drown to death
-----
Two women who own a day care in San Jose have been charged with three counts of child endangerment after two baby girls drowned in a pool on their property, according to officials from the Santa Clara County District Attorney's Office.Nina Fathizadeh, 41, and her mother, Shahin Gheblehshenas, 64, co-owners of Happy Happy Daycare, self-surrendered at the San Jose Police Department's headquarters Friday after warrants were issued for their arrests.
