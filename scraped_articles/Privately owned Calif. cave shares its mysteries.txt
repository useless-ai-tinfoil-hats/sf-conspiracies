Privately owned Calif. cave shares its mysteries
-----
Black Chasm Cavern is one of Earth's rarest creations.Although the Indigenous people of California likely stumbled onto it long before, the first recorded sighting was in the 1850s when the hills around Volcano, a hamlet about an hour east of Sacramento, were crawling with gold miners.
One of them stumbled onto an 18-inch crack into the mountain. After the disappointment that it held no gold subsided, he saw a new opportunity: tourism.
