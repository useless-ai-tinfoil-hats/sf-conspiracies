Woman allegedly steals Amazon delivery van because she needed a ride to San Jose
-----
A Bay Area woman allegedly stole an Amazon delivery van in Palo Alto because she needed a ride back to San Jose, police said.An Amazon delivery driver called the police on Thursday at about 3:35 p.m., saying an unknown person had stolen his van filled with packages from the 400 block of West Charleston Road in Palo Alto, the Palo Alto Police Department said in a news release.
