'Very eclectic' internet radio station BFF.fm claims SF's last open FM frequency
-----
San Francisco will soon have a new FM radio station. It may be the last for a while.In December, after a decadelong freeze, the Federal Communications Commission opened applications for low-power FM radio licenses. San Francisco had only one available FM frequency remaining: 104.9 FM. Now, local nonprofit internet radio station BFF.fm — short for "Best Frequencies Forever" — has claimed it after submitting a successful application to the FCC, leaving the city with no more unclaimed FM
