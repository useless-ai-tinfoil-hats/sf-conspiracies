Baby spiders are falling from the sky in white clumps around the Bay Area
-----
Baby spiders are falling from the sky, sprinkling parts of the Bay Area with early Halloween decorations.On social media, residents reported seeing gobs of white spider webs floating and sticking to surfaces in San Francisco, San Jose, Danville, Gilroy and more.
Inside those webs are baby spiders seeking new places to live, according to Fred Larabee, an assistant professor of biology at San Jose State University. The process is called ballooning or kiting.
