Juul anti-vaping settlement of $24.7 million will fund dozens of health-related jobs in S.F. schools
-----
Two of the biggest players in the e-cigarette industry will cover the salaries and benefits of 76 nurses, counselors and health educators in San Francisco schools next year in just the first of three expected rounds of funding from a legal settlement with the vaping giants.District officials plan to spend the initial chunk of the $24.65 million deal with Juul Labs Inc. and Altria Group Inc. during the 2024-25 school year and the rest over the following two years.
