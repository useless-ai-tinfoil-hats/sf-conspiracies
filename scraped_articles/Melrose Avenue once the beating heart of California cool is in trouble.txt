Melrose Avenue, once the beating heart of California cool, is in trouble
-----
When Caitlin Cutler was growing up in Los Angeles in the late 1990s and early 2000s, no place was cooler or more thrilling than Melrose Avenue. Cutler and her friends would meet up at the Coffee Bean and walk down the length of the street near West Hollywood, then densely peppered with irreverent vintage stores, funky dining institutions and record store gems.
