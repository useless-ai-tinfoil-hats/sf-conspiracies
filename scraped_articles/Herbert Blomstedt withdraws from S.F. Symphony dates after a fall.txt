Herbert Blomstedt withdraws from S.F. Symphony dates after a fall
-----
Herbert Blomstedt, the San Francisco Symphony's conductor laureate, has canceled his scheduled appearances with the orchestra next year as he recovers from a fall.Finnish conductor Jukka-Pekka Saraste is now expected to take his place on Feb. 2-4, leading the Symphony in performances of Beethoven's Symphony No. 7 and Schubert's Symphony No. 6, the orchestra announced Thursday, Dec. 14.
