The Killers release new single ahead of Outside Lands headlining set
-----
Fans of the Killers have a new song to sing along to for the band's headlining set at Outside Lands.The pop-rock band released "Bright Lights" the morning of the opening day of the San Francisco festival on Friday, Aug. 9, the same night as the band's performance at 8:25 p.m. on the Lands Ends Stage at Golden Gate Park.
