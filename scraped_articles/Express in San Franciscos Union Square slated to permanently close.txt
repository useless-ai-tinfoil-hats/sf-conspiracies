Express in San Francisco's Union Square slated to permanently close
-----
The Express in Union Square is slated to close later this month, per signs posted in the entryway of the clothing store over the weekend."It's not goodbye," the message read. "This store closes October 22nd."
A sale of up to 60% off all merchandise is currently underway, and customers were redirected to Express' only other remaining store in San Francisco, at Stonestown Galleria.
A manager for the store on the corner of Geary and Powell streets confirmed the looming closure
