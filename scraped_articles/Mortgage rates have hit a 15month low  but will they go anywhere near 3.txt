Mortgage rates have hit a 15-month low — but will they go anywhere near 3%?
-----
Potential California homebuyers have been disheartened by the meteoric rise in interest rates over the past two years.But as of Thursday, the average 30-year fixed-rate mortgage was available at 6.47% — nowhere near the low 2's and high 3's seen during the height of the pandemic, but down substantially from a high of 7.79% in October 2023. The last time mortgage rates were this low was in May 2023.
