Photos: San Francisco Opera kicks off 101st season
-----
Patrons filled the War Memorial Opera House on Friday, Sept. 8, as the San Francisco Opera launched its 101st season with a gala concert led by Music Director Eun Sun Kim.The vocal stars for the night were the husband-and-wife team of French tenor Roberto Alagna and Polish soprano Aleksandra Kurzak, who were joined by the San Francisco Opera Chorus in a selection of operatic excerpts before the evening concluded with a glittery after-party.
