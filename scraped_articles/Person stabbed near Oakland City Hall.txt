Person stabbed near Oakland City Hall
-----
A person is in stable condition after being stabbed near Oakland City Hall on Friday afternoon.Oakland police said the person was stabbed in the arm with a sharp object just before 3 p.m. in the plaza outside City Hall. Officers and paramedics arrived at the scene and transported the victim to a local hospital, according to police.
As of Friday night, the victim was in stable condition, Oakland police said.
