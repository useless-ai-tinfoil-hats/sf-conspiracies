Letters: How many people have to die before driverless cars are regulated?
-----
Regarding "Driver hits woman in S.F., then Cruise driverless car runs her over; photo shows victim trapped" (San Francisco, SFChronicle.com, Oct. 2): How many accidents and deaths will it take before autonomous vehicles are regulated? With all this hype about artificial intelligence, we are assured they can do little wrong.But time and again in unfamiliar situations computers fail, and we have emergency vehicles blocked and people injured. I ride a recumbent trike with blinking lights
