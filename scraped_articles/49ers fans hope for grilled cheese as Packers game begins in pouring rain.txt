49ers fans hope for ‘grilled cheese’ as Packers game begins in pouring rain
-----
Dark skies and pouring rain couldn't deter the sea of red and white flooding into the Levi's Stadium on Saturday, speckled with occasional green and yellow.Diehard fans started setting up in the parking lot more than five hours before the San Francisco 49ers were scheduled to kickoff against the Green Bay Packers in the NFC Divisional Round playoffs on Saturday night, getting ready for what they hope will be a major step toward Super Bowl LVIII.
"Can I get a grilled cheese?"
