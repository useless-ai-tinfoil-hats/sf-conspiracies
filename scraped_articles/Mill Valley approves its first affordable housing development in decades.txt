Mill Valley approves its first affordable housing development in decades
-----
Mill Valley approved a 100% affordable housing development this week, marking the first time this has happened in the Marin County town in 30 years, as first reported by the Marin Independent Journal.The four-story building is planned to include 44 housing units, with one-, two- and three-bedroom units available.
