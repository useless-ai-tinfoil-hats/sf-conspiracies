Ex-49ers QB Trey Lance feeding intel to Cowboys: ‘He’s been very helpful’
-----
The Cowboys hope their third-string quarterback has been a first-rate informant this week.Trey Lance's ability to provide useful intel has been a story line as Dallas readies for Sunday night's matchup against the San Francisco 49ers, who dealt Lance to the Cowboys on Aug. 25 for a fourth-round pick.
Quarterback Dak Prescott said Lance's knowledge of the 49ers' defense after spending two-plus seasons with the franchise was beneficial.
"He's been very helpful," Prescott said
