3rd death reported in crash with Mini Cooper stopped on Bay Bridge
-----
LATEST Feb. 15, 12:30 p.m. A third death was reported Thursday in a crash that occurred on the lower deck of the Bay Bridge when a Mini Cooper stalled and was struck by a Toyota Tacoma, CHP spokesperson Mark Andrews told SFGATE.The individual who was riding in the front passenger seat of the Mini Cooper died after being taken to San Francisco General Hospital with serious injuries, Andrews said.
