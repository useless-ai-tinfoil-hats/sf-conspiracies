30-year-old cafe in Oakland will close as business gets ‘harder and harder’
-----
A longtime cafe is shutting down after 30 years in Oakland.Caffe 817, at 817 Washington St., will close on Nov. 11. It was a "torturous" but long-coming decision, owners Scott and Emily Goldenberg wrote on Instagram. Many factors contributed, from a pre-pandemic limitation on the breakfast and lunch spot's space to rising costs and "an increase in crime and safety issues" in the surrounding neighborhood.
