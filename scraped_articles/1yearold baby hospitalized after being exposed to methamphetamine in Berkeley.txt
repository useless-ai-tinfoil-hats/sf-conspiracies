1-year-old baby hospitalized after being exposed to methamphetamine in Berkeley
-----
A 1-year-old baby has been in the hospital for multiple days after being exposed to methamphetamine, according to police.The child was first hospitalized on Monday afternoon, when someone flagged down a Berkeley police officer, reporting that a baby was in medical distress, officials said. Paramedics performed emergency medical aid to the baby, and then transported them to a local hospital where a toxicology test was performed, according to police.
