Treasured inn and restaurant sold to Wine Country mogul
-----
Wine and hospitality mogul Bill Foley has added a beloved Sonoma County hotel to his fast-growing collection of Wine Country holdings.Foley Entertainment Group has purchased a majority stake in Forestville's Farmhouse Inn, a luxury hotel located in the heart of Sonoma County's Russian River wine region. The inn is best known for its restaurant, which held a Michelin one-star rating for 14 consecutive years until its longtime chef departed in 2021.
