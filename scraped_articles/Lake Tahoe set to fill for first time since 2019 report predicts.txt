Lake Tahoe set to fill for first time since 2019, report predicts
-----
Melted snow will fill Lake Tahoe for the first time since June 2019, the United States Department of Agriculture predicted in a report earlier this month.The prediction stems from higher-than-usual snowpack recorded in the Lake Tahoe Basin on May 1, driven by high levels of snowfall in the first three months of the year.
