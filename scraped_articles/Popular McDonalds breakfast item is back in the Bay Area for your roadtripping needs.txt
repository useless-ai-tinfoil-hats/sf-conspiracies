Popular McDonald's breakfast item is back in the Bay Area for your roadtripping needs
-----
After a four year hiatus, McDonald's beloved bagel sandwich has returned to Bay Area breakfast menus, just in time for your spring break road trip.All San Francisco, Oakland and Berkeley McDonald's locations had the bagel sandwich, which comes with egg, cheese and your choice of bacon, sausage or steak (or no meat), available to order through the McDonald's app as of March 29, SFGATE confirmed. McDonald's did not return SFGATE's request for a list of locations offering the bagel sandwich
