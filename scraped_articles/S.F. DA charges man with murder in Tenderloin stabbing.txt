S.F. DA charges man with murder in Tenderloin stabbing
-----
A San Francisco man arrested in the fatal stabbing of a man in the Tenderloin neighborhood has been charged with murder, the district attorney's office said Thursday.At his arraignment Wednesday, Ronnie Randle, 38, pleaded not guilty to charges of murder, possession of a firearm by a felon, possession for sale of cocaine base and possession of a controlled substance with a firearm. If convicted of all charges, Randle faces 26 years to life in state prison, the district attorney's office
