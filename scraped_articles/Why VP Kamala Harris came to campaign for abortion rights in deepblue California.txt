Why VP Kamala Harris came to campaign for abortion rights in deep-blue California
-----
At first glance, it makes little sense that Vice President Kamala Harris made San Jose — a city in the heart of a state that has the nation's most progressive abortion rights protections — the second stop of her nationwide tour to talk about the importance of reproductive rights in this year's elections.Shouldn't she be spending her valuable campaigning time in a battleground state like Georgia or Arizona, where the issue might help turn out voters President Joe Biden desperately needs
