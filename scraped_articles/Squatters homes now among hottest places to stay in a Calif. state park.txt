Squatters' homes now among hottest places to stay in a Calif. state park
-----
Driving the Pacific Coast Highway between the affluent Southern California beach cities of Corona Del Mar and Laguna Beach, there's a stretch of coastline that remarkably doesn't have a resort or rows of million-dollar houses blocking the ocean view.The 3.2 miles of open beach and 2,400 acres of backcountry make up Crystal Cove State Park, and it's one of the last remaining examples of a natural shoreline in Orange County.
