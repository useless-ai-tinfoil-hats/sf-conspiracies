49ers sign undrafted quarterback after NFL Draft wraps up
-----
With San Francisco 49ers quarterback Brock Purdy only 24 years old, the team likely hopes his time at the helm is just beginning. But for a little insurance, the Niners picked up an undrafted quarterback on Saturday after the NFL Draft wrapped up.Tanner Mordecai, a senior out of Wisconsin, will be the 49ers' fourth quarterback behind Purdy, Joshua Dobbs and Brandon Allen.
