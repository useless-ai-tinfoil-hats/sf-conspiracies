Dungeness crab is here — sort of. Here’s what’s happening at Bay Area restaurants
-----
Sweet, long-awaited Dungeness crab is finally adorning pasta and being folded into dumplings at restaurants throughout the Bay Area. But it's most likely not yet coming from Northern California.After numerous delays, the California Department of Fish and Wildlife opened the commercial crab season between Oregon and the Sonoma/Mendocino county line on Friday, Jan. 5. However, a fishermen's strike and inclement weather means few California crabs are actually making their way into Bay Area
