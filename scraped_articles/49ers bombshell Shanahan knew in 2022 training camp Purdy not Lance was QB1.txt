49ers bombshell: Shanahan knew in 2022 training camp Purdy, not Lance, was QB1
-----
Last year, early in Brock Purdy's first NFL training camp, San Francisco 49ers head coach Kyle Shanahan already knew the last pick in the draft would be the future quarterback of the franchise that also employed a 22-year-old QB in whom three first-round picks had been invested.That's according to 49ers CEO Jed York, who revealed the fascinating story Thursday while speaking with a group of local reporters in advance of the 49ers' date with the Chiefs in Super Bowl LVIII on Feb. 11.
