The Warriors reportedly signed a guy Klay Thompson and Draymond Green made fun of
-----
A new Warriors signing has a chance to prove Draymond Green and Klay Thompson wrong.The Athletic's Shams Charania reported Monday that the Warriors are signing 32-year-old forward Rodney McGruder to compete for a spot on the roster during training camp. The seven-year veteran has played for the Heat, Clippers and Pistons throughout his career, averaging 5.8 points, 2.8 rebounds and 1.2 assists per game while shooting 36% from three.
