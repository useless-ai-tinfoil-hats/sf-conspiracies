Suspected serial killer arrives in California to face murder charges
-----
A long-haul trucker suspected of killing at least four women has been extradited to face charges in California. Warren Luther Alexander, 73, is believed to be responsible for the strangulation deaths of three Ventura County women in the 1970s, police said.Since the 1970s, the media and investigators have speculated that three killings, committed in 1977, were likely done by the same person.
