Bay Area man accused of installing a hidden camera in a keychain in cafe restroom
-----
A man was arrested on suspicion of installing a hidden surveillance camera inside a coffee shop's restroom in San Jose, authorities announced Wednesday.Louie Juarez Jr., a 35-year-old San Jose resident, was arrested last week and booked into Santa Clara County Main Jail, according to San Jose Police Department. He was charged with possession of child pornography, installing a hidden surveillance camera with audio, possession of high-capacity gun magazines, possession of an assault rifle
