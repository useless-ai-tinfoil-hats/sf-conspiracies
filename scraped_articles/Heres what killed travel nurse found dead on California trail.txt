Here's what killed travel nurse found dead on California trail
-----
A Michigan travel nurse who went missing while hiking in Calaveras County died from exposure, according to the county coroner.Ann Marie Herford went missing in mid-November and was found dead on a steep hillside on Nov. 30, according to the Calaveras County Sheriff's Office. The official cause of death was exposure, according to County Coroner Kevin Raggio.
