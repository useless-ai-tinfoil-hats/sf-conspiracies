2 prestigious San Francisco schools may change their admissions criteria
-----
Two top public San Francisco high schools may see big changes to their admissions processes in the coming years.Lowell High School and Ruth Asawa San Francisco School of the Arts could adopt less rigorous admissions requirements under a proposal from the superintendent that will be considered by the school board on Nov. 14.
