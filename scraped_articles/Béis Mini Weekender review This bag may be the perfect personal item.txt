Béis Mini Weekender review: This bag may be the perfect personal item

Such was the case with the Béis ad showcasing irresistibly color-coordinated carry-ons and their ride-on-top weekender bags. The ad dared me to "name a sweeter duo" than its suitcase-weekender combo in a rich, milk-chocolaty shade called Maple, and I honestly couldn't. I still can't.
