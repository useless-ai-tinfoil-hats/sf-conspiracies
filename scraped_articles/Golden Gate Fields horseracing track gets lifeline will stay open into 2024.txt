Golden Gate Fields horse-racing track gets lifeline, will stay open into 2024
-----
Golden Gate Fields will not close when its fall season ends on Dec. 11, as the track's owner previously announced in July.Instead, the Stronach Group through its racetrack subsidiary 1/ST RACING applied for dates for a winter-spring season at GGF, and the California Horse Racing Board allocated them at its monthly meeting Thursday. The season will run Dec. 26-June 11 and delay the track's closure for at least six months.
