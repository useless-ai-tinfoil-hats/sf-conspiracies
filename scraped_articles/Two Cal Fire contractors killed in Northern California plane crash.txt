Two Cal Fire contractors killed in Northern California plane crash
-----
Two California men were killed in a Humboldt County plane crash on Tuesday, officials said. Pilot Gabriel Joshua Kulp and Thomias Edward Smith, a helicopter mechanic, were employees of Amentum Aviation, a contractor for Cal Fire, the agency told the Times Standard.The crash occurred at approximately 11:15 a.m. near the Kneeland Airport, about 15 miles southeast of Eureka, the Humboldt County Sheriff's Office said in a news release.
