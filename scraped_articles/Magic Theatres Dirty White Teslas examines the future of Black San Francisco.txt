Magic Theatre’s ‘Dirty White Teslas’ examines the future of Black San Francisco
-----
The mind of playwright Ashley Smiley is awash in perception.The Bayview-Hunters Point native's thoughts about the San Francisco neighborhood where she was raised are full of contradictions. The picturesque nature of the city's southeast corner of rich culture and history remains intact in her memory, but the long list of well-documented challenges have certainly left an imprint.
