Prominent California winery owner accused of sexual abuse by sister in lawsuit
-----
The sister of a prominent Sonoma County winery owner has filed a lawsuit accusing him of repeatedly raping and sexually assaulting her in the 1970s when they were both children.Hilary McKean, now 60, said Mark Hanson abused her over a six-year period, ending when she was 14 and he was 17, in the complaint filed in San Francisco County Superior Court. She is seeking unspecified civil damages, citing symptoms including post-traumatic stress disorder, anxiety and depression.
