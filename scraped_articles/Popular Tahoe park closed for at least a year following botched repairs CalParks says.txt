Popular Tahoe park closed for at least a year following botched repairs, CalParks says
-----
The popular D.L. Bliss State Park will be closed for at least another year, California State Parks announced last week. It's an unplanned extension of a summer 2023 project to replace the Lake Tahoe park's aging pipes and waterlines.
