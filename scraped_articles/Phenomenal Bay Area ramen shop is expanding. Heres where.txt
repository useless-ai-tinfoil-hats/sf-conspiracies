‘Phenomenal’ Bay Area ramen shop is expanding. Here’s where
-----
One of the Bay Area's most in-demand ramen shops is expanding.Japanese chain Kajiken, which has drawn long lines since opening in San Mateo last February, will bring its brothless ramen to the East Bay. An outpost will open in Dublin in late 2024 or early 2025 at a new development at 3978 Summit Road, said master franchisee Timothy Lu. Dublin, the fastest-growing city in California, is also experiencing a restaurant boom.
