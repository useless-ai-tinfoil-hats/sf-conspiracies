SF mall Stonestown Galleria suing restaurant for over $1.4M in alleged unpaid rent, other fees
-----
San Francisco mall Stonestown Galleria filed a lawsuit on Tuesday alleging its former tenant Gangnam Korean BBQ owes the mall more than $1.4 million in unpaid rent and other fees, as first reported by the San Francisco Standard.Gangnam Korean BBQ first opened at the mall in 2019. Initially, the restaurant signed a two-year lease, but on March 5, 2020, both parties agreed to extend the lease to April 2026, according to the lawsuit.
