S.F. pizza favorite expanding to new neighborhood
-----
A beloved San Francisco pizzeria is expanding.Fiorella, known for its charred, chewy pizzas, will open at 4042 24th St. in Noe Valley. The opening was first reported by WhatNowSF.
Fiorella spokesperson Lisa Nourse confirmed the new location and said it will arrive next spring. The 3,900-square-foot space on 24th Street was previously occupied by deep dish pizza chain Patxi's, which closed last year.
