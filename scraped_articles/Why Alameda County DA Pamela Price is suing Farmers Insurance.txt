Why Alameda County DA Pamela Price is suing Farmers Insurance
-----
Alameda County District Attorney Pamela Price filed a lawsuit against Farmers Insurance, accusing the company of coming up with low estimates to rebuild homes in the event of a catastrophe such as a fire, allegedly leaving customers with insufficient coverage.The lawsuit, filed in Alameda County Superior Court last month, seeks civil penalties and a court order to stop the alleged scheme.
