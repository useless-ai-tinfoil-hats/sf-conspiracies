Student found dead in San Jose State dorm room
-----
A student died in their dorm room at San Jose State University over the weekend, according to the university.The student was found early Sunday afternoon in their room at Spartan Village on the Paseo, a new SJSU dorm. University police are investigating the cause of death, according to the university.
The death was an isolated incident and no foul play is suspected, according to the university. There was no known threat to other Spartan Village residents or to the larger campus,
