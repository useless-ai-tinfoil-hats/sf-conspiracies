A’s owner John Fisher, colleagues tour Sacramento’s Sutter Health Park
-----
The Oakland Athletics are taking steps toward ceasing their association with the Coliseum, where their lease expires following the 2024 season.Owner John Fisher and his inner circle of executives visited the Triple-A Sacramento River Cats' ballpark on Thursday, three days after the Chronicle reported that Sutter Health Park, home of the San Francisco Giants' top farm team, has emerged as a possible home for the A's in 2025, 2026 and 2027.
