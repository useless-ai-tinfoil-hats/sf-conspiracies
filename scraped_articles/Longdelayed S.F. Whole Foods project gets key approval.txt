Long-delayed S.F. Whole Foods project gets key approval
-----
San Francisco's Planning Commission voted unanimously Thursday to approve a Whole Foods for a long-vacant shopping center space in the Anza Vista neighborhood, a milestone for a project that has struggled for years to get off the ground.The Whole Foods is planned to go on the third floor of the City Center mall at Geary Boulevard and Masonic Avenue.
