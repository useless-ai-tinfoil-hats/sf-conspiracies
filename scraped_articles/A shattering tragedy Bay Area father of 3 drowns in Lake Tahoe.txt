'A shattering tragedy': Bay Area father of 3 drowns in Lake Tahoe
-----
A San Jose father of three drowned near the eastern shore of Lake Tahoe on Saturday, NBC Bay Area first reported.At around 1:20 p.m. Saturday, the Washoe County Sheriff's Office responded to a report of a possible drowning on the eastern shore of Lake Tahoe, near Bonsai Rock. Numerous search and rescue agencies, including the U.S. Coast Guard and Nevada State Parks rangers, located an unresponsive adult male at the scene. The man was pronounced dead after being taken to Sand Harbor
