Northern California storms delay rescue of critically injured hiker who fell off remote cliff
-----
Search and rescue crews battled Friday night's stormy weather as they rescued a hiker who was critically injured after falling off a cliff in Yolo County, authorities said.Rescuers worked overnight to reach a hiker who had fallen off a cliff about 100 feet tall near Camp Haswell off Highway 16, according to the Yolo County Sheriff's Office. Overhead, heavy rain and poor visibility prevented helicopters from reaching the fallen hiker, the California Highway Patrol said.
