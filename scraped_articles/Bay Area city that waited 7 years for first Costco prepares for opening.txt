Bay Area city that waited 7 years for first Costco prepares for opening
-----
A long-awaited Costco Wholesale store is slated to finally open in Pleasanton this summer.The retail giant announced upcoming openings throughout the country, and the forthcoming Pleasanton location is expected to open sometime in August. SFGATE reached out to Costco to learn more about the store, but a spokesperson said that Costco management had no further information.
