CHP investigates fatal hit-and-run near Discovery Bay
-----
A pedestrian was killed in what police described as a hit-and-run on Highway 4 near Discovery Bay late Thursday, according to the California Highway Patrol.CHP Contra Costa officials said on Facebook that a vehicle was traveling westbound on Highway 4 near Newport Drive when it fatally struck the pedestrian and fled. The pedestrian sustained fatal injuries and was pronounced deceased at the scene by emergency medical personnel.
