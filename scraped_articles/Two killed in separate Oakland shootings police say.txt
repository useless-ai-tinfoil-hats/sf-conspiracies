Two killed in separate Oakland shootings, police say
-----
Two people died after two separate shootings Thursday in Oakland, the police department said.Just after 7 p.m., officers responded to a ShotSpotter activation on the 7900 block of Ney Avenue near Ritchie Street. Officers found a man suffering from apparent gunshot wounds and rendered aid, but he succumbed to his injuries, Frederick Shavies, Oakland's deputy chief for the Bureau of Investigations, said in a news conference Friday.
