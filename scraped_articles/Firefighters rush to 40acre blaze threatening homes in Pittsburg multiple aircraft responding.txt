Firefighters rush to 40-acre blaze threatening homes in Pittsburg; multiple aircraft responding
-----
A fire spread from a building to about 40 acres of vegetation on the west edge of Pittsburg in Contra Costa County on Sunday afternoon, threatening nearby structures as firefighters rushed to get a handle on the blaze, officials said.It was not immediately known how many homes or businesses were threatened.
