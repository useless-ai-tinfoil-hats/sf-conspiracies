Kamala Harris and Tim Walz talk Springsteen, Prince and vinyl in new interview
-----
In a recent one-on-one conversation, Democratic presidential nominee Vice President Kamala Harris and running mate Minnesota Gov. Tim Walz shared their mutual love for Bruce Springsteen, Prince and analog music formats.Harris released a video of the first interview with her new running mate on the campaign's YouTube channel on Thursday, Aug. 15.
