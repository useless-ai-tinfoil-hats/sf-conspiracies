Newly discovered green comet to swing past Earth in 430-year orbit. Can you see it in the Bay Area?
-----
A newly discovered green comet will pass near Earth for the first time in over 430 years over the next few days, astronomers said.Comet Nishimura, or C/2023 P1 Nishimura, was discovered last month by amateur Japanese astronomer Hideo Nishimura, according to NASA and Gerald McKeegan, an adjunct astronomer at Chabot Space and Science Center in Oakland.
