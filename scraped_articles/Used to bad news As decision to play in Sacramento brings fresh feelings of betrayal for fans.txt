Used to bad news, A’s decision to play in Sacramento brings fresh feelings of betrayal for fans
-----
Oakland A's fans long inured to bad news said they weren't shocked at Thursday's announcement that the team will make Sacramento its home for the next three seasons.They were sad and disappointed, but the announcement was mostly greeted with feelings of betrayal, anger and frustration — most of it directed at A's owner John Fisher and his yet incomplete plan to move the team to Las Vegas in 2028.
