2 die in collision when Mini Cooper stops on Bay Bridge
-----
Two people in a Mini Cooper died in a collision on the Bay Bridge early Thursday, officials said.The collision occurred when the driver of the Mini Cooper was stopped in the slow lane the on eastbound lower deck near the Treasure Island tunnel, said Lt. Mariano Elias, a spokesperson for the San Francisco Fire Department.
