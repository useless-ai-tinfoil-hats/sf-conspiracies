Sabrina Carpenter to bring ‘Short n’ Sweet’ tour to San Francisco
-----
Pop sensation Sabrina Carpenter is set to bring her "Short n' Sweet" tour to San Francisco this fall.In anticipation of her forthcoming sixth studio album of the same name, set for release on Aug. 23, the 25-year-old singer-songwriter announced an arena tour across North America. It's set to kick off on Sept. 23 in Columbus, Ohio, and includes a stop at Chase Center, the home of the Golden State Warriors, on Nov. 9.
An exclusive presale for Cash App cardholders started at 10 a.m.
