Girl dies, search for man suspended after they're swept into Half Moon Bay
-----
LATEST Nov. 26, 4:11 p.m. The Coast Guard announced that the 5-year-old girl earlier rescued by firefighters has died after being taken to a hospital. It also announced it had called off the search for the 54-year-old man after 22 hours."The decision to suspend search efforts is one of the hardest decisions to make, but our crews searched for nearly 24 hours without any sightings of the missing person," said Capt.
