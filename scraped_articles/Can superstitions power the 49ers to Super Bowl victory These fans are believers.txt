Can superstitions power the 49ers to Super Bowl victory? These fans are believers
-----
When the Detroit Lions took a 24-7 halftime lead against the San Francisco 49ers during the NFC Championship Game, Sean Keane responded to the atrocious first half like many of us did: by looking for a distraction.The Bay Area-based comedian and podcaster, watching the game with family in Pleasant Hill, went outside to play fetch with his puppy Pinot Noir. When third quarter cheers erupted from the living room, marking the team's comeback, Keane's sister shouted, "Stay outside!"
