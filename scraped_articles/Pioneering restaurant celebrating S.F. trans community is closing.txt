Pioneering restaurant celebrating S.F. trans community is closing
-----
Asia SF, a SoMa restaurant, bar and cabaret starring transgender women will soon close its doors after 26 years. The venue's last day in business at 201 Ninth St. will be March 31, the International Day of Trans Visibility.Co-owner Larry Hashbarger announced the two-level venue, with a restaurant and downstairs nightclub, will close so that he and his business partners can retire.
