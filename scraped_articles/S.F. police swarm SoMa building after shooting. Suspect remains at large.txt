S.F. police swarm SoMa building after shooting. Suspect remains at large
-----
San Francisco police officers swarmed what appeared to be a residential building in the area of Sixth and Mission streets in the city's South of Market neighborhood Monday afternoon after a shooting.Police officials said officers responded to the scene shortly before noon and found a person suffering from gunshot wounds. The person, who was not immediately identified, was hospitalized. Police could not elaborate on the person's condition shortly after 3 p.m.
