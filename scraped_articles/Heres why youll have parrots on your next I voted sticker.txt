Here's why you'll have parrots on your next 'I voted' sticker
-----
The shirts and jackets of San Francisco voters will be adorned with new stickers after the next local election, thanks to the city's recent "I Voted!" sticker contest.Between August and September, over 650 designs were submitted to replace the typical firetruck-red "I Voted!" stickers that are handed out to voters on election day. Entries were discussed by a panel of city officials and voted on by residents between Oct. 10 and Oct. 17.
