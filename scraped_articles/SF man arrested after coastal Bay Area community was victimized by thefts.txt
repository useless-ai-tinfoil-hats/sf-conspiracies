SF man arrested after coastal Bay Area community was 'victimized' by thefts
-----
A San Francisco man was arrested on charges of burglary, grand theft and possession of tear gas, among others, after his alleged spate of crimes "victimized" a popular coastal community, officials said. One of the items he allegedly stole was a rare wooden bench.The Sonoma County Sheriff's Office said it returned a redwood pew that had been taken from the Sea Ranch Chapel last year after "a lengthy investigation" and the arrest of a 29-year-old man that it said was targeting Sea Ranch.
