Pedestrian dies, another badly injured in separate San Jose collisions
-----
One pedestrian died and another suffered life-threatening injuries Saturday in separate collisions in San Jose, police said.In the city's eighth fatal collision and eighth traffic death of 2024, police and paramedics responded shortly after 2 p.m. Saturday to a collision at Saratoga Avenue and Belvedere Drive. A pedestrian died after being taken to a local hospital with life-threatening injuries.
