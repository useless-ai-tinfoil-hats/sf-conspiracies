Ripley's Believe It or Not started with this weird Bay Area building
-----
Too few structures reveal themselves gracefully from the rear, but as it stands today —151 years after its steeple first pierced the sky — the Church of One Tree begins with its butt.A burgundy backside dominates the view of the building from the sidewalk along Sonoma Avenue in Santa Rosa. From the outside, the centerpiece is eight stained-glass panels depicting an old-growth redwood that rises beyond the window's white frame.
