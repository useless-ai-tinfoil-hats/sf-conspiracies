Want to understand Donald Sutherland? Watch these 8 movies
-----
Donald Sutherland had two film careers.There was the young Sutherland, who was quiet, awkward and spacy, whose blank expression somehow couldn't hide his depths of feeling ("Klute"). And then there was the older Sutherland, who was quiet, not because he was shy, but because he was withholding some sinister intent ("The Hunger Games"). Rather than spacy, he was spooky. He seemed like a man whose understanding of the world was deeper than anything we viewers possessed.
