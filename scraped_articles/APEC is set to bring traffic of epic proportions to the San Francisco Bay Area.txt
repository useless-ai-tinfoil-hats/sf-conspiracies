APEC is set to bring traffic of epic proportions to the San Francisco Bay Area
-----
From downtown gridlock to Bay Bridge lane closures and "vehicle exclusion zones," traversing San Francisco is likely to get considerably more difficult in the coming weeks as the city hosts its largest international event in decades.With more than 20,000 people expected to descend on downtown San Francisco for the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit, residents around the area are bracing for clogged roads and slower transit in the coming weeks to accommodate tighter security.
