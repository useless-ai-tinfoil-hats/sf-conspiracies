How Khalia Davis came into her own as a director: ‘I can’t wait to see what you bring’
-----
Director Khalia Davis creates the kind of rehearsal room where, if she instructs her cast to do a little step-touch bop to the music, they'll ask, semi-jokingly, if they can add a snap, too. Or if she tells them to point their arms toward Cinderella and her prince, they'll ask if they can add spirit fingers.But then when she begins a note by saying, "Let's try this," all the chattering and giggling among her multigenerational cast of 32 hushes.
