Who can Warriors target when free agency begins on Sunday?
-----
Free agency in the NBA begins at 3 p.m. Sunday, allowing the Golden State Warriors to barter with free agents from other teams.Contracts can be agreed upon afterward and signed July 6.
"We've got some spots to fill. We've got some needs to fill," said Golden State general manager Mike Dunleavy on Thursday after the NBA draft. "We'll turn the page here from draft to free agency and see what we can do."
