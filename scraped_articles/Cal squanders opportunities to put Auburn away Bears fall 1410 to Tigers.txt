Cal squanders opportunities to put Auburn away, Bears fall 14-10 to Tigers
-----
Cal went toe-to-toe with Auburn at Memorial Stadium on Saturday night, but the Bears squandered several opportunities as the Tigers pulled out a 14-10 decision.The Bears limited Auburn to 230 yards of total offense and forced four turnovers, but squandered multiple scoring opportunities — including three missed field-goal tries by Michael Luckhurst — as Cal fell to 1-1. The Bears also fell to 2-11 in games decided by seven points or fewer in the past two-plus seasons.
