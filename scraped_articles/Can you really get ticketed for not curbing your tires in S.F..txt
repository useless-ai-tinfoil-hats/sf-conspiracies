Can you really get ticketed for not curbing your tires in S.F.?
-----
San Francisco locals believe that forgetting to curb your tire while parked on a hill or slope makes cars a target for break-ins. But it also makes cars a target for San Francisco's parking enforcement officers.The San Francisco Municipal Transit Agency, which oversees both Muni and parking permits and citations, requires that drivers curb their tires on hills of "any perceptible grade," which it defines as a grade of 3% or more.
