East Bay refinery fire prompts temporary public health advisory
-----
Contra Costa County health officials temporarily issued a public health advisory early Sunday due to a refinery fire.Contra Costa Health said on social media at 1:34 a.m. that crews responded to a fire at the Martinez Renewable Fuels refinery "that may affect surrounding areas."
The fire prompted health officials to issue a public health advisory for Martinez, Pacheco, Concord and Clyde.
