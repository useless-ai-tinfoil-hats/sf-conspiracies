Tributes pour in for Willie Mays after SF Giants legend passes away
-----
Tributes quickly poured in on social media from around the sports world and greater San Francisco community after news that Giants legend Willie Mays, who many believe was the greatest baseball player in the history of the game, died Tuesday. He was 93.The Giants announced that Mays passed away peacefully Tuesday afternoon, with the news coming during the middle of the team's game Tuesday night. The team said it will hold a public celebration of his life, but details would be announced
