Report: 106-year-old North Bay Catholic school set to close in May, 68 layoffs
-----
A 106-year-old North Bay Catholic school amid a financial crisis is set to permanently close May 1, according to documents from the California Employment Development Department.In a letter sent to state officials, St. Vincent de Paul High School in Petaluma didn't specify how many school employees could lose their jobs, but a detailed WARN report from EDD indicates that 68 people will be laid off.
