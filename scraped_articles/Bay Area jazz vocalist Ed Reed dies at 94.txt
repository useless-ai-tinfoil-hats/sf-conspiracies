Bay Area jazz vocalist Ed Reed dies at 94
-----
Jazz vocalist Ed Reed, who released his first album at the age of 78 to critical acclaim and spent the next dozen years fulfilling his longtime dream of performing at the nation's top jazz venues, died at home in Richmond on Wednesday, two days before his 95th birthday.The cause of death was liver cancer, according to his wife, Diane Reed, with whom he wrote the braided memoir "Double Helix: A Memoir of Addiction, Recovery, and Jazz in Two Voices."
