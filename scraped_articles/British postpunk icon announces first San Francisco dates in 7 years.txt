British post-punk icon announces first San Francisco dates in 7 years
-----
British post-punk icon Paul Weller is set to return to the Bay Area for the first time in seven years.The successful solo artist and former frontman for the Jam and the Style Council announced his first North American tour since 2017 on Tuesday, April 9. The self-titled tour is expected to begin in New Jersey on Sept. 6.
Weller has scheduled two performances at the Fillmore in San Francisco on Sept. 22-23.
