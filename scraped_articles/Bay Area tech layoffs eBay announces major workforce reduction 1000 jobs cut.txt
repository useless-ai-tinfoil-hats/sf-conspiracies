Bay Area tech layoffs: eBay announces major workforce reduction, 1,000 jobs cut
-----
Bay Area tech giant eBay is the latest company in the region to announce a significant reduction in its workforce.In an email to employees Tuesday, Jamie Iannone, the president and CEO of the online auction site, said approximately 1,000 positions, constituting around 9% of full-time employees, will be eliminated. Additionally, eBay plans to scale back contracts with its contingent workforce in the coming months.
