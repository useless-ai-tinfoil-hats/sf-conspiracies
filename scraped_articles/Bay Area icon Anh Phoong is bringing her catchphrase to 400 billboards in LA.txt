Bay Area icon Anh Phoong is bringing her catchphrase to 400 billboards in LA
-----
A catchy slogan that's been burned into the brains of Bay Area residents will soon start infiltrating the collective consciousness of Los Angeles.Anh Phoong, the personal injury lawyer of "Something wrong? Call Anh Phoong" fame, who has taken billboards, radio ads and the sides of buses by storm over the past several years, is expanding into SoCal, with a familiar media blitz.
"We're doing the same thing as in the Bay Area," Phoong told SFGATE over Zoom about her ad takeover.
