Letters: Why Pelosi should step aside and not run for reelection
-----
Regarding "Nancy Pelosi will run for reelection" (Politics, SFChronicle.com, Sept. 8): Rep. Nancy Pelosi, at 83, is running for reelection. Continuing to work at such an advanced age is unheard of in most industries but for career politicians, it would seem that it is acceptable.There are 15 U.S. representatives and five senators over the age of 80. The average age of someone in Congress is over 60.
