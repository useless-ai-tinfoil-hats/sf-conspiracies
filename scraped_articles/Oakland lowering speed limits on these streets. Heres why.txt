Oakland lowering speed limits on these streets. Here's why
-----
In an effort to slow traffic — and reduce the crash deaths and injuries that result from speeding — Oakland is lowering the speed limits on 11 of the city's most dangerous and deadly streets in or near business districts.Slower speed limits on up to 39 more streets will be added in the coming year.
City officials announced the start of the slower speeds Monday, saying they hope it will make Oakland's streets safer. Two residents each week are killed or seriously injured in traffic
