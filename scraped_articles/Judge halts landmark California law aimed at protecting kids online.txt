Judge halts landmark California law aimed at protecting kids online
-----
A state law that would limit online businesses' use of information they gather from users younger than 18, and require them to take steps to protect the minors' privacy, was blocked Monday by a federal judge, who said it appears to violate the companies' freedom of speech.AB2273 by Assembly Member Buffy Wicks, D-Oakland, passed both legislative houses unanimously last year and was due to take effect next July.
