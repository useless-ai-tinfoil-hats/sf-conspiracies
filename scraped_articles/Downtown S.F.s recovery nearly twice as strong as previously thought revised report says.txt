Downtown S.F.’s recovery nearly twice as strong as previously thought, revised report says
-----
Downtown San Francisco's pandemic recovery is nearly twice as strong as previously reported, according to a revised analysis of U.S. cities led by the University of Toronto.The city's pandemic recovery rate was 67% from March to June compared to the same period in 2019, based on cell phone activity. That's nearly double the 34% rate reported in the prior three months — at the time ranked as the weakest in North America. San Francisco's recovery rate now ranks 38th out of 55 U.S. cities.
