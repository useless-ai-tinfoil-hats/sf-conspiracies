49ers player: Seahawks fans are 'somehow more pathetic' than Eagles fans
-----
Two days after the 49ers lost the Super Bowl, Niners long snapper Taybor Pepper's note summarizing the season and his emotions after the loss was quickly and thoroughly trolled by Seahawks fans. So Pepper decided to fire back."I live in seahawk minds rent free," Pepper posted.
"How are you guys somehow more pathetic than the eagles fan base? Trolling the long snapper after a Super Bowl loss truly shows how sad you guys are," he added with a crying-laughing emoji.
