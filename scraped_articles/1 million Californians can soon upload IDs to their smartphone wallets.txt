1 million Californians can soon upload IDs to their smartphone wallets
-----
Millions already use Google and Apple phones and watches for payments, transit cards and event tickets. Now, the digitization of driver's licenses is entering full swing in California.Gov. Gavin Newsom announced Thursday that the state's pilot program for digital IDs will soon expand onto the tech giants' much-used Wallet apps. Though digital IDs are already available on a California Department of Motor Vehicles app, the expansion is likely to vastly increase the uptake of digital IDs
