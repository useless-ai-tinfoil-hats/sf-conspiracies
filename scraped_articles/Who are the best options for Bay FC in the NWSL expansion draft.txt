Who are the best options for Bay FC in the NWSL expansion draft?
-----
Tuesday was about trades, and Wednesday about more planning for Bay FC.After making five trades to bump its roster to seven players, Bay FC finally knows who will be available in Friday's NWSL expansion draft.
Bay FC – which picks first Friday and will have up to six selections – will have the option to select two players each from North Carolina and San Diego, and one apiece from OL Reign and Chicago.
