Giants add shortstop Nick Ahmed; Brandon Crawford agrees to terms with Cards
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — The San Francisco Giants signed veteran shortstop Nick Ahmed to a minor-league deal Monday, the first signal that all hopes of re-signing shortstop Brandon Crawford were gone, and then Crawford agreed to terms with St. Louis.As the Chronicle reported early Monday, Crawford was fielding interest from two teams, with the Cardinals the front-runner on a deal that was expected to come together quickly — and two hours later, he was in the fold.
