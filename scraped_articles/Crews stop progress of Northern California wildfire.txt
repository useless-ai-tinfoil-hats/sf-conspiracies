Crews stop progress of Northern California wildfire
-----
Firefighters stopped a blaze in Clearlake from spreading Saturday, but were still working to stamp it out in the afternoon.Cal Fire and Lake County firefighters were at the scene of the rapidly spreading vegetation fire near the 16600 block of Dam Road before 1 p.m. Saturday. The Creek Fire was about 1.5 acres and threatened one structure, Cal Fire said. Officials ordered additional ground and air resources.
