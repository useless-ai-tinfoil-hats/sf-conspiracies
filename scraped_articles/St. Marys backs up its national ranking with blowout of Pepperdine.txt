St. Mary’s backs up its national ranking with blowout of Pepperdine
-----
St. Mary's on Thursday night played its first game as a ranked team since the first week of the season. The 18th-ranked Gaels took control of their matchup with Pepperdine late in the first half and cruised from there.Guard Augustas Marciulionis scored a career-high 28 points and swingman Alex Ducas had 18 — including a halfcourt shot to beat the halftime buzzer — as St. Mary's extended its winning streak to 13 games with a 103-59 romp in Moraga.
