East Bay highway crash snarls morning commute
-----
Crews were working to reopen a portion of Interstate 238 in San Leandro after a tractor-trailer overturned on a connector ramp Thursday morning.The northbound I-238 connector ramp to Interstate 880 is expected to reopen at 1:30 p.m., said California Highway Patrol officer Kylie Musselman.
The ramp was closed after a tractor-trailer hit the attenuator guardrail and overturned on the ramp about 1 a.m., prompting highway officials to issue a severe traffic alert, Musselman said.
