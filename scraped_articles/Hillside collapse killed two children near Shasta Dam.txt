Hillside collapse killed two children near Shasta Dam
-----
Two children who died Thursday near the Shasta Dam fell toward a river area during a hillside collapse, the Shasta County Sheriff's Office said.Deputies responded to a report that rocks and debris had broken away from the hillside toward the river around 9 a.m. in an area downstream from Shasta Dam.
The two children were caught in the debris and fell. The Sheriff's Office asked that water releases from the dam be reduced to aid in the recovery of their bodies.
