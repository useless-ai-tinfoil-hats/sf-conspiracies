Steph Curry's supernova saves Team USA in epic Olympic comeback win
-----
It's been an underwhelming Olympics on the floor so far for Steph Curry, but one of basketball's all-time greatest shooters picked the right game to get hot.Curry ended up one point short of a USA Basketball record Thursday, scoring 36 points to lead the Americans in an epic come-from-behind win over Serbia in the Olympic semifinals. The win sends the Americans to the gold-medal game against host France.
