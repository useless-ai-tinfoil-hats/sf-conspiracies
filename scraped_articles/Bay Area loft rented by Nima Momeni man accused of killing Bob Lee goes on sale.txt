Bay Area loft rented by Nima Momeni, man accused of killing Bob Lee, goes on sale
-----
The Emeryville loft once rented by Nima Momeni, the man accused of killing tech executive Bob Lee earlier this year, has hit the market for $799,000.The 1,278-square-foot loft is inside the Besler Building at 4053 Harlan St., and Momeni rented the unit until last April, when he was arrested in connection with the stabbing death of Cash App founder Lee.
Silvana Messing of Sotheby's International Realty listed the unit on Thursday without mention of the loft's connection to Momeni.
