String of break-ins shutters Jewish deli, plus more Bay Area restaurant closings
-----
The following is a list of notable Bay Area restaurants that closed in July. Click here for a list of June closings.The Wise Sons Jewish Deli near Lake Merritt in Oakland is now closed. The company confirmed the details to the Chronicle, citing nearly a dozen break-ins at the restaurant on 1700 Franklin St. over the past two years. Its San Francisco delis in the Mission, Hayes Valley and the Fillmore remain open.
