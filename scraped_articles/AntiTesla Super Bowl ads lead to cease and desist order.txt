Anti-Tesla Super Bowl ads lead to cease and desist order
-----
When a record 123 million people tuned into the Super Bowl last Sunday, one of the commercials that aired was a critique of Tesla and its autopilot technology from an advocacy ground called the Dawn Project.The advert, paid for by a Santa Barbara-based software company owner, mentioned an April 2021 accident that the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration is investigating. A logo of the agency's seal appeared in front of millions, legitimizing the group's claim.
