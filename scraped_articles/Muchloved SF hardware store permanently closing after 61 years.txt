'Much-loved' SF hardware store permanently closing after 61 years
-----
Glen Park Hardware, a staple of the Glen Park neighborhood for more than 60 years, is closing.The store announced the closure by recently posting several signs on its doors and windows. Its voicemail announced that it will be holding a liquidation sale up to 50% off this weekend.
