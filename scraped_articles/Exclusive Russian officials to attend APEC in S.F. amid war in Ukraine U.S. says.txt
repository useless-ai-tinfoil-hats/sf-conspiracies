Exclusive: Russian officials to attend APEC in S.F. amid war in Ukraine, U.S. says
-----
Russian officials will attend the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit in San Francisco, a U.S. State Department official told the Chronicle on Wednesday.Matt Murray, the U.S. State Department's senior official for APEC, confirmed that some Russian officials will attend the 20,000-person event, which runs from Nov. 11-17. Others like President Vladimir Putin, who is subject to U.S. sanctions, won't be present.
