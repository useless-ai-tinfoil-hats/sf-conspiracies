Review: ‘Longing’ is a weird but effective movie about grief and loss
-----
"Longing" takes strange turns and works in strange ways. It doesn't succeed entirely, but the fact that it works at all makes it interesting. It holds your attention, though at times it's hard to see why. Yet, if you allow yourself to get dropped into its world, you'll probably enjoy the journey.The film is based on the award-winning Israeli film of the same name, which was, like the remake, written and directed by Savi Gabizon.
