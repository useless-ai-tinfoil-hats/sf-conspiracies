Kamala Harris praised for being ‘just normal’ on late night TV
-----
Moments after Vice President Kamala Harris accepted her party's presidential nomination at the Democratic National Convention in Chicago, "The Daily Show" honored her with a satirical campaign video that could be considered one of the best ever.The seven-minute clip, titled "Kamala Harris is 'Just Normal,'" and narrated by "Arrested Development" star Jason Bateman, endearingly emphasizes her Bay Area roots and general mediocrity.
