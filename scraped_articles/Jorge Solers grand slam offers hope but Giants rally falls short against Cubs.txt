Jorge Soler’s grand slam offers hope, but Giants’ rally falls short against Cubs

CHICAGO — Jorge Soler has proved in his career that he can be an RBI machine.In the lone major-league season in which he played all 162 games, he drove in 117 runs with the Kansas City Royals in 2019. Last year, in 137 games with the Miami Marlins, he had 75 RBIs.
But from May 26 through Saturday in his first year with the San Francisco Giants, he was in a dry spell. He was only able to bring home three runs in 17 games and 61 at-bats.
