162-year-old California ranch is shutting down its meat operation
-----
Rancho Llano Seco, the historic Chico, California, beef and pork farm that counts Chez Panisse and Zazie among its customers, is getting out of the meat business. Llano Seco Meats, the 162-year-old ranch's meat brand, will shut down in October, as first reported by Eater.
