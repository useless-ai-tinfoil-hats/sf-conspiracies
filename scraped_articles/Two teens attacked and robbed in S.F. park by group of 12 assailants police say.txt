Two teens attacked and robbed in S.F. park by group of 12 assailants, police say
-----
Two 17-year-old boys were hospitalized after San Francisco police said they were attacked and robbed by around 12 people Wednesday afternoon at Hilltop Park in the city's Bayview district.The attackers, who police said ranged in age from around 12 to 20, stole two cellphones and a pair of shoes from the teenagers, police said.
The attack occurred around 4 p.m. at the park. The two victims suffered injuries that were not life-threatening and were taken to a hospital for treatment,
