Review: ‘The Taste of Things,’ with Juliette Binoche, is too much for viewers to digest
-----
You can love French movies all you want, but to enjoy "The Taste of Things," you really have to love French food at its most fatty and gross.The movie begins with a 15-minute scene of people cooking. Benoît Magimel plays a famous chef, and Juliette Binoche is his cook, and they are preparing a lavish meal.
