This data shows one critical way San Francisco’s national reputation is changing
-----
San Francisco seems significantly scarier to many people these days — even though technically speaking, it's safer than it used to be.That's according to a recent nationwide Gallup poll in which respondents were asked if they considered 16 major U.S. cities "safe to live in or visit." While a slight majority, 52%, rated San Francisco safe, that's a steep drop-off from 2006, when seven out of 10 respondents rated the city safe.
