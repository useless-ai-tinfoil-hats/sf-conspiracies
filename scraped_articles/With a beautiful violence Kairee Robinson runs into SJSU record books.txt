With a ‘beautiful violence,’ Kairee Robinson runs into SJSU record books
-----
Kairee Robinson's superb 2023 has helped provide San Jose State fans not only the enjoyment that comes from the Spartans finishing the regular season on a six-game winning streak, but also a sense of the program's history at running back.Because as Robinson has torn through defenses to the tune of 1,127 rushing yards, 18 rushing touchdowns and 20 total TDs this season, he has broken, tied or neared school records held by some of SJSU's most decorated players.
