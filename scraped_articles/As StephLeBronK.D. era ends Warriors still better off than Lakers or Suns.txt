As Steph-LeBron-K.D. era ends, Warriors still better off than Lakers or Suns
-----
That noise you heard coming from the NBA was the sound of a page turning. Of one chapter ending. A new one beginning.With the ousting of LeBron James and Kevin Durant in the first round, and Stephen Curry failing to make the playoffs, this feels — decidedly, emphatically — like a changing of the NBA guard. The league's two most popular players of the 21st century — James and Curry — won't be playing in May. Exciting young players are stepping into their places.
