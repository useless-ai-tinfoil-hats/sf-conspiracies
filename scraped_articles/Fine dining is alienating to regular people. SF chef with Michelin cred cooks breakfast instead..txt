Fine dining 'is alienating to regular people.' SF chef with Michelin cred cooks breakfast instead.
-----
On Thursday, an airy new brunch restaurant with a cheerful chicken mascot landed on the corner of Baker and McAllister in San Francisco's NoPa neighborhood.
