Blake Snell falters but Giants’ bullpen delivers in 4-3 win over Seattle
-----
SEATTLE — Blake Snell's starts are becoming must-watch events, and with the San Francisco Giants left-hander pitching in his hometown against an offensively challenged Mariners team, all the more so.Baseball delights in dashing expectations, and so it was Saturday. Snell held Seattle hitless, but he couldn't find the zone with consistency. He walked five and threw 40 pitches in Seattle's two-run second, and was out of the game after three innings.
