Halloween in the Castro returns to San Francisco, but without the chaos
-----
After a 17-year hiatus, Halloween in the Castro is back.On Saturday, Castro Street's sidewalks filled with costumed people and local merchants, with a zombie flash mob in Jane Warner Plaza dancing to Michael Jackson's "Thriller," twice.
Twin Peaks Tavern and Orphan Andy's Restaurant were filled with revelers. Inside the Castro Theatre, thematic films screened from 1 p.m. to midnight, and the Sisters of Perpetual Indulgence held a costume contest.
