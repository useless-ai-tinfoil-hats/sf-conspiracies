Violent Oakland weekend: 3 killed, 3 wounded in spate of shootings
-----
Oakland police are investigating six weekend shootings, which claimed three lives and left three people injured.Officers responded to a shooting at Eighth and Filbert streets about 9:45 p.m. Friday and found a male victim, according to Rosalia Lopez, a spokesperson for the Oakland Police Department. Paramedics treated the Oakland resident at the scene and transported him to a hospital, where he was pronounced dead.
