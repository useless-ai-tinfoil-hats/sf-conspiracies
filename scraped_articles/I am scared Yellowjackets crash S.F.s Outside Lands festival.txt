‘I am scared’: Yellowjackets crash S.F.’s Outside Lands festival
-----
Organizers of Outside Lands successfully kept most gatecrashers out. But the San Francisco festival at Golden Gate Park still had a few uninvited guests this year.Several attendees reported getting harassed by wasps, especially around the food stalls and benches. These aggressive black-and-yellow insects, also known as yellowjackets, had people swatting, dodging and running throughout the first day of the three-day festival on Friday, Aug. 9.
