S.F. voters send clear message supporting more aggressive response to drug crisis
-----
UPDATE: S.F. city workers try to block controversial plan to force welfare recipients into drug treatmentA contentious ballot measure sponsored by Mayor London Breed to mandate drug screenings for welfare recipients passed Tuesday, sending a clear message that voters want to see a more aggressive response to the city's drug crisis.
The measure was backed by 58.5% of the vote as of Friday afternoon.
