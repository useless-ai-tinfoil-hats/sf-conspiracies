Eight Novato middle-schoolers arrested for allegedly attacking students
-----
Novato police arrested eight middle school students this week who were allegedly involved in an assault at Sinaloa Middle School last week.Police said the students, who ranged in age from 12 to 14 years old, were booked into Marin County Juvenile Hall on conspiracy and felony assault charges. Police said they were involved in an assault on another student.
The students allegedly joined in on an assault, recording the incident on their cellphones and encouraging others to join in.
