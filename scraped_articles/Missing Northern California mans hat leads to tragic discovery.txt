Missing Northern California man's hat leads to tragic discovery
-----
A missing Northern California man was found dead after a search uncovered first his car and then his hat near a cliff's edge, the Mendocino County Sheriff's Office said.Gregory Noyes, 76, was reported missing to the Marin County Sheriff's Office on May 15. According to the reporting party, Noyes, who lived in Point Arena, was driving to Marin County. He hadn't been heard from since May 12.
