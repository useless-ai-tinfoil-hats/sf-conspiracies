Cal Berkeley? University task force recommends new name for UC Berkeley athletics
-----
A century and a half after the founding of UC Berkeley, its leaders determined that the name of the world-famous university was too confusing because so many people, usually athletes and fans, also called it Cal.Simplicity was the goal.
But a 19-member task force charged with adopting a coherent brand for the university's academic and athletic identities has recommended keeping the names separate, with one important change.
