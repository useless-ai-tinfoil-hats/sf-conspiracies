Review: Julia Louis-Dreyfus makes 'Tuesday' worth watching
-----
"Tuesday" blends two artistic universes, and it would have helped if both were of equal quality.It's a Julia Louis-Dreyfus movie that concentrates on a mother-daughter relationship, and to that extent, it's satisfying and, at times, even remarkable. Louis-Dreyfus, still best known as Elaine on "Seinfeld," won the 2018 Mark Twain Prize for American Humor, so we know she's funny. But "Tuesday" shows her to also be an extraordinary dramatic actress.
