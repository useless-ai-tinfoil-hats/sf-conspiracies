Bay Area photographer captures Perseid meteor shower like you've never seen it before
-----
Michaël Uyttersprot stood in the complete darkness of a remote campground in far Northern California, taking in countless flashes of space dust as they hurtled over his head. A couple of decades earlier, in his hometown of Brussels, Belgium — a place so light polluted it can be seen from space — he considered himself lucky if he could spot a single star.Now, the Oakland-based astrophotographer has made hunting for cosmic phenomena up to millions of light years away his life's pursuit.
