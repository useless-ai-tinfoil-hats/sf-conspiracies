Weather service issues flood advisories for SF Bay Area
-----
As rain pounded the San Francisco Bay Area on Monday morning, the National Weather Service issued a series of flood advisories. Water pooled on highways across the region, creating delays and traffic during morning rush hour.An advisory is in effect through 8:45 a.m. for a large portion of the North Bay, including Santa Rosa, Napa and Cloverdale, with a risk for minor flooding and flooding on highways, streets and underpasses.
