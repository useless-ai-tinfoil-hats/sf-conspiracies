Famous rapper loses $1.5M in Calif. pot deal gone bad, lawsuit alleges
-----
California's massive legal cannabis market once seemed like it would make anyone who got involved very rich, but one famous rapper found out that some pot investments can just go up in smoke.Tauheed K. Epps, better known by his stage name 2 Chainz, invested $1.5 million into a Los Angeles cannabis company in 2021 in a deal that quickly went sour, leaving the famous rapper out of nearly his entire investment, according to a lawsuit filed last week in Los Angeles Superior Court.
