Point Fire burning in Bay Area consumes 1,100 acres, destroys buildings
-----
A wildfire that started amid gusty winds and hot temperatures ripped through over 1,000 acres in Sonoma County Sunday. As of noon Monday, the Point Fire has burned 1,190 acres and is 20% contained.The blaze began shortly after 12:30 p.m. Sunday at Stewarts Point-Skaggs Springs Road in Healdsburg.
