Wind-driven Corral Fire near Tracy grows as crews advance, part of I-580 reopens
-----
The fast-moving Corral Fire in San Joaquin County, which forced evacuations Saturday around Tracy, grew to 14,000 acres Sunday and was 30% contained as a portion of Interstate 580 reopened.The fire destroyed a home near the Tracy Golf and Country Club, Cal Fire Battalion Chief Josh Silveira said Sunday as crews continued to assess the extent of the fire's damage.
