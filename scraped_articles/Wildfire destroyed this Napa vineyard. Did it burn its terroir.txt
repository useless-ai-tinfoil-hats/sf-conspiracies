Wildfire destroyed this Napa vineyard. Did it burn its terroir?
-----
I'll never forget the conversation I had with winemaker Chris Howell four years ago, just hours after the Glass Fire tore across his Napa Valley vineyard and home. At the time, according to Cal Fire, it was the tenth-most destructive wildfire in California history, leveling over 1,500 structures.
