Video: Steam pipe rupture forces closure of downtown S.F. streets
-----
An underground steam pipe ruptured at the corner of Grant Avenue and Geary Street in San Francisco on Sunday, prompting street closures near the area, fire officials said.Emergency crews and Pacific Gas and Electric Co. responded to the rupture just after 10:45 a.m., said a spokesperson from the Fire Department, which posted video of steam rising from the streets.
The cause of the rupture was unknown, and no one was injured, the spokesperson said.
