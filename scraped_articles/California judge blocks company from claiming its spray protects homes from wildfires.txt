California judge blocks company from claiming its spray protects homes from wildfires
-----
A California judge says a company must stop making unfounded claims that its coating-spray product will protect homes from catching fire and burning down during wildfires.Ads by Sunseeker Enterprise, which does business as Sun FireDefense, "falsely and misleadingly imply that an application of SPF 3000 on the exterior of a residence would prevent ignition and spread of wildfire on treated exterior surfaces," Santa Barbara County Superior Court Judge Colleen Sterne wrote in an order
