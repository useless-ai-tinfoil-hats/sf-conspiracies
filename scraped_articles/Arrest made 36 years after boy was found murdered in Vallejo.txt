Arrest made 36 years after boy was found murdered in Vallejo
-----
An arrest was finally made this week, 36 years after 6-year-old Jeremy Stoner was found dead near his home in Vallejo, in a case that gripped the Bay Area for years.After picking up the case last year for the first time in decades, detectives used DNA evidence to arrest a new suspect, Fred Cain III of Central Point, Oregon, on Monday, the Solano County District Attorney's Office announced. Cain, a former resident of Martinez, has prior arrests for suspicion of rape, the Mercury News
