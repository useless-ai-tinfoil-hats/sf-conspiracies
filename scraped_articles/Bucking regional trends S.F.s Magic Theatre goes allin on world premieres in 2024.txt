Bucking regional trends, S.F.’s Magic Theatre goes all-in on world premieres in 2024
-----
Many Bay Area theaters are responding to long-term pandemic difficulties in part by programming safer fare: the well-known titles and crowd-pleasers that might lure even couch potatoes away from Netflix for a night.But Magic Theatre, which has been led since 2021 by Sean San José, is taking a different approach. Three slots in its 2024 lineup, announced Monday, Oct. 2, are world premieres.
"It's the only way to do it," San José told the Chronicle with a smile.
