Student dies hiking at waterfall near Big Sur
-----
A Cal Poly San Luis Obispo student has died after falling at a Monterey County waterfall, officials said Sunday.Kenneth Aspen Taylor, 21, fell while hiking near Salmon Creek south of Big Sur on April 6, Andres Rosas, a commander with the Monterey County Sheriff's Office, said Sunday. Eyewitnesses flagged down a person who reported the fall, which occurred near a canyon that has poor cell service, Rosas said.
