Radu Paponiu announced as new music director for San Francisco Symphony Youth Orchestra
-----
Radu Paponiu has been announced as the new music director for the San Francisco Symphony Youth Orchestra, signing a three-year term set to begin with the 2024-25 season."I'm very honored to step into this wonderful tradition when it comes to the youth orchestra," Paponiu said on a phone call with the Chronicle. "I really think it's one of the most esteemed ensembles of its kind. I'm looking forward to learning more about it, to continue tradition."
