UC Berkeley-loved Chinese restaurant has closed after 25 years
-----
King Dong, a Chinese restaurant in downtown Berkeley, has quietly closed its doors after 25 years, as first reported by East Bay Nosh. "We are closing our business," read the announcement on the restaurant's website. "Thank you so much to our longtime customers."UC Berkeley students loved King Dong for both its affordable menu of Mandarin-style classics and its name (Dong is a common Chinese surname, but college students can't resist a dirty joke).
