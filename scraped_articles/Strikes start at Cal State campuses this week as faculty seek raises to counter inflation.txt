Strikes start at Cal State campuses this week as faculty seek raises to counter inflation
-----
Thousands of California State University students found themselves locked out of classes Monday as their instructors, counselors, librarians and coaches walked off the job at campuses up and down the state on the first day of a planned week-long strike.Dozens of striking faculty banged drums under a pounding rain Monday morning at Cal State East Bay in Hayward, as they chanted, "Whose university? Our university," echoing many a student protest.
