The oldest hotel in Palm Springs was the stomping ground of legends
-----
An unassuming hotel hides behind a stone wall lined with tall colorful bushes that surround the block not far from the main strip of downtown Palm Springs.The only indication that I've arrived at the historic Casa Cody inn — the oldest hotel still operating in Palm Springs — is a dainty vintage sign hanging near a wrought iron gate. It calls me to its only entrance. And it's like I'm walking into a secret garden.
