'People are so rude': Feud erupts over Lake Tahoe beach fence
-----
A controversy has bubbled up on the northern shore of Lake Tahoe after a fence and signs were installed at the back of a restaurant parking lot near a public beach.The 3-foot fence, security gate with a lock code and signs warning "residents only" were erected earlier this year behind Whitecaps Pizza, which backs onto the Kings Beach State Recreation Area. Another nearby sandwich board standing on the sand reads, "No public access to beach or street."
