Oakland Target slated to close saw 105 calls to police this year
-----
When it opened four years ago, Oakland's only Target store was part of a neighborhood revival in a scrappy part of downtown dominated by auto dealerships and supply shops.But this year it apparently became a casualty of rising crime that resulted in more than 100 calls to 911. Now the department store and pharmacy is among nine Targets across the country — including three in the Bay Area — that are scheduled to close next month.
