Review: Anthony Hopkins has another acting triumph in World War II drama ‘One Life’
-----
"One Life" is the story of Nicholas Winton, a British man who rescued 669 Jewish children from Czechoslovakia in 1938, just before World War II. It's a superb film that provides a showcase for yet another extraordinary Anthony Hopkins performance.Hopkins plays the 78-year-old Winton in 1987, and Johnny Flynn plays Winton almost five decades earlier, during the months in which he did his great work.
