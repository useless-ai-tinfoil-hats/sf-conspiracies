5-year-old girl rescued off Peninsula coast dies; search suspended for man swept into ocean
-----
The U.S. Coast Guard has suspended its search for a 54-year-old man who went missing off the coast of Half Moon Bay and reported that a 5-year-old girl who had been rescued has died.Crews searched 100 square miles of the sea for about 22 hours since the man was swept off Martins Beach near Half Moon Bay on Saturday afternoon, the Coast Guard said Sunday on X, formerly called Twitter.
