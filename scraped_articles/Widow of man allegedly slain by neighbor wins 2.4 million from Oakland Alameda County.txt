Widow of man allegedly slain by neighbor wins $2.4 million from Oakland, Alameda County
-----
Oakland and Alameda County have agreed to pay together $2.4 million to the family of a man who was allegedly shot and killed by his neighbor in 2020, according to the family's attorney.Miles Armstead, 44, was doing yardwork at his home on the 7500 block of Ney Avenue in East Oakland when his former neighbor fatally shot him around 2 p.m. on May 1, 2020, according to Oakland police.
The shooting occurred weeks after the suspect, Jamal Thomas, then 43, had been jailed and released
