Giants adding Mason Black and Hayden Birdsong to injury-depleted pitching corps
-----
Reinforcements for a depleted pitching staff are on the way.The San Francisco Giants recalled Mason Black on Tuesday and were set to call up Hayden Birdsong — one of the team's top pitching prospects — to make his major-league debut Wednesday.
Birdsong, 22, was selected in the sixth round of the 2022 draft out of Eastern Illinois. He had a 2.05 ERA, 61 strikeouts and 20 walks in 11 starts with Double-A Richmond this season, earning him a promotion to Triple-A Sacramento this month.
