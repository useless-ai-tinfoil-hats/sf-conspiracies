Giants get Luke Jackson back from IL to shore up struggling bullpen
-----
The timing annoyed Luke Jackson. Two pitches into his first outing on Opening Day against the San Diego Padres, Jackson felt his lower back tighten. Giving up first one baserunner, then another, then a third, he couldn't record an out.An MRI exam after the loss revealed a lower back strain, sidelining the right-handed reliever until he was activated Monday prior to the San Francisco Giants' series opener against the New York Mets at Oracle Park.
