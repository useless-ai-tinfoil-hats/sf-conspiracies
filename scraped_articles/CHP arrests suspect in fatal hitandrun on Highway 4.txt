CHP arrests suspect in fatal hit-and-run on Highway 4
-----
A man suspected in a fatal hit-and-run in Contra Costa County last week was arrested, the California Highway Patrol said Wednesday.Javier Dominguez Ramirez, 32, of Antioch was taken into custody in connection with the crash that occurred around 11:42 p.m. on Nov. 16 on westbound Highway 4, near Newport Drive in Discovery Bay, authorities said. Ramirez fled the scene in a car and the pedestrian was pronounced dead at the scene by medical personnel, the CHP said.
