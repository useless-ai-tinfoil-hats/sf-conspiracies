Key for Giants’ hopeful Daulton Jefferies is keeping emotions in check
-----
MESA, Ariz. — Daulton Jefferies' first outing since May 18, 2022, went smoothly — he gave up three hits and a run in two innings and he struck out one for the San Francisco Giants on Tuesday. On Wednesday, his surgically repaired elbow was feeling great, and he said that though he was eagerly anticipating that outing against the Mariners, he was able to harness his emotions."I was able to stay calm mentally," he said. "I think that's a sign of more maturity."
