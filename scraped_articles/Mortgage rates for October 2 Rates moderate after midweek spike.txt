Mortgage rates for October 2: Rates moderate after midweek spike
-----
The 10-second guide to mortgage rates this week* The average for a 30-year fixed rate mortgage was 7.44% as of September 22. That's up 0.05 points from the previous week.
* 10-year Treasury yields spiked mid-week, causing mortgage rates to jump to multidecade highs.
* The median monthly mortgage payment in the U.S. hit a new record for the third week in a row.
