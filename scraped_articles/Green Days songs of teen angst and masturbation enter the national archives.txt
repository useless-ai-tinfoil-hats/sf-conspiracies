Green Day has earned a prestigious spot in the Library of Congress.The East Bay punk band's 1994 major label debut, "Dookie," is among the latest round of inductees announced by the National Recording Registry on Tuesday, April 16. It's among 25 other additions, including ABBA's 1976 album "Visitors," Notorious B.I.G.'s 1994 "Ready to Die," Blondie's 1978 "Parallel Lines," and Jefferson Airplane's 1967 "Surrealistic Pillow," another entry from the Bay Area.
