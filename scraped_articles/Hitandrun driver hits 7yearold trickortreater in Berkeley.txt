Hit-and-run driver hits 7-year-old trick-or-treater in Berkeley
-----
A hit-and-run driver struck a 7-year-old boy trick-or-treating on Halloween night, breaking the child's leg, according to the Berkeley Police Department.The crash occurred on the 1200 block of Derby Street at 6:24 p.m., after the boy and several other children attempted to cross the street, police said.
