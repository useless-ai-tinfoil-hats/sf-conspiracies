North Bay highway crash leaves 1 dead
-----
A woman died and a man was injured Monday afternoon following a car crash on Highway 101 in Petaluma, authorities said.The car crash happened around 4:42 p.m. on the northbound lanes of Highway 101, just south of the East Washington Street exit in Petaluma, according to the California Highway Patrol.
