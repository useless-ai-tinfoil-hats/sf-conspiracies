S.F. police arrest 14-year-old girl suspected in more than a dozen retail thefts
-----
San Francisco police arrested a 14-year-old girl in connection with more than a dozen retail thefts — totaling more than $30,000 worth of merchandise — over an eight-month period this year.The girl, who is not being named by officials because of her age, was arrested Friday after police responded to a theft in progress at a store on Market Street. She was booked in connection with 15 thefts and is being held at San Francisco Juvenile Justice Center.
