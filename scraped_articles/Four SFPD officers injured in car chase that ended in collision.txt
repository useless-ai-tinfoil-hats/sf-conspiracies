Four SFPD officers injured in car chase that ended in collision
-----
Four San Francisco police officers were injured in a car chase that ended in a collision Friday night, the department said.Around 10:30 p.m., officers found a wanted vehicle and suspect and attempted a traffic stop. The driver didn't stop, they said, so officers pursued the vehicle.
The car chase ended at Third and Cesar Chavez streets, where marked police vehicles were involved in a crash, though officers did not say whether they crashed with the vehicle they were pursuing.
