Jaydn Ott rushes for 165 yards but ‘upset’ after Cal edges Arizona State
-----
Head coach Justin Wilcox and several of his Cal players spent much of their postgame-interview sessions Saturday doing a proverbial balancing act.The Bears' 24-21 win over Arizona State at Memorial Stadium was far from an artistic success, but the bottom line was successful. So, Wilcox and Co. alternated appreciating the victory with realizing Cal could have played considerably better.
"We will never minimize winning in this conference," Wilcox said.
