Elevated risk of sneaker waves extends hazard warning for Bay Area beaches
-----
National Weather Service officials extended a beach hazard warning for a long stretch of Northern California's coastline Tuesday meant to warn swimmers and surfers of potentially dangerous conditions through the evening.The warning was intended to draw attention to the increased risk of sneaker waves and rip currents at northwest-facing beaches.
