A's agree they're moving, will pay $45 million owed for Coliseum stake
-----
The Oakland Athletics are paying up on the Coliseum, after all.A week after Alameda County sent notice to A's owner John Fisher to pay the county $45 million by May 14 for the team's half of the Coliseum property, the A's responded on Thursday.
In a letter acquired by the Chronicle to Nate Miley, president of the Alameda County Board of Supervisors, the A's said they will follow the terms of the agreement from their purchase in 2019.
