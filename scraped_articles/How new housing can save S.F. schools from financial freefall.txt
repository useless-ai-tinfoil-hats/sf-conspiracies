How new housing can save S.F. schools from financial freefall
-----
The San Francisco Unified School District has seen better days. It started the year with a 21% vacancy rate for classroom staffing positions, and the rates were even higher for custodial, food, buildings and grounds staff. Its buildings have well over $1 billion in deferred maintenance.
