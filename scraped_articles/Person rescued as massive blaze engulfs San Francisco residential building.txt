Person rescued as massive blaze engulfs San Francisco residential building
-----
A massive blaze engulfed a residential building on the 700 block of Masonic Avenue in San Francisco on Monday morning, the San Francisco Fire Department said.The 3-alarm fire was first reported to the SFFD at approximately 3:20 a.m., according to KTVU. News of the blaze was posted on X, the site formerly known as Twitter, by the SFFD at approximately 5:20 a.m.
Multiple buildings have been evacuated and 1 person has been rescued and taken to the hospital.
