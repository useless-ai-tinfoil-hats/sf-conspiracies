North Bay driver arrested in suspected DUI death of 11-year-old girl
-----
A driver has been arrested in connection with the death of an 11-year-old girl in Fairfield last year, authorities said.Fairfield resident Elena Lindsey McGraw-Ogans was arrested last week on suspicion of vehicular manslaughter after investigators believe she struck and killed a sixth grader outside of Grange Middle School in March 2023, according to the Fairfield Police Department.
