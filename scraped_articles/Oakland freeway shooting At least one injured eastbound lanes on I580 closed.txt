Oakland freeway shooting: At least one injured, eastbound lanes on I-580 closed
-----
A freeway shooting prompted the closure of all lanes on eastbound Interstate 580 at Edwards Avenue in Oakland on Saturday, according to the California Highway Patrol.At least one person was injured in the shooting, said Sgt. Andrew Barclay, a CHP spokesperson. Authorities are still investigating the shooting, he added.
