Alaska Airlines says tech issues prompted FAA ground stop
-----
The Federal Aviation Administration temporarily issued a ground stop for all Alaska Airlines flights across the U.S. on Wednesday morning.The ground stop was issued just after 7:30 a.m. and lifted about an hour later, according to the FAA's air traffic control system command center.
A spokesperson for Alaska Airlines said in a statement that the airline experienced "an issue while performing an upgrade to the system that calculates our weight and balance."
