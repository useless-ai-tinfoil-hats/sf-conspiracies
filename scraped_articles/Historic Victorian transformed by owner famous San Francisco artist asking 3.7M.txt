Historic Victorian transformed by owner, famous San Francisco artist, asking $3.7M
-----
Welcome to 390 Page St., a grand Italianate Victorian, built circa 1875. In the 1980s and '90s, its original bones were restored — and then embellished — by a famous San Francisco artist. The resulting interior, still present, evokes the meticulous craftsmanship, wild creativity and over-the-top opulence of San Francisco's past. This home is for sale now for the first time in 24 years, asking $3.7 million.Historical records show the home was originally built on spec by Pierre B.
