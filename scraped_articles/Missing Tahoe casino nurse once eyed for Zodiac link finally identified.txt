Missing Tahoe casino nurse, once eyed for Zodiac link, finally identified
-----
The remains of a long-missing Tahoe nurse, who is sometimes linked to the Zodiac Killer by amateur sleuths, have finally been identified.Donna Lass, 25, went missing on Sept. 6, 1970, after leaving a late shift at the Sahara Tahoe casino, now the Golden Nugget, in Stateline, Nevada, where she worked as a first aid nurse.
