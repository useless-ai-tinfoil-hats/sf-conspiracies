Oakland man pleads guilty to attempting to smuggle weapons to Middle East
-----
An Oakland man pleaded guilty Friday to attempting to smuggle weapons to the Sultanate of Oman by hiding them inside vehicles being shipped through the Port of Oakland, according to federal prosecutors.Fares Abdo Al Eyani, 40, pleaded guilty to one count of conspiring to export defense articles and seven counts of attempting to export defense articles, according to the U.S. Attorney's Office of the Northern District of California.
