‘It’s too devastating’: Double burglary pushes popular Oakland restaurant to brink of closure
-----
Feeling, "heartbroken and angry," one of Oakland's most recognizable chefs is considering closing his 10-year-old restaurant.Alamar chef-owner Nelson German posted a video on Instagram on Friday, stating he may close his Dominican restaurant following two overnight burglaries. The break-ins, which resulted in the loss of top-shelf liquor and kitchen equipment, put extra strain on the restaurant at a time of decreased foot traffic and skyrocketing expenses, German said.
