Waymo must wait to learn whether it can expand robotaxi operations in Bay Area, L.A.
-----
The California Public Utilities Commission will hold off on deciding whether to allow Waymo's driverless ride-hailing expansion to the Peninsula and Los Angeles County until at least mid-June.The commission's Consumer Protection and Enforcement Division issued the 120-day suspension of Waymo's expansion request on Tuesday, meaning the state regulator won't vote on the issue until after June 19 at the earliest.
