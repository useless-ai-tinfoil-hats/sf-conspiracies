‘It’s going to be so different’: Castro Theatre fans bid farewell before temporary closure
-----
Emotions were high during the last picture show at the Castro Theatre.The screening on Sunday, Feb. 4, a singalong to the 1982 Blake Edwards comedy "Victor/Victoria," was the final program before the theater closes for 14-16 months (or possibly longer) for the 101-year-old movie palace's long-anticipated — and controversial — renovation.
