UC system is prioritizing Calif. students, and this campus fell slightly short
-----
The University of California reported that it enrolled its highest number of in-state students in seven years at the start of the current academic year, but one Bay Area campus fell below the system's average.According to data released on Friday, 83.4% of all undergraduate students enrolled last fall across the UC system were in-state residents, the highest number since 2016. The 194,571 undergraduate California students also marked a 2% increase from fall 2022.
