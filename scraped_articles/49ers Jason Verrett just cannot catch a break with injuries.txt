49ers' Jason Verrett just cannot catch a break with injuries
-----
For the sixth time in his eight-season NFL career, 49ers cornerback Jason Verrett has suffered a season-ending injury.Verrett, who was called up from the Niners' practice squad on Christmas, tore his rotator cuff on Thursday when he landed on his shoulder after jumping for a ball, according to coach Kyle Shanahan. The 32-year-old was expected to play Sunday's season finale against the Rams.
