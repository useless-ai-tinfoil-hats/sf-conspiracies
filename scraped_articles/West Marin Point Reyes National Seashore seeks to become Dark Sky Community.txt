West Marin, Point Reyes National Seashore seeks to become Dark Sky Community
-----
In West Marin, a group of environmental activists is determined to protect the region's view of the night sky.The group, DarkSky West Marin, has applied for certification with DarkSky International, a nonprofit based in Tucson, Arizona, that aims to "restore the nighttime environment," according to a recent story in the San Francisco Chronicle. (The Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms.)
