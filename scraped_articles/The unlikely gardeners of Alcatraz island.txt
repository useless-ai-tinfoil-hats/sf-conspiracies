The unlikely gardeners of Alcatraz island
-----
Among the rare roses, daisies and lush succulents, the blue lupines in the Alcatraz gardens may not stick out to wandering tourists. But they're the only plants on the island that have passed through three security checkpoints, several layers of concrete walls and the watchful gazes of armed guards.That's because the flowers first sprouted in the world's largest women's prison, in Chowchilla, California. Their two-year journey — from seed to stalk, prison to park — is the unlikely result
