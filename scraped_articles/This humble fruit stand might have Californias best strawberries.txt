This humble fruit stand might have California’s best strawberries
-----
A Laotian immigrant who came to California in 1983 has been growing strawberries on the same 4-acre parcel for the past 20 seasons.It's become a huge hit in the Central Valley.
Chai Saephan owns Phan's Strawberries just outside of Visalia, California. It has perfect five-star Google and Yelp ratings and customers flock to him for an antidote to bland summer snacks.
His secret? Saephan knows how to keep the strawberries "on the vine till the perfect time." Read more »
