49ers will host Packers on Saturday after Green Bay routs Dallas
-----
It has been almost a constant throughout the years: the San Francisco 49ers and the Green Bay Packers meeting in the playoffs. But who saw the record-breaking 10th playoff meeting between the marquee NFL franchises happening this year?The 49ers (12-5), the top seed in the NFC, will host the Packers at 5:15 p.m. Saturday in the divisional round because seventh-seeded Green Bay stunned second-seeded Dallas 48-32 in a wild-card game Sunday.
