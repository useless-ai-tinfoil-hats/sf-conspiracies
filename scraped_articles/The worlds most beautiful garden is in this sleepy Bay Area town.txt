The world's most beautiful garden is in this sleepy Bay Area town
-----
For as long as people have roamed the planet, we have tried to quantify beauty, whether it's applying the golden ratio, or more recently, counting likes. In the world of travel, that measurement is Tripadvisor rankings.Titan Travel, a global tour company, recently sifted through Tripadvisor's data and deemed the diminutive Ruth Bancroft Garden in Walnut Creek as the most beautiful, as reported by Travel + Leisure.
