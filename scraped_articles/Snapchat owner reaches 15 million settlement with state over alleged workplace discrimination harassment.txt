Snapchat owner reaches $15 million settlement with state over alleged workplace discrimination, harassment
-----
California's Civil Rights Department reached a $15 million settlement with Snapchat's parent company, Snap Inc., over alleged mistreatment of female employees.The settlement, which must be approved by the court, stems from a three-year investigation into claims of equal pay violations, sexual harassment, employment discrimination and retaliation at Snap Inc.
