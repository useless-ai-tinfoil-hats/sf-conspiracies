Is San Francisco dumping its homelessness problems on its last remaining Black neighborhood?
-----
On Thursday, San Francisco released its full point-in-time count report, detailing what was discovered about the city's homeless population during a single day in January. This snapshot in time, conducted every two years, provides San Francisco with a better understanding of who is homeless in the city and where, and helps state and federal agencies determine where funding should go.One neighborhood stands out.
