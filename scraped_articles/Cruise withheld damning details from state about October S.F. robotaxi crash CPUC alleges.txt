Cruise withheld damning details from state about October S.F. robotaxi crash, CPUC alleges
-----
The California Public Utilities Commission alleges that Cruise withheld details of the Oct. 2 crash in San Francisco involving one of its robotaxis, then misled the public about its cooperation with regulators after news of its California suspension became public.The PUC, which regulates commercial robotaxi service in the state, included the allegations in a Dec. 1 ruling ordering Cruise to appear at a Feb. 6 evidentiary hearing to respond to the commission's claims.
