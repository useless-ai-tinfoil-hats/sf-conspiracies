Police warn of pickpocketing ring targeting Costco shoppers in Oregon
-----
A pickpocketing ring is targeting Costco shoppers at various stores along the West Coast, according to authorities.The Portland Police Bureau posted a YouTube video on Thursday of surveillance footage showing three men grabbing a 93-year-old man's wallet from his back pocket as he was walking to his vehicle at a Costco parking lot. Later in the video, the same men are seen attempting to pickpocket other shoppers.
