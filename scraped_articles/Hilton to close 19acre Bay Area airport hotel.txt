Hilton to close 19-acre Bay Area airport hotel
-----
A major Bay Area hotel by an airport is closing its doors.The Hilton on Hegenberger Road, near the San Francisco Bay Oakland International Airport, announced it will close its 19-acre hotel by the end of the summer.
A Hilton spokesperson confirmed to SFGATE that the hotel's ownership has decided to close the Oakland location and it will officially shut down Aug. 28. Some employees may be able to transfer to a different Hilton location, the company said.
