WeWork goes bankrupt, files to abandon 7 Bay Area leases
-----
WeWork, the company that rents temporary office space to the laptop class, declared bankruptcy Monday in another blow to the Bay Area office market.The move caps off a tumultuous saga that saw the company land a sky-high valuation in 2019, fail a public listing on the stock market, take on billions of dollars in debt, oust its high-profile founder, finally go public and then drop into insolvency.
