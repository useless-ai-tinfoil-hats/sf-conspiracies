A first taste of Tiana’s Palace, Disneyland’s first new restaurant in years
-----
If you've ever dreamed of trying Tiana's famous gumbo from "The Princess and the Frog," well, you're almost there. Disneyland opened Tiana's Palace on Thursday — and the food is as close to true New Orleans cuisine as you're going to find in New Orleans Square (or, likely, anywhere nearby).I went to Wednesday's media preview of Tiana's Palace and was able to try several of the menu items.
