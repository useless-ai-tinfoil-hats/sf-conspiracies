Oakland police rescue young human trafficking victim, arrested alleged trafficker
-----
A tip from a national hotline led Oakland police to locate a girl who had been trafficked and arrest a person who allegedly was responsible.The Oakland Police Department said it received a tip from the National Human Trafficking Hotline about a potential trafficking victim located within Oakland. Working together with the Riverside County Anti-Human Trafficking Taskforce, police said they conducted an investigation and successfully located the girl.
