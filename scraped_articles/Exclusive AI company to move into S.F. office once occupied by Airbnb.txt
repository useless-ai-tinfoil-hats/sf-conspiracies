Exclusive: AI company to move into S.F. office once occupied by Airbnb
-----
Artificial intelligence firm Scale AI is scaling up in San Francisco. The company, which is based at 155 5th St., has signed a new lease that's more than three times the size of its current 50,000-square-foot downtown office.Multiple real estate market participants have confirmed to the Chronicle that Scale AI will be taking a chunk of sublease space — between 170,000 and 180,000 square feet — at 650 Townsend St., which was part of Airbnb's once sprawling Showplace Square headquarters
