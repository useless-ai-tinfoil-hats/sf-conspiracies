Top dumpling shop arrives, plus more Peninsula and South Bay restaurant openings
-----
September was an exciting month for Peninsula and South Bay diners. The region got the first Bay Area outpost of a famous Taiwanese boba shop (and the lines to show for it), as well as a location of a popular San Francisco dumpling restaurant. There are also new spots to slurp hand-pulled noodles, try vegan Chinese pastries and dine on caviar-topped nigiri.Read on for 10 new openings on the Peninsula and in the South Bay.
