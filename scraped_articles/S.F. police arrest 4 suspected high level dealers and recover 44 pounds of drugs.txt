S.F. police arrest 4 suspected ‘high level’ dealers and recover 44 pounds of drugs
-----
San Francisco police arrested four people suspected to be "high level" drug dealers and recovered more than 44 pounds of illegal narcotics in their investigation, according to law enforcement officials.Following a monthslong investigation, San Francisco Police Department narcotics officers identified suspects who they believed were selling narcotics to street-level dealers who often go to the Tenderloin, police said.
