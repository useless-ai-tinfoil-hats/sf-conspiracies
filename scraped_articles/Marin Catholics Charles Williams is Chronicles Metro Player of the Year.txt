Marin Catholic’s Charles Williams is Chronicle’s Metro Player of the Year
-----
Upon learning he was chosen the Chronicle's 2023 Metro Player of the Year, Charles Williams was at a loss for words. So instead, the multitalented Marin Catholic-Kentfield senior resorted to what comes naturally.Sharing.
"I really don't know what to say," he said. "My parents. My coaches. All my teammates. They all put me in this position. I'm just very, very grateful. I've been thoroughly supported. Throughout my career."
