Second shooting in a week at Oakland homeless camp
-----
Police are investigating a fatal stabbing and the second shooting in a week at a homeless camp, separate incidents that occurred in East Oakland.In the first incident, officers and paramedics responded to a stabbing on the 1500 block of 15th Avenue shortly after 7 a.m. Friday, the Oakland Police Department said Saturday. The victim, whose identity is being withheld pending notification of next of kin, died at the scene.
