‘KSMYAS5’: These vanity license plates were deemed too lewd for California DMV
-----
Want to tell the world you're "RACIST"? Taunt the car you pass on the highway to "KSMYAS5"? Force passersby to read "69MMHMM"?Not on the California Department of Motor Vehicles' watch.
The DMV rejected almost 7,000 requests for personalized plates in the first eight months of 2023. The Chronicle obtained the data for a look at some of the more surprising rejections, many of which are indeed frown-worthy.
