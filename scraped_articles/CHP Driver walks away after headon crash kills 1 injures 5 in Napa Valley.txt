CHP: Driver walks away after head-on crash kills 1, injures 5 in Napa Valley
-----
Authorities were searching for a driver who they say walked away after a head-on crash killed a woman and injured five other people near a Napa Valley vineyard Saturday afternoon.The crash occurred around 4:45 p.m. near Silverado Vineyards on Silverado Trail, south of Yountville Cross Road, when the driver of a Toyota Sienna minivan veered into the northbound lane, according to the California Highway Patrol.
The van crashed into a BMW X3 sport utility vehicle, the CHP said.
