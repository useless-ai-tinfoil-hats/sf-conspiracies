Driver dies after slamming into power pole in Walnut Creek, police say
-----
A driver died in Walnut Creek early Thursday after hitting a power pole on Ygnacio Valley Road near the East Bay city's downtown area, the police said.A passenger in the car was taken to a local hospital with serious injuries and is expected to survive, the Walnut Creek Police Department said in a news release.
