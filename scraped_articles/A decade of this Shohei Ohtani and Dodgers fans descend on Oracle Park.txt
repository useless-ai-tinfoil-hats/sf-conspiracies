A decade of this? Shohei Ohtani and Dodgers fans descend on Oracle Park
-----
One after another, blue-clad hitters took their cuts in the batting cage, a typical pregame ritual, but atypical was that every camera was pointed toward the grass in right field. Turns out Shohei Ohtani was playing catch.Everything Ohtani does is momentous, an international headline, so of course, many of the media members assigned to Monday night's Los Angeles Dodgers-San Francisco Giants game were going to document all things Ohtani, including the fact that he suited up at Oracle Park
