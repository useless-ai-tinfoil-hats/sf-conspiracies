Review: Jake Gyllenhaal acts superbly suspicious in ‘Presumed Innocent’
-----
"Presumed Innocent," writer-producer David E. Kelley's juicy limited-series adaptation of Scott Turow's novel, shares DNA with the taut 1990 Harrison Ford movie, but it is a very different organism.This Chicago-set, Jake Gyllenhaal-led show, premiering Wednesday, June 12, sometimes unfolds more like a David Mamet play than a Kelley series.
