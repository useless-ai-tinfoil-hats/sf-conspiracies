Man, child swept into Half Moon Bay amid sneaker wave warning
-----
A man and a young child were swept into Half Moon Bay Saturday amid a sneaker wave warning issued for parts of the California coast. The girl, who is five, was found safely; the man, 54, is still missing.The pair were reported in danger around 2 p.m. Saturday, and San Mateo County firefighters and the U.S. Coast Guard were called to Martin's Beach. Firefighters were able to rescue the girl, who was transported to a local hospital for treatment. The Coast Guard then took over the search
