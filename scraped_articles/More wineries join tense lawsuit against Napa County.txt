More wineries join tense lawsuit against Napa County
-----
Napa winery Hoopes Vineyard has been locked in a four-year legal battle with Napa County over its right to host wine tastings. Now, two additional Napa Valley wineries — Smith-Madrone and Summit Lake Vineyards — have joined Hoopes in a lawsuit that calls the county's enforcement of winery regulations into question.The new motions, filed in Napa County Superior Court on Thursday, illustrate mounting frustration against the county, which has notably increased its winery code enforcement
