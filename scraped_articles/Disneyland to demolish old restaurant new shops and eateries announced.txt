Disneyland to demolish old restaurant, new shops and eateries announced
-----
A longtime Downtown Disney restaurant will soon be met by the wrecking ball, although new restaurants and shops are coming to revive dead spaces in the shopping mall outside Disneyland Park.On Thursday, Disneyland filed a permit to demolish the two-story Tortilla Jo's building. The permit indicates that only the basement level will be spared.
