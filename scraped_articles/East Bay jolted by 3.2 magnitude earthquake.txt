East Bay jolted by 3.2 magnitude earthquake
-----
A 3.2 magnitude earthquake jolted parts of the East Bay on Thursday morning, according to data from the U.S. Geological Survey.The quake struck about 7 miles east of Fremont at around 9:35 a.m., the USGS reported, with an epicenter near the northern portion of Mission Peak Regional Preserve, a hiking area near Ohlone College.
