How a shrine to the 49ers' Joe Montana ended up on Kauai
-----
Tiki Iniki opened only 10 years ago on Kauai's north shore, but it's stocked with Tiki lore: a wooden drummer sculpture from Disneyland's Enchanted Tiki Room, a lofty bar with rare, vintage mugs and a chandelier from a Hawaiian resort made famous by an Elvis Presley movie before a hurricane destroyed it.There is one piece of decor here that is conspicuously not Tiki at all.
