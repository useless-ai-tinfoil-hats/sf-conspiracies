BioMarin slashes 124 more jobs in Bay Area as wave of biotech layoffs continues
-----
BioMarin Pharmaceutical, a San Rafael biotech company known for treating extremely rare diseases, said Wednesday it's laying off 225 workers, most of whom live and work in California. The cuts come just a few months after a 170-employee layoff round in May.The company announced the new 7% cut in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission and in a WARN document, a notification required by the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act in the event of mass layoffs.
