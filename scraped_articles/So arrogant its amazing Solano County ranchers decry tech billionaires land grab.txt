‘So arrogant it’s amazing’: Solano County ranchers decry tech billionaires’ land grab
-----
With six square miles of golden rolling hills and a long stretch of waterfront land along the Sacramento River, McCormack Ranch is one of the most spectacular pieces of land in southeast Solano County; a historic farm that is home to 1,800 sheep as well as wheat, barley, alfalfa and grape vineyards.But, these days, farm owners Jeannie McCormack and Al Medvitz wonder how much longer the 125-year-old ranch can survive given it is smack in the middle of a historic agricultural district
