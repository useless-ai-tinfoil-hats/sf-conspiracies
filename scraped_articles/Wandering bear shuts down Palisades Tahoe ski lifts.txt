Wandering bear shuts down Palisades Tahoe ski lifts
-----
Skiers at Palisades Tahoe are on the lookout for a bear that has been hanging around the mountain, forcing some lifts to shutter this week. The California Department of Fish and Wildlife said the young bear may just be trying to get home.The furry visitor was first seen on Monday near the summit of Granite Chief, "looking curiously at people and the garbage bin at the top," Palisades spokesperson Patrick Lacey told SFGATE on Thursday.
