Ex-Giants manager Bruce Bochy lauds Sabean, keeps door open for Bumgarner
-----
Bruce Bochy returned to a Northern California ballpark on the same night Brian Sabean was inducted into the Bay Area Sports Hall of Fame. That seemed fitting, given their collaboration in the San Francisco Giants' memorable run of three World Series titles in five seasons."I couldn't be happier for him," Bochy said of Sabean. "This guy did an amazing job, and I was lucky to be part of it."
