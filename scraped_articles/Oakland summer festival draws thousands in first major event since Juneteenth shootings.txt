Oakland summer festival draws thousands in first major event since Juneteenth shootings
-----
With the music bumping and the smell of grilled hot dogs and barbecue in the air, thousands of people strolled, snacked and sipped along the shores of Lake Merritt on Saturday afternoon for the annual summer Lakefest Oakland.It marked the first major organized event at the popular lake since at least 15 people were shot near the northern end of the lake just three days prior, after a Juneteenth celebration devolved into a raucous sideshow.
