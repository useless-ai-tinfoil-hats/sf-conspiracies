California College of the Arts announces new president
-----
Following a nine-month search, California College of the Arts announced David C. Howse as the school's new president on Wednesday, Oct. 25.Howse is now vice president of the Office of the Arts at Emerson College in Boston, where he has worked since 2015. He is also executive director of the school's theater, ArtsEmerson, and serves as special adviser to the school president.
