Costco to open two new Bay Area locations this fall
-----
Costco has announced plans to open two new warehouses in the Bay Area this fall.The membership-based retail giant's website states that the new locations will be in Napa and Pleasanton, both set for openings in October. Among the services offered, customers can expect a bakery, pharmacy, optical center, hearing aid testing center, food court, alcohol sales, and a tire sales and installation center at each location.
