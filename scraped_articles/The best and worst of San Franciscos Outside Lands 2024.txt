The best and worst of San Francisco's Outside Lands 2024
-----
This weekend, the Outside Lands Music Festival turned Golden Gate Park into a city within a city. Local restaurants hawked food from tents and attendees flocked to stages named after San Francisco landmarks to catch some of the world's most exciting musical talents.This year was the most mainstream in recent memory, with late replacement headliner Sabrina Carpenter making a strong case for "main pop girl" status and Chappell Roan attracting what appeared to be one of the biggest crowds
