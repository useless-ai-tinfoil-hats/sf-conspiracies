Barber killed, one other injured after shooting in San Leandro shop
-----
A barber was killed and another person injured in a shooting Wednesday at a San Leandro barbershop, according to San Leandro police and people at the scene.San Leandro police said the shooting happened at a business in the 2200 block of East 14th Street just after 1 p.m., in a commercial strip a few blocks south of downtown and one block away from an elementary school.
