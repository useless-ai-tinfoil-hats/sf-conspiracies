SFPD finds missing 4-year-old girl, accuses relative of kidnapping
-----
San Francisco police have located a missing 4-year-old girl and arrested the relative accused of kidnapping her.The girl went missing from her South of Market home sometime before 1:30 a.m. Tuesday and was believed to have been abducted by a relative, Enas Birawi, according to San Francisco police. She was later found around 5:30 p.m. that same day.
