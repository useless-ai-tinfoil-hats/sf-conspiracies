CEO arrested on felony charge after attack on teen at In-N-Out
-----
The CEO of a private equity firm has been arrested after a video showed him grabbing a teen by the neck and knocking him to the floor at a Colorado In-N-Out restaurant.Lucas Kalisher of Colorado turned himself into Larimer County Jail on Tuesday following an investigation by local police, according to a Facebook post by Loveland Police Department. Kalisher was taken in on a no-bond arrest and faces a felon charge of second-degree strangulation and a misdemeanor charge of child abuse.
