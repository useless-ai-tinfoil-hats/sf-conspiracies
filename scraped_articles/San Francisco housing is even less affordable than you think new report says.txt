San Francisco housing is even less affordable than you think, new report says
-----
San Francisco's housing costs are among the highest in the country, with about a third of households considered "cost-burdened." But some researchers are arguing that measure doesn't get at the true extent of California's housing crisis.Government agencies often use cost burden estimates, which calculate what share of households pay more than 30% of their income in housing costs, to determine who qualifies for affordable housing programs and how much help to give.
