Livermore police searching for ‘armed and dangerous’ man suspected in double killing
-----
Livermore police said Thursday that they were searching for a man suspected of killing two people in a hotel last weekend.In a release, Livermore police identified the suspect behind two fatal shootings on the morning of April 27 as Yonston Vazquez, 30, of Oakland.
Police said Vazquez was responsible for killing 26-year-old Lizabeth Murillo-Rodriguez of Oakland and 21-year-old Christopher Vera of Hayward.
