Exclusive: Monterey Jazz Festival names new artistic director
-----
The Monterey Jazz Festival, the world's longest continuously running jazz festival, has announced Darin Atwater as its next artistic director. He succeeds Tim Jackson, who is stepping down next year after a 32-year tenure."I applaud the current MJF leadership for their work in sustaining a gathering that fosters the perpetual spirit of jazz," Atwater said in a statement.
