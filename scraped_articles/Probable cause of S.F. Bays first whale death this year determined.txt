Probable cause of S.F. Bay’s first whale death this year determined
-----
The gray whale that recently washed ashore at an Alameda beach — the first reported whale death in San Francisco Bay this year — likely died due to blunt force trauma from a vessel strike, scientists said Tuesday.A necropsy conducted Saturday by scientists at the California Academy of Sciences and the Marine Mammal Center found that the 40-foot female adult whale had "full stomach contents and injuries consistent with blunt force trauma," according to a news release.
