'Building will be a total loss': 70 firefighters battle massive Oakland warehouse fire
-----
A fire that erupted in West Oakland on Wednesday night left a warehouse gutted, officials said.The Oakland Fire Department said on social media that it was called to the fire on the 1700 block of 24th Street at 10:15 p.m. The building housed a roofing company called Pacific Supply Company, department spokesperson Michael Hunt said.
"The building will be a total loss," Hunt said.
Some 70 firefighters battled flames at the site.
