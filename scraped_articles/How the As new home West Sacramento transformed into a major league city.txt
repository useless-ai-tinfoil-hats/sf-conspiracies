How the A’s new home, West Sacramento, transformed into a major league city
-----
The Oakland A's are baseball's biggest losers. But their new temporary home — West Sacramento — is a winner.No California city has had a better 21st century than West Sacramento. The municipality has grown in population and prosperity with striking speed, even as California has stagnated.
