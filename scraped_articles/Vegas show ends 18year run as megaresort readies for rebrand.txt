Vegas show ends 18-year run as megaresort readies for rebrand
-----
A popular Beatles show is ending its Las Vegas run after nearly two decades as the Mirage hotel prepares for its rebrand."The Beatles LOVE" debuted at the Mirage in 2006 and is part of the Las Vegas skyline: Sgt. Pepper-era portraits of the band stretch across the top of the hotel to advertise the show. The show was put on by Cirque du Soleil and featured the music of John, Paul, George and Ringo.
