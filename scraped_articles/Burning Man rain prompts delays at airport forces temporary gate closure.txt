Burning Man rain prompts delays at airport, forces temporary gate closure
-----
Rain showers ahead of this year's Burning Man caused delays for attendees trying to enter the Black Rock Desert on Saturday, though the weather was expected to be much calmer than the storms that trapped last year's visitors for days.Organizers closed the gate to Black Rock City — built and dismantled for Burning Man each year — around 4 a.m. Saturday as rain began to fall. The temporary airport at Black Rock City was also closed to flights, though organizers said they expected the rain
