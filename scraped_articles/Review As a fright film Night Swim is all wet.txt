Review: As a fright film, ‘Night Swim’ is all wet
-----
Just when you thought it was safe to go back in the water, the first horror movie of 2024 is about a killer swimming pool.Silly as its premise is, "Night Swim" earns points for giving its characters clear and consistent motivations. Demerits, though, for giving them too much unintentionally laughable dialogue. The underwater photography is suitably eerie in and of itself, but 90% of the scary stuff takes place beneath the surface and is too murky to really appreciate.
