Ex-deputy sentenced in 1994 killing found dead in San Quentin cell
-----
A former Los Angeles County sheriff's deputy who fatally shot a supermarket manager during a 1994 robbery has been found dead in his cell at San Quentin State Prison, according to state prison authorities.Stephen M. Redd, 78, was found unresponsive in his cell at 12:38 p.m. Thursday and pronounced dead by medical staff, the California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation reported in a news release. The Marin County Coroner's Office will determine the cause of death.
