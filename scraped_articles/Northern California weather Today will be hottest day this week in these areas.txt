Northern California weather: Today will be hottest day this week in these areas
-----
An area of high pressure is expected to nudge toward the West Coast on Wednesday, and it will give temperatures a boost across Northern California.The hottest day so far this year is on tap for the Sacramento Valley, where temperatures will be 10 to 18 degrees above normal. Redding may hit 100 degrees on Wednesday for the first time since September.
