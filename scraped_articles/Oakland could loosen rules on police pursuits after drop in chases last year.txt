Oakland could loosen rules on police pursuits after drop in chases last year
-----
Oakland's City Council ordered a review of the police department's pursuit policies this week that could result in officers getting more leeway to initiate pursuits amid a recent drop in police pursuits and enduring concerns about crime.The council directed the Police Commission to review its police-chase policies, even as some council members acknowledged the dangers of pursuits on city streets.
