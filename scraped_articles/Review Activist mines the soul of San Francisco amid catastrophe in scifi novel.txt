Review: Activist mines the soul of San Francisco amid catastrophe in sci-fi novel
-----
Apocalypse stories are the oldest subgenre of science fiction, and probably the most popular. Every generation has its doomsday prophets, and today most of them are betting on climate change. Their stories are full of melting glaciers, rising seas, freak storms and ecological collapse.
