Yosemite airport adds TSA-approved food to take on flights
-----
Just in time for Yosemite National Park's busy summer travel season, its nearest airport has added a variety of new food options.Fresno Yosemite International Airport is piloting a new food truck program that offers "approved travel-ready containers to enjoy prior to boarding flights or to carry onboard the aircraft for an in-flight meal." The food can be purchased outside the terminal and taken through TSA.
