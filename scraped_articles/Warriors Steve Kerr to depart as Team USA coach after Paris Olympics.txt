Warriors’ Steve Kerr to depart as Team USA coach after Paris Olympics
-----
DETROIT — Steve Kerr of the Golden State Warriors plans to step down as head coach of Team USA following the 2024 Olympics in Paris.Kerr, who won a gold medal as a player for Team USA at the 1986 FIBA World Championship, succeeded Gregg Popovich as head coach in 2022. The men's national team won their first nine games in international play under the 58-year-old's leadership.
