The failed colossus that was supposed to connect California to aliens
-----
If aliens ever descended upon our world, it might be helpful for them to know where exactly on the planet they've landed. Thankfully, Los Angeles has already thought of that.A cosmic homing beacon is just one of the many failed ambitions of downtown LA's massive Triforium, a 60-ton boondoggle that has befuddled observers for almost 50 years.
