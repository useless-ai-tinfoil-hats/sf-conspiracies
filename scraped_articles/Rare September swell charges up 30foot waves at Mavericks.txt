Rare September swell charges up 30-foot waves at Mavericks
-----
A rare September swell barreled through Mavericks on Tuesday, stirring up 30-foot waves in what local surfers hope is an omen of a strong season to come following a couple of weak surfing years at the famous big-wave break.Mavericks breaks on a reef off Pillar Point in Half Moon Bay but only materializes during high-energy swells, which typically roll in between November and April.
