Construction workers in Piedmont robbed at gunpoint, police say
-----
Two individuals robbed construction workers at gunpoint in Piedmont, California, on Tuesday, and the heist occurred in broad daylight, the police said.The thieves arrived at a property on the 200 block of Estates Drive at 11:40 a.m. on Sept. 5, driving a white Lexus IS 350 with tinted windows and backing the vehicle into a driveway, the Piedmont Police Department said in a news release on Wednesday. They hopped out of the vehicle, with one of them holding a gun, according to the release.
