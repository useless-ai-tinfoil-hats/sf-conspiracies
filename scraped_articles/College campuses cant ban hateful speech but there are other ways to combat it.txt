College campuses can’t ban hateful speech but there are other ways to combat it
-----
Above all, the First Amendment means that all ideas — even deeply offensive ones — can be expressed on a college campus. There are certain views that we all hope never would be voiced, but the central premise of the First Amendment is that it is worse to give the government the power to outlaw particular ideas than to allow them to be voiced.
