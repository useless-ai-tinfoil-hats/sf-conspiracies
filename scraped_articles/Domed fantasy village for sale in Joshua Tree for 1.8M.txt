Domed fantasy village for sale in Joshua Tree for $1.8M
-----
Part fantasy desert compound, part funky Flintstones homage, 6920 Bonita Ave. is an eye-catching property in Joshua Tree, California, for sale for $1.8 million.Bonita Domes is named for its distinctive roofline. Each dome encapsulates a separate room, and in its current configuration, there are two bedrooms, a kitchen, a living area and two bathrooms, as well as a separate self-contained "studio living quarter."
The concept for these domes is credited to Nader Khalili, an architect
