California pro rock climber gets life in prison for Yosemite rape
-----
A California rock climber who used brutal force to sexually abuse a woman in Yosemite National Park multiple times in 2016 was sentenced to life in prison on Tuesday, officials said.Charles Barrett, 40, was found guilty in February of two counts of aggravated sexual abuse and one count of abusive sexual contact. The crimes were prosecuted on the federal level because the park is federal land.
U.S. Attorney Phillip A. Talbert said in a news release that Barrett deserved a life
