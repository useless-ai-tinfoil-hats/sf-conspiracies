English cast for 'The Boy and the Heron' includes Mark Hamill and more stars
-----
The English-language voice cast for Hayao Miyazaki's highly anticipated new film, "The Boy and the Heron," was unveiled Tuesday, to the excitement of Studio Ghibli fans.The film's premise is similar to that of 2001's "Spirited Away," but Miyazaki takes the story of a child traveling to a magical world to save their family in an entirely new direction. "The Boy and the Heron" is scheduled to hit theaters Dec. 8.
