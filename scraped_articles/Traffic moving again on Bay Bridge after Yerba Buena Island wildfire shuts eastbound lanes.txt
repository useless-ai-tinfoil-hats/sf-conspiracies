Traffic moving again on Bay Bridge after Yerba Buena Island wildfire shuts eastbound lanes
-----
A grass fire on Yerba Buena Island prompted authorities to shut down eastbound traffic Saturday afternoon on the Bay Bridge for about two hours.After all eastbound lanes were closed at roughly 2:30 p.m. Saturday in response to an "uncontrolled wildfire" on the island, traffic eventually returned around 4:45 p.m., with all lanes reopened by 6 p.m., said fire department spokesperson Capt. Justin Schorr.
