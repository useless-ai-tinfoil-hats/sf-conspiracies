He spent millions restoring this S.F. hotel for tourists. Now he wants it to become homeless housing
-----
The online reviews of the Best Western Red Coach Inn, located on the edge of San Francisco's beleaguered Tenderloin district, are unsparing."If your room is near the alley you get to listen to screaming all night and smell the outside toilet," one reviewer wrote on TripAdvisor in November.
"I don't know how the hotel does it," another said in June. "The neighborhood is SO bad! Feces in the street (I stepped in some), homeless people everywhere, trash, smelly, loud; you name it."
