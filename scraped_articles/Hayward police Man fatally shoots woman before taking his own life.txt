Hayward police: Man fatally shoots woman before taking his own life
-----
A man fatally shot a woman in Hayward before taking his own life over the weekend, according to police.Responding to a report of gunshot victims shortly before 6:30 p.m. Saturday, officers found the wounded pair in the front seat of a vehicle on the 3000 block of West Winton Avenue, the Hayward Police Department reported Sunday.
