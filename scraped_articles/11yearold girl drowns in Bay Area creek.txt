11-year-old girl drowns in Bay Area creek
-----
The body of a child who drowned on Memorial Day in Alameda Creek in Fremont has been identified as an 11-year-old girl, the Alameda County Sheriff's Office said Tuesday.Dana Nino Gonzalez died after she became caught in the hole of structure that controls flow and the attempts to rescue her failed, the sheriff's office said.
Dana was with her family in the creek next to Niles Canyon Road, just north of Mission Boulevard, according to NBC Bay Area (KNTV-TV) and the sheriff's office.
