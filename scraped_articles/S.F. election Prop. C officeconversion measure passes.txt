S.F. election: Prop. C office-conversion measure passes
-----
Mayor London Breed's ballot proposal to make it more profitable for developers to turn empty downtown San Francisco offices into housing has passed.With 16,400 ballots left to count, Proposition C led with 52.95% of the vote as of Saturday afternoon.
The measure exempts office-to-housing conversion projects from the city's real estate transfer tax the first time they're sold to new owners. The transfer tax varies from 0.5% to 6%.
