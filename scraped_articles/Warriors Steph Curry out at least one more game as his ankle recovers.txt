Warriors’ Steph Curry out at least one more game as his ankle recovers
-----
DALLAS — Stephen Curry is closing in on a return to play for the Golden State Warriors.Re-evaluated Tuesday after missing two games with a sprained left ankle suffered Thursday in a loss to the Chicago Bulls, the guard is making "good progress," prompting a clearance for on-court workouts in the Bay Area, the team announced.
Curry is expected to join the Warriors on Friday for practice in Los Angeles.
