Video captures brawl breaking out over a seat on flight to SFO
-----
A social media post captured two flyers en route to San Francisco International Airport engaging in a physical altercation on Taiwanese airline EVA Air.The dispute began after an argument erupted over a seat change, a spokesperson for the airline told CNN. Three flight attendants intervened to break apart the passengers on the May 7 flight.
Approximately three hours into the 11-hour flight, a passenger decided to change seats due to discomfort caused by a coughing neighbor.
