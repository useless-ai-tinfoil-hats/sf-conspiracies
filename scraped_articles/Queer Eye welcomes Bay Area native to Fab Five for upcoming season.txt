‘Queer Eye’ welcomes Bay Area native to Fab Five for upcoming season
-----
A Bay Area native is set to join the Fab Five for the upcoming season of "Queer Eye."Born in Modesto, interior designer and HGTV star Jeremiah Brent will be the newest host alongside Antoni Porowski, Jonathan Van Ness, Karamo Brown and Tan France for season 9 of the popular Netflix makeover series.
Production for the upcoming season is scheduled to begin in Las Vegas this spring.
