19 new books to cozy up with this winter
-----
Winter is a season of transition, and the spirit certainly applies to the new batch of books readers can look forward to. As 2023 ends and 2024 begins, unsung and underrepresented writers are coming to the fore, with titles that offer fresh perspectives on history, community and all that it means to be human.As a whole, these books reflect voices, in both fiction and nonfiction, who are unafraid to confront and record human foibles, embracing the nature of our fleeting, imperfect lives.
