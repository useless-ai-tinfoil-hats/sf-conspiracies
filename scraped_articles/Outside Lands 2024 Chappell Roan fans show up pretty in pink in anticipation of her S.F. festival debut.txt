Outside Lands 2024: Chappell Roan fans show up pretty in pink in anticipation of her S.F. festival debut
-----
This year, Golden Gate Park is looking a little more pink than usual.Outside Lands attendees have been showing off their boldest looks that celebrate their favorite artists performing at the three-day music festival over the weekend. Fans of breakout pop star Chappell Roan were particularly vibrant on Sunday, Aug. 11, ahead of her highly anticipated performance on Land Ends Stage, decked in pink and cowgirl aesthetic, a nod to the singer's pop ballad "Pink Pony Club."
