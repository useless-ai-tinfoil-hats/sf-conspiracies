Climbing guide falls to death on Yosemite's El Capitan
-----
A Korean climbing guide died from a fall on Yosemite National Park's El Capitan on Sept. 28, according to multiple reports posted online.The climber was leading a group and rappelling from Sickle Ledge on the Nose, according to Tom Evans, who was the first to report death in his blog, ElCap Reports, which covers climbing activity on El Capitan, the granite formation that draws rock climbers from around the globe.
