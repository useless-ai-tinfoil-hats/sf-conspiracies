Oakland merchants to strike Tuesday to demand protections from crime
-----
Several Oakland merchants plan a one-day strike next week in response to rising crime and the city missing a deadline to secure millions of dollars in crime-prevention funds from the state, a prominent community leader said Monday.More than 200 businesses plan to take part in the strike, which will take place Tuesday and commence with a 10 a.m. news conference at Le Cheval restaurant at 1007 Clay St., according to a flyer distributed by the NAACP.
