Stanford basketball will play on Tara VanDerveer Court at Maples Pavilion
-----
Tara VanDerveer's Stanford women's basketball teams won 531 games at Maples Pavilion. The court at Maples now will bear the name of the winningest coach in the history of college basketball.Stanford announced Thursday that Tara VanDerveer Court will grace Maples. VanDerveer, who retired last month, picked up 1,216 victories in her 45 seasons as a head coach, with 1,065 of those wins coming in 38 seasons leading the Cardinal.
