Bone Thugs-N-Harmony to celebrate 30th anniversary in San Francisco
-----
Bone Thugs-N-Harmony, one of the most influential rap groups from the 1990s, are set to return to the Bay Area in March, fittingly, on "the first of the month."The pioneering rap group is scheduled to hit the Curran stage as part of its 30th anniversary celebration, an 11-show national tour ending in Beverly Hills on May 31. The San Francisco show is a one-night-only engagement for this year's Noise Pop Festival, the annual, multi-venue music event running from Feb. 22 through March 3.
