Review: Adam Sandler goes dramatic while on a spaceship with his giant spider buddy
-----
"Spaceman" at least deserves credit for being strange. It stars Adam Sandler as an astronaut who spends most of the movie in heart-to-heart conversations with a gigantic spider, and yet it's not a comedy. Or at least, it's not meant to be a comedy.If only it were better, it would be the meditative, soulful experience it was intended to be. If only it were a little bit worse, it would be the funniest Adam Sandler movie in years.
