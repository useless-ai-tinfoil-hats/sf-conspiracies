Award-winning ‘Check, Please!’ TV show returns to Bay Area
-----
A new season of the Bay Area's most famous food show is set to return on Sept. 7."Check, Please! Every week, the show's host, Leslie Sbrocco, brings on three local foodies to share their favorite restaurants with the group.
