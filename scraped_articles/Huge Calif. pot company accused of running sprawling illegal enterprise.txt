Huge Calif. pot company accused of running 'sprawling,' 'illegal' enterprise
-----
California's largest cannabis retailer is facing new allegations of engaging in an extensive "criminal enterprise" and running a network of illegal cannabis stores, according to a civil lawsuit filed by a former executive of the company.The lawsuit accuses the owners of Stiiizy, the largest cannabis retailer in California, of "running a sprawling enterprise of eighteen (18) or more unlicensed, illegal cannabis dispensaries."
