California, Las Vegas music festivals canceled over weekend due to high winds
-----
The third day of a Southern California music festival was canceled early Sunday night due to high winds, just two days after another festival in Las Vegas was called off for the same reason.At around 8 p.m. Sunday, organizers of the BeachLife Festival in Redondo Beach posted a message on Instagram letting attendees know that the remaining shows in the festival lineup had been canceled because of dangerously high winds.
