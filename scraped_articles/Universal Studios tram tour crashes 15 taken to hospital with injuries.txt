Universal Studios tram tour crashes, 15 taken to hospital with injuries
-----
Fifteen people needed hospitalization after a tram on Universal Studios' famous backlot tour crashed, the Los Angeles Fire Department said.The tram crashed shortly after 9 p.m. Saturday at the theme park on the 3900 block of Lankershim Blvd in Studio City. Fifteen people with "minor injuries" were transported to local hospitals, LAFD said on social media. California Highway Patrol is taking over the investigation into why the tram crashed.
