Oakland dive bar beloved by UC Berkeley students is closing after 3 decades
-----
The Graduate, an Oakland dive bar frequented by UC Berkeley graduate students, is closing after over three decades. The Rockridge bar known for its free popcorn, jukebox and student discounts recently shared the news on its Facebook page that its last day of operation will be June 30."We are going to close because I am almost 86 years old and I would like to retire," said the Graduate co-owner Javad Parsa.
