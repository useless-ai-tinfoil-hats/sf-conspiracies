Will Bay Area group help handball gain a foothold in the United States?
-----
Interest in team handball in the United States is as fleeting as the Olympics themselves. A hot topic for a couple weeks, it drops out of sight and out of mind the rest of the time.On the surface, it's the kind of sport that should draw American audiences. It's fast and high-scoring and is as physical as any sport played in this country.
But it's also one of the two Olympic ball sports — table tennis is the other — in which the United States has never won a medal.
