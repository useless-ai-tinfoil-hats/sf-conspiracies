Oasis reunites after 15 years: Rediscover how a San Francisco woman saved the band
-----
In a much-anticipated reunion after a 15-year hiatus, Noel and Liam Gallagher announced that Oasis will embark on a tour next year. "This is it, this is happening," read a post on the band's social media accounts on Tuesday, Aug. 27. The announcement confirmed 14 shows across the U.K. and Ireland, with hints of potential North American dates forthcoming."The guns have fallen silent. The stars have aligned. The great wait is over," the band added. "Come see. It will not be televised."
