A fire ravaged this acclaimed barbecue spot. Here’s where it’s reopening
-----
A top Bay Area barbecue spot that burned in a fire will reopen this week at a new permanent location.Horn Barbecue is reopening Friday, April 26, at 464 Eighth St. in Oakland, replacing another Horn restaurant, Matty's Old Fashioned, a spokesperson for chef-owner Matt Horn confirmed. The move comes after a fire destroyed the former Horn Barbecue location on Mandela Parkway in West Oakland.
