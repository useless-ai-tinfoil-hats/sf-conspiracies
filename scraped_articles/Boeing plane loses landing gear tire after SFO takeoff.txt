Boeing plane loses landing gear tire after SFO takeoff
-----
A United flight lost a tire while taking off from San Francisco International Airport Thursday morning, and it fell on an airport employee parking lot.United Airlines Flight 35 to Kansai International Airport in Japan lost a portion of its landing gear tire during takeoff around 11:35 a.m. SFO spokesperson Doug Yakel told SFGATE that the debris fell onto an airport parking lot used by SFO staff.
