S.F. State sells investments in arms makers after deal with pro-Palestinian students
-----
San Francisco State University has pulled investments from three companies it says don't meet its human rights standards after pressure from pro-Palestinian student activists.The moves resulted in changes to the university's $163 million investment portfolio.
The San Francisco State University Foundation confirmed Wednesday that it has sold its Lockheed Martin corporate bond position and stock positions in Leonardo, an Italian multinational defense company, and Palantir
