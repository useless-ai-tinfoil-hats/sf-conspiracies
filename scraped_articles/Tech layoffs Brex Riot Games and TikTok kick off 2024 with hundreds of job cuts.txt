Tech layoffs: Brex, Riot Games and TikTok kick off 2024 with hundreds of job cuts
-----
The beginning of the new year has brought little relief for tech workers as job cuts persist across various sectors of the economy.On Tuesday, Brex, the artificial intelligence-powered expense management company valued at $12.3 billion two years ago, announced a workforce reduction of 282 people, accounting for approximately 20% of its staff.
