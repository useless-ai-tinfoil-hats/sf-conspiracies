Golden Gate Park is getting its first new memorial in more than 30 years
-----
It's taken eight years, coordination between multiple groups, and countless planning sessions, but San Francisco's Golden Gate Park will soon open its first new memorial since the National AIDS Memorial Grove began in 1991.Bay Area Young Survivors — a nonprofit support community for young people with breast cancer — has dreamt-up and funded the memorial garden, which is scheduled to open in mid-2024.
