Road to Rickwood: Why everyone needs to know American hero Bill Greason
-----
America needs to know about Bill Greason.We need to know that he was the first Black pitcher on the St. Louis Cardinals, five years before Bob Gibson. We need to know he fought at the Battle of Iwo Jima during World War II. We need to know he has served as a minister in Birmingham, Ala., for more than a half-century and is still preaching.
