Kaiser to lay off dozens of workers in Bay Area, Southern California
-----
Kaiser Foundation Hospitals will lay off 49 employees by November, roughly half in the Bay Area and half in Southern California, the health care provider told the California Employment Development Department last month.In a Sept. 12 letter sent to the state agency, Kaiser's Human Resources Director Christine Neubauer said 21 workers would be laid off at its Pleasanton location, seven across three Oakland locations, and 21 from Southern California locations.
