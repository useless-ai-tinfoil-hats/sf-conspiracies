BART delayed after body found on train
-----
BART trains heading toward the East Bay were delayed Tuesday after a dead body was found on a train at the Embarcadero Station, the transit agency said.The person was found about 5:30 p.m., said Anna Duckworth, a spokesperson for BART. The station was closed temporarily and reopened shortly after 6 p.m.
Duckworth said the coroner was sent to the scene to investigate and there is no indication of foul play. Details regarding the identity of the person were not immediately available.
