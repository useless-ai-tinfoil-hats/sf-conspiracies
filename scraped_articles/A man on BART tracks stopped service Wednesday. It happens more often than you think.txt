A man on BART tracks stopped service Wednesday. It happens more often than you think
-----
The masses of people pressed together, shoulder to shoulder, at downtown San Francisco BART station platforms Wednesday evening was a strange scene for the regional rail agency.But the scene, which conjured sights of pre-pandemic BART crowds, wasn't spurred by an influx of office workers commuting home for the day. Rather, it was the result of a near two-hour service delay that began after a man reportedly entered the train tracks inside the Transbay Tube.
