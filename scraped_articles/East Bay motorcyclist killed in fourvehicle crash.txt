East Bay motorcyclist killed in four-vehicle crash
-----
A motorcyclist died Thursday afternoon after being hit from behind at an Antioch intersection, police said.The man had stopped for a red light at the intersection of Prewett Ranch Drive and Deer Valley Road just after 2 p.m. Thursday, according to the Antioch Police Department. A motorist apparently driving fast and not paying attention to the road slammed into the motorcycle from behind, throwing the cyclist, police said.
