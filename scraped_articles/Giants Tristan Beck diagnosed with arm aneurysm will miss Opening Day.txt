Giants’ Tristan Beck diagnosed with arm aneurysm, will miss Opening Day
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — San Francisco Giants rotation hopeful Tristan Beck is expected to miss significant time after having been diagnosed with an upper arm aneurysm.Beck, 27, left camp in Arizona to see a vascular specialist at Stanford earlier this week after experiencing a cold numbness in his right pitching hand while throwing, manager Bob Melvin said.
"We want to make sure we get it taken care of and then he moves on from there," Melvin said.
