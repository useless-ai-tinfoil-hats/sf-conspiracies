Mountain lion kills man in Northern California for first time in 30 years
-----
LATEST March 24, 5:04 p.m. California officials confirmed that a mountain lion they euthanized in the vicinity of a fatal attack in a remote area near Georgetown was the same animal that killed a 21-year-old man Saturday afternoon.DNA samples collected by the California Department of Fish and Wildlife at the scene matched those taken from the lion's carcass, the agency's wildlife forensics laboratory found.
