The Chronicle's curated list of notable new music releases for next month.DEBUTS
Tierra Whack, 'World Wide Whack' (Interscope)
Bringing absurdity into her genre-bending musical style, the Philadelphia rapper is back with what she refers to as her debut album. Over the past few years, the artist has released a slew of one-off singles and a handful of EPs, many complete with visuals, but "World Wide Whack" will be her first full-length, cohesive project in six years.
