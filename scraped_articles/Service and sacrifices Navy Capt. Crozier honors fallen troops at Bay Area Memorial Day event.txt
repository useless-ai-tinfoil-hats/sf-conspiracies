‘Service and sacrifices’: Navy Capt. Crozier honors fallen troops at Bay Area Memorial Day event
-----
The flutter of white doves broke the silence as a hushed throng gathered in Sonoma on Monday to commemorate fallen soldiers with a moment of silence.Organized by local veterans groups, the 66th annual Sonoma Valley Joint Memorial Day Observance at Sonoma Veterans Memorial Park featured military flyovers, a rifle salute and a bugler playing taps.
