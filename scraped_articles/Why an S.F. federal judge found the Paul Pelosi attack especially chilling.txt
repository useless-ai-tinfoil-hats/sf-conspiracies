Why an S.F. federal judge found the Paul Pelosi attack especially chilling
-----
David DePape, the man convicted of breaking into the home of Rep. Nancy Pelosi and bludgeoning her husband with a hammer, was sentenced Friday to 30 years in prison, with credit for time served, by a federal judge.After a four-day trial, DePape was convicted in November of attempting to kidnap a federal official and assaulting a federal official's family member. A jury returned a verdict after less than 10 hours of deliberation.
