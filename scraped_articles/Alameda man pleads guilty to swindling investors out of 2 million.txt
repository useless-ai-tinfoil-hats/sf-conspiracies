Alameda man pleads guilty to swindling investors out of $2 million
-----
An Alameda man has pleaded guilty to swindling at least 20 investors out of $2 million by posing as a billionaire and promising large returns on real estate, federal prosecutors announced Tuesday.Long Nguyen, 35, entered guilty pleas Friday to four felony charges of wire fraud. Prosecutors said his plea agreement includes repayment of at least $1 million to his investors.
