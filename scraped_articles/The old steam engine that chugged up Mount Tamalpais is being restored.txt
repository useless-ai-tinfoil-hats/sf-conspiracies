The old steam engine that chugged up Mount Tamalpais is being restored
-----
Sometimes, it takes a while for a great notion to come to life. For years, admirers of the late, great Mount Tamalpais and Muir Woods Railway dreamed of a way to acquire and restore old No. 9, the railroad's last remaining steam locomotive, which had been rusting away in the small lumber mill town of Scotia in Humboldt County.Six years ago, a group called Friends of No. 9 bought the engine at auction from the Scotia Community Services District for $56,240.
