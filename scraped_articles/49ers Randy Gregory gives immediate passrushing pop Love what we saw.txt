49ers’ Randy Gregory gives immediate pass-rushing pop: ‘Love what we saw’
-----
A silver lining from the San Francisco 49ers' first loss of the season: pass rusher Randy Gregory's performance in his first game with the team.Gregory's debut included a sack, two tackles for loss and three quarterback hits in 26 snaps in a 19-17 loss at Cleveland.
"I love what we saw," head coach Kyle Shanahan said. "I think the more he gets with us, the more he practices, the more he understands our scheme, the better he'll get."
