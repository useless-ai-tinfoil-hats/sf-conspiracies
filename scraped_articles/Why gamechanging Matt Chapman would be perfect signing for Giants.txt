Why ‘game-changing’ Matt Chapman would be perfect signing for Giants
-----
The closer the San Francisco Giants get to spring training, the more it makes sense for them to sign free agent Matt Chapman.What Chapman provides and what the Giants need would make the third baseman a solid fit in 2024 and well beyond.
"He's an awesome guy," Giants outfielder Michael Conforto said Saturday at the team's latest FanFest tour stop in Napa. "He's a leader. I think he's capable of being an MVP someday. I think he's capable of being one of those game-changing guys."
