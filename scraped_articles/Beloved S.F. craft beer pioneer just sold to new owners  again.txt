Beloved S.F. craft beer pioneer just sold to new owners — again
-----
One of San Francisco's most revered brew pubs has new owners.Magnolia Brewing, a Haight Street staple for small-batch beers and bites, will temporarily close while it formally changes hands. Kevin Kynoch — owner of neighborhood bars Fly Bar, Fishbowl and Polo Grounds — is taking over the long-standing brew pub from Little Lion Beverages. Joining him is business partner Brandon Phillips of 21st Amendment Brewing and Brian Reccow, who managed Magnolia Brewing through a previous bankruptcy
