This Italian sandwich shop creates a frenzy wherever it goes. Now it's coming to California.
-----
One of the most famous sandwiches in the world is readying for its big West Coast debut. All'Antico Vinaio, the Florence, Italy-based restaurant known for its long lines and wide sandwiches stuffed with cured meats, has landed in Los Angeles, with a near-the-water first location slated for Venice (the LA neighborhood, not the Italian city).After years of LA pop-ups and social media teases, the All'Antico Vinaio group plans to go permanent at a corner restaurant space at 1121 Abbot Kinney
