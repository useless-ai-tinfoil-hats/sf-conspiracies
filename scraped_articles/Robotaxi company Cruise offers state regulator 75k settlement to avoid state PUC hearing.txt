Robotaxi company Cruise offers state regulator $75k settlement to avoid state PUC hearing
-----
Cruise wants to privately settle a dispute with a state regulator, that accused the robotaxi company of withholding evidence of the Oct. 2 severe crash in San Francisco, by offering to pay a $75,000 fine and share more accident data.The California Public Utilities Commission, in December, ordered Cruise to appear at a Feb. 6 public hearing to respond to its claims.
