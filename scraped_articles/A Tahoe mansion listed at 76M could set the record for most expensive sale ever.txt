A Tahoe mansion listed at $76M could set the record for most expensive sale ever
-----
A home on Incline Village's prestigious Lakeshore Boulevard could become the most expensive private home ever sold in Lake Tahoe. For now, it stands as one of the most expensive listings ever, at an asking price of $76 million for the 5-acre property.The seven-bedroom home on the Nevada side of Lake Tahoe has a 200-foot stretch of private beach, plus a private pier and raised Jet Ski platform.
