49ers’ George Kittle on 1-catch game: ‘Whatever they ask of me, I’m going to do it’
-----
There are many mouths to feed in the San Francisco 49ers' loaded offense and one of their All-Pros, tight end George Kittle, could be getting hungry.Kittle had one reception for 9 yards in Sunday's 35-16 win over Arizona, his lowest yardage total since he had one catch for 8 yards in his third career game in 2017. Kittle is on pace for 60 receptions for 629 yards, which would be his fewest yards since his rookie year.
