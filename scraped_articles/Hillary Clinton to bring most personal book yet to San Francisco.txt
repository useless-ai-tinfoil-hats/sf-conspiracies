Hillary Clinton to bring most personal book yet to San Francisco
-----
Hillary Clinton is headed to the Bay Area as part of her five-city book tour.The former first lady, senator, and secretary of state plans to visit San Francisco's Davies Symphony Hall on Oct. 7 to promote her new compilation of essays, "Something Lost, Something Gained: Reflections on Life, Love, and Liberty," which is set for release on Sept. 17.
