Ms. Lauryn Hill blames media for low ticket sales, cancels Bay Area concert
-----
Ms. Lauryn Hill and the Fugees have canceled their co-headlining U.S. tour — just three days before it was set to begin — due to low ticket sales.The hip-hop trio of Hill, Wyclef Jean and Pras released a joint statement on social media Wednesday, Aug. 7, claiming they could not sell enough tickets for the 18-show outing due to media reports about their habit of showing up late to shows or postponing concerts at last minute. Among the shows canceled is their Sept. 13 stop at Shoreline
