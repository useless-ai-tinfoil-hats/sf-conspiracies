East Bay woman arrested in fatal stabbing of her mother
-----
A woman was fatally stabbed by her daughter Sunday in Berkeley, according to police, marking the city's first homicide of the year.Around 2:45 p.m., a caller told Berkeley police that someone had been stabbed at an apartment on the 1300 block of University Avenue, police said. Heather Riemer, 60, was found with stab wounds and declared dead.
