What to know about visiting Maui now, one year since the Lahaina fire
-----
A year after the Lahaina fire, Hawaii visitors are still wary about traveling to Maui, as evidenced by the island's decline in travelers year over year. In June 2024, Maui saw a significant decrease in visitors compared with the same month last year, down by almost 22 percent.The fire on Aug. 8, 2023, caused widespread devastation, destroying more than 2,000 homes and commercial buildings.
