Bankrupt and bleeding money, Calif. restaurant chain faces grim future
-----
Is the dream of the fast-casual salad restaurant dying a slow death? Perhaps. National chain Sweetgreen was "losing millions of dollars every month" as of last fall, according to the Wall Street Journal, and now a well-known West Coast competitor has caved and filed for bankruptcy.The parent company of Tender Greens, the Los Angeles salad chain with a small but vocal cult following, moved forward with bankruptcy proceedings late last week.
