The hero of summer on the American River is a Calif. man with a snorkel
-----
Growing up on the banks of the Lower American River in a Sacramento suburb, Karl Bly would join his father in a sit-on-top kayak while searching for "treasures" beneath the surface of the stream."I'd just follow the air bubbles of his scuba gear," recalled Bly, now 57 and still living in Carmichael. "My siblings and I were out there almost every summer weekend."
