'This would be a godsend': Wealthy investors plan to purchase SFAI campus
-----
A group of wealthy San Franciscans has formed a nonprofit intent on purchasing the former campus of the San Francisco Art Institute following the school's bankruptcy earlier this year.The nonprofit has yet to choose a name for itself, District 3 Supervisor Aaron Peskin told SFGATE. According to the San Francisco Business Times, the group includes businesswoman and philanthropist Laurene Powell Jobs, San Francisco Conservatory of Music President David Stull and Oberlin Dance Collective
