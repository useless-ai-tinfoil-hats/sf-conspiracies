2 flights headed to Sacramento diverted because of fog, airport says
-----
This article originally appeared on KCRA.com.Two inbound flights to Sacramento International Airport on Wednesday morning were diverted because of fog, an airport spokesperson confirmed.
Details on the flights, including more information on why fog forced the flights to divert and where they went, were not immediately available.
An online arrivals page on the airport's website said Southwest Flight 3210 from Ontario had been diverted.
KCRA 3 is trying to learn more.
