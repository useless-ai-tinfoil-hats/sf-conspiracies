Half Moon Bay restaurant owner defends pumpkin fest surcharge
-----
A social media post has garnered attention after a Half Moon Bay restaurant added a "festival" surcharge during the city's annual Art & Pumpkin Festival.After visiting Johnny's restaurant, Bay Area journalist Stanley Roberts shared a post on X that he was surprised by an 18% "festival service charge" added to his bill. As of Wednesday, his post has gained more than 117,000 views and hundreds of comments from readers with mixed reviews.
