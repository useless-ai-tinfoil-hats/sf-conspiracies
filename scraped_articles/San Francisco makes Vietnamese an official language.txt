San Francisco makes Vietnamese an official language
-----
The San Francisco Board of Supervisors on Tuesday voted unanimously to formally declare Vietnamese an official city language, joining Chinese, Spanish and Filipino as languages the city is required to provide translated services in.The move, alongside other changes to San Francisco's language access ordinance, is aimed at ensuring that residents can be served in the language they are most comfortable in.
