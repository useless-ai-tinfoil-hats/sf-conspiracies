How APEC is impacting San Francisco traffic this morning amid street closures
-----
While APEC-related events have started in San Francisco, the Monday morning commute remained largely unaffected.The biggest change — the closure of a lane on the Bay Bridge — doesn't take effect until Tuesday – and morning commute traffic seemed normally sluggish but largely unaffected. Many commuters seemed to avoid driving across the bridge Tuesday morning and the backup seemed to ease by 8 a.m.
