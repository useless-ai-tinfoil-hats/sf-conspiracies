Big Bay Area property could soon be vacant after 70 years, housing proposed
-----
A 17-acre Bay Area office park will soon be vacant, with possible housing development on the horizon, the Almanac first reported.In 2019, the U.S. Geological Survey announced it would move from its large Menlo Park campus to Moffett Field in Mountain View to better support its partnership with NASA. The complex was established in 1954 and includes the earthquake science center and the volcano science center, among other facilities.
