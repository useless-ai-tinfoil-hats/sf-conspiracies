New S.F. data shows out-of-town drug users may be getting cash aid from city
-----
Nearly half of the people cited for drug use over the past year in San Francisco live outside the city or declined to state where they lived, according to new data released by the mayor's office on Thursday.Of 718 people arrested or cited between March 30, 2023 and Feb. 2 of this year, 377 — 53% — were San Francisco residents, while 274 — or 38% — live in another county, state or country.
