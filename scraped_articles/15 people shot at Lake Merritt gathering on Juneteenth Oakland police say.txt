15 people shot at Lake Merritt gathering on Juneteenth, Oakland police say
-----
Gunfire erupted near Oakland's Lake Merritt during a Juneteenth gathering Wednesday, and at least 15 people were wounded, according to the Oakland Police Department and police union officials.The shooting occurred just after 8:45 p.m., as gunfire rang out on the 400 block of Grand Avenue, police said. Prior to the shooting, more than two dozen officers had gathered in the area to monitor a crowd of roughly 5,000 people participating in unsanctioned events near Lake Merritt, police said.
