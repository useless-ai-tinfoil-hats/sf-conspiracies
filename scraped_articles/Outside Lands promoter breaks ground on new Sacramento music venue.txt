Outside Lands promoter breaks ground on new Sacramento music venue
-----
Bay Area concert promotion company Another Planet Entertainment, known for staging the annual Outside Lands festival in Golden Gate Park and operating historic venues such as the Bill Graham Civic Auditorium in San Francisco and the Fox Theater in Oakland, has announced plans to expand into Sacramento next year.The Berkeley-based company is currently overseeing the construction of a midsize entertainment complex, Channel 24, in the heart of the California state capital.
