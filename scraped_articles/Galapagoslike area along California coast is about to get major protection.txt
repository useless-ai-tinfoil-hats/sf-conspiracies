‘Galapagos’-like area along California coast is about to get major protection
-----
A beautiful and ecologically rich stretch of the California coast is about to get major protection: a new national marine sanctuary that would preserve 5,617 square miles of the ocean along the San Luis Obispo and Santa Barbara County coast.The proposed Chumash Heritage National Marine Sanctuary also aims to honor the Indigenous people who have lived in the area for tens of thousands of years.
