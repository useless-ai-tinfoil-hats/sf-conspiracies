Automated speeding tickets close to reality in Bay Area after bill passes California Legislature
-----
In the near future, you might automatically get ticketed in California — without ever talking to a police officer — if you exceed the speed limit by more than 11 mph.Assembly Bill 645, which authorizes the use of speed cameras capable of ticketing California drivers, is now waiting for Gov.
