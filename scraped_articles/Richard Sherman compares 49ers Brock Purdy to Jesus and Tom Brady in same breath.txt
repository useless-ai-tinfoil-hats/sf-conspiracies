Richard Sherman compares 49ers' Brock Purdy to Jesus and Tom Brady in same breath
-----
Despite playing most of his career for a division rival, no member of the national media has been as big of a 49ers fan throughout this postseason as Richard Sherman, particularly when it comes to backing Brock Purdy.But Sherman's love of San Francisco's 24-year-old second-year quarterback may have reached new heights Sunday on his eponymous podcast when the former cornerback compared Purdy to two notable figures in American culture: Jesus and Tom Brady.
