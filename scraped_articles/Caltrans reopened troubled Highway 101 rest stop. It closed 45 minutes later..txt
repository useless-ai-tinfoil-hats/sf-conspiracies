Caltrans reopened troubled Highway 101 rest stop. It closed 45 minutes later.
-----
A rest area prone to issues on Highway 101 near a popular Central Coast beach has once again closed unexpectedly.Caltrans announced earlier this week that the northbound U.S. 101 Gaviota rest area was reopening Tuesday at noon. In recent years, the rest stop has swung open only to close again multiple times due to a variety of issues with its wastewater system.
