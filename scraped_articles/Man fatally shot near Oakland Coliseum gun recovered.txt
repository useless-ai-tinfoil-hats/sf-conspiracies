Man fatally shot near Oakland Coliseum; gun recovered
-----
A shooting near the Oakland Coliseum left a 30-year-old man dead Friday night, police said.The shooting occurred about 10:15 p.m. on Oakport Street, which runs parallel to the southbound lanes of Interstate 880.
When officers arrived, they learned that someone had taken the victim to a hospital, police said. The man later died there. His name was withheld, pending family notifications.
Officers recovered a gun at the scene, police said.
