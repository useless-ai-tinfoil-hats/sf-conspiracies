Popular margaritas and Tex-Mex restaurant closing after 11 years in S.F.
-----
A popular stop for Tex-Mex dishes and margaritas in the Mission District will be closing.West of Pecos announced on Instagram that it will close on Oct. 28 after 11 years at 550 Valencia St.
"It seemed like it went by in the blink of an eye," the message reads partially.
Mission Local first reported the coming closure. A representative for the business could not be immediately reached.
The post did not disclose any specific reasons behind the closing, but thanked staff
