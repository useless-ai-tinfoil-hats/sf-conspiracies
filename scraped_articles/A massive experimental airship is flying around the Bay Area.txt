A massive experimental airship is flying around the Bay Area
-----
A giant, high-tech airship that's been described as a "Goodyear blimp on steroids" has taken flight over the Bay Area.The 400-foot-long, helium-filled Pathfinder 1, built by Google co-founder Sergey Brin's research company LTA (which stands for Lighter Than Air), recently received FAA clearance to take to the skies.
