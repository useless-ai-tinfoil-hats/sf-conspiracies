KEXP acquires bankrupt Bay Area radio station for $3.75 million in auction
-----
Seattle radio station KEXP has successfully acquired San Francisco's KREV 92.7 FM for $3.75 million through a bankruptcy auction.In a message to donors, Ethan Raup, CEO of the nonprofit music station known for championing independent artists, revealed that KEXP emerged as the top bidder in October for the Alameda-licensed station, previously owned by Royce International Broadcasting.
