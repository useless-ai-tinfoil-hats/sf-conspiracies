‘Ten Bridges’ proves Oakland’s Brontez Purnell still a genre unto himself
-----
Brontez Purnell is always creating. Born in Alabama, the 41-year-old is a longtime Oakland staple with a prolific, punk-steeped career that's bled across multiple disciplines.Among his accolades, he's a founding member of seminal East Bay electro-rock band Gravy Train, a winner of the 2018 Whiting Award for fiction, the head of his own dance company, and the director of several documentaries.
