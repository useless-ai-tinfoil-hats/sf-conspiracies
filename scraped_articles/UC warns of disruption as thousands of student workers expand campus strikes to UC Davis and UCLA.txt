UC warns of ‘disruption’ as thousands of student workers expand campus strikes to UC Davis and UCLA
-----
The University of California's powerful legion of student workers expanded their strike Monday to three of UC's 10 campuses, as thousands of UCLA and UC Davis employees joined those at UC Santa Cruz who walked off the job last week.The students are stopping work, campus by campus, to protest UC's handling of pro-Palestinian protests, in which many of them took part.
