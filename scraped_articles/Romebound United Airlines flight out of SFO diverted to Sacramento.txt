Rome-bound United Airlines flight out of SFO diverted to Sacramento
-----
A Rome-bound United Airlines flight out of San Francisco International Airport was diverted to Sacramento International Airport on Tuesday evening, airline officials said.The flight, UA507, departed SFO around 4:30 p.m., bound for Rome's Leonardo da Vinci-Fiumicino Airport, but landed in Sacramento just before 6 p.m., according to flight tracker FlightAware. Sacramento airport officials confirmed that the flight landed safely.
The plane was diverted "to address a mechanical issue"
