Own a second home in South Lake Tahoe? Proposed vacancy tax could cost you thousands a year
-----
South Lake Tahoe voters will determine whether vacation home owners will pay a $3,000 "vacancy tax" on residences that sit empty for more than half the year after the El Dorado County Register of Voters verified that the group behind the initiative has collected enough signatures to get it on the November ballot.Locals for Affordable Housing submitted more than double the number of signatures needed to qualify for the ballot.
