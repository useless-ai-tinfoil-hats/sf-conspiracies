Wife of doctor who drove Tesla off cliff asks court to spare him prison time
-----
The wife of Dharmesh Patel, the doctor accused of intentionally driving a Tesla carrying his family off a San Mateo County cliff, wiped away tears as she spoke publicly for the first time Thursday morning, telling a judge she believes her husband should receive mental health treatment instead of prison time.Neha Patel, appearing in the courtroom over Zoom, read a statement saying she misses her "best friend" and her children miss their father. She said Patel had a mental health episode
