Man dies while fishing after being swept into the ocean at Point Reyes
-----
A man fishing at a beach Point Reyes National Seashore died after being swept into the water and rescued on Friday morning, officials said.The Marin County Fire Department said it responded to a report of a person in the water at 9 a.m. near the South Beach access point on Great Beach.
