Bay Area tech giant Cisco to lay off thousands
-----
San Jose tech giant Cisco is gearing up to lay off thousands of employees in its latest reorganization effort.While the exact head count is pending confirmation, sources familiar with the matter confirmed to Reuters on Friday that an official announcement is imminent, potentially aligning with the company's earnings call scheduled for Wednesday.
As of the fiscal year 2023, Cisco's workforce stood at 84,900.
At the end of 2022, the company reduced its workforce by about 4,000
