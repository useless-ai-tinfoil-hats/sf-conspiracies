S.F. director John Waters casts Aubrey Plaza in new film
-----
Director John Waters is set to begin filming his first feature film in 20 years with part-time Marin County resident Aubrey Plaza as his lead.The legendary director and gay icon whose boundary pushing in underground classics such as "Pink Flamingos," "Polyester" and "Female Trouble" earned him the nickname "the Pope of Trash," will be adapting his 2022 novel "Liarmouth."
