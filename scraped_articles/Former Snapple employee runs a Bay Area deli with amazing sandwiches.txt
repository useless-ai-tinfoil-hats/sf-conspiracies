Former Snapple employee runs a Bay Area deli with amazing sandwiches
-----
Just off Highway 1 in picturesque Pacifica lies a nondescript strip mall hiding a decades-old deli known for its bargain lunches.Located at 1023 Terra Nova Blvd. in Pacifica, Upper Crust Deli is among a cluster of local businesses found within Park Mall Center. The shopping strip is discreet, to say the least, but if you don't slow down, you might miss out on killer subs priced between $10 and $13.
