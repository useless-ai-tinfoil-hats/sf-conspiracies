Large fire hits Alamo Square house after resident received racist threats
-----
A large fire at a San Francisco home that sent two people to the hospital is being investigated, fire officials said.The fire on the 900 block of Grove Street in Alamo Square started at 11:31 a.m. and blazed through the home's second and third floors, the San Francisco Fire Department said in a social media post. One person is in serious condition and the other is in moderate condition, San Francisco fire Capt. Jonathan Baxter told SFGATE.
One building next door had minor damage
