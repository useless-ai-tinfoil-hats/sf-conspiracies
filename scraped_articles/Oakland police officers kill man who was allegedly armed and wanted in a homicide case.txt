Oakland police officers kill man who was allegedly armed and wanted in a homicide case
-----
Oakland police officers fatally shot a 32-year-old man suspected in a homicide investigation who allegedly emerged from a residence with a gun, the department said Thursday.The shooting occurred about 9:30 p.m. Wednesday on 16th Street near Market Street.
Police didn't say how many officers shot the man, who they said was suspected in a homicide in Sacramento.
