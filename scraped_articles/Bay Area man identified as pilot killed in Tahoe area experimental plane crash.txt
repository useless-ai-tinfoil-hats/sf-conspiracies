Bay Area man identified as pilot killed in Tahoe area experimental plane crash
-----
Randal G. Abraham, 68, of Redwood City has been confirmed as the pilot who died in the experimental plane crash in the Tahoe area earlier this week.The incident occurred Tuesday morning at Minden-Tahoe Airport, roughly 30 miles from Lake Tahoe, according to the Douglas County Sheriff's Office in Nevada.
The National Transportation Safety Board reported that the aircraft was an experimental, amateur-built Lockwood AirCam.
