14-year-old boy identified as suspect after Waymo vehicle was destroyed in fire in San Francisco, police say
-----
A 14-year-old boy was charged in an arson investigation after a crowd of people incinerated a driverless Waymo vehicle in Chinatown in February, authorities said in a news release Wednesday.The San Francisco Police Department's arson task force said it had "developed probable cause to obtain a search warrant" for the teenager's home, and on the morning of March 27, investigators served the warrant and subsequently found and seized evidence related to the vandalism.
