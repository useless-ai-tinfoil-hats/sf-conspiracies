Bay Area wunderkind heads to National Spelling Bee. Will he survive the vowel of death?
-----
Aren Lee's mom struggled to contain two emotions days before her son was set to compete in the Scripps National Spelling Bee. There was pride, of course, but also an overwhelming sense of bewilderment.While many of the 245 spellers heading to Maryland for the nationally televised competition will have spent 500 or more hours studying, memorizing and in some cases, reading the entire Merriam-Webster Unabridged dictionary, Aren, 12, was not one of them.
