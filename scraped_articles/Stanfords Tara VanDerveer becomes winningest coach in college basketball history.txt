Stanford's Tara VanDerveer becomes winningest coach in college basketball history
-----
The Bay Area's best basketball coach now has the all-time record for most wins by a college basketball coach.Stanford women's basketball head coach Tara VanDerveer passed former Duke men's coach Mike Krzyzewski for the top spot on Sunday, earning her Win No. 1,203 on Sunday when the Cardinal won 65-56 over Oregon State.
