California Kaiser workers begin multi-day strike: What it means for you
-----
Tens of thousands of Kaiser Permanente health care workers in the Bay Area and across the country began a planned three-day strike Wednesday amid tense contract negotiations.In California, the strike is scheduled from 6 a.m. Wednesday through 6 a.m. Saturday, Kaiser said. The company has 39 hospitals nationwide and provides medical coverage for nearly 13 million people.
