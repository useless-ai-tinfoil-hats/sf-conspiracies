Over-the-top beautiful doughnut cafe opening in S.F.
-----
A whimsical, all-day cafe devoted to doughnuts from an acclaimed local pastry chef is coming to San Francisco.George's Donuts & Merriment will open next summer at 163 West Portal Ave. Janina O'Leary, who most recently oversaw pastry for Bacchus Management Group's Bay Area restaurants (Spruce, Selby's, the Village Pub and others) after years of working in fine-dining restaurants, is behind the menu.
