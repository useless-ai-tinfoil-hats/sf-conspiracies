Bassist for globally popular pop rock band selling Bay Area home for $1.3M
-----
Ten years ago, Imagine Dragons bassist Ben McKee wanted a secluded home where he and his wife Mary could live quietly when Ben was not on tour. They chose 6262 Virgo Road in the Oakland Hills and spent more than $1 million transforming the house into their own custom sanctuary. Now, it's for sale, asking $1.295 million.When the McKees found the property in 2014, it already offered many of the features they'd hoped for.
