Stephen Curry hints at future in politics, maybe even president
-----
Stephen Curry is contemplating a potential entry into politics, even hinting at the possibility of a future presidential run.During an interview on "CBS This Morning" while promoting his new children's book, "I Am Extraordinary," on Tuesday, the Golden State Warriors star responded to a question about entering politics and making a run for the White House after retiring from basketball.
"Maybe," Curry, who turns 36 Thursday, told CBS News national correspondent Jericka Duncan.
