FBI raids home of Sonoma developer Ken Mattson after fraud allegations from business partner
-----
FBI agents have raided the home of embattled Sonoma County developer Kenneth Mattson, agency officials said, just weeks after his business partner alleged that Mattson had defrauded investors.Department officials confirmed they raided Mattson's property Friday morning on the 3000 block of Castle Road, a gated residence in unincorporated Sonoma County. They did not provide additional details.
Mattson's attorney declined to comment Friday.
The agency did not make any arrests
