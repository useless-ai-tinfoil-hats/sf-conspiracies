Photos: East Bay estate owned by Atari and PayPal exec on market for $7.5 million
-----
A luxurious estate, once the residence of a prominent sports mogul and video game executive, is now on the market in the East Bay's exclusive Blackhawk community for $7.5 million.Located at 1121 Eagle Nest Court in Danville, the opulent home was originally constructed in 1986 by Kenneth Hofmann. Hofmann not only co-developed the Blackhawk neighborhood but was also founder of the Hofmann Co., and once held ownerships in both the Seattle Seahawks and the Oakland A's.
