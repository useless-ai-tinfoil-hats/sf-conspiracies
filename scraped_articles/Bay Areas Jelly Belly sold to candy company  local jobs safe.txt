Bay Area’s Jelly Belly sold to candy company — local jobs safe
-----
The storied Bay Area confectioner Jelly Belly has been bought by a Chicago candy corporation.Jelly Belly Candy Co., headquartered in Fairfield, announced Thursday in a news release that it has been acquired by Ferrara Candy Co., which owns several popular sugar candy brands, including SweeTarts, Trolli and Laffy Taffy, among others.
