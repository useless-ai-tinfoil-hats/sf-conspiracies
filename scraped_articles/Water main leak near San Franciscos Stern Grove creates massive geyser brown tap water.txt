Water main leak near San Francisco's Stern Grove creates massive geyser, brown tap water
-----
A pillar of water gushed from the ground due to a water main break Friday morning at 30th Avenue and Wawona Street, near San Francisco's beloved Stern Grove park, the site of the city's long-running free summer music series.Around 10 a.m., the San Francisco Fire Department shared an alert on X, the social media site formerly known as Twitter. SFFD told SFGATE they were on the scene in a standby capacity for the San Francisco Public Utility Commission.
