Warriors to convert Usman Garuba’s 2-way deal to fill 15th roster spot
-----
The Golden State Warriors intend to fill their 15th roster spot by converting Usman Garuba's two-way contract into a standard deal, an NBA source told the Chronicle — confirming a report from the Athletic.Once the transaction is finalized procedurally, Garuba, 22, will be eligible to play in the NBA's postseason.
