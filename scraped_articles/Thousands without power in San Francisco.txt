Thousands without power in San Francisco
-----
Thousands of San Francisco residents are without power Wednesday morning. The unplanned outages are affecting 1,158 customers as of 9:40 a.m. near the Upper Market and Castro neighborhoods, according to the PG&E outage map.The outage had previously affected 7,050 customers as of 9 a.m. Wednesday, the San Francisco Chronicle reported. (The Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms.) The utility company told the outlet that power is expected to be restored
