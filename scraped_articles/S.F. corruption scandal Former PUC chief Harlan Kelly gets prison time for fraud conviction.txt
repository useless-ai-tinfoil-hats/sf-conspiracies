S.F. corruption scandal: Former PUC chief Harlan Kelly gets prison time for fraud conviction
-----
A federal judge on Monday sentenced former San Francisco Public Utilities Commission chief Harlan Kelly to four years in prison, following his conviction last year on fraud charges as part of the FBI's wide-ranging investigation into corruption in city government.Prosecutors had sought 6½ years behind bars — lower than the 15 years he could have faced but higher than what Kelly's attorneys had argued for.
