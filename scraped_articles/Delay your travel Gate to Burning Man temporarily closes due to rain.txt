'Delay your travel': Gate to Burning Man temporarily closes due to rain
-----
LATEST Aug. 24, 1:50 p.m. The gate to Burning Man is still closed, according to an update sent by event organizers at 1:16 p.m. However, the update noted that rain should be mostly over soon and that roads are starting to dry out. The organizers promised to "reopen the Gate when it is safe to do so."Aug. 24, 7:24 a.m. The gate to Burning Man temporarily closed early Saturday, the day before the start of the event held in Nevada's Black Rock Desert, due to rain, event organizers said.
