'We f—king did that': Rowdy, politically charged set shakes Outside Lands to life
-----
A lightning bolt of raw punk energy lit up the so-far, so-sleepy Outside Lands music festival on Saturday afternoon.Breakout Australian rockers Amyl and the Sniffers stole the show on the Twin Peaks stage, with everyone's eyes firmly locked on wildly charismatic front woman Amy Taylor.
