Giants shelve Marco Luciano with hamstring injury, recall Casey Schmitt
-----
The San Francisco Giants shifted the infield depth and added another player to the injured list on Friday.Shortstop Marco Luciano moved to the 10-day IL, retroactive to Thursday, with a right hamstring strain. The 22-year-old tweaked his hamstring Wednesday while running to first on a soft groundball in the first inning and exited the game against the Philadelphia Phillies in the third inning.
