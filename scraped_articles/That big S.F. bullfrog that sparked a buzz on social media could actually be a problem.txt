That big S.F. bullfrog that sparked a buzz on social media could actually be a problem
-----
A very big bullfrog photographed recently at Blue Heron Lake in San Francisco's Golden Gate Park has been drawing a lot of attention on social media.Bob Johansen, a Sunset resident, snapped a close-up of the bulging-eyed amphibian, its greenish skin casting an iridescent glow in the bright sunshine at the recently renamed lake and posted it on Feb. 1 to social media site Nextdoor.
"A new friend at Blue Heron Lake," he wrote. "Huge. At least eight inches long."
