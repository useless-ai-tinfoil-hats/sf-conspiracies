The best way to decompress from Burning Man is to go to Las Vegas
-----
When Burning Man is over and everybody has to leave the most outrageous, filthy, stimulating place on Earth, a thing called "decompression" happens. Basically, we all have to adjust to being in the real world again, and that can be tough.What often helps is to stop at a glitzy casino in Reno, swim in the hotel pool and begin reacquainting oneself with unavoidable aspects of modern life that were blissfully absent from the desert: money, advertising, consumerism, all-you-can-eat buffets.
