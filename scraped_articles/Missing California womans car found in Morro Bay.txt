Missing California woman's car found in Morro Bay
-----
Police in the Central Valley are seeking a woman who was reported missing by her family last week. So far, only her car has been located. According to the Tulare Police Department, Tiffany McClure, 47, was last in touch with family members on July 22. The next day, they reported her missing.
