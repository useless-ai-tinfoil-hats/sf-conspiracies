Rare beaver rescued from Palo Alto creek dies
-----
A female North American beaver that was rescued from a Palo Alto creek last week has died.The adult beaver was found Nov. 29 in Matadero Creek, huddled near a pile of leaves to keep warm, according to the Wildlife Center of Silicon Valley. North American beaver sightings are historically rare in the Bay Area.
