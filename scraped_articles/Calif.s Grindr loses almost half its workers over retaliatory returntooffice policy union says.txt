Calif.’s Grindr loses almost half its workers over ‘retaliatory’ return-to-office policy, union says
-----
Grindr, the West Hollywood-based dating app for LGBTQ people, offered its workers a sudden ultimatum in early August: work from an office or leave the firm.A month later, 80 workers are gone, according to the Grindr union, which alleges that the return-to-office requirement was presented to retaliate against the newly formed labor group. Grindr, in the note announcing the policy, wrote that it was simply following the mold of Bay Area tech giants like Meta, Google and Apple.
