Fire at Redwood City metal facility sends thick smoke into the air
-----
Crews were battling a fire at a Redwood City metal recycling facility Wednesday evening.The Mountain View Fire Department said, "Thick, dark smoke is in the air accompanied by the smell of burning plastic."
Officials with the Bay Area Air Quality District said they were investigating the blaze at Sims Metal and advised the public to remain indoors to avoid exposure.
