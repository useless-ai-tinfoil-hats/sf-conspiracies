One of S.F.’s most acclaimed bars has closed — for now
-----
Bar Agricole has closed again — but the James Beard Award-winning cocktail bar will reopen in May.The new home will be 2875 18th St., the Mission District space formerly occupied by Liliana (and briefly rechristened as the Bar at Osito). This will be the third location for Bar Agricole, which originally opened in 2010 in SoMa and then moved to 1540 Mission St. in 2022.
Owner Thad Vogler expects this to be Bar Agricole's final home.
