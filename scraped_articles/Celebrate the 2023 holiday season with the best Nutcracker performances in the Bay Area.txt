Celebrate the 2023 holiday season with the best ‘Nutcracker’ performances in the Bay Area
-----
The San Francisco Ballet's much loved multigenerational holiday production, set to Tchaikovsky's iconic score, rises to the top of many dance lovers' wish lists. But by no means is it the only "Nutcracker" game in town. There is a small army of local and touring ensembles bringing unique interpretations of the seasonal classic to stages across the Bay Area.Check out the Chronicle's guide to the top performances of the favorite Christmastime ballet.
