Driving to Tahoe this weekend? Brace for snowy roads
-----
UPDATE: Heavy snow and wind in Sierra, Tahoe — travel discouraged amid near-whiteout conditionsSkiers, snowboarders and snow-lovers headed to the Lake Tahoe region for the holiday weekend can expect treacherous conditions through Saturday as the snow level rises.
The main trans-Sierra highways to Tahoe, Interstate 80 and Highway 50, were both open as of Friday morning — without chain requirements — but that was expected to change as the next storm moved in Friday night.
