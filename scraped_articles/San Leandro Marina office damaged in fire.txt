San Leandro Marina office damaged in fire
-----
Alameda County firefighters said the former harbormaster's office at the San Leandro Marina caught fire Wednesday morning.Firefighters arrived at the bulding on Mulford Point Drive just before 8:30 a.m. to find the office engulfed in flames.
No injuries were reported.
Alameda County officials said the Department of Fish and Wildlife and the U.S. Coast Guard were notified.
What caused the fire was not immediately clear.
