Bay Area woman fatally hit by driver in foggy Walmart parking lot, police say
-----
A woman died Monday after a driver hit her in the parking lot of a Walmart in Mountain View, a California city about 40 miles southeast of San Francisco, officials said."The parking lot at the time was dark and densely covered in fog," the Mountain View Police Department said in a news released shared on social media.
