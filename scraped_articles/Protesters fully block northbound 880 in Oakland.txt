Protesters fully block northbound 880 in Oakland
-----
Traffic was completely stopped on northbound 880 in Oakland on Monday morning by protesters, the California Highway Patrol said. CHP confirmed the protest is still blocking traffic as of 7:45 a.m.KCBS reporter Mike DeWald shared video footage of protesters standing in the middle of the freeway holding sign reading, "AIS Economic Blockade for a Free Palestine." KCBS also reported that protesters had chained themselves to barrels.
