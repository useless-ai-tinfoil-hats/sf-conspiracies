S.F. woman accused of manslaughter, wrong-way driving after triple fatal West Portal crash
-----
UPDATE: Three family members killed by West Portal driver identifiedA San Francisco woman identified by authorities as the driver of an SUV that crashed into a Muni bus stop and killed three family members in West Portal over the weekend has been booked into city jail on suspicion of charges including felony vehicular manslaughter.
Mary Fong Lau, 78, was booked into jail on Sunday and remained in custody Monday morning, records show.
