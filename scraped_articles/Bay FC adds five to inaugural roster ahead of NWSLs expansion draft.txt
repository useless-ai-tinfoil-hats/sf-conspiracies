Bay FC adds five to inaugural roster ahead of NWSL's expansion draft
-----
The puzzle of Bay FC is inching toward completion after the expansion team more than tripled its roster size Tuesday.Three days before the NWSL expansion draft, Bay FC acquired center back Emily Menges, forward Scarlett Camberos, midfielder Joelle Anderson, defender Ellie Jean, and defensive midfielder Dorian Bailey in five separate transactions.
