Yosemite closes for several days due to 'major winter storm'
-----
LATEST March 3, 10:40 a.m. After a blizzard swept through California's Sierra, Yosemite National Park is set to partially reopen. The park has been closed since Friday, and visitors were told to leave for their own safety.Access into the park via highways 41 and 140 will reopen at noon, although drivers should "expect snowy conditions," park officials said.
