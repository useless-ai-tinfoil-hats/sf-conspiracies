Historic California gold mining town wiped out by Borel Fire
-----
The historic Kern County town of Havilah has been virtually razed by the Borel Fire. Once the county seat, Havilah now faces the daunting prospect of rebuilding again.Photos and video taken over the weekend show the destruction of the once-prosperous mining town located about 60 miles northeast of Bakersfield.
