Risk of sneaker waves elevated along these Bay Area beaches
-----
Sneaker waves pose an elevated risk Monday to people along Bay Area coastal shorelines, according to a warning from the National Weather Service.The beach hazards statement, affecting the San Francisco Peninsula and North Bay coasts, including Point Reyes National Seashore, is in effect through Monday evening, the weather service said.
The warning extends south to the Monterey Bay and Big Sur coasts.
"Be careful at the coast, and never turn your back on the ocean!" NWS Bay
