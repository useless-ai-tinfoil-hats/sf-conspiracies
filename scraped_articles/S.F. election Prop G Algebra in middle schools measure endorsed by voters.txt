S.F. election: Prop G Algebra in middle schools measure endorsed by voters
-----
San Francisco voters sent a clear and decisive message to the city's school board Tuesday, demanding through Proposition G that the district bring Algebra 1 back into middle schools.The result was evident from the first election returns, with 82% backing the measure as of Friday afternoon.
