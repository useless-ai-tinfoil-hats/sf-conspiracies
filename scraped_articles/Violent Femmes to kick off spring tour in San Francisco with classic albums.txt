Violent Femmes to kick off spring tour in San Francisco with classic albums
-----
Violent Femmes are preparing for a limited run of performances this spring, where they plan to play a pair of their classic albums.The tour kicks off at the Warfield in San Francisco on May 1, with the band performing its self-titled 1983 debut and its sophomore release, 1984's "Hallowed Ground," in their entirety.
Tickets, starting at $49.95, are available now at www.vfemmes.com.
