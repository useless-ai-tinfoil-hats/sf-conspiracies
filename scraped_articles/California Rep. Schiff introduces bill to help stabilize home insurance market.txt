California Rep. Schiff introduces bill to help stabilize home insurance market
-----
As the Bay Area deals with shrinking home insurance options and rising costs, California Rep. Adam Schiff introduced federal legislation Wednesday in an effort to stabilize the insurance market.The act proposed by Schiff, D-Burbank, titled Incorporating National Support for Unprecedented Risks and Emergencies, or INSURE, aims to help through the creation of federal reinsurance.
