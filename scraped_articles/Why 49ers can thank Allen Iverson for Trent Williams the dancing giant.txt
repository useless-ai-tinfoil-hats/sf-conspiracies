Why 49ers can thank Allen Iverson for Trent Williams, the dancing giant
-----
One of the greatest large players in NFL history traces his agility, in part, to one of the greatest small players in NBA history.To understand how San Francisco 49ers left tackle Trent Williams became a dancing giant, moving with quickness and grace that defy his 6-foot-5, 320-pound frame, it helps to recall the basketball wizardry of 6-foot, 165-pound Allen Iverson.
