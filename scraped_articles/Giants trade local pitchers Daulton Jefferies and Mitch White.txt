Giants trade local pitchers Daulton Jefferies and Mitch White
-----
The San Francisco Giants dealt two pitchers with Bay Area ties Friday after both had been designated for assignment this week.Daulton Jefferies (Cal) was sent to the Pirates in exchange for outfielder Rodolfo Nolasco, and reliever Mitch White (Bellarmine High, Santa Clara) went to the Brewers for cash considerations.
Nolasco, 23, was batting .173 with two homers and 38 strikeouts in 75 at-bats at High-A Greensboro.
