Two men get federal prison time after S.F. drug dealing convictions
-----
Two men from Honduras were sentenced to federal prison time in separate drug cases in San Francisco — one of them for selling drugs to an undercover officer, the other for intending to sell drugs, prosecutors said.The prosecutions were part of an ongoing crackdown on San Francisco's drug markets as the city saw a record number of overdose deaths — 813 — last year.
