Biotech company Grail to lay off 350, more than half in Bay Area
-----
The cancer test maker Grail announced 350 layoffs in a Tuesday filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, joining a surge of Bay Area biotech companies cutting staff. Grail is paring back its headcount and planned hires by 30% for the year, the filing said.The company's cuts will include 179 workers from its headquarters in Menlo Park, according to a WARN notice Grail filed with local officials.
