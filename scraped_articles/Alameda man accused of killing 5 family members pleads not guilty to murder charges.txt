Alameda man accused of killing 5 family members pleads not guilty to murder charges
-----
An Alameda man accused of shooting and killing five family members pleaded not guilty to murder charges during a brief court appearance Friday.Shane Killian, 54, is accused of shooting and killing his wife, his two young sons and his in-laws. Alameda County prosecutors charged him with five counts of murder.
