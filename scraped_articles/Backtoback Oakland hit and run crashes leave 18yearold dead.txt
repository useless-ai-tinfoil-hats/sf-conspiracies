Back-to-back Oakland hit and run crashes leave 18-year-old dead
-----
An 18-year-old was struck and killed in Oakland late Sunday in two hit-and-run collisions, CHP officials said.The 18-year-old was riding in the passenger seat of a Zipcar on Highway 13 when the vehicle collided with a black sedan, leaving the Zipcar disabled. The driver of the sedan fled.
