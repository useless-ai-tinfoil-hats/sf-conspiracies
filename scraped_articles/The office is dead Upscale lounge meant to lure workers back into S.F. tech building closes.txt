‘The office is dead’: Upscale lounge meant to lure workers back into S.F. tech building closes
-----
Tech company Expensify plans to shutter the upscale lounge at its San Francisco office, where it offered employees and guests free specialty cocktails and a daily Champagne sabering, just six months after its grand opening.CEO David Barrett cited a shift in workplace culture for the closure.
"(W)e're just never going back to a regular 9-5 office," he wrote in a blog post on Thursday. Barrett revealed that the lounge, which will close on Wednesday, was part of a "secret experiment"
