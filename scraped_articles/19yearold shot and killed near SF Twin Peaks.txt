19-year-old shot and killed near SF Twin Peaks
-----
A 19-year-old man was shot and killed Sunday near Twin Peaks in San Francisco, police said.SF police officers arrived at the 500 block of Twin Peaks Boulevard around 2 a.m. and found the man with a gunshot wound, the San Francisco Police Department said in a Tuesday news release.
The teen was transported to a local hospital and died from his injuries, police said. His name was not released.
No arrests have been made in connection to the shooting, which is under investigation.
