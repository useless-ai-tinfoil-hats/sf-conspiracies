Review: Marin Shakes christens new San Rafael venue with bold experiment
-----
In the balancing act of writing a play, one of the great questions is how much information to give the audience, and when.Carve out a knowledge gap, tease us just so, and we'll reward you with our attention, thirsting to quench our suspense. But we're fickle. Leave us dangling too long, and we'll wonder why we should invest our care in a game with scant promise of reward.
