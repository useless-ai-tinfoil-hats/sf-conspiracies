Two arrested in pistol-whipping and robbery in Walnut Creek
-----
Walnut Creek police have arrested two men in connection with a violent armed robbery Tuesday evening in the 1500 block of Bonanza Street.The incident unfolded about 7:30 p.m., when three perpetrators wearing dark clothing and masks confronted two victims, one of whom was an off-duty police officer for another agency, according to Walnut Creek police Lt. Bruce Jower.
