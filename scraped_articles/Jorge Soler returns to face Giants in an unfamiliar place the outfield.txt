Jorge Soler returns to face Giants in an unfamiliar place: the outfield
-----
Not once did Jorge Soler man the outfield this season with the San Francisco Giants, sticking strictly to the designated hitter role.Monday, now with the Braves, Soler played left field at Oracle Park for the first time since May 10, 2022, and the first time in the outfield in San Francisco since May 21, 2023.
Since he was traded back to Atlanta on July 29, Soler had played 10 games in the outfield and made one error entering Monday.
