Popular Bay Area soul food restaurant closing original location
-----
Popular soul food restaurant Minnie Bell's Soul Movement will close its original Emeryville location on July 28.Chef-owner Fernay McPherson opened Minnie Bell's, which is known for its rosemary fried chicken and macaroni and cheese, at the Public Market Emeryville in 2018. Just this April, she expanded to a second, larger location in the Fillmore neighborhood of San Francisco.
