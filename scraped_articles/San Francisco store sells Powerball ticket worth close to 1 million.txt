San Francisco store sells Powerball ticket worth close to $1 million
-----
Somebody who bought a ticket a San Francisco store narrowly missed winning Wednesday's $154 million Powerball drawing, falling short by just one digit, but still clinched a nearly $1 million prize, according to California State Lottery officials.The $987,439 winning ticket was sold at Buddies Market at 696 Chenery St. in the Glen Park neighborhood. The ticket matched all five numbers but missed the Powerball number.
