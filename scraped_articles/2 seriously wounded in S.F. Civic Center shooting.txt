2 seriously wounded in S.F. Civic Center shooting
-----
Two people were injured in a shooting Saturday night near San Francisco's Civic Center, police said.Officers were called to the area of Seventh and Market streets for a report of a shooting about 9 p.m., police said Sunday.
They found a person with gunshot wounds nearby at Jones and McAllister streets, police said. Paramedics provided aid to the victim, who was taken to a hospital for life-threatening injuries.
