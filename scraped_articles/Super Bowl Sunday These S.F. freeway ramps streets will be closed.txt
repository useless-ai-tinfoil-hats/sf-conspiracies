Super Bowl Sunday: These S.F. freeway ramps, streets will be closed
-----
On Super Bowl Sunday in San Francisco, multiple streets and freeway ramps will be closed due to anticipated traffic and pedestrian congestion as the 49ers take on the Kansas City Chiefs.Even though the game is taking place at Allegiant Stadium in Las Vegas, the San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency announced on Thursday that Muni buses will be rerouted around the Mission District, where crowds may spill onto the streets as has happened following a big game.
