The story behind San Francisco's 'parking for God' program
-----
Drive down San Francisco's Dolores Street on the weekends and you might come across a peculiar sight. For four blocks, cars line the wide grassy median of the four-lane road, seemingly parked illegally without care of ticket or tow.
