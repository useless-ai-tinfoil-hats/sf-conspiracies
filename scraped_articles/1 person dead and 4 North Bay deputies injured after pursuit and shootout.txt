1 person dead and 4 North Bay deputies injured after pursuit and shootout
-----
Four deputies were injured and one person is dead after a North Bay pursuit and shootout early Monday, officials said.The shooting occurred at approximately 12:10 a.m. in an unincorporated area of west Santa Rosa, the Sonoma County Sheriff's Office said in a news release. The pursuit ended near Stony Point Road and Todd Road, KTVU-TV's Henry Lee reported.
