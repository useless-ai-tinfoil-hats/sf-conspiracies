Exclusive: Castro Theatre reveals final movie screening before yearlong closure
-----
San Francisco's Castro Theatre has revealed its final program before temporarily closing for renovations.A singalong presentation of Blake Edwards' 1982 musical comedy, "Victor/Victoria!," is scheduled for Feb. 4, hosted by Laurie Bushman and Sara "Toby" Moore.
