Famed Hawaii hotel sells for $725 million, will become Ritz-Carlton
-----
Turtle Bay Resort on Oahu is on track to become a Ritz-Carlton from Marriott International.The luxury property includes the 450-room resort located on 1,300 acres hugging the shore. Also included in the sale are 65 acres of land for an undisclosed new development.
