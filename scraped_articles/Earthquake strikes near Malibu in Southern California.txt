Earthquake strikes near Malibu in Southern California
-----
An earthquake with the preliminary measurement of 4.6-magnitude struck an area about 7 miles northwest of Malibu Friday afternoon, according to data from the U.S. Geological Survey.The quake was detected shortly before 2 p.m.
A series of smaller quakes were reported by automated USGS data trackers, all with epicenters near the initial quake. It was not immediately clear if they were separate earthquakes, aftershocks or the USGS system downgrading the magnitude of the initial quake.
