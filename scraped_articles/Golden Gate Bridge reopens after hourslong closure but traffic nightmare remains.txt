Golden Gate Bridge reopens after hourslong closure but traffic nightmare remains
-----
LATEST April 15, 12:30 p.m. After an hourslong closure from a protest, both southbound and northbound lanes of the Golden Gate Bridge were open, the California Highway Patrol said at 12:21 p.m."Both directions are moving, but things are moving slowly," KCBS-AM reported.
"At least two lanes of northbound I-880" near Embarcadero were reopened after a complete closure of northbound lanes amid a protest, KNTV-TV posted on X at 12:17 p.m.
