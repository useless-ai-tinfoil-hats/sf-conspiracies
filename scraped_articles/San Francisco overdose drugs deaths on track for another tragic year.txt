San Francisco overdose drugs deaths on track for another tragic year
-----
San Francisco continued to see high drug overdose deaths last month, with 66 fatalities in May, according to preliminary findings released Friday by the Office of the Chief Medical Examiner.Of those 66 accidental overdoses, 48 people had the super powerful opioid fentanyl in their system.
