Cal defensive lineman Brett Johnson retires from football
-----
Cal defensive lineman Brett Johnson, who missed the 2021 and '22 seasons because of serious injuries but returned to play in all 12 regular-season games in 2023, announced his retirement from football with an Instagram post Wednesday morning.Johnson suffered an injury to his right knee in the Bears' regular-season finale, a 33-7 win over UCLA in the Rose Bowl. The injury forced him to miss Cal's 34-14 loss to Texas Tech in the Independence Bowl.
