These Bay Area restaurants are finalists for a prestigious national food award
-----
Bay Area restaurants and chefs were nominated Wednesday for James Beard Awards, inching closer to possibly winning what's considered the equivalent of the Oscars for the food world.Geoff Davis, chef and owner of acclaimed, 7-month-old Burdell in Oakland, is up for best new chef in California, as are Rogelio Garcia of Auro in Calistoga and Buu "Billy" Ngo of Sacramento Japanese restaurant Kru.
