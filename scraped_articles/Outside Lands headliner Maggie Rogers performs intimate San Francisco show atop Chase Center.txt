Outside Lands headliner Maggie Rogers performs intimate San Francisco show atop Chase Center
-----
Maggie Rogers has shared the stage with the likes of the Grateful Dead and Bruce Springsteen, her work famously left megaproducer Pharrell Williams speechless, and she can sell out arenas and command festivals.
