Bloody, nude woman walks into Petaluma High gym, students evacuated
-----
A bloodied nude woman walked onto the Petaluma High School campus on Tuesday and entered the gym, prompting an evacuation and calls to first responders, police said.The person walked into the gym at 3:07 p.m. after entering the campus from Webster Street, Principal Giovanni Napoli told parents in an email Tuesday evening. Napoli said the gym was evacuated immediately, and first responders were at the school "within a minute or two" of the person entering the gym.
