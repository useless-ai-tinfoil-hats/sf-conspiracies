Four swim to safety after fishing boat runs aground near Bodega Bay
-----
No injuries were reported after a fishing vessel ran aground near Sonoma Coast State Park on Friday night, according to the U.S. Coast Guard.The steel-hulled boat was apparently not leaking fuel, and in investigation is seeking the cause of the boat running aground in surf about 2 miles southwest of Bodega Bay in the Greater Farallones National Marine Sanctuary, said Coast Guard Lt. Christopher Payne.
