Here's how much snow fell in Tahoe's first big storm of the winter
-----
Bone-chilling temperatures were recorded in the Tahoe Basin after a winter storm delivered fresh snow over the weekend. The gauge at the Truckee Tahoe Airport sat at negative 2 degrees just before 7 a.m. Monday, and South Lake Tahoe was zero degrees, according to the National Weather Service.Weather service forecaster Chris Johnston called the negative-temperature reading at the airport "typical when there's snow on the ground."
