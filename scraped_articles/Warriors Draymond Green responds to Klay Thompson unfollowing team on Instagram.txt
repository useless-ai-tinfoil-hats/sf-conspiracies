Warriors' Draymond Green responds to Klay Thompson unfollowing team on Instagram
-----
Last week, Warriors star Klay Thompson unfollowed the team on Instagram and deleted much of the Golden State content from his profile. But this seeming slight on the eve of NBA free agency isn't worrying his teammate Draymond Green.On his podcast "The Draymond Green Show," Green laughed off Thompson's social media snub.
"I know all of you were probably looking forward to me talking about Klay unfollowing the Warriors," he started before admitting he didn't realize anything was awry
