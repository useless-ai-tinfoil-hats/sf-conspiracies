Grateful Dead-themed music festival cancels last minute, won't offer refunds
-----
On Tuesday, organizers for Skull & Roses, a Grateful Dead-themed tribute festival in Southern California slated for later this month, announced the event had been canceled, with the harrowing kicker that refunds will not be available.The announcement came just 10 days before the festival was supposed to begin on Friday, April 19.
