Rita Moreno, Lady Camden, Ana de la Reguera celebrate at San Francisco Ballet opening gala
-----
Entering the silver-draped rotunda of San Francisco City Hall on Wednesday, Jan. 24, the classical past and high-tech future collided at the San Francisco Ballet's 91st season opening gala.The evening, themed "Pandora: Curiosity Unveiled" in homage to the new ballet "Mere Mortals" (premiering Friday, Jan. 26) about the benefits and conundrums of artificial intelligence, nodded to the region's culture of innovation.
