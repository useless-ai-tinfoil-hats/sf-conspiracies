Nobody scores in the 49ers romance novel
-----
You will never date a San Francisco 49er. But you can approximate the feeling by reading what might be the world's only 49ers romance novel.The name of the book is "Between Sundays," and it was published in 2008 by Karen Kingsbury, whose novels frequently make the New York Times bestseller list. From what I gather from her website, Kingsbury has never lived in the Bay Area, which makes her decision to stage her novel in San Francisco a perplexing one.
