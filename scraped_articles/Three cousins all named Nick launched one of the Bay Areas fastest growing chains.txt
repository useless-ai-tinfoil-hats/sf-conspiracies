Three cousins all named Nick launched one of the Bay Area's fastest growing chains
-----
It's hard for Nick Tsigaris to imagine a time when he wasn't working inside a restaurant. His two cousins — also named Nick Tsigaris — would say the same.The trio, who are all named after their grandfather, spent years working at their family's classic San Jose diners, Flames Coffee Shop, before joining forces to open their popular namesake restaurant chain, Nick the Greek.
