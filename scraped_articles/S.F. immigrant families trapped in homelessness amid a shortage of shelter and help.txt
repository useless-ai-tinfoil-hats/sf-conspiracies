S.F. immigrant families trapped in homelessness amid a shortage of shelter and help
-----
Under a full moon and biting night winds, Luz Mejia waited about an hour on the sidewalk for her bed to arrive. This night it was the back of her friend's Camry parked in a driveway in San Francisco's Bayview.Minutes after her friend parked, Mejia — a recent immigrant from Peru — stepped into the back of the car clutching a pillow, blanket and small bag. After changing her clothes, she laid her head down to sleep.
