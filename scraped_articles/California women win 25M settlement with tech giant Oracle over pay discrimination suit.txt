California women win $25M settlement with tech giant Oracle over pay discrimination suit
-----
Oracle has agreed to pay $25 million to settle a long-running lawsuit brought by employees in California accusing the tech giant of underpaying women.The plaintiffs' lawyers filed a motion on Tuesday announcing the agreement and asking for a San Mateo judge to give it the go-ahead. Should it go through, the payout will put an end to a lawsuit filed in 2017, in which women alleged that the tech giant was willfully paying female employees lower wages than their male counterparts.
