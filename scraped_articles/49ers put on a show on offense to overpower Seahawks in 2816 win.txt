49ers put on a show on offense to overpower Seahawks in 28-16 win
-----
In October, a week after the San Francisco 49ers resembled the NFL's best team while scoring 42 points in a thrashing of the Cowboys, they lost as 9.5-point favorites against a team starting an underwhelming backup quarterback.On Sunday, the situation was similar: A week after looking like the prohibitive Super Bowl favorite while scoring 42 points in a smackdown of the Eagles, they were 10.5-point favorites against a team starting an underwhelming second-stringer.
