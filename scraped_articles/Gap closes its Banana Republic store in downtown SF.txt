Gap closes its Banana Republic store in downtown SF
-----
Downtown shoppers have lost another place to buy their business wear: The Banana Republic at Embarcadero Center closed this weekend, as first reported by KRON4-TV.The closure follows a string of shuttered locations this year from parent company Gap, which also closed its longtime Old Navy store at 801 Market St. this summer, as well as the Banana Republic location inside San Francisco Centre.
