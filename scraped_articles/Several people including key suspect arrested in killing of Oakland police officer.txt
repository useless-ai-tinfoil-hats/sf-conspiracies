Several people, including 'key suspect,' arrested in killing of Oakland police officer
-----
LATEST Jan. 3, 2:45 p.m. CBS Bay Area (KPIX-TV) and KTVU-TV reported that Mark Demetrious Sanders was arrested just after midnight on Tuesday morning in connection with the death of Oakland police officer Tuan Le, who was killed while responding to a report of a burglary last week.
