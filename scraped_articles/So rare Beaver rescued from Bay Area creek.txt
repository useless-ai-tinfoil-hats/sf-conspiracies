'So rare': Beaver rescued from Bay Area creek
-----
Palo Alto Animal Control officer Cody Macartney slid on his waders and slowly lumbered toward the furry brown lump curled up in a pile of leaves in lower Matadero Creek. Her paddled tail was wrapped around herself as if she was trying to stay warm, and as he trudged through the shallow water, he half-expected her to scuttle off.But the beaver didn't move. She didn't even look up at him.
