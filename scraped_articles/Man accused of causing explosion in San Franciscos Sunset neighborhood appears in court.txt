Man accused of causing explosion in San Francisco's Sunset neighborhood appears in court
-----
A man accused of operating an illegal drug lab that caused a deadly explosion at his home in San Francisco's Sunset neighborhood last year appeared in court on Thursday. During the hearing at San Francisco's Hall of Justice, Judge Eric Fleming set 54-year-old Darron Price's next court date for March 14.Price was arrested on Feb. 10, 2023, in connection with an explosion and fire the previous day at 1734 22nd Ave. in the Sunset District.
