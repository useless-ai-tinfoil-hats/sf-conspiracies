Rare home on the street where Bernard Maybeck lived and worked is for sale
-----
On the street where famed Bay Area architect Bernard Maybeck lived and worked, 3 Maybeck Twin Drive is for sale, asking $3.5 million. The Berkeley home, completely remodeled in 2014, offers a contemporary update on classic mid-century design.The street this house rests on is historic, as its name might imply.
