Bosa, Warner among 49ers holdovers seeking to erase pain of Super Bowl loss
-----
"The Holdovers" is a hit movie searching for some big awards and significant hardware.So are "the holdovers" of the San Francisco 49ers. The group of men who lost the Super Bowl four years ago are hoping for an award-winning performance this time.
The holdovers include vital members of the team. Vocal leaders and standard setters: Nick Bosa, Fred Warner, Arik Armstead, Dre Greenlaw, George Kittle, Deebo Samuel, Kyle Juszczyk.
