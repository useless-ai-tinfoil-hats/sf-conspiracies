49ers' Kyle ‘Juice’ Juszczyk appears in an unusual sign off Bay Area highway
-----
Bay Area drivers spotted an unusual sign for the San Francisco 49ers on Highway 101.Ahead of the playoff game on Jan. 20 between the 49ers and the Green Bay Packers, one particular red-and-gold family has been getting their name out. Kristin Juszczyk, wife of 49ers fullback Kyle Juszczyk, stitched a custom jacket for Taylor Swift last week, and now an homage to her husband and his nickname has appeared in Santa Clara.
