How the Giants plan to honor Willie Mays this season
-----
Thursday's game in Alabama at Rickwood Field gave the baseball world the perfect stage to remember Willie Mays, who began his Hall of Fame career at the old ballpark with the Birmingham Black Barons as a teenager.Memorials for Mays will continue back in the Bay Area when the San Francisco Giants return on Monday. That happens to be June 24, a fitting day to honor the incomparable No. 24 nearly a week after his death at age 93.
