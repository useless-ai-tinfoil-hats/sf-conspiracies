Cisco confirms layoffs of 5%, or over 4,000 jobs cut
-----
Cisco confirmed it will cut 5% of its workforce, or more than 4,000 people, as it becomes the latest tech giant to conduct major layoffs.The San Jose company reported lower revenue of $12.8 billion, down 6% from the prior year, for the fiscal second quarter on Wednesday. It also cut its annual revenue forecast to $51.5 billion to $52.5 billion, down from earlier forecasts of $53.8 billion to $55 billion.
