Founders of hip SF coffee chain step down, former Starbucks exec takes over
-----
Hip San Francisco coffee roaster Sightglass has something new brewing: Founders (and brothers) Jerad and Justin Morrison have stepped down, with the board appointing a former Starbucks executive as the company's new CEO.The brothers "have stepped down from the business to pursue other endeavors," according to a LinkedIn post from April first reported on by Sprudge.
