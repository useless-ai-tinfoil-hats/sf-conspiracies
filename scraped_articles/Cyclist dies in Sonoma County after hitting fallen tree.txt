Cyclist dies in Sonoma County after hitting fallen tree
-----
A cyclist died died in a crash on a Sonoma County roadway on Monday, according to the California Highway Patrol.The woman was traveling west on Hauser Bridge Road, west of King Ridge Road, in Cazadero when she swung around a curve at a fast speed and struck the tree that had fallen to the side of the road, CHP said. Officer David deRutte, a spokesperson with the CHP's Santa Rosa office, wrote to SFGATE in an email that the tree was about 10 feet off the roadway on the shoulder.
