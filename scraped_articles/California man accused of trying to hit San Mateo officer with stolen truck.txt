California man accused of trying to hit San Mateo officer with stolen truck
-----
San Mateo police said they arrested an Oakdale (Stanislaus County) man suspected of multiple felony charges, including assaulting a police officer with a deadly weapon stemming from a dramatic chase in November.Police said in a statement that they arrested 31-year-old Joshua Carmelich on Tuesday after he allegedly fled from officers in a stolen pickup truck, drove the vehicle onto curbs and directly at an officer and eventually escaped after crashing through a fence.
