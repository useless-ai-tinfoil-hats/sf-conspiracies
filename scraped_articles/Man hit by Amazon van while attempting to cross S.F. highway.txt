Man hit by Amazon van while attempting to cross S.F. highway
-----
A man was hospitalized with major injuries after he was struck by an Amazon van while attempting to cross a freeway Wednesday morning, California Highway Patrol officials said.CHP officials responded to a 911 call just after 11:46 a.m. at the bottom of the ramp connecting northbound Highway 101 to southbound Interstate 280. There they found a man who had attempted to cross the freeway and was hit by an Amazon van, spokespersons said.
