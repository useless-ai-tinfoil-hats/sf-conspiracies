Endorsement: Katie Porter deserves to make her case for Senate to California voters in November
-----
California has nearly 40 million people and only two senators to represent them. More than any other state, we need senators who deeply understand the full breadth of our disparate needs. This was among the strengths of the late Dianne Feinstein, whose love for California, Senate seniority and coveted spot on the Appropriations Committee allowed her to send billions in federal dollars back to the state each year for everything from mass transit to wildland preservation.Of the 27
