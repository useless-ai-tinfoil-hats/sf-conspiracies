Where are the fires that are sending smoke to the SF Bay Area?
-----
Several wildfires burning in Northern California and southern Oregon are sending smoke to the San Francisco Bay Area.The two main fires contributing to the smoke in the region are Oregon's Anvil Fire just east of Port Orford, Oregon, and a group of California fires given the very long name of 2023 SRF Lightning Complex and Redwood Lightning Complex, according to the Bay Area Air Quality Management District.
"They've both been creating smoke," said Kristine Roselius, a spokesperson
