Warriors’ Draymond Green ejected in opening minutes of game in Orlando
-----
ORLANDO, Fla. — Forward-center Draymond Green was ejected Wednesday in the first quarter of the Golden State Warriors' game against the Orlando Magic at Kia Center.Green was assessed back-to-back technical fouls after arguing with referee Ray Acosta at the 8:24 mark.
The back-and-forth happened after Orlando's Paolo Banchero drove through Andrew Wiggins for a layup and a foul.
