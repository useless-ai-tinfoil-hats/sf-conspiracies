The Walnut Creek Farmers' Market was closed Sunday after two people suffered serious injuries in a hit-and-run crash.Police said an officer found the sport utility vehicle they believe struck the pair and arrested the suspect, whose name was not released.
The crash occurred around 5:15 a.m. on Locust Street near Cole Avenue.
The victims were taken to a hospital, where they were listed in critical condition, police said. No further details about the crash were released.
