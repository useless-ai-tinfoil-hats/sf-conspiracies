22-year-old SF Castro restaurant in landmark building is closing
-----
Catch, a seafood restaurant in San Francisco's Castro District, is closing after 22 years, as first reported by Hoodline. The restaurant known for its beer-battered fish-and-chips, mussels and cioppino-inspired seafood stew plans to close after its final dinner service on March 9, owner Sanjay Gujral told SFGATE.Gujral, who bought Catch from original owner David Weiss in 2007, has decided to retire.
