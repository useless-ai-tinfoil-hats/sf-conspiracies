MAGA Republican running in Shasta implies sitting supervisor worships Satan
-----
A woman running for a spot on the Shasta County Board of Supervisors seemed to publicly accuse District 3 Supervisor Mary Rickert of worshipping Satan at a meeting of the board on Tuesday.The woman's proof? Rickert's California license plate number begins with 666.
Laura Hobbs, a self-described MAGA Republican who is running for District 2 Supervisor Tim Garman's seat on the board, brought a photograph of Rickert's license plate to the podium with her during the public comment
