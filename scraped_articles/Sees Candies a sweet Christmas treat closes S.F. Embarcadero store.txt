In another hit to downtown shopping in San Francisco, a See's Candies store on the Embarcadero permanently closed on Christmas Eve, a sign posted on the store's door said.The 102-year-old company closed its location at 3 Embarcadero Center next to newly opened pop-up shops, including Devil's Teeth bakery, which have attracted more shoppers to the languishing shopping center in an effort to revitalize downtown.
