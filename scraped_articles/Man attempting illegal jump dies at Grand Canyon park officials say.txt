Man attempting illegal jump dies at Grand Canyon, park officials say
-----
A person attempting an illegal BASE jump died at the Grand Canyon on Thursday, park officials said. It is the second death in two days at the national park.According to a statement released by the park, rangers received a report around 7:30 a.m. Thursday about someone attempting to BASE jump from Yavapai Point on the South Rim.
