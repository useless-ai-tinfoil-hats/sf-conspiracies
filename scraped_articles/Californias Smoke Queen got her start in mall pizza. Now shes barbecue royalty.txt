California's Smoke Queen got her start in mall pizza. Now she's barbecue royalty
-----
At Southern California's newest barbecue hotspot, there is no white bread available for sopping up the sauce.Instead, Smoke Queen Barbecue fills its trays with unique smoked meats and an array of sides seen nowhere else before. For perhaps the best example, check out the restaurant's take on Louisiana-style dirty rice (a Southern staple); theirs is infused with the flavors of Singaporean Hainan chicken and flecks of Chinese sausage.
