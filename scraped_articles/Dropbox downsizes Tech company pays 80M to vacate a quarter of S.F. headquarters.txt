Dropbox downsizes: Tech company pays $80M to vacate a quarter of S.F. headquarters
-----
Dropbox has reached an agreement to surrender over a quarter of its San Francisco headquarters to the building's owner, according to documents filed Friday with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.The tech company's lease amendment permits it to vacate 165,244 square feet of its space at 1800 Owens St. in the city's Mission Bay neighborhood in exchange for a termination fee totaling $79 million.
