How did ‘Fleet Week’ land in San Francisco? It started with Dianne Feinstein
-----
It was two weeks before the first Fleet Week in San Francisco, and Mayor Dianne Feinstein was on a charm offensive.When U.S. Navy Admiral Thomas Hayward visited City Hall on Oct. 14, 1981, Feinstein started listing all the freebies the sailors would get during shore leave, including tickets to a Cal football game in Berkeley and complimentary rides on Muni.
