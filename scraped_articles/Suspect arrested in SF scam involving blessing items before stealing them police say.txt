Suspect arrested in SF scam involving 'blessing' items before stealing them, police say
-----
A person was arrested earlier this year in connection with a scam in San Francisco in which people's items were stolen after being "blessed" in a ceremony, police said.The suspect, a 68-year-old man, was arrested in the Los Angeles area on Feb. 26 after police located his SUV, the San Francisco Police Department said in a news release. The man was arrested on suspicion of possessing stolen property, which police believe to be from the "blessing scam," and possession of child pornography.
