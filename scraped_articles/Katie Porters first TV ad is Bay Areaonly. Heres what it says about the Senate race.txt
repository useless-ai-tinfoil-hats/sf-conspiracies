Katie Porter’s first TV ad is Bay Area-only. Here’s what it says about the Senate race
-----
Orange County Rep. Katie Porter is dropping the first TV ad of her Senate campaign in the Bay Area on Wednesday, a campaign spokesperson told the Chronicle, a move that shows that Porter's campaign thinks Oakland Rep. Barbara Lee's support in the region is weak.Porter, Lee and Rep. Adam Schiff, of Burbank are the leading Democrats battling to replace Sen. Laphonza Butler, who Gov. Gavin Newsom appointed in October to replace Sen. Dianne Feinstein. Butler is not running for a full term.
