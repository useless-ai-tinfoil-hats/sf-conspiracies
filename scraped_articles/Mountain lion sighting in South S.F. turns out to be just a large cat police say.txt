Mountain lion sighting in South S.F. turns out to be just a large cat, police say
-----
A presumed mountain lion sighted this week walking atop a fence in South San Francisco turned out to be a large gray cat, police reported."UPDATE. We were able to confirm the Big Cat was not a mountain lion," the South San Francisco Police Department said in a post on its Facebook page. "We are happy to report there is no potential threat for the neighborhood."
The animal was spotted by a home security camera early Tuesday morning walking along the fence line separating two homes
