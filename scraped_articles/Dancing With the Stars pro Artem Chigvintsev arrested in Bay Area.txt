'Dancing With the Stars' pro Artem Chigvintsev arrested in Bay Area
-----
Reality TV star Artem Chigvintsev was arrested Thursday on suspicion of domestic abuse in Yountville, an affluent town in Napa County. Chigvintsev, 42, is a Russian dancer who gained fame on reality competition shows like "Strictly Come Dancing" and "Dancing With the Stars."According to Napa County booking records, Chigvintsev was arrested at 10 a.m. Thursday and released from jail four hours later after making a $25,000 bail.
