After 25 years of sausages and beer, S.F. classic to close
-----
Rosamunde Sausage Grill, a longtime San Francisco destination for sausages and beer, will close next month.The 2832 Mission St. business announced the closure on Instagram Friday, Oct. 27. The restaurant will shutter Nov. 21.
"Although we are sad to say goodbye to the neighborhood, we feel fortunate to have served such a wide and diverse community," the post reads.
It's the latest in a recent wave of local restaurant closures.
