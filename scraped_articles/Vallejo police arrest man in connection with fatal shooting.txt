Vallejo police arrest man in connection with fatal shooting
-----
Vallejo police have arrested a 47-year-old man in connection with a fatal shooting in the city that happened about three weeks ago.Fenton Dale Warren, a Vallejo resident, was arrested without incident Wednesday after the Vallejo Police Department's SWAT team successfully searched for him on the 200 block of Couch Street, department officials announced Saturday. Warren was then booked into Solano County Jail on suspicion of murder.
