Anyone who used the internet in the 1980s or 1990s vividly remembers the signature dial-up sound that meant a computer was connecting to the internet. That's because in the early days of the web, computers connected through telephone lines. When the internet was in use, the phone line was occupied and couldn't be used for calls until the computer disconnected.Internet access has come a long way since then.
