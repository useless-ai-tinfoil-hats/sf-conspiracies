A privately owned California cave, kept secret for decades, shares its mysteries
-----
The first person to peer into the 18-inch crack in an Amador County hillside got quite the surprise. The gap swallowed the sunlight, exhaling sweet, cool air. Translucent pillars flushed orange in the glow of weak candlelight. And deeper inside, the cavern held secrets, treasures and creatures that would be found — and forgotten — over the next 170 years.Black Chasm Cavern is one of Earth's rarest creations.
