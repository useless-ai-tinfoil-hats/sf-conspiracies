S.F. landlord who squeezed people into ‘unsafe,’ ‘infested’ apartments target of city lawsuit
-----
City Attorney David Chiu filed a lawsuit against a San Francisco property owner who is alleged to have illegally converted a single-family home into five apartments, taking in $9,300 a month in rent by squeezing 15 people into what the complaint says are illegal and unsafe living conditions.The lawsuit was filed Thursday morning against Rafael Garcia Sanchez, the owner of a three-bedroom home on a quiet residential block of Oakdale Avenue in San Francisco's Bayview District.
