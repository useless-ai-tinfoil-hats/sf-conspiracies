Endorsement: S.F. desperately needs affordable housing. Prop. A will give us more
-----
Of all the San Francisco ballot initiatives in recent years, Proposition A in the March 5 primary might be one of the least controversial. The $300 million general obligation bond would enable San Francisco to build more affordable housing for its workforce, in addition to moderate-income and low-income housing and units for people exiting homelessness.
