Steph Curry inspires French sarcasm in wake of Olympic heroics: ‘He disgusts me’
-----
PARIS — Three days after Stephen Curry put France's hopes of an Olympic basketball gold medal to sleep, Kendal and his friends played a spirited pickup game in Luxembourg Gardens.Kendal, 25, lives in Paris and wanted to attend the USA-France game Aug. 10 at Bercy Arena. But tickets were too expensive, so he watched on television with friends.
"I've played basketball since I was 10, and Steph Curry is my favorite player," Kendal said in English with a thick, lyrical French flavor.
