Leader of S.F.-based MS-13 gang sentenced to life in federal prison
-----
A former leader of a San Francisco branch of the MS-13 gang was sentenced to life in federal prison on Tuesday, according to federal prosecutors.In 2020, a federal grand jury indicted Elmer Rodriguez and 16 others on charges related to their alleged involvement in MS-13's 20th Street Clique, which operates in San Francisco's Mission District. All 17 have been convicted, including Rodriguez, according to the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Northern District of California.
