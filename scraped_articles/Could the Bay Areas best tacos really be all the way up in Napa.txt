Could the Bay Area's best tacos really be all the way up in Napa?
-----
When I was 16 years old, I had the best tacos al pastor of my life.I was traveling through Morelia in the central Mexican state of Michoacán, on a summer exchange program with my high school. My friends and I regularly frequented a local taqueria for inexpensive, delicious tacos carved from a pineapple-topped trompo, a vertical rotating spit of meat.
