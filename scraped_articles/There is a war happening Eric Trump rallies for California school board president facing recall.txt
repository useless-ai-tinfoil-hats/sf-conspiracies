‘There is a war happening’: Eric Trump rallies for California school board president facing recall
-----
TEMECULA, Riverside County — Eric Trump took the stage at a line-dancing club Wednesday night to the stomps of cowboy boots and chants of "USA," as the headlining act of a fundraiser to support far-right candidates in three Southern California school districts.The event was held by the Inland Empire Family PAC, which was put together by an Inland Empire pastor to elect a platform of conservative candidates to school boards in Temecula, Murrieta and Lake Elsinore, all in Riverside County.
