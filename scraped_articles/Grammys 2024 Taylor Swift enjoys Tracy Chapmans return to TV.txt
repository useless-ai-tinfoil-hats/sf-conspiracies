Grammys 2024: Taylor Swift enjoys Tracy Chapman’s return to TV
-----
Tracy Chapman made a rare public appearance at the 66th Grammy Awards — and Taylor Swift was more than happy to witness history in the making.Chapman, a longtime San Francisco resident, took the stage with country singer Luke Combs during the ceremony at Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles on Sunday, Feb. 4, to perform Chapman's 1988 hit "Fast Car" together.
