Cyndi Lauper announces farewell tour, including San Francisco date
-----
Cyndi Lauper is hitting the road for her first major outing in more than a decade, with a stop in San Francisco.The 70-year-old singer plans to visit nearly two dozen cities across North America this fall during her "Girls Just Wanna Have Fun" Farewell Tour, which is scheduled to kick off Oct. 18 in Montreal and conclude Dec. 5 in Chicago.
