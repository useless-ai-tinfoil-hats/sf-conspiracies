Pacifica police fatally shoot woman who fled after she shot her son, officials say
-----
Police officers in Pacifica fatally shot a woman who was fleeing after she shot her adult son Wednesday morning, authorities said.Just before 8 a.m., two officers responded to a 911 call from a man who reported he had been shot by his mother at their apartment in the 2500 block of Francisco Boulevard, the Pacifica Police Department said in a news release on social media.
When they arrived, they saw the woman driving away from the scene, police said.
