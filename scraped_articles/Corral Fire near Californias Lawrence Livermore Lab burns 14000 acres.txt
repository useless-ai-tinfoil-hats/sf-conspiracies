Corral Fire near California's Lawrence Livermore Lab burns 14,000 acres
-----
LATEST June 2, 5:15 p.m. All evacuation orders issued for the Corral Fire will be downgraded to evacuation warnings as of 6 p.m. on Sunday, Cal Fire said in a statement.June 2, 5:45 a.m. Northern California's first major wildfire of the season has burned 14,000 acres in San Joaquin County. The Corral Fire, which began Saturday afternoon along Corral Hollow Road near the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory Site 300, is 30% contained as of Sunday afternoon.
