Texas-style barbecue with Indonesian flavors will soon be available in San Francisco's Mission Bay.Alameda-based Fikscue Craft Barbecue is aiming to open a new location at the 11-acre San Francisco complex Thrive City in early 2025, according to a Thrive City press release. Husband-and-wife duo Fik and Reka Saleh had started the restaurant as a home business in 2020 before opening Fikscue in the East Bay to great acclaim last year.
"We are excited. We are anxious," said Reka Saleh.
