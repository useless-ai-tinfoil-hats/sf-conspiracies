5-year-old boy fatally stabs twin during fight in Santa Cruz County home
-----
A family in Santa Cruz County is mourning the death of their 5-year-old son after he was fatally stabbed by his twin brother Wednesday, officials said.Just before 4 p.m., Santa Cruz County sheriff's deputies rushed to the 200 block of Tucker Road in Scotts Valley, an unincorporated area, after a report of a 5-year-old stabbing victim. The stabbing occurred during a fight between the twins, when one grabbed a small kitchen knife and stabbed his brother, according to the sheriff's office.
