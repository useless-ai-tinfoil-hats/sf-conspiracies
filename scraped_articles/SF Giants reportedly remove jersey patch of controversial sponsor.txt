SF Giants reportedly remove jersey patch of controversial sponsor
-----
The San Francisco Giants have reportedly removed jersey patches for Cruise, the San Francisco driverless car company that had its permits to operate suspended last October. The news, which was first reported by the San Francisco Business Times, comes a day after the Giants began spring training.
