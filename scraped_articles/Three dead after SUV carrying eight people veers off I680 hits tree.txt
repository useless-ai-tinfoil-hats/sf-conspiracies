Three dead after SUV carrying eight people veers off I-680, hits tree
-----
Three people died and five were injured after a car crashed Monday afternoon on Interstate 680 near Pleasanton.A 2004 Honda Pilot heading south around 2:30 p.m. began to veer on the slick freeway, drifting from the far left lane to the far right before plummeting off the road at Sunol Boulevard, according to a spokesperson for the California Highway Patrol.
