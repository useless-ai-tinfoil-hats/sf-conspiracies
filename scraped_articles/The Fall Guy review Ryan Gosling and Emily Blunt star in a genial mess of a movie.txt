‘The Fall Guy’ review: Ryan Gosling and Emily Blunt star in a genial mess of a movie
-----
"The Fall Guy" is a sprawling, clumsy mess of a movie. But it's also genial and likable, with genuine star power and an engaging spirit about it.Most people will enjoy it. I enjoyed parts of it, and the rest I didn't hate. The problem is that the story is both ugly and uninteresting. It can't be made interesting because the filmmakers themselves don't seem to take it seriously, but neither can they joke their way to making the story less ugly.
