S.F. Mint Plaza $700-a-month sleeping pods deemed illegal, unsafe by building inspectors
-----
A building offering sleeping pods at 12 Mint Plaza was found to have violated building codes.The Department of Building Inspection began investigating the Mint Plaza pod housing complex for potential building code violations late last month. Brownstone Shared Housing, the firm behind the complex, advertises its $700-a-month sleeping pods as "perfect for students, interns and professionals."
But the city says the complex is illegal and a safety hazard.
