Pearl Jam to bring new album, ‘Dark Matter,’ to BottleRock. Here’s what you need to know
-----
Pearl Jam will bring new music to BottleRock Napa Valley as one of the headliners of the Memorial Day weekend concert.The Rock & Roll Hall of Fame act announced the title of its forthcoming album, "Dark Matter," and released a single of the same name on Tuesday, Feb. 13. The band also announced details of a world tour slated to kick off in May, which begins before its appearance at the three-day festival.
