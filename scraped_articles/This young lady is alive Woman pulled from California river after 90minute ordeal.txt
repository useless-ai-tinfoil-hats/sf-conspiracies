'This young lady is alive': Woman pulled from California river after 90-minute ordeal
-----
A 27-year-old woman swept away in the South Yuba River on Tuesday was rescued after being spotted on a rock by a helicopter in a "challenging" 90-minute ordeal. The woman was swimming in the river for the first time with four friends at Hoyt Crossing, the Nevada County Sheriff's Office said in a statement.
