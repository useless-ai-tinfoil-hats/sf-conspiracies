‘So proud’: Nepal Day Parade in S.F. attracts fast-growing population
-----
Shortly before the Nepal Day Parade got underway, Dikendra Maskey walked along the Civic Center Plaza, head down as he counted on his fingers the number of Nepalese who were living in the Bay Area when he arrived 30 years ago.
