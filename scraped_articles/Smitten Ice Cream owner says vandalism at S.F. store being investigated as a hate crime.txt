Smitten Ice Cream owner says vandalism at S.F. store being investigated as a hate crime
-----
The owner of Smitten Ice Cream, a popular Bay Area ice cream chain whose store on Valencia Street in San Francisco was vandalized last week, said Tuesday that the incident is being "investigated by authorities as a hate crime."Robyn Sue Fisher, Smitten's founder and CEO, said the flagship ice cream shop in the Mission District "was vandalized, badly damaged, and plastered with graffiti" in a statement posted to the website Tuesday morning, her first public comment since the incident.
