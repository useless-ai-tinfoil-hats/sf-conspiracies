Oakland police officer fired for sexual misconduct
-----
An Oakland police officer was fired for sexual misconduct after a complaint from a member of the public sparked an internal investigation, the Chronicle has confirmed with two people familiar with the investigation.The Oct. 11, 2022, complaint alleged that an unidentified officer violated department policy related to sexual activity while on duty.
