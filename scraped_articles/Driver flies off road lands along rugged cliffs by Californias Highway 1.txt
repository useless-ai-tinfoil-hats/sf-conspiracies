Driver flies off road, lands along rugged cliffs by California's Highway 1
-----
A man was rescued after swerving his car off the road and landing in a rugged coastal area along Highway 1 near Big Sur this past week, the California Highway Patrol's Coastal Division Air Operations wrote in a Feb. 28 Facebook post.An employee of Post Ranch Inn left work on Sunday, but never arrived home, according the post. Post Ranch Inn is a five-star hotel in Big Sur. About two days after his disappearance, CHP's air division was asked to search from the Post Ranch area in Big Sur
