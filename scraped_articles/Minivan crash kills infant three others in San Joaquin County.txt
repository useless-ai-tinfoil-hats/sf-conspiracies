Minivan crash kills infant, three others in San Joaquin County
-----
The driver of a minivan and three of his passengers, including an infant and a teen, were killed Saturday night in a solo crash on a rain soaked highway in San Joaquin County, authorities said.Three other passengers, ages 6 to 14, were sent to hospitals with major injuries, the California Highway Patrol said.
