Beloved Berkeley food market closing after 28 years
-----
Popular Bay Area grocery store Market Hall Foods is closing its Berkeley location after nearly three decades, citing an array of post-pandemic economic challenges.Market Hall Foods' last day at 1786 Fourth St. will be May 26, according to an announcement from co-owner Sara Wilson. The store's original location in Oakland will remain open.
