S.F. may make Sunset Night Market permanent after huge success
-----
After a successful, festive launch of the Sunset Night Market on Friday, Supervisor Joel Engardio said he hopes to make the street festival a monthly event in San Francisco's Sunset District.About 10,000 people flocked to Irving Street on Friday night for the inaugural Sunset Night Market, according to Engardio. There were food vendors, performers and long lines spilling out of the doors at local businesses.
