Review: In ‘The Union,’ Halle Berry and Mark Wahlberg lift a mediocre action comedy
-----
There's no good reason to watch "The Union" besides Halle Berry and Mark Wahlberg — but those are two good reasons right there.This latest Netflix movie trades on the two actors' individual charm and their rapport with each other. They provide an agreeable experience, even though they play characters who go through the movie killing people.
