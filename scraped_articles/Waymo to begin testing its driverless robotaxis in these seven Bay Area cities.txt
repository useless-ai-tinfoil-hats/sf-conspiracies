Waymo to begin testing its driverless robotaxis in these seven Bay Area cities
-----
Waymo's driverless robotaxis will soon be driving outside San Francisco's borders to cities north of San Mateo, as the company begins its expansion to the Peninsula.The autonomous vehicle company announced Friday that it will test fully driverless rides in Daly City, Brisbane, South San Francisco, Colma, San Bruno, Millbrae and Burlingame "in the coming weeks."
