After first full practice, 49ers’ Kyle Shanahan displeased with UNLV’s fields
-----
LAS VEGAS — The San Francisco 49ers remain unhappy about their practice fields at UNLV, and head coach Kyle Shanahan did little to conceal his displeasure after his team's first full practice Wednesday afternoon."We're here.
