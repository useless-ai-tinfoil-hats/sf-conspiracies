6 Sureños gang members get federal prison time for 7 separate killings in S.F. and East Bay
-----
Six men were sentenced to federal prison terms ranging from 11 to 32 years in connection with seven gang-related killings in San Francisco and Richmond, the U.S. Attorney's Office said Tuesday.The men pleaded guilty in October to various charges stemming from a flurry of violence and drug dealing the 19th Street/16th Street Sureños street gang carried out over the course of nearly 10 years, according to the U.S. Attorney's Office.
