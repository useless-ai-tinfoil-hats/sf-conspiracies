Peninsula suburb is fighting housing for the homeless. But residents say they’re not ‘NIMBYs’
-----
The fight over a proposed housing project for formerly homeless people in a quiet Peninsula suburb kicked into high gear Wednesday when supporters and opponents squared off at a one-day trial over the city of Millbrae's attempt to block the proposal.For months, many residents and city officials have decried the plan from San Mateo County to spend $33 million to buy the La Quinta Inn & Suites across the freeway from San Francisco International Airport and turn it into a 75-unit building
