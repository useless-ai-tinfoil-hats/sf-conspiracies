Stabbed Napa County homeowner pulls out knife, stabs assailant, sheriff says
-----
When a 61-year-old man was stabbed in the back on his Napa County property Saturday morning, he pulled the knife out of his back and stabbed the assailant, officials said.Andrew Kenneth Dixon, 33, is accused of stabbing the Napa County man and was arrested on suspicion of attempted murder, elder abuse and assault with a deadly weapon, the Napa County Sheriff's Office said. Both the homeowner and Dixon are recovering from non-life-threatening stab wounds.
