BART will stop accepting paper tickets soon. Here's what to know
-----
BART will officially stop accepting paper tickets at its stations starting Nov. 30, completing a full transition to Clipper cards that was interrupted last year by a supply chain hitch, the transit agency said.Bay Area Rapid Transit began eliminating the sale of its magstripe paper tickets in August 2019 to transition riders into using only Clipper cards, which are produced by the Metropolitan Transportation Commission and can be used on all Bay Area transit systems.
