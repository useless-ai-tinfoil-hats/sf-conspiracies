Spinouts reported over Donner Summit as snow falls in Calif.'s Sierra
-----
Chain control went into effect late Tuesday morning at Donner Summit on California's Interstate 80, a key route in and out of the Tahoe Basin, as snow levels started lowering in the northern Sierra Nevada, officials said.Caltrans called on drivers in the mountain range to slow down as traffic was being temporarily held on westbound I-80 in Truckee at the Donner Lake Interchange "due to spinouts over Donner Summit," the agency said on social media.
