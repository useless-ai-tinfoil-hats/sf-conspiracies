California prison officials are winning the war over whether to limit solitary confinement
-----
As several thousand California prisoners remain in solitary confinement for weeks or months at a time, isolated from visitors and fellow inmates, legislative efforts to restrict the practice are again hitting a brick wall.On Monday, the author of AB280, which would have limited solitary confinement sentences to 15 days and barred isolation of young and older inmates, said he had shelved the legislation.
