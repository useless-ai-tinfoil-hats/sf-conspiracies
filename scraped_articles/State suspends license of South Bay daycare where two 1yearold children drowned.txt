State suspends license of South Bay daycare where two 1-year-old children drowned
-----
A San Jose day care has had its license suspended after two children drowned in its backyard pool, according to California's Department of Social Services.Happy Happy Home Daycare had been a licensed residential day care in San Jose's Almaden neighborhood since 2021, state records show. On Monday, authorities received a call requesting a welfare check because several children were in the day care's backyard pool, according to officer Steve Aponte, a spokesperson for the San Jose Police
