Retro cocktail bar brings live jazz and cabaret to S.F.
-----
A new bar and cabaret venue opens in San Francisco on Thursday, Feb. 8, from the owners of Stookey's Club Moderne, a Lower Nob Hill cocktail lounge with an eccentric post-Prohibition theme.The Blue Room, located next door at 891 Bush St., will feature regular live music, spanning genres from jazz to punk. Under the light of a disco ball, bartenders will serve classic cocktails ($14-$16) like the French 75, Aviation and Sidecar, plus a menu of low- and no-alcohol options.
