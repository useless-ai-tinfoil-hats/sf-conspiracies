Measles case confirmed at California's busiest airport
-----
Public health officials are warning the public that a person with a confirmed case of measles traveled through California's busiest airport earlier this month.The Los Angeles County Department of Public Health is advising travelers who were at Los Angeles International Airport's Terminal B between 2 to 4 p.m. on Thursday, May 9, to assess themselves for the highly contagious virus.
The person with a confirmed case arrived at Terminal B, Gate 134, in LAX just before 2 p.m. via Qatar
