Treasured Haight-Ashbury clothing and costume store closing after four decades
-----
Distractions, a 41-year-old clothing store and Haight-Ashbury mainstay, will close by the end of this month.The store's founder, Jim Siegel, announced plans to retire at the end of October via a sign on the window late this summer, but had also been trying to find a buyer for the business so that it could remain open without him.
