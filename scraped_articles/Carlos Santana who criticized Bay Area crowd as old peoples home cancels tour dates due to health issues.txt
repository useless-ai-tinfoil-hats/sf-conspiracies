Carlos Santana, who criticized Bay Area crowd as ‘old people’s home,’ cancels tour dates due to health issues
-----
Carlos Santana has canceled some of his upcoming tour dates to undergo a "corrective procedure" for his back.The news coincides with a contentious performance by the Grammy-winning guitarist at the Shoreline Amphitheatre in Mountain View on Tuesday, Aug. 27, during which the 77-year-old Rock & Roll Hall of Fame musician who was an integral part of the San Francisco music scene criticized the hometown crowd for its lack of energy.
