San Francisco Dior store in Union Square robbed after car smashes facade
-----
Thieves drove a car into San Francisco's Dior store early Thursday, shattering the front doors of the facade and smashing glass cabinets to ransack the luxury store.The impact of the car hitting the door mangled the metal gates and broke through the glass double doors. Workers replaced the doors Thursday morning amid showers of glass scattered on the sidewalk and store entryway as police officers surveyed the damage.
