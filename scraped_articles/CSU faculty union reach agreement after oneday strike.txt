CSU, faculty union reach agreement after one-day strike
-----
California State University and the faculty union representing more than 29,000 CSU employees struck a tentative agreement Monday night after instructors, counselors, librarians and coaches walked off the job at campuses up and down the state.The deal means faculty will return to classrooms Tuesday pending ratification.
