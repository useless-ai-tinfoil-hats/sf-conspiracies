Growing airport in North Bay to add nonstop flight to Las Vegas
-----
Commercial air service, already on the rise at Charles M. Schulz-Sonoma County Airport, got another lift this week when Alaska Airlines announced a new direct flight to and from Las Vegas.The flights will start Oct. 1, the airline said Thursday, and will make Las Vegas the airline's seventh nonstop destination out of the Wine Country airport.
