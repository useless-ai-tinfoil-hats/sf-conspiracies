Warriors’ Moses Moody out for Bulls game with strained calf
-----
CHICAGO — The Golden State Warriors are down another player before the start of their four-game trip.Moses Moody surfaced on the Warriors' injury report Thursday, listed as out for Friday night's game against the Bulls because of a strained left calf. He presumably suffered the injury during Wednesday night's 141-105 loss to New Orleans at Chase Center.
