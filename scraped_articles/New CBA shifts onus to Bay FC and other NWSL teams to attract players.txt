New CBA shifts onus to Bay FC and other NWSL teams to attract players
-----
After an expansion cycle rife with player concerns about agency over their careers, the new CBA between the NWSL and NWSLPA gives players consent over where they can be traded and is the first in North American sports to eliminate a college draft.The deal will run through the 2030 season. The NWSL first approached the players' union about a new CBA in September, with the expiration of the current deal more than two years away.
