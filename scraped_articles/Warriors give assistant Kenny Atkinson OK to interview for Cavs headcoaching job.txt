Warriors give assistant Kenny Atkinson OK to interview for Cavs’ head-coaching job
-----
The Golden State Warriors have granted assistant coach Kenny Atkinson permission to interview with the Cleveland Cavaliers for their head coaching vacancy, a league source told the Chronicle — confirming a report from ESPN.The decision came after the Cavaliers dismissed head coach J.B. Bickerstaff last week after four-plus seasons, consecutive playoff berths and an Eastern Conference semifinal loss to the conference champion Boston Celtics.
