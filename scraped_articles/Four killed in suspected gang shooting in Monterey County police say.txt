Four killed in suspected gang shooting in Monterey County, police say
-----
A shooting in Monterey County on Sunday killed four people and injured others, initial reports indicated.Police in King City said a woman and three men were killed in the suspected gang shooting, KSBW reported shortly before 9 p.m.
Details on the other victims' injuries were not immediately available.
Monterey County Supervisor Chris Lopez, who represents King City, posted a brief message about the shooting on Facebook.
