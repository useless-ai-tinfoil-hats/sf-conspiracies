Cal softball series in Louisiana involves jeering for kneeling and ejections
-----
Cal softball players were jeered after kneeling during the national anthem of a game at Louisiana Lafayette on Friday, then — two days later — alleged verbal harassment and frustration led to benches clearing and ejections.Six Bears players kneeled before the Friday game. Boos and other jeers — "stand up California" and "liberal wokeness" — could be heard on the ESPN Plus broadcast and other Cal players can be seen blocking the view of the kneeling players before gesturing to the crowd
