United Airlines flight diverted to San Francisco after smoke detected onboard
-----
A United Airlines flight from Los Angeles to Hong Kong had to be diverted to San Francisco International Airport on Friday after smoke was detected inside the aircraft.The crew of United Flight 801 received an "indication from a smoke detector" warning from the cargo hold of the 7-year-old Boeing 787-9 Dreamliner just 90 minutes after departing Los Angeles International Airport at 10:40 a.m., aviation records show.
