Walnut Creek's Amit Elor will wrestle for Olympic gold: ‘I can’t believe this is real’
-----
PARIS – Amit Elor, the dominant 20-year-old wrestler from Walnut Creek, stoically bounced onto the mat upon her loud, splashy introduction Monday night at Champ-de-Mars Arena. Elor's mind clearly was fixated on her Olympic semifinal match.One minute and 44 seconds into the match, after she clinically dispatched North Korea's Pak Sol Gum 10-0, Elor crouched down and briefly held her head in her hands. She had reached the gold-medal match and assured Team USA of its first wrestling medal
