Influential developer's custom Bay Area home hits market for first time in 60 years
-----
Influential real estate developer Joseph Eichler's personal home in the exclusive town of Atherton has come on the market for the first time in 60 years, asking almost $6.4 million. The five-bedroom, five-and-a-half-bathroom home showcases an open, angular design that was groundbreaking for its time. Many of its elements — natural materials, floor-to-ceiling windows, built-in cabinetry and blending of indoor and outdoor space — later became hallmarks of Eichler's own post-and-beam California
