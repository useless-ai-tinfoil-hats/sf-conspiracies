How ‘Shortcomings’ brings Berkeley’s sartorial sensibilities to the screen
-----
When Randall Park's comedy "Shortcomings" was released on Netflix earlier this year, Bay Area viewers not only got to see local landmarks like Amoeba Records onscreen, they also may have recognized themselves in the characters' fashion sensibilities.
