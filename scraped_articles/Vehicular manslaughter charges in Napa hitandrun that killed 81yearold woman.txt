Vehicular manslaughter charges in Napa hit-and-run that killed 81-year-old woman
-----
A man has been charged with gross vehicular manslaughter while intoxicated after a hit-and-run that killed an 81-year old woman in Napa, according to the Napa County District Attorney's Office.Dominic Joseph Carvalho of Yountville struck Napa resident Jackie Rhodes last Sunday morning while under the influence of fentanyl and methamphetamine, the DA's office said Friday in a news release.
