S.F. election: One judge survives reelection challenge, second race remains close
-----
Facing a rare election challenge fueled by a tough-on-crime group, Superior Court Judge Michael Begert won a new six-year term Tuesday over attorney Albert "Chip" Zecher. Another judge, Patrick Thompson, held a narrow lead over Deputy District Attorney Jean Myungjin Roland, with many votes still to be counted.Begert had 59.5% of the vote Tuesday night to 40.5% for Zecher. Thompson led Roland 53.4% to 46.6%.
