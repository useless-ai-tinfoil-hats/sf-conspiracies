This Bay Area city declared a local state of emergency over the oncoming storm
-----
Officials in San Jose declared a local state of emergency Saturday night, warning residents to be ready to leave their homes on a moment's notice as a gathering storm threatens to cause flooding in the city.The city's proclamation, issued as rain spread across the bay, advised residents to take precautions such as moving vehicles out of flood hotspots, moving valuable items to the highest place in their homes and packing important documents and medications.
