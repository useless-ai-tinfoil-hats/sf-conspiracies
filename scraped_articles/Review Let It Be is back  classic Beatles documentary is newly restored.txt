Review: ‘Let It Be’ is back — classic Beatles documentary is newly restored
-----
The Beatles documentary "Let It Be" (1970) has been largely unavailable for years. Throughout the 1970s you could occasionally find it playing in theaters as a midnight show, and once VHS machines became available, it was available on tape. But for the most part, it's a movie you couldn't see.Now you can. As of Wednesday, May 8, the film, directed by Michael Lindsay-Hogg, is available on Disney+ in a brand new restoration.
