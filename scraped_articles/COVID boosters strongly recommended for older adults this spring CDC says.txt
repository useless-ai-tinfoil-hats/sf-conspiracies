COVID boosters strongly recommended for older adults this spring, CDC says
-----
Older adults should consider getting another COVID-19 shot this spring, even if they already received the latest vaccine in the fall, advisers to the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention said Wednesday.The advisory panel voted 11-1 to recommend an additional dose of the existing 2023-era vaccine available since September for Americans aged 65 and older. The suggestion is for them to wait at least four months after their last shot.
