A young bear was shot and killed in Tahoe. What happens next?
-----
On Memorial Day afternoon, in the woods near the edge of South Lake Tahoe, a man watching television with his dog was startled out of his easy chair when a young bear entered his home through his back door.The man yelled at the bear to scare it off but the bear advanced on him and his dog, he said. He grabbed a loaded rifle he keeps in the house and shot the animal twice, then followed the wounded creature outside and fired a killshot into its head at close range.
