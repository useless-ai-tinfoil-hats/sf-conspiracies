Bay Area city hit with more than 30 vehicle break-ins overnight
-----
Petaluma police are investigating more than 30 vehicle break-ins over the weekend.Officers began receiving reports Saturday evening of "window smash" break-ins starting throughout the city, including the Outlet Mall, St. Vincent's High School, the Capri Creek Apartments and the Sheraton Hotel, the Petaluma Police Department posted Sunday on Facebook.
