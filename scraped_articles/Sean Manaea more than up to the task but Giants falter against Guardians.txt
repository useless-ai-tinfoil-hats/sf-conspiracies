Sean Manaea more than up to the task, but Giants falter against Guardians
-----
Sean Manaea deserved better. In a perfect world, he would have gotten a break or two and received more offensive, defensive and bullpen support. But the San Francisco Giants, as their fans are well aware, are nowhere close to perfect.So when Manaea walked off the Oracle Park mound during the sixth inning and acknowledged cheers from the crowd of 23,541, little did he know Tuesday night's game was going to be decided moments later.
