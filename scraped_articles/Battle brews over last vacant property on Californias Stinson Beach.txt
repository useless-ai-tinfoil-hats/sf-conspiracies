Battle brews over last vacant property on California’s Stinson Beach
-----
The last undeveloped beachfront parcel on Stinson Beach is a wide stretch of sand surrounded by vacation homes. A lone chimney stands next to a berm of sand matted with ice plant — all that remains of the Smith family getaway.Descendants of the original owners want to build a modest summer house of their own at 21 Calle Del Onda, decades after their 1930s beach cottage burned down.
