Several people shot at Lake Merritt gathering on Juneteenth, Oakland police say
-----
Several people were shot at a gathering near Lake Merritt on Juneteenth, according to the Oakland Police Department.The shooting occurred just after 8:45 p.m. Wednesday, when gunfire rang out on the 400 block of Grand Avenue, police said. Prior to the shooting, more than two dozen officers had gathered in the area to monitor a crowd of roughly 5,000 people participating in unsanctioned events near Lake Merritt, police said.
