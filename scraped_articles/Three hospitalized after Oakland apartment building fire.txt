Three hospitalized after Oakland apartment building fire
-----
Three people were hospitalized "with varying levels of burns" Wednesday after a fire tore through the first floor of an apartment building, Oakland fire officials said.One person was listed in critical condition, according to an Oakland Fire Department official at the scene. The other two people suffered less severe injuries.
The three people were believed to have been doing renovations to the building when the fire started, fire officials said.
