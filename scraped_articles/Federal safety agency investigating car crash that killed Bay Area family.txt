Federal safety agency investigating car crash that killed Bay Area family
-----
The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration has launched an investigation into a single-car crash that killed a family of four in Pleasanton last month.In a statement to the Chronicle Wednesday, the agency said "NHTSA's Special Crash Investigations division has launched an investigation into a VinFast VF8 crash that occurred in Pleasanton, California, in April in which four people died. Specifically, SCI will document the crash circumstances and the ensuing fire."
