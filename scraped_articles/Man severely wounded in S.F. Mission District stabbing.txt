Man severely wounded in S.F. Mission District stabbing
-----
A man was severely injured in a stabbing Tuesday night in San Francisco's Mission District, the Police Department said.The man was taken to a hospital for life-threatening injuries after being stabbed near 20th and Mission streets just before 8 p.m., said San Francisco police spokesperson Eve Laokwansathitaya.
As of Wednesday morning, the victim's condition and identity were not immediately known. No arrests have been made, said Laokwansathitaya.
