Burning Man weather forecast has chance of rain before the event
-----
With an anomalously deep low-pressure system poised to sweep Northern California and Nevada Thursday into Saturday, there's a chance the Burning Man playa could see rain in the days leading up to the festival, according to the National Weather Service.The rain is predicted to be extremely light, with a total of up to 0.05 inches forecast to fall in Gerlach, the closest town to the festival, across Friday and Saturday, the agency said. Burning Man officially starts on Aug. 25.
