Eight games, 13 days, much at stake as Warriors brace for busy stretch run
-----
Now comes the home stretch for the Golden State Warriors, an eight-game, 13-day sprint to test their well-worn bodies and the power of their second-half surge.The Warriors are 21-10 since the season's numerical low point, marked by a double-overtime loss to the Los Angeles Lakers on Jan. 27. That deflating defeat dropped Golden State to 19-24, buried deep in the pack of a stacked Western Conference.
