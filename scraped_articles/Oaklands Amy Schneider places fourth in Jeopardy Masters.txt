Oakland’s Amy Schneider places fourth in ‘Jeopardy! Masters’
-----

Masters" tournament, which had six of the highest-ranking "Jeopardy!" contestants face off in a nine-episode competition, the Oakland quiz show star finished in fourth place on Monday, May 20, behind last year's "Jeopardy!
