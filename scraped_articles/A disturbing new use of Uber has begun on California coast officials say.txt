A disturbing new use of Uber has begun on California coast, officials say
-----
Dungeness crab has long been prime pickings for poachers in the Bay Area, but rarely to the extent it has been this year, and certainly not with the same tactics.Over the past few months, state wildlife officials have cited hundreds of people on the rural San Mateo County coast for taking crabs illegally, with many using ride-hailing services like Uber to get to their secluded spot after nightfall and carry the coveted shellfish home.
