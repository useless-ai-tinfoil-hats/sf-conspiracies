Jorge Soler undergoes physical at Giants camp; utilityman Otto López arrives
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — Jorge Soler moved closer to joining the Giants on Saturday when he arrived at Scottsdale Stadium to get his physical with the San Francisco Giants.Soler's three-year deal with the team has not yet been announced but could come Sunday or Monday. Physicals are usually routine events, but a year earlier, San Francisco's agreement with free-agent shortstop Carlos Correa fell apart over concerns about a past ankle injury.
