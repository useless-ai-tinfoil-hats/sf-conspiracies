Spare the Air alert extended as Bay Area air quality reaches unhealthy levels
-----
Bay Area air district officials extended a Spare the Air alert through Sunday as air quality ranged from moderate to unhealthy for some on Friday morning, owing to smoke from wood burning and winds bringing in pollution from the Central Valley.The Bay Area Air Quality Management District issued the winter season's first Spare the Air alert on Thursday in anticipation of the wind and wood smoke.
