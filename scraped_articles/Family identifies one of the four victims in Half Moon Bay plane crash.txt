Family identifies one of the four victims in Half Moon Bay plane crash
-----
A Bay Area family has identified their son as one of the four victims who died in a plane crash off the waters of Half Moon Bay on Sunday evening.Isaac Zimmern, a 27-year-old native of San Francisco, died along with his long-time partner, Emma "Pearl" Willmer-Shiles on Jan. 14, his family said in a statement shared with the Chronicle. The family spokesperson urged the public — and the media — to respect the family's privacy and did not release any other information.
