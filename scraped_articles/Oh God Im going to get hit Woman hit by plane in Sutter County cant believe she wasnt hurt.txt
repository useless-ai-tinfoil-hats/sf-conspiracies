'Oh, God, I'm going to get hit': Woman hit by plane in Sutter County can't believe she wasn't hurt
-----
This article originally appeared on KCRA.com.A Yuba City woman is lucky to be alive after a plane crashed into her pickup truck around 6 a.m. Tuesday. She not only survived, she walked away with no major injuries.
"I can't believe I got hit by an airplane and survived," said Dee Arata.
Arata, 41, said the Tuesday morning experience in Pleasant Grove was unbelievable. She is in disbelief.
"When I say I got hit by an airplane, I'm like, 'Wow, I got hit by an airplane,'"
