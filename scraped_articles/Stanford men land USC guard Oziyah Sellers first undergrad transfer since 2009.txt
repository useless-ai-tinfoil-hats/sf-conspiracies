Stanford men land USC guard Oziyah Sellers, first undergrad transfer since 2009
-----
The Stanford men's basketball program announced the signing of its first undergraduate transfer since 2009 on Saturday, welcoming USC shooting guard Oziyah Sellers.The 6-foot-5 Hayward native served as a reliable 3-point threat, mostly in a reserve role, for the Trojans, shooting 42.9% from long range on 56 attempts and averaging 5.2 points in 14.2 minutes per game.
