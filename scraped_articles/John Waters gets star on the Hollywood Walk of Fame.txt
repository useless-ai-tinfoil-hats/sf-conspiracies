John Waters gets star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame
-----
John Waters can't get any lower. In fact, you can now step all over the Pope of Trash.The part-time San Francisco resident, legendary underground filmmaker, gay icon and author has a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
"Here I am, closer to the gutter than ever," Waters quipped during the ceremony Monday, Sept. 18, which was livestreamed by the Hollywood Chamber of Commerce.
