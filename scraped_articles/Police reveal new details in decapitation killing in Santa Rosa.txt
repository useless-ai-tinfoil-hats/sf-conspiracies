Police reveal new details in decapitation killing in Santa Rosa
-----
The man who killed a woman in Santa Rosa and decapitated her was the victim's grandson, police said Monday.The Sonoma County Coroner's Office identified the victim as Elvia Lopez-Arroyo, 64, of Santa Rosa, police said. Police said her head was found on the south bank of Santa Rosa Creek, adjacent to the Prince Memorial Greenway, around 7:30 p.m. Saturday.
