Another Berkeley Rep world premiere is heading to Broadway
-----
"Swept Away," a musical with songs by the Avett Brothers that had its world premiere at Berkeley Repertory Theatre in 2022, is heading to Broadway in the autumn.The show, about a shipwrecked whaling expedition, features tracks from the North Carolina folk band's "Mignonette" and "True Sadness," along with other albums, and a book by John Logan, a Tony Award winner for "Red."
