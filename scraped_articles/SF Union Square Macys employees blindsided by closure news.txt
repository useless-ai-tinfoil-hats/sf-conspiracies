SF Union Square Macy's employees blindsided by closure news
-----
The Macy's store in San Francisco's Union Square was quiet Tuesday afternoon, with modest foot traffic, just hours after the retailer announced it will close 150 stores nationwide.Classic department store music played at medium volume throughout the aisles, as a steady but small number of customers frequented the five-floor store. According to local politicians, the iconic San Francisco location is among the Macy's stores slated to close.
