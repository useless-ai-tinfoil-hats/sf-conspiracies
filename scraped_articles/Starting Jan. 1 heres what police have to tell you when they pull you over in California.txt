Starting Jan. 1, here’s what police have to tell you when they pull you over in California
-----
Starting Monday, police in California who stop a driver will have to explain the actual reason, unless it could endanger the officer — a law intended to restrict "pretext stops."Police who stop millions of drivers and pedestrians in the state each year have been allowed, until now, to give them a misleading explanation, or none at all.
