Alameda County DA Pamela Price: Insurance scheme undervalued totaled vehicles
-----
Car insurance companies and affiliated software developers came up with a scheme that undervalued totaled vehicles, allowing the companies to underpay their customers for the vehicles, the Alameda County District Attorney's Office alleges in a lawsuit.The lawsuit says the companies, including Progressive and United Services Automobile Association, violated state laws that prohibit unfair competition and false advertising.
