Silicon Valley life science firm lays off 184 workers
-----
Laboratory device manufacturer Agilent is laying off 184 workers across California, according to a state filing.The company will cut 156 jobs at its Santa Clara headquarters, plus 17 in San Diego, four in Folsom (Sacramento County) and seven in Carpinteria (Santa Barbara County).
Layoffs are effective Aug. 9 and affect salespeople, finance workers, human resources staff, lawyers, manufacturing engineers and others.
