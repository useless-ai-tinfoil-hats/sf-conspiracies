Monterey Bay Aquarium mourns the loss of eldest sea otter
-----
The Monterey Bay Aquarium is mourning the loss of its eldest sea otter, officials announced Thursday.Rosa, a 24-year-old sea otter who had spent more than two decades at the aquarium, was euthanized Thursday after a recent health exam revealed she was suffering from several age-related conditions, according to officials. The aquarium noted she had outlived the life expectancy for female sea otters. According to the National Park Service, the maximum lifespan for sea otters is 23 years
