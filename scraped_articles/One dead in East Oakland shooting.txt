One dead in East Oakland shooting
-----
Police are investigating a shooting that left one person dead in the Sobrante Park neighborhood of East Oakland.The violent incident occurred shortly after 3:30 p.m. Tuesday on the 300 block of 105th Avenue, a tree-lined cul-de-sac bordering Interstate 880. Officers and paramedics found a victim suffering from gunshot wounds and attempted life-saving measures but were unsuccessful, representatives of the Police Department wrote in a statement.
