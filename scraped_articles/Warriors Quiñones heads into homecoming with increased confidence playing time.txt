Warriors’ Quiñones heads into homecoming with increased confidence, playing time
-----
NEW YORK — Golden State Warriors guard Lester Quiñones wouldn't leave the FedEx Forum floor Friday without reciprocating the support he so often received, signing autographs gleefully in the visiting tunnel after a 121-101 win in his old college town."I heard everybody screaming my name," the former Memphis Tigers standout said through a smile after scoring 10 points in 19 minutes against the NBA's Grizzlies.
"It was just surreal. It felt amazing to be here."
