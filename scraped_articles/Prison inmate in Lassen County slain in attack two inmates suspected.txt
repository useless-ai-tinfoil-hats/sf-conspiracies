Prison inmate in Lassen County slain in attack, two inmates suspected
-----
Officials at a Lassen County prison are investigating an inmate's death as a homicide, according to the California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation. Two inmates, Luis Alvarez, 30, and Vincent G. Martinez, 41, are suspects after staff saw them attacking the victim.At approximately 5:45 p.m. Wednesday, officers at High Desert State Prison in Susanville responded to Martinez and Alvarez attacking another inmate, Anthony R.
