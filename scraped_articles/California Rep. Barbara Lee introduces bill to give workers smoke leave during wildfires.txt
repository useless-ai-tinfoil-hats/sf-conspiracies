California Rep. Barbara Lee introduces bill to give workers smoke leave during wildfires
-----
Workers in this country generally enjoy federally required sick days when they're not feeling up to the job. New legislation proposes time off for smoke days too.A bill introduced in Congress this week by Rep. Barbara Lee, D-Oakland, would require employers to take steps to protect their employees from the bad air caused by wildfires.
