Texas-based pizza chain to open first Bay Area restaurant
-----
A Houston-based pizza chain is bringing four new outposts to California, including its first in the Bay Area, according to news outlet Chain Store Age.Russo's New York Pizzeria, an international chain serving pizza and pasta, is set to open franchises in Danville, San Joaquin, Modesto and Stockton. All four stores are slated to open this summer, according to the retail news outlet. SFGATE reached out to Russo's but did not hear back in time for publication.
