I followed a handwritten sign to a Calif. pizzeria at the edge of the world
-----
The craving for pizza practically sent us flying into the ocean.Point Arena Pizza, about 30 minutes north of Sea Ranch, is hidden on a residential street off Highway 1 that dead-ends into one of the most spectacular coves on the Mendocino coast. It overlooks unrelenting waves, massive emerald cliffs and an active fishing pier dating back to the 1800s. My family found the restaurant by following a handwritten sign for "brick oven pizza."
