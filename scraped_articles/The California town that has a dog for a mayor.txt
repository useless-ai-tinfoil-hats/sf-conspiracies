The California town that has a dog for a mayor
-----
I'd never seen anyone talk so excitedly about a mayor. I drove up to the California mountain town of Idyllwild in March 2022 for a day trip and was finishing a purchase at a local vintage store when the cashier informed me, "The mayor is in town." Before I could say anything, another shopper swung around with a big smile and asked, "Is he really?"I listened to them talk ecstatically about the mayor for a few minutes, until finally the cashier clued me in: The mayor was a dog.
