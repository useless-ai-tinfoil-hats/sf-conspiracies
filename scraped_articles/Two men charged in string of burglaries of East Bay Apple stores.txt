Two men charged in string of burglaries of East Bay Apple stores
-----
Alameda County District Attorney Pamela Price announced charges Friday against two men who allegedly stole devices from Apple stores in Emeryville and Berkeley.Undre Rialey, 28, and Tyler Mims, 23, were charged with multiple felony counts of commercial burglary and grand theft. The men, arraigned on Wednesday, were also charged with organized retail theft. They are scheduled for preliminary hearings later this month.
