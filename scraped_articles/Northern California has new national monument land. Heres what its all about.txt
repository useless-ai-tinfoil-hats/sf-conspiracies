Northern California has new national monument land. Here’s what it’s all about
-----
WALKER RIDGE, Colusa County — Up a long dirt road flanked by wildflower-drenched meadows and rolling green mountains, about 120 miles north of San Francisco, lies California's newest national monument land.President Joe Biden announced Thursday, after months of speculation, that 13,696 acres of federal land along the picture-perfect ridge known as Molok Luyuk would be added to the 330,000-acre Berryessa Snow Mountain National Monument. He cited the area's "prolific botanical richness"
