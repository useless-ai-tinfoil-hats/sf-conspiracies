Bay Area candy giant Jelly Belly bought by Chicago company
-----
Bay Area candy giant Jelly Belly has been purchased by Ferrara Candy Company, a Chicago confectionery.Jelly Belly, which is headquartered in Fairfield, announced the acquisition in a press release Wednesday. Once the transaction is completed, almost 800 Jelly Belly workers will become Ferrara employees.
