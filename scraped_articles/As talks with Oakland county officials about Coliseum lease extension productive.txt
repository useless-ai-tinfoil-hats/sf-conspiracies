A’s talks with Oakland, county officials about Coliseum lease extension ‘productive’
-----
The Oakland Athletics still do not have a lease extension to play at the Coliseum beyond this coming season.The A's met Thursday afternoon with representatives of the city of Oakland and Alameda County, for the second time, but no agreement was hammered out. The two sides plan to continue discussions, and there is another meeting scheduled for April 2.
