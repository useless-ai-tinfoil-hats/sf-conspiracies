S.F. Target store evacuated after someone released pepper spray in a bathroom
-----
San Francisco police said it appeared someone released pepper spray inside a bathroom at a San Francisco Target store on Mission Street on Thursday evening.Officers responded shortly after 7 p.m. to the store after receiving reports that employees and customers were "experiencing physical symptoms as a result of an unknown substance released in the store," police said in a statement.
