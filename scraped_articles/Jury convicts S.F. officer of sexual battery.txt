Jury convicts S.F. officer of sexual battery
-----
A San Francisco police officer was convicted of sexual battery, the San Francisco District Attorney's Office said Wednesday.Officer Michael Herrera, 31, was arrested in 2021 for groping a woman at Cow Hollow's Westwood Bar after a Police Department event. The incident was caught on the bar's security camera and corroborated by the bartender who witnessed it, prosecutors said.
