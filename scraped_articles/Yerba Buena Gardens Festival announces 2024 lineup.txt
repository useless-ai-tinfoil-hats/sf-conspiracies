Yerba Buena Gardens Festival announces 2024 lineup
-----
More than 100 programs and events are scheduled for this year's Yerba Buena Gardens Festival, spanning music and theater to dance and poetry.The free outdoor festival, marking its 24th year, is scheduled to return on May 4 with an opening concert featuring Cuban percussionist Pedrito Martinez.
"We look forward to bringing so many artists from around the world to Yerba Buena Gardens," Linda Lucero, the festival's executive and artistic director, said in a statement.
