Bay FC score first, but are blanked the rest of the way in loss to Chicago
-----
In their first eight games, Bay FC arguably have gotten better. They're more cohesive. They play with better pace.They're also finding different ways to lose, as in Sunday's 2-1 home decision to the Chicago Red Stars. Unlike the past three losses, Bay FC (2-6-0) held a 1-0 lead but made defensive mistakes and couldn't generate another goal.
