Here are the next BART stations set to receive fare evasion-proof gates
-----
BART announced Thursday which stations will receive the next round of the agency's new fare gates, which are meant to "boost safety" and increase revenue by reducing fare evasion.At a meeting of the agency's Board of Directors, BART staff said the new gates, the first of which were installed at the West Oakland Station last month, will soon expand to eight additional stations: Civic Center, Montgomery, Powell Street, 24th Street, San Francisco International Airport, Fruitvale, Richmond
