Here’s how the size of San Francisco’s homes on the market compares with other California counties
-----
Home buyers searching for extra room may have a hard time finding it in San Francisco.That's because the homes for sale in San Francisco tend to be significantly smaller than those in California overall, according to data from real estate listing site Realtor.com. In fact, the median size of for-sale homes in San Francisco, about 1,400 square feet, was the smallest of any county in California that had at least 100 active or pending listings in October.
