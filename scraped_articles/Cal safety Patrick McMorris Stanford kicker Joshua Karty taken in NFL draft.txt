Cal safety Patrick McMorris, Stanford kicker Joshua Karty taken in NFL draft
-----
Safety Patrick McMorris on Saturday extended a recent run of Cal defensive backs taken in the NFL draft when he was selected by the Miami Dolphins in the sixth round.Later in that round, Joshua Karty ended a lengthy drought for Stanford kickers being selected when the Los Angeles Rams picked him.
McMorris and Karty were the only two players from Bay Area colleges chosen.
