Guy Fieri-backed drive-thru restaurant is coming to NorCal
-----
A new Guy Fieri-brand restaurant is coming to Northern California this summer as part of a multirestaurant franchise agreement that intends to bring crunchy chicken tenders and a smorgasbord of sauces to the Bay Area and surrounding counties.The Chandi Hospitality Group plans to oversee the development of 10 new Chicken Guy restaurants in the coming years, according to a news release, with the first opening this summer at 200 American Canyon Road in American Canyon, part of Napa Valley.
