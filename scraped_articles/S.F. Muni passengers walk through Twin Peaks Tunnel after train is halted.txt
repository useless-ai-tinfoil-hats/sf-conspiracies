S.F. Muni passengers walk through Twin Peaks Tunnel after train is halted
-----
Passengers of a Muni train walked to their next stop at Forest Hill after the train unexpectedly stopped Friday afternoon in the Twin Peaks Tunnel.One passenger said on the social media platform X, formally known as Twitter, that about 50 passengers walked about a mile to Forest Hill Station after the train stopped on its way from Castro Station.
