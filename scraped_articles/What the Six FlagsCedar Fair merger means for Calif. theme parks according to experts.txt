What the Six Flags-Cedar Fair merger means for Calif. theme parks, according to experts
-----
In a somewhat shocking move for the theme park industry, the two juggernauts of regional theme parks have announced plans to merge. Six Flags and Cedar Fair will soon operate as one, under the name Six Flags, as a $3.5 billion conglomerate. The unprecedented portfolio will include 42 theme parks, 15 water parks and nine resorts across the United States, Canada and Mexico.While the deal has minimal market overlap in other parts of the country, that's not true in California.
