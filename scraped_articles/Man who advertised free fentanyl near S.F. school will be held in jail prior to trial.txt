Man who advertised ‘free fentanyl’ near S.F. school will be held in jail prior to trial
-----
An unhoused man who posted signs advertising free fentanyl near a Richmond District school is being held in jail as he awaits trial for allegedly fighting someone and creating a public nuisance.San Francisco jail records show Joseph Adam Moore, 46, was booked into county jail on Thursday. Moore was arrested on Oct. 20 and later charged with battery and creating a public nuisance.
