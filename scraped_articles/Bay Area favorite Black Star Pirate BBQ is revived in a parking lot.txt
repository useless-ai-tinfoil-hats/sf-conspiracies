Bay Area favorite Black Star Pirate BBQ is revived in a parking lot
-----
When the cult favorite Black Star Pirate BBQ closed last November, the Bay Area lost a touch of eclectic flavor. But now, the wildly popular spot, once tucked away behind the Richmond refineries, has fired up the smoker again.In an Instagram post shared Monday, chef Tony Carracci announced that the eatery — newly christened as Black Star BBQ — would return as a pop-up seven days a week in the parking lot of Baltic Kiss, his other Southern-comfort restaurant and music venue at 135 Park
