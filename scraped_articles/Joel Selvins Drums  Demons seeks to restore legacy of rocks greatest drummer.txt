Joel Selvin’s ‘Drums & Demons’ seeks to restore legacy of rock’s greatest drummer
-----
The legacy of rock drummer Jim Gordon hinges on two deaths: The first in 1983, when he murdered his mother with a hammer. The second in 2023, when he died from natural causes at age 77.At the time of the crime, Gordon's actions were blamed on excessive drug and alcohol consumption. It wasn't until after being sent to jail that he was diagnosed with schizophrenia.
