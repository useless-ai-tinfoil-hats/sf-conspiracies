Newsom unveils proposal to expedite abortion access for Arizonans
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom on Wednesday announced legislation to give Arizona doctors temporary, emergency licenses to perform abortions in California on the heels of the Arizona Supreme Court decision outlawing the procedure.The legislation, SB233 (co-written by Democratic state Sens. Nancy Skinner of Berkeley and Cecilia Aguiar-Curry of Winters) would allow licensed Arizona doctors in good standing to provide abortions and abortion-related care to Arizona patients traveling to California
