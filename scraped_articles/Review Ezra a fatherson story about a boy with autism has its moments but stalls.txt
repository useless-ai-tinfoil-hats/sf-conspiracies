Review: ‘Ezra,’ a father-son story about a boy with autism, has its moments, but stalls
-----
"Ezra" is an opportunity for Bobby Cannavale to show his abilities as a dramatic actor, but his performance is hampered by one thing: He plays an idiot.The film is intended as a tender story of a father's love for his autistic son, Ezra. But as written, this father can't seem to make a move without undermining himself and making matters worse. This creates within the audience a certain frustration and impatience with the character.
