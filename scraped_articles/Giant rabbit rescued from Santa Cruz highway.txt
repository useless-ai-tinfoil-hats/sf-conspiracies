Giant rabbit rescued from Santa Cruz highway
-----
A 26-pound rabbit was rescued after it was found hopping along Highway 17 in Santa Cruz over the weekend, according to the California Highway Patrol and the Santa Cruz County animal shelter.Construction crews saw the large animal near the Laurel Curve shortly before 2 a.m. Sunday, according to highway patrol spokesperson Israel Murillo. They caught the critter and contacted the CHP for assistance, keeping it safe in one of their vehicles for several hours until officers arrived, he said.
