Oakland police break up sideshow involving more than 50 vehicles
-----
Oakland police broke up an illegal sideshow involving more than 50 vehicles early Sunday, but reported no arrests.Officers responded to the sideshow at 98th Avenue and International Boulevard just after 3:30 a.m., and the vehicles dispersed, police said Sunday.
