Woman, 47, and boy, 14, killed in wrong-way collision on Bay Area highway
-----
A 47-year-old woman and a 14-year-old boy were killed when a man drove in the wrong direction on a Bay Area freeway in the middle of the day on Monday, officials said.The California Highway Patrol said in a news release that they received a report at 3:40 p.m. of a wrong-way driver traveling south in the northbound lanes of state Route 85 near Saratoga Avenue in the South Bay. The driver was in a white Toyota Tacoma and speeding, CHP said.
