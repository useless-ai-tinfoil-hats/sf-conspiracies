Corral Fire near Tracy grows amid evacuations, but part of I-580 reopens
-----
The fast-moving Corral Fire in San Joaquin County, which forced evacuations Saturday around Tracy, grew to 12,500 acres overnight and was 15% contained Sunday morning as a portion of Interstate 580 reopened.Updates: California Fire Map & Tracker
