Napa Valley supervisors to consider uprooting iconic Napa Valley welcome sign
-----
The Napa Valley welcome sign is one of the Bay Area's most iconic landmarks and a favorite photo spot for wine country visitors. It may also be a safety concern.The road sign, located just outside Robert Mondavi Winery in Oakville, beckons to travelers with the message: "Welcome to this world famous wine growing region: NAPA VALLEY."
The sign draws a near-constant stream of tourists, as the San Francisco Chronicle reported, with hundreds of thousands taking photos in front of it
