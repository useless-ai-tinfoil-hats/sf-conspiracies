Giants put LaMonte Wade Jr. on 10-day IL amid a handful of roster moves
-----
The San Francisco Giants added another big name to their long list of injuries.First baseman LaMonte Wade Jr. will be out at least a month with a Grade 2 left hamstring strain, the team announced before Tuesday's game against the Philadelphia Phillies. Wade was placed on the 10-day injured list as part of a series of roster moves that added a few more new faces to the Giants' clubhouse.
