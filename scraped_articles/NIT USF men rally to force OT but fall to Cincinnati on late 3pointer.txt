NIT: USF men rally to force OT, but fall to Cincinnati on late 3-pointer
-----
Sima Lukosius' 3-pointer with 3.1 seconds to play lifted host Cincinnati past USF 73-72 in overtime in the first round of the men's NIT on Wednesday.Lukosius finished with 28 points and was 8-for-10 from distance for the Bearcats (21-14).
USF (23-11) trailed 65-56 with 2:21 to go in regulation, but went on an 11-2 run to force the extra period. Malik Thomas scored nine of the Dons's 11 points during the spree, hitting a 3-pointer with nine seconds left that tied the score at 67-67.
