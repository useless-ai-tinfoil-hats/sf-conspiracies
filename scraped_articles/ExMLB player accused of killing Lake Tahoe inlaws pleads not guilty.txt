Ex-MLB player accused of killing Lake Tahoe in-laws pleads not guilty
-----
The former Major League Baseball pitcher who was arrested on suspicion of killing his father-in-law in a grisly Lake Tahoe shooting has pleaded not guilty to murder charges, Placer County Sheriff's Office officials said Tuesday.Daniel Serafini, 49, of Reno is accused of killing 70-year-old Robert Gary Spohr. He also pleaded not guilty to attempted murder charges in the shooting of 68-year-old Wendy Wood, Spohr's wife.
