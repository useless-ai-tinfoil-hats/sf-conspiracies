Family of Erik Salgado, shot and killed by CHP officers, to receive $7 million settlement
-----
Families of two unarmed people shot by California Highway Patrol officers in East Oakland in 2020 will receive a $7 million settlement, lawyers for the families said Wednesday.Erik Salgado and his girlfriend, Brianna Colombo, were parked in a Dodge Challenger Hellcat on June 6, 2020, on the 9600 block of Cherry Street when an unmarked police car investigating a report of a stolen vehicle approached them from the front as police cars boxed them in from the back.
