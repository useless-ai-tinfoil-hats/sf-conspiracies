'Full House' home in San Francisco for sale again for $6.5 million
-----
The iconic San Francisco home used as the facade of the Tanners' house in the 1990s sitcom "Full House" has hit the market for $6.5 million.Built in 1900, the 3,700-square-foot property was extensively remodeled in 2019 with a modern design and an open floor plan from architect Richard Landry. It features four bedrooms, four bathrooms, multiple living spaces, a gym and a two-car garage. Rooms sport soaring 11-foot ceilings, original molding and two fireplaces.
