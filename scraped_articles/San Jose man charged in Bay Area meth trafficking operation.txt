San Jose man charged in Bay Area meth trafficking operation
-----
A San Jose man was charged Thursday in connection with a drug trafficking operation that officials believe distributed kilogram quantities of methamphetamine around the Bay Area.Austin Santana-Farfan was charged with possession with intent to distribute methamphetamine by the U.S. Attorney's Office after a multiyear investigation and a search of his San Jose residence, according to court documents.
