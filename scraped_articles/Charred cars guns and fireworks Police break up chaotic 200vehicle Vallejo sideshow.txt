Charred cars, guns and fireworks: Police break up chaotic, 200-vehicle Vallejo sideshow
-----
Several cars caught fire at a huge Vallejo sideshow Sunday where people were shooting guns and lighting off fireworks, police said.Three vehicles were engulfed in flames among the 200 cars participating in the illegal sideshow when officers arrived at the intersection of Sonoma Boulevard and Lemon Street around 2:43 a.m., the Vallejo Police Department said in a statement.
Eyewitnesses told officers that participants were "shooting firearms and setting off fireworks," police said.
