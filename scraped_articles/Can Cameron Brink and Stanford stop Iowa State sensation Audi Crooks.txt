Can Cameron Brink and Stanford stop Iowa State sensation Audi Crooks?
-----
For the second time this season, a freshman put up a record-breaking performance at Maples Pavillion. Nearly two months after USC's Juju Watkins torched Stanford for 51 points in a conference win, Iowa State's Audi Crooks had her national coming-out party Friday in a 40-point performance in an NCAA Tournament first-round victory against Maryland.Crooks will be Stanford's toughest test on Sunday in a second-round matchup at Maples.
