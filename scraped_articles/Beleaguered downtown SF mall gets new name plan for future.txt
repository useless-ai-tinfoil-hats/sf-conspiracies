Beleaguered downtown SF mall gets new name, plan for future
-----
The owners of downtown San Francisco's troubled mall just bestowed on it a new name and plan for the future.The former Westfield San Francisco Centre will now be dubbed Emporium Centre San Francisco, in a nod to the original name of the department store that opened there in 1896, according to a news release from owners Trident Pacific and JLL.
