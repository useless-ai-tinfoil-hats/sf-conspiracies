Disneyland to officially rename 1988 land
-----
Critter Country is officially being renamed, Disneyland announced, marking yet another name change for the animal-themed land. To coincide with the opening of Tiana's Bayou Adventure on Nov. 15, Critter Country will become Bayou Country.This part of the park is no stranger to change. In the 1970s, it was named Bear Country in honor of its new attraction, the Country Bear Jamboree.
