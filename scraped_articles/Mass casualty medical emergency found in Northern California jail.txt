'Mass casualty' medical emergency found in Northern California jail
-----
A mass casualty incident was declared at the Mendocino County Jail in Ukiah on Friday after five inmates were found overdosing on an unknown substance. One of the men has since died.Authorities were alerted to the situation by an inmate who called out on the intercom system around noon Friday.
