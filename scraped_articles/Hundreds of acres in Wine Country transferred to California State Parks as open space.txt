Hundreds of acres in Wine Country transferred to California State Parks as open space
-----
Around 650 acres of open space on the much-debated Sonoma Developmental Center property in Wine Country has been transferred to California State Parks, the agency said this week, cementing its status as protected land.Part of the 945-acre property near Glen Ellen, which is owned by the state of California, once housed the Sonoma Developmental Center, one of several state-run institutions for severely disabled people.
