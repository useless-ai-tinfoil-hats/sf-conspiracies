San Francisco tech CEO retires amid controversy but is still set to rake in obscene pay
-----
John Riccitiello, CEO of San Francisco-based gaming giant Unity Technologies, is suddenly leaving the company, but he shouldn't be in financial trouble any time soon.Unity announced the executive's retirement Monday, just weeks after the company unveiled and then walked back an extremely unpopular pricing change.
