Instagram reportedly lays off 60, but they can reapply for a different role
-----
Instagram has reportedly cut around 60 jobs, but with a caveat: The laid-off workers can reapply for other roles.Technical program manager positions are being eliminated across the giant social network, according to Business Insider. The job includes elements of both technical roles like engineers and product managers. Affected workers can interview to become product managers, or else they will be laid off in March, according to Business Insider.
