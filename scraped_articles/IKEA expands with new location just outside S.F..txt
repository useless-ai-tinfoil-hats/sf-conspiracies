IKEA expands with new location just outside S.F.
-----
IKEA is opening a small pickup center that includes interior design planning services in Colma, continuing its Bay Area expansion.The Swedish furniture company said Thursday that the center would open this fall at 35 Colma Blvd. At 2,340 square feet, the facility will be far smaller than the typical IKEA warehouse as well as its smaller urban store in San Francisco, which has nearly 4,000 products.
