Warriors’ Kevon Looney extends regular-season streak to 200: ‘Keep it going’
-----
CLEVELAND — Fifteen centers were ranked ahead of Kevon Looney in ESPN's preseason player rankings. Few will have a chance to match the milestone he achieved Sunday against the Cleveland Cavaliers.Looney played in his 200th consecutive regular-season game for the Golden State Warriors. His streak of 235 appearances, regular season and playoffs combined, trails only Brooklyn's Mikal Bridges (437) for the longest in the NBA.
