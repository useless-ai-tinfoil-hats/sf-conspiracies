Man found dead at S.F. 16th Street BART Station plaza
-----
A man slumped over and died in BART's16th Street Mission Station plaza in San Francisco early Thursday, authorities said.Surveillance video showed the man collapsed about 15 minutes after he sat down in the plaza, BART spokesperson Chris Filippi said. In the video reviewed by police, he appeared "slumped over," Filippi said.
BART police officers called at 1:08 a.m. pronounced him dead and alerted the medical examiner's office, which removed the man's body from the plaza, Filippi
