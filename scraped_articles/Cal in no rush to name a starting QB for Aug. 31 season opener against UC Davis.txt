Cal in no rush to name a starting QB for Aug. 31 season opener against UC Davis
-----
Cal fans wanting to know who will start at quarterback for the Bears in their opener against UC Davis at Memorial Stadium on Aug. 31 must hope this phrase rings true: No news is good news.During a Zoom news conference Thursday, head coach Justin Wilcox declined to name a starter among the three candidates, incumbent Fernando Mendoza, Chandler Rogers (a transfer from North Texas) and CJ Harris (a transfer from Ohio).
