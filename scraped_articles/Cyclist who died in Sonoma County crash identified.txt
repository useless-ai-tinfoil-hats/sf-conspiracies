Cyclist who died in Sonoma County crash identified
-----
LATEST Jan. 17, 12:45 p.m. The Sonoma County Sheriff's Office on Wednesday identified the cyclist who died in a crash on a roadway in Cazadero as Zoe Cheng, a 42-year-old woman from San Jose. The crash occurred Monday; Cheng hit a tree that was on the shoulder of Hauser Bridge Road, the sheriff's office said.Jan. 16, 7:19 a.m. A cyclist died in a crash on a Sonoma County roadway on Monday, according to the California Highway Patrol.
