SFMOMA announces largest acquisition of art by developmentally disabled artists
-----
The San Francisco Museum of Modern Art announced the acquisition of more than 150 works by 23 artists with developmental disabilities from three Bay Area art centers. It is the largest acquisition focusing on the disability community in the museum's history.Oakland's Creative Growth represents 113 works in the acquisition while San Francisco's Creativity Explored makes up 31 and Richmond's NIAD Art Center includes 12.
