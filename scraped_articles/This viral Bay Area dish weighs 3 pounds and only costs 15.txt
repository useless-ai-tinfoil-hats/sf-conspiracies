This viral Bay Area dish weighs 3 pounds and only costs $15

As far as Pupuseria las Cabanas is concerned, there are two types of pupusas in the world: classic pupusas and pupusa locas.On paper, both styles seem similar with an identical set of ingredients, but at Pupuseria las Cabanas, pupusas locas, or "crazy pupusas," are the equivalent of about five pupusas and weigh a whopping 3 pounds.
"Around the Bay Area, not a lot of people sell it," said owner Frankie Martinez. "It gets a lot of curiosity."
