S.F. pedestrian fatally struck by Muni bus, authorities say
-----
A pedestrian was struck and killed by a Muni bus early Thursday in San Francisco, authorities said.The crash happened about 12:51 a.m. near O'Farrell Street and Van Ness Avenue, the San Francisco Police Department said in a statement.
When officers arrived, they found a pedestrian dead on the street, police said. The victim was identified as Thomas Duba, a 61-year-old man from San Francisco, according to the medical examiner's office.
