Residents told to stay inside as smoke from California's Park Fire races over Nevada
-----
Nevada officials issued an air quality emergency Tuesday afternoon as smoke from Northern California's Park Fire drifted over the Reno-Sparks area.The Stage 1 emergency is due to "unhealthy" levels of air quality that are forecast for Wednesday in the aftermath of the wildfire. The Park Fire started nearly a week ago and has since spread to over 386,000 acres with 14% containment, per Cal Fire. Conditions are likely to persist through Thursday morning.
