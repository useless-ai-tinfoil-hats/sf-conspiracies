5 people found dead in California desert, sheriff says
-----
Five people's bodies were found off a highway in a remote area of the California desert. As of Wednesday morning, law enforcement officials are sharing few details.The San Bernardino County Sheriff's Department said the bodies of the five individuals were found off Highway 395 in El Mirage after deputies were asked to respond to the area for a welfare check. El Mirage is a small community in the Mojave Desert about a 90-minute drive northeast of Los Angeles.
