‘Totally catastrophic’: Vital Calif. cannabis program is nearly broke
-----
October is always busy for cannabis farmers as they hurry to harvest their crops before the fall rains. But this year, Karla Avila, a pot farmer in Northern California's Trinity County, isn't only racing against the weather. She's also rushing to harvest her plants fast enough to pay a December licensing bill to the state's Department of Cannabis Control.If she fails to pay the bill, her Flowerdaze farm could go out of business.
