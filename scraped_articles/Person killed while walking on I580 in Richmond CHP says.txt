Person killed while walking on I-580 in Richmond, CHP says
-----
A pedestrian was fatally struck by a driver Thursday morning on Interstate 580 in Richmond, according to the California Highway Patrol.The victim was struck by a motorist driving a Chevrolet Silverado just before 1:30 a.m. on the Harbour Way exit, the CHP said. Responding firefighters announced the victim dead before CHP officers arrived. The victim's identity was not immediately released.
The driver of the vehicle remained on scene and cooperated with investigators, authorities
