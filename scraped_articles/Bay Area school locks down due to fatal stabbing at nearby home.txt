Bay Area school locks down due to fatal stabbing at nearby home
-----
A man was killed and a woman injured in a stabbing at a home in Pacifica on Thursday morning, officials said.The Pacifica Police Department said it learned about the stabbing when responding to a report of domestic violence at 7:45 a.m. on the 200 block of Naomi Avenue in the coastal town that's about 5 miles south of San Francisco.
At the scene, officers found the man and woman with stab wounds. The woman was taken to a local hospital and the man was pronounced dead.
