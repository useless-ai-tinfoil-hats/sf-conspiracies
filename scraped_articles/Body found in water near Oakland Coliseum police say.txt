Body found in water near Oakland Coliseum, police say
-----
A body was found floating in water near the Oakland Coliseum over the weekend, police officials said.Oakland police received a call Sunday just before 4:30 p.m. about a body floating in a waterway in the 5400 block of Coliseum Way, according to an email from Police Department representatives. The waterway is a flood channel that runs into the East Creek Slough, police said, citing Alameda County officials.
