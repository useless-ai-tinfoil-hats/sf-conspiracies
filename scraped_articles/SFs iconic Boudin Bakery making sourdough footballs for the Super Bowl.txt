SF's iconic Boudin Bakery making sourdough footballs for the Super Bowl
-----
San Francisco's legendary Boudin Bakery is getting in on the Super Bowl fun by selling football-shaped sourdough loaves. While the edible footballs are not explicitly 49ers-themed, the local bakery has made its allegiances clear: "GO NINERS!" reads the product's description in Boudin's online store.Each 1-pound sourdough football costs $9.99 in-store, and they are available at a number of Bay Area locations. The San Francisco Boudin bakeries at Pier 39 and 160 Jefferson Street
