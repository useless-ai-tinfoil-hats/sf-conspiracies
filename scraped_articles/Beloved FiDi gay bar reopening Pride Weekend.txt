Beloved FiDi gay bar reopening Pride Weekend
-----
After four years in hibernation, a much loved gay bar in the Financial District is returning in time for Pride Weekend.Ginger's will reopen in its home since 2017 at 86 Hardie Place on Friday, June 28. An opening celebration will include a blessing from the Sisters of Perpetual Indulgence drag group and a party hosted by Bay Area drag performers Miss Shugana and Madd-Dogg with special guests Kylie Minono, MGM Grande, Laundra Tyme, and Helixir Jynder Byntwell.
