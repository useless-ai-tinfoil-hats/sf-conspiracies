Top brewery opens massive Oakland pizza restaurant, plus more East Bay openings
-----
New spots to drink were on tap for the East Bay in August.Acclaimed brewery Cellarmaker opened its big new Oakland taproom and restaurant, while the historic Buffalo Bill's Brewery in Hayward came back to life with a surprising new owner who is preserving its classic lineup of beers. In Berkeley, Japanese winery Noria Wines opened its first tasting room in Berkeley, and a new izakaya is making shochu cocktails.
