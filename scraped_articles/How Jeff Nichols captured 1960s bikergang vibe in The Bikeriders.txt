How Jeff Nichols captured 1960s biker-gang vibe in ‘The Bikeriders’
-----
Jeff Nichols admits that even though the idea for his latest film, "The Bikeriders," has been gestating in his mind since 2003, he still has trouble describing his movie succinctly to people who haven't seen it."You don't want to sound goofy or befuddled about your own film, but the truth is, I just wanted to make a film that has a feeling," writer-director Nichols told the Chronicle on a recent video call from Los Angeles.
