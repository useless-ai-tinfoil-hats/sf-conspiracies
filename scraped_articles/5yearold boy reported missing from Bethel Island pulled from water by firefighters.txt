5-year-old boy reported missing from Bethel Island pulled from water by firefighters
-----
A 5-year-old boy who had gone missing on Bethel Island was found in the water and pulled out by rescuers, according to the Contra Costa County Fire department.Update: 5-year-old boy, pulled from water in East Bay, later dies at hospital
Firefighters responded to a call reporting the boy missing around 4:45 p.m. Saturday near the 400 block of Halcyon Place, a street in the Delta Coves housing development that stretches out into the Sacramento-San Joaquin River Delta.
