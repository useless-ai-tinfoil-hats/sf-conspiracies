Elite S.F. private schools increase security after man arrested for photographing kids in Pacific Heights
-----
A man has been arrested for taking photographs of students exiting several private schools in San Francisco's Pacific Heights, school officials said.The man, who was not immediately identified, was allegedly seen taking pictures of students during dismissal times at Convent & Stuart Hall, the Hamlin School and St. Vincent de Paul School, according to a message Convent & Stuart Hall President Ann Marie Krejcarek sent to parents and employees last week that was reviewed by the Chronicle.
