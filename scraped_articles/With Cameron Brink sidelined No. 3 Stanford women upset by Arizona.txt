With Cameron Brink sidelined, No. 3 Stanford women upset by Arizona
-----
The Stanford women's basketball team has struggled most of the season when Cameron Brink hasn't been on the court. Friday night's loss to Arizona is the latest example.The senior missed her second game of the season with a "non-COVID-related illness," and the Cardinal offense struggled in a 68-61 defeat to Arizona (14-12, 7-9 Pac-12), the Wildcats' first win at Maples Pavilion since 2001.
