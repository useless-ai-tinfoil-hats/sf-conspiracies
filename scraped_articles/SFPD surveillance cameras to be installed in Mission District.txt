SFPD surveillance cameras to be installed in Mission District
-----
The San Francisco Police Department plans to install surveillance cameras at two busy Mission District intersections as part of an effort to deter street crime.At a Wednesday community meeting about the public safety cameras, police Chief Bill Scott said the two locations, 19th and Mission streets and 24th and Mission streets, have experienced high crime rates.
