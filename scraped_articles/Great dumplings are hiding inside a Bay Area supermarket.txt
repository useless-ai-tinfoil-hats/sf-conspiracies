Great dumplings are hiding inside a Bay Area supermarket
-----
Like a trip to Costco, shopping at 99 Ranch can have the same thrilling and exhausting intensity. After hoarding the seafood counter and filling my cart with endless snacks like Calbee Shrimp Chips and chocolaty Hello Panda, a refueling stop at the hot deli feels like coming home.The warm smells of comforting dishes — yes, including dumplings — straight from the hot deli lend a home-cooked, grandma vibe, a salve to the almost clinical, sterile environment of a neon-lit supermarket.
