How S.F.’s Embarcadero could be transformed by this $13.5 billion proposal
-----
If federal planners have their way, and San Francisco can raise its share of the money, the city's bay shoreline is on the verge of becoming a radically different place.Much of the Embarcadero would be seven feet higher than it is now. So would the Ferry Building — but only after massive construction likely to force the iconic landmark to be closed for months or years.
