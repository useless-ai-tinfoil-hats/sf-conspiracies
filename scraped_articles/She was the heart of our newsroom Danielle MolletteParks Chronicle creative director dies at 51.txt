‘She was the heart of our newsroom’: Danielle Mollette-Parks, Chronicle creative director, dies at 51
-----
Danielle Mollette-Parks, who brought a spirit of invention, a keen visual sense and an empathetic warmth to her job as creative director at the San Francisco Chronicle, has died at age 51. The cause was metastatic breast cancer, said her husband, Tim Mollette-Parks, who was at her side when she died early Monday, Dec. 18, in their Oakland home."She was the heart of our newsroom," said Emilio Garcia-Ruiz, Chronicle editor in chief.
