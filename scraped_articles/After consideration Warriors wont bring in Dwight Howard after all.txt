After consideration, Warriors won't bring in Dwight Howard after all
-----
The Golden State Warriors have decided not to invite free-agent center Dwight Howard to training camp, a league source confirmed with the Chronicle on Friday morning.This news comes two days after the team finished a two-day interview process with Howard, which included meetings with Warriors brass and workouts with players.
