Missing California woman found dead off coast of beach town
-----
The body of a California woman was found Monday by U.S. Coast Guard officers in the waters off Morro Bay, a beach town in San Luis Obispo County, officials said.The U.S. Coast Guard was "conducting a routine patrol" when it spotted a woman's body at 7:15 p.m. Monday, the Tulare Police Department said in a news release.
