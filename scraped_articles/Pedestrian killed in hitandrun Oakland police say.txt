Pedestrian killed in hit-and-run, Oakland police say
-----
A pedestrian was struck and killed by a driver who fled the scene in East Oakland on Monday morning, authorities said.An unidentified male was found lying on the ground at the intersection of 98th Avenue and San Leandro Street at about 5:45 a.m., according to the Oakland Police Department. The victim suffered injuries "consistent with being struck by a vehicle," they said.
The victim was pronounced dead at the scene. Further information about his identity was pending, officials
