Review: Novel lays bare society's injustice amid Berkeley firestorm
-----
Sarah Ruiz-Grossman's new novel features a diverse cast of Northern Californians wrestling with a host of pressing societal problems, from climate change and income inequality to racial discrimination and sexual assault. The first-time author tosses lots of narrative balls into the air, and she doesn't keep them all aloft.
