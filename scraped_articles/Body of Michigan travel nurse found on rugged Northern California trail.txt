Body of Michigan travel nurse found on rugged Northern California trail
-----
A travel nurse from Michigan missing for weeks in a wooded area of Calaveras County was found dead Thursday, officials said.Ann Marie Herford's body was found Thursday morning by a search team on a steep hillside beneath a heavy tree canopy and dense foliage, officials from the Calaveras County Sheriff's Office said.
The area where her body was found was north of where her vehicle was parked on Nov. 12 at a trailhead of the Arnold Rim Trail System. She was reported missing Nov. 14.
