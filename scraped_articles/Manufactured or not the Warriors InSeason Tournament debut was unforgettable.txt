Manufactured or not, the Warriors' In-Season Tournament debut was unforgettable
-----
If the NBA is going to force an obvious cash grab down our throats, it better be entertaining as hell to watch.Such was the case in the Golden State Warriors' thrilling 141-139 win over the Oklahoma City Thunder on Friday. The game was Golden State's first with ramifications for the "In-Season Tournament," the NBA's new gimmick that nobody asked for but that is sure to net the league millions and millions.
