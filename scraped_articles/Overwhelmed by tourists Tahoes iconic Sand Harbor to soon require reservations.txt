Overwhelmed by tourists, Tahoe's iconic Sand Harbor to soon require reservations
-----
With its white granite boulders, azure water and long half-moon-shaped strip of sand, Sand Harbor is one of Tahoe's most famous and popular beaches.But like a lot of popular destinations in the Lake Tahoe Basin, Sand Harbor is buckling under the strain of so much tourism. Over the past decade, the number of visitors to Sand Harbor State Park has skyrocketed, with a million more visitors in 2023 compared with 2012.
