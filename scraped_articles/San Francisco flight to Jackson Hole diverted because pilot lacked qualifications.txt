San Francisco flight to Jackson Hole diverted because pilot lacked qualifications
-----
An Alaska Airlines flight from San Francisco International Airport to Jackson Hole, Wyo., was unexpectedly diverted to Salt Lake City last week after the pilot informed passengers of his lack of qualifications to land at the intended destination.Flight 3491, operated by SkyWest on a twin-engine Embraer E175 aircraft, touched down at Salt Lake City International Airport on Aug. 8 after abandoning its approach to Jackson Hole Airport in Grand Teton National Park.
