Giants reportedly interview Alyssa Nakken for manager position
-----
As the San Francisco Giants continue to interview potential managers, one candidate is making history just by being involved in the process at all.The Giants interviewed Alyssa Nakken last week, according to a report The Athletic's Andrew Baggarly that was later confirmed by the San Francisco Chronicle. (The Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms.) Nakken is believed to be the first woman to officially interview for an MLB manager job.
