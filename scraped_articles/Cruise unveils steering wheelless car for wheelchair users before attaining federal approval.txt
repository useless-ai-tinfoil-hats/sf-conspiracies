Cruise unveils steering wheel-less car for wheelchair users before attaining federal approval
-----
Cruise, the San Francisco-based autonomous vehicle company, has unveiled a blockish new addition to the fleet of driverless cars that is already provoking mixed reactions from city residents.Cruise CEO Kyle Vogt introduced the new vehicle, which Cruise is calling the "WAV," in a video posted to social media Thursday morning. The car has neither a steering wheel nor an obvious front and back, with the company's stated goal being to maximize accessibility within the boxy interior. Vogt
