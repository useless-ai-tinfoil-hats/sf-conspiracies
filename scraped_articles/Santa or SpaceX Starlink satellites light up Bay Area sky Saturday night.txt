Santa or SpaceX? Starlink satellites light up Bay Area sky Saturday night
-----
A string of lights crossed the Bay Area sky Saturday night, but it wasn't falling stars or an early trip from Santa — instead, it was another sighting of satellites launched by Elon Musk's SpaceX.Just after 6:40 p.m., a chain of Musk's Starlink satellites passed through the sky above the Bay Area, according to Find Starlink and Heavens Above — two websites that track the satellites.
