Peggy Moore and Hope Wood, couple rooted in East Bay politics, killed in car crash
-----
A longtime East Bay political leader who worked on campaigns for former President Barack Obama and built solid ties in Oakland City Hall died in a car crash in Southern California, according to friends.Tributes to Peggy Moore, 60, rippled through social media after reports that she and her wife, Hope Wood, 48, had died Friday night in a head-on collision on Highway 76 in unincorporated San Diego County.
