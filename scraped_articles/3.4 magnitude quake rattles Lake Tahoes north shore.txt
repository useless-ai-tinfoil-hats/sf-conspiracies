3.4 magnitude quake rattles Lake Tahoe's north shore
-----
A 3.4 magnitude earthquake rattled the north shore of Lake Tahoe Sunday night, according to data from the U.S. Geological Survey.The quake was measured just over 2 miles west of Tahoe Vista in Placer County.
There were no immediate reports of damage as a result of the quake, which was detected at a depth of a little more than a mile underground.
