Palo Alto police arrest woman on suspicion of auto burglary
-----
Palo Alto police say they have arrested a 42-year-old woman on suspicion of auto burglary.Officers received a call shortly before 9 a.m. Thursday of an auto burglary in progress after a witness reported a woman sitting in a white 2020 Ford Escape that wasn't hers on the 900 block of High Street, the Palo Alto Police Department said in a statement Saturday. The suspect got out of the car and walked away, and officers contacted her without incident, police said.
