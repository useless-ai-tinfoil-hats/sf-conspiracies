Zero Bay Area restaurants win James Beard awards for second year in a row
-----
On Monday, the James Beard Foundation crowned its 2024 award winners at a ceremony in Chicago. Despite having four Bay Area finalists in the running, not a single local chef or restaurant won any of the prestigious awards.This is the second year in a row that the Bay Area has been snubbed by what many consider the Oscars of the food world.
