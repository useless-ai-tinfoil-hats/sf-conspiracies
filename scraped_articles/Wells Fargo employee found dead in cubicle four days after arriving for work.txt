Wells Fargo employee found dead in cubicle four days after arriving for work
-----
A Wells Fargo employee in Tempe, Ariz., was found dead in her cubicle four days after arriving for work.Denise Prudhomme, 60, was discovered by a co-worker on Aug. 20, having clocked in at 7 a.m. on Aug. 16. Emergency services personnel pronounced her dead at 4:55 p.m., the Tempe Police Department said.
While police are investigating her death, foul play is not suspected.
