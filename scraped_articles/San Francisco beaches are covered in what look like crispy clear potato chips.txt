San Francisco beaches have peculiar new visitors: Thousands of gelatinous sea creatures called Velella velella have washed ashore in the city, as well as throughout California in recent weeks.Ocean Beach in particular was littered on Monday with the creatures, which are also known as by-the-wind sailors because their fins float above the surface of the water and catch gusts, allowing them to travel long distances.
