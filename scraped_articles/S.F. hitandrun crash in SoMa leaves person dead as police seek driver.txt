S.F. hit-and-run crash in SoMa leaves person dead as police seek driver
-----
A hit-and-run collision left a person dead early Thursday morning in San Francisco, police said.Police spokespersons said officers responded to the 500 block of Sixth Street, at Bryant Street, after a report of a vehicle collision involving a pedestrian. Upon arriving at the scene, police located a person suffering from life-threatening injuries.
Despite rendering aid, the pedestrian was pronounced dead at the scene.
The driver fled, police said.
