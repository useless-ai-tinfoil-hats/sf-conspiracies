Sting plans a February visit to the S.F. Symphony
-----
British composer and singer-songwriter Sting plans to join the flood of pop artists taking an orchestral approach to their material when he makes a pair of guest appearances with the San Francisco Symphony in February.The concerts in Davies Symphony Hall, set for Feb. 14-15, are slated to be conducted by Edwin Outwater.
