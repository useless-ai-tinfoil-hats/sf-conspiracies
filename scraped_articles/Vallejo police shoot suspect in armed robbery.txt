Vallejo police shoot suspect in armed robbery
-----
A suspect in an armed robbery was shot Monday by Vallejo police, the department said.Around 11:43 a.m., officers responded to reports of an armed robbery on the 3400 block of Sonoma Boulevard. The suspect attempted to flee in a car before crashing into an uninvolved vehicle near Tennessee Street and Sonoma Boulevard, police said. Police chased the suspect on foot and the shooting "took place soon thereafter," according to police.
