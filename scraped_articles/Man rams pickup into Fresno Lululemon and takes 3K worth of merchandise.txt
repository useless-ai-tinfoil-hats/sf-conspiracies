Man rams pickup into Fresno Lululemon and takes $3K worth of merchandise
-----
Police are looking for a man who burglarized a Fresno Lululemon store last month after ramming his truck through the entrance.The burglary, which occurred at 712 West Shaw Ave. on March 23, was caught on surveillance footage, the Fresno Police Department said in a social media post. The theft happened just before 4 a.m, Christopher Clark, a spokesperson for Fresno police, told SFGATE.
