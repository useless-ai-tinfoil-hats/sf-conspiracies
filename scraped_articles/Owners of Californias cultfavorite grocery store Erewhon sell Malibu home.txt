Owners of California's cult-favorite grocery store Erewhon sell Malibu home
-----
The owners of Los Angeles-area ultra-luxury grocery store chain Erewhon have sold their former Malibu mansion, the Real Deal reported Wednesday.Josephine and Anthony Antoci, the couple responsible for the store's meteoric rise to fame, closed the deal on the more than 3,000-square-foot waterfront residence for $15.5 million, just below its most recent asking price of $16 million.
