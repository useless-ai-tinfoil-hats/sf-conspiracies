These SF chefs went to In-N-Out after their restaurant won a Michelin star
-----
After the Michelin Guide awarded San Francisco's 7 Adams its very first star at Monday evening's ceremony, chef-owners David Fisher and Serena Chow Fisher celebrated the monumental win with a double-double and a side of animal fries at In-N-Out."That's how Serena and I celebrate," David told SFGATE. "It was our hurrah meal," Serena added.
