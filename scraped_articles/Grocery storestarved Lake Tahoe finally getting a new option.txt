Grocery store-starved Lake Tahoe finally getting a new option
-----
Colorado-based health food chain Natural Grocers is opening a new store in Lake Tahoe on Wednesday, July 31. Located at 873 Tahoe Blvd. in Incline Village, it will be the third major grocery store in the Nevada town, including a Raley's and a Grocery Outlet.The addition of Natural Grocers will give North Shore residents and tourists another grocery option after last year's closure of the 50-year-old Save Mart in Tahoe City.
