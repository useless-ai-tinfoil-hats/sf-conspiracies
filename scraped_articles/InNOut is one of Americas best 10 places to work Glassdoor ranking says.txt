In-N-Out is one of America’s best 10 places to work, Glassdoor ranking says
-----
In-N-Out isn't just garnering laughably long lines and Golden Globe Award-winning diners — the California chain is one of America's best large companies to work at, according to job review site Glassdoor.The burger empire took the No. 6 spot on Glassdoor's 2024 "Best Places to Work" list of large, United States-based companies.
