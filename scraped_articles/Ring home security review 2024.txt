Ring home security review 2024
-----
Ring home security at a glanceRing is a great home security solution for minimalists who only want to pay for what they need. It offers a variety of devices and monitoring services at some of the most affordable prices in the industry, and you can add new devices seamlessly over time. That being said, Ring has past issues with privacy and security that potential customers should know about.
* Equipment costs: $49.99 to $389.99*
* Monthly service fees: $4.99 to $20
