Warriors’ Andrew Wiggins leaves game with ankle injury
-----
ATLANTA – Golden State Warriors wing Andrew Wiggins exited Saturday's game against the Atlanta Hawks with a left ankle injury.He was ruled out in the third quarter, according to the Warriors.
Wiggins appeared to suffer the injury late in the second quarter during a collision with Hawks center Clint Capela. He limped into the tunnel afterward and didn't re-emerge in the second half.
