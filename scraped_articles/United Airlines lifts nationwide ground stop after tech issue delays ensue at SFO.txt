United Airlines lifts nationwide ground stop after tech issue, delays ensue at SFO
-----
United Airlines issued a nationwide ground stop on the morning of Sept. 5 due to a "systemwide technology issue" that delayed 40 flights from the airline at San Francisco International Airport.The airline declared a ground stop at 10:38 a.m. and asked the FAA to pause the airline's departures nationwide.
