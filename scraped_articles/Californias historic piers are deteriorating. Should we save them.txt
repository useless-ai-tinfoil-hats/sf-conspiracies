California's historic piers are deteriorating. Should we save them?
-----
After a strong winter storm ripped through the California coast in February, one of the state's oldest historic piers looked like it might crumble into the ocean. Forceful waves had knocked away several of the wooden posts that hold up the deck under the end of the Cayucos Pier two hours north of Santa Barbara, leaving it teetering over the water without support.
