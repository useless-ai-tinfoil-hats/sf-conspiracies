Police chase through Bay Area ends in arrest in S.F.
-----
A fleeing homicide suspect led officers from three different law enforcement agencies through several Bay Area cities before the pursuit ended in San Francisco on Thursday afternoon.The chase started around 11:15 a.m. when Oakland police officers observed a vehicle "driving erratically" on the 4700 block of Belfast Avenue in Oakland, according to the Oakland Police Department. Officers tried to stop the vehicle, but it fled from officers, Officer Darryl Rodgers said in a statement.
