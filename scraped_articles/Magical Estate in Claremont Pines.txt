Magical Estate in Claremont Pines
-----
Elegant Architectural Masterpiece in Rockridge(ad) It is a rare opportunity to experience a home that would be nearly impossible to replicate today. Built in 1912 for the visionaries behind the development of this thirty-lot section of Rockridge, this Arts & Crafts Swiss Chalet residence was the bold promotional statement for what would become Claremont Manor, one of the most sought-after neighborhoods in Oakland.
