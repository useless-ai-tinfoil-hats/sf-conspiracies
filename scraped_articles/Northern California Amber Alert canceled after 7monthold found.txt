Northern California Amber Alert canceled after 7-month-old found
-----
A 7-month-old child who was allegedly abducted by her step father Thursday morning, prompting an Amber Alert in six Northern California counties, has been found, and the step father was arrested, authorities said.The child and Lance Colon were found safely at a home in Rancho Cordova (Sacramento County), about 55 miles south from where Colon allegedly abducted the infant around midnight, according to the Yuba County Sheriff's Department.
