Bay Area man admits to killing girlfriend and burning her dismembered remains in barrel, police say
-----
A South Bay man accused of killing and dismembering his girlfriend admitted to shooting her inside his car, cutting her body into pieces in a bathtub and burning her body, according to police documents reviewed by the Chronicle.Ivan Alfaro Escobedo, 31, of Gilroy is being held behind bars on suspicion of killing his girlfriend, Alyssa Salazar, 33, who had accused him of rape. The Gilroy Police Department began investigating the case after Salazar disappeared in late July.
