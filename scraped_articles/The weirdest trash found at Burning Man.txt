The weirdest trash found at Burning Man
-----
"Have you heard of the world's oldest MOOP?" Haley Anthonisen asks, using the Burning Man acronym that stands for "matter out of place" (which is playa slang for trash).I shake my head, and she presents me with a briefcase. Inside, there is a case containing a scrunchie attached to a glow stick, some string, a piece of duct tape and part of a zip-tie with an inscription: "Oldest known MOOP, Black Rock City, NV, c. 1990."
