Lawsuit: More claims of racial profiling, excessive force by Antioch police
-----
A new federal lawsuit accuses nearly two dozen Antioch police officers of engaging in racial profiling and using excessive force, building upon two previous lawsuits that allege similar civil rights violations as the fallout from racist text messages within the department continues.The lawsuit, filed Thursday in U.S. District Court, names the city, three former police chiefs and 20 officers as defendants.
