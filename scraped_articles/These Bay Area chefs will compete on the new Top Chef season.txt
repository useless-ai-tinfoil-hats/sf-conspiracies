These Bay Area chefs will compete on the new ‘Top Chef’ season
-----
Three San Francisco-affiliated chefs will compete on the new season of the acclaimed TV show "Top Chef."Season 21 will feature David Murphy, chef and co-owner of Mission District restaurant Shuggie's Trash Pie + Natural Wine; Laura Ozyilmaz, chef and co-owner of Mediterranean newcomer Dalida; and Rasika Venkatesa, the former chef of Moroccan fine-dining spot Mourad, among other chefs.
