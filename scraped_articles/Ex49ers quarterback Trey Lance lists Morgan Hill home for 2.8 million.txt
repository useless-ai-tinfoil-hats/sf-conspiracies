Ex-49ers quarterback Trey Lance lists Morgan Hill home for $2.8 million
-----
Former 49ers quarterback Trey Lance has listed his Morgan Hill home for $2.8 million.The two-story home spans more than 4,000 square feet and includes five bedrooms and 3.5 bathrooms. Built in 1996, it has a grand entryway and a large living room with a fireplace. There's also a butler's pantry off the kitchen.
