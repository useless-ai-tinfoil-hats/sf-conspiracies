Giants offered Shohei Ohtani same $700M, $680M deferred deal Dodgers did
-----
Some San Francisco Giants fans might take solace in the fact that, for the second year in a row, the team matched the terms that landed the winter's top free agent. The Giants offered Shohei Ohtani $700 million. They were willing to defer $680 million of that, as per Ohtani's request.Ohtani went to the Dodgers for the same deal. Last year, the Yankees went up to San Francisco's $360 million offer for Aaron Judge, and Judge remained in New York.
