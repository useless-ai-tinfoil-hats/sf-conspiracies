Olympic star Katie Ledecky rankles some Stanford fans with Florida swim cap
-----
Katie Ledecky is arguably the greatest female swimmer in American history and the greatest distance swimmer in the sport. But during the Olympics trials in Indianapolis this week, the Stanford alum has drawn some added attention and even rankled some supporters for her choice of headwear.Throughout the week, Ledecky has been swimming while wearing caps from the University of Florida, including a black cap with the school's alligator logo on it and a blue cap with an orange "Gators"
