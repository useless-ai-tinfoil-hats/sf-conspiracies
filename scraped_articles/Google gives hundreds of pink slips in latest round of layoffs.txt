Google gives hundreds of pink slips in latest round of layoffs
-----
In the latest round of layoffs to hit the tech world, Google gave pink slips to a few hundred members of its recruiting staff around the globe."As we've said, we continue to invest in top engineering and technical talent while also meaningfully slowing the pace of our overall hiring," Courtenay Mencini, a spokesperson for the Mountain View-based company, said in a statement emailed to SFGATE on Friday morning. "In line with this, the volume of requests for our recruiters has gone down.
