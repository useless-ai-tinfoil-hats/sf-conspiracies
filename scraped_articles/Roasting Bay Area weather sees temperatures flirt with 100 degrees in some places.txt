Roasting Bay Area weather sees temperatures flirt with 100 degrees in some places
-----
UPDATED FORECAST: California's heat wave is about to peak. Which areas will see record temps?Summer weather blazed in early Tuesday, with a scorching sun bringing 100-degree temperatures to the outer reaches of the Bay Area and misery for people stuck either laboring outside or fanning themselves in homes without air conditioning.
Regions east of the Caldecott Tunnel or north or south of San Francisco took a blast of heat usually unseen until after the start of summer on June 20.
