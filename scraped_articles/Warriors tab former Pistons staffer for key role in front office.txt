Warriors tab former Pistons staffer for key role in front office
-----
The Golden State Warriors are adding to their front office, hiring Jon Phelps to be their salary-cap strategist and team counsel, a league source confirmed to the Chronicle.Phelps comes to Golden State by way of the Detroit Pistons, for whom he worked the past two years as senior director of basketball strategy. Prior to that, he was a basketball strategy analyst for two years after earlier serving as the general manager of Detroit's G League affiliate, the Grand Rapids Drive.
