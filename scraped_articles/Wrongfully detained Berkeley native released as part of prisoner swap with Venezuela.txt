Wrongfully detained Berkeley native released as part of prisoner swap with Venezuela
-----
A Berkeley native was one of several U.S. nationals released Wednesday after being wrongfully detained in Venezuela, according to the U.S. State Department.Savoi Wright was detained while traveling in Venezuela on Oct. 24, according to a GoFundMe set up by his family. The Associated Press reported that Wright, 38, grew up in Berkeley and had been splitting his time between Oakland, Miami and South America while working remotely as a mortgage loan officer.
