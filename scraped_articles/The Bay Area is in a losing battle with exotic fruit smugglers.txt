The Bay Area is in a ‘losing battle’ with exotic fruit smugglers
-----
On Wednesday morning, a UPS driver discovered a dozen small boxes that didn't look quite right.That's because they contained 600 pounds of June plums, says Michelle Thom, Santa Clara County's deputy agricultural commissioner. June plums are among many clandestine fruits that are often smuggled into the Bay Area from Vietnam, India, Cambodia, China and Laos.
