Critics fault Oakland budget for failing to fix underlying financial problems
-----
Oakland's budget crisis is drawing increased scrutiny as the City Council nears a vote on a spending plan and critics take aim at the city's efforts to address its huge deficit.An informal group called Citizens Oakland has been circulating a detailed report on the budget that highlights what it says are unresolved financial problems that could haunt the city in the future. Oakland's leaders are working to close a $292 million gap over the next two years.
