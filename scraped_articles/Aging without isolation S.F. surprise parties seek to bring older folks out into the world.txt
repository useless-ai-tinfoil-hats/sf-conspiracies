Aging without isolation: S.F. surprise parties seek to ‘bring older folks out into the world’
-----
Because he was born on Christmas Eve, in 1952, Harry Wong never got a birthday party, or any other kind of party, that was just about him."I felt cheated," said Wong, who finally had that injustice corrected at age 71½ on Sunday afternoon when he walked, with the help of his white cane, into a back room at Taishan Restaurant in San Francisco's Chinatown.
