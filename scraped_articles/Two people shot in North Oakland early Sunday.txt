Two people shot in North Oakland early Sunday
-----
Two people were seriously injured in a shooting just after midnight Sunday in the 900 block of 63rd Street in North Oakland, police said.Officers responded to a ShotSpotter activation to find a victim suffering from a gunshot. The victim was treated on the scene by paramedics and transported to the hospital.
