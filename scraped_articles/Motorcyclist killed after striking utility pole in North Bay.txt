Motorcyclist killed after striking utility pole in North Bay
-----
A motorcyclist died Saturday afternoon after striking a utility pole in the North Bay, according to the California Highway Patrol.Santa Rosa CHP officers responding to the crash shortly before 2:30 p.m. reported that the motorcyclist had lost control of his vehicle and veered to the left of Tomales Petaluma Road, east of Twin Bridge Road. He was ejected after colliding with a fence and utility pole, the CHP said.
