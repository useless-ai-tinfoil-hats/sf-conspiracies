BottleRock 2024 unveils after-hours lineup with St. Vincent, Norah Jones, Cold War Kids
-----
The organizers of BottleRock Napa Valley festival plan to ensure the music doesn't stop after curfew by arranging a series of intimate, late-night concerts during Memorial Day weekend.The festival's AfterDark concerts are scheduled to run from May 22-27 at various venues across the Bay Area.
Artists like Norah Jones, St. Vincent, Stephen Marley, Cold War Kids and Miike Snow are set to headline these late-night performances, with many of them also taking the stage during the daytime
