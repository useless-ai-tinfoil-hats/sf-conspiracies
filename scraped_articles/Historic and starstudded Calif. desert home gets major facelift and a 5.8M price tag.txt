Historic and star-studded Calif. desert home gets major facelift and a $5.8M price tag
-----
The Tamarisk Country Club, established in the 1950s, is known as a place that "old Hollywood called home in the desert," and notably embraced both Jewish and non-Jewish members, which was not always the case at the time.The home at 70889 Tamarisk Lane in Rancho Mirage, California, was one of the first built there, and now, after a major remodel, it's for sale at $5.8 million.
