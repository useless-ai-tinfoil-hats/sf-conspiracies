Discover Joaquin Miller Park, a crown jewel in the Oakland Hills
-----
My family and I are lucky to live close to Oakland's Joaquin Miller Park, the city's "jewel in the redwoods." The urban park is home to second- and third-growth redwood groves, oak forests, expansive meadows and a network of creek beds.
