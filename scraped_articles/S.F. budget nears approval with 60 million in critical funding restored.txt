S.F. budget nears approval with $60 million in critical funding restored
-----
Loud cheers echoed across City Hall after a key committee passed a budget deal with Mayor London Breed that restores nearly $60 million in funding cuts she proposed to child care, food insecurity and violence prevention programs, among others.The deal was struck just after 6 p.m. Wednesday, an unusually early end time to negotiations that in past years have kept officials at City Hall well past midnight.
The deal for the record $15.9 billion budget passed with four votes in favor
