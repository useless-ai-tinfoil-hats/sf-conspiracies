Idris Elba cancels Outside Lands appearance: ‘Please don’t be mad’
-----
Idris Elba announced he won't be making it to Outside Lands, just three days before the start of the San Francisco festival this week.The British actor and DJ shared the news with his followers via an Instagram story on Tuesday, Aug. 6, citing "unforeseen circumstances" for his absence.
"I won't be able to make it to ÎLESONIQ in Montreal and Outside Lands in San Francisco. I was really looking forward to these gigs and am gutted that I can't be there with you all," Elba wrote.
