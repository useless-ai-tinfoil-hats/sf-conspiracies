Former Oakland Mayor Libby Schaaf announces run for state treasurer in 2026
-----
Former Oakland Mayor Libby Schaaf announced Thursday she's planning to run for state treasurer in the 2026 election.Schaaf, who led Oakland from 2015 to 2023, said that as mayor she oversaw the city's $3 billion public budget and has experience in responsible fiscal management. If elected, she said she plans to improve public infrastructure and educational opportunities, as well as tackling climate goals and the state's housing crisis. Her candidacy was first reported by Politico.
