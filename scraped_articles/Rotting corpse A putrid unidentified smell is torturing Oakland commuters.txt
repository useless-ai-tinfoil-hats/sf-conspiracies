'Rotting corpse': A putrid, unidentified smell is torturing Oakland commuters
-----
If you commute from Oakland to San Francisco each morning, chances are you've smelled it.It sort of smells like gasoline, or the ancient layer of dust that gets burned off the old, outdated heater that your landlord never bothered to replace. Some Reddit users said the odor reminded them of a "rotting corpse," while others complained it smelled like chemicals, or, even more ominously, corn nuts.
