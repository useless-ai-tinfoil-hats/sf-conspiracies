Golden Gate Ferry rides indefinitely suspended between S.F. and Sausalito
-----
Golden Gate Ferry service between San Francisco and Sausalito was suspended indefinitely Friday after a routine inspection of the Sausalito pier turned up a problem, ferry officials said.Officials did not disclose specifics about the issue, but oursausalito.com said Golden Gate Ferry described the problem as a "structural compromise at the pier."
