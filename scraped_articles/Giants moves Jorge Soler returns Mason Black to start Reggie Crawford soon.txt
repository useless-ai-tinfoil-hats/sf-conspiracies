Giants moves: Jorge Soler returns, Mason Black to start, Reggie Crawford soon?

One player returned from the San Francisco Giants' long injury list.Jorge Soler, out since May 8 (retroactive to May 5) with a right shoulder strain, was activated from the 10-day IL Friday and returned to the lineup batting leadoff as the designated hitter against the Colorado Rockies.
