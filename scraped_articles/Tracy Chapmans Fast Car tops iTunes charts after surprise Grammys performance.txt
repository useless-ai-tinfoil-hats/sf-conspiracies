Tracy Chapman’s ‘Fast Car’ tops iTunes charts after surprise Grammys performance
-----
Tracy Chapman's original rendition of "Fast Car" raced to the top of the iTunes charts following her performance alongside Luke Combs at the 66th Grammy Awards.The longtime San Francisco resident, who rarely makes public appearances, appeared onstage at Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles on Sunday, Feb. 4, revisiting her 1988 hit that experienced a resurgence on the charts due to Combs' 2023 remake.
