Avid Bay Area cyclist killed by driver during club ride in Marin, police say
-----
An avid long-distance cyclist was hit and killed by a driver in a crash during an organized ride with his bicycle club in San Rafael on Saturday, officials said.The coroner identified the deceased as Peter Grace, 70. He was on a 200-kilometer ride across Marin County with the San Francisco Randonneurs Saturday when he apparently fell off his bike and was run over by a driver on Andersen Drive at the Sonoma Marin Area Rail Transit railroad grade crossing, the San Rafael Police Department
