Two men accused of S.F. robberies that began with fake online ads
-----
Two San Francisco men were arrested and charged with robbing at least eight people after placing online advertisements to sell a vehicle, police said Thursday.Jevonte Powell, 19, and Jermaine Williams, 20, were arrested last month after a yearlong investigation into the series of robberies. Both pleaded not guilty to the charges in San Francisco Superior Court and remain in jail.
