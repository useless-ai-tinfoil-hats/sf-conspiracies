Thousands gather at S.F. Civic Center in solidarity with Palestinians
-----
Thousands of protesters gathered Saturday at San Francisco's Civic Center Plaza to call for an end to the horrors unfolding in the Middle East.The protest was one of many across the country and around the world as part of International Day of Solidarity to free Palestinians. That includes a sister rally in Washington, D.C.
