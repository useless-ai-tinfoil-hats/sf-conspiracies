S.F. police search for suspects after ‘scary and horrific’ car fires in Bernal Heights
-----
San Francisco police said Wednesday that investigators are still probing an incident of arson that torched four cars on a Bernal Heights block, hours before the end of New Year's Eve.Police said they were dispatched to the 300 block of Park Street just before 9:30 p.m. after receiving reports of multiple vehicles on fire. San Francisco fire officials also went to the scene and helped extinguish the four unoccupied vehicles, which were set ablaze.
No individuals were injured, police
