Oakland encampment fire near Lake Merritt leaves piles of singed debris
-----
Billows of smoke could be seen rising from an encampment fire near Lake Merritt in Oakland on Thursday morning.The smoky inferno near Oakland's Henry J. Kaiser Convention Center was put out in minutes by responding firefighters, said Oakland Fire Department Chief of Staff Michael Hunt.
The fire was likely called in by multiple commuters in the heavily trafficked area, said Hunt.
