Oakland mayor calls for reopened application process in picking police chief
-----
Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao has asked the police commission to reopen the application process for the position of the city's police chief, her office said Thursday.Her request comes after she rejected the commission's list of nominees for the next chief, one of whom she previously fired and another who's on leave and under investigation in his current job. Thao has also asked that the commission send her a new list of finalists by the end of January.
