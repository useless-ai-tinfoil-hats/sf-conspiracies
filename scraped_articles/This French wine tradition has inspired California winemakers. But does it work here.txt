This French wine tradition has inspired California winemakers. But does it work here?
-----
The French word vigneron doesn't have a perfect English translation.It refers to a person who both farms a vineyard and makes wine from it. Vignerons are commonplace in France but scarce in California, where the jobs of farming and winemaking are almost always separate. Over the past decade, however, the natural wine movement — which is more than a little obsessed with French wine culture — has tried to make the vigneron ideal happen in California.
