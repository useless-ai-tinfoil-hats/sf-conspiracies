Driver topples historic Death Valley tower while using it to get vehicle unstuck
-----
A 113-year-old wooden tram tower in Death Valley was knocked over when a driver attached a winch to it in an attempt to pull their vehicle out of a patch of deep mud, the National Park Service said Monday.A Park Service news release said the historic tower near the Saline Valley lake bed was toppled sometime between April 1 and April 24.
