Change in season ushers in a bounty of arts happenings
-----
As spring begins to bloom, there's already a vibrant array of fresh arts events and performances to look forward to across the Bay Area.From the beats of the California Roots Music and Arts Festival, the largest reggae music festival in Northern California, to the first-ever Latin music-focused Festival La Onda as well as operas featuring luminaries like Jakub Józef Orliński and the husband-and-wife powerhouse duo of Amina Edris and Pene Pati, let music lift your spirits.
