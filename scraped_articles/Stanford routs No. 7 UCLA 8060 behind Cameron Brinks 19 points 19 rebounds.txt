Stanford routs No. 7 UCLA 80-60 behind Cameron Brink’s 19 points, 19 rebounds
-----
What a difference a game — and a superstar — makes.Cameron Brink totaled 19 points, 19 rebounds and seven blocks as No. 4 Stanford led early and didn't look back en route to a 80-60 thrashing of No. 7 UCLA in front of a sellout crowd at Maples Pavilion on Sunday. The Cardinal's win came less than 48 hours after its loss to No. 15 USC, which got a 51-point performance from Trojans freshman JuJu Watkins.
