How America's most bizarre KFC ended up in California
-----
All-seeing in its gaze, one of Los Angeles' most iconic structures towers over a major north-south thoroughfare. No, not the Hollywood Sign. Rather, it's an angular Kentucky Fried Chicken planted on Western Avenue, with fish-like steel fins jutting out of its two-story structure.Depending on the angle and your vantage point, the building either resembles "the biggest bucket of Kentucky Fried Chicken that ever existed," as the Los Angeles Conservancy has described it, or a chicken itself
