Why California is grappling with a 'substantial' rise in tuberculosis cases
-----
California is grappling with a "substantial" rise in tuberculosis cases, a February 2024 advisory from the California Department of Public Health reads, returning to levels not seen in four years.According to the advisory, the number of reported TB cases in California rose 15% last year, from 1,842 cases in 2022 to 2,113 in 2023. It was the third year of an increase after the number of cases plunged 19% with the onset of the pandemic.
