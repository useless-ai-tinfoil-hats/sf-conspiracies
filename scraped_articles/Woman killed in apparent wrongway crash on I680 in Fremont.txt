Woman killed in apparent wrong-way crash on I-680 in Fremont
-----
A crash on Interstate 680 in Fremont killed a passenger and severely injured two drivers early Saturday, the California Highway Patrol said.The driver of a gray Dodge Ram was apparently heading the wrong way southbound when the vehicle struck a white BMW 33i south of Auto Mall Parkway at about 1:15 a.m., according to CHP officer Tyler Hahn.
