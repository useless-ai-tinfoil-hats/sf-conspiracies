Northern California weather: Heat returns this week after unusual August storm
-----
After an usually cool, damp weather pattern in late August, more typical summer conditions will return to Northern California this week.Over the weekend, Reno recorded its coldest August day in 131 years of records, snow accumulated at several high-elevation Sierra Nevada ski resorts, and scattered rain showers hit areas from Eureka to Oakland.
