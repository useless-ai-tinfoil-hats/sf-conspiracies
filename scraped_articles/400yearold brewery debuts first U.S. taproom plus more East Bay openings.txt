400-year-old brewery debuts first U.S. taproom, plus more East Bay openings
-----
New restaurant openings haven't let up in the East Bay, even as the year winds down.The latest arrivals include a variety of cuisines, from Sichuan malatang to Roman pizza and tacos from a Michelin bib gourmand spin-off. There are plenty of new things to sip on, too, with more bubble tea, Yemeni coffee and a revamped Berkeley taproom.
