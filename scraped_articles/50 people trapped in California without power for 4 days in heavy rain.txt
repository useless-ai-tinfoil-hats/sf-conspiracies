50 people trapped in California without power for 4 days in heavy rain
-----
A deluge of rain in Northern California on Saturday knocked out power and washed out access roads in the small Humboldt County community of Redwood Valley, leaving dozens of people stranded and in the dark for four days, officials said."The situation for us is rather serious. People are without electricity and without a way to easily get food and supplies," Redwood Valley resident Lana Borealis told the Eureka Times-Standard in a story published Tuesday.
