Two arrested on torture, murder charges after boy, 2, found in East Bay bathtub
-----
Two people have been arrested on suspicion of murder and child abuse after a 2-year-old toddler was found unresponsive in a bathtub.Just before 9 p.m. Monday, deputies with the Contra Costa County Sheriff's Office responded to the 300 block of California Street in Rodeo after receiving a call that a toddler had drowned in a bathtub, a spokesperson for the sheriff's office said. Emergency personnel tried to resuscitate the boy, who was taken to a hospital. He was later pronounced dead,
