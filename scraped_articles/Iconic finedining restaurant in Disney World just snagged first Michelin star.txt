Iconic fine-dining restaurant in Disney World just snagged first Michelin star
-----
Not all restaurants have Michelin-star bragging rights — much less can say they're located in "the most magical place on earth" — but Victoria & Albert's can now claim both.The fine-dining restaurant tucked inside Disney World's stylish Grand Floridian Resort & Spa earned its first Michelin star this month. The win was twofold: Disney World is now the only U.S. theme park to own a restaurant with the prestigious honor, Disney Parks Blog wrote.
