11-year-old boy reported missing in San Francisco, police say
-----
The San Francisco Police Department is urging the public to be on the lookout for a missing at-risk child last seen a few blocks from San Francisco's Panhandle.LeMichael Humphrey, 11, was last seen on Tuesday at approximately 2:55 p.m. on the 400 block of Stanyan Street. LeMichael was seen wearing a black and gray hoodie, a green shirt, gray slacks and white Nike shoes. LeMichael is "at-risk" because he is diagnosed with autism and schizophrenia, police said.
