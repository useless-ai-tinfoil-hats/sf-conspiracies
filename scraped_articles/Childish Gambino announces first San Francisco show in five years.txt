Childish Gambino announces first San Francisco show in five years
-----
Donald Glover once told a San Francisco Bay Area audience it was witnessing the last Childish Gambino tour ever.Not so.
On Monday, Glover (aka Childish Gambino, aka Lando, aka Simba) announced he's bringing Gambino out of retirement for a world tour, with a stop at San Francisco's Chase Center on Saturday, Sept. 21.
