This Michelin-approved S.F. Chinese restaurant just opened a new spot devoted to duck
-----
The chef behind one of Chinatown's top restaurants has opened a new spot across the street with a focus on duck.Li Jun Han, of the celebrated Chinatown restaurant Z&Y and Richmond District restaurant Chili House, has opened Z&Y Peking Duck at 606 Jackson St. The location is across the street from the original Z&Y, known for its fiery Szechuan food, and was previously home to offshoot Z&Y Bistro, which opened with a more modern look and an eclectic menu including ramen and grilled skewers
