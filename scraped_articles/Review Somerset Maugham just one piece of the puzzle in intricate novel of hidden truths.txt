Review: Somerset Maugham just one piece of the puzzle in intricate novel of hidden truths
-----
Two famous men walk the pages of Tan Twan Eng's novel, long-listed for the 2023 Booker Prize: the celebrated British author W. Somerset Maugham and Dr. Sun Yat-sen, often called "the Father of the Nation" of the Republic of China. But it's two women — one stunted by marriage and the other on trial for the murder of her lover — who bring "The House of Doors" to life.Eng, born in Malaysia, based this intricate novel on actual events.
