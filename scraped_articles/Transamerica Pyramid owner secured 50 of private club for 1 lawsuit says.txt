Transamerica Pyramid owner secured 50% of private club for $1, lawsuit says
-----
A glitzy, members-only club that was slated to take up three floors of San Francisco's iconic Transamerica Pyramid will not open in the foreseeable future, according to a new lawsuit filed by the New York-based company Core.On Wednesday, the attorney for Core filed a complaint against Transamerica Pyramid owner and real estate developer Michael Shvo. It alleged that Shvo orchestrated a "sinister and fraudulent scheme" intended to mislead Core into signing leases with "exorbitant rents"
