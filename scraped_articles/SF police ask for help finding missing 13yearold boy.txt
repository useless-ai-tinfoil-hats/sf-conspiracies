SF police ask for help finding missing 13-year-old boy
-----
The San Francisco Police Department is calling on the public to keep an eye out for a missing at-risk 13-year-old boy.Knowledge Shepard was last seen at 7 p.m. on Friday, Sept. 15, walking into the Embarcadero BART Station, according to an SFPD news release published on social media.
Police said the boy "has a fascination with buses and trains and usually rides MUNI or BART."
Knowledge has brown eyes and a mostly bald head; he is 5 feet, 5 inches tall and weighs 98 pounds.
