Review: Russell Crowe can’t even save himself in ‘The Exorcism’
-----
There's half of a good idea behind "The Exorcism," which probably seemed like a justification for going ahead with the movie: Imagine if an actor gets demon-possessed while acting in a film about an exorcism.That might be interesting, right?
Maybe it could have been. But without a second idea about how to make that premise fly, there's nowhere for "The Exorcism" to go — beyond having Russell Crowe channel Linda Blair.
