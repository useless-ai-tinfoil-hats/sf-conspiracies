Williams-Sonoma is delaying the closure of its only S.F. store
-----
San Francisco cookware and home furnishings maker Williams-Sonoma has pushed back the closure of its only store in the city by a year.The company told the Chronicle that it was "thrilled to confirm" that the 340 Post St. store will stay open until 2025. The prominent space is just north of Union Square, between Tiffany & Co. and Apple.
Luxury brand Chanel bought the three-story building for $63 million in 2022 and plans to move from nearby 156 Geary St.
