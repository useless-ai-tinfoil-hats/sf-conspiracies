CVS security guard fatally shot at Bay Area store, police say
-----
A security guard at a CVS store in Fremont was fatally shot following an altercation Thursday night, police said.The shooting occurred around 11 p.m., when "reporting parties" said the on-duty security guard at the CVS in the Fremont Hub shopping center got into a verbal altercation, according to officials.
