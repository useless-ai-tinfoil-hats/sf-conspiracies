San Francisco police arrest stabbing suspect after 4-hour standoff
-----
A woman allegedly stabbed someone and then barricaded herself inside a San Francisco residence for four hours before police were able to arrest her, according to the San Francisco Police Department.Around 11:40 a.m. Saturday, San Francisco police officers responded to reports of a fight near the intersection of Folsom and Essex streets, where they found one person suffering from a non-life-threatening stab wound, according to Sgt. Kathryn Winters.
