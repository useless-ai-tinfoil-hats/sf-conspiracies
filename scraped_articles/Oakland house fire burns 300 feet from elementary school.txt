Oakland house fire burns 300 feet from elementary school
-----
Oakland firefighters said they were battling a "fully involved" fire that began in a detached garage at 552 Montclair Ave. on Tuesday before moving to the main structure, a house.The house is about 300 feet from Cleveland Elementary School on Cleveland Street in Oakland, according to publicly available maps.
Oakland Fire Department officials said no one was injured or displaced as a result of the fire, which was deemed under control shortly after noon.
