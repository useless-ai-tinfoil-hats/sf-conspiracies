Historic California pier teeters over ocean after massive storm
-----
A pier on the Central California coast with a history dating back more than 150 years is teetering over the ocean after a massive winter storm pummeled the state early this week.Cayucos Pier, in the small beach town of the same name in San Luis Obispo County, has been closed to the public after its last row of pilings was swept away by the high surf and strong winds.
Footage shared by the county appeared to show parts of the broken pier among other debris floating on the storm
