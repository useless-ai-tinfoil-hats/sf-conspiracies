SoCal storm downs historic palm trees along Santa Barbara coastline
-----
Santa Barbara County lost some of its most beloved palm trees earlier this month after a massive storm walloped Southern California, taking out as many as four trees at Refugio State Beach in Goleta, California.Multiple palms fell last week and another two were down on Wednesday, according to Dena Bellman of the State Parks Channel Coast District, who spoke with Noozhawk, a local Santa Barbara news outlet.
