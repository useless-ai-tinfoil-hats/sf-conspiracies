Bay Area Oktoberfest 2023: Celebrate with the best beer, brats, polka music and dance events
-----
Get ready for snappy lederhosen and pretty dirndl skirts twirling around tented dance floors, carnival rides, warm pretzels, beers, brats and gingerbread hearts. It's time to raise steins and spirits in the biergarten with ein prosit der gemütlichkeit — a toast to good feelings — in honor of the upcoming fall season.Oktoberfest was originally a community wedding celebration in October 1810 for a Bavarian crown prince and princess. Officially taking place from Sept. 16- Oct. 3 in Munich
