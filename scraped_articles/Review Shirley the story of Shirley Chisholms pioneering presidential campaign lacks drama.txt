Review: ‘Shirley,’ the story of Shirley Chisholm’s pioneering presidential campaign, lacks drama
-----
The best thing to be said of the new Netflix film "Shirley," is that it's sure to increase awareness of Shirley Chisholm, the first Black woman to attempt a presidential run as the nominee of a major party.The biopic succeeds in conveying how a presidential campaign by a Black woman was regarded by the media and the public in 1972: not quite regarded as a joke but rather as so hopeless it was almost absurd.
