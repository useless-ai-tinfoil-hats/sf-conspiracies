California sees historic return of pandas at San Diego Zoo
-----
While uncertainty looms over San Francisco Mayor London Breed's plan to bring pandas to the city, the San Diego Zoo is set to showcase its new black-and-white residents.The pair of pandas who arrived from China this summer will make their public debut on Thursday, marking the first time in over two decades that pandas have entered the United States.
