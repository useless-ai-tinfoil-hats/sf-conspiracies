Oakland man allegedly robbed Bay Area banks by slipping tellers a handwritten note
-----
An Oakland resident was charged with bank robbery of several Alameda County banks after allegedly passing handwritten notes to bank tellers demanding money and stating he had a gun, officials said.The 30-year-old man allegedly robbed three banks and attempted to rob two more from Oct. 23, 2023, through Jan. 13, 2024, according to a news release from the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Northern District of California.
