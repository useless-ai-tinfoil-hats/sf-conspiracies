Review: IU shows California love at first Bay Area concert
-----
To mark the occasion of IU's first Bay Area appearance, her charm offensive started before the South Korean artist even sang a note. Standard ticket holders received a special concert keepsake at their seat: a metal Altoids-like tin containing IU picture cards, a magnet and key ring."It's a gift from my mom for all of you," IU told fans at the start of her Oakland Arena debut on Tuesday, July 30. "I hope you treasure it."
