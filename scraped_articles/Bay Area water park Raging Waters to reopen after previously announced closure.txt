Bay Area water park Raging Waters to reopen after previously announced closure
-----
After San Jose's Raging Waters announced a permanent closure at the end of last year's summer season, city officials confirmed on Thursday that the park is set to reopen in time for summer 2024.The water park's lease with its operator ended last year, resulting in its indefinite closure.
