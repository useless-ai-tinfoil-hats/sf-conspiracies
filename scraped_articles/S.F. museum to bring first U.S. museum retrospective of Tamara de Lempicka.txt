S.F. museum to bring first U.S. museum retrospective of Tamara de Lempicka
-----
Tamara de Lempicka, the 20th century painter famed for her Art Deco aesthetic and as a symbol of liberated womanhood, is finally getting her first full museum retrospective in the United States."Tamara de Lempicka" is set to open at the de Young Museum on Oct. 9 as a joint presentation by the Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco, which includes the de Young and Legion of Honor Museum, and the Museum of Fine Arts, Houston, where it will travel next.
