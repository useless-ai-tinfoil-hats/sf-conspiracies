San Francisco scientists are trying to save a 24-armed cannibalistic sea creature from extinction
-----
A critically endangered sea creature may have a chance at survival thanks to recent repopulation efforts spearheaded in Southern California and now underway in San Francisco.The sunflower star is a swift-moving predator with up to 24 arms that dines on prey like crabs and snails by trapping them within its 15,000 tube feet.
