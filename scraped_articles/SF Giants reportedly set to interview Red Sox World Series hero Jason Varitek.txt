SF Giants reportedly set to interview Red Sox World Series hero Jason Varitek
-----
The search for the next manager of the San Francisco Giants took an unexpected turn on Friday. Joel Sherman of the New York Post and MLB Network reported the Red Sox granted the Giants permission to interview Boston Red Sox World Series hero Jason Varitek, who is currently employed as a game planning coordinator/catching coach with Boston.Sherman said Varitek is expected to take part in an "informal phone call" with the Giants brass in the next few days.
