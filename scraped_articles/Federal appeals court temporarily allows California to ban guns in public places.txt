Federal appeals court temporarily allows California to ban guns in public places
-----
A California law banning concealed firearms in communal spaces such as amusement parks and libraries will take effect — for now — after a federal appeals court ruling Saturday.The order by a panel of judges at the 9th Circuit Court of Appeals temporarily froze a district court decision earlier this month that deemed the law unconstitutional.
