Man hurls metal chair through window of beloved San Francisco restaurant Sam's Burgers
-----
A man threw a chair through the front window of Sam's Burgers, a beloved late-night burger joint in San Francisco's North Beach neighborhood, on Monday. Anthony Bourdain once said Sam's made a "top three burger in the world.""Look, it sucks. Why would someone do that?" Emad ElShawa, owner of Sam's, told SFGATE. "But at the end of the day, we don't really care. No one got hurt. It's mostly just a pain in the ass. That's really it."
