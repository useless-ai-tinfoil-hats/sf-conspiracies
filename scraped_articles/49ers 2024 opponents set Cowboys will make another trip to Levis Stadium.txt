49ers’ 2024 opponents set; Cowboys will make another trip to Levi’s Stadium
-----
The Dallas Cowboys, already a possible playoff opponent for the San Francisco 49ers this month, definitely will return to Santa Clara next season.The Cowboys landed on the 49ers' 2024 schedule Sunday when Dallas beat the Commanders 38-10 to win the NFC East. The 49ers will play a first-place schedule next season by virtue of winning the NFC West. That means they also will play the winner of the NFC South (Tampa Bay) and AFC West (Kansas City), as well as Dallas.
