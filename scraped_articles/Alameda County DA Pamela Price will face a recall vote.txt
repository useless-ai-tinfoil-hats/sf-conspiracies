Alameda County DA Pamela Price will face a recall vote
-----
Alameda County District Attorney Pamela Price is set to face a recall election, the Registrar of Voters announced Monday.Critics of Price who organized a campaign to remove her from office collected 74,757 valid signatures, the Registrar of Voters said in a statement. The campaign needed at least 73,195 valid signatures to trigger a recall election.
