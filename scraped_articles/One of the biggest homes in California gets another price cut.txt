One of the biggest homes in California gets another price cut
-----
One of the biggest, priciest and most lavish homes in all of California is apparently struggling to find a new owner, as the listing price keeps dropping.Knoll House — a stately 32,000-square-foot Colonial Revival manor in Pasadena — is being offered at auction on June 19 after a string of hefty price cuts. Back in 2021, the listing broke records in Pasadena when it hit the market at $48 million. After failing to sell, it reappeared in 2023 at $36.5 million.
Now it's marked at $32
