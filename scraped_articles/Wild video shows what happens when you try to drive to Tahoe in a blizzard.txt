Wild video shows what happens when you try to drive to Tahoe in a blizzard
-----
Despite days of warnings that a potentially catastrophic storm was on the way, some drivers were still on the roads to Tahoe when a blizzard hit this weekend. Now, astonishing footage shows the world of hurt those motorists found themselves in.A series of drone videos taken by the account Chris Weather Chasing shows trucks and cars trapped on Donner Pass on Saturday.
