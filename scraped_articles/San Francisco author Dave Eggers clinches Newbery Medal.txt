San Francisco author Dave Eggers clinches Newbery Medal
-----
Dave Eggers clinched this year's John Newbery Medal for "The Eyes and the Impossible," adding yet another accolade to his illustrious career.The San Francisco author and 826 Valencia writing workshop co-founder, renowned for acclaimed adult works like "A Heartbreaking Work of Staggering Genius" and "What Is the What," received recognition for his outstanding contribution to children's literature from the American Library Association on Monday, Jan. 22.
