Huge fire torches Oakland apartment building and nearby house
-----
Firefighters in Oakland battled a fast-moving fire that consumed an apartment building in Oakland on Tuesday afternoon at 600 E. Eighth St.Oakland Fire Department officials posted to social media that dozens of firefighters were summoned to confront the blaze. Flames were seen shooting through the building's roof, fire officials said.
The fire was eventually deemed to be under control at around 2:45 p.m.
