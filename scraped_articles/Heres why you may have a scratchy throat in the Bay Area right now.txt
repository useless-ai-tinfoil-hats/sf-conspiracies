Here's why you may have a scratchy throat in the Bay Area right now
-----
If you woke up with a scratchy throat in the Bay Area this morning, smoke in the air might be to blame.Poor air quality, likely the result of smoke from fireplaces and wood stoves, led officials to issue a Spare the Air alert on Tuesday. Residents are urged to stay indoors while the alert is in effect.
