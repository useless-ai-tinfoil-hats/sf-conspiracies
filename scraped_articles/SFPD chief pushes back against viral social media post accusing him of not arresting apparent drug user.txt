SFPD chief pushes back against viral social media post accusing him of not arresting apparent drug user
-----
San Francisco Police Chief Bill Scott pushed back against a viral social media post accusing him of not arresting an apparent drug user he encountered while patrolling the city Wednesday.The post came from photographer Thomas Hawk, who alleged a man was smoking fentanyl near a BART elevator on Market Street. Hawk said an unmarked SUV arrived and an SFPD officer talked with the man. Then, Hawk said, Scott exited the vehicle and watched the other officer talk to the man.
