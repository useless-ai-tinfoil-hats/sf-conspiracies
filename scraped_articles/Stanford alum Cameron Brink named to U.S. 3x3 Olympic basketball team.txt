Stanford alum Cameron Brink named to U.S. 3x3 Olympic basketball team
-----
Cameron Brink can add another accomplishment to her long list after she was named Wednesday to the U.S. Olympic 3x3 basketball team for the Paris Games this summer.The three-time All-American at Stanford has had success in the 3x3 version of the sport. Brink was the MVP of the 2023 FIBA World Cup in Vienna, Austria, where Team USA won a gold medal. She was second with 5.1 points per game and led the tournament with 39 rebounds and 10 blocks.
