Giants’ Nick Ahmed takes lead over Marco Luciano in shortstop battle
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — This was supposed to be Marco Luciano's time. He was finally going to bust out of the San Francisco Giants' farm system to secure not just a big-league roster position but the starting job at shortstop.President of baseball operations Farhan Zaidi said after last season that the Giants planned to give Luciano "the chance to be the everyday guy" in 2024, the heir apparent to four-time Gold Glover Brandon Crawford.
