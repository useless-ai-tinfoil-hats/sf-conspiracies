SFPD increases reward for Mission District's double fatal shooting in 2016
-----
The San Francisco Police Department has increased its reward for information over the 2016 fatal shooting of two unhoused people in the Mission District to $200,000. In a news release, issued Thursday, the department included a sketch of a person of interest in the investigation drawn in 2019 and asked the public for help in identifying the individual.The shootings happened on Dec. 16, 2016, on the corner of 16th Street and South Van Ness Avenue at 8:46 p.m., police said.
