Celebrity favorite sushi spot opens speakeasy-style restaurant in the Bay Area
-----
A posh Los Angeles-based restaurant known for its A-list celebrity following has opened its first Bay Area outpost.Michelin-recognized Sushi by Scratch Restaurants, which is part of the Scratch Restaurants Group family, debuted at The Matheson restaurant in Healdsburg on Dec. 1. Like its other California outposts, the Bay Area Sushi by Scratch location evokes a speakeasy, tucked within a private room inside The Matheson.
