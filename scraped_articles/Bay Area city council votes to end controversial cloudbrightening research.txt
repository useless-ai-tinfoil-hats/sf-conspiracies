Bay Area city council votes to end controversial cloud-brightening research
-----
Alameda's city council voted this week to permanently halt research being conducted onboard one of the city's decommissioned naval ships.The project, led by the University of Washington, involved scientists using specialized machines to spray sea salt particles into the air above the USS Hornet.
