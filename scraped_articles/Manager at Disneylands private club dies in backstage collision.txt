Manager at Disneyland's private club dies in backstage collision
-----
An employee at Disneyland's exclusive private club died Friday after a backstage collision two days prior.On Wednesday, Bonnye Mavis Lear, 60, of Fullerton was riding in a golf cart in the backstage area of Disneyland Park when she was thrown from the moving vehicle, Anaheim police said in a statement. Lear hit her head, suffering "grave" injuries, according to police. Anaheim fire personnel and police arrived at the scene around 11:30 a.m., and Lear was rushed to the hospital. She died
