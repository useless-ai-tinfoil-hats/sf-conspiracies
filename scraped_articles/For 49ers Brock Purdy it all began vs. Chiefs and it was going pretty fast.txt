For 49ers’ Brock Purdy it all began vs. Chiefs and ‘it was going pretty fast’
-----
LAS VEGAS – The situations couldn't be much different, but the opponent is the same.On Sunday, San Francisco 49ers quarterback Brock Purdy, an NFL MVP finalist, will play against the Chiefs in Super Bowl LVIII, 476 days after he threw his first NFL passes in garbage time against Kansas City as an anonymous rookie seventh-round pick.
Purdy recalled his emotions Wednesday when he was asked about replacing starter Jimmy Garoppolo with 3:39 left and the 49ers trailing the Chiefs 44-23
