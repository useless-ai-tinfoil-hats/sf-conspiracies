Ex-rock musician arrested in killing of girlfriend who was last seen in Santa Cruz
-----
Capitola police said they arrested an ex-rock musician suspected of killing his girlfriend, who went missing last month.El Cerrito resident Theobald "Theo" Lengyel, 54, was taken into custody by authorities and booked into Santa Cruz County Jail on Tuesday. Lengyel played saxophone, clarinet and keyboards in the rock group Mr. Bungle.
