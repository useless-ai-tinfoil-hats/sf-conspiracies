Two injured in shooting near West Oakland BART Station
-----
Two people were injured in a shooting near the West Oakland BART Station on Friday evening, according to Oakland police.Police said the shooting happened in the 700 block of Center Street — across the street from West Oakland Station — sometime before 5 p.m. Friday. Investigators learned of the shooting after two people arrived separately at a local hospital with gunshot wounds, according to the Police Department.
Both victims were in stable condition as of Friday night, officials
