Woman who went missing on hike in California found dead
-----
A 66-year-old Michigan woman who went missing while hiking in California's Sierra Nevada was found dead, officials said Thursday.The body of Ann Marie Herford was located amid dense brush and under a thick tree canopy on a steep slope off the Arnold Rim Trail near the community of Arnold, according to a news release from the Calaveras County Sheriff's Office.
