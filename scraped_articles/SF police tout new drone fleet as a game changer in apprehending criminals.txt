SF police tout new drone fleet as a 'game changer' in apprehending criminals
-----
The San Francisco Police Department is touting its fleet of six new surveillance drones as a "game changer" in terms of apprehending criminals.At a press conference on Tuesday, police officials showed footage shot by drones as they were used to make arrests in two separate auto burglary cases, and to disperse a large crowd that was setting off fireworks in a residential neighborhood on the Fourth of July. Police described the examples as "success stories" of deploying drones for law
