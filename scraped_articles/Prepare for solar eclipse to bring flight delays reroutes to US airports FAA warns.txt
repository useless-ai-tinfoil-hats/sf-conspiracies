Prepare for solar eclipse to bring flight delays, reroutes to US airports, FAA warns
-----
The total solar eclipse set to make its way across a swath of North America is likely to cause flight delays, reroutes and holds on April 8, the Federal Aviation Administration warned.Special air traffic procedures will be in place from April 7-10, the FAA said in a notice to aviators, with the bulk of the impacts happening on the day of the eclipse.
