Where the locals eat in one of California's wealthiest coastal towns

Given the world-class golf and bougie vibes, it's not exactly my go-to beach town. But on a recent weekend, I made the drive south on Interstate 280, and up and over the curvy Highway 17, to explore another side of one of the wealthiest resort towns along the coast of California, a place that calls to celebrities like Taylor Swift.
