Park Fire grows to 5th largest wildfire in California history
-----
California's Park Fire had swelled to 383,619 acres as of Tuesday morning, making it the fifth-largest wildfire in Golden State history, according to Cal Fire.Containment on the fire burning in Butte and Tehama counties has increased from 12% to 14% and more evacuations were lifted Monday, but the fire fight is long from over as many areas around the blaze's perimeter are burning actively and spot fires continue to present challenges, the agency said.
