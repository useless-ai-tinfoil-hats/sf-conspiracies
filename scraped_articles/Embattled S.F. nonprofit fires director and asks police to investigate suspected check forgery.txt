Embattled S.F. nonprofit fires director and asks police to investigate suspected check forgery
-----
UPDATE: Fired director of S.F. nonprofit headed East Bay group accused of submitting bogus invoicesTroubled crime prevention nonprofit San Francisco SAFE has fired its executive director and temporarily halted operations after finding a lack of funds in the organization's bank accounts and suspecting check forgery, two officials with the nonprofit told the Chronicle.
The officials also said the nonprofit notified the fraud unit of the San Francisco Police Department.
