NIMBY pushback isn’t the only obstacle to San Francisco’s housing plans
-----
In an ideal world, San Francisco will fulfill its state mandate to add 82,000 housing units by 2031. It will do so by living up to the city's "housing element" plan by enacting zoning changes the Planning Department is weighing to allow for more residential density along transit corridors, including in low-slung areas such as the Richmond and Sunset districts, Cow Hollow and the Marina.
