CBS Super Bowl promo reveals 49ers star's deep knowledge of "SpongeBob"
-----
It's long been known how goofy San Francisco 49ers star tight end George Kittle can be, even while he's in the middle of a game. And with this Super Bowl being the first airing on Nickelodeon, Kittle was destined to be the perfect guy to help promote the alternate broadcast.CBS shared a video on TikTok over the weekend from a promotional shoot in which Kittle is standing in front of a "SpongeBob Squarepants"-inspired backdrop.
