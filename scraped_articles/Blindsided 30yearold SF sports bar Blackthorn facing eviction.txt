‘Blindsided’: 30-year-old SF sports bar Blackthorn facing eviction
-----
After 30 years in the Inner Sunset, neighborhood sports bar Blackthorn is facing eviction, shortly after the pub's owners paid for extensive renovations to the space they've leased for over a decade.The bar's landlord, McGrath Enterprises, filed a complaint to the San Francisco Superior Court on Oct. 3, 2023, alleging that Blackthorn's owners had been occupying the space at 834 Irving St. illegally since their rental agreement ended on Aug. 31, 2023.
