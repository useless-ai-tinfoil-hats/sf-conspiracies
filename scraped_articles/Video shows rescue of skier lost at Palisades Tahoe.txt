Video shows rescue of skier lost at Palisades Tahoe
-----
Placer County Sheriff's Office officials released a video Thursday documenting the rescue of a skier lost at a popular Lake Tahoe resort.Sheriff's officials deployed their Falcon 30 — the department's helicopter — after a skier who had gotten lost at Palisades Tahoe was able to use their limited phone reception to contact the office for help, spokespersons said.
A video posted by officials of the rescue shows the skier waving their ski poles in the air to flag down help in a rescue
