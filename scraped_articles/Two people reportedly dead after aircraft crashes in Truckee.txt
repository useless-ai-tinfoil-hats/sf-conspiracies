Two people reportedly dead after aircraft crashes in Truckee
-----
UPDATE March 30, 9:06 p.m. At least two people are dead after an aircraft crashed in Truckee, according to CBS Sacramento.-----
The Truckee Fire Protection District said it is responding to the scene of a downed aircraft Saturday evening.
The crash occurred between the Truckee River and nearby railroad tracks in the Olympic Heights subdivision, Truckee Fire said on X, formerly Twitter.
