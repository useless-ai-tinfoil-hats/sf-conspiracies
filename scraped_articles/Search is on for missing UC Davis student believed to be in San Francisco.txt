Search is on for missing UC Davis student believed to be in San Francisco
-----
UC Davis police are seeking the public's help in locating a missing student seen last week in San Francisco.University police said Tuesday that Diane Shoyeon Min, 18, left the campus Friday evening and took an Amtrak train to Mission Street in San Francisco, where she was last seen. Min, a freshman computer engineering major, was reported missing Monday evening.
