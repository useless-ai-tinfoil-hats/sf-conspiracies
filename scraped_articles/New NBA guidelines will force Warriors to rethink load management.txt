New NBA guidelines will force Warriors to rethink load management
-----
The NBA's Board of Governors has taken its fight against perceived unnecessary player rest a step further when it voted Wednesday to approve a policy that would, among other scenarios, prevent teams from resting two star players in the same game, the league announced.This comes after the NBA ratified in April a policy that players must participate in 65 regular-season games to be eligible for postseason awards as the league pushes back against the load-management trend.
