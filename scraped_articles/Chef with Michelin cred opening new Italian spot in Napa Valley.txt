Chef with Michelin cred opening new Italian spot in Napa Valley
-----
St. Helena's bid to become Wine Country's most exciting culinary destination will get another boost in May with the arrival of Violetto, a tasting menu spot from a chef who has worked at major Michelin-starred restaurants.Set inside a grand 1907 mansion a few minutes north of downtown St. Helena, Violetto will weave northern Italy and southern France into refined dishes like truffle-stuffed hen and braised Wagyu beef cheek.
