Christian McCaffrey hurdles his way toward the end zone on day he sets 49ers record
-----
Christian McCaffrey didn't settle for just setting a San Francisco 49ers franchise mark. He added a viral highlight on what's proving to be a big scoring day for him.With a 1-yard plunge early in the first quarter of Sunday's game against the Arizona Cardinals at Levi's Stadium, the running back scored a touchdown in 13 consecutive games, including the playoffs, breaking the mark set by Hall of Fame wide receiver Jerry Rice in 1987.
