New data shows it’s still one of the best times to rent in the Bay Area
-----
When rental prices dropped dramatically across the Bay Area during the pandemic, it was a welcome reprieve for renters. But that decline in prices has stayed stable much longer than many predicted — and it still may be one of the best times to rent in the Bay Area in years.A new report from the rental website Apartment List shows that rents in San Francisco were down more than 4% in August 2023, compared with the same time last year.
