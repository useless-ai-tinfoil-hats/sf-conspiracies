Massive fire contained at Oakland lumberyard — ‘hot spots’ remain, officials say
-----
More than 80 firefighters contained a massive blaze that erupted Sunday night at a lumber warehouse near Interstate 880 in Oakland, officials said Monday morning.The four-alarm fire broke out around 8 p.m. Sunday at Economy Lumber Co. on the 700 block of High Street, said Oakland Fire Department spokesperson Michael Hunt. The warehouse is a few blocks from the High Street homeless encampment, but it appeared that no one was living in the area of the fire, Hunt said.
