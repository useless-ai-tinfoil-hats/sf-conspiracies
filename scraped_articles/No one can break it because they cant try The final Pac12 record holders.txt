The Pac-12 as we know it is about to be no more.This week's baseball tournament marks the final Pac-12 event before 10 of the 12 schools depart the West Coast's legacy college sports league. The two remaining schools, Oregon State and Washington State, are technically still conference members and will try to keep it alive, even hiring a commissioner, Teresa Gould, to try to help them through these turbulent times in college sports.
