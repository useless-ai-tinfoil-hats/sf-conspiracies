Fatal shooting near S.F.’s Civic Center — possible suspect detained
-----
A person was fatally shot Friday night near San Francisco's Civic Center, police said.Update: Suspect arrested, ID'd in fatal shooting near S.F.'s Civic Center
Officers responded to reports of a shooting around 7:15 p.m. on the 1200 block of Mission Street in the South of Market area, where they found the victim, investigators said. Police on the scene performed CPR before paramedics transported the person to the hospital, where the victim was declared dead.
