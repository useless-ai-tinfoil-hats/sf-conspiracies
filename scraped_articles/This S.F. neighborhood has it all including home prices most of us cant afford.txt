This S.F. neighborhood has it all, including home prices most of us can't afford
-----
Noe Valley is a classic San Francisco neighborhood at its best — colorful, safe, an interesting mix of old and new. It has the best weather in the city and an optimistic spirit. No doom loop here. "It's a village inside a city," said Cathie Staley, who lives in Noe Valley and leads walking tours.However, Noe Valley also has a classic San Francisco problem — housing prices put it out of reach for most San Franciscans.
