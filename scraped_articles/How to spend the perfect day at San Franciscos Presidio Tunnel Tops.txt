How to spend the perfect day at San Francisco's Presidio Tunnel Tops
-----
There aren't many parks built on top of freeway tunnels, but then again, there aren't too many parks like the Presidio, a sprawling oasis just a stone's throw from downtown San Francisco.In 2022, the Presidio added one more reason to visit: Presidio Tunnel Tops, a 14-acre park-within-a-park located directly above Highway 101.
