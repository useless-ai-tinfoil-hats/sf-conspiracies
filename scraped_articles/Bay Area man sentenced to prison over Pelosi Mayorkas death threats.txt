Bay Area man sentenced to prison over Pelosi, Mayorkas death threats
-----
A Bay Area man has been sentenced to 11 months in federal prison for making voice-mail threats against then-House Speaker Nancy Pelosi and Homeland Security Secretary Alejandro Mayorkas, prosecutors said.U.S. District Judge William Alsup issued the sentence Tuesday despite recommendations from both the prosecution and defense of a term of four years probation with no prison time for Daniel Carrier, who had pleaded guilty to two felony charges in December.
