Paul Pelosi faces alleged attacker in S.F. courtroom as David DePape trial continues
-----
Paul Pelosi, husband of House Speaker Emerita Nancy Pelosi, testified Friday about what was going through his mind in the moments before he was attacked by an intruder — who is now facing attempted murder charges — on an October night in 2022.In his testimony, which was attended by a slew of reporters and members of the public, Paul Pelosi spoke of being in a "panic mode" when he attempted to to appease the "large man" who had broken into his home and woke him to demand: "Where's Nancy?"
