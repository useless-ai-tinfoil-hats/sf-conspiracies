Legacy Calif. business See's Candies is an uncanny anchor for a new Bay Area food trail
-----
Oakland's Rockridge district is synonymous with great food, from breakfast sandwiches and kouign-amann to the finest gourmet pasta. Lately, it's also becoming a destination for chocolate. The 1-mile stretch of College Avenue from Broadway to Ashby is peppered with no fewer than five confectioneries.Plunked in the center sits an old-school See's Candies shop.
