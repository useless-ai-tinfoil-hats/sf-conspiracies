U.S. Olympian, Bay Area swim coach died by drowning, autopsy finds
-----
The investigation into the death of famed U.S. Olympic swimmer Richard Thornton concluded that he died by drowning at a Santa Cruz beach earlier this month, the Santa Cruz County Sheriff's Office said Wednesday.Thornton's death was accidental, a "seawater drowning," said Ashley Keehn, a spokesperson for the Santa Cruz County Sheriff's Office, which oversees the coroner's office that conducted the autopsy on Thornton, 65.
