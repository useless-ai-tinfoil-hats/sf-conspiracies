'Pirates of the Caribbean' actor killed in Hawaii shark attack
-----
Veteran Hawaii lifeguard, surfer and occasional actor Tamayo Perry, 49, has died after a shark attack on Oahu's North Shore, authorities say.Perry died on Sunday shortly before 1 p.m. after being bitten by a shark while surfing near Goat Island, Shayne Enright of the Honolulu Emergency Services Department said at a press conference. First responders from the Honolulu Ocean Safety team pulled Perry in via jet ski. He was pronounced dead on the shore.
