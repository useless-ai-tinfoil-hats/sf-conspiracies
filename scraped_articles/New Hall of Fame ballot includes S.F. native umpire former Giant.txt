New Hall of Fame ballot includes S.F. native umpire, former Giant
-----
The Hall of Fame announced its latest Contemporary Baseball Era Committee ballot that considers managers, executives and umpires. No players, so Barry Bonds wasn't eligible this time.The eight-person ballot, announced Thursday, includes umpire Eddie Montague, a San Francisco native, and Bill White, a former New York and San Francisco Giant who's being considered as an executive.
