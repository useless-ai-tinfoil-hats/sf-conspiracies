Cameron Brink settles in as L.A.’s newest star, shining with WNBA’s Sparks
-----
There's a new star on the showy Los Angeles sports scene. Tara VanDerveer will be happy to know it's one of her own.With her high-fashion sense, outgoing nature and unlimited marketability, Cameron Brink has found an ideal place to land — the WNBA's L.A. Sparks — after being the league's second overall pick out of Stanford.
In a recent e-mail, the recently retired VanDerveer said "this is a very exciting time for women's basketball" and took note of Brink making an immediate impact
