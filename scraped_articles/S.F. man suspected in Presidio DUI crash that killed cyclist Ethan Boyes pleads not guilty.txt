S.F. man suspected in Presidio DUI crash that killed cyclist Ethan Boyes pleads not guilty
-----
The San Francisco man accused of striking and killing USA Cycling champion Ethan Boyes while allegedly drunk driving in April pleaded not guilty during his first appearance in federal court on Wednesday.Arnold Kinman Low, 81, is facing one count of involuntary manslaughter and one count of driving under the influence of alcohol in the fatal crash that killed Boyes as he was cycling near the Presidio's Inspiration Point the afternoon of April 4.
