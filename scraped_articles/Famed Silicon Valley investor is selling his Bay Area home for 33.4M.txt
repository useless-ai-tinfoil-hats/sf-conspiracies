Famed Silicon Valley investor is selling his Bay Area home for $33.4M
-----
One of Silicon Valley's most well-known investors is selling his estate in the swanky town of Atherton for upward of $30 million.Marc Andreessen and his wife, Laura Arrillaga-Andreessen, put their property up for sale Monday, according to the Wall Street Journal. The five-bedroom home, which sits on 1.55 acres and is described in its listing as "one of Atherton's most exceptional estates," last sold in 2007 for $16.6 million, according to property records.
