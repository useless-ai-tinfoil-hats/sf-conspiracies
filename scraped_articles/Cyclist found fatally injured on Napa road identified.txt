Cyclist found fatally injured on Napa road identified
-----
A bicyclist found fatally injured on a Napa road Tuesday night has been identified as Ignacio Alfaro, 45.A driver spotted Alfaro around 8:40 p.m. on the ground with serious injuries, officials said. Alfaro was with his bike on the south side of Wine Country Avenue between Dumas Street and Emperor Way.
Napa Fire Department and American Medical Response personnel arrived at the scene and took Alfaro to Queen of the Valley Medical Center, where he later died, officials said.
