Lightning strike obliterates Northern California home during storm
-----
A mobile home exploded and caught fire in Northern California after it was struck by lightning over the weekend, officials said.The explosion occurred at approximately 7:30 p.m. Saturday in Newcastle, David Smith, a spokesperson for the Placer County Sheriff's Office, told SFGATE. Newcastle is about 30 miles northeast of Sacramento on Interstate 80.
