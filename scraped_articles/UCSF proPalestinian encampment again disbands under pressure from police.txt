UCSF pro-Palestinian encampment again disbands under pressure from police
-----
Pro-Palestinian demonstrators at UCSF's Parnassus campus disbanded Saturday morning after being asked by police to leave the area, the university confirmed."This morning, we closed the encampment on our Parnassus Heights campus. We gave the individuals who were present the opportunity to leave the site with their personal possessions, and they disbanded peacefully," a university spokesperson said in a statement.
