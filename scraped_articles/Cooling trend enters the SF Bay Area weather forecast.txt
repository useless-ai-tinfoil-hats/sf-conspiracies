Cooling trend enters the SF Bay Area weather forecast
-----
With more clouds on the coast, cooler temperatures are forecast across the San Francisco Bay Area through the weekend and into early next week, forecasters said. Inland areas that have recorded afternoon temperatures in the high 80s to 90s in recent days are expected to see the mercury drop into the 70s and 80s."This isn't a drastic cooling trend by any means," said Brayden Murdock, a forecaster with the National Weather Service's Bay Area office in Monterey.
