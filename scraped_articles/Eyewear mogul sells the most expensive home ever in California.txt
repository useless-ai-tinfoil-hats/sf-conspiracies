Eyewear mogul sells the most expensive home ever in California
-----
The priciest home ever sold in California was just purchased for $210 million. And the mansion wasn't owned by a tech magnate or high-profile celebrity.James Jannard, the billionaire founder of the popular glasses and sportswear brand Oakley, offloaded his Malibu home in one of the biggest real estate deals of the year. The news was first reported by the Los Angeles Times.
