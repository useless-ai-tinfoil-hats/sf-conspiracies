The world’s first Ohlone restaurant closed after a one-year pilot. Now it’s coming back
-----
When the world's first Ohlone restaurant opened at the UC Berkeley campus to much excitement last summer, its presence there was momentous, but temporary.Cafe Ohlone closed in May at the end of its one-year pilot serving food in a courtyard outside the Phoebe A. Hearst Museum of Anthropology. But now, under a new agreement with the university, it will return as a permanent space with broader, campuswide reach.
