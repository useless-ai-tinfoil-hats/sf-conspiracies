Persistent smoke in Bay Area leads to Spare the Air extension to Saturday
-----
A Spare the Air alert has been extended through Saturday as wildfire smoke stubbornly lingers in the Bay Area.The extension of the alert indicates that air quality officials expect at least some smoke — whose behavior can be hard to forecast — to remain in the region into Saturday.
