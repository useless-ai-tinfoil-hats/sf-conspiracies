Space Force plan for sacred Hawaii volcano sparks opposition from locals
-----
Rising above the clouds over 10,000 feet, the summit of Haleakala volcano on the Hawaiian island of Maui is widely known for its otherworldly views, enhanced even more at sunrise and sunset. About 1 million people visit Haleakala National Park every year to see its natural wonders.Besides the national park, Haleakala is a place where scientists observe the sky.
