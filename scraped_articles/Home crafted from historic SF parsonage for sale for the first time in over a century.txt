Home crafted from historic SF parsonage for sale for the first time in over a century
-----
San Francisco's Swedenborgian Church was established in 1895, an iconic structure from its inception. The parsonage next door at 2121 Lyon Street was created by the same architects, though it was later separated from the church property and transformed into a private residence.
