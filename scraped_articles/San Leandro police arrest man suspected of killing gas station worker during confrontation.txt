San Leandro police arrest man suspected of killing gas station worker during confrontation
-----
A man was arrested on suspicion of murder in the death of a 78-year-old San Leandro gas station employee who was allegedly shoved into a pole during a confrontation last month, police said Monday.The employee, Wadih Badran, died Feb. 22, at the Speedway gas station on East 14th Street near Bancroft Avenue after he and another man who was loitering behind the business got into an argument, police said.
