World’s largest rainbow flag is illuminating S.F.’s Market Street. Here’s how to see it
-----
The world's largest Pride flag, a 4.1-mile laser spectacular, has returned to San Francisco to mark Pride Weekend.Illuminate, the organization that lit up the Bay Bridge for 10 years with the "Bay Lights" installation, first beamed the laser flag up Market Street from the Embarcadero to Twin Peaks during a ceremony Friday featuring music, dancing and dignitaries, including Mayor London Breed, state Sen. Scott Wiener and longtime AIDS activist Cleve Jones.
