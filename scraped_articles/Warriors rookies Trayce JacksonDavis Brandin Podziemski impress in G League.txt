Warriors rookies Trayce Jackson-Davis, Brandin Podziemski impress in G League
-----
Golden State Warriors rookies Trayce Jackson-Davis and Brandin Podziemski impressed in their first G League assignment, helping Santa Cruz to a season-opening 125-114 defeat of the Stockton Kings on Friday.Jackson-Davis, the 57th pick who was acquired by the Warriors on draft night, finished with a game-high 28 points on 11-for-13 shooting and 12 rebounds with head coach Steve Kerr in attendance. Podziemski, drafted 19th overall, added 24 points and made 10 of his 18 attempts.
