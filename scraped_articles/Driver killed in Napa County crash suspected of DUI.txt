Driver killed in Napa County crash suspected of DUI
-----
A driver police suspect was intoxicated crashed into a utility pole and died in Napa County early Sunday.The driver, identified as a 37-year-old Calistoga man, was killed shortly before 1 a.m. while driving a 2005 Honda north of St. Helena on Highway 29, California Highway Patrol spokesperson Vince Pompliano said in a statement.
Investigators suspect that the driver may have been under the influence of drugs or alcohol at the time of the crash, Pompliano said.
