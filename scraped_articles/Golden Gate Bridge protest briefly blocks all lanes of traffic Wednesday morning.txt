Golden Gate Bridge protest briefly blocks all lanes of traffic Wednesday morning
-----
A protest on the Golden Gate Bridge Wednesday morning briefly blocked all lanes.California Highway Patrol's traffic incident page reported at 7:44 a.m., vehicles stopped on the bridge and people began blocking all lanes. By 8 a.m., all northbound and southbound traffic was at a standstill, but around 8:20 a.m., northbound traffic was moving once again. All lanes were back open shortly before 8:45 a.m.
