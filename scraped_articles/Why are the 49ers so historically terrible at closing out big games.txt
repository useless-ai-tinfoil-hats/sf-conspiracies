Why are the 49ers so historically terrible at closing out big games?
-----
After the San Francisco 49ers lost 10 games by five points or fewer in their first two non-playoff seasons under Kyle Shanahan and John Lynch, the head coach and general manager agreed they wanted players who were "finishers."They added it to their vision statement before the 2019 season, went 13-3, reached Super Bowl LIV and then couldn't close: They became the third team in NFL history to lose a double-digit fourth-quarter lead in a Super Bowl.
