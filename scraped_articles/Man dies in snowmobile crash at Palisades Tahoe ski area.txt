Man dies in snowmobile crash at Palisades Tahoe ski area
-----
A man died after a snowmobile crash Thursday morning at the Alpine Meadows ski area in North Lake Tahoe, according to the California Highway Patrol.The CHP received a call at 7:30 a.m. that an employee at Palisades Tahoe, which owns and operates Alpine Meadows, had been found at 6:30 a.m. at the edge of the ski area where his snowmobile had collided with a rock that was partly covered in snow, according to the agency.
