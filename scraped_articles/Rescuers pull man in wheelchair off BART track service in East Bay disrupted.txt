Rescuers pull man in wheelchair off BART track, service in East Bay disrupted
-----
BART service on the Richmond line came to a halt Monday afternoon due to a "major medical emergency" at North Berkeley Station after a man in a wheelchair ended up on the tracks.Jim Allison, BART's media relations manager, said the incident began around 3:30 p.m. when a man in a wheelchair entered the trackway.
