Gavin Newsom says 'it's true' that San Francisco got a facelift ahead of APEC
-----
California Gov. Gavin Newsom recently confirmed a fact that, for anyone paying attention, really didn't need much confirmation: San Francisco underwent a serious facelift ahead of the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) summit.At a press conference last week alongside San Francisco Mayor London Breed, Newsom talked about some of the struggles facing San Francisco's downtown. The two were gathered at what his office described in a news release as a "previously vacant, blighted lot"
