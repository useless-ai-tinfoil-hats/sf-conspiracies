Remember the 50-story Sunset skyscraper? Shenanigans by S.F. supes all but ensure it gets built
-----
For much of 2023, a leviathan loomed over San Francisco's Outer Sunset neighborhood in the form of a 55-story, 714-unit development residential tower proposed for 2700 Sloat Boulevard, just a few blocks from the ocean. After renderings of the giant development were made public, neighbors revolted. The city said the project didn't meet planning codes.
