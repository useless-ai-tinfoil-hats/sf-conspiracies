Photos: Hiero Day 2023 celebrates 50th anniversary of hip-hop
-----
Hundreds of music lovers headed to Frank Ogawa Plaza in Oakland on Monday, Sept. 4, for the 11th edition of Hiero Day, ready to bob their heads and get hyphy.Thankfully the weather cooperated, providing warm temperatures and sunny skies on Labor Day as this year's event commemorated the 50th anniversary of hip-hop as well as the 30th anniversary of the Oakland group Souls of Mischief, part of the collective rap Hieroglyphics, which founded Hiero Day in 2012.
