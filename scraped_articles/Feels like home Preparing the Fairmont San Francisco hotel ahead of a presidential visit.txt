'Feels like home': Preparing the Fairmont San Francisco hotel ahead of a presidential visit
-----
The Fairmont San Francisco hotel in the Nob Hill neighborhood has been dubbed the "White House of the West."It's a fitting name, considering nearly every living president has stayed there at one point. That includes President Joe Biden, who, as well as Vice President Kamala Harris, stayed at the Fairmont while they were in San Francisco last week for the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) summit.
