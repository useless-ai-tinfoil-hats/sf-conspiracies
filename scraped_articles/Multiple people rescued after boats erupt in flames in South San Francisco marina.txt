Multiple people rescued after boats erupt in flames in South San Francisco marina
-----
Several people were rescued from the waters of Oyster Cove Marina in South San Francisco after three boats caught fire Wednesday morning, fire officials said.Firefighters responded to numerous reports of multiple boats on fire at the Oyster Cove Marina just before 7:30 a.m., said South San Francisco Deputy Fire Chief Matt Samson.
Three boats were on fire and approximately four people were rescued from the water, Samson said. One person was taken to a hospital for minor injuries.
