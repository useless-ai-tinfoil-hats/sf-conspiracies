'Mean Girls' 2024: Where to watch the musical movie
-----
For those of us born in the late '80s and early '90s, the original "Mean Girls" movie, released in 2004, caught us at the start or in the middle of our soft and vulnerable pre-teen and teen years — and became, in a way, a canon text about friendship, drama and hyperbolically toxic cliques. Now, two decades later, the film's gotten a remake — and it's at theaters starting today.Here's everything we know so far about the film's plot, as well as where it'll be available to stream.
