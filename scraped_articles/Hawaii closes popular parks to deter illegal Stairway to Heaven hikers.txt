Hawaii closes popular parks to deter illegal 'Stairway to Heaven' hikers
-----
As permanent removal of the controversial Haiku Stairs on the island of Oahu progresses, the state of Hawaii and the city of Honolulu are shutting down a section of a Honolulu forest reserve and a neighborhood park in an effort to deter hikers from reaching the stairs.Hawaii's Department of Land and Natural Resources (DLNR) previously shut down the "Middle Ridge" area of the Moanalua section of the Honolulu Watershed Reserve for 90 days for the demolition of the notorious stairway.
