Cal men win for fourth time, surpass last season's total in 11 games
-----
It might be measured in baby steps, but the Cal men's basketball team is making progress.Fardaws Aimaq had 19 points and 10 rebounds, Jalen Cone scored 15 points on five 3-pointers and Cal had a 26-point lead erased before beating UC San Diego 71-67 at Haas Pavilion on Wednesday.
The victory improved the Bears, who had lost six of seven, to 4-7. Last season, Cal was an all-time program-worst 3-29.
Cal will open Pac-12 play against No. 4 Arizona on Dec. 29.
