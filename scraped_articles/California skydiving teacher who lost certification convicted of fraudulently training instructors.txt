California skydiving teacher who lost certification convicted of fraudulently training instructors
-----
A San Joaquin County man has been convicted of wire fraud and aggravated identity theft for fraudulently training skydiving instructors after a former student fell to his death, federal authorities announced.Robert Allen Pooley, 49, trained prospective skydiving instructors in Acampo, north of Stockton, after receiving official ratings and certifications to do so in 2010, the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Eastern District of California said Friday.
