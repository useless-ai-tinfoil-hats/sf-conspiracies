San Mateo officials: Husband killed wife in murder-suicide
-----
San Mateo officials say they determined that the father of twins killed his wife before taking his own life in a murder-suicide earlier this month that also included the death of the boys.Anand Henry, 37, suffered from a single gunshot, while Alice Benziger, his 38-year-old wife, suffered from multiple gunshot wounds. The bodies of the couple and their 4-year-old sons were discovered Feb. 12, when police were called to check on the wellbeing of the family.
