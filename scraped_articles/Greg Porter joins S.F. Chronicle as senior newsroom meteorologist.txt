Greg Porter joins S.F. Chronicle as senior newsroom meteorologist
-----
From Editor of Emerging Product Sarah Feldberg:The Chronicle is pleased to announce that Greg Porter has accepted a position as senior newsroom meteorologist.
In his new role, Porter will join the Chronicle's Weather Science team, which launched in 2022 and quickly established itself as a vital part of the newsroom and a favorite of Chronicle readers. He'll report to Weather Science Editor Hannah Hagemann.
