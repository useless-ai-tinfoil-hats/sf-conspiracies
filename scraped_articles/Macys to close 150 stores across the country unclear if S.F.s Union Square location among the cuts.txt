Macy’s to close 150 stores across the country, unclear if S.F.’s Union Square location among the cuts
-----
Macy's will be closing 150 stores, including 50 this year, the company said Tuesday during an earnings call. As of now, it has not been announced if the company's massive flagship store in San Francisco's Union Square will be among the closures.Representatives of Macy's investor relations team told the Chronicle that the company would not be releasing the list of stores slated for closures at this time.
