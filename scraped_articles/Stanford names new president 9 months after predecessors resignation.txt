Stanford names new president 9 months after predecessor’s resignation
-----
Stanford University announced Thursday that it has chosen the dean of its business school — an economist and member of President Joe Biden's Council of Advisors on Science and Technology — as its 13th president, ending a seven-month search for a permanent head to replace Marc Tessier-Lavigne, who resigned in July.Jonathan Levin, 51, will take over for interim President Richard Saller on Aug. 1.
A Stanford alumnus, Levin has led the School of Business since 2016.
