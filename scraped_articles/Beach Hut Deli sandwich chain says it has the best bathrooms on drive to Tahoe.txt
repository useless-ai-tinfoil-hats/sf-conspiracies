Beach Hut Deli sandwich chain says it has the best bathrooms on drive to Tahoe
-----
I've probably made the drive from the San Francisco Bay Area to Lake Tahoe more than a hundred times in my life. Two things I'll never turn down on the drive up: tasty sandwiches and clean bathrooms.Beach Hut Deli takes equal pride in both.
A surf-themed chain of sandwich shops, Beach Hut has dozens of locations across the state, but its delis in the foothills off Interstate 80 and U.S. 50 make for perfect stops en route to the Sierra Nevada.
