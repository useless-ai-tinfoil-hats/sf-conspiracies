These national parks require a reservation this summer
-----
If you're headed to one of America's many great national parks this summer, it's a good time to make a checklist of what you might need. For an increasing number of parks, a reservation is now just as essential as water, sunscreen and bug spray.As visitors to national parks skyrocketed during the pandemic, some parks added day-use reservations to better control crowds.
