Jogger killed in East Bay hit-and-run, DUI suspect turns himself in, police say
-----
A jogger was struck and killed by a hit-and-run driver Sunday night in Fremont, according to police, and a suspect was arrested soon after the incident.According to police, the pedestrian, whose name and other identifying information have not yet been released, was jogging northbound along Mission Boulevard near Nursery Avenue about 8:40 p.m. when a driver crossed the curb line and struck the pedestrian.
