‘Inspired choice’ for A’s temp home? Sacramento bigger, better than Las Vegas
-----
Sacramento, known best in the baseball world as the cradle of big-league managers, is heating up as a potential market for the sport.The Oakland Athletics, searching for a home for the 2025-27 seasons — and maybe more — have investigated Sutter Health Park. That's where the San Francisco Giants' Triple-A affiliate, the River Cats, play. On Tuesday, the independent Pioneer League continued its West Coast expansion by adding a team in Davis, the Yolo High Wheelers.
