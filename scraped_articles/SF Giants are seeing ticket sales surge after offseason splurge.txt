SF Giants are seeing ticket sales surge after offseason splurge
-----
With 10 days until their home opener, the San Francisco Giants say they're seeing a surge in ticket sales after the team finally delivered on landing some big-name free agents this offseason.Russ Stanley, the Giants senior vice president of ticket sales, told SFGATE on Tuesday that the Giants "will sell out" Opening Day at Oracle Park on April 5 against the Padres, even though tickets were still available for the game as of Tuesday.
