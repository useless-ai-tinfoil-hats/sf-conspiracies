For 49ers’ brass, it’s decision time on Brandon Aiyuk. Will they find their backbones?
-----
As the NFL's most perplexing contract standoff drags on, with Brandon Aiyuk's hold-in now in its fourth week, it's time for the San Francisco 49ers to make some decisions.Are CEO Jed York, general manager John Lynch and coach Kyle Shanahan willing to flex their power, stick to their convictions and live with the consequences? Or are they content to let a talented but non-transcendent player continue to hijack their summer and make them look toothless?
