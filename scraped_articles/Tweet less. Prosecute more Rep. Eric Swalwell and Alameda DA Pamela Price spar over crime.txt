‘Tweet less. Prosecute more’: Rep. Eric Swalwell and Alameda DA Pamela Price spar over crime
-----
An online feud has erupted between two East Bay Democrats, Rep. Eric Swalwell and Alameda County District Attorney Pamela Price. Swalwell criticized the embattled prosecutor as "soft on crime," and Price implied in a retort that his remarks were racist.The battle comes at a time when Price is politically vulnerable and could use all the help she can muster, particularly from an East Bay House member like Swalwell who regularly appears on national media.
