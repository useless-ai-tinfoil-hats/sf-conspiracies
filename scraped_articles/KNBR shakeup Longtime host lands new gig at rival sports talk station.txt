KNBR shakeup: Longtime host lands new gig at rival sports talk station
-----
Longtime KNBR host F.P. Santangelo, released from the once-preeminent Bay Area sports talk station last month, has landed a new radio gig.The radio personality and former MLB player took to social media Monday to reveal that he will join rival station 95.7 "The Game" as a part-time host.
