This top Bay Area coffee roaster is finally opening a cafe
-----
After almost 10 years of roasting without its own cafe, one of the Bay Area's most ubiquitous coffee companies will finally open a brick-and-mortar location of its own.Proyecto Diaz Coffee, known for its stand at the Ferry Plaza Farmers Market and its mission to support small-scale coffee farms in Mexico and Latin America, is opening its own space in West Oakland at 1416 20th St.
