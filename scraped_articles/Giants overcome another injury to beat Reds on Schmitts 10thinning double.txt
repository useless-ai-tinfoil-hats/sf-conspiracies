Giants overcome another injury to beat Reds on Schmitt's 10th-inning double
-----
With teammates dropping injured by the hour, the San Francisco Giants' youth movement has been unexpectedly thrust into action.On Mother's Day, the youth came through.
Casey Schmitt, who arrived in San Francisco on Friday for the injured shortstop Nick Ahmed, hit a walk-off ground-rule double in the 10th to lift the Giants to a 6-5 win over the Cincinnati Reds on Sunday afternoon.
