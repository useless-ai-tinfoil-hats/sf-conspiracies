Small aircraft lands on road outside North Bay Area airport
-----
A small aircraft carrying two people landed on a dirt road outside a Marin County airport Thursday after experiencing engine trouble.The Novato Fire District received a call around 1 p.m. about an "experimental aircraft" that went down on a road just south of Gnoss Field in Novato, said Jeff Whittet, battalion chief for the fire district.
