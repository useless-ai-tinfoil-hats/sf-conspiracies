Man convicted of murder in shooting at Mission District BART station
-----
A San Francisco man was convicted of first-degree murder and premeditated attempted murder in a fatal shooting that killed a man and injured another at BART'S 24th Street Mission Station, according to the district attorney's office.Keshon Wilson, 23, faces 82 years to life in prison after a jury convicted him on the charges.
