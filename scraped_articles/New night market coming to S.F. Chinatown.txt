New night market coming to S.F. Chinatown
-----
Another night market jam-packed with food and vendors is set to take over San Francisco's streets.A Chinatown night market is set to run along a two-block stretch of Grant Avenue on Nov. 10 and 11, just a few days before the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation forum comes to San Francisco. An S.F. Municipal Transit Authority temporary street closures committee agenda included an item for the event.
