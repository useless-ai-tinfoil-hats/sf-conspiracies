Oakland officer shoots, kills suspected gunman outside City Hall
-----
Oakland police are investigating an incident in which an officer shot and killed a man early Tuesday morning outside City Hall, spokespeople for the department say.A sergeant in the Police Department's Internal Affairs division drove up to City Hall at 250 Frank Ogawa Plaza to begin his shift at 4 a.m. Tuesday morning, according to Harry Stern, an attorney who represents the officer.
Stern said that a man police have not identified fired a pistol at the sergeant, who returned fire
