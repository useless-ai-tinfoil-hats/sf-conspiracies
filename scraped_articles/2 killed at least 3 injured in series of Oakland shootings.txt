2 killed, at least 3 injured in series of Oakland shootings
-----
Two people were killed and at least three others injured Friday and Saturday nights in four Oakland shootings, the city's Police Department said Saturday.Officers and paramedics first responded to a shooting on the 7300 block of Weld Street just after 8:30 p.m. Friday. One person was treated for gunshot wounds and transported to a hospital, where the victim was listed in stable condition Saturday.
