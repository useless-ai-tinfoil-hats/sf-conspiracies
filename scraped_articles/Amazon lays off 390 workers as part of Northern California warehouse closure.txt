Amazon lays off 390 workers as part of Northern California warehouse closure
-----
Amazon is laying off 390 workers as it closes a Stockton warehouse, but the e-commerce giant will open two new facilities in the same city.Workers at the Stockton Sort Center at 4601 Newcastle Drive will lose their jobs by July 30, according to state filings. An Amazon spokesperson said all of them would be offered opportunities to transfer to nearby facilities in Stockton, Tracy and Manteca.
