Police probe reported attack by 4 teens on Dublin High student
-----
A Dublin High School student was taken to a hospital Friday after four teenagers reportedly assaulted him, police said.Dublin police responded to Dublin High School around 3:30 p.m. Friday for a report of a fight on campus, police said Tuesday. When officers arrived, a male student, 15, was transported to the hospital for non-life-threatening injuries to his face and head. He was treated and released.
An investigation found that a Dublin High School parent confronted the student.
