Woman stabbed to death in San Francisco's Tenderloin district
-----
San Francisco police said Wednesday that a woman was stabbed to death on the 300 block of Leavenworth Street in the city's Tenderloin district on Monday afternoon.Paramedics advised police shortly before 7:20 p.m. that they were dispatched following a report that a woman was suffering from stab wounds.
The woman, who was not immediately identified, was hospitalized with life-threatening injuries and later pronounced dead at the hospital, police said.
