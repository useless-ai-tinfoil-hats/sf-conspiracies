Smoking, sparking outlet at S.F.'s Westfield mall prompts firefighter response
-----
San Francisco Fire Department officials said firefighters were dispatched Tuesday to the Westfield mall on Market Street, where an electrical lighting outlet was malfunctioning, throwing off sparks and smoke.Firefighters arrived at the mall shortly before 8 a.m., Fire Department officials said.
