Bay Area firefighters, one who appeared on 'Mythbusters', sue NASA claiming age discrimination
-----
Two career firefighters at NASA's Ames Research Center are suing the space agency for age discrimination, saying they were fired for not having associate degrees in fire science despite working at the Mountain View facility for decades.Cameron Gazaway and Robert Wilson, both 53, said in their lawsuit that in August 2022 they were told they needed to complete an associate degree in fire science within 45 days, despite having worked as firefighters at the agency's Moffett Federal Airfield
