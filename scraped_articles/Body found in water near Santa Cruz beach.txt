Body found in water near Santa Cruz beach
-----
A person was found dead in the water near a Santa Cruz beach Wednesday, authorities said.Around 4:30 p.m., Santa Cruz County sheriff's deputies, along with firefighters, responded to Moran Lake Beach for reports of an unresponsive person in the water, according to authorities. Upon arrival, first responders began attempting life-saving measures but the person was pronounced dead at the scene.
