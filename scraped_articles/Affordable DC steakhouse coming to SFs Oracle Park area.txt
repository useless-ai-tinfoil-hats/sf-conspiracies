Affordable DC steakhouse coming to SF's Oracle Park area
-----
Rejoice, SF Giants fans: An affordable, family-friendly restaurant known for steak is opening near Oracle Park in San Francisco's China Basin neighborhood.Medium Rare, a small steakhouse chain from the Washington, D.C. area, is bringing its famous $28.95 meal to California for the first time. As first reported by the San Francisco Standard, the restaurant has signed a lease for 266 King St., in the former Tender Greens, with plans to open in summer 2024.
