SF man fatally shot someone from Muni bus in drive-by, DA says
-----
A San Francisco man was charged Wednesday with fatally shooting someone last month from the window of a Muni bus he was riding, the San Francisco District Attorney's Office said.The drive-by shooting happened just after 11 p.m. on June 18 at the intersection of Mission Street and Silver Avenue. Jose Adrian Flores Garcia, 28, allegedly opened a window and shot an unarmed man who had just exited the bus with a friend, the DA's Office said.
