Oakland police search for thieves who used a backhoe to break into a business
-----
Thieves broke into an AM-PM store in West Oakland apparently using a backhoe and fled after causing "an unknown amount of loss" Monday morning, according to police officials and news reports.A burglary alarm went off just after 2:15 a.m. at a business on the 800 block of West Grand Avenue, said police spokesperson Kim Armstead. The business was an AM-PM store at a gas station, according to Google Maps and surveillance camera footage obtained by ABC 7.
