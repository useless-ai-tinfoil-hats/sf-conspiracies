Tech layoffs: Google lays off workers in second cut in two months
-----
Google laid off a "small number" of workers in its Google News division, becoming the latest Bay Area tech giant to cut staff.The company's layoffs affected dozens of employees, according to CNBC, which first reported the news. It was the second time in two months that Google downsized, after laying off recruiters in September. Parent company Alphabet's Waymo self-driving division also had recent layoffs.
