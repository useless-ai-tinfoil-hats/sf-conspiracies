They made history by saying ‘I do.’ Three S.F. couples reflect on Newsom’s impact on same-sex marriage
-----
There once was a tradition among queer activists fighting for same-sex marriage rights: Every year around Valentine's Day, gay and lesbian couples would troop to San Francisco City Hall and request marriage licenses. Every year, they were rejected.Until Feb. 12, 2004. That morning, the answer was yes.
