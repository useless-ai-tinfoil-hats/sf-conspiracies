‘Shock and fear’: S.F. restaurateur on visit to Israel describes Hamas missile attack
-----
Manny Yekutiel, the owner of a popular San Francisco civic events space, was with his family in Israel on Saturday when air raid sirens sounded, signaling the start of a surprise attack by Palestinian militants that rocked the country this weekend."The whole city was ringing with bomb sirens," Yekutiel said in an interview Sunday evening from Bet Shemesh, Israel, where he is sheltering with his family.
