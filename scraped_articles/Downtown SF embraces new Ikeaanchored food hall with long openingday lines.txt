Downtown SF embraces new Ikea-anchored food hall with long opening-day lines
-----
On Thursday morning, the highly anticipated downtown food hall Saluhall flung open its doors to a long line of eager San Franciscans."It smells so good down here," said Mayor London Breed, who marked the momentous occasion with a speech and a pastry demo. And it did — the aroma of fresh cinnamon swirls from in-house bakery Smörgåsland wafted through the air.
