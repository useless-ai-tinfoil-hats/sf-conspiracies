New Giants catcher Tom Murphy looks forward to working with Patrick Bailey
-----
This isn't Tom Murphy's first go-round with the San Francisco Giants. The veteran catcher was with the team for three days in 2019 after being acquired on waivers from the Rockies."It was kind of wild," Murphy said by phone from his home in upstate New York. "It was during the Bay Bridge series, right at the end of camp, when they picked me up. I put on a jersey, had one at-bat in an exhibition game and 48 hours later, they DFA'd me."
