Over 100,000 eligible for payout in huge Disneyland class-action case
-----
Over 100,000 Disneyland passholders are eligible for a cash payment from Disney after the company agreed to settle a class-action lawsuit over alleged false advertising.On Thursday, a preliminary agreement was reached between Disney and plaintiff Jenale Nielsen, a Bay Area woman who filed on behalf of all former Dream Key holders.
