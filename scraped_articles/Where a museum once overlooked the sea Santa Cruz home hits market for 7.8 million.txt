Where a museum once overlooked the sea, Santa Cruz home hits market for $7.8 million
-----

Now, more than a century later, 1004 West Cliff Drive is a private residence, and it's for sale, asking $7.75 million.The property qualifies as a minicompound, with the home being a total of 3,236 square feet.
