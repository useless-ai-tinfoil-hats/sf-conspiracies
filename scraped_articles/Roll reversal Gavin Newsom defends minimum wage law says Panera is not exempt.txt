Roll reversal: Gavin Newsom defends minimum wage law, says Panera is not exempt
-----
A day after Bloomberg reported that Gov. Gavin Newsom pushed for an exemption from a fast-food minimum wage law that benefits Panera Bread, Newsom's office said it had reviewed the law and determined Panera is not exempt after all.The law requires fast-food chains to increase their workers' minimum wages to $20 an hour on April 1.
