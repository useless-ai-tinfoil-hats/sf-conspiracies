Sean Manaea is Giants' lone bright spot in error-riddled loss to Padres
-----
Sean Manaea should get a do-over for his 2023 season and start the entire way for the San Francisco Giants.Manaea turned in his fourth consecutive excellent start Wednesday, allowing two hits in six innings and striking out eight in San Francisco's error-strewn, 10-inning 5-2 loss to the Padres at Oracle Park. The Giants made two errors in the decisive 10th inning and three errors in all to increase their major-league lead to 117, 16 more than runner-up Boston.
