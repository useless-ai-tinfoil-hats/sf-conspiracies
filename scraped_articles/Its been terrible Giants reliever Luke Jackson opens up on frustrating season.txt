‘It’s been terrible’: Giants reliever Luke Jackson opens up on frustrating season
-----
San Francisco Giants reliever Luke Jackson is a straight shooter, and he's not going to try to sugarcoat his season."It's been terrible," Jackson said before Friday's game against the Dodgers.
He entered the day with a 9.31 ERA in June and his strikeouts are way down, 6.92 per nine innings compared with 11.61 last year. His average fastball velocity is actually up a tick, at 94.7 mph.
