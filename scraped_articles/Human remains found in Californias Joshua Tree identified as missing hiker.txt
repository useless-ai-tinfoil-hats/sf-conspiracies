Human remains found in California's Joshua Tree identified as missing hiker
-----
LATEST Jan. 31, 5:10 p.m. Human remains found in California's Joshua Tree National Park have been identified as those of Trammell Evans, a 25-year-old hiker who was reported missing more than eight months ago, the Riverside County Sheriff's Office said in a news release Wednesday.The skeletal remains were first discovered on Jan. 25 in the Black Rock area of the park.
