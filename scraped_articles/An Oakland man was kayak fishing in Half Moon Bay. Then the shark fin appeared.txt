An Oakland man was kayak fishing in Half Moon Bay. Then the shark fin appeared
-----
An Oakland earth sciences teacher experienced a jarringly up-close encounter with nature when he and a friend confronted a shark as they fished on pedal kayaks in Half Moon Bay.Ian Walters, 35, who recorded part of the encounter on his cell phone, said the shark — he believes it was a great white about 14 to 15 feet long — trailed him and his friend for about five minutes while they were nearly a mile out from Pillar Point Harbor around 10 a.m. last Tuesday.
