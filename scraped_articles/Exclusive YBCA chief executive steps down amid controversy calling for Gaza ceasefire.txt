Exclusive: YBCA chief executive steps down amid controversy calling for Gaza ceasefire
-----
Yerba Buena Center for the Arts interim CEO Sara Fenske Bahat has told the Chronicle in an exclusive statement that she plans to step down, effective immediately, after two years.The news, announced Wednesday, March 6, comes amid a turbulent few weeks for the San Francisco art center that Fenske Bahat described as "excruciating. Not just as a leader, but as a Jewish leader."
