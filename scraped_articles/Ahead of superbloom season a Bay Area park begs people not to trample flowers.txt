Ahead of superbloom season, a Bay Area park begs people not to trample flowers
-----
As wildflower season blooms, volunteers at an East Bay park are pleading with influencers not to trample all over its native plants.At one of the entrances to Shell Ridge Open Space in Walnut Creek, a peculiar sign is stationed in the mulch: "Don't Location Tag on Social Media," it reads. "Protect this place. Use generic tags like Bay Area."
