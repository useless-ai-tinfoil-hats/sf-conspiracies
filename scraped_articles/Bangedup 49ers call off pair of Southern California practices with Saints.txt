Banged-up 49ers call off pair of Southern California practices with Saints
-----
The San Francisco 49ers have canceled their scheduled joint practices with the Saints in Southern California due to injury issues that kept at least 15 players out of practice last Thursday.The 49ers had planned to practice against the Saints on Thursday and Friday at UC Irvine. The teams will meet Sunday night in a preseason game at Levi's Stadium.
