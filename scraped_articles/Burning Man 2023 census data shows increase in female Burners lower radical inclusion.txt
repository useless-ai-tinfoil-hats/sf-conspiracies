Burning Man has fired back up this week as thousands of Burners flock back to Black Rock City. As they file in, some attendees will inevitably make up the 2024 Burning Man census, a random survey of participants in person and online.The census has been conducted annually by volunteers since 2002, excluding 2020 and 2021, when the pandemic forced the cancellation of the event.
