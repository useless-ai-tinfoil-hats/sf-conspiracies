Warriors’ Steph Curry looks tired to his coach. Will Chris Paul’s return help?
-----
Chris Paul's influence on the Golden State Warriors stretches beyond his sparkling assist-to-turnover ratio, and it doesn't stop during his recovery from hand surgery.Paul recently offered some wisdom to rookie guard Brandin Podziemski, a freshly minted starter. Podziemski now spends more time on the court alongside Stephen Curry, which requires understanding unspoken cues from Curry.
