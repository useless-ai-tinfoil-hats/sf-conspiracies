49ers game review: How unflinching Brock Purdy put the Cardinals away
-----
Brock Purdy is adept at standing stationary and throwing under pressure. And the San Francisco 49ers quarterback is skilled at escaping the pocket due to pressure and throwing on the move.Both vital and disparate QB qualities – the fortitude not to flinch when a hit is imminent and the athleticism to flee when, say, a block is missed – were on display on back-to-back plays that basically put the Cardinals away in the 49ers' 45-29 win at Arizona on Sunday.
