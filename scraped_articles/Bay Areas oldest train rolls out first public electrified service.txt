Bay Area's oldest train rolls out first public electrified service
-----
The oldest continually operating railroad west of the Mississippi is roaring into the 21st century this month by officially rolling out its electrified service to riders.On Sunday, Aug. 11, Caltrain is slated to begin public rides on its new electric fleet following seven years of planning and redeveloping its 160-year-old route that was first used by steam locomotives.
