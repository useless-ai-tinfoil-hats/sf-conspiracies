California's Sierra Nevada poised to see snow in rare cold summer storm
-----
A robust area of low-pressure that meteorologists are calling "rare" and "highly unusual for August" was spinning off the coast of southwest Oregon and northwest California early Friday morning, bringing scattered showers to Northern California.
