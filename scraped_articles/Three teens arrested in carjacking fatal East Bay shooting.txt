Three teens arrested in carjacking, fatal East Bay shooting
-----
Three teenagers were arrested last week in connection with the killing of an 18-year-old man who was carjacked and shot in an unincorporated area near San Leandro, Alameda County sheriff's officials said.The three suspects are accused of luring the victim, Leandro Iquite, from Redwood City to an area just outside of Ashland, where officials say the teens stole Iquite's vehicle and shot him at an apartment complex on Miramar Avenue around 10:45 p.m. on Oct. 8.
