’It’s a big stretch’: Warriors play 10 of their next 11 games at home
-----
Here's an NBA rarity: Over the next three-plus weeks, the Golden State Warriors play exactly one road game.Aside from that trip to Denver, for a Christmas Day matchup with the Nuggets, the Warriors play 10 of 11 games at Chase Center. They launched this uncommonly long home stretch Tuesday night, with a riveting 132-126 overtime victory over Boston.
