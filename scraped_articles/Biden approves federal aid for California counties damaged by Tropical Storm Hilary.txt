Biden approves federal aid for California counties damaged by Tropical Storm Hilary
-----
President Biden approved federal aid Wednesday for five counties affected by Tropical Storm Hilary after it inundated California communities with record rainfall in early August.The federal aid was approved to supplement recovery efforts in Imperial, Inyo, Kern, Riverside and Siskiyou counties due to rain, wind, flooding and mudslides that began Aug. 20 when the Category 4 hurricane made landfall in Baja California, according to a White House news release.
