Don’t miss ‘Crafting Radicality’ in its final week
-----
Before the year's end, there's an exhibition at the de Young Museum worth a visit."Crafting Radicality: Bay Area Artists From the Svane Gift" closes on Dec. 31 and features works by 12 local artists, spanning painting, sculpture and works on paper. The pieces are part of the Svane Family Foundation's 2022 gift of 42 works by 30 local artists.
