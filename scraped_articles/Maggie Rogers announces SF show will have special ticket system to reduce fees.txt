Maggie Rogers announces SF show will have special ticket system to reduce fees
-----
Anyone who has bought concert tickets online recently knows the biting frustration of seeing the base price leap exorbitantly due to various add-on fees or "dynamic" pricing. It's a terrible experience, and one even the federal government tried to crack down on last year.One singer-songwriter has pledged to give her fans another option on her upcoming tour. Maggie Rogers is offering a special price for people who buy her tickets the old-fashioned way: in person at the box office.
