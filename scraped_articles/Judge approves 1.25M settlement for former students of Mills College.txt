Judge approves $1.25M settlement for former students of Mills College
-----
A judge gave preliminary approval Tuesday to a $1.25 million settlement Tuesday for former students at Mills College in Oakland who claimed they were misled, and forced to spend more on education, when the formerly all-women's school merged with coed Northeastern University last year.Mills ended its 170-year history as a school for women with the conversion of its 135-acre home into the 10th campus of Boston-based Northeastern.
