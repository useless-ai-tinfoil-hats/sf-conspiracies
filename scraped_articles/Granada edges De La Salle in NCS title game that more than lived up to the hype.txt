Granada edges De La Salle in NCS title game that more than lived up to the hype
-----
It was billed as one of the biggest high school baseball games ever in the Bay Area.Fittingly, Granada-Livermore and De La Salle-Concord delivered one of the region's greatest.
Granada's 1-0 North Coast Section Division 1 championship win Saturday was basically two games in one, a 14-inning classic that took 24 hours to complete and featured remarkable pitching, defense and drama.
