Former Yosemite National Park employee accused of rape, strangulation
-----
A former Yosemite National Park employee was indicted and charged with aggravated sexual abuse on Thursday, U.S. Attorney Phillip A. Talbert announced.After meeting a female park employee on the night of May 31, 2024, 36-year-old Nathan Baptista allegedly raped and strangled her, according to a statement from the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Eastern District of California.
If convicted, Baptista could face life in prison and a $250,000 fine.
