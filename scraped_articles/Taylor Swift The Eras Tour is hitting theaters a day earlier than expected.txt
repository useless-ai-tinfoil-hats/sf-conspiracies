‘Taylor Swift: The Eras Tour’ is hitting theaters a day earlier than expected
-----
"Taylor Swift: The Eras Tour" will arrive in theaters a day earlier than expected, due to "unprecedented demand."The highly-anticipated concert film originally scheduled to open on Friday, Oct. 13, will now have its public premiere at 6 p.m. Thursday, Oct. 12 at theaters across North America, the pop singer announced.
