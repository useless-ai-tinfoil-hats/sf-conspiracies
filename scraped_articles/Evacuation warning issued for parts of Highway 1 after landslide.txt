Evacuation warning issued for parts of Highway 1 after landslide
-----
LATEST April 3, 2:29 p.m. The evacuation warning for parts of Big Sur along Highway 1 has prompted closures of Captain Cooper Elementary School and Apple Pie Preschool on Thursday and Friday, per a press release from the Carmel Unified School District's superintendent Sharon Ofek.
