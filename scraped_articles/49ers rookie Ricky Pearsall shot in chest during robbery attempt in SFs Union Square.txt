49ers rookie Ricky Pearsall shot in chest during robbery attempt in SF's Union Square
-----
San Francisco 49ers rookie receiver Ricky Pearsall was shot in the chest during a robbery attempt in San Francisco's Union Square on Saturday, the team said in a statement. The team said Pearsall is in stable condition."He sustained a bullet wound to his chest and is in serious but stable condition," the organization wrote. "We ask that you please respect his privacy at this time."
