Alameda County DA’s office charges three police officers in controversial death of Mario Gonzalez
-----
Alameda County prosecutors filed involuntary manslaughter charges against three officers in the death of Mario Gonzalez, a man they arrested in Alameda in April 2021 and pinned to the ground until he went limp, according to court records.The decision to file felony charges reverses the decision of the previous district attorney, Nancy O'Malley, who cleared the officers — Eric McKinley, James Fisher and Cameron Leahy — of criminal wrongdoing in 2022.
