One confirmed dead in avalanche in Palisades Tahoe during major snowstorm
-----
A major avalanche at the Palisades Tahoe ski resort Wednesday morning left one person dead and others with non life-threatening injuries.The avalanche occurred about 9:30 a.m. on the Palisades side of the resort. All lifts closed for the day, and first responders from around the region immediately joined the search and rescue effort.

The male victim who died in the avalanche at was a guest at the resort
