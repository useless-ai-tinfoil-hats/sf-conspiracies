Popular Berkeley deli announces abrupt closure, plans new concept
-----
Berkeley's popular pastrami destination Delirama will close after just shy of two years in the Bay Area.On Monday, Cash Caris, who co-owns Delirama with partner Anahita Cann, shared an emotional message in an Instagram story indicating bouts of challenges since the restaurant opened in 2022. Beginning this weekend, Delirama as we know it will change and stop selling sandwiches. The "Delirama concept is over," Caris confirmed to SFGATE via text.
