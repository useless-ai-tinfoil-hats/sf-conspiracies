Hardly Strictly Bluegrass 2023 announces set times and stage lineups
-----
Ready to start planning your Hardly Strictly Bluegrass weekend? Good, because the organizers have unveiled the set times and stage lineups for the free festival in San Francisco's Golden Gate Park.The three-day outdoor concert, from Sept. 29 through Oct. 1, will see the return of festival veterans like Emmylou Harris performing her traditional closing set on the Banjo Stage at 5:45 p.m. Sunday; Buddy Miller hosting his daylong Cavalcade of Stars on the Rooster Stage beginning at 12:30
