Tahoe town could charge homeowners thousands if they keep their homes empty
-----
A new vacancy tax with the goal of easing Lake Tahoe's affordable housing crisis has gained more than double the signatures needed to appear on the November ballot.South Lake Tahoe residents with homes that sit empty for more than half the year would pay $3,000 the first year under the new law if approved, doubling to $6,000 the second year.
