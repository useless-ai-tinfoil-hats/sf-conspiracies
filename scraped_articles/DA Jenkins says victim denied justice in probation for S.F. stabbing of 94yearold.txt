DA Jenkins says victim ‘denied justice’ in probation for S.F. stabbing of 94-year-old
-----
San Francisco District Attorney Brooke Jenkins said a 94-year-old stabbing victim was "denied justice" after her attacker was given probation.Jenkins had sought prison for Daniel Cauich in the June 2021 attack on Anh "Peng" Taylor on Lower Nob Hill, but San Francisco Superior Court Judge Kay Tsenin instead ordered him Friday to enter a behavioral and mental health treatment program, the San Francisco Standard reported.
Cauich will be supervised in a facility with limited freedom
