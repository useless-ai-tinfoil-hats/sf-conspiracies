Brazen Oakland theft: Jaws of Life stolen during firefighters’ rescue operation
-----
Oakland police are investigating a brazen theft that occurred Friday evening as firefighters extricated a girl from a swing set: A perpetrator allegedly stole the Jaws of Life rescue tool they had placed on the ground.The $15,000 hydraulic tool, used to breach doors and quickly remove people who are trapped — typically in car crashes — was swiped at 7:15 p.m. from the 1600 block of West Street, police said.
