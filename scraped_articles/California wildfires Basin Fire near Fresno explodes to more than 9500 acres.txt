California wildfires: Basin Fire near Fresno explodes to more than 9,500 acres
-----
The Basin Fire, a wildfire burning in the Sierra National Forest near Fresno, grew to more than 9,500 acres by Saturday afternoon, threatening to eclipse the size of another nearby amalgamation of fires charring forest land to the southwest.The blaze prompted evacuation orders for an area east of Barnes Mountain and west of the Wishon Reservoir. The border of the mandatory evacuation zone was about 30 miles east of Fresno, according to Cal Fire Data.
