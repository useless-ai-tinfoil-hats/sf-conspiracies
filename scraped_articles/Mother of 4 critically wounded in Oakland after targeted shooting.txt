Mother of 4 critically wounded in Oakland after targeted shooting
-----
Oakland police are investigating a shooting that left a woman critically injured early Monday morning on the 1500 block of 92nd Avenue, a dense residential neighborhood in the city's flatlands.Representatives of the Police Department did not provide any identifying details about the victim, but a law enforcement source familiar with the investigation said she was a mother of four shot in her home, and that the attack seemed to be targeted.
