Living Jazz to embrace ‘expansive’ take on Martin Luther King Jr. concert
-----
New leadership at Living Jazz is taking on the tradition of celebrating Martin Luther King Jr.'s birthday, applying to the annual concert an intergenerational lens and a fresh, collaborative sound.* Guide: Celebrate Martin Luther King Jr. Day with the best events in the Bay Area
The 20th "In the Name of Love" concert, an East Bay bash that honors King's legacy, embodies both the organization's deep Oakland roots and the evolution of its vision under a new executive director.
