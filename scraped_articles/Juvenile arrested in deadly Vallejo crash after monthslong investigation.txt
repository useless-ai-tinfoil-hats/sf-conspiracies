Juvenile arrested in deadly Vallejo crash after months-long investigation
-----
Vallejo police have arrested a juvenile they say caused a fatal crash months earlier before fleeing the scene.The August crash occurred near Springs Road and Tregaskis Avenue after Vallejo police tried to stop a Toyota Avalon they identified as being used in armed robberies in the city. The driver led officers on a chase, which authorities called off due to public safety concerns, police said.
