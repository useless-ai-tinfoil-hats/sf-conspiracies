Team with Michelin cred will turn historic Bay Area post office into a restaurant
-----
A massive new Peninsula development has landed a big-name Bay Area restaurant group as its anchor retail tenant.Bacchus Management Group, known for Michelin-starred restaurants including the Village Pub in Woodside and Selby's in Redwood City, has signed on to 220 Park, a 185,000-square-foot, six-story building in downtown Burlingame. The group will restore Burlingame's historic post office at 220 Park St. and build an 8,000-square-foot restaurant there as part of the development.
