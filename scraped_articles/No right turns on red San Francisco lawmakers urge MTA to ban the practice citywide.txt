No right turns on red? San Francisco lawmakers urge MTA to ban the practice citywide
-----
San Francisco lawmakers on Tuesday unanimously urged the Municipal Transportation Agency to ban vehicles from making right turns on red lights at every intersection in the city. The MTA is soon to consider a more limited ban.Supervisor Dean Preston introduced the advisory resolution to prohibit drivers from making right turns on red lights to stem the number of people injured or killed by drivers in San Francisco.
