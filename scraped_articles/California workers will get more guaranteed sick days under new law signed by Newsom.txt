California workers will get more guaranteed sick days under new law signed by Newsom
-----
Many California workers will soon receive five paid sick days per year thanks to a bill that Gov. Gavin Newsom signed Wednesday.Previously, state law guaranteed workers three paid sick days per year. Beginning in January 2024, Senate Bill 616 bumps that number up to five, and increases the amount of sick hours employees can accrue. In the past, employers could cap the number of accrued sick hours at 48 (six work days), but SB 616 increases that cap to 80 hours (10 days).
