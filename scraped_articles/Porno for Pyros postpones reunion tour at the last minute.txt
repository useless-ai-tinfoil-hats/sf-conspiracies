Porno for Pyros postpones reunion tour at the last minute
-----
Porno for Pyros, the band led by Perry Farrell of Jane's Addiction, has delayed its reunion tour set to commence next week.The decision to postpone its first full tour since 1997, including a show at the Masonic in San Francisco on Tuesday, Oct. 10, was announced on social media. The platinum-selling band, best known for the single "Pets," cited the need to finish work on new material as the reason for pushing back the shows for "a few months."
