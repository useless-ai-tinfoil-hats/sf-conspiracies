Bay Bridge traffic flowing normally after vehicle fire near Treasure Island
-----
UPDATE Feb. 1, 7:34 p.m. Westbound traffic on the San Francisco Bay Bridge is flowing normally after a vehicle fire that erupted just before the Thursday evening commute.The fire was reported about 3:30 p.m. in the westbound direction just before Treasure Island.
