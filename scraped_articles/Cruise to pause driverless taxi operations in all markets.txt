Cruise to pause driverless taxi operations in all markets
-----
Cruise says it is pausing its entire driverless operations after California regulators suspended the autonomous vehicle company earlier this week from commercially deploying its robotaxis."The most important thing for us right now is to take steps to rebuild public trust.
