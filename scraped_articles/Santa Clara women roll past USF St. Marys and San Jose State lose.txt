Santa Clara women roll past USF; St. Mary's and San Jose State lose
-----
Nearly perfect free-throw shooting in the final 90 seconds propelled the Santa Clara women to a 72-59 defeat of USF at Leavey Center on Saturday.The Broncos (15-3, 3-2 WCC) made 7 of 8 free throws down the stretch to remain unbeaten in eight home games. Jayde Cadee made all four of hers in that stretch and finished with 12 points.
Tess Heal scored 21 points and Olivia Pollerd 17 (and seven rebounds) for Santa Clara, which has equaled last season's win total.
