Apple takes aim at AllTrails with major upgrade for hikers
-----
Apple isn't always first with popular mobile features, but you can depend on the company to add them to its iPhones eventually.Consider AllTrails, the hiking app built by a San Francisco-based company that Apple named as the iPhone App of the Year in 2023. Now, later this year with iOS 18, the Cupertino tech giant plans to integrate some of AllTrails' best features into its own Apple Maps for free.
