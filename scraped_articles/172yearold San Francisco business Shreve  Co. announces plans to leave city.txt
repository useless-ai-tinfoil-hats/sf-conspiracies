172-year-old San Francisco business Shreve & Co. announces plans to leave city
-----
High-end jeweler Shreve & Co. announced Monday that it will be shuttering its downtown San Francisco location next to Union Square, ending the company's 172-year run in the city.In a statement posted to the company's website, Shreve & Co. shared that it plans to move operations to a new flagship store in Palo Alto, slated to open later this summer.
