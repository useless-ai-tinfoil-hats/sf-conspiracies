Point Bonita Lighthouse closed to public for suspension bridge repairs
-----
The Point Bonita Lighthouse just beyond the San Francisco Bay is closed to visitors for the foreseeable future while the historic site's suspension bridge undergoes needed repairs.Built in 1855, the lighthouse still guides ships away from the treacherous rocky coast along the Golden Gate straits. Point Bonita is on a steep cliff promontory and visitors can only access the site by walking across a narrow suspension bridge.
