Multiple Hawaii coastlines hit with brown water advisories
-----
Several coastal areas in Hawaii are under a brown water advisory after high surf reached roadways, potentially bringing animal feces, pesticides, pathogens and a brew of other unwanted debris into the islands' normally pristine ocean, according to Hawaii's Clean Water Branch division of the Department of Health.
