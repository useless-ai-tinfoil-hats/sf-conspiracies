S.F. woman charged with stealing $15K worth of Bath & Body Works items
-----
San Francisco District Attorney Brooke Jenkins has charged a San Francisco woman in connection with a series of retail thefts at a particular Bath & Body Works location in the city, spokespersons said.Carmelita Barela, 30, is accused in at least 20 retail theft incidents, totaling more than $15,000 in goods, between March 30 and Oct. 29 at the store in the Westfield San Francisco Centre. She was arrested on Monday, Nov. 6, spokespersons said.
