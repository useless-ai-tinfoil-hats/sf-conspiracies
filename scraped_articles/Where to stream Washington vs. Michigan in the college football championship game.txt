Where to stream Washington vs. Michigan in the college football championship game
-----
After a long college football season, the CFP National Championship game is here. The No. 1 Michigan Wolverines take on the No. 2 Washington Huskies tonight at 7:30 p.m. Eastern from Houston's NRG Stadium. The game will be nationally broadcast on ESPN (streaming with Sling TV or Hulu + Live TV).
