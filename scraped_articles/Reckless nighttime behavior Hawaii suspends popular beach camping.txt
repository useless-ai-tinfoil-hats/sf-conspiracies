'Reckless nighttime behavior': Hawaii suspends popular beach camping
-----
Any Honolulu tourist or local hoping to camp on one of the city's beaches will have to go elsewhere as the Department of Land and Natural Resources shutters a popular recreational area for campers in the state's capital.DLNR said in a news release earlier this month that it would no longer allow camping at Sand Island State Recreation Area because of "reckless nighttime behavior" from visitors.
