S.F. police officer faces felony DUI charges after allegedly injuring other motorist in crash
-----
A San Francisco police officer faces felony DUI charges following a crash last year in which he allegedly rear-ended and injured another driver while his blood-alcohol level was at over twice the legal limit.Jason Hernandez was driving near the intersection of Skyline Boulevard and Chateau Drive in Hillsborough at about 6:30 p.m. on Dec. 15 when he plowed into a vehicle stopped at a stop sign, according to prosecutors with the San Mateo County District Attorney's Office.
