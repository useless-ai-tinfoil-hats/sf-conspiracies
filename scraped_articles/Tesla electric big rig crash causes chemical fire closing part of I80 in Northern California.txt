Tesla electric big rig crash causes chemical fire closing part of I-80 in Northern California
-----
A 20-mile section of Interstate 80 in Placer County was closed after a Tesla big rig crashed and caught fire early Monday, according to the California Highway Patrol.At around 3:15 a.m., a Tesla Semi crashed into a cluster of trees while traveling east on I-80, about half a mile west of Yuba Pass. Its batteries caught on fire, releasing toxic fumes, CHP officials said.
