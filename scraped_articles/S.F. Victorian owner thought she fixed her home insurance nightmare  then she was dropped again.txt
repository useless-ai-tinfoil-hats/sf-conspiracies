S.F. Victorian owner thought she fixed her home insurance nightmare — then she was dropped again
-----
California's insurance crisis has left tens of thousands of homeowners scrambling to find coverage — but it seems to have peculiarly singled out one San Francisco woman.Kay Magilavy's small Victorian apartment building, which is also her home, has been dropped twice by State Farm Insurance — the first time because of an error, the second time in the thousands of property and commercial apartment policy nonrenewals announced in March by California's largest home insurer.
