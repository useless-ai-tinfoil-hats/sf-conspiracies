Google fires worker who yelled 'Free Palestine' at Israeli tech conference
-----
Google fired an engineer who interrupted a tech conference to protest the company's contract with the Israeli government, a spokesperson for the Bay Area tech giant told SFGATE on Friday. CNBC first reported the firing.On March 4, the Google worker stood up during a talk by Google Israel managing director Barak Regev at the Mind the Tech conference, held in New York City by the Israeli newspaper Calcalist.
