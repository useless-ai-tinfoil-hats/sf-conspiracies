More than 8,000 without power in San Francisco
-----
More than 8,000 PG&E customers were in the dark in San Francisco on Tuesday morning, according to the website PowerOutage.us.PG&E's outage map indicated that the households without power were centered in the Pacific Heights neighborhood. The estimated time for restoration in this neighborhood is 3:30 p.m. Tuesday. PG&E's website said the cause of the outage is under investigation.
The agency did not immediately respond to a request for comment.
