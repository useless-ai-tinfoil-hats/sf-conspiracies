‘The Box’ hits the market in SOMA
-----
Opportunity to own historical, versatile buildingAvailable for purchase: The Historic 1069 Howard St. building, built in 1926. Built to withstand all the elements of time, it is in excellent condition with many upgrades. This is a very rare, once-in-a-lifetime opportunity.
Currently operating as The Box SF, the listing offers corporate meeting and off-site meeting spaces and classes/workshops. It also houses the largest antiquarian paper and advertising store in the United States
