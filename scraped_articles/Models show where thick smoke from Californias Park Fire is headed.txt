Models show where thick smoke from California's Park Fire is headed
-----
Southerly winds are pushing smoke from the raging Park Fire burning across Butte and Tehama counties into California's northeast corner and northwest Nevada.The winds are expected to continue to blow in this direction for the foreseeable future, according to Sara Purdue, a meteorologist with the National Weather Service.
"That's it for the next 24 to 48 hours," Purdue said. "I don't see a change in direction in the near future."
