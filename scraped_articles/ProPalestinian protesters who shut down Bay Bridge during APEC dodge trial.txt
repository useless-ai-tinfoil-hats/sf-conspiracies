Pro-Palestinian protesters who shut down Bay Bridge during APEC dodge trial
-----
The San Francisco District Attorney's Office said Thursday that 78 pro-Palestinian protesters who were arrested for blocking the Bay Bridge in November agreed to pay restitution and participate in community service to avoid going to trial.The 78 people will be required to complete five hours of community service within two months, the district attorney's office said.
