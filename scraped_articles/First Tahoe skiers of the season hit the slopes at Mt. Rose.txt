First Tahoe skiers of the season hit the slopes at Mt. Rose
-----
After last season's unbelievable snowfall in the Sierra, followed by five or so months of no skiing, three 14-year-olds from Reno, accompanied by a dad, were so anxious to return to the slopes that they showed up Friday at Mount Rose-Ski Tahoe at 4 a.m.The resort, in Nevada, was the first in the Tahoe area to open for the new season. In Central California, Mammoth Mountain, frequented mainly by Southern California skiers, also opened Friday.
