Costco cracks down on membership sharing in San Francisco. What you need to know
-----
In a bid to bolster security and prevent unauthorized access, Costco has introduced membership scanners at the entries to several locations across California, including its San Francisco warehouse and other Bay Area sites.The new policy mandates that customers scan the barcode or QR code on their membership card upon entry, replacing the previous practice of showing their card to an attendant.
