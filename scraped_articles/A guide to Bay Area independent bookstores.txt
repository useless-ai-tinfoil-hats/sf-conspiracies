A guide to Bay Area independent bookstores
-----
When East Bay Booksellers burned down in late July, the outpouring of support showed just how important independent bookstores are to the community.While the beloved Oakland business recovers from the blaze, it continues to plan popup shops around the Bay Area and fulfill orders online. But for those still wanting that in-person shopping experience, here are some of East Bay Booksellers owner Brad Johnson's recommendations, which he considers his shop's "closest kin."
