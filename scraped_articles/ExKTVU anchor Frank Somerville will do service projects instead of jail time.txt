Ex-KTVU anchor Frank Somerville will do service projects instead of jail time
-----
LATEST May 14, 2:30 p.m. Ex-KTVU anchor Frank Somerville won't be behind bars after receiving a 30-day jail sentence in a plea agreement for reckless driving and a probation violation.Instead, Somerville was accepted Tuesday into an alternative program in which he will perform community service projects for the Alameda County Sheriff's Office, his attorney, Shannan Dugan, told SFGATE.
