A Bay Area city saw one of the steepest rent drops in the country, report shows
-----
Bay Area rents have long been expensive. But rents in Oakland have finally granted some relief.According to the latest national rent report from rental website Zumper, released Tuesday, the city saw a 9.1% year-over-year drop in one-bedroom apartment rents. That plunge means that it tied with Scottsdale, Arizona, for the fourth-steepest drop in the country in single-bedroom rents over the past 12 months.
