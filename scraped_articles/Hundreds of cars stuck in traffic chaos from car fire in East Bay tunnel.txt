Hundreds of cars stuck in traffic chaos from car fire in East Bay tunnel
-----
LATEST June 4, 1:30 p.m. All lanes were open by 1 p.m. Wednesday afternoon on Highway 24 after a car fire in the Caldecott Tunnel closed the eastbound lanes.The Oakland Fire Department received a call at 9:54 a.m. about the vehicle fire, and approximately 35 firefighters were sent to the scene, Michael Hunt, a spokesperson for the department, told SFGATE.
