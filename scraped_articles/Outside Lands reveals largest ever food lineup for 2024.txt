Outside Lands reveals largest ever food lineup for 2024
-----
Attendees to this year's Outside Lands festival at San Francisco's Golden Gate Park are advised to bring sunscreen, a light jacket and a big appetite.The three-day festival, Aug. 9-12, will feature 90 performers, including headliners Tyler, the Creator; the Killers; and Post Malone. And to eat, there will be 100 restaurants as part of its Taste of the Bay Area culinary showcase, making it the largest in the festival's 16-year history.
