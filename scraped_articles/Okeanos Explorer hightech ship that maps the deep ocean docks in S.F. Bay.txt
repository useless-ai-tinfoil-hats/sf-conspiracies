Okeanos Explorer, high-tech ship that maps the deep ocean, docks in S.F. Bay
-----
Exploring the deep ocean floor is daunting. To start, there's the enormous pressure, the lack of natural light and the frigid temperatures.But a former Cold War U.S. Navy ship retrofitted with cutting-edge ocean exploration tools — including a submersible robot that can travel to some of the deepest parts of the ocean — is doing just that.
The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration's Okeanos Explorer pulled into San Francisco Bay and docked near the Exploratorium on Friday
