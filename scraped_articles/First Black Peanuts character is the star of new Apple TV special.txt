First Black ‘Peanuts’ character is the star of new Apple TV+ special
-----
Franklin, the first Black character in the "Peanuts" comic strip, takes center stage in a new animated special on Apple TV+."Snoopy Presents: Welcome Home, Franklin" is scheduled to premiere on Friday, Feb. 16. It explores the backstory of Franklin Armstrong, who was casually introduced in Charles M. Schulz's original newspaper comic strip on July 31, 1968, returning a stray beach ball to Charlie Brown.
