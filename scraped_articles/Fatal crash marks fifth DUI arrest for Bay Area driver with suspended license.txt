Fatal crash marks fifth DUI arrest for Bay Area driver with suspended license
-----
A Bay Area man has now been arrested five times on suspicion of driving under the influence after a fatal car crash.The Santa Rosa Police Department announced on social media that police and paramedics responded to the crash around 1:35 p.m. Friday at the corner of Lombardi Court and Sebastopol Road. Investigators said they determined that Santa Rosa resident Nhan Van Nguyen, 68, was under the influence of alcohol when his Dodge truck hit a Honda sedan.
