Officials identify remains in 50-year-old Lake Tahoe cold case
-----
A skull found near a Placer County highway in 1986 has been identified as belonging to a woman who went missing in South Lake Tahoe more than 50 years ago, authorities said Wednesday.The Placer County Sheriff's Office said the skull belonged to Donna Lass, a nurse who was reported missing in 1971 and whose case had remained cold for decades.
