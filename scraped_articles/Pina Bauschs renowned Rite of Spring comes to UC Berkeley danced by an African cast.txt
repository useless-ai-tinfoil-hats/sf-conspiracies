In 2009, just five days after receiving a cancer diagnosis, the world-renowned German choreographer Pina Bausch died. She left behind an electrifying body of work that changed dance forever, and just one heir: her son Salomon Bausch.At the time of her death, she had been working with acclaimed director Wim Wenders on a film, which her company completed in tribute.
