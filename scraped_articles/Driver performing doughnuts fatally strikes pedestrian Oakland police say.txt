Driver performing doughnuts fatally strikes pedestrian, Oakland police say
-----
A pedestrian was struck and killed in Oakland early Monday by a driver performing doughnuts, police said.The collision occurred just before 12:45 a.m. at the intersection of International Boulevard and 42nd Avenue, Oakland police said. Authorities said the pedestrian was transported to a local hospital and died there.
