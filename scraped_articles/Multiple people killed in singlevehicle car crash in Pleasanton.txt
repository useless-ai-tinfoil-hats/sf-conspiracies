Multiple people killed in single-vehicle car crash in Pleasanton
-----
Police in Pleasanton said they are investigating a single-vehicle car crash that occurred late Wednesday that "resulted in multiple fatalities," officials said in a statement.The accident occurred around 9 p.m. on Foothill Road near Stoneridge Drive, police said.
The number of people killed or injured was not immediately available, nor was information about what precipitated the crash.
