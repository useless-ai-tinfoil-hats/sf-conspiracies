Storm hitting California isn't an atmospheric river. What is it?
-----
Early weather forecasts suggested California may see a super-soaking atmospheric river this week.Last week's forecast doesn't hold. Meteorologists are no longer using that label to describe the system that's bringing a chance of rain to the Golden State Tuesday through Sunday.
"It's not an atmospheric river," said Brian Garcia, a National Weather Service meteorologist said. "This is a classic Gulf of Alaska low."
This means the storm will be dropping down from the Gulf
