Photos: S.F. maritime tradition Opening Day on the Bay kicks off boating season
-----
Classic and contemporary watercraft delighted spectators Sunday during the official kickoff to San Francisco Bay's boating season.The Opening Day on the Bay parade drew dozens of historic workboats, fireboats and towboats. The San Francisco fireboat St. Francis led the parade, which was preceded by the blessing of the fleet.
Dating to 1917 and organized by the Pacific Inter-Club Yacht Association, the parade runs off the shore of Crissy Field in the Presidio to the first Fort Mason
