Fire at East Oakland cannabis warehouse sparked by vehicle blaze
-----
A vehicle fire spread to a nearby cannabis warehouse in East Oakland on Saturday morning, investigators said. No injuries were reported.The fire broke out before 4:45 a.m. in a recreational vehicle parked in front of a cannabis warehouse at 1000 40th Ave., according to Oakland Fire Department spokesperson Michael Hunt.
More than 60 firefighters responded to the blaze, which was brought under control shortly after 6 a.m., Hunt said.
