‘Why do my eyes hurt’: Google searches for eye pain surge after eclipse
-----
Internet searches for terms about eye pain surged after Monday's rare solar eclipse that had spectators looking skyward in the Bay Area and across the country.According to Google Trends, which tracks the volume of certain search terms, "my eyes hurt" and "why do my eyes hurt" spiked significantly as the solar eclipse's shadow passed over the country.
Searches for "my eyes hurt" reached their peak around 3 p.m. EST. The eclipse's path terminated in Maine shortly around 3:30 p.m.
