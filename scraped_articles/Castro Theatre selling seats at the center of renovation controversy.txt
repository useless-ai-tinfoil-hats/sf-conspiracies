Castro Theatre selling seats at the center of renovation controversy
-----
The Castro Theatre is selling its orchestra-level seats at the center of the "Save Our Seats" controversy, again pitting the theater's preservationists against Another Planet Entertainment, which took over management of the venue two years ago.Single seats are priced at $349, with pairs available for $499, the venue announced on Tuesday, April 16. Proceeds from the sale will go to Oasis Arts and the Castro Organ Devotees Association, nonprofit partners with the Berkeley-based production
