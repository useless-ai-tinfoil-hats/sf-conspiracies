49ers say All-Pro safety Talanoa Hufanga likely suffered an ACL tear
-----
A year after Talanoa Hufanga's breakout season, his 2023 campaign is likely over due to a bad break.The San Francisco 49ers' All-Pro safety suffered a non-contact knee injury in the third quarter of a 27-14 win over the Buccaneers and head coach Kyle Shanahan said Hufanaga likely suffered a torn ACL. Hufanga will undergo further testing Monday.
"We're concerned," Shanahan said. 'We don't know for sure. Most likely it's (an) ACL, but can't confirm it."
