San Francisco's popular Dumpling Club to close
-----
Dumpling Club, a popular weekly food group and educational space based in San Francisco's Mission District, is winding down operations and will serve its last Chinese breakfast on Oct. 8, per an Instagram post shared by founder Cathay Bi on Sunday."Starting Dumpling Club was hard, but choosing to end it was infinitely harder," Bi wrote. "It took every ounce of my courage — more courage than I thought I even had — to make this decision."
