At a San Jose gun buyback event, someone turned in a rocket launcher
-----
A gun buyback event in Santa Clara County last weekend secured a large haul of weapons far beyond just handguns.In a statement Thursday, Santa Clara County Supervisor Otto Lee said the event, held at San Jose's Reid-Hillview Airport, collected 408 weapons, with $40,050 distributed to residents in return.
