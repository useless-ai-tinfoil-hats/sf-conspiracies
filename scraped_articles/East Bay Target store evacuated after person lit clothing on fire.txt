East Bay Target store evacuated after person lit clothing on fire
-----
A Target store in the East Bay was evacuated Wednesday night after a small fire broke out on the sales floor, Oakland fire officials and store representatives said.Oakland fire crews responded to a scene at the Emeryville Target just after 9:10 p.m. and were told that a person had set fire to clothing near the dressing room area, Oakland fire officials said.
A total of eight personnel responded to the scene. No injuries were reported.
