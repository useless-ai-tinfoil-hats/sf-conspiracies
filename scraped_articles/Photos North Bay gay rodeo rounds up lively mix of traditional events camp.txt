Photos: North Bay gay rodeo rounds up lively mix of traditional events, camp
-----
The Bay Area chapter of the International Gay Rodeo Association corralled two days of fun and fundraising over the weekend with a Sonoma County competition and celebration.The Best Buck in the Bay Rodeo & Festival attracted fans of the country life to Duncans Mills on Saturday and Sunday for traditional Western events as well as activities with a distinctly LGBTQ flair, including a Wigs & Whiskey drag party.
Competitors vied for thousands of dollars in prize money during the event
