With help from Bob Melvin, Giants’ Jung Hoo Lee meets idol Ichiro Suzuki
-----
PEORIA, Ariz. — On a split-squad day, a manager won't typically separate himself from the regulars and trek to the away game. But San Francisco Giants skipper Bob Melvin wanted to facilitate a warm introduction in Seattle Mariners camp between his rookie center fielder and an old friend.Before the first pitch of Sunday's exhibition game, Jung Hoo Lee met his childhood idol Ichiro Suzuki, whom Melvin knows well having managed the 2001 AL MVP during his early years with the Mariners.
