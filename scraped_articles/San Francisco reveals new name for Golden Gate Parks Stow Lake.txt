San Francisco reveals new name for Golden Gate Park's Stow Lake
-----
After months of discussion and dozens of suggestions from San Francisco residents, the city's most romantic destination has been given a new name.For 130 years, Stow Lake and its boathouse, rowboats and island hike have been a beloved escape for San Franciscans. But in recent years, the antisemitic beliefs of its namesake, William Stow, have prompted a renaming campaign for the Golden Gate Park destination.
