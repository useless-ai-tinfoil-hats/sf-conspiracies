Wrong family picks up photos for Tom Brady's mom at Bay Area CVS
-----
It'd be hard for anyone to mix up the careers of future Hall of Famer Tom Brady and current 49ers practice squad tight end Jake Tonges. Mixing up their respective photos, however, is a different story.Katie Tonges, the tight end's sister, shared on TikTok on Monday that her mom had mistakenly received a photo of Brady and his children in a CVS order of printed photos instead of photos of her own family. The post's caption read, "We can do a trade if you have our photo...??"
