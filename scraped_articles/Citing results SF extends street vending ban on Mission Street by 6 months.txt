Citing results, SF extends street vending ban on Mission Street by 6 months
-----
San Francisco Mayor London Breed said Monday that the city will extend the ban on street vending along Mission Street for six months to "further enhance public safety and cleanliness" in the neighborhood.The city initially put a 90-day ban on the practice in place on Nov. 27, saying that "unauthorized vending and illegal activities," including the sale of stolen goods, led to "unprecedented safety concerns" that impacted residents and nearby business owners.
