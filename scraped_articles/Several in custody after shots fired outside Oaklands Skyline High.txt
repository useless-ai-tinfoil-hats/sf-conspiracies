Several in custody after shots fired outside Oakland's Skyline High
-----
Several people were taken into custody following a shooting outside Oakland's Skyline High School Tuesday morning, district officials said.Police responded to a report of shots fired around 11:20 a.m., sending the school into lockdown for nearly four hours. No one was injured in the incident and the lockdown was lifted around 2 p.m., official said.
It was unclear what prompted the shooting or whether anyone was targeted.
