Diana Hanna spent a recent morning naked in the woods, trying to hunt down an invasive feral hog."I was running around out there buck-naked except for my flip-flops, carrying a handgun, looking for the darn thing," she said.
Other than the pig hunt — which was legally permitted by California's Department of Fish and Wildlife, Hanna noted — it's how she's spent most of her mornings for the past 68 years.
