Self-driving Waymos secure final clearance for expansion beyond S.F.
-----
UPDATE: Waymo opens its driverless robotaxi service to anyone in S.F.Autonomous vehicle companies won two key regulatory battles this week that allow Waymo to move forward with expansion plans to the Peninsula and preserve California's authority over the emerging industry.
On Thursday, the California Public Utilities Commission upheld its March decision to approve Waymo's expansion from San Francisco to San Mateo County and Los Angeles over protests from local officials.
