UC Berkeley-loved Italian restaurant set to close after 33 years
-----
Steps away from UC Berkeley, a student-loved Italian restaurant announced it will close this month. The last day of service for the 33-year-old eatery is scheduled for Dec. 15.Pasta Bene, at 2565 Telegraph Ave., operated near downtown Berkeley for the past 13 years, and before that, it had been located at 88 Hardie Place in San Francisco since 1990. The owners, Farhad Jalali and Ladan Sanjani, are retiring.
