10 S.F. schools will see Algebra I back in classrooms this fall after long battle
-----
Ten San Francisco middle schools will see Algebra I courses back on the schedule this fall for the first time in a decade, the first group in a three-year phase-in to fully integrate the course back into eighth grade students schedules.District officials announced Friday which of the 13 middle schools and six K-8 schools would offer Algebra I in the upcoming school year and how it would be piloted at each site.
