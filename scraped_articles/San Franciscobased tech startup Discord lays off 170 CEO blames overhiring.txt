San Francisco-based tech startup Discord lays off 170, CEO blames overhiring
-----
Discord is laying off 160 workers tied to its San Francisco headquarters, the social messaging startup announced in a WARN notice to the city on Thursday.The notice, required when companies perform mass layoffs, said the employees would officially leave Discord on Feb. 2. Dozens of engineers are among the casualties, as are several trust and safety employees, product managers and data scientists, according to the notice.
