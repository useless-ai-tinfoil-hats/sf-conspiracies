Despite losses, Salesian boys and Mitty girls among greatest Metro teams
-----
The 2023-24 high school basketball season ended late Saturday night at Golden 1 Center in Sacramento with back-to-back Open Division losses for the Bay Area's top-ranked teams: Mitty's girls and the Salesian-Richmond boys.With wins, both likely would have qualified as the best the Metro has ever seen. Considering their body of work, each still should be considered among the area's all-time elite.
