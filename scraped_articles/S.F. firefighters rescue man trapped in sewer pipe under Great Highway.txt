S.F. firefighters rescue man trapped in sewer pipe under Great Highway
-----
A man was rescued early Sunday from a sewer pipe approximately 20 feet below the road of San Francisco's Great Highway, fire officials said.Fire crews responded to the intersection of the Great Highway and Vicente Street at about 3:41 a.m. after a person reported hearing a man calling for help near a hole cover, said San Francisco Fire Department Lt. Mariano Elias.
