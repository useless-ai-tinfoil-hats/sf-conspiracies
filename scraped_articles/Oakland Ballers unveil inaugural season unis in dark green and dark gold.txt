Oakland Ballers unveil inaugural season unis in dark green and dark gold
-----
The Bay Area's newest professional baseball team is enjoying tweaking the Oakland Athletics an awful lot as the major-league team makes its way out the door.The same week that the A's painted over the "Rooted in Oakland" logo on the Coliseum, the Oakland Ballers, who launch this season in the independent Pioneer League, unveiled green and gold jerseys and "Oakland" on both the home white and gold away uniforms to underscore their commitment to the city.
