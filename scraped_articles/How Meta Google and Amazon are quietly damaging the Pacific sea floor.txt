How Meta, Google and Amazon are quietly damaging the Pacific sea floor
-----
In 2020, Edge Cable Holdings, a Facebook subsidiary, was burying a new fiber-optic cable into the seabed near Tierra Del Mar, Ore. Working beneath a rugged mixture of basalt rock mounds, unconsolidated sands and sandstone bedrock, the company's drilling operation went awry.
