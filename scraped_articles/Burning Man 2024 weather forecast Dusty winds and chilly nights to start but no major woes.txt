Burning Man 2024 weather forecast: Dusty winds and chilly nights to start, but no major woes
-----
Flash floods and celebrities forced to walk for miles in the mud were some of the lasting images from Burning Man in 2023. Last year's edition of the event was severely impacted by extreme weather conditions, but thankfully that won't be the case this year."It's a completely different pattern compared to last year," said Chris Johnston, a meteorologist with the National Weather Service in Reno.
