Why hundreds of Oakland restaurants and other businesses are striking Tuesday
-----
Over 200 Oakland businesses plan to participate in a strike Sept. 26 to demand support from city officials amid rising crime."If you don't do anything, it's pretty much a do-or-die situation for our businesses," said Carl Chan, president of Oakland Chinatown's Chamber of Commerce, who is organizing the strike. "Public safety is impacting our small businesses, and customers are afraid to come out."
