Major cosmetics brand closes store in Bay Area shopping center
-----
Popular makeup retailer Ulta Beauty is permanently closing its East Bay Bridge Center store later this week, an employee confirmed to SFGATE over the phone Wednesday.Signs posted at the front of the store also alerted customers to the closure, as first shared by the E'ville Eye, which redirected them to nearby locations at South Shore Center in Alameda and on Fitzgerald Drive in Pinole.
