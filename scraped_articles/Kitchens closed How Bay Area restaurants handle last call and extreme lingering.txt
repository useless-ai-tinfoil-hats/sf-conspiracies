Kitchen’s closed: How Bay Area restaurants handle last call and ‘extreme lingering’
-----
It's the end of the week, and you want to celebrate at that new spot you've been hearing about. The meal is great, fantastic even. Perhaps you pay and head home — it's getting kind of late. But maybe you stick around and order another cocktail. Maybe you linger, chatting until the lights turn on. Then the check arrives without you asking.The Chronicle conducted a survey of how 20 Bay Area restaurants handle their closing times and lingering guests, inspired by a recent experience
