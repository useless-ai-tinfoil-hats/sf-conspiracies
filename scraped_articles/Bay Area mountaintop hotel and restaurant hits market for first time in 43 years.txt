Bay Area mountaintop hotel and restaurant hits market for first time in 43 years
-----
A storied hotel and restaurant, perched on the edge of Mount Tamalpais, is hitting the market for the first time in nearly half a century this week.With its panoramic views of Marin County and tranquil setting on a foggy ridgeline about 10 miles north of San Francisco, the Mountain Home Inn has been an escape for travelers, hikers and locals since it was built by a Swiss German couple on the steep hillside in 1912.
