PG&E rates could rise again after California regulatory vote
-----
State regulators Thursday are voting on another bill hike for Pacific Gas and Electric customers that could add just under $5 a month for typical households, adding to significantly higher bills that hit in January.PG&E asked for the increase to recover expenses paid for projects in 2020 through 2022 that mostly dealt with wildfire prevention and modernizing the company's infrastructure. PG&E said most of those projects were mandated by the state.
