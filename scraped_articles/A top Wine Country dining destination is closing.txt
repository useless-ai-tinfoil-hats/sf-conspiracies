A top Wine Country dining destination is closing
-----
Animo, an acclaimed Wine Country fine dining restaurant known for its unusual combination of Korean and Basque cuisines, will shut down next month.The Sonoma restaurant will close on Dec. 17, the owners announced on Instagram, so they can focus on the arrival of their newest project, Golden Bear Station in Kenwood. But Animo will reopen next summer in an undisclosed location in Sonoma, said co-owner Joshua Smookler.
