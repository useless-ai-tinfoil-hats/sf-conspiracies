Pets Best Pet Insurance review: Pros, cons, and pricing
-----
Pets Best Pet Insurance at a glancePets Best gives pet parents a ton of options. One of the best pet insurance companies out there, it offers a full suite of plans (accident-only, accident-and-illness, and wellness policies) at affordable prices. Its accident-and-illness plans come with a wide range of deductibles and reimbursement rates. Still, expect fewer choices when it comes to selecting a coverage limit and a long(ish) claims process.
