S.F. election: Prop. A, $300 million affordable-housing bond, passes
-----
An affordable-housing bond that will pump $300 million into subsidized homes has passed, with Proposition A grabbing 70% of the vote. It required a two-thirds majority to pass, and supporters were worried it would not make it.Supported by a coalition of politicos from across the San Francisco political spectrum, the bond funds will be split into three buckets to help the city meet its goal of building more than 80,000 units of housing by 2031.
