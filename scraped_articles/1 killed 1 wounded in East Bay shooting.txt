1 killed, 1 wounded in East Bay shooting
-----
Gunfire left one person dead and another injured in Fremont on Sunday, police said.Eyewitnesses told officers called to the scene on the 34000 block of Fremont Boulevard near Interstate 880 around 4 p.m. that two shooting victims had been transported to a local hospital, Fremont police said in a statement. ABC7 identified the location as Motel 6.
One victim was declared dead at the hospital, police said. His name has not been made public.
