Giants to bring Pablo Sandoval to spring training as a nonroster invite
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — The San Francisco Giants have many of their marquee former players — Dave Righetti, Will Clark, Barry Bonds, Hunter Pence — coming to help in camp this spring and now they'll have a popular member of their three title teams on hand, too.Pablo Sandoval, who last played in the majors three years ago, will be coming in as a nonroster invitee, as first reported by NBC Sports Bay Area's Alex Pavlovic and confirmed by the Chronicle.
