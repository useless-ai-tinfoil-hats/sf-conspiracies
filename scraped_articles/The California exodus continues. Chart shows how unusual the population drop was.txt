The California exodus continues. Chart shows how unusual the population drop was
-----
The number of people living in California fell below 39 million this year, according to new census estimates, the lowest count since 2015.California's population dipped by about 75,000 from 2022 to 2023, estimates released Tuesday by the Census Bureau shows, with about 38,965,000 million people in the state this year. The state's population has fallen since its 2019 peak of 39.5 million, though the annual loss has also slowed each year.
