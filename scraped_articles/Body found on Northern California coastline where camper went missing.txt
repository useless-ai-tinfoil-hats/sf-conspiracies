Body found on Northern California coastline where camper went missing
-----
Rescue workers searching for a missing camper in Mendocino County discovered a body trapped in rocks on the ocean shoreline this weekend but were unable to recover it due to dangerous surf conditions.The body was found near Hardy Creek Beach in Westport, where Rebecca Jones was camping before she disappeared Friday morning, the Mendocino County Sheriff's Office said.
