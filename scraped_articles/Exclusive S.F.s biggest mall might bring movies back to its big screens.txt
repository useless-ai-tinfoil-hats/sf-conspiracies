Exclusive: S.F.'s biggest mall might bring movies back to its big screens
-----
Memories of the nine-screen theater built into the fifth floor of San Francisco's largest downtown mall have faded. The big screens that once inspired movie lovers to brave multiple flights of escalators have sat dark behind locked doors for the past year.But, in a surprising turn of events, an encore could be in store as an individual with knowledge of ongoing leasing efforts at the former Westfield mall has confirmed that a new theater operator is closing in on the deserted space.
