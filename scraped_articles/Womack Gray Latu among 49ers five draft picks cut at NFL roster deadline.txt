Cut-down day was doomsday for a host of recent San Francisco 49ers draft picks.In the process of paring their roster to 53 players Tuesday, the 49ers parted with five players they selected in their nine-man draft classes of 2022 and 2023. The team waived wide receiver Danny Gray (2022, third round), cornerback Samuel Womack (2022, fifth), linebacker Jalen Graham (2023, seventh) and tight ends Cameron Latu (2023, third) and Brayden Willis (2023, seventh).
