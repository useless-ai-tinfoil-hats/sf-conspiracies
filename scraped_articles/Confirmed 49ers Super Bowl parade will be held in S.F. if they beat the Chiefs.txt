Confirmed: 49ers Super Bowl parade will be held in S.F. if they beat the Chiefs
-----
It's official: if the San Francisco 49ers emerge victorious in Super Bowl LVIII against the Kansas City Chiefs this Sunday, the ensuing celebration and parade will unfold in San Francisco, the team's hometown, rather than in Santa Clara, where the team's home stadium is located.The San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency updated its log of special event street closures this week to incorporate a potential parade route down Market Street, followed by festivities in Civic Center
