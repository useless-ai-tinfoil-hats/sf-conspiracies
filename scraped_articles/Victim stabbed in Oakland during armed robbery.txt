Victim stabbed in Oakland during armed robbery
-----
Oakland police are investigating an armed robbery that left a victim with stab wounds Friday afternoon.Police responded to the 2880 block of High Street just after 5 p.m. Friday and found a victim who had been approached from behind by two people, one of whom pointed a gun at him. One of the robbers stabbed the victim and took his belongings before the pair fled.
Paramedics treated the victim and transported him to a local hospital, where he was listed in stable condition Saturday.
