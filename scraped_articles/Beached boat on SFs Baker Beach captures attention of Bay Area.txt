Beached boat on SF's Baker Beach captures attention of Bay Area
-----
A boat named the Lorelei washed up at San Francisco's Baker Beach on Monday, and the romantic scene, with a stranded vessel on the city's prettiest stretch of sand and the Golden Gate Bridge in the background, has quickly caught the attention of the Bay Area, with photographs popping up on social media."It looks like something out of a movie, it's so neat," said Capt. Justin Schorr, a spokesperson for the San Francisco Fire Department.
