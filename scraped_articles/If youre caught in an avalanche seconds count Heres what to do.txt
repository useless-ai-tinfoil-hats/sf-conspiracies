-----
When an avalanche overtakes someone, survival often comes down to a matter of minutes.According to the National Avalanche Center, an avalanche, defined as "a rapid flow of snow down a hill or mountainside," can occur on any steep slope but is more likely under certain conditions.
Experts called Wednesday's fatal avalanche at Palisades Tahoe unusual, striking within resort boundaries when snow had been relatively thin and low in water content.
