Smoke from Sites Fire impacting air quality in Bay Area
-----
Smoke from the Sites Fire continued to filter into the region this morning, impacting air quality in Sonoma, Napa, Contra Costa and Santa Clara counties. By midday on Tuesday, dozens of monitoring stations in Sonoma, Concord and Walnut Creek were registering moderate to high amounts of particulate matter in the air.
