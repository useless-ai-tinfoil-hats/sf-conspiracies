Oakland faces worsening budget deficit, likely triggering cuts in services
-----
Less than a year after staring down a record budget deficit, Oakland is facing a worsening financial picture that has opened up an unexpected $177 million hole in the city's two-year fiscal plan as it struggles to turn the corner on pandemic-era economic setbacks and battle rising crime.Oakland's two-year, $4.2 billion budget, agreed to last June, managed to avoid layoffs and major service cutbacks by freezing hiring and consolidating departments.
