East Bay refinery spews unhealthy dust for 4th time in a year
-----
An oil refinery in Martinez spewed petroleum coke dust into the air Friday morning, the latest in a series of emissions problems at the facility since last year.Martinez Refining Company officials alerted the public about noon Friday on social media that a problem had occurred earlier that morning "when equipment was opened for maintenance."
The Contra Costa County health department's hazmat team and regulators with the Bay Area Air Quality Management District were investigating.
