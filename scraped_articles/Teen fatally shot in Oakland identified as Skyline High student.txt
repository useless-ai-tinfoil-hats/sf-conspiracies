Teen fatally shot in Oakland identified as Skyline High student
-----
A 16-year-old boy who was fatally shot in Oakland last week was a Skyline High School student who "got along with everyone," his mother said.Nathan Chanthavong was shot around 1:30 a.m. June 10, on Filbert Street, not far from Lowell Park, according to police, who released his name Monday.
