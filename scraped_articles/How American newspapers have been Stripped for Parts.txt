How American newspapers have been ‘Stripped for Parts’
-----
Every time East Bay filmmaker Rick Goldsmith drives by the Tribune Tower, he is reminded that it used to house one of the most storied newspapers in the Bay Area. Founded in 1874, the winner of two Pulitzer Prizes, the Oakland Tribune died abruptly on April 4, 2016, when the hedge firm Alden Global Capital folded it and three other papers into a new entity, the East Bay Times.
