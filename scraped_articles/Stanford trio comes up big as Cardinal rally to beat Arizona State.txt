Stanford trio comes up big as Cardinal rally to beat Arizona State
-----
Brandon Angel and Maxime Reynaud posted double-doubles and Michael Jones hit five 3-pointers and scored 18 points as Stanford rallied to beat Arizona State 71-62 in Tempe on Thursday night.Angel had 19 points and 10 rebounds, notching his first double-double of the season for the Cardinal (11-9, 6-4 Pac-12). Reynaud finished with 12 points and a career-best 17 rebounds for his eighth double-double. Jones matched his season high from beyond the arc, missing twice.
