BART operator stops moving train in S.F. after passenger and leashed dog separated by train doors
-----
A dog and its owner became trapped on opposite sides of a closed train door Wednesday at San Francisco's Civic Center Station before a BART worker halted the moving train.The leashed dog and its owner were uninjured after the operator saw what was happening and reopened the doors, Bay Area Rapid Transit spokesperson Alicia Trost said.
