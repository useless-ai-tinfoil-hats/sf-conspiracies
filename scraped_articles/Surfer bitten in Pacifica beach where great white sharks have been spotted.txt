Surfer bitten in Pacifica beach where great white sharks have been spotted
-----
Officials in Pacifica responded to a report of a surfer being bitten at a beach where great white sharks have been known to swim around.Around 3:45 p.m. Friday, officials with the Pacifica Police Department and the North County Fire Authority received a call from a surfer who reported possibly being bitten by a shark at Linda Mar Beach.
