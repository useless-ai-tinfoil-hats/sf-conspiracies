Famed bar Trick Dog bringing cocktails and hot dogs to new S.F. restaurant
-----
A spinoff from one of San Francisco's top hot dog spots will be opening its own permanent location next year.Josh Harris, founder of celebrated Mission District cocktail bar Trick Dog and hospitality management company Bon Vivants announced on Instagram its popular pandemic-era takeout project, Quik Dog, will serve its sought-after hot dogs, crispy fries and cocktails at a new location in Mission Rock, a development adjacent to the the S.F. Giants' Oracle Park.
