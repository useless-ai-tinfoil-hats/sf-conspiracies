'King of the Culinary Airwaves': Bay Area food legend Narsai David dies
-----
Bay Area food legend Narsai David, the "King of the Culinary Airwaves," died last week, KCBS radio announced. He was 87.Long before David's warm, friendly voice became a constant presence on the radio station, his life was already worthy of a biopic. He was born in South Bend, Indiana, but the Davids decamped to Turlock when they learned David's father had been diagnosed with leukemia. There, 11-year-old David and his family were embraced by the large Assyrian community.
