40 'prized' turtles discovered at California mail facility
-----
A man has been arrested in connection with the discovery of protected turtles contained in falsely labeled packages in a California mail facility, officials said.On Feb. 25, Sai Keung Tin, 53, was arrested in New York on suspicion of attempting to smuggle approximately 40 eastern box turtles from the U.S. to Hong Kong last year, according to a March 11 news release from the U.S. Department of Justice.
