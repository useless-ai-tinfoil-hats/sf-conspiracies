How a Chronicle photographer got these stunning Yosemite Firefall images
-----
No embers, flames or fire are involved in Yosemite's annual firefall but the naturally occurring phenomenon is drawing thousands of visitors to see and photograph the event at Horsetail Falls.Firefall occurs in Yosemite Valley on February evenings as the setting sun hits the falling waters at just the right angle, and casts a reddish glow that resembles fire.
