Warriors waive 3, will sign 2; all expected to start season in G League
-----
The Golden State Warriors waived guard Kendric Davis, forward Javan Johnson and forward Donovan Williams on Monday.The Chronicle confirmed that the Warriors will sign Javonte Green and Yuri Collins to fill two roster spots ahead of their fourth preseason game against the Sacramento Kings at Chase Center on Wednesday. Golden State's training camp roster will be at 20 players once those signings become official.
