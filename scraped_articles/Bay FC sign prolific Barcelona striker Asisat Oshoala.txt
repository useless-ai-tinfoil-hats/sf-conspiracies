Bay FC sign prolific Barcelona striker Asisat Oshoala
-----
Bay FC made one of its largest signings to date on Thursday, with the NWSL expansion team adding Barcelona striker Asisat Oshoala via transfer.Oshoala, who represents Nigeria internationally, signed through 2026 with an option for 2027. She is a six-time African women's footballer of the year and ranks fourth all-time in scoring in Barcelona history.
