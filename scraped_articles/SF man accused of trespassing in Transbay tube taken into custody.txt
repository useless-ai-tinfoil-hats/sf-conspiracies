SF man accused of trespassing in Transbay tube, taken into custody
-----
A San Francisco man accused of trespassing in the Transbay tube during the Wednesday evening commute was taken into custody Thursday, BART announced on X, formerly Twitter.Jerry Howard of San Francisco entered the Transbay tube by pulling a emergency door release while a train was moving through the tube Wednesday, BART said. Train service through the tube was halted in both direction for hours after alarms sounded.
Police conducted a search and reviewed surveillance camera
