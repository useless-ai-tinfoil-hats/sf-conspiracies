Earthquakes secure rare playoff berth with 1-1 tie against Austin FC
-----
A capacity crowd of 18,000 filled PayPal Park on Saturday night. The fans arrived early, waved flags, beat on drums and loudly exhorted their team to complete an uncommon achievement: make the Major League Soccer playoffs.The San Jose Earthquakes complied, even if it wasn't entirely satisfying.
