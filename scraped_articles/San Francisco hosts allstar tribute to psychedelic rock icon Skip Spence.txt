San Francisco hosts all-star tribute to psychedelic rock icon Skip Spence
-----
An all-star tribute concert this weekend will honor one of the most obscure but seminal figures in the San Francisco psychedelic rock scene.The Chapel in San Francisco will host a tribute to Skip Spence on Saturday, June 15.
