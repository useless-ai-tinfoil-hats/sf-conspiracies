Major Leonardo DiCaprio movie has officially begun filming in Northern California
-----
Production appears to be underway in Northern California for the Leonardo DiCaprio movie with the working title "BC Project" after residents reported clusters of police cars, camera crews and Warner Bros. reps in the Redwood Region earlier this week. The film also stars Sean Penn and Regina Hall, and is written and directed by auteur Paul Thomas Anderson, who is known for classics like "Boogie Nights," "There Will Be Blood" and his latest coming-of-age romance "Licorice Pizza."Details
