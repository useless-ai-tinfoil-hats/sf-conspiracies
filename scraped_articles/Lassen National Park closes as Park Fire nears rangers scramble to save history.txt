Lassen National Park closes as Park Fire nears, rangers scramble to save history
-----
Lassen National Park closed Saturday afternoon as the advancing Park Fire races through California's dry terrain at a nearly unprecedented rate. In only three days, the Park Fire became the seventh-largest wildfire in state history, tearing through 5,000 acres an hour."Visitors have been evacuated from all campgrounds, and reservations have been canceled," Lassen National Park officials said in a statement.
