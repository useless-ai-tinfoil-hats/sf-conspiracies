What to know about the APEC lane closures on the SF-Oakland Bay Bridge
-----
San Francisco drivers will endure heavy congestion as several freeway offramps and lanes, including on the Bay Bridge, are set to close starting Tuesday. The closures are due to the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation conference, one of the world's largest global leader summits.APEC, which hosts heads of state including President Joe Biden, President Xi Jinping of China and President Andrés Manuel López Obrador of Mexico, is categorized as a National Special Security Event by the U.S.
