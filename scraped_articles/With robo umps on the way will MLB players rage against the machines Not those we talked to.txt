With robo umps on the way, will MLB players rage against the machines? Not those we talked to
-----
The robo-ump is coming to Major League Baseball, maybe as soon as 2026. The lords of the game, empowered by the general acceptance — embrace, even — of major tweaks such as the universal designated hitter and the pitch clock, are making plans to do more heavy tinkering with the grand old game.So cue the march of the robo umps, who will, ideally, energize baseball by getting more balls in play, creating more action in a sport that had been racing in the other direction in recent years.
