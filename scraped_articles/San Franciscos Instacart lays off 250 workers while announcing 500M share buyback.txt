San Francisco's Instacart lays off 250 workers while announcing $500M share buyback
-----
Instacart is laying off 250 employees, the San Francisco-based grocery delivery company announced Tuesday.The layoff round will hit 7% of the company's global workforce, according to a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission from Tuesday. Instacart announced the cuts alongside its quarterly financial report, just the company's second since going public through an initial public offering in September.
