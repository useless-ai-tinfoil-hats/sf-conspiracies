Assembly Member Evan Low joins crowded Bay Area House race
-----
Assembly Member Evan Low officially filed paperwork Monday to replace retiring Rep. Anna Eshoo, joining a crowded field jostling to win a rare open Bay Area congressional seat.Low, hopes to become the first LGBTQ House member elected from the Bay Area. Ten openly LGBTQ members serve in the House, after former New York Rep. George Santos was expelled last week.
