Super Bowl viewers baffled by 'foot fetish' commercial
-----
An evangelical Christian group's multimillion dollar Super Bowl ad may have backfired. Their mistake? Making it all about feet.The ad, purchased by an evangelical Christian campaign called He Gets Us, is a one-minute slideshow of photos of people washing each other's feet.
A high schooler pours the contents of her water bottle onto her friend's feet. A police officer washes a man's feet in an alleyway. One protester washes another's feet in the middle of a shouting crowd.
