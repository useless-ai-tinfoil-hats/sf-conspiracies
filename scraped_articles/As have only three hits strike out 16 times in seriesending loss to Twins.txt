A's have only three hits, strike out 16 times in series-ending loss to Twins
-----
The Oakland Athletics picked up Sunday where they left off Saturday at the Coliseum, where for the second straight game they couldn't seem to solve the starting pitching for the Minnesota Twins.The A's mustered two hits against Minnesota's Pablo Lopez, striking out 14 times against the 28-year-old right-hander and 16 times overall during a 3-0 loss in the series finale.
