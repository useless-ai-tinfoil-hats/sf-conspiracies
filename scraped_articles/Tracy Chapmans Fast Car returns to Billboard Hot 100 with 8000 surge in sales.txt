Tracy Chapman’s ‘Fast Car’ returns to Billboard Hot 100 with 8,000% surge in sales
-----
Tracy Chapman's captivating performance of "Fast Car" alongside country sensation Luke Combs at the 66th Grammy Awards has — perhaps unintentionally — sparked a revival in the reclusive San Francisco singer-songwriter's music career.The original version of "Fast Car," released 36 years ago, shot back onto the Billboard Hot 100, securing the No. 42 spot this week, bolstered by a surge in streams and sales of the classic track, after her rare public appearance onstage at Crypto.com Arena
