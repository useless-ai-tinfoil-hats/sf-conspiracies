Irish genes are strong: Visit to ‘green family’ helps fill in details of ancestry

California is a complicated place. Some people have families who have been in California for generations — native sons, like me. Some are children of immigrants. I am both. It's the California story.The Golden State is a curious place, like a garden with deep roots mixed with transplants. Half the people here are from someplace else. Californians have their feet in two worlds — some in the American Midwest, some in the African American South, some in Mexico, or China, or the Philippines.
