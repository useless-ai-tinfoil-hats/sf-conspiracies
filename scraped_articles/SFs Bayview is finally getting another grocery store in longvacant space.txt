SF's Bayview is finally getting another grocery store in long-vacant space
-----
Mi Rancho Supermarket, a local Mexican grocery chain, is opening its first San Francisco location in the city's Bayview neighborhood. The grocery store is slated to open in the long-vacant space at 5900 3rd St., formerly home to Duc Loi's Pantry, as first reported by the San Francisco Standard.SFGATE could not reach Mi Rancho for comment in time for publication, but the company confirmed to the Standard that it plans to open the new store in the coming months.
