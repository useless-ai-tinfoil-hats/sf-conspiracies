The latest plan to save downtown San Francisco? Street musicians
-----
The latest effort to breathe life into San Francisco's struggling Mid-Market neighborhood involves bringing amateur musicians to the streets.The nonprofit Mid-Market Business Association unveiled a new initiative, Busk It!, on Thursday, Feb. 29. The project aims to attract local musicians for weekly live performances on the sidewalks from Fifth to Eighth streets, running from March through November.
