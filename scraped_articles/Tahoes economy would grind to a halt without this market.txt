Tahoe's economy would 'grind to a halt' without this market
-----
All afternoon on the day before a holiday, when tourism really starts to slam Lake Tahoe, the parking lot outside La Mexicana Meat Market & Taqueria in Kings Beach repeatedly turned over like a spit, with locals and visitors alike. They stopped by the small, backstreet grocery store for eats or errands that only this North Shore community pillar can provide.A man from Reno waited for carne asada tacos by the taqueria window as Alicia Espinoza placed a lunch order, flanked by her children
