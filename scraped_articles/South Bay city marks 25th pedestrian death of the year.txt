South Bay city marks 25th pedestrian death of the year
-----
A pedestrian was struck and killed by a vehicle in San Jose early Sunday, authorities said.A vehicle collided with a pedestrian at 12:34 a.m. at the intersection of Capitol Expressway and Copperfield Drive, the San Jose Police Department said on X, formerly called Twitter.
The victim was taken to a local hospital and died of injuries, police said. The victim's identity has not been released.
