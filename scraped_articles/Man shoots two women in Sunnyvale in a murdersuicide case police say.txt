Man shoots two women in Sunnyvale in a murder-suicide case, police say
-----
A murder-suicide investigation is underway after a man shot two women in Sunnyvale on Monday, killing one, before shooting and killing himself, according to police.Officers responded to a report of gunshots in the 1700 block of Karameos Drive around 5:28 a.m. Upon arriving at the scene, officers found two female victims with gunshot wounds. One of the victims died at the scene, while the second victim was transported to a local hospital, police said.
