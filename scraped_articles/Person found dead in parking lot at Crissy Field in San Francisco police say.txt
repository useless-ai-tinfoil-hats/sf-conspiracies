Person found dead in parking lot at Crissy Field in San Francisco, police say
-----
Authorities are investigating the death of a person whose body was found near the East Beach parking lot at Crissy Field in San Francisco on Sunday morning.U.S. park police and the San Francisco Fire Department were dispatched to 1199 East Beach at approximately 6:30 a.m. after receiving a report about the individual, who was not immediately identified. The San Francisco Fire Department said it was unable to release any details regarding possible causes.
