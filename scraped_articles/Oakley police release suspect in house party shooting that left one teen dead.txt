Oakley police release suspect in house party shooting that left one teen dead
-----
Oakley police have released the man they previously suspected of shooting four teenagers at a party, killing one.On Friday, Police Chief Paul Beard announced the 18-year-old man who had been arrested on suspicion of committing the shooting was released from custody "pending further developments in our investigation."
"This latest development is always considered to be a possibility in major cases like ours where there are large amounts of evidence to process and sort through,"
