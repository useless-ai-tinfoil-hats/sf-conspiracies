Should S.F.'s mayor get more power? Here's what one powerful moderate group is pushing
-----
A moderate political advocacy group has halted plans for a November ballot measure aimed at strengthening the power of San Francisco's mayor.The We Need SF to Work Initiative would have given the mayor the sole authority to appoint and remove members of most of the city's commissions and delegate responsibilities to deputy mayors.
