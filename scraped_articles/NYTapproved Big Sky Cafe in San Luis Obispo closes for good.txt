NYT-approved Big Sky Cafe in San Luis Obispo closes for good
-----
Big Sky Cafe, a New York Times-featured restaurant in downtown San Luis Obispo, closed permanently after service on Oct. 8. A sign posted on the front door alerted patrons of the news. The restaurant, which fed tourists, locals and college students alike, was nearly 30 years old, according to the San Luis Obispo Tribune."To our beloved customers, it is with a heavy heart that we have made the difficult decision to close our beloved restaurant, Big Sky Cafe," read the sign.
