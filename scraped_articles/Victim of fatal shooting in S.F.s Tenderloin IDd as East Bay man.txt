Victim of fatal shooting in S.F.’s Tenderloin ID’d as East Bay man
-----
San Francisco police have identified the victim of a fatal Tenderloin shooting last weekend as an East Bay man.Around 4:20 a.m. Saturday, San Francisco police officers responded to a reported gunfight on the 200 block of Golden Gate Avenue. They found two male gunshot victims, both of whom were taken to a hospital, police said.
One of the men died at the hospital, officials said. The medical examiner identified him as Jose Valle Rodriguez, a 40-year-old Alameda County resident.
