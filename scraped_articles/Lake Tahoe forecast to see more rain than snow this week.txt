Lake Tahoe forecast to see more rain than snow this week
-----
The Tahoe basin straddling California and Nevada is forecast to record more rain than snow this week as a pair of warm and wet systems sweep the Sierra Nevada on Monday through Wednesday.Snow levels are expected to hover between 8,000 and 8,500 throughout the duration of the first two systems, likely dropping to about 7,000 feet for a period Tuesday night into Wednesday, the National Weather Service said.
