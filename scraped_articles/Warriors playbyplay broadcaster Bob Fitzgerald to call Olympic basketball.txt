Warriors play-by-play broadcaster Bob Fitzgerald to call Olympic basketball
-----
Golden State Warriors play-by-play broadcaster Bob Fitzgerald was tabbed the play-by-play voice broadcaster for Olympic basketball in Paris for NBCUniversal, the team announced Monday afternoon. The Olympics begin July 26 and conclude Aug. 11.Fitzgerald has called five Summer Olympics for NBCUniversal, broadcasting basketball in 2004 on radio, 2012 and 2020, water polo in 2008 and swimming in 1996.
