Warriors convert Lester Quiñones’ two-way deal into standard NBA contract
-----
Second-year guard Lester Quiñones earned a role in the Golden State Warriors' rotation.Now the two-way guard has earned a standard NBA contract.
The Warriors intend to convert Quiñones' deal, according to a league source — allowing the 23-year-old combo guard the 14th spot on the roster. Golden State had until Thursday to fill one of its remaining two roster spots after trading away reserve guard Cory Joseph.
