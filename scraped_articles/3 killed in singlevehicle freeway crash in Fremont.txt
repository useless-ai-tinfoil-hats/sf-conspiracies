3 killed in single-vehicle freeway crash in Fremont
-----
A driver and two passengers were killed early Sunday in a single-vehicle crash on Interstate 880 in Fremont.The vehicle was headed north just before 6 a.m. when the driver crashed into a concrete barrier near the Warren Avenue overpass, according to the California Highway Patrol.
The cause of the crash is under investigation. No further details were immediately available.
