East Bay attorney convicted of embezzling $400K from PG&E fire victims
-----
In 2013, Pacific Gas and Electric Co. agreed to pay $400,000 to settle two cases with families affected by a fire north of Sacramento. Instead, their lawyer spent the money on himself, the Contra Costa County District Attorney's Office argued.A Martinez jury on Tuesday found that attorney, William James Reed, guilty of two counts of felony grand theft by embezzlement. Reed, 80, faces imprisonment for three years and eight months.
