Best pizza in the US includes 2 SF restaurants, according to TimeOut list
-----
Two San Francisco restaurants and one Los Angeles-based pizzeria are among the best in the U.S., according to TimeOut.In a recent best-of list that featured deep dish, Neapolitan and New York-style pizzas, TimeOut included San Francisco restaurants Flour + Water Pizzeria (No. 4) Tony's Pizza Napoletana (No. 13), and Pizzeria Sei in Los Angeles (No. 9) among 19 top pizzerias around the U.S.
