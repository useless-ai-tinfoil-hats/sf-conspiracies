11-year-old drowns in East Bay creek
-----
An 11-year-old girl drowned in Alameda Creek near Fremont last week, authorities said Tuesday.Fremont police officers responded to Alameda Creek, adjacent to Niles Canyon Road and north of Mission Boulevard, just before 6 p.m. for a report of a drowning, according to the Alameda County Sheriff's Office, which is investigating the incident.
