S.F. North Beach’s street fair season off and running with Festa Italiana
-----
Her lungs burning as she climbed toward the finish of Sunday's 8K Statuto Race, Chloe Glare-Conti could see the finish lane in the form of a row of 3-pound salami sticks suspended high over Stockton Street in San Francisco."You see the hanging salamis, and you hear the crowd," she said afterward as she held a trophy in one hand and the first prize from Columbus Meats in the other — her reward for winning the over-40 division. "You've got to go hard to win the big salami."
