Santa Rosa freshman hospitalized after stabbing at high school
-----
A high school student was taken to the hospital after another student stabbed him at a Santa Rosa high school Friday, according to the Santa Rosa Police Department.Around 12:30 p.m., police responded to a reported stabbing on the campus of Elsie Allen High School. During a fight between two students, both freshmen boys, one stabbed the other with a folding knife, police said.
