2-year-old dies after being hit by truck in Yolo County, police say
-----
A 2-year-old was fatally stuck by a truck Saturday afternoon in Woodland (Yolo County), according to local police.Officers arrived at the site of the crash, a gas station on the 300 block of West Street, around 3:45 p.m., but by the time they arrived, the child was already being taken to the hospital by family members, the Woodland Police Department said in a statement. The child died of injuries at the hospital.
