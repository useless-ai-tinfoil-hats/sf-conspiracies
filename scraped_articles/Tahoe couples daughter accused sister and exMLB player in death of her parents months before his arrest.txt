Tahoe couple’s daughter accused sister and ex-MLB player in death of her parents months before his arrest
-----
The daughter of a prominent couple shot in Lake Tahoe two years ago, leaving the father dead and the mother wounded, quietly sued her sister and her sister's husband, former professional baseball player Daniel "Danny" Serafini, in the spring — accusing them, months before Serafini's arrest last week, of murdering or helping to murder her parents.One purpose of the lawsuit, reviewed by the Chronicle, is apparently to keep Serafini and his wife, Erin Spohr, from collecting inheritance
