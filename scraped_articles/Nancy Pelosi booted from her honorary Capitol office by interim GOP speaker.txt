Nancy Pelosi booted from her honorary Capitol office by interim GOP speaker
-----
In his first action as speaker pro tempore, Rep. Patrick McHenry, R-N.C., ordered Speaker Emerita Nancy Pelosi to vacate her Capitol hideaway office Tuesday evening, according to a spokesperson for her office."The speaker pro tempore is going to re-assign H-132 for speaker office use. Please vacate the space tomorrow, the room will be re-keyed," read an email, reviewed by the Chronicle, from an aide on the House Administration Committee to Pelosi's office.
