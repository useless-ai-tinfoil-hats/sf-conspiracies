Robbie Ray excited to join Giants’ rotation, but ‘couple of bats wouldn’t hurt’
-----
January is quite the month for new San Francisco Giants left-hander Robbie Ray. The 2021 American League Cy Young winner was traded from Seattle to San Francisco five days into the new year, and five days later, his wife, Taylor, gave birth to their fourth child, a daughter named Sophia."It's been quite a whirlwind," Ray said on a video call with reporters Friday. "But my wife is a rock star. She's awesome. She handles it all."
