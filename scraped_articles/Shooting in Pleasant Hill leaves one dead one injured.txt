Shooting in Pleasant Hill leaves one dead, one injured
-----
Two men were shot, one fatally, on a street in a Pleasant Hill neighborhood Tuesday, authorities said.The shooting was reported just before noon and occurred in the area of Twinbridge Circle near Longbrook Way, near the border with Walnut Creek. Witnesses told police they heard multiple gunshots and saw two victims lying on the ground injured, police said.
According to scanner feeds from the incident, the shooting took place near 313 Twinbridge Circle.
