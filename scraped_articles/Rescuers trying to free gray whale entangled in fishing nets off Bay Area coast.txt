Rescuers trying to free gray whale entangled in fishing nets off Bay Area coast
-----
Rescuers on Wednesday were hoping to locate and free a gray whale entangled in fishing nets and buoys off the Bay Area Peninsula coast, according to the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.The whale, spotted most recently on Tuesday off Pacifica and Daly City, was first seen off Laguna Beach (Orange County) on March 22, according to a statement from Oceana, an ocean conservation advocacy group.
