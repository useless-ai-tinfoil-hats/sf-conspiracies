Fallen Disneyland light pole injuries three, one severely
-----
A Disneyland light pole fell over Monday morning on Main Street, injuring three people, officials said.The Anaheim Fire Department arrived at the park just before 8:30 a.m. for a report of the fallen lamppost, Jon McClintock, a spokesperson for the department, told SFGATE. Fire and rescue officials said one person was transported to a local hospital with severe injuries and two people suffered minor injuries. The two individuals with minor injuries were treated at the park and released
