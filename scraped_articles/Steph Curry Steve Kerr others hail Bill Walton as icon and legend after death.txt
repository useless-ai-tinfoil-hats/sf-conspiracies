Steph Curry, Steve Kerr, others hail Bill Walton as ‘icon’ and ‘legend’ after death
-----
NBA champion and broadcaster Bill Walton, who died Monday, is being remembered for his larger-than-life personality and his impact both on and off the court, as athletes and sports teams in the Bay Area and people across the country mourned the former UCLA center and celebrated his legacy.Walton's death was announced shortly after 10 a.m. by NBA Commissioner Adam Silver, who said Walton had a prolonged bout with cancer. He was 71.
"Bill Walton was truly one of a kind," Silver said.
