Hayward police shoot, kill man armed with BB gun
-----
Hayward officers fatally shot a man who was armed with a BB gun, police spokespeople said.Officers were dispatched to the 22000 block of City Center Drive at 9:40 p.m. Wednesday after reports that a man had pointed a gun at several people. When officers arrived they tried to speak with the man, who pointed what appeared to be a firearm in the direction of officers, spokespeople said during a news conference Thursday afternoon.
