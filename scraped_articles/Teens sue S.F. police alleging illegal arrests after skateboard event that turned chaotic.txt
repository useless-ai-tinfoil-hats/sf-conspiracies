Teens sue S.F. police alleging ‘illegal’ arrests after skateboard event that turned chaotic
-----
Four teens filed a lawsuit Tuesday seeking class action status against San Francisco police alleging they wrongfully arrested a group of young participants and revelers after a summer skateboarding event known as the "Dolores Hill Bomb" turned chaotic.The lawsuit, filed in San Francisco federal court, alleges officers in riot gear needlessly "corralled" teens and young adults and detained them for hours in the cold without legal grounds in August at the event.
