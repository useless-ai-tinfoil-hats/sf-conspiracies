Buzzy craft brewery opening S.F. waterfront taproom
-----
A popular Northern California brewery known for its "foggy IPAs" will open its first San Francisco location.Humble Sea Brewing Co. will open at Pier 39, confirmed company spokesperson Lee DeGraw. The waterfront location will accommodate around 80 seated customers. A final number of taps is still to be decided, but the company's sought-after foggy IPAs, Humble Sea's term for hazy IPAs, and more beers will be available by the pour and in cans.
