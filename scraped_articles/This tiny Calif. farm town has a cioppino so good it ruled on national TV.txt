Nearly 110 miles south of San Francisco is a tiny unincorporated town surrounded by fields of greens and scenic views. You've likely driven past the 1.02-square-mile Castroville on the way to Monterey or Carmel-by-the-Sea, without ever thinking twice to pull over. But if you don't hit the brakes, you'll miss out on some great food in the "Artichoke Center of the World."The city slogan is no exaggeration.
