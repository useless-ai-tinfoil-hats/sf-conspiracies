SF Pride Parade draws thousands celebrating LGBTQ community: ‘We love our people’
-----
Thousands of people came out Sunday for San Francisco's Pride Parade, a rainbow-colored love letter to the LGBTQ community that attracts celebrants from all over the world.Spectators lined up along Market Street hours in advance for SF Pride, one of the largest LGBTQ celebrations in the world and the culmination of weeks of events. "Beacon of Love," this year's SF Pride theme, resonated with many because of the tense political climate in the U.S. and abroad.
