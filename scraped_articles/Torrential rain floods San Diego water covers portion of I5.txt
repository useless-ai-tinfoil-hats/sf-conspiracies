'Torrential rain' floods San Diego, water covers portion of I-5
-----
A burst of heavy rain caused flash floods that covered roadways and inundated homes across San Diego County on Monday morning and afternoon. With rains continuing to fall over the region in the late afternoon, the city of San Diego was on track to record nearly a third of its annual rainfall in a single day."Torrential rain from today is leading to flooded roads, including Interstate 5 in downtown San Diego," the National Weather Service shared on social media just before 2:30 p.m.
