S.F. landlords fear for APEC summit security’s effect on ‘alarmed’ senior residents
-----
The owners of more than half a dozen buildings that house and serve San Francisco seniors said residents are "very alarmed" about security measures announced last week in light of the coming Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit.San Francisco will host the high-profile event — which is expected to draw more than 20,000 visitors (including executives, politicians and diplomats) downtown — during the week of Nov. 12.
