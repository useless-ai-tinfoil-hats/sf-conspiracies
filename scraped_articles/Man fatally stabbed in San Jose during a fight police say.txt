Man fatally stabbed in San Jose during a fight, police say
-----
San Jose police are investigating a homicide in which a man was stabbed during a fight Tuesday.Around 4:31 p.m. Tuesday, officers responded to a hospital for a report that a stabbing victim was dropped off at an emergency room. Despite receiving medical care, the man was pronounced dead at the hospital, police said Wednesday.
Police discovered the stabbing occurred in the area of Spring and Taylor streets. The victim was involved in a physical altercation when he was stabbed,
