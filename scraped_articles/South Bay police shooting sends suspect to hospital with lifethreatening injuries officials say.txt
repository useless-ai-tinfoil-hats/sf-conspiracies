South Bay police shooting sends suspect to hospital with ‘life-threatening’ injuries, officials say
-----
San Jose officers shot a man who was "actively firing a firearm" Sunday afternoon, sending the suspect to the hospital with major injuries, the police department said.Officers opened fire while responding to reports of an active shooter on the 2700 block of Kollmar Drive, the department said. Police did not provide details, and the circumstances of the shooting were unclear.
