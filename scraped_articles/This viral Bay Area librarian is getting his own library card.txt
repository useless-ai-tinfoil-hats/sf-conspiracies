This viral Bay Area librarian is getting his own library card
-----
Bay Area book lovers can soon get a limited edition library card featuring viral sensation Mychal Threets.Threets is the former Solano County librarian and social media star who is known for sharing uplifting stories between the bookshelves. He shared in an Instagram video on Aug. 11 that he was surprised by Berkeley Public Library with a giant library card with his portrait that says, "Library Love is Revolutionary!"
