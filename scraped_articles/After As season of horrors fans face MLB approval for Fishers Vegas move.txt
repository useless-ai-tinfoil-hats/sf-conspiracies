After A’s season of horrors, fans face MLB approval for Fisher’s Vegas move
-----
The closer we get to the day the Oakland Athletics are officially lame ducks, the less sense it all makes.Did you catch the playoff attendance figures for the Tampa Bay Rays? The Game 1 crowd was 19,704. Game 2 was 20,198. That's a combined 39,902 fans to watch the Rays get swept by Bruce Bochy's Rangers — or 14,103 fewer than what the A's drew in their last non-pandemic playoff game.
And the Rays are the team getting a new ballpark? And the A's are the team that's relocating?
