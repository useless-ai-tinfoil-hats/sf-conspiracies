S.F. drug dealer who had 15 pounds of fentanyl gets 6 years in federal prison
-----
A man convicted of selling drugs in San Francisco's Tenderloin neighborhood was sentenced to more than six years in prison, prosecutors said Wednesday.U.S. District Judge Charles R. Breyer sentenced Oakland resident Melvin Alexis Diaz Arteaga, 30, to 78 months in federal prison, followed by four years of supervised release.
