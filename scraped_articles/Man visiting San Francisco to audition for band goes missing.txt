Man visiting San Francisco to audition for band goes missing
-----
A man who came to San Francisco to audition for a band has been missing for nearly a week.Eric Travers, 30, was visiting San Francisco from Asheville, North Carolina. After meeting with the band, he checked into the Cathedral Hill Hotel in the Tenderloin. He reportedly left the hotel and never returned. He last contacted his brother Kyle Travers on April 30, according to a missing person poster and GoFundMe page set up by his brother.
