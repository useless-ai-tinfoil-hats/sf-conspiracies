Warriors blow double-figure lead as Nuggets complete four-game season sweep
-----
The sense of inevitability the Golden State Warriors once had belongs to the Denver Nuggets.The Warriors on Sunday squandered a 16-point first-half lead to the defending NBA champions, falling 119-103 at Chase Center in the last of four games between the teams this season.
The Warriors haven't beaten the Nuggets in the regular season since Mar. 10, 2022, allowing a 28-4 run Sunday that covered the second and third quarters during their 10th loss to Denver in the past 11 meetings.
