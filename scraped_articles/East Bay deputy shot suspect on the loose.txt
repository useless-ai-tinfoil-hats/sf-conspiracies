East Bay deputy shot, suspect on the loose
-----
An Alameda County Sheriff's Office deputy was shot while serving an eviction notice, according to Union City police.Police said deputies were serving an eviction notice in the 33000 block of Dowe Avenue just after 1:30 p.m. Wednesday when a person began firing a weapon at them. Officials did not immediately disclose whether the suspect in the shooting was the person being evicted.
One deputy was shot and suffered non-life-threatening injuries and was taken to a local hospital,
