Giants bringing back popular Dusty Baker as dual-role special assistant
-----
The San Francisco Giants are bringing back another familiar face to the organization.Dusty Baker, three months after announcing his retirement as a big-league manager, is ready to assume a new role with his old team, as a special assistant in the front office.
