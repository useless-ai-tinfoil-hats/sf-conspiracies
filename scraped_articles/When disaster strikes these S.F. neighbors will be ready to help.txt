When disaster strikes, these S.F. neighbors will be ready to help
-----
"People are screaming — they need help," the firefighter said to the group of volunteers triaging disaster victims at San Francisco's Mission Bay on Saturday. "What are you going to do?"The volunteers thought about it for a moment, assessing the victim at the Spark Social community gathering space. They decided the person should be marked yellow — hurt, but not in dire need of help. Then they moved on to assess the next dummy lying on the grass.
