Northern California fire explodes overnight, grows to 15,565 acres
-----
UPDATE June 18, 6:04 p.m. The wind-whipped Sites Fire in Colusa County continues to grow, expanding to 15,565 acres, according to the latest update from Cal Fire.Containment is at 5%
June 18, 12:30 p.m. A wind-driven wildfire in Colusa County, 100 miles northwest of Sacramento, exploded overnight, growing from 50 acres on Monday afternoon to 10,000 acres on Tuesday morning, according to Cal Fire. The Sites Fire was 15% contained as of 12:30 p.m. Tuesday.
