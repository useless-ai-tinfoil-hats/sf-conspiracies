Pro-Palestinian activists take over building near Berkeley’s People’s Park
-----
A group of pro-Palestinian activists took over an abandoned UC Berkeley building near People's Park on Wednesday afternoon and set up tents outside, staging what they called an occupation just one day after administrators had peacefully resolved a weeks long encampment on campus.The unoccupied building, on Bowditch and Channing, was covered in parts with fresh graffiti reading, "Avenge" and "our house," with Palestinian flags hanging from the roof.
