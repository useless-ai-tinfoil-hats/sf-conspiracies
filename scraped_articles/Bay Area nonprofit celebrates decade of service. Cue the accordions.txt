Bay Area nonprofit celebrates decade of service. Cue the accordions
-----
A South Bay nonprofit serving Latino families and essential workers celebrated its 10th anniversary over the weekend with a musical mix of culture and community.The program Saturday by Ayudando Latinos a Soñar (Helping Latinos Dream) featured an accordion session by a farmworker ensemble, Tonantzin folk dancing, a performance of traditional Mexican music by the children's group Mariachi Media Luna, and singing from the Corazones de Oro seniors group.
