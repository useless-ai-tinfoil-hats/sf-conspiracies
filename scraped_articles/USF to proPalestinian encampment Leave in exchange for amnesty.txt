USF to pro-Palestinian encampment: Leave in exchange for amnesty
-----
University of San Francisco officials said Tuesday that students protesting the war in Gaza on the college's main lawn must disperse by 3 p.m. or face potential discipline.But as the deadline came and went, the students showed no intention of departing their cluster of about 50 tents erected on Welch Field. The campers were joined by dozens of additional students, faculty members and community organizers for a rally to reiterate their dedication to the encampment.
