Three people, including officers, hospitalized after San Jose police shooting
-----
San Jose officers exchanged gunfire with a man Thursday night, the police department said, landing two officers and the suspect in the hospital.Police said officers were investigating a disturbance at a hotel on San Ignacio Avenue near the Bernal Plaza shopping center when they encountered the man, who allegedly shot two officers. Police shot back and struck the man, the department said.
The two officers and the man were taken to the hospital.
