Berkeley police shoot person who opened fire at East Bay car service center
-----
Berkeley police shot a suspect who opened fire on at least one person at a car service center in Albany Thursday afternoon, according to the Police Department.Police say the suspect shot at least one person at the Toyota of Berkeley Service Center in Albany shortly before noon Thursday. Police were able to evacuate the building, but were met with gunfire when they tried to enter, an official said at a Thursday press briefing.
