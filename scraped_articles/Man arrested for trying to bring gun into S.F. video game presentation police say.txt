Man arrested for trying to bring gun into S.F. video game presentation, police say
-----
A man was arrested for trying to bring a gun to a video game awards presentation that was canceled beforehand Saturday night because of a "potential security threat," the U.S. Park Police said.The Park Police said they were notified at about 2:15 p.m. Saturday of a "security concern" posted online related to the Roblox Developers Conference, which was scheduled to host its award ceremony Saturday night at Fort Mason Center.
