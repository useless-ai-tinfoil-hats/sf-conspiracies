‘Idyllic’ private island on edge of San Francisco Bay on sale for $75 million
-----
A small private island on the fringes of San Francisco Bay went up for sale this week for the pretty price of $75 million.Billed as a "great escape for sports gatherings or corporate events," the roughly 50-acre plot, largely undeveloped with big bay views and assured solitude, is one of the few islands in the Bay Area not owned and protected by the state or federal government.
