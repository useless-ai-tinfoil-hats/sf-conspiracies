In ‘mixup,’ Californians get MyShake earthquake test alert at 3 a.m.
-----
Many Californians awoke at 3:19 a.m. to their phones sounding in a test of the earthquake alert phone warning system.It was jolting, so to speak — especially after loud alerts on Wednesday for what was initially believed to be a major, 5.7-magnitude earthquake in Sacramento County — only to be revised downward to 4.2 magnitude.
