Northern California tree surveyor rescued by helicopter after falling 20 feet into ravine
-----
After a tree surveyor fell into a ravine while working, state and local agencies launched a successful effort to rescue him, the Sierra County Sheriff's office announced in a Facebook post Thursday.A Sierra Pacific Industries surveyor was working near the unincorporated community of Alleghany when he slipped on the ground and fell into the embankment at 1:30 p.m. on Tuesday. Another individual on the scene called 911, and emergency services from multiple agencies were dispatched.
