WeWork bankruptcy brings more pain to S.F.’s struggling office market
-----
WeWork's bankruptcy caps a staggering fall for a co-working company once valued at $47 billion — and is another hit to San Francisco's struggling office market.The company grew into a behemoth before the pandemic. In San Francisco, it had leased or purchased nearly 2 million square feet of office space across 32 buildings by the end of 2019, according to data from brokerage Colliers, about 2.3% of all the office space in the city.
