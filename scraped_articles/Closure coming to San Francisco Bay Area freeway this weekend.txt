Closure coming to San Francisco Bay Area freeway this weekend
-----
An East Bay highway closure that was previously delayed due to weather is slated to occur this weekend and could cause traffic issues, officials said.Southbound Interstate 680 in Pleasanton is scheduled to be fully closed to traffic between the Interstate 580/I-680 Connector and Koopmann Road from 9 p.m. Friday to 4 a.m. Monday to allow work crews to replace deteriorating pavement, Caltrans said.
The following on-ramps onto southbound I-680 are scheduled to be impacted: I-580/I-680
