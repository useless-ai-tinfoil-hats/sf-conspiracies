California fire weather watch: These are all the active warnings right now
-----
The Chronicle is tracking watches, advisories and warnings after air quality in the Bay Area plunged on Tuesday and was expected to stay relatively poor at least into Thursday, as smoke from wildfires in Oregon and far Northern California drifted into the region.San Francisco smelled of smoke as Tuesday wore on, with the air at 3 p.m. deteriorating to "unhealthy." Twin Peaks was draped in a cloud of smoke, and the air was incredibly and visibly smoky elsewhere in the city.
