Aaron Peskin kicks off S.F. mayoral campaign, seeks ‘mature discussion about our future’
-----
Aaron Peskin, the progressive, polarizing president of San Francisco's Board of Supervisors, kicked off his mayoral campaign Saturday morning in Portsmouth Square, the heart of Chinatown and the gathering place of a key voter bloc in his district."I believe San Francisco deserves a mature discussion about our future, not a shouting match," Peskin said to well over 500 supporters as a loud protest with about 30 people took place outside the square.
