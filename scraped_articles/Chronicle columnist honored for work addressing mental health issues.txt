Chronicle columnist honored for work addressing mental health issues
-----
Chronicle columnist Kevin Fisher-Paulson has been named the winner of the Northern California Psychiatric Society's Media Award in print for 2023.The award, announced at a ceremony on Oct. 18, honored Fisher-Paulson for his weekly column, which focuses on his family's life and how they face everyday challenges, including issues of mental and emotional health.
