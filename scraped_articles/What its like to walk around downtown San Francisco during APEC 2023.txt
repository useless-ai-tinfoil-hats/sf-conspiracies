What it's like to walk around downtown San Francisco during APEC 2023
-----
At least one thing has been left unchanged in San Francisco amid the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit.The people's love of Target.
As I walked around the APEC security perimeter downtown on Wednesday afternoon — the first official day of the APEC Economic Leaders' Meeting featuring President Joe Biden, Chinese President Xi Jinping and other world leaders — the hardest-working person I saw was the lone police officer manning the exit for the gated barrier outside the Target
