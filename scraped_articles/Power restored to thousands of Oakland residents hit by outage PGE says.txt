Power restored to thousands of Oakland residents hit by outage, PG&E says
-----
LATEST Oct. 2, 1:30 p.m. A power outage that impacted thousands in Oakland on Monday was fully restored by 1 p.m., the utility said in an emailed statement.Oct. 2, 11:55 a.m. Thousands of residents in Oakland lost power Monday, according to PG&E. The outage started at 9:42 a.m. and impacted 8,731 customers at its peak, according to an emailed statement from Tamar Sarkissian, a PG&E spokesperson.
