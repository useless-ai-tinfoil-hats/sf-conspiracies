SJ man charged by DOJ in connection with international bike theft scheme
-----
A Bay Area man is accused of taking part in an international scheme involving the stealing and selling of high-end bicycles.The suspect, a 53-year-old San Jose man, stole performance bicycles from Bay Area homes and resold them in Jalisco, Mexico, the Justice Department said in a news release. The man appeared in federal court on Thursday, and a federal grand jury indicted him, charging him with one count of conspiracy to transport stolen goods and two counts of transporting stolen goods
