Second avalanche hits Palisades Tahoe ski resort
-----
A second inbounds avalanche struck the Palisades Tahoe resort Thursday afternoon, one day after a major avalanche there killed a Bay Area man.Thursday's avalanche happened near Wolverine Bowl on the Alpine Meadows side of the mountain around 12:30 p.m., according to Patrick Lacey, a spokesperson for Palisades Tahoe. No employees or guests were injured, though ski patrol searched the area with probes, beacons and a dog team to be certain, Lacey said.
