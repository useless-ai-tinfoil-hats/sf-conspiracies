BART train derails, catches fire in East Bay
-----
A BART train caught fire after it derailed near the Orinda station Monday morning, leading to the evacuation of all cars and a closure of two stations.The incident occurred when an eastbound train derailed just after 9 a.m. between the Orinda and Lafayette stations. Two cars reportedly caught fire.
There were no reported injuries, and firefighters extinguished the blaze, said BART spokesman Jim Allison.
Passengers were evacuated from the train and escorted back to the Orinda
