Harry and David pears in stock at SF Trader Joe's stores 'for a very limited time'
-----
The holidays are here and Trader Joe's is celebrating big by bringing back a beloved seasonal item. Beyond the egg nog products and all things peppermint flavored, the oh-so-popular Harry & David holiday comice pears have officially hit the shelves across store locations in the San Francisco area."They just came in yesterday," a Trader Joe's employee at the Masonic Avenue store excitedly told SFGATE over the phone on Tuesday.
