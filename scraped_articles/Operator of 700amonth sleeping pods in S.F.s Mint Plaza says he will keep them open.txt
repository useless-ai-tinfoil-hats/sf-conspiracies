Operator of $700-a-month sleeping pods in S.F.’s Mint Plaza says he will keep them open
-----
The operator of a sleeping pod complex in San Francisco's Mint Plaza — which offers small, dormitory-style bunks for $700 a month — said he will keep the units open while the building's owner applies for new permits and tries to get the building up to code.The Department of Building Inspection issued a violation notice for the building on Tuesday, finding that its owner, 12 Mint Plaza LLC, didn't obtain the proper permits to turn the former office building into a residential space.
