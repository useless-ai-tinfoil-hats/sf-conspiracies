Wildfire smoke from Sierra foothills blaze floods Tahoe Basin, I-80 corridor
-----
Smoke from the Crozier Fire burning in the Sierra Nevada foothills flooded the Tahoe Basin and the Interstate 80 corridor on Wednesday night with the acrid air lingering on Thursday, the National Weather Service said.
