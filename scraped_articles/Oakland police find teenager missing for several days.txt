Oakland police find teenager missing for several days
-----
Oakland Police Department officials said Monday they have located a 14-year-old girl who had been missing for several days.Roxana Ramirez Jimenez had not been heard from since the morning of Sept. 20, on the 4300 block of Broadway.
Roxana was safely located, police said. Police did not release further information about the case.
