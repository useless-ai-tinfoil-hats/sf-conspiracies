Bay Area car rental service Gig Car Share to shut down
-----
Gig Car Share, which garnered fans with its ultra-convenient car rentals in the Bay Area, is shutting down in December.The team behind Gig, which was launched in 2017 by an offshoot of the not-for-profit American Automobile Association, announced the closure in an email to customers Wednesday. The service is scheduled to stop running Dec. 27, the email said, blaming the shutdown on "decreased demand, rising operational costs, and changes to consumer commuting patterns."
