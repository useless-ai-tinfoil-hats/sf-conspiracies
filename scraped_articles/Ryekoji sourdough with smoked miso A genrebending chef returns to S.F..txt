Rye-koji sourdough with smoked miso? A genre-bending chef returns to S.F.
-----
Here's senior editor Janelle Bitker:Nick Balla is one of California's singular chefs.
Alongside co-chef Cortney Burns, he made San Francisco's now-shuttered Bar Tartine the talk of the city for its genre-defying, deeply flavored, often oddball cuisine. Presenting as Hungarian, the dishes were steeped in Japanese influence and boosted by a mind boggling larder of preserved ingredients.
