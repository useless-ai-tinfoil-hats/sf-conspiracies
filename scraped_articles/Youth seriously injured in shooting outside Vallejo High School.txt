Youth seriously injured in shooting outside Vallejo High School
-----
A youth was seriously injured Friday night in a shooting outside Vallejo High School after a football game, police said.The juvenile was struck by at least one round of gunfire shortly after 9:50 p.m., police said. The victim was taken to a local hospital with a serious but non-life-threatening injury.
