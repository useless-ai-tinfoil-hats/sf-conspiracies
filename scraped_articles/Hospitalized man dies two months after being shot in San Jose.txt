Hospitalized man dies two months after being shot in San Jose
-----
A San Jose shooting victim died Monday, two months after being hospitalized with a life-threatening gunshot wound, according to San Jose police.Police said the man was shot at least once just after 11 p.m. on Nov. 12 near the intersection of North 14th Street and East St. John Street. Officers responding to the shooting saw David Robert Cardoza, 37, running from the scene and arrested him, according to the police department.
