Too Short to host Bay Area hip-hop 50th anniversary bash
-----
Oakland rapper Too Short is set to host a Bay Area hip-hop 50th anniversary celebration.The event, organized by the independent San Francisco record label Empire, is scheduled to take place at SVN West on Oct. 15. It will feature break-dancing demonstrations, graffiti artists, local vendors and DJ sets. Among the local turntablists performing at the San Francisco event are Steady, Cream of Beat, Big Von, Scotty Fox and others.
