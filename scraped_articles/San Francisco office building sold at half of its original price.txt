San Francisco office building sold at half of its original price
-----
The owners of a San Francisco office building have sold the property for about half of what it was purchased for, the San Francisco Business Times first reported.An affiliate of New York-based Blackstone sold the building at 650 7th St. to Southern California-based LBA Realty for $25.4 million, according to the Business Times. Blackstone purchased the building about seven years ago for $50.5 million.
