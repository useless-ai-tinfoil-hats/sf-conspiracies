SF's favorite punk rock pizza shop is expanding to the city's west side
-----
Golden Boy, the punk rock pizza shop that's been slinging slices in North Beach since 1978, has a new San Francisco location in the works. Golden Boy Pizza plans to open a new shop in Parkside at 1447 Taraval St., as first reported by Eater and confirmed by the San Francisco Chronicle (the Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms)."Taraval just has some good vibes and they're only going to get better," Golden Boy owner Julian Sodini told SFGATE.
