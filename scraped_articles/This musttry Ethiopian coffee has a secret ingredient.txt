This must-try Ethiopian coffee has a secret ingredient
-----
Here's food reporter Mario CortezMany people associate coffee with bitterness. But what if I told you that a bitter herb can actually sweeten your brew?
Yagere owners Dagmawit Bekele and Getu Yilma brew Ethiopian single-origin coffee at Bay Area farmers' markets, mixing water and ground coffee inside jebenas — tall Ethiopian ceramic pots used to brew coffee — over a propane burner.
