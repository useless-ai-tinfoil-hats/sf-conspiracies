GoFundMe launched to 'seek justice' for Disneyland death
-----
The family and friends of a Disney employee who died from her injuries following a crash in the backstage area of Disneyland earlier this month have launched a crowdfunding campaign to cover memorial and legal costs.A GoFundMe for Bonnye Lear's family was launched and is seeking to raise $100,000 to cover funeral expenses, litigation costs and expenses for Lear's children.
