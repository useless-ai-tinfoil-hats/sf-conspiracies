Post Fire in SoCal rages as high winds whip up the wildfire
-----
UPDATE June 15 10:11 p.m. The Post Fire burning in Gorman near Interstate 5 has torched 4,400 acres, according to the latest update from Cal Fire.The blaze is zero percent contained as high winds and dry conditions are making it difficult for crews. Wind gusts up to 30 mph are expected, with stronger winds of up to 50 mph expected at ridge tops, according to the latest incident update.
