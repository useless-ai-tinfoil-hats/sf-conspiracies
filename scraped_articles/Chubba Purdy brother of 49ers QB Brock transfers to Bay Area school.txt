Chubba Purdy, brother of 49ers QB Brock, transfers to Bay Area school
-----
Two Purdy brothers will soon be slinging passes in the Bay Area: On Sunday, Chubba Purdy announced he'll be playing for San Jose State in the fall.This will be Chubba Purdy's third school. After graduating Perry High in Gilbert, Ariz., the same school where brother Brock became a star, Chubba Purdy attended Florida State.
