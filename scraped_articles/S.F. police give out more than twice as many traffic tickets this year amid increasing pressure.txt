S.F. police give out more than twice as many traffic tickets this year amid increasing pressure
-----
San Francisco Police officers are issuing more traffic citations than in years past, reversing a precipitous decline in the number of tickets issued over the last decade, according to new data from the city's Police Department.The jump comes after two hearings on the issue and amid political pressure to crack down on reckless driving and make the city's roads safer.
