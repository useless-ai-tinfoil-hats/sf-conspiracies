San Francisco streets reopen after Gaza ceasefire protest
-----
All lanes on the I-80 Westbound ramp to U.S.-101 North reopened Monday afternoon around 3:15 p.m., about an hour after authorities alerted residents and drivers to avoid the area because of demonstrators marching in support of a ceasefire in the war in Gaza.San Francisco's Department of Emergency Management had issued the alert for the area of the Central Freeway between Duboce Avenue and Octavia Avenue.
People should expect residual delays.
