Man sues S.F., claiming he was wrongfully arrested and jailed during Bay Bridge Gaza protest
-----
A Pleasanton man filed a lawsuit against the city of San Francisco, claiming he was wrongfully arrested during a Bay Bridge protest that blocked traffic on the week of the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit.In a lawsuit filed in federal court Tuesday, Masoud Barukzai said he was seeking damages and financial relief, claiming that he was not a part of the Nov. 15 protest and should never have been arrested and jailed by San Francisco police officers.
