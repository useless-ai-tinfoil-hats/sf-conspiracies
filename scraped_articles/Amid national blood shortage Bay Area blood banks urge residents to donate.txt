Amid national blood shortage, Bay Area blood banks urge residents to donate
-----
Bay Area blood banks are urging residents to donate blood amid what the American Red Cross is calling a national shortage emergency.The number of Americans donating blood is at a 20-year low, the Red Cross said this week, and more than a third of the nation's 59 community blood centers have only a one-day supply or less, according to America's Blood Centers, the organization that represents 60% of the blood centers in the United States.
