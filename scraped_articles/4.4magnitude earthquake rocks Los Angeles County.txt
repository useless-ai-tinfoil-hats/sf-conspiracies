4.4-magnitude earthquake rocks Los Angeles County
-----
A 4.4-magnitude earthquake rocked Southern California Monday afternoon, rattling much of Los Angeles, but producing little in the way of major damage.The temblor was detected about 2.5 miles southeast of Highland Park, according to data from the U.S. Geological Survey.
