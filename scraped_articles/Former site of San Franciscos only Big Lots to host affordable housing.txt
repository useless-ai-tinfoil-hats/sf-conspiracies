Former site of San Francisco's only Big Lots to host affordable housing
-----
A parcel of what was once San Francisco's only Big Lots store is slated to soon begin the process of being redeveloped into affordable housing for seniors.Preliminary planning applications filed by the Bernal Heights Neighborhood Center in December propose that what is currently a parking structure on the south end of the site at 3333 Mission St. become 70 units of 100% affordable housing for seniors.
