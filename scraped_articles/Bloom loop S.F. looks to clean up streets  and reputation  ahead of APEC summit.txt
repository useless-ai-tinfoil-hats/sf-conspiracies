‘Bloom loop’? S.F. looks to clean up streets — and reputation — ahead of APEC summit
-----
APEC in S.F.: Live updates on closures, traffic, transit as city hosts huge summitOfficials in charge of cleaning San Francisco streets are pushing a new narrative of the city ahead of the upcoming Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation conference: It's not the "doom loop," Public Works spokesperson Rachel Gordon said — it's the "bloom loop."
To date, San Francisco has raised more than $20 million to help bring that vision to life, with improvements across the city's built environment.
