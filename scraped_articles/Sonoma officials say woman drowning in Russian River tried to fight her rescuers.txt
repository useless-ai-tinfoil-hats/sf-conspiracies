Sonoma officials say woman drowning in Russian River tried to fight her rescuers
-----
A water rescue in the Russian River on Sunday afternoon turned contentious between authorities and the person drowning, Sonoma County Sheriff's Office officials said.Sheriff's deputies were called to the Johnson's Beach area of the Russian River in Guerneville, where a 911 caller said a person was being swept down the river, officials said.
