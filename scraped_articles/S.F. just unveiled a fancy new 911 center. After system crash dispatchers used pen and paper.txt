S.F. just unveiled a fancy new 911 center. After system crash, dispatchers used pen and paper
-----
San Francisco's 911 computer system crashed for several hours Thursday, forcing dispatchers to resort to what's called a "manual mode": recording the details of a call with pen and paper, and then handing it to a "runner" to summon emergency crews.The runners serve as messengers, taking written information from one side of a call center and transporting it to another, in order to deploy police, fire, paramedics and street crisis teams, said Department of Emergency Management Executive
