Civil War-era cannonballs found beneath Presidio fire station
-----
Two Civil-War era cannonballs were found in the Presidio last Friday, officials said. The ammunition was found to pose no explosion threat.Crews unearthed the 10-inch diameter cannonballs while carrying out a soil remediation project adjacent to the Presidio Fire Station. With the help of the Travis Air Force Base's emergency ordnance disposal team, colloquially known as the bomb squad, park officials verified that the cannonballs posed no explosion risk and disposed of them safely.
