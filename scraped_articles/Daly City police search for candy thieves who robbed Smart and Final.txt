Daly City police search for candy thieves who robbed Smart and Final
-----
Daly City police are searching for a pair of thieves who punched a Smart and Final store manager before taking off with boxes of candy.On May 14, Daly City police went to the Smart and Final store at 6967 Mission St. at 7:36 p.m following a report of a robbery. According to the police incident report, a store manager noticed two people exit through the stock room with a shopping cart filled with about 40 to 50 candy boxes.
