California doctors who deliver COVID falsehoods no longer committing ‘misconduct’
-----
A short-lived law allowing California to punish doctors who give patients false information about COVID-19 is dead, months after a federal judge blocked it.California became the first state to include COVID misinformation in the official definition of physician misconduct when the law took effect Jan. 1 of this year.
