Luis Alfaro’s ‘The Travelers’ wins the Will Glickman Award for best play to premiere in the Bay Area
-----
Luis Alfaro's "The Travelers," which ran last spring at Magic Theatre, won the Will Glickman Award for best play to premiere in the Bay Area in 2023, administrator Theatre Bay Area announced Tuesday, Jan. 23.Alfaro, like fellow playwrights John Fisher and Jonathan Spector, is now a two-time recipient of the award, among the highest honors in local theater. The Los Angeles playwright previously won for "Oedipus El Rey," which Magic Theatre also produced, in 2010.
