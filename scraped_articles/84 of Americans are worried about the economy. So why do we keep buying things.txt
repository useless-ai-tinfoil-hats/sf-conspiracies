84% of Americans are worried about the economy. So why do we keep buying things?
-----
On paper, everything's going great in the United States.On Friday, the Labor Department reported that the U.S. economy added 199,000 jobs in November, with hourly earnings up by 4% during 2023. Holiday shopping, a yearly indicator of economic health, is looking up, too: Retail experts foresee decent sales growth for luxury goods, toys and tech during the holiday season, especially among younger generations. Gen Z and Millennials are expected to spend more this year compared to 2022.
