Calif. community Altadena outside LA fights to protect 'oldest large-scale Christmas lighting' tradition
-----
The California community of Altadena was about to start an annual countdown it has done 101 times.The unincorporated area along the foothills of the San Gabriel Mountains northeast of downtown Los Angeles was gathering for a December tradition it's had to fight to keep.
At the heart of this community is Christmas Tree Lane, where a row of 138-year-old deodar cedars was waiting to begin glimmering with strung-up bulbs.
