Guy Fieri to be named honorary mayor of his childhood home in Northern California
-----
Celebrity chef and TV personality Guy Fieri, the self-proclaimed Mayor of Flavortown, is about to become another type of mayor.This Friday, Fieri, the humanitarian, restaurateur and brains behind Food Network's "Diners, Drive-Ins and Dives," is to be named an honorary mayor of his California childhood home, Ferndale, and moving forward, every April 5 will be Guy Fieri Day, according to the Lost Coast Outpost.
