Kehlani proves their superstar status with headlining San Francisco concert announcement
-----
Kehlani got their start in the Bay Area. It feels fitting, then, that they're scheduled to finish their tour there.On Tuesday, Kehlani announced a concert at San Francisco's Chase Center. The Nov. 2 stadium show is slated to be the final stop on the North American leg of the artist's Crash World Tour. British girl group Flo, whose track "Cardboard Box" spawned a viral TikTok dance, and up-and-coming Atlanta rapper Anycia are booked to open for the stadium show.
