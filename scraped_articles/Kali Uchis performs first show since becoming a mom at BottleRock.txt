Kali Uchis performs first show since becoming a mom at BottleRock
-----
Kali Uchis made a grand comeback to the stage during the second day of BottleRock Napa Valley, her first live performance since welcoming her first child with rapper Don Toliver in March.The Colombian American singer-songwriter delivered a glamorous show at the Verizon Stage on Saturday, May 25, offering a striking contrast to Pearl Jam's stalwart classic rock that dominated the main JaM Cellars Stage.
