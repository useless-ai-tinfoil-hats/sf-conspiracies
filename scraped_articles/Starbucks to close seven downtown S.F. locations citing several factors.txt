Starbucks to close seven downtown S.F. locations, citing ‘several factors’
-----
Starbucks will close seven of its downtown San Francisco stores by the end of the month, the company said. Employees were notified of the closures Tuesday.A company spokesperson said that employees at each of the closing locations — Mission and Main streets, Geary and Taylor, 425 Battery St., 398 Market St., Fourth and Market, 555 California St., and Bush Street and Van Ness Avenue — will be offered the opportunity to transfer to another location. The stores will close Oct. 22.
