Yosemite's storm chasers: When the snow hits, these diehards head for the park
-----
When a big winter storm is forecast for Yosemite National Park, visitors often cancel their reservations and rebook for another time, and if they're already in the park, they head home. But for a handful of intrepid photographers, videographers and adventure fiends, a storm has the opposite effect.
