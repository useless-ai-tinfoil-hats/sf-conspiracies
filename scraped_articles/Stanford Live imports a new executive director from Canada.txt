Stanford Live imports a new executive director from Canada
-----
Stanford University has appointed Iris Nemani, currently the chief programming officer at Harbourfront Centre in Toronto, as the new executive director of the Stanford Live performing arts program and the university's presenting venues, Bing Concert Hall and Frost Amphitheatre.She is scheduled to take up the position on April 1, the university announced Tuesday, Nov. 28. She succeeds Chris Lorway, who stepped down earlier this year after seven years at the organization's helm.
