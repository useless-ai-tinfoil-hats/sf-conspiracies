The dark history and future cleanup of a $1.5 million house with a meth lab inside
-----
A San Jose home with a dark history involving bomb-making materials and a meth lab is for sale for $1.5 million, according to a Zillow posting.Peter Karasev, who previously lived in the home with his wife and three young children, was found with explosive materials and arrested in March. At the time, San Jose police called for federal backup because the home at 668 Potomac Court was a "powder keg" filled with explosives and materials for making drugs, KTVU-TV reported.
