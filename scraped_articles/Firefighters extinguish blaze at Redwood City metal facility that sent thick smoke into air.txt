Firefighters extinguish blaze at Redwood City metal facility that sent thick smoke into air
-----
A fire that erupted at a Redwood City metal facility has been extinguished Wednesday evening.Earlier in the afternoon, the Mountain View Fire Department said, "Thick, dark smoke is in the air accompanied by the smell of burning plastic." Multiple local agencies warned the public that the smoke was spreading throughout the region.
