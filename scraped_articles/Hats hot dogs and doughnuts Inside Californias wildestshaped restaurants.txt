Hats, hot dogs and doughnuts: Inside California’s wildest-shaped restaurants
-----
A Hollywood hangout shaped like a brown hat. An ice cream parlor built to resemble an owl. A long hot dog stand that looks like, well, a hot dog.California has plenty of famous architecture, but perhaps none is as definitively distinct to Southern California as the early to mid-century phenomenon of programmatic, or mimetic, architecture.
