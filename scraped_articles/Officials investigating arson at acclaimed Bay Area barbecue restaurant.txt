Officials investigating arson at acclaimed Bay Area barbecue restaurant
-----
Evidence found at the scene of a fire that temporarily shut down one of the Bay Area's most acclaimed restaurants, Horn Barbecue in Oakland, indicates it was set intentionally, according to fire officials.The Oakland Fire Department is now investigating the Nov. 21, 2023, fire at Horn Barbecue as arson, according to a statement. The investigation remains open and ongoing, as first reported by Eater SF.
