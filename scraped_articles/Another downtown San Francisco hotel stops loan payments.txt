Another downtown San Francisco hotel stops loan payments
-----
The real estate developer that owns Four Seasons San Francisco at the Embarcadero, has not made loan payments on the property in months, according to published reports.The San Francisco Business Times reported that Westbrook Partners received a notice of default for the more than $70 million loan attached to the 155-room hotel at Sansome and California streets. The company has failed to make loan payments since December, according to the San Francisco Business Times.
