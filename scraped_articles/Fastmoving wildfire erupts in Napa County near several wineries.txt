Fast-moving wildfire erupts in Napa County near several wineries
-----
Firefighters battled a 60-acre wildfire in Napa County as temperatures in Wine Country approached triple digits.The blaze, dubbed the Crystal Fire, broke out around 1:40 p.m. Wednesday between St. Helena and Calistoga, near the Bell Canyon Reservoir. More than a dozen fire engines rushed to the scene and two helicopters were assigned to aid the firefight, according to Cal Fire. Thursday afternoon, Cal Fire reported the blaze was 75% contained and forward progress of the blaze was halted.
