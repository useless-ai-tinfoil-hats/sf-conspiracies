Linebacker Eric Kendricks spurns 49ers to join Cowboys in free agency
-----
Linebacker Eric Kendricks decided to sign with the Dallas Cowboys after agreeing to join the San Francisco 49ers early Wednesday.ESPN's Adam Schefter reported that the "change of heart" happened thanks to newly hired Cowboys defensive coordinator Mike Zimmer.
"Mike Zimmer wanted Kendricks to help run his defense and Kendricks preferred to play in Dallas," Schefter tweeted.
