Deputies search for thieves who stole $30,000 in goods from Solano County winery
-----
Solano County deputies are searching for three burglars who broke into a Fairfield winery Monday morning and stole about $30,000 in merchandise, including an all-terrain vehicle.The Solano County Sheriff's Office said surveillance video from Bally Keal Estate Winery in Suisun Valley showed two men and a woman stealing a Polaris ATV at 12:30 a.m. The thieves also stole a charger for the ATV as well as tools, according to the sheriff's office.
