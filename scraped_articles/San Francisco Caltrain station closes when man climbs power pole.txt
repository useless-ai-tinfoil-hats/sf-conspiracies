San Francisco Caltrain station closes when man climbs power pole
-----
The San Francisco Caltrain station was closed on Thursday morning when a man climbed a power pole, KCBS Radio reported.Video footage shared by KTVU-TV showed the man at 6 a.m. a the station at Fourth and King streets.
Train traffic at the station was stopped as of 7:30 a.m.
San Francisco police and firefighters were on scene, trying to convince the man to come down from the pole, KCBS said.
