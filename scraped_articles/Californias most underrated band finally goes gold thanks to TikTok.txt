California's most underrated band finally goes gold thanks to TikTok
-----
The most influential musical exports from Stockton were never exactly chart-toppers, yet revered indie rockers Pavement have found unlikely late-career success thanks to — of all things — choreographed dance routines.Fronted by Stephen Malkmus, the band formed in 1989 to release a slew of wildly influential albums before disbanding in 1999. They were known for unique guitar melodies that were as acrobatic as their lyrics, telling vivid and off-kilter stories too quirky for alt-rock
