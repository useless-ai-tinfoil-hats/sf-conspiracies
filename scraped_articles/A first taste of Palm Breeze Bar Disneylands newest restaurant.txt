A first taste of Palm Breeze Bar, Disneyland's newest restaurant
-----
It's a good time for dining at Disneyland. In the last two months, the resort has opened Tiana's Palace, the "Princess and the Frog"-themed restaurant in New Orleans Square, and the Lounge at the Disneyland Hotel, a low-key, upscale lobby bar open in the evenings, serving classic cocktails and shareable plates.Now, there's a third brand-new dining option. On Thursday, Disneyland opened Palm Breeze Bar, also in the Disneyland Hotel. The all-outdoor restaurant is technically the pool bar
