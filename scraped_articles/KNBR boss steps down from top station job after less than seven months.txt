KNBR boss steps down from top station job after less than seven months
-----
KNBR programming director Adam Copeland will step down from his position effective immediately after a tenure of seventh months, according to an internal memo obtained by SFGATE.The announcement came through an email sent Thursday morning from Cumulus Media executive Larry Blumhagen. The email, reviewed by SFGATE, states that Copeland will instead "focus on his PM Drive show 'Tolbert and Copes.'" Barrett Sports Media had the news first.
