Review: S.F. Symphony’s guest conductor leads a brisk, athletic triumph
-----
Jaap van Zweden, the Dutch conductor who came to Davies Symphony Hall this week to lead the San Francisco Symphony in music of Beethoven and Shostakovich, has an unmistakable interpretive style — perhaps the most distinctive and recognizable approach of anyone in the business.He barrels headfirst into the music, as if he were some sort of martial artist determined to knock his opponent onto their keister.
