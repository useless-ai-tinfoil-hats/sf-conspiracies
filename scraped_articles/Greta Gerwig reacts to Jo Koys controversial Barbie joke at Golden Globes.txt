Greta Gerwig reacts to Jo Koy’s controversial ‘Barbie’ joke at Golden Globes
-----
Comedian Jo Koy's monologue at the 81st Golden Globe Awards had its share of flat jokes. But few drew more audible groans at the Beverly Hilton than his quip about Greta Gerwig's "Barbie."During the CBS broadcast on Sunday, Jan. 7, Koy compared the Sacramento native's record-breaking movie about the iconic doll to Christopher Nolan's "Oppenheimer."
