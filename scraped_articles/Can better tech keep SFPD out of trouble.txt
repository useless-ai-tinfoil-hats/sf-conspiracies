Can better tech keep SFPD out of trouble?
-----
The San Francisco Police Commission is considering upgrading the case management system used to identify problem officers before they get the city sued.The new software, "First Sign" from Benchmark Analytics, is an early intervention system to identify "at-risk" officers. According to the Chicago software company, two-thirds of injuries and excessive force incidents can be traced to at-risk officers, who comprise about 5% of San Francisco's police force.
