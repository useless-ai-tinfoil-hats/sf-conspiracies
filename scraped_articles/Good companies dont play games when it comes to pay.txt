Good companies don’t play games when it comes to pay
-----
Photos by ShutterstockPay is the most obvious way employees judge how much their organization values them. And if they feel their pay is low, it's hard to convince them the organization truly cares – particularly if the employer is reaping profits or investing elsewhere.
Of course, workers need to make money to provide for themselves and their families. And employees who feel valued by their organization are more likely to be engaged and work harder to help the organization
