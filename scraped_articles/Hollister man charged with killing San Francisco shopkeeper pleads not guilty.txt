Hollister man charged with killing San Francisco shopkeeper pleads not guilty
-----
A Hollister man has pleaded not guilty to murder charges after he was accused of killing a beloved Richmond District shopkeeper, allegedly while trying to steal two beers.Santos De La Rosa, 21, was arrested in Hollister earlier this month and charged with murder, robbery and assault with a deadly weapon. The San Francisco District Attorney's Office alleges that De La Rosa tried to steal two cans of beer from Richmond Market in late August.
