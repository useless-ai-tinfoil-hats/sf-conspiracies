More than 29,000 CSU employees could walk off job in first systemwide strike
-----
California State University faculty members across all 23 campuses plan to strike next week after failing to reach a wage agreement with university officials.The strike, set for Jan. 22 to 26, also marks the first week of instruction for the spring semester for many CSU campuses. If all faculty members participate, more than 29,000 union employees will walk off the job.
