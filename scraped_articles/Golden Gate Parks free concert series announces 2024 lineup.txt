Golden Gate Park’s free concert series’ announces 2024 lineup
-----
Being a casual music fan just got a lot cheaper.More than 125 performances are scheduled for this year's Illuminate Live concert series, which returns to Golden Gate Park's Bandshell this week.
This season's musical lineup includes a diverse range of artists such as Novato-native Ben Lang, jazz singer Sony Holland and Oakland born alternative rock band Whiskerman covering everything from R&B to indie and psychedelic rock to EDM.
