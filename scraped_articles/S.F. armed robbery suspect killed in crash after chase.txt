S.F. armed robbery suspect killed in crash after chase
-----
A suspected auto burglary thief was killed and another person was hospitalized Saturday afternoon after a crash in San Francisco, police said.Shortly after 4 p.m., officers observed a vehicle that police said was wanted in connection with an armed robbery and an unspecified number of auto burglaries.
The driver allegedly sped away while officers were preparing to stop the vehicle. Officers pursued the vehicle onto a highway, police said in a statement Saturday night.
