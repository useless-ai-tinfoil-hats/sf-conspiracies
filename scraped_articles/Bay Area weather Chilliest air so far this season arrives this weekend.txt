Bay Area weather: Chilliest air so far this season arrives this weekend
-----
Calmer weather returns to the Bay Area on Thursday, with mainly sunny skies allowing the region to dry out. Morning temperatures will be in the 30s inland and 40s on the coast before warming up to the upper 50s to lower 60s in the afternoon.Light showers are possible Friday morning, especially in the higher terrain, but accumulations are only expected to be a few hundredths of an inch.
