SF woman identified as one of two people killed in Half Moon Bay plane crash
-----
The San Mateo County Coroner has identified one of the two people who died in a plane crash Sunday off the coast of Half Moon Bay as Emma Willmer-Shiles, a passenger on the plane and a 27-year-old San Francisco resident.Sgt. Phillip Halworth, a spokesperson for the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office, told SFGATE that a person who was fishing found Willmer-Shiles' body in the water on Monday near the wreckage. At the time, 30 people from the sheriff's office were searching for survivors.
