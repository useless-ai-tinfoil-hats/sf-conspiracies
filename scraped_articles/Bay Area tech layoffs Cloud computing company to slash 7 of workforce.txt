Bay Area tech layoffs: Cloud computing company to slash 7% of workforce
-----
Bay Area cloud computing firm Five9 is set to lay off approximately 7% of its global workforce as part of a restructuring initiative to reduce operating expenses.In regulatory filings on Tuesday, the San Ramon company, which provides services for call centers, stated that the workforce reduction aligns with its broader strategy to achieve "balanced, profitable growth." The move is expected to enhance the company's long-term outlook and increase shareholder value.
