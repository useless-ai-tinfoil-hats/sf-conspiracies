Emerging Black Composers Project announces 2024 winner
-----
Louisville-born composer and horn player Tyler Taylor has been selected as the winner of the fourth annual Emerging Black Composers Project.As recipient of the Michael Morgan Prize, which was renamed last year in honor of the late Oakland Symphony music director, Taylor is poised to receive a $15,000 commissioning fee, mentorship from EBCP committee members and resources to workshop his piece with the San Francisco Conservatory of Music.
