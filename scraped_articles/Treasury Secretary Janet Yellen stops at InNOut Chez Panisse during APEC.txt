Treasury Secretary Janet Yellen stops at In-N-Out, Chez Panisse during APEC
-----
U.S. Treasury Secretary Janet Yellen has been a regular sight for Bay Area diners during APEC, the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation forum.Bloomberg News Treasury reporter Viktoria Dendrinou posted a photo of Yellen on X, the platform formerly known as Twitter, at a Bay Area In-N-Out. The photo shows a petite Yellen in a tan trenchcoat ordering at the restaurant's counter, lined with the California chain's iconic red-white palm tree tiles.
