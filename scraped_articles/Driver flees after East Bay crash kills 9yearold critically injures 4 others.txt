Driver flees after East Bay crash kills 9-year-old, critically injures 4 others
-----
A solo car crash on Interstate 680 in Concord killed a 9-year-old girl and critically injured four other people early Sunday, and the driver fled the scene, according to the California Highway Patrol.The crash happened just before 5:14 a.m. on northbound I-680 at the Highway 242 split near Concord, the agency said in a statement. A gray Mazda CX-9 was traveling north when the driver veered off the road, and the vehicle flipped over several times before landing on its roof, investigators
