Review: Berkeley Rep’s ‘Bulrusher’ glitters like Boonville’s Navarro River
-----
The river that laps and foams still courses underneath. It might splash you in jest, snaking and bobbing, or mirror you with a mother's love on its glassy surface, but it still barrels toward its destination, unstoppable.The title character of "Bulrusher" was found as an infant in such a river, and playwright and Berkeley native Eisa Davis unwinds the young woman's story in kind.
