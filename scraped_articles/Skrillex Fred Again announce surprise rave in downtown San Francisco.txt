Skrillex, Fred Again announce surprise rave in downtown San Francisco
-----
In a callback to a long-gone festival that introduced raving to many Bay Area youths, a huge electronic dance party is coming to downtown San Francisco at Civic Center.On Friday, Skrillex announced in a nonchalant social media post that he and producer Fred Again were coming to the city. "Fred and I have way too much new music we need to test out so we're going to throw a rave in San Francisco next week BRB," he posted on X.
