Tony-winning actress to join Esa-Pekka Salonen, S.F. Symphony for first time
-----
Tony Award-winning singer and actress Cynthia Erivo is set to perform with the San Francisco Symphony for one night only this fall.Erivo is scheduled to join Esa-Pekka Salonen and the Symphony for her first time on stage at Davies Symphony Hall on Sept. 14. She plans to perform songs by her personal female musical inspirations, including artists such as Aretha Franklin, Nina Simone, Shirley Bassey and Billie Holiday.
