Jordan Hicks pitches through illness but Giants fall flat in 3-1 loss to Astros
-----
A punishing week followed Jordan Hicks onto the mound.The flu, not yet completely out of his system, had him laid up in bed all week with IVs in his arms. All that time horizontal made his back tighten up the more he threw the ball. As a result, Hicks' velocity was down a few mph Tuesday and the coaching staff had drawn a line that he'd not pitch beyond the fifth inning.
