Giants’ Jorge Soler not alone in frustration; MLB hitters in ‘survival mode’
-----
Jorge Soler can identify the exact year major-league pitching became near impossible to hit.It was during the 2022 season that he noticed how impenetrable every opposing pitching staff had become. Not only were starters throwing harder, but they were backed by an arsenal of relievers hurling just as hard — but more importantly, they weren't missing.
