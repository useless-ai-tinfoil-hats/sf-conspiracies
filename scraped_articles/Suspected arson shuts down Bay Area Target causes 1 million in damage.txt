Suspected arson shuts down Bay Area Target, causes $1 million in damage
-----
A fire forced the closure of a Target in Vallejo on Saturday and caused an estimated $1 million in damage to the store, officials said.The fire, which officials suspect was arson, started just before 3 p.m. in the paper towel aisle of the store at 904 Admiral Callaghan Lane, Kevin Brown, a spokesperson for the Vallejo Fire Department, wrote in an email. Brown said over the phone that the majority of the damage was caused within the roof structure and in the area where the fire started.
