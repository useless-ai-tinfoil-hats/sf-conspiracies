Review: In ‘Skincare,’ Elizabeth Banks finds that beauty is the beast
-----
As an Los Angeles aesthetician whose life is falling apart piece by piece, Elizabeth Banks is in such constant motion, the camera can barely keep up with her. She power walks in big sunglasses, trying to balance a loaded purse, a cigarette and a cup of latte; she stalks, she glowers, she drives with wide-eyed terror."Why is this happening to me?" she seems to be saying, even when not saying a word.
