Metallica’s frontman tattoos Lemmy Kilmister’s cremated ashes into his finger
-----
Metallica singer and guitarist James Hetfield has taken his admiration for Motörhead to a new level.The frontman for the Bay Area rock band revealed that he had the cremated ashes of the English group's frontman, Ian "Lemmy" Kilmister, tattooed into an Ace of Spades insignia on his middle finger.
