Annie Leibovitz's 66-acre Bay Area farm comes to market at $9M
-----
In 1932, legendary American photographer Ansel Adams was traveling the Bay Area, taking picture after iconic picture of the stunning landscapes he encountered. Among those landscapes was a Bolinas farm, the subject of "Stump and Barn Near Olema." Most recently, another legendary American photographer, Annie Leibovitz, has owned this farm, a bucolic 65.8-acre property dubbed "The Hideaway."Now, the Hideaway is for sale, asking $8.995 million.
