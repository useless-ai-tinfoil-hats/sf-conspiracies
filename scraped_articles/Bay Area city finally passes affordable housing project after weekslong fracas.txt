Bay Area city finally passes affordable housing project after weekslong fracas
-----
Half Moon Bay's planning commission approved a permit to begin work on a 40-unit, five-story affordable housing complex for older farmworkers on Tuesday night, which, at least for now, marks the end of a squabble that has embroiled the tiny coastal city for three weeks.The five-person commission approved the project on a 4-0-1 vote, with Vice Chair Hazel Joanes abstaining.
