You could call it "a case of the Mondays." But if those Mondays don't go away, it might be job burnout. And the syndrome can turn serious, leading to depression, and even high blood pressure."Job burnout also may involve feeling useless, powerless and empty," a November 2023 Mayo website blog notes.
