Two adults rescued after car falls down a slope on San Francisco road
-----
Two adults were rescued Wednesday afternoon after falling 150 feet down an embankment on a road near Glen Canyon Park in San Francisco.A four-door sedan fell down a slope on O'Shaughnessy Boulevard near Del Vale Avenue around 2:45 p.m., Justin Schorr, a public information officer for the San Francisco Fire Department, told SFGATE.
