Stabbing in S.F. Bayview leaves 1 dead, 1 injured as police search for suspects
-----
A stabbing in San Francisco's Bayview neighborhood left one person dead and another injured Sunday afternoon, police said.Officers called to Bayshore Boulevard and Cortland Avenue at 4:13 p.m. found two men suffering from stab wounds, San Francisco police said Monday in a statement.
Paramedics from the San Francisco Fire Department transported the two victims to a local hospital, where one died and one was treated for life-threatening injuries.
