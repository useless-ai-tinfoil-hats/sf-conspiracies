Police, FBI arrest murder suspect in North Bay car crash that killed Oakland man
-----
Police arrested a man on suspicion of murder this month in the killing of an Oakland man whose autopsy revealed that he had been struck by a bullet before crashing his truck in rural Solano County, authorities said."What Fairfield Police initially thought was a single-vehicle accident is now being investigated as a homicide," police spokesperson Jennifer Brantley said in a statement Sunday.
