‘Three blocks wasn’t enough’: Thousands pack into S.F.’s Sunset District for Taiwanese-inspired night market
-----
Three years ago it may have been impossible to imagine three blocks of Irving Street packed to the brim with people, performers and food.But the inaugural Sunset Night Market Friday night was just as crowded as the night markets of Taipei it was inspired by.
