California weather likely to be a mixed bag this holiday weekend
-----
Don't plan that outdoor barbecue just yet, as California weather is forecast to be a mixed bag this holiday weekend. Areas across the state are expected to see cooler temperatures Friday and Saturday before experiencing sunnier and slightly warmer days as the weekend goes on.In the Bay Area, a low-pressure system is forecast to move inland Friday and stick around through Saturday.
