Inside the Bay Area's first taste of the global women's soccer boom
-----
The 1990s should have been the golden years for Brandi Chastain, tearing up the country as a professional soccer superstar. But reality didn't work out that way.Instead, the winning penalty kick scorer at the 1999 FIFA Women's World Cup would play only one season of professional soccer in the decade — and would have to fly halfway across the world to do it.
