Yet another rooftop restaurant opens in S.F. — from a team with Michelin cred
-----
San Francisco's only major new hotel of the year has arrived downtown — bringing with it a restaurant and rooftop terrace powered by a local team with Michelin-starred sushi and Wagyu beef spots.Tucked upstairs inside the Jay Hotel at 433 Clay St. and simply called the Third Floor, it's the latest project from the Omakase Restaurant Group, known for luxe sushi and steak at Omakase and Niku, and the ever popular Dumpling Time (six locations and counting).
