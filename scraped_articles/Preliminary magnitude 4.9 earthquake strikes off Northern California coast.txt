Preliminary magnitude 4.9 earthquake strikes off Northern California coast
-----
An earthquake with a preliminary magnitude of 4.9 was recorded about 58 miles off the coast of Petrolia, a small rural community in Northern California's Humboldt County, on Friday morning, the U.S. Geological Survey said.The quake struck with a depth of 6.2 miles at 7:09 a.m.
There were 216 reports of having felt shaking via the USGS' Felt Report. Weak shaking was felt in McKinleyville, Eureka and Fortuna.
