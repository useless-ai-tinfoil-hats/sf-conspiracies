Legendary winemaker’s massive Calif. vineyard resort for sale for $21 million
-----
A legendary winemaker's 160-acre California estate is for sale. Ceàgo Vineyards and Lakeside Casitas, featuring a massive lakefront compound and vineyard, is listed at $21 million, setting a record-high listing price for Clear Lake, California.The sprawling lakeside development is owned by Jim Fetzer, who purchased the land in 2001 and created the resort-like property.
