Protest shuts down northbound I-880 in Oakland, causing huge delays
-----
Protesters decrying the ongoing war in Gaza shut down a large portion of Interstate 880 near Fifth Street in Oakland Monday morning.All of the northbound lanes of I-880 were blocked due to the protest activity. Traffic appeared backed up for several miles on the highway, according to data from Google Maps.
Some protesters held a banner that said "A15 Economic Blockade / Free Palestine," television coverage of the event showed. Monday, April 15, was a planned day of economic protest
