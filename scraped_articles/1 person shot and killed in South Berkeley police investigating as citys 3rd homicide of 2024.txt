1 person shot and killed in South Berkeley, police investigating as city's 3rd homicide of 2024

One person was shot and killed in South Berkeley on Friday afternoon, according to a 3 p.m. statement from police. Roads are now closed in the area.At 12:45 p.m., the Berkeley Police Department received reports of gunshots heard near the 3200 block of Adeline Street, which is between Fairview Street and Harmon Street and a few blocks south of the Ashby BART station.
