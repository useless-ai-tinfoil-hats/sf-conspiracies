Exclusive: Pay cuts for S.F. city leaders? Supervisor proposes move to save money
-----
As San Francisco faces severe budget cuts to address a huge deficit, one city supervisor wants to cut her own paycheck and those of other city leaders to save money that would go into a rainy day fund.Supervisor Connie Chan, chair of the city's Budget and Appropriations Committee, told the Chronicle on Tuesday she intends to ask Mayor London Breed, her fellow city supervisors, other elected leaders and city department heads to cut their pay by 10%.
