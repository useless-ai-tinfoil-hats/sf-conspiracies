S.F. crime is plummeting, with the exception of one affluent neighborhood
-----
Reported crime has plummeted in San Francisco. In the first four months of 2024, the number of reported crimes fell by 30% compared with the same time period in 2023. The most notable declines have been in property crimes such as larceny theft.But not every neighborhood is experiencing the trend the same way.
