San Francisco's Twin Peaks Tunnel is closed for the next week
-----
An eight-day closure of San Francisco's Twin Peaks Tunnel began Thursday morning as the San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency embarks on a project to retrofit the 106-year-old bore connecting Castro and West Portal train stations.During the work, which is slated to run through Thursday, Aug. 29, all service through the tunnel will be suspended, with trains on the K, L and M lines turning back toward Embarcadero Station at Castro.
