2 killed, 3 taken to hospital after a crash in San Francisco's West Portal
-----
Two people, including a child, were killed and three others were taken to the hospital after a crash in the West Portal neighborhood of San Francisco on Saturday afternoon, the San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency said.The collision occurred near Ulloa Street and Lenox Way, according to SFMTA.
The San Francisco Fire Department on social media urged people to avoid the area, as it "will be closed for a good portion of the day."
