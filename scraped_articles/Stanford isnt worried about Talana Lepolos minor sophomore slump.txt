Stanford isn’t worried about Talana Lepolo’s minor sophomore slump
-----
Twice in the first quarter of Wednesday's win over UC Davis, Talana Lepolo stepped back, took a breath and netted a 3-pointer.Head coach Tara VanDerveer was happy to see it. In the ideal Stanford offense, the sophomore point guard is a threat to score not only from range, but from anywhere on the floor. She certainly showed flashes of that as a freshman, and the Cardinal are hoping she expands on that.
