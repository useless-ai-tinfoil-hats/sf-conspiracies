'Jews and allies' at S.F. vigil demand end to Israel's bombing of Gaza
-----
As the sun set on chilly San Francisco Tuesday, hundreds of pro-Palestine supporters gathered in Dolores Park for a candlelight vigil to honor the lives lost in Gaza since last week's attack by Hamas militants on Israeli citizens that sparked a war and flung the country into chaos.The event was organized by Jewish Voice For Peace Bay Area, a progressive Jewish anti-Zionist organization that has demanded Palestinian liberation from Israeli military control.
