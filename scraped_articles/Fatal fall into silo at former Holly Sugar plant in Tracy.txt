Fatal fall into silo at former Holly Sugar plant in Tracy
-----
A person fell into a large silo and died Saturday at the former Holly Sugar plant in Tracy, authorities said.Firefighters called to the plant around 10:20 a.m. found that the person had fallen about 80 feet into machinery near the bottom of the silo, the South San Joaquin County Fire Authority said in a statement.
The cause of the fall remained under investigation Sunday. The location was previously a sugar refinery, but its current use is unclear.
