Timber company sues PG&E for $225 million in damages from 2021 Dixie fire
-----
A timber company is suing Pacific Gas and Electric Co. for $225 million in damages from the 2021 Dixie Fire, a nearly million-acre blaze ignited by utility power lines.Collins Pine Co. said that the wildfire burned across 55,000 acres of unique Sierra Nevada timberland managed like natural forests with protected habitat and trees of all ages, killing an estimated 65,000 old-growth trees and leaving behind inhospitable, sterilized soils.
