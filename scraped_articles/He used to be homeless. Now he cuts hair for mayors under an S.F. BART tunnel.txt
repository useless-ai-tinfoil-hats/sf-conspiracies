He used to be homeless. Now he cuts hair for mayors under an S.F. BART tunnel
-----
It was Willie Brown on the phone, and he was a bit lost.The former San Francisco mayor was trying to find Beautiful Losers Barbershop, which he understood to be in a tunnel in the bowels of the Montgomery Street BART station. With the mayor on the other end of the line, the barber, Cesar Ramos, walked out with phone in hand and greeted Brown as he walked down the stairs and into the long white tube for a haircut.
