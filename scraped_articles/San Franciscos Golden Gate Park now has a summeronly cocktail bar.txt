San Francisco's Golden Gate Park now has a summer-only cocktail bar
-----
The summertime-only beer and wine garden in Golden Gate Park is reopening this season with a new name and fresh menu.The Whale's Tail — formerly known simply as the Beer and Wine Garden — is scheduled to open on JFK Promenade just in time for Memorial Day weekend, San Francisco's Recreation and Park Department announced in a news release. The garden's new name is inspired by Street Whale, a nearby public art installation featuring a 49-foot whale, part of which juts out from the asphalt
