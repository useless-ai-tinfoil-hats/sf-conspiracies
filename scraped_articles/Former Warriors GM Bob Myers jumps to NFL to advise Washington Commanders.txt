Former Warriors GM Bob Myers jumps to NFL to advise Washington Commanders
-----
Former Golden State Warriors general manager Bob Myers is getting into football.Myers, 48, is part of an advisory group that will work with Washington Commanders owner Josh Harris to search for a head of football operations and head coach after the team fired Ron Rivera early Monday, the Commanders announced.
