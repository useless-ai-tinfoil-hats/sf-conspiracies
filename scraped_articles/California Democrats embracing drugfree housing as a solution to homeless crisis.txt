California Democrats embracing drug-free housing as a solution to homeless crisis
-----
In the face of persistent homelessness, Democrats are embracing more drug-free housing policies even though such ideas have often been viewed as antithetical to "housing first," long the bedrock concept of liberal homeless policy.The dynamic was on display at the Capitol Wednesday morning, when lawmakers voted to advance a bill by Assembly Member Matt Haney, D-San Francisco, that would allow drug-free housing programs to compete for state funding.
