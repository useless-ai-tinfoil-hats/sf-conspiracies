Bay Area casino linked to cluster of 11 tuberculosis cases since 2018
-----
Eleven cases of tuberculosis have been detected in Contra Costa County, with most linked to workers and customers at the California Grand Casino in Pacheco, 2 miles west of Concord, county health officials said Thursday.The cases date back to 2018, and health officials urge anyone who has been to the casino between 2018 and now to get tested.
