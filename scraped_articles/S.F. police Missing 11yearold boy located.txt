S.F. police: Missing 11-year-old boy located
-----
San Francisco police on Wednesday said that an 11-year-old boy who went missing Monday has been found."Thank you to those who assisted in spreading our alert," the department said on social media.
Police did not disclose any information about the boy's condition or where he was located.
