Northern lights illuminate the San Francisco Bay Area night sky
-----
Auroras, also known as northern lights or the aurora borealis, were visible throughout the San Francisco Bay Area on Friday night and early Saturday morning and were reported as far south as San Diego. The rare occurrence was due to a phenomenon known as a solar storm.Graded as a Level 4 storm (out of 5), it marked the first severe geomagnetic storm that the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration has tracked in nearly 20 years.
