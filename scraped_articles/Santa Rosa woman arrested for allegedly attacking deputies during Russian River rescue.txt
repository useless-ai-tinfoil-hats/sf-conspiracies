Santa Rosa woman arrested for allegedly attacking deputies during Russian River rescue
-----
Officials identified the woman who was arrested after resisting authorities who tried to save her as she was drowning in the Russian River, Sonoma County Sheriff's Office officials said Tuesday.The woman was identified as 38-year-old Lacey Mosher, of Santa Rosa. She was arrested on several felony charges, including resisting arrest, battery on a peace officer, violation of probation, and misdemeanor being under the influence of drugs. She is being held without bail, officials said.
