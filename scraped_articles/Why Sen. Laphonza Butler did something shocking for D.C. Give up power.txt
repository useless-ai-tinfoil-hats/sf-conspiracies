Why Sen. Laphonza Butler did something shocking for D.C.: Give up power
-----
Politicians rarely do the unexpected. It's too risky. And risk translates to their greatest fear: losing power.Yet last week, Sen. Laphonza Butler, whom Gov. Gavin Newsom appointed a little over two weeks ago to fill Sen. Dianne Feinstein's seat after her death, surprised many Californians by choosing to give up power and not seek a full six-year term. And she did so for the right reasons — humble ones — and perhaps that's why her move was so surprising. And inspiring.
