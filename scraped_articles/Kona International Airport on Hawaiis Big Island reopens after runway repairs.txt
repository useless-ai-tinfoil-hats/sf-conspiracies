Kona International Airport on Hawaii's Big Island reopens after runway repairs
-----
One of the largest airports on the Big Island was forced to close due to infrastructure damages, triggering cancellations and delays over back-to-back days, but officials announced the worst is over.Ellison Onizuka Kona International Airport at Keahole reopened Tuesday morning after visible cracks in the runway forced staff to shut it down Monday afternoon, according to statements from the Hawaii Department of Transportation and the Hawaii Tourism Authority.
