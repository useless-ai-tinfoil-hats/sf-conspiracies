Armistead Maupin leaves the ‘City’ for final book in famed S.F. series
-----
"I still believe San Francisco is the greatest city in the world," Armistead Maupin tells the Chronicle from his London home.The 80-year-old author would know. For nearly 50 years, the longtime San Franciscan's work was synonymous with the idealized concept of the city, a place where you could move without a job or prospects, be taken in by a friendly landlord who welcomes you with a joint, and where "doom loop" is something you might get from a bad bottle of poppers.
