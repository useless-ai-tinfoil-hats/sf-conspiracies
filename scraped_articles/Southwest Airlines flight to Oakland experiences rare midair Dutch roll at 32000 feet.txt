Southwest Airlines flight to Oakland experiences rare midair Dutch roll at 32,000 feet
-----
A Southwest Airlines flight from Phoenix to Oakland experienced a Dutch roll at 32,000 feet midflight last month, causing the aircraft to be taken out of service.* Read more: What is a Dutch roll? A look at the phenomenon that grounded a Southwest jet
The crew of Southwest Flight 746, operated with a Boeing 737-8 MAX, regained control and safely landed at San Francisco Bay Oakland International Airport about an hour after the incident, according to a Federal Aviation Administration
