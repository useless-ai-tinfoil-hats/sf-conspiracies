California’s home insurer of last resort could see ‘substantial’ rate increase
-----
Californians on the FAIR Plan — the state's "insurer of last resort" — can expect to see their rates rise sharply in the near future, the plan's president testified Thursday."There's going to be a substantial increase. That's as much as I can share," FAIR Plan President Victoria Roach told the Little Hoover Commission, a state oversight committee.
