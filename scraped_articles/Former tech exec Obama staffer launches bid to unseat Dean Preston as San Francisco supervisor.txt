Former tech exec, Obama staffer launches bid to unseat Dean Preston as San Francisco supervisor
-----
Bilal Mahmood, a former technology executive, is launching his campaign against Dean Preston to represent San Francisco's District 5 on the Board of Supervisors.Mahmood posted the announcement Tuesday on X, formerly known as Twitter, alongside a wide-ranging interview with the San Francisco Standard.
The Democrat will formally file election papers on Thursday, he told the Standard, and has announced a campaign kick-off event for Saturday, Jan. 27.
