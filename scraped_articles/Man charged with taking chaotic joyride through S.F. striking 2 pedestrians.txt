Man charged with taking chaotic joyride through S.F., striking 2 pedestrians
-----
A man accused of driving recklessly Tuesday and striking two pedestrians with his car, including a middle school student, was charged with multiple crimes, including leaving the scene of an accident and driving with a suspended license."San Francisco's streets must be safe for pedestrians, motorists, bicyclists and our children from dangerous and reckless drivers," District Attorney Brooke Jenkins said in announcing the charges filed against Rodney Jefferson.
