Major increase in the cost to camp in Joshua Tree National Park under consideration
-----
Soon, camping in one of the most surreal parks in California may come at a higher cost.According to a June news release from the National Park Service, staying overnight in Joshua Tree National Park could become about 50% to 66% more expensive starting in 2025. The proposed fees, which would go into effect Jan. 1, are still under consideration and open for public comment until the end of this month.
