Historic downtown Oakland building, designed by Julia Morgan, sells for $22.5M
-----
Oakland's YWCA building has seen a lot in its 110 years, including arts students sleeping in its spaces — it was a California College of the Arts dorm — and even a co-living experiment (a company called Common once rented out private bedrooms in the building). Now it's reportedly going to house teachers who work at the school downstairs.Built in 1914, 1515 Webster St. is filled with enviable design elements. Upon entry, visitors are met with gray-veined white and pinkish-beige marble
