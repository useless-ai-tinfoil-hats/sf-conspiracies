Warriors' Draymond Green suspended five games by NBA after scuffle
-----
Draymond Green has received a five-game suspension from the NBA for his role in Tuesday's altercation between the Golden State Warriors and Minnesota Timberwolves.Green's suspension will begin Thursday when the Warriors host Oklahoma City. According to an NBA release, the length of the Warriors forward's suspension is based in part on his history of unsportsmanlike acts. In addition, Warriors guard Klay Thompson and Minnesota's Jaden McDaniels and Rudy Gobert have each been fined
