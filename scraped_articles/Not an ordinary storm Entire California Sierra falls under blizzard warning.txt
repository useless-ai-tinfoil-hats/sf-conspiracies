'Not an ordinary storm': Entire California Sierra falls under blizzard warning
-----
A blizzard warning was issued for California's entire Sierra Nevada on Thursday and it is slated to remain in effect through at least Sunday morning as a fierce storm bears down on the region. All areas of the mountain range down to 2,000 feet fall under the warning, according to the National Weather Service.
