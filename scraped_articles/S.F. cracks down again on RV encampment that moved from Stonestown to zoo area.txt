S.F. cracks down again on RV encampment that moved from Stonestown to zoo area
-----
One week after San Francisco officials cleared an RV encampment on Winston Drive outside Stonestown Galleria, they're cracking down on the group's new home by towing or threatening to tow their vehicles.On Thursday, the city conducted at least eight tows from the RV encampment's new location near the San Francisco Zoo, according to the mayor's office.
