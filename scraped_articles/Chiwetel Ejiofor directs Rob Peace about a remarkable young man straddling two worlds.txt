Chiwetel Ejiofor directs ‘Rob Peace,’ about a remarkable young man straddling two worlds
-----
When Jeff Hobbs' acclaimed nonfiction book "The Short and Tragic Life of Robert Peace" came out in 2014, it drew praise for its humane portrait of a brilliant young man straddling two very different worlds.The real-life Robert DeShaun Peace, now the subject of a new movie written and directed by Chiwetel Ejiofor, grew up in an impoverished neighborhood in 1990s East Orange, N.J., and followed his insatiable curiosity about evolutionary science to Yale.
