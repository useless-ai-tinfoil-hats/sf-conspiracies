More details on Half Moon Bay plane crash: Third body found
-----
LATEST Jan. 29, 3:15 p.m. The San Mateo County Sheriff's Office found a third body Sunday near the area of a Jan. 14 Half Moon Bay plane crash, Gretchen Spiker, a spokesperson for the office, told SFGATE. The body has not been identified by authorities yet, the San Mateo County Coroner's Office told SFGATE.According to the National Transportation Safety Board, the home-built plane crashed shortly after takeoff from Half Moon Bay Airport with four people aboard.
