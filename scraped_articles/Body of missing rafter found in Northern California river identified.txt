Body of missing rafter found in Northern California river identified
-----
LATEST June 17, 4:41 p.m. The man who was swept away while rafting with a group of people on the American River over the weekend has been identified as Zongchar Moua, 34, of Sacramento, the Sacramento County Coroner's Office confirmed to SFGATE. His cause of death has not yet been released.June 16, 5:26 p.m. Officials found the body of a man in his 30s who went missing while rafting with a large group on the American River near Clay Banks in Rancho Cordova over the weekend.
