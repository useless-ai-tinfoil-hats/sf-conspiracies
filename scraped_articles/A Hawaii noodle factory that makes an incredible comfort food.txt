A Hawaii noodle factory that makes an incredible comfort food
-----
Every time I go home to Hawaii, I always make a special stop to Chun Wah Kam Noodle Factory. The family business has been serving made-from-scratch noodles since 1942, but the reason I make the trip repeatedly is for their manapua.I grew up on manapua, fluffy steamed or baked buns filled with meat. My favorite is char siu. It's a beloved food in Hawaii, right up there with malasadas and mochi.
