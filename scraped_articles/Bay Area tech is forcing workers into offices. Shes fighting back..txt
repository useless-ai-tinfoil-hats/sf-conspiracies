Annie Dean's position is firm, as she told SFGATE recently: If you as a worker don't want to go to an office, "Vote with your feet" and find a remote-friendly company.Dean is a vice president at San Francisco and Sydney-based Atlassian, where she is tasked with leading the roughly 10,700-worker company's "Team Anywhere" efforts.
