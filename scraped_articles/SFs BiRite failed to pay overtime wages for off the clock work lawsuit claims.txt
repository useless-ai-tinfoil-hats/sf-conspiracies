SF's Bi-Rite failed to pay overtime wages for 'off the clock' work, lawsuit claims
-----
A former Bi-Rite employee has filed a class action lawsuit against the San Francisco grocery chain with a series of allegations that include unpaid overtime, wage statement violations and rest break violations.In the lawsuit filed on Jan. 3 at the Superior Court of California for the County of San Francisco, lawyers for Connor Brown, a former employee at the Bi-Rite store at 550 Divisadero St., claimed that Brown and members in the class action lawsuit regularly worked "off-the-clock"
