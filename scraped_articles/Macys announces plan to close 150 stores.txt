Macy's announces plan to close 150 stores
-----
Macy's announced plans Tuesday to close 150 of its weakest namesake stores nationwide through 2026 in order to focus on modernizing the shopping experience at approximately 350 stores that the retailer will continue to operate.The New York company said in a statement that it's also bolstering its luxury brands with plans to open 15 Bloomingdale's department stores.
