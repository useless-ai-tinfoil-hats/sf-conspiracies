Bay Area celebrity chef Michael Chiarello dies at 61
-----
Michael Chiarello, the celebrity chef who ran renowned restaurants in San Francisco and Napa Valley, died Friday night, sources told the Chronicle. He was 61.The cause of death could not be immediately confirmed. Former Chronicle food critic Michael Bauer posted on X, formerly known as Twitter, that Chiarello died of an acute allergic reaction that led to anaphylactic shock.
