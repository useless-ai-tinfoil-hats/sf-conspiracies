Stanford Live announces 2024-25 season lineup
-----
Stanford Live boasts a 2024-25 season lineup featuring more than 60 performances, including five newly commissioned productions as well as one U.S. and one world premiere.It all kicks off on Sept. 29 with "Fiesta Sonora: A Celebration of Community," starring Mariachi Arcoiris de Los Angeles, Ballet Folklórico Nueva Antequera and Filarmónica Maqueos Music for a celebration of Central American music at Bing Concert Hall.
