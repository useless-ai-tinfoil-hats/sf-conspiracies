Bay Area campuses hit hard by big enrollment drop at California State Universities
-----
California State University has suffered stunning enrollment losses since 2019, with some of the steepest declines at three Bay Area campuses: San Francisco State, Cal State East Bay and Sonoma State, a new analysis shows.Enrollment in the 23-campus system — the nation's largest public university — fell by 27,881 students, or 6.5%, between 2019 and 2023, indicating that CSU has yet to recover from the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic.
