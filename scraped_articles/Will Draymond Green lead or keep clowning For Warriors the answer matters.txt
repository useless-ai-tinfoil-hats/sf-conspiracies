Will Draymond Green lead or keep clowning? For Warriors, the answer matters
-----
Clown.That's what Minnesota Timberwolves center Rudy Gobert called Draymond Green after Tuesday night's tiff at Chase Center in the Golden State Warriors' latest loss.
Forget, for now, the irony of Gobert calling someone a clown. Ol' Elbows Gobert has a bit of a clown rep around the league himself.
But Gobert might have struck a nerve. Green had never tried to win Mr. Congeniality. He embraces his role as a villain, but he is edging dangerously close to clown status.
