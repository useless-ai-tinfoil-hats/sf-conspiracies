Israeli musicians revise plans for season opener at Kohl Mansion
-----
The Israeli Chamber Project, set to open the concert season at Music at Kohl Mansion, plans to perform as scheduled, but with a different program and a somewhat different lineup of musicians after the latest war between Israel and Hamas prevented some performers from traveling.Fighting has continued unabated since the incursion into Israel on Saturday, Oct. 7, by members of Hamas. More than 2,300 people have been killed in Israel and Gaza, according to the Associated Press.
