Large fire rips through building in Berkeley
-----
A fire ripped through a large building in an industrial area of West Berkeley near Aquatic Park on Wednesday morning, sending up a massive plume of smoke that was visible from Interstate 80, multiple media outlets reported. The fire was first reported just before 3 a.m. near 7th Street and Heinz Avenue, according to KGO-TV.A blown transformer, downed power line and a collapsed roof were among the challenges firefighters faced when battling flames this morning, Berkeley Fire Department
