Fire weather watch issued for portions of SF Bay Area
-----
The National Weather Service issued a fire weather watch for the interior North Bay mountains of Sonoma and Napa counties, 11 p.m. Wednesday into 11 a.m. Thursday.Gusty northerly winds and low humidity levels are expected to create conditions where wildfires could spark and spread easily. The weather service advised against outdoor burning.
A fire weather watch is less severe than a red flag warning. The weather service said humidity levels won't be low enough and winds powerful
