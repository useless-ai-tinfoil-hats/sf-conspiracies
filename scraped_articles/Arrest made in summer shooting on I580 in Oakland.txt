Arrest made in summer shooting on I-580 in Oakland
-----
An Oakley man was arrested Thursday on suspicion of shooting at another car on I-580 in June, according to the California Highway Patrol's Golden Gate office.Nestor Palacios, 57, is accused of shooting at a car driving down I-580 near State Route 24, hitting the car but not the driver. CHP officers arrested Palacios Thursday night and found a concealed loaded pistol on him, officials said Saturday.
