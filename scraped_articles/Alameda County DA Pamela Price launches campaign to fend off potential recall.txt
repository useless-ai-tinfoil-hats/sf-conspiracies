Alameda County DA Pamela Price launches campaign to fend off potential recall
-----
Alameda County District Attorney Pamela Price kicked off a campaign in Oakland to marshal her supporters against a recall drive that appears to be building momentum.Hip-hop artist Mistah F.A.B., civil rights attorney Walter Riley and actor Danny Glover hosted the event Thursday to commemorate the Protect the Win committee, an effort to keep Price in office and honor the will of the voters, a campaign spokesperson said.
