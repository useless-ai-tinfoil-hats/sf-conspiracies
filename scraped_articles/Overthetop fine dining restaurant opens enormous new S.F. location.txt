Over-the-top fine dining restaurant opens enormous new S.F. location
-----
When San Francisco's shape-shifting restaurant Merchant Roots — among the Bay Area's top fine dining spots — reopens on Friday, Aug. 23, it will unveil its biggest change to date.After closing its original Fillmore district restaurant last spring, Merchant Roots will resurface inside a massive, 4,500-square-foot space, magnifying the restaurant's footprint by five times — yet will serve only 12 customers per seating.
