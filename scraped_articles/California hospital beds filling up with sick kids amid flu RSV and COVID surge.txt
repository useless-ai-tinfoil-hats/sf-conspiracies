California hospital beds filling up with sick kids amid flu, RSV and COVID surge
-----
Hospitals and emergency rooms in California and across the nation may have to ration care by the end of the month, federal health authorities warned this week.Officials are particularly worried about an insufficient number of beds for children in pediatric hospitals and wards throughout the country as respiratory illnesses hit especially hard among Americans younger than 18 years old.
