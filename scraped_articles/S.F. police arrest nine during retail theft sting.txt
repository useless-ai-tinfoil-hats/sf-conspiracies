S.F. police arrest nine during retail theft sting
-----
San Francisco police arrested nine people on suspicion of stealing from a Bernal Heights grocery store on Friday.Police assigned to the Ingleside Station arrested five adults who were cited and released and four minors who were transported to the Community Assessment Referral Center, an organization that manages cases of youth who are arrested. Seven of the nine people arrested were suspected of organized retail crime.
