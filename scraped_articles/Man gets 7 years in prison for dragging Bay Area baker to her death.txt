Man gets 7 years in prison for dragging Bay Area baker to her death
-----
The man accused of killing the owner of Angel Cakes in Oakland has been sentenced to seven years in prison, according to multiple reports.Ishmael Burch, 20, was sentenced Friday after he pleaded guilty to one count of voluntary manslaughter and one count of robbery, according to a statement sent to SFGATE from the Alameda County District Attorney's Communications Bureau.
