Arrest made 36 years after boy was found murdered in Vallejo, according to reports
-----
An arrest was finally made this week, 36 years after 6-year-old Jeremy Stoner was found dead near his home in Vallejo, in a case that gripped the Bay Area for years, according to newspaper reports.After picking up the case last year for the first time in decades, detectives used DNA evidence to determine a new suspect, Fred Cain III of Central Point, Oregon, according to CBS News, which first reported the arrest.
