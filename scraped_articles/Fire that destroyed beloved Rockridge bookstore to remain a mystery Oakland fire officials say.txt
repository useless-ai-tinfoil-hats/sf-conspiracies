Fire that destroyed beloved Rockridge bookstore to remain a mystery, Oakland fire officials say
-----
Fire officials said Tuesday they could not determine the cause of a fast-moving fire that ravaged a beloved bookstore in Oakland's Rockridge neighborhood last month.Firefighters responded to the July 30 fire at East Bay Booksellers on the 400 block of College Avenue just before 5:30 a.m. The blaze destroyed the bookstore. The second floor above the bookstore, which housed a handful of offices, collapsed.
