Identities, other details released in fatal shootings at East Bay auto center
-----
Authorities have released new details in last week's deadly shootings at an East Bay auto service center, including the identity of the gunman who law enforcement said killed the mother of his child before he was fatally shot by responding officers.In a news release Thursday night, the Alameda County Sheriff's Office identified Lamar Walker, 24, of Vacaville as the man who killed 24-year-old Richmond resident Alonna Gallon last Friday at the Toyota of Berkeley Service Center in Albany.
