Fueled by 5 Taco Bell stops, she ran a marathon through San Francisco
-----
As she ran through San Francisco on Sunday, Camille Fassett fueled herself with Taco Bell. First, a Breakfast Crunchwrap. Then, throughout the day, Cinnamon Twists, Cheesy Street Chalupas, Cinnabon Delights and Spicy Potato Soft Tacos.By the time she finished her 26.3-mile route, she'd stopped and eaten at five of the fast-food company's locations.
