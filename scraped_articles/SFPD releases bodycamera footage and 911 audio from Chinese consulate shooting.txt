SFPD releases body-camera footage and 911 audio from Chinese consulate shooting
-----
On Thursday, the San Francisco Police Department released 911 dispatch audio and body-camera footage from the Oct. 9 crash at the Chinese consulate building, revealing that the man who crashed his vehicle — then was shot and killed at the scene by police — was armed with a knife.The recordings were played during a virtual town hall hosted by the police department, where SFPD Chief Bill Scott also answered questions regarding the details of the crash and subsequent shooting.
