Forget Taylor Swift, 49ers bring out the celeb big guns, including Grateful Dead's Bob Weir
-----
The NFL's favorite celebrity, Taylor Swift, may have been on hand to watch her beau Travis Kelce and the Kansas City Chiefs take down the Baltimore Ravens in the AFC championship game. But with celebrities like the Grateful Dead's Bob Weir on hand, the NFC championship game between the San Francisco 49ers and the Detroit Lions was hardly lacking for star power.Weir, a documented Bay Area sports fan, arrived, characteristically, in open-toed sandals.
