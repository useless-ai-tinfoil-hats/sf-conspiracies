Well-loved Bay Area bookstore 'completely burned' in morning fire
-----
A two-story building with a well-loved independent bookstore that fronts College Avenue in Oakland's Rockridge district was destroyed in an early morning fire Tuesday, officials said.The fire department received a 911 call just before 5:30 a.m. reporting the fire at 5433 College Ave., said Michael Hunt, a spokesperson for the Oakland Fire Department. "It was a three-alarm fire," he said. "Typically, the alarms are based on both the size of the fire and the risk the fire presents."
