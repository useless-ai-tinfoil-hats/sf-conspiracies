$75 million yacht owned by tech billionaire spotted in San Francisco Bay
-----
Some people arrive in the Bay Area by car or plane. Others, like Whatsapp co-founder Jan Koum, make larger waves.The Ukraine-born billionaire's superyacht was spotted in the San Francisco Bay on Tuesday, with its presence confirmed by ship-tracking website MarineTraffic.org. It's a monstrous ride: Named "Mogambo," the custom-built ship is more than 240 feet long and sold to Koum for $75 million in 2012, Luxury Launches reported.
A 2022 article said the yacht holds 17 crew members
