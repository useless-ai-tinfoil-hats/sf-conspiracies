Family rescued from difficult desert trail by helicopter near Palm Springs
-----
A green, white and gold rescue helicopter whipped up a cloud of dust east of Palm Springs on Wednesday, lowering itself toward a popular canyon hike near Coachella. Minutes later, the Riverside County Sheriff's Office aviation unit's helicopter took off with two people, after one person in a family party experienced a medical emergency.
