Yelp names top 100 pizzerias in the U.S. based on Yelp Elites. Bay Area has 3.
-----
Feb. 9 was National Pizza Day, and without missing a beat, Yelp dropped a list of the top 100 pizza parlors in the country, as chosen by Yelp Elites. As a state, California had 10 restaurants listed and the Bay Area had three.Representing the Bay Area were Golden Boy Pizza (15th), the popular North Beach joint that is slated to expand to San Francisco's west side, Cheese Board Pizza (20th) in Berkeley and Tony's Pizza Napoletana (32nd) from San Francisco's most famous pizza chef.
