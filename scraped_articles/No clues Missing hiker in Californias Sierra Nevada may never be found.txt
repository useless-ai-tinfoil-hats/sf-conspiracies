‘No clues’: Missing hiker in California’s Sierra Nevada may never be found
-----
The search for a 71-year-old hiker who went missing in California's Sierra Nevada forest more than a week ago has been suspended.The Tuolumne County Sheriff's Office said Michael Moore was last seen on Aug. 16 after obtaining a permit to traverse Powell Lake in the Emigrant Wilderness in Sonora.
Moore's dog was found alive by hunters the next day near Forest Road, west of the Gianelli Trailhead, where his backpack was found a few days later.
