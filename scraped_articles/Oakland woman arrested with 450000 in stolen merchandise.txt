Oakland woman arrested with $450,000 in stolen merchandise
-----
An Oakland woman accused of possessing more than $450,000 from Bay Area retail stores has been arrested, the California Highway Patrol said Friday.Claudia Cruz, 35, was booked into an Alameda County jail on suspicion of organized retail crime, possession of stolen property and conspiracy to commit a crime, according to the CHP.
