S.F. IKEA food hall will get bakery from ‘world's best’ restaurant co-founder
-----
A highly anticipated Scandinavian food hall joining the downtown San Francisco IKEA will include a bakery from the co-founder of a restaurant that has frequently topped a list of the World's 50 Best Restaurants.IKEA's real estate arm, Ingka Centres, announced that Claus Meyer, who started Coppehagen's Noma with chef Rene Redzepi, will open a bakery cafe at their Market Street mall's Scandinavian food court, named Saluhall.
