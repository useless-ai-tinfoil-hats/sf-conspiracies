Firefighters extinguish smoky blaze at Redwood City metal recycling facility
-----
A fire that erupted at a Redwood City metal recycling facility was extinguished Wednesday evening.Officials with the Bay Area Air Quality District said they were investigating the blaze at Sims Metal at 699 Seaport Blvd. and advised the public to remain indoors to avoid exposure.
