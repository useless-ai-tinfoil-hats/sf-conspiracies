Annual S.F. 4/20 gathering in Golden Gate Park canceled this year. Will crowds stay home?
-----
San Francisco's 4/20 festivities in Golden Gate Park, a massive celebration of cannabis and cannabis culture, will not take place this year, organizers and city officials said Monday.The city-sanctioned event, they said, was canceled for three reasons: economic challenges in the cannabis industry, a lack of sponsorships, and city budget cuts, which left the Recreation and Park Department unable to staff the festivities.
