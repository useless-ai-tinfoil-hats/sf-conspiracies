Another luxury watch store opens in S.F.’s Union Square: ‘The visibility is huge’
-----
Breitling has joined Union Square's luxury watch boom with the opening of a new boutique, the Swiss company's first in San Francisco.The 1,400-square-foot store at 299 Post St., by the northeast corner of Union Square, opened last month. Breitling spent upward of $1 million to build out the space, previously occupied by Johnston & Murphy, and signed a 10-year lease, said Thierry Prissert, president of Breitling USA.
