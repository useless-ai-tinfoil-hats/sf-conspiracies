How have recent California storms impacted the Sierra snowpack?
-----
California's Sierra Nevada snowpack, one of the state's most crucial water sources, was paltry at the start of the state's wet season. The fall months were abnormally dry, and many of the early winter storms brought more rain than snow.Things improved in February. A series of storms, some of them colder, swept the Golden State, piling up powder, especially in the northern half of the mountain range.
