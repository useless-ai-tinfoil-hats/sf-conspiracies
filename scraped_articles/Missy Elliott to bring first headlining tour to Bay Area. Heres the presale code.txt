Missy Elliott to bring first headlining tour to Bay Area. Here’s the presale code
-----
Missy Elliott plans to set off on her first-ever headlining tour this summer with support from Busta Rhymes, Ciara and Timbaland.The multiplatinum-selling rapper plans to bring Out of This World — The Missy Elliott Experience to two dozen North American cities, kicking off the tour in Vancouver, British Columbia, on July 4, and concluding in Rosemont, Ill., on Aug. 22. She is scheduled to make a Bay Area stop on July 9, at Oakland Arena.
A Live Nation presale begins at 10 a.m.
