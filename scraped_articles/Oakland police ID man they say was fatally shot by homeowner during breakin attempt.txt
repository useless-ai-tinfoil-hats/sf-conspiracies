Oakland police ID man they say was fatally shot by homeowner during break-in attempt
-----
Police on Monday released the name of a 40-year-old man who they say was fatally shot by an Oakland homeowner in a break-in attempt last week.Martel Ford was one of three people who tried to break into a home on 98th Avenue near Burr Street around 6 p.m. on June 10, according to police. The trio scaled a backyard fence, but the homeowner confronted them and shot Ford, Oakland police said.
