Healthy Paws Pet Insurance review: Pros, cons, and pricing
-----
Healthy Paws Pet Insurance at a glanceHealthy Paws' big selling point is unlimited coverage (no maximum annual or lifetime payouts) with a wide range of customizable deductibles and reimbursement rates. It also promises a fast claims process. Keep in mind, however, that the pet insurer only offers one plan type (accident-and-illness), and you might encounter other limitations based on your pet's age, breed, location, or other details.
