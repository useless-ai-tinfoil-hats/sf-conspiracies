1 person killed in 'major crash' on East Bay freeway, CHP says
-----
A person driving a motorcycle died Thursday morning in a collision on westbound Interstate 580 in Livermore that involved multiple vehicles, California Highway Patrol spokesperson Tyler Hahn told SFGATE.Hahn said that two or three vehicles and a motorcycle were involved in the collision. Officers are "still gathering information," he said. CHP received a call about the collision on westbound 580 just west of North Livermore Avenue at 5:40 a.m.
