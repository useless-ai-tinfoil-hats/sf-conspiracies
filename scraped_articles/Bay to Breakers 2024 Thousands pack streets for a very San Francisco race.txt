Bay to Breakers 2024: Thousands pack streets for ‘a very San Francisco race’
-----
Clad in jogging gear, colorful costumes and occasionally little beyond their birthday suits, thousands of runners and revelers took to San Francisco's streets Sunday morning for the 111th Bay to Breakers.World famous as much for its imaginative attire as its competitive runners, the 12-kilometer run stretches from the bay at the Embarcadero to the breakers at Ocean Beach, just beyond the Great Highway.
