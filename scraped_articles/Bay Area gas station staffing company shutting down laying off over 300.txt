Bay Area gas station staffing company shutting down, laying off over 300
-----
A Bay Area gas station staffing company is shutting down and laying off 337 workers, according to state filings.Walnut Creek-based Aura Management Co. said in a state filing this month that its contract with gas company BP was terminated, forcing Aura to "terminate all its active employees and cease operations" by June 26.
