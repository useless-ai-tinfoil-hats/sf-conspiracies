S.F. Carnaval parade brings Indigenous communities to forefront: ‘Let the world know we exist’
-----
When word reached Fernando Gordillo that the theme for San Francisco's 46th Carnaval Grand Parade was "Honoring Indigenous roots," he had an executive decision to make in his position as mayor of Yanga, a municipality in Veracruz, five hours south of Mexico City."We have to be there to let the world know that we exist," Gordillo said through an interpreter as he waited to march in the Sunday morning sun.
