Three people shot near West Oakland BART Station
-----
Three people were shot in Oakland Monday morning and were in stable condition after receiving medical treatment at a hospital, according to police.Around 11 a.m., police received a call to the 1400 block of Eighth Street, where they found multiple shell casings. While police investigated the casings, they received a call from a hospital that there were three people in the hospital for gunshot wounds. Two adults and one minor were in stable condition.
