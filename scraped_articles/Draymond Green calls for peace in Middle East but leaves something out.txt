Draymond Green calls for peace in Middle East, but leaves something out
-----
After Draymond Green's first full practice back with the Golden State Warriors, there were plenty of basketball questions for the star to answer. But before answering those, Green went out of his way at the start of the post-practice press conference to passionately address the ongoing conflict between Israel and Hamas.In a two-minute address to begin his press conference, Green called for an end to the "killing of innocent people" in this conflict.
