Brush fire shuts down portion of I-580 in East Bay
-----
A grass fire shut down a portion of Interstate 580 in Livermore Tuesday morning, creating traffic delays during the busy morning commute, officials said.The California Highway Patrol said just after 8 a.m. that the right three lanes of I-580 at North Flynn Road were closed. "Expect delays," the agency said. You can check current road conditions with Caltrans.
