A rare piece of Bay Area history is for sale on Craigslist
-----
A rare piece of Bay Area history is up for sale on Craigslist, and for $8,500, you can be its proud new owner.A neon sign from Lanai Liquors — a liquor store that once sat next to Lanai, a fabled Tiki bar and restaurant in San Mateo from the atomic era — has been unearthed from storage. The double-sided, 10-foot-5-inch-by-10-foot-5-inch relic's neon tubes are mostly intact, the post reads, and it offers a unique window into Bay Area history.
