Largest green space in Los Angeles named to Fodor's 'No List 2024'
-----
One of California's most accessible and protected lands has been named to Fodor's "No List" for 2024.The annual list looks at over-tourism, habitat destruction and other factors that are harming some of the world's most beautiful natural spaces. These issues lead to a rare do-not-travel recommendation from the famous guidebook company, and for 2024, only one California spot has landed on the list.
