9-year-old dies in East Bay highway crash, driver flees
-----
A 9-year-old girl died after an SUV with six occupants rolled over multiple times on an East Bay freeway Sunday.The California Highway Patrol was alerted to the crash at 5:14 a.m. when a Mazda CX-9 traveling northbound on Interstate 680 overturned with five passengers and the driver, CHP said in a Sunday social media post. The crash happened near Concord, at the split near State Route 242, when the driver veered off from the road and into the dirt median.
