Stanford football edged by Arizona on fourth-quarter TD to drop to 0-3
-----
Stanford and Arizona were evenly matched in the statistics Saturday at Stanford Stadium and were nearly even on the scoreboard.The Wildcats pulled out a 21-20 decision, handing Stanford its third consecutive loss.
Joshua Karty connected on a 46-yard field-goal attempt with 3:09 left to cut Stanford's deficit to 21-20.
Arizona (3-1, 1-0 Pac-12) made two first downs on the ensuing possession and was able to run out the clock.
