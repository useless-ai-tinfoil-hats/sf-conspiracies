‘Speed and reckless driving’ blamed in Antioch crash that killed two boys, 12 and 13
-----
Two boys, ages 12 and 13, were killed Friday night in a hit-and-run crash in Antioch, police said.Officers responded to reports of a collision with injuries at the intersection of West Sixth and G streets around 11:30 p.m. and found two vehicles, one of which had the 12- and 13-year-olds inside. They were taken to a local hospital, where they were pronounced dead.
