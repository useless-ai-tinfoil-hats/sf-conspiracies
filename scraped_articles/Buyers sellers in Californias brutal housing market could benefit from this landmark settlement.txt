Buyers, sellers in California's brutal housing market could benefit from this landmark settlement
-----
A real estate industry policy that critics accuse of making homes more expensive is set to end after an industry giant said Friday it will settle a lawsuit against it.The rule changes will likely cause many home buyers to pay their agents' directly, instead of having the fee included in the sale price of the home, as is currently the standard practice. That could encourage sellers, who no longer have to account for commissions they pay to the buyer's agent in their listing price, to sell
