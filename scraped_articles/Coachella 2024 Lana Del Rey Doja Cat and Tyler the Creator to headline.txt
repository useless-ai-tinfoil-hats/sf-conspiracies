Coachella 2024: Lana Del Rey, Doja Cat and Tyler, the Creator to headline
-----
Lana Del Rey, Tyler, the Creator and Doja Cat are set to headline this year's Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival.The festival is scheduled for two weekends, April 12-14 and April 19-21, in Indio (Riverside County). The lineup, announced on Tuesday, Jan. 16, also includes a surprise: Gwen Stefani's former ska-pop band, No Doubt, will reunite and perform live for the first time in over a decade.
