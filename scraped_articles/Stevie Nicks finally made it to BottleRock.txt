Stevie Nicks finally made it to BottleRock
-----
The third time was a charm for Stevie Nicks at BottleRock Napa Valley.Initially postponing her first two scheduled appearances at the Wine Country music festival, the iconic Rock & Roll Hall of Fame singer finally headlined the main stage on the opening night of the Memorial Day weekend concert on Friday, May 24.
