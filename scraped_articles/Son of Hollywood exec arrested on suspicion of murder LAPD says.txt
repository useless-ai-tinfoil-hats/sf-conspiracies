Son of Hollywood exec arrested on suspicion of murder, LAPD says
-----
The son of a Hollywood executive was arrested in Los Angeles on Wednesday on suspicion of murder after a woman's dismembered torso was found in a dumpster, officials said. Samuel Bond Haskell IV, 35, was detained after the grisly discovery, a news release from the Los Angeles Police Department said.The dismembered body part may belong to Haskell's wife, Mei Haskell, who is missing, LA police Capt.
