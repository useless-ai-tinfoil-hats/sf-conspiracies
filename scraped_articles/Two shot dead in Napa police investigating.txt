Two shot dead in Napa; police investigating
-----
Napa police are investigating a shooting that killed two people on Saturday evening.Officers responded to a report of a shooting on the 400 block of Riverside Drive, a road that runs along the west side of the Napa River, around 8:10 p.m., the police department said. Two female victims were found on the street suffering from at least one gunshot wound each.
