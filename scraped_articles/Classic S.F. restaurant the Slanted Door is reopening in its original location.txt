Classic S.F. restaurant the Slanted Door is reopening in its original location
-----
The Slanted Door, the acclaimed Vietnamese restaurant from chef-owner Charles Phan, said goodbye to the Ferry Building in May. Now it is preparing to resurface at a familiar location.The restaurant will return to 584 Valencia St. in the Mission District, where Phan first opened the restaurant in 1995. A publicist for the restaurant confirmed the news to the Chronicle via email, with a projected opening set for spring 2025.
