Protests shut down Golden Gate Bridge lanes and I-880 in Oakland
-----
Demonstrators protesting Israel's war in Gaza and calling for a ceasefire blocked two Bay Area highways during the rush hour commute on Monday, causing significant delays and bringing traffic to a halt across the region as part of a day of disruption around the country.A group of protestors blocked Interstate 880 in the northbound lane near 5th Avenue, just before Jack London Square, while the another group blocked off parts of the Golden Gate Bridge, leading officials to close the span,
