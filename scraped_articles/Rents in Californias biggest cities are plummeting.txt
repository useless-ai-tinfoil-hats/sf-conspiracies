Rents in California's biggest cities are plummeting
-----
Rents in the majority of California's big cities posted substantial annual declines, according to a new report from rental site Zumper.Rent for one-bedroom units plummeted 9.1% in Oakland, while San Francisco and San Jose saw dips of 1.7% and 2.3% since May 2023, respectively. In Southern California, Los Angeles rents dropped 5% in the past year, while Long Beach rents declined 1.1%.
Even San Diego, which has seen housing prices explode in recent years, saw a one-bedroom rent drop
