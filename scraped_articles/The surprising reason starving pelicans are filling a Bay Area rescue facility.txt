The surprising reason starving pelicans are filling a Bay Area rescue facility
-----
The aviary at International Bird Rescue in Fairfield is stuffed with brown pelicans. The gangly brown birds are spending their time preening, splashing in the pool and most of all, gulping down buckets of desperately needed fish.The Bay Area rescue center and its Southern California counterpart together have treated 300 birds since April 20, all suffering from malnutrition and many with additional related injuries.
