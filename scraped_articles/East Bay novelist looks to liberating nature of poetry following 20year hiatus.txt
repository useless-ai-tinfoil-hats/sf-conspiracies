East Bay novelist looks to ‘liberating’ nature of poetry following 20-year hiatus
-----
In one of his old author photos, Joseph Di Prisco recalls "looking askance like a champ / At nothing in particular." That's how the poet, memoirist, and novelist describes the picture in a poem from his latest collection, part of a retrospective titled "My Last Resume."That condition — looking "askance" at the world — is "a vocation of mine," the poem continues.
