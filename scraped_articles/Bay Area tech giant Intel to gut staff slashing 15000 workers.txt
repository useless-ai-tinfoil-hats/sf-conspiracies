Bay Area tech giant Intel to gut staff, slashing 15,000 workers
-----
Intel, the Santa Clara tech behemoth known for its computer chips, announced plans to slash 15,000 jobs on Thursday.In an email sent to employees and published on Intel's website, CEO Pat Gelsinger called Thursday an "incredibly hard day for Intel as we are making some of the most consequential changes in our company's history."
