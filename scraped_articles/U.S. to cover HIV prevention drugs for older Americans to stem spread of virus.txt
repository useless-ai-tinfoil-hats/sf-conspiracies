U.S. to cover HIV prevention drugs for older Americans to stem spread of virus
-----
A proposed federal policy aims to protect older Americans from contracting HIV by offering free preventive medication, the latest effort to catch up to much of Europe and Africa in stemming the spread of the virus.Under the plan from the Biden administration, Medicare would cover patients' full cost of pre-exposure prophylaxis drugs, which prevent HIV transmission. The drugs, known by the shorthand "PrEP," would be free in pill form and, for the first time, as long-acting injectables
