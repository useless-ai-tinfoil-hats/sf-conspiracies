S.F. Mission District shooting leaves man dead
-----
A man died from a gunshot wound in San Francisco's Mission District on Monday morning, police said.Police responded to a report of a physical assault involving a firearm shortly after 5 a.m. near Shotwell and 20th streets. At the scene, officers found an adult man suffering from a gunshot wound, police said.
The man died on scene despite the lifesaving efforts of police and paramedics.
The man's name was not immediately released.
