On tax deadline day, IRS gives most Californians a new extension
-----
In a last-minute announcement Monday, the Internal Revenue Service once again extended the federal tax filing deadline for most Californians in response to the deadly winter storms that pounded the state at the beginning of the year.The agency said the new deadline is Nov. 16 for businesses and residents in 55 of California's 58 counties to file 2022 federal tax returns and to make payments. The IRS had previously postponed the deadline to Monday.
