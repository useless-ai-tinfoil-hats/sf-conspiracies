Woman assaulted by stranger while running on popular Sonoma County trail
-----
A woman was assaulted on a Sonoma County trail, and the assailant has not been found, authorities said.The assault occurred Thursday evening. At about 8 p.m., Sonoma County sheriff's deputies responded to Joe Rodota Regional Trail, which connects downtown Santa Rosa and Sebastopol, for a report of an attempted sexual assault. Deputies arrived at the scene within minutes and spoke with the victim.
