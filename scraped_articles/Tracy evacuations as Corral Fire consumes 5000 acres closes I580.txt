Tracy evacuations as Corral Fire consumes 5,000 acres, closes I-580
-----
An evacuation warning was issued for portions of Tracy on Saturday evening as firefighters battled a blaze that jumped Interstate 580, according to Cal Fire.Cal Fire and the Alameda County Fire Department reduced containment from 40% to 10% for the growing Corral Fire, which was at roughly 5,000 acres and began Saturday afternoon. Two fire workers have been injured, said Cal Fire Santa Clara Unit Chief Baraka Carter.
