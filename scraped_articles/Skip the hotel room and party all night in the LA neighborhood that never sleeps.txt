Skip the hotel room and party all night in the LA neighborhood that never sleeps
-----
It's about 2:30 a.m. on a Tuesday night turned Wednesday morning, and a lively crowd of 20-somethings — sporting mullets and JNCOs, bumming cigarettes off one another under the spectral glow of street lights — congregate outside a dive bar in LA's Koreatown, one of the city's greatest destinations for food and nightlife. They're not hanging around waiting for a hailed ride, or assessing their last move now that the bars have closed.For many of these barflies, the night is just beginning.
