Man armed with knives threatens people in S.F. near Panhandle; arrest made
-----
San Francisco police arrested a knife-wielding man who they said entered a business in a shopping center near the Panhandle on Monday afternoon.At least five police vehicles, as well as K-9 units and a San Francisco police specialist truck, descended on the area near Fulton Street and Masonic Avenue at around 1:40 p.m. on a report of an armed person at a Chase bank, according to a witness who asked not to be named.
