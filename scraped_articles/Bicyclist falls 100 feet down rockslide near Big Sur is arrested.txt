Bicyclist falls 100 feet down rockslide near Big Sur, is arrested
-----
A bicyclist had to be rescued and airlifted to a hospital after he fell more than 100 feet down a cliff face while trying to illegally traverse a rockslide on Highway 1 just south of Big Sur on Friday, according to multiple reports.The Bay Area News Group reported the biker — identified as a 28-year-old from Lucerne Valley in San Bernardino County — was told by a construction worker that there was no safe passage through Regent's Slide, an active rockslide located near Mile Post 27.5.
