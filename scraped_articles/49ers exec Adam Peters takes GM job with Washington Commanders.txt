49ers exec Adam Peters takes GM job with Washington Commanders
-----
Adam Peters, who was San Francisco 49ers general manager John Lynch's first hire on his second day on the job in January 2017, has been hired elsewhere, becoming the GM of the Washington Commanders on Friday.Peters, 44, the Niners' assistant general manager since 2021 who served as Lynch's right-hand man for seven seasons, accepted the top job in Washington, a league source said.
