California insurance crisis: Another withdrawal to leave tens of thousands without coverage
-----
American National is the latest home insurer to withdraw from California, citing persistent unprofitability in the state.The Texas insurer filed documents with the California Department of Insurance to withdraw from the homeowners insurance business starting this fall, a company spokesperson told the Chronicle.
As of December 2023, American National insured 36,475 homeowners policies in California worth about $37.9 million in premiums, according to the spokesperson.
