San Jose State coach Ken Niumatalolo ready to debut new-look Spartan football
-----
Following Brent Brennan as San Jose State's head coach could have been considered a daunting task for Ken Niumatalolo. Brennan led SJSU to the Mountain West title in 2020 and took the Spartans to three bowl games in the past four seasons before becoming Arizona's head coach in January.SJSU soon thereafter chose Niumatalolo, who spent 15-plus seasons (2007-22) as Navy's head coach.
