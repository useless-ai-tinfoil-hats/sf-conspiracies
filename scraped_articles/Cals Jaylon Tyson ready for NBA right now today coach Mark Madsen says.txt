Cal’s Jaylon Tyson ‘ready for NBA right now, today,’ coach Mark Madsen says
-----
Having spent nine seasons as an NBA forward and six as an assistant coach with the Los Angeles Lakers, Cal head coach Mark Madsen clearly knows what it takes to thrive in the league — and Madsen clearly believes Jaylon Tyson will do just that."The passing, the playmaking, his ability to make reads coming off of ball screens — he's ready for the NBA right now, today," Madsen said in a phone interview with the Chronicle on Tuesday morning.
