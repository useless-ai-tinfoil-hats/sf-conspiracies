Ex-rocker sought in disappearance of Bay Area woman
-----
An East Bay woman has been reported missing and police are searching for her boyfriend, an ex-rock musician who they are calling a "person of interest" in connection with her disappearance.Capitola resident Alice "Alyx" Kamakaokalani Herrmann, 61, has been missing since Dec. 3, when she was seen in Santa Cruz, the El Cerrito Police Department said Sunday. Hermann's relatives reported her disappearance to police after not hearing from her for over a week, the department said.
