‘Killers of the Flower Moon’ review: At nearly 4 hours long, this Scorsese film leaves little impact
-----
Martin Scorsese's "Killers of the Flower Moon" is a bladder-buster of a movie with no obvious bathroom break, no section where the story starts to sag.This makes it, almost by definition, a good and admirable piece of work.
But "Killers of the Flower Moon" is also a lumbering mess, an ungainly and tonally odd film that, for all the strength of its parts, has little cumulative impact.
