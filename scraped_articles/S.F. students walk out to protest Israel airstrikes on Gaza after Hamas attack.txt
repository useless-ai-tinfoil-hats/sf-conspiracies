S.F. students walk out to protest Israel airstrikes on Gaza after Hamas attack
-----
High school students across San Francisco walked out of classrooms Wednesday to protest Israel's bombing of Gaza and demand a cease-fire in the Israel-Hamas war, organizers said.The walkout came a day after a hospital in Gaza City was destroyed, killing hundreds of Palestinians and others sheltering there. Hamas and Israel have blamed each other for damage to the hospital.
