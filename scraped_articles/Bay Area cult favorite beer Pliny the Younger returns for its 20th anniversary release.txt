Bay Area cult favorite beer Pliny the Younger returns for its 20th anniversary release
-----
Russian River Brewing Company's most anticipated beer — Pliny the Younger — is back for its 20th anniversary release.Starting next month, fans of the sought-after triple IPA can grab a pitcher of Pliny the Younger at the brewery's Santa Rosa and Windsor pub locations from March 22 to April 4. You can enjoy a pint even earlier, at SF Beer Week, which starts Feb. 9 at participating locations.
