See Why Lodi Wine Country Should be on Your Bucket List this Holiday Season
-----
As a producer of nearly 85% of all wine in the U.S., California offers plenty of options when it comes to exploring award-winning viticulture. One varietal-rich wine growing region within the Golden State that continues to garner acclaim is Lodi Wine Country. Situated in San Joaquin County and nestled between the scenic San Francisco Bay and Sierra Nevada Mountains, Lodi Wine Country is home to over 125 different award-winning varietals, and more than 85 boutique wineries.
