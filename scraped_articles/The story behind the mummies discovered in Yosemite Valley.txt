The story behind the mummies discovered in Yosemite Valley
-----
More than a century ago, miners claimed to have found two ancient mummies at what is now Yosemite National Park. The news spread like wildfire across America.A mummified woman — said to be a "giantess mummy" at almost 7 feet tall — was reported to be cradling a mummified child in her arms when she was found nestled in a cave near Bridalveil Fall.
