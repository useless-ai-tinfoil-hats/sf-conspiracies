Poll: 72% of voters say S.F. is on the wrong track, 84% support downtown business incentives
-----
San Francisco voters are wary of the city's recovery but remain supportive of city financial incentives to attract businesses and revive downtown, a new poll shows.A strong majority of 84% of respondents said they support offering financial incentives for small businesses to stay or move downtown, while 81% supported the same for large businesses.
