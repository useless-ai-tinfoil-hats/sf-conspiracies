Prolific S.F. burglar convicted of breaking into 6 cars in a single night
-----
A jury convicted a man with an extensive history of car break-ins, officials from the San Francisco District Attorney's office said Friday.Victor Matute-Casco, 28, of San Francisco was found guilty of six counts of second-degree burglary, one count of attempted second-degree burglary and multiple misdemeanor counts after a trial by jury.
The misdemeanor counts were possessing stolen property and resisting, obstructing or delaying a peace officer.
