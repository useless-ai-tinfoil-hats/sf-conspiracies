S.F. jury convicts man of stabbing his wife to death in a church on Palm Sunday
-----
A San Francisco Superior Court jury found a man guilty of fatally stabbing his wife in a church in 2018, prosecutors said Thursday.Wyn Leung, 47, was convicted Tuesday of first-degree murder, domestic violence and a restraining order violation in connection with the death of Jieyun Zhou, according to the San Francisco District Attorney's Office. The jury deliberated for four and a half days.
