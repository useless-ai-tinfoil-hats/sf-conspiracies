Waves of protesters block entrances, demonstrate at SF Federal Building in support of Gaza
-----
Protesters blocked off entryways to the Nancy Pelosi Federal Building in San Francisco on Thursday afternoon, an act meant to persuade Pelosi to sign onto a congressional resolution calling for a cease-fire in Gaza.The demonstration began just after 2 p.m. on Thursday, when dozens of protesters massed at the building at the corner of Seventh and Mission streets. They quickly locked doors with chains and padlocks, and smaller groups of protesters bound themselves together with handcuffs
