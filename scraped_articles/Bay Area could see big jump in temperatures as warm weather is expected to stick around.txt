Bay Area could see big jump in temperatures as warm weather is expected to stick around
-----
Stubborn clouds were slow to clear out of the Bay Area on Monday, but California will be firmly in the grasp of a high-pressure system Tuesday, which should result in a sunnier, warmer day.Clear skies are expected to prevail by midmorning throughout the Bay Area, allowing temperatures to jump 5 to 7 degrees under the strong mid-April sun.
