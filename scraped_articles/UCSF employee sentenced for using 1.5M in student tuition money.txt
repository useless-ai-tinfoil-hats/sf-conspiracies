UCSF employee sentenced for using $1.5M in student tuition money
-----
A UC San Francisco administrator was sentenced Friday to 20 months in prison for redirecting $1.5 million in student tuition money to her personal bank account, officials said.Sandra Eileen Le "abused her position" as an academic program officer at the UCSF School of Nursing's post-master's program by telling students to write tuition checks to her, which she deposited in her own bank account, according to a sentencing memorandum.
