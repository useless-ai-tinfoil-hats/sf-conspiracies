Family, friends mourn popular S.F. shopkeeper, fatally beaten during attempted theft
-----
Family and friends gathered at a San Pablo gravesite Friday morning to say their goodbyes to a beloved San Francisco store clerk who died from injuries he sustained during an attempted theft.Dozens of men and women — most of whom wore customary netelas, white scarf-like garments to signal mourning — stood on each side of the coffin of Yohannes "John" Tewolde as a priest recited prayers and blessings.
