16-year-old killed, 3 other juveniles wounded in shooting at East Bay house party
-----
A 16-year-old was killed and three other juveniles were wounded Saturday night in a shooting at an Oakley house party, according to police.Gunfire erupted about 11:12 p.m. at a home on the 400 block of Shannon Way, where four juveniles were shot, Oakley Police Chief Paul Beard said in a statement posted Sunday on Facebook.
The 16-year-old was pronounced dead at the scene, and the three others were taken to John Muir Medical Center in Walnut Creek with non-life-threatening injuries,
