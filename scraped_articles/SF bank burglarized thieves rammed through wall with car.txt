SF bank burglarized, thieves rammed through wall with car
-----
A business in San Francisco's Outer Richmond neighborhood was burglarized early Wednesday morning, causing "significant damage" to the building, officials said.Police arrived in the area of 3700 block of Balboa Street around 3:40 a.m. and determined that thieves used a vehicle to ram through the building and steal cash, Allison Maxie, a spokesperson for the San Francisco Police Department, told SFGATE. The building's address matches the location of a Bank of America branch.
