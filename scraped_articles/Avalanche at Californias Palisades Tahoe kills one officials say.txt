Avalanche at California's Palisades Tahoe kills one, officials say
-----
One person died and another was injured in an avalanche at Palisades Tahoe in California's Olympic Valley around 9:30 a.m. Wednesday, officials said."The search is complete," Sgt. Dave Smith, a spokesperson for the Placer County Sheriff's Office, said over the phone at 1:30 p.m. Dozens of rescue workers had descended on the scene to search in the snow.
