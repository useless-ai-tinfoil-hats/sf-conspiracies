Cal lineman Brett Johnson honored for persevering through major injuries
-----
Defensive lineman Brett Johnson, who played in all 12 of Cal's regular-season games after missing the previous two seasons because of serious injuries, on Tuesday was named one of the two December recipients of the CalHOPE Courage award.The monthly award, which first was given in February 2022, goes to student-athletes at California colleges and universities "who have overcome the stress, anxiety and mental trauma associated with personal hardships and adversity."
