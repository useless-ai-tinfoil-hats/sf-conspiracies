Wildfire triggers evacuations in California's Sierra foothills
-----
LATEST Aug. 7, 2:20 p.m. The Crozier Fire burning near Placerville had grown to 182 acres by 10:30 a.m. An hour later, Cal Fire announced 5% containment.Evacuation orders and warnings remained in place for the "Mosquito/Swansboro community and surrounding areas," Cal Fire said.
Cal Fire is investigating the cause of the fire.
