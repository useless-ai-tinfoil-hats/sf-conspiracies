Warriors announce California Classic schedule at Chase Center
-----
The Golden State Warriors return to play next month at Chase Center in the sixth annual California Classic.As do the Sacramento Kings, Los Angeles Lakers and Miami Heat.
The showcase before NBA Summer League in Las Vegas begins July 6 at Chase Center and concludes July 10. The Warriors play the Heat on the first day, the Lakers on July 7 and the Kings on July 10. The Lakers play the Kings on July 6 and the Heat on July 10, and Miami plays Sacramento on July 7.
