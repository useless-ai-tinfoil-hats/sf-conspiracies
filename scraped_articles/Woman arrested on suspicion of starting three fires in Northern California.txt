Woman arrested on suspicion of starting three fires in Northern California
-----
A woman has been arrested on suspicion of starting three separate fires in El Dorado County.Officers with Cal Fire and the El Dorado County Sheriff's Office arrested Lacy Young, 41, of Waterford (Stanislaus County) on Thursday after crews extinguished three blazes earlier in the day that were determined to have been intentionally set.
