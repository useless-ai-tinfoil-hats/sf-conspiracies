S.F. supervisor to introduce resolution calling for cease-fire in Gaza, release of all hostages
-----
San Francisco Supervisor Dean Preston is planning to introduce a resolution calling for a cease-fire in Gaza at next week's Board of Supervisors meeting.The Arab Resource and Organization Center announced the action on social media Wednesday evening, and Preston's office confirmed to the Chronicle he is working on resolution language with other supervisors. He expects to introduce a resolution calling for a cease-fire and the release of all hostages on Tuesday.
