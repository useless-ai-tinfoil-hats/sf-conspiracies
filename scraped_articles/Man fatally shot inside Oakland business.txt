Man fatally shot inside Oakland business
-----
A man was shot and killed in an Oakland business late Saturday morning, police said.The shooting occurred about 11:45 a.m. on International Boulevard near 88th Avenue. Officers found the Oakland resident suffering from at least one gunshot wound, police said. The man died at the scene.
The victim's name was not released, pending family notifications.
