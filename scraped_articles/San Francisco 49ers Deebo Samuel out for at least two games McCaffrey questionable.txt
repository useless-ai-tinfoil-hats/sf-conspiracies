San Francisco 49ers' Deebo Samuel out for at least two games, McCaffrey questionable
-----
The San Francisco 49ers will have to make do without star wide receiver Deebo Samuel for at least their next two games. Samuel suffered a shoulder injury in the 49ers' loss to the Cleveland Browns on Sunday. Now, it appears he won't be back until at least November.Samuel had been the team's second-leading receiver, with 20 receptions for 302 yards. The do-everything weapon was also the team's third-leading rusher, with 18 carries for 95 yards.
