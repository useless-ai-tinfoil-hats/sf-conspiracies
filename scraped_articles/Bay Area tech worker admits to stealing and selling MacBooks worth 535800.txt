Bay Area tech worker admits to stealing and selling MacBooks worth $535,800
-----
Andrew Halvorsen, a 49-year-old tech worker from Livermore, pleaded guilty in federal court Monday to stealing MacBook computers worth a total of $535,800 from his Redwood City company.Halvorsen used his position as senior director of information technology to order new Apple MacBooks for company use, which he then sold for cash, according to a plea agreement signed in October and filed Monday in California's Eastern District Court.
