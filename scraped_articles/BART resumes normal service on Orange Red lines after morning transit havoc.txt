BART resumes normal service on Orange, Red lines after morning transit havoc
-----
Equipment issues caused significant morning travel issues for East Bay BART riders Friday morning, knocking out Red and Orange line services between its Richmond and MacArthur stations for hours.After reporting equipment problems early Friday morning, BART said that normal service had resumed on the Red and Orange lines shortly after 11 a.m.
The transit agency did not immediately specify what types of equipment issues workers confronted Friday.
