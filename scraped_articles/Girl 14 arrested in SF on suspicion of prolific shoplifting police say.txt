Girl, 14, arrested in SF on suspicion of 'prolific' shoplifting, police say
-----
Amid an investigation into retail crime at San Francisco Centre mall and nearby stores on Market Street, San Francisco police said Wednesday they arrested a 14-year-old girl accused of stealing more than $30,000 in goods from local stores.The girl, whose name is being withheld due to her age, stole from stores on 15 separate occasions, between April and November, the San Francisco Police Department said in a news release.
