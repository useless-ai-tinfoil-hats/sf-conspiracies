San Francisco's next big statue could be of Bruce Lee
-----
There are statues of Bruce Lee in Hong Kong, Los Angeles, the suburbs of Sydney and a city in the Balkans. So why isn't there one in the city of his birth?A group of San Francisco community members, led by Bruce Lee memorabilia collector Jeff Chinn, are working with the Bruce Lee Foundation to place a statue of the famed martial artist in the City by the Bay.
