Which transfers might Stanford softball pursue to rebuild its depleted roster?
-----
After consecutive Women's College World Series appearances, Stanford softball finds itself back at the drawing board with the transfer of ace pitcher NiJaree Canady.The Cardinal are also losing seniors Regan Krause (pitcher) and Aly Kaneshiro (catcher) and graduate student outfielder Kaitlyn Lim. They still lean young with some promising talent, but in order to contend without Canady, they'll have to turn to players in the transfer portal.
