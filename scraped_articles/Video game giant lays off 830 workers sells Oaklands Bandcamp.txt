Video game giant lays off 830 workers, sells Oakland’s Bandcamp
-----
Epic Games, maker of the hugely popular "Fortnite" video game, is laying off 830 workers and selling the Oakland-based music distributor Bandcamp after just 1½ years of ownership.Epic, which also makes the venerable Unreal Engine for game developers, will cut around 16% of its workforce in the face of lower profits that were outpaced by growing expenses, CEO Tim Sweeney wrote in a memo.
