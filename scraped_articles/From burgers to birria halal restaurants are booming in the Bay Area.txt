From burgers to birria, halal restaurants are booming in the Bay Area
-----
Growing up Muslim, Ahmad Tarin wanted nothing more than to enjoy burgers with his peers. But 15 years ago in Fremont, it wasn't easy finding a great option that was also halal, or permissible under Islamic law. So Tarin took to flipping patties at home for family parties and friends using halal certified beef."Everyone raves about In-N-Out, Shake Shack, Five Guys," said Tarin. "So why can't we do something like that?"
