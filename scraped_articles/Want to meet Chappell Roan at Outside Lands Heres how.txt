Want to meet Chappell Roan at Outside Lands? Here’s how
-----
Chappell Roan fans can enter for the chance to win a meet-and-greet with the highly anticipated Outside Lands performer this year.To enter, festivalgoers must donate to Outside Lands Works, a charitable organization that supports Bay Area music and arts programs. Donors will then be eligible for the opportunity to meet the "Good Luck, Babe!" singer-songwriter on the last day of the San Francisco outdoor music festival on Sunday, Aug. 11.
