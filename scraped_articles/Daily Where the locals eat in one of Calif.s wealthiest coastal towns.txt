Daily: Where the locals eat in one of Calif.'s wealthiest coastal towns
-----
SFGATE food reporter Nico Madrigal-Yankowski recently drove south on Interstate 280, up and over the curvy Highway 17, to explore another side of Carmel-by-the-Sea, one of the wealthiest resort towns along the coast of California.On the surface, it's the kind of place you'd expect to see only high-class restaurants doing haute cuisine, like Michelin-starred Chez Noir and Aubergine. But there's a more down-to-earth, quirky side that pays homage to the town's literary and artistic past...
