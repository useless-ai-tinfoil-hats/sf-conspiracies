Suspect in 1987 killing of Vallejo boy may have killed another child a month earlier, police say
-----
Police believe a man suspected of strangling a Vallejo child in 1987 may have been behind the slaying of another child a month earlier in Martinez.Last week, police in Oregon arrested Fred Cain III, 69, after DNA evidence implicated him in the slaying of Jeremy Stoner, according to the Solano County District Attorney's Office. Jeremy was 6 years old when he was abducted in Vallejo and later found sexually assaulted and strangled.
