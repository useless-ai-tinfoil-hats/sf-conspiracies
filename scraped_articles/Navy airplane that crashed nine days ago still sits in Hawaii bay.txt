Navy airplane that crashed nine days ago still sits in Hawaii bay
-----
A large U.S. Navy aircraft still sits in a Hawaii bay after it crashed into the ocean nine days ago. The plane can be seen partially submerged in Kaneohe Bay, a popular site for snorkeling and boating. It's sitting mostly on its landing gear atop coral and sand, but its left engine is resting directly on coral.The P-8A Poseidon aircraft overshot the runway on Nov. 20 at Marine Corps Base Hawaii in Kaneohe.
