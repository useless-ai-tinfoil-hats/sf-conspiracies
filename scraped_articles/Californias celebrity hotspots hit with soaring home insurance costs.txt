California’s celebrity hotspots hit with soaring home insurance costs
-----
Some of California's most famous — and priciest — ZIP codes are also the areas where home insurance costs are soaring the most.A recent analysis of state data by the Chronicle reveals that homeowners in luxury havens like the 90210 ZIP code, encompassing Beverly Hills, and 91302, which includes Calabasas and Hidden Hills, could shell out over $10,000 annually to insure their homes.
