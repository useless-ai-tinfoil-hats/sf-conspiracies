United passenger tries to get in cockpit on California-bound flight
-----
A United flight to California was forced to return to the gate after a passenger allegedly attempted to open the cockpit.On Sept. 8, flight UA1641 was expected to take off from Chicago O'Hare Airport at 7:40 p.m. and fly to LAX, according to FlightAware. However, while the plane was taxiing to the runway, the flight was forced to return to the gate because of a "passenger disturbance," a spokesperson for United Airlines told SFGATE in a statement.
