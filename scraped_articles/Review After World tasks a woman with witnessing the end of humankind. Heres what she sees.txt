Review: ‘After World’ tasks a woman with witnessing the end of humankind. Here’s what she sees
-----
Let's get this out of the way: Debbie Urbanski, with her debut novel, "After World," has created a beautiful end for the human race.The story is told through the lens of an artificial intelligence known as storyworker ad39-393a-7fbc, which has read "64,213 novels … to learn the craft of human documentation" and takes place hundreds of years in the future when a virus known only as S. has rendered all humans infertile.
But things are not always as they seem in this birthless world.
