Queer Cat Productions closes six years of adventurous theater with healing ritual
-----
"If you'd like to, you are invited to approach the living altar," a person wearing small cat ears said to those who entered the Z Space lobby in San Francisco on Friday, Nov. 3. "You can tell the living altar something that you don't want to ever forget."Niches and sculptures adorned with lavender, yucca, yarrow and marigolds encircled the room.
