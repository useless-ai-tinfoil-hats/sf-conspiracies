3 killed in Bay Bridge crash identified as East Bay residents
-----
Three people killed last week in an early morning crash on the Bay Bridge were Alameda County residents, the San Francisco Medical Examiner's office announced Thursday.The fatal crash unfolded Feb. 15 on the bridge's lower level shortly before 4:30 a.m. when the driver of a white Toyota Tacoma pickup truck rear-ended a red Mini Cooper that was stopped in the fourth lane just before the tunnel, officials said.
