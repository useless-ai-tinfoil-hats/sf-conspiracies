SF braces for heavy traffic ahead of Outside Lands and Kamala Harris event
-----
As city officials prepare for thousands of music lovers to congregate at Golden Gate Park for the Outside Lands music festival, some entrances to the park and streets through it began to close in the days leading up to the event.John F. Kennedy Drive from Transverse Avenue to 36th Avenue is closed from Monday, Aug. 5, to Wednesday, Aug. 14, for the festival, which runs from Friday, Aug. 9, to Sunday, Aug. 11. Other street closures began at Middle Drive, the Polo Field and McLaren Path
