Cluster box of mailboxes stolen from Fremont townhouses in broad daylight
-----
Over a dozen mailboxes were stolen in broad daylight from a Fremont housing complex last week.The theft, first reported by NBC Bay Area, and caught on surveillance footage, shows two people lifting an entire standing container, called a cluster box, of at least 16 mailboxes and putting it into a pickup truck after using a wrench to remove the bolts that secured it to the sidewalk.
