Berkeley police arrest suspect in double stabbing that prompted shelter-in-place order
-----
Berkeley police have arrested a 21-year-old man in connection with a double stabbing that occurred Dec. 8 in the hills near the Claremont Hotel, prompting a temporary shelter-in-place order.Jail records show that Byron Decles was booked Tuesday morning at 3:51 a.m. on a raft of felony charges, including attempted murder, battery with serious bodily injury and second degree burglary. Decles is being held without bail in the Berkeley city jail.
