Hi Felicia ordered to pay more than $100,000 to former workers
-----
A shuttered high-profile Oakland fine dining restaurant has been ordered to pay more than $100,000 to former workers following an investigation by the city.Hi Felicia, which closed in May, violated the city's minimum wage law, according to a letter Oakland's Department of Workplace and Employment Standards sent Wednesday to owner Imana, who goes by her first name only.
The letter directed the business to send restitution checks to over a dozen former employees totaling $105,555.36
