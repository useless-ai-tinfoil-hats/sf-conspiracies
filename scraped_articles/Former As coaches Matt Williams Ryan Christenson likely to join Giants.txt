Former A's coaches Matt Williams, Ryan Christenson likely to join Giants
-----

For the San Francisco Giants, that is more than likely going to mean that Matt Williams and Ryan Christenson will be joining the staff.Both Williams, 57, and Christenson, 49, worked for new Giants manager Bob Melvin in Oakland and in San Diego, and both have strong Bay Area ties.
