Sex work has gotten so rampant again in S.F.’s Mission District, residents are suing the city
-----
A year after San Francisco officials put up bollards to deter sex work on Capp Street in the Mission District, residents say the activity hasn't disappeared — it has just migrated a couple of blocks east.Now, Shotwell Street is the Mission's epicenter for the illicit sex trade, and all the noise and bumper-to-bumper traffic that comes with it. And frustrated neighbors are suing.
