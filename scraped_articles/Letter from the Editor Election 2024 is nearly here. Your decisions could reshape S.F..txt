Letter from the Editor: Election 2024 is nearly here. Your decisions could reshape S.F.
-----
On Nov. 5, 2024, San Francisco voters will decide who will be responsible for leading the city out of one of the most difficult periods in its recent history. The pandemic, the ensuing economic repercussions to the downtown core from remote work, the continuing homelessness crisis and a record level of drug overdoses fueled in part by the emergence of fentanyl have frustrated and angered residents eager for the problems to be solved.
