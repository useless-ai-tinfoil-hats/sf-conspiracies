All rabbits at East Bay petting zoo dead after contracting rare virus
-----
All of the rabbits at the Little Farm at Tilden Regional Park died last month after contracting a viral illness.The farm's seven Dutch rabbits fell ill and died after being infected with myxomatosis, Berkeleyside reported. Myxomatosis is a virus carried by bloodsucking insects such as mosquitoes and fleas, and can be spread by wild rabbits.
