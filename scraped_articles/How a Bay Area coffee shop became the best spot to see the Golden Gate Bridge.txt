How a Bay Area coffee shop became the best spot to see the Golden Gate Bridge
-----
There's a buzz happening inside the coffee shop nestled off Highway 101, just before the Golden Gate Bridge. You've probably zipped by heading north and wondered what lurked inside the circular structure. This unique site is home to Equator Coffee, located inside the historic Round House building, where customers stop for a cup of coffee and a front-row seat to one of the world's most iconic bridges.On a recent morning, lively chatter filled the space as a steady line of customers formed
