Shuttered Japanese cafe will reopen in S.F.
-----
The trendsetting Japanese cafe that helped introduce premium matcha to the Bay Area is making a comeback.Stonemill Matcha, known for its hand-whisked drinks made with premium matcha, is set to reopen its doors at 561 Valencia St. with the owner of nearby Taishoken, one of the Chronicle's best ramen restaurants, acquiring the business. Mission Local first reported the news.
