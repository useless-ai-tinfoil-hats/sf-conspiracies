Cal recovers from 17-0 deficit to defeat FCS opponent Idaho
-----
Thoroughly outplayed by Idaho for the first 21 minutes Saturday afternoon at Memorial Stadium, Cal righted itself to dominate its FCS opponent.Behind a rushing attack that gained 256 yards and a defense that pitched a shutout over the final 39 minutes, the Bears grabbed a 31-17 victory in a game they trailed 17-0 in the second quarter.
