Offense-challenged Oakland Roots replace head coach Noah Delgado
-----
The Oakland Roots announced on Sunday evening that Noah Delgado has been replaced as the team's head coach."Noah has been an instrumental figure within our club, having been on staff since 2021, contributing his expertise, dedication to community, and passion to our collective goals," the team said in a statement. "We express our sincere appreciation for his efforts during his time with us."
