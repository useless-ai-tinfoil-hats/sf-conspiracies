Bay Area man dies in hot air balloon crash in Arizona after skydivers leap out of basket
-----
A Cupertino computer programmer died Sunday, along with three others, when the hot air balloon he chartered with a dozen others crash-landed in the Arizona desert, according to police.Atahan Kiliccote, 24, died, along with three others, while another woman was critically injured, when the balloon crashed in Eloy, Ariz., about 40 miles southeast of Phoenix.
