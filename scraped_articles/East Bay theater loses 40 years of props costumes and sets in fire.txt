East Bay theater loses 40 years of props, costumes and sets in fire
-----
Dianna Schepers was at home planning Pittsburg Theatre Company's summer workshop when she started getting text message alerts: Her theater company's warehouse was on fire."I got there at the same time as the fire department," she recalled. "I see flames billowing out of the roof. I'm like, 'Well, that's lost.' "
