Man fatally shot at business in San Jose
-----
A man was fatally shot in a business in San Jose early Sunday, police said.Multiple 911 callers reported the shooting around 3:20 a.m. on McLaughlin Avenue near Interstate 280, police said in a statement.
The victim suffered at least one gunshot wound. He was taken to a hospital where he died, police said. His name and age were not released.
