Mario Alioto, architect of Giants celebrations, looks back at 50 years of fun
-----
For every celebration or reunion that featured Willie Mays or Buster Posey, and every Johnnie LeMaster and Brad Wellman in between, Mario Alioto was somewhere in the background.Creative and with a flair for the element of surprise, Alioto is the San Francisco Giants' long-time marketing guru and point man for many memorable and historic days at both Candlestick Park and Oracle Park.
