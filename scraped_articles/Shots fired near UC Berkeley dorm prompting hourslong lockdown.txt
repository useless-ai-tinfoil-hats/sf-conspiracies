Shots fired near UC Berkeley dorm, prompting hours-long lock-down
-----
A report of shots fired near a UC Berkeley dormitory prompted an hours-long lockdown on Thursday as police searched for a suspect.Police responded to the area near Clark Kerr, a complex of Spanish-style dorms and courtyards on the south side of campus, at 7:40 a.m., following reports of gunfire near the outdoor track.
Witnesses described the alleged perpetrator as a man in black pants with a red backpack and bleached hair, possibly highlighted red or orange. Nobody was injured
