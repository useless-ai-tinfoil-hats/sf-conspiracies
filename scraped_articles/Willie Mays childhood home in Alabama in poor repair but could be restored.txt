Willie Mays’ childhood home in Alabama in poor repair, but could be restored
-----
FAIRFIELD, Ala. — Willie Mays' childhood house is where his father and aunts watched over him. Where he felt safe and loved. Where he had friends over. Where he could walk to school. Where he cherished the nearby ballfields. Where he signed a contract with the New York Giants.It's a home Mays, who died Tuesday at 93, never forgot.
"They told me that Willie Mays, the great Willie Mays, grew up in that house," said Patrick Davis, 59, who moved across the street from Mays' old house
