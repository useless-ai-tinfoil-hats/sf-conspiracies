BART service to SFO slowed after equipment problem prompts ‘major delay’
-----
BART service on multiple lines was delayed after an equipment problem caused major systemwide delays Monday, the transit agency said.BART first reported delays on X, formerly Twitter, at 10:46 a.m., which continued throughout the day.
Travel to San Francisco International Airport and Millbrae was interrupted, the agency said. The San Francisco Line in the East Bay was also affected.
BART said the equipment problem originated at the 24th and Mission Station in San Francisco.
