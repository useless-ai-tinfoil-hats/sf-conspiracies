Lawyers of man accused of killing Bob Lee can access 7 years of phone records, court says
-----
A San Francisco judge has granted a request from the defense team of Nima Momeni, the man accused of killing Cash App founder Bob Lee, to access seven year's worth of Lee's phone records.Momeni, who is facing murder charges in Lee's death, appeared in court in San Francisco's Hall of Justice on Tuesday with his mother in attendance. Lee was fatally stabbed on April 4, 2023, in the Rincon Hill neighborhood of San Francisco.
