Ed Hardy’s Tattoo City in San Francisco to close amid founder’s health issues
-----
San Francisco's iconic Tattoo City, which first opened in 1977, will close its North Beach shop by year's end, as its founder, Don Ed Hardy, battles dementia."We have decided that the time has come for this part of San Francisco tattoo history to come to an end," Tattoo City announced on social media this week.
