Let the travel begin: ACC outlines initial football foes for Cal, Stanford
-----
Cal and Stanford learned Monday which conference opponents they'll have for the next seven seasons when the Atlantic Coast Conference announced its future-schedules model.With the imminent demise of the Pac-12, the Bears and Cardinal will join the ACC next season, as will SMU. The ACC will have 17 teams, and there will be no divisions. Teams will play an eight-game conference schedule.
