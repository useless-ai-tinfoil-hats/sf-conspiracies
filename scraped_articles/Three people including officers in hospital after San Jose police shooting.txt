Three people, including officers, in hospital after San Jose police shooting
-----
San Jose police officers exchanged gunfire with a man Thursday night, the police department said, landing two officers and the suspect in the hospital.Police said officers were investigating a disturbance on San Ignacio Avenue near the Bernal Plaza shopping center when they "made contact with" an adult male suspect, who allegedly shot at the officers. Police shot back at the man, the department said, and he was taken into custody.
