SFO reveals new airport logo after 18-month process
-----
It's an era of change for Bay Area airports. San Francisco Bay Oakland International Airport is settling into its new official title that places geography over city while San Jose Mineta International Airport couched its namesake last year. Now, flyers out of San Francisco International Airport can expect a new brand after 24 years of the "flight lines" logo.SFO released a 90-second "brand evolution video" on Tuesday that mirrors a dramatized reveal typically seen from tech companies
