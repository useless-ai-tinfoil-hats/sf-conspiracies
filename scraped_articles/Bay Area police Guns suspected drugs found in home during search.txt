Bay Area police: Guns, suspected drugs found in home during search
-----
A search at the Santa Rosa home of a woman who was out on bail in a gun-related case turned up two semiautomatic handguns and 12.5 grams of suspected fentanyl Friday afternoon, police said.The search was tied to a 39-year-old resident who previously was arrested on suspicion of carrying a loaded and concealed gun. The woman, Corrina Gonzalez, posted bail and, as part of the conditions of release, was prohibited from possessing guns and ammunition, police said.
