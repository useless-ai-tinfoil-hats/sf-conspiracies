Trump will be on the March primary ballot, secretary of state announces
-----
Former President Donald Trump will be on California's primary ballot this March despite efforts to remove him, Shirley Weber, the state's secretary of state, announced Thursday.California's primary election is scheduled for March 5, 2024. Trump will be among several candidates vying for the Republican nomination for next year's general election.
The push to remove Trump from the ballot in California came after a landmark ruling from the Colorado Supreme Court earlier this month
