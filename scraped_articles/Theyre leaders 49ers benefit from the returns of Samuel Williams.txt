’They’re leaders’: 49ers benefit from the returns of Samuel, Williams
-----
Wide receiver Deebo Samuel missed most of the Cleveland game Oct. 15 after he sustained a hairline fracture in his shoulder. Then he missed the Minnesota game Oct. 23 and Cincinnati game Oct. 29. The San Francisco 49ers lost all three.Samuel returned Sunday in Jacksonville, where the 49ers coasted to a 34-3 victory. His presence alone doesn't explain the abrupt turnaround, but it's probably not a coincidence, either.
