Bicyclist killed in Oakland hit-and-run crash, police say
-----
A bicyclist was struck and killed in a hit-and-run crash in Oakland early Friday morning, police said.The crash occurred just after midnight on the 500 block of 105th Avenue, according to police. Officers responded to the area for a report of a serious injury collision involving a bicyclist.
