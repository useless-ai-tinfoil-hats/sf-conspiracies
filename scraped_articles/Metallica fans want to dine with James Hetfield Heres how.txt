Metallica fans, want to dine with James Hetfield? Here’s how
-----
Metallica frontman James Hetfield could be joining you and your family for dinner — as long as you pay for it.The singer and guitarist for the legendary Bay Area heavy metal band is prepared to dine with the highest bidder on the Adaptive Sports Foundation's auction item on Charitybuzz, a site where nonprofit organizations can raise money.
As of the morning of Monday, Aug. 19, the highest bid stands at more than $30,000.
The auction is scheduled to close at 12:02 p.m.
