Suspect steals septic truck in Sonoma County, hits SUV and injures driver, then runs
-----
Sonoma County authorities are asking for the public's help to locate a suspect who crashed a stolen septic truck in what police are calling a hit-and-run incident.The suspect allegedly stole the septic truck and lost control while driving it in rural Sonoma County, California Highway Patrol announced on Facebook last week. The suspect struck and injured the driver of an oncoming car before fleeing the scene, CHP officials said.
