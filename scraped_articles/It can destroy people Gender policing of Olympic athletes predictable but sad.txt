‘It can destroy people’: Gender policing of Olympic athletes predictable but sad
-----
PARIS — The gender hysteria and bullying started immediately, of course. Obsession over female athletes qualifications and "true" gender identity have been a predictable part of the Olympics ever since women have been allowed to compete.The persecution of Algerian boxer Imane Khelif, with its whiff of Nazi Germany's 1936 Olympics amplified by modern-day social media, is still simmering. And the witch hunt may ramp up even higher this week, in part because of a track star from Aptos.
