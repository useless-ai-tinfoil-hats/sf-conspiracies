Pregnant mother of 4 children is killed in California's Silicon Valley
-----
A pregnant woman and her unborn child were killed Sunday at home in San Mateo, a suburb of San Francisco, officials said.The woman's boyfriend fled the residence after she died and was arrested about five hours later in Southern California, San Mateo police said. Andrew Coleman is a suspect in the woman's death, Jerami Surratt, a spokesperson for the San Mateo Police Department, told SFGATE.
