Giants have yet to commit to Jefferies as Wednesday starter in Boston
-----
BOSTON — The San Francisco Giants' pitching plans are up in the air for the second game of their three-game series against the Boston Red Sox.Right-handed starter Daulton Jefferies — up from Triple-A Sacramento — had a locker in the visiting clubhouse at Fenway Park as part of the taxi squad Tuesday, but manager Bob Melvin did not commit to whether he might start Wednesday. Jefferies is on the 40-man roster, but no move has been announced that adds him to the 26-man roster.
