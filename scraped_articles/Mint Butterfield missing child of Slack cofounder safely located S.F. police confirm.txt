Mint Butterfield, missing child of Slack co-founder, safely located, S.F. police confirm
-----
Mint Butterfield, the teenage child of Slack co-founder and former CEO Stewart Butterfield, has been found after being reported missing Tuesday, authorities said Sunday.Spokespeople with the San Francisco Police Department and Marin County Sheriff's Office confirmed that the 16-year-old has been recovered. No further details were immediately released.
Sgt. Adam Schermerhorn of the sheriff's office said more information would be released shortly.
