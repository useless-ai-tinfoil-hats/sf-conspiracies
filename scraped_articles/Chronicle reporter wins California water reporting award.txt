Chronicle reporter wins California water reporting award
-----
Longtime Chronicle environmental reporter Kurtis Alexander has won a major statewide water reporting prize.The award, presented in Sacramento on Wednesday, is given by the Water Education Foundation, a group widely respected for nonpartisan work on water issues.
Jenn Bowles, the foundation's executive director, cited the "great context" that Alexander weaves into his stories.
