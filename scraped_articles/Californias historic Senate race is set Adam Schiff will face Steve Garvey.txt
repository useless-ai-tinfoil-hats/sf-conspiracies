California's historic Senate race is set: Adam Schiff will face Steve Garvey
-----
Rep. Adam Schiff will face Republican Steve Garvey in California's once-in-a-generation Senate race in November. Schiff led all candidates with 36.9% of the vote Tuesday night, with more votes left to be counted, and Garvey had 29%.*
