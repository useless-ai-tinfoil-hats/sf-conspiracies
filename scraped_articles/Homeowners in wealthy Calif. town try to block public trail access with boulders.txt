Homeowners in wealthy Calif. town try to block public trail access with boulders
-----
Several residents of an affluent Santa Barbara County town face hefty fines and potential prosecution after placing obstructions along a public right-of-way near the trailhead to a popular hot spring.The obstructions in question include large boulders and plants placed along the sides of East Mountain Drive and Riven Rock Road in Montecito, blocking spots people would otherwise be able to use as parking before hiking to nearby Montecito Hot Springs.
