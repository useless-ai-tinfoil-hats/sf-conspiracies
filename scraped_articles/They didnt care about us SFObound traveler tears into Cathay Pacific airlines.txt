'They didn't care about us': SFO-bound traveler tears into Cathay Pacific airlines
-----
The first time her flight tried to land at a windswept San Francisco International Airport during the atmospheric river on Feb. 4, passenger Crystal Ross told SFGATE that she felt her body leave the seat.But when the Cathay Pacific pilot tried for a second time to land at SFO, Ross said the dramatic drop in the sky sent half the people around her reaching for barf bags. "The plane was rocked so hard each time that half the plane was getting sick," she said.
