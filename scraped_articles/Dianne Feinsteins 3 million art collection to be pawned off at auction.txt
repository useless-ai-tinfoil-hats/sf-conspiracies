Dianne Feinstein's $3 million art collection to be pawned off at auction
-----
Following a family squabble over the late Sen. Dianne Feinstein's estate, Feinstein and husband Richard Blum's collection of rare Himalayan paintings and sculptures worth an estimated $3 million is going up for auction March 20.According to a press release from upscale auction house Bonhams, 42 pieces from the former senator's collection are slated to go up for auction in New York.
