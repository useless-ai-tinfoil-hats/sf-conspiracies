Cal’s Justin Wilcox on ACC move: ‘We talked about it for about 7 seconds’
-----
Head coach Justin Wilcox and his Cal team had quite an eventful 48 hours or so in the Dallas-Fort Worth metropolitan area. The Bears arrived Thursday, had a walk-through at the Dallas Cowboys' facility Friday and then dusted North Texas 58-21 on Saturday.And, most significant for the long-term outlook for Wilcox's program and the entire Cal athletic department, the university announced Friday morning that it will join Stanford and SMU in becoming members of the Atlantic Coast Conference
