Giants' Logan Webb throws eight shutout innings as S.F. beats Mets
-----
Tuesday's crowd of 25,453 at Oracle Park saw Logan Webb at his best and in his element.A thick, cold air aiding his changeup and sinker mix, Webb pitched eight scoreless innings while throwing 106 pitches en route to a 5-1 San Francisco Giants win over the New York Mets, securing a series win for the Giants.
Webb lowered his ERA to 2.33 having allowed one run over his past three starts, going seven innings in each of his previous two.
