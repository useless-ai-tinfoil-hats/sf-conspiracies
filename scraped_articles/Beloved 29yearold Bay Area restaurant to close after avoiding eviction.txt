Beloved 29-year-old Bay Area restaurant to close after avoiding eviction
-----
Just six months after narrowly avoiding eviction, a beloved Palo Alto restaurant has announced its closure.Mike's Diner Bar at 2680 Middlefield Road, which has been a staple of Palo Alto's Midtown neighborhood for nearly three decades, will close after Sunday, June 16.
Owner Mike Wallau said the closure is due to the increasing cost of operating a full-service restaurant in the Bay Area.
