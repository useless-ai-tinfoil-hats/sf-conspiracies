TravelSkills 07-31-24 SFO's second biggest airline opens 11,000-square-foot lounge
-----
Alaska Airlines welcomed the public to its newest lounge, inside the newly remodeled Terminal 1, last week.The capacious, 11,000-square-foot space adds more seating than its former SFO lounge, with the promise of plug-ins for charging devices at "nearly every seat," the airline wrote in a news release.
