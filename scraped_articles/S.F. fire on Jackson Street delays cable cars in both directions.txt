S.F. fire on Jackson Street delays cable cars in both directions
-----
A fire on a residential section of Jackson Street near Leavenworth Street in San Francisco on Thursday morning delayed Muni cable cars in both directions, city fire officials said.A video of the incident posted to social media by the San Francisco Fire Department showed a roaring blaze and a column of smoke billowing from behind a row of houses on Nob Hill.
