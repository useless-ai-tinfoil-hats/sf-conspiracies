SF Ferry Building is getting a new seafood restaurant and fish counter
-----
A casual seafood restaurant and traditional fish market is coming to San Francisco's most iconic waterfront food destination. Nopa Fish, a collaboration between Nopa chef Laurence Jossel and Joe Conte, owner of seafood distributor Water2Table, is slated to open at the Ferry Building in early 2025.The counter-service restaurant will serve seafood dishes, chowders and farm salads.
