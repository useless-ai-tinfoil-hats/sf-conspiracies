One American dead, one injured after hot tub accident at Mexican resort
-----
Two tourists were reportedly electrocuted in a hot tub at a private resort in Puerto Peñasco, along the Gulf of California.The man, identified as 43-year-old Jorge by the state of Sonora attorney general's office, died, while his wife, Lizeth, suffered life-threatening injuries. Authorities did not release their last names.
