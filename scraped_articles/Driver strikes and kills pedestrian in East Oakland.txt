Driver strikes and kills pedestrian in East Oakland
-----
A pedestrian was struck and killed by a driver Tuesday evening in East Oakland, the Police Department said.The motorist was traveling westbound on International Boulevard at about 6:15 p.m. when they struck a pedestrian crossing the street near 58th Avenue, Oakland police spokesperson Darryl Rodgers said in a statement.
Paramedics provided aid to the pedestrian, but the victim died from their injuries at the scene, Rodgers said. The victim's identity was not immediately released.
