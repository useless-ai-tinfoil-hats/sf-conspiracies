70-year-old found in Tahoe backcountry after being missing for 5 nights
-----
Warren Elliott, the 70-year-old man who went missing in the Tahoe backcountry last Friday, was found safe this morning, a spokesperson from Placer County Sheriff's Office said.Rescuers found Elliott at Hell Hole Reservoir, miles away from where he was last seen at Rubicon Springs before he disappeared last week.
"The helicopter is picking him up right now," said Elise Soviar, spokesperson for the Placer County Sheriff's Office.
