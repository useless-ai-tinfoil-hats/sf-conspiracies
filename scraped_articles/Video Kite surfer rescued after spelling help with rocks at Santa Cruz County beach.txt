Video: Kite surfer rescued after spelling ‘help’ with rocks at Santa Cruz County beach
-----
A stranded kite surfer was rescued after using rocks to spell out "help" on the Santa Cruz County beach where he became trapped Sunday afternoon, officials said.People riding in a private helicopter above Davenport Landing spotted the message in the sand and alerted authorities, who pulled the kite surfer to safety, representatives for Cal Fire's San Mateo-Santa Cruz unit said on X.
