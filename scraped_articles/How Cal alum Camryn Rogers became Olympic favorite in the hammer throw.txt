How Cal alum Camryn Rogers became Olympic favorite in the hammer throw
-----
As she sat in the crowd last year in Budapest, Hungary, before her only child spun around and around and heaved the hammer far into the distance, Shari Rogers struggled to stop shaking.Her mind raced amid the thick tension of the track and field World Championships. She pictured young Camryn swinging for hours at the playground … and quietly doing her homework at the shop where Shari worked as a hairdresser … and watching the 2012 Olympics on television, a 12-year-old new to the hammer
