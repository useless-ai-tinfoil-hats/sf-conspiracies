Highway 1 in Santa Cruz County reopens after fatal crash
-----
A man was killed Sunday in a single-vehicle crash on Highway 1 in Santa Cruz County, closing both sides of the road for several hours, the California Highway Patrol said.The crash, just north of Wilder Ranch State Park, occurred around 12:40 p.m. on the northbound side of the highway when a Chrysler Town & Country veered to the right and struck a metal guardrail, then a dirt embankment, the CHP said.
