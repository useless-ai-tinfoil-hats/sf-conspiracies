Beleaguered SF mall signs 7 new leases but none with a major retailer
-----
San Francisco's troubled downtown mall, recently rebranded as Emporium San Francisco Centre, announced the signing of seven new leases on Thursday. None of them, however, is with a major retailer. New businesses include a cellphone repair shop, a toy store and ... a pet vending machine?The toy store, Hey Hi Toys, opened this spring. The recently opened cellphone repair shop is called A&S Cell Accessories and Repairs, and the other newcomer, Paw Box, is a "pet vending machine pilot
