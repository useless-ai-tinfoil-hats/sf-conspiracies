New Michigan poll finds voters prefer Trump to Newsom in hypothetical matchup
-----
A new Michigan poll found voters in the critical swing state would pick former President Donald Trump, the presumptive Republican nominee, in a hypothetical presidential matchup against California Gov. Gavin Newsom.The poll, conducted by the Lansing, Mich.-based Glengariff Group and commissioned by The Detroit News and WDIV-TV, which wrote about it Tuesday, found that 45% of voters would cast a ballot for Trump, compared with 40% who said they would vote for Newsom.
