People searching X for updates on California's largest wildfire encounter torrent of porn spam
-----
As the Park Fire exploded in size in Northern California this week, people logged on to X for in-the-moment updates on evacuations and weather forecasts. But some Californians found the social media platform rendered basically unusable by spambots.The accounts plagued users with post after post directing them toward pornography websites, rankling those on the hunt for information about California's largest wildfire of the year so far.
