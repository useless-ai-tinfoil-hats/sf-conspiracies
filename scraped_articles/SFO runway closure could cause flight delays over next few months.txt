SFO runway closure could cause flight delays over next few months
-----
Travelers flying in and out of San Francisco International Airport could face delays beginning next week as a scheduled five-month runway closure takes effect.Runway 28 Left will close on Tuesday as part of a taxiway improvement project, the airport said. The runway, one of four at the airport, is expected to reopen by July 1.
