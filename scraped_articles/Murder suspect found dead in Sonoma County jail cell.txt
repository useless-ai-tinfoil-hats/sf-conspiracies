Murder suspect found dead in Sonoma County jail cell
-----
A Santa Rosa man died at the Sonoma County jail this week, authorities said Thursday.On Wednesday, Skyler Rasmussen, 28, was found unresponsive in his jail cell, the Sonoma County Sheriff's Office said. Correctional officers attempted lifesaving aid, including using Narcan, performing CPR and using a defibrillator, but Rasmussen was pronounced dead when first responders arrived, according to authorities.
