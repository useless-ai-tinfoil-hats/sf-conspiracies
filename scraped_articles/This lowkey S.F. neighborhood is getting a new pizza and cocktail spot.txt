This low-key S.F. neighborhood is getting a new pizza and cocktail spot
-----
A new bar and restaurant from a seasoned drinks veteran is seeking to fill a nightlife need in San Francisco's Portola neighborhood.Chris Lawrence, founder of craft brewery Southern Pacific and Dogpatch hangout the Sea Star, opened his new bar, Out the Road, on Friday. (He sold Southern Pacific in 2020 and the Sea Star in 2016). The new bar at 2636 San Bruno Ave. offers beer and cocktails, as well as pizza and snacks.
