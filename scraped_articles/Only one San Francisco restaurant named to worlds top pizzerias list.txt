Only one San Francisco restaurant named to world's top pizzerias list
-----
50 Top Pizza has released its annual list of the world's top pizzerias and named a sole Bay Area winner.San Francisco's Tony's Pizza Napoletana claimed the No. 19 spot on a list of the top 100 pizzerias around the world. Tony's was among a fraction of U.S. pizzerias in the ranking and the only California restaurant recognized in the roundup.
