S.F. Police Commission votes to end pretextual traffic stops
-----
The San Francisco Police Commission voted Wednesday to restrict officers' ability to pull over drivers for low-level infractions, a form of traffic stop disproportionately used against people of color.Commissioners approved the policy on a narrow 4-3 vote, despite concerns that the Police Officers' Association might sue to block the city from implementing it.
