This is how Dianne Feinstein spent her last day
-----
Sen. Dianne Feinstein spent Thursday working and meeting with a friend, before dying with her daughter Katherine by her side around 2am Eastern Time on Friday.Public Senate proceedings and information shared by those close to her give a sense of the long-time senator's last day.
Feinstein cast her final vote on the Senate floor on Thursday. The Senate convened at 10 a.m. and during the day three record votes were taken, though Feinstein participated in only one.
