Is 49ers' Christian McCaffrey being intentionally headhunted?
-----
On the first play of Sunday's blowout win against the Cowboys, San Francisco 49ers running back Christian McCaffrey endured a very familiar experience: His facemask was grabbed.This was the third straight game where the star player was grabbed there during a tackle attempt. Sunday, it was Dallas safety Donovan Wilson; the week before, it was Arizona linebacker Ezekiel Turner; and the game before that, it was New York linebacker Jihad Ward.
