Menorah vandalized in Oakland prompts hate crime investigation
-----
Oakland police are investigating the destruction of a large menorah located near Lake Merritt that was vandalized early Wednesday morning.In a statement to SFGATE, the Oakland Police Department said it's investigating the vandalization, which happened around 1:30 a.m. near 12th Street and Lake Merritt Boulevard, as a hate crime. The incident comes during Hanukkah, which is celebrated this year from Thursday, Dec. 7, to Friday, Dec. 15.
