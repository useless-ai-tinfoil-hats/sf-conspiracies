Bay Area-built houses could be solution for Tahoe’s affordable housing crisis
-----
South Lake Tahoe is a resort town with a big need for affordable housing — and a very short window in which to build it.From October to May, the Tahoe Regional Planning Agency prohibits grading and digging, which helps protect the lake's famous clear waters by preventing sediment from washing into storm water systems. When the ski slopes open at Heavenly, the hammering stops and the crews descend into the valley to work in warmer climates.
