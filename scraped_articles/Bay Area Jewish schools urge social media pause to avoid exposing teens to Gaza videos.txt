Bay Area Jewish schools urge social media pause to avoid exposing teens to Gaza videos
-----
At least two Jewish schools in the Bay Area asked parents to consider deleting their teenagers' social media apps over fears that online images from the war in Gaza could harm the teens' wellbeing.The Brandeis School of San Francisco and Jewish Community High School of the Bay said in emails to parents this week that viewing social media posts could traumatize students in the wake of Hamas' surprise attack against Israel.
