You asked, we answered: Can you walk, bike across the SF-Oakland Bay Bridge?
-----
Developing a metropolitan area around a giant body of water has its advantages — beautiful views, sea breezes, lots of great nature — but it also presents impracticalities. One example: the trip from the East Bay into San Francisco. The trip is often a traffic-choked, expensive pain if you're driving, or can be a delayed, crowded annoyance if you're taking BART.Since 2013, however, there's been a third option: the Bay Bridge Trail. You can't walk or bike across the full span of the Bay
