Ready or not, Ballers host first Oakland home opener at Raimondi Park on Tuesday
-----
On Tuesday night, Oakland's newest pro sports team takes its home field for the first time, and Raimondi Field is ready.Well, basically ready. The finishing touches still were being added Monday as the Ballers prepared to face the Yolo High Wheelers in a Pioneer League game in West Oakland, the first pro game at the field in more than 100 years.
