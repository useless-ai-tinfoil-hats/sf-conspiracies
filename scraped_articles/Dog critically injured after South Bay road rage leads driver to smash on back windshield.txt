Dog ‘critically’ injured after South Bay road rage leads driver to smash on back windshield
-----
San Jose police arrested a man they say stomped on another car's back windshield, shattering the glass and "critically injuring" a dog inside.Video taken on Nov. 11 shows three men getting out of two vehicles near the intersection of Capitol Expressway and Tully Road to yell at a vehicle stopped in front of them. One of the men — who officials identified as Armando Cano — can be seen jumping on the back of the car and stomping on its back windshield, breaking the glass.
