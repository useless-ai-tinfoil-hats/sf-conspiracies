Some people should get another COVID booster now, CDC says
-----
Some Americans should now get a second dose of the COVID-19 vaccine introduced last fall, the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention says.An advisory panel to the federal health agency on Wednesday recommended that individuals aged 65 and above get the additional dose four months after their last vaccination, aiming to enhance protection, particularly for the age group most vulnerable to severe outcomes.
