Hourslong police chase through Bay Area cities exceeds 160 mph, 5 arrested
-----
Five people were arrested over the weekend after an hourslong police chase through the Bay Area involving a stolen car.On Saturday, a California Highway Patrol officer attempted to pull over a Dodge Charger speeding more than 130 mph on Interstate 880 near Hesperian Boulevard in Hayward, CHP said in a news release.
The car exited on Tennyson Road after being tracked by the Alameda County Sheriff's Office air unit. When CHP tried to stop the vehicle after exiting, the driver fled
