The story behind California's Pumpkin Rock is a tightly guarded secret
-----
You can see the giant orange orb for miles. Situated high atop a hill in a mostly flat area of the Inland Empire sits a gleaming boulder painted like a jack-o'-lantern, its black Tim Burton-inspired face grinning back at you.Hundreds, if not thousands, of people make the trek up to see this popular tourist attraction in Norco, California, each year, with an unsurprising boost of visitors in October in the spooky weeks leading up to Halloween.
