Coofandy to Debut Collection at Eiffel Tower During Paris Fashion Week
-----
On June 25, Coofandy will debut its latest couture collection at the Eiffel Tower as part of Paris Fashion Week. This special unveiling represents a major achievement for the brand, underscoring its rapid rise in the fashion industry and putting its designs in front of some exciting new faces.A master of versatile and affordable menswear, Coofandy has quickly built a reputation for creating timeless pieces that effortlessly transition from day to night.
