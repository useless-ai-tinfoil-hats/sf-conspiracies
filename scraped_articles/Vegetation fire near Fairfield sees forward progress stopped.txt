Vegetation fire near Fairfield sees forward progress stopped
-----
An 12-acre vegetation fire forced evacuations west of Fairfield on Wednesday morning, but forward progress was stopped within a few hours and the evacuation orders downgraded.The fire, which erupted around 9:46 a.m., drew a response from Cal Fire and firefighting agencies.
Winds were gusting over 20 mph, but relative humidity in the 65%-75% range was favorable for firefighting efforts.
