Three food truck employees were injured Saturday night in Union Square when something on their stove burst, according to the San Francisco Fire Department.Firefighters responded to the incident at Stockton and Geary streets shortly after 8 p.m. Saturday, the Fire Department said in a news release. The three employees were transported to local hospitals with burn injuries.
No active fire was detected, and the area soon reopened, the Fire Department said.
