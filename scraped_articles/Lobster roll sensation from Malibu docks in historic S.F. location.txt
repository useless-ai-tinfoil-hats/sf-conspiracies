Lobster roll sensation from Malibu docks in historic S.F. location
-----
A southern California hotspot known for New England-style seafood dishes and its raw bar is now ready to serve diners at a San Francisco landmark.Broad Street Oyster Co. opens Wednesday at Ghirardelli Square, offering the lobster rolls that have made the Los Angeles area restaurants a sensation, along with raw bar offerings like freshly shucked oysters, seafood towers and other warm dishes like clam chowder.
