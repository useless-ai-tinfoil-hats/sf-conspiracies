Rite Aid to close 16 California stores, 1 in Bay Area
-----
The number of Rite Aid store closures following the company's Chapter 11 bankruptcy filing in October keeps growing, and 16 California stores were recently added to that list.The pharmacy and retailer initially announced it would close 154 stores after its bankruptcy filing in October, including 31 stores in California, two of them in the Bay Area. Plans to close an additional 30 stores were revealed in November and December, with 55 more locations slated for closure as of this month,
