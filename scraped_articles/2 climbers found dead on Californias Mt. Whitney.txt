2 climbers found dead on California's Mt. Whitney
-----
A pair of mountaineers reported missing after climbing Mount Whitney this week were found dead by searchers and recovered from the peak on Wednesday, according to the Inyo County Sheriff's Office.The two climbers ascended and summited the peak on Tuesday morning via the mountaineer's route, according to a Facebook post from a third climbing partner. But the trio became separated on the descent down a perilously steep section near the mountaintop known as the Final 400.
