California doesn’t have an ‘exit tax’ — but it can still tax some people who move away
-----
People leaving California — to work remotely, escape its highest-in-the-nation state income tax or for other reasons — may find that some or all of their income could still be taxable in California.As more people flee California for states with no income tax such as Texas, Nevada, Florida, Washington and Tennessee, the internet is full of information — and misinformation — about this subject.
