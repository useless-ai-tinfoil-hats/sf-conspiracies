Another West Coast national park adds reservations to deal with overcrowding
-----
Another national park on the West Coast is adding a reservation system to deal with overcrowding as summer mobs continue to strain the capacity of America's outdoor icons.Over the last 10 years, Mount Rainier National Park in Washington has seen a 40% increase in visitors, the park said in a statement.
