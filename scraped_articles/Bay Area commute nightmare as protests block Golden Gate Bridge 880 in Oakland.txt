Bay Area commute nightmare as protests block Golden Gate Bridge, 880 in Oakland
-----
In the middle of rush hour Monday morning, protesters blocked two major highways — 101 across the Golden Gate Bridge and sections of northbound and southbound Interstate 880 in Oakland — causing a traffic nightmare for commuters.Both southbound and northbound directions were stopped on the Golden Gate Bridge, and the California Highway Patrol said there's no estimated time for when the bridge will reopen.
