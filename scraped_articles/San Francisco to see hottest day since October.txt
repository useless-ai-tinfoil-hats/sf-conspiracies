San Francisco to see hottest day since October
-----
The hottest day so far this year is expected in the Bay Area on Thursday, with highs ranging from the 70s at the coast to the lower 80s near the bay shoreline and mid-80s to near 90 inland.Temperatures this high haven't been observed in the Bay Area in more than six months, last occurring in mid-October. While highs will be 10 to 18 degrees above normal, spring hot stretches are common. San Francisco's average first 80-degree day is April 29, while Vacaville and Concord often reach 90
