'On the ropes': Northern California weekly newspaper to shutter after 27 years
-----
Another local newspaper is calling it quits in Northern California, and this time, economic trouble in the pot industry is partially to blame.The Humboldt Independent likely printed its last edition this week and plans to close after 27 years in business, according to an interview that Joe Kirby, the newspaper's editor, gave to KMUD-FM this week. Kirby said the newspaper is open to receiving donations but is otherwise unable to maintain operations.
