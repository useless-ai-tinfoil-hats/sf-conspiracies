An SF condo building has sat empty for years. It may finally become rentals.
-----
A San Francisco condo building that has sat vacant for three years may no longer be available to potential homeowners.Investor Steven Hong, who has taken ownership of the loan for 1554 Market St. through an LLC, has submitted an application to the SF Planning Commission to transform the two 12-story conjoined towers into rentals, putting 109 apartments up for lease if approved.
