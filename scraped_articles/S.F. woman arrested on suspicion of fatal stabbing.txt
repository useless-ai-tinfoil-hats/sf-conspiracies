S.F. woman arrested on suspicion of fatal stabbing
-----
San Francisco police officers arrested a San Francisco resident in an alleged stabbing that left one person dead last week, officials said Tuesday.Officers responded to a residence on the 700 block of Post Street on the afternoon of May 15, where they found an adult victim lying on the ground, suffering from multiple stab wounds, spokespersons said.
The victim was pronounced deceased at the scene.
