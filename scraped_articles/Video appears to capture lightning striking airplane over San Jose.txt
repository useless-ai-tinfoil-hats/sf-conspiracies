Video appears to capture lightning striking airplane over San Jose
-----
An airplane appeared to be struck by lightning over San Jose during Saturday's storm, according to a video posted on social media.William Justo of San Jose posted a video on X, formerly called Twitter, capturing what looks like lightning striking an airplane. It was unclear what type of aircraft it was or where it was headed.
"How lucky I was to capture this?" Justo tweeted.
