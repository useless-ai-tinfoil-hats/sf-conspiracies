‘Sickening feeling’: SF tech CEO blames himself as Airtable lays off more than 230 workers again
-----
San Francisco tech startup Airtable is laying off more than 230 workers for the second time in a year.The decade-old company, which rode pandemic-era interest in easy software coding to an $11 billion valuation in 2021, announced the 237-worker cut in a published note from CEO Howie Liu on Friday. The firm also laid off 254 workers last December, amid wider industry layoffs and a company pivot toward larger customers.
