Expect stadium full of 49ers fans in Jacksonville for Jaguars game
-----
The San Francisco 49ers will play about 2,700 miles away from Santa Clara on Sunday, but they should feel at home.The 49ers will meet the Jaguars in Jacksonville with 57% of the crowd at EverBank Stadium consisting of 49ers fans, according to Vivid Seats' Fan Forecast, which projects crowd outcomes. In 2021, when the Niners beat the host Jaguars 30-10 with the seats heavily dotted with red, 49ers fans made up 54% of the crowd.
