Unusually cold storm expected to bring more thunderstorms and snow to California
-----
UPDATED FORECAST: Big weather swings on tap for Bay Area this week from 80-degree temperatures to rainAn unusually cold April low-pressure system moved across the Bay Area on Thursday, dropping snow down to 1,500 feet and hail to the coast and valleys. On Friday, the storm will continue to move south, popping thunderstorms up between San Jose and San Diego, with accumulating snow as low as 3,000 feet.
