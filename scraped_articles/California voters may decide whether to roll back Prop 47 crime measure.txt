California voters may decide whether to roll back Prop 47 crime measure
-----
Californians may have the chance to roll back parts of a law they approved in 2014 that downgraded drug possession and thefts worth less than $950 to misdemeanors, after a measure to reimpose felony punishments for those offenses qualified Tuesday for the November ballot.The 2014 law, Proposition 47, aimed to reduce jail populations, but critics say it has emboldened thieves.
