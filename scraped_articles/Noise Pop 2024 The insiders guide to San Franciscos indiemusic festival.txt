Noise Pop 2024: The insider’s guide to San Francisco’s indie-music festival
-----
The Noise Pop Festival is making its return to San Francisco for its 31st year.The unique weeklong celebration of independent music and culture boasts an impressive lineup of about 150 acts performing across 25 different venues, showcasing some rather creative booking choices.
