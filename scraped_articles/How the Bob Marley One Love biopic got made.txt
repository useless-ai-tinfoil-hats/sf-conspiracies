How the ‘Bob Marley: One Love’ biopic got made
-----
When writer-director Reinaldo Marcus Green ("King Richard") received a draft of the "Bob Marley: One Love" script, the ardent fan's initial reaction was, "Wait, there hasn't been a Bob movie yet?"It seemed improbable that no filmmaker had brought the reggae star's life story to the screen, other than in documentary form (like Kevin Macdonald's 2012 "Marley") in the 43 years since his death at just 36 of a rare form of cancer.
