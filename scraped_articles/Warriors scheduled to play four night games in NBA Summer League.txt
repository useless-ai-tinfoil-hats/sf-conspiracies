Warriors scheduled to play four night games in NBA Summer League
-----
The Golden State Warriors know whom and when they'll play during NBA Summer League in Las Vegas. Released Friday, the league's schedule pits the Warriors against the Phoenix Suns, Chicago Bulls, Cleveland Cavaliers and Oklahoma City Thunder.The Warriors play Phoenix at 8 p.m. July 13 at UNLV's Thomas & Mack Center, the Bulls at 7 p.m. July 14 at Thomas & Mack, the Cavaliers at 7 p.m. July 17 at UNLV's Cox Pavilion and the Thunder at 7:30 p.m. July 19 at Thomas & Mack.
