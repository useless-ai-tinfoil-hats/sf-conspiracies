Giants’ addition of Soler might cost Flores at-bats, but he’s OK with that
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — When the San Francisco Giants asked Wilmer Flores to hop on a Zoom call with free-agent outfielder Jorge Soler, Flores didn't hesitate. His recruiting efforts, along with Thairo Estrada's, helped convince Soler to come to San Francisco.It was selfless work by Flores, who has emerged in the past year as the team leader among the position players. With Soler joining S.F. on a three-year deal, Flores will lose one of his usual jobs, handling the designated-hitter spot
