4.9-magnitude Northern California earthquake detected in ocean
-----
UPDATE: 3.4-magnitude quake strikes near S.F. ZooA 4.9-magnitude earthquake was detected in the ocean about 58 miles west of Petrolia in Humboldt County, according to data from the U.S. Geological Survey.
