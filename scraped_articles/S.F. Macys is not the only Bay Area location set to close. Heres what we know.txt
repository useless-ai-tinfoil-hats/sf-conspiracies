S.F. Macy's is not the only Bay Area location set to close. Here's what we know
-----
As San Francisco was rocked by the news Tuesday that Macy's flagship Union Square store was among 150 U.S. locations slated for closure, officials confirmed that at least one other Macy's in the Bay Area would also shutter: the store at the Mall at Northgate in San Rafael.Leslie Piper, spokesperson for the San Rafael Chamber of Commerce, said she was "just learning that the Macy's at the Mall at Northgate is among those slated for closure."
