Months of 'serious delays' as construction starts on major stretch of Calif.'s I-5
-----
Repair work begins today on a section of California's Interstate 5 that passes through the Sacramento corridor and will likely be completed by October, state officials said.Caltrans called the construction "major" in a message to the public and said to expect "serious delays" as multiple lanes of the highway and on- and off-ramps will be closed intermittently.
