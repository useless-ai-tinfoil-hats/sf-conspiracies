Nine-month pregnant woman hurt in Bay Bridge car crash
-----
A woman in her ninth month of pregnancy was injured in a two-car crash on the Bay Bridge Friday afternoon and was taken to San Francisco General Hospital to be examined, the California Highway Patrol said.The woman was a passenger in one of two cars that collided, deploying their airbags, on the Bay Bridge just west of Treasure Island in the westbound direction, according to CHP officer Mark Andrews.
