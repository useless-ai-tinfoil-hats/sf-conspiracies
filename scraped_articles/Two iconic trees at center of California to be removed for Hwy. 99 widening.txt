Two iconic trees at ‘center’ of California to be removed for Hwy. 99 widening
-----
The beloved Palm and Pine trees, once considered the geographic center of California, will be removed next year for a highway widening project.These iconic trees, a Canary Island date palm to the south and a Deodar cedar posing as a pine to the north, have stood in the center divider of Highway 99 in Madera County for nearly a century, symbolizing the divide between Northern and Southern California.
