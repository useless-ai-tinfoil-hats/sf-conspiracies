S.F. Mayor Breed wants to spend more money on police staffing despite huge budget shortfall
-----
San Francisco Mayor London Breed proposes spending more money to hire police officers, sheriff's deputies and 911 dispatchers, even as she looks to close a huge deficit in her upcoming city budget plan that may lead to painful reductions in other areas.Breed announced Thursday that she will not reduce funding to San Francisco's key public safety agencies and will instead use her next proposed budget to try to expand the ranks of local law enforcement.
