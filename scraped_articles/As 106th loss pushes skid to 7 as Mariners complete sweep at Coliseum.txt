A's 106th loss pushes skid to 7 as Mariners complete sweep at Coliseum
-----
Wednesday's dose of this-can't-be-true-but-it-is for the Oakland Athletics: Their 6-3 loss to Seattle extended their skid to seven games. That's understandable, but this part is much harder to fathom: At no point in the 63 innings of the losing streak have the A's owned a lead.Yes, Oakland last led a game at the end of its last win, a 6-2 decision at Houston on Sept. 12.
