SFO reveals bold new logo in sweeping brand overhaul
-----
San Francisco International Airport has unveiled a new logo and brand identity. After nearly 25 years, the airport is retiring its "flight lines" logo in favor of a simpler design emphasizing the "SFO" lettering.The logo "reflects the exciting changes happening at SFO," Ivar Satero, the airport director, said in a statement.
