Oakland celebrates solidarity with combined Lunar New Year, Black History Month event
-----
Members of Oakland's Black and Asian communities came together Saturday afternoon to celebrate culture and solidarity at a combined Lunar New Year and Black History Month event.The date of Lunar New Year changes each year but often falls in early February, coinciding with Black History Month. The overlap presents the opportunity to use Lunar New Year as a time to highlight solidarity between the two communities.
