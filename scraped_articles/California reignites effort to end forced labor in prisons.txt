California reignites effort to end forced labor in prisons
-----
The campaign to end 175 years of forced labor in California prisons has taken a step toward the November ballot, but there are still obstacles in its path.Since 1849, California's Constitution has prohibited slavery and "involuntary servitude," except as a punishment for crime.
