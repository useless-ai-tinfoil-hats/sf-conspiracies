Scott Peterson hearing: LA Innocence Project lawyers seek DNA evidence in exoneration effort
-----
Attorneys for Scott Peterson said Tuesday they are seeking DNA testing and evidence to challenge his conviction for murdering his wife and their unborn son after uncovering what they described as "alarming deficiencies" in the evidence prosecutors presented at Peterson's high-profile trial.Peterson, 51, appeared in San Mateo Superior Court from Mule Creek State Prison in Amador County via a Zoom feed during a hearing that lasted a half hour.
