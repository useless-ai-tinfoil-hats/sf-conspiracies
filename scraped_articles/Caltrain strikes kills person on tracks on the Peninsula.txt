Caltrain strikes, kills person on tracks on the Peninsula
-----
A Caltrain struck and killed a person on the tracks in San Mateo on Tuesday night, according to the transit agency.Around 6:25 p.m., a Caltrain carrying 161 people north through San Mateo collided with a person standing on the tracks at the Hillsdale station, according to SamTrans spokesperson Randol White. White said officials do not know why the person was on the tracks.
