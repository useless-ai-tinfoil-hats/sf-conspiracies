San Jose Unified discriminated against Christian student club, Ninth Circuit rules
-----
San Jose public schools committed religious discrimination by refusing to recognize the Fellowship of Christian Athletes as an official student group because of its denial of leadership positions to LGBTQ students and their supporters, a federal appeals court ruled Wednesday.The 30,000-student San Jose Unified School District has required organizations since 2019 to be open to all students in order to qualify as an official student group, allowing it to keep funds in a school bank
