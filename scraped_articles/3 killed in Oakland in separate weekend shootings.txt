3 killed in Oakland in separate weekend shootings
-----
Oakland police are investigating three separate shootings that killed a woman and two men over the weekend.No arrests had been announced as of Sunday morning. The identities of all three victims have not been publicly released, pending notification of next of kin, according to the Police Department.
