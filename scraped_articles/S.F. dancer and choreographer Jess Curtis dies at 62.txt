S.F. dancer and choreographer Jess Curtis dies at 62
-----
In a four-decade career in the Bay Area, Jess Curtis eschewed what he called "pretty dancing."He could conjure "the death throes of a spawning salmon," as Chronicle critic Steven Winn put it in 2000. His "bone-crunching" dance "burst open like exploding stars," fellow Chronicle critic Marilyn Tucker wrote in 1990. Two years earlier, she called him one of the "bad boys" of Bay Area dance.
