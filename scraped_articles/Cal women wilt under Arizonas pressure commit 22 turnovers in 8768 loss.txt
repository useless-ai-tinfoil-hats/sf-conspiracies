Cal women wilt under Arizona’s pressure, commit 22 turnovers in 87-68 loss
-----
The Cal women struggled against a smothering Arizona defense again Sunday and were outscored by 21 points in the second quarter of an 87-68 loss at Haas Pavilion.The Bears (16-12, 6-10 Pac-12) committed 22 turnovers, their second most of the season. Their most, 23, came in a 66-55 loss at Arizona on Jan. 26. In both games, the Wildcats had 12 steals — the most against Cal this season.
"I'm just frustrated that we didn't handle the pressure better," Cal head coach Charmin Smith
