Just because ‘Oppenheimer’ is the frontrunner doesn’t mean it’s going to win
-----
"Oppenheimer" is nominated for 13 Academy Awards and goes into this year's Oscar race as the obvious favorite for best picture. But what that means is that "Oppenheimer" probably won't win.Ever since 2009, when the Motion Picture Academy adopted ranked voting, members have been able to vote strategically, which has meant that whatever film starts out the favorite goes into the race with a target on its back.
