Dreamforce conference will return to San Francisco in 2024 despite threats to leave
-----
Dreamforce, the massive tech conference hosted by Salesforce, will return to San Francisco in 2024.Mayor London Breed and Salesforce CEO Marc Benioff shared the news on X, formerly known as Twitter, Tuesday morning, with a selfie taken together at a Golden State Warriors game. The event will run from Sept. 17-19, 2024; in past years, Dreamforce has taken over several wings of downtown's Moscone Center.
