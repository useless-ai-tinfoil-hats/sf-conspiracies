Man found fatally shot inside car in San Jose, police say
-----
San Jose police on Friday said they were investigating the city's ninth homicide of the year after a man was found fatally shot a day earlier.Around 1:18 a.m. Thursday, officers responded to a parking lot on the 5100 block of Moorpark Avenue after the shooting was reported, police said. Upon arriving at the scene, officers found a man inside a car with at least one gunshot wound. He was pronounced dead at the scene, police said.
