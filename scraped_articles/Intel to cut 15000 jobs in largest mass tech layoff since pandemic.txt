Intel to cut 15,000 jobs in largest mass tech layoff since pandemic
-----
Intel confirmed on Thursday that it plans to cut about 15,000 positions, or 15% of its roughly 100,000-employee global workforce, and suspend dividend payments starting in the fourth quarter until cash flows improve.The semiconductor giant, based in Santa Clara, will undergo the largest mass layoff of any company since the COVID-19 pandemic.
