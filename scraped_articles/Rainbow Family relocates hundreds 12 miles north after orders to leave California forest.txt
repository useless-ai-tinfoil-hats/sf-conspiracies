Rainbow Family relocates hundreds 12 miles north after orders to leave California forest
-----
Hundreds of Rainbow Family members moved from Plumas National Forest as ordered, but relocated to a nearby camp Saturday afternoon, officials said.The U.S. Forest Service ordered the group, a nomadic community that gathers often unauthorized at a different national forest each year, to leave Plumas National Forest on Wednesday within 48 hours or face $5,000 in fines or jail time.
