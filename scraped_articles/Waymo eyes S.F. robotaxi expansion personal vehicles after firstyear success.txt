Waymo eyes S.F. robotaxi expansion, personal vehicles after first-year ‘success’
-----
After a year of commercial driverless ride-hailing in San Francisco under its belt, Waymo says it's determined to expand faster, with plans to eventually bring its robotaxis to the East Bay and San Jose, as well as Bay Area freeways.And if the company succeeds in its master plan, the Waymo Driver software powering its driverless robotaxis would be included for other uses such as trucking, deliveries and, eventually, personal vehicles, the company told the Chronicle.
