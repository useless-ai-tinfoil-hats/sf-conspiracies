The Disneyland-esque Carmel pancake house that once hosted Julia Child
-----
The Dutch door to the Little Swiss Cafe, in Carmel-by-the-Sea, is a portal to another place and time. The front dining room's timber framing and blue-shuttered windows evoke a European cottage. Inside, classical music is periodically interrupted by the chirps of a cuckoo clock. And a small chalkboard lists specials that might as well be written in permanent marker."They're today's specials, yesterday's specials, last year's," owner Steven Disseldorp told SFGATE.
