Why so many 49ers players live in this sleepy Bay Area suburb
-----
Football players have to live somewhere and in the Bay Area, for several 49ers, that hometown is an unexpected city about 30 minutes from Levi's Stadium.Morgan Hill is a sleepy suburb of about 45,000 people. It has a growing population as new homes are built and the downtown sheds its agricultural roots for a bustling city center.
