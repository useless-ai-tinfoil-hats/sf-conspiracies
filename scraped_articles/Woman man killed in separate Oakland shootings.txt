Woman, man killed in separate Oakland shootings
-----
Oakland police are investigating two separate shootings that killed a woman and a man Saturday.The first shooting was reported just after 5:45 p.m. on the 5900 block of San Leandro Street in East Oakland, the Police Department said Sunday in a statement.
An injured woman was driven to a fire station on 66th Avenue for help and was taken to Highland Hospital, where she died, the Police Department said.
