Total SF is back to help you believe in San Francisco
-----
Do you remember when you fell in love with San Francisco?For me, it was on a concrete slide in Eureka Valley, around the corner from my mother's childhood home on Douglass Street, where my grandparents still lived. It was the late 1970s, I was a small child, and a little homesick. My grandmother held my hand and walked two blocks to a pair of 40-foot slides that contained so much of what remains great about the city.
The Seward Street Slides were fun, fast, one-of-a-kind, imperfect
