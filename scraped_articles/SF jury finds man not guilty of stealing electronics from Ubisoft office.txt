SF jury finds man not guilty of stealing electronics from Ubisoft office
-----
A San Francisco jury last week found a man not guilty of burglarizing the San Francisco offices of video game developer Ubisoft.David Wesser, 37, was acquitted Jan. 24 of felony charges, the San Francisco Public Defender's Office said in a news release Tuesday. Deputy Public Defender Christopher Garcia argued that police's evidence did not prove Wesser was involved in the burglary.
