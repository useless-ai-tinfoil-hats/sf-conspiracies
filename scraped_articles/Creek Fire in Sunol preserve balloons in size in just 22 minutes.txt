Creek Fire in Sunol preserve balloons in size in just 22 minutes
-----
UPDATE July 27, 6:07 p.m. The Creek Fire burning in the Sunol Wilderness Regional Preserve has grown to 734 acres, according to an update provided by Cal Fire at 2:24 p.m. Saturday.The fire is at 10% containment, Cal Fire said.
-----
The Creek Fire raging in the Sunol Wilderness Regional Preserve ballooned in size Friday evening, going from 350 acres to 577 acres in just 22 minutes.
