Lounge blending Persian and Mexican cuisines opening in downtown S.F.
-----
A new restaurant and lounge combining Persian and Mexican influences is opening Thursday night, Oct. 5, in San Francisco.Movida arrives in a two-level space at 555 Second St. in San Francisco's SoMa, the former home of the bar Lord George. Inside, diners can try a blend of Persian and Mexican cuisines in dishes like saffron chile relleno stuffed with rice, lamb and Oaxaca cheese; and lime-cured halibut aguachile with Persian cucumbers, dill oils and spiced with serrano chiles.
