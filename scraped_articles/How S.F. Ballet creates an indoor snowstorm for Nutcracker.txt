How S.F. Ballet creates an indoor snowstorm for ‘Nutcracker’
-----
There is one place in San Francisco where you can count on seeing white fluffy snow every winter — and it's indoors.Backstage at the War Memorial Opera House, in a brightly lit storage area called "the patch," dozens of white trash bins are packed to the rim with thousands of flakes of this "snow." Made from tiny pieces of white paper that are coated in a flame-proof salt solution, the faux snow transforms San Francisco Ballet's annual production of "Nutcracker" into a whimsical winter
