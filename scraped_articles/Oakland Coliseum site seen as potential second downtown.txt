Oakland Coliseum site seen as potential second downtown
-----
Oakland and Alameda County officials said Friday that redevelopment at the Coliseum site could be a major boon to the city and create a second downtown, a week after the A's rejected an offer for the team's share of the property from local investors who are working with the city.Oakland Vice Mayor Rebecca Kaplan and Alameda County Supervisor Nate Miley spoke at a meeting of the Oakland-Alameda Coliseum Authority, the governing body of the site, and described their vision for the land
