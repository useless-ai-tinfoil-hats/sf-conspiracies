Bay Area resident Guy Fieri's Flavortown Fest is canceled
-----
The Mayor of Flavortown's next big campaign stop has been canceled.Guy Fieri — Santa Rosa resident, "Diners, Drive-Ins and Dives" host and donkey sauce empresario — announced earlier this year that he was launching his first music festival. The event, dubbed Flavortown Fest, was to take place in Fieri's birthplace of Columbus, Ohio, on the first weekend of June. Unfortunately for Fieri fans, the festival will no longer take place.
