Former Facebook exec pleads guilty to stealing more than $4 million from company
-----
A former global diversity executive at Meta has pleaded guilty to wire fraud for stealing more than $4 million from the company over four years.Barbara Furlow-Smiles, 38, of Atlanta admitted to orchestrating the multimillion-dollar scheme to defraud the Menlo Park-based technology giant while she oversaw diversity, equity and inclusion programs from 2017 to 2021, federal prosecutors announced Wednesday.
