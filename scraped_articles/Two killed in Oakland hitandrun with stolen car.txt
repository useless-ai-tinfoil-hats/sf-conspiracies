Two killed in Oakland hit-and-run with stolen car
-----
An Oakland man who allegedly fled after striking and killing two people and injuring a third while driving a stolen car on Sunday has been arrested, police said.Oakland police officers were dispatched to the crash around 10 a.m. at 73rd Avenue and Holly Street, where they discovered a Lexus sedan with major damage and a rolled-over Lexus SUV carrying three victims, Oakland Police Department spokesperson Kim Armstead said.
