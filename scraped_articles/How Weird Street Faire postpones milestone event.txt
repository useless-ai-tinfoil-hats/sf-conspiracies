How Weird Street Faire postpones milestone event
-----
San Francisco's annual How Weird Street Faire has been postponed just days before its milestone anniversary celebration due to a storm being forecast for this weekend."We are looking at a half inch of rain and 36 mph winds" for Saturday, organizers told the Chronicle via email on Thursday. "Due to severe weather and unforeseen circumstances, we unfortunately have to postpone the event."
