Bay Area artists join the call for a cease-fire in escalating Israel-Hamas conflict
-----
Thousands of artists and writers, including prominent figures from the Bay Area, have penned open letters urging President Biden to de-escalate and secure a cease-fire in the ongoing Israel-Hamas war.Hollywood heavyweights such as Bradley Cooper, Cate Blanchett and Alan Cumming, alongside musicians Michael Stipe, Dua Lipa, and Ani DiFranco, are among the signatories.
