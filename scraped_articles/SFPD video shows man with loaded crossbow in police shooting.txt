SFPD video shows man with loaded crossbow in police shooting
-----
A suspect detained during an officer-involved shooting last week was carrying a loaded crossbow in the Bayview district, San Francisco police revealed Friday.San Francisco police responded to reports of a man dressed in camouflage-printed clothing walking around with an AK-47 just after 3 p.m. on May 23. Upon finding him near Jennings Street and Donner Avenue, officers realized the male suspect was carrying a crossbow instead.
