Wealthy Bay Area enclave to add surveillance cameras
-----
A wealthy enclave surrounded by Oakland will add new surveillance cameras by the end of September, police said.Piedmont is adding cameras to four more intersections after installing cameras at the intersection of Oakland and Grand avenues in 2021, Chief of Police Jeremy Bowers told SFGATE.
