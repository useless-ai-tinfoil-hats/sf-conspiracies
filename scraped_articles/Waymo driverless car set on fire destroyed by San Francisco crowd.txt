Waymo driverless car set on fire, destroyed by San Francisco crowd
-----
A crowd of people incinerated a Waymo driverless car in San Francisco on Saturday night, dramatic photos from the city's fire department show.The car was traveling through Chinatown on Jackson Street near Stockton and Grant around 9:30 p.m. Approximately 10 to 15 people surrounded the car, according to a spokesperson from the San Francisco Fire Department, and began breaking its windows and spraying graffiti.
