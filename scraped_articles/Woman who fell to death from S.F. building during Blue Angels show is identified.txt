Woman who fell to death from S.F. building during Blue Angels show is identified
-----
The woman who fell to her death from a building on San Francisco's Van Ness Avenue during a Blue Angels show on Saturday afternoon has been identified as 51-year-old Patricia Hamon.Hamon was a San Francisco resident, the San Francisco medical examiner's office said Tuesday. She lived in the Diamond Heights neighborhood, according to public records.
