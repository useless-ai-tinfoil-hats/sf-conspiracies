Two dead in 'chaotic' 35-vehicle crash on California's I-5
-----
Two people died Saturday in a catastrophic chain-reaction crash on California's Interstate 5 in Kern County.Amid foggy conditions around 7:30 a.m. Saturday, multiple southbound vehicles collided between Millux and Old River roads about 20 miles north of the Grapevine. As more cars encountered the scene and slammed on the brakes, the pileup worsened. Caltrans officials said 17 vehicles and 15 big rigs were involved in the incident.
