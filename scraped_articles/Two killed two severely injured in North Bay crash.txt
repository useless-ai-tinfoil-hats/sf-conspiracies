Two killed, two severely injured in North Bay crash
-----
Two people died and two others were severely injured in a car crash on Highway 116 outside Santa Rosa early Saturday, authorities said.The two-vehicle collision occurred about 12:10 a.m. in Sebastopol near Sonoma Avenue, according to the California Highway Patrol.
