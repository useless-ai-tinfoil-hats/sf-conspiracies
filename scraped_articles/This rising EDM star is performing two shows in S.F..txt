This rising EDM star is performing two shows in S.F.
-----
House/techno DJ John Summit plans to play two shows in the Bay Area to keep the party going weeks after San Francisco's flagship electronic dance music celebration, Portola Festival, is scheduled to return to Pier 80.Celebrating his debut album, "Comfort in Chaos," released in July, the record producer is set to headline the Cow Palace on Oct. 18-19.
Ticket presales begin at 10 a.m. Tuesday, Sept. 3. Fans can register for the presale online by entering their phone number now.
