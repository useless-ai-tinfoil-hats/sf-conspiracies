‘Inside Out 2’ takes in $295 million worldwide in its first weekend. What does it mean for struggling Pixar?
-----
Anxiety has been replaced by Joy at Emeryville's Pixar. Badly in need of a hit, the struggling animation studio delivered with the second-biggest opening weekend in its history."Inside Out 2" took in an estimated $155 million in the North American box-office and an astonishing $295 million globally, according to studio estimates released Sunday, June 16.
It's the biggest opening weekend at the box office since "Barbie," which took in $162 million domestically and $337 million
