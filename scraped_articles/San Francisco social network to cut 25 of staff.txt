San Francisco social network to cut 25% of staff
-----
Nextdoor, the hyperlocal neighborhood-based social network, is laying off 25% of its workforce or nearly 200 workers, joining the ongoing wave of near-daily Bay Area tech layoffs.The San Francisco-based company reported a third quarter net loss of $38 million on Tuesday, citing a "difficult advertising backdrop," up from a $35 million loss the year prior.
