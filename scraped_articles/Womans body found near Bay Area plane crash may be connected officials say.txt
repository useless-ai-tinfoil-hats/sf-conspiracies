Woman's body found near Bay Area plane crash may be connected, officials say
-----
LATEST Jan. 15, 3:20 p.m. A woman's body was found in the waters near Ross' Cove, which could be connected to a plane crash that occurred Sunday off the coast of Half Moon Bay, Sgt. Philip Hallworth, a spokesperson for the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office told SFGATE.Hallworth said her body was found about one mile south of the wreckage around 9:30 a.m. on Monday.
