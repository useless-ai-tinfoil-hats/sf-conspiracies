Stanford women pressed by Duke, but hold on to win in overtime
-----
Stanford got a preview of ACC life in Sunday's matchup with Duke, and finally got a challenge.Stanford (4-0) survived Duke's (3-2) late comeback to win 82-79 in overtime at Maples Pavilion against their future conference opponent in their toughest game of the season to date. They saw yet another strong showing from junior Kiki Iriafen (a career-high 27 points) and Cameron Brink, who finished with 29 points – also a career-high – and 11 rebounds and six blocks.
