Gunshots were fired in the heart of San Francisco's famed Haight-Ashbury district Saturday afternoon, but no one appears to have been hurt, police said.San Francisco police did not find any suspects or injured people when they responded at 2:43 p.m. to a reported shooting on the 1400 block of Haight Street, near Masonic Avenue.
Officers learned that "a possible verbal argument led to shooting between parties," the police department said in a statement. No one has been arrested yet
