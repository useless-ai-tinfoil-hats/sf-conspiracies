Road to Rickwood: MLB to announce details on incorporating Negro Leagues stats
-----
Willie Mays hit a home run in a league game for the 1948 Birmingham Black Barons. There's no disputing that. It was documented in newspaper clippings and the season's final Negro American League statistics.Yet it won't count, an unfortunate circumstance but certainly not the end of the baseball world. Mays has no problem with his official total of 660 homers. After all, just five men in Major League Baseball history hit more.
