Downtown S.F. street party draws thousands: ‘It’s bringing electricity to the area’
-----
The first of San Francisco's monthly Downtown First Thursdays block parties drew thousands to the city's core as thumping bass, dancing crowds and food vendors enlivened the usually mellow area, with a giant disco ball hanging from a crane taking over Minna Street's alleyway.Attendees browsed arts and crafts vendors on Second Street, painted a communal mural, and listened to sketch comedy.
