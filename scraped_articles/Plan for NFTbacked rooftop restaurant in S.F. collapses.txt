Plan for NFT-backed rooftop restaurant in S.F. collapses
-----
A plan to bring two restaurants and a private club, funded through NFTs, to San Francisco's Salesforce Park is no more.Shō Group is no longer opening the two-story space in the lush elevated park above the Salesforce Transit Center, CEO Josh Sigel confirmed. The news was first reported by SFGate. (The San Francisco Chronicle and SFGate are both owned by Hearst, but operate independently).
