Outside Lands 2024 introduces onsite wedding venue where festivalgoers can get married
-----
Music lovers can now tie the knot at Outside Lands this year, as festival organizers plan to debut an onsite wedding venue in San Francisco's Golden Gate Park.The new activation, named City Hall, celebrates the 20th anniversary of the Winter of Love, which marked the first same-sex marriage ceremonies in the U.S., presided over by then-San Francisco Mayor Gavin Newsom.
