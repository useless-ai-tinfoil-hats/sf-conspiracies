One Bay Area restaurant makes Yelp's top 100 barbecue spots in the US
-----
Only one Bay Area restaurant made Yelp's 2024 list of the top barbecue spots in the country. Capelo's Barbecue, a Southern barbecue restaurant in Redwood City, was ranked No. 33.Capelo's is run by John Capelo, a pitmaster who grew up in Harlingen, Texas. Capelo started out with a food truck around 2010 and opened his brick-and-mortar beer and wine garden on Middlefield Road in Redwood City in 2021.
