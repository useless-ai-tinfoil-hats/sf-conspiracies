SF Giants will try 'several' PA announcers in 2024 to replace Renel Brooks-Moon
-----
San Francisco Giants fans have plenty of reasons to be excited about the team on the field. But up in the announcer's booth, it's another story.As Opening Day nears, the team is dealing with the fallout from the decision to part ways with longtime public address announcer Renel Brooks-Moon.
Giants President and CEO Larry Baer announced Thursday at the team's media open house at Oracle Park that the Giants won't have a permanent replacement for Brooks-Moon to start the 2024 season.
