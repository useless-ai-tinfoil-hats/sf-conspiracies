San Francisco police say missing girl, 14, has been safely located
-----
San Francisco police told the Chronicle a missing 14-year-old girl who disappeared from her San Francisco home on Friday has been safely located.Family members had been searching for Genesis "Raquel" Barrera-Hernandez all weekend. She had last been seen near Turk and Hyde streets in the Tenderloin, according to a San Francisco Police bulletin.
