Ken Niumatalolo will succeed Brent Brennan as SJSU's football coach
-----
San Jose State didn't take long to replace Brent Brennan as its head football coach. Less than a week after Brennan left the Spartans to take over the Arizona program, SJSU on Sunday named Ken Niumatalolo as Brennan's successor.Niumatalolo spent 15-plus seasons (2007-22) as Navy's head coach, compiling a 109-83 record. He led the Midshipmen to double-digit wins in three seasons (2009, 2015 and 2019) and went 6-5 in bowl games.
