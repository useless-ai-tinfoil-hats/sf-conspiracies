Childish Gambino is coming to S.F. Here's how to get tickets
-----
Childish Gambino is coming to the Bay Area this fall.The Grammy-winning rapper and actor, known outside the realm of music as Donald Glover, is scheduled to perform at San Francisco's Chase Center on Sept. 21, following two nights at Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles on Sept. 18-19 as part of the New World Tour. He last performed in the city during Outside Lands in 2019, during which he claimed he drew the largest crowd of the festival's then 12-year run.
