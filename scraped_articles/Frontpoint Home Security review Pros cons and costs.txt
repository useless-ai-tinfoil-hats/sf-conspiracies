Frontpoint Home Security review: Pros, cons, and costs
-----
Frontpoint Home Security at a glanceFrontpoint's big draw as a top home security company is simplicity. All of its packages feature easy do-it-yourself (DIY) installation and around-the-clock professional monitoring for the same monthly fee. Still, these monthly fees run high compared to the industry average (unless you qualify for special pricing), and people in the market for a self-monitoring system will need to look elsewhere.
