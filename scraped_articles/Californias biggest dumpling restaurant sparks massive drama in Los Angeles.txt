California's biggest dumpling restaurant sparks massive drama in Los Angeles
-----

Din Tai Fung, the Taiwanese behemoth that has become one of America's most famous soup dumpling restaurants, is set to depart from its most recognizable LA location this month, after years of serving xiao long bao and other specialities to millions of tourists and locals.
