Women’s pro soccer is back in Bay Area. This time, it’s poised to stick
-----
This weekend, for the third time in Bay Area sports history, a women's professional soccer team will make its debut.The smart money says that, this time, it has staying power.
Bay FC begins play Sunday, visiting Angel City in Los Angeles. The NWSL expansion team enters a 12-year-old, 14-team league that is thriving, with a new four-year $240 million media-rights deal, rising attendance and a changed landscape that now embraces professional women's sports.
