Brandon Crawford embodies fun era of Giants baseball. Will it ever be the same?
-----
Music for losers?The San Francisco Giants are all about creating a new approach to baseball, and it looks like their most noteworthy innovation is music in the clubhouse after losses.
The Giants reportedly unwind after losses to the mellow grooves of Bob Marley.
If this column stands for anything, it is innovation and tradition-busting. The phony graveside mourning that old-school managers demand after losses is so Miller Huggins.
But music in the losing clubhouse?
