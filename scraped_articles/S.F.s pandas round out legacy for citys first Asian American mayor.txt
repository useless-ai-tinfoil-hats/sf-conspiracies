S.F.’s pandas round out legacy for city’s first Asian American mayor
-----
The announcement that San Francisco is getting giant pandas is a dream come true for the city — and a dream of its late mayor, Ed Lee.Lee, San Francisco's first Asian American mayor, saw his historic position as an opportunity to strengthen ties between China and San Francisco, according to Francis Tsang, his former deputy chief of staff. The child of immigrants from Taishan, China, Lee served as mayor from 2011 until his unexpected passing in December 2017.
