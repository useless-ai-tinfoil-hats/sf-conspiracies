The Black Keys abruptly cancel tour, including Bay Area date
-----
The Black Keys abruptly canceled their upcoming International Players Tour, which included a fall performance in the Bay Area, catching many fans by surprise.The Grammy-winning duo, composed of singer-guitarist Dan Auerbach and drummer Patrick Carney, posted a message to social media on Sunday, May 26, to address the unexpected change of plans for their North American trek. "The band wants to assure everyone that Dan & Patrick are alive and well," the Black Keys wrote in a statement
