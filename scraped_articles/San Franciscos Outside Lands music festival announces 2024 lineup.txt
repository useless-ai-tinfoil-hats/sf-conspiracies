San Francisco's Outside Lands music festival announces 2024 lineup
-----
After weeks of scrutiny and speculation, the Outside Lands lineup is here.The three-day festival, which takes place in Golden Gate Park from August 9 to 11, announced the full lineup on Tuesday. Headliners include rapper Tyler, the Creator, who recently performed a (literally) explosive Coachella set, and 2000s rock darlings the Killers, who penned the infectious "Mr. Brightside."
