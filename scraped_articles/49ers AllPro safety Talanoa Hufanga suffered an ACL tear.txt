49ers All-Pro safety Talanoa Hufanga suffered an ACL tear
-----
A year after Talanoa Hufanga's breakout season, his 2023 campaign is over due to a bad break.The San Francisco 49ers' All-Pro safety suffered a non-contact knee injury in the third quarter of a 27-14 win over the Buccaneers, and head coach Kyle Shanahan confirmed on Monday that Hufanga suffered a torn ACL. Shanahan said Hufanga did not suffer additional ligament damage and will be ready for the start of the 2024 season.
The injury was "as clean as it could be," Shanahan said.
