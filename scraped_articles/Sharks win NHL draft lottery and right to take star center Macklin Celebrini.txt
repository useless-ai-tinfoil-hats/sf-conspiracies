Sharks win NHL draft lottery and right to take star center Macklin Celebrini
-----
The San Jose Sharks will pick first overall in June's NHL draft after winning a potentially franchise-altering lottery on Tuesday night.Sharks general manager Mike Grier isn't shying away from the obvious: They're going to take Boston University center Macklin Celebrini, who would become the centerpiece of their rebuild.
"I would think so, yes," Grier responded when asked on ESPN whether the Sharks would take the 17-year-old with the first selection.
