Opera San José makes a key cast change for ‘Florencia en el Amazonas’
-----
Soprano Elizabeth Caballero plans to take over the title role in the production of "Florencia en el Amazonas" at Opera San José.She will replace the originally scheduled Marlen Nahhas, the company announced Monday, April 8. No reason was given for the switch.
The 1996 opera by the late Mexican composer Daniel Catán, which opens April 20, is a magical-realist tale of an opera singer's steamboat journey up the Amazon for an appearance at the opera house in Manaus, Brazil.
