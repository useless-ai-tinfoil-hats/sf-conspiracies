Neil Young ends Spotify boycott, returns music to platform
-----
Neil Young has reluctantly decided to return his music to Spotify, two years after pulling it from the platform due to concerns about the spread of COVID-19 vaccine misinformation on Joe Rogan's popular podcast.In a message to his fans, Young explained that he ended his boycott after Spotify relinquished exclusive rights to "The Joe Rogan Experience," following a new $250 million deal that allows Rogan to distribute the show on other platforms.
