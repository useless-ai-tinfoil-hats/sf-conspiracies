Anchor Brewing has been bought by billionaire behind Chobani. Here are his plans
-----
The billionaire behind the Chobani yogurt brand has acquired Anchor Brewing Co. with plans to modernize and reopen the historic San Francisco brand that closed last year after 127 years in operation.Hamdi Ulukaya, Chobani founder and CEO, announced Friday that his family office had bought all of Anchor's assets: the famous steam beer recipes, the 2.1-acre Potrero Hill campus and all the brewing equipment in the De Haro Street warehouses. The price was not disclosed.
