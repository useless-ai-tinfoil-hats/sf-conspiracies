Major S.F. radio station pulls the plug on annual all-star holiday concert
-----
San Francisco radio station Alice at 97.3 has canceled its annual holiday concert, "Alice in Winterland."The lineup for the show, which was scheduled for Dec. 1 at SAP Center in San Jose, included station favorites such as Nashville-raised country rapper Jelly Roll, San Francisco pop-rock band Train and TikTok singer-songwriter Benson Boone.
Organizers at the station, which is owned by Audacy, did not explain why they pulled the plug on the show.
