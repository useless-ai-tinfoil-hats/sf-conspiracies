Bay Area weather: Drier air and clearer skies expected this week
-----
Pleasant weather is on tap for the Bay Area this week, with near-seasonal temperatures, occasionally gusty winds and mainly clear skies.Several low-pressure systems are expected to bring precipitation to the Pacific Northwest and Intermountain West throughout the week. Rainfall is not in the cards for the Bay Area, but the proximity of these systems will alter local weather.
