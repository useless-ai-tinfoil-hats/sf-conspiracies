‘Taylor Swift: The Eras Tour’ is coming to the Castro Theatre for one day. Here's when
-----
Here's something you don't see every day: longtime Castro Theatre organist David Hegarty opening for Taylor Swift."Taylor Swift: The Eras Tour," the film version of Swift's record-breaking concert tour, has been booked at its biggest Bay Area venue yet. The 101-year-old Castro, which seats 1,400, is set to screen it on Dec. 10.
The box office opens at 2 p.m., doors open at 3 p.m. and an organ pre-show begins at 3:45 p.m.
