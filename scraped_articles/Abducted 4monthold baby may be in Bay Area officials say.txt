Abducted 4-month-old baby may be in Bay Area, officials say
-----
A 4-month-old baby who was abducted from his home in San Joaquin County may have been taken to the Bay Area, according to the local sheriff's office.Officials said the child was taken from his home in Thornton around 4 p.m. Saturday.
The sheriff's office initially suspected the ex-boyfriend of the child's mother, who is not the baby's father, may have taken him, ABC10 reported, but shortly after the baby was reported missing, officials located him in Sacramento without the child.
