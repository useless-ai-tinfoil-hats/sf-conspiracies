Slayer, Iron Maiden and Mötley Crüe to headline Northern California’s largest rock festival
-----
Slayer, Slipknot, Iron Maiden and Mötley Crüe are set to headline the 2024 Aftershock Festival, the largest heavy metal concert in Northern California.The four-day event, scheduled to take place from Oct. 10-13 at Discovery Park in Sacramento, features eight headliners in all and a lineup of more than 130 acts, including Pantera, Judas Priest, Five Finger Death Punch, Disturbed, Evanescence, Staind, Mastodon, Anthrax and more.
