Is Oakland close to getting a new police chief?
-----
Oakland police commissioners say they are close to short-listing the city's next police chief, a development that comes seven months after the last one was fired and days after a report clearing the former chief of mishandling misconduct investigations circulated widely.The Oakland Police Commission said on Monday that it expects to recommend three finalists in mid-October to Mayor Sheng Thao, who will be responsible for selecting the new chief.
