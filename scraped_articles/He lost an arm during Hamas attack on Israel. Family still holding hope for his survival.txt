He lost an arm during Hamas attack on Israel. Family still holding hope for his survival
-----
The one thing Rachel Goldberg knows for sure about her son, 23-year-old Hersh Goldberg-Polin, is that he lost an arm in the Hamas attack on the bomb shelter where he was hiding out and was marched to a pickup truck bound for Gaza, wearing a tourniquet he made for himself."As a mother, I can just hope and pray that Hersh is still alive," she told the Chronicle from Jerusalem on Tuesday. "It's been 10 days and we have no idea."
