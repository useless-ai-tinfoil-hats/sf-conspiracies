East Bay police: Unlicensed handgun recovered during vehicle search
-----
Union City officers recovered an unlicensed handgun during a search of a suspicious vehicle Saturday, according to the city's Police Department.Two officers patrolling the area of 30073 Industrial Parkway observed narcotics in plain view while they investigated the vehicle, the agency posted on Facebook. During a search, they recovered a loaded self-manufactured, unregistered semiautomatic pistol, the Police Department posted on Facebook.
