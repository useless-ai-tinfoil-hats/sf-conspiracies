California train Pacific Surfliner suspends area service following San Clemente mudslide
-----
Southern California is recovering from torrential rains that flooded parts of San Diego. In the wake of the storm, sections of the coastline crumbled in a landslide that has disrupted service indefinitely for a popular train line.Amtrak and Metrolink announced Wednesday evening that portions of the Pacific Surfliner and other commuter lines are temporarily suspended due to a mudslide in San Clemente.
