New Bay Area museum opens dedicated to Eames mid-century modern furniture
-----
If you know one name associated with mid-century modern furniture, it's probably Eames.Charles and Ray Eames, a married couple who died in 1978 and 1988 respectively, left a permanent stamp on the field of design with their iconic lounge and dining chairs. Although best known for their furniture, the duo collaborated across nearly every discipline of the design field. Their stamp can be found on everything from Frank Lloyd Wright buildings to the classic educational film "Powers of Ten."
