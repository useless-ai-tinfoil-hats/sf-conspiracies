San Jose State heads to Toledo for final pre-Mountain West game
-----
Saturday's gameWho: San Jose State (1-2) at Toledo (1-1)
When: 4 p.m.
Where: Glass Bowl, Toledo, Ohio
TV/Radio: ESPN+ (streaming)/860
Story line: The Spartans' 59-3 thumping of Cal Poly, an FCS school, last Saturday followed losses by wide margins to ranked Pac-12 teams USC and Oregon State. This is SJSU's final nonconference game before Mountain West play. The Rockets, who won the Mid-American Conference title last year, almost upended Illinois (losing 30-28)
