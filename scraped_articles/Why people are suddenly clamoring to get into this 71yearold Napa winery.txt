Why people are suddenly clamoring to get into this 71-year-old Napa winery
-----
Stony Hill is one of Napa Valley's most storied estates, but few people have actually set foot on the property throughout the winery's 71-year history.Now, that's finally changing.
A rare Napa winery that's better known for its white wines than its reds, Stony Hill was founded long before tourists began making their way to the region.
