Quirky SF shop shutters amid plummeting foot traffic, neighborhood trouble
-----
Jenny Lemons, a quirky boutique on 24th Street in the Mission, is closing at the end of November amid plummeting foot traffic.The 6-year-old store, which sells funky items including hair clips shaped like hot dogs, shiny mushroom stickers and brightly patterned T-shirts, has seen declining in-person sales since the pandemic, according to owner Jennie Lennick.
