Man arrested after allegedly assaulting S.F. church-goer and throwing explosive at police car
-----
A man was arrested Sunday after allegedly assaulting a parishioner at a San Francisco church and setting off a pipe bomb as he fled from authorities, city officials said.The incident unfolded shortly before 6 p.m., when officers responded to a report of an assault at the iconic Saints Peter and Paul church in North Beach, according to the San Francisco Police Department and Board of Supervisors President Aaron Peskin, who represents the area.
