PG&E outages in S.F. leave thousands without power
-----
A Pacific Gas & Electric Co. outage left about 7,050 customers in San Francisco without power Wednesday morning.The outage was centered on the Castro neighborhood, spanning the hilly areas around Twin Peaks to the edge of the Mission District.
Throughout the morning, PG&E was assessing the cause of the outage and had sent workers to the area to determine possible damage, the utility company said.
