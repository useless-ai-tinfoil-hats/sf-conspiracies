Giants lose shot at sweeping Tigers as Hayden Birdsong scuffles again
-----
The San Francisco Giants lost an opportunity for their third sweep of the season with a 5-4 loss to the Detroit Tigers on Sunday afternoon.Without his best fastball command for a second start in a row, Hayden Birdsong clawed through his outing against the Tigers. Matt Vierling launched his lazy slider for a leadoff home run and Dillon Dingler erased a Giants lead in the second inning with his first career home run the next inning.
