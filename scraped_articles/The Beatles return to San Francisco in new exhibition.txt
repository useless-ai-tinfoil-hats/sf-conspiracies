The Beatles return to San Francisco in new exhibition
-----
Almost 60 years after their final concert at Candlestick Park, the Beatles are coming back to San Francisco.The exhibition "Paul McCartney Photographs 1963-64: Eyes of the Storm," which comes to the de Young Museum on March 1, will feature more than 250 images of the musician and his bandmates John Lennon, George Harrison and Ringo Starr — also known as "The Fab Four" — during a period of three months on tour in England and the United States from December 1963 to February 1964.
