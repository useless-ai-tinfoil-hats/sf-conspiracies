Exclusive: Fintech company in talks for large S.F. office lease in former Pinterest building
-----
Amsterdam-based financial tech company Adyen is slated to scoop up a sizable chunk of office space in San Francisco, two individuals with direct knowledge of the deal have confirmed.Adyen, which leases space in San Francisco at 274 Brannan St. in SoMa, will remain nearby: The company would take over a 130,000-square-foot office at 505 Brannan St., the sources, who requested anonymity to preserve business relations, said Monday. The deal is expected to close in the coming weeks.
