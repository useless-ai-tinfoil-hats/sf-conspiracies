Woman carjacked in Piedmont as she put baby in car seat, police say
-----
Thieves stole a car from a woman on Sunday morning as she was putting her baby in a car seat in Piedmont, Calif., and later that day, police arrested a 19-year-old man on suspicion of stealing the woman's BMW, officials said.The carjacking occurred at 9 a.m. Sunday on the 1200 block of Grand Avenue, the Piedmont Police Department said.
