Bay Area startup lays off 75% of employees after FDA rejection
-----
Bay Area startup Lykos Therapeutics laid off 75% of its workforce this week after a surprising federal rejection of its pharmaceutical application for MDMA-assisted therapy, according to a Thursday announcement from the company.It's unclear how many workers have been affected by the layoffs. Lykos did not return an SFGATE request for comment and the company did not file a WARN notice with the state, which is required for layoffs of more than 50 California employees.
