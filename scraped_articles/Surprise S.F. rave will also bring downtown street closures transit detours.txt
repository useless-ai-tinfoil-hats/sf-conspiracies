Surprise S.F. rave will also bring downtown street closures, transit detours
-----
The last-minute announcement of a rave in downtown San Francisco by electronic music artists and DJs Skrillex and Fred Again.. will bring with it a set of hastily arranged road closures, detours and transit changes as 20,000 fans flood Civic Center Plaza for the Saturday event.Street closures in the area started Tuesday, even before the concert was confirmed, and some will continue through Monday, according to the San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency.
