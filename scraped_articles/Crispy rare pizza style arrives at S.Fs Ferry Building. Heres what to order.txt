Crispy, rare pizza style arrives at S.F’s Ferry Building. Here’s what to order
-----
San Francisco's A16 has long been known for executing Italian classics well, and its newest restaurant is no different.A16 La Pala, a casual spinoff that serves Roman-style pizza, focaccia sandwiches and pastries, opened at the Ferry Building earlier this month. There, a glass case is set with thin, rectangular slices of pizza al taglio ($8-$11) with toppings like roasted cherry tomatoes, soppressata or braised escarole.
