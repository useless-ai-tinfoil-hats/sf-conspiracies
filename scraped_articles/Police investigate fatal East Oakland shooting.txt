Police investigate fatal East Oakland shooting
-----
A man was fatally shot Saturday in East Oakland, authorities said.The victim died at a hospital after being shot just before 3 p.m. near 94th Avenue and A Street, the Oakland Police Department said Sunday. His identity was unavailable, pending notification of next of kin.
Details surrounding the shooting, including a motive, were not available, and no arrests had been announced.
