How Brock Bowers went from a winless Napa High team to best tight end in 2024 NFL draft
-----
When Richie Wessman was hired to lead Napa High School's moribund football program five years ago, the former NFL assistant coach didn't dream he was inheriting a team with a future NFL player.The Grizzlies were coming off an 0-10 season and the intel Wessman had gathered before their first spring practice suggested Napa had very much earned its winless record.
"I had heard from all the people in the town how horrible things were," Wessman said. "I had no idea what was there."
