Alex Tatusian joins S.F. Chronicle as deputy creative director
-----
Alex Tatusian has joined the San Francisco Chronicle as deputy creative director.At the Chronicle, Tatusian will help lead a team of seven designers and engineers building and designing the Chronicle's most ambitious stories across all platforms. He will report to Chronicle Creative Director Alex K. Fong.
"Having the 'other' Alex join us is huge," Fong said. "He brings deep experience in branding, design and development, meaning he will have a hand shaping every newsroom editorial
