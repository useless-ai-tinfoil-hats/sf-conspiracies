‘Need some blood pressure meds’: 49ers fans exhale after tight win over Packers
-----
Extreme tension turned swiftly to joy at Levi's Stadium on Saturday night as Dre Greenlaw's interception sealed the San Francisco 49ers' 24-21 victory over the Green Bay Packers.Fans exhaled. They cheered. They whooped. They wondered about their blood pressure.
This was no romp — it was a gritty, hard-fought game in the rain, the type the 49ers had rarely had to tough out before.
