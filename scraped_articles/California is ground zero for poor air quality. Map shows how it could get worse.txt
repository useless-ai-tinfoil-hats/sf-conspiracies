California is ‘ground zero’ for poor air quality. Map shows how it could get worse
-----
The Clean Air Act of 1970 has helped Americans breathe easier, driving measurable improvements in air quality over the past two decades. But human-caused climate change is turning back those gains across the United States, with wildfires in California and the West driving the reversal.
