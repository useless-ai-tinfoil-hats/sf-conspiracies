Person electrocuted, dies after climbing into utility vault in Richmond
-----
A person died in Richmond after they climbed into a utility vault Tuesday, according to fire officials.Around 3 a.m.,the person climbed into an underground Pacific Gas and Electric vault at South Second Street and Cutting Boulevard, where they were electrocuted by high voltage power lines, said Michael Smith, Interim Chief of Richmond Fire Department.
The person then fell 4-6 feet further into the vault and standing water, where they were found dead, Smith said.
