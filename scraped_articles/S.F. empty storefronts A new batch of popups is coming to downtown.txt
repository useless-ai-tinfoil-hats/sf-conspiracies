S.F. empty storefronts: A new batch of pop-ups is coming to downtown
-----
Those city-backed pop-ups in San Francisco's Financial District show signs of taking root.Eight of the nine tenants who opened commercial storefronts last October in the vacancy-riddled blocks between Montgomery Street and Embarcadero Center are being invited to stick around through at least March, City Hall announced Thursday. The program also intends to add another cluster of downtown pop-ups by early summer.
