Feds probe airplane crash that killed pilot in Marin
-----
Federal investigators were searching for clues in the wreckage of a plane crash that killed a pilot at Marin County's Gnoss Field over the weekend.The pilot was confirmed dead after he became trapped inside the single-engine Aero Commander 200D aircraft that struck an airplane hangar Saturday afternoon, said Sgt. Adam Schermerhorn of the Marin County Sheriff's Office. Both the airplane and the hangar burst into flames in the crash, killing the pilot before he could be rescued,
