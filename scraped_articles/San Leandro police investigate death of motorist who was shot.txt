San Leandro police investigate death of motorist who was shot
-----
A homicide investigation is underway in San Leandro after a person was shot, crashed their car and died in the East Bay city last weekend.At 6:24 p.m. Sunday, officers responded to a report of a solo car crash on the 1800 block of Benedict Drive to find the driver of the car suffering from a gunshot wound, police said. The victim was pronounced dead by paramedics, according to police.
Authorities said they believe the shooting occurred in the same area of the crash.
