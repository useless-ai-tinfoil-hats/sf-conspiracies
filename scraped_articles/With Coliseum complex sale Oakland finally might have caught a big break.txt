With Coliseum complex sale, Oakland finally might have caught a big break
-----
Oakland, the town that just can't catch a break, might have caught one. A big one.It was announced Monday that Oakland A's owner John Fisher has agreed to sell his team's half-interest in the Oakland Coliseum site to the African American Sports and Entertainment Group.
AASEG recently reached an agreement to buy the other half of the Coliseum from the city of Oakland, so it soon will own the whole shebang.
