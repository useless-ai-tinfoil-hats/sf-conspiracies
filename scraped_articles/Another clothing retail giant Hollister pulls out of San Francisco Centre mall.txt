Another clothing retail giant, Hollister, pulls out of San Francisco Centre mall
-----
Another retailer at San Francisco Centre has bitten the dust.Hollister, the preppy teen clothing brand once sandwiched between Guess and Victoria's Secret on the second floor of the mall, unceremoniously closed its doors last month, marking the closure of its only store in the city, the San Francisco Chronicle first reported. (The Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms.)
