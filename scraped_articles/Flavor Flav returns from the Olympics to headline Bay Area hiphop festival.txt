Flavor Flav returns from the Olympics to headline Bay Area hip-hop festival
-----
Fresh from his gig as the official hype man for the USA water polo teams at the Paris Olympics, Flavor Flav will return to the Bay Area with his group Public Enemy for a special one-off music festival.The rap pioneers, whose politically charged "It Takes a Nation of Millions to Hold Us Back" is hailed as one of the greatest hip-hop albums ever, are slated to join Cypress Hill and De La Soul at Hip-Hop 101: From New York to the Bay, scheduled to take place at the Oakland Arena on Nov. 9.
