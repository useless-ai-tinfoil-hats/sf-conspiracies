Hundreds of items owned by San Francisco legend Tony Bennett are up for sale
-----
Jazz fans in San Francisco have a chance to get their hands on some items once owned by venerated singer Tony Bennett, the artist behind one of the most famous Bay Area songs ever recorded.A whopping 756 items of Bennett memorabilia are slated to be auctioned off by April 19. Julien's Auctions, a pop culture auction house based in California, is hosting the event.
