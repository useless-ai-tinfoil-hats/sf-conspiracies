Steph Curry delivers daggers as Team USA earns gold against France in Paris
-----
PARIS — Stephen Curry will leave France with the souvenir he craved — an Olympic gold medal.Two nights after Curry rescued the U.S. from what would have been an inglorious semifinal loss, the Americans withstood a persistent France team Saturday night. Head coach Steve Kerr's team stumbled and then surged down the stretch, again fueled by Curry, for a spirited 98-87 victory at Bercy Arena.
