Warriors’ Klay Thompson questionable for Sunday’s game in Cleveland
-----
CLEVELAND – Golden State Warriors guard Klay Thompson is listed as questionable for Sunday's game against the Cleveland Cavaliers because of right adductor tightness.The 33-year-old missed Monday's game against the New Orleans Pelicans because of right knee soreness. The Warriors will conclude their second back-to-back set of the season on Monday in Detroit.
