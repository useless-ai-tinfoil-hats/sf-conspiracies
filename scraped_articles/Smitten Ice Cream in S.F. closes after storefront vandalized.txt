Smitten Ice Cream in S.F. closes after storefront vandalized
-----
UPDATE: Smitten Ice Cream owner says vandalism at S.F. store being investigated as a hate crimeSmitten Ice Cream, the popular Bay Area ice cream chain, has closed its Valencia Street store after its exterior was vandalized.
"Our San Francisco (Valencia Street) location is closed right now," the chain wrote last week on Instagram.
The post did not indicate whether the closure is temporary or permanent. Smitten owner Robyn Sue Fisher did not respond to a request for comment.
