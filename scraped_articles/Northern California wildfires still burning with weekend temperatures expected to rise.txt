Northern California wildfires still burning, with weekend temperatures expected to rise
-----
Firefighters have made significant progress containing several wildfires burning in Northern California since early this week, but officials said Friday they were keeping a watchful eye on the weekend weather forecast, with temperatures expected to rise in some areas.The largest of the active fires statewide, the Sites Fire in Colusa County, had consumed almost 20,000 acres and was 25% contained as of Friday, according to Cal Fire.
