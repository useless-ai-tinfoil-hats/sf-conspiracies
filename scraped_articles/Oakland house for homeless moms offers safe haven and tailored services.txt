Oakland house for homeless moms offers safe haven and tailored services
-----
An Alameda resident has raised more than a half-million dollars to create a home in East Oakland for unhoused mothers and children in memory of a childhood best friend who struggled with homelessness.Jackie Downing, who has worked in Bay Area nonprofits for 20 years, said she was compelled to tackle the local homelessness crisis after her friend Anna Flynn committed suicide. Flynn had struggled for years with mental illness and spent time living on the street.
