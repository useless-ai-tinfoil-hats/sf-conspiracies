49ers vs. Lions: Dramatically different weather on tap for NFC Championship Game
-----
Dry, warm weather is expected for Sunday's NFC Championship Game between the San Francisco 49ers and Detroit Lions at Levi's Stadium.The forecast is a welcome turnaround from the soggy, slippery conditions during the 49ers' comeback playoff win Saturday, when downpours in Santa Clara likely had a hand in quarterback Brock Purdy's unusually erratic throws.
