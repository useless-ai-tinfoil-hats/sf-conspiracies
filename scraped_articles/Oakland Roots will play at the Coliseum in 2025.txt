Oakland Roots will play at the Coliseum in 2025
-----
The Oakland Roots soccer team signed a deal last week that ensures they will play at the Coliseum next year.The agreement, signed Thursday evening, allows the team to play their 2025 games at the stadium while continuing negotiations with Oakland to open a temporary stadium in 2026 with 10,000 seats at the Malibu lot nearby.
