Giants' All-Star Alex Cobb departs in 3rd inning with apparent hip injury
-----
PHOENIX - The San Francisco Giants' hopes for a late dash into the postseason took a sharp and unfortunate turn Tuesday night at Chase Field when All-Star starter Alex Cobb left with what appeared to be a major flareup of his recurring hip problem.Cobb threw 33 pitches in the second inning, an inning made longer by two Giants errors on one play and one in which Arizona scored four runs, and he looked in discomfort coming off the mound.
