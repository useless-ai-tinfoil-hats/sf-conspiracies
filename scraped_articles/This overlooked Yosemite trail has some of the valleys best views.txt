This overlooked Yosemite trail has some of the valley's best views
-----
Yosemite is a dancing valley. The incredible landscape — massive cliffs and giant waterfalls — are the product of millions of years of motion as granite mounds pushed to the surface and glaciers and water cut them into dramatic shapes.But the current movement in Yosemite's dance is decidedly less interesting, as the valley is filled again, but this time with hordes of tourists.
