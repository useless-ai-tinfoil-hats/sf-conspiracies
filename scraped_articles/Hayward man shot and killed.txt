Hayward man shot and killed
-----
A 55-year-old Hayward man was shot and killed Sunday afternoon, police said Monday.Officers were dispatched to the 400 block of Berry Avenue following reports of a shooting. They found the man suffering from gunshot wounds.
Medical personnel who arrived at the scene pronounced the man dead.
Officials did not immediately release additional identifying information about the slain man, but said no suspects had been taken into custody as of Monday afternoon.
