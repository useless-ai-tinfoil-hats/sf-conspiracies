Safeway store in Santa Clara to close after over six decades
-----
A Safeway store in Santa Clara is slated to permanently close in early November, a spokesperson for the grocery chain confirmed.The store, located in the Valley Fair Shopping Center at Stevens Creek Boulevard, has been open for 67 years. "The decision to close this store isn't one we made lightly or without a great deal of deliberation," a Safeway spokesperson said.
