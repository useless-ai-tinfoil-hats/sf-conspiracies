SF Giants' nightmare scenario comes true as Shohei Ohtani signs with Dodgers
-----
The San Francisco Giants' worst fears have been realized. After a whirlwind day of speculation and false reports, two-way superstar Shohei Ohtani announced on Instagram that he will be joining the Los Angeles Dodgers.MLB insider Jeff Passan reports the deal is for 10 years and $700 million, which would make it the largest contract in American professional sports history, shattering the record for largest contract in baseball history by more than $250 million.
