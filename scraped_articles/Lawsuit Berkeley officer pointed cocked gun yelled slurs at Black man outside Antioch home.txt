Lawsuit: Berkeley officer pointed cocked gun, yelled slurs at Black man outside Antioch home
-----
A veteran Berkeley police officer is being sued for allegedly punching a man in the face and pointing a cocked gun at him while yelling racial slurs during an altercation that occurred near the officer's Antioch home last year.Sgt. David Marble was off duty during the alleged incident, according to a lawsuit filed by Brian Lindhurst Jr., who is Black.
In the complaint, filed last week in federal court, Lindhurst alleges Marble used excessive force and violated his civil rights
