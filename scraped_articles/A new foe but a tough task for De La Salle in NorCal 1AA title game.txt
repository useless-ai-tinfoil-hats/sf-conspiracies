A new foe, but a tough task for De La Salle in NorCal 1-AA title game
-----
With the possibility of playing Sac-Joaquin Section power Folsom a fourth time in the past 15 months, De La Salle-Concord football coach Justin Alumbaugh was pleased that a California Interscholastic Federation football committee gave his team a new challenge.That is, until he took a deep dive glance at the Clovis North Broncos, the Spartans' Northern California 1-AA opponent Friday night. De La Salle (10-2) hosts the 13-0 Central Section Division 1 champions at 7:30 p.m.
