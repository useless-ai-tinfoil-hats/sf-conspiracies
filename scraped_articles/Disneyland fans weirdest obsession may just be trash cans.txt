Disneyland fans' weirdest obsession may just be trash cans
-----
Of all the things Disneyland fans are passionate about, one of the most unusual — and strangely most popular — is the trash can.At the Disney theme parks, trash cans are themed to each individual land. In Fantasyland, they have medieval-looking patterns. In Frontierland, they're painted to look like rough-hewn wood.
