Review: All Hamlet’s moods make perfect sense at Santa Cruz Shakespeare
-----
Hamlet's moodiness isn't just sulkiness — pacing the palace halls and burying his face in a book when others want to socialize. Denmark's best-known fictional prince turns on those he once loved, mocking their ministrations with apparently nonsensical replies; for all his celebrated complexity, he can often come off as a jerk.Not so in Santa Cruz Shakespeare's production of the tragedy that bears his name.
