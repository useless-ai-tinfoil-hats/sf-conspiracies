Officials: Exclusive Central Valley golf club discovered as illegal cannabis business
-----
An exclusive Central Valley golf club was allegedly operating an illegal cannabis business, officials said.Investigators arrived at the AOA Private Golf Club on 1817 N. Helm Ave. in Fresno on Thursday morning, where they found thousands of dollars' worth of illegal cannabis, cannabis products and paraphernalia, the Fresno City Attorney's Office said in a news release.
