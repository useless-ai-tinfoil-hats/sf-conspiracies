Portion of San Francisco Bay Trail to close Monday for construction
-----
A portion of the San Francisco Bay Trail will close Monday as construction moves forward on a bridge replacement project in Redwood City, Caltrans told reporters this weekend.Approximately 3 miles of trailway will be closed between Bair Island Road and Twin Dolphin Drive from sunrise to sunset Monday to accommodate the Cordilleras Creek Bridge Replacement Project adjacent to U.S. Highway 101, according to NBC and other outlets, citing Caltrans reports.
