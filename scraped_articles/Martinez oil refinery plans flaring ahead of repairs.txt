Martinez oil refinery plans flaring ahead of repairs
-----
The Martinez Refining Company, saddled with a series of emissions problems last year, warned nearby residents to expect intermittent flaring this week while operators shut down a portion of its facility to make repairs.Contra Costa County health authorities also sent alerts to nearby residents, stating the department "will monitor activity and notify the public if conditions change that impact the community."
