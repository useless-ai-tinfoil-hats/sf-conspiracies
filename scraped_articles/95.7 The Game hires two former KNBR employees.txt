95.7 The Game hires two former KNBR employees
-----
It's been nearly three weeks since the major shakeups at KNBR, and the effects are still rippling throughout the Bay Area's sports media scene.On Monday, two people who were laid off from KNBR announced on social media that they had joined 95.7 The Game. Former KNBR reporter Jake Hutchinson will go over to work on the digital side, and F.P. Santangelo will join the station as a part-time host.
