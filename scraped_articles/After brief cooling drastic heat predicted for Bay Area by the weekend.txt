After brief cooling, 'drastic' heat predicted for Bay Area by the weekend
-----
After a brief period of cooler temperatures in the San Francisco Bay Area, heat will make its way back into the area starting Thursday and ramping up during the weekend.A significant warming trend is forecasted for the weekend, Dalton Beringer, a metrologist for the National Weather Service told SFGATE. In San Francisco, Saturday will be the hottest day, with temperatures in the mid to upper 70s in the city's eastern side, and into the 60s in the western side of the city, Beringer said.
