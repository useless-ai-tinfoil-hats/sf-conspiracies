Two injured as massive blaze engulfs San Francisco residential building
-----
Two people were injured in a massive blaze that engulfed a three-story duplex two blocks from the Panhandle in San Francisco on Monday morning, the San Francisco Fire Department said.One person jumped from the building on the 700 block of Masonic Avenue to escape flames and has moderate injuries; another person who was escorted from the building has minor injuries, Lt. Mariano Elias, a public information for the fire department, told SFGATE.
