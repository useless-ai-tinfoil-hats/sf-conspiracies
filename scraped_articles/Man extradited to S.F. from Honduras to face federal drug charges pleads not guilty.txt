Man extradited to S.F. from Honduras to face federal drug charges pleads not guilty
-----
One of the three men captured in Honduras by local police and extradited to the United States to face drug charges pleaded not guilty during an appearance in federal court Tuesday.Elmer Matute is facing four counts of possession with intent to distribute drugs, including heroin and meth. He entered the not guilty plea and waived his right to a detention hearing, said U.S. attorney office spokesperson Abraham Simmons.
