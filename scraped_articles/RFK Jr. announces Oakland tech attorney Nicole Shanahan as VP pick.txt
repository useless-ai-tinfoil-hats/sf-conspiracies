RFK Jr. announces Oakland tech attorney Nicole Shanahan as VP pick
-----
Independent presidential candidate Robert F. Kennedy Jr. named Oakland-born tech attorney Nicole Shanahan as his vice presidential nominee Tuesday at a rally in Shanahan's hometown.Shanahan, 38, a self-described "lifelong Democrat" who was formerly married to Google co-founder Sergey Brin, is the founder of the private nonprofit Bia-Echo Foundation, which focuses its grant-making on reproductive rights, criminal justice reform, environmental issues and equality.
