Global pop sensation Janet Jackson announces concert at San Francisco's Chase Center
-----
A global pop sensation is about to take control of San Francisco.Janet Jackson's decadeslong musical career landed her a spot in the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame in 2019 as well as five Grammy awards for dance floor anthems like "Rhythm Nation," "That's the Way Love Goes" and "All for You." It was announced Tuesday that she'll be stopping at Chase Center on June 12 as part of her extended "Together Again" tour.
