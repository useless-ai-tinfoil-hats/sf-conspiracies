As the Coliseum turns, A’s vs. fans drama creates questions. We have answers
-----
The Oakland Athletics' management and organized groups of A's fans are like bickering next-door neighbors, glaring at one another over the back fence.The Chronicle has learned that any détente between the two will occur approximately never. The A's are trying to leave town, the fans are mad about it, and that's not going to change. Tensions might ease after the A's leave Oakland, but that day is at least one season in the future, and possibly four or five.
