Video wows with spectacular explosion that cleared California rockslide
-----
Work crews used explosives to blow up a pile of massive rock boulders — some as large as a minivan — blocking a roadway in California's Sierra Nevada foothills.Placer County posted a video of the explosion on Facebook, and the footage is something you'd expect to see in a Hollywood film.
In the comments, people wrote, "Wow," "Super cool," "Awesome shot," and "Tremors movie," which is likely a reference to the 1990 classic thriller that featured an explosion scene.
