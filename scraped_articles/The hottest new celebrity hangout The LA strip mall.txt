The hottest new celebrity hangout? The LA strip mall
-----
If you're pulling into this two-story retail complex on the Sunset Strip in West Hollywood, chances are you're knocking a few errands off your to-do list before going about your day. The nondescript strip mall with a metallic copper curlicue railing, dubbed the Sunset Collection, has a FedEx Office Print & Ship Center, a clothing alteration shop, and a Starbucks. Visitors with a little more time and disposable income can park their car underground and then swing by the nail or hair salons
