Lyrid meteor shower peaks this weekend — will it be visible in the Bay Area?
-----
The annual spring meteor shower called the Lyrids is underway and peaks this weekend — but despite a promising weather forecast, Bay Area stargazers might not have the best view this year.Skies should be clear or mostly clear across the region during the peak on Sunday night into Monday morning, according to the National Weather Service. But astronomers say that Lyrid meteors will be competing with the nearly full moon that coincides with the shower.
