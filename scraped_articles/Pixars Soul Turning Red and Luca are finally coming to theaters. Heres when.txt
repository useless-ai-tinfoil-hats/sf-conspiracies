Pixar's 'Soul,' 'Turning Red' and 'Luca' are finally coming to theaters. Here's when
-----
Pixar Animation Studios apparently would like a do-over.The Emeryville-based division of the Walt Disney Company announced it intends to bring three pandemic-era films that never received a domestic theatrical release to the big screen in early 2024: the Oscar-winning "Soul," scheduled to hit theaters on Jan. 12; "Turning Red," starring the voice of Fremont actress Rosalie Chiang, on Feb. 9; and "Luca," featuring the voice of San Francisco actress Emma Berman, on March 22.
