A’s reunion of 1974 World Series champs bittersweet as team preps for move to Las Vegas
-----
Amid the fraternizing Sunday with friends and former teammates at the Coliseum inside an outfield suite, former Oakland Athletics relief pitcher Darold Knowles had a sobering realization."This is the last time, probably, that we'll be getting together."
The last time they'll muse, banter and laugh about the happenings of yesteryear, the championships they won.
The last time the A's — in Oakland, anyway — would celebrate their three consecutive World Series championship teams
