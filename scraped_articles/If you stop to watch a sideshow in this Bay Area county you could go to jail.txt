If you stop to watch a sideshow in this Bay Area county, you could go to jail
-----
A new law in Sonoma County will make it a crime to watch a sideshow.Under the ordinance passed unanimously by the county's Board of Supervisors earlier this month, you can be fined or jailed for participating in — or watching — a sideshow in any area of the county outside city boundaries.
