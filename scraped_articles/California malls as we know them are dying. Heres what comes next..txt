California malls as we know them are dying. Here's what comes next.
-----
The American mall is in a quandary. Stores are papered over and echos bounce around mostly empty halls. In the past decade, online shopping has taken a considerable bite out of in-person retail, leaving empty, palatial wastelands in its wake.
