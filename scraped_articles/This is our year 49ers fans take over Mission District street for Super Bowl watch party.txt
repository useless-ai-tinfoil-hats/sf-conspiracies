‘This is our year’: 49ers fans take over Mission District street for Super Bowl watch party
-----
Lifelong 49ers fan Leroy Bermudez wanted the community to come together in the Mission District, so he threw a watch party for the Super Bowl outside of his home: a two-story house painted red and gold.About 150 fans showed up and took over a small stretch of Bryant Street near 19th Street, where a large TV was set up in front of Bermudez's home, known as the Faithful House.
