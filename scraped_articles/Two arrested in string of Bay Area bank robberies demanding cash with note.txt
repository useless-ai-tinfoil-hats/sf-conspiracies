Two arrested in string of Bay Area bank robberies, demanding cash with note
-----
Two Antioch residents were arrested last week on suspicion of being involved in a string of Northern California bank robberies.One suspect, a 37-year-old man, allegedly used a tactic involving slipping the teller a note demanding money in six separate robberies, the San Jose Police Department said in a news release Wednesday. The second suspect, a 43-year-old woman, was the getaway driver on each occasion.
