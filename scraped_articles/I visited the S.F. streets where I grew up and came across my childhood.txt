I visited the S.F. streets where I grew up and came across my childhood
-----
Thomas Wolfe said it best: You can't go home again. That's true, but you can walk by and look in the window.That's what I did on a long walk through the old neighborhood on Potrero Hill the other afternoon. I walked past three of the houses where I grew up. The houses all looked familiar, like the homes that are backdrops in old family pictures.
