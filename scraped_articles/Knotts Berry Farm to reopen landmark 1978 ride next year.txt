Knott's Berry Farm to reopen landmark 1978 ride next year
-----
A historic Knott's Berry Farm roller coaster is reopening next year after an extensive renovation, the park teased in a video posted Saturday. Montezooma's Revenge closed in 2022 to be "reimagined" and will be revived as MonteZOOMa: The Forbidden Fortress next year.
