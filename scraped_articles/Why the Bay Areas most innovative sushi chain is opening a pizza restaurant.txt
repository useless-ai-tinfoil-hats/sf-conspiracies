Why the Bay Area's most innovative sushi chain is opening a pizza restaurant
-----
There's a new Bay Area pizzeria crafting pies with an unexpected ingredient: soft mochi.Mochiko Mochi Pizza, the first of its kind, opened Saturday in Burlingame after a soft opening phase early last month. Inside Mochiko, customers will find a selection of rare pizzas with an unexpectedly soft, chewy center reminiscent of mochi desserts.
