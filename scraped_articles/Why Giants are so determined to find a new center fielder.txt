Why Giants are so determined to find a new center fielder
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — Beyond the San Francisco Giants' dreams of acquiring Shohei Ohtani or Yoshinobu Yamamoto, there's this little matter of fixing the defense. In particular, the outfield. And to get even more specific …"Center field is going to be a priority for us," Giants president of baseball operations Farhan Zaidi said from the general managers' meetings, which ended Thursday.
