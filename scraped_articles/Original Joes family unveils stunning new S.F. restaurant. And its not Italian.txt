Original Joe’s family unveils stunning new S.F. restaurant. And it’s not Italian
-----
A splashy new restaurant from the family behind San Francisco institution Original Joe's arrives next week — though it won't serve Italian food.Elena's, a Mexican American restaurant, opens Monday, Feb. 5, at 255 West Portal Ave. in San Francisco. It pays homage to co-owner Elena Duggan's two oldest daughters, Catherine and Julia Alcantara, whose father's family is from San Bartolo Morelos, Mexico.
