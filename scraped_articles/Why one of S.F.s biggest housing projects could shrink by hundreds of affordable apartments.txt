Why one of S.F.’s biggest housing projects could shrink by hundreds of affordable apartments
-----
It's been six years since the San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency began working on an ambitious plan to build hundreds of affordable apartments alongside a replacement for its aging Potrero Yard bus facility.Construction on the new transit facility is slated to start early next year. But there are still significant funding hurdles for what is the biggest active project in the Mission — and more than 75% of the proposed housing is at risk of never being built.
