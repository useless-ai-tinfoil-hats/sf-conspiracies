Close relative of extinct S.F. butterfly species reintroduced in the Presidio
-----
The Xerces Blue butterfly, once native to San Francisco's coastal dunes, became the first butterfly species in the U.S. to disappear due to human development over 80 years ago.But after years of research and the assistance of modern technology, a butterfly species closely related to the Xerces Blue has been reintroduced to the dunes of Presidio National Park.
