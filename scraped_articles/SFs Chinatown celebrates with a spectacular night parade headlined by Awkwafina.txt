SF's Chinatown celebrates with a spectacular night parade headlined by Awkwafina
-----
San Francisco's Lunar New Year celebrations for the year of the dragon Saturday night may have been the city's most spectacular in decades, with a parade headlined by Golden Globe winner Awkwafina.As grand marshal, the "Shang-Chi" actor – who was born in the year of the dragon, according to a press release from the parade – wowed thousands of spectators along Chinatown's streets and in the sold-out grandstand seats.
