SF Giants lose another San Francisco institution
-----
The saga for the future of Anchor Brewing, the 127-year-old San Francisco brewery that announced it was ceasing operations last year, is still ongoing. But the final Anchor outpost is no more.The Anchor Bar in Scoreboard Plaza at Oracle Park, the home of the San Francisco Giants, has been shut down.
