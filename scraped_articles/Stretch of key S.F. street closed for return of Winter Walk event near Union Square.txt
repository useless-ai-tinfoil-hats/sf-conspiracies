Stretch of key S.F. street closed for return of Winter Walk event near Union Square
-----
A portion of Stockton Street in San Francisco has been closed through the Christmas holiday to make way for the return of the Winter Walk pop-up event at Union Square, transit officials said.Stockton Street between Post and O'Farrell streets closed Monday and will remain closed until 11:59 p.m. Dec. 28 for Winter Walk, according to the San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency.
