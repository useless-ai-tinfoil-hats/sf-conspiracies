Never in her wildest dreams did Molly Welton think she would hold the keys to one of Golden Gate Park's oldest and most iconic institutions.But here she is. Welton, the owner of San Francisco's pandemic-born mobile coffee caravan, California Kahve, is the new operator of Golden Gate Park's 110-year-old carousel as well as its food and beverage kiosk, located in the park's southeast corner.
