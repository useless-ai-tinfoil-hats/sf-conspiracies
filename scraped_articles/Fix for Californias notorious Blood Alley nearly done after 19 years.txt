Fix for California's notorious 'Blood Alley' nearly done after 19 years
-----
A fix for the deadly stretch of California highway that claimed James Dean's life is nearing completion over six decades after his tragic death.On Sept. 30, 1955, Dean hopped in his new Porsche 550 Spyder and headed up to the coast to Salinas for a road race. Around 5:45 p.m., his sports car passed through the intersection of Highways 41 and 46 in the town of Cholame.
