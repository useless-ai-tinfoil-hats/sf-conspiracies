Bay Area police: Machete-wielding woman arrested after dispute in home
-----
Petaluma police have arrested a woman they say wielded a machete while chasing a person during a dispute.Officers responded to a report of a dispute shortly before 4:30 p.m. Thursday on the 700 block of Marin Way, the Petaluma Police Department said Saturday. While they were en route, the victim reported that the woman, identified as 50-year-old Heather Doyle of Petaluma, had armed herself with a machete and chased the victim into a room, where he barricaded himself.
