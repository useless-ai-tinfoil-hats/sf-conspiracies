Pinole police searching for armed suspect in killing of woman
-----
Police are searching for a Pinole man they said shot and killed a woman Friday morning.Around 8:45 a.m., Jeffrey Kendall, 36, entered a home in the 500 block of Summit Drive and shot a woman inside, killing her, according to Pinole police.
Officials said Kendall was gone by the time officers arrived, but a nearby witness identified him as the suspect. Police later reviewed footage showing Kendall leaving the area in a gray 2013 Hyundai Elantra with the license plate 8BLM036,
