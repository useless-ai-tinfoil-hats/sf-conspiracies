Exclusive: Five of S.F.’s biggest companies create volunteer coalition to clean up downtown
-----
Five of San Francisco's largest employers — Gap Inc., Levi Strauss & Co., Visa, Wells Fargo and JPMorgan Chase — have created a new volunteer coalition with the goal of cleaning up and revitalizing downtown.Their first joint volunteer day was Thursday, with around 250 employees from all five companies gathering at Embarcadero Plaza, not far from each of their local headquarters.
