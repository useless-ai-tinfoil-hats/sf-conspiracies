S.F. man accused of vandalism in Paradise wearing hoodie saying ‘Remember kids graffiti is fun’
-----
Police in Paradise (Butte County) arrested a San Francisco man suspected of vandalizing several businesses with graffiti.Brian Orison, 39, was arrested last week wearing a sweatshirt that read "Remember kids graffiti is fun" that investigators had spotted on surveillance footage at a grocery store he allegedly tagged. According to the Paradise Police Department, officers also found "numerous spray paint cans and a black face mask" during a search of Orison's car.
Orison was booked
