S.F. Symphony names a musically inspiring choral director as season begins
-----
The San Francisco Symphony has named Jenny Wong as the new director of the Symphony Chorus, filling a vacancy created when Ragnar Bohlin resigned the post in 2021.The announcement on Monday, Sept. 18, makes Wong, 36, only the fourth choral conductor to lead the ensemble in its 50-year history.
Wong currently serves as associate artistic director of the Los Angeles Master Chorale, a post she said she plans to retain.
