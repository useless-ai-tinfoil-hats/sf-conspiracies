New Tahoe area recreation trail network to connect Truckee, Nevada City
-----
A 72-mile trail network through Tahoe National Forest should relatively soon allow hikers, bicyclists and equestrians to travel car-free between Truckee and Nevada City.Construction of the Pines to Mines Trail will begin later this year, the U.S. Forest Service announced Thursday. The planned path connects 50 miles of existing trails with 22 miles of newly constructed trails and is slated to be accessible to pedestrians, equestrians, bicyclists and Class 1 e-bike users.
