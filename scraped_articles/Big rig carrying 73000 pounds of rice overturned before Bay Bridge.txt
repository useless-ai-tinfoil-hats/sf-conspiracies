Big rig carrying 73,000 pounds of rice overturned before Bay Bridge
-----
A big rig carrying 73,000 pounds of rice overturned Wednesday morning just before the Bay Bridge in Oakland.The crash occurred at the westbound I-80 on-ramp from West Grand Avenue at approximately 2:13 a.m., Adib Zeid, a spokesperson for the California Highway Patrol, told SFGATE. There were no injuries or hazmat risks as a result of the collision, he said, and none of the rice spilled onto the roadway.
