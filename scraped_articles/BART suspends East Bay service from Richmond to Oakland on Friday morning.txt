BART suspends East Bay service from Richmond to Oakland on Friday morning
-----
Bay Area Rapid Transit has suspended train service on the Red and Orange lines that run from Richmond to MacArthur stations, the transit system announced Friday morning.BART first announced the lines would not be running due to equipment issues shortly after 5 a.m., according to KTVU. At 6:15 a.m., a BART update on X (formerly known as Twitter) said crews continue to make repairs, but the Red and Orange lines still were not running. Extra staff are at each station to provide bus bridge
