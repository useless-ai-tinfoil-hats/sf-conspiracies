Redwood City police say $2,000 wedding dress was stolen from front porch
-----
Redwood City police officials asked for the public's help in finding a person who allegedly stole a wedding dress — among other items — from a front porch, spokespersons said.Police spokespersons shared a video of an alleged thief stealing multiple packages from a Redwood City resident's porch on the 800 block of Adams Street on the evening of April 11.
One of the items was a wedding dress, officials said, valued at $2,000.
