49ers’ sluggish offense without Deebo Samuel raises concerns for NFC title game
-----
So much for all the chatter about Christian McCaffrey vs. Brock Purdy atop the San Francisco 49ers' galaxy of marquee offensive players. As it turns out, maybe Deebo Samuel is the real MVP.Samuel twice left with injuries in the first half of Saturday night's 24-21 divisional playoff victory over Green Bay. He ultimately departed for good in the second quarter, amid an uncommonly sluggish performance from his team's offense.
