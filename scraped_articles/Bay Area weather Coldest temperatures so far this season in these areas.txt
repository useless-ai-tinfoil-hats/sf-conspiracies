Bay Area weather: Coldest temperatures so far this season in these areas
-----
A frigid Friday morning in parts of the Bay Area will kick off a dry, but cold, weekend weather pattern.Rain and snow chances will remain over the Pacific Northwest, leaving the Golden State under an area of high pressure this weekend. Starry nighttime skies will allow inland areas to plummet into the 20s and 30s. Parts of Wine Country and the Santa Clara Valley could experience their coldest temperatures since February.
