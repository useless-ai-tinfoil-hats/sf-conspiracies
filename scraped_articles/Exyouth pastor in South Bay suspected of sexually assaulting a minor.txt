Ex-youth pastor in South Bay suspected of sexually assaulting a minor
-----
A former San Jose youth pastor was arrested on suspicion of sexually assaulting a minor who came forward to detectives when she was an adult, police spokespersons said Monday.San Jose resident Brett Bymaster was arrested last week in Manteca (San Joaquin County), police said. Bymaster previously worked as a youth pastor at the River Church Community, where he is alleged to have sexually assaulted a minor multiple times between 2014 and 2018.
