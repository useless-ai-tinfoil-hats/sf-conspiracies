Pixar hit by layoffs as Disney cuts 175 jobs at Bay Area animation studio
-----
Pixar has initiated long-anticipated layoffs at its Emeryville animation studios.President Jim Morris sent an email to staff on Tuesday, revealing that approximately 14% of the workforce — roughly 175 employees — would be let go as part of a restructuring plan by Disney.
