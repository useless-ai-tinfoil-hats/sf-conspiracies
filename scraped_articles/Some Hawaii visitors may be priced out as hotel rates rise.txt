Some Hawaii visitors may be priced out as hotel rates rise
-----
Hawaii's hotel room rates have increased dramatically in the past five years. It's causing some travelers to look elsewhere, as the number of visitors to Hawaii is declining.The state's average daily hotel room rate increased 35% in April 2024 compared to April 2019, according to the Hawaii Tourism Authority's Hawaii Hotel Performance Report. Oahu increased the least, at about 20%.
