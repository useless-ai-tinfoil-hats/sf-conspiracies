California lawmakers got free tickets while trying to regulate Ticketmaster
-----
A new analysis of campaign finance disclosures from Politico shows that many members of the California Legislature accepted free tickets to concerts, sports games, movie premieres and other events amid efforts to regulate ticket sellers like Ticketmaster.All told, the analysis found that 66 of California's 120 state assemblymembers and senators received more than $30,000 worth of tickets last year.
