This lighter strawberry lemon trifle makes a luscious no-bake dessert
-----
An English dessert for a French holiday? That's the way things unfolded, resulting in no discontent among us. How could there be? There were layers of tangy-sweet lemon curd folded into whipped cream, boozy ripe strawberries, and lemon cookies soaked in that very lemon cream. Does such a blend sound improper for any summer day?
