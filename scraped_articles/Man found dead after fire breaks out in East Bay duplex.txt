Man found dead after fire breaks out in East Bay duplex
-----
A man was found dead after a fire broke out in a Crockett duplex Sunday morning, authorities said.Crews received a report just before 7 a.m. that smoke was coming from the bottom floor of a duplex on Lillian Street in Crockett, said Steve Trotter, a spokesperson for the Crockett-Carquinez Fire Department.
When crews arrived, they encountered "heavy fire conditions" inside the home, Trotter said.
