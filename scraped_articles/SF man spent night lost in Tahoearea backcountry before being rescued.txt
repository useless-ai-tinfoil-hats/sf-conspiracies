SF man spent night lost in Tahoe-area backcountry before being rescued
-----
A San Francisco man was rescued by helicopter from a remote location in Amador County after getting lost while skiing over the weekend, officials said.Mihayl Petkov, 34, was skiing at Kirkwood Mountain Resort on Feb. 10 when he skied "out of bounds off the back side of the mountain," the Amador County Sheriff's Office wrote in a Facebook post. Kirkwood Mountain Resort is located in the town of Kirkwood, south of Lake Tahoe.
