Police arrest 13-year-old in fatal stabbing inside Bay Area Safeway
-----
More than a month after an 18-year-old was fatally stabbed inside a Safeway store, San Leandro police have arrested a suspect in the homicide case.San Leandro police arrested a 13-year-old suspect on May 16 in connection with the April 2 stabbing that occurred inside the Safeway store at 555 Bancroft Ave. in San Leandro, according to a police news release. Zion Gooden, 18, sustained stab wounds to his arm and chest and later died from his injuries at a hospital.
