Third man charged with murder in shooting of Oakland officer
-----
A third man was charged Friday with murder in the fatal shooting of Oakland police officer Tuan Le, the Alameda County District Attorney's Office said Sunday.Marquise Cooper's arraignment is scheduled for Tuesday at Wiley W. Manuel Courthouse in Oakland. Few other details about Cooper's alleged role in Le's death were available Sunday.
The 36-year-old officer was shot after a burglary at a cannabis business on Embarcadero near Fifth Avenue about 4:30 a.m. on Dec. 29, police said.
