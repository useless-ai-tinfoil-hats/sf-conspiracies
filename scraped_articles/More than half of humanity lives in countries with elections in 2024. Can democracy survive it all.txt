More than half of humanity lives in countries with elections in 2024. Can democracy survive it all?
-----
2024 will be the biggest election year in history. 4.2 billion people, or more than half of humanity, live in countries with upcoming elections.Can democracy survive it?
That question may sound cynical. But, in the 21st century, romantic ideas of democracy are dying. The latest global reports show democracy contracting across every world region.
