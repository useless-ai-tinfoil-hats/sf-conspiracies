Fire at S.F. Japantown housing complex leaves person hospitalized
-----
A person was hospitalized Friday following a fire at a 51-unit senior housing complex in San Francisco's Japantown neighborhood, fire officials said.People were evacuated from the complex, in the 1700 block of Fillmore Street, after the fire started and were being evaluated by paramedics.
The cause of the fire was not immediately clear. Anyone in the area of Fillmore Street between Post and Sutter streets was advised to avoid the area as firefighters continued securing the scene.
