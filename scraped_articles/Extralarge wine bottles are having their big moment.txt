Extra-large wine bottles are having their big moment
-----
Wine bottles come in a number of different sizes, but a particular model is having a moment right now: the one-liter.It's bigger than a regular 750ml bottle and smaller than a magnum. But it's stealthy. Sometimes the liter bottle can look and feel so similar to a 750 that you might guzzle it down with some friends and not even realize that you just drank 33% more wine than you'd planned to.
