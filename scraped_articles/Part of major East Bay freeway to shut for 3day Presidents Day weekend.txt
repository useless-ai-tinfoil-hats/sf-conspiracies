Part of major East Bay freeway to shut for 3-day Presidents Day weekend
-----
A section of southbound Interstate 680 in the Pleasanton area will shut down for the long Presidents Day weekend for repaving, according to Caltrans.The road work was originally scheduled to happen on the Martin Luther King Jr. Day holiday weekend in mid-January, but was postponed due to weather.
The shutdown is now planned to take place from 9 p.m. Feb. 16, through 4 a.m. Feb. 20, Caltrans said in a news release.
