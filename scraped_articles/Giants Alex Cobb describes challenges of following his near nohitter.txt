Giants’ Alex Cobb describes ‘challenges’ of following his near no-hitter
-----
SAN DIEGO — Alex Cobb looked forward to Sunday's start not just because he wanted to pitch the San Francisco Giants to victory but because he wanted to see how he responded five days after throwing a career-high 131 pitches in a near no-hitter."I think there's definitely going to be challenges to it," Cobb said Saturday. "It's a little more challenging to throw that many pitches and come back."
