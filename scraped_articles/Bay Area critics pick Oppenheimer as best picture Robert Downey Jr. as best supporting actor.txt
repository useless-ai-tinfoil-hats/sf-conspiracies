Bay Area critics pick ‘Oppenheimer’ as best picture, Robert Downey Jr. as best supporting actor
-----
"Oppenheimer," Christopher Nolan's epic blockbuster about scientist J. Robert Oppenheimer and the development of the atomic bomb, won three awards, including best picture, to top the San Francisco Bay Area Film Critics Circle award winners for 2023.The 44-member group announced its picks on Tuesday, Jan. 9, after final votes were cast on Sunday, Jan. 7.
