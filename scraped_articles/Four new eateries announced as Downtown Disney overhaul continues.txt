Four new eateries announced as Downtown Disney overhaul continues
-----
Four new food options have been announced as part of Downtown Disney's huge renovation, adding even more eateries to the slate of restaurants coming to the outdoor shopping area outside Disneyland.Disneyland announced four kiosks will be part of the new "Parkside Market," a food hall-style building on the west end of Downtown Disney. Although they didn't specify where exactly Parkside Market will go, the former Earl of Sandwich and the long-shuttered AMC theater area may be its future
