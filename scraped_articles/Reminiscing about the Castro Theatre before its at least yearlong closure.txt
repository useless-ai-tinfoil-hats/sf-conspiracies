Reminiscing about the Castro Theatre before its at least year-long closure
-----
Ahead of the yearlong (or longer) closure of the Castro Theatre — which embarks on a renovation project planned to include the controversial removal of the fixed orchestra seating, renovating the building's systems and restoring parts of the interior — Chronicle staff writers Allen Johnson and Tony Bravo got together to share their memories of the 101-year-old movie house.
