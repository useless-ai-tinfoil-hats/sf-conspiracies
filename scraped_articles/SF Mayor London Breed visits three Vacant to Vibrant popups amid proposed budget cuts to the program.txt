SF Mayor London Breed visits three Vacant to Vibrant pop-ups amid proposed budget cuts to the program
-----
San Francisco Mayor London Breed visited three Vacant to Vibrant pop-up businesses in SoMa on Thursday morning, just days after the Board of Supervisors Budget and Legislative Analyst released a report that recommended slashing the program's funding.The report called for cutting $750,000 from the $15,000,000 for the Downtown Core Recovery Project, a program Breed has championed. The second cohort of Vacant to Vibrant launched earlier this month, adding more businesses to the first group
