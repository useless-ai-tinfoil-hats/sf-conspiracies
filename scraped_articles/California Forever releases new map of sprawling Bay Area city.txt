California Forever releases new map of sprawling Bay Area city
-----
The would-be architects of a sprawling Bay Area utopia in rural Solano County have released a new map of the city's borders. The map gives an aerial view of where the city would fit in the larger Bay Area and how close it would be to Travis Air Force Base, a major point of contention while the group was buying up land in the county.The map shows a compact series of neighborhoods anchored by parks between Rio Vista and Fairfield.
