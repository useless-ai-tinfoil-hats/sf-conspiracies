When an Olympic sportswriter watches his daughter compete in the Olympics
-----
PARIS — I first met Wayne Coffey in Lillehammer in 1994. He had an 11-month-old who — with her mom — was in Norway, and I had a 2-year-old back home. I saw Coffey, who wrote for the New York Daily News, at the next eight Olympics. Eventually, he had three kids. I had two and every couple of years, we sat next to each other in Olympic press rooms and traded stories about our kids.I saw Wayne again the other night in the lobby of my hotel in Marseille.
