Shooting injures four people in Vallejo
-----
Four people were shot and injured in Vallejo early Sunday, police said.The shooting occurred about 2:35 a.m. near Monterey Street and Springs Road, not far from Amador Park. The victims were taken to a hospital for treatment and were expected to survive, Sgt. Rashad Hollis said.
No arrests were made as of Sunday morning, Hollis said. No other details were immediately available, such as the victims' ages and genders.
