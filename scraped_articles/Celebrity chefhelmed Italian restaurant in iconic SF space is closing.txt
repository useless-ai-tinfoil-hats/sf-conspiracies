Celebrity chef-helmed Italian restaurant in iconic SF space is closing
-----
After just three-and-a-half years in San Francisco, vegan Italian restaurant Baia is closing."It is with a heavy heart that we announce the closing of BAIA," read a post on the restaurant's Instagram. "We invite you to join us one last time before our last service on March 30th to reminisce and bid farewell. From the bottom of our hearts, thank you for your unwavering support over the years. It has been an honor serving you."
