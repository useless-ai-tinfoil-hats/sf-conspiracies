Fleet Week is over. Here are the best Blue Angels photos.

Greeted with crystal clear skies and hot temperatures, the Blue Angels delighted thousands of San Francisco Bay Area spectators with the acrobatic display during Fleet Week 2023.The annual Fleet Week event brings shiploads of United States Navy sailors, Marines and Coast Guard members, who dock their vessels in the City by the Bay and offer tours of their boats, and give the public a chance to interact with military members as they stroll the streets of San Francisco.
