Supreme Court lets workers sue Uber, Lyft under California law it tried to dismantle
-----
The U.S. Supreme Court on Monday allowed workers in California to join one another and sue their employer for allegedly violating state labor law, two years after the high court seemingly closed the door on such lawsuits.The justices denied review of appeals by ride-hailing companies Uber and Lyft from state appeals court rulings allowing drivers to sue the companies, in the name of the state, for classifying them as independent contractors rather than employees.
