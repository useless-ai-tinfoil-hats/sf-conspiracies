CDC reviews cruise ship in S.F. after passengers, crew get stomach illness
-----
The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention is reviewing protocols on a luxury cruise ship stopped in San Francisco after more than 150 passengers and crew members reported gastrointestinal illnesses.The Queen Victoria, a cruise vessel operated by United Kingdom-based Cunard Line, was in San Francisco on Tuesday and Wednesday as part of a 55-day cruise that began in Hamburg, Germany, according to Cruise Mapper. Records on the website show the ship is heading next to Hawaii.
