Popular Bay Area neighborhood vaults into top 10 most expensive ZIP codes in U.S.
-----
Stinson Beach — where a home owned by the late Sen. Dianne Feinstein was recently previewed at nearly $8.5 million — is now the sixth most expensive ZIP code in the United States, according to an annual ranking by real estate website PropertyShark.It is Stinson Beach's first time in the top 10. Last year it ranked 21st, but its median home sale price jumped from $3.5 million in 2022 to $4.5 million so far this year.
