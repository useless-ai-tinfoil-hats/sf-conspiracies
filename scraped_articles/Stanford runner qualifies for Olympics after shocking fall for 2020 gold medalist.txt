Stanford runner qualifies for Olympics after shocking fall for 2020 gold medalist
-----
Stanford middle-distance runner Juliette Whittaker has the chance to become the United States' newest Olympic gold medalist in the women's 800-meter race after a shocking spill from the reigning champion.Athing Mu won a gold medal in this race during the 2020 Tokyo Games, but she will not make it to Paris this summer. Mu, the fifth-ranked 800-meter runner in the world, fell about a quarter of the way into Monday's Olympic trial race in Eugene, Oregon.
