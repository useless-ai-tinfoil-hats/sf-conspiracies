People loved this San Francisco airline. Why did it disappear?
-----
For 11 years, it was San Francisco's hometown airline, flying travelers across the country in shiny white aircraft with jocular names like "Let there be flight" and "Jefferson Airplane." Purplish-pink mood lighting gave the cabin a fun, nightclub-like glow, and passengers could order food right from their seats instead of waiting for the flight attendant's trolley to roll down the aisle.Virgin America perfectly fit the bold brand that Richard Branson created.
