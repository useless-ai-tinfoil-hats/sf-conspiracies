UC Berkeley’s contentious People’s Park project gets boost as Newsom signs bill into law
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom has signed a state bill into law that amends California's environmental law so housing projects no longer need to study the noise generated from future residents — a big win for UC Berkeley in its quest to build at the historic People's Park.AB1307, by Assembly Member Buffy Wicks, D-Oakland, whose district includes Berkeley, could allow the university's controversial development to move forward.
