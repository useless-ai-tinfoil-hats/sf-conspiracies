Kaiser to pay $47 million to settle claims it unlawfully dumped medical waste, confidential patient info
-----
Kaiser has agreed to pay $47 million to resolve allegations brought by state and local prosecutors that it unlawfully disposed of medical waste and confidential patient information in dumpsters headed to public landfills, California Attorney General Rob Bonta announced Friday.The settlement stems from inspections of 16 Kaiser facilities throughout the state from 2015 to 2017 that found that syringes, prescription drugs and medical tubing containing bodily fluids were improperly disposed
