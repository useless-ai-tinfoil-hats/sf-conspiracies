SF tech company Eventbrite lays off 100 workers as ticket sales fall
-----
Eventbrite, the San Francisco tech company behind a ticketing and events platform, is laying off 100 workers, according to a new financial filing.The company announced the 11% cut in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission dated Wednesday, alongside a middling quarterly earnings report. In the April through June period, Eventbrite turned a slim profit. Ticket sales were down from the year prior, and the company missed its revenue goals. Eventbrite's stock price has dropped
