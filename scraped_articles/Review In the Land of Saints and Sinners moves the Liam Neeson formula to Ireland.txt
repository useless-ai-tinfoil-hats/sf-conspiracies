Review: ‘In the Land of Saints and Sinners’ moves the Liam Neeson formula to Ireland
-----
"In the Land of Saints and Sinners" is a solid Liam Neeson drama that breaks his successful action movie formula enough to be a little different and surprising. At the same time, it retains just enough of the usual Neeson elements to not be disappointing.The new film is set in Ireland (that's something new) with Neeson starring as Finbar, an old hit man (of course), who's getting tired of killing people for a living.
