19-year-old USC student arrested in fatal stabbing on Greek row, police say
-----
A 19-year-old University of Southern California student was arrested Monday night on suspicion of fatally stabbing someone who was allegedly burglarizing cars on Greek row, officials said.Ivan Gallegos, a Los Angeles resident, was booked on suspicion of murder and is being held on $2 million bail, according to jail records. Gallegos is a junior in USC's business administration program, a university spokesperson told SFGATE. He is also a fraternity member, the Los Angeles Times reported.
