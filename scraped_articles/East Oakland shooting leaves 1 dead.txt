East Oakland shooting leaves 1 dead
-----
A man was shot and killed Sunday night in East Oakland, authorities said.Officers responded to a report of a ShotSpotter activation on the 1900 block of Crosby Avenue just after 7 p.m. and found a man who had been shot on the 3500 block of Foothill Boulevard, Oakland police spokesperson Kim Armstead said.
Paramedics pronounced the man dead at the scene, Armstead said. His name was not released pending identification and notification to next of kin.
