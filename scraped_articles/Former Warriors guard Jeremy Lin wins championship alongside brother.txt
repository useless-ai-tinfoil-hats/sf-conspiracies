Former Warriors guard Jeremy Lin wins championship alongside brother
-----
For the second time in his career, former Golden State Warriors guard Jeremy Lin is a basketball champion.Lin clinched the second title of his career — he won an NBA title in 2019 with the Toronto Raptors — on Thursday when the New Taipei Kings defeated the Taoyuan Pauian Pilots in five games, 4-1, to win Taiwan's Plus League championship.
