Antioch police investigate death on regional trail they say arose from fight
-----
Antioch police are investigating a homicide they say arose from a fight on a regional trail.Police received a report around 9:30 a.m. Friday of a fight on the Delta de Anza Regional Trail near the 4100 block of Null Drive, the department said in a statement. The caller told police one person appeared unconscious.
