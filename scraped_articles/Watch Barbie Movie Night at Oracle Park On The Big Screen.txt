Watch Barbie Movie Night at Oracle Park On The Big Screen
-----
The year's biggest movie is coming to the biggest screen in San Francisco on Halloween weekend. In collaboration with Warner Bros. and Mattel, join us on Friday, October 27 for a special celebration of everything Barbie.Guests will enjoy Barbie-inspired photo opportunities, specialty activations and themed food and beverages for purchase. And in true Halloween spirit, attendees are encouraged to come dressed as their favorite Barbie or Ken – or Allan – character. Prizes will be awarded
