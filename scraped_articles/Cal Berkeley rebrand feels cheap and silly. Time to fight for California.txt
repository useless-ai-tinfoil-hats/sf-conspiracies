‘Cal Berkeley’ rebrand feels cheap and silly. Time to fight for California
-----
Long before Chuck Berry christened it "The Promised Land" in 1964, California was one of the world's most enviable brands, a hyper-prosperous state replete with natural beauty, an innovative spirit and a rich and diverse cultural landscape.So why on earth would my esteemed alma mater simply give that away?
There is only one university on God's earth that can proudly declare itself to be the University of California, and that institution is in the midst of an inane identity crisis
