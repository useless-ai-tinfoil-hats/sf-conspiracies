Olga Murray, Marin attorney dedicated to helping poor children of Nepal, dies at 98
-----
While on a retirement trek to the Himalayas in 1987, Sausalito attorney Olga Murray slipped on some mud, broke her leg, and had to be carried by sherpas in a shoulder basket for eight days to reach the hospital. That ordeal changed her life, though it had nothing to do with a broken leg.It had everything to do with a young girl she met in the hospital, who had both legs amputated but was studying her lessons.
