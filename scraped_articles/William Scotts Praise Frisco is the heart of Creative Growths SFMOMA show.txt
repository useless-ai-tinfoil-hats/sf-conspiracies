William Scott’s ‘Praise Frisco’ is the heart of Creative Growth’s SFMOMA show
-----
Watching William Scott put the finishing details on a face in one of his paintings is like watching light enter a room.The cheeks of his figures seem to take on the glint of the sun, with their eyes coming alive as Scott's subtle touches add white to the pupils. The San Francisco painter's signature style of figuration has an almost animated softness, but the knowingness of the expressions on his subjects' faces gives them a sharp humanity.
