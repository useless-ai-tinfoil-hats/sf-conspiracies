Mom, child found dead in North Bay apartment
-----
Police found the bodies of a mother and son in a Santa Rosa apartment Wednesday morning.Around 7:50 a.m., Santa Rosa police said they received a call about two people possibly dead in an apartment on the 500 block of Summerfield Road. Officers responded to the residence and found a woman and child, who was about 10 years old, unresponsive, according to police. Medical personnel pronounced both dead at the scene, police said.
