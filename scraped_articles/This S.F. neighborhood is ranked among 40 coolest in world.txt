This S.F. neighborhood is ranked among 40 ‘coolest’ in world
-----
Most folks who live in or visit San Francisco's Richmond District appreciate its wide variety of restaurants and its proximity to the Presidio, Golden Gate Park and Ocean Beach. But it's rarely been described as cool, except in reference to its breezy, often foggy weather.Nevertheless, Time Out, a global travel publication, has ranked the Richmond District among the "40 coolest neighborhoods in the world" in its sixth annual survey.
