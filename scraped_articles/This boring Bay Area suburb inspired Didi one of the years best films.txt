This 'boring' Bay Area suburb inspired 'Didi,' one of the year's best films
-----
When asked about his upbringing in Fremont, the first place that 30-year-old film director Sean Wang mentions is Denny's. If you grew up in a suburb before the age of smartphones, you probably understand why, and the answer isn't the Grand Slam breakfast."Freshman year, what we were doing was walking to Denny's at 2 a.m.," Wang told SFGATE. "Then we would just sit there, and talk in parking lots, then go climb trees and hang out in playgrounds until three in the morning and just talk.
