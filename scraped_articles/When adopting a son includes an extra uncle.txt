When adopting a son includes an extra uncle

Sometimes we go where we want to go, but most days we go where we need to go.That, Sister Lil would tell me, is because we don't pick the pilgrimage. The pilgrimage picks us. We put one foot in front of the other, hoping that the where of the journey will make sense, but also hoping that the how of the journey will matter. And sometimes we find out the why.
