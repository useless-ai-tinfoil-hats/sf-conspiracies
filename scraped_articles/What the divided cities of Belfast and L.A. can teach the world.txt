What the divided cities of Belfast and L.A. can teach the world
-----
To govern a divided city, you need to balance your remembering with some forgetting.That was one takeaway from a recent public event linking live audiences in two famously divided cities on opposite sides of the world.
One city, Belfast, is so full of physical divisions that the differences between people can feel inescapable. The other, Los Angeles, lacks a shared historical memory and thus manages to forget the depths of its divides.
