Oakland boosts police in commercial corridors to encourage holiday shopping
-----
Oakland will deploy more police officers throughout the city for the next 30 days as part of an effort to combat retail crime in its business corridors and encourage people to shop locally, officials said.Capt. Jeff Thomason of the Oakland Police Department said that starting Thursday, the department will increase foot patrols, traffic enforcement, community resource officers and members of the anti-violence Ceasefire unit throughout the city.
