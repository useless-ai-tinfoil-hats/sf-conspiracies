Jeremy Renner calls Tahoe accident 'one of the greatest things' to happen to him
-----
Eighteen months after Jeremy Renner broke more than 30 bones in a snowplow accident in Tahoe, the actor discussed the near-death experience and what it was like to "see the light" on Monday's episode of "Smartless," a popular podcast hosted by Will Arnett, Jason Bateman and Sean Hayes.The accident occurred around 9 a.m. on New Year's Day 2023 during a heavy snowstorm.
