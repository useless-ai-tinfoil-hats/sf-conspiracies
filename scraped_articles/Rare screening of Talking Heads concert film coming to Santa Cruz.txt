Rare screening of Talking Heads concert film coming to Santa Cruz
-----
The classic Talking Heads concert film, "Stop Making Sense," is celebrating its 40th anniversary this year. To commemorate the milestone, it will get a rare outdoor screening in Santa Cruz.The event, organized by San Francisco's Noise Pop Industries in partnership with UC Santa Cruz, will take place at the university's Quarry Amphitheater on May 18, marking the start of the historic venue's summer season.
