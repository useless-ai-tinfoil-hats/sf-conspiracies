S.F. judge tosses attempted murder charge against alleged Paul Pelosi attacker mid-trial
-----
A San Francisco Superior Court judge dismissed three of the counts against David DePape, who San Francisco prosecutors have accused of brutally attacking House Speaker Emerita Nancy Pelosi's husband, Paul Pelosi, with a hammer in 2022.Judge Harry Dorfman dismissed counts of attempted murder, elder abuse and assault with a deadly weapon after several hours of tense arguments on Wednesday and Thursday.
