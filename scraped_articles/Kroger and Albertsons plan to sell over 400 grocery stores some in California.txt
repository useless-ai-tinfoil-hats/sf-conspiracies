Kroger and Albertsons plan to sell over 400 grocery stores, some in California
-----
Kroger and Albertsons are in talks to sell more than 400 grocery stores to C&S Wholesale Grocers, according to Reuters. Bloomberg was the first to break the news Tuesday, reporting that a potential sale is a move meant to win regulatory approval for Kroger and Albertsons' $25 billion merger.The deal, which could be announced as early as this week, would primarily affect stores in the Pacific Northwest and the mountain states, as well as some in California, Texas, Illinois and the East
