Bay Area man arrested after allegedly stabbing co-worker multiple times during an argument
-----
A San Mateo man was arrested Tuesday after allegedly stabbing a co-worker multiple times during an argument, according to police.Officers responded to the intersection of 36th Avenue and Hacienda Street to find a man suffering from stab wounds in his leg and arm.
Robert Ridenour, 60, was arrested Tuesday at 3:15 p.m. Police learned that during a verbal argument with the co-worker, Ridenour grabbed a knife and stabbed him, police said.
