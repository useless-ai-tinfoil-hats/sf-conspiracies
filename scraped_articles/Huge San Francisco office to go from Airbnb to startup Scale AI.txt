Huge San Francisco office to go from Airbnb to startup Scale AI
-----
Another huge San Francisco office is changing hands from a well-known tech giant to a fast-growing artificial intelligence startup.In October, OpenAI leased two buildings from Uber's massive Mission Bay headquarters. Now, Scale AI has subleased a large chunk of office space from Airbnb's complex in Showplace Square, according to a Tuesday report from the San Francisco Chronicle that was confirmed to SFGATE by an Airbnb spokesperson.
