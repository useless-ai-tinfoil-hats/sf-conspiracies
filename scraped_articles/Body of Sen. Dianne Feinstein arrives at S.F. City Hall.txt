Body of Sen. Dianne Feinstein arrives at S.F. City Hall
-----
Early Wednesday morning, while San Francisco was starting its day, the late Sen. Dianne Feinstein returned to City Hall, the place where she began her rise to political prominence.Feinstein died Friday in her Washington, D.C., home at the age of 90 of natural causes. Her body was returned to San Francisco over the weekend on a military flight.
