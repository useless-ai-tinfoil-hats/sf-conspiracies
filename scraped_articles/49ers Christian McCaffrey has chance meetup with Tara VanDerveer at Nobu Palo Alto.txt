49ers' Christian McCaffrey has chance meetup with Tara VanDerveer at Nobu Palo Alto
-----
One day after Tara VanDerveer broke the all-time coaching wins record in college basketball, she had an admirer walk up to introduce himself at the famous Nobu restaurant and hotel in Palo Alto: 49ers star running back Christian McCaffrey.The chance encounter was posted on the Stanford women's basketball social media accounts, describing the meetup as McCaffrey walking up to introduce himself to VanDerveer, but VanDerveer knowing exactly who the Stanford alum and NFL Pro Bowler was.
