Warriors’ Klay Thompson credits Steve Kerr with changing his ‘whole mindset’
-----
Golden State Warriors guard Klay Thompson entered the New Year in a familiar if uncomfortable position: trying to find his famously feathery shooting touch.Thompson took a step forward in Tuesday night's win over Orlando, scoring 12 of his 15 points in the second half to help the Warriors end their three-game losing streak. Then, afterward, he offered some rare introspection — crediting a conversation with head coach Steve Kerr on Monday for changing his "whole mindset."
