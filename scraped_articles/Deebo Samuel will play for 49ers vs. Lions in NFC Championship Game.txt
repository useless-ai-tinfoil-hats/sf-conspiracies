Deebo Samuel will play for 49ers vs. Lions in NFC Championship Game
-----
San Francisco 49ers wide receiver Deebo Samuel will play in the NFC Championship Game on Sunday against the Lions after he suffered what he revealed was a deep shoulder bruise in last Saturday's divisional-round win against Green Bay.Samuel does not have an injury designation for the game after his status appeared to be in doubt at the beginning of the week. The ultra-physical All-Pro pass-catcher didn't hesitate when asked Friday how he could play with his rugged style against Detroit
