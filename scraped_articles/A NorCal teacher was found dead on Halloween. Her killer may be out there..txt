A NorCal teacher was found dead on Halloween. Her killer may be out there.
-----
The last person to see fifth grade teacher Cherilyn Hawkley on the Friday before Halloween, 1993 was a 10-year-old student. Around 5 p.m., the boy saw Ms. Hawkley leaving Oakhills Elementary School in Granite Bay, headed for her boyfriend's house. When she didn't show, her boyfriend reported her missing.Two days later, on Halloween evening, a Placer County sheriff's deputy making his usual rounds saw an abandoned Dodge Caravan on Elmhurst Drive.
