Amy Sherald, the American artist whose portraits of Michelle Obama and Breonna Taylor have become a part of popular culture, will be the subject of a major exhibition at the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art this fall.The first mid-career survey of the New York artist "Amy Sherald: American Sublime" is scheduled to open Nov. 16. The exhibition will be curated by Sarah Roberts, SFMOMA's head of Painting and Sculpture, and brings together more than 50 paintings from 2007 to the present
