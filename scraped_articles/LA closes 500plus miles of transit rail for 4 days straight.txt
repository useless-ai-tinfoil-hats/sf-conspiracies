LA closes 500-plus miles of transit rail for 4 days straight
-----
Want to hop a train from Los Angeles to Disneyland, or head west from the Inland Empire to the beach? Sorry, not this week. Southern California's largest regional rail network has gone completely dark, and service won't be restored for several more days.The large-scale commuter rail line, known colloquially as Metrolink, connects six different counties from Ventura to San Diego to Riverside.
