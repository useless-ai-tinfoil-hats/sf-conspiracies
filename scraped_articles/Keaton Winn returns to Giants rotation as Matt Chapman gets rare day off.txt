Keaton Winn returns to Giants rotation as Matt Chapman gets rare day off
-----
ARLINGTON, Texas — The San Francisco Giants took a name off its long injured list on Sunday, reinstating pitcher Keaton Winn from the 15-day IL before his start in the series finale against the Texas Rangers.Winn, out since May 17 with a right forearm strain, had been tagging along with the Giants for this two-city road trip and squeezed in a 62-pitch, 3⅔-inning rehab start in the Arizona Complex League on Tuesday.
