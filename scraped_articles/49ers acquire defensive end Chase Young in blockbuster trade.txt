49ers acquire defensive end Chase Young in blockbuster trade
-----
The San Francisco 49ers have acquired defensive end Chase Young from the Washington Commanders in exchange for a third-round pick, according to ESPN's Adam Schefter.The deadline day move brings one of the most heralded edge rushers in the league to a defense that has been criticized in recent weeks for their inability to get to the quarterback.
