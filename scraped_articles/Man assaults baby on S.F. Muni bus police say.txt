Man assaults baby on S.F. Muni bus, police say
-----
An infant was assaulted by a man on a San Francisco Muni bus Friday morning, police said.Officers responded to the area of Van Ness Avenue and O'Farrell Street just after 9 a.m., where they located a father and his infant child, who had been physically assaulted by an unknown man without provocation, police spokespersons said.
Paramedics evaluated the child and found no serious injuries, police said.
The infant's father told police that he and his child were on a Muni bus
