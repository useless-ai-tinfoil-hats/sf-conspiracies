Why 49ers’ Fred Warner is the prototype for today’s elite middle linebacker
-----
Beyond his teammates and opposing offensive coordinators, Fred Warner also has seized the attention of previous San Francisco 49ers linebackers.They understand how the middle linebacker position has evolved throughout NFL history. That spot once was the province of hulking, menacing characters, from Dick Butkus to Mike Singletary to Ray Lewis. Now it demands speed and quickness, as Warner routinely illustrates.
