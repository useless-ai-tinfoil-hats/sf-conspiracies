6 people found dead in California desert, sheriff says
-----
LATEST Jan. 24, 2:13 p.m. The San Bernardino County Sheriff's Department said Wednesday morning that a sixth person's body was found close to where five other people were found dead in a remote area of California's Mojave Desert, the Victor Valley News Group said.A Nixle alert from the sheriff's department said that six people's bodies were found at about 8:16 p.m. by deputies in El Mirage near Lessing Avenue and Shadow Mountain Road.
