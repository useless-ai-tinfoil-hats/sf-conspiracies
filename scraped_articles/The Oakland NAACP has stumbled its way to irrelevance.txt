The Oakland NAACP has stumbled its way to irrelevance
-----
The Oakland NAACP has long been the prominent voice representing the Black community in the city. This has to end.The branch has completely undermined itself by advocating for harsher policing measures that were part of past over-incarceration eras; using right-wing political rhetoric; making controversial allies in the community, and attempting to manipulate public safety narratives with a crime-weary public.
