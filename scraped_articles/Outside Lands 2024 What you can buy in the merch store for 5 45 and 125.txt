Outside Lands 2024: What you can buy in the merch store for $5, $45 and $125
-----
Some of the longest lines at Outside Lands in Golden Gate Park were for merchandise booths, and it wasn't just for tourists who forgot to pack layers. Festival attendees eagerly awaited the annual release of unique products, ranging from beer koozies to trucker caps.The Chronicle was among the first in line at Ranger Dave's Station on Friday, Aug. 9, to inventory the items before they inevitably sell out ahead of Sturgill Simpson's closing set on August 11.
