Why San Francisco isn't saving its most stunning view
-----
There's a shot in the Dashiell Hammett classic "After The Thin Man" that shows famed crime-fighting couple Nick and Nora Charles driving up to their San Francisco home. Their 1935 Packard cruises by an expansive view of the bay and Russian Hill. A half-finished Golden Gate Bridge can even be seen through the fog, from what purports to be their city hilltop driveway, but is actually the newly built Coit Tower parking lot.
