Remains found in Nevada desert in 1980 identified as California man
-----
A body found by a dirt road in the Nevada desert 44 years ago has been identified as a California man.In October 1980, Nye County sheriff's deputies found the remains of an unknown adult male around a mile east of Highway 95, 60 miles south of Tonopah. The victim had suffered numerous gunshot wounds, and cause of death was determined to be homicide.
