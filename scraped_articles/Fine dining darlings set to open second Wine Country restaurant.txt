Fine dining darlings set to open second Wine Country restaurant
-----
The owners of Sonoma's fine dining gem Animo will open a second Wine Country restaurant this fall. Named Golden Bear Station, the American brasserie will move into the former home of barbecue hot spot Tips Roadside in Kenwood.While Animo gained acclaim for its unusual Korean-Basque mashup, Golden Bear Station will lean on hearty European-inspired dishes — like wood-fired Neapolitan pizzas and steak frites — alongside seasonal produce.
