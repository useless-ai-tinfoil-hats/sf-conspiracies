Bay Area rappers drop new anthem ahead of 49ers playoff run
-----
In anticipation of the San Francisco 49ers' playoff run, Bay Area rappers Saweetie and P-Lo have released a new track titled "Do It for the Bay."The song, produced in collaboration with the football franchise, is intended to serve as a rallying call for fans as the team faces off Saturday night against the Green Bay Packers in the NFL divisional round playoffs at Levi's Stadium in Santa Clara.
