S.F. police arrest suspect in fatal Market Street stabbing
-----
A man was arrested on suspicion of stabbing another man to death this week in San Francisco, police said Friday.San Francisco police officers responded to the 1800 block of Market Street early Thursday and found a man suffering from a stab wound. Despite being transported to the hospital, he died of his injuries.
The victim was stabbed just before 1 a.m. on Mint Hill, near a stretch of Market Street that intersects with Laguna, Octavia, Pearl and Guerrero streets.
