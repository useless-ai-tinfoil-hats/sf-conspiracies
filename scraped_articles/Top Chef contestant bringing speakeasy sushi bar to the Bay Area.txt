‘Top Chef’ contestant bringing speakeasy sushi bar to the Bay Area
-----
A buzzy, once Michelin-starred sushi restaurant from Santa Barbara County is opening its first location in the Bay Area.Sushi by Scratch Restaurants will bring its speakeasy-style omakase to the Matheson in Healdsburg on Dec. 1. There will be 17 courses of nigiri ($185), like albacore wrapped in sake-soaked nori, served in a reservations-only back room in the Healdsburg restaurant. The owners describe their sushi as "new wave," with dishes like roasted bone marrow nigiri and hamachi
