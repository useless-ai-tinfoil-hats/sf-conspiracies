S.F. residents applaud calls for aggressive policing as shopkeeper's death goes unsolved
-----
Richmond District residents cheered calls for more aggressive policing and prosecution at a Thursday town hall with San Francisco Police Chief Bill Scott and District Attorney Brooke Jenkins.The public safety meeting was one of several the pair have planned around the city, but it came at a particularly poignant time for the Richmond District.
