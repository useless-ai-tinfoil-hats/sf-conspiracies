Houseboat stumbles onto body of missing man in Sacramento park
-----
A houseboat that scraped a car submerged in Northern California park uncovered the body of a missing person, officials said.Last week, a houseboat driver told Sacramento County Regional Parks that they had struck something near the Discovery Park boat launch. Discovery Park is located at the confluence of the Sacramento and American rivers, just north of Old Sacramento.
