Plan to change Oakland International Airport’s name slammed by SFO, S.F. officials and airline
-----
San Francisco International Airport leaders, elected officials and at least one airline are pushing back on a plan to change the name of Oakland International Airport.The name under consideration? The San Francisco Bay Oakland International Airport. The airport's code — OAK — and visual brand would not change, according to officials with the Port of Oakland, which runs the East Bay airport.
