Investigation finds Bay Area school officials slow to act after teacher pushed antisemitic conspiracies
-----
Hayward school administrators failed to adequately respond to complaints about a teacher using antisemitic materials in his high school English classes, according to the findings of a six-month outside investigation into the incident.Three administrators at Mount Eden High School denied they failed to properly address concerns raised by several teachers at the school starting in December 2022, but "the evidence contradicted their accounts," Hayward Unified Superintendent Jason Reimann
