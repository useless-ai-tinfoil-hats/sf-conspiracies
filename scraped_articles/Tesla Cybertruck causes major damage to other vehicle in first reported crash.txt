Tesla Cybertruck causes ‘major damage’ to other vehicle in first reported crash
-----
A Toyota Corolla crashed into a Tesla Cybertruck while driving up the Peninsula on Thursday afternoon, marking the first reported collision involving the controversial electric vehicle.The incident occurred just after 2 p.m. Thursday on Highway 35 south of Page Mill Road, a California Highway Patrol spokesperson said. A Toyota Corolla driving south crossed into the wrong side of traffic, crashing into the Tesla Cybertruck headed north, according to the agency.
