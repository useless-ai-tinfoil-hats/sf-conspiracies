Robots cook Korean barbecue while this Bay Area food truck is driving
-----
Pork slices, shrimp and vegetables tumble inside a pot with the tempo of a dryer at the end of a cycle. A couple of robot arm twists and turns later, the machine drops meat and vegetables into a bowl, a plume of steam trailing behind it.The robot didn't cook this meal at a futuristic new restaurant, but inside a box truck summoned through a smartphone app in Foster City.
