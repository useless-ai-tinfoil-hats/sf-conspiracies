S.F. financial tech company valued at $855 million shuts down, lays off entire staff
-----
San Francisco financial technology company Tally, which specialized in helping clients manage and pay off credit card debt, has ceased operations after depleting its funds."This was not the outcome we had hoped for, but after exploring all options, we were unable to secure the necessary funding to continue our operations," founder and CEO Jason Brown announced in a LinkedIn post on Monday, calling the decision "sad and difficult."
