'Curb Your Enthusiasm': How to watch the 12th and final season
-----
"Curb Your Enthusiasm," after 25 years of comedy, has us now trying to contain our disappointment, as HBO announced the 12th and final season of its runaway hit this week.Beginning Sunday, Feb. 4, fans and stans of Larry David's HBO original series get 10 more episodes in the lead-up to the series finale on April 7. New episodes drop once per week — Sundays at 10 p.m. PST and EST — as the series wraps up its astonishing decades-long run.
