Campolindo's Pinkie Schnayer followed a unique path to state track and field meet
-----
The name on her birth certificate is Avery Elizabeth Schnayer but everyone around Campolindo High School in Moraga knows and loves her as Pinkie.Tagged by older brothers Colby and Dalen, then 6 and 4, when she was born, the nickname has stuck even though everything about the 6-foot senior is larger than life, including her path to emerge as California's top shot putter.
"There's only one Pinkie," said Campolindo football coach Kyle Macy.
