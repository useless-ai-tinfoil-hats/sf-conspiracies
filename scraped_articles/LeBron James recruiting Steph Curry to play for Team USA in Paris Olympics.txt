LeBron James recruiting Steph Curry to play for Team USA in Paris Olympics
-----
LeBron James has been recruiting Golden State Warriors guard Stephen Curry to participate at the 2024 Summer Olympics in Paris, a league source confirmed with the Chronicle on Monday morning.The Athletic initially reported that Curry is "prepared to commit" to his first career Olympics. He won two World Cups with the United States team, in 2010 and 2014, but has turned down previous opportunities to play in the Olympics because he wanted to prioritize rest ahead of the NBA regular
