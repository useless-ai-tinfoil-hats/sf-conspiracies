California woman who walked to a gas station 5 weeks ago still missing
-----
A 76-year-old California woman has been missing under mysterious circumstances for at least 5 weeks. Detectives are now seeking the public's help for any information on the whereabouts of Judy Ann Klein, of the Northern California town of Upper Lake near Clear Lake."Judy walked away from her vehicle in Upper Lake, CA, around December 26, 2023, and hasn't been seen or heard from since," Lauren Berlinn, public information officer for the Lake County Sheriff's Office, told SFGATE over email
