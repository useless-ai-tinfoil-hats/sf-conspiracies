Taco empire closes almost 50 restaurants, including its last in the Bay Area
-----
Casual Mexican and fish taco chain Rubio's Coastal Grill will no longer operate in the Bay Area.The company headquartered in Carlsbad (San Diego County) abruptly closed 48 stores, around one third of its total franchises, on Friday. This included two outlets in the South Bay, a company spokesperson told the Chronicle. A company statement said it cut "underperforming" locations and cited the "rising cost of doing business in California."
