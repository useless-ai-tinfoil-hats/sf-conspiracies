Swanky SF Victorian-themed bar and restaurant is closing
-----
After eight years in the Marina District, Victorian-themed bar and restaurant the Dorian is set to serve its last martini next month. The restaurant plans to host a final party and then close at the end of October before it is transferred to new ownership, reported Eater."We will certainly miss the Dorian, and it will always hold a uniquely special place in our hearts," read a post from the Dorian on Facebook.
