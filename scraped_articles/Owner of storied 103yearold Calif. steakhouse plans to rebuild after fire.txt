Owner of storied 103-year-old Calif. steakhouse plans to rebuild after fire
-----
After an early-morning fire scorched the storied Pacific Dining Car restaurant, plans to restore the 103-year-old Los Angeles steakhouse are underway.On Saturday, Los Angeles Fire Department firefighters arrived at 1310 W. Sixth St. at around 12:14 a.m. to battle flames consuming the exterior part of the building. After nearly an hour, 75 firefighters put the flames out, according to the fire incident report. An investigation for the cause of the fire is underway.
