For someone famous for his grand plans and his very big ideas, Walt Disney sure loved tiny things.The multi-hyphenate who pioneered the animated motion picture and the theme park as we know it today was famously obsessed with trains, but he had another passion, one which, in an indirect way, inspired Disneyland. The man was obsessed — obsessed! — with miniatures. If it was a microcosm of real life, Disney wanted it in his possession.
