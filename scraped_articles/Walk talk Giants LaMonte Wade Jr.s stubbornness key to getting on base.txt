Walk talk: Giants' LaMonte Wade Jr.'s stubbornness key to getting on base
-----
DENVER — From LaMonte Wade Jr.'s first at-bat with the San Francisco Giants in 2021, hitting coach Justin Viele knew he would be fun to watch."He just loves competing in the at-bat," Viele said. "That's something I thought from the moment I saw him — he locks in and he's just not letting the pitcher win."
