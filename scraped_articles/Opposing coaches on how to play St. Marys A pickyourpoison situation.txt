Opposing coaches on how to play St. Mary’s: ‘A pick-your-poison situation’
-----
Few people know how good a team is better than do the opposing head coaches who face that team. So to help put in perspective what No. 17 St. Mary's has done and is doing this season, the two men whose teams lost in Moraga last week provide plenty of insight.The Gaels are "arguably one of the most physical teams in the country — and that's a credit to them," USF head coach Chris Gerlufsen said after St. Mary's beat the Dons 70-66 on Feb. 20. "They've been doing that to teams all year."
