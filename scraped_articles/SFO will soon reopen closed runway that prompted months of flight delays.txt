SFO will soon reopen closed runway that prompted months of flight delays
-----
After six months of flight delays, San Francisco International Airport on Friday will reopen a runway that has been under construction since January, the airport confirmed.Runway 28 Left, one of four runways at the airport, was closed starting Jan. 16 for a taxiway improvement project that would allow planes to exit the runway more safely after landing, airport officials said. The project originally was scheduled to end by July 1.
Airlines reduced flights in and out of the airport
