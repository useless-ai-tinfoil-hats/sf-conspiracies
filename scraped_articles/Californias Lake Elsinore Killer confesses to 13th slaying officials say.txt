California's 'Lake Elsinore Killer' confesses to 13th slaying, officials say
-----
Riverside County's most infamous serial killer has confessed to another slaying, officials said, bringing resolution to the long-cold homicide of a 19-year-old. William Lester Suff, now 73 and incarcerated on San Quentin's death row, was convicted of killing 12 women in the 1980s and 1990s but is suspected of many more killings.Suff was born in California in 1950 and moved to Texas with his young family when he was stationed at Carswell Air Force Base in Fort Worth.
