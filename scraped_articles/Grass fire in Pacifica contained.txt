Grass fire in Pacifica contained
-----
A vegetation fire that erupted in Pacifica on Sunday filled the air with smoke but did not threaten homes, fire officials said.Firefighters with the North County Fire Authority, which serves Brisbane, Daly City and Pacifica responded to the scene of a grass fire between Hickey Boulevard and Inverness Drive, the agency said on X, formerly Twitter, around 5 p.m.
The wildfire was contained and no structures were threatened as of 5:30 p.m., officials said.
