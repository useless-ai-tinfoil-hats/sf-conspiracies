A year after Half Moon Bay mass shooting, farmworkers still waiting for better housing
-----
Painful memories flooded back to Juan Flores-Lopez in recent weeks as the tight-knit coastal community of Half Moon Bay prepared to commemorate the one-year anniversary of the mass shooting that killed seven fellow farmworkers.He worked at California Terra Garden, the first of two farms where police said a disgruntled fellow employee opened fire, killing four. "Unfortunately, I saw everything," Flores-Lopez said.
