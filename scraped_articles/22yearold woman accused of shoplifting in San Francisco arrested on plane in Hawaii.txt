22-year-old woman accused of shoplifting in San Francisco arrested on plane in Hawaii
-----
A woman accused of stealing merchandise at San Francisco stores, including downtown luxury retailers, was arrested on a plane in Hawaii on Valentine's Day, officials said.The 22-year-old SF resident has a history with organized retail crime in the city, the San Francisco Police Department said. She "was arrested by US Marshalls for multiple outstanding warrants as she exited the plane" that had landed in Honolulu, the department said in a news released issued Tuesday.
