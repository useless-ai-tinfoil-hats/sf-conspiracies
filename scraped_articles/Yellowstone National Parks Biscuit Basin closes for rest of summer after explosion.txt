Yellowstone National Park's Biscuit Basin closes for rest of summer after explosion
-----
Yellowstone National Park's popular Biscuit Basin will be closed for the rest of the summer following a dramatic hydrothermal explosion last week. The explosion happened on July 23 just before 10 a.m., sending steam and debris "hundreds of feet" in the air and destroying a nearby boardwalk, the National Park Service said in a news release.Some visitors captured the intense explosion in social media videos, which show visitors running from a tall, dark burst of steam. No one was injured
