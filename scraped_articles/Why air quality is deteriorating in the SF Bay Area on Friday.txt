Why air quality is deteriorating in the SF Bay Area on Friday
-----
Air quality in the San Francisco Bay Area deteriorated Friday afternoon due to a prescribed burn in Marin County. Similar conditions occurred on Thursday, according to the Bay Area Air Quality Management District.On Friday just before 3 p.m., air quality levels were moderate across a large swath of the region, according to the federally run AirNow website that tracks air quality across the country. The popular PurpleAir website showed the majority of the region with moderate air quality
