As Warriors near return, a lot rides on Draymond Green and Steph Curry
-----
They need to be different coming out of this. Any kind of change would be welcome for a Golden State Warriors team reeling from the sudden, shocking death of assistant coach Dejan Milojevic. If they return to the court bearing no resemblance to the team we've witnessed this month, all the better.Even the All-Star break doesn't last this long: Eight days between games, a proper spell for mourning and, eventually, the restoration of image.
