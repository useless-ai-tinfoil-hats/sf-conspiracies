Shots fired near Albany shopping area, police say
-----
Berkeley and Albany police are responding to an active crime scene near the Toyota Service Center in Albany, officials said just before noon.Two people were reportedly shot in the area of Eastshore Highway between Buchanan and Harrison streets, according to a tweet from KTVU-TV crime reporter Henry Lee.
Police reported shots were fired and said an individual was down after officers went inside, KTVU-TV said.
Officials said to avoid the area until further notice.
