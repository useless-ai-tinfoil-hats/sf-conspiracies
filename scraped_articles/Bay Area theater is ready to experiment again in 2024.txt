Bay Area theater is ready to experiment again in 2024
-----
A truism of late-pandemic theater is that companies are relying on famous titles and safer bets to entice audiences back. The familiar can be wonderful, of course — plays and musicals usually become classics for a reason — but it's not always great at prodding the art form forward, at quenching a zeitgeisty thirst.So the spate of new and under-the-radar plays on the books for the new year is especially heartening, suggesting that theater might be in a new phase: ready to experiment
