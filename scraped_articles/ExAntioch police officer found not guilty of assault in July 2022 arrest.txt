Ex-Antioch police officer found not guilty of assault in July 2022 arrest
-----
A Contra Costa County jury found a former Antioch police officer not guilty Thursday of assault in a July 2022 incident in which prosecutors said he kicked, punched and kneed a man who was handcuffed.Jurors deliberated for less than a day before they reached the verdict in the trial of Matthew Nutt, 29, who faced a misdemeanor count of assault under the color of authority. Nutt faced up to a year in county jail.
