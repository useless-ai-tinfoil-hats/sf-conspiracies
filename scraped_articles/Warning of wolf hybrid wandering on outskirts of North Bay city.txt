Warning of wolf hybrid wandering on outskirts of North Bay city
-----
Sebastopol authorities are warning residents of a wolf hybrid spotted on the outskirts of the city Sunday morning.Officials from the state's department of Fish and Wildlife alerted Sebastopol police of a wolf hybrid seen "heading south of the Highway 12 bridge on the eastern edge of town," the Sebastopol Police Department said on Facebook.
Several agencies, including the California Highway Patrol and North Bay Animal Services, are searching for the animal.
