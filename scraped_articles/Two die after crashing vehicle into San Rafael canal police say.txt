Two die after crashing vehicle into San Rafael canal, police say
-----
Two men died Saturday morning after driving their vehicle into a canal in San Rafael, according to the San Rafael Police Department.At about 7 a.m. Saturday, a witness saw a vehicle driving around 70 miles per hour plunge into the water at the end of the 500 block of Canal Street, officials said. When police and fire personnel arrived, they found skid marks in the gravel and a vehicle submerged in the water.
