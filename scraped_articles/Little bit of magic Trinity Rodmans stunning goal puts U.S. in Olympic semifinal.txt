‘Little bit of magic’: Trinity Rodman’s stunning goal puts U.S. in Olympic semifinal
-----
PARIS — It wasn't the prettiest of games.But it was the prettiest of goals.
Trinity Rodman put the U.S. women's team into the Olympic semifinals with a dazzling finish in the 107th minute of the quarterfinal against Japan on Saturday afternoon, assuring that this group will go further than it did last summer at the Women's World Cup. The Americans will face Germany on Tuesday for a trip to the gold-medal match.
