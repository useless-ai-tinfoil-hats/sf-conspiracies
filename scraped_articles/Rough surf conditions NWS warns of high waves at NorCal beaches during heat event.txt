'Rough surf conditions': NWS warns of high waves at NorCal beaches during heat event
-----
Various coastal areas in the Bay Area are under a beach hazards statement, even as temperatures across the region soar in a major heat wave.The National Weather Service is urging the public to be careful of high, dangerous waves along San Francisco, Point Reyes National Seashore, the San Francisco Peninsula coast, southern Monterey Bay and the Big Sur coast. The hazards statement is in effect from Wednesday morning to Thursday evening.
