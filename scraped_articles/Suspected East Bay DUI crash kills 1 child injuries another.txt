Suspected East Bay DUI crash kills 1 child, injuries another
-----
A car crash on an East Bay highway killed a 5-year-old child and severely injured another child, authorities said.The driver was arrested on charges of manslaughter and suspicion of driving under the influence, the California Highway Patrol said.
The crash happened Sunday just after 12 a.m. on the westbound lanes of Highway 4 near Morello Avenue, CHP officials said.
