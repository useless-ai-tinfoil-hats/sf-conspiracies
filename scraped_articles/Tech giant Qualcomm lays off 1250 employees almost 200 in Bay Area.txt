Tech giant Qualcomm lays off 1,250 employees, almost 200 in Bay Area
-----
Qualcomm, the tech giant known best for its dominance of the smartphone chip industry, is laying off 1,258 California workers, according to documents submitted to the California Employment Development Department. The documents — known as WARN notices after the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act, which mandates companies give 60 days' notice to employees before they are let go — were filed Wednesday.Overall, Qualcomm will deliver pink slips to 194 workers in the Bay Area
