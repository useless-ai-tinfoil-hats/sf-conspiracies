El Cerrito enters football season mourning legendary coach Frank Milo
-----
If El Cerrito eighth-year football coach Steve Rincon flashes a thumbs-up overhead Friday, it won't be a directive to quarterback Jonathan Craft or a signal to the Gauchos' speedy receivers Gavin Thompson, Savion Bandy and Gary Youngblood.It will be a thank you to the heavens to late coach Frank Milo, who died Aug. 1 at the age of 83.
"Maybe he'll be looking down on us and we'll get some balls to bounce our way," Rincon said.
