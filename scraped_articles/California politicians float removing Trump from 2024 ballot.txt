California politicians float removing Trump from 2024 ballot
-----
A group of Democratic lawmakers in California have asked state Attorney General Rob Bonta to expeditiously seek a court ruling on whether Donald Trump should be removed from California's March primary ballot.In a letter obtained by Politico and the New York Times, nine California lawmakers reportedly wrote to Bonta that he as state attorney general is "uniquely positioned to proactively seek the court's opinion to confirm Mr. Trump's inability to hold office."
