Warriors’ defense has fallen off in March, and Steve Kerr doesn’t know why
-----
The Golden State Warriors in February were the NBA's fifth-rated defensive team.Through their first 10 games in March, however, there's been some slippage and they've rated 23rd.
"It's not always clear (why)," head coach Steve Kerr said Friday before the Warriors played the Indiana Pacers at Chase Center.
"There are some games where we just appear sharper."
