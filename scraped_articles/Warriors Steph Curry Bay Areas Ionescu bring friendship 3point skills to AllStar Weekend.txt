Warriors’ Steph Curry, Bay Area’s Ionescu bring friendship, 3-point skills to All-Star Weekend
-----
Stephen Curry and Sabrina Ionescu shared a phone call the night she shot the net off the rim — a call peppered with a steady stream of shared laughter. Having won the WNBA's 3-point shootout in record fashion during its All-Star Weekend in Las Vegas, Ionescu processed her thoughts with the only person she knew who could possibly relate.Who else but Curry knows what it feels like? The feeling she couldn't explain Tuesday to reporters — for which Curry needed not an iota of explanation.
