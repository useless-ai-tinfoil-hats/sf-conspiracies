S.F. Mayor Breed needed a political win on Election Day. She got one
-----
San Francisco Mayor London Breed needed a political victory to show strength as she heads into a tough reelection fight that polling shows she could lose.She got one on Tuesday, when voters rallied behind at least two of the three measures she placed on the city's March ballot. Propositions E and F — both tailored to address major public policy challenges — passed. Proposition C was ahead and likely to pass.
