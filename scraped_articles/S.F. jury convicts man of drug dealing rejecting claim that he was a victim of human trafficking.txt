S.F. jury convicts man of drug dealing, rejecting claim that he was a victim of human trafficking
-----
A San Francisco jury on Thursday found a 20-year-old man guilty of seven charges of drug dealing, rejecting the defendant's claim that he was a victim of human trafficking and therefore not responsible for the crimes.The verdict marks the first time jurors have come to a unanimous decision on what has become an increasingly common defense argument in San Francisco's drug-dealing cases.
