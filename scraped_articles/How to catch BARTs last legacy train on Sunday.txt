How to catch BART’s last ‘legacy train’ on Sunday
-----
The death of BART's "legacy fleet" now has a time and a date.BART will operate its last legacy train on the Red Line leaving Millbrae for Richmond at 7:39 p.m. Sunday, the agency announced Saturday night.
BART's so-called "legacy fleet" contains more than 650 cars stretching from the 1990s back to 1972, when the agency first opened, according to their website.
