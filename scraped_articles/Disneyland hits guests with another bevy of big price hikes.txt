Disneyland hits guests with another bevy of big price hikes
-----
It's a tale as old as time: Price increases are once again hitting guests visiting the Disneyland Resort.On Wednesday, Disney announced a slew of increases that will impact everything from ticket prices to annual passes to the paid skip-the-line service Genie Plus. Exactly one year ago, SFGATE reported on Disneyland's last round of price hikes.
