Tahoe blizzard: ‘Life-threatening’ conditions expected Saturday
-----
An intense winter storm blasting the Tahoe region with snow and lashing winds was only expected to worsen Saturday.Blizzard warnings were in place through Sunday for much of the Sierra Nevada, including Truckee, South Lake Tahoe, Mammoth Lakes and Grass Valley, with heavy snow and high winds expected to create dangerous conditions.
