Giant, historic California building engulfed in flames
-----
One of the largest free-standing wooden structures on Earth was destroyed by fire Tuesday morning.A historic 17-story hangar at the former Tustin Air Base in Southern California, built to house blimps for use during World War II, is still sending plumes of smoke into the Orange County sky.
