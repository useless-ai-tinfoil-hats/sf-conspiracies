S.F. native Devin Haney’s next stop on path to boxing stardom: Chase Center
-----
Devin Haney set down his iced coffee as he struggled to remember the name of his new weight class."I'm the king of the lightweight division, and I'm the soon-to-be king of the, um … " Haney said in mid-October, pausing as he turned toward his father, Bill. "Is it super welterweight? Wait, what's" 140 pounds?
Reminded that he's entering the super-lightweight division, Devin nodded as he tried to imagine Saturday night's World Boxing Council championship fight against Regis Prograis.
