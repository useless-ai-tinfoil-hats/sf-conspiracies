Home prices grew the fastest in 2023 in these small California cities
-----
The housing market in California slowed down substantially in 2023, as interest rates soared to a 23-year high and inventory was sluggish across the state. Home prices weren't increasing like they did during the first few years of the pandemic, especially in the Bay Area, and in some places price growth even declined.
