Two turtles, three birds rescued from East Bay house fire
-----
Alameda County firefighters rescued two pet turtles and three birds from a house fire in Castro Valley on Friday night.Officials said the fire started just before 5:30 p.m. Friday at a house in the 19000 block of Garrison Avenue. In total, 21 firefighters responded to the fire and put it out within 20 minutes, according to the fire department.
In the process of fighting the fire, officials rescued three pet birds and two turtles from the home, officials said.
