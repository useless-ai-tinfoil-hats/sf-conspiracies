Stephen Vogt’s managerial career off and rolling, as Guardians rout A’s
-----
Stephen Vogt's winning streak to begin his major-league managerial career likely won't match the hitless streak to begin his big-league playing career — but it remained theoretically possible Saturday.The former Oakland Athletics catcher improved to 3-0 in charge of the Cleveland Guardians, who racked up 15 hits and six stolen bases in a 12-3 romp on a brisk, breezy Saturday afternoon at the Coliseum. The announced attendance was 5,425.
