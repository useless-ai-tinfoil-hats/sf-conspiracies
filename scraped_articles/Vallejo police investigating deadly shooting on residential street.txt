Vallejo police investigating deadly shooting on residential street
-----
A man was killed in a shooting Tuesday in Vallejo, authorities said.Officers were called to the 100 block of Marquette Avenue just before 4:30 p.m. for a reported shooting, according to the Vallejo Police Department. They found a man suffering from at least one gunshot wound and provided medical aid until paramedics arrived.
