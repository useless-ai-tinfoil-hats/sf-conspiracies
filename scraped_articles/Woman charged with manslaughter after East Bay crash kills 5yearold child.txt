Woman charged with manslaughter after East Bay crash kills 5-year-old child
-----
The Contra Costa County District Attorney's Office has charged a woman with vehicular manslaughter and driving under the influence after she crashed into a tree in Martinez, killing a 5-year-old child.Shakela Janey Dominick, 32, was arrested just after midnight on Sunday after she crashed her vehicle while allegedly driving under the influence, killing a 5-year-old passenger and seriously injuring an 11-year-old passenger, according to the California Highway Patrol.
