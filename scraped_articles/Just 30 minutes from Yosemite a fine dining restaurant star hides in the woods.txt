Just 30 minutes from Yosemite, a fine dining restaurant star hides in the woods
-----
High-end restaurants reveal their luxuries in different ways. Most simply drop expensive food in front of diners; others rely on heavily tufted chairs and rare artwork; some famously go to great lengths to make magical social moments happen inside the dining room.At the Elderberry House, they put a loose piece of tree bark on the table.
