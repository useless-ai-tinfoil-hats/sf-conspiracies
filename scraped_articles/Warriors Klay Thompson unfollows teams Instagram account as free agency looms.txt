Warriors’ Klay Thompson unfollows team’s Instagram account as free agency looms
-----
Klay Thompson isn't following the Golden State Warriors on Instagram anymore.It's worth remembering it's possible he won't play basketball for them anymore as well.
An unrestricted free agent when the league year ends, Thompson, social media users noticed, wasn't following the franchise Friday afternoon, having cleansed his account of Warriors content dating to April 2022.
