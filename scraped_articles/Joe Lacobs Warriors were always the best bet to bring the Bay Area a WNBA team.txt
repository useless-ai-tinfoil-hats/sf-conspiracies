Joe Lacob’s Warriors were always the best bet to bring the Bay Area a WNBA team
-----
The Golden State Warriors, unless something goes unexpectedly awry, will be awarded a WNBA team sometime within the next 10 days.This is cause for celebration. But not for surprise.
The Bay Area has long been a prime market for WNBA expansion, and there has been understandable frustration that the mighty Warriors, while always paying lip service to the idea of investing in the WNBA, had not seemed particularly proactive.
