LinkedIn lays off 668 workers, slashes S.F. office space
-----
LinkedIn is laying off 668 workers, joining a new flurry of downsizings hitting the tech industry.The Sunnyvale-based professional social network will cut around 4% of its staff across engineering, product, talent and finance teams, the company said Monday.
