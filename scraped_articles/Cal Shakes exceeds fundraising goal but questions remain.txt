Cal Shakes exceeds fundraising goal, but questions remain
-----
California Shakespeare Theater will live to mount another show.The beloved Orinda company has exceeded the $350,000 goal of the last-minute fundraising campaign it started to save "As You Like It," Executive Director Clive Worsley announced Monday, Aug. 5. Now Shakespeare's romantic comedy, the first Cal Shakes show to play at the Bruns Amphitheater in two years, can proceed next month as planned.
