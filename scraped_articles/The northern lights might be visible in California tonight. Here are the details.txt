The northern lights might be visible in California tonight. Here are the details
-----
It's just possible that Northern Californians could be in for a glimpse of the northern lights after midnight tonight, as solar activity sends the phenomenon further south than usual.If visible, northern Californians would likely only see a reddish glow in the sky, according to Travis Rector, a professor of astronomy and physics at the University of Alaska at Anchorage. He said that the early hours of Friday will be the best chance of getting a view.
