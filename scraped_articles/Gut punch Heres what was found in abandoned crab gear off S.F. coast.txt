‘Gut punch’: Here’s what was found in abandoned crab gear off S.F. coast
-----
A highly endangered turtle from a species that produces the world's largest turtles has turned up dead in abandoned crab gear off the San Francisco coast, sparking a renewed spotlight on Dungeness crab fishing shortly before the commercial season is due to start.The adult turtle was entangled and killed in the abandoned gear near the Farallon Islands, the California Department of Fish and Wildlife confirmed late Monday.
