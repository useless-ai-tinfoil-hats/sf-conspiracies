Infant killed in DUI crash near Sea Ranch
-----
An infant was killed and a 19-year-old driver was arrested on suspicion of driving under the influence following a single-car crash near Sea Ranch on Saturday, authorities said.In addition to the 1-year-old infant, a 4-year-old passenger was also injured in the crash at 10:25 p.m. on Highway 1, north of Leeward Spur, in the Sea Ranch area, according to California Highway Patrol's Santa Rosa division.
