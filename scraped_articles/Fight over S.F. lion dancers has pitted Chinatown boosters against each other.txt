Fight over S.F. lion dancers has pitted Chinatown boosters against each other
-----
Ruby Cai's daughter was just a baby when she first saw lion dancers in San Francisco's Chinatown.Cai remembered standing on Grant Avenue, baby in her arms, watching lion dancers from a local troupe, Lion Dance ME, perform stunts across 6-foot-high metal discs. Cai was inspired to sign up her daughter, Anthea Zhang, now 7, to learn lion dancing as soon as she was old enough.
