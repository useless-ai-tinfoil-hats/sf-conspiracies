Bay Area-based Sony division lays off 900 workers, CEO says change was 'inevitable'
-----
Video game giant Sony Interactive Entertainment is laying off 900 workers, the San Mateo-based company announced on Tuesday.The layoffs will hit 8% of SIE's workforce, CEO Jim Ryan wrote in a Tuesday email to staff that's now published on the company's website. SIE is Japanese tech giant Sony's video game arm; the subsidiary develops and manages the popular PlayStation gaming consoles and their accompanying products.
