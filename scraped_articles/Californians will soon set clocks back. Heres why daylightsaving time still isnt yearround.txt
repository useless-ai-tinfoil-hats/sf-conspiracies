Californians will soon set clocks back. Here’s why daylight-saving time still isn’t year-round
-----
Californians will set their clocks back an hour this weekend, bringing an end to daylight-saving time — and the biannual time change ritual isn't likely to change anytime soon.Daylight-saving time will end at 2 a.m. on Sunday, Nov. 5, when clocks "fall back" an hour to standard time. Things will stay that way until the second Sunday in March, when we "spring forward" again.
