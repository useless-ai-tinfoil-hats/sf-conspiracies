Bay Area whooping cough outbreak reported by county health officials
-----
Marin County is experiencing a significant increase in whooping cough cases, primarily centered on one of its largest high schools.The county's health officer, Matt Willis, issued an advisory last week indicating that out of the 93 cases recorded in the region since mid-December, 65 have occurred at Tamalpais High School in Mill Valley.
