These Bay Area counties saw the biggest increase in 'stale' home listings this summer
-----
The number of Bay Area homes sitting on the market for more than 30 days is increasing, a new real estate report shows.Real estate agents refer to these properties as "stale" homes because sellers struggle to find buyers quickly in several Bay Area counties, according to Redfin data viewed by SFGATE.
In June 2024, Solano County had the biggest increase in stale listings compared with last year, with 57.7% of homes remaining on the market for more than 30 days, the data shows.
