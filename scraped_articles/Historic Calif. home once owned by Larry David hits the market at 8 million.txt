Historic Calif. home once owned by Larry David hits the market at $8 million
-----
A Santa Barbara property that was home to Larry David, creator of "Seinfeld" and "Curb Your Enthusiasm," is for sale. Built in 1929, 175 Miramar Ave. has not only celebrity street cred, but also architectural significance.The home rests on a large, leafy lot in the Montecito area of Santa Barbara County, California.
