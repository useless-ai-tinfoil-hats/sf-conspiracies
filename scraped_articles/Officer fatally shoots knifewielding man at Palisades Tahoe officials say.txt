Officer fatally shoots knife-wielding man at Palisades Tahoe, officials say
-----
A State Park Peace Officer shot and killed a man armed with a knife Friday morning at Palisades Tahoe ski resort in Olympic Valley, the California Department of Parks and Recreation said in a news release.California State Parks spokesperson Adeline Yee said the man was in a car that had been involved in a carjacking and the agency was called on by CHP to assist in a pursuit of the car.
