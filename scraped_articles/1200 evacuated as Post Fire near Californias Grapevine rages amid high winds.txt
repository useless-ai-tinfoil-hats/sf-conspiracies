1,200 evacuated as Post Fire near California's Grapevine rages amid high winds
-----
A wildfire burning near California's Grapevine has torched thousands of acres and forced evacuations as high winds fed the blaze on Saturday. The Post Fire is burning in Gorman off Interstate 5, prompting evacuations orders for all areas west of I-5 between Pyramid Lake and Gorman. Over 1,200 people were evacuated from Hungry Valley State Vehicular Recreation Area on Saturday, Cal Fire officials said.The fire started shortly after 1:45 p.m. near Gorman Road and began moving southeast
