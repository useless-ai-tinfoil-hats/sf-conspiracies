Isadora Duncan Dance Awards ceremony makes in-person return
-----
After five years, the Isadora Duncan Dance Awards made their in-person return, providing a space for the Bay Area's vibrant dance community to connect, celebrate and reflect on what's happened since the pandemic.Informally known as the Izzies, the annual dance awards were announced for the 2022-23 winners at a ceremony on Monday, March 25, at the ODC Theater in San Francisco.
