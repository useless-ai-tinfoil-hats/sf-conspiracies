BART train derails, catches fire in East Bay; 9 passengers injured
-----
A BART train caught fire after it derailed near the Orinda station Monday morning, injuring nine people and leading to the evacuation of all cars and a partial service shutdown.The train had just left Orinda for Lafayette shortly after 9 a.m. when "it appears the front two cars derailed," BART spokesman Jim Allison told reporters at the Orinda station.
