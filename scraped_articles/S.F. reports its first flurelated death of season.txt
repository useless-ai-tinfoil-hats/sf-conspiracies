S.F. reports its first flu-related death of season
-----
San Francisco has reported its first flu-related death of the 2023-24 respiratory virus season, public health officials announced Monday.Officials did not disclose the gender or age of the person who died, other than to say they were younger than 65 years old, had not gotten vaccinated and had preexisting medical conditions. The person died last week.
