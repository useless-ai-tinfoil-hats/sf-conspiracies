Dianne Feinstein funeral: Leaders remember a ‘great American hero’ who ‘turned passion into purpose’
-----
San Francisco bid farewell to Dianne Feinstein Thursday, celebrating a life of public service that bashed through multiple glass ceilings in her native city as well as the nation's Capitol, her legacy memorialized on the steps of her beloved City Hall, her life honored by family and friends as well as a rare gathering of dozens of political luminaries.The service was relatively brief, with a handful of select speakers chosen to celebrate Feinstein's five-plus decades of accomplishments
