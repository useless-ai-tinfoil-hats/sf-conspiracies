Three killed in Oakland shootings, including two teens
-----
Two teens and a man were killed in two shootings in Oakland that occurred within a half hour of each other early Monday, police said.Investigators believe the shootings aren't connected, police said.
The first shooting occurred around 1:30 a.m. on Filbert Street near 10th Street, not far from Lowell Park. The victim, a 16-year-old boy, died at the scene, police said.
