San Francisco biotech companies slash hundreds of jobs amid restructuring wave
-----
Two San Francisco biotechnology companies slashed hundreds of jobs in the past week, according to state regulatory filings.Vir Biotechnology announced a plan to permanently cut 141 employees, or approximately a quarter of its workforce, at its facility at 1800 Owens St., according to a state filing.
The layoffs, first reported by Fierce Biotech, are scheduled to begin on Sept. 30 and continue through October. The reductions will impact upper management, including vice presidents
