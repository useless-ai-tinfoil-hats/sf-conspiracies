Fogcutter 08-26-24 The longest standing ovation I've ever seen in SF

The season of concerts ended on Sunday with a powerhouse combo of soul siren Chaka Khan and British reggae veterans Steel Pulse.Reggae and Stern Grove proved to be a perfect pair.
