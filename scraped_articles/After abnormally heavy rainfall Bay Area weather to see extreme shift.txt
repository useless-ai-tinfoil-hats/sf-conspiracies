After abnormally heavy rainfall, Bay Area weather to see extreme shift
-----
After a bout of unusually heavy rainfall, the pendulum of Bay Area weather is about to swing in a completely different direction.The totals are in, and Saturday's storm produced as much as 1.89 inches at the peak of Mount Tamalpais and 1.87 inches in Fairfax in Marin County. Ben Lomond down in Santa Cruz County won out with a total of 1.9 inches of rain, the National Weather Service's Bay Area office reported.
