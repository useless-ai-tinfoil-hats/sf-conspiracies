Massive fire in California's Central Valley reportedly forces evacuations
-----
A massive fire at a recycling facility forced evacuations in Tracy, a town in California's Central Valley, according to multiple media outlets.The blaze burned out of control among the wooden pallets at the GMA Pallet Recycling Corporation, tearing through some 5 acres, KCRA reported.
