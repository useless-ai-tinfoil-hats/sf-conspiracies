Suspect arrested in death of man found in Santa Rosa home
-----
A suspect was arrested Saturday on suspicion of killing a man whose body was found inside a Santa Rosa home, police said.Officers responded to a report of a person who was possibly dead in a home on Sweetgum Street in the Coffey Park neighborhood just before 10 a.m., the Santa Rosa Police Department said on its Facebook page.
Authorities said they found a man standing outside the home and arrested him as a suspect. He was described as a man in his 40s who cooperated with law
