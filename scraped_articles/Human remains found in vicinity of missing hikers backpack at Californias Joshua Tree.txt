Human remains found in vicinity of missing hiker's backpack at California's Joshua Tree
-----
A backpack found near a hiking trail in California's Joshua Tree National Park belongs to a hiker who was reported missing more than eight months ago, the National Park Service said Tuesday.NPS confirmed the bag was the property of Trammell Evans, a 25-year-old hiker. The agency said the discovery of the backpack, which happened last Thursday, led them to find human remains "off a trail in the Black Rock area of the park."
