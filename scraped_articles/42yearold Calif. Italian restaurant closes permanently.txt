42-year-old Calif. Italian restaurant closes permanently
-----
A family-owned Italian restaurant near downtown Santa Cruz has closed permanently after 42 years.Ristorante Italiano, located at 555 Soquel Ave. Ste. 150, part of Branciforte Plaza, was known for its outdoor wall that was painted to resemble a building from an Italian village, complete with crumbling stucco facade, line-dried clothes and a cafe scene at the bottom.
