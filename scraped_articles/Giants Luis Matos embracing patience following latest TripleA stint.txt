Giants' Luis Matos embracing patience following latest Triple-A stint
-----
San Francisco Giants' centerfielder Luis Matos, freshly returned to the major leagues from Triple-A, turned a knuckle-curve pitch into a difference-maker in the fifth inning Wednesday, hammering the ball into the left-field bleachers.It broke a tie and gave the Giants a 4-3 lead against Chicago that they did not relinquish
"He gets good pitches to hit," manager Bob Melvin said. "He's going to hit. As we saw, it's about shrinking the zone up and getting a good ball to hit."
