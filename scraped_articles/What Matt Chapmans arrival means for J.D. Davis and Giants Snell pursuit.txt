What Matt Chapman’s arrival means for J.D. Davis and Giants’ Snell pursuit
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — The closing credits from the new movie "Dune: Part Two" were rolling when J.D. Davis turned his phone on to a cluster of text messages from family and friends. They came with surprising news: Matt Chapman had agreed to a three-year, $54 million contract with the San Francisco Giants on Friday night.Chapman was spotted at the Giants' facility in Scottsdale on Saturday morning, which means the contract could become official sooner than later pending a physical.
