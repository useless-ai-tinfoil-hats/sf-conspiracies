Video: Man rescued after driving off cliff on Highway 1 in Sonoma County
-----
A man who was critically injured after he drove off a coastal cliff along Highway 1 and was ejected about 350 feet from his car was rescued Sunday morning by Sonoma County crews.The car's automatic crash notification system alerted 911 and provided dispatchers with the crash location near Jewell Gulch north of Jenner on Highway 1 around 8 a.m., the Sonoma County Sheriff's Office said in a statement.
