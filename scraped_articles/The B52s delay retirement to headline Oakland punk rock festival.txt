The B-52s delay retirement to headline Oakland punk rock festival
-----
The B-52s will return to the Bay Area as headliners for Oakland's annual Mosswood Meltdown this summer, marking a surprising return after their farewell tour.The full lineup for the festival on July 6-7 was announced by organizers on Thursday, Feb. 1. In addition to the B-52s, the event will feature East Bay garage rock band Hunx and His Punx as well as New Orleans' "Queen of Bounce," Big Freedia.
