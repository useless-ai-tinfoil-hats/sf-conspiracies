E-40 to receive key to the city from Vallejo officials
-----
Rapper E-40 may soon hold the key to his hometown.In a memo issued by the mayor's office, city officials said they would like to recognize the Vallejo native, born Earl Stevens, for his exceptional contributions to arts, culture, business and philanthropy in Vallejo.
The Vallejo City Council will consider the motion at its next regular meeting on Tuesday, Oct. 10.
