S.F. election: Prop. B ‘cop tax’ related to police staffing fails
-----
San Francisco voters on Tuesday rejected a controversial ballot measure that supporters said was meant to increase police staffing but was branded by its opponents as a "cop tax."Proposition B failed to pass with 72% of voters against the measure.
