San Francisco's unemployment rate jumped almost half a percentage point in January from the previous month, to 4% amid heavy tech layoffs, according to state data released Friday.It's the highest level since October 2021, when it was also 4% and the Bay Area was recovering from the worst of the pandemic. San Francisco's unemployment rate was a revised 3.6% in December 2023 and 2.8% in January 2023. The rates are not seasonally adjusted.
