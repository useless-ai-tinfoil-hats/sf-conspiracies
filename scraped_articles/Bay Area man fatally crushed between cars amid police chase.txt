Bay Area man fatally crushed between cars amid police chase
-----
A 76-year-old pedestrian was fatally crushed between two cars as Bay Area police attempted to pull over a driver for reckless driving, officials said.The pedestrian was identified as Russell Hawk, a Vallejo resident, the Solano County Coroner's office told SFGATE.
