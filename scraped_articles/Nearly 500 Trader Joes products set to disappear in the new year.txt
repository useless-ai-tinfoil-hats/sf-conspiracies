Nearly 500 Trader Joe's products set to disappear in the new year
-----
Trader Joe's is at it again. Every holiday season, the store known for butter chicken and Perfectly Pickled Pups unveils hundreds of unique seasonal products that are available for a limited time. Once they're gone, they're gone.In a recent Trader Joe's podcast, hosts Tara Miller and Matt Sloan revealed that this season there are a whopping 469 winter-themed products on the store's shelves.
