San Francisco's Noise Pop festival announces first 2024 lineup
-----
San Francisco's hippest music festival is back.On Thursday, Noise Pop announced the Phase 1 lineup for its first 2024 festival. From Feb. 22 to March 3, Noise Pop will host concerts at venues across San Francisco, including Great American Music Hall, 1015 Folsom, Gray Area, Bottom of the Hill and Grace Cathedral. Compared with festivals like Outside Lands and Hardly Strictly, Noise Pop typically focuses on a mix of up-and-coming bands and legacy indie rock acts.
