Mark Zuckerberg is raising wagyu beef on a macadamia nut diet on Hawaii's Kauai

On Tuesday, in a Facebook post, Zuckerberg revealed more of his plans for the property: He will raise a herd of cattle on a homegrown, incredibly expensive diet.
