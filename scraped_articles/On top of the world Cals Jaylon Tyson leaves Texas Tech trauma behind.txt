‘On top of the world’: Cal’s Jaylon Tyson leaves Texas Tech trauma behind
-----
Jaylon Tyson made his Cal basketball debut Friday night, but only after what he described as the longest two weeks of his life.He already had waited eight months since former Texas Tech coach Mark Adams used the words "slave" and "master" in characterizing their relationship, Tyson alleged, and nearly six months since he decided to transfer to Cal. Tyson, who is African American, expected the circumstances of his departure would allow him to play immediately for the Bears.
