He played for Roger Craig's "Humm-Baby" Giants and managed the A's with distinction for 11 years.That's quite enough to erase the wave of Bruce Bochy nostalgia sweeping across the landscape with his return to the World Series with the Texas Rangers, but there's so much more to Melvin's story.
