Eyeing playoffs, Giants try to look on bright side of loss to Braves
-----
Thirty thousand fans went to watch the San Francisco Giants at Oracle Park on Monday night, and a postseason game broke out."That's a playoff game right there," confirmed outfielder Mike Yastrzemski, at least figuratively, after the Giants lost 1-0 to the Atlanta Braves in a game that lasted 10 innings and featured 155 minutes of superior pitching. "The atmosphere was great."
"It felt a little different, yeah," manager Bob Melvin said, "especially with the quality of the pitching.
