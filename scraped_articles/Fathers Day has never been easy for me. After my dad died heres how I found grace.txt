Father’s Day has never been easy for me. After my dad died, here’s how I found grace
-----
When my dad was alive, Father's Day was not a holiday I marked with him. It would have registered as — weird. Sarcastic, even. He and I had a loving relationship as adults, but he had left my mother when I was born, and my brother and I never lived with him.
