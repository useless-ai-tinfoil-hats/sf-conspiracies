Trump will be on California primary ballot in March, secretary of state says
-----
Former President Donald Trump will be on California's primary ballot in March despite efforts to remove him, Shirley Weber, the state's secretary of state, announced Thursday.California's primary election is scheduled for March 5. Trump will be among several candidates vying for the Republican nomination for next year's general election.
