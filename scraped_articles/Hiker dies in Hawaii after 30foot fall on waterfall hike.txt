Hiker dies in Hawaii after 30-foot fall on waterfall hike
-----
A 30-year-old man from New York died on Sunday after falling 30 feet from a hiking trail on the Hawaiian island of Kauai, the police said.Matthew Wu was on a trail below Hanakapiai Falls, one of the island's most famous waterfalls, when he fell, the Kauai Police Department said in a news release Jan. 16.
