Hundreds of dead sea lion babies found off Bay Area coast
-----
Hundreds of dead sea lion pups appeared off the coast in the Bay Area this month, but researchers don't know why.Patrick Robinson, director of UC Santa Cruz's Año Nuevo Reserve, first counted 50 stillborn or aborted sea lion fetuses in early May — which is about the most he would find in a given year.
After an aerial survey one week later, the researchers counted 288 on the shores of Año Nuevo Island in San Mateo County. It's unclear how many more may be along the California coast,
