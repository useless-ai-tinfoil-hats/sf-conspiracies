This is the top metro region where Bay Area residents consider buying homes
-----
When Bay Area residents search for a home outside the region, many of them don't look far.More home buyers in the San Francisco metropolitan area check the Sacramento region for listings than any other destination, according to a recent report from real estate brokerage site Redfin. The report, based on an analysis of search trends among Redfin users, also found the Seattle metro area was the top out-of-state destination searched by Bay Area home buyers.
