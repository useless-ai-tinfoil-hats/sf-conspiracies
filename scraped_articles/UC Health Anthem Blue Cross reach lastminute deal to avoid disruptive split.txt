UC Health, Anthem Blue Cross reach last-minute deal to avoid disruptive split
-----
More than a half million Californians won't need to find new doctors, change their health plans or pay higher out-of-network medical fees after hospital chain UC Health and insurer Anthem Blue Cross settled a dispute over a new contract."UC Health has reached a fair agreement with Anthem Blue Cross that will allow UCSF Health physicians, hospitals and clinics to remain in Anthem's provider network," the health care provider announced on Monday.
