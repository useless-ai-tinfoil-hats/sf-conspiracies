Man found dead on platform at S.F. Glen Park BART Station
-----
A man was found unresponsive and pronounced dead on a platform at the Glen Park BART Station in San Francisco early Wednesday, authorities said.Paramedics called to the station at 1:31 a.m. rendered care to the man, who transit officials said was "unresponsive on the platform floor." He was pronounced dead, and coroners took his body for autopsy, officials said.
Foul play was not suspected in the man's death, BART spokesperson Chris Filippi said in an email Wednesday.
