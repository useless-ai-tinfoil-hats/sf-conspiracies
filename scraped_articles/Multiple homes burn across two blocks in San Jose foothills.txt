Multiple homes burn across two blocks in San Jose foothills
-----
Flames overtook three houses in the San Jose foothills on Sunday, officials said.Three people were displaced from their homes after flames overtook a residential garage on Sand Point Drive in the Holly Oak neighborhood and spread to nearby homes around 5 p.m., said San Jose Fire Department spokesperson Jake Pisani.
"Despite hot and windy conditions, firefighters did well to limit damage to three homes," Pisani told the Chronicle.
The structure fires were "in close proximity"
