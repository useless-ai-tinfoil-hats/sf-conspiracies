‘In great pain’: California hiker rescued after seven hours pinned beneath boulder
-----
A man hiking in the Inyo Mountains was airlifted by helicopter to a Fresno hospital early Wednesday morning after he became trapped beneath a boulder weighing thousands of pounds for seven hours, necessitating a challenging rescue in dark and cold circumstances, authorities said.Inyo County Search and Rescue was called to comb the area for the man on Tuesday afternoon and found him below Santa Rita Flat near Independence, per a recent statement from the Inyo County Sheriff's Office.
