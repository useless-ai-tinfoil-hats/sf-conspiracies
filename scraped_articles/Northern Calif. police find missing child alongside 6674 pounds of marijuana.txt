Northern Calif. police find missing child alongside 6,674 pounds of marijuana
-----
A child was found early Tuesday morning at an illegal marijuana farm on her parents' property, several hours after the child's mother reported her missing, the Butte County Sheriff's Office said in a news release on Thursday.The woman reported the 3-year-old girl missing from the family home in Bangor, about an hour north of Sacramento, around 10:35 p.m. on Monday, the Sheriff's Office said.
