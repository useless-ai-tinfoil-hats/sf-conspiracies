California sweeps Yelp’s list of top 100 places to eat in 2024
-----
On Tuesday, Yelp revealed its annual list of the best places to eat in 2024. In perhaps a shock to no one, California has essentially swept the list with 18 restaurants named as Yelpers' favorites. The collection of restaurants is deeply inclusive, with nods to vegetarian tacos in Tucson, Arizona and Ethiopian food east of downtown Los Angeles — but one thing you won't find much are Michelin stars or big, buzz-worthy names.
