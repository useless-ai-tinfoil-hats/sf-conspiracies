San Rafael police say thieves are impersonating federal agents before robbing people
-----
Police in San Rafael sought to warn the public Thursday after multiple instances in which impersonators identified themselves as federal agents before robbing people of their wallets and cash.The robbers targeted people in their vehicles and on the street, police said.
Police described the robberies as a series but did not say the total number of cases reported. Police also did not say when or where exactly the robberies occurred or how many robbers were involved.
