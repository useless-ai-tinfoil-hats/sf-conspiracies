Resurgent Michael Conforto giving Giants some pop and accountability
-----
Michael Conforto is right in the middle of everything to start the season, mostly a nice feeling for a player who was at times last year a forgotten man with the San Francisco Giants.Two caveats: As Conforto noted emphatically after the team's 3-2 win over the Padres on Friday, we're just eight games in. Second, as strong as he has been at the plate, batting .419 with three homers and 10 RBIs entering Saturday, he has been a little less successful at times on the bases.
