Steph Curry’s game-winner caps wild Warriors win over Thunder in tourney opener
-----
OKLAHOMA CITY — Stephen Curry needed just a sliver of daylight on Friday night.The game clock was winding down. His defender, Luguentz Dort, likely was expecting a long jumper from the NBA's 3-point king. So a simple crossover is all it took for Dort to bite. And Curry's high-arching floater over Chet Holmgren's 7-foot-6 wingspan with 0.2 seconds left proved to be the game-winner.
