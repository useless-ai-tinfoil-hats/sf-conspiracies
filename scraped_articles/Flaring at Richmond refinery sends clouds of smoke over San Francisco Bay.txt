Flaring at Richmond refinery sends clouds of smoke over San Francisco Bay
-----
A flaring incident at the Chevron refinery in Richmond sent plumes of smoke above San Francisco Bay on Monday afternoon.As people posted photos of the dark smoke on social media, the company said in a Facebook post that the flaring was a result of a power outage and was considered a "Level 1" incident, meaning that residents do not have to take special precautions.
