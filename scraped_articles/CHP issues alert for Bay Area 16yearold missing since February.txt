CHP issues alert for Bay Area 16-year-old missing since February
-----
An Ebony Alert was issued in five California counties on Sunday for a Bay Area teen who has been missing since February.A'niya Maxey, 16, was last seen on Feb. 24 just after 10 p.m. in East Palo Alto, near Verbena Drive and Gardenia Way, California Highway Patrol said.
The teen is 5 feet, 1 inch tall, weighs 110 pounds and has black hair and brown eyes. Police said she was wearing a black top and black pants when she was last seen.
