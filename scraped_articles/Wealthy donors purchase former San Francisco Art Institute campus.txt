Wealthy donors purchase former San Francisco Art Institute campus
-----
A nonprofit backed by several wealthy San Francisco arts donors has purchased the campus of the former San Francisco Art Institute, which houses a famous Diego Rivera mural.The nonprofit, backed by Laurene Powell Jobs, purchased the campus on Thursday for about $30 million, the San Francisco Chronicle reported.
