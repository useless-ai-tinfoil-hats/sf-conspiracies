‘Inside Out 2’ review: Anxiety gets the best of this Pixar sequel
-----
"Inside Out 2" is the follow-up to one of the best films Pixar ever made, and it has great things in it. It once again communicates complicated ideas in simple visual terms, and has true and interesting things to say about how anxiety is the enemy of joy. But its bare-bones story is not enough to sustain interest for 96 whole minutes.Indeed, not only is there not enough story, but what little story it has feels wrong for what the movie tries to accomplish.
