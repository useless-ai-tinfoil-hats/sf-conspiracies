Santa Rosa man accused of threatening restaurant worker with a sword
-----
Police in Santa Rosa arrested a man Monday who threatened to stab a restaurant employee with a sword on Sunday, officials said.Santa Rosa officers who were dispatched to a restaurant in the 500 block of Fourth Street just before 2 p.m. were told by a restaurant employee that an unknown man leaving the restroom threatened him with a sword.
As the victim was hiding, the suspect allegedly stole an alcoholic beverage from a cooler before leaving the restaurant, officials said.
