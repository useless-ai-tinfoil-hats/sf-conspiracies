Google fires 20 more employees involved in protests of Israeli contract
-----
Google has fired an additional 20 employees who participated in a protest last week against the company's defense contract with the Israeli government.This brings the total number of employees laid off due to the protest to 50, according to the group that organized the demonstrations, No Tech for Apartheid.
Jane Chung, a spokesperson for the group, said some of the dismissed employees were "nonparticipating bystanders" during the sit-in protests held on April 16 at Google's offices
