Review: Anne Lamott on love in all its forms from her own very human and flawed perch
-----
Anne Lamott has been writing candidly about family, her recovery from alcoholism and her growing faith for 40 years. Those who've read her may feel like they've watched her grow up, or expect that she's worked out the secret to life by now.
