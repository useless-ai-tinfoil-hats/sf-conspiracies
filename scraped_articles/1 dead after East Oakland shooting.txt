1 dead after East Oakland shooting
-----
A man was fatally shot in East Oakland on Tuesday morning, authorities said.The shooting happened at about 2:30 a.m. in the 2300 block of 23rd Avenue, according to Oakland police spokesperson Rosalia Lopez.
Officers responded to reports of several ShotSpotter activations and found a man from Tulare suffering from gunshot wounds, Lopez said. He was pronounced dead at the scene.
The victim's identity was not immediately released pending notification of next of kin, authorities
