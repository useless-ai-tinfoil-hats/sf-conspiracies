Cash-burning Planet Labs conducts second San Francisco layoff in 11 months
-----
Planet Labs, a San Francisco-based tech company that uses satellites to monitor huge swaths of Earth, is laying off dozens of workers in the city and elsewhere.The cuts will hit 180 workers total, or 17% of the company's workforce, according to a Wednesday filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission.
