East Bay police: Loaded gun, cash and marijuana seized in traffic stop
-----
An East Bay traffic stop Saturday led to the seizure of a loaded gun, cash and marijuana, according to the Pinole Police Department.Officer Mario Esparza stopped a vehicle on Interstate 80 between Pinole and Hercules and, during a search, discovered a loaded semiautomatic pistol, cash and a stash of marijuana, which was determined to be for sale, police said
