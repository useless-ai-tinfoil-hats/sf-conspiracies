Rare Bay Area Julia Morgan duplex hits market for $1.8 million
-----
Built in 1904, 2537-2539 Etna Street was once part of a three-home compound designed by celebrated architect Julia Morgan. Located in Berkeley's Elmwood neighborhood, the duplex is for sale for the first time since 1975, asking $1.8 million.Though she is best known for iconic and elaborate buildings like Hearst Castle and the Berkeley City Club, Morgan's residential homes are often more simple, designed with details meant to make living easy and enjoyable.
