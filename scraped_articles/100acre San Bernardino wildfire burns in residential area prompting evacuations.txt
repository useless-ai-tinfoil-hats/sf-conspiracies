100-acre San Bernardino wildfire burns in residential area, prompting evacuations
-----
LATEST Aug. 5, 5:50 p.m. The Edgehill Fire has destroyed multiple homes in San Bernardino County, according to live footage from KABC-TV. The blaze is burning amid scorching temperatures in the area of more than 100 degrees.The fire grew rapidly to 100 acres and is prompting evacuations for nearby residents, which have been posted by the San Bernardino County Fire Protection District's account on X.
