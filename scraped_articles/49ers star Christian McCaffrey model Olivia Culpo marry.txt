49ers star Christian McCaffrey, model Olivia Culpo marry
-----
San Francisco 49ers star Christian McCaffrey married model Olivia Culpo on Saturday, capping off an eventful month for the running back.The wedding took place at a chapel in the coastal town of Watch Hill, R.I., about 45 minutes south of Culpo's hometown, according to several media reports.
