Driver dies after being ejected during rollover crash in S.F.
-----
A driver died after being ejected Saturday morning during a rollover crash in San Francisco's Noe Valley, police said.Emergency crews responded to a vehicle rollover over at Diamond and Clipper streets around 10:30 a.m. The driver was ejected and three other vehicles were damaged in the crash, San Francisco Fire Department spokesperson Lt. Mariano Elias told the Chronicle.
