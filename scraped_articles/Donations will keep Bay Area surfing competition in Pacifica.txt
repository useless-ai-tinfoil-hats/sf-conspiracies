Donations will keep Bay Area surfing competition in Pacifica
-----
LATEST April 30, 12:10 p.m. The organizers of an annual dog surfing championship in Pacifica announced Monday that the competition will take place as planned in August now that the group has raised enough money in donations to keep the event afloat.World Dog Surfing Championships asked the public for $10,000 in donations earlier this month, saying that event costs such as permits, fees and other requirements had increased by more than 100% over the last three years.
