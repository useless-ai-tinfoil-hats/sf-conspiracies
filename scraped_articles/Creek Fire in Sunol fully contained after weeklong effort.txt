Creek Fire in Sunol fully contained after weeklong effort
-----
The Creek Fire, a wildfire that burned about 750 acres in Alameda County's Sunol community, is now completely contained.Fire crews had stopped the blaze, roughly 10 miles east of Fremont, from growing soon after it started on July 26. But the steep terrain of the Sunol Wilderness Preserve and high temperatures made fighting the fire challenging, fire officials said. Firefighters achieved 100% containment by Friday night.
