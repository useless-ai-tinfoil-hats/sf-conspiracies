Electric vehicle drivers may not be able to ride solo in Calif. carpool lanes anymore
-----
A program that allows solo drivers of electric and plug-in hybrid vehicles to use carpool lanes on freeways and bridges in California may be removed for good next year.Drivers of cars with a Clean Air Vehicle decal issued by the state have enjoyed the privilege of using an HOV lane without the required number of passengers since 2005 thanks to a federal transportation bill. But unless Congress renews the program through new legislation, it's set to expire Sept. 30, 2025.
