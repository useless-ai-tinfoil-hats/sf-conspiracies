A nearly 70-year-old Bay Area Safeway may be replaced by parking
-----
A parking lot might replace a Safeway in the South Bay that's set to close in November.The grocery store, located in Santa Clara at Stevens Creek Boulevard and next to Westfield Valley Fair Shopping Center, has been open for 67 years and shares a parking plaza with the mall.
