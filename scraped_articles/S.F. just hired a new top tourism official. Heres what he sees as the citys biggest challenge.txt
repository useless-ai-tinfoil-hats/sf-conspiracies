S.F. just hired a new top tourism official. Here’s what he sees as the city’s biggest challenge
-----
San Francisco Travel Association, the city's tourism bureau and marketing organization, has hired a new president and CEO: Scott Beck, who is currently head of Destination Toronto.Beck will join the organization on Oct. 30, and succeed nearly 18-year veteran Joe D'Alessandro, who is retiring in December.
He arrives at a critical time for the city, whose biggest industry, tourism and hospitality, supports over 53,000 jobs. Tourists are returning, but a full recovery to record 2019
