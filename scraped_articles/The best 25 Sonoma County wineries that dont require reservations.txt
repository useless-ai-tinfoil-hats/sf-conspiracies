The best 25 Sonoma County wineries that don’t require reservations
-----
Wine tasting has changed since the pandemic. Bottle prices and tasting fees are on the rise. The casual, belly-up-to-the-bar format is nearly extinct. And the majority of wineries in Napa and Sonoma have shifted to a by-appointment structure.While the reservation system offers visitors a more exclusive and intimate wine tasting experience, it also removes the thrill of spontaneity — of pulling over at whichever winery looks interesting as you cruise the vineyard-dotted back roads.
