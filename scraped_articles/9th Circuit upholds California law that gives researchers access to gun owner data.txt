9th Circuit upholds California law that gives researchers access to gun owner data
-----
A California law allowing researchers to obtain records of all guns and ammunition bought in the state does not violate gun owners' privacy or their right to keep and bear arms, a federal appeals court ruled Wednesday.The law, AB173, was signed by Gov. Gavin Newsom in 2021 but blocked in November 2022 by a San Diego judge in a privacy-rights suit by gun advocacy groups.
