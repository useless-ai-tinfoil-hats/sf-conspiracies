St. Mary's men, a No. 5 seed for 3rd straight year, hope to get past 2nd round
-----
For the third year in a row, the St. Mary's men hosted a Selection Sunday viewing party attended by a few hundred fans at University Credit Union Pavilion.And for the third year in a row, the Gaels (26-7) have received a No. 5 seed in the field of 68. St. Mary's is in the West Region this time and will face WAC champion Grand Canyon (29-4) in Spokane, Wash., on Friday night.
