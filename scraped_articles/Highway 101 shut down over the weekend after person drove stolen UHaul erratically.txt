Highway 101 shut down over the weekend after person drove stolen U-Haul 'erratically'
-----
LATEST June 11, 10:05 a.m. A person who initiated a police pursuit beginning in downtown San Jose on Sunday evening was allegedly driving a stolen U-Haul involved in a hit-and-run collision.The San Jose Police Department responded to a hit-and-run in the area of Santa Clara Street and Almaden Avenue at approximately 6:23 p.m., SJPD told SFGATE. The suspect was driving "erratically," prompting the Santa Clara County Sheriff's Office to assist SJPD in the pursuit, sheriff's office
