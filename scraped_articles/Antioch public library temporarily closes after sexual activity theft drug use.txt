Antioch public library temporarily closes after sexual activity, theft, drug use
-----
Contra Costa County officials temporarily closed the public library in Antioch due to several "dangerous incidents" in the last few months, officials said, including theft, drug use and public sexual activity.The county announced the temporary closure in a statement Friday, citing "repeated dangerous incidents in the last few months that have threatened the safety and security of patrons and staff." It will reopen on Tuesday at noon.
