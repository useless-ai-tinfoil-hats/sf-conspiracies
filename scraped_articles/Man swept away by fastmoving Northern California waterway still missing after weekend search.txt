Man swept away by fast-moving Northern California waterway still missing after weekend search
-----
A man was presumed to have drowned after falling into the Delta-Mendota Canal on Grant Line Road northeast of Oro Lomo in Fresno County, the Alameda County Sheriff's Office said.Officials were dispatched to the canal shortly before 10 p.m. on Friday after a group that was fishing in the area reported that a man had fallen into the waterway and was swept away by a fast-moving southbound current.
