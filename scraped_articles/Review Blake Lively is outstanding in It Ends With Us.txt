Review: Blake Lively is outstanding in ‘It Ends With Us’
-----
The big takeaway from "It Ends With Us" is that it confirms what 2020's "The Rhythm Section" made clear: Blake Lively is a major talent.One of the best onscreen thinkers, Lively's thought processes are readable, original and idiosyncratic. She is constantly surprising yet always true, and that combination is the mark of a star.
