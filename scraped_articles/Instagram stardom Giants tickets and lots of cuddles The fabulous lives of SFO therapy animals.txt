Instagram stardom, Giants tickets and lots of cuddles: The fabulous lives of SFO therapy animals
-----
On a recent rainy afternoon at San Francisco International Airport, Terminal 2 was buzzing with activity: Children cried for their parents, indistinct voices boomed over the intercom and the wheels of rolling suitcases clattered over terrazzo tiles.But the din quieted almost as soon as airport passengers caught sight of Duke Ellington Morris, the airport's first — and only — therapy cat. Curled up on the fuzzy floor of his carriage, the 14-year-old seemed wholly unbothered by the frenzy
