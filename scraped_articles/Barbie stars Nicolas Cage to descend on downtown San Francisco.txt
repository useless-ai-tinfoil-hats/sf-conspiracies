'Barbie' stars, Nicolas Cage to descend on downtown San Francisco
-----
Big names, bigwigs and Gerwigs will descend on San Francisco's Yerba Buena Center for the Arts for the SFFILM awards night on Dec. 4, an event that IndieWire described as San Francisco's "awards stop on the road to the Oscars."SFFILM runs the San Francisco International Film Festival, which boasts the title of the longest-running film festival in the country. It has been held since 1957 for an uninterrupted stretch of 66 years.
