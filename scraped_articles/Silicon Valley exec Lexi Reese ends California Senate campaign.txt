Silicon Valley exec Lexi Reese ends California Senate campaign
-----
Silicon Valley business executive Lexi Reese ended her Senate campaign Tuesday."The reason? Money," Reese wrote in a statement posted online Tuesday.
"We've raised approximately $2 million. Which sounds like a lot of money until you compare it with the top competitors in the Senate race who have been serving in the House of Representatives for a collective 50+ years," Reese, who has never run for elective office, wrote.
