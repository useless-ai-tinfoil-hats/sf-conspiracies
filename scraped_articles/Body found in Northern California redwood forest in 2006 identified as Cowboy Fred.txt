Body found in Northern California redwood forest in 2006 identified as 'Cowboy Fred'
-----
A body found in a redwood forest in Northern California 18 years ago has been identified, and authorities are now trying to learn more about the life and death of a man who went by "Cowboy Fred."The skeletal remains were found in October 2006 on timber company land near Fieldbrook in Humboldt County, the county sheriff's office said in a statement shared with SFGATE.
