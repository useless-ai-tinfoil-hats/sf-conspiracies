It’s Sharktober on the S.F. coast. Here's what a close encounter with white shark is like
-----
It wasn't until the second shark that I felt fear.I was on a boat near the Farallon Islands on Oct. 2 with Scot Anderson and Paul Kanive, white shark experts who spend most of each fall monitoring the apex predators as their numbers peak in the Bay Area – especially during the month known as Sharktober. The first shark we saw was a young gray beauty that gave off a peaceful and curious vibe as she swam around the boat without coming to the surface. She was hard to see in the dark water
