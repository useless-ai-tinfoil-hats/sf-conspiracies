Bay Area woman who vanished on Valentine's Day feared poisoning, police say
-----
A Bay Area woman who was killed on or around Valentine's Day may have suspected her husband was poisoning her, court testimony revealed this week.Anu Anand Hobson, 53, was reported missing on Feb. 15 by someone who was concerned about her safety, Fairfield police said at the time. At first, her husband, Gregory Grant Hobson, 62, was also included in the missing person report.
