Former South Bay Republican Congressman Paul ‘Pete’ McCloskey dies at 96
-----
Paul N. "Pete" McCloskey, a maverick former Republican congressman from the South Bay who led six bayonet charges in the Korean War, co-chaired the first Earth Day in 1970 and helped write the federal Endangered Species Act, died Wednesday.The granite-chinned war hero, who spent most of his life fighting for peace, died of heart and kidney failure at his home in Winters (Yolo County), said his wife of 42 years, Helen Hooper McCloskey. He was 96.
