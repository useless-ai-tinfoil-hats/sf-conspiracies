Fairfield police investigate fatal hit-and-run
-----
Fairfield police said Tuesday they were investigating a suspected hit-and-run crash that left a person dead.Around 1 a.m. Tuesday, an officer was driving on Air Base Parkway near Wylie Lane when they noticed "a person down on the shoulder" of the road, Fairfield police said in a statement.
When the officer checked on the person, they saw evidence that the person had been struck by a car, but no vehicles were nearby, police said.
