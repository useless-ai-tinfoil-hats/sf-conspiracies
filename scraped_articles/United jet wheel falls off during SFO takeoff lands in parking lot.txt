United jet wheel falls off during SFO takeoff, lands in parking lot
-----
A United Airlines flight headed to Japan lost a wheel during takeoff Thursday at San Francisco International Airport, officials said.At approximately 11:35 a.m., a piece of a landing gear fell off United Flight 35, which was departing to Osaka, SFO spokespersons said. The wheel debris landed in an on-airport employee parking lot.
