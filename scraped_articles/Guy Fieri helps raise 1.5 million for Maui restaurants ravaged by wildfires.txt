Guy Fieri helps raise $1.5 million for Maui restaurants ravaged by wildfires
-----
Celebrity chef Guy Fieri played host at an exclusive soiree in Sonoma County this past weekend, aiming to provide relief for Maui's fire-ravaged restaurants.The "Diners, Drive-Ins and Dives" host helped raise over $1.5 million at the "Chefs for Maui" fundraiser dinner, a star-studded event that brought together Food Network notables including Michael Voltaggio, Brooke Williamson, Kelsey Barnard Clark, Mei Lin and Antonia Lofaso at a private estate.
