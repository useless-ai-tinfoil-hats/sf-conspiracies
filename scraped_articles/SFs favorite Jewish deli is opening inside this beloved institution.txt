SF's favorite Jewish deli is opening inside this beloved institution
-----
Wise Sons, the classic Jewish delicatessen based in San Francisco, is set to open a new shop this fall.Announced on Instagram, the restaurant known for bagels, babka, pastrami and more plans to open at the San Francisco Jewish Community Center this September in the Laurel Heights neighborhood.
"The JCC is at the very center of the Jewish community in San Francisco, and it's a real honor for us to serve both its members and the neighborhood at large," read the post by Wise Sons.
