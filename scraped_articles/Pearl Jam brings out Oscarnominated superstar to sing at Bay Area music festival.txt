Pearl Jam brings out Oscar-nominated superstar to sing at Bay Area music festival
-----
In a high-energy performance, Pearl Jam electrified the BottleRock stage on Saturday evening, but not without bringing out a surprise, Oscar-nominated guest and treating fans to some souvenir instruments.BottleRock, a three-day festival held in Napa Valley during Memorial Day weekend, brought thousands of attendees to enjoy musical acts including Stevie Nicks, Megan Thee Stallion and Ed Sheeran.
