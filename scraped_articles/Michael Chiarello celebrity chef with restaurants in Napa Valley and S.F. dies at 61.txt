Michael Chiarello, celebrity chef with restaurants in Napa Valley and S.F., dies at 61
-----
Michael Chiarello, the celebrity chef who ran renowned restaurants in San Francisco and Napa Valley, died Friday night, his family confirmed. He was 61.The chef died at a Napa hospital where he was being treated for an acute allergic reaction that led to anaphylactic shock, his company, Gruppo Chiarello, announced Saturday.
