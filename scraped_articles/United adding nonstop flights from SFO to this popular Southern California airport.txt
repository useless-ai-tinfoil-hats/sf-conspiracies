United adding nonstop flights from SFO to this popular Southern California airport
-----
Ahead of the holiday season, Bay Area travelers will soon see more flights between San Francisco and a popular Southern California airport.United Airlines announced additional routes between San Francisco International Airport (SFO) and Hollywood Burbank Airport (BUR), increasing the current schedule from four to six flights a day beginning in November. Tickets went on sale Saturday.
