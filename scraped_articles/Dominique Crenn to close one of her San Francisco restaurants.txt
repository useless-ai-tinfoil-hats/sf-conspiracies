Dominique Crenn to close one of her San Francisco restaurants
-----
The sister restaurant of Dominique Crenn's three-Michelin-starred San Francisco restaurant, Atelier Crenn, will close permanently in the coming months.Petit Crenn, at 609 Hayes St. in San Francisco's Hayes Valley neighborhood, was a slightly more affordable dining experience for those looking to enjoy the French cuisine of the world-famous Crenn. Originally opened in 2015, the restaurant closed during the pandemic and never fully reopened. Rather, it pivoted to feeding the food insecure
