2 Bay Area colleges make list of highest-paid graduates
-----
Two private Bay Area universities made it to the top 10 of a list ranking the highest-paid college graduates.Stanford University and Santa Clara University were the first California schools to appear on the list from Payscale ranking the salaries of graduates with bachelor's degrees, with Stanford No. 5 and Santa Clara No. 9. The college salary report also has a separate list for graduates with associate's degrees.
