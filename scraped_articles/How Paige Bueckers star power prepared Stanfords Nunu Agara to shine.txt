How Paige Bueckers’ star power prepared Stanford’s Nunu Agara to shine
-----
When the clock ticked down to overtime in a game against Duke in mid-November, Stanford freshman Nunu Agara was still on the floor.Tara Starks wasn't surprised. Agara's coach at Hopkins High School in the Minneapolis suburb of Minnetonka had seen this poise plenty of times. Agara was a part of Hopkins teams that not only won Minnesota state titles but drew 4,000 people per game to witness the talents of her teammate, Connecticut standout Paige Bueckers.
