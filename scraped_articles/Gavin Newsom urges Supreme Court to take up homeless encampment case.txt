Gavin Newsom urges Supreme Court to take up homeless encampment case
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom urged the U.S. Supreme Court on Friday to restore local governments' authority to sweep homeless encampments while working to provide shelter for their residents, saying restrictions by lower courts are endangering the unhoused as well as the public.Newsom said California can live with a federal appeals court's 2018 ruling that prohibited cities from making it a crime to sleep on a public street or sidewalk when no homeless shelters are available.
