40-year-old S.F. deli closing last downtown locations
-----
The last two locations of a downtown San Francisco deli and lunch empire will soon close.Lee's Deli owner Lee Quan is in the process of finalizing the closure of his two remaining locations, and considering bankruptcy protection to protect the business. The San Francisco Business Times reported the news.
