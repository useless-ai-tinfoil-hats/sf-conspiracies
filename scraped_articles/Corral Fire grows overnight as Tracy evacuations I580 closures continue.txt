Corral Fire grows overnight as Tracy evacuations, I-580 closures continue
-----
The fast-moving Corral Fire in San Joaquin County, which forced evacuations around Tracy and closed Interstate 580 in both directions Saturday, grew to 12,500 acres overnight and was 15% contained Sunday morning.An evacuation order remained in place Sunday morning west of the California Aqueduct, south of Corral Hollow Creek, west to Alameda County line and south to Stanislaus County line. The San Joaquin County Office of Emergency Services released an evacuation map.
