Their S.F. Italian restaurant was a smashing success. Next: a wine bar
-----
A new Italian wine bar is coming to San Francisco from the former owners of beloved restaurant La Ciccia.Binu Bonu — "good wine" in Sardo, the language of Sardinia — will open in late April at 230 West Portal Ave. The space's current occupant, Que Syrah wine bar, will remain open through the end of March.
The restaurant newsletter Tablehopper was first to report the news.
