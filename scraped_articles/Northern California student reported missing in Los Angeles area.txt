Northern California student reported missing in Los Angeles area
-----
A 23-year-old Northern California woman went missing in the Los Angeles area last week, and her family is engaged in a frantic search with support from the Los Angeles Police Department.Noelle Lynch, a Sacramento State University student who lives with her family in the Sacramento area, disappeared after driving to Southern California on April 1. Her father, Mark Lynch, told SFGATE that his daughter is vulnerable and left home in a "difficult state of mind."
