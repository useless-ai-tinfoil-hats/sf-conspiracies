Escape from Alcatraz, then four Olympic events? Taylor Knibb eyes rare two-sport feat
-----
The Escape from Alcatraz triathlon is a spectator's delight: stunning views of the Golden Gate Bridge, images of adrenaline-fueled athletes diving from a boat deck into the choppy San Francisco Bay and cyclists battered by winds and hills.This year's race offers another unique attraction: the chance to watch Taylor Knibb, who has qualified for the Paris Olympics in both triathlon and cycling.
