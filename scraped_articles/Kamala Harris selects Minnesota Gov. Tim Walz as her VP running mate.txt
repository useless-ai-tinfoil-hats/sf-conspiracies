Kamala Harris selects Minnesota Gov. Tim Walz as her VP running mate
-----
Democratic presidential nominee Kamala Harris named Minnesota Gov. Tim Walz as her running mate Tuesday, adding to the ticket a no-nonsense Midwest presence aimed at countering criticism that Harris is too liberal.Walz is the two-term governor of Minnesota who previously spent 12 years representing a swing district in the House.
