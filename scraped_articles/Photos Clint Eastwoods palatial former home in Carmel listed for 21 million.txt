Photos: Clint Eastwood’s palatial former home in Carmel listed for $21 million
-----
The grand estate once owned by Clint Eastwood during his tenure as mayor of Carmel-by-the-Sea has hit the market. The century-old Las Ondas Estate, sprawling over 11,400 square feet, commands a significant piece of land in this picturesque Northern California coastal town at San Antonio and Ninth avenues.The three-story Spanish Revival home features four bedrooms, 4½ bathrooms and an interior courtyard. It is available for $21 million, listed by Tim Allen of Coldwell Banker Realty.
