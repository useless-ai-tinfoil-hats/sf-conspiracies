‘Ghostbusters’ review: ‘Frozen Empire’ forces a star-studded cast into a dead end
-----
When people are dead, they come back as ghosts. When hit movies are dead, they come back as sequels.The problem with "Ghostbusters: Frozen Empire" isn't that it's terrible, but rather that it's adequate but dead, a full 40 years past any inspiration or reason for being. What started off as an excuse to throw together a handful of young, funny guys has become a big mess with a sprawling cast of actors flailing for laughs that aren't there.
