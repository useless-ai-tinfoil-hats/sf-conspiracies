S.F. is set to see major traffic and transit impacts this weekend. Here's what to expect
-----
With Pride weekend just days away, people planning to travel to or through San Francisco should be prepared for closed streets, traffic detours, rerouted Muni lines and crowded BART trains.Pride events, official and unofficial, will draw big crowds in the city beginning Friday evening – if not sooner – and will continue through Sunday night.
