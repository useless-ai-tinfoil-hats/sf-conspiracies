Oakland names new chief for violence prevention department to combat homicides
-----
Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao announced Monday that a former city police officer and expert on gender-based violence will lead the Department of Violence Prevention.Holly Joshi, who worked in the Oakland Police Department for 14 years and most recently served as the senior director of Glide's Center for Social Justice in San Francisco, will take over the department starting Dec. 11.
