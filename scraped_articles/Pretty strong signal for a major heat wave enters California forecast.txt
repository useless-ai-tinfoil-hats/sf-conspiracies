'Pretty strong signal for a major heat wave' enters California forecast
-----
Expect to be back to summer weather next week in California, with forecasts showing a heat event bringing blistering temperatures to some locations. After a relatively mild Labor Day weekend, the Golden State is forecast to see a warm-up beginning Tuesday and continuing into Friday as high pressure builds. Downtown Los Angeles is predicted to get into the 90s next week and Palm Springs into the 110s, according to the National Weather Service.This heat wave is not expected to be as severe
