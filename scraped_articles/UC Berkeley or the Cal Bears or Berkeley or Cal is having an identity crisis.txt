UC Berkeley, or the Cal Bears, or Berkeley, or Cal, is having an identity crisis
-----
UC Berkeley, or the Cal Bears, or Berkeley, or Cal, is having an identity crisis.Apparently, there has been some confusion about what, precisely, to call the legendary educational institution, particularly as it relates to Cal athletics.
According to an unintentionally amusing but brilliantly compelling article by Nanette Asimov of the Chronicle, this confusion was spawned by athletes and fans calling the athletic teams "Cal," which, of course, could be confusing to some. Maybe.
