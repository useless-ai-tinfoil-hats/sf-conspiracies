California Highway Patrol investigating car crash on I-80 in Berkeley that injured 1
-----
A person was transported to a local hospital Friday after a car crash in Berkeley, according to the California Highway Patrol.Officers received a report at 4:49 p.m. about a car crash with injuries on the Ashby Avenue on-ramp to eastbound Interstate 80, authorities said. Other 911 callers told CHP that a person in the vehicle, a silver Toyota Camry, had been shot by a person in another car just before the crash occurred. CHP said it could not confirm reports of the shooting.
