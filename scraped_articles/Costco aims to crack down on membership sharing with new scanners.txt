Costco aims to crack down on membership sharing with new scanners
-----
Costco has unveiled a new feature at store entrances in its latest effort to crack down on membership sharing.Select stores across the US have rolled out new entry scanners that replace the routine of customers flashing their membership cards to Costco employees. A viral Reddit post shared earlier this month showed a glimpse of a new scanner at the main entrance of the Issaquah, Washington, Costco location.
