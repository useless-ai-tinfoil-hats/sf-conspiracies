Red Lobsters across the U.S. abruptly closed, including 2 in Bay Area
-----
Some Bay Area fans of Red Lobster will have to travel farther to get their lobster and shrimp specials after two of the chain restaurants abruptly closed on Monday.The closures of the Red Lobster restaurants in Rohnert Park and Fremont were part of a wave of closures around the U.S. More than 50 of the seafood restaurants closed suddenly on Monday as the chain faces possible bankruptcy, according to a UPI report.
