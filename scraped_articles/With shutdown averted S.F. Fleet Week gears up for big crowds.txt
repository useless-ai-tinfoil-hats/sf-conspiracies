With shutdown averted, S.F. Fleet Week gears up for big crowds
-----
With a federal government shutdown averted for now, preparations kicked into high gear Sunday for Fleet Week, San Francisco's celebration of the armed forces.The annual event runs Oct. 2-10 this year with events including ship tours, a neighborhood concert series and a veterans art exhibition, culminating with the flying of the U.S. Navy's Blue Angels next weekend. The activities draw large crowds of locals and visitors to honor the nation's service members.
