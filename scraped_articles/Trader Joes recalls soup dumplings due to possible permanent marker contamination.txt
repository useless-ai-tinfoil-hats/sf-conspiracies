Trader Joe’s recalls soup dumplings due to possible ‘permanent marker’ contamination
-----
Trader Joe's has issued a recall for approximately 62,000 pounds of its frozen chicken soup dumplings due to the possible presence of foreign materials, "specifically hard plastic from a permanent marker pen," according to federal regulators.The recall affects "Trader Joe's Steamed Chicken Soup Dumplings" with a best-by date of March 7, 2025, the retail chain said in an announcement.
