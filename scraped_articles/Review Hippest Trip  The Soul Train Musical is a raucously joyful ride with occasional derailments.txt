Review: ‘Hippest Trip — The Soul Train Musical’ is a raucously joyful ride, with occasional derailments
-----
It's the damndest thing: a musical that resists its own songs.Actors seem hungry to keep singing. Audience members, recognizing period hits by the Commodores, Sister Sledge and more, are ready to get up and dance. But "Hippest Trip — The Soul Train Musical" keeps teasing little snippets of numbers only to cut them off. Over and over again, a performer starts kindling a fire, then poof! We're left with fumes.
