This home in a historic SF neighborhood just hit the market for $5.5 million
-----
The historic, posh San Francisco neighborhood of St. Francis Wood has a home listed for sale at the highest price per square foot the historic neighborhood has ever seen.A home in San Francisco's historic St. Francis Wood at 55 Santa Paula Ave. just listed for $5.5 million, a standout price for the neighborhood. At $2,011 per square foot, it sets a new record for the highest price per square foot in St. Francis Wood since at least the year 2000, according to real estate agents.
