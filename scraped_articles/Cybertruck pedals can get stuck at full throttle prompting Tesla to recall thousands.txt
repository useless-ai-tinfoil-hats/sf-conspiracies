Cybertruck pedals can get stuck at 'full throttle,' prompting Tesla to recall thousands
-----
First, Cybertruck owners were noticing orange rust marks on their expensive new Tesla vehicles. Then it emerged that the "apocalypse-proof" truck couldn't drive over sand. And now, there's a much more pressing issue: Cybertrucks' accelerator pedals are prone to getting stuck.Tesla is recalling the approximately 3,880 Cybertrucks it manufactured from Nov. 13, 2023, to April 4 to repair or replace their accelerator pedal assemblies, the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration
