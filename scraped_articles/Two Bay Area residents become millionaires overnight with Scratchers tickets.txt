Two Bay Area residents become millionaires overnight with Scratchers tickets
-----
Two people from the Bay Area won big on Scratchers tickets this week, making them millionaires overnight, according to California Lottery officials.Vahidin Arnaut won $10 million with a $30 Bonus Scratchers ticket he bought at a Shell gas station on Winchester Boulevard in San Jose.
Meanwhile, Mugdim Dedic scored $2 million on a $20 Instant Prize Crossword Scratchers ticket from a 76 station on Webster Street in Alameda.
