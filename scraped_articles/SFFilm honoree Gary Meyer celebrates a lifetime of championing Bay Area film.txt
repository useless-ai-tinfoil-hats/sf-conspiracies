SFFilm honoree Gary Meyer celebrates a lifetime of championing Bay Area film
-----
When the longest student-led strike in the history of American higher education began in November 1968 at San Francisco State, Gary Meyer was quietly waging his own brand of revolution.Meyer, a student at what was then San Francisco State College, was a film production major, a projectionist at the school's media center and prominent in several campus film clubs.
