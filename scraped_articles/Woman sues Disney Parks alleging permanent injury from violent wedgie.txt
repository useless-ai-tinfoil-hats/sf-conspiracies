Woman sues Disney Parks alleging permanent injury from violent ‘wedgie’
-----
A woman is suing Disney Parks and Resorts alleging a violent "wedgie" left her with permanent injuries. According to a civil suit filed in Orange County, Florida, last week, the woman suffered "severe" lacerations and "damage to her internal organs" after visiting Disney's Typhoon Lagoon water park in Orlando, Florida.Plaintiff Emma McGuinness says her family was visiting Disney World for a two-week vacation in October 2019 as part of her 30th birthday celebration.
