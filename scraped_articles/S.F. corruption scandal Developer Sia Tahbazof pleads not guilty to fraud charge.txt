S.F. corruption scandal: Developer Sia Tahbazof pleads not guilty to fraud charge
-----
A multimillionaire developer and his nephew who are both accused of bribing building inspectors and plan checkers with cash, meals and interest-free loans pleaded not guilty to fraud charges in federal court Friday.Siavash "Sia" Tahbazof, 72, was charged Nov. 9 with one count of conspiracy to commit wire fraud, as were his business partner, Reza Khoshnevisan and his nephew, Bahman Ghassemzadeh.
