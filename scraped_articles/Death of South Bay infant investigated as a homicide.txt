Death of South Bay infant investigated as a homicide
-----
The death of a 2-month-old this spring is being investigated as a homicide, San Jose police said Tuesday.Officers were called to a hospital on April 4 to investigate the death and interviewed the child's parents, the Police Department said in a statement.
No arrests have been made, and further details surrounding the death remained under investigation, police officials said.
