Elon Musk announces fresh layoffs at Tesla, going ‘hard core about headcount’
-----
Elon Musk reportedly enacted a fresh round of layoffs at Tesla on Tuesday, cutting jobs that included the entire supercharger team, telling senior managers "we need to be absolutely hard core about headcount and cost reduction."After expressing dissatisfaction with declining sales and slow-paced downsizing efforts in an email, the CEO terminated two executives: Rebecca Tinucci, senior director of Tesla's Supercharger division, and Daniel Ho, head of the new vehicles program.
