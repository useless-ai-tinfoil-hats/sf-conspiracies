Walmart employee arriving to work at Bay Area store fatally strikes woman in parking lot
-----
A Walmart employee arriving to work at the Mountain View store fatally struck a woman in the parking lot Monday morning, police said.Officers were dispatched to the Walmart located on 600 Showers Drive after reports of the collision around 6 a.m. Officers at the scene learned that the woman, who police said was in her 60s, was in the middle of the parking lot when the Walmart employee arriving to work ran her over.
The woman was rushed to a local hospital where she later died,
