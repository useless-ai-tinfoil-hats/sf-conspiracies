CVS to shutter another downtown S.F. pharmacy as it cuts 900 locations
-----
CVS is closing another downtown San Francisco pharmacy, this one at 601 Mission St., as it slashes 900 stores across the company.The South of Market store will close on Oct. 23 and prescriptions will be transferred to the CVS at 701 Van Ness Ave., a company spokesperson said. Employees at the location are being offered comparable roles elsewhere at the company.
