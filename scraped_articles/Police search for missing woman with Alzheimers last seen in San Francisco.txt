Police search for missing woman with Alzheimer’s, last seen in San Francisco
-----
Police are searching for a missing 78-year-old woman with Alzheimer's disease, according to the San Francisco Police Department.Carmen Aleman has been missing since Tuesday and authorities consider her "at risk" because of her condition. She was last seen at 5:30 a.m. Tuesday on the 100 block of Lexington Street, police said.
