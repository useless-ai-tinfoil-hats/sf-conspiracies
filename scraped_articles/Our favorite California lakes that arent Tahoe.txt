Our favorite California lakes that aren't Tahoe
-----
Lake Tahoe provides an iconic California lake experience: waterside restaurants, boating and beaches, with plenty of places to swim and wade. It's perhaps so iconic that it has become too crowded and chaotic for visitors to fully enjoy a day at the lake. Plus, the long drive through the mountains to Lake Tahoe on the weekends is no joke.We've compiled a list of 15 lakes worth visiting — five in each region of California — so those tired of the crowds and the drive can find a new favorite
