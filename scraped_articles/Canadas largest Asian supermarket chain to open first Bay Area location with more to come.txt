Canada’s largest Asian supermarket chain to open first Bay Area location, with more to come
-----
Rapidly-expanding Asian grocery chain T&T Supermarket is breaking into the Bay Area's retail scene.The company, long a staple in Canada, announced Thursday that it will open its first California location at Westgate Center in San Jose, at Saratoga Avenue and Campbell Avenue.
