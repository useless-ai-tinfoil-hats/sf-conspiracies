49ers bring down Giants 30-12 behind big games by Deebo Samuel, Brock Purdy
-----
Quarterback Brock Purdy threw for a career-high 310 yards, running back Christian McCaffrey joined Jerry Rice in the franchise record book, wide receiver Deebo Samuel had a jaw-dropping, tackle-busting performance and the San Francisco 49ers eventually dismissed a pesky underdog in a 30-12 win against the Giants on Thursday night at Levi's Stadium.The 49ers, 10.5-point favorites who improved to 3-0 for just the third time since 1998, dominated New York (1-2) statistically.
