Jon Stewart roasts Bay Area tech CEOs, says Apple stopped him from interviewing critic
-----
Jon Stewart skewered his former boss Apple on Monday's episode of "The Daily Show."The comedian and talk show host devoted much of the new episode to the tech industry. He began with a segment on artificial intelligence, then interviewed Federal Trade Commission Chairwoman Lina Khan. During his discussion with Khan, who has gained notoriety for bringing an antitrust lawsuit against Amazon, he shed some light on his canceled Apple TV+ show, "The Problem With Jon Stewart."
