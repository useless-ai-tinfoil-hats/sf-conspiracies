Kamala Harris, Donald Trump to host fundraisers in San Francisco this week
-----
Vice President and former San Francisco District Attorney Kamala Harris and former President Donald Trump are expected to be in San Francisco this week to attend separate fundraisers one day apart.Politico reported that the Harris fundraiser on Wednesday is scheduled be held at a venue near Manny's Cafe in the city's Mission District. Manny Yekutiel, the cafe's owner, is hosting the event along with "mega Democratic donors" Shannon Hunt-Scott, Stacy Mason and Sheila Thompson, the outlet
