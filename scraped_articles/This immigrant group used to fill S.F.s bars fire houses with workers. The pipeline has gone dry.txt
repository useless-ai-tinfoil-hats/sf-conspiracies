This immigrant group used to fill S.F.’s bars, fire houses with workers. The pipeline has gone dry
-----
Back in 2008, when bar owner Brian Sheehy had just three San Francisco drinking establishments, nearly half of his 30 workers were immigrants from Ireland — transplants from counties ranging from Derry to Limerick, Cork to Carlow.Now, the company, Future Bars Group, owns 13 bars with 220 employees. None but Sheehy, who grew up on a farm in County Kerry, speaks with a brogue.
