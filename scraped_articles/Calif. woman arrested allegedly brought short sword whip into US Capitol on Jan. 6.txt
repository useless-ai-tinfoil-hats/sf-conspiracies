Calif. woman arrested, allegedly brought short sword, whip into US Capitol on Jan. 6
-----
A California woman has been charged with bringing several weapons — including a "short sword" and a "tactical whip" — into the U.S. Capitol Building on Jan. 6, 2021, according to an FBI affidavit.The woman, Kennedy Lindsey, took a flight from Los Angeles to Washington, D.C., sometime after Dec. 19, 2020, when then-President Donald Trump used social media to urge his supporters to be in Washington on Jan. 6 for a "big protest." "Be there, will be wild!" Trump wrote.
