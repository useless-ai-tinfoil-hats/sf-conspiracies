Man arrested on suspicion of robbing Peninsula Whole Foods cashier after shopper followed him home
-----
Redwood City police arrested a man accused of robbing a Whole Foods cashier.Police said Juan Linares Cesena, 32, entered a Whole Foods store on Tuesday and demanded that a cashier hand over money from the cash register "while posturing and insinuating that he was armed with a gun," officials said in a statement.
The cashier complied and Cesena fled the store, police said. Another Whole Foods customer followed Cesena to an apartment on Vera Avenue and called police, officials said.
