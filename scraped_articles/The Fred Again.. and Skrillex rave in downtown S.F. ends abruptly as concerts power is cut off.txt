The Fred Again.. and Skrillex ‘rave’ in downtown S.F. ends abruptly as concert’s power is cut off
-----
Superstar DJs Fred Again.. and Skrillex's last-minute Civic Center Plaza "rave" came to an abrupt end on Saturday, June 1, after the duo hit the city's 10 p.m. curfew.The two were performing mid-song when the audio stopped. The music ended, but the pair remained on stage waving to the crowd and garnering cheers for five minutes afterwards, providing the San Francisco audience a proper farewell.
