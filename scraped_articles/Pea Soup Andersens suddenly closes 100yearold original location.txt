Pea Soup Andersen's suddenly closes 100-year-old original location
-----
An eye-catching landmark restaurant known by road trippers has closed without warning.Pea Soup Andersen's original location in the Central Coast town of Buellton (Santa Barbara County) is now closed after almost 100 years of serving its famous split pea soup and Danish-style pumpernickel.
