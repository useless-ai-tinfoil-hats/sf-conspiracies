Magnitude 4.2 earthquake strikes North Bay
-----
A magnitude 4.2 earthquake struck in Lake County on Friday afternoon, giving the far North Bay a shake, the U.S. Geological Survey said.The quake hit at 1:28 p.m. with a depth of 1.4 miles nearly 4 miles northwest of Cobb, a community just south of Clear Lake. The epicenter was near the Geysers geothermal field, which includes several geothermal power plants.
