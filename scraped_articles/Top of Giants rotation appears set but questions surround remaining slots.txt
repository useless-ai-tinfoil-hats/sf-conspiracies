Top of Giants’ rotation appears set, but questions surround remaining slots
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz — The San Francisco Giants' rotation still has two open spots with just more than two weeks left in the Cactus League.The first three spots appear settled. Logan Webb is the No. 1-apparent after a Cy Young-caliber 2023 season. For now, the next two spots belong to rookie Kyle Harrison and converted reliever Jordan Hicks, both with plenty to prove still as consistent starters as the organization pushes for its starters to pitch deeper into games.
