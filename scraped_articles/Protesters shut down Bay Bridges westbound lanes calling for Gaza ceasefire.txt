Protesters shut down Bay Bridge's westbound lanes, calling for Gaza ceasefire
-----
Protesters calling for a ceasefire in Gaza shut down the westbound lanes of the Bay Bridge Thursday morning, just east of Treasure Island, California Highway Patrol said.CHP said the demonstration unfolded shortly before 8 a.m. and that there was no estimated time of reopening.
