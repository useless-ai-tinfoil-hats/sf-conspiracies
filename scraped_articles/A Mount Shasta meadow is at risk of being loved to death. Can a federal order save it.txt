A Mount Shasta meadow is at risk of being ‘loved to death.’ Can a federal order save it?
-----
An idyllic meadow on Mount Shasta that some people feel is at risk of being "loved to death" by tourists was brought under new restrictions this month to curb visitation and bad behavior and help the environment recover.Panther Meadows, a lush and remote landscape of waist-high wildflowers and bubbling springs on Shasta's southern flank, is sacred to the native Winnemem Wintu tribe.
