Woman rescued after spending 15 hours in an East Bay creek
-----
A woman was rescued from a creek Tuesday morning after spending the night sitting on the top of her truck to stay out of the water, according to officials.The Alameda County Fire Department got the call about a woman stuck in Del Valle Creek in Livermore around 10 a.m. Tuesday, 15 hours after officials believe she was swept into the water, according to a spokesperson for the department.
