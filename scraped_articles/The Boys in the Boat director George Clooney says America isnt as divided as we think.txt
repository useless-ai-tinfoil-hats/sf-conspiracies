‘The Boys in the Boat’ director George Clooney says America isn’t as divided as we think
-----
It might seem like "The Boys in the Boat" is an old-fashioned tale, a longing for a simpler time. But director George Clooney asserts the message of his film, about the University of Washington rowing team surmounting long odds during the Great Depression to win gold at Adolf Hitler's 1936 Berlin Olympics, is quite modern."The themes aren't completely antiquated, if you think about it," Clooney told the Chronicle in a recent video interview from Los Angeles.
