The estate that pizza built: Papa Murphy's founders' massive $12.5M Bay Area home is for sale
-----
An ode to the power of pizza, 11 Mount Rose Ln. commands almost 35 acres in the hills of west Petaluma. Custom-designed for Terrance and Rose Collins, who founded Papa Murphy's, a once-modest business venture that grew into one of the world's most successful take-and-bake pizza chains, the estate is on the open market for the first time at $12.5 million.Whether seen from above or close up, the property has the effect of a European country castle tucked into the sunny mountains of Sonoma
