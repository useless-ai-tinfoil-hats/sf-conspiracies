All charges dropped against 1 of 4 men tied to killing of Oakland police officer
-----
The Alameda County District Attorney's Office on Wednesday dismissed all charges against one of four men accused in connection with the December killing of Oakland police officer Tuan Le, according to court records.Marquise Cooper, 35, and three others were initially charged with murder, burglary and other crimes tied to the fatal shooting on Dec. 29, which occurred after Le and other officers responded to a burglary at a marijuana business.
