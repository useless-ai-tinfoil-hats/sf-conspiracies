Three dead after early morning Bay Bridge crash
-----
Three people were killed early Thursday morning in a crash on the Bay Bridge, a San Francisco Fire Department official said.San Francisco emergency personnel were dispatched to a reported car crash on the bridge's lower level eastbound lanes shortly before 4:30 a.m. Thursday, according to San Francisco fire Lt. Mariano Elias.
The driver of a white Toyota Tacoma pickup truck rear-ended a red Mini Cooper that was stopped in the fourth lane just before the tunnel, officials said.
