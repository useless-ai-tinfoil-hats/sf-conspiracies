Golden State Warriors gear up for Olivia Rodrigo's ‘Guts’ tour stop in style
-----
The Golden State Warriors have found a new friend in Olivia Rodrigo — at least according to a video showing the NBA players sporting bracelets in anticipation of her summer San Francisco tour stop.In a video posted to Chase Center's TikTok account on Wednesday, April 24, several players are shown being handed bracelets in the pop singer's signature purple with phrases such as "Warriors," "Dubs" and "Guts" on them.
"Olivia Rodrigo + Warriors = Besties," the caption reads.
