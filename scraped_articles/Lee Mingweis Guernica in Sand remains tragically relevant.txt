Lee Mingwei’s ‘Guernica in Sand’ remains tragically relevant
-----
Lee Mingwei's "Guernica in Sand" is now gone from his exhibition "Rituals of Care" at the de Young Museum, but the questions it inspired have remained with me.After first viewing the sand painting recreating Picasso's famous anti-war masterpiece "Guernica" (named for the Basque village destroyed by Nazi bombs in April 1937), I thought of the war in Ukraine, the Israel-Hamas conflict and any number of other violent incursions that make this an eternally relevant work.
