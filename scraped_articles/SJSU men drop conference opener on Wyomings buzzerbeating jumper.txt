SJSU men drop conference opener on Wyoming’s buzzer-beating jumper
-----
San Jose State's men's basketball team lost its Mountain West Conference opener Tuesday when Wyoming's Akuel Kot sank an 18-foot jumper at the buzzer to give the host Cowboys a 75-73 victory.Kot's basket was the only scoring by either team in the game's final 2½ minutes. San Jose State missed two 3-point tries and committed a turnover in the final 147 seconds after tying the score on Alvaro Cardenas' layup. Kot had missed a layup with 43 seconds to play before hitting the game-winner.
