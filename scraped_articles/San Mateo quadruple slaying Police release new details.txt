San Mateo quadruple slaying: Police release new details
-----
San Mateo police detectives said Tuesday that the man and woman found dead in a San Mateo home on Monday died from gunshot wounds.What caused the death of the two children also found dead inside the home remained under investigation, police said Tuesday.
Police officers were dispatched to the 4100 block of Alameda de las Pulgas on Monday after 9 a.m. after receiving a welfare check request, spokespersons said. Police did not say Tuesday who requested the welfare check.
