Historic sequoias survive prescribed burn in California's Calaveras Big Trees State Park
-----
Twin sequoias, fondly known as "The Orphans," in Calaveras Big Trees State Park were in danger after they were scorched by a prescribed burn in October 2022. Experts and nature enthusiasts worried whether they would survive.Now, the park says that the trees are not only alive, they might not be without family for much longer.
