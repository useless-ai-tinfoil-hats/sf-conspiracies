Marin fashion designer Simon Ungless on his new chapter with Angelina Jolie
-----
On the deck of his tree-shaded home in Larkspur, Simon Ungless scraped black ink through a silk printing screen onto a white coat. The designer, clad in a splatter-pattern hoodie and camouflage pants, made for a striking juxtaposition as he executed his punk-tinged art form amid the tranquility of Marin."The whole point of this print is that it looks run over," said Ungless, the former director of the School of Fashion at the Academy of Art University in San Francisco.
