What does APEC mean for S.F.’s Chinese American community?
-----
San Francisco's Chinese American leaders are hopeful that the global spotlight during the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit boosts the city's image as the gateway to Asia — even as daily protests underscore the many controversies surrounding the conference and region."It is putting San Francisco in the spotlight as an international hub," said Supervisor Connie Chan, the only Asian American member of the Board of Supervisors.
