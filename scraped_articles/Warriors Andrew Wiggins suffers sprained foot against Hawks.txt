Warriors’ Andrew Wiggins suffers sprained foot against Hawks
-----
ATLANTA — Golden State Warriors wing Andrew Wiggins exited Saturday's game against the Atlanta Hawks with a left foot injury and did not return.After the 141-134 loss, head coach Steve Kerr said Wiggins sprained his foot and that X-rays were negative.
