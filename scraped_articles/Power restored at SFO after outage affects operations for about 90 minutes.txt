Power restored at SFO after outage affects operations for about 90 minutes
-----
Power was restored at San Francisco International Airport after a partial outage shut down multiple terminals Wednesday morning.The outage was first reported just after 6 a.m. and impacted parts of Terminals 1 and 3 and SFO's International Terminal, causing a temporary ground stop and flight delays.
Crews responded to the incident, and by 7:37 a.m., power had been restored, according to an update posted on SFO's X account at that time.
