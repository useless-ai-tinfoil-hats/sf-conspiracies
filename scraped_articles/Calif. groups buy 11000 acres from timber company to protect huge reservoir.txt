Calif. groups buy 11,000 acres from timber company to protect huge reservoir
-----
A San Francisco nonprofit has purchased 11,000 acres of land at the headwaters of the Trinity River from a timber company, aimed at protecting the source of the massive Trinity Reservoir.The Pacific Forest Trust spent $15.5 million on the checkerboard of properties before transferring their ownership to a local nonprofit, the Watershed Research and Training Center, per an Aug. 5 press release.
