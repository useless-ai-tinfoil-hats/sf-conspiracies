Bay Area postal workers are being robbed of essential keys. Thousands are now at risk
-----
Keys granting access to mailboxes inside apartment complexes and postal collection boxes were stolen in San Jose last month when robbers attacked a U.S. Postal Service mail carrier at gunpoint, a security risk that could affect thousands of people in the city, officials said.It's the latest example of what officials called a growing threat to mail carriers that continues to jeopardize mail and package deliveries across the region.
