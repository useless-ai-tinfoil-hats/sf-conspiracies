Pilot reportedly ‘horrified’ after alleged attempt to crash SFO-bound jet
-----
In his first interview after allegedly trying to crash a plane bound for San Francisco, an Alaska Airlines pilot described to the New York Times how the choice to take mushrooms while mourning a friend led to the moment where he nearly cut off fuel to both of the plane's engines.On Oct. 22, federal prosecutors allege Joseph David Emerson, a 44-year-old pilot from Pleasant Hill, was under the influence of psychedelic mushrooms as he boarded a flight headed from Oregon to San Francisco.
