Popular Calif. beach to close for 7 years to protect vulnerable sea lions
-----
La Jolla Beach in San Diego is known for its beauty and rugged rocks. But what makes it even more remarkable is the sea lion population that's chosen Point La Jolla to breed and nurse. However, the species often come in close contact with humans at the popular beach.Continuing their efforts to protect the sea lion population, the San Diego City Council unanimously voted Sept. 18 to close the area to the public, according to La Jolla Light.
