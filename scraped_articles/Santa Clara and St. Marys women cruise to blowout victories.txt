Santa Clara and St. Mary's women cruise to blowout victories
-----
The Santa Clara women's basketball team scored the game's first 17 points, held Cal State Northridge to 10 or fewer points in three quarters and cruised to a 71-39 victory at Leavey Center on Tuesday night.The Broncos (3-1) led by 15 after the first quarter, 21 at halftime and 28 after three.
Tess Heal scored a game-high 19 points — and made 4 of 5 3-point tries — to lead Santa Clara. The Broncos shot better from distance (11-for-28, 39.3%) than they did on 2-point shots
