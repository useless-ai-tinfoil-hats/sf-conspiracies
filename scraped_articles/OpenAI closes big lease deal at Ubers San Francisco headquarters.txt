OpenAI closes big lease deal at Uber’s San Francisco headquarters
-----
A closely watched lease deal between a ride-hailing behemoth and a fast-growing company that is at the forefront of San Francisco's burgeoning artificial intelligence boom has made it over the finish line.A spokesperson for Uber confirmed on Thursday that it has leased two of four buildings that make up its Mission Bay headquarters campus to OpenAI, the maker of ChatGPT and DALL-E 2.
