Woman sues Berkeley fraternity after falling from roof during party
-----
A young woman who fell off the roof of a Berkeley fraternity is suing, accusing the fraternity chapter of negligence. Jasmine Wang, who suffered serious injuries in the 2023 fall, filed the lawsuit in Alameda County on Aug. 16, a suit first reported on by the Daily Californian.On April 22, 2023, Wang attended a Cal Day party at Delta Kappa Epsilon.
