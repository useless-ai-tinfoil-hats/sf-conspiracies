LA’s Hollywood sign used to actually have a swimming pool
-----
Los Angeles is a city filled with famous faces and places, though perhaps none more so than the historic Hollywood sign that hangs in the hills above it all. The 50-foot white HOLLYWOOD block letters have been immortalized in popular culture the world over, standing in as a backdrop for an actual place — that being Hollywood, the neighborhood directly beneath the sign — and a pop culture vision of the sprawling, glamorous film and television industry that keeps the world entertained.
