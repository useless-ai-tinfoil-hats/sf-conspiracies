S.F. school board explores alternatives to district elections after lawsuit threat
-----
San Francisco schools could face a potentially costly and distracting lawsuit after making a last-minute decision Tuesday night to spurn a Bay Area's attorney's demand to shift from citywide to district elections in choosing a school board.Instead, the board voted 6-1 to look at moving to district elections or another method that would still satisfy the rules of the California Voting Rights Act.
