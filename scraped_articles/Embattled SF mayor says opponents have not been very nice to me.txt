Embattled SF mayor says opponents 'have not been very nice to me'
-----
As far as political debates go, the first showdown between San Francisco's major mayoral candidates was relatively tame.The sold-out event at the Sydney Goldstein Theater on Wednesday evening marked the first time those five candidates — Mayor London Breed, Board of Supervisors President Aaron Peskin, former Supervisor and interim Mayor Mark Farrell, local nonprofit leader and Levi Strauss heir Daniel Lurie and District 11 Supervisor Ahsha Safaí — appeared together in a formal debate
