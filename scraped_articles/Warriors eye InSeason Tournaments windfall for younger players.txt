Warriors eye In-Season Tournament's windfall for younger players
-----
Show them the money.That's probably the abiding motive for NBA players in the league's inaugural In-Season Tournament. The Golden State Warriors enter Tuesday night's game in Sacramento with a chance to advance to the quarterfinal "knockout" round, and head coach Steve Kerr offered an honest response when asked about his team's incentive.
