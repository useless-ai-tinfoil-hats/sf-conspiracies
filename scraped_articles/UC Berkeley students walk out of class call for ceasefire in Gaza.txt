UC Berkeley students walk out of class, call for cease-fire in Gaza
-----
Waving placards and filling the air with cheers, hundreds of students walked out of their classes at UC Berkeley on Wednesday to call for a cease-fire in Gaza and an end to military support for Israel.Amid chants of "Free Palestine" and "Occupation no more," dozens of speakers exhorted the crowd in English and Arabic to urge the university to divest itself from companies such as Lockheed Martin that manufacture arms they said wind up in Israel.
