San Francisco has a new nightlife trend. It smells.
-----
At his Castro apartment, DIY perfumer David Le Brun stands in front of an olfactory operation that would make most chemists blush: an armoire crammed with dark glass vials of plant extracts and chemical isolates.Le Brun dips a paper swab into one of the vials and then hands it to me. It smells woody, but I also catch whiffs of sweat, smoke and leather. This is oud, an extremely rare tree resin and one of the world's most expensive fragrance materials.
