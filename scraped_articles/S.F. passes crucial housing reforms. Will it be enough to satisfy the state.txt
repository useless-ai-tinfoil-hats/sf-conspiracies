S.F. passes crucial housing reforms. Will it be enough to satisfy the state?
-----
Critical housing legislation was OK'd on Tuesday in San Francisco as the city tries to avoid losing control over approvals of residential projects. Key funding for housing and transportation was also at risk if officials didn't reform the city's cumbersome process.State officials gave the city 30 days from Oct. 25 to approve legislation from Mayor London Breed that would streamline the notoriously long and complicated process to get homes approved and built.
