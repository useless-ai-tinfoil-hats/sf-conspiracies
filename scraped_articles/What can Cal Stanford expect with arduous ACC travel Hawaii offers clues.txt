What can Cal, Stanford expect with arduous ACC travel? Hawaii offers clues
-----
In a college-sports landscape rife with seven-figure NIL deals and state-of-the-art facilities, new Hawaii athletic director Craig Angelos has a relatively modest goal: securing charter flights to bring the Rainbow Warriors' football team back home from away games."Having to fly commercial is a real killer for us," Angelos said in a recent phone interview. "Life would be a lot easier if we could at least fly charter on the return leg."
