SF Porchfest, San Francisco's tiniest music festival, returns to the Mission on Saturday
-----
San Francisco's tiniest music festival returns Saturday, with dozens of acts lined up to play at SF Porchfest 2024. As the name suggests, artists perform from city porches, allowing any passersby to attend the free festival.Nine porch stages are scheduled to run mostly simultaneously from noon to 6 p.m. The porches are all in the Mission District, with the majority in a tight cluster between Mission and Guerrero streets and 20th and 22nd streets.
