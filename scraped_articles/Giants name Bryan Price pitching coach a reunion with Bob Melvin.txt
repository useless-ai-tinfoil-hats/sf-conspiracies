Giants name Bryan Price pitching coach, a reunion with Bob Melvin
-----
Bob Melvin and Bryan Price, two Bay Area natives and Cal alums who worked together for a combined six seasons with the Mariners and Diamondbacks, have reunited with the San Francisco Giants.On Tuesday, the Giants announced Price will be the pitching coach on Melvin's staff. Price replaces Andrew Bailey, who had been pitching coach the past four seasons under former manager Gabe Kapler.
Melvin, 62, went to Menlo-Atherton High and Price, 61, is a Tamalpais-Mill Valley alum.
