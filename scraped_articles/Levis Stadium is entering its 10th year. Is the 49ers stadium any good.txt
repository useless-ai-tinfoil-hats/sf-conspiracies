Levi's Stadium is entering its 10th year. Is the 49ers' stadium any good?
-----
On my worst day as a 49ers fan, I blamed Levi's Stadium.It was Nov. 20, 2016, and the team was about to endure its ninth loss in a row under the stewardship of way-over-his-head coach Chip Kelly. During a rainy halftime, the team honored beloved former owner Eddie DeBartolo. The stands were maybe one-eighth full to thank a man who brought the team — and arguably all of San Francisco — back to greatness with five Super Bowl wins.
