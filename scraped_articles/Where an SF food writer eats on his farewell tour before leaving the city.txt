Where an SF food writer eats on his farewell tour before leaving the city
-----
When famed food writer Kee Byung-keun left New Orleans, a dream team of friends joined him for an ultimate sprint through the city's restaurants, like the seven samurai assembling for a doomed final battle. Win or lose, boot or rally, it's the end.I read the story years ago in now-defunct Lucky Peach, a publication of San Francisco's McSweeney's. I used to linger on that article, used to wonder if I'd have a city that meant as much to me as the Big Easy did to Byung-keun.
