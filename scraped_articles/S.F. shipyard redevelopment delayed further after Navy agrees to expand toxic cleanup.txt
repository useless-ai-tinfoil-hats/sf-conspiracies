S.F. shipyard redevelopment delayed further after Navy agrees to expand toxic cleanup
-----
The U.S. Navy will expand an ongoing toxic cleanup of the 500-acre San Francisco Shipyard project at the site of the former Hunters Point Naval Shipyard on two land parcels where soil sampling unearthed radioactive objects last year, a Navy spokesperson confirmed on Thursday.The new plan is expected to delay the former shipyard's cleanup by as many as 18 months.
