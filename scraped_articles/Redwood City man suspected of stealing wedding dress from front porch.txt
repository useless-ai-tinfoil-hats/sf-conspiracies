Redwood City man suspected of stealing wedding dress from front porch
-----
A Redwood City man was arrested on suspicion of stealing a wedding dress valued at $2,000 from a resident's porch this month, police said Thursday.Police released video of a person apparently grabbing a package containing the dress from a porch on the 800 block of Adams Street in Redwood City in an effort to solicit the public's help to track down the alleged thief.
