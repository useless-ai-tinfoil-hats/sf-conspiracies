How Alex Loera’s season-ending injury will affect Bay FC’s struggling defense
-----
Bay FC's first-ever player is done for the year.Alex Loera tore her ACL in Bay FC's 5-2 loss in Kansas City, Mo., on Saturday, she posted on her social media late Sunday night. She was injured in the 31st minute and immediately waved to trainers to be helped off the pitch.
The captain in Bay FC's first match at Angel City, Loera has been the starting pivot midfielder in four of five matches, missing only the match at Washington when she was out for personal reasons.
