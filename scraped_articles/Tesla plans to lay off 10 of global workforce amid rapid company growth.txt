Tesla plans to lay off 10% of global workforce amid 'rapid' company growth
-----
Tesla CEO Elon Musk said in an internal companywide email that the electric carmaker would be laying off 10% of its global workforce to reduce costs, according to multiple news outlets."There is nothing I hate more, but it must be done," Musk said of the layoffs in the memo, first reported by Electrek, an American news outlet that covers electric transportation. "This will enable us to be lean, innovative and hungry for the next growth phase cycle."
CNBC confirmed Electrek's report
