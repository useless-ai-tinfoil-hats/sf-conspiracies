Section of Bay Area highway closes due to ‘hot asphalt oil slurry’ spill
-----
LATEST Sept. 6, 2:30 p.m. The California Highway Patrol said as of 2:20 p.m. westbound Interstate 780 at East Second Street in Benicia remains closed after a tanker overturned in a collision.The tanker spilled "hot asphalt oil slurry" across all westbound lanes, Solano County's Department of Resource Management, Environmental Health Division, told SFGATE.
Traffic is being directed off I-780 at Fifth Street; it's unknown when the stretch of freeway will reopen, CHP said.
