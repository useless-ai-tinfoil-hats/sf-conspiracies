'What are those?': Lasers shooting across SF's sky don't seem real
-----
People gathered at the top of Dolores Park on Monday night pulled out their phones to capture photos of the giant, glowing lasers shooting across the San Francisco skyline. The view depicted a scene that didn't even seem real."What are those?" people were asking.
