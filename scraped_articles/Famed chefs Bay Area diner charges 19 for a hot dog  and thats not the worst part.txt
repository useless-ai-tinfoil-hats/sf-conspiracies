Famed chef’s Bay Area diner charges $19 for a hot dog — and that’s not the worst part
-----
A $19 hot dog is already obscene, but it's especially insulting when it's also a chore to eat. This unholy creation comes from famed barbecue chef Matt Horn's new Oakland restaurant, which sucks all the fun out of the dish in an attempt to elevate it.The bun was so small it only reached half of the frankfurter, making it a logistical nightmare. An utterly bizarre garnish of floppy cucumbers, cut lengthwise, kept spilling over. It made me long for Costco.
