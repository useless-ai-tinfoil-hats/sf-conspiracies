Longtime Oakland business owner nearing retirement killed in his own auto shop
-----
Aristeo Zambrano's family worried constantly about his safety.For more than three decades, Zambrano ran an auto repair shop in a neighborhood plagued by frequent robberies and shootings.
Zambrano, a 68-year-old farmworker turned business owner, was shot and killed while working at his shop, Bay City Alternators, on International Boulevard near 88th Avenue shortly before noon on Feb. 3.
