Robert Allen, academic and author of ‘The Port Chicago Mutiny,’ dies at 82
-----
Robert Allen published "The Port Chicago Mutiny," his nonfiction account of the deadly World War II munitions explosion on Suisun Bay, in 1989.He then waited for the U.S. Navy to follow the story by exonerating 258 sailors who were court-martialed and convicted of mutiny for refusing to go back to work in 1944 loading supply ships at a naval ammunition base 6 miles north of Concord.
