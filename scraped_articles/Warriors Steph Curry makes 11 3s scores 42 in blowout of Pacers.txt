Warriors’ Steph Curry makes 11 3s, scores 42 in blowout of Pacers
-----
INDIANAPOLIS — Stephen Curry already had made his first five 3-pointers when the Indiana Pacers offered him a second defender at the top of the key.So as comfortably as a couched-out San Francisco 49ers fan Sunday, the 35-year-old guard rocked his dribble back and forth — ignoring the double-team as if it didn't exist — before burying a 30-footer.
And with it, the Pacers — as the Golden State Warriors would for three more quarters Thursday night.
