Person dead in Oakland after shooting in Prescott neighborhood
-----
Gunfire killed a person in Oakland's Prescott neighborhood Sunday evening, police said.Oakland police officers discovered the person suffering from gunshot wounds on the 1700 block of 11th Street around 6 p.m., spokesperson Rosalia Lopez said. The shooting is being investigated as a homicide.

Paramedics pronounced the victim dead, Lopez said. Police were working to notify family members Sunday evening.
