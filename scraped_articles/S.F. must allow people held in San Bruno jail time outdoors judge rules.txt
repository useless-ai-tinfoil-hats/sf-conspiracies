S.F. must allow people held in San Bruno jail time outdoors, judge rules
-----
San Francisco has violated the rights of people held at its jail in San Bruno by keeping them indoors all day and must allow some of them at least 15 minutes a day of access to sunlight, a federal magistrate ruled Tuesday.The 768-bed jail holds men who are held before trial, some for many years.
