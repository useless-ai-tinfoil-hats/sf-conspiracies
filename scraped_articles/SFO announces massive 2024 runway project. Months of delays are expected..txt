SFO announces massive 2024 runway project. Months of delays are expected.
-----
The runways at San Francisco International Airport are sinking, and last year the Bay Area's largest airport closed its shortest runway for a repaving job, but SFO's most extensive improvement project in years is about to begin.The airport announced Monday its plan to close one of its longest runways for five months starting in January 2024. The airport warns travelers that the closure will trigger delays of up to an hour for a third of all its flights during the construction period,
