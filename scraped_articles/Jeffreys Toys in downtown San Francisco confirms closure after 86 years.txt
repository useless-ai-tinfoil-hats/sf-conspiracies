Jeffrey's Toys in downtown San Francisco confirms closure after 86 years
-----
The San Francisco toy shop that inspired the adventures of Woody and Buzz Lightyear in Pixar's "Toy Story," and claimed to be the oldest store of its kind in the city, announced it will be permanently closing in the next couple of weeks after more than 85 years in business.Jeffrey's Toys "had this real magical thing" about it, co-owner Matthew Luhn told SFGATE in an interview last month.
