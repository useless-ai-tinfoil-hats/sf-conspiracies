A pair of mountaineers reported missing after climbing Mount Whitney last week were found dead by searchers and recovered from the peak on a day later, according to the Inyo County Sheriff's Office.The two climbers ascended and summited the peak on the morning of May 7 via the mountaineer's route, according to a Facebook post from a third climbing partner. But the three climbers became separated on the descent down a perilously steep section near the mountaintop known as the Final 400.
