Selected Shorts: The Litquake Edition!
-----
Join Litquake and host Andrew Sean Greer for an unforgettable night of stories live onstage, with special guest readers Tate Donovan, Baron Vaughn, and Michaela Watkins. Selected Shorts is a radio show and podcast broadcast on more than 150 stations around the country. The series began in 1985 and enjoyed sold-out performances to this day.
