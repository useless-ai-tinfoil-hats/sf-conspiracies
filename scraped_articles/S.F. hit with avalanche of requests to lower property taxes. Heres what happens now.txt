S.F. hit with avalanche of requests to lower property taxes. Here’s what happens now
-----
Reflecting a drop in real estate values, the San Francisco Assessment Appeals Board received an avalanche of applications from home and business owners requesting a reduction on this year's property taxes.Between July 2 and Sept. 15 — the window for filing a formal appeal application in San Francisco — the board received 6,836 applications from people or companies seeking a lower assessment on their 2023-24 tax bills, according to a Chronicle analysis of appeals board data.
