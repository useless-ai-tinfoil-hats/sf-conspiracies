Uber, Lyft drivers face major dilemma with express lanes on Bay Area highways
-----
As more and more express lanes appear on Bay Area highways, one cohort of drivers is stuck in a no-win situation: either sit in traffic, losing time, or pay the toll out of pocket, which can exceed $10 depending on the day.The predicament uniquely impacts drivers for Uber and Lyft, which operate using a fixed fare that can't update mid-trip with added express lane tolls.
