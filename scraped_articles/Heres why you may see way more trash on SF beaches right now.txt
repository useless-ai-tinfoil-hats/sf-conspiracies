Here's why you may see way more trash on SF beaches right now
-----
Plastic bags, old pants and newspaper wads: That's a small selection of the kind of trash you're likely to find on San Francisco's Fort Funston beach right now.Both Fort Funston and Ocean Beach have dealt with an influx of detritus since heavy rains surged through the Bay Area. Unfortunately, this concentration of garbage isn't surprising, according to the National Park Service, and San Francisco's sewer system — specifically its "outfalls" — are to blame.
