With storm coming, you may want to reconsider going to Disneyland on Monday
-----
With gusty winds and heavy rain in the forecast, guests planning on visiting the Disneyland Resort on Monday may wish to reschedule their plans, if possible. The National Weather Service is anticipating the possibility of "catastrophic" rain over the next few days in Southern California.Forecasters believe an atmospheric river with a "slow moving trough" will bring heavy rainfall to Southern California.
