Berkeley man arrested for allegedly shooting maintenance worker he thought was a burglar, police say
-----
A Berkeley man was arrested Monday after allegedly shooting a maintenance worker he claimed had been attempting to burglarize his apartment despite the worker making numerous attempts to reach him before entering his unit, authorities said.Giancarlo O'Hara, 43, was charged with attempted homicide and is being held without bail.
The Berkeley Police Department received a report of a shooting inside an apartment building around 11:41 a.m. that day on the 2500 block of Hillegass Avenue
