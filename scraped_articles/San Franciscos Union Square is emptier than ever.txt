San Francisco's Union Square is emptier than ever
-----
The general sense of malaise that pervades discussions about San Francisco's Union Square has new data points to back it up.Real estate firm Cushman & Wakefield just released a report about the city's retail market, which found that Union Square reached a new peak vacancy rate in the first quarter of 2024, clocking in at 20.6%. That record-breaking number helped drive San Francisco's overall retail vacancy rate to a new high as well, at 7.9%.
