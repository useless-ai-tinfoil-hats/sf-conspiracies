Massive, 6-county Northern California manhunt for man accused of abducting daughter
-----
An Amber Alert was issued in six Northern California counties Thursday morning as authorities search for a man accused of abducting his 7-month-old child.The Yuba County Sheriff's Office said they received a call just after midnight from a woman who said her ex-boyfriend left her Marysville (Yuba County) home with their 7-month-old daughter after an argument.
The father, 36-year-old Lance Colon, is believed to be driving a 2004 black Chevrolet Tahoe with the license plate 9DHK322.
