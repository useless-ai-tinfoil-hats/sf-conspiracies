Art & Soul Oakland canceled due to delay in city funding
-----
The East Bay has lost one of its most popular arts and culture events.The 23rd annual Art & Soul Oakland has been canceled due to a lack of city funding. Featuring a range of live music performances, an artisan marketplace and local food vendors, the outdoor music and art festival has been a downtown Oakland tradition since 2001, drawing tens of thousands from all over the region.
