San Francisco’s Noodle in a Haystack, Shuggie’s make Bon Appetit’s Best New Restaurants list
-----
Two popular San Francisco restaurants have been named to Bon Appetit's Best New Restaurants of 2023.Shuggie's Trash Pie & Natural Wine and Noodle in a Haystack were among the 24 restaurants across the U.S. recognized Thursday for their exemplary food and service. They were the only Bay Area spots that made the cut.
