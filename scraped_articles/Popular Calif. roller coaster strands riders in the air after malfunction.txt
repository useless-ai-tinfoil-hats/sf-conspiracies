Popular Calif. roller coaster strands riders in the air after malfunction
-----
Over a dozen people got stuck on the Viper roller coaster at Southern California's Six Flags Magic Mountain around 7 p.m. on Sunday when the ride malfunctioned.After about half an hour, crew members helped passengers down a nearby stairwell. The reason for the malfunction has not been publicly stated.
SFGATE has reached out for comment from Six Flags Magic Mountain, but did not hear back before publication.
