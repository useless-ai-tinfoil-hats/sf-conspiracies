Supervisor Dean Preston pushes Mayor Breed for action on S.F. affordable housing site
-----
Supervisor Dean Preston is pushing Mayor London Breed to commit to a timeline for construction of a long-delayed affordable-housing project in Hayes Valley after she missed her own deadline to create a plan.Breed had promised in March to set up a timeline by August for the affordable-housing project slated for Parcel K, home of the temporary Proxy outdoor gym and movie theater and one of the lots opened up by the demolition of the Central Freeway in the early 2000s.
