San Bruno uses PG&E pipeline explosion settlement to open new $51 million recreation center
-----
San Bruno officials on Saturday opened a sprawling new Recreation and Aquatic Center, paid for with restitution funds from the devastating 2010 pipeline explosion that killed eight residents and injured 58 others.Comprising indoor and outdoor swimming pools, gym equipment and indoor courts for basketball, volleyball and pickleball, the center represents the final chapter of a long and grueling recovery process.
