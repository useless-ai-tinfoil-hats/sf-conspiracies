CHP investigating fatal hit-and-run near Bay Bridge
-----
A pedestrian was fatally struck in a hit-and-run on Interstate 80 near the Bay Bridge Monday morning, according to the California Highway Patrol.A vehicle traveling westbound struck a pedestrian near the Fifth Street exit at about 1:35 a.m., said CHP officer Mark Andrews. The driver fled before CHP officers arrived.
