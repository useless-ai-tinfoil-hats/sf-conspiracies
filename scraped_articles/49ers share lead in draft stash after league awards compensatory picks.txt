49ers share lead in draft stash after league awards compensatory picks
-----
The San Francisco 49ers share the NFL lead with 11 selections in April's draft after the league awarded compensatory picks Friday.The 49ers' 11 picks are tied for the most with the Rams, Cardinals and Packers. The 49ers haven't made 11 picks in a draft since 2016, the year before head coach Kyle Shanahan and general manager John Lynch were hired.
