Tech startup Discord lays off 160 San Francisco workers, CEO blames overhiring
-----
Discord is laying off 160 San Francisco workers, the social messaging startup announced in a WARN notice to the city on Thursday.The notice, required when companies perform mass layoffs, said the employees would officially leave the San Francisco-based company on Feb. 2. Dozens of engineers are among the casualties, as are several trust and safety employees, product managers and data scientists, according to the notice.
