Billion-dollar AI company abandons San Francisco for more ‘livable’ Bay Area city
-----
Amjad Masad, the founder and CEO of Replit, an artificial intelligence company in the Bay Area valued at over $1 billion, announced this week that the company has relocated its headquarters from San Francisco to Foster City.The move was motivated by a desire for a more "livable" city and to establish a new home base for the AI coding startup, akin to how Google is associated with Mountain View and Apple with Cupertino.
