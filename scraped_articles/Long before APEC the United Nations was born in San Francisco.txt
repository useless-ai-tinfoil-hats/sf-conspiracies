Long before APEC, the United Nations was born in San Francisco
-----
"Oh, what a great day this can be in history!"It was one of the most resounding quotes ever spoken in San Francisco, as President Harry Truman watched his secretary of state put pen to paper at Herbst Theatre on June 26, 1945, before the leaders of 50 other countries, and the United Nations was born.
