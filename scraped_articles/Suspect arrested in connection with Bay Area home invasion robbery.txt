Suspect arrested in connection with Bay Area home invasion robbery
-----
A suspect was arrested last month in connection with a Bay Area home invasion robbery, police said.The suspect, a 37-year-old North Carolina resident, is one of three people that San Jose Police have identified and the second of the group to be arrested. The third named suspect remains at large while the fourth has yet to be identified, the San Jose Police Department said in a news release Friday.
