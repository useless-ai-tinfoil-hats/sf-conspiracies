McDonald's McRib sandwich to reappear in Nov. despite 2022 'farewell tour'
-----
In the fall of 2022, McDonald's announced the McRib sandwich would be going on a "farewell tour," seemingly finishing a cult-favorite menu item forever.Yet longtime fans of the vaunted fast-food brand know that these "goodbyes" are often more like "see you laters," which meant it was barely a surprise when McDonald's announced Oct. 4 that the McRib would make a return to some restaurants beginning in November, according to Today.com.
