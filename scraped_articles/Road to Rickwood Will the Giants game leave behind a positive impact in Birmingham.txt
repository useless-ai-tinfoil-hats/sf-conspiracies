Road to Rickwood: Will the Giants’ game leave behind a positive impact in Birmingham?
-----
BIRMINGHAM, Ala. — Kids around Birmingham know Papa Jack. He tries to make sure of it. He's Ron "Papa Jack" Jackson, who played 10 seasons in the major leagues and eventually returned to his Birmingham roots "because the kids need me."Jackson's mission is to get as many kids as possible involved in baseball, which has been challenging around Birmingham, where opportunities and interest have been lacking. He's trying to be a positive influence and is open to any resource available.
