Individual detained on BART after slashing at riders with 'shiny metal object'
-----
The West Dublin-Pleasanton BART station was temporarily closed on Tuesday morning after an individual "slashed at two to three people with a shiny metal object," Jim Allison, a spokesperson with the transportation agency, said.Allison said the individual was taken into police custody. "At this point we don't have any reported injuries," Allison told KCBS-AM.
