From Whiskers to Moxie, counting time in dog years
-----
Whiskers, a terrier mix, was my first rescue dog and my responsibility. As a kid, I got up at 6 to walk him, and on winter mornings when I got back, my mother, Nurse Vivian, would have a bowl of Frosted Mini-Wheats for me at the Formica table and a bowl of French toast for Whiskers on the floor.Sullen teenager that I was, the one time I asked why, Nurse Vivian replied, "He's more excited to see me."
