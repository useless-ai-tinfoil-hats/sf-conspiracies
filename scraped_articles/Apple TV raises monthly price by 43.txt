Apple TV+ raises monthly price by 43%
-----
Apple has ratcheted up the cost of its Apple TV+ streaming service for the second time in a year, marking a significant 43% increase, on Wednesday, Oct. 25.This uptick is part of a broader trend of price hikes sweeping through the Cupertino-based Apple's flagship services, including Arcade, News+, and its bundled offering, Apple One.
