Lesbian bars were vanishing — until this ‘euphoric’ spot opened in S.F.
-----
Mother's just a year and a half old, but people fit in at the bar like they've been coming here all their lives. Groups of friends get tipsy under projections of floating hyenas by the back wall. A woman comfortably reads her book at a table. The new customers trickling in grab cash from the ATM in the corner, order drinks like the gin-and-ginger Ex or the rye- and Curaçao-based Crush, and squeeze into the old-timey photo booth. As the sun sets, the light slowly dims, filtering through windows
