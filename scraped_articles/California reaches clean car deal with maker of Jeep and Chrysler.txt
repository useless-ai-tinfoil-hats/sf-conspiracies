California reaches clean car deal with maker of Jeep and Chrysler
-----
The car company that owns the Jeep and Chrysler brands has agreed to meet California's climate goals and slash greenhouse gasses in the next several years by improving designs and promoting cleaner models like electric vehicles.Gov. Gavin Newsom on Tuesday announced the deal between Stellantis, the brand producing Jeep, Chrysler, Dodge, Ram and Fiat vehicles, and the California Air Resources Board.
