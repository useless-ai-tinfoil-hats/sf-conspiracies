California senators seek to rename S.F. post office near Embarcadero after Dianne Feinstein
-----
A U.S. post office near the Embarcadero could be named after the late Sen. Dianne Feinstein, whose advocacy helped shape the texture of San Francisco's waterfront.San Francisco's California Sens. Alex Padilla and Laphonza Butler introduced a bill Monday to rename the post office at 180 Steuart St. the "Dianne Feinstein Post Office." Congress often names post offices in honor of important individuals, usually after they die, and efforts to do so are typically without controversy.
