Playfulness, and hard conversation, about Black hair
-----
Founded in 1984 by Jawole Willa Jo Zollar, Brooklyn-based Urban Bush Women is known for dance theater that lives up to the company's core value of "catalyzing for social change." And its current touring show, "Hair & Other Stories," is a prime example, with a comedy-peppered first half that leads to a second half of deeper discussion.One thing it is not: timid.
