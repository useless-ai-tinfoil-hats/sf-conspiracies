FTX sues SBF's parents, alleges they funneled millions to Stanford, other 'pet causes'
-----
Longtime Stanford University professors Joseph Bankman and Barbara Fried — the parents of Sam Bankman-Fried — were sued by the now-bankrupt cryptocurrency company FTX on Monday. In the lawsuit, filed in federal court in Delaware, the couple is accused of using FTX funds to enrich themselves and give to their "pet causes," including millions of dollars in donations to Stanford.The lawsuit alleges that both Bankman and Fried "fraudulently transferred and misappropriated" tens of millions
