SF office vacancies hit record high, but there are 'positive signs' ahead
-----
The San Francisco office market has continued to struggle this year, but there are glimmers of hope ahead.The latest preliminary data from commercial real estate firm CBRE shows that office vacancies in the city hit a new record in the first quarter of the year. The vacancy rate hit 36.6%, according to the data, which is a slight increase from the 35.6% in the fourth quarter of 2023.
