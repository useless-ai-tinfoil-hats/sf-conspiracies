‘They know what’s at stake’: Steve Kerr, Warriors confident in postseason bid
-----
He drank Champagne during All-Star Weekend. Sat in a hot tub. Spent time with his family.Absent from Steve Kerr's mind was his 500th victory — along with basketball in general.
"I didn't spend a whole lot of time reflecting on it," said the Golden State Warriors' head coach, having reached 500 NBA wins in fewer games than all but four coaches in league history.
"Kind of crazy, but here we are 10 years in."
With 29 games still left to play in this regular season.
