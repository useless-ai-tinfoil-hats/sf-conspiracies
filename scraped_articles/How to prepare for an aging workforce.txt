How to prepare for an aging workforce
-----
Photos by ShutterstockBy 2031, Bain research estimates that workers 55 and older will make up a full quarter of the workforce, up from 20% in 2011. And 41% of U.S. workers say they plan to work past the age of 65.
This shift to an older workforce is driven by many factors, including increasing longevity and health as people stay vibrant longer. It's also partly due to the rising costs of the necessities of life and inflation.
