This popular brewery will headline a highly anticipated Bay Area food hall
-----
A popular East Bay brewery will anchor West Oakland's huge, long-awaited food hall, which also announced an opening timeline this week.Almanac Beer Co. will be the focal point of the 12,000-square-foot Prescott Market at 1620-40 18th St., Oakland. The craft beer company will take up 1,800 square feet of the refurbished industrial structure, outfitted with a 20-tap system, a full kitchen, 74 indoor seats and an outdoor seating area.
