Ayesha Curry stays busy with new acting gig in Netflix romantic comedy
-----
In an unlikely pairing, entrepreneur and Bay Area royalty Ayesha Curry is set to appear in an upcoming Netflix film alongside none other than Lindsay Lohan.The romantic comedy, titled "Irish Wish," stars Lohan as Maddie, a woman who travels to Ireland to attend her best friend's wedding. Her friend is marrying Paul, a man whom Maddie has long loved from afar and quietly wishes she was with. One night, she wishes too hard and wakes up in an alternate reality where she's switched places
