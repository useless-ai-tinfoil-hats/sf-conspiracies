Plan revealed to curb commercial helicopter tours at Hawaii Volcanoes National Park
-----
Noisy helicopter tours over Hawaii Volcanoes National Park are facing a reckoning.A new federal management plan seeks to reduce noise levels generated by commercial helicopter tours over Hawaii Volcanoes National Park by significantly scaling back the number of tours allowed each day and limiting which routes they can take.
