Man arrested after big Berkeley brawl leads to gunfire
-----
A large fight escalated when a man fired a gun in Berkeley early Sunday, but no one was injured, police said.The brawl was reported just before 2 a.m. near Telegraph and Durant avenues, just south of UC Berkeley, police said.
Officers confiscated a gun and arrested the suspect in connection to the shooting, police Lt. Peter Lee said.
No further information was available.
