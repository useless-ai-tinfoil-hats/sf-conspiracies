Bay FC’s Asisat Oshoala prepared for ‘weird’ matchup with former team Barcelona
-----
Asisat Oshoala could be excused for feeling a bit overwhelmed. Back from the Olympics, Oshoala is preparing for Bay FC's second-half push, looking to score at a higher rate and find her footing in her new league.First, though, Bay FC will host Oshoala's old club, FC Barcelona, in a friendly Tuesday at PayPal Park. It will be Oshoala's first time since she departed in January that she will see the team with which she had thrived.
With Barcelona, Oshoala scored 117 goals in 162 games
