Former Giants PA announcer Brooks-Moon honored in Sacramento, headed to Birmingham
-----
Renel Brooks-Moon's voice no longer is heard at Oracle Park, but her presence remains so prevalent that she was honored Tuesday with two prestigious acknowledgments at the state Capitol in Sacramento.Brooks-Moon, the San Francisco Giants' public-address announcer from 2000 through last season, was named Woman of the Year on the California Assembly floor.
Also, Brooks-Moon was recognized on the Senate floor with a Senate Resolution for her trailblazing career and community service.
