Warriors bemoan ‘quiet defense’; Klay Thompson returns to starting lineup
-----
MIAMI — On Sunday, Draymond Green referred to the Golden State Warriors as a "very quiet team" on the defensive end of the floor.On Tuesday, head coach Steve Kerr didn't disagree.
"We're very quiet across the board. We don't have a lot of guys who are loud defensively," he said before the Warriors beat the Miami Heat.
"Draymond is, obviously. (Kevon) Looney is. And we've missed that," Kerr added.
Looney rejoined the rotation Tuesday with backup big Trayce Jackson-Davis
