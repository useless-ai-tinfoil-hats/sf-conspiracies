Oakland mayor appeals to MLB owners to vote no on A's move to Las Vegas
-----
Days before Major League Baseball owners will vote on relocating the Oakland Athletics to Las Vegas, Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao sent letters to MLB owners in a plea to keep the team in the East Bay.In the vote at next week's owners' meetings in Arlington, Texas, 75% approval is required for A's owner John Fisher to move ahead on pulling the team out of Oakland for a future in the Nevada desert.
