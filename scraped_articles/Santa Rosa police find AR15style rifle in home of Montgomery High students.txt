Santa Rosa police find AR-15-style rifle in home of Montgomery High students
-----
Santa Rosa police have arrested two siblings, both Montgomery High School students, after an assault rifle was found at their home during an investigation of a recent gang-related fight at Slater Middle School, authorities said Friday.The initial fight occurred Sept 1., when school officials reported that a 13-year-old student was attacked around 12:30 p.m. by several young people who were not Slater students and were trespassing on campus, police said.
