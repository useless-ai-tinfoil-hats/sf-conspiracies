Bay Area cannabis shops are closing as pot sales slump
-----
California's legal cannabis market continues to descend into financial turmoil, according to the latest annual tax figures released this week. The total market is shrinking in size, according to state-released data, and additional tax information obtained by SFGATE shows hundreds of pot stores are at risk of going out of business.California's pot shops sold goods worth a total of $5.1 billion in 2023, down for the second year in a row and nearly 11% less than what was sold in 2021,
