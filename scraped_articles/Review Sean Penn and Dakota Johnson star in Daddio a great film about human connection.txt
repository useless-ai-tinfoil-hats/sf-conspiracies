Review: Sean Penn and Dakota Johnson star in ‘Daddio,’ a great film about human connection
-----
"Daddio" is one of the best movies of 2024, and a really smart film about how people forge connections. You might have to go back to 1995 and "Before Sunrise" to find as good a depiction of two people making the journey from strangers to intimates.But what's different and possibly unique about "Daddio" is that the connection presented here isn't a romantic one.
