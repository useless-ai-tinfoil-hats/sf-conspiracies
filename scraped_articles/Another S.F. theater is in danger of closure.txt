Another S.F. theater is in danger of closure
-----
2023 didn't mark the end of financial crises in Bay Area theater.Cutting Ball Theater, the 25-year-old San Francisco company known for form-breaking new plays and rejuvenated classics, announced an emergency fundraising campaign on Friday, Feb. 9. The leadership team says it needs to raise $45,000 by March 1 and $200,000 by June, out of a $750,000 annual budget, in order to stay open.
