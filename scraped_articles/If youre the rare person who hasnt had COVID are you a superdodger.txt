If you’re the rare person who hasn’t had COVID, are you a super-dodger?
-----
More than four years have elapsed since the start of the COVID-19 pandemic, and during that time nearly everyone in the U.S. has been exposed to the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Yet, there remains a subset of people who have still never gotten sick or tested positive, myself included.Experts say it's not easy to gauge how many of these people are true "Novids," or super-dodgers, because some may have had COVID without knowing it.
