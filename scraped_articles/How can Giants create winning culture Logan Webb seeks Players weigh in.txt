How can Giants create ‘winning culture’ Logan Webb seeks? Players weigh in
-----
Logan Webb has organizational cachet, and when he calls for the San Francisco Giants to create a "winning culture," it becomes an eye-opening dose of reality for owners, executives and teammates.Webb never pushed for manager Gabe Kapler to get fired, but the standout right-hander did stress the need for "big changes" on a team that has been sub-.500 the past two years combined and is in desperate need of a better roster, approach and mindset.
