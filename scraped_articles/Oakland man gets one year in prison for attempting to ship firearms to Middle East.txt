Oakland man gets one year in prison for attempting to ship firearms to Middle East
-----
An Oakland man was sentenced to about a year in prison for attempting to smuggle firearms and other defense materials to the Sultanate of Oman, according to the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Northern District of California.Fares Abdo Al Eyani, 41, tried to hide the smuggled goods in a shipping container bound for Oman at the Port of Oakland, prosecutors said.
