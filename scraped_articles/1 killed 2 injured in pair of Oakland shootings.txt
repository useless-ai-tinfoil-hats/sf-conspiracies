1 killed, 2 injured in pair of Oakland shootings
-----
One person was killed and two others were injured in a pair of Oakland shootings Saturday night, according to the city's Police Department.Officers responded to a shooting just after 8:30 p.m. Saturday on the 500 block of Merritt Avenue. Paramedics treated a victim, who was hospitalized and listed in stable condition Sunday.
