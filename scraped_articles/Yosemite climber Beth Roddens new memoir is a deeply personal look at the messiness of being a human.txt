Yosemite climber Beth Rodden's new memoir is a deeply personal look at 'the messiness of being a human'
-----
The apex of Beth Rodden's professional climbing career came in 2008 when she became the first to ascend a 70-foot crack near the mouth of Yosemite Valley, a feat widely heralded at the time as one of the hardest traditional pitches in the world.In the pressurized world of pro climbing, the feat should have granted her a brief feeling of fulfillment or at least a moment of relief. But it didn't.
