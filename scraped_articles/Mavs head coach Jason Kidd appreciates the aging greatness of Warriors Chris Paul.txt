Mavs head coach Jason Kidd appreciates the aging greatness of Warriors’ Chris Paul
-----

"He's going to pass it then. I mean, there's no better player to pass it. He's had an incredible Hall of Fame career. … When you talk about the point-guard position, he's the blueprint of success."
