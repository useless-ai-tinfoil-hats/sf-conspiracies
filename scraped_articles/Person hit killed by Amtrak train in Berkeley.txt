Person hit, killed by Amtrak train in Berkeley
-----
A person was killed Wednesday when hit by an Amtrak train in Berkeley, according to police.At 5:35 p.m., the Berkeley Police Department received a report that a person had been struck by an Amtrak train near Harrison Street. After arriving at the scene, officers discovered the person had already died, according to the Police Department.
The investigation is being handled by Amtrak police, who did not respond to a request for comment.
