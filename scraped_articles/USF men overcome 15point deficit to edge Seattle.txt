USF men overcome 15-point deficit to edge Seattle
-----
USF trailed Seattle by as many as 15 points in the first half on the Hilltop on Wednesday night, and the Dons didn't own a lead for the first 32 minutes.The Dons still managed to improve to 8-3 as they outlasted the Redhawks 62-59.
At the outset of his postgame news conference, USF head coach Chris Gerlufsen made a point to credit the Redhawks (6-4), who won a combined 43 games over the past two seasons.
