Car fatally strikes North Bay man who was crossing street to get his mail
-----
A man, described by authorities as elderly, was fatally struck by a car Monday night in Petaluma, according to the California Highway Patrol.Around 8:20 p.m., the man was walking across Skillman Lane, east of Thompson Lane, to get his mail when a Land Rover struck him, the CHP said. The Land Rover was being driven west on Skillman Lane, within the speed limit, when the crash happened, according to the CHP.
The man was pronounced dead at the scene, the CHP said.
