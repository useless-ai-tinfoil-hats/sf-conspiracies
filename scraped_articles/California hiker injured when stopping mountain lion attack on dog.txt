California hiker injured when stopping mountain lion attack on dog
-----
A California hiker was injured Tuesday when stopping a mountain lion's attack on a dog, officials said.The individual was walking a small dog in Solstice Canyon in the Santa Monica Mountains National Recreation Area when the mountain lion attempted to pounce on the dog. Upon intervening, the person was scratched by the mountain lion and received a puncture wound on the hand, a social media post from the recreation area said.
