Warriors' Draymond Green expected to play Monday in Memphis
-----
Golden State Warriors forward Draymond Green has been cleared to play and is expected to return to action Monday against Memphis.Green has missed 16 consecutive games in the wake of his ejection Dec. 12, when he wildly swung his arm and hit Phoenix center Jusuf Nurkic in the face. The NBA handed Green an indefinite suspension, which it lifted after 12 games. He missed the Warriors' past four games while working his way back into shape.
