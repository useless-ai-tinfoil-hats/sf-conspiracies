Librarians’ top picks for young readers to celebrate Black History Month
-----
From friendships and celebrations to histories both triumphant and painful, the fullness of the Black experience makes for excellent reading for readers of any age, at any time of the year, as this roundup of children's books shows.To celebrate Black History Month, we asked two local librarians — Mahasin A. Aleem, senior community library manager with the Contra Costa County public library system, and Jesse Nachem, children's collection management librarian at the Oakland Public Library
