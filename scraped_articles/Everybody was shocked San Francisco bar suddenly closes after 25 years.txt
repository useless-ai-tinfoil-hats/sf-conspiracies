'Everybody was shocked': San Francisco bar suddenly closes after 25 years
-----
A popular bar in the heart of San Francisco's Mission District, once known for its glittery retro ambiance and $10 martinis served alongside manicures, is calling it quits after 25 years.When Beauty Bar, at the corner of 19th and Mission streets, opened in 1999, it was a new branch of a quirky chain with locations in New York and Los Angeles.
