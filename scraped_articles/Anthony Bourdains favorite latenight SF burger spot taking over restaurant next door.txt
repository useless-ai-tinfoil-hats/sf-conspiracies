Anthony Bourdain's favorite late-night SF burger spot taking over restaurant next door
-----
A San Francisco classic and an Anthony Bourdain favorite, Sam's Burgers, is opening a new restaurant next door.The late-night burger joint in North Beach plans to debut a new fast-casual restaurant called Sam's East, which will focus on Middle Eastern dishes. Sam's East will take over the former space of Tacorea located at 620 Broadway St. and is expected to open by the end of November, co-owner Emad ElShawa told SFGATE.
