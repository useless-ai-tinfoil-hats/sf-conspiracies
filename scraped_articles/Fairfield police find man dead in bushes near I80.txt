Fairfield police find man dead in bushes near I-80
-----
Fairfield police officials said they were investigating the death of a man who was found lying on the side of a road near Interstate 80 on Wednesday morning.Shortly after 4 a.m., a person called 911 to report a man was lying in the bushes on the north side of Travis Boulevard near the highway and appeared to not be breathing, according to the Fairfield Police Department.
