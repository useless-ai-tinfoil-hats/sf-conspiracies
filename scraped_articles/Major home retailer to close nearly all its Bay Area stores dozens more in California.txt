Major home retailer to close nearly all its Bay Area stores, dozens more in California
-----
Big Lots, the discount retail chain, is shuttering more than 50 stores in California, including nearly a dozen in the Bay Area, amid flagging sales.The company said in a June filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission that it expects to close 35 to 40 stores in 2024, but it did not disclose which locations or when they would shutter. However, the number of stores closing in California is higher, according to closure notices on the company's website.
