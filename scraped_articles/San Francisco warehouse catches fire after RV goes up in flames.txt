San Francisco warehouse catches fire after RV goes up in flames
-----
A fire that started in an RV in San Francisco early Monday morning spread to a warehouse, officials said.The RV was parked on the 500 block of Newhall Street in the Bayview when it went up in flames, the San Francisco Fire Department said on X (formerly Twitter).
The blaze was first reported to the fire department at 2:42 a.m. and firefighters had it under control by 3:15 a.m.
