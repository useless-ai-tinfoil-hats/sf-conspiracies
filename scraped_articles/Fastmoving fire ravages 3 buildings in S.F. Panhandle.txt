Fast-moving fire ravages 3 buildings in S.F. Panhandle
-----
A fire that erupted early Monday morning in San Francisco's Panhandle neighborhood charred three duplexes on Masonic Avenue and left two people injured, spokespeople for the Fire Department said.At least one hundred firefighters battled the flames that ignited at 3:45 a.m., devouring a three-story residential building at 720 Masonic Ave., then spreading to homes on either side of it.
