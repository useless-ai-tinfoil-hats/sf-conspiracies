New lessees plan to reopen SF Cliff House after 'significant remodel'
-----
The National Park Service, which owns the historic Cliff House perched on the shores of San Francisco's Lands End, has awarded a 20-year lease to Sutro Lands End Partners, LLC, to take over the property.The partnership said the building would undergo "a significant remodel" and open in late 2024.
