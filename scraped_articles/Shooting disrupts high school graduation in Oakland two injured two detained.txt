Shooting disrupts high school graduation in Oakland, two injured, two detained
-----
A shooting disrupted a graduation celebration Thursday night at Skyline High School, sending graduates and their families running and leaving two people injured, police and witnesses said.Two people were detained after the attack and police sealed off the area around the high school for hours before they deemed the scene safe.
