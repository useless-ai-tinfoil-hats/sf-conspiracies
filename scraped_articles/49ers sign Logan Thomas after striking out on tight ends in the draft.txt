49ers sign Logan Thomas after striking out on tight ends in the draft
-----
George Kittle finally might have company: The San Francisco 49ers have added a backup tight end who, well, catches passes.On Monday, the 49ers addressed a screaming need by signing former Washington tight end Logan Thomas, 32, to a one-year deal, a league source said. Thomas, a 10-year veteran, had 55 receptions for 496 yards and four touchdowns last season.
