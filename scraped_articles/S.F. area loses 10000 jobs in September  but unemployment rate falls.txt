San Francisco and San Mateo County lost 10,000 jobs in September, a 0.8% decline for the region's jobs total, as tech layoffs continued.But, counterintuitively, the unemployment rate fell substantially, from 3.5% in August to 3% for the two county-area, as the number of residents looking for work contracted, according to new state data released Friday. The rate was higher than last September's 2.2%.
