Body of slain woman found in unusual Vallejo location
-----
The body of a woman found on Mare Island has been ruled a homicide, Vallejo police said Friday.On Monday around 11:30 p.m., Vallejo police responded to a report of an unresponsive person on the 500 block of Walnut Avenue. Officers then located a deceased woman on the roadway.
The Solano County Coroner's office declared the death a homicide on Friday, according to Vallejo police. The woman's identity is being withheld until her next of kin is notified.
