101-year-old Bay Area man goes missing after driving away from home
-----
A 101-year-old man has not been seen since he drove away from his Santa Rosa home on Monday afternoon, officials said.William Gunther was behind the wheel of a white 2018 Subaru Legacy (license plate: 8CWR708) and the Santa Rosa Police Department is calling on the public to keep an eye out for him and his vehicle.
Gunther drove away from his home on the 5000 block of Montgomery Drive at some point between noon and 4:30 p.m. He was likely headed for Goat Rock Beach in Jenner, police
