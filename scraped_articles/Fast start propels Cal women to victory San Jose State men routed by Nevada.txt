Fast start propels Cal women to victory; San Jose State men routed by Nevada
-----
The Cal women's basketball team doubled-up Arizona State in the first quarter Friday night, giving the Bears all the cushion they would need in a 67-55 victory at Haas Pavilion.The Bears (16-11, 6-9 Pac-12) led 26-13 after the first quarter. ASU (11-16, 3-12) pulled to within 33-30 in the second quarter, but Cal closed the first half on a 8-2 run and was never seriously threatened again.
Cal struggled from deep, making only 7 of 26 3-point attempts, but was a sizzling 21-for-35
