19-year-old fatally shot at SF's Dolores Park identified as Lowell graduate
-----
The 19-year-old fatally shot at San Francisco's Dolores Park on Wednesday night was identified Friday by the San Francisco Medical Examiner's Office.Luis Manuel Arguello-Inglis of San Francisco was a graduate of Lowell High School and a freshman at San Jose State University, a friend of the family said.
"He was always with his sister," Alexis Aviles said. "He was loving, caring and wise beyond his age."
