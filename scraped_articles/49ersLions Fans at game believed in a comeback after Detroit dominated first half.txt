49ers-Lions: Fans at game believed in a comeback after Detroit dominated first half
-----
As the San Francisco 49ers took on the Detroit Lions Sunday, fans came in hoping for a blowout but quickly began wishing for a comeback instead.The 49ers walked into Levi's Stadium as the predicted favorites to advance to Super Bowl LVIII against the Kansas City Chiefs, who beat the Baltimore Ravens shortly before the 49ers game kicked off.
But the Lions took a 24-7 lead in the first half and the confidence that fans brought into the stadium was put to the test.
