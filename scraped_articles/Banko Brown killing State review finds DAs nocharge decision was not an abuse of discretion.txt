Banko Brown killing: State review finds DA’s no-charge decision was not an ‘abuse of discretion’
-----
San Francisco prosecutors did not abuse their powers when they declined to file charges against the Walgreens security guard who shot and killed Banko Brown last year during a suspected shoplifting attempt, according to the California Attorney General's Office.Brown's April 2023 killing ignited controversy after San Francisco District Attorney Brooke Jenkins declined to file charges against the guard, Michael Earl-Wayne Anthony, who cited self-defense, though Brown was unarmed.
