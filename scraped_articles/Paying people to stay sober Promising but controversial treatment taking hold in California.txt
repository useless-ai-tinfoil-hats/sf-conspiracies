Paying people to stay sober: Promising but controversial treatment taking hold in California
-----
For the past 30 years, if Daniel Costa was sober for the holidays, it was because he was in jail.Now everything is different. Costa, a 61-year-old San Jose native who has struggled with both methamphetamine addiction and housing instability for most of his life, is five months sober for the first time in over three decades, thanks to a new substance-use treatment program called Rewarding Recovery.
