Mill Valley Film Festival to honor Jude Law this fall
-----
Jude Law, who plays Henry VIII in the new film "Firebrand" and has been a major star for more than a quarter of a century, will be the subject of a special in-person tribute during this year's Mill Valley Film Festival.The tribute includes an onstage conversation and presentation of the MVFF award in October, the California Film Institute announced.
