San Francisco office tower to sell for less than half of its previous value
-----
An 18-story office tower near San Francisco's waterfront is expected to sell for less than half of what it sold for 10 years ago.The tower at 201 Spear St. sold for $121 million in 2013 before landing tenants like Google and WeWork. Now, Strada Investment Group is expected to buy it for about $60 million, sources confirmed to the San Francisco Chronicle this week. (SFGATE and the Chronicle are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms.)
