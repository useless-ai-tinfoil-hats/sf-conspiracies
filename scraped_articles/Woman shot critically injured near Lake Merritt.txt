Woman shot, critically injured near Lake Merritt
-----
A young woman was spending Labor Day at Lake Merritt on Monday afternoon when she was shot and critically injured during an apparent drive-by assault, according to an Oakland police spokesperson and dispatch audio reviewed by the Chronicle.Oakland police spokesperson Kim Armstead said the woman was an "innocent bystander" in the shooting, which happened just before 12:30 p.m. near Madison Street and Lakeside Drive.
