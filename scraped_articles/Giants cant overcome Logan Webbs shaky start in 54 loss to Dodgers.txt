Giants can’t overcome Logan Webb’s shaky start in 5-4 loss to Dodgers
-----
LOS ANGELES — Logan Webb needed the relief.The Dodgers were all over his off-speed pitches, drilling his changeup and slider to pile five runs on the ace in an eventual 5-4 San Francisco Giants loss on Tuesday night at Dodger Stadium. Mookie Betts clobbered his fifth home run on a changeup that fell out of the zone and Webb gave up more than three runs at Dodger Stadium for the first time in seven appearances over his career.
