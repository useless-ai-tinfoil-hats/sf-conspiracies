What ‘King Lear’ can show us about Biden, Harris and Trump
-----
A few days after President Joe Biden's doddering performance in this year's first presidential debate, Oakland playwright Jonathan Spector remarked on X, "Every Shakespeare Festival in the country is kicking themselves right now for not having programmed 'King Lear' this summer."Perhaps not since 2021, when "Julius Caesar" and its biddable mobs seemed to speak to the storming of the Capitol, has a work of Shakespeare seemed so apt to help us understand an extraordinary political moment.
