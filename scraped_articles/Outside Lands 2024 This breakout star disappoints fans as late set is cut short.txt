Outside Lands 2024: This breakout star disappoints fans as late set is cut short
-----
South African artist Tyla was more than 30 minutes late to stage during the first day of Outside Lands, disappointing fans who had been excited for her debut festival performance.The Grammy-winning artist, who broke into the Billboard Hot 100 last year with the Afrobeats-infused single "Water," performed for less than 20 minutes after starting her set at 6:37 p.m. Friday, Aug. 9. She was scheduled for 6:05 p.m set at the Sutro Stage.
