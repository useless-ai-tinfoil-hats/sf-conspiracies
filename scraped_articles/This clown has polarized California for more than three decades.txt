Los Angeles has no shortage of lovable local oddballs, but even by average standards, the Venice Boardwalk stands out. The cluttered, heavily touristed stretch south of Santa Monica is known as much for its characters as its proximity to the beach. Sculptor Jonathan Borofsky had long taken stock of the quirky people strolling, skating and singing along the breezy thoroughfare.
