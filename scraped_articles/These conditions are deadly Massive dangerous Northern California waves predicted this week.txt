‘These conditions are deadly!’: Massive, dangerous Northern California waves predicted this week
-----
The National Weather Service issued a high surf warning for Bay Area beaches on Thursday, forecasting waves that could reach 26 feet.Much of the Northern and Central California coastlines were expected to see massive waves, the NWS said.
In addition to large breaking waves, the swell was expected to bring rip currents and sneaker waves. the NWS warned.
Such swells can be extremely dangerous, particularly when they catch swimmers off guard and pull them into deeper waters. NWS
