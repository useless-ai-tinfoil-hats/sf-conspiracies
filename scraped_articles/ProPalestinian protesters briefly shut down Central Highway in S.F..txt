Pro-Palestinian protesters briefly shut down Central Highway in S.F.
-----
Thousands of protesters calling for a cease-fire in Gaza marched Saturday afternoon along the Central Freeway via Octavia Street in San Francisco for about 45 minutes, disrupting traffic."Civic Demonstration is affecting US 101 at Northbound 101 at 101/80 Split. Expect delays and use alternate routes," city officials reported shortly before 5 p.m. Soon after that, the crowd began exiting the highway into the Mission District, according to a Mission Local reporter on the scene.
