Cal squanders opportunities to put Auburn away, Bears fall 14-10
-----
For the past two-plus seasons, Cal usually has been able to hang with the top teams in the Pac-12 and across the nation. What the Bears haven't been able to do is beat those teams.Saturday night's 14-10 loss to Auburn at Memorial Stadium provided yet another example. The Bears outgained the Tigers (273 yards to 230), had more first downs (19-12), owned a nearly 10-minute advantage in time of possession (34:48-25:12) and forced four Auburn turnovers.
