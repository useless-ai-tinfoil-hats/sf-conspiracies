John Legend, Beatles tributes on ‘Summer With the Symphony’ lineup
-----
The San Francisco Symphony's summer season boasts hot tickets to programming that showcases the old with the new, from Mozart to a Beatles tribute and guest performances by John Legend and Ben Folds.The "Summer With the Symphony" lineup, announced Wednesday, May 1, includes nine concerts at Davies Symphony Hall in addition to several outdoor performances at Frost Amphitheater in Stanford, Shoreline Amphitheater in Mountain View and Stern Grove in San Francisco.
