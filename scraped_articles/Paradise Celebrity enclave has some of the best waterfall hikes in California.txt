'Paradise': Celebrity enclave has some of the best waterfall hikes in California
-----
Driving up the winding road to the Cold Spring trailhead in Montecito feels like you've already won the lottery. The serpentine street is lined with fortressed-off properties distinguished only by slightly differently hued stucco walls and the labels on the work trucks parked in front of them.In this exclusive enclave on the southern tip of Santa Barbara, every jogger could be a billionaire or an Oscar winner.
