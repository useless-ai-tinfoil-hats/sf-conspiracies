This new Disneyland practice is a game changer

Ask any group of Disneyland Magic Key holders what their biggest frustration with the park's annual pass is, and the consensus is likely the same. The no-show policy penalizes guests who miss reservations for days they were scheduled to be in the park, and it's practically impossible to navigate.
