Vibrant S.F. neighborhood gets a makeover from AI-generated paint jobs
-----
San Francisco's Excelsior district — a largely residential neighborhood south of Interstate 280, anchored by a southerly stretch of Mission Street — probably isn't what comes first to mind for most people when they think of the city's booming AI scene.But for a handful of small businesses in the area, artificial intelligence is now literally splashed across their storefronts. Over the past week, they've all been repainted with designs created with an AI tool from Spanish software startup
