Lineup for new annual Golden Gate Park concert revealed
-----
The promotion company behind the Outside Lands music festival has announced the initial lineup for a new annual concert in San Francisco's Golden Gate Park.System of a Down, Deftones, the Mars Volta, Viagra Boys and Vowws are slated to perform Aug. 17, the weekend following this year's Outside Lands, at the park's Polo Field.
