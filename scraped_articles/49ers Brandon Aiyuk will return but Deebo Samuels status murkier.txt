49ers’ Brandon Aiyuk will return, but Deebo Samuel’s status murkier
-----
A week after Deebo Samuel played while his running mate, Brandon Aiyuk, was sidelined, it's possible the San Francisco 49ers' accomplished wide receivers could flip roles Sunday when the Cardinals visit Levi's Stadium.Aiyuk has been cleared to play after sitting out a 30-12 win over the Giants on Sept. 21 with a shoulder injury, but Samuel (ribs, knee) is questionable after returning to practice Friday on a limited basis.
