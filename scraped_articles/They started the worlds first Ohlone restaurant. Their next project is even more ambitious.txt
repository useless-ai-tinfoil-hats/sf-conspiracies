The team behind the first and only Ohlone restaurant has announced plans to purchase land for an ambitious new project in the East Bay.The founders of Cafe Ohlone, Louis Trevino and Vincent Medina, launched a $5 million fundraising campaign on Monday to buy land and build three new restaurant spaces, native gardens and a culinary and educational center. They are currently looking for 40 or more acres of land, hopefully in Sunol, where the Ohlone tribe has deep roots.
