Antioch police find 16-year-old fatally shot inside vehicle
-----
Antioch police are investigating the fatal shooting of a 16-year-old Pittsburg resident.Shortly after 5 a.m. Saturday, officers responded to a report of a wounded male inside a vehicle on the 1700 block of Observation Way, the Antioch Police Department said in a news release. They found a youth with at least one gunshot wound and summoned paramedics. The victim was transported to a local hospital, where he was pronounced dead.
