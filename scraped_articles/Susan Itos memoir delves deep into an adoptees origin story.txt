Susan Ito’s memoir delves deep into an adoptee’s origin story
-----
A half-Japanese girl braces herself to knock on a hotel room door. Though in her 20s, she knows that she looks much younger. She also knows — as some people have made her painfully aware — that she doesn't look "full Japanese." What is her other half? Who is her father? Who is she?Her name is Susan Kiyo Ito, and she is about to meet a woman she does not know — the woman who gave birth to her.
