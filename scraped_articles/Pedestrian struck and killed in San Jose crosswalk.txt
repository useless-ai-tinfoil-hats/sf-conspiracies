Pedestrian struck and killed in San Jose crosswalk
-----
A man was hit and killed Sunday night in San Jose, the seventh pedestrian to be killed by a vehicle in the city this year.Police said Monday that at approximately 7:40 p.m., officers responded to a collision at Saratoga Avenue near the Interstate 280 overpass. Police said their preliminary investigation revealed that a black 2021 Mitsubishi Mirage, driven by a man, was traveling northbound on Saratoga Avenue when it struck the pedestrian, who was crossing eastbound in the north
