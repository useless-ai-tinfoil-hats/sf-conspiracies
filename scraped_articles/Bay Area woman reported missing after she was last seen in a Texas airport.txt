Bay Area woman reported missing after she was last seen in a Texas airport
-----
A Bay Area woman has been reported missing after officials said she failed to board a flight in Texas bound for California.Piedmont resident Danielle Friedland, 36, has been missing since Nov. 22, the day before Thanksgiving. She was last seen at the George Bush Intercontinental Airport in Houston, police said.
Friedland was wearing a blue and orange jacket, dark pants, and was carrying a black backpack, police said. She had a beaded bracelet on her wrist.
