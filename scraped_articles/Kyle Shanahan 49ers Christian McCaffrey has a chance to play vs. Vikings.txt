Kyle Shanahan: 49ers’ Christian McCaffrey has a chance to play vs. Vikings
-----
San Francisco 49ers head coach Kyle Shanahan said All-Pro running back Christian McCaffrey is day-to-day with an oblique injury and could play Monday against the Vikings.In other words: The result from the MRI exam that McCaffrey had this week was encouraging.
"I thought it was," Shanahan said of the testing. "Anyone's that's day-to-day — that means they've got a chance."
