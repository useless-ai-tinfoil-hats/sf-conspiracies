Yosemite's Tioga Pass to remain closed over Memorial Day weekend
-----
Tioga Road, a seasonal road in Yosemite that typically reopens for the summer season in late May, will remain closed over Memorial Day weekend, the National Park Service announced.Road crews were still working on plowing the full width of Tioga Road as of May 16, along with side roads in Tuolumne Meadows. Additionally, the avalanche zone at Olmsted Point — a famous vista point overlooking Half Dome and Tenaya Canyon — is still active, making it unsafe for visitors.
