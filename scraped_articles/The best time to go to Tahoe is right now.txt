The best time to go to Tahoe is right now
-----
Going to the beach in Tahoe is an early morning mission, with a mandatory arrival before 8 or 9 a.m. to find a parking spot, or else be forced to park somewhere sketchy on the side of a busy highway and haul your beach gear a long, long way.But on a recent Sunday morning, I drove to the beach at Meeks Bay, on Tahoe's West Shore, at a more leisurely 11:30 a.m. and found a parking spot in the front row, in the shade.
