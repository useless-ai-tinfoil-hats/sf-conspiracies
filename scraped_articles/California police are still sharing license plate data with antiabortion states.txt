California police are still sharing license plate data with anti-abortion states
-----
Rejecting the advice of Attorney General Rob Bonta, some law enforcement agencies in California, including several in the Bay Area, are continuing to share automated license plate reader data with police departments in other states, which could use the information to track seekers and providers of abortions.
