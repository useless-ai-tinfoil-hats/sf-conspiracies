SFMOMA cuts 20 staff positions, citing a 35% drop in attendance
-----
The San Francisco Museum of Modern Art has cut 20 staff positions, citing a 35% drop in attendance since the start of the COVID-19 pandemic.SFMOMA Director Christopher Bedford justified the reduction in staff as a necessary "response to our current economic landscape," according to a "letter to the community" posted to the museum's website on Friday, Nov. 10. The letter was emailed to museum staff the same day.
The decision, effective immediately, includes laying off seven staff
