Review: ‘Ordinary Angels’ stars Hilary Swank as a woman set out to help an ailing girl and her family
-----
"Ordinary Angels" has some of the feeling of an after-school special because it's a heartwarming movie in which everybody is nice. But it's more well-made than most. It hooks the audience from the first scene and then builds in tension over the course of its almost two-hour running time.It's based on a true story, and the first thing you'll want to know about "Ordinary Angels" — and wonder about throughout — is how much of the story is actually true.
