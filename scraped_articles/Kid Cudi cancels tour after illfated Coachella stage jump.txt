Kid Cudi cancels tour after ill-fated Coachella stage jump
-----
Rock star antics aren't always glamorous. Just ask Kid Cudi, whose ill-fated Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival stage jump ended in a broken bone and, now, a canceled tour.The New York rapper, whose moody, melodic spin on hip-hop made waves in the late aughts, was a surprise addition to the festival's Weekend 2 lineup. But Cudi's highly anticipated Sunday performance was cut short after he attempted to jump off the stage.
