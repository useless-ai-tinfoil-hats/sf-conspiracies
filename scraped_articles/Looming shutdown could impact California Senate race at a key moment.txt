Looming shutdown could impact California Senate race at a key moment
-----
The looming federal government shutdown that could happen at midnight next Friday in Washington could potentially affect California's Senate campaign at a key moment for the candidates.If House members have to remain in Washington to cast votes to keep the government running, then the top three Senate candidates — Reps. Barbara Lee, Katie Porter and Adam Schiff — will miss all or part of next weekend's three-day California Democratic Party convention in Sacramento that begins Friday.
