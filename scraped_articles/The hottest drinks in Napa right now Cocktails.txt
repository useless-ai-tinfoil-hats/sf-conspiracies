The hottest drinks in Napa right now? Cocktails
-----
If you've imbibed in Napa over the past year, chances are you've sipped from a highball glass or a coupe along with the standard Riedel stemware. Napa has emerged as a hot spot for spirits with a rush of new craft cocktail bars opening since 2022.They range from tropical tiki styles to cocktail programs focused specifically on gin or Mexican spirits.
