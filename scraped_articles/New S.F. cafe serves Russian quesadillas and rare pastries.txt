New S.F. cafe serves ‘Russian quesadillas’ and rare pastries
-----
You can now bite into warm, flaky pastries from a central Russian region at a new SoMa cafe and bakery.AyDea, at the corner of Sixth and Bryant streets, opened this week with traditional flavors from Tatarstan, a republic within Russia that is home to Tatars, an ethnic group with its own language and culture.
