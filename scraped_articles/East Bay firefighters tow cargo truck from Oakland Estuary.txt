East Bay firefighters tow cargo truck from Oakland Estuary
-----
Rescue crews on Sunday pulled an apparently empty flatbed cargo truck from the Oakland Estuary, where it had been submerged for at least several hours.Scuba divers from the Oakland and Alameda fire departments searched the estuary for people but did not locate anyone who may have been in the truck when it entered the water, Oakland Fire Department spokesperson Michael Hunt said Sunday evening.
An eyewitness spotted the truck and called rescuers to an estuary boat ramp around 4:25
