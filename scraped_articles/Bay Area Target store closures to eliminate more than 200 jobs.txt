Bay Area Target store closures to eliminate more than 200 jobs
-----
The closure of three Target stores in the Bay Area will result in the termination of more than 200 jobs by the end of the year, regulatory filings show.In October, Target shuttered three of its stores in the region, citing concerns about theft and organized retail crime jeopardizing the safety of both employees and customers.
