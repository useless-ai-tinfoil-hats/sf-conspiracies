One of California’s only three-Michelin-star restaurants is finally reopening
-----
One of the Bay Area's only three-Michelin-star restaurants is reopening after a monthslong remodel, with a reimagined look and approach to fine dining.Quince, at 470 Pacific Ave., will return on Nov. 28. The dining room has been transformed into a lighter, warmer space. It's more intimate, with just 10 tables.
