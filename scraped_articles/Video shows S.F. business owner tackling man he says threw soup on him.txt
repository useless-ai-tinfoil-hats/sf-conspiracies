Video shows S.F. business owner tackling man he says threw soup on him
-----
San Francisco authorities are investigating an incident that was captured on surveillance video in which a South of Market business owner tackled another man to the ground on Wednesday, saying the man had thrown soup on him.The incident came to light Wednesday night when Mark Sackett, the owner of Box SF, a corporate events venue, said on social media that he had been attacked, while sharing an image showing three people on the ground.
