It's time, Farhan Zaidi: SF Giants need to call up the rookies right away
-----
Everything seemed to be going according to plan for the San Francisco Giants this winter.Last season was massively disappointing, no doubt, but there was one silver lining: the debut of several rookies who looked like they could be future contributors. After an offseason that saw the team finally, FINALLY spend money to improve the roster, it felt like the long-term plan back to relevancy had finally been put into motion: build through the system and supplement through free agency.
