Man arrested for alleged stabbing at San José Mineta International Airport
-----
A man was arrested on suspicion of stabbing a person at San José Mineta International Airport on Monday evening, authorities said.The unidentified suspect was taken into custody for stabbing a man at Terminal B just before 8:30 p.m., according to the San Jose Police Department. Flights were not affected, but vehicle and pedestrian traffic was temporarily directed away from the terminal as a precaution, said airport spokesperson Ana Maria State.
