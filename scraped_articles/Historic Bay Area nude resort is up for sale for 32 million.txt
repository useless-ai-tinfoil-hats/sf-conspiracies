Historic Bay Area nude resort is up for sale for $32 million
-----
Lupin Lodge, a historic nudist resort in the Los Gatos mountains, is up for sale, according to owner Lori Kay Stout.The property — which spans roughly 110 acres and includes sun decks, a pool and hot tub, lodging, a restaurant, hiking trails and other amenities — has grown to be too much for Stout to handle, she told SFGATE.
"It's been stressful. I'm really retiring for my health," she said. The Mercury News first reported the sale.
