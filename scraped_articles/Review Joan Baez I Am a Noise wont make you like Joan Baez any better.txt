Review: ‘Joan Baez I Am a Noise’ won’t make you like Joan Baez any better
-----
Despite all the filmmakers' flattering attention to its subject, "Joan Baez I Am a Noise" doesn't make a viewer come away liking Joan Baez. There are reasons for this, not the least of which is Baez's remarkable voice, which no normal human being could ever live up to.She sounds like an angel. She also sounds like an era.
