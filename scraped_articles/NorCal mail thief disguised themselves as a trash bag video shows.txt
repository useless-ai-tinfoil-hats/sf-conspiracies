NorCal mail thief disguised themselves as a trash bag, video shows
-----
A person caught on camera stealing mail in Sacramento got creative.Doorbell camera footage from a local resident published by KABC-TV shows a crouched person wearing a black trash bag over their body, with only their shoes showing, hobbling toward a front porch.
The disguised person then grabs the package and shuffles away.
The resident told news agency Storyful that he discovered the theft after he got a notification about the delivery on Friday but didn't find the package.
