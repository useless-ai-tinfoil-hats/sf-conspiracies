Oakland Zoo's Glowfari lights up the longest nights of the year
-----
As the sun sets and the Oakland Zoo's animal residents move indoors, the outdoor walkways and hills of the zoo awake into a winter wonderland known as Glowfari. Thousands of lights and hundreds of animal lanterns greet visitors while brightening up the longest Bay Area winter nights.The lantern festival replaced Zoolights, a feature at the Oakland Zoo for many years.
