45,000-home mega-development nears approval in Central California
-----
A massive development that could include 45,000 new homes and 150,000 residents is nearing approval in Central California.The project, called the Southeast Development Area, would transform the city of Fresno. In a 124-page proposal for SEDA, Fresno officials call it the "final frontier" for the city. The area, which is currently mostly rural, covers 9,000 acres.
