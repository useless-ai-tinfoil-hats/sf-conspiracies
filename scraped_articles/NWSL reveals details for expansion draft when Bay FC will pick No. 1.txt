NWSL reveals details for expansion draft, when Bay FC will pick No. 1
-----
There finally is a date for when Bay FC will select the bulk of their roster, and it's six weeks away.The National Women's Soccer League announced Wednesday that its expansion draft will take place virtually on Dec. 15, and the Bay Area club will have the first selection. The league's other expansion team, the Utah Royals, will pick second, as has been determined by coin flip.
