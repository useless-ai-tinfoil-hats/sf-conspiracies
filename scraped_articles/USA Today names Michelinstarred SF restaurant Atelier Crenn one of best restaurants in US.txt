USA Today names Michelin-starred SF restaurant Atelier Crenn one of best restaurants in US
-----
USA Today named three-Michelin-starred San Francisco restaurant Atelier Crenn one of the best restaurants in the country in its inaugural Restaurants of the Year. The list, which was announced on Thursday, features 47 restaurants, with Atelier Crenn being the only Bay Area pick.While USA Today says its journalists "didn't select the places you would find on your average roundup of best restaurants," Atelier Crenn is no stranger to accolades.
