Burning Man: Aerial photo shows how flooding turned playa into muddy mess
-----
An aerial photo showed widespread flooding at Burning Man as standing water remained days after rains began, plunging the festival into chaos.The photo, released by the Washoe County sheriff's office Sunday afternoon, showed the festival's namesake effigy and surrounding campsites inundated with water left over from the weekend's deluge. Heavy rains stranded 73,000 attendees in the muddy encampments with limited food, water and mobile phone connectivity.
