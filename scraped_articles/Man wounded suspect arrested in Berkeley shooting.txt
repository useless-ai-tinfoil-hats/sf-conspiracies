Man wounded, suspect arrested in Berkeley shooting
-----
A man was wounded and a second man was arrested in a shooting in Berkeley on Sunday morning, police said.The shooting occurred about 6 a.m. near a homeless shelter on the 700 block of Harrison Street. Police were told there were possibly several victims, prompting officers from multiple agencies to respond to the possibility of a situation involving an active shooter or several victims.
Police ultimately determined there was one victim: a man suffering from at least one gunshot
