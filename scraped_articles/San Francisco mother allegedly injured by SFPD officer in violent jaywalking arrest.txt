San Francisco mother allegedly injured by SFPD officer in violent jaywalking arrest
-----
A 34-year-old San Francisco mother of five was allegedly injured by a San Francisco police officer in a violent arrest after being stopped on suspicion of jaywalking last month.Christiana Porter was apprehended and cited on suspicion of jaywalking and resisting arrest near her home in the Inner Richmond on July 29 at approximately 7 p.m., Treva Stewart, her attorney, told SFGATE.
