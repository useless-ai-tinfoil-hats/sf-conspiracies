Maggie Rogers to perform surprise afternoon concert at the end of Dreamforce 2023
-----
Salesforce's annual conference, Dreamforce, just added another high-profile musical guest: Maggie Rogers.The singer and songwriter is scheduled to perform an hourlong show at 2:30 p.m. Thursday, in the final hours of the San Francisco tech conference. Rogers, who owns a home in Los Angeles, is known for emotional indie songs with cunning lyrics. She last performed in San Francisco in March.
