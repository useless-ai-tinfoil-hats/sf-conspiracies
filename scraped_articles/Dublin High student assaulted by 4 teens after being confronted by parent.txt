Dublin High student assaulted by 4 teens after being confronted by parent
-----
LATEST Aug. 27, 4 p.m. A 15-year-old boy Dublin High School student was assaulted on Friday by four teens, police said.The student was first confronted by a Dublin High School parent, the Dublin Police Department said in a news release, and then fled to the boys' locker room. The parent and four people in hoodies who ranged in age from 16 to 19 years old followed the student to the locker room, police said.
