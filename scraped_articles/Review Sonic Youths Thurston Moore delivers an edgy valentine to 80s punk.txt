Review: Sonic Youth’s Thurston Moore delivers an edgy valentine to ’80s punk
-----
Few musicians have more indie rock credibility than Thurston Moore, co-founder of Sonic Youth and the band's guitarist for 30 years. But despite his onstage and recording bona fides and boyish appearance, Moore, 65, does not use the pages of "Sonic Life" to spill too many tales of rock 'n' roll glory or a young man's fancy.
