The Port of San Francisco is evicting two Fisherman's Wharf restaurants that accrued a combined $1.4 million in debt, according to a port representative, adding to vacancies along the waterfront.Fishermen's Grotto No. 9, at 2847 Taylor St., and Tarantino's, at 206 Jefferson St., were named in unlawful detainer notices in San Francisco Superior Court earlier this week, after they received three-day notices dated Sept. 13 to pay rent or vacate.
