Man killed in San Francisco stabbing
-----
A man died after being stabbed in San Francisco Thursday morning.Just before 1:30 a.m. Thursday, San Francisco police said a 40-year-old man got into a confrontation with a 39-year-old man near the intersection of Market and Octavia streets.
During the confrontation, the 39-year-old man stabbed the other man with a knife, according to police records.
The victim was transported to a local hospital with life threatening injuries and later died, according to the Police
