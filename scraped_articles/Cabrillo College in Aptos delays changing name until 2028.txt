Cabrillo College in Aptos delays changing name until 2028
-----
A California college named after a Spanish explorer known for "discovering" California, and also for the brutal conquest of countless Indigenous people, has decided to delay changing its name until 2028.Cabrillo College in Aptos has had its name on the chopping block since 2020, when the college established a "name exploration committee" to decide whether the school's name should reflect the Spanish conquistador Juan Rodriguez Cabrillo.
