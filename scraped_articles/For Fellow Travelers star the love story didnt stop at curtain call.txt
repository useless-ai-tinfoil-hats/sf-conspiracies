For ‘Fellow Travelers’ star, the love story didn’t stop at curtain call
-----
A tale rife with paranoia, political persecution and personal betrayal, the opera "Fellow Travelers" is also a profound love story, on- and offstage.Based on the 2007 novel by Thomas Mallon, the opera is set during the McCarthy era, when fear of communist subversion collided with a hunt to remove homosexuals from government positions in what came to be known as the Lavender Scare.
