Broadway’s unlikely usher: How ‘A Strange Loop’ became a decorated musical
-----
The description of Michael R. Jackson's Pulitzer Prize- and Tony Award-winning musical "A Strange Loop" may sound a little convoluted. Not so simply put, it is the story of a Black gay man named Usher who is writing a musical called "A Strange Loop," about a Black gay man who is writing a musical.But while very meta and a bit tricky to fully parse, the show is quite straightforward and unapologetic when it comes to hilarity, biting lyrics and intense themes.
