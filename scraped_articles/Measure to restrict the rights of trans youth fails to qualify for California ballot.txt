Measure to restrict the rights of trans youth fails to qualify for California ballot
-----
An initiative targeting the rights of transgender youth that would ban gender-affirming care for minors and require schools to inform parents if their children identify as trans has failed to qualify for the November ballot.Proponents of the measure said Tuesday that they collected about 400,000 signatures, short of the 546,651 valid signatures required to appear on the ballot.
