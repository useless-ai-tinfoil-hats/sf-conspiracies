Google pulls its widely reviled 'Dear Sydney' Olympics advertisement
-----
LATEST Aug. 3, 9:45 a.m. Google has pulled its widely reviled Olympics ad touting its AI products, Variety reported Friday. The 60-second spot called "Dear Sydney," which has been playing during Olympics coverage, showed a Google chatbot helping to write a fan letter to an Olympian."While the ad tested well before airing, given the feedback, we have decided to phase the ad out of our Olympics rotation," Google told Variety in a statement.
