For once, Napa’s BottleRock music festival will serve good wines
-----
Historically, the wine offerings at the BottleRock Napa Valley music festival, taking place over Memorial Day weekend, have been notoriously boring. The wine lineup has repeatedly consisted of inexpensive, mass-market productions found at most grocery stores — a fact that's especially puzzling considering the festival is based in North America's most famous wine region.But this year, a handful of small, premium Napa wineries have been added to the typically lackluster roster.
