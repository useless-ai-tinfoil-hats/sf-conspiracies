Bay Area man arrested after girlfriend is killed, dismembered
-----
A South Bay man has been arrested on suspicion of killing a woman whose dismembered remains were found burned in a car in Santa Clara County in August, authorities said.Iban Alfaro Escobedo, 31, of Gilroy was held on murder charges in the death of Alyssa Salazar, 33, the Gilroy Police Department said in a statement.
The pair were in a relationship before Salazar went missing in late July, police said.
