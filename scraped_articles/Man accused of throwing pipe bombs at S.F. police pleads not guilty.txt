Man accused of throwing pipe bombs at S.F. police pleads not guilty
-----
A man accused of attacking a churchgoer and throwing pipe bombs at police has pleaded not guilty to several felony charges related to the attack and following police chase.San Francisco police allege Daniel Richard Garcia, 42, tried to rob a parishioner at Sts. Peter and Paul Church in North Beach, and then led police on a chase from San Francisco to Martinez, throwing two improvised pipe bombs in the process.
