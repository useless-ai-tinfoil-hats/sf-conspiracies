Giants break through again, beat Tigers 3-1 behind Logan Webb’s gem

Were it not for their tendency to start slow, the San Francisco Giants might well be in a playoff spot by now.The trend continues, but the Giants are learning how to work around it. Saturday, for the second day in a row, they were hitless through the early going — four innings this time. But in the fifth, the bottom of the lineup got to work with three hits, including Brett Wisely's two-run double, and Heliot Ramos scored Wisely with a base hit.
