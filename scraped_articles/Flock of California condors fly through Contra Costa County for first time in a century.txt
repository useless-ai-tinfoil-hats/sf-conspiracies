Flock of California condors fly through Contra Costa County for first time in a century
-----
Six California condors flew west of Mount Diablo recently, marking the first time a flock has visited Contra Costa County in a century, according to the nonprofit Save Mount Diablo.California condors nearly went extinct in the 1970s. In the mid-1980s, all wild condors were captured and taken to zoos to help protect the population, according to the California Department of Fish and Wildlife.
