How to watch the London NFL games this October
-----
Since 2007, the NFL has increased its global reach by hosting games in London. In October, there's three games scheduled across the pond: Atlanta Falcons vs. Jacksonville Jaguars (Oct. 1), Jaguars vs. Buffalo Bills (Oct. 8) and Baltimore Ravens vs. Tennessee Titans (Oct. 15). Because of the time difference between the U.S. and U.K., all three games will be broadcast at 9:30 a.m. EDT.The first game of the international series between the Falcons and Jaguars will be broadcast exclusively
