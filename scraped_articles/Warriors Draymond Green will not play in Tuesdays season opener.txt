Warriors’ Draymond Green will not play in Tuesday’s season opener
-----
Golden State Warriors forward Draymond Green will not play in Tuesday's season opener against Phoenix at Chase Center.Green was cleared to participate in 5-on-5 drills Sunday for the first time since suffering a left lateral ankle sprain in an offseason workout. The Warriors said his return to action will be based on continued progress as he works his way into game conditioning.
