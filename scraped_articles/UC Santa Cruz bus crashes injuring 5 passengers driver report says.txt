UC Santa Cruz bus crashes, injuring 5 passengers, driver, report says
-----
An on-campus bus crash at UC Santa Cruz injured six people Tuesday night, according to the Santa Cruz Sentinel.The bus, carrying five passengers and the driver, was traveling downhill on Coolidge Drive and left the road and struck the historic Cowell Ranch building around 8:30 p.m., the Sentinel reported. The bus, which sustained front-end damage, came to a rest in a grassy area.
