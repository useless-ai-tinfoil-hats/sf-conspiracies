South Bay day care where two children drowned was warned about pool, records show
-----
The owners of a South Bay day care where two children drowned Monday morning had been warned in the past that their pool could be a hazard, according to state records.Multiple local agencies are investigating Happy Happy Home Daycare, a residential day care in San Jose's Almaden neighborhood where two children died after being found in the day care's off-limits pool.
