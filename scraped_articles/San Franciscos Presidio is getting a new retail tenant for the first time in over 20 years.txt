San Francisco's Presidio is getting a new retail tenant for the first time in over 20 years
-----
Bay Area-based retailer Pet Food Express signed a lease to occupy renovated warehouse space in San Francisco's Presidio, the Presidio Trust announced Monday. It joins longtime retail tenant Sports Basement, which has been in the park since 2003.The mixed use area on former military property has been devoid of other traditional retail since Sports Basement opened.
