S.F. students demand SFUSD do more to prevent school shootings after incidents involving guns
-----
Students from San Francisco's public high schools walked out of their classrooms Friday afternoon to protest what they say are deficient security measures to prevent gun violence on campuses.The action comes after reports of firearms on two San Francisco high school campuses in December led to renewed calls to beef up security systems, with students and families questioning whether district officials are taking the threats seriously.
