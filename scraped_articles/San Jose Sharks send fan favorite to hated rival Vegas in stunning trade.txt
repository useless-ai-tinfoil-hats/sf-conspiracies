San Jose Sharks send fan favorite to hated rival Vegas in stunning trade
-----
That the San Jose Sharks traded fan favorite Tomas Hertl is itself not a big surprise. But trading him to the team's most hated rival, the Vegas Golden Knights? That would have seemed unthinkable before Friday.But Sharks general manager Mike Grier made that exact move happen on Friday, sending Hertl along with two future third-round picks to the Golden Knights for a 2025 first-round pick and prospect David Edstrom, Vegas' first-round pick from the 2023 draft.
