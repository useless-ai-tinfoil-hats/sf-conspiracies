2 Bay Area public markets nominated for best in the country by USA Today
-----
Two Bay Area food landmarks are in the running for best marketplace in America.San Francisco's historic Ferry Building Marketplace and Napa's popular Oxbow Public Market have been nominated for spots on this year's USA Today Readers' Choice list of the 10 best markets in the country. Two LA markets, the Original Farmers Market and Grand Central Market, made the cut.
