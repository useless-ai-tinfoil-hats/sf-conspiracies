SF's Hardly Strictly Bluegrass confirms more artists for free October festival
-----
Hardly Strictly Bluegrass, San Francisco's free music festival in Golden Gate Park, is less than two months away, and just announced its second round of confirmed artists Wednesday.What started as a one-day local show in 2001 put on by venture capitalist-by-day, banjo player-by-night Warren Hellman has grown into an annual three-day celebration of artists and bands across multiple genres. This year, the free festival is scheduled take place Oct. 4 through 6.
