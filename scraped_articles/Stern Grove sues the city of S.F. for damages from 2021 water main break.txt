Stern Grove sues the city of S.F. for damages from 2021 water main break
-----
Three years after a ruptured water main on Sloat Boulevard caused hundreds of thousands of gallons of water to flood San Francisco's Stern Grove outdoor amphitheater, the nonprofit group behind the annual Stern Grove Festival has sued the city over the incident.The lawsuit, filed in San Francisco Superior Court on Aug. 23 by the Stern Grove Festival Association, seeks nearly $336,000 in damages.
