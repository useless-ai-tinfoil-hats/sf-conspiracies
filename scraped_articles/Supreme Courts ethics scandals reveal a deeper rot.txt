Supreme Court’s ethics scandals reveal a deeper rot
-----
Other than breathtaking power over society, exorbitant side perks and total lack of accountability, being a Supreme Court justice today sure seems like a tough gig.The court is historically unpopular. Justices are under constant fire for their ethical lapses and partisan behavior. And no one seems to like, much less trust, each other — unpleasantness that may have split even those among the court's conservative supermajority.
The justices also increasingly live under a microscope.
