Major budget-friendly grocery chain Aldi plans for hundreds of US stores, with more in Calif.
-----
Aldi, a popular national grocery chain, aims to open hundreds of new stores throughout the U.S., including future outposts in California.In early March, Aldi CEO Jason Hart announced that the German-based grocery chain, which has family ties to Trader Joe's, plans to invest $9 million to open 800 stores across the U.S. by 2028. Hart shared that the western expansion would feature Aldi stores in Phoenix, Las Vegas and Southern California.
