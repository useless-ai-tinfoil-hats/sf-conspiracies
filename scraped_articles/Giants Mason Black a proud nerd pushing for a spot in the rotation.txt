Giants’ Mason Black, ‘a proud nerd,’ pushing for a spot in the rotation
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — Mason Black doesn't even whisper when he says it. The San Francisco Giants right-hander, who is emerging as a frontrunner for a rotation spot going into the season, is, he announced, "a proud nerd.""Did you just admit you're a nerd?" fellow starter Jordan Hicks said in disbelief as he walked past.
"I did!" Black said, practically thumping his chest.
