40-year-old San Francisco Burmese restaurant Mandalay wins James Beard Award
-----
On Wednesday, the James Beard Foundation announced this year's winners of the America's Classics Award. One 40-year-old San Francisco restaurant made the list.Mandalay, a Burmese restaurant in San Francisco's Inner Richmond neighborhood, was founded in 1984. While it was not the first Burmese restaurant to open in San Francisco, it is the oldest still standing, according to a news release from the James Beard Foundation.
