What Nancy Pelosi said about her husband’s S.F. attack — and Trump — in new book, ‘The Art of Power’
-----
The media frenzy that arose in the aftermath of the attack on Paul Pelosi was a painful experience that led to a "second assault" on her family, Speaker Emerita Nancy Pelosi said in her new book, "The Art of Power."The book, she was careful to point out, is not a memoir but a "rendering of the most consequential challenges of my time in the leadership of the U.S. House of Representatives."
The book details several political battles, including the passage of the Affordable Care Act
