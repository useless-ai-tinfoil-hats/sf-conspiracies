Conflicting reports over possible Warriors trade for 9-time NBA All-Star Paul George
-----
As the Golden State Warriors work the phones in the final week before NBA free agency, rumors are flying around the possibility of adding Clippers star Paul George.George has a $48.8 million player option for the 2024-25 season that he could choose to decline and become a free agent, with NBA free agency officially beginning on Sunday. But George is reportedly considering picking up his option as a means to facilitate a trade, and the Warriors are viewed by some as a viable suitor.
