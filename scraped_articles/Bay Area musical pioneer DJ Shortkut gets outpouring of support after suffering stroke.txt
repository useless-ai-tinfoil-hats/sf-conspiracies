Bay Area musical pioneer DJ Shortkut gets outpouring of support after suffering stroke
-----
In the wake of a medical emergency, the Bay Area's music scene and DJs around the world are rallying behind a local legend.Jonathan Cruz, better known as DJ Shortkut, suffered a hemorrhagic stroke on July 1, according to a GoFundMe co-organized by his manager, leaving him with several medical complications, including speech, vision and cognitive difficulties and body paralysis.
The stroke occurred just two days after he spun records for a crowd of 17,000 at the Hollywood Bowl
