California woman, 93, fatally attacked by neighbor's dogs, police say
-----
A 93-year-old California woman was mauled Thursday by two dogs in the Central Valley town of Modesto, and she died the next day from her injuries, officials said.Chanthy Philavong was knocked to the ground by the dogs outside her home, KCRA reported. "It was chaos, it was a matter of 80 seconds — looking back it was 80 seconds — and just like that everybody's lives changed. It was awful," neighbor Jenece Dendulk told the TV station.
