New ‘Megalopolis’ trailer declares Francis Ford Coppola ‘has always been ahead of his time’
-----
If you hated "The Godfather" and "Apocalypse Now," you'll surely despise "Megalopolis."That's one way to read Lionsgate's official trailer for Francis Ford Coppola's dream project, released Wednesday, Aug. 21. It seems to operate as both a promotion of the film that hits theaters Sept. 27 as well as damage control from the fallout from its divisive Cannes Film Festival reception in May.
