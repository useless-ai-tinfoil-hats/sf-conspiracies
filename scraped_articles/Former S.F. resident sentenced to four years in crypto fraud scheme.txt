Former S.F. resident sentenced to four years in crypto fraud scheme
-----
A former San Francisco man who admitted swindling cryptocurrency investors out of millions of dollars was sentenced to four years in federal prison and fined $5 million Thursday, prosecutors said.William Ichioka, 30, pleaded guilty in July to five charges of fraud related to the funds he collected from more than 100 customers, starting in 2018, telling them they could make 10% profits in 30 days from investments in cryptocurrency, futures and foreign exchange currency transactions.
