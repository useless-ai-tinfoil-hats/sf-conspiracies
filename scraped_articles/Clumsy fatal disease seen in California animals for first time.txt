'Clumsy' fatal disease seen in California animals for first time
-----
Chronic wasting disease has been detected in deer and elk in California for the first time, the state's Department of Fish and Wildlife announced.The CDFW confirmed the disease's presence Monday after officials collected samples from two deer in Madera and Inyo counties, a May 7 news release reads.
