Yosemite rockfall sends hundreds of boulders onto key segment of John Muir Trail
-----
A significant rockfall in Yosemite National Park has closed a key segment of the John Muir Trail near one of the most popular hiking areas of the park.According to a Wednesday Facebook post from the park, a large rockfall that occurred on April 15 near Nevada Fall at the rear of Yosemite Valley sent "hundreds of boulders" onto the John Muir Trail.
