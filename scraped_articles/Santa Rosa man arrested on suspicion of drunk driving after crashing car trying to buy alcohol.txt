Santa Rosa man arrested on suspicion of drunk driving after crashing car, trying to buy alcohol
-----
Santa Rosa police arrested a man on suspicion of drunken driving Wednesday who they said tried to enter a business and buy alcohol after crashing his car into the side of the property.Around 2:40 p.m. Wednesday, officers responded to a report that a Toyota truck crashed into a business on the 2400 block of Dutton Avenue. Photos posted by Santa Rosa police seemed to indicate the business was Ray's Food Center at 2455 Dutton Ave.
