SFPD has a new foot pursuit policy. Will it change anything?
-----
For the first time in its 174-year history, the San Francisco Police Department will have a policy concerning officer foot pursuits. But it's unclear whether the policy, written by Police Department personnel and described by an oversight official as "the most permissive" in the U.S., will change anything at a department that has been criticized both for being too aggressive and not aggressive enough.The San Francisco Police Commission adopted the four-page policy Wednesday.
