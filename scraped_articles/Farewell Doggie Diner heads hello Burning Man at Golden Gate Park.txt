Farewell Doggie Diner heads, hello Burning Man at Golden Gate Park
-----
The newest piece of public art to take up space on Golden Gate Park's Golden Mile — the glorious stretch of John F. Kennedy Drive closed to cars — is here, and it came from Burning Man.Fully assembled across from the Skatin' Place, the 20-foot-high fabric and metal sculpture reads "NO DANCING."
