Two East Bay men arrested, accused of holding hostage at gunpoint
-----
Police have arrested two East Bay men they say held a kidnapping victim at gunpoint at a San Joaquin Valley home.Police learned Friday that the victim was being held hostage at a home in the 1500 block of Lankershire Drive in Tracy. Authorities surrounded the residence and used a drone to surveil the area, spotting and arresting one of the suspects who tried to run out the back of the home, Tracy police said.
