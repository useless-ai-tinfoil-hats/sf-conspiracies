Costco food court dumps churros for a new dessert. Here's how it tastes.
-----
Costco's food court choices are so limited that any change is a big deal for fans. Recently, rumors began swirling that one of its few dessert options was headed for the fast food graveyard. The change is indeed happening nationwide, as the churro, which went for about $1.50, is being replaced with a giant chocolate chip cookie for $2.49.I went in search of this food court treat at our local Bay Area Costcos.
