The verdict is in on whether Burning Man passed its post-festival trash inspection
-----
After a chaotic festival plagued by rain, mud and dirty toilets, Burning Man has passed its annual Bureau of Land Management inspection, by the narrowest of possible margins.That Burning Man passed its inspection at all is somewhat surprising given how much of a mess the festival created this year.
