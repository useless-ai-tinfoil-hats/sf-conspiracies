Catch the artists playing Monterey Jazz Festival around the Bay Area
-----
In 1958's first Monterey Jazz Festival, giants of jazz including Billie Holliday, Art Farmer and Wes Montgomery took the stage. Still going strong, this year's 66th iteration of the historically long-running music fest offers up a lineup true to its stellar roots and reputation.And whether they attend the famed Monterey festival, Bay Area jazz fans are in luck with live shows from festival artists and other stellar musicians performing regionally before, during and after the main event
