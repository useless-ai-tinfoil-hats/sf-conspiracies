Woman arrested on suspicion of attempted murder in San Mateo shooting
-----
San Mateo police have arrested a woman they say was responsible for a shooting that injured another woman hours earlier.The shooting occurred around 4:45 p.m. Thursday on the 1500 block of Lago Street, the San Mateo Police Department said. Officers found shell casings and blood droplets at the scene, but the victim had left. She was later found in San Carlos and taken to a local hospital, the police department said. She remained there in stable condition as of Saturday, the agency added.
