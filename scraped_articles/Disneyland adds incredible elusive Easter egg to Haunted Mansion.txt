Disneyland adds incredible, elusive Easter egg to Haunted Mansion
-----
A one-of-a-kind Easter egg was added to Disneyland's Haunted Mansion during its yearly conversion into "Haunted Mansion Holiday," the seasonal overlay for both Halloween and Christmas.The tribute was first spotted by MiceChat, which captured footage of it during the ride's reopening on Friday. Blink and you'll miss it: a spooky figure seemingly made of white candle wax hidden among a cluster of colorful presents.
