UC Berkeley Gaza protesters start to break down camp after talks with university
-----
Students demanding that UC Berkeley divest from companies doing business in Israel began breaking their campus encampment Tuesday afternoon as the action entered its fourth week and the group's leaders held discussions with university administrators.Representatives for the university said that the school had been in talks with the students and that UC Berkeley would release more information if the clearance process continued and was completed peacefully.
