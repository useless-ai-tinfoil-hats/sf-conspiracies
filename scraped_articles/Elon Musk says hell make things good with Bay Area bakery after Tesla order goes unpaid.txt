Elon Musk says he'll 'make things good' with Bay Area bakery after Tesla order goes unpaid
-----
A San Jose pie company says it lost out on thousands of dollars after Tesla reneged on a catering order at the 11th hour. After the story went viral, Elon Musk, owner of X, formerly known as Twitter, vowed that he would "make things good with the bakery.""People should always be able to count on Tesla trying its best," he wrote on Friday.
