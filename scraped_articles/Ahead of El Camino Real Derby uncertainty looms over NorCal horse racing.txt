Ahead of El Camino Real Derby, uncertainty looms over NorCal horse racing
-----
The $100,000 El Camino Real Derby on Saturday at Golden Gate Fields is the only race of the hundreds during the track's final season that didn't experience a purse cut.With the track closing June 9 and trying to eliminate a $3.1 million deficit in purse funding from previous seasons, the El Camino Real Derby and the Grade 3, $175,000 San Francisco Mile on April 27 are the track's only stakes in 2024. There were 15 stakes in the previous winter-spring season.
