Giants designate Joey Bart, once Buster Posey’s heir, for assignment
-----
SAN DIEGO — Joey Bart, once deemed Buster Posey's heir apparent behind the plate for the San Francisco Giants, was designated for assignment to make room for starting pitcher Daulton Jefferies on the 40-man roster on Sunday.Bart initially made the Opening Day roster as a third catcher behind Patrick Bailey and Tom Murphy to protect him from waivers, a situation that was never sustainable for long.
