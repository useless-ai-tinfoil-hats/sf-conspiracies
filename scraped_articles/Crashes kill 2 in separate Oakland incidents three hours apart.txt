Crashes kill 2 in separate Oakland incidents three hours apart
-----
Two people died in the span of three hours in separate traffic collisions in Oakland, police said Monday.Oakland police officials said they were investigating the first fatal collision that occurred on Sunday at 11:30 p.m. along the 1600 block of 73rd Avenue, where a person on a scooter was struck dead in a collision with a driver.
The rider was pronounced dead, and the driver of the vehicle remained at the scene, police said.
