Serial killer who left three bodies in San Francisco's Golden Gate Park dies
-----
A former Bay Area police officer who murdered six people, leaving three bodies stuffed into barrels in Golden Gate Park, died Friday.Anthony Sully, 79, died of natural causes at a medical facility outside the San Quentin Rehabilitation Center, where he had been housed for decades, according to the California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation.
