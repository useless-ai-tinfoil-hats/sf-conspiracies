In full circle moment, Angel Blue returns to San Francisco for ‘Il Trovatore’
-----
Two-time Grammy winner Angel Blue is set to take center stage in the San Francisco Opera's upcoming production of Giuseppe Verdi's 1853 drama "Il Trovatore," making her role debut as Leonora, the noblewoman who gets caught up in a difficult love triangle.But the soprano is no stranger to the city or the company — in 2009, she played Clara in the Opera's production of "Porgy and Bess."
