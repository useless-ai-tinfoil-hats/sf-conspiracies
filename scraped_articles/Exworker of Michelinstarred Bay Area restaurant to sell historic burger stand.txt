Ex-worker of Michelin-starred Bay Area restaurant to sell historic burger stand
-----
A 101-year-old slice of Americana in Northern California is ready to be sold and will hit the market soon.The current owners of Pick's Drive-In, an old-school burger joint in Cloverdale, are parting with the historic spot after three years, according to the Press Democrat, but they are waiting to find the "right buyer." They are currently taking inquiries and bids before officially listing the business at 117 S Cloverdale Blvd.
