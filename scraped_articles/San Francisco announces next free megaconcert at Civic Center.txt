San Francisco announces next free megaconcert at Civic Center
-----
San Francisco music fans have another treat on the horizon. Adding to what has already been an epic year of free concerts, Another Planet Entertainment just announced the second in a series of three downtown concerts that won't cost attendees anything more than an email address.Following a massive dance party on the Embarcadero last month featuring DJs from Dirtybird Records, the next event is scheduled to take place at Civic Center featuring Portland rock band Portugal.
