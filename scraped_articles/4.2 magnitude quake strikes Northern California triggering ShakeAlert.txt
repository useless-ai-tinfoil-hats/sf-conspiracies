4.2 magnitude quake strikes Northern California, triggering ShakeAlert
-----
UPDATE: Californians got a MyShake earthquake test alert at 3 a.m. Many aren't happyA 4.2 magnitude earthquake struck Wednesday morning in Sacramento County, according to preliminary data from the U.S. Geological Survey.
The quake, which struck at 9:29 a.m., was centered near Isleton, the agency's website showed.
