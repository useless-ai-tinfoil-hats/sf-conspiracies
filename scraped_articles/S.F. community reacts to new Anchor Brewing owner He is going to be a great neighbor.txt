S.F. community reacts to new Anchor Brewing owner: ‘He is going to be a great neighbor’
-----
When billionaire yogurt entrepreneur Hamdi Ulukaya announced his acquisition of Anchor Brewing Co. Friday morning, the man behind the Chobani yogurt empire made one thing clear: The investment was about the beer, not the real estate.That distinction was a relief to neighbors and city officials who feared that an investor would seize on the 2.17-acres of prime Potrero Hill property as ripe for condos or biotech research space, while brewing Anchor beer far from San Francisco.
