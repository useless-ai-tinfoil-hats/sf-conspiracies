Richmond police seek outside investigation of arrest of Black man struck by officer
-----
UPDATE: Man struck by Richmond police officer files assault claim against cityThe Richmond Police Department has hired an outside investigator to review an incident in which an officer repeatedly struck, then arrested, a Black man who was filming police from a public sidewalk earlier this month.
In an email to the Chronicle, Lt. Donald Patchin, a spokesperson for the police department, said that Chief Bisa French "has requested this investigation be handled by an outside entity."
