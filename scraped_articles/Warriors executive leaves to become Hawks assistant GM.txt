Warriors executive leaves to become Hawks assistant GM
-----
Golden State Warriors executive Onsi Saleh is leaving the franchise to become an assistant general manager with the Atlanta Hawks, the Chronicle confirmed Friday.Saleh was Golden State's vice president of basketball strategy and team counsel, working collaboratively with general manager Mike Dunleavy. Included in Saleh's duties: assistance with roster construction, salary-cap management and the oversight of compliance with the NBA.
