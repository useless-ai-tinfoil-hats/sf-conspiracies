'Shame on you': Uber drivers blast CEO at San Francisco headquarters protest
-----
Dozens of Uber drivers gathered at the ride-hailing giant's San Francisco headquarters on Wednesday to protest the company's treatment of its gig workforce.A caravan of gig drivers honked horns and waved signs out their windows as they circled Uber's Mission Bay headquarters around noon, cars adorned with slogans about working conditions.
