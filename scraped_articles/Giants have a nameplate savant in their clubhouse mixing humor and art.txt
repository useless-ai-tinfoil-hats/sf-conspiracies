Giants have a nameplate savant in their clubhouse, mixing humor and art
-----
On the day pediatric cancer patients provide player portraits for the scoreboard — this year, that's Sunday — the San Francisco Giants players walk into the clubhouse to find those same kid-produced sketches featuring prominently on their locker nameplates.Most of the players, staff and media immediately walk around to see every portrait, with oohs of appreciation heard throughout the morning.
