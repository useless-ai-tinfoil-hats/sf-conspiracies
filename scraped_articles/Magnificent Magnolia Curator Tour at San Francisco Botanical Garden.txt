Magnificent Magnolia Curator Tour at San Francisco Botanical Garden
-----
Experience one of the most significant conservation collections of Magnolias in the United States in bloom from mid-January through March. Admire the sights and scents on the branches of more than 200 elegant trees as velvety silver buds and saucer-sized pink, white, and magenta flowers bloom in this always spectacular annual bloom at the Garden. Join us for a beautiful and informative Magnolia tour through the Garden led by our experts. Each tour will run for approximately 90 minutes. Garden
