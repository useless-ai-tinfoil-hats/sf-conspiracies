S.F. is getting a new kind of bike parking next year
-----
San Franciscans will get a new secure bike-parking option in the Yerba Buena area starting early next year: bike hangars, or lockable, half-cylinder-shaped metal sheds along the curb.The colorful hangars, which will cost 5 cents an hour and are already placed in two locations — at Fourth and Minna streets by the Metreon, and at Howard and Second streets — are part of a two-year pilot project supported by Mayor London Breed meant to provide a safer bike parking option, said San Francisco
