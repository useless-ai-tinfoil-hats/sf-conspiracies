Chappell Roan scolds VIPs at San Francisco’s Outside Lands festival: ‘You’re not fun’
-----
Chappell Roan displayed little tolerance for the passive spectators in the VIP section during her highly anticipated set during the last day of Outside Lands."It's so weird that VIP thinks they're so way too cool to do this!" the pop sensation admonished the crowd, who had paid hundreds of dollars extra for the privilege of standing near the Lands End Stage, as she introduced her hit "Hot To Go!" a few songs into her nearly hour-long set on Sunday, Aug. 11.
