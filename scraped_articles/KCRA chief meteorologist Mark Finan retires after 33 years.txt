KCRA chief meteorologist Mark Finan retires after 33 years
-----
TV news meteorologist Mark Finan, whose presence guided Californians through decades of floods, wildfires, sunshine and snow, has retired. KCRA's chief meteorologist signed off for the final time on Friday.Finan, who turns 65 later this month, joined KCRA in 1991. He became a comforting, ubiquitous figure during natural disasters, especially in 1997 when catastrophic flooding inundated Northern California.
