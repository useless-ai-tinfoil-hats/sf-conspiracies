‘Jeopardy!’ champ Amy Schneider's new memoir talks trivia, transphobia — and what's next
-----
When you've spent months on national television demonstrating a dazzling mastery of history, geography, world literature and so much more, people tend to remember you.For Amy Schneider, whose 40-game victory streak on "Jeopardy!" was the second-longest in the history of the popular television quiz show, it's as if the cameras are always potentially rolling.
"You were on 'Jeopardy! ' — weren't you?" a passing stranger called out to her in the middle of a recent Chronicle interview
