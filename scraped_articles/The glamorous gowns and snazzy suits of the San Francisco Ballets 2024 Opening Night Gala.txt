The glamorous gowns and snazzy suits of the San Francisco Ballet's 2024 Opening Night Gala
-----
On Wednesday evening, Civic Center was awash in captivating, glamorous, and sometimes glitzy gowns and suits for the San Francisco Ballet's annual Opening Night Gala.With a theme of "Pandora: Curiosity Unveiled," San Francisco City Hall's atrium and grand staircase once again served as the elegant backdrop for the gala's pre-show dinner and after-party for the first time since 2020.
