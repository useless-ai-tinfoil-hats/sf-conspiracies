Review: State of Play Festival proves the place to be for the future of West Coast dance
-----
"Wow, everybody's here!" a patron shouted while searching for a seat on opening night of ODC Theater's State of Play Festival.A quick survey of the stadium-style rows indeed revealed several artistic directors and dancers representing the Bay Area's most notable troupes in attendance. And yet the mood was more family reunion than powerhouse conference.
