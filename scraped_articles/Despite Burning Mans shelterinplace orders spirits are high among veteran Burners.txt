Despite Burning Man’s shelter-in-place orders, ‘spirits are high’ among veteran Burners
-----
Even after unusually heavy rain and flooding forced them to shelter in place, one veteran Burning Man couple says they're choosing to focus on the rainbow after the rain.Karen Calisterio, 57, and David Calisterio, 64, of Isleton (Sacramento County) were serving cocktails at a bar on the campground in the Black Rock Desert of northwestern Nevada when the rain began around 3 p.m. Friday, Karen Calisterio said.
