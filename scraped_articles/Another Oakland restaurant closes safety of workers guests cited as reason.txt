Another Oakland restaurant closes; safety of workers, guests cited as reason
-----
Oakland's sole Denny's restaurant, located just minutes from the city's crime epicenter, has closed for good, the company said Wednesday.In a statement provided to the Chronicle, the company said the decision to close the location at 601 Hegenberger Road was made because "the safety and well-being of Denny's team members and valued guests is our top priority."
