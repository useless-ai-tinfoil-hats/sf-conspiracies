Two bodies pulled from waterfall near Yosemite
-----
Two bodies were recovered from a waterfall south of Yosemite Wednesday, Madera County authorities said.At around 11:30 a.m. Wednesday, search and rescue teams in Madera County responded to a call reporting an unresponsive woman in the water at Angel Falls, near Bass Lake. At the scene, deputies found items that led them to believe a second person was missing.
