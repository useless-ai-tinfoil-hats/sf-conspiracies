Inmate found dead at San Mateo County jail
-----
A 34-year-old man was found dead in his cell at a San Mateo County jail on Saturday, authorities said.Correctional officers discovered the unresponsive man at 9:18 p.m. during a routine welfare check, Sgt. Javier Acosta of the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office said in a statement Sunday. He was housed alone in a section of the Maguire Correctional Facility devoted to residents with serious mental illness, Acosta said.
