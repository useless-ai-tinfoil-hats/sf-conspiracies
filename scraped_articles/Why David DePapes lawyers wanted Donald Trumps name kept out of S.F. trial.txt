Why David DePape’s lawyers wanted Donald Trump’s name kept out of S.F. trial
-----
Attorneys for the man accused of violently attacking Paul Pelosi with a hammer last year asked a San Francisco Superior Court Judge to exclude any mention of one specific person: former President Donald Trump.Judge Harry Dorfman denied that request this month, just weeks before opening statements in the state trial of David DePape began Wednesday morning, meaning its possible the deeply controversial former president could come up during proceedings.
