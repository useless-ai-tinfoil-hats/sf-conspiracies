UC Berkeley spent nearly $8 million on its whirlwind seizure of People's Park in January
-----
UC Berkeley's January seizure and whirlwind transformation of People's Park racked up quite the bill: The school spent nearly $8 million on the operation, according to a UC Berkeley news release.The school's efforts, which included walling off the 2.8-acre park with shipping containers, marked a flashpoint in the decadeslong battle for control of the famous and controversial site. Since the late 1960s, the university-owned land had become a space of community and activism for students
