Slow start dooms San Jose State in Hawaii Bowl loss to Coastal Carolina
-----
Seemingly on a vacation in the islands for three quarters, San Jose State finally got to work in the fourth quarter of the Hawaii Bowl in Honolulu on Saturday night.The Spartans, though, couldn't complete their comeback. Coastal Carolina pulled out a 24-14 victory, ending SJSU's winning streak at six games.
