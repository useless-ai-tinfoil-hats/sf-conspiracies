Graduates of these Bay Area universities rank near the top for pay nationwide
-----
Two Bay Area universities cracked the top 10 on a new ranking of schools with the highest-paid graduates.Stanford University came in at No. 5 on the College Salary Report by Payscale, a software company that tracks compensation data across industries.
According to the report, which the company says is based on millions of survey responses from graduates, the median early career pay for a Stanford grad is $98,900, while the same figure for mid-career grads reaches $177,500.
