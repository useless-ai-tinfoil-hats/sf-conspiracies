Dead whale washes ashore at S.F.’s Fort Funston, emitting ‘terrible and pungent’ odor
-----
A whale carcass washed ashore at San Francisco's Fort Funston, according to eyewitnesses and the California Academy of Sciences.Leila Taleghani, who visits the park every weekend, said she noticed the carcass for the first time Saturday.
"It had already started to decompose and was bubbling at the surface," said Taleghani, who described the odor as "terrible and pungent."
