How a Las Vegas strike could impact travelers' hotel, casino plans
-----
A massive strike from one of Las Vegas' most prominent hospitality workers unions may be on the way that could severely impact 22 casinos and resorts on the Las Vegas Strip.On Tuesday, the Culinary and Bartenders Unions announced that 95% of workers voted to authorize a strike. The union is composed of guest room attendants, cocktail and food servers, porters, bellpeople, cooks, bartenders, laundry and kitchen workers.
