Golden State's WNBA team officially has its name
-----
The San Francisco Bay Area's WNBA franchise officially has a name: the Golden State Valkyries.The team officially announced its name at 5:30 a.m. local time through social media channels with a 15-second video that showcased the Valkyries' logo along with the tagline "Join our ascent." The video also unveils the color scheme, which is a light purple and black.
