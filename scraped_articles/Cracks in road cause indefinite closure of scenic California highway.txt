Cracks in road cause indefinite closure of scenic California highway
-----
Part of state Route 154, an oft-debated shortcut from US 101 on the way to or from Santa Barbara, is closed indefinitely while repairs are made to cracks in the road discovered June 21."At this time, there is no estimate for a re-opening or partial re-opening," Genelle Padilla, a Caltrans spokesperson wrote in an email to SFGATE. "Each project varies, depending on a variety of factors. The completion of the 24-hour drilling operation could be an indicator for a timeline."
