Missing California woman found dead in orchard, 2 men arrested
-----
Two men have been arrested in connection with the killing of a 19-year-old woman who disappeared on Friday night. The arrests come after a weekend of police work that involved a high-speed chase, a burning car and a body found in an orchard on the outskirts of Madera, California.Melanie Camacho was last seen finishing her shift at AutoZone on Nov. 24 at about 10:15 p.m. Surveillance footage showed her 2014 white Nissan Altima heading north on Gateway Drive in Madera at about 10:30 p.m.
