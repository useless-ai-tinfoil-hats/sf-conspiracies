Janet Yellen in S.F: ‘California is a fitting place’ for efforts to reengage with China
-----
Treasury Secretary Janet Yellen said Friday that "California is a fitting place" for U.S. efforts to strengthen its relationship with China on the eve of the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation summit, when San Francisco will host leaders from around the world.Yellen met with Chinese Vice Premier He Lifeng on Thursday and Friday in San Francisco to discuss issues including economic competition and climate change.
