SFO flight to Israel boomerangs back as fighting erupts
-----
A United Airlines flight from San Francisco International Airport bound for Tel Aviv was diverted back to California on Oct. 6 just as Hamas launched a surprise attack on Israel.The flight departed SFO at 8:25 p.m. local time and was bound for Ben Gurion International Airport, according to data from the flight tracking site Flightradar24. The Tel Aviv hub is considered Israel's main airport.
