Warriors are one of NBA’s smallest teams. They’re also elite at rebounding
-----
BOSTON — The Golden State Warriors are collectively the smallest team in the NBA.But it hasn't stopped them one iota from being among the biggest on the boards.
With an average height of 6 feet, 5.4 inches, the Warriors are second in the NBA in rebounds per game (46.9) and third in rebounding rate (52.1%) — thereby proving their optimal small-ball lineup isn't easily bullied on the glass.
