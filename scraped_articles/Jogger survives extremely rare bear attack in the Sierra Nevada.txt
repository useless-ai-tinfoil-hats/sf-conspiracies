Jogger survives 'extremely rare' bear attack in the Sierra Nevada
-----
A 24-year-old man survived a rare bear attack while out on a run in the Sierra Nevada earlier this week.The attack happened on Monday at approximately 5 p.m. on North Fork Road in Long Barn, an unincorporated area in Tuolumne County, Patrick Foy, captain of the California Department of Fish and Wildlife's law enforcement division, told SFGATE.
