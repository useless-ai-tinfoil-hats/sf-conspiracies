Elementary school students uncover first-of-its-kind fossil in the Santa Cruz Mountains
-----
Kindergartners and first-graders from Tara Redwood School in Soquel made an astonishing discovery last spring while playing in the Santa Cruz Mountains: a fossil sloth bone that's the first of its kind in Santa Cruz County.The elementary school students brought their finding to the Santa Cruz Museum of Natural History, where paleontology advisor Wayne Thompson recognized it as an arm bone that likely belonged to an ancient sloth.
