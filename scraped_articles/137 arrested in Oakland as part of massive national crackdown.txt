137 arrested in Oakland as part of massive national crackdown
-----
The Oakland Police Department and the U.S. Marshals Service arrested 137 fugitives and closed 156 warrants as part of a national crackdown, federal authorities announced Wednesday.The initiative, called Operation North Star III, was a three-month collaboration between the federal and local law enforcement agencies in 20 cities that resulted in 4,455 arrests and the confiscation of 555 firearms, more than $1 million in illicit currency and 85 kilograms of illegal narcotics, the U.S.
