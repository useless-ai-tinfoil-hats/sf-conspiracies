Coachella headliner tells fans to put down their phones
-----
One of the headlining acts for this year's Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival has a simple message for fans ahead of Weekend 1: Please put your phones down.The annual music-focused festival out in Indio, California, first debuted in 1999, but only in the past 15 or so years have high-quality cellphones and social media been added to the mix.
