Mayor Breed wants S.F. voters to give police more power. Here are the details
-----
Mayor London Breed plans to ask San Francisco voters to expand police powers by easing restrictions on when police can chase suspects by car, reducing officers' paperwork requirements and giving them greater ability to use surveillance technology.Breed announced Tuesday that she will put an ordinance on the March 2024 ballot that would roll back 10-year-old limits the city's Police Commission placed on vehicle pursuits and also loosen some more recent administrative reporting
