U.S. Olympian, Bay Area swim coach dies while surfing in Santa Cruz
-----
U.S. Olympian and Bay Area swim coach Richard Thornton died Thursday while surfing in Santa Cruz, according to his family."Sorry for having to share this information, but Richard Thornton passed away surfing today at The Hook in Santa Cruz," Marc Thornton, Richard's younger brother, posted on Facebook. "Doing what he loved. More to follow, but wanted to let everyone know."
Thornton, 65, was a member of the 1980 U.S. Olympic swim team and had been the head coach of San Ramon Valley
