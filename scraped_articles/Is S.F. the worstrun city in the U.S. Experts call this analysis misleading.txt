Is S.F. the worst-run city in the U.S.? Experts call this analysis ‘misleading’
-----
This week, the personal finance website WalletHub released an analysis examining which cities in the U.S. are best and worst run by their local governments. The verdict? Of the 148 cities they studied, San Francisco's governance was judged to be at the very bottom of the list.The city's last-place ranking was highlighted in stories from Fox News, the Daily Mail and numerous other news outlets.
But experts caution that the analysis is flawed, and that there's more to running a city
