CSU tuition hike goes to trustees despite student, faculty protests. Here's what it looks like
-----
Over protests from students and faculty, California State University trustees are poised to raise tuition Wednesday by 34% over five years, with an annual increase of 6% beginning next fall.The price hikes are the first since 2017 and would infuse CSU with $840 million through 2028-29, with a third used to buy back tuition entirely for about 60% of undergraduates.
