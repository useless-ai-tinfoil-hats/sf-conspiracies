Rock icon Patti Smith hospitalized after ‘sudden illness’ while on tour
-----
Rock icon Patti Smith was hospitalized during her tour in Italy due to a sudden illness, leading to the cancellation of her scheduled performance at the Teatro Duse in Bologna this week."With great regret, we inform the kind audience that Patti Smith's concert will not be able to go on stage due to a sudden illness that struck the artist," Duse representatives wrote in a statement, translated into English, released Tuesday, Dec. 12.
