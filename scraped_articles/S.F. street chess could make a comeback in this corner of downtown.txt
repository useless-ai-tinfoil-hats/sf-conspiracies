S.F. street chess could make a comeback in this corner of downtown
-----
Chess has saved downtown.Well, not all of it. But for an hour on Thursday afternoon the Mechanics Monument Plaza at Market and Battery streets felt like a scene out of an architectural drawing, with passersby lounging in sling back chairs as chess players hunched over boards beneath the looming bronze statue that gives the triangular plaza its name.
