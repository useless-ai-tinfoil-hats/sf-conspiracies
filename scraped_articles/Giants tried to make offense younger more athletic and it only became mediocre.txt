Giants tried to make offense younger, more athletic and it only became mediocre

After all the team's recent postseason misses, the San Francisco Giants' brass pointed to a lack of athleticism as the main focus of improvement for 2024. If the Giants wanted to compete for the postseason again, they'd have to match up better with their more dynamic competitors at the plate and on the bases.For a variety of reasons, the Giants' attempts to get younger and more athletic haven't been convincing or lucky.
