Classic S.F. pasta restaurant closing after 31 years
-----
A cozy Italian restaurant in San Francisco will soon close after 31 years.Bacco, at 3913 24th St. in Noe Valley, will close Jan. 31, putting an end to its run of serving fresh pasta, beet salads and grilled calamari.
Owner Sharri Dominici told the San Francisco Chronicle she is closing Bacco to open a new restaurant at the same location along with a new business partner. The new restaurant won't be Italian and is planned to open mid-March, but she declined to share more details.
