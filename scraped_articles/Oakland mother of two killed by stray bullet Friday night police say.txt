Oakland mother of two killed by stray bullet Friday night, police say
-----
A mother of two was struck and killed by a stray bullet while inside her Oakland home Friday night, according to police and news reports.The shooting happened just before 9:45 p.m. Friday in the 3600 block of 38th Avenue, Oakland police said. When officers arrived at the scene, they found one victim who had suffered at least one gunshot wound. The victim was transported to a local hospital, but did not survive, according to officials.
