Driver and 5 passengers injured in UC Santa Cruz bus crash
-----
Five passengers and the driver on a UC Santa Cruz bus were injured Tuesday night when the driver crashed near the main campus entrance, university officials said.UC Santa Cruz campus police received a call about the crash on Coolidge Drive just after 8:30 p.m., the university said in an email to the campus community on Wednesday.
