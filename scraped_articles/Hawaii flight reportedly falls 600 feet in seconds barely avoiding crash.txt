Hawaii flight reportedly falls 600 feet in seconds, barely avoiding crash
-----
Authorities are investigating what caused a flight from Hawaii to dive toward the ocean, coming within 400 feet of hitting the water.The Federal Aviation Administration began investigating the Southwest Airlines flight right after the drop occurred on April 11, a spokesperson for the agency told SFGATE. No injuries were reported, and the flight returned to its departure airport, Daniel K. Inouye International in Honolulu.
