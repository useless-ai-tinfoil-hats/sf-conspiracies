Outside Lands artist with SF ties has the Billboard Hot 100's top song. He played it twice.
-----
There's a lot of star power at 2024's Outside Lands festival in San Francisco, but only Shaboozey can boast the United States' current single most popular song. And boast he did.The genre-blending singer and "Cowboy Carter" feature artist delivered a 45-minute set to a vast crowd on Friday afternoon, often teasing — and then, gloriously, capitalizing on — his country megahit, "A Bar Song (Tipsy)." The earworm has spent four weeks at the top of Billboard's Hot 100, and was played twice
