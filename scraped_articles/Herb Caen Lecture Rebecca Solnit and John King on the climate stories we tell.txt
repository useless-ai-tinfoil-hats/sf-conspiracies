Herb Caen Lecture: Rebecca Solnit and John King on the climate stories we tell
-----
Acclaimed author and activist Rebecca Solnit, a Berkeley Journalism alum, is in conversation with award-winning San Francisco Chronicle Urban Design Critic John King about her 2023 co-edited anthology "Not Too Late: Changing the Climate Story from Despair to Possibility" and other insights on local and global climate stories.Join us for the first re-launched Herb Caen Lecture series, sponsored by Berkeley Journalism and the San Francisco Chronicle.
