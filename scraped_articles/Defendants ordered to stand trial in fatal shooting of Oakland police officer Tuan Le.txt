Defendants ordered to stand trial in fatal shooting of Oakland police officer Tuan Le
-----
Three men accused in the fatal shooting of Oakland police officer Tuan Le were ordered Wednesday to stand trial on multiple charges, including murder.But Alameda County Superior Court Judge Delia Trevino dismissed a murder charge against a fourth defendant, who prosecutors said was the lookout during a burglary in December at a marijuana grow house in the moments before Le, 36, was shot.
