Are S.F. coworking spaces making a comeback? WeWork thinks so
-----
Prior to the pandemic in 2019, a total of 32 flexible coworking space providers were active in San Francisco, operating some 3.2 million square feet of office space across 108 locations.At the start of this year, the number of providers had dropped to 24, and the total amount of office space dedicated to coworking decreased by more than half, to just over 1.5 million square feet, according to data provided by real estate brokerage CBRE.
