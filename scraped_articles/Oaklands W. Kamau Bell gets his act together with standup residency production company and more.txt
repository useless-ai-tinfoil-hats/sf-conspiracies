Oakland’s W. Kamau Bell ‘gets his act together’ with stand-up residency, production company and more
-----
On any given Saturday night, rain or shine, you can find W. Kamau Bell at Berkeley Repertory Theatre's Bakery Studio. Often tucked away upstairs in the venue's greenroom sitting quietly alone, pen in hand, he starts by jotting down new jokes or revising the ones he's tried out before.Then it's showtime.
