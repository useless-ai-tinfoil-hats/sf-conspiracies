Kevin Leary, ex-Chronicle reporter who loved his ringside seat to history, dies at 83
-----
It wasn't so much riding next to piled corpses on a "death wagon" that got to Kevin Leary when he covered the civil war in Somalia in 1993. Or the constant gunfire, or the starving children on roadsides with twiglike limbs and flies in their eyes.It was one moment when a mother shuffled out to the death wagon, a ramshackle cart picking up cadavers, "carrying a little package wrapped in white cloth with little red booties sticking out the bottom," he recalled.
