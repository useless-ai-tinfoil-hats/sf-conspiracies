Review: ‘The Beekeeper’ is perfect for pure, stupid action-movie fun
-----
"The Beekeeper" is the purest stupid fun I've had in a movie theater since "F9: The Fast Saga" in 2021.It does everything that an action movie needs to do and does it in witty, unexpected ways. It has a situation guaranteed to get an audience worked up, a villain you just want to slap, and little touches of macabre humor.
