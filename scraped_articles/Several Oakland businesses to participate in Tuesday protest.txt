Several Oakland businesses to participate in Tuesday protest
-----
Several Oakland businesses planned to participate in a strike Sept. 26 to demand support from city officials amid rising crime."If you don't do anything, it's pretty much a do-or-die situation for our businesses," said Carl Chan, a public safety advocate in Oakland who said he was organizing the strike. "Public safety is impacting our small businesses, and customers are afraid to come out."
