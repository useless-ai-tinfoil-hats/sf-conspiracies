'Like a dead flamingo': Carmel residents disagree on whether Bavarian-themed hotel is historic
-----
What happens when a building slated for demolition features noteworthy works of art on it? Should it all come down? Or is it up to the powers that be to protect it even if consensus is that the structure is no longer viable … not to mention a bit of an eyesore?These are the questions facing Carmel-by-the-Sea as the 76-year-old Hofsas House, a four-story Bavarian-themed hotel — owned and operated by the same local family for three generations — now faces a date with the wrecking ball.
