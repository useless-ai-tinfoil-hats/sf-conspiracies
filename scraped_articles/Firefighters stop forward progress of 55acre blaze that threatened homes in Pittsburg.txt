Firefighters stop forward progress of 55-acre blaze that threatened homes in Pittsburg
-----
A fire spread from a building to 55 acres of grass and bushes on the west edge of Pittsburg in Contra Costa County on Sunday afternoon, threatening nearby structures as firefighters rushed to get a handle on the blaze, officials said.Firefighters halted the fire's progress around 6:30 p.m., officials with the Contra Costa County Fire Protection District announced on X.
Strong winds spread flames from the initial structure fire to nearby vegetation, said Lauren Ono, a spokesperson
