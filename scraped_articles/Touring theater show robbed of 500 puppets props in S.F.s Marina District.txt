Thieves stole a truck filled with hundreds of handmade puppets used in a nationally touring theater show of large-scale shadow puppetry early Monday, leaving the production company empty-handed after four San Francisco shows.The stolen U-Haul truck contained 500 puppets, props, costumes, projectors and other items used to stage puppet master Hamid Rahmanian's Persian epic "Song of the North," which wrapped up a two-day run Sunday at the Fort Mason Center for Arts & Culture.
