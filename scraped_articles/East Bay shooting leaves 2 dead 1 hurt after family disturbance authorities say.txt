East Bay shooting leaves 2 dead, 1 hurt after ‘family disturbance,’ authorities say
-----
A shooting erupted among family members this weekend in unincorporated San Leandro, leaving two men dead and a third injured, authorities said.Alameda County sheriff's deputies responded around 3:30 p.m. Saturday to a report of gunfire at an apartment complex on the 2000 block of Miramonte Avenue at Foothill Boulevard, officials said in a news release Sunday. The officers found a man in his 30s, identified in the release as the suspect, on the sidewalk. The man was unresponsive to aid
