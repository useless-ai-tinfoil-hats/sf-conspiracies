After steady heat, Bay Area temperatures are about to drop
-----
A ridge of high pressure centered off the coast of Southern California and responsible for some interesting storm behaviors has been slow to loosen its grip on Bay Area weather this week. That's been noticeable or, perhaps more appropriately, not noticeable in the recent weather.Weather conditions have varied little from day to day under the influence of the high pressure ridge, but that starts to change this weekend.
