Crash involving bus and six cars creates backup on Highway 17 near Los Gatos
-----
A bus crashed into six cars on Highway 17 Friday morning, sending four people to the hospital.The incident occurred at approximately 7:15 a.m. in the northbound direction of Highway 17, Ross Lee, a spokesperson for the California Highway Patrol, told SFGATE. The bus hit the back of the cars that were stopped in traffic after being "unable to stop in time," Lee said. The crash happened just south of the The Cats, a barbecue restaurant in Los Gatos, he said.
