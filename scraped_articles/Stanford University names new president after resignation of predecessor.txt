Stanford University names new president after resignation of predecessor
-----
Stanford University's Board of Trustees has unanimously appointed Jonathan Levin as the school's new president, set to take office on August 1.Levin, 51, is a Stanford University alumnus and economist. He has been the dean of Stanford's Graduate School of Business since 2016, the university said.
Stanford had been looking for a new president since Marc Tessier-Lavigne announced last year he would resign as its 11th president "for the good of the university."
