Thousands of Californians potentially exposed to measles by sick traveler
-----
A traveler with measles flew from Munich to Los Angeles and then to the Yosemite area, potentially exposing thousands of people along the way to the highly infectious disease, according to health officials.The Los Angeles County Department of Public Health confirmed the case on Monday, bringing the number of documented measles cases in California in 2024 to nine.
The infected passenger, described as a nonresident, arrived at Los Angeles International Airport around 3 p.m. on May 19
