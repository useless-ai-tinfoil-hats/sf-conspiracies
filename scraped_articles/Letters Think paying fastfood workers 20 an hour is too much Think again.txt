Letters: Think paying fast-food workers $20 an hour is too much? Think again
-----
Fast food workers will now make $20 an hour. That sounds amazing until you do the math and find out that someone working 40 hours a week will only gross $3,467 a month. That's if they can find a full-time position at a fast-food chain.Making $3,467 a month is not enough to survive in this economy. When will the state Legislature understand that it needs to tackle the rising costs of everything in California, not just throw a few more dollars onto the minimum wage?
