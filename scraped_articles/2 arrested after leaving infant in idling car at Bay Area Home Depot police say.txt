2 arrested after leaving infant in idling car at Bay Area Home Depot, police say
-----
Two men who allegedly left an infant in their idling car while buying a crowbar after a shoplifting attempt were arrested outside a Home Depot store in Hercules on Monday, officials said.The Hercules Police Department said in a Tuesday post on Facebook that officers rushed to the store in the East Bay suburb after receiving a call at 4:24 p.m. Monday about a "theft in progress."
