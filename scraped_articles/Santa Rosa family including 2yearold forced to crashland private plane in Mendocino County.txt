Santa Rosa family, including 2-year-old, forced to crash-land private plane in Mendocino County
-----
A Santa Rosa family suffered minor injuries after they were forced to crash-land their plane Friday in Mendocino County, authorities said.The family — a 38-year old man, a 38-year-old woman and a 2-year-old girl — took off in a privately-owned airplane from Shelter Cove Airport just after 1 p.m., and the engine lost power about five minutes later, according to Capt. Quincy Cromer, public information officer for the Mendocino County Sheriff's Office.
