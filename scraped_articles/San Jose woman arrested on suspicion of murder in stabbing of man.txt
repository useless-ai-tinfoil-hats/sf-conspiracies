San Jose woman arrested on suspicion of murder in stabbing of man
-----
A woman suspected in the fatal stabbing of a man last week in San Jose has been arrested, police said Friday.Sylvia Ortega, 28, of San Jose was arrested at a home in the South Bay city on Wednesday and booked into Santa Clara County Jail on suspicion of murder, authorities said.
