USF men, eyeing a big win on a bigger stage, host No. 23 Gonzaga at Chase
-----
Chris Gerlufsen's USF Dons have won 14 of their 15 games on the Hilltop this season. But when they face No. 23 Gonzaga on Thursday night with second place in the WCC at stake, the Dons won't benefit from what figured to have been a particularly boisterous crowd at the Sabrato Center.The Zags (22-6, 12-2) and Dons (22-7, 11-3) will meet at Chase Center. USF announced in late September that it was moving the Gonzaga matchup from the Hilltop to the Warriors' home.
