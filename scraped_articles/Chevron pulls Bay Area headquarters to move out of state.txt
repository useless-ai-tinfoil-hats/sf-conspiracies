Chevron pulls Bay Area headquarters to move out of state
-----
Oil and gas giant Chevron is finally moving its official headquarters from San Ramon, California, to Houston.Chevron announced the news in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission on Friday, writing that the move will be effective on the first day of 2025. In a news release, Chevron wrote that employees who support the company's California operations will remain in San Ramon but that all corporate work will move to Houston over the next five years.
