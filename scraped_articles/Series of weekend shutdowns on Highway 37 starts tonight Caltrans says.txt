Series of weekend shutdowns on Highway 37 starts tonight, Caltrans says
-----
The first in a series of weekend shutdowns on busy Highway 37 in the North Bay — two in each direction — kicks off Friday night, according to Caltrans.The four consecutive 55-hour closures between Vallejo and Sears Point, originally scheduled to start April 12, had been postponed due to rain.
