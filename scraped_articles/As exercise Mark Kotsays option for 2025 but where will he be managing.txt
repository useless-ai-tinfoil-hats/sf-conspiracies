A’s exercise Mark Kotsay’s option for 2025, but where will he be managing?
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — Two years ago, Oakland Athletics manager Mark Kotsay accepted the grueling position of running a bad team and answering for a tight-lipped ownership that's trying to relocate the A's to Las Vegas. On Tuesday, the A's rewarded him by exercising their option on his contract for 2025, giving him two guaranteed seasons.The announcement comes in the wake of Kotsay interviewing for the New York Mets' managerial job.
