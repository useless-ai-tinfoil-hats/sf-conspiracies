49ers sign 2019 All-Pro Eric Kendricks to ease uncertainty at linebacker
-----
The San Francisco 49ers signed former Chargers linebacker Eric Kendricks to a one-year contract Wednesday to help solidify a position group filled with uncertainty beyond its headliner, first-team All-Pro Fred Warner, ESPN reported.Kendricks, 32, who has eight straight 100-tackle seasons and was a first-team All-Pro in 2019, initially could serve as a fill-in for Dre Greenlaw, whose season-opening availability is uncertain after he suffered a torn Achilles in Super Bowl LVIII last month.
