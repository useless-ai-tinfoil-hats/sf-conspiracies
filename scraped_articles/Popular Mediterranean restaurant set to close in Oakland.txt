Popular Mediterranean restaurant set to close in Oakland
-----
A popular Oakland stop for Mediterranean dishes and cocktails is closing in the coming weeks.Shakewell, located at 3407 Lakeshore Ave. in the Grand Lake neighborhood, announced on social media its final day in business will be Aug. 31. Manager Nicole Orong confirmed the closing to the Chronicle, explaining that rising costs and declining business forced the decision, spelling the end for the 10-year-old restaurant.
