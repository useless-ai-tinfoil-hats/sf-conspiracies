'Once-in-a-lifetime' Death Valley experience emerges after California storms
-----
It's kayaking season in Death Valley.No, really. Park officials declared it so on Friday after "a temporary lake" at Badwater Basin exploded in size due to the recent deluge of rain in Southern California. The ephemeral lake — fondly known as Lake Manly — is six miles long, three miles wide, one foot deep and sits in the shadow of the snow-capped Panamint Range.
