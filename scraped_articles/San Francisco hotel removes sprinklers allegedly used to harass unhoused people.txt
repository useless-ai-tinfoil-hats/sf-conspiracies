San Francisco hotel removes sprinklers allegedly used to harass unhoused people
-----
The Best Western Red Coach Inn in the Tenderloin removed a sprinkler system attached to the building's exterior on Thursday, the same day the San Francisco Examiner published a report alleging the hotel had been using them to deter homeless people from camping on a sidewalk behind the hotel, SFGATE has found.On Friday, a reporter visited the alley and found no visible sprinklers.
