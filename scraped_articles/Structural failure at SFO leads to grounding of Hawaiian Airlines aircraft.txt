‘Structural failure’ at SFO leads to grounding of Hawaiian Airlines aircraft
-----
A Hawaiian Airlines aircraft remained grounded in San Francisco on Monday following a passenger boarding bridge collapse last week.The incident, which caused damage to the plane, occurred at Gate A1 at approximately 7 p.m. on Thursday, according to Doug Yakel, spokesperson for San Francisco International Airport.
