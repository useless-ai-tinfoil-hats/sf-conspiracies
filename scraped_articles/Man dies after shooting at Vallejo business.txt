Man dies after shooting at Vallejo business
-----
A man who was shot last week inside a business in Vallejo has died, police said Sunday.The man was shot around 9:45 a.m. Thursday inside an undisclosed business on Solano Avenue, just west of Interstate 80.
Paramedics took the man to a hospital, where he died Saturday, police said. His name withheld, pending family notifications.
The circumstances surrounding the shooting — the 15th homicide in the city this year — were under investigation, police said.
