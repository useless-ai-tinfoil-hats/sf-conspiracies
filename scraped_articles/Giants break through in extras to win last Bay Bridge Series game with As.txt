Giants break through in extras to win last Bay Bridge Series game with A’s
-----
OAKLAND — The baseball gods couldn't let the Battle of the Bay end so soon for the San Francisco Giants and Oakland Athletics, or their fans. They wouldn't let a historic rivalry end conventionally, either.So the two rival teams, for decades just separated by the San Francisco Bay and a 20-minute drive over the Bay Bridge, played an extra inning jam-packed with action in the very last game ever to be played in this Bay Bridge Series.
