Larry Allen, Pro Football Hall of Famer and former 49ers guard, dies at 52
-----
Larry Allen, the Hall of Fame guard who played at Sonoma State and finished his 14-season NFL career with the San Francisco 49ers, died unexpectedly Sunday while on vacation with his family in Mexico. Allen was 52.Allen was a 10-time Pro Bowl selection as a guard — only three guards in NFL history had more — and added another Pro Bowl honor as a left tackle in 1998 when the Cowboys, his team for his first 12 seasons, needed him to change positions. He also was named a first-team All-Pro
