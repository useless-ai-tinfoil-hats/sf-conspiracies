Lana Del Rey praises Joan Baez as ‘toughest woman I have ever met’
-----
Lana Del Rey says Joan Baez is "the toughest woman I have ever met."Speaking at a screening of Baez's deeply personal biographical documentary, "I Am a Noise," at the Nuart Theatre in Los Angeles on Friday, Oct. 13, Del Rey praised the folk singer and activist's enduring spirit and unflinching honesty in the film, which chronicles her six-decade career amid bouts of anxiety and stage fright.
