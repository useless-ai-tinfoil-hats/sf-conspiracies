East Bay Kohl’s ransacked after ram-raid burglary
-----
Three thieves crashed a van through the entrance of a Pleasant Hill Kohl's on Thanksgiving evening, ransacking store shelves before they fled in a getaway car, police said.The incident at 7:45 p.m. Thursday marked the latest in a spate of ram-raid burglaries — a brash method of retail theft in which perpetrators slam a vehicle into a storefront and then quickly plunder merchandise.
