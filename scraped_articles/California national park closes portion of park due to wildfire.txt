California national park closes portion of park due to wildfire
-----
Prompted by a wildfire that has been burning since early August, Sequoia National Park said Thursday that it's closing public access to the Mineral King area, including campgrounds, trailheads and hiking trails.The Coffee Pot Fire was ignited by lightning on Aug. 3 in the southwest corner of the park, southeast of the town of Three Rivers in Tulare County, park officials said.
