The controversial and iconic Yosemite experience returning this year
-----
Nearly six years since Yosemite National Park's iconic High Sierra Camps last hosted guests, the glamping opportunity of a lifetime is slated to reopen, and the lottery — which determines who will secure the few coveted spots in the summer of 2024 — is underway."We are so excited," Chelsie Layman, director of sales and marketing for Yosemite Hospitality, a subsidiary of Aramark and the concessionaire for the park, told SFGATE. "We're all pushing really hard to make sure that it happens."
