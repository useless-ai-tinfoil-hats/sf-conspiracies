70 firefighters battle massive fire in West Oakland warehouse
-----
About 70 firefighters were battling a huge fire that broke out at a construction materials warehouse on Wood Street in Oakland on Wednesday night, sending enormous plumes of black smoke over the city.The fire was reported at 10:15 p.m. at the Pacific Supply warehouse at Wood and 24th streets, according to Michael Hunt, a spokesperson for the Oakland Fire Department. The building is near a large homeless encampment alongside Interstate 880.
