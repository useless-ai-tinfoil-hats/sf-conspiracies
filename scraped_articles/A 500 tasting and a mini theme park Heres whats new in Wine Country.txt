A $500 tasting and a mini theme park: Here’s what’s new in Wine Country
-----
A summer visit to Wine Country promises many new places to sip wine and dine.After two years of anticipation, the Moroccan street food stall from acclaimed San Francisco chef Mourad Lahlou is finally open at Napa's Oxbow Marketplace. Signorello, a Napa winery that burned to the ground in the 2017 wildfires, just unveiled its new winery and visitor center. Visitors to St. Helena will find two new and drastically different food options: a breakfast taco spot and a tasting menu restaurant
