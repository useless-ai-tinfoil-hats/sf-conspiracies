Former Bay Area sports star Drew Gordon dies in autocycle crash
-----
Former NBA forward Drew Gordon, who grew up in the Bay Area and was a high school sports star at Archbishop Mitty, died in a vehicle crash in Oregon. He was 33.Clackamas County sheriff's deputies responded to the scene of a crash in an unincorporated part of the county around 1 p.m. Thursday.
