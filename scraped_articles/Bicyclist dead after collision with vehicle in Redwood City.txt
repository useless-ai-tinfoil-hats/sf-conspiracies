Bicyclist dead after collision with vehicle in Redwood City
-----
A bicyclist died last week after a collision with a vehicle in Redwood City, according to the Redwood City Police Department.Police responded to a traffic accident involving a vehicle and a bicyclist at Arch Street and Broadway Street at approximately 6:34p.m. Friday. The bicyclist was unconscious and was immediately transported to Stanford Hospital but later succumbed to injuries sustained from the collision, according to police.
