Celebrate 4/20 with events and activities for cannabis-loving crowds
-----
The long days of spring seem a bit greener on 4/20 as a very California — and very San Francisco Bay Area — holiday celebrates local cannabis culture with its emerald buds and flowers. The large, annual outdoor festival at Golden Gate Park's Hippie Hill, usually ground zero for the occasion, will not be happening this year, but organizers hope to welcome the festival back into the park in 2025.
