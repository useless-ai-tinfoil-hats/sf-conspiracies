Swimmers transform S.F. Bay into sea of charity, raise $386,000 for cancer research
-----
The shore at Little Marina Green was packed with swimmers of all ages Saturday morning, jumping slightly to keep warm as they waited for the signal to leap into the frigid San Francisco Bay.Amid the crowds of cheering spectators, a person with a megaphone shouted "3, 2, 1, go!" and the swimmers — some in full wet suits, some in just Speedos, and all wearing temporary tattoos — jumped into the bay in waves, resembling a pack of dolphins as they swam out.
