Glamorous ‘pizza lounge’ opens in Berkeley
-----
After two years and over a million dollars, a long-promised "swanky pizza lounge" is set to open in Berkeley's Westbrae neighborhood. The restaurant will serve 10 crispy and chewy pies, as well as local beer, imported wines and a selection of spirits.When Brandon Wilson picked up the keys to the former Lalime's space at 1329 Gilman St., his plan was to open Three One Four in the fall of 2022.
