Will S.F. grocery stores need to notify city before closing? Safeway saga drives plan for input
-----
San Francisco Supervisor Dean Preston is proposing that grocers be required to notify and involve the community when they plan to close stores in the city. The proposed legislation comes in the wake of a battle with Safeway over the fate of its Fillmore district location.On Monday, Safeway said it will keep its Fillmore supermarket open longer than first announced after pushback from the community.
