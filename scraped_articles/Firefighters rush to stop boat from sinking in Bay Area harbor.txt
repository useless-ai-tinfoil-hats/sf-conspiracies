Firefighters rush to stop boat from sinking in Bay Area harbor
-----
Authorities rushed to the Petaluma marina Sunday afternoon to stop a partially sunken boat from submerging underwater.At 1:12 p.m., Petaluma firefighters equipped with an oil spill response trailer and inflatable rescue boat arrived at the scene. They immediately assessed the vessel for signs of fuel or oil leaking and found none, according to a press release from the fire department.
