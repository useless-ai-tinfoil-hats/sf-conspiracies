What to know about the partial solar eclipse in California next week
-----
On Monday, the moon will pass between the Earth and the sun, and a total solar eclipse will trace a narrow path across the United States. California doesn't fall within the area where day will turn to complete darkness, but the Golden State will still be treated to a partial solar eclipse.The moon will appear to block about 45% of the sun's diameter in San Francisco, 58% in Los Angeles and 41% in Redding.
