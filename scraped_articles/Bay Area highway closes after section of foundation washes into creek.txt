Bay Area highway closes after section of foundation washes into creek
-----
Niles Canyon Road, also known as Highway 84, west of Sunol closed Monday night after a section of the road washed into Alameda Creek amid stormy weather.The road is shut down indefinitely to traffic between Old Canyon Road and Main Street in Sunol, Caltrans said.
