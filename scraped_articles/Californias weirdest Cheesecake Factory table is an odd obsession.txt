California's weirdest Cheesecake Factory table is an odd obsession
-----
If a potential suitor wanted to wine and dine yours truly — be it on Valentine's Day or otherwise — there would be no need for a fancy tasting menu or a Nebbiolo-soaked Italian dinner.No, if said suitor wanted to really impress me, a professional food writer for more than 20 years, I would implore them to book the most romantic table in all of Los Angeles: the glassed-in Cheesecake Factory nook that overlooks the lobby of a movie theater at The Grove.
