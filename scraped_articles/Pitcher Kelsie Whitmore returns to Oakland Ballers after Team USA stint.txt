Pitcher Kelsie Whitmore returns to Oakland Ballers after Team USA stint
-----
Kelsie Whitmore shrugged off the advice of one of her representatives when the Oakland Ballers went through a difficult patch recently and returned to the team after playing for the U.S. Women's national baseball team at the World Cup in Thunder Bay, Ontario."I'm so excited to be back in Oakland playing for the Ballers," Whitmore said in a statement provided by the Ballers.
