How did Warriors’ Brandin Podziemski turn himself into a ‘Rising Star’?
-----
When Brandin Podziemski was drafted by the Golden State Warriors in June, he began to wonder why their rookies rarely played: How could he crack their rotation?As he so often does, he started to study.
"For me, it was … learning how to play with" Stephen Curry, Podziemski said. "All those guys from the championship team know how to get Steph and Klay (Thompson) open. … I knew my basketball IQ would be good enough to fulfill a role here. To what limit? I didn't know."
