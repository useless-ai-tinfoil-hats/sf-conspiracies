Every day the Giants do nothing increases the pressure to do something
-----
Major League Baseball's Winter Meetings whimpered to a close midweek with few free agency signings and little action, including by the San Francisco Giants. The Great Shohei Ohtani Dam remains in place, and until that barrier is removed and the river of money starts flowing, free agency is effectively stalled.But just because it's quiet for everyone doesn't mean the Giants can breathe easy or feel fine about being part of the status quo.
