The Giants are raving about Jung Hoo Lee. Here’s what they see in their new leadoff man
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — Mike Yastrzemski isn't just convinced Jung Hoo Lee will fare well in the major leagues. Yastrzemski's one of a number of teammates who thinks the San Francisco Giants' new leadoff man will be one of the savviest hitters in the game.They expect he will swing at very few bad pitches.
"We're trying to figure out how many at-bats he'll give away," Yastrzemski said. "We set the line at seven, and I'd take the under. I think he'll give away fewer than that."
