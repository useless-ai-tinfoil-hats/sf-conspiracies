This spirit was mislabeled for years — by law. That’s finally changing
-----
In a perfect example of how nonsensical American alcohol laws can be, Japanese distillers have been required to mislabel some of their spirits if they want to sell them in certain California restaurants.For the past 25 years, many bottles of shochu — a Japanese liquor — had to be incorrectly labeled as soju, a Korean liquor, in order to be sold in restaurants with a beer and wine license.
