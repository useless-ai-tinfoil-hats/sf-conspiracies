Bicyclist killed by driver in San Rafael near railroad crossing
-----
A bicyclist was struck and killed by a vehicle near a railroad crossing in San Rafael last weekend, authorities said Thursday.The bicyclist was heading north on Andersen Drive just before 7:45 a.m. Saturday when he drove over a Sonoma-Marin Area Rail Transit railroad crossing near Francisco Boulevard West and fell "into the path of a northbound traveling pickup truck that was towing a trailer," the San Rafael Police Department said in a statement.
