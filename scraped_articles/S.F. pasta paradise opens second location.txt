S.F. pasta paradise opens second location
-----
A temple for fresh pasta is opening in San Francisco's Mission District.The second location of Pasta Supply Co., joining the first in the Inner Richmond, arrives at 3233 22nd St. this weekend. It will open for retail only, stocked with more than 30 kinds of fresh pasta, sauces and accoutrements, then dine-in next weekend.
The new outpost has a much bigger kitchen than the original Pasta Supply Co., which owner Anthony Strong opened last year. This means more and some new hot pasta
