In-N-Out rival falters, closing multiple locations across California
-----
A nationwide burger chain with New York City ties is set to close nine restaurants across the country by Sept. 25, and more than half of them will be in California.Shake Shack, the Big Apple-based competitor to In-N-Out known for smash burgers, crinkle-cut fries and milkshakes, announced that six California storefronts, including the only Oakland location, would close by the end of next month.
