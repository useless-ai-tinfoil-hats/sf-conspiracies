Warriors’ Draymond Green ejected for wild arm swing against Suns
-----
PHOENIX – Stop us if you've heard this before, but Golden State Warriors forward Draymond Green was ejected from Tuesday night's game against Phoenix.Green got tossed with 8:23 left in the third quarter and the Warriors leading 65-60. On an inbounds play, Green became tangled with Suns center Jusuf Nurkic and wildly swung his right arm, striking Nurkic in the face.
