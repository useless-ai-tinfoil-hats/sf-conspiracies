‘I will be reborn’: S.F. Ballet superstar Yuan Yuan Tan looks beyond her final company bow
-----
Contrary to stereotypes of Chinese women and internationally famous ballerinas, Yuan Yuan Tan is neither submissive nor a diva.This is quickly evident when she shows up for dinner at a Hayes Valley sushi restaurant after a long day of rehearsal wearing a rust-colored designer jacket with flower-shaped diamond earrings, her long black hair in a ponytail. The longest-dancing San Francisco Ballet principal ever — the Tom Brady of the art form, her husband jokes — is courteously assertive
