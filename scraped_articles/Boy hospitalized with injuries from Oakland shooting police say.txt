Boy hospitalized with injuries from Oakland shooting, police say
-----
A juvenile was wounded in a shooting Wednesday night near Lake Merritt in Oakland, police said.The boy was shot on a cul-de-sac near the lake in the 1400 block of Lakeshore Avenue just before 11:15 p.m., according to the Oakland Police Department. He was listed in stable condition as of Thursday afternoon.
Officers said they responded Wednesday night to a report of a shooting at the cul-de-sac and found a residential window had been struck by the gunfire.
