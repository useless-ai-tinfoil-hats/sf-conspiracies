Deadly shooting at East Bay auto center began as domestic violence incident
-----
Two people involved in a shooting on Thursday morning at the Toyota of Berkeley Service Center in Albany have died, according to law enforcement.The incident occurred shortly after 11 a.m., when an armed man allegedly entered the business at 1025 Eastshore Highway and shot a woman who worked there, according to witnesses who called 911. Investigators from the Alameda County Sheriff's Office later characterized the violent confrontation as an act of domestic violence.
