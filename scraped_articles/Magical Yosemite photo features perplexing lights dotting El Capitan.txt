Magical Yosemite photo features perplexing lights dotting El Capitan
-----
Yosemite National Park shared a photo in social media on June 4 showing El Capitan just after sunset with bright lights dotting the face of the mountain. It's a majestic if somewhat mystifying sight: Why are there bright lights shining in the wilderness?Turns out they're the headlamps of rock climbers on the mountain.
