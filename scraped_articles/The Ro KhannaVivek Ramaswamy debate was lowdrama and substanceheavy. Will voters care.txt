The Ro Khanna-Vivek Ramaswamy debate was low-drama and substance-heavy. Will voters care?
-----
The Wednesday debate between Santa Clara Rep. Ro Khanna and Republican presidential candidate Vivek Ramaswamy was an opportunity for two politically ambitious men to help their campaigns, both current and future.For Khanna, it was a chance to test out his message for an expected 2028 presidential run, and for Ramaswamy, it was a chance to help his struggling polling numbers in the 2024 primary (he now has 6.3% support, a distant fourth to former President Donald Trump's 57.3%).
