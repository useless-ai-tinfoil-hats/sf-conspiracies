49ers Christian McCaffrey and model Olivia Culpo tie the knot in Rhode Island
-----
San Francisco 49ers running back Christian McCaffrey tied the knot with fiance Olivia Culpo Saturday in Rhode Island, the bride's home state.McCaffrey, 28, the NFL's offensive player of the year last season, and model Culpo, 32, were married at a church in Watch Hill, an affluent seaside enclave in the town of Westerly. Getting married in a chapel was "our number one priority," Culpo told Vogue, which reported on the wedding.
