How is Sen. Dianne Feinstein's replacement decided?

California Democratic Sen. Dianne Feinstein has died at age 90, her office confirmed Friday. The oldest member of Congress, Feinstein grappled with health issues, including a serious case of shingles, in recent years. Her cause of death has not yet been reported.California Gov. Gavin Newsom will select Feinstein's replacement.
