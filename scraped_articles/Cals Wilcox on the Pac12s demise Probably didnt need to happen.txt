Cal’s Wilcox on the Pac-12’s demise: ‘Probably didn’t need to happen’
-----
Whenever Cal football head coach Justin Wilcox would get a question last season about the imminent demise of the Pac-12 and Cal's impending move to the ACC, he almost invariably would respond with something like this: "That's a question we should discuss in a meeting after the season."Sure enough, Wilcox recently did talk with eight media members for about an hour in a relaxed setting in a Memorial Stadium meeting room. The Pac-12/ACC issues were discussed, as were several other topics.
