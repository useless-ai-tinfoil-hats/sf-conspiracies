The 49ers are investigating a fan tailgate after a hanging skeleton was seen in online videos
-----
The 49ers are investigating why a prominent fan tailgate group hung a skeleton wearing a Seahawks jersey by its neck before Sunday's game.On Sunday, the tailgate group 408 Faithfuls set up its usual "Faithful Boulevard" event in the Green Lot at Levi's Stadium. The group also hosted Travis King, a Bay Area musician who makes 49ers songs, for a music video shoot for King's 49ers version of the song "Ball'n" by Mustard and Roddy Ricch.
