Giants’ Joc Pederson fires back at notion his weight is affecting his play
-----
DENVER — Joc Pederson doesn't dispute the idea that he has put on some pounds this season. He long has had a tendency to put on weight over the course of the season, and it has been a point of discussion numerous times in his career.Pederson is the San Francisco Giants' highest-paid player, though, which brings extra scrutiny; his power numbers are down, and he's a free agent after the season. Some extra pounds might not help his market, especially now that he's in his 30s.
