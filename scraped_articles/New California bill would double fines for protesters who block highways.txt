New California bill would double fines for protesters who block highways
-----
A new law proposed by a Southern California Assembly member would double the fines protesters who block highway traffic face.Assembly Member Kate Sanchez, a Republican who represents parts of Orange and Riverside counties, introduced the legislation after recent protests calling for a cease-fire in Gaza snarled traffic on major highways in both the Bay Area and in Southern California.
