San Francisco officials call residence unsafe after inspecting $700-a-month bed 'pods'
-----
The building with $700-a-month tiny bed "pods" in downtown San Francisco is missing a few permits and a necessary safety feature, city inspectors say.Set up this June in SoMa's Mint Plaza, the 28-bed residence run by Brownstone Shared Housing hosts an assortment of frugal renters, including artificial intelligence startup founders and other tech workers, media reports say.
