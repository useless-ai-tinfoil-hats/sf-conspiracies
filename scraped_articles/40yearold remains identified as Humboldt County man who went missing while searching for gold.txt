40-year-old remains identified as Humboldt County man who went missing while searching for gold
-----
For more than 40 years, Arizona officials have been trying to identify human remains found in a desert in 1982. Now new technology has given them an answer: a Humboldt County man who went missing while searching for gold.In September 1982, human remains were found in a rural desert near Kingman, Ariz., just off Route 66, according to the Mohave County Sheriff's Office.
