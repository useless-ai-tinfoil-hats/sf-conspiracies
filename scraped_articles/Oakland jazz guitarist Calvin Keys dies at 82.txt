Oakland jazz guitarist Calvin Keys dies at 82
-----
Oakland jazz guitarist Calvin Keys, an essential force on the Bay Area jazz scene for five decades, died Sunday at Alta Bates Summit Medical Center in Berkeley surrounded by friends and family. He was 82.Keys suffered a major stroke on April 13 and was actively preparing for a string of high-profile gigs, including his debut as a co-leader at the Newport Jazz Festival this summer with Los Angeles multi-instrumentalist and producer Terrace Martin.
