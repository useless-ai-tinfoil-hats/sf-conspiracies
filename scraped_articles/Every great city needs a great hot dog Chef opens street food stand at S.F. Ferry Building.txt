‘Every great city needs a great hot dog’: Chef opens street food stand at S.F. Ferry Building
-----
The steady stream of new culinary tenants at the San Francisco Ferry Building — which now includes the likes of Bernal Cutlery and famed restaurant Nopa — continues with a new vendor preparing to offer warm hot dogs and snacks next week.Palmvy Hot Dogs will open on Sept. 4 with a menu of dogs and sausages inspired by Reuben sandwiches, caprese salads and the pepper-and-onion-topped dogs of California street vendors.
