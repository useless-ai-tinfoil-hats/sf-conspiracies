Review: In ‘Apples Never Fall,’ a tennis family’s strengths and weaknesses are thoughtfully investigated
-----

That gives this Peacock limited series a winning edge.An intriguing mystery is also in the mix that — like "Big Little Lies," another series based on a Liane Moriarty novel — makes for constantly compelling viewing.
