S.F. police arrest 135 people and seize 10 pounds of drugs in first 2 months of 2024
-----
San Francisco police officers arrested 135 people and seized more than 10 pounds of narcotics in the first two months of the year amid ongoing efforts to clamp down on drug dealing and other crime in the city.Across San Francisco, plainclothes officers also seized 11 firearms and recovered eight vehicles in January and February.
