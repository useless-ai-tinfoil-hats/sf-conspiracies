Oakland man arrested in armed robbery of Peninsula 7-Eleven
-----
An Oakland man was arrested after robbing a Millbrae 7-Eleven using a handgun, according to the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office.The robbery occurred just before 2:15 p.m. Friday in a 7-Eleven store at 401 El Camino Real, authorities said. Deputies arrived and spoke to the victim, who described the man who robbed the store, according to the sheriff's office.
