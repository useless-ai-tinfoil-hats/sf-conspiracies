SF swimmer attacked and carjacked near Fisherman's Wharf
-----
A San Francisco swimmer was beaten and carjacked early Friday morning near the Aquatic Park Cove, police said. Officers from the San Francisco Police Department responded to a robbery just before 6 a.m. in the area of Beach and Hyde streets, a few blocks from Fisherman's Wharf, police spokesperson Paulina Henderson told SFGATE.When officers arrived on the scene, they found a man with non-life threatening injuries, Henderson said.
