Man calling for help rescued from water under collapsed S.F. Bay pier, officials say
-----
A man calling for help from the water under a collapsed pier along the shore in San Francisco's Bayview district was rescued early Friday, officials said.Authorities received a call at 6:07 a.m. from passersby who reported hearing a man's cries from the water off Amador Street, according to San Francisco Fire Department spokesperson Lt. Jonathan Baxter.
Over 25 members of marine and ground firefighter rescue teams were dispatched and arrived at the scene by 6:12 a.m., Baxter said.
