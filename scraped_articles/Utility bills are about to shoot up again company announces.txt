Utility bills are about to shoot up again, company announces
-----
PG&E is ringing in the new year with a spike in energy bill costs, the company announced.Starting Monday, most households' gas and electric bills will increase about 13%, or $34.50, according to a PG&E press release Friday. The increase comes as the company's General Rate Case from November goes into effect in the new year.
