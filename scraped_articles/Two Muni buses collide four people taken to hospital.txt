Two Muni buses collide, four people taken to hospital
-----
Four people were taken to the hospital after two Muni buses collided at the intersection of Third Street and Palou Avenue on Wednesday afternoon, according to the San Francisco Fire Department.Both bus operators were transported to a local hospital with minor injuries as a result of the collision, which occurred around 3:55 p.m., a fire department spokesperson said.
