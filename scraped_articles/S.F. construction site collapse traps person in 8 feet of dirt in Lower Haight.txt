S.F. construction site collapse traps person in 8 feet of dirt in Lower Haight
-----
San Francisco Fire Department rescuers were working to rescue a person who was trapped in an estimated 8 feet of dirt, officials said Thursday.The incident was reported around 10 a.m. at Divisadero and Oak streets in San Francisco, officials said.
People were advised to avoid the area.
A video posted by Fire Department officials on social media showed personnel digging out the trench.
