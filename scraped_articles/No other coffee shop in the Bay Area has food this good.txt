No other coffee shop in the Bay Area has food this good
-----
The Bay Area has no shortage of superb coffee shops, but finding one with great food is virtually unheard of. However, Ain't Normal Cafe in Oakland's Rockridge neighborhood is single-handedly changing that. In addition to brewing one of the Bay Area's finest cups, the shop has blossomed into a low-key restaurant.Owner Faunus Lucas opened Ain't Normal in 2020.
