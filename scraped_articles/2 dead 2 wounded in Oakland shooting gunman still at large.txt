2 dead, 2 wounded in Oakland shooting; gunman still at large
-----
Two people were killed and two others were wounded after gunfire erupted in Oakland on Saturday morning, according to multiple reports.Police found a man and woman dead outside an apartment complex on the 1600 block of 83rd Avenue, according to the Mercury News, which also reported that two other women were found wounded and taken to hospitals. KRON-TV said their condition was listed as critical according to the Oakland Police Department.
