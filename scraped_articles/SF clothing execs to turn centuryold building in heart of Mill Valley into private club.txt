SF clothing execs to turn century-old building in heart of Mill Valley into private club
-----
Executives at a local outdoor apparel company plan to turn a 112-year-old building in the heart of Mill Valley into a private club, with the cost of a general membership ranging between $150 and $300 per month.On Tuesday, Mill Valley's planning commission approved a proposal for the project submitted by Treehouse MV, a group founded by Mike Natenshon and Adam Lynch.
