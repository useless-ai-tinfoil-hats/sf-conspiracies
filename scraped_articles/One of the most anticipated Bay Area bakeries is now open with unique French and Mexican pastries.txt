One of the most anticipated Bay Area bakeries is now open with unique French and Mexican pastries
-----
One of the most anticipated bakeries in the Bay Area drew long lines when it opened Wednesday with buttery, layered croissants and rich, sugary buns inspired by French and Mexican traditions.At Forma Bakery, at 4920 Telegraph Ave. in Oakland's Temescal neighborhood, owner Jackson Schnetz is producing a variety of pastries with enriched doughs along with other baked treats like Mexican conchas.
