S.F. firefighters extinguish blaze at bank in Castro district
-----
San Francisco firefighters extinguished a blaze at a U.S. Bank branch in the Castro district Saturday afternoon.Flames were coming out of the side of the building when crews arrived, according to Jonathan Baxter, spokesperson for the San Francisco Fire Department.
