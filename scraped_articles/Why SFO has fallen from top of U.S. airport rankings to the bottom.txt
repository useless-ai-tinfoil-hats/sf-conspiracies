Why SFO has fallen from top of U.S. airport rankings to the bottom
-----
In 2022, San Francisco International Airport was named the top large U.S. airport by the Wall Street Journal, in part due to its best-in-the-nation percentage of on-time departures and arrivals.Now, two years later, the airport is at the bottom of on-time rankings nationally, according to federal data as of February 2024.
