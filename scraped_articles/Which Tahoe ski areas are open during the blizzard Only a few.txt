Which Tahoe ski areas are open during the blizzard? Only a few

UPDATE: Tahoe storm conditions expected to get worse as blizzard warnings remain in effectLake Tahoe's 10 major ski areas struggled on Friday to stay open during the blizzard that is dumping snow and blasting wind across the region.
As of Friday morning, six ski areas had announced they were closed for the day, while others had shut down several of their chairlifts and hustled to keep operations running.
