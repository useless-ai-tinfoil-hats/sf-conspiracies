40-foot-long whale washes up dead on Bay Area beach
-----
A dead whale washed ashore in Alameda early Sunday morning, marking the first cetacean mortality to be reported in the Bay Area so far this year.The animal was an adult female, estimated to be close to 40 feet long, and was on her northbound migration to Alaska for summer feeding, responders from the California Academy of Sciences confirmed with SFGATE.
