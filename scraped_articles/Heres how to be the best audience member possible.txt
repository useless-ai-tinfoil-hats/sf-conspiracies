Here's how to be the best audience member possible
-----
The words "manners" and "etiquette" seem to have taken on an unfortunate connotation of snobbery for some in recent years.This is distressing. Codes of behavior in shared spaces — saying "please" and "thank you," being quiet when requested and abiding by other social norms — are not meant to punish or persecute people. They exist to make the experience of interacting with other human beings more pleasant for everyone.
