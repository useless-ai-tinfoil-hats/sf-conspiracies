47-year-old seafood empire closes casual S.F. restaurant
-----
A stop for clam chowder and East Coast-style lobster rolls in the Fillmore district is now closed.Scott's Chowder House, at 1235 Fillmore, closed on Saturday, Sept. 23, according to a message from the business on social media. The restaurant opened in June of last year and was a casual spinoff location of the 47-year-old seafood empire Scott's Seafood.
The San Francisco Standard first reported the closure.
