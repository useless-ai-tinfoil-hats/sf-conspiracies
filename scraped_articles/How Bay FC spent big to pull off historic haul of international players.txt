How Bay FC spent big to pull off historic haul of international players
-----
Lucy Rushton has been clear that she intends to build Bay FC with a heavy international influence. Over the past few weeks, the general manager's plan has materialized in a dramatic way.On paper, Bay FC now feels like a legitimate NWSL club. Since mid-January, they have added Princess Marfo (Ghana), Deyna Castellanos (Venezuela), Asisat Oshoala (Nigeria), Jen Beattie (Scotland) and Racheal Kundananji (Zambia).
