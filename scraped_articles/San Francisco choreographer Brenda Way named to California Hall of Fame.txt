San Francisco choreographer Brenda Way named to California Hall of Fame
-----
Brenda Way, the visionary choreographer and founding artistic director of ODC, has been inducted into the California Hall of Fame along with nine other luminaries of Golden State arts, politics and culture.The honor, which was announced Thursday, Jan. 11, by Gov. Gavin Newsom and first partner Jennifer Siebel Newsom, comes as a recognition of Way's contributions to the world of dance in San Francisco and beyond.
