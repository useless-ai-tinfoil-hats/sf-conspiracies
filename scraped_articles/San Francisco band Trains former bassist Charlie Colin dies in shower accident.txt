San Francisco band Train's former bassist Charlie Colin dies in shower accident
-----
Charlie Colin, the original bassist for San Francisco band Train, has reportedly died at the age of 58.Colin died after slipping and falling in the shower while house-sitting in Brussels, Belgium, according to TMZ, which spoke with his mother.
Formed in San Francisco in the 1990s, Train became one of the most successful soft-rock bands of the past 30 years, with multiple Grammy wins and platinum records.
