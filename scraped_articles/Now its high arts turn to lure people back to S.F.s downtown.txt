Now it’s high arts’ turn to lure people back to S.F.’s downtown
-----
Night markets with bhangra. After-work jazz ensembles. Block parties with drag stages.These are just a few of the arts offerings the city of San Francisco has deployed, many through direct financial sponsorship, to entice residents and workers back downtown since the pandemic popularized working from home.
