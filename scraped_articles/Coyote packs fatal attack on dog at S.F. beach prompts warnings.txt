Coyote pack’s fatal attack on dog at S.F. beach prompts warnings
-----
Following a deadly coyote attack this week on a dog at San Francisco's Baker Beach, Golden Gate National Recreation Area officials have posted more — and more visible — signs warning park visitors of the possible presence of coyotes and have added notices online as well.A couple walking their Pomeranian on Baker Beach early Thursday notified park police that a pack of coyotes surrounded, attacked and killed their dog in an incident first reported by NBC Bay Area.
