49ers' Brock Purdy ranked by ex-Raiders CEO as worst remaining QB
-----
Brock Purdy is the only quarterback remaining in the NFL playoffs who wasn't drafted in the first round. That's a fact.The San Francisco 49ers' signal-caller is the worst QB remaining in the NFL playoffs. That's the opinion of Amy Trask.
The former Raiders CEO ranked Purdy last among the eight playoff QBs after Purdy posted the 14th-highest passer rating (113.0) in NFL history and the ninth-highest yards-per-attempt average (9.6) this season.
