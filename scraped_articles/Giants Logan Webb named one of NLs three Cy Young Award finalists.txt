Giants’ Logan Webb named one of NL’s three Cy Young Award finalists
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — Logan Webb, one of the few bright spots in the San Francisco Giants' disappointing 2023 season, was named one of three finalists Monday for the National League Cy Young Award.The other finalists are Blake Snell of the San Diego Padres and Zac Gallen of the Arizona Diamondbacks. The winner will be announced Nov. 15.
