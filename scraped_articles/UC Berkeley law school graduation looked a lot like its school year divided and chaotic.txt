UC Berkeley law school graduation looked a lot like its school year: divided and chaotic
-----
Friday's UC Berkeley law school commencement ceremony began with an offstage speaker offering a cautionary note: "Disruptions of any kind are not acceptable and will not be tolerated."The law school has for the entire school year been a crucible of controversy involving attitudes and action over the Israel-Hamas war and the boundaries of free speech.
That tumult continued Friday as 692 students gathered to receive their diplomas at an often cacophonous ceremony at the Greek Theatre
