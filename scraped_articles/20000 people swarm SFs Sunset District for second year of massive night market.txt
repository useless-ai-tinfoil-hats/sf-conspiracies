20,000 people swarm SF's Sunset District for second year of massive night market
-----
For the first three hours of Friday's Sunset Night Market, an unrelenting sun beamed down on San Francisco's people-packed Irving Street corridor, casting a spotlight on a seven-block strip that felt like the center of the world.Inspired by the bustling night markets of Asia, the long-awaited west-side event returned at double the size of last fall's inaugural night market, which it's estimated drew more than 10,000 attendees.
