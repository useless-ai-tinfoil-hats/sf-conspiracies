S.F. police retail theft crackdown nets 17 arrests at a single store on Mission Street
-----
San Francisco police said Thursday that 17 people were arrested this week at a single store for suspected shoplifting as part of a retail-theft crackdown officials named "Blitz '23."In a statement, police did not specify the store, but said it was on the 700 block of Mission Street and "was chosen for the operation due to the high number of shoplifting incidents the store was experiencing."
