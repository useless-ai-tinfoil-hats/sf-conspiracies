Fewer than 500 people cast ballots in special Berkeley City Council race
-----
Fewer than 500 people may decide who represents a Berkeley City Council district that includes the main UC Berkeley campus and several city blocks to the south, including People's Park.In a special election Tuesday, Cecilia Lunaparra — a 22-year-old UC Berkeley student — earned 284 votes, or 59.79% of ballots cast. Her opponent, city employee and Berkeley graduate student James Chang, won 191 votes, or 40.21% of ballots.
