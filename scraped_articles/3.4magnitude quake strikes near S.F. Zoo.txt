3.4-magnitude quake strikes near S.F. Zoo
-----
A 3.4-magnitude earthquake that struck about 3 miles west of the San Francisco Zoo Friday morning jolted parts of San Francisco Friday afternoon.The quake, which was measured at a depth of about 6 miles around 1:30 p.m., was felt sharply by some, but went almost unnoticed by others.
Residents in Noe Valley reported feeling it sharply. while some in SoMa said it barely registered.
