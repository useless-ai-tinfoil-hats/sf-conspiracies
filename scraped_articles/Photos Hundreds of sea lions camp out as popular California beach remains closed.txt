Photos: Hundreds of sea lions camp out as popular California beach remains closed
-----
A beach in the tourist heart of Monterey remained closed Saturday as a huge cluster of sea lions camped out on the sand during their yearly journey north.The city closed San Carlos Beach, on the south end of Cannery Row, on Monday after hundreds of the rotund pinnipeds carpeted the sand and created a spectacle for visitors, who were warned to keep a safe distance.
