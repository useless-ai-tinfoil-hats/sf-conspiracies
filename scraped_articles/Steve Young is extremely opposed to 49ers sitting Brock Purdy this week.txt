Steve Young is extremely opposed to 49ers sitting Brock Purdy this week
-----
The San Francisco 49ers will have the luxury of resting a couple of key players like Christian McCaffrey and Brock Purdy for Sunday's regular season finale, thanks to having already locked up the NFC's top seed. But not everyone agrees with the idea of keeping Purdy on the sidelines.In his weekly interview with KNBR's "Tolbert and Copes" show, Niner legend Steve Young made it clear that he does not believe skipping a game is a good idea for a quarterback.
