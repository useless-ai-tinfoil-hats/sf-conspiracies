Skip the In-N-Out-style bao and try these foods at Disney's signature festival
-----
It's officially eating season at Disneyland: The annual Disney California Adventure Food & Wine Festival runs through April 22 in Disney California Adventure Park, and offers an extensive array of new and returning foods to try.The kitchens at the festival are themed to California cuisine: There's Garlic Kissed (but unlike in Gilroy, no garlic ice cream), the food truck-inspired LA Style, the spicy Peppers Cali-Ente, the meat-free Earth Eats, and California Craft Brews and Uncork
