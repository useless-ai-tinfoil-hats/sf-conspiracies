49ers will host seventh-seed Packers after Green Bay routs Dallas
-----
It's been a constant throughout the years – the San Francisco 49ers and the Green Bay Packers battling in the playoffs – but who saw the record-breaking 10th playoff meeting between the marquee NFL franchises happening this year?The 49ers (12-5), the top seed in the NFC, will host the Packers next Saturday or Sunday in the divisional round because seventh-seeded Green Bay stunned No. 2 Dallas 48-32 in a wild-card game Sunday.
