Giants snap skid vs. Dodgers behind Webb's six shutout innings, Yaz HR
-----
At last, the San Francisco Giants beat their rivals.The Giants avoided a three-game sweep Wednesday night, beating the Los Angeles Dodgers 4-1 at Oracle Park, moving to 1-5 against them this season.
Logan Webb continued his bounce-back at home and redeemed himself from a rocky five-run outing against the Dodgers in L.A. the first week in April. The Giants' ace needed 32 pitches to work around a bases-loaded situation in the first inning, retiring the side without letting a run
