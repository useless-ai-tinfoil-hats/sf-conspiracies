Bay Area mail theft suspect arrested after ramming vehicle into officer, police say
-----
A driver was arrested Sunday morning after police say she intentionally rammed into a Los Altos police vehicle and pinned an officer against it.The driver and a passenger were suspected of stealing mail at an apartment complex on the 900 block of North San Antonio Road, an incident that officers responded to at about 3:10 a.m., police said.
