12 protesters charged with burglary, vandalism and conspiracy in occupation of UC Berkeley building
-----
In the end, the peace could last only so long in Berkeley.The relative calm that had characterized the pro-Palestinian protests at UC Berkeley for more than three weeks ended Thursday after police raided an abandoned building that a renegade band of protesters had taken over near campus, arresting 12 of them and accusing at least one of assault with a deadly weapon. Only one of those arrested was a student, said UC Berkeley spokesperson Dan Mogulof.
