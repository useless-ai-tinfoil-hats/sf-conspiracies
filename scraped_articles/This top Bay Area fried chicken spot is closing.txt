This top Bay Area fried chicken spot is closing
-----
A popular Oakland restaurant offering soul food at affordable prices will soon close.Lena's Soul Food announced its last day of business will be Sunday, Dec. 24. The closure comes after almost 11 years of offering dishes like fried chicken plates, mac and cheese, smothered pork chops and baked fish.
