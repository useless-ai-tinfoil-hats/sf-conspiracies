There's a faster way to board a plane. But airlines might never use it.
-----
United Airlines rolled out a system for boarding economy passengers this week called WILMA, loosely translated to "window-middle-aisle," which boards window passengers first, followed by the middle and then aisle seats.The approach is expected to shave minutes off boarding and address one of the most dreaded parts of flying: waiting in lines.
