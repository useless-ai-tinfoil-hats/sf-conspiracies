Economists blast 49ers' report on Levi's Stadium's local impact
-----
A report commissioned by the San Francisco 49ers that presented Levi's Stadium's impact on the local economy in a glowing fashion is receiving significant pushback.The 49ers released an economic impact report Monday that claims Levi's Stadium has generated "more than $2 billion" for the local community since it opened in Santa Clara in 2014.
But on Tuesday morning, economist J.C. Bradbury blasted the report and its methodology in a thread on social media.
