Don’t be alarmed when Democrats skip California in Tuesday’s DNC roll call vote
-----
CHICAGO — For the first time in its history, California will be the last state to cast its votes for a Democratic presidential nominee.Vice President Kamala Harris will receive her party's nomination Tuesday evening in a ceremonial roll call vote at the Democratic National Convention in Chicago.
