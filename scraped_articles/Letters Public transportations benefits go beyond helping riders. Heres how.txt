Letters: Public transportation’s benefits go beyond helping riders. Here’s how
-----
In my 30 years in city planning, "farebox recovery" in public transportation was a point of pride. The more that transit services were paid for by riders, rather than government subsidies, the better.Before the pandemic, BART and Caltrain recovered over 70% of their operating costs through fares, whereas the national average for public transit agencies was 36%.
