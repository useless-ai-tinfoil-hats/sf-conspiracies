San Francisco’s Muni is better than it's been in years. Here’s why
-----
It used to be the single public service that San Franciscans loved to hate. Herb Caen, the legendary columnist from years ago, called it "the Muniserable Railway."But now, San Francisco's Municipal Railway — Muni for short — seems to have turned some kind of corner. You could make the case that Muni is better than it has been in years. The buses and rail cars are cleaner, there is less graffiti, there are fewer accidents, and service is more reliable.
