S.F. mayor seeks new laws against ‘dangerous’ sideshows
-----
San Francisco Mayor London Breed on Friday announced plans to introduce legislation that would make it a crime for people to plan and participate in sideshows, even if they're not the ones behind the wheel.If passed, the law would create misdemeanor penalties for those who promote the unsanctioned stunt driving events, who block or obstruct city streets to set one up, or who impede law enforcement attempts to break them up.
