Camping banned at Big Sur's only free beach site due to excess of trash, 'human waste'
-----
California Central Coast residents are losing overnight access to the only free beach campsite in the Big Sur area because visitors wouldn't clean up after themselves.On Thursday, the California Coastal Commission announced that overnight camping and campfires will be banned at San Carpoforo Beach, located off Highway 1 in northern San Luis Obispo County, for up to two years.
San Carpoforo Beach, also known as San Carpo, is the only free beach campsite in the Big Sur area.
