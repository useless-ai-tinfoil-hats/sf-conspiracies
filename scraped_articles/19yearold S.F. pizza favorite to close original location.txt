19-year-old S.F. pizza favorite to close original location
-----
Little Star Pizza, a beloved deep-dish pizzeria, is closing its original location after nearly two decades in San Francisco.The restaurant's first home at 846 Divisadero St. will shutter at the end of the year, according to an announcement posted on Instagram Sunday.
"It has been a marvelous run, and an honor to have been a part of the Western Addition/Nopa/Alamo Square neighborhood," the post reads.
