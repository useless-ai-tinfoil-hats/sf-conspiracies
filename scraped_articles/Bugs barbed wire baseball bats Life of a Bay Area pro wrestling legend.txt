Bugs, barbed wire, baseball bats: Life of a Bay Area pro wrestling legend
-----
By day, Vinnie Massaro battles cockroaches and rats, crawling under houses and through attics, dealing harsh justice to ugly critters as a pest control guy.By night, it's the same deal, only more dangerous. It's rough, nasty work, but if Vinnie won't do it, who will? Who else will blow a chef's kiss to his fans, step into the ring to the music of "That's Amore," and go toe-to-toe with lowlifes, bullies and cheaters?
