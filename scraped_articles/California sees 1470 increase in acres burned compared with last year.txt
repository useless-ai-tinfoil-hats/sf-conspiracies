California sees 1,470% increase in acres burned compared with last year
-----
A series of wildfires broke out in California in June, and many are wondering whether the eruption of grass fires was the start of a destructive season. No doubt the state saw a more active start to peak wildfire season this year compared with last: A total of 106,453 acres have burned since the start of 2024, compared with 6,781 acres in the same time frame in 2023, according to Cal Fire.That is a 1,470% increase in acres burned.
