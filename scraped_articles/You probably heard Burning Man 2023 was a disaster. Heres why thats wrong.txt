You probably heard Burning Man 2023 was a disaster. Here's why that's wrong
-----
Since leaving Burning Man, I've thought a lot about the role that principles play in a society, and what to do when people don't live up to them.Burning Man attracts more than 70,000 people each Labor Day weekend to an inhospitable dry lake bed called "the Playa" in northwestern Nevada. Burners marvel at incredible art installations, boogie to electronic dance music, and create and engage in hundreds of different participatory experiences at camps with a staggering variety of themes.
