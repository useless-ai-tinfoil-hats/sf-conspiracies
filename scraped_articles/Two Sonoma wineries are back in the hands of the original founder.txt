Two Sonoma wineries are back in the hands of the original founder
-----
In 2013, Phil Hurst took his two Sonoma County wineries public and quickly realized it was a mistake. Eleven years later, he bought them back.Hurst, who once again owns wineries Truett Hurst and VML, is the third California vintner in six months to reclaim a wine business he founded and sold.
