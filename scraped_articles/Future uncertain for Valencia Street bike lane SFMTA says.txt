Future uncertain for Valencia Street bike lane, SFMTA says
-----
The fate of Valencia Street's contentious center bike lane hangs in the balance as the San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency considers drastic changes based on community input.In a highly anticipated update on the pilot project published online Thursday, officials acknowledged that the experimental design is not working for everybody, citing feedback gathered from "hundreds of emails, phone calls and petitions." They said they are actively seeking viable alternatives.
