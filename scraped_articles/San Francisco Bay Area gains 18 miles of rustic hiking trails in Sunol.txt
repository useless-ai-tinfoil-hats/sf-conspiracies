San Francisco Bay Area gains 18 miles of rustic hiking trails in Sunol
-----
After over two years of construction, the East Bay Regional Park District officially opened access to 18 miles of new hiking trails across 2,800 acres in the Pleasanton Ridge Regional Park.The trails are accessible from the freshly minted Tyler Ranch Staging Area in Sunol, California, a country town about 15 minutes east of Fremont.
Sunol is also home to the Alameda Creek Overlook, formerly known as Little Yosemite, in the Sunol Regional Wilderness.
