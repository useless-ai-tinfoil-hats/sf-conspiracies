Santa Barbara woman whose racist comment sparked a protest apologizes in interview
-----
A white woman captured on video making racist statements — which sparked a large protest in Santa Barbara over the weekend — has apologized for her "inappropriate remarks," saying they were the result of a lapse in judgement.Two videos posted to Instagram show the Saturday confrontation between the woman, identified as Jeanne Umana, and a Latino construction worker, identified as Luis Cervantes. In one video, Umana is shown inside a house at an active construction site in Santa Barbara.
