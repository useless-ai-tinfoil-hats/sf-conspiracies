49ers add pass rusher Randy Gregory in trade with Broncos
-----
The San Francisco 49ers made a low-risk move Friday that they hope will have a big-time payoff.The 49ers acquired pass rusher Randy Gregory, 30, and a 2024 seventh-round pick from the Broncos in exchange for a 2024 sixth-round selection. Denver reportedly will pay Gregory about $10 million this season and the 49ers will pick up a prorated veteran-minimum salary of roughly $1 million. Gregory, who signed a five-year, $70 million deal with Denver last year, does not have guaranteed money
