Bay Area man killed in fire may have been trying to save dog, officials say
-----
A Vallejo man died Sunday after apparently rushing into a burning motor home to save his dog, the Vallejo Fire Department said in a social media post.Authorities responded to reports of a fire at 1:14 p.m. and found the vehicle "fully engulfed" by flames when they arrived. While firefighters were en route, the department received reports that a man may have entered the burning RV.
