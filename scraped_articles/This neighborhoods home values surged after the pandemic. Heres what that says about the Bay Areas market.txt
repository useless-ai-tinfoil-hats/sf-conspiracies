This neighborhood’s home values surged after the pandemic. Here’s what that says about the Bay Area’s market
-----
When a three-bedroom, two-bath home in a small Fremont neighborhood sold in 2016, it went for $821,000, or about $1.04 million in 2023 dollars. It resold in 2023 for almost $1.53 million, according to real estate listing site Zillow.That huge jump in price isn't uncommon for Fremont's 28 Palms neighborhood, a quiet locale of single-story houses and cul-de-sacs lined, fittingly, with palm trees.
