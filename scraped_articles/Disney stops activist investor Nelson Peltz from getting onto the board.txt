Disney stops activist investor Nelson Peltz from getting onto the board
-----
Disney's leadership team, and nine members of Walt Disney's family, have successfully batted down a billionaire's attempt to force his way into the company's inner circle.For some time, Nelson Peltz has used his investment company Trian Partners to buy ownership stakes in companies in order to wield influence on the businesses. After successfully wedging his way into Procter & Gamble this way in 2017, Peltz and his allies set their sights on Disney.
