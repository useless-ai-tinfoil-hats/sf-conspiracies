Four missing California Hells Angels were secretly cremated, feds say
-----
Four missing California Hells Angels were clandestinely cremated in a Fresno funeral home, federal prosecutors claimed last week.The straight-from-Hollywood allegations were made in a sentencing memorandum filed Oct. 12 to the San Francisco division of the U.S. District Court. The U.S. District Attorney's Office filing was first reported by the Mercury News.
