San Jose BART extension to take years longer and billions more to complete
-----
The highly anticipated BART extension project meant to loop service through downtown San Jose and up toward Silicon Valley is now expected to take several years and billions of dollars more than what was originally projected, transit officials confirmed.The Santa Clara Valley Transportation Authority announced in a press release Wednesday that updated risk assessments have delayed the estimated completion date of the agency's BART-to-Silicon Valley project from 2033 to 2036.
