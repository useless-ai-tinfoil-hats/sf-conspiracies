Here are the 200 intersections where S.F. is banning right turns on red
-----
Turning right during a red light will be prohibited at most stoplight intersections in downtown San Francisco, with the first bans coming this month.The city's Municipal Transportation Agency plans to ban such maneuvers at 200 intersections in the South of Market, Union Square, Chinatown, North Beach and Financial District neighborhoods.
