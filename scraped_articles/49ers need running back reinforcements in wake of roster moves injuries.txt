49ers need running back reinforcements in wake of roster moves, injuries
-----
It has been a rough week for the San Francisco 49ers when it comes to their running backs.On Monday, Elijah Mitchell suffered a hamstring injury in practice that will require season-ending surgery and rookie Isaac Guerendo suffered a groin injury that could place his season-opening status in doubt. On Wednesday, Cody Schrader, a promising undrafted rookie from Missouri whom they waived and hoped to sign to their practice squad, was claimed off waivers by the Rams.
