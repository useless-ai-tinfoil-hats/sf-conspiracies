The California coffee shop at the end of the world
-----
At first, the Nuova Simonelli espresso machine looks like a mirage. The reflective box is set at the top of a brutal hill, rubbing against the clouds as they dance along a mountain ridge line. For a moment, it's impossible to tell where machine ends and sky begins.There is no booming generator or idling engine, just the low thump of grounds being discarded from a portafilter and the high-pitched burble of a milk steamer.
