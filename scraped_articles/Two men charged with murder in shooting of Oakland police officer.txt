Two men charged with murder in shooting of Oakland police officer
-----
Two men were charged with murder Wednesday in the fatal shooting of an undercover Oakland police officer near Jack London Square last week, officials announced.Mark Sanders, a 27-year-old Tracy resident, and Allen Starr Brown, 28, of Chico were charged in Alameda County Superior Court with the murder of officer Tuan Le, among other charges, District Attorney Pamela Price said at a news conference Wednesday night.
