Giants' LaMonte Wade Jr. exits game against Phillies with apparent leg injury
-----
LaMonte Wade Jr., the San Francisco Giants' steadiest bat, exited Monday's game against the Philadelphia Phillies with an apparent leg injury.Wade grabbed the back of his left knee while running into second base on a double off the right field wall in the fifth inning. The 30-year-old limped off the field with head athletic trainer Dave Groeschner assisting him.
