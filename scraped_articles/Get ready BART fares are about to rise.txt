Get ready: BART fares are about to rise
-----
BART riders will pay more for their rides beginning New Year's Day under a fare hike approved months ago that is about to take effect.Fares will increase by 5.5%, BART said. That means, for example, that a trip from downtown Berkeley to the Embarcadero Station in S.F., which costs $4.50, will rise to $4.75 beginning Jan. 1, while a trip from Antioch to the Montgomery Street Station, which costs $8.20, rises to $8.60 starting Jan. 1.
