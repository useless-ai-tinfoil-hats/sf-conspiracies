Marin's Cinemark Century Regency is closing permanently
-----
The Cinemark Century Regency, a six-screen movie theater that's been open since 1983 in San Rafael, has closed permanently, as first reported by the Marin Independent Journal.Its last day operating was Nov. 26, concluding a 40-year run at the location at 280 Smith Ranch Road. A spokesperson for the movie theater chain confirmed the closure, saying the company's lease was up at the building.
