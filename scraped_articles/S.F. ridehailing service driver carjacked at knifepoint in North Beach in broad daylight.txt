S.F. ride-hailing service driver carjacked at knifepoint in North Beach in broad daylight
-----
A ride-hailing service driver was carjacked at knifepoint in San Francisco on Wednesday afternoon, San Francisco police said.San Francisco police said a passenger in a ride-hailing service vehicle paid the driver, but then demanded the money back and pulled out a knife during the interaction, which took place in the North Beach neighborhood of San Francisco. Police reports indicated the incident happened shortly after 3 p.m.
