Delicious Tahoe road-trip burgers are hidden behind a gas station
-----
There aren't many restaurants along the historic state Route 49 in Auburn, Calif. But, for the last four decades, one burger joint has remained a cherished pit stop for the droves of travelers on their way to Tahoe.The Sardella family has been at the helm of flipping delicious burgers since 1985, first at a Fosters Freeze outpost at 403 Grass Valley Highway and later, at Burger & Cream, which opened at the same location in 2017.
