Preliminary 4.6 magnitude earthquake strikes near Los Angeles
-----
An earthquake with a preliminary magnitude of 4.6 hit Southern California on Monday afternoon, according to the U.S. Geological Survey.The quake struck in the Santa Monica Mountains with a depth of 8.6 miles at 1:47 p.m., the agency said. The epicenter was approximately 7 miles northwest of Malibu.
According to a USGS shockwave map, the quake was felt in several counties including Santa Barbara, Riverside, Ventura and Orange County.
