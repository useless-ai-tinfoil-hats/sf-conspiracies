Bay Area BevMo gets liquor license suspended after 3 clerks caught selling to minors, police say
-----
A Menlo Park BevMo was forced to temporarily close after undercover operations found employees selling alcohol to minors, according to police.Agents from the California Department of Alcohol Beverage Control posted a 65-day liquor-license suspension at the BevMo on El Camino Real last week. The suspension will remain in effect until Nov. 11, according to the police department's statement.
