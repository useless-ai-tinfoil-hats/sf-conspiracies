Bay FC survives blunders, beats Seattle 3-2 for first home-field win
-----
Bay FC has an electric attack unit. Racheal Kundananji and Asisat Oshoala, starting alongside each other for the first time this season, combined for nine shots Sunday.But it wasn't that dynamic duo, or Tess Boade or even Deyna Castellanos who sparked the Bay FC offense. No, it was a defensive unit that needed a win, and they got that in a 3-2 victory over Seattle at PayPal Park.
