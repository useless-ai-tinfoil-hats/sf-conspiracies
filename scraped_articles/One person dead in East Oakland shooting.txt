One person dead in East Oakland shooting
-----
A person died Tuesday at an East Bay hospital following an early morning shooting in East Oakland, authorities said.The shooting death marked Oakland's 46th homicide this year, and came one week after 14 people were wounded in a shooting at a Juneteenth celebration at Lake Merritt.
