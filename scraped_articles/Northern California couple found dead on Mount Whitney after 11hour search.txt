Northern California couple found dead on Mount Whitney after 11-hour search
-----
LATEST May 13, 12:25 p.m. The hikers found dead on Mount Whitney last week were identified by the Tulare County Sheriff's Office as two Northern California residents. Patricia Bolan, a 29-year-old from Davis, and Andrew Niziol, a 28-year-old from South Lake Tahoe, were found dead on the mountain after an 11-hour search conducted by several local agencies.According to the Sacramento Bee, the two were a couple on an extended hiking trip, and Bolan had just earned her doctorate in physics
