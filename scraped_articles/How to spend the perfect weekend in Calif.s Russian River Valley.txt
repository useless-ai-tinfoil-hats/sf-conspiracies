How to spend the perfect weekend in Calif.'s Russian River Valley
-----
Roughly two hours north of San Francisco is California's Russian River Valley. Located in Sonoma County, the area is known for its temperate climate and diverse range of soils, making it particularly well suited for growing a variety of grapes, especially those used in producing pinot noir and chardonnay wines.
