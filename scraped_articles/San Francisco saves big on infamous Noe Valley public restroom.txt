San Francisco saves big on infamous Noe Valley public restroom
-----
The San Francisco Recreation and Parks department has released two images of the now-infamous public restroom that officials expect to install in Noe Valley sometime this spring. Though at one time the bathroom was set to cost a whopping $1.7 million, it'll come at almost 60% cheaper.One image is a photo of the actual restroom in a garage. The second is a rendering of what the bathroom could look like once it's officially placed in the Noe Valley Town Square on 24th Street.
