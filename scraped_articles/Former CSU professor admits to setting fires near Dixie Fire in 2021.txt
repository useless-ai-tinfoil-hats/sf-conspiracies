Former CSU professor admits to setting fires near Dixie Fire in 2021
-----
A Bay Area man and former California State University professor pleaded guilty on Thursday to setting fires near the Dixie Fire in 2021.According to a Feb. 1 news release from the U.S. Attorney's Office of the Eastern District of California, Gary Stephen Maynard, 49, a San Jose resident, went on an "arson spree" in the Shasta Trinity National Forest in July and August of 2021, near the area where California's second-largest wildfire in California history was ablaze.
