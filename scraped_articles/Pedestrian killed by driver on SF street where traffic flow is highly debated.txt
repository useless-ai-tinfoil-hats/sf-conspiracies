Pedestrian killed by driver on SF street where traffic flow is highly debated
-----
A pedestrian was killed on San Francisco's Valencia Street on Wednesday by a left-turning driver, the second pedestrian death on that section of Valencia Street this year, and the recent death is sparking new conversation about the traffic flow on one of the city's busiest commercial corridors.Jian Huang, an 80-year-old San Francisco resident, was crossing Valencia Street at 18th Street when he was struck by the driver of an SUV.
