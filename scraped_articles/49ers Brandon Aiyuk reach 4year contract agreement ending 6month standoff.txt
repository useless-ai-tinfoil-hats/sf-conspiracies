49ers, Brandon Aiyuk reach 4-year contract agreement, ending 6-month standoff
-----
The standoff that threatened to possibly stand in the way of the San Francisco 49ers' path to the Super Bowl is finally over.The offseason contract saga involving the 49ers and Brandon Aiyuk ended Thursday when the All-Pro wide receiver signed a four-year contract extension, a league source said. The deal is reportedly worth $120 million and includes $76 million in guarantees.
