Sonoma County sewage spill leaks into Russian River
-----
An equipment failure at a Guerneville sanitation facility late Friday and early Saturday morning night caused a nearly 277,000-gallon sewage leak, some of which flowed into the nearby Russian River, officials said.Pam Jeane, assistant general manager of operation and maintenance at Sonoma Water, told SFGATE that last week's heavy rains caused a power outage at the plant that led to a failure in a valve.
