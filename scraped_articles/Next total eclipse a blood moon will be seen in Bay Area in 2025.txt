Next total eclipse, a ‘blood moon,’ will be seen in Bay Area in 2025
-----
The Bay Area missed out on Monday's rare total solar eclipse and will have to wait two decades for the next one — but a total lunar eclipse will be visible here in just under a year, according to NASA.A total lunar eclipse, such as the one that will occur throughout California and the continental U.S. on March 14, 2025, happens when the Earth moves between the sun and a full moon, so that the moon falls completely within the Earth's shadow.
