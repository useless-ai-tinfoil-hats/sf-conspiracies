Driver fatally strikes pedestrian in Oakland, flees the scene
-----
A pedestrian died Monday afternoon after being hit by a vehicle in Oakland, police said.The collision occurred just after 12:45 p.m. on the 9500 block of International Boulevard, according to police. Authorities said a "light-colored vehicle" was traveling eastbound when it struck a 73-year-old man who was walking northbound in a crosswalk at International Boulevard and 95th Avenue. The driver fled the scene after the crash, police said.
Oakland firefighters and medics provided aid
