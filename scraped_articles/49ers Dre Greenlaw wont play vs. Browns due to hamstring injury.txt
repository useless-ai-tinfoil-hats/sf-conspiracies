49ers’ Dre Greenlaw won’t play vs. Browns due to hamstring injury
-----
CLEVELAND – San Francisco 49ers linebacker Dre Greenlaw won't play Sunday against the Browns due to a hamstring injury he suffered in practice during the week.Greenlaw will likely be replaced in the starting lineup by Demetrius Flannigan-Fowles, who has three career starts, the last in 2021.
