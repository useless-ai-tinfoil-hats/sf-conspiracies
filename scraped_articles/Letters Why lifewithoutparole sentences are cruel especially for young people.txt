Letters: Why life-without-parole sentences are cruel, especially for young people
-----
Regarding "California Supreme Court upholds life-without-parole sentences for adults 25 and under" (California, SFChronicle.com, March 4): Life without parole continues to be an inhumane sentence, particularly imposed on emerging adults who are 18-25.I applaud the dissenting opinions by Justices Kelli Evans and Goodwin Liu, which pointed out the extreme racial disparities involved in life without parole. Over 60% of people sentenced to life without parole in California were 25 or under
