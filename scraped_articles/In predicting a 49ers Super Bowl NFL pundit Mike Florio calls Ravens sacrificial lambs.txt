In predicting a 49ers Super Bowl, NFL pundit Mike Florio calls Ravens ‘sacrificial lambs’
-----
The Christmas night showdown between the San Francisco 49ers and Baltimore Ravens has been billed as a Super Bowl preview, with many experts expecting a thrilling game as the league's top two teams do battle.But don't count NBC Sports' Mike Florio among them.
"If it's a Super Bowl preview, the Super Bowl is gonna suck," said Florio on his Thursday NBC show.
The pundit used some colorful language to describe how dominant the 49ers have been when at full strength.
