Big rig carrying strawberries in South Bay overturns, causes severe traffic at 2 highways
-----
A big rig truck carrying thousands of pounds of strawberries overturned in the South Bay on Wednesday morning, causing severe traffic delays on Highway 101 and Interstate 880.The crash blocked the northbound connector ramps to both highways in San Jose, Ross Lee, a spokesperson for the California Highway Patrol, told SFGATE. A severe traffic alert was issued at about 9 a.m.
