‘All Out for Gaza’: Thousands in S.F. rally for Palestinians
-----
A large crowd marched passionately Saturday in San Francisco to support Palestinians struggling to flee areas of the Gaza Strip targeted by the Israeli military as the Middle East conflict escalates.Holding placards that read "No more human suffering" and "This is not war. This is genocide," a throng gathered at Justin Herman Plaza and marched up Market Street during the "All Out for Gaza" rally, which was organized by several Bay Area organizations supporting Palestinian rights.
