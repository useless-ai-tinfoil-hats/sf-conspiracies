Dre Greenlaw’s ball-hawking seals 49ers’ win as he hunts for pick-sixes
-----
Dre Greenlaw is a part of one of the NFL's best linebacker duos, but he's spent his five-season career in the shadow of his more decorated partner, Fred Warner.On Saturday night, however, Warner, the San Francisco 49ers' unanimous first-team All-Pro stepped to the podium in the auditorium at Levi's Stadium and smiled at his questioners after a thrilling 24-21 divisional-playoff win against the Packers.
"I know," Warner said, "I'm not the linebacker you're looking for."
