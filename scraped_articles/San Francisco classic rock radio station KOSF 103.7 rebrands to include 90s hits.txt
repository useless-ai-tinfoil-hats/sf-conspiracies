San Francisco classic rock radio station KOSF 103.7 rebrands to include '90s hits
-----
Like the protagonist in Bowling For Soup's "1985", Bay Area radio listeners are going to have to learn to be a little less "preoccupied" with the 1980s. On August 2nd, station KOSF 103.7, part of the iHeartRadio network, officially rebranded from "80s+ @103.7" to "Classic Hits 103.7."But fans of hair metal and new wave don't need to worry too much, KOSF 103.7 will sound pretty much the same as it has since 2021.
