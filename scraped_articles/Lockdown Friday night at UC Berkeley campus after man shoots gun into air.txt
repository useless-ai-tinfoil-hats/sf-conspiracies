Lockdown Friday night at UC Berkeley campus after man shoots gun into air
-----
A man shot several bullets into the air on UC Berkeley's campus Friday night, sending panicked students into lockdown.Around 8:40 p.m., a 59-year-old man got into an "altercation" in Lower Sproul Plaza and fired several shots into the air, according the university. He was arrested within minutes of firing the weapon, and no one was injured, according to Dan Mogulof, assistant vice chancellor at UC Berkeley.
