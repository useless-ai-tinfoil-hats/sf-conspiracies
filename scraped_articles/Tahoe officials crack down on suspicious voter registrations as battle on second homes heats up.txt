Tahoe officials crack down on suspicious voter registrations as battle on second homes heats up
-----
Second homeowners can't vote in local elections. But in May, a suspicious letter sent to people who own second homes in South Lake Tahoe outlined steps to mislead election officials and register to vote at their vacation residence. The letter asked second homes to register so they can vote against a controversial tax measure this fall.SFGATE reviewed a photo of the letter that was sent to second homeowners.
