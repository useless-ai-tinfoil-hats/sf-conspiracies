Beloved SF bar ransacked, neighbors rally to help keep it open
-----
On Tuesday, July 30, around 3 a.m., a beloved neighborhood bar in San Francisco's Bernal Heights neighborhood was broken into, ransacked and robbed. But that's just the beginning of the story. Now, thanks to a few good Samaritan neighbors, Rock Bar is making way in recouping its losses."We kind of tucked our tails between our legs and were like, 'This is another hit that we gotta handle,'" Rock Bar owner Kevin Cline told SFGATE via phone. He never expected the outpouring of local support
