The San Francisco 49ers are the first team in the NFL playoffs in their quest to be the last team standing for the first time in 29 years.The 49ers (10-3) clinched a postseason berth on Monday night by virtue of the Packers' 24-22 loss to the Giants.
The 49ers, who didn't reach the playoffs for five straight seasons from 2014-18, have since qualified for the postseason in four of the past five years. They can clinch their second straight NFC West title with a win at Arizona (3-10)
