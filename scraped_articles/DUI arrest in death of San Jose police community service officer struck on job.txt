DUI arrest in death of San Jose police community service officer struck on job
-----
A San Jose police community service officer was struck by a vehicle and killed Saturday night, and the driver was arrested on suspicion of driving under the influence of alcohol, police said.A second community service officer was also struck and taken to a hospital. She was expected to survive, police said.
