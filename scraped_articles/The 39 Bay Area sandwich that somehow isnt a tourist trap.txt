The $39 Bay Area sandwich that somehow isn't a tourist trap
-----
If you were searching for the best crab sandwich in the Bay Area, you might expect to find it in a place like Nick's. Just steps from Rockaway Beach, the nearly century-old Pacifica restaurant oozes old-school coastal charm.Diners sit at tables adorned with white tablecloths and vases of fresh roses, and the walls are decorated with historical black-and-white photos, wood-carved pelicans and a giant blue marlin allegedly caught by the restaurant's former owner.
