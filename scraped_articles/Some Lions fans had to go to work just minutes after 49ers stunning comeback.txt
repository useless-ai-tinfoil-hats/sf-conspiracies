Some Lions fans had to go to work just minutes after 49ers' stunning comeback
-----
In an effort to allow Detroit Lions fans to watch Sunday night's NFC championship against the San Francisco 49ers, General Motors told the Detroit Free Press its Flint plant would push back its third shift by an hour, allowing some employees to arrive at 10:42 p.m. Eastern time and others at 11 p.m.Not only did GM employees watch their team blow a 24-7 halftime lead and a possible trip to the Super Bowl, but then they still had to go to work, too.
