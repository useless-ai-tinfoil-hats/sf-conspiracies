California judge issues first-of-its-kind ruling to rein in groundwater pumping
-----
As Californians pump increasing amounts of water from the ground, sometimes siphoning flows from the rivers above and hurting fish, wildlife and other water users, an old state law is proving to be a new and successful means of reining in excessive pumping.A Superior Court judge ruled last week that Sonoma County must do more to ensure responsible groundwater pumping under the state's Public Trust Doctrine.
