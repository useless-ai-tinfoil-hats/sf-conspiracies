49ers' Brock Purdy is about to be another huge test of NFL's concussion protocol
-----
The relationship between the NFL and concussions is nowhere near as strong as that between concussions and the sport of football, yet while the NFL has spent time doing everything it can to downplay the damage from the past, the league claims to have a comprehensive approach to limiting damage in the future.This approach is where 49ers quarterback Brock Purdy finds himself in as of Wednesday, when the team announced the player was placed in concussion protocol.
