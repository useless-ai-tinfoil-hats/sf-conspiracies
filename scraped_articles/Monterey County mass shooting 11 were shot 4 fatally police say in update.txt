Monterey County mass shooting: 11 were shot, 4 fatally, police say in update
-----
Eleven people were struck by gunfire at a birthday party in front of a home in Monterey County over the weekend in a mass shooting that left four dead, King City police said Tuesday.Authorities initially said that a total of seven people were shot, but at a news conference Tuesday, they released the latest updates regarding the case, adding that they are still searching for the shooters.
