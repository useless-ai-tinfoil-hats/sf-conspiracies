Bay Area man sentenced to 11 years to life in prison for Taco Bell shooting
-----
A Santa Rosa man was sentenced to life in prison after he shot and wounded a person at a Taco Bell restaurant in 2022, the Sonoma County District Attorney's Office said Wednesday.Brad Adams, 29, was sentenced to 11 years to life in state prison after a jury found him guilty of attempted murder in August. According to the district attorney's office, Adams was a convicted felon and was not allowed to own guns at the time of the shooting.
