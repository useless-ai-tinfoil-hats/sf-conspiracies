A single piece of homemade audio is fueling a San Francisco positivity push
-----
Branden Kirkman has given videographers in San Francisco a way to reset the city's image.Thousands of Instagram and TikTok posters have used Kirkman's 21-second "Good Morning San Francisco" audio clip to show footage of the city since October. The videos tend to be cinematic: As the audio plays, sunlight gleams on Victorians and the Golden Gate Bridge cuts across bright blue skies. The city's much-publicized struggles sit far off-frame.
