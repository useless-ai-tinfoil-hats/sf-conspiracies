Hans Zimmer adds another Oakland tour date
-----
Acclaimed German composer and musician Hans Zimmer has added a second Oakland date to his North American tour.The additional show on his "Hans Zimmer Live" tour is scheduled for Oct. 9 at Oakland Arena, less than a week after his sold-out Oct. 3 show at the same venue. The tour will mark his first North American performances in seven years, and is scheduled to kick off in Duluth, Ga., on Sept. 6.
