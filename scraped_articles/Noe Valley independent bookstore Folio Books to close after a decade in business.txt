Noe Valley independent bookstore Folio Books to close after a decade in business
-----
Another one of San Francisco's independent bookstore is shuttering: After ten years in business, Folio Books in Noe Valley is set to close its doors at the end of the month.Folio's store at 3957 24th St. will close on Feb. 29, and its website will stop completing online delivery orders after March 15, the store posted in a closing announcement on its website.
