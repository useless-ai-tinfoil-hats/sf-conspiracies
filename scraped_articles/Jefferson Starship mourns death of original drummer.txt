Jefferson Starship mourns death of original drummer
-----
The surviving members of both Jefferson Airplane and Jefferson Starship this week paid tribute to John Barbata, the drummer who contributed to the essence of both bands, along with a slew of other influential acts in the late 1960s and early '70s.Barbata died on May 8 at his home in Ada, Okla., as first reported by Best Classic Bands. He was 79. No cause of death was disclosed.
