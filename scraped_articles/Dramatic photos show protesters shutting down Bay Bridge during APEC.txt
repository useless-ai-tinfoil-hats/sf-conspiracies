Dramatic photos show protesters shutting down Bay Bridge during APEC
-----
Protesters calling for a ceasefire in Gaza shut down the westbound lanes of the Bay Bridge just east of Treasure Island on Thursday morning, resulting in multiple arrests and a hourslong traffic jam, the California Highway Patrol said.About 50 people were arrested and 15 vehicles were towed, according to CHP Chief Ezery Beauchamp. Some people abandoned their cars and tossed their keys off the bridge, Beauchamp said.
