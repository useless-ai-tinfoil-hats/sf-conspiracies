San Francisco's infamous Sea Cliff mansion is selling cheap
-----
San Francisco's infamous salmon pink mansion that looks over Baker Beach is finally entering a new chapter.224 Sea Cliff Ave., with its winding cliff-face staircase, has housed stolen art in its garage, been raided by the FBI, and was the longtime home of a convicted felon.
