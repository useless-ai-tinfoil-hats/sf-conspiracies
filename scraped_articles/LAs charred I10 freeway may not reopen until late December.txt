LA's charred I-10 freeway may not reopen until late December
-----
A section of a major Los Angeles freeway that's charred and damaged from a suspected arson fire may reopen in time for Christmas.California Gov. Gavin Newsom said in a press conference on Tuesday that it will take an estimated three to five weeks to repair the damaged section of Interstate 10.
The I-10 has been closed in both directions, "generally between the 110 Freeway and Alameda Street," since the fire broke out just after midnight on Nov. 11.
