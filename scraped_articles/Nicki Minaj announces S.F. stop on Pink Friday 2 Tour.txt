Nicki Minaj announces S.F. stop on ‘Pink Friday 2’ Tour
-----
Nicki Minaj already kicked off her "Pink Friday 2" world tour in Oakland, but the rapper isn't done with the Bay Area just yet.The "Starships" singer plans to take over San Francisco's Chase Center on Sept. 23, marking her second local stop on the tour, which celebrates her fifth studio album of the same name. Minaj opened the tour at Oakland Arena in March.
