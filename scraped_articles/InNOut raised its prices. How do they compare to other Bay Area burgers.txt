In-N-Out raised its prices. How do they compare to other Bay Area burgers?
-----
It's not your imagination. The price of the Double-Double did go up.California cornerstone burger chain In-N-Out bumped its prices statewide, the company confirmed to the Chronicle.
"We raised prices in California restaurants to accompany a raise given to the associates at those locations," In-N-Out Chief Operating Officer Denny Warnick wrote in a statement to the Chronicle.
