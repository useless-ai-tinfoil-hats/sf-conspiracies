Former SF Giants coach enters United cockpit, FAA investigating
-----
A video posted by a former San Francisco Giants coach is leading to a federal investigation into United Airlines for breaking multiple policies.The Federal Aviation Administration has opened an investigation, according to several media reports, after Colorado Rockies hitting coach Hensley "Bam Bam" Meulens posted a video on Instagram showing himself in the cockpit on the Rockies' flight from Denver to Toronto on April 10.
