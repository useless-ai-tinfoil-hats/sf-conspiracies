James Beard winner and top US pizzaiolo eyes new Bay Area restaurant
-----
Celebrated restaurateur and pizzaiolo Chris Bianco plans to debut his first Bay Area restaurant this fall.Bianco, a James Beard recipient and Netflix "Chef's Table: Pizza" alum, is expected to open a trattoria at Farm restaurant, which is at Carneros Resort and Spa in Napa. Once renovations at the space are complete, Farm will keep its name but transform into the new restaurant concept.
