Rob Schneider issues apology to daughter Elle King for ‘toxic’ relationship
-----
Rob Schneider issued a public apology to his musician daughter, Elle King, following her recent description of their relationship as "toxic" during an interview.The 60-year-old former "Saturday Night Live" star admitted his failures as a father in a conversation with conservative commentator and fellow San Francisco native Tucker Carlson.
"I hate to start with this, but I read what looked like a family tragedy playing out in the news," the host of "The Tucker Carlson Show" said.
