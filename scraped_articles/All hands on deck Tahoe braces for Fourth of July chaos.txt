'All hands on deck': Tahoe braces for Fourth of July chaos
-----
Tahoe City resident Sarah Lowis was standing in front of her calendar, counting how many days she had until the Fourth of July. After more than 40 years in Lake Tahoe, Lowis has developed a strategy to survive the Fourth, and she was doing the math to figure out how many days of food she would need in order to avoid leaving her house during Tahoe's busiest week of the year.
