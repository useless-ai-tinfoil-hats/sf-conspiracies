California is home to one of the world's largest South Asian diasporas, with nearly a million people — one in every five South Asians in the United States — calling the state home. And the number of South Asians has increased dramatically over the past decades.That South Asian population is diverse, with people coming from a variety of different countries in the subcontinent and speaking many different languages.
