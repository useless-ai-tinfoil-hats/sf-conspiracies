Boy dies after medical emergency at California water park
-----
A young boy died after experiencing a medical crisis at a California water park Saturday, officials said.First responders were called to Wild Rivers water park in Irvine for a medical emergency on Saturday. Wild Rivers officials said in a statement that the boy "experienced a non-water, but medical related incident outside one of our pools."
