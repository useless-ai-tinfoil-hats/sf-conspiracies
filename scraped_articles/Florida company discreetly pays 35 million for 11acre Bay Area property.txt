Florida company discreetly pays $35 million for 11-acre Bay Area property
-----
Sterling Organization, a multibillion-dollar real estate firm based in Florida, has bought a defunct Fry's Electronics location in Fremont for over $30 million, Bay Area News Group reported.The investment company already owns over 70 properties, including shopping centers throughout Los Angeles, San Francisco and San Diego, its website shows. Sterling Organization press representatives and booking agents did not respond to SFGATE's request for more information at the time of publication,
