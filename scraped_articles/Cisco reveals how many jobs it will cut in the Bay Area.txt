Cisco reveals how many jobs it will cut in the Bay Area
-----
Tech giant Cisco has revealed how many Bay Area employees it will lay off as part of its plan to cut 5% of its workforce.According to regulatory filings to state and local officials, approximately 730 jobs will be affected across the region. That includes 447 positions in San Jose, 174 in Milpitas and 108 in San Francisco.
