Oakland police seek help locating teenager missing for several days
-----
Oakland Police Department officials issued an alert Monday asking for the public's help in locating a 14-year-old girl who has been missing for several days.Roxana Ramirez Jimenez had not been heard from since the morning of Sept. 20, on the 4300 block of Broadway.
Ramirez Jimenez is 5-feet tall and weighs about 100 pounds, police said. She has black hair and brown eyes and was last seen wearing a green hoodie, green pants and dark-colored shoes.
