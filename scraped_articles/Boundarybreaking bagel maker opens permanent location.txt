Boundary-breaking bagel maker opens permanent location
-----
After years of pop-ups and deliveries, one of the Bay Area's most popular bagel operations will soon welcome customers at its new shop and bakery.Hella Bagels opens Wednesday at 1019 San Pablo Ave., just a few blocks from the Albany-Berkeley border. Fans can come in for chewy, New York-inspired bagels with classic toppings from poppy to "everything" seasoning mix, and with a variety of schmears like horseradish-lemon-dill and jalapeno-cilantro.
