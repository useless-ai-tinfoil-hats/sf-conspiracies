Las Vegas Strip burger restaurant to close after 14 years
-----
A burger restaurant that's been on the Las Vegas Strip since 2010 is closing permanently to become a new concept. Holsteins inside the Cosmopolitan will shutter on July 9; the news was first reported by Vital Vegas.Holsteins was known for its beef burgers, alcoholic milkshakes and bar snacks like chicken wings and egg rolls.
