Rivian to cut 150 Bay Area jobs as stock hits all-time low
-----
Approximately 150 workers in the Bay Area will face job cuts as part of Rivian's plan to reduce its salaried staff by 10%, according to regulatory filings with state and local authorities.Last week, the electric vehicle manufacturer's stock experienced a more than 25% drop to an all-time low following the announcement of the layoffs, which were prompted by a combination of mixed fourth-quarter results and a missed profit forecast.
