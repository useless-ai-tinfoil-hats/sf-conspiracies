Fire at Los Angeles cannabis facility sends five firefighters to the hospital
-----
A raging fire at an illegal cannabis facility in downtown Los Angeles on Saturday sent five firefighters to the hospital after they were exposed to sulfuric acid, the LA Fire Department said in a news release.The injured firefighters were the first on the scene Saturday morning and were surrounded by a "caustic mist," later to be determined as sulfuric acid, as they forced entry into the building.
