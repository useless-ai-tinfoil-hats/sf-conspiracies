UC Berkeley’s latest building will decrease its impact on global warming by 54%. Here’s why
-----
When a new academic building opens at UC Berkeley next school year, students will be able to enjoy the polished wood ceiling that looks like it was carved from a tree in a forest, and hopefully feel a connection to the building.But that ceiling of black spruce is actually made of mass timber, a prefabricated wood product that forms most of the building — the first structure of its kind on campus. Supporters say it is not only environmentally friendly but often cheaper and faster to build
