CHP officer shoots pedestrian on I-280 in S.F.
-----
A California Highway Patrol officer shot a pedestrian following a "struggle" on southbound Interstate 280, snarling traffic during the Wednesday afternoon commute, according to CHP officials.Around 4:30 p.m., officers were dispatched to respond to a pedestrian on the freeway south of Mariposa Street, CHP Officer Andrew Barclay said. Upon arrival at the scene, "a struggle ensued" between the pedestrian and the officer. The officer then shot the pedestrian with their gun.
