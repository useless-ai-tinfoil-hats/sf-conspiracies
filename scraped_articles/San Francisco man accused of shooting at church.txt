San Francisco man accused of shooting at church
-----
South San Francisco police arrested a man suspected of firing a gun at a church Tuesday afternoon, officials said.Around 1:25 p.m., officers responded to a report of a shooting at St. Augustine's Catholic Church, on the 3700 block of Callan Boulevard, officials said. No one was injured by the gunfire and the man fled, police said.
After a 10-hour search, officials identified the suspect as Debari Charvel Augustine, 22, of San Francisco.
