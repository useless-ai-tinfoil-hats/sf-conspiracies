Review: A Jew walks into a white nationalist meeting and comes out with a winning show
-----
Theater people love empathy. All that understanding how someone else feels, all that making the world a more compassionate, less toxic place, it gives us endorphins. One local theater, San Francisco Playhouse, even calls itself "the empathy gym."One achievement of "Just for Us," Alex Edelman's delightful solo show, is to probe empathy's limits but without being cynical or nihilistic about it.
