Apple is cutting hundreds of workers in SF Bay Area
-----
Apple is giving pink slips to more than 600 employees working at offices in California's Silicon Valley, according to filings with the state's Employment Development Department.This is the company's first big round of job cuts since tech companies started shedding huge numbers of workers after the height of the COVID-19 pandemic.
