Moderates have been ascendant in S.F. politics. This new group is fighting back
-----
San Francisco moderates have notched political gains in the city over the last few years, successfully recalling progressive District Attorney Chesa Boudin and three school board members.They replaced a progressive incumbent on the Board of Supervisors with a moderate and have tapped into deep-pocketed tech and other wealthy donors as Mayor London Breed has espoused more conservative policies.
Now, a group of progressives say they have a new vehicle to fight back.
