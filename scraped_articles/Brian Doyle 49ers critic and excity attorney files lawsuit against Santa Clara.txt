Brian Doyle, 49ers critic and ex-city attorney, files lawsuit against Santa Clara
-----
Brian Doyle, the former Santa Clara city attorney who frequently clashed with the San Francisco 49ers before his firing in September 2021, filed a lawsuit against the city alleging unlawful termination and retaliation for whistleblowing.The 13-page complaint, filed last week in Santa Clara County Superior Court, accuses the 49ers of playing a crucial role in Doyle's dismissal. He alleges team-backed City Council members fired him in retaliation for "working to hold the 49ers to account"
