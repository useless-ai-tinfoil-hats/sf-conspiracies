14-year-old girl missing near SF's Tenderloin neighborhood
-----
San Francisco police are asking the public for help locating a 14-year-old girl last seen in the Tenderloin neighborhood.Genesis Barrera-Hernandez, a Hispanic teen, is 5 feet, 6 inches tall and 130 pounds. She has brown hair and brown eyes and was last seen near Turk and Hyde streets wearing a yellow shirt, brown leggings and black Croc shoes, the San Francisco Police Department said.
