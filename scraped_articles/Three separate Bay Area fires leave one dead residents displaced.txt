Three separate Bay Area fires leave one dead, residents displaced
-----
Three separate Bay Area Christmastime fires have left one person dead, one injured and others displaced, according to multiple reports.A Sunday morning fire in Menlo Park killed a woman and two pet dogs and injured another person, according to the Menlo Park Fire District. Firefighters responded to the blaze at 3:17 a.m. at 1017 Madera Ave.
Firefighters said both victims were women in their 60s, and there were no smoke detectors in the unit, according to Bay City News.
