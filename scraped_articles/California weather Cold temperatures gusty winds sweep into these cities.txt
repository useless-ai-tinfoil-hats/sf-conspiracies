California weather: Cold temperatures, gusty winds sweep into these cities
-----
Following Tuesday's cold front, the North Pacific High is expected to nudge inland to northwestern California on Wednesday, increasing the pressure gradient and resulting wind speeds across the state. Northwest gusts of 25 to 35 mph are likely in the Bay Area, while Sundowner and Santa Ana winds in Southern California are forecast to gust up to 50 mph.The National Weather Service has issued wind advisories across Southern California through Thursday morning for local gusts up to 50 mph
