Elevated risk of sneaker waves puts these Bay Area beaches at risk
-----
National Weather Service officials issued a beach hazard warning for a long stretch of Northern California's coastline that was scheduled to last through Tuesday morning.The warning was intended to draw attention to the increased risk of sneaker waves and rip currents at northwest-facing beaches. The ocean could produce waves capable of suddenly dragging swimmers out to sea, creating dangerous conditions for inexperienced swimmers and surfers.
