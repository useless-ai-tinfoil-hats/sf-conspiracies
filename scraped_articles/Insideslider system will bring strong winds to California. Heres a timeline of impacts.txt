Inside-slider system will bring strong winds to California. Here’s a timeline of impacts
-----
Signs point to a parched area of low pressure sliding into California over the next few days. This so-called "inside-slider" system will set off a trifecta of winds — Diablos in the Bay Area, Sundowners along the Santa Barbara coast and Santa Anas in Southern California.
