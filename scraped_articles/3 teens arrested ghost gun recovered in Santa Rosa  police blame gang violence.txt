3 teens arrested, ‘ghost gun’ recovered in Santa Rosa — police blame gang violence
-----
Santa Rosa officers arrested three 15-year-old boys after a fight between more than a dozen juveniles that the Police Department blames on gang violence.Officers responding to a midday disturbance Thursday at the Santa Rosa Transit Mall chased two of the teens into a nearby retail store and found them hiding behind a large piece of furniture, police said in a news release.
