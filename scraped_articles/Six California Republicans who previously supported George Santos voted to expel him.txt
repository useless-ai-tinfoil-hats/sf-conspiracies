Six California Republicans who previously supported George Santos voted to expel him
-----
The House of Representatives voted to expel now-former Rep. George Santos from the chamber Friday with the assistance of eight California Republicans.Six of those lawmakers — Reps. Ken Calvert of Palm Desert (Riverside County), John Duarte of Turlock (Stanislaus County), Young Kim of Anaheim, Jay Obernolte of Hesperia (San Bernardino County), Michelle Steel of Cypress (Orange County) and David Valadao of Bakersfield — had previously voted against expelling Santos during a failed Nov. 1
