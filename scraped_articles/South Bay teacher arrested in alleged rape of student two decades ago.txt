South Bay teacher arrested in alleged rape of student two decades ago
-----
A high school teacher in San Jose was arrested on suspicion of raping and sexually assaulting a student more than two decades ago, police said Monday.Shawn Thomas, 56, of Los Gatos was arrested in San Jose on Friday and booked into Santa Clara County main jail in connection with the rape and sexual assault of a minor at Leigh High School, according to police.
The sexual assault, which authorities said occurred sometime between 2002 and 2003, was reported to police on March 8.
