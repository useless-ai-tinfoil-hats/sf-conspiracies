Caltrain strikes vehicle in Burlingame, causing three-car collision
-----
A Caltrain train was involved in a collision with three occupied vehicles in Burlingame on Wednesday afternoon, according to the public transportation agency.Just after 4:30 p.m., a train headed north through Burlingame with 195 passengers on board got into a collision, Dan Lieberman, a spokesperson for Caltrain, said in a statement. The agency did not release specific details, but on X, formerly known as Twitter, Caltrain said the train "clipped" a vehicle that was on the tracks.
