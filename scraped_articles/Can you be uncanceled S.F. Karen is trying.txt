When Evan Nierman first saw the viral "S.F. Karen" video that prompted the internet to shun Lisa Alexander, he was appalled. But not for the same reason as much of the general public.The video, recorded in June 2020, showed Alexander, founder of LaFace Skincare, interrogating James Juanillo as he stenciled "Black Lives Matter" on a wall outside of his Pacific Heights home.
