The secretive strategy behind Napa Valley’s priciest wines — and why some think it needs to change
-----
If you bought a bottle of Robert Craig Winery's 2019 Howell Mountain Cabernet Sauvignon directly from the winery, you paid $118. But last month, a San Francisco retailer sent out a promotional email offering the wine to its customers for $99.95 — a tempting price for a bottle that critic Antonio Galloni awarded 97 points.That promotional email has caused Robert Craig's CEO, Elton Slone, some "sleepless nights," he said. Soon, customers were calling him complaining that they'd overpaid.
