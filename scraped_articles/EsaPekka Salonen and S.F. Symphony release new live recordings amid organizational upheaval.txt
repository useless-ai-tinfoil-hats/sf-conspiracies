Esa-Pekka Salonen and S.F. Symphony release new live recordings amid organizational upheaval
-----
Esa-Pekka Salonen and the San Francisco Symphony are set to release three new digital-only spatial audio recordings.Igor Stravinsky's "The Firebird" was made available Friday, June 21. It was to be followed by Sergei Prokofiev's "Music From Romeo and Juliet" on July 26, and Hector Berlioz's "Symphonie Fantastique" on Sept. 23, produced through SFS Media, the Symphony's audio/video label.
