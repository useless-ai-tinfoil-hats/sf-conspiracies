Narsai David, Bay Area chef and beloved radio host, dies at 87
-----
Chef and Bay Area broadcast personality Narsai David has died after complications from a fall in his home that occurred last year, his former radio station, KCBS reported Friday. He was 87 years old.The son of Assyrian immigrants, David got his start in the Bay Area culinary industry in the late 1950s at Henry Rubin and Ed Brown's Pot Luck Restaurant in Berkeley.
