Photos of Bay Area crash show a Lexus underneath a box truck
-----
Cal Fire shared images of a vehicle collision on Highway 92 near Half Moon Bay that occurred late Tuesday afternoon. The pictures shared on social media show a Lexus sedan underneath a toppled-over box truck.Cal Fire spokesperson Cecile Juliette said the agency received a call about the collision on the 500 block of San Mateo Road (Highway 92) at Spanish Town, near Half Moon Bay, just before 4 p.m.
