Southwest angers loyal customers by raising certain boarding fees
-----
Southwest Airlines flyers accustomed to some of the airline's quirky features are having a turbulent year. Two months after cutting some of its routes to cut costs, Southwest just upped the cost of some of its fees.On Tuesday, Reddit users noticed that the airline raised its price range for two features that help secure optimal seats: upgraded boarding and EarlyBird Check-In. The new prices went into effect May 23.
