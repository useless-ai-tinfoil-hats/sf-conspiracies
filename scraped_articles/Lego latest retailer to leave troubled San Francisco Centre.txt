Lego latest retailer to leave troubled San Francisco Centre
-----
The Lego store inside San Francisco Centre permanently closed its doors Thursday, according to a company official, the latest retailer to leave the troubled mall."We are always reviewing our store portfolio to ensure that we can provide the best LEGO experience for shoppers and fans. As a result, we recently made the decision to close our LEGO Store in the San Francisco Centre," LEGO spokesperson Colleen Arons said in an email to the Chronicle.
