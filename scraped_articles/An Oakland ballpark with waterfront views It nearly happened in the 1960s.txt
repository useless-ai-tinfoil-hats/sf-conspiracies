An Oakland ballpark with waterfront views? It nearly happened in the 1960s
-----
For Oakland Athletics fans, a 1960 baseball stadium concept drawing found in the Chronicle archive shows what might have been.The bold design showcased midcentury modern flourishes, and an outfield that opened up to waterfront views of Lake Merritt and the Oakland hills. Good restaurants and transit hubs were both a short walk away.
