Big Sur Bakery, community icon and tourism hot spot, destroyed in fire
-----
Firefighters in scenic Big Sur battled a structural blaze Friday that destroyed a decades-old community staple: the Big Sur Bakery.The bakery, located in Lomo Vista Gardens along Highway 1, could not be saved, Blaise Godbe Lipman, the son of Lomo Vista Gardens property owner Hilary Lipman, confirmed in an Instagram post Saturday.
