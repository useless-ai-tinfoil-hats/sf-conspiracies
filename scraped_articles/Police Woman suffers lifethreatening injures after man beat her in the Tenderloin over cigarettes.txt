Police: Woman suffers life-threatening injures after man beat her in the Tenderloin over cigarettes
-----
A woman was hospitalized with life-threatening injuries after a man severely beat her after she declined to provide him with cigarettes, San Francisco police said Thursday.The woman said she had no cigarettes to offer the man after he approached her, police said. The man struck her multiple times before fleeing the scene, police said.
Police responded to the incident at Hyde and O'Farrell streets around 3:40 a.m. Wednesday, spokespersons said.
