2 injured, including child, in S.F. by driver suspected of fleeing police
-----
A driver struck and injured two pedestrians Tuesday morning in San Francisco, including a child, police said.San Francisco police officers were dispatched to Jones and Market streets just before 8 a.m., following a report that a vehicle struck a pedestrian and fled the scene. The pedestrian was transported to a hospital with non-life-threatening injuries, they said.
