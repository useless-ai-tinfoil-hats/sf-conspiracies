Caltrain fatally strikes person on tracks on Peninsula
-----
A person crossing the Caltrain tracks was fatally struck by a train early Sunday in San Carlos, the transit agency said.The person was struck by a test train at 12:10 a.m., according to Mahmoud Abunie, a spokesperson for Caltrain. Local authorities responded to the crash, and the victim was pronounced dead shortly afterward.
The victim's identity was not immediately available, and the cause of the crash was under investigation, Abunie said.
