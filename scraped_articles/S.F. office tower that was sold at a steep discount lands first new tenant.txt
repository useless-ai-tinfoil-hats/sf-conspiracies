S.F. office tower that was sold at a steep discount lands first new tenant
-----
When a 22-story office tower in San Francisco's Financial District traded hands last summer at a nearly 75% price drop from what it was worth prior to the pandemic, the transaction was largely viewed as a sign that the city's ailing office market was headed toward a reset.Some five months later, the longtime home of the Union Bank at 350 California St. has landed its first new tenant.
