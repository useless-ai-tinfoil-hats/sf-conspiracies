This SF public school is tops in UC Berkeley acceptance rate. It's not Lowell.
-----
Students at a San Francisco public high school were more successful at being accepted into UC Berkeley for the fall 2023 academic year than their peers at any other secondary school in California.According to data from the University of California released on March 13, Mission High School had an acceptance rate of 43% at the flagship UC campus, the highest of any high school surveyed and almost three times the state average of 15%.
