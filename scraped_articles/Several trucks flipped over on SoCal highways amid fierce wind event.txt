Several trucks flipped over on SoCal highways amid fierce wind event
-----
A severe wind event in Southern California caused several big rigs to overturn on Inland Empire highways on Monday.Two trucks flipped over at the interchange of southbound Interstate 15 and eastbound Interstate 10 in Ontario, Calif., Rodrigo Jimenez, a spokesperson for the California Highway Patrol, told SFGATE. Later in the afternoon, at least three trailers flipped over in the unincorporated city of Fontana area, he added.
