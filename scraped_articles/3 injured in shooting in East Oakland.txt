3 injured in shooting in East Oakland
-----
Three people were injured in a shooting Sunday in Oakland, police said.The three victims were shot just after 7:45 a.m. on the 1100 block of 47th Avenue in East Oakland, according to the Police Department. They were all taken to a local hospital, where they were listed in stable condition.
Detectives said they believe the victims and the shooter had a verbal dispute before the shooting.
