CHP investigating fatal Bay Bridge hit-and-run
-----
A pedestrian was fatally struck in a hit-and-run on the Bay Bridge Monday morning, according to the California Highway Patrol.A vehicle traveling westbound struck a pedestrian near the Fifth Street exit at about 1:35 a.m., said CHP Officer Mark Andrews. The driver fled before CHP officers arrived.
The pedestrian was pronounced dead at the scene and has not yet been identified. Andrews said the victim's body was so "mangled" that they were unable to identify the victim.
