Arrest in killing of Northern California deputy who was mother of ‘Sandlot’ actor
-----
A Northern California man has been arrested on suspicion of killing his girlfriend, a Del Norte County deputy and mother of an actor from "The Sandlot."Deanna Esmaeel, a deputy with the Del Norte County Sheriff's Office, was discovered dead in her Crescent City home Thursday morning, according to the sheriff's office. Law enforcement issued a warrant for the arrest of her boyfriend, Daniel James Walter, 54, who recently changed his legal name to Edward Patrick Davies.
