National Signing Day offers one last hurrah for state runner-up Serra
-----
For the third straight year, Serra's football team ended a superb, record-breaking season with a lopsided, futile loss to a national champion.In 2021, it was 44-7 to Mater Dei-Santa Ana; in 2022 against St. John Bosco-Bellflower, 45-0; and on Dec. 9 again to Mater Dei, 35-0. That's a combined 124-7 count, and stands in sharp contrast to what the Padres did against West Catholic Athletic League and Bay Area opponents over the past four seasons.
