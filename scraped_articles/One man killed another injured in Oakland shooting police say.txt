One man killed, another injured in Oakland shooting, police say
-----
A shooting in East Oakland left one man dead and another injured Tuesday night, authorities said.Officers responded to a report of a shooting just after 7 p.m. on the 10500 block of Foothill Boulevard near Interstate 580 and found a man who had been shot, the police department said.
The victim, an Oakland resident, died from his injuries at a hospital, authorities said. His identity is being withheld pending notification of next of kin.
