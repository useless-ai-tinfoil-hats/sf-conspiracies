Bay Area woman jailed for illegally selling fruit infested with fruit flies
-----
A businessperson in San Jose was sentenced to jail for illegally importing fruit fly-infested fruit from Vietnam, according to a news release from Santa Clara County District Attorney Jeff Rosen.Hanh Hong Huynh, 43, was sentenced to three months in jail after being convicted of conspiracy to import and sell fruit, and her co-defendant, Thanh Tuyen Huynh, 38, was sentenced to community service after pleading guilty to participating in a conspiracy.
