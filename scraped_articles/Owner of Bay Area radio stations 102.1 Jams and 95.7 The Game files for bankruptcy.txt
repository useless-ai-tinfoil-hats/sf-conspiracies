Owner of Bay Area radio stations 102.1 Jams and 95.7 The Game files for bankruptcy
-----
The radio and podcast giant that owns Bay Area radio stations KCBS, 102.1 Jams and 95.7 The Game has filed for bankruptcy.Philadelphia-based Audacy announced it filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection – often called "reorganization bankruptcy" – on Sunday, claiming $1.9 billion in debt. The company plans to slash that figure to $350 million by restructuring, according to a statement it shared.
