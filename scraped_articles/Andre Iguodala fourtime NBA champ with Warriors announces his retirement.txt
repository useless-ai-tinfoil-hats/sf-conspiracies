Andre Iguodala, four-time NBA champ with Warriors, announces his retirement
-----
Four-time NBA champion Andre Iguodala is retiring from the league after 19 years to focus on his career as a startup investor. The 2015 Finals MVP won his four rings with the Golden State Warriors.Iguodala, 39, was selected by Philadelphia with the ninth pick in the 2004 NBA draft and his 19-year career included stints with Denver and Miami as well as Golden State. He was named an All-Star in 2012 and made two NBA All-Defensive teams. Iguodala played in 1,231 career regular-season games
