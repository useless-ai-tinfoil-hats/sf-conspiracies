San Francisco prepares for 49ers-Lions game with possible bus reroutes, road closures
-----
San Francisco is gearing up for Sunday's playoff game between the 49ers and the Detroit Lions by closing some streets and on-ramps and adjusting Muni services in preparation for a lively day.SFMTA has a contingency plan on the day of the game "in anticipation of possible public reactions to the outcome of the game that may impact Muni service in the Mission District," the agency announced.
