Glitzy SF rooftop bar failed to pay $1.9M to construction company, lawsuit alleges
-----
Massive rooftop bar Chotto Matte opened in Union Square last October to much fanfare and booked-out reservations. Now, the glitzy 400-seat restaurant is facing a lawsuit from CalAsia Construction, Inc., to the tune of nearly $1.9 million.In the lawsuit filed in San Francisco Superior Court on April 18, Los Angeles-based CalAsia Construction alleges that Chotto Matte, LLC, failed to pay the full amount owed for mechanical work, plumbing, concrete, painting and other work CalAsia completed
