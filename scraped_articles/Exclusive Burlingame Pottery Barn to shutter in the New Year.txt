Exclusive: Burlingame Pottery Barn to shutter in the New Year
-----
Pottery Barn is slated to shutter one of its last remaining locations on the San Francisco Peninsula at the start of 2024.An employee of the upscale furniture chain's Burlingame location confirmed Thursday that the store, located at 1230 Burlingame Ave., will shutter in January, but declined to give details on the reason for its closure.
Pottery Barn and Williams Sonoma Inc., which owns the Pottery Barn brand, did not immediately respond to messages seeking comment.
