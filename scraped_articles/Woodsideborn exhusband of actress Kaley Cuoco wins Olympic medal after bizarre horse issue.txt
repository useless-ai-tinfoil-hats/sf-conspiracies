Woodside-born ex-husband of actress Kaley Cuoco wins Olympic medal after bizarre horse issue
-----
A Woodside man helped the United States equestrian jumping team win its third straight Olympic silver medal on Friday after he was subbed in to compete one day earlier with less than a few hours' notice.Karl Cook arrived at the Paris Games as an alternate alongside three veteran riders but was still practicing on his horse, Caracole De La Roque, just in case he was called upon. At 33, he was the youngest member of the four-person Olympic team.
