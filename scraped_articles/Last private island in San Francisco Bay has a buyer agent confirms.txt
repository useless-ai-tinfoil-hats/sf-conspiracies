Last private island in San Francisco Bay has a buyer, agent confirms
-----
Red Rock Island, the isolated property touted as the last private island on San Francisco Bay, has found a buyer.The six-acre outcropping, reachable only by helicopter or boat, is in escrow to an undisclosed party, listing agent Chris Lim confirmed to the Chronicle on Monday. The island was put on the market about a year ago for $25 million.
