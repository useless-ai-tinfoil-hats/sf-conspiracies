‘Mass casualty attack’ thwarted at Elon Musk’s Tesla event, police say
-----
Authorities have arrested a Florida man they say threatened to carry out a "mass casualty attack" at a Texas event launching Tesla's new Cybertruck pickups.Police said Paul Ryan Overeem, a 28-year-old from Orlando, told an Instagram group chat he planned to kill people at the public event Thursday, according to the San Antonio Express News. He was arrested Wednesday in Travis County, where Tesla's factory outside of Austin is located, a day before the event.
