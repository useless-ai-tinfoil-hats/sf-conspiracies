Eli Noyes, untrained animator who turned a college film into critically acclaimed career, dies at 81
-----
In his dorm room at Harvard, Eli Noyes put off studying by making an 8-minute animated film that involved the torturously slow act of building and moving clay figures around a miniature set.It was years later, when Noyes was 23 and in his first year of graduate school of architecture, that the film, titled "Clay or the Origin of Species," was nominated for an Academy Award.
