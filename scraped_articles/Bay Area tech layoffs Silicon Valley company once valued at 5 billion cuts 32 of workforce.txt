Bay Area tech layoffs: Silicon Valley company, once valued at $5 billion, cuts 32% of workforce
-----
The latest wave of job cuts in the Bay Area has led to significant workforce reductions at semiconductor giant Intel, the self-driving startup Ghost Autonomy and Checkr, a background-screening platform once valued at $5 billion.Intel plans to eliminate 62 positions within its sales and marketing departments at its Santa Clara headquarters, state regulatory filings show. The layoffs are expected to begin on May 25.
