SFUSD finally scraps faulty, multimillion-dollar payroll system
-----
The San Francisco Unified School District said this week it is scrapping a flawed payroll system that was responsible for underpaying or sometimes failing to pay teachers altogether.The district announced Thursday that it would adopt Frontline Education's California Enterprise Resource System, the "most-used" payroll and human resources system in California. The district said it will bring a contract to the Board of Education on March 12 and is looking to implement the system by July
