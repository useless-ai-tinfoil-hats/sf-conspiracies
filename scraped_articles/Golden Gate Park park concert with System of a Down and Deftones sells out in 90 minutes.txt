Golden Gate Park park concert with System of a Down and Deftones sells out in 90 minutes
-----
The first-ever nighttime concert at Golden Gate Park, featuring System of a Down and Deftones, sold out its tickets in just 90 minutes.Another Planet Entertainment, the company behind the annual Outside Lands festival in the park, announced that all 50,000 tickets for the new series were gone by 11:35 a.m. on Friday, April 12. The general ticket sale started at 10 a.m., following presale events for artists and the promoter. Ticket prices were $169 for general admission and $399 for VIP
