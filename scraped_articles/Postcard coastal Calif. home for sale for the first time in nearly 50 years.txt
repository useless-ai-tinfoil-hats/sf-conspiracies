Postcard coastal Calif. home for sale for the first time in nearly 50 years
-----
The strip of brightly colored homes along a stretch of beach in Capitola is one of the most frequently snapped photos of the California coast. Now, one of those picturesque houses is for sale for the first time in nearly 50 years.The stately blue house at the end of the second row, 1500 Wharf Road No. 12, hit the market for $5.5 million last month.
