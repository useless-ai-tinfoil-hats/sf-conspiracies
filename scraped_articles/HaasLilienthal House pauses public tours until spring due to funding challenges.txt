Haas-Lilienthal House pauses public tours until spring due to funding challenges
-----
San Francisco's historic Haas-Lilienthal House will temporarily stop offering public tours as the building's owners take time to figure out how to navigate a decline in both the amount of funding coming in and the number of visitors.While preparing the budget for 2024, Woody LaBounty, CEO of San Francisco Heritage — the nonprofit that owns the house at 2007 Franklin St. — said the organization realized it was facing a shortfall.
