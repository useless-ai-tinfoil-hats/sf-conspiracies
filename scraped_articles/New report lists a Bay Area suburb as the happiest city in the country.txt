New report lists a Bay Area suburb as the happiest city in the country
-----
The Bay Area suburb of Fremont, home to Tesla's largest California factory, may be the happiest city in the country.On Tuesday, WalletHub released its 2024 ranking of the happiest cities in the country. The personal finance company regularly publishes lists comparing US cities on various metrics, like jobs and fun. When it comes to happiness, California cities scored highly, with 16 of the top 50 happiest cities landing in the Golden State.
