A $15 million state grant will turn Oakland motel into supportive housing
-----
The city of Oakland will use a newly awarded grant of over $15 million to turn the Imperial Inn, a motel in the city's Temescal neighborhood, into supportive housing for unhoused residents.The money comes from the state's Homekey Initiative, which will use $156.4 million to help fund 12 affordable-housing projects across California. One of those projects will use $15,536,504 in Homekey funds to convert the Imperial Inn into 47 units of permanent affordable housing, the city of Oakland
