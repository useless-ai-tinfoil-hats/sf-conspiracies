Exclusive: Stonestown mall owner gets 30-day deadline extension on $180 million mortgage
-----
Brookfield, the real estate giant that owns San Francisco's Stonestown Galleria, has received a 30-day mortgage deadline extension to repay a $180 million loan on the property.The company confirmed to the Chronicle that it is planning to refinance the loan, whose repayment was due this month.
