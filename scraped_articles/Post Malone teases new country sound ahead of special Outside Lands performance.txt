Post Malone teases new country sound ahead of special Outside Lands performance
-----
Ahead of his special country music set at this year's Outside Lands, rapper and singer Post Malone is immersing fans further into his new sound with the single "Pour Me a Drink."The track is off the Grammy-nominated artist's forthcoming sixth studio album, "F-1 Trillion," which he is touting as his debut country release. It's due out Aug. 16, less than a week after his performance on the final day of the San Francisco music festival, which is slated for Aug. 9-11 at Golden Gate Park.
