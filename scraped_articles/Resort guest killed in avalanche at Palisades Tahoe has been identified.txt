Resort guest killed in avalanche at Palisades Tahoe has been identified
-----
UPDATE Wednesday 6:59 p.m. A man killed in an avalanche Wednesday at Palisades Tahoe in California's Olympic Valley has been identified, the Placer County Sheriff's Office announced.Kenneth Kidd, 66, a resident of both Point Reyes and the Truckee area, was killed and another person was injured in the Wednesday morning avalanche that occurred at the top of the KT-22 chairlift.
