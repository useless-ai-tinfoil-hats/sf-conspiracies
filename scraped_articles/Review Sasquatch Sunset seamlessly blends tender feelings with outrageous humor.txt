Review: ‘Sasquatch Sunset’ seamlessly blends tender feelings with outrageous humor
-----
"Sasquatch Sunset" is a movie in its own category.It's one of the most low-down comedies you could ever expect to see, with the coarsest sight gags imaginable. For instance, at one point two Sasquatches notice that the corpse of a friend is getting eaten by crows, so they defecate into their hands and start flinging feces at the crows. You either respond to a comic moment like that, or it repels you. (I thought that moment was absolutely hilarious.)
