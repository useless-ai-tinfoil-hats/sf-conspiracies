Mill Valley Music Festival returns for third year with Fleet Foxes
-----
The Mill Valley Music Festival is gearing up for its third year, featuring headliners Fleet Foxes and Greensky Bluegrass.Organized by San Francisco-based Noise Pop Industries and the Mill Valley Chamber of Commerce, the two-day concert is scheduled for May 11-12 at Friends Field in Mill Valley.
Two-day passes, starting at $217, can be purchased at www.millvalleymusicfest.com.
