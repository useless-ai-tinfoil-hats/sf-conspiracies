San Francisco gets new Park Commission president for first time in 13 years
-----
San Francisco labor attorney and Bayview business owner Kat Anderson is the new presiding president of the San Francisco Recreation and Park Commission.She succeeds longtime commission President Mark Buell, who resigned Tuesday upon his appointment to the San Francisco Airport Commission. Anderson has been vice president of the commission for three years and will automatically become president according to commission bylaws.
