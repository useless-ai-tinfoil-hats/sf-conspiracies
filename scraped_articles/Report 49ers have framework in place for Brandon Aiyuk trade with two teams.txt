Report: 49ers have framework in place for Brandon Aiyuk trade with two teams
-----
On July 23, when the San Francisco 49ers reported for training camp, general manager John Lynch couldn't hide his frustration or rule out a trade when asked about All-Pro wide receiver Brandon Aiyuk.Nearly two weeks and 10 training camp practices later, the situation has deteriorated as Aiyuk continues his quest to land a contract extension. With negotiations stalled, the 49ers have worked out the frameworks of deals for Aiyuk with the Patriots and Browns, NBC Sports Bay Area reported.
