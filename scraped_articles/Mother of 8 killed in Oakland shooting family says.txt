Mother of 8 killed in Oakland shooting, family says
-----
A person who was fatally shot last week in Oakland was identified by family members as a mother of eight. The shooting happened on Aug. 7 just before 2:30 p.m. on the 5600 block of Hilton Street, the Oakland Police Department told SFGATE.The mother, Maria Ramos, 33, "apparently released pepper spray" into a car of teenagers she suspected of selling a marijuana vape pen to one of her children, KTVU reported.
