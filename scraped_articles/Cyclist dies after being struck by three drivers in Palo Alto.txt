Cyclist dies after being struck by three drivers in Palo Alto
-----
A female cyclist died in Palo Alto on Monday night after being hit by three separate drivers, with only one of those drivers staying on scene, officials said.The crashes occurred amid light rain. The woman was stopped, straddling her bicycle, on eastbound Embarcadero Road at the crosswalk at Newell Road, near Rinconada Cultural Park, according to the early police investigation.
