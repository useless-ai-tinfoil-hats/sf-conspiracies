Woman kidnapped during carjacking in South Bay located safely
-----
A woman reported missing during a carjacking in San Jose has been found safe, the Police Department reported Saturday afternoon.Police had sought the public's help in locating a passenger in a vehicle that was stolen around North Fifth and St. John streets around 2 a.m. Saturday, the Police Department said. They later reported that the vehicle and the victim have been found.
