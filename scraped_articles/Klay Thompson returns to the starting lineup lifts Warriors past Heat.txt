Klay Thompson returns to the starting lineup, lifts Warriors past Heat
-----
MIAMI — Klay Thompson wasn't thinking Tuesday night about the NBA's postseason.He was thinking about the Orlando Magic, whom he and the Golden State Warriors were set to play Wednesday night.
"I would like to repeat this performance because these games are so crucial," he said after scoring 28 points against the Miami Heat. "We still have (11) games left and we want to finish as strong as possible — whether its nine wins, 10 wins or 11, it's doable."
