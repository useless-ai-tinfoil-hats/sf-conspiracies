California storm live updates: Evacuations ordered in San Jose
-----
Evacuations issued in San Jose as rivers riseFeb. 4, 6:50 a.m.
Mandatory evacuations have been issued in San Jose as rain continues to fall throughout the Bay Area. The evacuations are "in effect immediately for the areas on and near the banks of Coyote, Los Gatos, Penitencia and Ross Creeks and the Guadalupe River," the city announced on Facebook. More information from San Jose officials can be found on their emergency notifications page. A flood watch is in effect for San Jose
