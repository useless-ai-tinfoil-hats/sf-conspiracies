Giants’ Kapler to oppose Guardians’ Francona likely for the final time
-----
Two players on Terry Francona's 2004 World Series-champion Boston Red Sox are managing in the big leagues: the Los Angeles Dodgers' Dave Roberts and the San Francisco Giants' Gabe Kapler.Francona has hinted strongly that he'll retire after this season as manager of the Cleveland Guardians, who are wrapping up a three-game series at Oracle Park on Wednesday, likely Francona's last day managing in the Bay Area.
