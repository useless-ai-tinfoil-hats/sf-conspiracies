Two men die of drug overdoses at BART stations, with third man revived
-----
Two men died from drug overdoses at BART stations in San Francisco and Oakland on Wednesday night despite being administered Narcan, according to BART police.Authorities tried to revive both with a dose of Narcan, the overdose reversal drug, but neither responded. The first death was reported after 6 p.m. at the Fruitvale Station in Oakland, and the second just before midnight at the Powell Street Station in San Francisco.
