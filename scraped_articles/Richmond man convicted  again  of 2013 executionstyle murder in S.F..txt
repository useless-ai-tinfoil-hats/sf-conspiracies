Richmond man convicted — again — of 2013 execution-style murder in S.F.
-----
A San Francisco jury convicted a Richmond man for a 2013 execution-style murder in Sunnydale, spokespersons for the San Francisco District Attorney's Office said Monday.Zuri Wilson, 47, was convicted of first-degree murder after prosecutors said he used a firearm and lay in wait to murder a man as he was gathering items from his car in August 2013 on Blythdale Avenue, officials said.
