South Bay vegetation fire threatens ‘multiple’ buildings and at least one home
-----
A vegetation fire in San Jose spread to multiple abandoned buildings and at least one home, San Jose fire spokespersons said Thursday.Fire officials responded to a vegetation fire that broke out around noon and burned at least three acres in North San Jose, officials said.
Fire officials said multiple abandoned buildings and one home were impacted by the fire, which began in the vicinity of Montague Expressway and Seely Avenue.
The structures were unoccupied and no injuries
