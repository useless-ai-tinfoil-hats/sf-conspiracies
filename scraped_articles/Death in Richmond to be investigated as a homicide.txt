Death in Richmond to be investigated as a homicide
-----
Someone died early Wednesday in Richmond, and police are investigating the death as a homicide, according to the Richmond Police Department.A person called the police around 5:50 a.m. and said someone was injured on the 300 block of South 36th Street, authorities said. Officers and medical personnel arrived at the location and found the person, who was declared dead at the scene, police said.
