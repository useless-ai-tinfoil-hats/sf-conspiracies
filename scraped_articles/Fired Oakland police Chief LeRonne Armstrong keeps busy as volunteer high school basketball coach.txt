Fired Oakland police Chief LeRonne Armstrong keeps busy as volunteer high school basketball coach
-----
Former Oakland police Chief LeRonne Armstrong is managing to keep busy while he waits to learn what his future in law enforcement holds. Controversially removed as chief earlier this year, Armstrong has found new work as an assistant high school basketball coach for the Bishop O'Dowd High School boys basketball team.Armstrong joined the Oakland Police Department in 1999, working his way up to chief in 2021.
