California jogger killed man over 'minor inconvenience,' prosecutor says
-----
A California jogger filmed himself killing a man who was blocking his path, prosecutors from the Orange County District Attorney's Office said.On the afternoon of Sept. 28, prosecutors say Craig Sumner Elliot, 68, of Garden Grove was on a run with two dogs and a pushcart. While passing through the 10400 block of Katella Avenue, about 2 miles from Disneyland, Elliott allegedly came across Antonio Garcia Avalos, 40, an unhoused man sleeping across the sidewalk.
