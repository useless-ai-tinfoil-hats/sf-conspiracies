Late-night San Francisco doughnut shop immortalized on film
-----
It may never be possible to document San Francisco in its entirety. But if you look through the center of a doughnut, you might glance at the city in microcosm."Circle of Donuts," a documentary short by Tokyo director Toshikazu Hosoki, is scheduled to screen at the Mill Valley Film Festival on Oct. 6, 13 and 16.
