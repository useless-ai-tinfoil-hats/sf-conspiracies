I-680 at San Ramon reopens after police arrest man who had barricaded himself
-----
Police activity partially shut down Interstate 680 in San Ramon on Saturday evening as authorities responded to a crash and a barricaded suspect whom they had believed was armed.The highway reopened by 7 p.m., the California Highway Patrol said. The CHP said there had been a two-vehicle crash with minor injuries at the location. "A driver was taken into custody for driving under the influence of drugs. No gun was located or ever seen," the agency tweeted.
