Harmful algae detected in Oakland's Lake Merritt could be 'an ominous sign for the Bay'
-----
Earlier this month, signs posted around Oakland's Lake Merritt made some joggers stop in their tracks. "Avoid water contact," they cautioned in bold letters. "Harmful algae may be present."The warnings came after a mysterious red substance emerged in the water between the Lake Merritt Sailboat House and the Rotary Nature Center on March 7, as the Oaklandside first reported.
