More details on people in Half Moon Bay plane crash: Second person identified
-----
Authorities have identified a second person killed in a Jan. 14 private plane crash near Half Moon Bay as Cassidy Petit, a 26-year-old Oakland woman.The San Mateo County Sheriff's Office told SFGATE a team from the San Mateo County Harbor District assisted in the recovery of a woman's body on Wednesday. Officials from the Sheriff's Office found Petit's body near the site of the crash, the San Mateo County Coroner's Office told SFGATE.
