Project 'Ghostbusters': Facebook secretly spied on Snapchat data, docs allege
-----
Facebook spied on some Snapchat users' traffic data from 2016 through 2019 using a tool installed on their phones, according to newly released court documents from an antitrust lawsuit against the Bay Area tech giant.Now known as Meta, the Menlo Park-based company deployed traffic-intercepting software onto iPhone and Android devices through a virtual private network app called Onavo, allege lawyers for the plaintiffs, who are advertising buyers.
