Corral Fire near California's Lawrence Livermore Lab burns 12,000 acres
-----
Northern California's first major wildfire of the season has burned over 12,000 acres in San Joaquin County. The Corral Fire, which began Saturday afternoon along Corral Hollow Road near the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory Site 300, is 13% contained as of Sunday morning.The San Joaquin County Office of Emergency Services ordered residents west of California Aqueduct, south of Corral Hollow Creek, west to Alameda County and south to Stanislaus County to leave their homes.
