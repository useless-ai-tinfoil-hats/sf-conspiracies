Acclaimed S.F. Italian restaurant unveils stunning new location
-----
At Che Fico in San Francisco, the Italian restaurant's tiny wine room doubles as cold storage for pizza dough. Staff have to reach around resting dough to pull out bottles during dinner.But at the owners' shiny new sister restaurant in Menlo Park, there's a large, temperature-controlled room, set at a constant 55 degrees, just for pizza dough. Down the hall is an impressive wine room with space for over 3,000 bottles.
