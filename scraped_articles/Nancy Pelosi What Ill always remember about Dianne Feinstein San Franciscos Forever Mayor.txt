Nancy Pelosi: What I’ll always remember about Dianne Feinstein, San Francisco’s Forever Mayor
-----
On Dianne Feinstein's final flight home to San Francisco this past weekend, her daughter, Katherine, and I reminisced about a personal visit the late senator once made to her Catholic high school, the Convent of the Sacred Heart. At the event, Dianne spoke about how, as a Jewish student, she felt welcomed and respected by the community there, despite their different faiths.
