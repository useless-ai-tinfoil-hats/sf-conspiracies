Brittney Griner reacts to news of U.S.-Russia prisoner exchange: ‘Great day’
-----
VILLENEUVE-D'ASCQ, France — Brittney Griner, who spent 293 days in a Russian prison and now is playing in her third Olympics as a member of the U.S. women's basketball team, reacted with relief and joy Thursday over the news of an extensive prisoner exchange involving the United States and Russia."Great day," Griner said after her team's game against Belgium. "It was a great day. I am head-over-heels happy for the families right now. Any day Americans come home, that's a win."
