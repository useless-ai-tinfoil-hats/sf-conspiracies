After 30 years the smell is gone from this LA riverbank, but questions remain
-----
For decades, commuters on the 605 freeway east of Los Angeles were hit with the smells and sounds of thousands and thousands of ducks lining the freeway. Nayellie Diaz, who grew up in the area, remembers covering her nose as a child whenever her family would pull onto the freeway's onramp, particularly around communities like La Puente and El Monte, "because the smell was very overpowering."
