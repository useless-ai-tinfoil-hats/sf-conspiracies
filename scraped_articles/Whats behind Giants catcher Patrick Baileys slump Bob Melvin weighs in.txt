What’s behind Giants catcher Patrick Bailey’s slump? Bob Melvin weighs in
-----
OAKLAND — Patrick Bailey has been mired in a deep slump since the All-Star break that's pushed him further down in the lineup of late.The catcher is slashing .120/.163/.141 with a .306 OPS without a home run and just two extra-base hits in 26 games since the break. The rough stretch sits in stark contrast to his first half at the plate, where he slashed .283/.354/.430 with a .784 OPS in 69 games as one of the steadiest San Francisco Giants hitters.
