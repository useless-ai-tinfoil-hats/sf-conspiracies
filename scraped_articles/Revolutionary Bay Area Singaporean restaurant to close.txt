‘Revolutionary’ Bay Area Singaporean restaurant to close
-----
Lion Dance Cafe, an acclaimed Bay Area restaurant beloved for its boundary-pushing Singaporean food, is closing.The restaurant's last day at 380 17th St. in Oakland will be April 27, owners C-Y Chia and Shane Stanbridge announced on Instagram Thursday, calling it "the hardest decision we've ever had to make." Though the business is successful, they said, they're closing for personal reasons, to relocate to be closer to Chia's family, most of whom live in Singapore.
