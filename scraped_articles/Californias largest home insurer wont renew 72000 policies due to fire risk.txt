California’s largest home insurer won’t renew 72,000 policies due to fire risk
-----
State Farm plans to non-renew approximately 30,000 property insurance policies and 42,000 commercial apartment policies within the state of California over fire risk, the company has announced.The insurer stopped writing new homeowners policies in the state last year and subsequently raised rates by an average of 20% for remaining customers.
