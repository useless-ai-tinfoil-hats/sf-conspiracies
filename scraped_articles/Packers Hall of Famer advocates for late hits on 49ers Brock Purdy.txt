Packers Hall of Famer advocates for late hits on 49ers' Brock Purdy
-----
A Packers Hall of Famer advocated in a radio hit Wednesday for Green Bay players to hit 49ers quarterback Brock Purdy after the whistle, ostensibly to try and injure Purdy and knock him out of Saturday's 49ers-Packers playoff game.Former Green Bay tight end Mark Chmura told his "Jen, Gabe, and Chewy" morning show co-hosts that they are "going to think I'm crazy" before he called on Packers defenders to intentionally commit penalties to send a message to Purdy.
