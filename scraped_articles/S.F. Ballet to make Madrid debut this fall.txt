S.F. Ballet to make Madrid debut this fall
-----
The San Francisco Ballet is bringing "Swan Lake" to Madrid for its first international tour under Artistic Director Tamara Rojo this fall.The limited engagement, set to run Oct. 15-22 at Teatro Real, will double as a homecoming for Rojo, who grew up in Madrid.
