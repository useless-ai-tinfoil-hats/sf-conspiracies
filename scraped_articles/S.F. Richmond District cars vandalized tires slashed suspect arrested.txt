S.F. Richmond District cars vandalized, tires slashed, suspect arrested
-----
A San Francisco man has been arrested in connection with the vandalism of more than 20 cars in the Richmond District, police said Wednesday.Osiel Torres, 22, was arrested Tuesday night and booked into San Francisco County jail on 22 counts of felony vandalism, the San Francisco Police Department said.
