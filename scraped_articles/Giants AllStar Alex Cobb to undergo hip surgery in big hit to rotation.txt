Giants’ All-Star Alex Cobb to undergo hip surgery in big hit to rotation
-----
Alex Cobb, an All-Star this year for the first time, was to undergo hip surgery Tuesday and is expected to miss the start of the 2024 season, which underscores the San Francisco Giants' need for more top-of-the-rotation pitchers.Cobb, 36, finished this season on the injured list and was hoping to rehab his left hip — and try multiple treatment therapies — without needing an operation, but a decision was made to address his labrum and impingement.
