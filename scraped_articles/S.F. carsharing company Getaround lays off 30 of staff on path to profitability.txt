S.F. car-sharing company Getaround lays off 30% of staff on ‘path to profitability’
-----
Getaround, the San Francisco car rental service, is set to implement a restructuring plan that includes laying off approximately 30% of its North American workforce to accelerate "its path to profitability."The company announced the cuts Wednesday as part of an overall cost reduction program expected to yield annual savings of around $7 million.
