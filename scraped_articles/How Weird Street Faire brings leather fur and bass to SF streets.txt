How Weird Street Faire brings leather, fur and bass to SF streets
-----
It's 5 p.m. on a Saturday and there are no cars on 2nd Street in San Francisco. But the road isn't exactly empty, either.A stage is planted squarely over the street's double yellow lines, framed by stacks of speakers and subwoofers. The surrounding crowd is peppered with colored wigs, bug-eyed glasses, roller blades and cat ears.
