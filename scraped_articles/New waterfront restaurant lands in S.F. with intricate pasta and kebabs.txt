New waterfront restaurant lands in S.F. with intricate pasta and kebabs
-----
When the owners of hit Indian restaurant Rooh landed a prime lease on San Francisco's waterfront, they locked in one of the city's specialists in Spanish cooking. Then, they all began talking about kebabs.The result is Alora, a broadly Mediterranean restaurant that opens on the Embarcadero on Thursday, Jan. 25. There, chef Ryan McIlwraith, best known for his tenure at S.F. Spanish restaurants Bellota and nearby Coqueta, prepares dishes like broiled Kusshi oysters with lemon-Prosecco
