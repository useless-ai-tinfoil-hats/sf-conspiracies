In-N-Out signage disappears from shuttered Oakland restaurant
-----
It's the end of an era for Oakland and fast food lovers everywhere: The In-N-Out on Oakport Street has closed, and the ketchup red and mustard yellow signage adorning the restaurant have disappeared along with it.Photos shared on Reddit over the weekend showed the stark white exterior of the once-bustling drive-thru, which served long lines of customers seeking out double-doubles and animal-style fries since 2005. The awnings dotted with its iconic palm tree motif have also been stripped
