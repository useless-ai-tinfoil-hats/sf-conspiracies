How we reported on dozens of sexual abuse cases against Bay Area public schools
-----
Child abuse and the sexualization of children in schools has become a hot-button issue in the U.S., but many of the conversations on the topic take place in social media posts that lack detail, context and reliable information.A recent state law that allowed for a wave of lawsuits against child-serving institutions provided a unique trove of public documents that describe the way abuse in schools can actually look.
