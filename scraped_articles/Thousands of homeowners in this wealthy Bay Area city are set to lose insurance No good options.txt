Thousands of homeowners in this wealthy Bay Area city are set to lose insurance: ‘No good options’
-----
The talk of the town in Orinda — the small, affluent city nestled on the eastern side of the wooded Berkeley hills in Contra Costa County — is not about a recent spate of home burglaries or the downtown movie theater going dark two nights a week to save on electricity.Instead, the buzz is all about the California home insurance crisis — a far drier subject, but a particularly dire one in Orinda.
