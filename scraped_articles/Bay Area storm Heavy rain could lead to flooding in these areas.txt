Bay Area storm: Heavy rain could lead to flooding in these areas
-----
Rain showers continued throughout the Bay Area on Tuesday, but flooding rains could return Tuesday night into Wednesday. The second storm of the week is moving toward California, and weather models predict it will pack a punch.A band of heavy rainfall is expected in Marin County, San Francisco and along the Peninsula on Wednesday morning, dropping up to 2 inches of rain in a narrow swath.
