The NorCal riverside campground where you don't need a reservation
-----
I'm straddled over a large tree trunk suspended over the Van Duzen River, inching slowly and carefully toward the middle, where the water seems deep enough for a jump.It's late in the summer, so the river is running pretty low. And frankly, most people who venture out on this log probably weigh less than 50 pounds. That's because we're at Swimmer's Delight, a riverside campground about 260 miles north of San Francisco that mostly attracts local families.
