In-N-Out to open new East Bay restaurant after Oakland closure
-----
In-N-Out is opening a new East Bay restaurant after it closed its only Oakland location.The burger chain will open at 709 Harder Road in Hayward, according to a job listing on its website. KRON-TV reported that the company did not yet have an opening date.
In March, the company closed its only Oakland location, citing customers who had been "victimized by car break-ins, property damage, theft and armed robberies."
