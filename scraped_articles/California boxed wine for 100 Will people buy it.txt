California boxed wine for $100: Will people buy it?
-----
Would you pay $100 for boxed wine?It sounds like a bold proposition, but that's the going rate for some of the higher-end boxed wines available today. During the last few years, new brands have rushed onto the scene, including from acclaimed California wineries. They aim to capture a discerning wine consumer who may be persuaded to buy bag-in-box because of its lower carbon footprint compared with glass bottles.
