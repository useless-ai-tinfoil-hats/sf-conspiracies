The 1975’s Matty Healy announces ‘indefinite hiatus’ at Sacramento tour opener
-----
Matty Healy of the 1975 had some bad news for fans at the band's tour opener in Sacramento.The singer and guitarist told the audience at the Golden 1 Center on Tuesday, Sept. 26, that the British hitmakers are going on an "indefinite hiatus" following their "Still … at Their Very Best" tour in support of the 2022 album, "Being Funny in a Foreign Language."
"We love coming to this place and playing for you guys whenever we have the chance, and it's wonderful you're all here," Healy
