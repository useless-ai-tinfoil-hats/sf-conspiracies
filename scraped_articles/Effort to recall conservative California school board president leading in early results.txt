Effort to recall conservative California school board president leading in early results
-----
An attempt to recall the conservative president of a Riverside County school board was passing in early results Wednesday, following a campaign in which proponents argued the district under his leadership has put in place deeply problematic policies, largely targeting the LGBTQ community.Joseph Komrosky, who was elected to the Temecula Valley Unified School District Board of Trustees in November 2022, faced a recall after shepherding a series of conservative policies that stunned some
