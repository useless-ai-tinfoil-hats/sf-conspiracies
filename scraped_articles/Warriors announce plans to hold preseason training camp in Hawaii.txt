Warriors announce plans to hold preseason training camp in Hawaii
-----
The Golden State Warriors are taking their annual preseason training camp to Hawaii this fall.From Oct. 1 through Oct. 4, the Warriors will practice at Brigham Young University-Hawaii on the north shore of Oahu, a team spokesperson said Monday. Their media day is Sept. 30, after which they'll fly to Hawaii's most populated island.
Golden State conducted four training camps at BYU-Hawaii, the last in 2007.
