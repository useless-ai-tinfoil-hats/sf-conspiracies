San Francisco worker dead after work site collapses
-----
A utility worker in San Francisco died Thursday after being trapped under 8 feet of dirt and concrete when a trench collapsed at a work site on the 1100 block of Oak Street.A two-hour rescue attempt involving around 50 firefighters trying to reach and save the victim failed, the San Francisco Fire Department said.
