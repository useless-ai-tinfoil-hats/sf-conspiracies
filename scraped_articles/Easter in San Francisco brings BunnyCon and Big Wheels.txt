Easter in San Francisco brings BunnyCon and Big Wheels
-----
BunnyCon, an event that invites participants to don bunny suits and hop from bar to bar much like winter's annual SantaCon, is attempting to make its return to San Francisco.While BunnyCon has been a well-established Easter tradition in Seattle, previous efforts to replicate the event in the Bay Area have not been well attended. This year's BunnyCon, advertised as the first ever set for noon on Saturday, March 30, may face similar challenges.
