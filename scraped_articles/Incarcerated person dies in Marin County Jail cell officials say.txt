Incarcerated person dies in Marin County Jail cell, officials say
-----
An incarcerated person died at the Marin County Jail early Thursday morning, marking the third in-custody death at the jail in six months, officials said.During a routine check around 5:45 a.m., a sheriff's deputy found someone in need of medical attention alone in their single-occupancy cell at the facility in San Rafael, according to a news release from the Marin County Sheriff's Office.
