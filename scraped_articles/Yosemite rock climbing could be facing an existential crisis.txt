Yosemite rock climbing could be facing an ‘existential crisis’
-----
Could big-wall rock climbing in places like Yosemite Valley be regulated out of existence?That's a question swirling around the climbing community this week in the wake of new proposals by federal land agencies to tighten regulations on outdoor pursuits like rock climbing, ice climbing, mountaineering, canyoneering and caving, all of which typically depend on protection from fixed anchors.
