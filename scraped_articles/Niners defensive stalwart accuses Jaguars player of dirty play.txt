Niners defensive stalwart accuses Jaguars player of 'dirty play'
-----
Late in the first quarter of the 49ers' 34-3 dismantling of the Jaguars on Sunday, defensive lineman Arik Armstead was slow to get up because of "a dirty play."Armstead told reporters after the game that while trying to rush the quarterback, Brandon Scherff, Jacksonville's right guard, used a pretty illegal move to try and stop the Niner.
"He tripped me, kicked me in my knee and kind of messed up my day," Armstead said of the incident.
