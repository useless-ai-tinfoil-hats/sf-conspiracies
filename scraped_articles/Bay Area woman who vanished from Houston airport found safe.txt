Bay Area woman who vanished from Houston airport found safe
-----
A Piedmont woman who went missing in Houston after failing to board a flight back to California was found safe, her family said in a statement Wednesday.Danielle Friedland, 36, was located safely around 2:30 a.m. Wednesday in Houston with the help of the Piedmont Police Department, her family said. Spokespersons did not say where she was found.
