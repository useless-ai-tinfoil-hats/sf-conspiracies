S.F. spent nearly $2 billion on the Central Subway. Did it really help Chinatown?
-----
Shaobing Yi, 82, and her husband, Youwai Chen, 84, take the T-Third Street line into Chinatown after one transfer from their Ingleside home five days a week to buy affordable Chinese groceries and to socialize. While she shops for freshly made wontons, her husband plays pingpong in Chinatown with his friends."It is very important for the elderly to have the subway," Chen said in Mandarin.
