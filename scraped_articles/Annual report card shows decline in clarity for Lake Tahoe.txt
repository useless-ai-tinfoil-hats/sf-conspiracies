Annual report card shows decline in clarity for Lake Tahoe
-----
Lake Tahoe is among the clearest, bluest lakes in the world, but a report published Monday by UC Davis shows that its water clarity declined last year.In 2023, Lake Tahoe's overall clarity declined by 3.7 feet, year over year; however, last year's clarity was the 10th lowest on record, in more than five decades of monitoring.
