The Hawaii condominium that keeps breaking Honolulu sales records
-----

This makes it the most expensive residential condominium to be sold in Honolulu ever.Fronting Ala Moana Beach Park, the 5,668-square-foot penthouse features four bedrooms, 4.5 bathrooms and a 1,188-square-foot lanai (outdoor space).
