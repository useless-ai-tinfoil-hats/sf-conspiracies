Members-only club sues Transamerica Pyramid owner for $600 million, seeks to exit lease
-----
A brewing dispute between Transamerica Pyramid owner Michael Shvo and a private social club set to open in the iconic San Francisco office tower has been elevated to a New York court.In a lawsuit filed in New York on Wednesday, New York-based CORE Club — an exclusive members-only business with fees up to $100,000 — accused Shvo of "self-dealings" and fraud related to the San Francisco project and other planned CORE Club locations that he is developing.
