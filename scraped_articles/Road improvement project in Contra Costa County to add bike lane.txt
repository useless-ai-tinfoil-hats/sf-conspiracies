Road improvement project in Contra Costa County to add bike lane
-----
A shoulder widening project will close a section of one road in Brentwood from Monday until late July.The project includes shoulder widening along an 860-foot segment of Walnut Boulevard between Marsh Creek and Vasco roads to add bike lanes, pavement and signage improvements, a news release from the Contra Costa County Public Works Department said.
Crews will work weekdays from 7 a.m. to 5 p.m., except for holidays and as weather allows, the release said.
