Renderings give first look at European-style city planned for Bay Area
-----
The first renderings have emerged for a built-from-scratch city being planned for a rural part of the Bay Area.Since 2018, a shadowy company called Flannery Associates has been purchasing plots of primarily agricultural land between Vacaville and Rio Vista. Their 52,000 acres, which cost the company nearly $1 billion, make them the single biggest landowner in Solano County.
