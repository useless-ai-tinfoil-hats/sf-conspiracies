Warriors’ Brandin Podziemski dismayed by his Rising Stars team’s low effort
-----
INDIANAPOLIS — He played Wednesday against the Los Angeles Clippers and Thursday against the Utah Jazz, but Brandin Podziemski wasn't too tired to treat the NBA's Rising Stars Challenge like a regular-season game.The Golden State Warriors' rookie guard sprinted up and down the Gainbridge Fieldhouse court Friday night, diving on it a couple of times as if the game were on the line.
His efforts were to no avail in 41-36 loss for Podziemski and Team Pau.
