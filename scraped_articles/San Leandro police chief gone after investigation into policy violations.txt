San Leandro police chief gone after investigation into policy violations
-----
San Leandro Police Chief Abdul Pridgen left his position last week after an investigation into allegations that he violated department policies, according to the city.Pridgen's last day was Feb. 20.
The city selected Los Altos Police Chief Angela Averiett to step in as interim chief in late April. Current interim Chief Kevin Hart will serve until Averiett takes over, the city said.
