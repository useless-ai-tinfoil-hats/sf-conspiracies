It took nearly two weeks, but Giants get first stolen bases of the season
-----
Finally, the San Francisco Giants stole a base. It took 13 games, but Tyler Fitzgerald finally legged out the Giants' first steal of the season Wednesday. And their second steal, too, in their 7-1 win over Washington at Oracle Park. Matt Chapman also stole a base.Before Wednesday, the Giants weren't exactly taking advantage of MLB's rules put into effect last season to benefit base stealers.
