'People went nuts': Largest In-N-Out Facebook group bans new posts about Oakland closure
-----
On Jan. 21, In-N-Out announced it would be closing its only Oakland outpost at the end of March. Chief operating officer Denny Warnick confirmed the news to SFGATE and put out a statement citing an "unsafe environment" as the reason for the closure.Of course, everyone on the internet had an opinion on this announced closure. It even made an appearance at a California Senate race debate that included Rep. Barbara Lee, congresswoman for California's 12th congressional district.
