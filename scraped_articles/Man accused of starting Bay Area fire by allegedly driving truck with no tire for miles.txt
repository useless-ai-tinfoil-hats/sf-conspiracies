Man accused of starting Bay Area fire by allegedly driving truck with no tire for miles
-----
A Shasta County man was arrested in connection with the 36-acre Flora Fire in the North Bay, which Cal Fire officials said he started last Wednesday after driving his truck without a front right tire for over four miles.The vehicle "grinded on the asphalt," releasing sparks that caught on dry grass and ignited three separate blazes that spread on both sides of Chalk Hill Road in Geyserville, threatening multiple structures, the agency said.
