Body of teenager who went missing in Kaweah River found by volunteer diver
-----
A volunteer diver found the body of a 17-year-old girl who went missing on the Kaweah River earlier this week.The girl fell into the river on Wednesday and was later identified by her family as Bree Scott of Tulare. Scott, who was an honor roll student, cheer captain and ASB president at Tulare Western High School, had been sitting on a rock with a friend when she fell into the river, KFSN-TV Action News reported.
