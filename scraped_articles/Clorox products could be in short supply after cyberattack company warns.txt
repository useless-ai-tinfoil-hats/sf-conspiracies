Clorox products could be in short supply after cyberattack, company warns
-----
Clorox, the Oakland-based maker of bleach and other household products, warned consumers and investors this week of product shortages that might impact shoppers and crimp earnings in the wake of a cybersecurity attack last month.In an online statement and a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission on Monday, the Clorox Co. said that unauthorized activity in its information technology infrastructure had forced the company to take systems offline and process product orders
