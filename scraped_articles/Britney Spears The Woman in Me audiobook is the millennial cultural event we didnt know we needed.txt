Britney Spears’ ‘The Woman in Me’ audiobook is the millennial cultural event we didn’t know we needed
-----
The audiobook of Britney Spears' memoir "The Woman in Me," narrated by five-time Academy Award nominee Michelle Williams, feels like the millennial cultural event my generation didn't know we needed.Williams is terrific reading the words of Spears and ghostwriter Sam Lansky. And as anyone who's followed the 13 years of the pop star's conservatorship and resulting #FreeBritney movement knows, the material is juicy.
