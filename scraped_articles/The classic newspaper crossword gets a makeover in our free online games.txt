The classic newspaper crossword gets a makeover in our free online games
-----
Hearst Newspapers acquired gaming platform Puzzmo last year, and it's a place for thoughtful puzzles that's reinventing the classic newspaper games page. A brainchild of award-winning game developers Zach Gage and Orta Therox, Puzzmo opened a wait list in October 2023, and in just over a month, 100,000 puzzlers made more than a million attempts to gain access.You can find all our puzzles on sfgate.com/puzzles.
