Missing rafter's body found by divers after disappearing in Northern California river
-----
Officials found the body of a man in his 30s who went missing while rafting with a large group on the American River near Clay Banks in Rancho Cordova over the weekend.Firefighters with the Sacramento Metropolitan Fire District and several other agencies were called to the scene around 11:30 a.m. on Saturday morning to search for the man. The large party had been floating toward Rossmoor Bar when he vanished.
