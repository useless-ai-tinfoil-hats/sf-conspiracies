SF police shoplifting crackdown leads to 17 arrests in one day at single store
-----
As part of a determined effort to curb retail theft, the San Francisco Police Department arrested 17 adults on suspicion of shoplifting in a one-day operation, the agency said Thursday afternoon. On Wednesday, the department announced the arrest of a 14-year-old girl accused of stealing more than $30,000 in goods from local stores.The department said the latest bust is part of special operation "Blitz '23," which is aimed at cracking down on organized retail crime.
