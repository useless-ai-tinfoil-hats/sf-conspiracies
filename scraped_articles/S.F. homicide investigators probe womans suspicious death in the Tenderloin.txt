S.F. homicide investigators probe woman’s ‘suspicious death’ in the Tenderloin
-----
San Francisco police said Wednesday that they were investigating what they described as a woman's "suspicious death" in the Tenderloin.Officers were summoned to a residence on the 200 block of Taylor Street just before noon on Sunday "regarding an unconscious adult female," police said in a statement.
Paramedics declared the woman dead at the scene, and medical examiners ruled the death to be suspicious.
