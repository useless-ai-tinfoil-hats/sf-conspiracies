Helicopter rescue crew finds person dead in boat crash on Marin County coast, officials say
-----
One person was found dead on a boat that crashed on the rocky Marin County shoreline Thursday, officials said.A rescue swimmer from the U.S. Coast Guard was first deployed to the boat, which had run into Chimney Rock on the Point Reyes National Seashore, according to the Sonoma County Sheriff's office.
The damaged vessel was listing to the side on rocks, battered by incoming waves and taking on water, sheriff's officials said on social media.
