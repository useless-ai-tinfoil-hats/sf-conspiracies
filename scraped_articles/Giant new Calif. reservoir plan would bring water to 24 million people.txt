Giant new Calif. reservoir plan would bring water to 24 million people
-----
California's reservoirs are not only vital to the state's complex water systems, providing millions of people and the state's agricultural economy with needed access to water; they're also important gauges for how healthy the state is overall. This year's at-capacity reservoirs have been a boon for a region besieged by drought over much of the past decade, but more work is needed to help ensure a plentiful and water-wise future for the most populous state in America.Enter Sites
