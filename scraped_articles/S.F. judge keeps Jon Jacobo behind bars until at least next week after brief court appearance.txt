S.F. judge keeps Jon Jacobo behind bars until at least next week after brief court appearance
-----
UPDATE: S.F. judge releases Jon Jacobo from custody ahead of rape trialJon Jacobo will remain behind bars until at least Aug. 13, the day a San Francisco Superior Court judge scheduled bail arguments after a brief hearing on Friday.
The former nonprofit leader, who stands accused of sexual assault, has a preliminary hearing scheduled to begin Aug. 14. At the end of that hearing, a judge will decide if there is sufficient evidence to proceed to a trial.
