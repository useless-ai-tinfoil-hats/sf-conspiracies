$1.9 billion airline merger: Alaska acquiring Hawaiian
-----
Alaska Airlines announced Sunday that it plans to acquire Hawaiian Airlines in a $1.9 billion deal, including the latter's debt.The merger will allow both airlines to maintain their brands but with a single operating platform, Alaska Airlines said in a news release Sunday. The combined company will provide service to 138 locations, including nonstop flights to airports in Asia and Australia, as well as in the Americas and the South Pacific.
