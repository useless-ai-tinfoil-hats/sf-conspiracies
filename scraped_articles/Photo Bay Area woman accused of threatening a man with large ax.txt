Photo: Bay Area woman accused of threatening a man with large ax
-----
A woman was arrested on suspicion of threatening to attack a man with an ax in downtown Vallejo last week, authorities said.The woman was allegedly arguing with a man while holding an ax on the 400 block of Marin Street just before 4:30 p.m. Saturday, the Vallejo Police Department said.
It was unclear what they were arguing about, but the woman and man did not know each other, said Vallejo police spokesman Sgt. Rashad Hollis. The man's wife called 911 to report the woman.
