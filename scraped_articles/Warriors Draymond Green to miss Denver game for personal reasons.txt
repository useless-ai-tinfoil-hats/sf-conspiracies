Warriors’ Draymond Green to miss Denver game for ‘personal reasons’
-----
DENVER — Golden State Warriors forward Draymond Green missed Wednesday's game against the Nuggets for personal reasons and guard Gary Payton II was also ruled out because of illness.The absences of Green and Payton against the reigning NBA champs created a sizeable challenge for head coach Steve Kerr, who will need to find ways to stop two-time MVP Nikola Jokić without arguably his two best defenders.
