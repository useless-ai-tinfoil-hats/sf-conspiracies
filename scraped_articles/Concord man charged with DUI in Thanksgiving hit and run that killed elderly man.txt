Concord man charged with DUI in Thanksgiving hit and run that killed elderly man
-----
East Bay officials allege a Concord man was intoxicated and didn't have a license when he crashed into two vehicles on Thanksgiving night, killing an 86-year-old man.Just before 7 p.m. on Thanksgiving, Concord police responded to two hit and runs within half a block of each other. The day after the crash, police arrested Damian Lopez-Avelino, 19, of Concord.
