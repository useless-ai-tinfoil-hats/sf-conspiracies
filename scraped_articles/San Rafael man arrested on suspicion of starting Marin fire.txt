San Rafael man arrested on suspicion of starting Marin fire
-----
A San Rafael man has been arrested on suspicion of igniting a vegetation fire near a Marin County neighborhood, authorities said Tuesday.Colin Crook, 34, was taken into custody near the Safeway on Nave Drive in Novato after a brief struggle with police, according to the Marin County Sheriff's Office.
