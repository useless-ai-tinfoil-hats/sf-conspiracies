Famed S.F. Vietnamese restaurant closes its last location
-----
San Francisco's final location of Vietnamese institution Turtle Tower has permanently closed.The restaurant at 501 Sixth St., in the SoMa neighborhood, shut down on Nov. 19, owner Steven Nghia Pham confirmed.
This follows the closure of the original Turtle Tower on Larkin Street in September. Since 2000, the restaurant had been known for northern-style pho, particularly pho ga, or chicken noodle soup, and affordable prices.
