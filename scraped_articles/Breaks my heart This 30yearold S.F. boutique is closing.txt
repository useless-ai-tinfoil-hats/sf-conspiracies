‘Breaks my heart’: This 30-year-old S.F. boutique is closing
-----
A beloved boutique selling handcrafted goods and vintage furniture in the Mission District will close at the end of February.Owner Yaeko Yamashita, 70, has been running Laku for over 30 years. She spent the past 23 in her current location at 1089 Valencia St., according to Andrea Reed, a longtime customer and friend of Yamashita. But a recent change in building ownership is now pushing Laku out, Reed claimed.
