Finally, wines from this underdog California region are coming to Napa
-----
Yet another tasting room is opening in downtown Napa, but this one offers something the dozens of others don't: wines from one of California's most underrated regions.In July, Santa Barbara County's Fess Parker Family will open in the coveted First Street space formerly occupied by the trendy Brendel Wines, which closed suddenly in February.
