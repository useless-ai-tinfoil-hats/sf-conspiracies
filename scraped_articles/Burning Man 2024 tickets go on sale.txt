Burning Man 2024 tickets go on sale
-----
For a weeklong jaunt in an anarchic, commerce-free society, Burning Man attendees will gladly endure sweltering heat, eight-hour traffic jams and even an endless expanse of mud. But to even reach Black Rock Desert, they must overcome another hurdle, one that requires an element of chance, not grit, to overcome: ticket sales.Burning Man's main ticket sale kicks off at noon on Wednesday, April 17.
