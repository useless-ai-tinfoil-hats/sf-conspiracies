Warriors' Draymond Green's return to Cleveland had an ironic twist
-----
Warriors veteran Draymond Green was once again involved in a below-the-belt incident while playing in Cleveland against the Cavaliers on Sunday.However, unlike in the 2016 NBA Finals, Green was the victim this time around in the 114-105 loss. Early in the first quarter, Cavs center Jarrett Allen kicked the Dubs forward in the groin while trying to go after a rebound in the air, although Allen didn't exactly look like he was going for the ball all that much.
