S.F. crews repair large water main break near Stern Grove
-----
Crews from the San Francisco Public Utilities Commission repaired a large water main break on Wawona Street near Stern Grove on Friday, officials said.When the water main ruptured midmorning Friday, the San Francisco Fire Department assessed risks to structures and residents and encouraged people to stay away from the area, while SFPUC crews worked to repair the main break, impacting traffic in the area.
