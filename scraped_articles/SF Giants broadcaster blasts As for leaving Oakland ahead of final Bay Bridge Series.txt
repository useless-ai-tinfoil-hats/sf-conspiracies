SF Giants broadcaster blasts A's for leaving Oakland ahead of final Bay Bridge Series
-----
San Francisco Giants broadcaster Mike Krukow was one of two ball club legends that took to the airwaves on Friday to mourn the loss of the Bay Bridge Series against the Oakland A's.This weekend's two-game series will be the last proper cross-bay duel between these Bay Area teams as A's owner John Fisher convinced MLB and its owners that he should move the team to Las Vegas.
