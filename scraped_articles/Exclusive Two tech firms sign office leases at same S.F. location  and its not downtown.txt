Exclusive: Two tech firms sign office leases at same S.F. location — and it’s not downtown
-----
Snapchat parent company Snap Inc. and Supercell, the mobile game maker known for giving the world "Clash of Clans" and "Clash Royale," have signed new office leases within a month of each other, at the same location — and it's not downtown.While both declined to comment on the leases on Monday, landlord Jamestown Properties confirmed that it has closed deals to bring the tech companies to Levi's Plaza, a nine-building, 900,000-square-foot campus that it owns at the Northern Waterfront.
