Three WCAL girls volleyball teams playing for state championships
-----
Three West Catholic Athletic League girls volleyball teams — including fourth-place Sacred Heart Cathedral and fifth-place Valley Christian — have reached the state championships.Co-champion Mitty (33-4) is the third WCAL team to qualify for the state's top prize at Santiago Canyon College in Orange after beating fellow co-WCAL champion St. Francis 25-12, 25-21, 19-25, 25-10 in the Northern California Open Division championship Tuesday night.
