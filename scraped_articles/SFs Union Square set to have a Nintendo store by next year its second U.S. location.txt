SF's Union Square set to have a Nintendo store by next year, its second U.S. location
-----
Get ready, gamers: Mario is on the move. Nintendo, which only has one U.S. retail location, in New York, is set to open another store in San Francisco in 2025, the company announced Friday.While the company hasn't released details of the new store, it will allow customers to "experience the world of Nintendo, its products, and characters," Nintendo said in a news release.
San Francisco Mayor London Breed responded to Nintendo's announcement on social media Friday.
