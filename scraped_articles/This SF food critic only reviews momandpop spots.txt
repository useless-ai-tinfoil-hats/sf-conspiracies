This SF food critic only reviews mom-and-pop spots
-----
Oscar Cuadra, the man behind The Snack Sensei on Instagram, doesn't consider himself a food critic. Instead, he thinks of his alter ego as the home of "The People's Palate," because he generally rates everyday meals from mom-and-pop businesses, street food and longstanding business (with the occasional trendy dish thrown in).In the four years since launching The Snack Sensei, he has visited hundreds of restaurants, many of which have become personal favorites.
