Burglars steal thousands of dollars in merchandise from stores in Oakland's Temescal Alley
-----
Several small businesses in Temescal Alley, a popular cluster of shops in North Oakland's Temescal neighborhood, were burglarized early Friday morning, according to one store owner who lost tens of thousands of dollars worth of merchandise in a break-in.At about 1 a.m., two men jumped over the gate that blocks the alley off from the main street after hours, "scoped the alley out" and returned a couple of hours later with a truck, said Liz Thayer, owner of custom leather goods store
