‘You still feel this unity’: San Francisco celebrates the legacy of MLK during annual march
-----
A vibrant throng of longtime San Francisco residents, civic leaders, parents with young children and countless others marched downtown Monday to celebrate the legacy of Martin Luther King Jr. on the federal holiday bearing his name.The city's latest of its annual marches to honor King drew a diverse crowd, many of whom held signs bearing King's likeness or featuring one of his famous quotes.
