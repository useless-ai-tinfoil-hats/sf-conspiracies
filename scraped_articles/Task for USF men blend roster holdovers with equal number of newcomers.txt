Task for USF men: blend roster holdovers with equal number of newcomers
-----
Not counting the pandemic-altered season of 2020-21, USF has recorded at least 20 wins in each of the past six seasons. Whether the Dons can extend that streak to seven will revolve — at least in part — around how quickly and smoothly seven newcomers mesh with the program's seven holdovers."Having seven new guys, we need some more time together to grow, to get know each other and to adjust to the system," USF head coach Chris Gerlufsen said in an interview in his office on the Hilltop.
