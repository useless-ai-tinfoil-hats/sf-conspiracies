Bobbie Stauffacher Solomon, graphic artist credited with bringing Supergraphics to the Bay Area, dies at 95
-----
As a widowed mother in her early 20s, Bobbie Stauffacher needed a steadier living than being a flamenco dancer in North Beach. Complete reinvention was in order, and that's what took her from her native San Francisco to Basel, Switzerland, where she became a graphic designer.She brought back a style called Supergraphics, which melded Swiss modernism with American pop art.
