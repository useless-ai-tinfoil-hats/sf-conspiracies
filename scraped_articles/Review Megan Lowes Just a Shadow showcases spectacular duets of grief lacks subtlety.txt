Review: Megan Lowe’s ‘Just a Shadow’ showcases spectacular duets of grief, lacks subtlety
-----
Megan Lowe is without doubt one of San Francisco's most fascinating dancers, a natural mover with an apparently insatiable need to test the laws of physics, whether rebounding off walls or balancing on the bodies of brave partners. Often she makes it all look like child's play.But the penultimate section of her new full-evening "Just a Shadow" is indeed a darker spectacle, both hypnotizing and human.
