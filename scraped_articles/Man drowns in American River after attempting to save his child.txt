Man drowns in American River after attempting to save his child
-----
A man drowned Sunday morning in the American River near the historic "No Hands Bridge" after trying to save his child, according to an X post from the Placer County Sheriff's Office.After seeing his child struggle in the water, the father went after his child, the post said, citing accounts from family members. Another man not affiliated with the family also went in and was able to bring the child to shore. The child was taken to a hospital and is expected to recover.
