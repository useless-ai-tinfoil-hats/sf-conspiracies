Humboldt County judge resigns after admitting to unwanted touching, improper conduct
-----
The state's judicial disciplinary agency says a Humboldt County judge has resigned after admitting numerous wrongful acts, including failure to disclose friendships with parties in his court, and grabbing or slapping a husband and wife on the rear end at a social gathering.Superior Court Judge Gregory Kreis presided over at least 44 cases in which he did not reveal his social or working relationships with the lawyers or their clients, the Commission on Judicial Performance said Tuesday.
