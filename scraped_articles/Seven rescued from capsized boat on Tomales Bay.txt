Seven rescued from capsized boat on Tomales Bay
-----
A pleasure craft capsized in rough and stormy waters on Tomales Bay on Saturday afternoon, causing seven people to require rescue after being thrown into the icy cold.According to a Marin County Fire Department post on Facebook, the incident happened at around 3 p.m. when a call came in that six adults and one child were in the water clinging to a capsized boat.
