Missing California woman found a year later in hospital morgue
-----
The family of a 31-year-old California woman who died at a Sacramento-area hospital believed for a year that she was alive and missing because the hospital failed to notify it of the death, according to a lawsuit filed in Sacramento Superior Court.Jessie Peterson was admitted to Mercy San Juan Medical Center in Carmichael on April 6, 2023, after suffering a diabetic episode.
