Calif. agency warns some legal cannabis products could be 'adulterated'
-----
California's cannabis regulator has issued an embargo on several cannabis products, claiming that three batches of legal weed products may have been "adulterated," according to a Nov. 9 warning letter obtained by SFGATE and sent to companies whose products are affected.A spokesperson for the California Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) confirmed the embargo to SFGATE, saying the products are suspected of being dangerous to consume.
