Memoir ‘Straitjackets and Lunch Money’ unearths writer’s haunting memories of childhood hospitalization
-----
In the opening pages of journalist Katya Cengel's incredibly affecting new memoir, "Straitjackets and Lunch Money," she recounts volunteering with a writing program at an East Bay juvenile detention facility in 2012 while living in Richmond.
