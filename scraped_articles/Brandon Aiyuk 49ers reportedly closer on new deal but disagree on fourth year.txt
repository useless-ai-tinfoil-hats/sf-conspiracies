Brandon Aiyuk, 49ers reportedly closer on new deal but disagree on fourth year
-----
Brandon Aiyuk is the San Francisco 49ers' top wide receiver, but he never has been among the star-studded team's most-discussed players.Until this offseason. Aiyuk, of course, has inspired endless stories over the past six months due to his contract impasse with the 49ers, with Aiyuk inspiring some of that content with his often-cryptic social-media messages.
Aiyuk has acknowledged during the saga that he has enjoyed the spotlight.
"I'm having fun," Aiyuk said on "The Pivot"
