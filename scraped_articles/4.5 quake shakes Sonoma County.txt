4.5 quake shakes Sonoma County
-----
A 4.5 magnitude earthquake was detected at 12:34 p.m. Saturday about 4.4 miles northwest of the Geysers in Sonoma County, according to the U.S. Geological Survey. No major damage was reported.The Geysers, which have the largest complex of geothermal power plants in the world, supply energy to Sonoma, Marin, Napa, Mendocino and Lake counties, according to the USGS.
Though Northern California is seismically active, the withdrawal of steam to produce power can also induce small quakes
