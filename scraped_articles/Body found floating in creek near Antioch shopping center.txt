Body found floating in creek near Antioch shopping center
-----
A body was found floating in a creek near a shopping center in Antioch on Sunday morning, police said.The body in the creek along Slatten Ranch Road near Lone Tree Way was reported to police around 11:15 a.m.
The cause of the person's death was unknown, with an autopsy pending, police said. The person's name was withheld, pending family notifications. No further identifying information about the person was released.
