Highly anticipated new SF grocery store is finally opening next week
-----
The highly anticipated new Bi-Rite Market on San Francisco's Polk Street finally has an opening date: Aug. 29, according to a news release. Customers have been hungrily anticipating the 2140 Polk St. location, the local grocer's third in the city, since it was first announced in October 2022.At 4,000 square feet, the new grocery store will also be Bi-Rite's largest yet, complete with a kitchen, sandwich counter, baked goods section and fish and meat cases, reported the San Francisco
