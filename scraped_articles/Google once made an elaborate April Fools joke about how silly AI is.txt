Google once made an elaborate April Fools' joke about how silly AI is
-----
In 2009, Barack Obama was president, Steph Curry was in college and at Google, artificial intelligence was just an incredibly elaborate April Fool's joke.How things change.
Now, AI is fueling the tech hype cycle, and Google is shelling out tens of billions of dollars to develop it. The company is making stiff-talking chatbots, predictive health care models and code that writes itself. Engineers who build the tech are paid handsomely, and Wall Street has rewarded the company's AI
