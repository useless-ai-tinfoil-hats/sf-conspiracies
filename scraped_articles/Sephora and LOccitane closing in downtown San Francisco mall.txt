Sephora and L'Occitane closing in downtown San Francisco mall
-----
The former Westfield San Francisco Centre may have a new name, but that isn't stopping it from losing stores.Popular cosmetics purveyors L'Occitane and Sephora will no longer operate in the mall, as first reported by the San Francisco Business Times. L'Occitane, a French skin care brand, has already shuttered its first-floor outpost.
