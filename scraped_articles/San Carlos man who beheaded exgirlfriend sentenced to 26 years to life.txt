San Carlos man who beheaded ex-girlfriend sentenced to 26 years to life
-----
A San Mateo County man was sentenced to serve 26 years to life for the grisly murder of his ex-partner in 2022, the San Mateo County District Attorney's Office said Wednesday.Jose Rafael Solano Landaeta, 34, was found guilty of first-degree murder and an additional allegation of using a samurai sword for an incident where he beheaded his ex-partner outside the apartment complex where she lived in San Carlos.
