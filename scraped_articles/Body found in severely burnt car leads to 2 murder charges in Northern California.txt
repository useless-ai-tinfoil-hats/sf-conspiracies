Body found in 'severely burnt' car leads to 2 murder charges in Northern California
-----
Two men were charged with murder in connection with human remains that were discovered inside a "severely burnt" vehicle on private property near the Covelo dump in Northern California on Halloween morning, authorities said.Lawrence Allen Joaquin, 35, and Iran Lee Hoaglen, 42, both of Covelo, were booked into the Mendocino County Jail without bail this week, per a press release from the Mendocino County Sheriff's Office.
