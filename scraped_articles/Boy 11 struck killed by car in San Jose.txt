Boy, 11, struck, killed by car in San Jose
-----
A boy was struck and killed by a car Tuesday afternoon in San Jose, police said.The collision occurred around 4:21 p.m. at the intersection of Leigh Avenue and Camden Avenue. Police said a preliminary investigation found that a 2006 Chevy pickup truck was driving westbound on Camden Avenue with a green light when the car struck the boy, who was riding a scooter northbound in a marked crosswalk at the intersection. The boy, 11, was pronounced dead at the scene, police said.
