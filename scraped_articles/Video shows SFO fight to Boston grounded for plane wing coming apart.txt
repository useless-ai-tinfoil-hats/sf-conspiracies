Video shows SFO fight to Boston grounded for plane wing 'coming apart'
-----
A United flight from San Francisco to Boston was forced to divert to Denver after its crew discovered damage to one of the plane wing's flaps.On Monday, United Flight 354 left San Francisco International Airport around 1:45 p.m., according to FlightAware, and was scheduled to land around 10 p.m. Eastern time in Boston. However, the plane diverted to Denver because of "an issue with the slat on the wing of the aircraft," a spokesperson for United Airlines told SFGATE in a statement.
