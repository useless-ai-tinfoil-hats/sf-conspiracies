Cal men within one of last season's win total; Cal women stay unbeaten
-----
All five starters scored in double figures and the Cal men, in their third game, moved within one of matching last season's win total with an 83-63 defeat of Cal State Bakersfield at Haas Pavilion on Monday night.Jalen Cone's seven 3-pointers accounted for all of his made field goals as he scored a game-high 23 for the Bears. Fardaws Aimaq added 14, Jaylon Tyson collected 13 points and game highs in rebounds (12) and assists (seven), Devin Askew chipped in 12, and Grant Newell 10.
