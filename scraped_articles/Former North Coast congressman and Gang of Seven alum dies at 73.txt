Former North Coast congressman and ‘Gang of Seven’ alum dies at 73
-----
Frank Riggs, a three-term Republican congressman who represented Northern California in the 1990s from the Oregon border to Mare Island in Solano County, has died. The cause involved complications following heart valve surgery, said former wife Cathy Riggs.
