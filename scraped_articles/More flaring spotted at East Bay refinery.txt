More flaring spotted at East Bay refinery
-----
Flaring occurred Wednesday evening at the Martinez Refinery Co.'s East Bay facility due to an "operational incident," officials said.The flaring began around 4:30 p.m., according to a statement posted on social media by the refinery, and was visible offsite. The refinery said it had notified the appropriate agencies and issued a community notification.
