Runway at Sonoma County airport reopens after sinkhole repair
-----
The main runway at Sonoma County Airport reopened Friday afternoon after a sinkhole was repaired. The sinkhole, discovered after the last flight on Thursday had landed, closed the runway for about 16 hours and disrupted two commercial flights on Friday.The runway reopened at about 2 p.m., said Daniel Virkstis, a county spokesperson. Repairs to the 12-18 inch sinkhole were completed by 10 a.m. but were not reviewed and approved by the Federal Aviation Administration until 2 p.m.
