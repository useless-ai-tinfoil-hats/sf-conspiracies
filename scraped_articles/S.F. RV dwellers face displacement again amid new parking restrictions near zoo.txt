S.F. RV dwellers face displacement again amid new parking restrictions near zoo
-----
About two dozen families living in their RVs could be displaced for the second time in two weeks after city officials announced new parking restrictions on the street near the San Francisco Zoo where they now live.The families moved to Zoo Road, a narrow, one-way street, late last month after the San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency ordered RVs and vehicles to vacate Winston Drive near Stonestown Galleria, where many had been parked for months or years.
