Bay Area theater company cancels ‘Death of a Salesman’ after key actor was killed in S.F.
-----
African-American Shakespeare Company is canceling its production of "Death of a Salesman" following the death of actor Richard D. May, who was to play Uncle Ben in the show, the company announced Thursday, Nov. 2.May, who was in his 60s, was struck by a vehicle in Lower Nob Hill on Saturday, Oct. 28 — the same day of the production's first performance and May's debut as a professional actor.
