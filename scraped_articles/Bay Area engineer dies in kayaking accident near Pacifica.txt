Bay Area engineer dies in kayaking accident near Pacifica
-----
A Bay Area engineer died in a kayaking accident near Pacifica over the weekend. The San Mateo County Coroner's Office identified the man as Zongde Qiu, a 45-year-old Cupertino resident.According to a GoFundMe page, Qiu was fishing in the ocean off the Pacifica coast on Saturday before he fell off his kayak.
