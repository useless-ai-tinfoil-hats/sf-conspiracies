49ers tackle Colton McKivitz enters second season as starter with new confidence
-----
Colton McKivitz's debut was a dud. In fact, his coming-out party went so poorly that he worried he was going back to the bench.Last year, in the first game of his first full season as an NFL starter, the San Francisco 49ers' right tackle was the lone blemish in a 30-7 season-opening win at Pittsburgh. McKivitz allowed three sacks to All-Pro pass rusher T.J. Watt, inspiring thoughts of being relegated to second-string in Week 2.
