Aerosmith has announced new dates for its Peace Out farewell tour, seven months after a vocal injury to frontman Steven Tyler forced the band off the road after just three shows.The Rock & Roll Hall of Fame band, best known for hits like "Dream On," "Walk This Way" and "I Don't Want to Miss a Thing," now plans to perform Nov. 30 at Chase Center in San Francisco, making up for a date originally scheduled for last December. The band also added a stop at SAP Center at San Jose on Dec. 4.
