Warriors rookie makes unreal full-court heave at Rising Stars practice
-----
A stellar rookie season for Golden State Warriors guard Brandin Podziemski has earned him a trip to Friday night's Rising Stars Challenge at NBA All-Star Weekend. Hours before the game, which is meant to showcase the league's best young talent, the left-hander was already stealing the show in Indianapolis.Podziemski made a full-court heave at the Rising Stars practice on Friday, which the Warriors captured for a video posted on social media.
