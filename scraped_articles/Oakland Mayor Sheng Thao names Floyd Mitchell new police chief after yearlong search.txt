Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao names Floyd Mitchell new police chief after yearlong search
-----
Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao announced the selection of a new police chief Friday, ending more than a year of uncertainty over the high-profile post as a sharp increase in crime draws heightened attention to public safety in the city.Floyd Mitchell, who was the first Black police chief in Lubbock, Texas, will take charge of the department in Oakland after being picked by Thao from a group of four offered by the Oakland Police Commission.
