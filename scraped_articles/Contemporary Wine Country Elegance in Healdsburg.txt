Contemporary Wine Country Elegance in Healdsburg
-----
A rare super-modern new-construction house with easy walkability to downtown Healdsburg, by a builder/owner with a sterling reputation for high-end design, is being listed for $8.75 million.The two-story home at 728 University St. in Healdsburg offers 4,104 square feet of interior space, four bedrooms, four full bathrooms and two partial bathrooms. It has direct views of Healdsburg's iconic Fitch Mountain.
