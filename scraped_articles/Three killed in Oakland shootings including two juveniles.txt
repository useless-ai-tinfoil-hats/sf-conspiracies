Three killed in Oakland shootings, including two juveniles
-----
Two boys and a man were killed in two shootings in Oakland that occurred within a half hour of each other early Monday, police said.The first shooting occurred around 1:30 a.m. on Filbert Street near 10th Street, not far from Lowell Park. The victim, a male juvenile, died at the scene, police said.
The second shooting happened around 2 a.m. on 102nd Avenue near Graffian Street near San Leandro's city limits. Paramedics took an Antioch man to a hospital, where he died, police said.
