Burglars strike multiple businesses in the Oakland hills
-----
A spree of break-ins in Oakland's Montclair Village left local business owners out several thousands of dollars Friday morning.Eric Kim, owner of EM Deli & Catering, saw the break-in happen on his security cameras around 5 a.m. Friday and said he immediately called the police. By the time he arrived at the store, the thieves were gone.
Kim said three burglars took $5,000 in cash and caused a couple more thousand dollars in damage to his door, cash register and Point of Sale system.
