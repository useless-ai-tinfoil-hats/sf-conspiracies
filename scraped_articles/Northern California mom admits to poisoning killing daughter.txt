Northern California mom admits to poisoning, killing daughter
-----
A Northern California woman has pleaded guilty to kidnapping, poisoning and killing her young daughter, the Shasta County District Attorney's Office said in a press release.On Jan. 5, 2023, Shasta County 911 dispatchers received a call about an unresponsive child in a car at Deschutes Road and Highway 44 near Redding. Despite life-saving efforts, the two-year-old girl died of a drug overdose.
