Man crushed to death between parked cars by driver fleeing Vallejo police
-----
A pedestrian died in Vallejo on Tuesday afternoon after a driver fleeing police rammed into two parked vehicles, killing a 76-year-old man, police said.The man was standing between the parked vehicles on the 300 block of Carolina Street when the driver collided with the cars, "crushing the pedestrian between them," the Vallejo Police Department said in a statement.
