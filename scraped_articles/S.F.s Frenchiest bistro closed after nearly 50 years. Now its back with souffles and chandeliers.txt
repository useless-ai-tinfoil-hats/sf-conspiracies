S.F.’s ‘Frenchiest bistro’ closed after nearly 50 years. Now it’s back with souffles and chandeliers
-----
Chef Bernard Moutal may be 78 years old, but he recently accepted a new job with pleasure. Moutal has donned his toque and descended into an underground kitchen in Union Square, serving one of the most weird and wonderful dining rooms in the city.Jeanne d'Arc, once called "the Frenchiest French bistro in San Francisco" by the Chronicle, is back from the dead.
