Filipino artist Pacita Abad’s ‘vibrant spirit of rebellion’ lives on at SFMOMA
-----
Color is everywhere in the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art's exuberant Pacita Abad exhibition, the first major career retrospective of the peripatetic Filipino artist.Fifty of her vibrant works, on view through early next year, are collectively a riot of vivid tones and textures, but they are also an affecting testament to Abad's adventurous, transnational life.
