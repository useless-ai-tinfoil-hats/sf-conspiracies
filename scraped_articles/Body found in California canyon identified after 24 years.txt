Body found in California canyon identified after 24 years
-----
After 24 years, the remains of a Jane Doe found in Southern California have finally been identified.On Jan. 7, 2000, the body of a middle-aged adult woman was found by a surveyor working on Grimes Canyon Road, near the city of Fillmore in Ventura County. The remains were found with some clothing and a backpack. It was determined that the deceased was between 5-foot-3 and 5-foot-6, and had undergone "quality" dental work within the last five years.
