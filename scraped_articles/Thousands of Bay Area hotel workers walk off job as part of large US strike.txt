Thousands of Bay Area hotel workers walk off job as part of large US strike
-----
Thousands of hotel workers have gone on strike in the Bay Area, joining a labor action spanning the nation.Members of Unite Here, which represents 300,000 North American workers, many in the hospitality industry, walked off jobs at Hilton, Hyatt, Marriott and Omni hotels on Sunday. The action is in response to failed contract negotiations with the hotel giants. In late July, union heads signaled the parties were not close on contract terms.
