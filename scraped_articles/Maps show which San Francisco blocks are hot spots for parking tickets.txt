Maps show which San Francisco blocks are hot spots for parking tickets
-----
The Chase Center was a full house for the Warriors first preseason game on Oct. 7, as basketball fans packed the arena to watch the team play the Los Angeles Lakers. The Warriors secured the home win, but for some fans who parked on Terry Francois Boulevard, what should've been a triumphant walk back to their car ended with a $108 parking ticket.Walking distance from the Chase Center, the 500 block of Terry Francois Boulevard is ground zero for parking tickets in San Francisco.
