Cal Poly Humboldt closes for remainder of semester amid ‘bloody’ Gaza protests
-----
A Northern California university campus said Friday that it would close for the remainder of the school year after the occupation of an academic and administrative building by pro-Palestinian activists.Since Monday, hundreds of students have occupied Siemens Hall and Nelson Hall East at California State Polytechnic University, Humboldt, in Arcata, leading to the ongoing closure of the campus.
