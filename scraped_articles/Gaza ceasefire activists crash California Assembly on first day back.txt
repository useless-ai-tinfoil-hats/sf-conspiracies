Gaza cease-fire activists crash California Assembly on first day back
-----
Hundreds of activists calling for a cease-fire in Gaza interrupted the year's first meeting of the California state Assembly on Wednesday, forcing the body to adjourn for the day just minutes after it had been called to order.Videos and images on social media show protesters crowded into the Capitol rotunda and into the chamber's viewing gallery above the lawmakers' seats on the green-carpeted assembly floor.
