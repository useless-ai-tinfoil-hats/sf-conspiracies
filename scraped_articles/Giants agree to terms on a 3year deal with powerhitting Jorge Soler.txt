Giants agree to terms on a 3-year deal with power-hitting Jorge Soler
-----
The San Francisco Giants, trying to contend with Southern California teams full of star power, have reached terms with a right-handed hitter who crushed 36 homers last year.The Chronicle learned Monday night that the Giants have agreed to sign designated hitter and outfielder Jorge Soler to a three-year deal, with a physical pending. The financial terms are unknown but the annual average value is likely to be in the $12 million to $17 million range, based on recent signings.
