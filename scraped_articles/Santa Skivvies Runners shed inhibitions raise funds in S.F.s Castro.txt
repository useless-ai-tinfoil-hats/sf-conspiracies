Santa Skivvies: Runners shed inhibitions, raise funds in S.F.’s Castro
-----
Temperatures in the low 50s didn't stop dozens of participants from shedding their inhibitions — as well as most of their clothes — for the annual Santa Skivvies Run through San Francisco's Castro neighborhood Sunday.The annual 1-mile run and walk, a benefit for the San Francisco AIDS Foundation, is a holiday staple in the LGBTQ neighborhood, beginning and ending at the Lookout bar at Noe and 16th streets.
