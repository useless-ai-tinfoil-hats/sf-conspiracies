USWNT reclaims legacy for Olympic greatness and soccer joy with Paris gold
-----
PARIS — The U.S. women's soccer team reclaimed its legacy on Saturday evening. And launched a thrilling new era.The U.S. team's 1-0 victory over Brazil in the gold-medal game at Parc des Princes — a game decided with another dazzling goal created by the young American forwards — firmly put last year's World Cup disaster in the past. More importantly, it kickstarted the Emma Hayes era with a golden flourish.
