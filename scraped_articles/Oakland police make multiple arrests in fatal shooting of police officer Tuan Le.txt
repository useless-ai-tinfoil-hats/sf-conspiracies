Oakland police make multiple arrests in fatal shooting of police officer Tuan Le
-----
Oakland police said Tuesday they made several arrests in connection with the fatal shooting of Oakland police officer Tuan Le."Our officers continue to work tirelessly to bring justice to the Le family," the Oakland Police Department said in a statement. "We are all devastated by this senseless act of violence, and our hearts continue to mourn his passing."
The department said officials hope the arrests "start the healing for Officer Le's grieving family and our entire OPD family."
