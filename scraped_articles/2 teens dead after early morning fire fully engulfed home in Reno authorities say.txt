2 teens dead after early morning fire 'fully engulfed' home in Reno, authorities say
-----
Two teenagers died after a house fire "fully engulfed" a home in south Reno early Friday morning, authorities told SFGATE.At approximately 2:54 a.m., the Reno Fire Department responded to a report of a fire at 470 Teramo Court. When firefighters arrived on the scene, they discovered a two-story, single-family home up in flames, and two residents outside while two others were reported to be still inside the house.
