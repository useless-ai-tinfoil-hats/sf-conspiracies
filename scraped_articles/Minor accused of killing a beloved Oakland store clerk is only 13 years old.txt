Minor accused of killing a beloved Oakland store clerk is only 13 years old
-----
New details have emerged about the suspect arrested in connection with the killing of a beloved store clerk in Oakland earlier this year.In early January, 46-year-old Maged Alazzani, known as a fixture of his community who helped out his neighbors, was fatally shot during an armed robbery while working at Orlando's Market, a corner store in West Oakland, SFGATE reported at the time. Alazzani, an immigrant from Yemen, is mourned by his wife and four children, according to ABC7.
