Where Tesla sales have fallen the most in California
-----
Following years of rapid growth, Tesla sales in California are down so far this year.Tesla sold about 104,000 cars to Californians in the first half of 2024, down 13% from the 120,000 sold in the same period in 2023, according to a Chronicle analysis of newly released data. In contrast, electric car sales for all other companies combined increased by 19%. For the first time since at least 2021, non-Tesla sales exceeded Tesla sales in the first half of the year.
