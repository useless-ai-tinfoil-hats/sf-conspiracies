Blizzard leaves Tahoe covered with fresh powder and a lot of snow to shovel
-----
SOUTH LAKE TAHOE — For the first time since a rare blizzard descended on Lake Tahoe four days ago, blue skies and sunshine settled in Monday morning, and residents and visitors took the chance to dig out cars and houses, take to the powder-packed ski slopes — or get out of town.Since Thursday, unrelenting snowfall accompanied by gusting winds had created blizzard conditions in the Sierra Nevada.
