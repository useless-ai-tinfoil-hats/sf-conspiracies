Nearly 1,000 acres of private land transferred to California forest
-----
Nearly 1,000 acres of private land have been transferred to the Mendocino National Forest for public use, opening a "critical wildlife corridor" for the forest's sprawling ecosystem, a Dec. 12 news release from the Wilderness Land Trust reads.
