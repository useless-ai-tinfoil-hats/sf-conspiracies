Burglars set menu board on fire at West Oakland deli
-----
After breaking into a West Oakland deli and barbecue restaurant on Friday morning, burglars started a fire inside before fleeing the scene.According to Stay Gold Deli owner Jason Herbers, the deli's manager arrived at the restaurant on Friday morning to find the back gate cut, its ATM torn open and its wall-mounted menu board set ablaze.
