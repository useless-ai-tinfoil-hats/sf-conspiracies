Longstanding S.F. French restaurant shutters
-----
One of San Francisco's longtime French restaurants has closed.Cocotte at 1521 Hyde St. is listed as "permanently closed" on OpenTable and was reported as shuttered by the San Francisco Business Times. Owners Aurore Martinez and Mikael Audry could not be immediately reached for comment.
The casual French restaurant temporarily closed for a seismic retrofit in January, according to social media posts. It's unclear whether it ever reopened.
