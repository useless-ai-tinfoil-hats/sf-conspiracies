Wrong-way truck driver hits Tesla, kills two, on Route 85 in San Jose
-----
Two people died in a head-on collision Monday after a pickup truck went the wrong way on state Route 85, according to the California Highway Patrol.Around 3:40 p.m., officers received calls of a white Toyota Tacoma pickup truck traveling south on the northbound side of Route 85, crossing lanes to avoid vehicles. Around 3:51 p.m., the truck hit a white Tesla Model Y, according to CHP.
