2 young people die in river near Shasta Dam, sheriff says
-----
Two young people died in a river near Shasta Dam in Northern California on Thursday morning, officials said.The Shasta County Sheriff's Office provided few details in a post on Facebook but said the juveniles had fallen from a camping area into a river.
Multiple agencies responded to "a campground north of Shasta Dam" at 9 a.m. and located the two juveniles, the sheriff's office said. The two children were declared deceased at the scene.
