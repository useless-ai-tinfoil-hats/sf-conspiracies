Maggie Angst joins the Chronicle as a homelessness and addiction reporter
-----
From Emily Fancher, Deputy Metro Editor:We are pleased to share that Maggie Angst has joined the San Francisco Chronicle as a reporter covering homelessness, addiction and mental illness for the City Hall team.
Angst joins the Chronicle from the Sacramento Bee, where she covered California politics and Gov. Gavin Newsom. Previously, she worked for the Mercury News, where she reported on San Jose City Hall, wildfires and the pandemic, among other beats.
