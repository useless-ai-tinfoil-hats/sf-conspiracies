S.F. fire damages neighborhood hardware store, displaces two adjacent residents
-----
Firefighters extinguished a blaze that damaged Great Wall Hardware in San Francisco's Parkside neighborhood early Sunday.The San Francisco Fire Department received a call at 3:48 a.m. about a blaze at 1821 Taraval St. The agency dispatched 40 firefighters who stopped the blaze's spread within 30 minutes, said Justin Schorr, SFFD rescue captain.
