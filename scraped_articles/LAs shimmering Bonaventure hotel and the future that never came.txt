LA's shimmering Bonaventure hotel, and the future that never came
-----
In 1978, a stainless steel gull-winged sports car was brought to Los Angeles, a prototype in search of investors. The futuristic ride, built by John DeLorean, needed an equally forward-thinking backdrop to showcase its one-of-a-kind entrance into the automobile market. That same year, the movie "Buck Rogers in the 25th Century" was in production, and the director was desperately looking for a location that could stand in for LA circa the year 2491.In both cases the answer was obvious.
