'Noticeably colder' weather enters Bay Area, some spots to dip into 30s
-----
The sun shone brightly over the San Francisco Bay Area on Wednesday morning, and people out-and-about were wearing T-shirts on a fall day. Don't let the warmth fool you, though: It's going to get chilly tonight, and the cooler overnight temperatures are expected to stick around through the work week.Overnight lows Wednesday into Thursday are forecast to dive down into the low 50s along coast and into the high to low 40s inland, even dropping into 30s in the chilliest spots.
