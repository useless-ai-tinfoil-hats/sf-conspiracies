East Bay pizza delivery driver fatally shot while on break outside home
-----
A pizza delivery driver was shot and killed while taking a break outside his Oakley home on Wednesday, according to police.Oakley police said the shooting occurred just before 5 p.m. while the victim was parked outside his home near the intersection of Rutherford Lane and Bynum Way. Another vehicle pulled up alongside the man, who was taking a break from delivering pizza, and a passenger exited the vehicle and shot the man several times, according to police.
