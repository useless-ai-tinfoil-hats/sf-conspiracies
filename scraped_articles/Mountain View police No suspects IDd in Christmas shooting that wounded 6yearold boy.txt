Mountain View police: No suspects ID'd in Christmas shooting that wounded 6-year-old boy
-----
Mountain View police said Wednesday that they were investigating a shooting that left a 6-year-old boy wounded on Christmas night.Dispatchers received calls around 8:30 p.m. on Dec. 25 that a child had been shot on the 900 block of Clark Avenue. When they arrived, officers found the boy suffering from a gunshot wound to his lower body, police said. He was hospitalized and expected to make a full recovery.
A man, who was not identified, was also injured in the shooting, police said.
