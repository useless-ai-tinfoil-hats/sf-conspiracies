Walmart layoffs result in 568 cuts at Bay Area offices
-----
Walmart is planning significant cuts at two of its Bay Area offices, resulting in more than 560 job losses.According to regulatory filings, the retail giant plans to permanently lay off 388 employees at its San Bruno e-commerce center and an additional 180 at its Sunnyvale location, beginning Aug. 9.
