SF Giants fire manager Gabe Kapler after 4 seasons
-----
The San Francisco Giants announced that they have dismissed manager Gabe Kapler after four seasons on the job. Bench coach Kai Correa will take over for the final series of the season against the Dodgers this weekend.It was clear that Kapler's job was in jeopardy as early as Thursday, when president of baseball operations Farhan Zaidi gave an open-ended reply regarding whether the manager would be back for next season.
