‘The last of his tribe’: Historic Bay Area bar loses its most famous regular
-----
For more than 50 years, Richie Cortese was the Hotsy Totsy Club's most famous regular. He presided over the historic Albany bar every afternoon from his perch, just to the right of the bar's center well, in a stool embroidered with his name.
