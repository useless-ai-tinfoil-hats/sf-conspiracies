One of San Francisco’s most famous chefs serves lab-grown chicken. Here’s how it tastes
-----
Dominique Crenn hasn't served chicken at her restaurants in years, but I recently found myself staring down a morsel of the chef's tempura-fried chicken at her eponymous Bar Crenn. The fried fillet, served dramatically on a pedestal amid the curling vapors of dry ice, was uncannily uniform, without the varied muscle striations and lines of fat that accompany meat that comes from a body.
