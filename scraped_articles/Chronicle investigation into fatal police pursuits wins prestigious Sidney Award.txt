Chronicle investigation into fatal police pursuits wins prestigious Sidney Award
-----
From Sarah Feldberg, Editor of Emerging Product:The San Francisco Chronicle's investigation into deadly police chases has been honored with the prestigious Sidney Award, administrators announced Wednesday. The national award, given by the Sidney Hillman Foundation, recognizes one exceptional piece of journalism published in the previous month.
