Stanford investigate false shooting threat on campus
-----
Officials at Stanford University said investigated a false report of a potential shooting threat on campus Friday afternoon, according to school officials.Around 12:40 p.m., a man called Palo Alto police and claimed he was at the entrance of campus and planned on committing a shooting, according to Stanford's Department of Public Safety. "Out of an abundance of caution," the Department issued an emergency notification about the threat.
