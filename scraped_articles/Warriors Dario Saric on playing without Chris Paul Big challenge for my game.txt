Warriors’ Dario Saric on playing without Chris Paul: ‘Big challenge for my game’
-----
As the Golden State Warriors labored through the first half Friday night, Dario Saric stood out. While scoring a team-high 12 points, he hit two long-range shots that were set up by his longtime sidekick, Chris Paul.And now Saric's sidekick is gone, sidelined by a fractured left hand. Paul will have surgery this week and figures to miss a sizable chunk of games, complicating the challenge facing the Warriors in general and Saric in particular.
