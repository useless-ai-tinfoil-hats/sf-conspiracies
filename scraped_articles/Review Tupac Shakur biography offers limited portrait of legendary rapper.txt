Review: ‘Tupac Shakur’ biography offers limited portrait of legendary rapper
-----
Late last month, the Los Angeles Police Department indicted Duane Keith Davis in the murder of Tupac Shakur. His arrest, the first involving Shakur's long-unsolved death, comes some 27 years after a white Cadillac pulled up next to the rapper and actor at a Las Vegas stoplight and sent four bullets into his body, ultimately killing him at the age of 25.
