Tulip Day blooms in S.F. as thousands claim 80,000 free flowers
-----
An annual San Francisco tradition was in full bloom Saturday afternoon as the giveaway of 80,000 tulips in Union Square attracted an estimated 40,000 people.Tulip Day, a tradition the city adopted from the Netherlands in 2018, represents the strong bonds between Holland and the U.S. and anticipates the arrival of spring later this month, according to Dirk Janssen, San Francisco's Dutch consul general.
