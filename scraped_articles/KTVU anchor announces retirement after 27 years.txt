KTVU anchor announces retirement after 27 years
-----
Longtime KTVU anchor Julie Haener is heading into retirement, she announced on Tuesday evening.Haener told viewers during the 6 p.m. broadcast that she will leave her post after 27 years at KTVU to spend more time with family. Haener's last broadcast will be on June 20.
