Man shot near I-280 off-ramp in San Jose
-----
A man was shot Thursday evening near a freeway off ramp in San Jose and was transported to a local hospital with life-threatening injuries, police said.The shooting happened at 5:46 p.m., in the area of the Interstate 280 off-ramp at McLaughlin Avenue, San Jose police said. The off-ramp was blocked Thursday evening as officers investigated the incident.
The man was stabilized at the hospital, but remained in critical condition, police said Thursday night.
