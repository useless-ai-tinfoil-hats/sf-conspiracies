Massive search underway for missing hiker and his dog in Bay Area park
-----
A multi-agency search is underway in an East Bay park for a missing hiker and his dog.On Saturday evening, May 18, authorities were notified that Alex Stecher had not returned from a hike in the Ohlone Wilderness, east of Fremont in Alameda County.
