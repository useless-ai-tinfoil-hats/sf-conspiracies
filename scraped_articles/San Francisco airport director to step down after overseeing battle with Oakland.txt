San Francisco airport director to step down after overseeing battle with Oakland
-----
Ivar C. Satero, director of San Francisco International Airport, announced on Friday he will retire from his position, which he has held for eight years, before the end of 2024."I am immensely proud of what we have accomplished together in our mission to deliver an airport where our passengers, our people and our planet come first," Satero said in a statement.
In the coming months, a search will be conducted for his successor. Satero will continue to serve in his current role
