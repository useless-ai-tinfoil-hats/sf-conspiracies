As of Monday, one of San Francisco's most popular breakfast sandwiches can now be found right downtown. Devil's Teeth Baking Company, known for its buttermilk biscuit sandwiches stuffed with scrambled eggs, bacon and cheese, has ventured all the way from the city's western neighborhoods to Embarcadero Center.Devil's Teeth is one of 17 pop-ups opening in nine vacant spaces in the Financial District in late September and early October as part of a program called "Vacant to Vibrant"
