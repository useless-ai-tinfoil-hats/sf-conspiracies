Review: In ‘The Grab,’ the rich and ruthless seek control of food and water
-----
"The Grab" details how rich, mostly repressive nations and wealthy, often quite American investment entities — some with scary, international mercenary connections — are trying to corner the world's supply of arable land and fresh water.Plenty of details about all that are gathered in this years-in-the-making documentary from Gabriela Cowperthwaite.
