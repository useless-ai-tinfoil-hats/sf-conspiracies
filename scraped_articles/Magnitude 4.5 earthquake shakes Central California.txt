Magnitude 4.5 earthquake shakes Central California
-----
An earthquake with a magnitude of 4.5, with an epicenter near the Central Valley town of Patterson, struck Monday evening, the U.S. Geological Survey said.The temblor hit at 9:13 p.m. PT with a depth of 2.17 miles (3.5 km). The epicenter was 9.4 mi (15.1 km) southwest of Patterson, 22.6 miles (36.4 km) southwest of Modesto and 33.5 miles (53.9 km) northeast of San Jose.
