Can movie nights bring people back to Oakland's business centers? City launches events program
-----
Facing low foot traffic in business centers and another bad year for violent crime, Oakland Mayor Sheng Thao announced Thursday that the city will invest just under half a million dollars in a new events-sponsorship program for the city's commercial corridors.Thao said the program, called Activate Oakland, will offer $400,000 in grants for events to boost foot traffic and help the city's economy that is still struggling from the pandemic.
