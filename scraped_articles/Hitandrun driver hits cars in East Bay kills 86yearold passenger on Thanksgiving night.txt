Hit-and-run driver hits cars in East Bay, kills 86-year-old passenger on Thanksgiving night
-----
An 86-year-old passenger died after the car he was riding in was struck by a hit-and-run driver on Thanksgiving night, according to Concord police.Police said a driver struck two cars before fleeing the scene just before 7 p.m. First the driver collided with a vehicle near the intersection of Oak Grove Road and Treat Boulevard, police said. Then, about half a block away, the driver struck another car with an 86-year-old male passenger inside, police said.
