Hayward man arrested in shooting of a motorist in Santa Cruz County
-----
A Hayward man has been arrested on attempted murder charges connected to a shooting last May that injured a motorist and a 3-year-old child in Santa Cruz County.The suspect, Raphael Shakur Abduh-Salam aka "Abdul-Salam," 33, of Hayward, is being held in Contra Costa County on a parole hold. He is set to be booked into jail in Santa Cruz County on attempted murder and other charges.
