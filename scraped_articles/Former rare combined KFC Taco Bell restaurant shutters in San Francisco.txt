Former rare combined KFC, Taco Bell restaurant shutters in San Francisco
-----
A KFC store that formerly doubled as a rare Taco Bell-KFC combination restaurant, has closed in San Francisco, as first reported by the San Francisco Standard.KFC's parent company Yum! Brands, which also owns Taco Bell, Pizza Hut and The Habit Burger Grill, confirmed the restaurant closure at 200 Duboce Ave. with SFGATE via email. According to a KFC spokesperson, the franchise owner decided to close due to "a normal course of business."
