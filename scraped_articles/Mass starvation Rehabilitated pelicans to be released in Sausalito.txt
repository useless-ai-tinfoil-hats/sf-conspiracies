Mass starvation: Rehabilitated pelicans to be released in Sausalito
-----
Some of the hundreds of pelicans found starving earlier this year will be released back into the wild near the base of the Golden Gate Bridge on Thursday.The starving and injured seabirds came mostly from the Monterey and Santa Cruz areas into the care of International Bird Rescue starting in April. After weeks of rehabilitation, the conservation group with a crisis center in Fairfield will release a first group of rehabilitated pelicans at Fort Baker in Sausalito.
