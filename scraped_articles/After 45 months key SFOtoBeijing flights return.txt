After 45 months, key SFO-to-Beijing flights return
-----
Air China has resumed two weekly direct flights between San Francisco and Beijing, restoring another link to San Francisco's most lucrative source of tourism before the pandemic.The Wednesday and Saturday flights returned this week after a pause of more than three years, having stopped in February 2020 just before the pandemic erupted in the U.S.
