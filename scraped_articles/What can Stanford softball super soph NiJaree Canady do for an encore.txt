What can Stanford softball super soph NiJaree Canady do for an encore?
-----
Two sounds rise above all others to indicate when NiJaree Canady is in the pitching circle: the swoosh of a swing and a miss, and a loud, echoing pop of the catcher's mitt as her fastball slams against leather.Mostly, that was the only sound to contend with the cheers and yelps from the Stanford softball season ticket holders during an exhibition in late January.
