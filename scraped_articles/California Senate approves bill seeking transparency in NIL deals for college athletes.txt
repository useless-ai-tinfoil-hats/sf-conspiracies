California Senate approves bill seeking transparency in NIL deals for college athletes
-----
On Wednesday night the California Senate passed SB906, a bill seeking to bring transparency to name, image and likeness (NIL) deals in college sports.State Sen. Nancy Skinner, D-Berkeley, proposed the legislation, which earned unanimous approval in the Senate after previously sailing through the state Assembly. SB906 now moves to Gov. Gavin Newsom's desk.
The bill's passage comes more than five years after Skinner introduced SB206, known as the Fair Pay to Play Act, in February
