Pregnant preschool teacher killed in Bay Area hit-and-run, police say
-----
A pregnant woman and her unborn child were killed by a driver in a stolen car who fled the scene, officials said Thursday.The fatal collision occurred on Blossom Hill Road near Winfield Boulevard by the Westfield Oakridge shopping mall at about 8:40 p.m. Tuesday. The woman — identified by the the Santa Clara Medical Examiner as April Zoglauer, 21, of San Jose — was driving eastbound in a 2008 black Nissan sedan on Blossom Hill Road.
