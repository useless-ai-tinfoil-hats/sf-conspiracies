4 Bay Area restaurants make Esquire's Best New Restaurants in America list
-----
Four Bay Area restaurants made the cut for Esquire's 2023 "The Best New Restaurants in America" list.Esquire food writers dined at over 200 restaurants across the country to select their 50 favorites. Two of these were San Francisco restaurants: Dalida and Kiln.
