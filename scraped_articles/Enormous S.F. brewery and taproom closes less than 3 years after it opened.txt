Enormous S.F. brewery and taproom closes less than 3 years after it opened
-----
A popular brewery's ambitious taproom in San Francisco is now closed.Colorado's New Belgium Brewing, best known for its Fat Tire ale and Voodoo Ranger IPA, announced it has ceased operations at its Mission Bay brew pub and restaurant. A press release states the location has "suffered financially in recent years."
The taproom opened in 2021, featuring beer brewed on-premises and food from chef couple Laura and Sayat Ozyilmaz, who have since gone on to their own restaurant, Dalida.
