Dozens of protesters take over lobby of Israeli consulate in San Francisco
-----
A group of pro-Palestinian protestors entered and occupied the lobby of the Consulate General of Israel in San Francisco's Financial District Monday morning in an effort to call attention to the ongoing war in Gaza.San Francisco police officials said officers arrived on scene to the building at 456 Montgomery St. after dozens of protesters entered the building. San Francisco Lt. Jesse Cruz estimated about 70 people were inside the building on the ground floor around 11 a.m.
