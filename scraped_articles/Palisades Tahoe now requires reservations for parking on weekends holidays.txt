Palisades Tahoe now requires reservations for parking on weekends, holidays
-----
Palisades Tahoe will require visitors to reserve a parking spot in advance on weekends and holidays this ski season.The new reservation system is meant to mitigate traffic on local roads and eliminate the need to show up hours before the ski lifts open just to guarantee a parking spot, according to the resort.
Palisades will require reservations on weekends from Dec. 2 through March 31, as well as Dec. 23-31, Jan. 13-15 and Feb. 17-25.
