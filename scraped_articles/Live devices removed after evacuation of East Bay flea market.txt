‘Live devices’ removed after evacuation of East Bay flea market
-----
Two "live devices" were removed from a popular East Bay flea market Saturday evening, authorities said.Firefighters were called to Canyon Lake Drive in Port Costa around 5:30 p.m. for reports of two pieces of military ordnance, according to the Crockett Carquinez Fire Department. Law enforcement authorities blocked traffic at the end of Reservoir Street and Carquinez Scenic Drive, and the area was evacuated.
