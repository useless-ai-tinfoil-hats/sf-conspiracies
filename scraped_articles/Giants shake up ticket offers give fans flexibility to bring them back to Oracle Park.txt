Giants shake up ticket offers, give fans flexibility to bring them back to Oracle Park
-----
SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. — Now that the San Francisco Giants have their 2024 team assembled and some new and notable names among them — Cy Young winner Blake Snell, 2021 World Series MVP Jorge Soler and Platinum Glove winner Matt Chapman — the business of selling tickets begins in earnest.The team ran a flash sale this week that included tickets for as low as $10, with a beer included for any April or May home games.
