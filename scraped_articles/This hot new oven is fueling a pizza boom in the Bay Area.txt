This hot new oven is fueling a pizza boom in the Bay Area
-----
When Thomas McNaughton, chef and co-founder of San Francisco Italian restaurant Flour+Water, first heard about electric ovens being used in pizza restaurants instead of traditional wood or gas, he dismissed the idea. "The first thing that came to mind is an Easy-Bake oven," he said.But today, Flour+Water Pizzeria in North Beach has two shiny electric PizzaMaster ovens in which staff bake East Coast-style pies with a crispy thin crust.
