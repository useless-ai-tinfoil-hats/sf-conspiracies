How a topless arrest made Carol Doda a San Francisco legend
-----
Carol Doda was standing topless on her white piano that descended from the Condor Club ceiling on April 22, 1965, when the performer realized she had company.San Francisco Police Department officer Robert Kafka climbed beside her on the elevated perch, grabbing her arm and shielding the crowd and its chorus of boos from the bare breasts they had paid $3.25 apiece (plus two-drink minimum) to see.
