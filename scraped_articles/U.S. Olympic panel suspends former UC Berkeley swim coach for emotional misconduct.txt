U.S. Olympic panel suspends former UC Berkeley swim coach for ‘emotional misconduct’
-----
The U.S. Center for SafeSport, created in 2017 to investigate abusive conduct in Olympic and Paralympic sports, suspended former UC Berkeley swim coach Teri McKeever this week for "emotional misconduct."McKeever led a storied career at Cal that spanned three decades and included several stints coaching the U.S. Olympic women's swim team.
