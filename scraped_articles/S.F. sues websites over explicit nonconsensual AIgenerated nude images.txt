S.F. sues websites over explicit, nonconsensual AI-generated nude images
-----
San Francisco City Attorney David Chiu is suing 16 websites that his office says use AI to create nonconsensual, fake nude images of women and girls, the first lawsuit of its kind, according to the city.The sites allow users to create AI-generated images of real people, swapping their faces onto nude images in a violation of state and federal laws prohibiting deepfake pornography, revenge pornography and child pornography, Chiu's office said.
