Cal men storm back from 20 points down to shock Colorado
-----
Looking listless and overmatched for 23 minutes at Haas Pavilion on Wednesday night, Cal got off the mat to pick up the first signature win of the Mark Madsen era.Jaylon Tyson scored a career-high 30 points — 23 coming in the second half when he didn't miss a shot — and the Bears overcame a 20-point deficit to shock Colorado 82-78.
