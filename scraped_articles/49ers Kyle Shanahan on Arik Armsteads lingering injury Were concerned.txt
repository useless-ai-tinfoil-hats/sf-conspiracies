49ers’ Kyle Shanahan on Arik Armstead’s lingering injury: ‘We’re concerned’
-----
San Francisco 49ers head coach Kyle Shanahan said Saturday that he's concerned about defensive tackle Arik Armstead's foot injury that will cause him to miss his third straight game when the 49ers host the Ravens on Monday night.Armstead is also dealing with a knee injury, but Shanahan said his foot was the more significant issue. Last year Armstead missed a game due to plantar fasciitis. Shanahan indicated Armstead was dealing with the same injury and answered affirmatively when asked
