Steve Jobs' dramatic cruelty takes center stage in San Francisco opera
-----
San Francisco Opera's newest show is about the impact and humanity of the iconic Steve Jobs, but his cruelty takes center stage."The (R)evolution of Steve Jobs," running at the 3,146-seat War Memorial Opera House through Oct. 7, is a nonchronological romp through the Apple founder's life, set to a unique score that merges orchestral and electronic music.
