‘It’s a battleground’: Why restaurants and patrons are at odds over surcharges
-----
Diners say they hate them. Restaurants say they need them.Customers and owners couldn't be more at odds over the subject of surcharges, which are due to become illegal on July 1 as part of California's new "junk fees" law. The legislation will affect many industries, but nowhere has it provoked as much debate as at restaurants.
"It's a battleground," said San Francisco diner Adolfo Liu.
