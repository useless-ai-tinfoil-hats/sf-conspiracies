Spanish Harlem Orchestra at the Lesher Center for the Arts
-----
Spanish Harlem Orchestra (SHO) is a three-time Grammy Award-winning band that sets the gold standard for excellence in authentic, New York-style salsa. Their energy on stage, rich sound, and musical precision leave audiences mesmerized until the very last note.
