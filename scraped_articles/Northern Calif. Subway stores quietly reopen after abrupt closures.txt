Northern Calif. Subway stores quietly reopen after abrupt closures
-----
A handful of Subway stores in Northern California that abruptly closed earlier this year have reopened under different ownership, as first reported by Action News Now.In January, 18 Subway restaurants in Shasta, Tehama and Glenn counties shuttered under the ownership of franchisee Delight Foods, but following the closures, Subway granted Mike Sharma, a new franchisee, three of the closed locations, according to the outlet.
