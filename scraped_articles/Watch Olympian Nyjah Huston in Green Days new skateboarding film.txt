Olympic skateboarder Nyjah Huston and Green Day have teamed up for an energizing new skate film."Monsters & Saviors," released Wednesday, Aug. 14, features exclusive footage of Huston and other Monster Energy-sponsored skaters shredding to Green Day hits from its 1994 breakout "Dookie," Grammy-winning "American Idiot" and the East Bay band's latest album "Saviors."
Directed by Russell Houghten, the 11-minute video has a nostalgic feel as it celebrates Green Day's skate-punk legacy
