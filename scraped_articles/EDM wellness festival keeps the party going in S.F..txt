EDM wellness festival keeps the party going in S.F.
-----
Tucked away in Hellman Hollow Meadow at Golden Gate Park, hundreds of music lovers braved San Francisco's windy weather for an outdoor festival with a self-care twist.All Day I Dream of San Francisco Cloud 9 kicked off with a guided yoga session at noon Saturday, June 8, and shortly turned into a dance party as electronic music producers Lost Desert, Roy Rosenfeld and Lee Burridge took turns on the stage.
