Video shows substitute at Bay Area school dragging student from classroom
-----
A substitute teacher at a school in Vallejo was caught on video knocking down a student and then dragging her out of a classroom.The confrontation happened Monday at Elite Public Schools in Vallejo. In a news release, school officials said the substitute, who was hired through an agency, was quickly removed from campus and will not return to Elite Public Schools "in any capacity."
