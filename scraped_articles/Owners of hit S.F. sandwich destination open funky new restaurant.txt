Owners of hit S.F. sandwich destination open funky new restaurant
-----
The owners of San Francisco's Palm City Wines never meant to open a hit sandwich shop. But when the pandemic derailed their plans for a neighborhood wine bar with great food, they rejiggered to survive.Now, four years and many hoagies later, their original dream has come to fruition. Dennis Cantwell and Monica Wong opened Bar Jabroni at 698 Haight St. in San Francisco on March 21. It's a "restaurant disguised as a bar," Cantwell said.
