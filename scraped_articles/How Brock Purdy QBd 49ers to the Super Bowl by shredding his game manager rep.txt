How Brock Purdy QB’d 49ers to the Super Bowl by shredding his ‘game manager’ rep
-----
Brock Purdy stood in the far corner of the San Francisco 49ers' locker room, his left sock torn but his trademark cool intact. He had done something epic Sunday, shaking off a 17-point halftime deficit to lead his flailing team to the Super Bowl, and it still seemed surreal, a magical microcosm of the second-year quarterback's incredible football journey.Then Purdy stopped, took a deep breath and allowed himself to contemplate just where he was and what lay ahead: a Super Bowl LVIII
