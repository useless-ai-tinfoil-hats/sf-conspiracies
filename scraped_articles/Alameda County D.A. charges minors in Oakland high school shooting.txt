Alameda County D.A. charges minors in Oakland high school shooting
-----
The Alameda County District Attorney's Office charged three minors in connection with last week's shooting at Skyline High School in Oakland, according to a video statement released Friday."Our office has charged three juveniles we believe were involved in the incident," District Attorney Pam Price said. "We should all be grateful that this appears to have been an isolated incident and other students and teachers were not the target."
