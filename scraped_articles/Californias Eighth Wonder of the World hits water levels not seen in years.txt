California's 'Eighth Wonder of the World' hits water levels not seen in years
-----

It's just the latest example of how the state's wet winter is transforming its landscapes after years of dry drought conditions.Big Bear Lake, less than two hours east of much of Los Angeles County in the San Bernardino Mountains, is hovering around 68.25 feet deep.
