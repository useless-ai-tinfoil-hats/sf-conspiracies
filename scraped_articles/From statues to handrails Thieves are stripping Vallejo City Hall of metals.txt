From statues to handrails: Thieves are stripping Vallejo City Hall of metals
-----
Vallejo City Hall has been hit by a series of thefts, with thieves stripping the building of everything from statue heads to handrails.City staff and the Vallejo Police Department are investigating the thefts of the metal objects. One of those was a bust of Francisca Benicia Carrillo de Vallejo, the wife of Gen. Mariano Vallejo, the man for whom the city was named, according to Vallejo officials. Her bust formerly sat on a fountain between City Hall and the John F. Kennedy Library.
