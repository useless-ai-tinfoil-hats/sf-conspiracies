Warriors’ Draymond Green says he’ll return from injury Sunday at Houston
-----
Golden State Warriors forward Draymond Green said he will make his regular-season debut Sunday in Houston."Tomorrow. For sure," Green told reporters following Saturday's practice in Sacramento.
The 33-year-old does not know if he will start or come off the bench, but expects to be on a minutes restriction. He also said he wants to play in the second game of the Warriors' first back-to-back set of the season against the New Orleans Pelicans on Monday.
