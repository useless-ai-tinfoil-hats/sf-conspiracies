S.F. police arrest 13, confiscate huge amount of drugs during 1-day operation in Civic Center
-----
San Francisco police officers seized more than 13 pounds of narcotics and made 13 arrests in a one-day enforcement operation this week, spokespersons said Friday.The operation took place around United Nations Plaza and the San Francisco Public Library on Tuesday.
Five of the 13 people arrested were suspected of possessing drugs with the intent to sell them. The other arrests were related to stay-away orders, arrest warrants or possession of controlled substances, officials said.
