Massive building fire on SF Peninsula triggers evacuations
-----
A fire engulfed an affordable housing complex under construction in San Mateo County on Monday morning, triggering evacuations in the area and pumping out smoke that spread across the Bay Area.The fire was first reported on the 2700 block of Middlefield Road in an unincorporated part of the county near Redwood City at about 10:15 a.m., according to the San Mateo County Sheriff's Office.
