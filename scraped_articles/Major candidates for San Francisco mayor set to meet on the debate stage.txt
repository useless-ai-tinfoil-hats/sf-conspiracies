Major candidates for San Francisco mayor set to meet on the debate stage
-----
The stage is set for this year's first official San Francisco mayoral debate.All five of the race's major candidates — Mayor London Breed, Board of Supervisors President Aaron Peskin, former interim Mayor Mark Farrell, local nonprofit leader and Levi Strauss heir Daniel Lurie and District 11 Supervisor Ahsha Safaí — are scheduled to face off on Wednesday at 6:30 p.m. at the Sydney Goldstein Theater.
