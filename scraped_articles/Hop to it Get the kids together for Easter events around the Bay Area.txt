Hop to it! Get the kids together for Easter events around the Bay Area
-----
Ready, set, go! With Easter on March 31 this year, egg hunts will be hopping into parks and green spaces all over the Bay Area a bit earlier than usual. It's time to dapper up, don your frilliest frocks and construct a creative Easter bonnet, because community Easter events and activities for the kids are happening in full force for 2024.Check out the Chronicle's guide to the Bay Area's public Easter egg hunts, which cater to the under-13 set along with their adoring caregiver paparazzi.
