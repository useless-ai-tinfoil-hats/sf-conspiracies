Two people shot at East Bay BART station
-----
Two men were shot at BART's Pittsburg station Friday during the afternoon commute, shutting down the station for more than four hours as police launched an investigation.The transit agency said the shooting was reported to police at 3:51 p.m. An initial report of the shooting said that a male suspect shot a male victim on the platform before fleeing the station, BART said.
