Kehinde Wiley exhibition from S.F.’s de Young put on hold by other museums after sexual assault allegations
-----
At least two museums nationwide have declined to host an exhibition by Kehinde Wiley that originated from San Francisco's de Young Museum, after the acclaimed artist faced multiple sexual assault allegations.The Minneapolis Institute of Art considered hosting the traveling exhibit, "An Archaeology of Science," which was open at the de Young last year, but decided against it. The Pérez Art Museum Miami also "suspended" its plans to open the exhibition, the New York Times reported.
