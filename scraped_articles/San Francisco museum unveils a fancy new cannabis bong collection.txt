San Francisco museum unveils a fancy new cannabis bong collection
-----
The San Francisco Museum of Modern Art is world famous for its collection of art pieces from the upper echelon of the art world, with paintings from Andy Warhol and Jackson Pollock. But a new display at the museum's gift shop is taking the museum's art collection to an entirely new high.For the first time, the museum's gift shop is selling a collection of ceramic cannabis smoking devices, including water pipes that are more commonly known as "bongs."
