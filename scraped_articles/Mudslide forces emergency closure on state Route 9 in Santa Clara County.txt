Mudslide forces emergency closure on state Route 9 in Santa Clara County
-----
A wet and windy storm is barreling through the Bay Area, transforming the region into a Pacific Northwest clone and spawning roadway hazards.Following a Friday mudslide in Santa Clara County, Caltrans closed a stretch of the mountainous state Route 9, according to a traffic advisory issued by the agency. A stretch of the road is blocked near Saratoga, between Sanborn and Redwood Gulch roads.
