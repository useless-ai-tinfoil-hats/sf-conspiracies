2-year-old fatally shot in Vallejo
-----
A 2-year-old was fatally shot in Vallejo on Friday, according to the city's police department.Just before noon Friday, Vallejo officers were called to a local hospital where a toddler had been brought in with a gunshot wound, according to a spokesperson for the police department. The toddler died from the injuries that day, police said.
Police learned the shooting happened in the 400 block of Alhambra Avenue, authorities said. Officers secured the scene and began investigating
