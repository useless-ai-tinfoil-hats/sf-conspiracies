S.F. fire erupts at Hayes Valley home of dog walker who faced racist threats
-----
Two people were injured when flames overtook a three-story home in San Francisco's Hayes Valley on Tuesday, fire officials said.Firefighters pulled two adults from the home after the fire broke out for unknown reasons on the 900 block of Grove Street near Alamo Square around 11:30 a.m., San Francisco Fire Department spokesperson Jonathan Baxter said. One adult was hospitalized in critical condition and another was hospitalized with moderate injuries, he said.
