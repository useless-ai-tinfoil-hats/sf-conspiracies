Finally, a Bay Area restaurant dedicated to Texas-style breakfast tacos
-----
The signature breakfast of Texas has landed in Wine Country. Ray Ray's Tacos, the only restaurant dedicated to breakfast tacos in the Bay Area, opens Tuesday in St. Helena at 1304 Main St.The farmers' market standout-turned-restaurant comes from chef-owner Rachel Williams, who moved to San Francisco from Austin with her family. Upon arrival, she quickly realized that burritos abound, but her beloved breakfast tacos were nowhere to be found.
