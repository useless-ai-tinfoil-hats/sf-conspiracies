‘Lovely chaos’: What it’s like to accidentally own a California ghost town
-----
The deserted town of Amboy looks like a Harley Davidson fever dream drenched in neon.Just along Route 66 in the Mojave Desert, it appears like a mirage, or a gritty 1950s Western on acid. But amid the barren nothingness, just past the mysteriously placed Buddha statue and volcanic crater, you'll also see the abandoned town's most prominent landmark glowing in the distance: Roy's Motel and Cafe, a remote gas station that has become an enduring, if surreal, symbol of Americana.
