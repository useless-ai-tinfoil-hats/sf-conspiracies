California Highway 101 roadside landmark forced to move due to eminent domain
-----
The Garlic Shoppe, a popular landmark along Highway 101 in Gilroy, has been given days to relocate after its building was acquired under eminent domain by the Santa Clara Valley Transportation Agency for the construction of a new intersection.For close to four decades, the Garlic Shoppe has stood as a roadside attraction in the South Bay, offering a diverse range of products from hot sauces to garlic ice cream.
