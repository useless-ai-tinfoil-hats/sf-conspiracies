Motorcyclist, driver killed in separate collisions Monday in San Jose
-----
Two men died in two separate crashes that occurred less than two hours apart in San Jose on Monday morning, according to police.The first collision occurred around 6:36 a.m. between a motorcycle and a car in the city's historic Alameda neighborhood, police said. The motorcyclist was transported to a local hospital where he was later pronounced dead.
