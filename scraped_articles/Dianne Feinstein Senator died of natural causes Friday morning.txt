Dianne Feinstein: Senator died of natural causes Friday morning
-----
Sen. Dianne Feinstein died early Friday morning Eastern Standard Time at her home in Washington, D.C., according to her office, leaving a storied legacy of public service stretching from San Francisco to the nation's capitol.Feinstein's death prompted an outpouring of condolences and remembrances from officials across the country.
