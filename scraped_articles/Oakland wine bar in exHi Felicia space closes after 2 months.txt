Oakland wine bar in ex-Hi Felicia space closes after 2 months
-----
Jellybean, an Oakland wine bar run by the owner of former high-profile restaurant Hi Felicia, has closed after two months.Owner Imana, who goes by her first name only, announced on Instagram that Jellybean's final day at 326 23rd St. was Sept. 10.
"Jellybean is a very special place, and I'm so happy with the experiences we've had at our wine bar," she wrote. The Instagram account, as well as the business' website, have since been taken down.
