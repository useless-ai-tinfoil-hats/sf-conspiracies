Bay Area farmers accuse billionaire-backed company of ‘pressure tactics’
-----
Farmers and landowners are accusing Flannery Associates, a company backed by tech billionaires, of using "pressure tactics" to buy land for a new city the group is planning to build in Solano County.The allegations were made public last week in a report summarizing a conference between the two parties about an ongoing lawsuit, which Flannery Associates filed in May.
