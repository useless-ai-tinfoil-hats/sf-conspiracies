Deebo Samuel returns to 49ers practice, Trent Williams still sidelined
-----
San Francisco 49ers All-Pro wide receiver Deebo Samuel missed the two games before last week's bye, but he returned to practice Monday for the first since he suffered a hairline fracture in his shoulder in a loss to the Browns on Oct. 15. Head coach Kyle Shanahan said at the time that Samuel would be reevaluated after the bye.
