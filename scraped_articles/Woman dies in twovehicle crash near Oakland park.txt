Woman dies in two-vehicle crash near Oakland park
-----
A 25-year-old woman died in a two-vehicle crash Sunday morning near Joaquin Miller Park in Oakland, police said.The crash occurred on Joaquin Miller Road about 5 a.m., when a southbound Infiniti struck a northbound Honda, police said.
The woman, a rear-seat passenger in the Honda, suffered major injuries. Despite medical aid, she died at the scene, police said. She was identified as an Oakland resident, but her name was not released, pending family notifications.
