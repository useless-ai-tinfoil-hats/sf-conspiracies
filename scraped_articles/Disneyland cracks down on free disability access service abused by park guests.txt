Disneyland cracks down on free disability access service abused by park guests
-----
A Disneyland service that became notorious for guest abuses is receiving a major retool.Disney's Disability Access Service is available for guests who cannot stand in lines for protracted periods of time due to spectrum disorders like autism. Eligible DAS guests can skip ride lines without paying for Genie Plus, which has turned into an unethical "hack" for some Disney visitors trying to cheat the system.
