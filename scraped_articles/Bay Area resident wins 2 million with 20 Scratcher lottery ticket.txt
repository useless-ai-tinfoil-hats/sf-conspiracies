Bay Area resident wins $2 million with $20 Scratcher lottery ticket
-----
A San Jose resident won a big payoff playing a California Lottery Scratcher game.Thuy Ho of San Jose won $2 million. Ho purchased the $20 Instant Prize Crossword ticket at TPS Liquor in East San Jose, California Lottery officials said Thursday.
The $2 million is the top prize in the Crossword Scratcher game.
