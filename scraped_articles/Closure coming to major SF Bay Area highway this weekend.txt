Closure coming to major SF Bay Area highway this weekend
-----
A major closure is coming to a San Francisco Bay Area highway this weekend. Caltrans said it will be shutting down a portion of southbound Interstate Highway 680 in Pleasanton from 9 p.m. Friday, Nov. 3 to 4 a.m. Monday, Nov. 6. The closure impacts the section of southbound I-680 between the I-580/I-680 connector and State Route 84.
