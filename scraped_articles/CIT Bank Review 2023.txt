CIT Bank Review 2023
-----
As the online division of First Citizens Bank, CIT Bank offers several online-only banking products including savings accounts, CDs and their eChecking account. Most of their products offer competitive interest rates, no monthly maintenance fees, and third-party ATM fee reimbursements.CIT Bank review
Pros and Cons of CIT Bank
Pros
* Competitive interest rates on some CD terms and savings accounts
* No monthly maintenance fees
* No minimum balance
