Snell makes progress as Giants open homestand with win over MLB-best Phillies
-----
After getting swept by the Phillies in Philadelphia earlier this month, the San Francisco Giants on Monday afternoon kicked back with an 8-4 win against the team with the best record in baseball.Blake Snell inched closer to his first quality start as a Giant. A laborious 35-pitch, two-run, one-walk fourth inning that included a pair of mound visits and a Memorial Day tribute between at-bats at 3 p.m. sharp sullied Snell's second outing since his return from injury.
