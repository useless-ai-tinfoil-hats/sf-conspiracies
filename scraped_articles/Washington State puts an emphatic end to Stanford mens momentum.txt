Washington State puts an emphatic end to Stanford men's momentum
-----
Stanford entered Thursday night's matchup with Washington State at Maples Pavilion having won four of its past five games, but the Cardinal hardly looked like a team on a roll.The Cougars did not trail, put together a 13-0 run late in the first half to take control and led by as many as 19 as they rolled the Cardinal 89-75. Stanford committed 17 turnovers.
