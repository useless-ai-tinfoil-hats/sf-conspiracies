Longtime basketball exec Ohemaa Nyanin named GM of Bay Area’s WNBA team
-----
The Bay Area's WNBA expansion team has a general manager, and she has ample WNBA — and international basketball — experience.On Monday, the WNBA Golden State franchise, preparing for its inaugural season in 2025, announced the addition of Ohemaa Nyanin to the front office as the team's first GM. Nyanin comes from the New York Liberty, for whom she had been an assistant GM since 2022. Prior to that, she worked as the organization's director of basketball relations.
