‘Rare and valuable’: The oldest fish in any aquarium in the world is in San Francisco
-----
The oldest living fish in any aquarium in the world arrived at its home at the California Academy of Sciences in November of 1938, when sand dunes still covered the Outer Sunset and a second World War was on the horizon.Now, scientists have confirmed the official age of Methuselah, a 4.5-foot-long Australian lungfish with an affinity for figs and belly rubs.
