Review: ‘Bees & Honey’ might’ve worked better as TV
-----
Let's say a man wants to rhapsodize about how fine his woman's booty is. Excellent! Such paeans to the female form make up a noble tradition in theater — so long as they pass the get-off-the-couch test.It's a bar that streaming, notably, needn't overcome. There, garland that monologue with a few zesty turns of phrase, bachata a step or two outside the reins of naturalism, and boom! It's practically prestige TV.
