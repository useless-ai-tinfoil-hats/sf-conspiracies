Slurp ramen at a Tokyo metro station... in Oakland
-----
Here's reporter Mario Cortez:Ever get the craving for a quick bowl of ramen inside a Tokyo metro station? Yeah, same.
Ramen Station in Oakland serves bowls of brothy noodles in a space mimicking Japanese rail stations, or the quick-serve ramen shops found in their vicinity. Inside, screens show train schedules while announcements cycle through the speakers. There are vending machines, ubiquitous in Japan, offering fruit drinks, canned coffee and several snacks.
