Longtime S.F. business owner attacked: 'Cuts, bruises and a lump on my head'
-----
A San Francisco business owner said he was injured Wednesday after an attack in the city's South of Market neighborhood.Mark Sackett, the owner of Box SF, a corporate events venue, said on social media that he was attacked, and he shared an image that appeared to be taken from security footage showing three people on the ground. He said he had a "massive" lump on the back of his head and cuts on his knees as a result of the attack.
