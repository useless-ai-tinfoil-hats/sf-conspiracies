Key player in S.F. school board recall to jump into supervisor race for Tenderloin
-----
For years, San Francisco resident Autumn Looijen has pushed for change through loud, uncompromising community activism — including a big win in the school board recall — but she's ready to stop yelling and start working within the system.Looijen, a moderate, announced Monday that she will be running for the District 5 seat on the Board of Supervisors, currently held by Dean Preston, the board's only Democratic Socialist, who's running for reelection.
