SFO security gates and roadways reopen after pro-Palestinian protest
-----
A protest that saw approximately 200 pro-Palestinian demonstrators streaming through San Francisco International Airport blocked multiple roadways and security gates for several hours Wednesday morning.Just after 8:30 a.m., protesters barricaded the A and G checkpoints at SFO's International Terminal to demand a permanent cease-fire in the Israel-Hamas war raging in Gaza.
