Sammy Hagar invites David Lee Roth on tour celebrating Van Halen: ‘Come out and join us’
-----
Sammy Hagar unveiled plans for the "Best of All Worlds" summer 2024 tour this week, featuring former Chickenfoot bandmates Michael Anthony and Joe Satriani, along with drummer Jason Bonham.The self-styled Red Rocker has also extended an invitation to former Van Halen singer and longtime rival David Lee Roth to join the festivities.
The tour's name pays homage to Van Halen's 2004 "Best of Both Worlds Tour," which featured both Hagar and Roth.
