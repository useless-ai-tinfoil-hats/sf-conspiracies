A man died after his vehicle plunged off a cliff near Devil's Slide, a notoriously dangerous portion of Highway 1 near Pacifica.The crash was reported just after 12:30 p.m. Thursday on southbound Highway 1 near Gray Whale Cove, a beach also known as Devil's Slide, California Highway Patrol spokesperson Mark Andrews said.
The CHP responded to the scene with a helicopter, he said. The driver, an older man, was pronounced dead at the scene around 1:45 p.m, he said.
