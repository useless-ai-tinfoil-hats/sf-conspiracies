Parts of Sequoia and Kings Canyon National Parks to reopen after lengthy closure
-----
Areas within Sequoia & Kings Canyon National Parks will reopen this season after being closed for over a year, the National Park Service announced on Friday.Cedar Grove, a portion of Kings Canyon National Park that sits along Highway 180 and the south fork of the Kings River, will reopen to the public on June 10, the Park Service wrote in a news release. The area features a visitors' center and seasonal lodge, along with several campgrounds and trailheads that lead into the Sierra Nevada
