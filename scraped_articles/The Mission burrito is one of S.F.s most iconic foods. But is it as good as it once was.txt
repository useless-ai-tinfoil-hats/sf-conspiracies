The Mission burrito is one of S.F.’s most iconic foods. But is it as good as it once was?
-----
The Mission-style burrito is San Francisco's living contribution to Mexican food — a crystallization of the working-class barrio's spirit and taste. And it deserves infinite props for becoming the most popular burrito style — not just in the Bay Area but nationwide.
