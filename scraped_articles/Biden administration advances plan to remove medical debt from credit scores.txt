Biden administration advances plan to remove medical debt from credit scores
-----
Americans would no longer have to worry about medical debt dragging down their credit scores under federal regulations proposed by the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau.If enacted, the rules would dramatically expand protections for tens of millions of Americans burdened by medical bills they can't afford.
