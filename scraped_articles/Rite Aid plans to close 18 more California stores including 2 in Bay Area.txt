Rite Aid plans to close 18 more California stores, including 2 in Bay Area
-----
Rite Aid plans to close 53 stores across the country, including 18 in California, as first reported by The Hill. The drugstore chain has already closed about 200 stores since it filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection last year amid decreasing sales and lawsuits related to opioid prescriptions.Two Bay Area Rite Aid stores are among those closing: one at 1030 South White Road, San Jose, and another at 1650 Decoto Road, Union City.
