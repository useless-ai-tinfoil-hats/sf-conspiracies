Warriors’ Kerr, Heat’s Spoelstra forged a friendship on Team USA staff
-----
Miami Heat head coach Erik Spoelstra and the Golden State Warriors' Steve Kerr have plenty in common. Each man is in his 50s, each has been the head coach of his team for quite some time and each has led his team to a conference title six times, with Spoelstra winning two NBA championships and Kerr four.Kerr is the head coach of USA Basketball and Spoelstra is one of Kerr's assistants.
