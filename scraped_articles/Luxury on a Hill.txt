Luxury on a Hill
-----
Contemporary Luxury in Santa Rosa(ad) Every detail in this recently built contemporary showplace perched on a knoll on two levels shows exceptional quality, design, and craftsmanship. The 10-foot ceilings, eight-foot doors, and trimless windows, all contribute to the feeling of spaciousness, while the sumptuous materials in every room make it feel cozy and luxurious.
