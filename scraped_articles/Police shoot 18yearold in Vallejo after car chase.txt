Police shoot 18-year-old in Vallejo after car chase
-----
American Canyon police shot an 18-year-old man man in Vallejo after a car chase on Thursday afternoon, the Police Department said.The man's injuries were non-life-threatening, officials said.
Around 3:15 p.m. Thursday afternoon, police tried to pull over a car for a vehicle code violation near southbound Highway 29 and American Canyon Road. Police said the driver did not stop, prompting officers to chase the vehicle.
