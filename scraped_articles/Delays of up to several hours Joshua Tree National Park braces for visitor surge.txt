‘Delays of up to several hours’: Joshua Tree National Park braces for visitor surge
-----
A California national park is bracing itself for an onslaught of visitors ahead of one of the most dazzling spectacles visible from planet Earth.The annual Perseids meteor shower is expected to peak between August 10 and 12. The event is sending droves of eager stargazers to Joshua Tree National Park with hopes of seeing hundreds of streaks of light hurtle across the dark sky each night.
