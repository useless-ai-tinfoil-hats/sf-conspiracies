New Giants coaches include Matt Williams, Pat Burrell, Ryan Christenson
-----
Matt Williams, who made four All-Star teams in 10 seasons with the San Francisco Giants, is returning as third-base coach on new manager Bob Melvin's coaching staff.Williams, 57, was a Gold Glove third baseman for the Giants in the late 1980s and early 1990s. He managed the Washington Nationals in 2014 and 2015, coached third under Melvin in both Oakland and San Diego and was also his teammate for two seasons in San Francisco.
