Bay Area mother found safe after missing flight to SFO for Thanksgiving
-----
LATEST Nov. 29, 10:43 a.m. A Bay Area mother who has been missing since before Thanksgiving was found safe early Wednesday, the family said in a statement. Danielle Becker Friedland, 36, was located around 2:30 a.m. Wednesday, Houston news station KHOU-TV reported."We are relieved to say that around 2:30 a.m. last night, with the help of the Houston Police Department, we were able to locate Dani, make contact with her, and she is now safe," the family said.
