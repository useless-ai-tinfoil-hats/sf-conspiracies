Bay Area indie musician found dead in suspected overdose
-----
Former Bay Area musician Jose "Luis" Vasquez, the sole member of the post-punk act the Soft Moon, was among three people found dead last week in a Los Angeles loft of a suspected fentanyl overdose.The incident also claimed the lives of John "Juan" Mendez, a 46-year-old DJ who performed as Silent Servant, and his partner, Simone Ling, 43, at her residence in the Pacific Electric Lofts on Main Street.
