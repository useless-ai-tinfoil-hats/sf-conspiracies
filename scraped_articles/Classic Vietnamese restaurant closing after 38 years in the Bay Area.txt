Classic Vietnamese restaurant closing after 38 years in the Bay Area
-----
After 38 years, a popular family-run Vietnamese restaurant in Oakland is closing at the end of the month.Owners of Le Cheval announced on social media that the restaurant's last day at 1007 Clay St. will be Sept. 30. While "this chapter comes to a close," they wrote, they will seek a new location elsewhere.
