176-year-old Bay Area home hits market for $2.1 million
-----
Built before the turn of the 20th century, 2243 Redwood Road is one of the oldest wooden buildings in Napa. It's for sale now, asking $2.1 million.The property is a mini-estate that spans the 16,182-square-foot lot. The main home features four bedrooms and four and a half bathrooms spread over two floors and 2,998 square feet. There's also a separate two-bedroom, one-bathroom guest house. The grounds include a pool and lawns, plus covered and uncovered patios.
