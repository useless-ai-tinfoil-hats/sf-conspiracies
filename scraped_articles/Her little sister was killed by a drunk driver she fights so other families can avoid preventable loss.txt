Her little sister was killed by a drunk driver; she fights so other families can avoid preventable loss
-----
Rhonda Campbell was 15 years old the day she learned that a drunken driver killed her 12-year-old sister, her only sibling.That was more than 40 years ago, but she remembers that day, Aug. 27, 1981, as if it were yesterday.
"I knew something bad was coming," Campbell said. "Walked into my house that morning and I felt like somebody just pulled the air out of my lungs."
