New Stud shows you can go home again in S.F.
-----
City life is often about loss, and trying to be graceful in the face of change. Cherished legacy businesses close, populations leave, buildings come and go, and the character of neighborhoods transforms. We make a bargain when we live in places like San Francisco to accept that some losses are inevitable.I temporarily abandoned that philosophy as I walked into the new home of the Stud at 1123-1125 Folsom St. for its grand reopening last month.
