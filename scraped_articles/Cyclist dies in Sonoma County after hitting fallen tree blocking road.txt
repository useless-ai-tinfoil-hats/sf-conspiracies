Cyclist dies in Sonoma County after hitting fallen tree blocking road
-----
A cyclist died in Sonoma County after hitting a fallen tree on a roadway, according to the California Highway Patrol.The woman was traveling west on Hauser Bridge Road, west of King Ridge Road, in Cazadero when she swung around a curve at a fast speed and struck the tree before riding off the road, CHP said in a news release. The woman was riding with a friend who called 911.
CHP responded to the call and declared the woman deceased at the scene.
