Police: ‘Critical’ shooting at Palisades Tahoe ski resort under investigation
-----
California State Park police are investigating a shooting that involved at least one officer at Palisades Tahoe ski resort Friday morning, prompting law enforcement to steer people away from the area around Olympic Village Event Center.As of Friday afternoon, few details were available about the incident, which the Placer County Sheriff's Office described as "critical" on X, formerly Twitter. Officers subsequently called the scene "stable," but warned of a large police presence.
