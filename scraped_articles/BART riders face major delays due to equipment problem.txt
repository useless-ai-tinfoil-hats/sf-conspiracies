BART riders face major delays due to equipment problem
-----
BART riders were facing major delays Friday night due to what the agency said was an equipment problem on a track near Oakland's MacArthur Station.Service was stopped between Ashby, West Oakland, Rockridge and Lake Merritt stations, BART said. AC transit buses were providing rides for passengers at those stations, in addition to passengers at Fruitvale, 19th Street and 12th Street stations in Oakland.
