Stark reality for Bay Area homebuyers: a $10,000-month mortgage
-----
Those who are looking to buy a home in the Bay Area should be ready to spend as much as $10,000 a month on their mortgage payments.That's according to a new report from real estate listing site Redfin, which estimates that a household needs to make at least $400,000 a year to afford a median-priced home in the San Francisco metropolitan division. The typical home in the area, the report says, sold for about $1.48 million in August.
