S.F. police are pulling over a lot more drivers. These citations have tripled from last year
-----
Nearly every day, Jorge Polanco peers out the window of his small candle shop in San Francisco's Crocker-Amazon neighborhood, bewildered by dangerous drivers.Speeding. Ignoring traffic lights. Double parking. At the corner of Geneva and Mission Streets, he said, it feels like "everyone ignores the law."
"If you have a license, it's supposed to be because you know the rules," Polanco said. "Not here."
But Polanco has noticed those risky drivers are being stopped much more
