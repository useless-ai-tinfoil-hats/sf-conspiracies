Skrillex and Fred Again.. announce surprise San Francisco show
-----
Skrillex and Fred Again.., two of the world's most renowned DJs and electronic music artists, have unveiled their plan to "throw a rave" right in the heart of San Francisco.The EDM titans hinted at the upcoming performance on social media last week, suggesting they have an abundance of new music they're eager to test out on a live audience.
Confirming the event details on Tuesday, May 28, the duo revealed that the last-minute show will take place in San Francisco's Civic Center
