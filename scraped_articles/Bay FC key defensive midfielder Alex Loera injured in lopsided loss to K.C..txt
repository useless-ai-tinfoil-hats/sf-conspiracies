Bay FC key defensive midfielder Alex Loera injured in lopsided loss to K.C.
-----
Bay FC lost 5-2 in Kansas City on Saturday, and a key injury only added to the setback.Defensive midfielder Alex Loera, who scored in last week's win against Seattle, exited the game in the 32nd minute with what appeared to be a knee injury. She was tended to on the field for several minutes before hobbling off. Head coach Albertin Montoya didn't have an update on Loera's status after the match.
