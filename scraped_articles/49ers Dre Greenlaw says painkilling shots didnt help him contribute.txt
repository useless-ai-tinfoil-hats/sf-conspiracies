49ers’ Dre Greenlaw says painkilling shots didn’t help him contribute
-----
Linebacker Dre Greenlaw said he took painkilling shots for ankle and shoulder injuries that didn't have the desired effect before the San Francisco 49ers' 31-17 loss to the Bengals on Oct. 29.Greenlaw consistently struggled to get up after making tackles as the 49ers allowed season highs in points and first downs (29) while Bengals quarterback Joe Burrow completed 28 of 32 passes.
"I thought maybe if I got the shots, I'd feel better," Greenlaw said. "And it didn't feel the same.
