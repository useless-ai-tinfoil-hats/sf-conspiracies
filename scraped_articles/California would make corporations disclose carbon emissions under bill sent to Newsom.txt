California would make corporations disclose carbon emissions under bill sent to Newsom
-----
California could require large corporations to disclose their greenhouse gas emissions under a bill lawmakers sent to Gavin Newsom on Tuesday.The bill, SB253 by Sen. Scott Wiener, D-San Francisco, would make California the first state to require such disclosures from companies that operate in the state if their annual revenue exceeds $1 billion.
