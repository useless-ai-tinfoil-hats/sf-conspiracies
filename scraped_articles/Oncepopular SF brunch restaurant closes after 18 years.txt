Once-popular SF brunch restaurant closes after 18 years
-----
Presidio Social Club, a restaurant housed in former military barracks overlooking the lush San Francisco national park, has closed after 18 years. Its final day of service was May 12, Mother's Day, as first reported by the San Francisco Chronicle (the Chronicle and SFGATE are both owned by Hearst but have separate newsrooms)."With profound regret and sorrow, I am sad to announce the closing of Presidio Social Club," read a note from owner Ray Tang on the Presidio Social Club website.
