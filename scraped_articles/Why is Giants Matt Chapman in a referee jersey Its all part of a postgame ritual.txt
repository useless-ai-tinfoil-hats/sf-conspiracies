Why is Giants’ Matt Chapman in a referee jersey? It’s all part of a postgame ritual
-----
WASHINGTON — Matt Chapman's get-up caught in a San Francisco Giants social-media clip after Blake Snell's no-hitter in Cincinnati generated a buzz.Why was the third baseman wearing a referee shirt and blowing a whistle during the celebration in the visiting clubhouse? Turns out, it's all part of the Giants' postgame ritual. After wins, Chapman chooses the players of the game worthy of a free throw and referees a free-throw attempt.
