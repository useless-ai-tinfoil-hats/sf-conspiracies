Brandon Aiyuk reportedly shows up to 49ers training camp, even without deal
-----
At least for the moment, Brandon Aiyuk is still a San Francisco 49er. Which means the star wide receiver, like every other player on the team, had to report for the first day of training camp on Tuesday.NFL Network's Ian Rapoport broke the news on Tuesday morning that Aiyuk would be present at training camp. Under the league's current bargaining agreement, players get fined for every mandatory training camp day they miss.
