These are the books San Francisco loved most in 2023, according to the library
-----
With the prevalence of little libraries across the city and the many beloved bookstores that anchor each neighborhood, it's clear San Francisco is a city of readers.The San Francisco Public Library recently released its list of the top books of 2023, ranked by how frequently a title was checked out during the year. Many of the titles may even be familiar to those less bookish, as they've been adapted for film or television as well.
