15-year-old boy killed, 13-year-old boy injured in Hayward shooting
-----
A 15-year-old boy was killed and a 13-year-old boy was injured in a shooting in Hayward early Wednesday, police said.The shooting occurred about 2 a.m. on Second Street near Hayward High School. The older teen, a Rodeo resident, suffered from gunshot wounds and died at the scene, police said. His name was not released.
