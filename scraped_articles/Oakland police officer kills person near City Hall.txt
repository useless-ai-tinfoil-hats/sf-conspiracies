Oakland police officer kills person near City Hall
-----
An Oakland police officer shot and killed an individual near City Hall early Tuesday morning, officials said.The officer was reporting to his 4 a.m. shift at Oakland City Hall when he "was confronted by an armed individual who opened fire," according to the Oakland Police Officers' Association. The officer fired his gun and shot the person, the association said in an emailed statement.
