Gavin Newsom increases state police in Oakland nine-fold to address rising crime
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom is sending 120 additional CHP officers to Oakland and the East Bay to crack down on crime, including carjacking, retail theft and violence.The added officers will represent a nine-fold increase in the number of state police patrolling the region, according to Newsom's office. The officers will be concentrated in Oakland, where violent crime has surged.
