Goldman Prize honors California activist credited for new clean trucking rules
-----
An industry promising next-day shipping seemed to be taking over California's Inland Empire, replacing residential blocks with warehouses and drowning neighborhoods in diesel fumes.San Bernardino County activist Andrea Vidaurre knew working-class communities were suffering most from poor air quality, and she launched a multiyear campaign that in 2023 led California to pass groundbreaking emissions limits for the trucking and rail industries.
