Arrests made after 2 children drown in pool at Bay Area day care
-----
Two women were arrested Friday for child neglect resulting in death, child endangerment and criminal negligence after two children drowned and one child was injured in a swimming pool at their South San Jose day care facility earlier this month, authorities said.Nina Fathizadeh, 41 and Shahin Gheblehshenas, 64, were the owners and operators of Happy Happy Daycare on the 1000 block of Fleetwood Drive near Almaden Hills Estates.
