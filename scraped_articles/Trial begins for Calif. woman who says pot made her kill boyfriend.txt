Trial begins for Calif. woman who says pot made her kill boyfriend
-----
The trial has begun for a California woman who said smoking marijuana caused her to stab her boyfriend over 100 times, killing him in his apartment in 2018.Bryn Spejcher, 32, is facing a felony involuntary manslaughter charge in the death of Chad O'Melia, whom Spejcher admitted to stabbing and killing in a 2018 incident at the man's apartment in Thousand Oaks, California, about 40 miles west of Los Angeles. The trial is taking place in Ventura County Superior Court. Jury selection began
