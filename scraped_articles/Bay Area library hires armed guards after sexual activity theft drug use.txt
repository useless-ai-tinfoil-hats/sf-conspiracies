Bay Area library hires armed guards after sexual activity, theft, drug use
-----
The public library in Antioch was scheduled to reopen Tuesday after it was closed over the weekend due to several "dangerous incidents" in the last few months, officials said, including theft, drug use and public sexual activity.Contra Costa County officials announced the temporary closure in a statement Friday, citing "repeated dangerous incidents in the last few months that have threatened the safety and security of patrons and staff." They said it would reopen Tuesday at noon.
