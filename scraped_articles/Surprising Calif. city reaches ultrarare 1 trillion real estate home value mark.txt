Surprising Calif. city reaches ultra-rare $1 trillion real estate home value mark
-----
A surprising Southern California city has reached an eyebrow-raising new milestone. Anaheim, famously the home of Disneyland in Orange County, is now one of only eight trillion-dollar cities in America. Reaching that bold threshold means the combined values of all the houses in Anaheim are now worth, together, a trillion dollars.
