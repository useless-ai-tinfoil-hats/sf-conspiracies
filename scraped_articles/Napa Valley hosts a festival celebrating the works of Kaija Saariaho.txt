Napa Valley hosts a festival celebrating the works of Kaija Saariaho
-----
New Music Decanted, a Wine Country chamber music ensemble, plans to perform the complete works of the late Kaija Saariaho at its inaugural festival this summer.The New Music Festival is set to take place from Aug. 16-18 at the Napa Valley College Performing Arts Center, featuring all 44 chamber music works by the celebrated Finnish composer, who died in 2023.
