New 'Megalopolis' trailer names and shames Francis Ford Coppola's critics
-----
Bay Area director Francis Ford Coppola has had quite the journey making his latest film "Megalopolis," and the newest chapter includes a direct confrontation with his critics.The self-financed film, a high-minded concept about a futuristic version of New York City featuring parallels to the fall of Rome, has been a dream of Coppola's for decades. The production has been plagued by controversy, including accusations of unwanted sexual advances by Coppola, gratuitous cannabis use on set
