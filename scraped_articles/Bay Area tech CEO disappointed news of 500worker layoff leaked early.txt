Bay Area tech CEO 'disappointed' news of 500-worker layoff leaked early
-----
Twitch, the popular San Francisco-based video streamer, is laying off about 500 workers, CEO Daniel Clancy confirmed in a published blog post on Wednesday. In the post, he took a moment to admonish his staff for leaking news of the layoff to the press.Bloomberg News first reported the layoff round on Tuesday night, writing that the cuts would hit about 35% of Twitch's staff.
