Singaporeans are obsessed with this tiny new Bay Area restaurant
-----
A common order echoed inside the Bay Area's most exciting new Singaporean restaurant on opening day: "We'll take one of everything."Customers waltzed out balancing stacks of boxes against their chins. Staff eagerly suggested additional orders of chicken curry to reheat at home later in the week. It's been a routine sight at Mama Judy Singapore Hawker, which opened last week in downtown Alameda.
