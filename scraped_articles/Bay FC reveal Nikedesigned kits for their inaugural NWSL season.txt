Bay FC reveal Nike-designed kits for their inaugural NWSL season
-----
Bay FC revealed their inaugural kits for the 2024 NWSL season at the team's media day in San Jose on Monday.The jerseys are designed by Nike and sport the logo of Sutter Health, a Northern California-based nonprofit health system. The Bay FC crest is on the left side of the chest of the white jersey that features a splash of gray zig-zagging across it. The Nike swoosh is on the right, with league sponsor Ally bank on the left sleeve.
