Google lays off hundreds of Bay Area workers, shuts down Mountain View child care center
-----
Google laid off hundreds of workers from multiple corporate divisions Wednesday night. The company is also letting go of a few dozen Bay Area teachers.The tech giant is shuttering a child care facility not far from its headquarters in Mountain View and laying off its 73 employees, according to a WARN notice filed with the city on Dec. 8 and obtained by SFGATE on Thursday.
