'S—t mercilessly': Oakland's official bird is a beautiful menace
-----
The first time I heard it, I thought it was human. The sound — a cross between a feral cat and one of the many people I've seen gagging outside my local karaoke bar on a Friday night — stopped me dead in my tracks.Before I had time to think, torrential white vomit fell from the sky, narrowly missing my head by a few inches and splattering on the ground in front of me. That's when I looked up and saw them: hordes of bizarre, beautiful-looking black-crowned night herons and snowy egrets
