Cal coach commits to start redshirt freshman QB Fernando Mendoza vs. Utah
-----
When he entered Cal's interview room late Saturday night, quarterback Fernando Mendoza earnestly introduced himself to the assembled media members.The announced crowd of 34,930 at Memorial Stadium surely had been impressed by the way Mendoza introduced himself on the field against No. 15 Oregon State on that warm evening.
Though the Beavers prevailed 52-40, the redshirt freshman distinguished himself in his first start in college football. He completed 21 of 32 passes for 207 yards
