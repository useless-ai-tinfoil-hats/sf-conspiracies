While many award-winning hamburgers are usually found in big cities, one of the best burgers in California actually resides in the East Bay suburb of Albany, Calif. Don't just take it from me, though; the Michelin Guide was first to acknowledge the burger at this neighborhood bistro and named it one of the best burgers in all of California.Much to my delight, I absolutely concur.
