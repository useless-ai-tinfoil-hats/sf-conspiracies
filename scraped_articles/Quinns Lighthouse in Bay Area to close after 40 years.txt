Quinn's Lighthouse in Bay Area to close after 40 years
-----
Quinn's Lighthouse, a cherished seafood restaurant in Oakland, is closing its doors after 40 years.The restaurant, which is best known for its unique location within a historic lighthouse, is set to close Wednesday following a lack of the corporate events and game day crowds that it saw before the pandemic, a manager told the San Francisco Chronicle.
