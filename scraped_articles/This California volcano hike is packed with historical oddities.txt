This California volcano hike is packed with historical oddities
-----
Sometimes a woman just wants to be by herself.Such was the case for Mary Downen, a Lake County widow who relocated to a wooden cabin on the slopes of the dormant volcano Mount Konocti in the early 1900s. For nearly three decades, she resided high above Clear Lake, growing vegetables, drinking rainwater and signaling to family down below with a mirror. The daily reflection of light meant: "I'm OK!"
