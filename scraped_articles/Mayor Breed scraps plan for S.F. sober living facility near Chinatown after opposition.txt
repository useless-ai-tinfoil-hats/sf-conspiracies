Mayor Breed scraps plan for S.F. sober living facility near Chinatown after opposition
-----
San Francisco Mayor London Breed on Tuesday scrapped a plan for a sober living facility on the edge of Chinatown for people recovering from addiction after vigorous opposition from local advocates and merchants.The proposal to transform the 150-unit hotel at 935 Kearny St. was the latest push by Breed to fight the drug crisis that caused record overdose deaths in the city last year and sparked anger over open-air drug dealing and use.
