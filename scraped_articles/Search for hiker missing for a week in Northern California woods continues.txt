Search for hiker missing for a week in Northern California woods continues
-----
Calaveras County search and rescue teams were still combing a wooded area in Northern California Tuesday where a woman went missing while hiking last week.Rescue teams have been searching for Ann Herford, a 66-year-old travel nurse from Michigan, who was reported missing on Nov. 14 on the Arnold Rim Trail in Calaveras County, Sheriff's Office officials said.
