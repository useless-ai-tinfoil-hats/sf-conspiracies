Newsom sending more California National Guard troops to border for drug enforcement
-----
Gov. Gavin Newsom is increasing the number of California National Guard service members working on drug enforcement at the Mexican border by 50%, he announced Thursday.The increase will bring the number of Cal Guard members helping with drug enforcement at the Southern border from 40 to 60, said Maj. Gen. Matthew Beevers, who leads the Cal Guard.
