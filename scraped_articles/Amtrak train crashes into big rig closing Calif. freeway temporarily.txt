Amtrak train crashes into big rig, closing Calif. freeway temporarily
-----
An Amtrak commuter train traveling to San Diego crashed into a big rig on Dec. 12, around 6:20 p.m.The Pacific Surfliner train crashed into an "unoccupied tractor-trailer" near Camarillo, Calif., Amtrak told SFGATE in a statement. The accident occurred along state Route 118, according to a tweet from California Highway Patrol Moorpark.
There were 95 passengers and five employees on board.
