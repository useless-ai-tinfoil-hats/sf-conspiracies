Steph Curry-funded Howard golf team coming to Bay Area for USF event
-----
Stephen Curry's adopted golf team is coming to the Bay Area.The second annual Howard-USF Intercollegiate, a unique tournament designed to give unheralded college programs a chance to play at marquee venues, will take place Monday and Tuesday at Harding Park. The inaugural event was held last year at TPC Potomac in Maryland, not far from Howard's campus in Washington, D.C.
