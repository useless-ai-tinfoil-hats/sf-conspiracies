One person arrested in connection with Oakland shooting near Temescal
-----
Oakland police arrested one person following a burst of gunfire Saturday night near the Temescal neighborhood.At about 5:45 p.m., officers responded to the scene on the 4000 block of Telegraph Avenue. When they arrived they found a gun and evidence of a shooting, but nobody was injured, police said.
The investigation is ongoing and as of Saturday night officers said they had one person in custody in relation to the shooting.
