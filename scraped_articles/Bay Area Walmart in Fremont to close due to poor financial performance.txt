Bay Area Walmart in Fremont to close due to poor financial performance
-----
Walmart announced on Thursday that it will close one of its Fremont stores due to poor financial performance.The store at 40580 Albrae St. "will officially close to the public on May 24," KRON4 reported. A Walmart spokesperson told the outlet that underperforming in profit margins drove the decision.
