Bay Area man dies after falling 120 feet while hiking Hawaii trail
-----
A Bay Area man who was vacationing with his family in Hawaii died after falling 120 feet below a waterfall last week, Hawaii officials said in a news release.Spokespersons from the Kauai Police Department identified the man as 66-year-old Mark Debarr, who had apparently fallen off the trail at Waipoo Falls in Kokee State Park just after 10 a.m. on Friday.
Debarr was a longtime resident of Concord, according to public records.
An autopsy is pending, according to Hawaii
