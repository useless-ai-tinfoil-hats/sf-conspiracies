Hundreds of lightning strikes reported across California from tropical moisture
-----
Hundreds of lightning strikes were reported across California on Monday and Tuesday as remnant moisture from a tropical storm surged into the Golden State and triggered thunderstorms.Over 1,000 lightning strikes were recorded across three hours in eastern Fresno County, sparking at least 5 wildfires, according to Cal Fire.
The lightning occurred across the baking hot San Joaquin Valley, but National Weather Service forecaster J.P. Kalb said the strikes were concentrated in Fresno
