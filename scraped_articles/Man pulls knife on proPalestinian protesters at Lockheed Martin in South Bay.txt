Man pulls knife on pro-Palestinian protesters at Lockheed Martin in South Bay
-----
Bay Area protesters said a man brandished a knife and attempted to run over them during a Sunnyvale demonstration against the war in Gaza on Thursday morning.About 250 people participated in a protest led by Bay Area Palestine Solidarity, Bay City News reported. Some blocked three employee entrances to Lockheed Martin's campus in Sunnyvale using traffic cones and some laid on the roadway, while 50 made themselves into a human chain, Bay City News said.
