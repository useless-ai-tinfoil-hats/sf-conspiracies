Bay Area tech layoffs: Google and Rivian cut more jobs
-----
According to official state government filings, tech giant Google and electric vehicle manufacturer Rivian Automotive plan to cut dozens more jobs in the Bay Area.Google has announced it will eliminate 57 positions across various roles in San Francisco, including managers, engineers and analysts, as per a WARN notice filed with California authorities.
