49ers superfan Jeremy Renner is obsessed with the Super Bowl
-----
When the San Francisco 49ers head to Super Bowl LVIII, they know the biggest celebrity attending the game won't be supporting them. But that doesn't mean they won't have some solid star power. Movie star Jeremy Renner is a well-known big 49ers fan and recently told People Magazine not only would he very likely go to the Super Bowl, but he'd feel pretty good about the Niners' chances regardless of opponent."It's good to have a squad to root for like the Niners, man.
