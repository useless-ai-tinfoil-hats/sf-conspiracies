'A major issue': Marine mammal harassment on the rise across California
-----
Last July, the Marine Mammal Center was flooded with a series of disturbing calls.A juvenile sea lion was trapped on Ocean Beach in San Francisco, emaciated and surrounded by a crowd of people trying to feed it with off-leash dogs running around nearby. When trained responders arrived at the scene and took the animal back to the Sausalito-based organization's hospital, they discovered it was one of at least 70 pinnipeds that would wash up along the California coastline that summer,
