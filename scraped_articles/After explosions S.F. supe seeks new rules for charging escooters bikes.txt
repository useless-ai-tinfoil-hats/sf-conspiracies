After explosions, S.F. supe seeks new rules for charging e-scooters, bikes
-----
After dozens of fires caused by combusting batteries used in electric scooters and bikes in recent years, the San Francisco Board of Supervisors is set to consider imposing new restrictions on the rechargeable devices on Tuesday.The lithium-ion batteries used in electric bikes, scooters, skateboards and hoverboards would be subject to stricter rules while charging or not in use under an ordinance proposed by San Francisco Supervisor Aaron Peskin.
