‘Staggeringly’ better market: SF real estate could finally be bouncing back
-----
The rain and wind pummeling San Francisco lately haven't dampened the real estate market: Despite the foul weather, Compass agent Alexander Fromm Lurie has noticed lots of people are showing up to open houses, umbrellas in tow."The rainy days this year have brought more traffic than the sunny days of Q4 last year," he said. (Fromm Lurie's brother, Daniel Lurie, is a candidate for SF mayor.)
