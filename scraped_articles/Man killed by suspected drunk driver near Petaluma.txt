Man killed by suspected drunk driver near Petaluma
-----
A man was killed by a suspected drunk driver in a head-on collision Saturday evening near Petaluma, according to authorities.California Highway Patrol officers responded to the fatal crash shortly after 5 p.m. Saturday on Roblar Road. A woman driving a Volvo SUV was reportedly traveling westbound on Roblar Road when her vehicle drifted into oncoming traffic and crashed into a Toyota truck traveling eastbound.
The man driving the truck was pronounced dead at the scene. The Sonoma
