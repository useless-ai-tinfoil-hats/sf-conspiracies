Pride takes over San Francisco streets with eye-catching costumes and fantastic floats
-----
A month of Pride festivities was capped off today with a joyous parade down Market Street. The annual event, which reportedly draws over a million people each year with an economic impact of over $500 million, is the city's largest-scale public event.
