Latest protests on UC campuses: Workers demand higher pay, help with housing
-----
Thousands of University of California workers rallied on campuses up and down the state Wednesday, but not to protest Israel's war in Gaza, as students have done in this season of angry demonstrations.There is plenty else to be angry about, they said.
"No housing? No peace!" chanted dozens of employees waving signs and pounding drums in the chilly breeze outside of UCSF's hospital on Parnassus Avenue in San Francisco.
