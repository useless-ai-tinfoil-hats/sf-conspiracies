Muni bus driver fatally strikes pedestrian in SF, police say
-----
A Muni bus driver fatally struck a pedestrian in San Francisco early Thursday morning, officials said.The San Francisco Police Department said it responded to the crash at 12:51 a.m. near O'Farrell Street and Van Ness Avenue.
Officers found the pedestrian in the middle of the road. The individual was declared dead at the scene, according to an emailed news release.
The driver cooperated and stayed at the scene, SFPD said.
