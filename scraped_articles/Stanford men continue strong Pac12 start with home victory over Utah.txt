Stanford men continue strong Pac-12 start with home victory over Utah
-----
Three weeks into the Pac-12 season, Stanford finds itself alone in third place.The Cardinal held off Utah 79-73 at Maples Pavilion on Sunday afternoon.
Stanford (9-7, 4-2) got 20 points and 11 rebounds from forward/center Maxime Raynaud to pick up its fourth win in five games.
