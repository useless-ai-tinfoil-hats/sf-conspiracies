Outbreak of shigella strikes San Jose homeless encampments
-----
An outbreak of shigellosis, a highly infectious gastrointestinal infection, is sweeping through homeless encampments in San Jose, public health officials announced Tuesday.Three cases were confirmed from June 3 to 17, with four others under evaluation and at least 19 more suspected, according to Dr. Monika Roy, Santa Clara County's assistant health officer. Two cases have led to hospitalization.
