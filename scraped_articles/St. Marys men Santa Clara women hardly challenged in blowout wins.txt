St. Mary's men, Santa Clara women hardly challenged in blowout wins
-----
St. Mary's did not trail and got a career-high 20 points off the bench from sophomore center Harry Wessels in a 71-34 blowout of Middle Tennessee State in Moraga on Tuesday night.The 7-foot-1 Australian went 10-of-13 from the field for the Gaels (7-5). Alex Ducas scored 13 points while shooting 5-for-8, including 3-for-6 from beyond the arc, and added five rebounds. Mitchell Saxen finished with 13 points.
