‘No other choice’: 14-year-old Italian restaurant closing in downtown S.F.
-----
Downtown San Francisco is losing another one of its long-running restaurants: Barbacco.The more casual sister restaurant to Perbacco, Barbacco will permanently close on Sept. 29 after 14 years. Owner Umberto Gibin said there are simply not enough people in the Financial District to sustain the Italian restaurant.
