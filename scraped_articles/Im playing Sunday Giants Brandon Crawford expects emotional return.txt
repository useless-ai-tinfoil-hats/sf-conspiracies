'I'm playing Sunday': Giants' Brandon Crawford expects emotional return
-----
All signs point toward Brandon Crawford coming off the injured list Sunday and playing the final game of the San Francisco Giants' season."I'm playing Sunday," the four-time Gold Glove shortstop said.
Crawford, who hasn't said whether he'll continue playing beyond 2023, was removed from the roster Thursday with a strained right hamstring and has been diligently working to recover in time for when he's eligible to be reinstated, which is Sunday.
