30 Films, 49 Guests, and 26 Premieres – DocLands 2024
-----
Join us for the 2024 DocLands Documentary Film Festival, where inspiration meets entertainment from May 2-5 at the esteemed Smith Rafael Film Center. Immerse yourself in 30 captivating titles, including 26 premieres—4 World, 1 North American, and 2 US debuts—curated to ignite your curiosity and celebrate the human experience. With 49 filmmakers and film protagonists slated to attend, this vibrant celebration of nonfiction storytelling promises diverse perspectives and impassioned discussions.
