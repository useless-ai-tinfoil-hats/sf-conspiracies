Their first S.F. restaurant won a Michelin star. Now this chef duo is back
-----
After winning their first Michelin star and suddenly splitting from their San Francisco restaurant, a chef couple is back with a new fine-dining establishment.David Fisher and Serena Chow Fisher, previously of Marlena in San Francisco, are opening 7 Adams at 1963 Sutter St. in San Francisco on Wednesday, Nov. 1. They teamed up with San Francisco's Hi Neighbor Hospitality Group, known for Mama in Oakland and Trestle and the Vault in San Francisco, on the new restaurant.
