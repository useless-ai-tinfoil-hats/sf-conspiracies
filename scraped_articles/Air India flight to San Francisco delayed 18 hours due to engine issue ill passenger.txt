Air India flight to San Francisco delayed 18 hours due to engine issue, ill passenger
-----
An Air India flight from Mumbai to San Francisco was delayed by 18 hours Friday due to a potential technical problem and at least one passenger falling ill while the plane taxied on the runway for six hours.Flight AI-179 was scheduled to depart from Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport at 4 p.m. local time but was delayed when an engine problem was reported, according to an Air India spokesperson.
