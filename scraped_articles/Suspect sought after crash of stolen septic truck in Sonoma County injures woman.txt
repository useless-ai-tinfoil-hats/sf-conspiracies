Suspect sought after crash of stolen septic truck in Sonoma County injures woman
-----
Sonoma County authorities are asking for the public's help to locate a man who crashed a stolen septic truck in what police are calling a hit-and-run incident.A man stole the septic truck and lost control while driving it in rural Sonoma County, California Highway Patrol announced on Facebook last week. He struck and injured the driver of an oncoming car before fleeing, CHP officials said.
