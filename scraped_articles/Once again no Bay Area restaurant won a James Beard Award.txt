Once again, no Bay Area restaurant won a James Beard Award
-----
The James Beard Foundation awards, considered the "Oscars" of the food world, named its winners from the Lyric Opera of Chicago on Monday evening, and Bay Area restaurants and chefs walked away empty handed for the second year in a row.The best chef California award had two Bay Area finalists: Geoff Davis of Burdell in Oakland and Rogelio Garcia of Auro in Calistoga.
