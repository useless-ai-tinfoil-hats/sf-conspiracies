Giants’ Patrick Bailey on concussion IL, will miss at least a week
-----
PHILADELPHIA — The San Francisco Giants will be without Patrick Bailey for at least a week.The catcher was placed on the seven-day concussion injured list on Saturday after Bailey had blurred vision and exited Friday's game in the second inning.
Bailey took an especially hard foul ball off his face mask in the first inning. Pitcher Jordan Hicks said Bailey looked off, noting that he swiped at a pitch to catch it — something he hasn't seen the catcher do before.
