Lane closures coming to major SF Bay Area highway this week
-----
Drivers traveling on Highway 101 in the South Bay this week could face traffic and delays.Lanes will be closed along a stretch spanning about 12 miles of the major freeway for four days, during daytime hours only, to allow work crews to sweep the highway, according to Caltrans.
