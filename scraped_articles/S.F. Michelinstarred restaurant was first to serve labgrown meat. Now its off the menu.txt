San Francisco's only restaurant offering chicken meat made from cells cultivated in a bioreactor will no longer serve the item.Berkeley's Upside Foods announced the end of its monthly dinners at Bar Crenn, from acclaimed S.F. chef Dominique Crenn, featuring its cultivated chicken, sometimes referred to as lab-grown meat. Instead the company will launch traveling events "with chef Crenn and others." An Upside Foods representative did not share dates and venues for these events.
