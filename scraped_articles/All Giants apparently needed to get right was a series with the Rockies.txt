All Giants apparently needed to get right was a series with the Rockies
-----
Many good things happened for the San Francisco Giants in their series against their personal punching bags, the Rockies.The Giants got a pep talk from the president of baseball operations, they ended a six-game losing streak, they got their offense back on track and, Sunday, they had another neat development: Keaton Winn bounced back from one rough inning with a superb overall effort to forge a 6-3 victory, complete the three-game sweep and get his first major-league win.
