Pieces of Disney's closed 'Star Wars' hotel are coming to Disneyland
-----
A long time ago — March 2022 — in a galaxy far, far away, Disney opened its most immersive and wildly ambitious project. For $5,000 or more, "Star Wars" fans could board the Galactic Starcruiser, a two-night experience in which guests acted out their Jedi, Resistance and First Order fantasies, following storylines and carrying out missions on a trip through the cosmos.
