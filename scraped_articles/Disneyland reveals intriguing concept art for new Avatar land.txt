Disneyland reveals intriguing concept art for new 'Avatar' land
-----
The first concept art has been released for Disneyland's proposed new land based on the blockbuster "Avatar" movies. The rendering isn't a carbon copy of the Pandora land in Disney World's Animal Kingdom; instead, it's themed more closely to the recent sequel, "Avatar: The Way of Water."The teaser was dropped as part of Wednesday's company shareholders meeting.
