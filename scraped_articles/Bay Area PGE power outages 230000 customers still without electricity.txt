Bay Area PG&E power outages: 230,000 customers still without electricity
-----
More than 235,000 Pacific Gas and Electric Co. homes and businesses across the Bay Area remained without power Monday morning in the wake of a powerful atmospheric river-fueled storm Sunday that led to street closures, landslides and downed trees.One person died in Santa Cruz County on Sunday when a tree fell on a home, trapping the person inside, authorities said Monday.
