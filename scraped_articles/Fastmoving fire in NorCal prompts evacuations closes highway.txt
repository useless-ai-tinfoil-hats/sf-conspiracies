Fast-moving fire in NorCal prompts evacuations, closes highway
-----
A fast-moving wildfire in the Sierra Foothills of Northern California is tearing through grassland, triggering evacuations and closing a major highway, officials said.The 5,000 acre Aero Fire was first reported at 3:23 p.m. Monday at Rock Creek Road and Highway 4 just north of the town Copperopolis in Calaveras County, according to Cal Fire.
