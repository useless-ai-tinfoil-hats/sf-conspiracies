Imperiled monarch butterflies cling to survival in California
-----
Though it doesn't look like it, the monarch butterfly is a true warrior.Despite facing an onslaught of existential threats like habitat loss, drought and severe winter storms, the population is still hanging on, conservationists announced Tuesday. However, recent data shows that it's still far from what it once was, and experts say that there are few protections in place to help them return to normal levels.
