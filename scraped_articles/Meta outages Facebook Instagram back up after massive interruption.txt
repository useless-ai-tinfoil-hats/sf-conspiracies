Meta outages: Facebook, Instagram back up after massive interruption
-----
Hundreds of thousands of users reported outages Tuesday morning on Facebook and Instagram, social media sites owned by the company Meta.At 7:30 a.m. more than half a million people reported trouble accessing Facebook, according to Downdetector, which tracks website outages. Another 80,000 people reported trouble accessing Instagram.
Meta spokesperson Andy Stone took to another social media site, X, formerly known as Twitter, to address the problem.
