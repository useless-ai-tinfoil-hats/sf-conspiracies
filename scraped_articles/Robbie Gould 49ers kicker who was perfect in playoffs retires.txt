Robbie Gould, 49ers kicker who was perfect in playoffs, retires
-----
Robbie Gould, 41, one of the most accurate kickers in NFL history who never missed a field-goal attempt in the playoffs, announced his retirement Thursday after spending the final six seasons of his 18-year career with the San Francisco 49ers.Gould, who made his announcement on the Players' Tribune, ranks among the top 10 in league history in field goals made (447, eighth), field-goal percentage (86.5, ninth) and points scored (1,961, 10th).
