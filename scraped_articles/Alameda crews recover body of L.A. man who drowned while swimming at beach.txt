Alameda crews recover body of L.A. man who drowned while swimming at beach
-----
Alameda Fire Department crews said they found a Los Angeles man's body underwater Friday evening after he started having trouble swimming at Encinal Beach.The fire department received a request for a water rescue at 190 Central Ave. at about 4:30 p.m. Friday, the agency said.
Before fire department crews arrived, a 32-year-old man, whom the Alameda County Sheriff's Coroner's Office identified as Jonathan Santiesteban Leon, went under the water.
