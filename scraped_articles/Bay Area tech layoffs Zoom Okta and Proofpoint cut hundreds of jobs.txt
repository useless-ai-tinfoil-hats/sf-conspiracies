Bay Area tech layoffs: Zoom, Okta and Proofpoint cut hundreds of jobs
-----
Zoom and the identity security company Okta announced layoffs on Thursday, marking the latest workforce reductions in the Bay Area tech industry.The San Jose-based video communications platform Zoom, which became a remote-work staple during the COVID-19 pandemic, confirmed it will cut approximately 150 jobs, representing less than 2% of its workforce. The move is part of a strategic effort to enhance efficiency, according to a company spokesperson.
