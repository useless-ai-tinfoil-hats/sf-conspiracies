Iconic Bay Area home by architect of jazz legend's 'house in the sky' for sale for $1.6M
-----
Just over 100 years ago, on July 9, 1924, Beverley Thorne was born. He would grow up to become one of the most innovative mid-century modern architects. His houses showcase an iconic — and often gravity-defying — aesthetic. Case in point: Thorne's Music House, for sale now in the East Bay community of Kensington, asking $1.6 million.Beverley Thorne's houses are "very rare and sought after," listing agent Mike Plotkowski of Compass wrote in an email to SFGATE.
