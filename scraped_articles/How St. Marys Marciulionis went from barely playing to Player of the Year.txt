How St. Mary’s Marciulionis went from barely playing to Player of the Year
-----
SPOKANE, Wash. — More than two years ago, before Augustas Marciulionis ever suited up in a game for St. Mary's, former Golden State Warriors forward Chris Mullin attended a Gaels practice to check out his former teammate's son.Mullin remains good friends with Sarunas Marciulionis, a pioneer in the migration of European players to the NBA. Mullin told Marciulionis he was going to Moraga to see Augustas and would reconnect afterward.
