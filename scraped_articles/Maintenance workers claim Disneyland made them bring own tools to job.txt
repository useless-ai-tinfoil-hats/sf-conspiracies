Maintenance workers claim Disneyland made them bring own tools to job
-----
Disney is being sued by more than 100 current and former employees of its Disneyland hotels in a class-action lawsuit.The lawsuit alleges that the entertainment giant required maintenance workers at its hotels to provide their own tools and equipment. Under California law, employers must pay at least twice the minimum wage if they require workers to bring their own tools to the job. The lawsuit alleges that not only did Disney fail to do so, it also did not provide required rest periods
