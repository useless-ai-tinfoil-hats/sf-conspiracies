Stern Grove Festival sues the city of San Francisco over massive flood
-----
A massive flood that covered a venue in mud and canceled a concert three years ago has pitted San Francisco's oldest outdoor music series against the city, spilling over into a lawsuit filed Friday.The Stern Grove Festival Association, which has been hosting free, open-air concerts in the city park of the same name since 1932, sued the city on Friday, demanding more than $335,000 in damages after a 2021 water main break flooded the historic venue.
