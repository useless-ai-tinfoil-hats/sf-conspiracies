Critical darlings of Wine Country opening new pasta restaurant
-----
Here's Food + Wine editor Janelle Bitker:The team behind one of Sonoma County's best restaurants is opening a new spot focused on fresh pasta and wood-grilled meats.
Stella, slated to open late next summer, will be the Cal-Italian sister to the Glen Ellen Star. ("Stella" means "star" in Italian.) Owners Ari and Erin Wieswasser and Spencer and Ashley Waite are taking over the former Cafe Citti location at 9049 Sonoma Hwy. in Kenwood, as first reported by Sonoma Magazine.
