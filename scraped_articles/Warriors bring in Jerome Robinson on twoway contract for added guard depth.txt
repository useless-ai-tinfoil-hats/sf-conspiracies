Warriors bring in Jerome Robinson on two-way contract for added guard depth
-----
The Golden State Warriors announced Friday that they have signed guard Jerome Robinson to two-way contract.Robinson is a 6-foot-4 guard who has spent the past two seasons playing for the Warriors' G League affiliate in Santa Cruz. In the 2022-23 season, he averaged 14.8 points, 4.3 rebounds and 4.9 assists while shooting 46.3% from the field and 36.5% from 3-point range.
