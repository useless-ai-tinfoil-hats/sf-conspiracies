Body of Sen. Dianne Feinstein arrives at SFO
-----
The San Francisco fog held back for blue skies to welcome the body of late Sen. Dianne Feinstein back to her home city Saturday night.Feinstein died of natural causes in her Washington, D.C., home in the small hours of Friday morning Eastern Standard Time. A military flight transporting her body back to San Francisco landed at San Francisco International Airport at 5:58 p.m. Saturday.
