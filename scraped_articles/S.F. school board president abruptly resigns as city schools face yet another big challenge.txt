S.F. school board president abruptly resigns as city schools face yet another big challenge
-----
The president of the San Francisco school board abruptly resigned Friday, citing health and personal reasons, a decision that adds another element of drama to a district facing one of the most difficult years in recent memory.Lainie Motamedi submitted her resignation Friday morning, with her departure from public office effective as of noon.
