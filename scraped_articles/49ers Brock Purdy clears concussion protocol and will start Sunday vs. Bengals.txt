49ers' Brock Purdy clears concussion protocol and will start Sunday vs. Bengals
-----
After a week of uncertainty for the San Francisco 49ers, the team announced on Saturday that quarterback Brock Purdy has cleared concussion protocol and will start on Sunday against the Cincinnati Bengals.Purdy suffered a concussion last Monday in the 49ers' loss to the Minnesota Vikings, although he never came out of the game and did not start displaying symptoms until the team's flight home.
