Oakland to start dishing out $100 fines for this parking violation
-----
Parking on sidewalks throughout Oakland is about to receive much stricter enforcement from the city.Oakland's Department of Transportation (OakDOT) announced this week that the city will begin citing people who park their cars on the sidewalk starting Oct. 1, Oaklandside first reported. The citations will first be delivered as warnings, and then $100 fines will be issued starting Jan. 1.
