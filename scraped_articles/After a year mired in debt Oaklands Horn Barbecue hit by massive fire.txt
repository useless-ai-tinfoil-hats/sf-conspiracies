After a year mired in debt, Oakland's Horn Barbecue hit by massive fire
-----
A massive fire ravaged West Oakland's Horn Barbecue early Tuesday morning, leaving the building unsafe for occupation, according to Michael Hunt, a spokesperson for the Oakland Fire Department.The fire started around 4:19 a.m. at 2534 Mandela Parkway, and firefighters contained it within 30 minutes, Hunt told SFGATE. No employees had been cooking when the fire started, according to Hunt. There were no injuries, and the fire didn't spread to other businesses or properties in the area.
