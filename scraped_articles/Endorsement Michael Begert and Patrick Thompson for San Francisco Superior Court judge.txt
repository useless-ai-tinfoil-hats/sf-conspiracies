Endorsement: Michael Begert and Patrick Thompson for San Francisco Superior Court judge
-----
Most California Superior Court judges are appointed by the governor, and only after a rigorous vetting process that can take months. The state Commission on Judicial Nominees Evaluation makes hundreds of calls to ascertain a candidate's character and competency. Only after surviving these inquiries is a candidate's application then forwarded to the governor, who makes the final decision on whether to grant a judgeship.That's the way it usually goes.
