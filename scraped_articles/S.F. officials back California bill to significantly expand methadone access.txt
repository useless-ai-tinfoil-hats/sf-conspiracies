S.F. officials back California bill to significantly expand methadone access
-----
In the face of rising fentanyl overdose deaths, San Francisco officials are pushing to change state law to expand the types of clinics that can dispense methadone and make it easier for patients to get take-home doses of the drug.Assembly Member Matt Haney, D-San Francisco, called the measure his "most important bill of the year."
