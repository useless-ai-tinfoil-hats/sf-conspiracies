Letters: Can self-driving cars prevent accidents like one in West Portal that killed S.F. family?
-----
When self-driving cars get into an accident, the hysteria around them and their safety is deafening. With the horrible March 16 accident in San Francisco, one has to wonder if the 79-year-old woman suspected of driving the car that killed a family in West Portal would not have been better off riding in a self-driving vehicle.How many lives would be spared if self-driving cars were the norm?
