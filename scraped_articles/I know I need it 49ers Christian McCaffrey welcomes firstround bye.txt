'I know I need it'; 49ers' Christian McCaffrey welcomes first-round bye
-----
LANDOVER, Md. — After starting the season's first 16 games and racking up an NFL-high 339 touches in the process, Christian McCaffrey appeared visibly relieved Sunday when he looked ahead to something foreign: rest.The San Francisco 49ers' workhorse running back was likely among the most thrilled in the postgame locker room when the 49ers secured the NFC's No. 1 seed and a first-round bye when the Eagles lost 35-31 to the Cardinals.
