Old-school punk bands to descend on Cow Palace with traveling festival
-----
Southern California's Punk in the Park music and craft beer festival will mark its fourth year with the American Road Trip, a multicity tour boasting a lineup of old-school hardcore and punk acts.Organized by Brew Ha Ha Productions, based in Orange County, the all-ages festival is slated to kick off on May 4 at the Cow Palace in Daly City, just outside of San Francisco, with additional dates announced soon.
