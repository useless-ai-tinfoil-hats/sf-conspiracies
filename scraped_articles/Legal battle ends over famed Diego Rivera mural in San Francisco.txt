Legal battle ends over famed Diego Rivera mural in San Francisco
-----
The San Francisco Museum of Modern Art and City College of San Francisco have reached a settlement regarding the two organizations' legal battle over "Pan American Unity," a large mural by renowned artist Diego Rivera.On Wednesday, the two organizations announced plans to withdraw any pending litigation against each other over fees associated with transporting the massive fresco from CCSF to a free public gallery at SFMOMA in 2021.
