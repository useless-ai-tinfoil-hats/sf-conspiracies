How San Francisco’s ‘queer abundance’ inspires the fiction of R.O. Kwon
-----
A night out at the San Francisco Ballet changed author R.O. Kwon's life — and the trajectory of her latest novel, "Exhibit.""I had an ecstatic experience," Kwon told the Chronicle from her home in San Francisco. "I really felt as though the bodies onstage were cell-to-cell speaking to my body in a way that I had not quite encountered before."
