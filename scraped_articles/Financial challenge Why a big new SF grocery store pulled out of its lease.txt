'Financial challenge': Why a big new SF grocery store pulled out of its lease
-----
Island Pacific Supermarket's plans to open its first San Francisco store are no more: The Filipino grocery chain has pulled out of its lease in the Outer Mission, according to the San Francisco Business Times.
