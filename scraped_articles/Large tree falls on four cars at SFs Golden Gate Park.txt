Large tree falls on four cars at SF's Golden Gate Park
-----
A large tree fell on four cars at Golden Gate Park in San Francisco on Monday.Emergency crews were on the scene of 19th Avenue and Martin Luther King Jr. Drive, evaluating the cars' occupants, the San Francisco Fire Department said.
Fire officials said 11 people are safe and two of them had non-life threatening injuries as of 4:40 p.m.
