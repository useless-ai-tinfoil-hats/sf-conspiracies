‘Find my phone’ app used after Bay Area carjacking; toddler returned
-----
Marin County sheriff's deputies arrested two people suspected of stealing a car that had a 2-year-old child in the back seat, authorities said Wednesday.The suspects were identified as Oakland resident Viorel Andrei Morari, 35, and San Leandro resident Carly McVicker, 32. Both were booked into Alameda County Jail on charges including kidnapping and carjacking.
