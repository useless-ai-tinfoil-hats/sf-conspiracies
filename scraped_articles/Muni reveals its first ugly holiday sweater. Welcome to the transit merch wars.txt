Muni reveals its first ugly holiday sweater. Welcome to the transit merch wars
-----
The perfect holiday outfit for your ride on the 38-Geary has finally arrived.After years of watching BART's ugly sweaters sell out, Muni unveiled its first holiday sweater design on Tuesday morning, then immediately started taking pre-orders. The transit agency, which previously let others distribute its merchandise, hopes to make $75,000 on the holiday sweater, then build a larger e-commerce site later this year.
