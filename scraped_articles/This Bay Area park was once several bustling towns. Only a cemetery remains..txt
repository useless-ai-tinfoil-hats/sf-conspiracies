This Bay Area park was once several bustling towns. Only a cemetery remains.
-----
At the end of a twisty, 2-mile drive deep into the hills above Antioch, there's a canyon that once roared with the commotion of several bustling mining towns.Today, the only commotion left is the frenzied bleats of nearby sheep. Stepping out of my truck at the parking lot of Black Diamond Mines Regional Preserve, I'm greeted by a muddy cattle dog, his handler nowhere in sight.
