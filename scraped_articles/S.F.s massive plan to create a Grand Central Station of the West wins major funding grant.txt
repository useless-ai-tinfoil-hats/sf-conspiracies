S.F.’s massive plan to create a ‘Grand Central Station of the West’ wins major funding grant
-----
San Francisco's vision to make the desolate Transbay transit center the "Grand Central Station of the West" has received support from the federal government, which has pledged $3.4 billion toward the city's downtown rail extension project.The massive, four-block transit hub in SoMa opened in 2018 equipped with a dormant train platform in its basement and has, so far, lived a muted existence.
