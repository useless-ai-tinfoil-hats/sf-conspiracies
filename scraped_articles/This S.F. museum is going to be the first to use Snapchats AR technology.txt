The Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco is collaborating with the technology company behind Snapchat to introduce the first interactive augmented reality mirrors in a U.S. museum.The three mirrors, to be installed at the de Young Museum next year, will be part of the institution's "Fashioning San Francisco: A Century of Style," set to launch on Jan. 20.
