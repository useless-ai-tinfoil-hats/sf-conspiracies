Crews battle fire that broke out at Potrero Hill home
-----
Firefighters battled a blaze Saturday afternoon that broke out at a Potrero Hill home and was threatening to spread to another building.The San Francisco Fire Department responded to a report of a residential fire shortly after 4 p.m. on Kansas Street. No one was in need of rescue when crews arrived as firefighters worked to limit the blaze's damage to the home and surrounding properties.
