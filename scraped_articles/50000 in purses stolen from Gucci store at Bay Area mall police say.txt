$50,000 in purses stolen from Gucci store at Bay Area mall, police say
-----
Five masked men allegedly robbed a Gucci store at San Jose's Valley Fair mall on Oct. 5, and on Tuesday, officials said one suspect was in custody.The suspect, a 27-year-old man from Henderson, Nevada, was a fugitive on a wanted listed in Nevada with a warrant out for his arrest, the San Jose Police Department said in a news release.
