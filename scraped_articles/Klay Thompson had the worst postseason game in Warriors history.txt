Klay Thompson had the worst postseason game in Warriors history
-----
LATEST April 16, 9:30 p.m. Klay Thompson finished off the Warriors' 118-94 loss on Tuesday by going 0-for-10 from the field, and 0-for-6 from 3-point range, which is not only the worst postseason performance of his career, but it's perhaps the worst postseason performance in franchise history, as Josh Dubow of the Associated Press notes.It's the first time Thompson has been held scoreless in a postseason game in his career.
