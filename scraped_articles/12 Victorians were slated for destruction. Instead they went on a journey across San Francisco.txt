12 Victorians were slated for destruction. Instead, they went on a journey across San Francisco
-----
San Francisco houses began moving in caravans on Nov. 8, 1974, with the kind of entourage one would associate with a presidential visit.The procession included 30 tow trucks and 30 motorcycle police officers to clear the curbs, plus hundreds of workers to lift power lines, swivel street lamps and make sure no small children got caught underneath the rolling three-story homes.
