Driver in stolen car kills couple going to church in Oakland, police say
-----
Two people were killed and one person was injured in Oakland on Sunday when a driver in a stolen Lexus crashed into another car, authorities said.A 64-year-old Richmond man, who was driving the car that got hit, and a 67-year-old woman from Antioch, who was the front passenger, both died, the Oakland Police Department said in an emailed statement.
