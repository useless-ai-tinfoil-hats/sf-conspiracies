This Calif. fast food spot rivals Nobu Malibu for celebrity sightings
-----
At Nobu Malibu, one of the most sought-after restaurant reservations in Los Angeles, it's not uncommon to see a Bieber or Kardashian (or Jenner, for that matter) noshing on $72 lobster shiitake salad or $98 king crab with uni. Since 2012, the celebrity hotspot (famously brought to a prime stretch of the Pacific Coast Highway by billionaire Larry Ellison) has been serving up chef Nobu Matsuhisa's pristine sashimi along with sweeping ocean views.
