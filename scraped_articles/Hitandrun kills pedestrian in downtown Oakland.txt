Hit-and-run kills pedestrian in downtown Oakland
-----
A pedestrian died early Friday after being hit by a vehicle in downtown Oakland, according to the Oakland Police Department.Police said the collision happened just after 12:30 a.m. Friday on the 2200 block of Martin Luther King Jr. Way. Officers responded to the scene and found a pedestrian who had been struck by a vehicle while crossing at the crosswalk, according to police. The driver fled the scene before police arrived.
