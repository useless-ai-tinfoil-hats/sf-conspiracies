Pedestrian badly injured in South Bay crash
-----
A pedestrian suffered life-threatening injuries after being struck by a vehicle Saturday morning in San Jose, according to the city's Police Department.Police and paramedics responded to the collision at Story Road and Galahad Avenue around 10 a.m. The pedestrian was taken to a local hospital.
Eastbound Story Road from Leeward to Capital avenues was closed, and police urged drivers to use alternative routes.
The driver remained on the scene and cooperated with investigators,
