'Yelling, screaming' fistfight breaks out on Oakland to Hawaii flight
-----
The Federal Aviation Administration is investigating a passenger disturbance in which a fight allegedly broke out on a Southwest Airlines flight from the Bay Area to Hawaii.Flight SWA 128 took off on time from Oakland on Monday and was headed to Lihue in Kauai around 10 a.m., according to FlightAware. However, two passengers "became disruptive" on the flight, a spokesperson for Southwest Airlines told SFGATE over email.
