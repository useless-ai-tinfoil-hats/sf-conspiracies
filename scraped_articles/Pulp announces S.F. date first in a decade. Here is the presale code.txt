The Brit-pop band Pulp has announced its first set of North American tour dates in more than a decade, with a stop scheduled for the Bay Area.The five-date This Is What We Do for an Encore Tour is scheduled to start in Chicago on Sept. 8, and reach San Francisco's Bill Graham Civic Auditorium on Sept. 16.
Pulp, hailing from Sheffield, England, last performed in the city at the Warfield in 2012.
