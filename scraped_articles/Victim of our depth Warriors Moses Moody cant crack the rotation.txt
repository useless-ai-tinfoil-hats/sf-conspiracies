‘Victim of our depth’: Warriors’ Moses Moody can’t crack the rotation
-----
Golden State Warriors guard Moses Moody finds himself in a curious situation: Despite playing well this season, he is out of the rotation for a sub-.500 team.This speaks to head coach Steve Kerr's rotational dilemma. These Warriors are deep, but their lack of an obvious hierarchy after Stephen Curry often makes it tough for Kerr to decide whom to leave out of the lineup.
