A giant party shut down Calif.'s oldest freeway last weekend
-----
The Arroyo Seco Parkway, an eight-mile stretch linking Los Angeles with Pasadena, opened in 1940 to become the first-ever freeway in America.Typically a bumber-to-bumber slog, the highway is not cause for celebration.
However, for a few hours last weekend, the road was open exclusively to pedestrians, cyclists, roller skaters and anyone else on non-motorized wheels for a party unlike any other dubbed ArroyoFest. Read more »
