Former UC Davis water polo coach pleads guilty to distributing child pornography
-----
Daniel Joseph Noble, a former assistant water polo coach at UC Davis, pleaded guilty Thursday to distributing imagery of young girls engaged in sexual acts on a messaging app used by teenagers, officials said.The case against Noble outlined his repeated participation in a group chat dedicated to child pornography videos on the app Kik between March 10, 2022, and April 12, 2022, according to a news release from the United States Attorney's Office Eastern District of California.
