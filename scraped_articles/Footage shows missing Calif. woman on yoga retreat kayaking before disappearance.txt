Footage shows missing Calif. woman on yoga retreat kayaking before disappearance
-----
New video taken shortly before the disappearance of California woman Nancy Ng in Guatemala has been released.The footage shows the 29-year-old waving happily at the camera while kayaking out onto Lake Atitlan on Oct. 19. Ng, of Monterey Park in Southern California, disappeared while on a yoga retreat at in the Guatemalan Highlands.
