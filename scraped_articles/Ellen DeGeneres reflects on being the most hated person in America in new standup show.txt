Ellen DeGeneres reflects on being the ‘most hated person in America’ in new stand-up show
-----
Ellen DeGeneres is finally ready to talk about getting "kicked out of show business" in her new stand-up set.The comedian quipped about being labeled as "mean" and the cancellation of "The Ellen DeGeneres Show" during a stand-up performance at Largo in Los Angeles on Wednesday, April 24, according to a report by Rolling Stone. The show was one of a handful of gigs at the venue that kicked off her new stand-up tour, "Ellen's Last Stand …Up," which is set to stop at Luther Burbank Center
