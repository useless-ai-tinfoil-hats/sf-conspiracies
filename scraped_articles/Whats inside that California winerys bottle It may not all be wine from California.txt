What’s inside that California winery’s bottle? It may not all be wine from California
-----
That cheap bottle of wine you picked up at the grocery store? Even if it's from a California winery, some of its contents may have come from another country entirely.Every year, the state's largest wine companies import significant volumes of bulk wine: the equivalent of 19 million cases last year, according to leading industry analyst the Gomberg Fredrikson Report, and 30 million in 2022.
