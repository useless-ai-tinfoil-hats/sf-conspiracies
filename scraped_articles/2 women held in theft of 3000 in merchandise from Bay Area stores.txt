2 women held in theft of $3,000 in merchandise from Bay Area stores
-----
Two San Leandro women have been arrested in the theft of $3,000 worth of merchandise from Vacaville outlet stores.Officers received a call Friday afternoon about merchandise theft at the store and located Margarita Bravo, 36, and Alison Rodriguez, 18, in a nearby parking lot, the Police Department reported.
