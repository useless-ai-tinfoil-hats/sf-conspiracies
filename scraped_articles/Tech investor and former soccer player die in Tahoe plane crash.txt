Tech investor and former soccer player die in Tahoe plane crash
-----
Details are emerging surrounding a plane crash in Truckee, Calif., on Saturday night that took two lives.At 6:38 p.m. on March 30, a single-engine aircraft crashed near the Union Pacific railroad tracks around a mile east of Truckee's historic downtown, and around two miles from Truckee Tahoe Airport. The plane came down between the Truckee River and the tracks during a storm that left over a foot of snow in the mountains around Lake Tahoe over the weekend.
