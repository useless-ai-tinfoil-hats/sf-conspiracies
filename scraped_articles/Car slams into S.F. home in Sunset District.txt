Car slams into S.F. home in Sunset District
-----
A car crashed into a home in the Sunset District of San Francisco on Sunday, police said.Officers responded to the 1300 block of 17th Avenue on a report of a vehicle collision involving two vehicles -- one of which struck a building -- just after 4 p.m.
A photo from Citizen, a neighborhood crime app, shows the car crashed into a home at the corner of Judah and 17th Avenue.
No injuries were reported during the crash.
Officers are still investigating the incident.
