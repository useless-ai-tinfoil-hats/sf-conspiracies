Body recovered from San Francisco Bay near Treasure Island
-----
Authorities recovered a body from San Francisco Bay near Treasure Island on Friday afternoon.Police responded to a call just before 1 p.m. Friday about a body in the water near the 400 block of Clipper Cove Way, on the south side of Treasure Island, according to the San Francisco Police Department.
The body was recovered by the San Francisco Fire Department.
