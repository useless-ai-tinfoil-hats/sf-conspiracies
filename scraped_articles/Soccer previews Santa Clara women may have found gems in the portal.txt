Soccer previews: Santa Clara women may have found gems in the portal
-----
Jerry Smith was hoping to avoid the transfer portal for as long as possible. As a mid-major program, all the portal could do, he thought, was take from Santa Clara.So he was surprised to be fielding a team at the start of preseason with seven transfers who all are battling for a chance to start. After losing WCC Defender of the Year Annie Karich to a pro team in Germany, the 38-year head coach found himself re-evaluating how to run his program.
