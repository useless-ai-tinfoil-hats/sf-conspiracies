His silence kept his innocent friend in prison. A funeral brought them together again
-----
After three decades in prison, Joaquin Ciria emerged from the San Francisco jail in April 2022 — free, exonerated and with a very important phone call to make.His mother was ailing back in Cuba. Ciria hadn't seen her since he was 17 in 1980, when he fled his conscripted position in the Army. He had told her that he'd find a better life in the U.S., and ended up with a life sentence for a murder he didn't commit.
