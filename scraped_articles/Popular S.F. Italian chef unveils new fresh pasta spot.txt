Popular S.F. Italian chef unveils new fresh pasta spot
-----
The team behind one of San Francisco's most popular Italian restaurants will soon be preparing fresh pasta for the Mission District.Starting Friday, Sept. 15, Barberio Osteria will offer seasonal Italian dishes at 557 Valencia St., the former site of upscale seafood restaurant Ancora. Nick Kelly, the chef at Nob Hill's AltoVino, named the restaurant in honor of his maternal grandmother's surname.
