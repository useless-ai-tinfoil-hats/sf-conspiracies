Why California’s reinvestigation of an infamous quadruple murder case is a sham
-----
In the spring of 2021, Gov. Gavin Newsom took the extraordinary step of ordering a reinvestigation into Kevin Cooper's infamous quadruple murder case. Cooper — who has been on death row since 1985 for killing Doug and Peggy Ryen, their 10-year-old daughter and an 11-year-old friend — has always proclaimed his innocence but had exhausted all of his appeals.
