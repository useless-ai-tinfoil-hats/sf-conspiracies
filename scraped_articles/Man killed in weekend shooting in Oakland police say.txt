Man killed in weekend shooting in Oakland, police say
-----
A man was shot and killed Saturday in West Oakland, the police department said.Officers responded to a report of a shooting at about 11:30 p.m. on the 1600 block of 14th Street and found the man, an Oakland resident, suffering from a gunshot wound, authorities said. He was pronounced dead at the scene.
The victim's identity was not immediately released, pending notification of next of kin, officials said.
Further details surrounding the shooting remained under investigation.
