Four national parks in California make sobering new list
-----
Four of California's national parks topped a recent list of the most polluted in the nation.A new air and climate report from the National Parks Conservation Association found that Sequoia-Kings Canyon, Joshua Tree, Mojave and Yosemite struggle with unhealthy air more than any other parks in the country.
