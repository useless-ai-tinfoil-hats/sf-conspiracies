S.F. man charged after dead body found in Crissy Field parking lot
-----
Federal prosecutors have charged a San Francisco man with aiding and abetting the murder of a man found shot in the head in the Crissy Field parking lot.The U.S. Attorney's Office of the Northern District of California alleges that cellphone location data and surveillance camera footage implicated Leion Butler, 20, in the murder of a man whose body was found in the early morning of Nov. 12.
Officials with the FBI reviewed surveillance camera footage of the area near Crissy Field
