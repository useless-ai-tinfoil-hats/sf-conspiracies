70% of the top 10 high schools in California are located in the Bay Area
-----
Private high schools in the Bay Area dominated a recent list of the best high schools in California, with local schools claiming 70% of the list's top 10 spots.Bay Area schools accounted for 7 out of 10 spots on the annual Niche Best Schools in California list, which was published last week. Niche is a school rankings website. Stanford Online High School, an online school based in Redwood City, took second place on the list after Harvard-Westlake School in Los Angeles' Studio City.
