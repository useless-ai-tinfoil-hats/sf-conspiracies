San Francisco's office market hits another vacancy record, though 'market is stabilizing'
-----
San Francisco's office market has hit another vacancy high, according to a preliminary quarterly report from commercial real estate firm CBRE. But there are signs the market is gradually changing course: This was also the city's second-best leasing quarter in the past two years, behind fourth quarter 2023.The vacancy rate grew to 37% this quarter, up from the 36.4% measured last quarter. Yet leasing activity is up 25% compared with last year, largely driven by artificial intelligence
