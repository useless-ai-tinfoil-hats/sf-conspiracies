'It broke my heart': Oakland restaurant closes after 38 years
-----
A favorite place for Vietnamese food in Oakland is closing its doors after 38 years.Le Cheval will serve its last plates of steaming garlic noodles and lemongrass chicken topped with peanuts — two popular menu items — in its grand Clay Street dining room on Sept. 30, according to a Facebook post from the Tran family, which owns the establishment.
