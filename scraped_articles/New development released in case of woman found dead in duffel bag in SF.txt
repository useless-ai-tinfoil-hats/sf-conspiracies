New development released in case of woman found dead in duffel bag in SF
-----
Two people have been arrested in connection with a woman found dead in a duffel at Golden Gate Park during the Outside Lands Festival over the summer, officials said Thursday.Alexander Madden, 26, and Emilia Beckhusen, 33, were arrested on suspicion of unlawful disposal of human remains and conspiracy, the San Francisco Police Department said in a news release.
While the police department announced the new development in the case yesterday, the arrests were made earlier this month.
