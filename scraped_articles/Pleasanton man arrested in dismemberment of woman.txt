Pleasanton man arrested in dismemberment of woman
-----
A Pleasanton man has been arrested in the killing of a woman whose remains were found in a bag on the shoreline near the Bay Farm Island Bridge in Alameda in July, authorities said.Joseph C. Roberts, 42, was charged with murder in the death of Rachel Elizabeth Imani Buckner, 27, the Alameda Police Department said in a statement.
The pair were classmates at Golden Gate University School of Law, from which Buckner had recently graduated, university spokesperson Jenny McKeel said
