Northern lights: Will California see a return of spectacular skies Sunday night?
-----
The strongest solar storm to hit Earth in more than two decades is expected to continue Sunday into early Monday, according to the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, which forecasts space weather events.A "secondary peak" in the solar storm is likely early Sunday into Sunday night, NOAA said, which will probably result in another period of enhanced aurora borealis activity, also known as the northern lights.
