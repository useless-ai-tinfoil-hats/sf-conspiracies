Green Day drops ‘Dilemma’ along with festive music video
-----
East Bay punk trio Green Day just dropped a new single from the forthcoming album "Saviors," complete with a holiday-themed music video."'Dilemma' was one of those songs that was kind of easy to write because it was so personal to me," frontman Billie Joe Armstrong said in a statement about the release on Thursday, Dec. 7. "We've seen so many of our peers struggle with addiction and mental illness. This song is all about the pain that comes from those experiences."
