S.F. students plan walkout to protest Israel bombing Gaza
-----
High school students in San Francisco plan to walk out of classrooms Wednesday to protest Israel bombing Gaza and demand a ceasefire in the Israel-Hamas war, organizers said.A news conference is scheduled for 10 a.m. on the steps of City Hall ahead of the walkout, according to the Arab Resource & Organizing Center, the San Francisco-based group that organized the event. Some city supervisors, religious leaders, and Arab and Jewish activists are expected to speak at the event.
