Review: Spectacular cast supercharges S.F. Opera revival of Handel’s ‘Partenope’
-----
Handel's 1730 opera "Partenope" has the kind of plot that's typical of his comedies: romantic competition, an undecided heroine, a woman disguised as a man, conflicted heroes. Arsace, Armindo, Emilio and Eurimene all want to marry Partenope, Queen of Naples.Partenope is in love with Arsace, but — surprise! — Eurimene is really Rosmira, who was previously Arsace's betrothed.
