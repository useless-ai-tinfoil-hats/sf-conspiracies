Festival Napa Valley’s summer lineup features more than 100 artists
-----
Kicking off with Bottle Rock Napa Valley and the inaugural Festival La Onda, Napa has had a hot start to the summer — and it's just the beginning.Joining previously announced headliner Lionel Richie, string and vocal ensemble Time for Three and French pianist Jean-Yves Thibaudet are among the more than 100 artists lined up to perform during Festival Napa Valley from July 6-21.
