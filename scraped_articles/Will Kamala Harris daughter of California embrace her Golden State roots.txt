Will Kamala Harris, ‘daughter of California,’ embrace her Golden State roots?
-----
CHICAGO – A short biographical video played between speakers at the Democratic National Convention this week. It showed the house where Kamala Harris grew up, the street she lived on and photos of her as a child.But it didn't mention two words integral to Harris' life: "Oakland," where she was born, or "Berkeley," where she grew up. Instead, it said she grew up in the generic-sounding "East Bay."
