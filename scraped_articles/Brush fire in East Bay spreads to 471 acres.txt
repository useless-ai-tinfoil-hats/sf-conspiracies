Brush fire in East Bay spreads to 471 acres
-----
LATEST July 27, 7:44 a.m. The Point Fire, currently burning in Contra Costa County, grew slightly overnight but crews made substantial progress in slowing the blaze. As of Saturday morning, the fire had grown to 471 acres with 40% containment, according to Cal Fire.Cal Fire noted that all evacuation orders had been downgraded to warnings.
