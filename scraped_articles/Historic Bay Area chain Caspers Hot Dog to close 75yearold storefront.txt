Historic Bay Area chain Caspers Hot Dog to close 75-year-old storefront
-----
Bay Area restaurant chain Caspers Hot Dogs will have one less store once an iconic and decades-old Hayward location permanently closes next month.The store, located at 951 C St., is expected to close for good on July 13. Co-owner Ron Dorian told SFGATE that the closure comes as the company plans to downsize its existing five locations to make operations more efficient.
