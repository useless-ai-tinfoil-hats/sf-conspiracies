Snow on the way for Lake Tahoe this weekend
-----
A small storm this weekend could at least inspire Lake Tahoe skiers to start getting their gear ready. But visitors and locals anxiously awaiting the first big pow day of the season may have to wait a while longer.Storms are expected to move into the Tahoe basin on Thursday evening, bringing daytime temperatures in the 30s at least through Saturday. While that's ideal for resort snowmaking, the unsettled system may not bring too much in the way of natural snow.
