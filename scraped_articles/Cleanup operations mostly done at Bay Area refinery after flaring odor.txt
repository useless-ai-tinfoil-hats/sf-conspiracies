Cleanup operations ‘mostly done’ at Bay Area refinery after flaring, odor
-----
Cleanup operations were wrapping up Sunday after a mechanical problem at Benicia's Valero Refinery caused flaring and released strong-smelling hydrogen sulfide vapor in the area Saturday, the city announced.An alert issued shortly after 7 a.m. Saturday notified city residents about the odor and said the Benicia Fire Department was responding. The department recommended that people remain indoors and close doors and windows if the odor was strong in their area.
