Letters: S.F. needs more housing but not at the cost of protecting California’s coast
-----
Regarding "California coastal protections versus housing: The battle is on" (Bay Area, SFChronicle.com, Jan. 25): All those who treasure California's coast and beaches should be alarmed by a recently introduced bill in Sacramento — SB951. The bill, if passed, seeks to remove the California Coastal Commission's jurisdiction from the neighborhood fronting San Francisco's Ocean Beach.
