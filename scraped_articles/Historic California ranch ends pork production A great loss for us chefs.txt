Historic California ranch ends pork production: ‘A great loss for us chefs’
-----
One of the largest premium pork suppliers to Bay Area restaurants will cease production.The quickly rising cost of feed, fuel and labor are forcing Rancho Llano Seco in Chico to leave the pork business, the company announced Monday. The 162-year-old farm will continue to produce heirloom beans, walnuts, beef and other products.
