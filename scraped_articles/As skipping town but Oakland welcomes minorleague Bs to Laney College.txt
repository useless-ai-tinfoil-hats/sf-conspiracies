A's skipping town, but Oakland welcomes minor-league B's to Laney College
-----
Laney College, of all places, will play host to Oakland's next professional baseball team.The A's struck out in their plans to build a stadium on the campus six years ago and are fleeing town to Las Vegas, and now, the Oakland Ballers - that's right, the Oakland B's - will play in the existing facility while helping to fund some improvements.
"There's a certain irony in coming back around - and we think it's a great place to play," co-founder Paul Freedman said.
