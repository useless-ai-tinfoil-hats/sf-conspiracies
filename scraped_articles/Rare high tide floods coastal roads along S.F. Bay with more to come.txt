Rare high tide floods coastal roads along S.F. Bay, with more to come
-----
A rare combination of high tides and an ocean swell from a storm in the Pacific swamped roads along the San Francisco Bay shoreline on Sunday morning, the National Weather Service said."This isn't any major flooding concern. There is some inundation in parking lots along the bay line," said meteorologist Roger Gass. A coastal flood advisory had been posted to expire at noon Sunday, but flooding was minimal, Gass said.
