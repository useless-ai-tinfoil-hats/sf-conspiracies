Oakley founder’s Malibu property sets new California home price record
-----
James Jannard, the founder of eyewear brand Oakley, has sold his Malibu property for $210 million, setting a record for California home sales.The transaction surpasses the previous record set by Beyoncé and Jay-Z, who paid $200 million last year for a sprawling 40,000-square-foot estate overlooking the Pacific Ocean. Before that, billionaire venture capitalist Marc Andreessen held the record with his $177 million purchase of a neighboring Malibu property in 2021.
