Bay Area counties are recovering from California population decline, data shows
-----
New data shows that several Bay Area counties are now growing in population, signaling a recovery from losses incurred during the pandemic.Four Bay Area counties — Alameda, San Francisco, Santa Clara and San Mateo — grew in population between July 2022 and July 2023, after a period of population decline during the height of the pandemic, according to a new report conducted by California's Department of Finance.
