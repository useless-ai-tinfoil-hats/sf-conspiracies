California's Central Coast gets first-ever 'Hurricane Force Wind Warning'
-----
As dangerous winds and heavy rain bear down on California's Central Coast, the National Weather Service issued an extremely rare "Hurricane Force Wind Warning" for the region Saturday night. CNN reports it is the first time in recorded state history the weather service has given this type of warning.The weather service pulled no punches in its late-night alert, cautioning there will be "extremely dangerous conditions for mariners."
