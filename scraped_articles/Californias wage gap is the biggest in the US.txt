California's wage gap is the biggest in the US
-----
Anyone living in California knows it's a state with a deep divide between the extremely rich and the extremely poor.But some new numbers crunched by the Southern California News Group show the wage gap is the largest of any state in the U.S. When it compared May 2023 salaries in the 75th percentile ($93,250 in the Golden State) with those in the 25th percentile ($37,890), there was a 146% gap.
