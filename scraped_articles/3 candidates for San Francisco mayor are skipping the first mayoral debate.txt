3 candidates for San Francisco mayor are skipping the first mayoral debate
-----
Three candidates for San Francisco mayor dropped out of an upcoming debate hosted by TogetherSF Action, which was supposed to be the first showdown among the race's five serious candidates.Mayor London Breed, Board of Supervisors President Aaron Peskin and Daniel Lurie, a local nonprofit leader and an heir to the Levi Strauss fortune, have each said they would not be participating in a debate that is, at least for now, still scheduled for Monday at 6 p.m.
