Making family history into historical fiction in ‘The Storm We Made’
-----
Every history has its gaps, especially in the context of war and occupation. Lives are lost, not only as casualties to tally but as little worlds snuffed out by violence.And even though history can't tell all those lost stories, historical fiction can try.
