Calif. musician petitions TSA to close travel loophole
-----
A California musician is petitioning the government to require identification for children on flights after he was stopped by the airline for suspected child trafficking.On Sept. 15, singer-songwriter David Ryan Harris flew on an early morning American Airlines flight from the Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta Airport to LAX with his two mixed-race children.
