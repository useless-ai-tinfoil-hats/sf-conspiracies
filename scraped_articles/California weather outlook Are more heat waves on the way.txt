California weather outlook: Are more heat waves on the way?
-----
June arrived in California with a bang this year, as the first heat wave of 2024 broke daily temperature records in Fresno and Santa Rosa. Temperatures have cooled the past few days, with the clash between cool maritime and hot continental air masses returning to California.That clash, which is typical in the spring, will be turbo-charged this year and continue to influence the rest of the month.
