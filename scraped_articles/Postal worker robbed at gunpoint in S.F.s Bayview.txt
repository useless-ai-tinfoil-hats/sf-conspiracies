A postal worker was robbed at gunpoint Saturday morning in San Francisco's Bayview district.Police responded to Bowman Court, about one block from the U.S. Postal Service Office on Evans Avenue, at 11:48 a.m. and learned that two unknown people had held a gun to a Postal Service employee while taking property.
The San Francisco Police Department did not say what the pair stole before they fled in an unidentified car. No one was injured.
