Duran Duran returns to Bay Area for intimate concert for 500 fans
-----
Duran Duran is set to return to the Bay Area for another intimate concert.The iconic British pop band is scheduled to perform at the 500-capacity Guild Theatre in Menlo Park on Feb. 9, with a portion of the proceeds from the concert going towards supporting Hamilton Families, an organization dedicated to combating family homelessness.
