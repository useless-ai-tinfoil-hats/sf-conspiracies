SF's Salesforce is having its worst stock market day in more than decade
-----
It's a rough day to be a Salesforce employee.The San Francisco-based software giant announced its financial results for the quarter ending in April on Wednesday, and investors were most displeased. The company's share price plummeted in after-hours trading, and remained low Thursday morning. Salesforce hasn't seen such a dramatic stock market drop since the week of its initial public offering in 2004, when the company was a fraction of its present size, according to CNBC.
