Tech firm paying $76 million fee to relinquish over 25% of its SF office space
-----
File-hosting firm Dropbox will relinquish a big chunk of its San Francisco office space over the next few years, doubling down on its promise to remain a remote-first company for the long haul, according to a new company filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission.Between now and the first quarter of 2025, the company is set to surrender to its landlord 165,244 square feet of space, amounting to over a quarter of the company's Mission Bay headquarters.
