Warriors’ Curry scores 16 as East rolls West in defense-free All-Star Game
-----
INDIANAPOLIS — Golden State Warriors guard Stephen Curry went into Sunday's All-Star Game at Gainbridge Fieldhouse as the NBA's sixth-leading scorer this season.He didn't threaten that 28-point mark, checking in with a comparatively tepid 16 on 6-of-17 shooting. And nine players outscored him in a game that likely irked NBA commissioner Adam Silver.
