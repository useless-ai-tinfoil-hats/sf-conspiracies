Pandas at the S.F. Zoo? Some say it should fix ‘dilapidated’ conditions first
-----
Three years ago, the San Francisco Zoo enthusiastically shared plans to convert an old sea lion exhibit into a new habitat for a pair of Andean condors.The exhibit was supposed to open by last year, but today, a banner advertising the "future home of Andean Condor" hangs in front of an overgrown lot. While the birds were brought to the zoo eight years ago, the only sign of progress on their future home is that the sea lions' former swimming pool is now filled with concrete.
