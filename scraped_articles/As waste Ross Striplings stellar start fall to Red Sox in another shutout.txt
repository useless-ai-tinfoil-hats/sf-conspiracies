A’s waste Ross Stripling’s stellar start, fall to Red Sox in another shutout
-----
For the third time in their season-opening, seven-game homestand, the Oakland Athletics absorbed a shutout loss Wednesday. And for only the third time in their 57-season history in Oakland, the A's have begun a season 1-6.Boston completed a three-game sweep at the Coliseum with a 1-0 victory. The A's 1-6 record after seven games ties the 1979 and 2021 teams for the worst in Oakland history.
