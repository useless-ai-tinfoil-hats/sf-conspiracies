S.F. police investigate death of woman who checked herself into the hospital with gunshot wounds
-----
San Francisco police said Thursday that they are investigating the death of a woman who died after walking into a city hospital with gunshot wounds.Police responded to the hospital just after 11:30 p.m. Wednesday. Despite the efforts of hospital staff, the woman died.
The woman was not immediately identified. There had been no arrests made in connection to the death as of Thursday afternoon, police said.
