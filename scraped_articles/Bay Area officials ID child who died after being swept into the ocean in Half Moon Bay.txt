Bay Area officials ID child who died after being swept into the ocean in Half Moon Bay
-----
Authorities on Monday identified the 5-year-old girl who died after being swept into the ocean off the coast of Half Moon Bay over the weekend.San Mateo County coroner's officials identified the girl as Yaretzi Noemi Biorato of Merced. The U.S. Coast Guard previously called off its search for her 54-year-old grandfather, who was also swept off Martin's Beach Saturday.
The man has not been publicly identified, officials said.
Yaretzi was rescued by San Mateo County fire crew
