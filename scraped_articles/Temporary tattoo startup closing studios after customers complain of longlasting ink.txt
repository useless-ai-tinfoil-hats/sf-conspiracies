Temporary tattoo startup closing studios after customers complain of long-lasting ink
-----
A temporary-tattoo startup whose ink remained visible on some customers' skin for significantly longer than advertised is closing all of its studios this month.Ephemeral Tattoo announced the news in an email to customers Friday with the final dates for appointments at its locations in San Francisco, Houston, Chicago, Washington, D.C., Los Angeles and Brooklyn, N.Y.
Ephemeral originally advertised a new kind of tattoo "made-to-fade" over nine to 15 months using a proprietary ink
