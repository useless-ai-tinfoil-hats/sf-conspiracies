Embrace Pet Insurance review: Pros, cons, and pricing
-----
Embrace Pet Insurance at a glanceEmbrace offers solid pet insurance policies with options for pet parents prioritizing affordable rates and comprehensive coverage. You'll find a wide range of customizations and a few significant stackable discounts with Embrace policies. Overall, we consider it a top pet insurance company. However, whether your pet is eligible for coverage depends on its age and location.
* Standard waiting periods: 14 days for illnesses; zero days for accidents
