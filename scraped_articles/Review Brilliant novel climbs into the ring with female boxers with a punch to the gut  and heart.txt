Review: Brilliant novel climbs into the ring with female boxers with a punch to the gut — and heart
-----
Boxing's mix of artistry and brutality has inspired novelists like Ernest Hemingway and San Francisco's Jack London, but for a variety of antiquated reasons, virtually all of the stories set in the ring have been written by — and about — men. Rita Bullwinkel's new book runs counter to this history.
