SF is trying to shut down hot dog vendors outside Giants games. It's not working.
-----
The hot dog vendors outside Oracle Park have quickly become as big a part of San Francisco Giants games as garlic fries, crab sandwiches and Ghirardelli sundaes.But not everyone is excited about the public vendors. In fact, the Giants and a task force involving several San Francisco government agencies seem to be trying to stop what they deem is the sale of unsafe food.
