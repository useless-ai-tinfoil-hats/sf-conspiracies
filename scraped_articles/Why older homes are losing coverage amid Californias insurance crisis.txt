Why older homes are losing coverage amid California’s insurance crisis
-----
After 26 years with the same insurance provider, Ruby Rich's San Francisco home was dropped because of its age.The three-unit, 1904 building — which survived two major earthquakes and was in great shape, according to Rich — was cut last year after Farmers Insurance changed its policies to exclude multi-unit homes built before 1925.
"I was terrified," Rich said. "My house is my major asset and suddenly I had no insurance."
