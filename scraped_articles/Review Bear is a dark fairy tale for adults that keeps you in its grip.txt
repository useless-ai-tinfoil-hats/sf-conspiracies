Review: 'Bear' is a dark fairy tale for adults that keeps you in its grip
-----
In 2019, "Disappearing Earth" — Julia Phillips' haunting, suspenseful debut about two young sisters who get kidnapped on the Kamchatka Peninsula in the Russian Far East — was a finalist for the National Book Award, and rightfully so. The sinewy, propulsive narrative combined with Phillips' richly atmospheric sentences showcased her talent for encapsulating a sense of time and place in just a few choice words."Bear," Phillips' follow-up, is equally moody, with plenty of prescient moments
