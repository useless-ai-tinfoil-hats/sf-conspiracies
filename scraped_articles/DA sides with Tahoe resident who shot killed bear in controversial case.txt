DA sides with Tahoe resident who shot, killed bear in controversial case
-----
The South Lake Tahoe resident who shot and killed a small bear on Memorial Day did not break the law, an investigation by the El Dorado County District Attorney's Office found."The suspect in this incident shot the bear out of a concern for the safety of himself and his property," reads a statement issued by the district attorney's office on June 25.
