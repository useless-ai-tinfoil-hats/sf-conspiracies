In the AI age, how have artists envisioned robots in theater, film and ballet?
-----
Historically, when robots have appeared in art, it's often been to kill us or to make us feel loved.Among the murderers are the replicants in "Blade Runner," HAL in "2001: A Space Odyssey" and Skynet in the "Terminator" franchise. The machines of "The Matrix," by contrast, were content to merely suck out our energy.
