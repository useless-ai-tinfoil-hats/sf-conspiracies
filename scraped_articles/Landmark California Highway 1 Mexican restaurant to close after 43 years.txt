Landmark California Highway 1 Mexican restaurant to close after 43 years
-----
Colorful Mexican restaurant The Whole Enchilada plans to close at the end of the month after over 40 years in Moss Landing, the restaurant shared on Instagram. A popular landmark on Highway 1, the restaurant is known for its festive atmosphere, live music, seafood enchiladas and margaritas.The closure announcement elicited an outpouring of love and support from customers on social media, sharing memories of eating there after camping on the beach, going on dates with their now-spouses
