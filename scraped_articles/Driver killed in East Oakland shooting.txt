Driver killed in East Oakland shooting
-----
A driver in East Oakland died from a gunshot wound early Saturday, police said.Officers received reports of a shooting and vehicle crash on the 1500 block of 12th Avenue before 2:30 a.m., the Oakland Police Department reported.
Upon arrival, officers found a collision where the driver, the only occupant in the car, had been shot, according to police.
Medical personnel and officers administered lifesaving measures to the man, but he died at the scene.
