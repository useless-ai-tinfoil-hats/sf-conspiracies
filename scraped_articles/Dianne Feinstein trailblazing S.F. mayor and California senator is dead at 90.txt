Dianne Feinstein, trailblazing S.F. mayor and California senator, is dead at 90
-----
Dianne Feinstein, the former San Francisco mayor who steered the city through the aftermath of an assassination and whose lengthy career as California's senior U.S. senator included passing pioneering gun control and environmental legislation, died of natural causes at 2 a.m. EDT Friday.Feinstein, a pragmatic, centrist Democrat who had served in the Senate since 1992, was 90 and had been in ill health for several years.
