Mendocino property featured in HBO series is headed to auction
-----
After failing to find a buyer on the open market, The East Ranch, a massive property in Mendocino County is headed to auction.The ranch is an astounding 1,745 acres in Mendocino County, comprising 10 parcels. The estate also boasts star power: HBO's "Sharp Objects," a miniseries based on the Gillian Flynn novel by the same name, was filmed here.
