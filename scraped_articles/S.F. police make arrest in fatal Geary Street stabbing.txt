S.F. police make arrest in fatal Geary Street stabbing
-----
San Francisco police said Thursday that they arrested a person suspected of committing a fatal stabbing on Wednesday morning.Officers responded early Wednesday to the 900 block of Geary Street, where they found a victim with an apparent stab wound. After responders and paramedics attempted CPR, the victim was pronounced dead at the scene.
Officers were told the possible male suspect was still inside a nearby building located in the Tenderloin. They were able to detain the suspect
