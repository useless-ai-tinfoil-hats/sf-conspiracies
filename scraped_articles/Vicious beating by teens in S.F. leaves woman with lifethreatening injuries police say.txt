Vicious beating by teens in S.F. leaves woman with life-threatening injuries, police say
-----
A 63-year-old woman was hospitalized with life-threatening injuries Wednesday after she was assaulted by a group of teenagers in downtown San Francisco, police said.Officers are searching for two girls and one boy ages 16 to 18 suspected of attacking the woman "with multiple punches, kicks, and her stick" around 3 p.m. at a commercial parking garage on Mission Street near Yerba Buena Gardens, the San Francisco Police Department said.
