Fire investigators in Oakland have identified an "area of origin" inside a lumber warehouse near Interstate 880 that burned down over Memorial Day weekend, officials said Tuesday.Investigators were still determining the exact cause of the four-alarm fire but have ruled out the possibility that it was started by someone camping nearby, said Oakland Fire Department spokesperson Michael Hunt.
