Fresno County voters reject measure that would reinstate offensive town name
-----
Voters in Fresno County rejected a measure last week that would have reversed the naming of Yokuts Valley from Squaw Valley, removing a term known to be a racist slur against Native American women.Measure B would have adjusted the county's charter to specify that renaming unincorporated areas would fall under the jurisdiction of local entities like the Fresno County Board of Supervisors.
