Bay Area sideshow spectators ‘attack’ police vehicle, injuring officer, officials say
-----
Police are investigating after spectators of a sideshow in San Jose jumped on a police vehicle and stomped on it, smashing the windshield, in a video-recorded incident that officials said left a volunteer officer with minor injuries."This conduct is unacceptable and will not be tolerated in our city," acting Police Chief Paul Joseph said in a statement. He called the spectators an "out of control mob" and described their actions as a "deplorable attack" on the officer.
