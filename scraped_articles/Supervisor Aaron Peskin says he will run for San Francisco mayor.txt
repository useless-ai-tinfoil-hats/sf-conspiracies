Supervisor Aaron Peskin says he will run for San Francisco mayor
-----
San Francisco Supervisor Aaron Peskin said on Wednesday that he plans to run for mayor and will make a formal announcement in the coming days, according to multiple reports.Peskin, who represents District 3 and is president of the Board of Supervisors, had previously hinted at challenging Mayor London Breed in the Nov. 5, 2024 election. Peskin joins of field of candidates that includes District 11 Supervisor Ahsha Safai, philanthropist and Levi's heir Daniel Lurie, and former SF Mayor
