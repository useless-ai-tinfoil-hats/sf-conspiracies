‘No restaurant is really safe’: S.F. sandwich spot closing after 6 years
-----
A casual stop for Cuban sandwiches and drinks in the Mission is closing after more than six years.Media Noche, at 3465 19th St., announced its final day in business will be Oct. 28 via a message on Instagram.
"It's been such a pleasure serving this community from our little (Mission) corner. We hope to see your faces in the next two weeks," the message partially reads.
