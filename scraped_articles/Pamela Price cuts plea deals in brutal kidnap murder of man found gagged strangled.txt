Pamela Price cuts plea deals in brutal kidnap, murder of man found gagged, strangled
-----
Alameda County District Attorney Pamela Price announced Monday she had cut plea deals to drop murder charges for two of the three men accused of killing a man who was found strangled to death on the side of an Oakland hills road in 2021.Price said the deals reflected that the men had "accepted responsibility for their actions" in the home invasion robbery, kidnapping and murder of Benjamin Hemmann, whose body was discovered in the Oakland hills bound, gagged and strangled to death.
