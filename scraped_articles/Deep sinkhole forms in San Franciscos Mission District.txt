Deep sinkhole forms in San Francisco's Mission District
-----
A sinkhole appeared on Wednesday in San Francisco's Mission District, leading to two popped tires and a repair team on the scene.An SUV was driving on 14th Street between Ramona Avenue and Guerrero when the passengers heard a noise like a "bomb went off," NBC Bay Area's Sergio Quintana reported. When they got out of the car, they saw the hole behind them.
