Review: Giuseppe Tornatore’s ‘Ennio,’ about film composer Ennio Morricone, kicks off retrospective
-----
Film composer Ennio Morricone (1928-2020) wrote scores for many great movies ("The Battle of Algiers," "Days of Heaven," "Once Upon a Time in America"), and at a certain point, you have to wonder: Maybe this is no coincidence. Maybe Morricone had something to do with what made them great.He also wrote scores for many unremarkable films, so the issue isn't entirely clear cut.
