NorCal man admits to stealing more than 700 pieces of mail in East Bay
-----
A Northern California man was sentenced last month to two years in prison after breaking into a postal truck and stealing 700 pieces of mail, the U.S. Department of Justice announced Tuesday.Under a plea agreement, the Sacramento man admitted to breaking into a U.S. Postal Service mail truck in Castro Valley on Dec. 30, 2022, after approaching the truck in a stolen Hyundai.
The man, 35, said he intentionally broke the lock of the mail truck parked on Redwood Road near Seven Hills
