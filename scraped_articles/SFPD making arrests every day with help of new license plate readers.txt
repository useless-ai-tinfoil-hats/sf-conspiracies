SFPD ‘making arrests every day’ with help of new license plate readers
-----
A new network of high-tech cameras has begun photographing thousands of vehicle license plates as drivers zip through San Francisco intersections, building a vast database of information that police say is already leading to arrests on a daily basis.About 100 of San Francisco's new automated license plate readers are now live, with another 300 to be installed by July.
