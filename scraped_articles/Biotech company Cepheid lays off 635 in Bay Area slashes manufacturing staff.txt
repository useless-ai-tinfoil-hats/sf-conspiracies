Biotech company Cepheid lays off 635 in Bay Area, slashes manufacturing staff
-----
Cepheid, a large biotechnology company known for single-use tests for various diseases like strep throat, is laying off 635 Bay Area employees.The company announced the dramatic cuts in WARN filings on July 24, as is required in the event of mass layoffs under the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act.
