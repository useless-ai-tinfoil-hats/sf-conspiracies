Caltrain fatally strikes 2 pedestrians on Peninsula in separate incidents
-----
Caltrain fatally struck two pedestrians in separate incidents Friday and Saturday on the Peninsula, according to reports.Just before 3 p.m. Friday, a Caltrain struck and killed a pedestrian north of Redwood City Station, KTVU reported. The commuter train was carrying 149 passengers at the time.
Transit police are investigating the death, and information about the victim was not immediately available Saturday.
